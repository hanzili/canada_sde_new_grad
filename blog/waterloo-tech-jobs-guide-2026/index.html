Why UW Co-op Matters for Your Career

  1. Built-in experience: 2 years of work experience by graduation
  2. Network effect: Alumni at every major tech company
  3. Hiring pipeline: Many companies hire their co-ops full-time
  4. Skills development: Real projects, not coffee runs

When to Choose Waterloo

  • You want Google or top-tier startup compensation
  • You prefer smaller city lifestyle
  • You value work-life balance
  • You're a UW student/alumni with local network
  • You want higher savings rate

When to Choose Toronto

  • You want maximum job options
  • You prefer big city lifestyle
  • You're targeting finance/fintech specifically
  • You want more networking opportunities
  • You're international and want larger community
