FAQ

Which companies are best for new grads?

For learning: Big Tech (structured programs, mentorship) For work-life balance: Banks, government For compensation: Big Tech, high-growth startups For job security: Banks, government, large enterprise

Do I need internship experience?

Strongly preferred at competitive companies. DoorDash explicitly requires "at least 2 previous Software Engineering internships or equivalent work experience."

What if I have no internships?

Target: 1. Smaller startups (less competitive) 2. IT consulting firms 3. Government positions 4. Contract-to-hire roles

How many companies should I apply to?

Plan for 100-200+ applications for an average new grad. See Job Application Strategy 2026.
