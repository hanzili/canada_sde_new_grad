Frequently Asked Questions

What does a compliance analyst do at a bank?

Compliance analysts ensure the bank follows regulations like FINTRAC (anti-money laundering), OSFI guidelines, and securities laws. Day-to-day work includes reviewing transactions, investigating alerts, writing policies, and supporting regulatory examinations.

Is risk management a good career for new grads?

Yes. Risk management is one of the fastest-growing areas in Canadian finance. Banks are expanding their risk teams due to increasing regulatory scrutiny. It offers stable employment, good work-life balance compared to IB, and salaries competitive with other finance roles.

What certifications help in risk and compliance?

FRM (Financial Risk Manager) is the gold standard for risk roles. CAMS is essential for AML/compliance. CFA can also be valuable for credit risk roles. Many employers sponsor certification costs.
