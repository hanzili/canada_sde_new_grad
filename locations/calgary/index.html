Jobs in Calgary, Alberta

28 positions available (13 full-time, 15 internships)

Calgary's tech sector is rapidly growing, with energy tech, fintech, and agtech companies leading the way.

Calgary's tech sector has grown significantly as the city diversifies beyond oil and gas. Energy tech companies leverage Alberta's industry expertise, while fintech startups and agtech firms are rapidly expanding. Entry-level salaries range from $60,000-$95,000 CAD—lower than Toronto but offset by Alberta's 0% provincial tax and lower housing costs. Major employers include Neo Financial, Attabotics, and Benevity. The city offers an excellent quality of life with easy access to the Rocky Mountains.

💰 Entry-Level Salary Range: $60,000 - $95,000 CAD
🏢 Top Employers: Neo Financial, Attabotics, Benevity, ATB Financial, Pason, Suncor
