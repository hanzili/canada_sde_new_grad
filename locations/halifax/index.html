Jobs in Halifax, Nova Scotia

15 positions available (11 full-time, 4 internships)

Halifax is Atlantic Canada's tech hub, with ocean tech, gaming studios, and a growing startup scene near world-class universities.

Halifax offers a unique tech scene with specializations in ocean technology, gaming, and defense tech. The city's lower cost of living and relaxed lifestyle attract remote workers and those seeking work-life balance. Dalhousie University provides a steady talent pipeline. Entry-level salaries range from $50,000-$75,000 CAD—lower than major metros but with significantly cheaper housing. Companies like Verafin (now Nasdaq), Proposify, and gaming studios provide opportunities for new grads.

💰 Entry-Level Salary Range: $50,000 - $75,000 CAD
🏢 Top Employers: Verafin, Proposify, IBM, CGI, DHX Media
