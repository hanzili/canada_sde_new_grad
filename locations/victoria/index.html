Jobs in Victoria, British Columbia

5 positions available (4 full-time, 1 internships)

Victoria offers a unique tech lifestyle with ocean tech, clean energy, and numerous remote-friendly companies on Vancouver Island.

Victoria offers an enviable lifestyle with mild weather, ocean access, and a growing tech scene. Specializations include ocean technology, clean energy, and government IT (BC provincial government). Many companies embrace remote work, making Victoria attractive for those seeking work-life balance. Entry-level salaries range from $55,000-$85,000 CAD. The University of Victoria provides local talent, and the smaller community offers easier networking than big cities.

💰 Entry-Level Salary Range: $55,000 - $85,000 CAD
🏢 Top Employers: AbeBooks (Amazon), Pixel Union, LlamaZOO, BC Government, Vivid Solutions
