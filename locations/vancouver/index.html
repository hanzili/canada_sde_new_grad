Jobs in Vancouver, British Columbia

68 positions available (41 full-time, 27 internships)

Vancouver offers a thriving tech scene with companies like Amazon, Microsoft, EA, and a growing startup ecosystem.

Vancouver is Canada's second-largest tech hub, known for its strong gaming industry, Amazon's massive presence, and Microsoft's growing campus. The city offers a unique combination of tech opportunities and exceptional quality of life. Entry-level salaries range from $65,000-$110,000 CAD. The BC Tech Hub and downtown core host hundreds of startups. Vancouver is also attractive for its pathway to Canadian permanent residency through the BC PNP Tech Pilot program, which offers expedited immigration processing.

💰 Entry-Level Salary Range: $65,000 - $110,000 CAD
🏢 Top Employers: Amazon, Microsoft, EA, SAP, Slack, Hootsuite, Telus, Clio

Frequently Asked Questions about Jobs in Vancouver

How does Vancouver compare to Toronto for tech jobs?

Vancouver has fewer total jobs but strong specializations in gaming, Amazon, and Microsoft. Salaries are slightly lower but cost of living is similar. Quality of life is often cited as better.

What is the BC PNP Tech Pilot?

The BC Provincial Nominee Program Tech Pilot offers expedited immigration processing (2-3 months) for tech workers in eligible occupations. It's a major advantage for international new grads.
