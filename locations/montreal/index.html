Jobs in Montreal, Quebec

65 positions available (30 full-time, 35 internships)

Montreal is a world leader in AI and gaming, hosting Mila, Ubisoft, Google AI, and a vibrant French-English tech community.

Montreal has emerged as a global AI capital, home to Mila (one of the world's top AI research institutes), Google Brain, Meta AI, and Samsung AI. The gaming industry is also massive with Ubisoft, WB Games, and EA having major studios. Entry-level salaries range from $60,000-$100,000 CAD—lower than Toronto, but cost of living is 20-30% less. While French is important for some roles, many tech companies operate primarily in English. The city's affordable lifestyle, vibrant culture, and strong universities make it especially attractive for new grads.

💰 Entry-Level Salary Range: $60,000 - $100,000 CAD
🏢 Top Employers: Google AI, Mila, Ubisoft, Meta AI, Microsoft, SAP, Morgan Stanley, CN

Frequently Asked Questions about Jobs in Montreal

Do I need to speak French for tech jobs in Montreal?

Not always. Many tech companies operate in English. However, French is an asset and required for some roles, especially in smaller companies or client-facing positions.

Is Montreal good for AI/ML careers?

Absolutely! Montreal is a global AI hub with Mila, Google Brain, Meta AI, and Samsung AI. It has one of the highest concentrations of AI researchers in the world.
