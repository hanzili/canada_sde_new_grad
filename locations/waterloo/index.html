Jobs in Waterloo, Ontario

9 positions available (2 full-time, 7 internships)

Waterloo is home to the University of Waterloo, Google's Canadian engineering hub, and countless successful startups.

Waterloo punches far above its weight in tech, thanks to the University of Waterloo's renowned co-op program. Google's Canadian engineering headquarters is located here, alongside Intel, SAP, and hundreds of startups. The region has produced unicorns like Kik, Vidyard, and ApplyBoard. Entry-level salaries range from $65,000-$110,000 CAD. The strong university pipeline means excellent networking opportunities and a young, vibrant tech community. Many students stay after graduation due to the strong job market and lower cost of living compared to Toronto.

💰 Entry-Level Salary Range: $65,000 - $110,000 CAD
🏢 Top Employers: Google, Intel, SAP, Shopify, ApplyBoard, Vidyard, Communitech
