Jobs in Winnipeg, Manitoba

5 positions available (3 full-time, 2 internships)

Winnipeg features a diverse tech sector including fintech, agtech, and aerospace, with a lower cost of living than major metros.

Winnipeg offers a small but growing tech scene with strengths in fintech, insurance tech, and agricultural technology. The extremely affordable cost of living means your salary goes further here than almost anywhere else in Canada. Entry-level salaries range from $50,000-$70,000 CAD. Companies like Bold Commerce, Skip The Dishes (now Just Eat), and agricultural tech firms provide opportunities. The city is also home to a growing aerospace sector.

💰 Entry-Level Salary Range: $50,000 - $70,000 CAD
🏢 Top Employers: Bold Commerce, SkipTheDishes, Canada Life, Great-West Life, Magellan Aerospace
