London, Ontario has a growing tech sector anchored by digital health, insurance tech, and Western University talent.

London's tech sector is growing around digital health, fintech, and insurance technology. Western University provides local talent, and the city offers significantly lower costs than Toronto with good highway access to the GTA. Entry-level salaries range from $50,000-$75,000 CAD. Companies like Digital Extremes (gaming) and numerous health tech startups provide opportunities.
