Frequently Asked Questions about Remote Tech Jobs

Do remote jobs pay the same as in-office jobs?

It varies. Some companies pay the same regardless of location, while others adjust for local cost of living. Shopify and many tech companies pay Toronto-equivalent salaries for remote workers anywhere in Canada.

How do I find remote-first companies?

Look for companies explicitly advertising 'remote-first' or 'distributed' cultures. Shopify, Automattic (WordPress), GitLab, and many startups are fully remote. Also check job boards like WeWorkRemotely and RemoteOK.
