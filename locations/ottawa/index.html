Frequently Asked Questions about Ottawa Tech Jobs

Are there many government tech jobs in Ottawa?

Yes! Federal government departments like CRA, StatsCan, and ESDC have large IT teams. These roles offer job security, pension, and good work-life balance, though may pay less than private sector.

What is Kanata North?

Kanata North is one of North America's largest tech parks, housing over 500 companies including Nokia, Ciena, and many startups. It's sometimes called 'Silicon Valley North.'
