Quebec City blends historic charm with modern tech, featuring gaming studios, insurance tech, and government IT positions.

Quebec City combines rich history with a growing tech sector. The gaming industry is strong with Beenox, Frima, and Ubisoft. Insurance tech companies like Beneva provide stable employment. Government IT positions are also available at the provincial level.
