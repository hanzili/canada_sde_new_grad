Jobs in Hamilton, Ontario

6 positions available (2 full-time, 4 internships)

Hamilton's tech scene is booming with steel city reinvention, health tech innovation, and proximity to the Toronto corridor.

Hamilton is undergoing a tech renaissance as the city reinvents itself. Health tech and life sciences are particularly strong, anchored by McMaster University's research. Proximity to Toronto (45 minutes by GO Train) provides access to GTA opportunities while offering much lower housing costs. Entry-level salaries range from $55,000-$80,000 CAD. The Innovation Factory and growing startup ecosystem provide opportunities for new grads.

💰 Entry-Level Salary Range: $55,000 - $80,000 CAD
🏢 Top Employers: ArcelorMittal Dofasco, McMaster Innovation Park, Mohawk College, RBC Hamilton
