Tech Jobs in Edmonton, Alberta

2 positions available (1 full-time, 1 internships)

Edmonton offers a growing tech ecosystem with strengths in AI, clean energy tech, and government innovation initiatives.

Edmonton's tech sector is anchored by the University of Alberta's world-class AI research, including the Alberta Machine Intelligence Institute (Amii). The city offers a lower cost of living than Calgary with growing opportunities in AI, clean tech, and gaming. Entry-level salaries range from $55,000-$85,000 CAD. BioWare (a major gaming studio) and growing startups provide opportunities for new grads. Alberta's lack of provincial sales tax is an added financial benefit.
