New grad salaries typically range from $70,000-$120,000 CAD, with top companies offering $100,000-$150,000+ including stock and bonuses. Frequently Asked Questions about Toronto Tech Jobs

What is the average salary for new grad software engineers in Toronto?

Entry-level software engineers in Toronto typically earn $70,000-$90,000 CAD. Top tech companies like Google, Amazon, and Shopify offer $100,000-$150,000+ with stock and bonuses.

Is Toronto a good city for new grad tech jobs?

Yes! Toronto has the highest concentration of tech jobs in Canada with over 400,000 tech workers. Major tech companies, banks, and startups actively hire new grads year-round.

What areas of Toronto have the most tech companies?

Downtown Toronto (King West, Queen West), MaRS Discovery District, and the Financial District have the highest concentration. North York and Scarborough also have growing tech presence.
