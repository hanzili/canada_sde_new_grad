Jobs in Toronto, Ontario

237 positions available (90 full-time, 147 internships)

Toronto is Canada's largest tech hub, home to companies like Shopify, RBC, TD, and hundreds of startups in the MaRS Discovery District.

Toronto's tech ecosystem employs over 400,000 workers, making it one of North America's fastest-growing tech markets. The city hosts Canadian headquarters for Google, Microsoft, Amazon, and Meta, alongside homegrown giants like Shopify and Wealthsimple. New grad salaries typically range from $70,000-$120,000 CAD, with top companies offering $100,000-$150,000+ including stock and bonuses. The MaRS Discovery District, one of the world's largest innovation hubs, houses over 150 startups and provides excellent networking opportunities.

💰 Entry-Level Salary Range: $70,000 - $120,000 CAD
🏢 Top Employers: Shopify, Google, Amazon, RBC, TD, Meta, Wealthsimple, Microsoft

Frequently Asked Questions about Jobs in Toronto

What is the average salary for new grad software engineers in Toronto?

Entry-level software engineers in Toronto typically earn $70,000-$90,000 CAD. Top tech companies like Google, Amazon, and Shopify offer $100,000-$150,000+ with stock and bonuses.

Is Toronto a good city for new grad tech jobs?

Yes! Toronto has the highest concentration of tech jobs in Canada with over 400,000 tech workers. Major tech companies, banks, and startups actively hire new grads year-round.

What areas of Toronto have the most tech companies?

Downtown Toronto (King West, Queen West), MaRS Discovery District, and the Financial District have the highest concentration. North York and Scarborough also have growing tech presence.
