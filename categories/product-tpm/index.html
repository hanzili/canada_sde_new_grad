Frequently Asked Questions

Can new grads get Product Manager roles?

It's competitive but possible through Associate Product Manager (APM) programs at companies like Google, Microsoft, and Meta. Strong internship experience or a technical background helps.

What's the difference between PM and TPM?

Product Managers own product strategy and roadmap. Technical Program Managers coordinate complex technical projects across teams. Both require technical understanding.
