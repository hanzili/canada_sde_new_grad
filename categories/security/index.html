Frequently Asked Questions

What certifications are most valuable for cybersecurity in Canada?

CompTIA Security+ is the best starting point. OSCP is valued for penetration testing roles. CISSP is for senior positions. Many companies sponsor certification costs.

Can I transition into security from software development?

Absolutely! Many security engineers come from development backgrounds. Your coding skills are valuable for application security, security automation, and building security tools.
