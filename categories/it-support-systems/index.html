Frequently Asked Questions

Is IT Support a good career path?

Yes! IT Support provides hands-on experience with systems and networking. Many professionals transition to DevOps, cloud engineering, or cybersecurity after building foundational skills.

What certifications help for IT Support roles?

CompTIA A+ and Network+ are excellent starting points. Microsoft and AWS certifications are valuable for cloud-focused roles.
