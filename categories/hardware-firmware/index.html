Frequently Asked Questions

What education do I need for hardware engineering in Canada?

Electrical Engineering or Computer Engineering degrees are typically required. Some firmware roles accept CS degrees with embedded systems experience.

Which Canadian cities have the most hardware jobs?

Ottawa has AMD, Nokia, and defense contractors. Waterloo has Intel, Qualcomm. Toronto has various consumer electronics and automotive suppliers.
