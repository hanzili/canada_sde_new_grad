Frequently Asked Questions

Does Hanzilla include non-software engineering jobs?

Yes. The engineering page is built for Canadian students and new grads across mechanical, electrical, civil, industrial, environmental, manufacturing, construction, and other non-software engineering paths.

What should engineering students look for in entry-level postings?

Look for EIT eligibility, co-op terms, field/site requirements, CAD or analysis tools, safety certifications, province-specific licensing context, and whether the employer offers mentorship toward professional engineering experience.
