How to use this engineering jobs board

This daily-updated page is built for Canadian students and recent grads searching for engineering internships and co-op roles: mechanical, electrical, civil, industrial, manufacturing, environmental, hardware, and project engineering roles. Hanzilla groups engineering student roles across consulting firms, utilities, manufacturers, infrastructure companies, public agencies, and startups instead of only surfacing software jobs.

1.Use this page for Canadian engineering internships, co-ops, EIT-track roles, junior engineering positions, and project/coordinator roles with technical teams.
2.Browse opportunities across civil, mechanical, electrical, industrial, environmental, manufacturing, construction, energy, and hardware-adjacent teams.
3.Check city pages for Toronto, Vancouver, Calgary, Edmonton, Ottawa, Waterloo, and remote Canada roles when licensing, site work, or hybrid requirements matter.
4.Current listings include active openings from employers such as Bombardier, Dometic Marine Canada, Inc., Pratt & Whitney, MDA Space, MDA when those roles are available.

Frequently Asked Questions

Does Hanzilla include non-software engineering jobs?

Yes. The engineering page is built for Canadian students and new grads across mechanical, electrical, civil, industrial, environmental, manufacturing, construction, and other non-software engineering paths.

What should engineering students look for in entry-level postings?

Look for EIT eligibility, co-op terms, field/site requirements, CAD or analysis tools, safety certifications, province-specific licensing context, and whether the employer offers mentorship toward professional engineering experience.
