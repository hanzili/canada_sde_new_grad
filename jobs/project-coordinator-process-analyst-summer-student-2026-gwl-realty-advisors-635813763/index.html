About the Role

Coordinate projects, document meetings, support budget processes

About GWL Realty Advisors

Real estate firm; budget process improvement focus

Full Description

GWLRA is a leader in both the commercial and multi-residential real estate industries. We believe in generating value by creating vibrant, sustainable communities that engage, excite, and inspire. Our people live our values through the work they do and the results they deliver. Our core values are keeping our word, working better together and embracing diversity.

Working in a collaborative team-based environment, the Project Coordinator & Process Analyst will support the Budget Process Improvement team during the implementation of the new budget process.

Responsibilities

  • Working with the Project Manager to coordinate resources and project-related activities
  • Documenting project meetings and activities
  • Supporting the implementation of the new budget process, which can include end-user testing, process documentation, developing process dashboards and training materials
  • Engaging with stakeholders to monitor adoption, gather improvement feedback and overall benefits

Qualifications

  • Enrolled in an Industrial Engineering program or equivalent and planning to return to school fall 2026
  • Eager for exposure to best-practice real estate processes and procedures
  • Proficient in Microsoft Visio, Word, Excel, and Outlook, experience using Power BI would be an asset
  • Detail-oriented and able to manage multiple priorities within tight deadlines
  • Appreciation and willingness to work within a standard set of parameters
  • Excellent communication skills and initiative that lead to effective solutions in a deadline-driven environment

The base salary for this position is $21.00 hourly.

What We Offer

  • Half-day every Friday before a long weekend
  • A friendly, welcoming, and supportive culture
  • Many social and team events!
