About the Role

Analyse marketing data to optimise campaigns

About VIR Properties

Property and marketing analytics-focused team

Full Description

We are looking for a detail-oriented and analytical Business Analyst to join our growing marketing team. This role is ideal for someone who enjoys working with data, identifying trends, and helping optimise marketing strategies to drive business growth.

You will support decision-making by analysing campaign performance, market insights, and operational processes, helping the team deliver more effective marketing initiatives.

Key Responsibilities

  • Analyse marketing campaign performance and identify key insights
  • Support business planning and growth strategies with data-driven recommendations
  • Conduct market research and competitor analysis
  • Track KPIs and prepare reports for internal teams and clients
  • Identify opportunities to improve processes and campaign effectiveness
  • Collaborate with marketing and sales teams to align objectives
  • Assist with forecasting and performance tracking

What We’re Looking For

  • Strong analytical and problem-solving skills
  • Excellent attention to detail and organisational abilities
  • Good communication skills with the ability to present insights clearly
  • Comfortable working with data and performance metrics
  • Ability to work in a fast-paced, team-oriented environment
  • Interest in marketing analytics, business strategy, or data analysis

What We Offer

  • Ongoing training and professional development
  • Supportive and collaborative team environment
  • Clear progression opportunities within the company
  • Hands-on experience analysing marketing campaigns
  • Opportunity to influence strategy and business growth

Ideal Candidate This Role Is Perfect For Someone Who Is

  • Analytical, curious, and detail-oriented
  • Interested in business strategy and marketing performance
  • Comfortable working with data and reporting
  • Looking to grow within a fast-paced marketing environment
