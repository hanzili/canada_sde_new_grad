Full Description

About Us

At RMES Inc., we’re proud to be one of the fastest-growing organizations in the Kitchener-Waterloo region.

Position Summary
We’re hiring Sales / Customer Service Representatives to join our growing team. This is a full-time, entry-level opportunity focused on direct business-to-consumer sales.

Help us Representing Rogers and our meal-kit partner in the region.

Our company is built on a simple mission to develop leaders, promote from within, and create real opportunities for professional growth. As we expand into new markets, we’re looking for motivated, positive individuals who are ready to build a rewarding career with us.

Why Work With Us

  • Continuous hands-on training and mentorship
  • 90-Day Success Bonus
  • Weekly and monthly performance incentives
  • Supportive, energetic team environment
  • Clear paths for career advancement
  • Flexible scheduling options
  • Company social events and team outings
  • Free on-site parking

In this role, you’ll act as the main point of contact between our clients and their customers helping match them with customized telecom or meal kit solutions that fit their needs.

If you enjoy connecting with people, solving problems, and working in a team-driven atmosphere, this could be the perfect fit for you.

Key Responsibilities

  • Interact with residential customers to identify needs and recommend the right products or services
  • Deliver professional, high-quality service while addressing questions or concerns
  • Build rapport and long-term relationships through field and in-office engagement
  • Work collaboratively with teammates to achieve individual and collective goals
  • Keep accurate notes and client information up to date
  • Take part in ongoing development sessions to strengthen your skills

What You Bring

  • Excellent communication and interpersonal abilities
  • A people-first mindset with a genuine desire to help others
  • Motivated, dependable, and results-oriented attitude
  • Strong organization and time management skills
  • Open to learning and growing within a fast-paced environment

Compensation

  • Pay based on experience and performance
  • Average weekly earnings: $820 – $1,100+
  • Additional bonuses and rewards for achieving milestones
