About the Role

We’re looking for a reliable, detail-oriented Junior Accountant to support our day-to-day operations. This role is a mix of administrative work, bookkeeping, and entry-level accounting, and is ideal for:

  • Accounting students looking for hands-on experience (volunteer/co-op style experience can be considered), or
  • Recent graduates in accounting with some job experience, or
  • Someone early in their accounting career looking for a steady part-time or long-term role.

Key Responsibilities

Bookkeeping / Accounting Support

  • Enter and reconcile transactions (bank/credit card)
  • Assist with accounts payable and accounts receivable (invoices, payments, follow-ups)
  • Prepare and organize documents for month-end / year-end
  • Maintain accurate records and filing (digital + paper)
  • Assist with basic reporting and spreadsheets (Excel)

Administrative Support

  • Scanning, uploading, and organizing client/company documents
  • Email follow-ups and basic client communication (templates provided)
  • Scheduling, document requests, and general office support
  • Maintaining checklists and keeping tasks on track

Who You Are

  • Currently studying Accounting/Business (college/university) OR completed accounting studies
  • Basic understanding of bookkeeping and accounting (entry-level is fine)
  • Strong attention to detail and organization
  • Comfortable with computers, email, and spreadsheets (Excel)
  • Reliable, professional, and willing to learn

Nice to Have (Not Required)

  • Experience with bookkeeping software (e.g., QuickBooks)
  • Previous admin/office experience
  • Experience with data entry, payroll support, or tax-related admin
  • Can speak Punjabi & English

What We Offer

  • Practical experience across admin + bookkeeping + accounting
  • Training and clear processes/checklists
  • Supportive environment with learning opportunities
  • Opportunity to grow into a longer-term position
