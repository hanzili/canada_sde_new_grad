Full Description

Lakeridge Auto Gallery is looking to hire a full time Junior Accounting Administrator.

Job Summary:

We are seeking a detail-oriented and motivated individual to join our finance team. This position involves overseeing all accounts payable and accounts receivable activities, ensuring accurate and timely processing of invoices, payments, and collections.

The Junior Accounting Administrator enters vendor invoices into the accounting system, reconciles with vendors’ statements and prepares vendors payments, including employee expense disbursement, and reconciles daily bank deposit. The Junior Accounting Administrator ensures that the company’s daily accounting functions run accurately and effectively.

If you are passionate about maintaining financial accuracy and integrity, we encourage you to apply.

Duties and Responsibilities

Accounts Payable, generally including:

  • Posting of invoices
  • Preparing payments after reconciling vendors’ statement, if required
  • Following up on disputed items with vendors and internal departments
  • Pay on time as per payment term
  • Send weekly AR reports and follow up with service and parts managers on subsequent collection
  • Post daily licensing and fuel
  • Reconcile company credit cards on a monthly basis
  • Process and reconcile daily bank deposits
  • Enter and balance cash invoices
  • Process cheque requisitions
  • Filing as required
  • Mail cheques upon approval
  • Other projects as needed

Job Requirements

  • Strong oral and written communication skills
  • Excellent interpersonal skills
  • Proficient with: Microsoft Office (Intermediate or higher Microsoft Excel skill required)
  • Organized and detail oriented
  • Excellent time management
  • Ability to take ownership of problems with a positive attitude and bring them to completion
  • Ability to work in a team environment to achieve common goals
  • Pleasant, polite, and professional
