Full Description

Join us at Sylvite Holdings Inc! We are seeking a dedicated IT Help Desk Technician to join our team.

Reporting to the IT Manager, this position is responsible for resolving computer and system-related issues to ensure end users can effectively perform their work. Key duties include receiving, prioritizing, documenting, and actively addressing help desk requests. Problem resolution may require both technical troubleshooting and hands-on support.

**About Sylvite**

Sylvite, as Canada's largest independently owned fertilizer supplier, delivers competitive solutions in the agriculture supply chain. Our services encompass full-service Ag retail, specialized fertilizers, industrial material sourcing and handling, as well as a robust transportation, warehousing, and logistics division. We strive to ensure a seamless product flow, effectively linking agricultural production with efficient distribution.
