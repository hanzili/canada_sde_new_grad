Position Summary We are looking for a Finance Intern for the months of May 2026 to August 2026. Positions will be based out of our Head Office in Montreal. You will have the opportunity to partner on key projects from various facets of our business, such as financial reporting and analysis, accounting, or internal audit. This position is an existing vacancy.

A Little Bit About Us Nestlé Nespresso SA is the pioneer and reference for highest-quality portioned coffee. The company works with more than 120,000 farmers in 15 countries through its AAA Sustainable Quality™ Program to embed sustainability practices on farms and the surrounding landscapes. Launched in 2003 in collaboration with the NGO Rainforest Alliance, the program helps to improve the yield and quality of harvests, ensuring a sustainable supply of high-quality coffee and improving livelihoods of farmers and their communities.

In 2022, Nespresso has achieved B Corp™ certification - joining an international movement of 4,900 purpose-led businesses that meet B Corp’s high standards of social and environmental responsibility and transparency.

Headquartered in Vevey, Switzerland, Nespresso operates in 81 countries and has over 13'000 employees. In 2021, it operated a global retail network of 802 boutiques. For more information, visit the Nespresso corporate website: www.nestle-nespresso.com

What will make you successful? As the successful candidate, you will be a proactive problem solver, driving innovation with new or unique solutions. You will inspire those around you and demonstrate a proactive approach in your work. You can already see the story behind the numbers and will have the opportunity to act like an owner by bringing value for our brands and customers. Additionally, you are a highly organized individual who isn’t afraid to ask questions, be continuously curious and inquisitive.

You will also:

  • Bilingualism in English and French language skills are a requirement, as this position requires collaboration with stakeholders across the Canadian market and/or worldwide.
  • Be currently enrolled in an undergraduate program, majoring in Accounting, Finance, Economics, Business or any other related field.
  • Have involvement in extracurricular activities which demonstrate initiative and leadership.
  • Have strong communication, analytical, organizational and interpersonal skills.
  • Agile, innovative, and results focused individual who is comfortable working within a highly collaborative environment.
  • Strong computer proficiency (mainly in Excel – Vlookup, Pivot table, sumif).

We have a friendly, supportive team with a coaching and mentoring environment. There are real opportunities for future development and progression – this really could be a move towards the exciting Finance career you’ve always wanted.

Benefits

  • Flexible and hybrid work arrangements
  • Excellent training and development programs as well as opportunities to grow within the company
  • Up to 50% off – Nespresso Coffee Machine, Capsules and accessories
  • Access to the Discount Company store with Nestlé, Nespresso, and Purina products (Located across various Nestle offices/sites)
  • Additional discounts on a variety of products and services offered by our preferred vendors and partnerships
