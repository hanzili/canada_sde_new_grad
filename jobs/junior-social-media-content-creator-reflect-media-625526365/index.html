About Reflect Media

Digital marketing agency focused on client growth

Full Description

We’re looking for a creative and enthusiastic Junior Social Media Content Creator to join our growing digital marketing team. You will be responsible for supporting our social media team by creating engaging content for multiple client accounts, with a strong focus on short-form video, Reels and trend-based content.

This role is a key part of our social media workflow, working closely with social media managers, designers and account managers to bring creative ideas to life. You will help maintain high-quality, consistent content across client accounts while experimenting with new formats, trends and creative approaches.

This role is fast-paced and hands-on. If you’re highly organized, comfortable working independently and excited to push creative ideas forward in a collaborative agency environment, this role is for you.

Role Responsibilities:

  • Create scroll-stopping social content for multiple client accounts, including static posts, carousels, Stories and Reels
  • Develop, adapt and optimize creative assets for Meta Ads and Google Display and Demand Gen campaigns
  • Build polished, on-brand social assets using Canva and brand guidelines
  • Edit short-form video content for TikTok, Instagram, Facebook and YouTube Shorts
  • Stay ahead of trends, audio and formats and proactively bring fresh creative ideas to the team
  • Support social media managers with scheduling and content execution
  • Manage tasks and deadlines in ClickUp to keep projects on track
  • Test new creative ideas and refine content based on performance insights and feedback
  • Deliver high-quality, accurate and on-brand content across all assigned accounts

Keys To Success In This Role:

  • Socially Plugged In:

Naturally active on social media and proactive in bringing ideas forward - Platform Curiosity:

Actively follows platform updates, trends and emerging formats - Iterative Mindset:

Comfortable experimenting, learning quickly and improving flopped content - Attention to Detail:

Strong time management skills with a sharp eye for the small details - Clear Communication:

Direct and consistent with the team and account managers - Agency Energy:

Thrive in a fast-paced, collaborative and ever-changing environment - Ready For Feedback:

Receptive to feedback and able to turn revisions around efficiently - Reliable Ownership:

Organized and capable of managing the needs of multiple client accounts - Deadline Driven:

Consistent punctuality and ownership of daily workload

Role Requirements:

  • 1–2 years of experience creating content for social media; agency experience is an asset
  • Strong interest in Canva and social media trends, Reels and short-form video
  • Familiarity with ClickUp or similar project management tools is an asset

Job Details

  • Approximately 10–15 hours per week to start
  • Fully remote
  • Must be based in the Lower Mainland, Okanagan or Calgary
  • Opportunity to grow hours and responsibility over time

About Reflect Media

Reflect Media is a full-service digital marketing agency that brings strategy, creativity and execution together under one roof. We partner with purpose-driven businesses to strengthen their digital presence, connect with the right audiences and drive measurable growth.

Our work is grounded in thoughtful strategy and real-world performance. Every digital touchpoint is planned with intent, from campaign execution to content and optimization, ensuring our clients’ stories reach the people who matter most.

We are a team of digital marketing specialists, ad pros, social media diehards, SEO experts, PR powerhouses, designers and copywriters, all united by a shared focus and passion for generating results.

Send your resume and a link to your portfolio (or your best social work) to careers@reflectmedia.ca.

Note: We’ll be reaching out to approved applicants currently based in the Lower Mainland, Okanagan, or Calgary.
