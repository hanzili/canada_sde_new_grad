About DAFK AI

Full Description

Company Description

DAFK AI is a Vancouver-based AI technology company building real-time communication tools that help people and businesses overcome language barriers. The company focuses on voice translation, multilingual communication, and practical AI solutions that make conversations across languages more natural, efficient, and accessible.

Role Description

This is a full-time remote role for an AI Product & Operations Intern at DAFK AI. In this role, you will support the development and improvement of AI-driven communication products, especially in real-time voice translation and multilingual user experiences. Responsibilities include product testing, performance analysis, user feedback review, research support, and collaboration with cross-functional teams on product and growth initiatives.

Qualifications

  • Strong analytical and problem-solving skills
  • Basic understanding of AI, machine learning, and large language models
  • Familiarity with model testing, prompt evaluation, and output analysis
  • Interest in natural language processing, speech technology, or real-time AI systems
  • Ability to help test and improve AI-powered product features
  • Comfort with APIs, dashboards, spreadsheets, or basic scripting for analysis
  • Strong written and verbal communication skills
  • Ability to work well with product, engineering, and operations teams
  • Strong interest in AI, multilingual technology, and communication tools
  • Degree in Computer Science, Data Science, Software Engineering, or a related field is preferred
