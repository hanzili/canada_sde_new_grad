What we offer:

At Magna, you can expect an engaging and dynamic environment where you can help to develop industry-leading automotive technologies. We invest in our employees, providing them with the support and resources they need to succeed. As a member of our global team, you can expect exciting, varied responsibilities as well as a wide range of development prospects. Group Summary:

The Mechatronics, Mirrors and Lighting group specializes in automotive technologies that are driving the future of mobility. Combining a deep systems knowledge to develop unique vehicle access experiences, intelligent visions systems and advanced automotive lighting technologies, MML's expertise light the path to innovation, safety and styling.

Job Responsibilities:

SALARY RANGE: $21.00 - $23/Hourly

RESPONSIBILITIES:

  • Maintain strict confidentiality of all information obtained, written or verbal, within the Human Resources Department.
  • Develop an auditing timing process for our HR teams to monitor and update key employee data within our HRIS systems.
  • Assist in organizing communications, training, workshops, and employee engagement activities.
  • Assist with the full recruitment cycle for internal/external postings from job requisitions to issuing employment contracts.
  • Prepare Word, Excel, and PowerPoint documents for site-wide communications.
  • Participate in HRIS projects and initiatives as needed.
  • Review, update, and create documentation as required.
  • Additional duties and responsibilities as assigned

QUALIFICATIONS:

  • Working towards post-secondary education in Human Resources, Business Administration, Social Sciences, or a related discipline

KNOWLEDGE, SKILLS, & ABILITIES

  • Strong organizational and time management skills.
  • Excellent communication and interpersonal abilities.
  • Detail-oriented with a high level of accuracy.
  • Ability to handle confidential information with discretion.
  • Enthusiastic team player with a positive attitude.
  • Highly motivated to work, learn, and teach.
  • Proficient Computer knowledge and skills, Microsoft Office (Word, Excel, PowerPoint) preferred.
  • Professional Communication skills (written and verbal) preferred.
  • Strong technical and analytical competence in select areas of technology preferred.

CORE COMPETENCIES

  • Think Strategically
  • Act Decisively
  • Champion Change
  • Inspire Others
  • Be Agile and Adapt
