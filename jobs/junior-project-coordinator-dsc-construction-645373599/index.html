Full Description

About DSC Construction

DSC Construction is a growing construction company specializing in residential and commercial projects in Edmonton and surrounding areas. We are committed to delivering high-quality workmanship, maintaining strong client relationships, and completing projects safely and efficiently.

Job Summary

DSC Construction is looking for a motivated Junior Project Coordinator to support our project management team in planning, coordinating, and executing construction projects. This is an excellent opportunity for someone early in their career who wants to gain hands-on experience in project coordination within the construction industry.

Responsibilities

  • Assist Project Managers with planning and coordinating construction projects
  • Track project timelines, tasks, and deliverables
  • Prepare project documentation, reports, and meeting notes
  • Coordinate with subcontractors, suppliers, and internal teams
  • Help manage project schedules and update project tracking tools
  • Monitor project progress and report potential delays or issues
  • Assist with permits, contracts, and project files
  • Support site visits and project meetings when required

Qualifications

  • Diploma or Bachelor’s degree in Construction Management, Business, Engineering, or a related field
  • 0–2 years of experience in project coordination or construction (co-op/internship experience welcome)
  • Strong organizational and time management skills
  • Excellent communication and teamwork abilities
  • Proficiency with Microsoft Office (Excel, Word, Outlook)
  • Familiarity with project management software is an asset
  • Valid driver’s license is preferred

Benefits

  • Competitive salary
  • Opportunities for training and career growth
  • Supportive team environment
  • Exposure to real construction projects

Schedule

  • Monday to Friday
