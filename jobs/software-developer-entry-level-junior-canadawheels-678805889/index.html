About CanadaWheels

CanadaWheels: leading automotive e-commerce retailer.

Full Description

Company Description

CanadaWheels, founded in 2012 and headquartered in Ottawa, Ontario, is Canada’s leading online retailer of automotive wheels, tires, and parts. Recognized with over 19 national awards, including Deloitte Technology Fast 500™, the company utilizes advanced fitment technology, real-time supplier integrations, and an innovative e-commerce platform to simplify and enhance the automotive shopping experience. Having served hundreds of thousands of customers and achieved over $100M in lifetime sales, CanadaWheels continues to expand globally with ventures such as USAWheels.com and plans for European and Asian markets. Dedicated to innovation and transparency, CanadaWheels is shaping the future of automotive e-commerce.

Role Description

This is a full-time, on-site role located in Ottawa, ON, for an entry-level or junior Software Developer. The Software Developer will be responsible for designing, developing, testing, and maintaining software solutions to enhance the company’s technology-driven e-commerce platform. Daily responsibilities include collaborating with cross-functional teams to implement new features, troubleshooting and resolving software issues, and contributing to scalable back-end web development. The role offers the opportunity to work with innovative tools and technologies in a fast-paced environment.

Responsibilities

•    Design, develop, and maintain features across our Rea﻿ct front end and PHP back end

•    Test and improve existing code; help keep the system stable and performant

•    Troubleshoot, debug, and support the live production system

•    Participate in code review, technical discussions, and team planning

•    Take on increasing ownership and scope over time

What we’re looking for

•    Knowledge of at least one modern programming language (e.g., JavaScript, PHP, Python, Java, C#)

•    Experience building web applications with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript

•    Solid grasp of algorithms, data structures, and database/SQL fundamentals

•    Familiarity with software development best practices: object-oriented programming, writing reusable and maintainable code

•    Experience with version control (Git)

•    Self-learner, comfortable picking up new languages, frameworks, and platforms

•    An interest in understanding how things work, not just how to use them

•    Clear written and verbal English

•    Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science or related discipline, or equivalent experience

Nice to have

•    Experience with React, PHP, or Magento

•    Familiarity with PHP template engines (PHTML, Zend, Twig, etc.)

•    Experience with cloud platforms (AWS) or containerization (Docker)

•    Experience using AI tools as part of a coding workflow
