About the Role

About J.D. Irving

Full Description

What We Offer

  • Co-op fee reimbursement up to $1000, if eligible
  • Opportunities to take part in job related training and development
  • Growth and progression opportunities within Irving Personal Care and the larger network of the J.D. Irving group of companies
  • Signing incentives for full-time roles for graduating students who have completed a work term within J.D. Irving (if applicable)

Safety

  • Ensure that health and safety guidelines are always followed while working within the facility, including and not limited to the operation of heavy equipment (floor scrubber, etc.).
  • Meet the expectations of all Irving Personal Care employees in following site and enterprise-wide policies and procedure Actively promote an autonomous and compassionate safety culture

Quality Assurance Management

  • Perform routine and non-routine sample testing on production lines using specified methods; verify and accurately report results in systems such as PARCview and ITOP.
  • Identify and act on abnormal results, promptly engaging Operations and Quality leadership to resolve issues; support Hold process for non-conforming products.
  • Conduct quality audits and supports Operator Quality Workmanship Certification and development trials.
  • Supports quality documentation and compliance, including calibration/verification of equipment, raw material inspections, and track-and-trace activities.
  • Facilitate sample requests and interlaboratory testing, ensuring timely mock-ups, shipping, and collaboration on quality improvement initiatives.

Audit Compliance Support

  • Assist in preparation and maintenance of BRCGS audit evidence packs, including document control, training records, calibration certificates, and clause mapping.
  • Conduct internal GMP audits using standardized checklists; document findings, assign corrective actions, and track closure.
  • Participate in mock audits and readiness reviews; maintain CAPA register and ensure timely closure.

Round Robin & Method Validation

  • Coordinates round robin testing for QA technicians; ensure blind sample distribution and unbiased execution.
  • Perform GRR (Gauge Repeatability & Reproducibility) studies and report findings; initiate retraining if bias or variation exceeds limits.
  • Maintain method validation documentation for all QA test methods.

Statistical Analysis & Reporting

  • Collect and validate QA data for statistical analysis.
  • Develop and maintain control charts (X̄-R, I-MR) and monitor process stability.
  • Calculate and report Cp/Cpk for critical-to-quality parameters; escalate when capability falls below targets.
  • Prepare and publish weekly capability reports and recommend corrective actions for process centering.

Document Control & Training

  • Maintain SOP master list with revision history and controlled distribution.
  • Audit training records for QA and Operations teams; ensure 100% compliance for quality-related procedures.

Calibration & Verification

  • Manage calibration schedules and verification logs for QA equipment.
  • Ensure traceability of calibration certificates; perform impact assessments for out-of-tolerance equipment.

Risk Management

  • Support PFMEA updates and maintain CTQ register.
  • Participate in root cause analysis for quality deviations using structured problem-solving tools (5-Why, Ishikawa).

5S

  • Adheres to IPC’s 5S program which includes the standards to maintain cleanliness, organization, and safety at the facility. In addition, it outlines the audit program including zones, frequencies, and responsibilities.

  • Education Required: College Diploma, science preferred

  • Years of Experience Required: <2 years
  • Knowledge of and testing experience in a laboratory environment
  • Basic understanding of statistics (control charts, Cp/Cpk, GRR).
  • Proficiency in Microsoft Excel (pivot tables, formulas).
  • Strong documentation and organizational skills.
  • Familiarity with quality systems (BRCGS/GFSI) and GMP principles.
  • Exposure to PFMEA and HACCP concepts.
  • Ability to analyze data and present findings clearly
