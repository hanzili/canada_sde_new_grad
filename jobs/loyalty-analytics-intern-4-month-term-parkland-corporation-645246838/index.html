About the Role

Analyze campaigns, build KPIs, deliver insights

About Parkland Corporation

Canadian energy retailer with loyalty program insights

Full Description

Description As of November 1, 2025, Sunoco LP has successfully completed its acquisition of Parkland Corporation. For more information, please visit www.sunocolp.com

Salary Range: $20.50-$26.50/hour The Opportunity: The Loyalty Analytics Intern will support the Loyalty team in analyzing campaign and program performance, generating customer insights, and supporting data-driven decision making. During this 4-month term, the student will assist with campaign assessment, KPI reporting, and segmentation analysis to help optimize loyalty offers and engagement strategies.

Responsibilities:

  • Assist with campaign and offer performance analysis to measure effectiveness and identify optimization opportunities.
  • Document campaign performance results and consolidate key learnings into a campaign playbook to inform future marketing strategies.
  • Support A/B testing initiatives for offers, messaging, and customer engagement strategies.
  • Contribute to customer segmentation and profiling by analyzing behavioral patterns within loyalty member groups.
  • Prepare program and customer KPI updates for management and executive audiences.
  • Identify statistical patterns and insights in data using analytics tools such as Excel, SQL, PowerBI and Tableau.
  • Support ad-hoc data analysis requests from Loyalty, Marketing, and Retail business units.
  • Work collaboratively with marketing, loyalty, and analytics teams to ensure insights translate into clear recommendations and business actions.

Education:

  • Currently enrolled in a Canadian post-secondary institution pursuing a degree in Business Analytics, Economics, Commerce, Mathematics, or a related discipline. Preference given to 2nd, 3rd, or 4th year students.

Requirements:

  • Strong analytical mindset with the ability to translate data into meaningful business insights.
  • Advanced Excel skills and familiarity with data analysis techniques.
  • Basic experience with SQL or data querying is considered an asset.
  • Experience with data visualization tools such as Tableau or PowerBI is an asset.
  • Ability to create clear and visually effective presentations using PowerPoint.
  • Strong attention to detail, organization, and problem-solving skills.
  • Excellent communication and interpersonal skills, with the ability to collaborate across teams.
  • Demonstrated leadership through academic, internship, or extracurricular experience.
  • Ability to work in a fast-paced and collaborative environment.
