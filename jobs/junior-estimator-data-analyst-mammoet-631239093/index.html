About the Role

Prepare estimates, analyze data, build dashboards

About Mammoet

Global heavy-lift logistics leader

Full Description

Summary Of The Role Mammoet is looking for a task-oriented and enthusiastic individual to join our Tendering and Estimating team in the Edmonton region. This role will be responsible for the creation of basic estimate packages in alignment with internal bidding protocols as well as collecting, processing, and analyzing data to provide actionable insights for business decision-making. This ensures that the company receives well-considered, comprehensive proposals that meet both customer expectations and internal bidding standards, all within the established timelines.

What You’ll Be Doing Responsibilities (including but not limited to)

  • Conduct all business in a professional, respectful manner
  • Contribute to and implement Tendering/Estimating policy and determine execution strategies in line with the sales strategy
  • Estimates the cost of work and provide quotes to the client
  • Follows up with clients to determine the status of the quoted work and answer any questions they may have
  • Gathering data from various sources (databases, APIs, surveys, etc.), ensuring data accuracy and consistency, and maintaining data systems
  • Developing reports, dashboards, and visualizations to communicate findings to stakeholders, using tools like Power BI, or similar
  • Working with cross-functional teams, including business users and technical teams, to understand requirements, present findings, and collaborate on solutions
  • Identifying opportunities to improve data collection, analysis, and reporting processes, and suggesting solutions for data quality issues
  • Assist with implementation and training of new software system

What You’ll Need Education & Experience

  • Diploma or degree in a related technical field preferred (CET, EIT)
  • Heavy Lift/Crane/Transport technical understanding desired
  • Industrial or Pre-Construction Management experience in similar industry
  • Proficiency in statistical analysis, data mining, and interpretation.
  • Basic level experience in sales systems
  • Knowledge of Power BI is an asset

Competencies Of a Successful Candidate

  • Deciding and Initiating Action
  • Leading and Supervising
  • Analysing
  • Relating and Networking
  • Planning and Organizing
  • Delivering Results and Meeting Customer Expectations
  • Adapting and Responding to Change
  • Coping with Pressure and Setbacks
  • Supporting internal/external customers

What You’ll Get About our Company Mammoet helps clients improve construction efficiency and optimize the uptime of plants and installations. For that purpose, we provide solutions for lifting, transporting, installing, and decommissioning large and heavy structures.

We Provide The Following Services

  • Engineered lifting and transportation services
  • Lifting, transportation, and installation of modular constructions
  • Factory-to-foundation projects
  • Plant turnaround logistics
  • Decommissioning of onshore and offshore installations
  • Trading in new and used equipment worldwide

About Our Region This will be a full-time position operating out of our Edmonton, AB location on a full-time basis.

Benefits Salary will be commensurate based upon education and experience. Mammoet offers competitive wages, health benefits and RRSP contributions. In addition to this, we offer an engaging environment in which a variety of career development and growth opportunities exist to our employees both locally and internationally. Mammoet is an existing benefactor for variety of local and national charity organizations and also provides support for our own employees’ extracurricular activities.

WANT TO KNOW MORE? Mammoet is dedicated to building a workforce that reflects diversity. Mammoet encourages applications from all qualified individuals. We thank all applicants for their interest in Mammoet, however we will only contact those selected for an interview. Applicants who require accommodation due to disability during the selection process should notify human resources when contacted for an interview.
