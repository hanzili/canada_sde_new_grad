About the Role

Prototype, test, and debug electronic PCBs and systems

About ExcelSense Technologies Corp.

Industrial tech leader in autonomous equipment

Full Description

ExcelSense Technologies Corp. is a global leader in the design and manufacturing of maintenance-free self-cleaning cameras and laser platforms for autonomous equipment and remote-monitoring in harsh industrial environments.

ExcelSense is continuing to grow aggressively in 2026 and beyond, along with a number of new product releases on the horizon. Additionally, our core technology is increasingly being adopted in different market sectors such as mining, oil & gas, and autonomous vehicles.

Role Description

We have an exciting opening within our dynamic team for a Junior Electrical Test Engineer. This role presents the opportunity to help the product development team create cutting edge products. This role is ideal for someone with a strong foundation in electrical engineering principles and a passion for prototyping, testing, and continuous learning and improvement. You will work closely with cross-functional teams to validate, debug, and improve our electronic products. The successful candidate should be comfortable working with electronics test tools and equipment such as multimeters and oscilloscopes.

ExcelSense strives to achieve a comfortable, safe, and positive work environment for all of its employees. A successful member of the ExcelSense team will have a great team attitude, with an emphasis on maintaining positive and respectful relationships with all of their colleagues.

Required Skills

  • Bachelors in Electrical Engineering or equivalent
  • Comfortable with electrical engineering theory and lab practices
  • Experience in troubleshooting circuits and performing root-cause analysis
  • Knowledge of common protocols, including Ethernet, I2C, SPI, UART, and USB
  • Familiarity with EMI/EMC and mixed-signal PCB fundamentals
  • Able to work independently under project timelines and adapt in a dynamic team environment
  • Strong written and oral communication skills, comfortable with developing professional detailed documentation

Additional Skills

  • Exposure to PCB design tools (e.g. Fusion 360, EAGLE, KiCAD)
  • Experience in scripting and debugging with Python an asset
  • Familiarity with software revision control (e.g. Git)

Compensation and Benefits

  • Competitive compensation based on experience
  • Health and dental coverage
