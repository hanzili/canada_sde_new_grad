About EPH Consulting Associates

Full Description

We take pride in delivering exceptional service to both our customers and our clients. We operate with purpose, passion, and a commitment to excellence that defines everything we do. Our team is made up of forward-thinkers, problem-solvers, and high achievers who consistently raise the bar and deliver outstanding results. With every project and every customer interaction, our standard remains the same: quality without compromise!

As we continue to grow, we are excited to welcome motivated individuals who want to be part of a company that values ambition, teamwork, and long-term success. When you join us, you step into a supportive, energetic environment that recognizes your dedication, encourages your ideas, and rewards your effort.

What Our Entry Level Sales Associates Excel At:

  • Identifying and pursuing new sales opportunities with confidence and initiative
  • Building meaningful, lasting relationships with prospects and customers through in-person and phone-based interactions
  • Presenting our full line of products and services in a clear, compelling way
  • Delivering tailored solutions that genuinely meet each customer’s needs
  • Preparing persuasive proposals, quotes, and presentations that highlight our value
  • Collaborating with the team to build and execute effective sales strategies
  • Staying current with market trends, product knowledge, and competitor insights
  • Sharing customer feedback to support ongoing improvements and innovation

Qualifications:

  • Experience in sales, customer service, or communications is an asset, but not required – we provide full, comprehensive paid training
  • Strong communication and interpersonal skills
  • Ability to work independently while staying accountable and productive
  • A genuine commitment to delivering exceptional customer experiences
  • Adaptability, curiosity, and a willingness to continuously improve
  • Interest in developing negotiation, communication, and sales skills

Perks & Benefits:

  • Competitive compensation with exciting performance bonuses
  • Company-paid training and ongoing skills development
  • Streamlined onboarding with full product education
  • Additional earning potential tied to quality and service excellence
  • A clear advancement path into leadership roles
  • Supportive, high-energy workplace culture
  • Consistent schedule and reliable workflow
  • Personalized coaching, mentorship, and leadership development
  • Recognition programs that celebrate strong performance
  • Annual, company-paid retreats to beautiful Caribbean destinations

We are excited to welcome individuals who take pride in their work, strive for excellence, and want to grow with a company that invests in its people. If this opportunity aligns with your goals, we invite you to apply and begin your journey with us.
