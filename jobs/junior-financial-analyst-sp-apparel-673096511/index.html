Full Description

Welcome to SP Apparel!

Since 1975, SP Apparel has specialized in the manufacturing of professional and custom sports uniforms. We rely on a passionate team of 250 employees who contribute to our excellence every day. Thanks to their expertise and dedication, we collaborate with the most prestigious brands in the industry, such as Fanatics, Nike, Adidas, CCM, and Bauer.

JOB DESCRIPTION

The Junior Analyst plays a key role in supporting and coordinating the organization’s financial activities. Reporting to the Finance Director, this individual contributes to the management of day-to-day accounting operations while also participating in the preparation and analysis of financial information.

RESPONSIBILITIES

  • Participate in the preparation of monthly financial statements;
  • Contribute to variance analysis and to understanding financial results;
  • Support the monitoring and management of cash flow;
  • Participate in month-end close processes and account reconciliations;
  • Ensure the management and monitoring of accounts payable and receivable;
  • Work with the development team to establish product cost structures;
  • Conduct the annual review of product costs;
  • Participate in the annual budgeting process;
  • Collaborate on the improvement of accounting and financial processes;
  • Participate in the implementation of an ERP system;
  • Support the team with various financial analyses and reports.

QUALIFICATIONS

  • Bachelor’s degree in Finance, Accounting, or a related field;
  • Minimum of 1 year of experience in accounting;
  • Strong communication skills (French and English, both spoken and written);
  • Advanced proficiency with Microsoft Office, particularly Excel;
  • Familiarity with an ERP system, BI tools, or other enterprise systems is an asset;
  • Interest in learning data and reporting tools (e.g., Power BI, automation tools);
  • Comfortable working in a fast‑paced and evolving environment;
  • Ability to work independently while collaborating effectively within a team;
  • Adaptability and comfort with change;
  • Strong analytical skills and high attention to detail;
  • Excellent problem‑solving skills and a proactive attitude.
