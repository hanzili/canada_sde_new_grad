About TUYA CONSTRUCTION LTD

Full Description

About Us
Tuya Construction Ltd. is a growing civil construction company serving the Fraser Valley and surrounding areas. Our Aggregate Division supplies quality aggregate materials for construction, infrastructure, and development projects. We are looking for a motivated Junior Sales Representative to support our sales efforts and help expand our customer base.

Position Overview
The Junior Sales Representative will assist with developing and maintaining relationships with contractors, builders, and other customers requiring aggregate materials. This is a great opportunity for someone looking to start or grow a career in construction materials sales.

Key Responsibilities

· Support the sales team in promoting and selling aggregate products

· Build and maintain relationships with contractors, builders, and local clients

· Respond to customer inquiries and provide product information and pricing

· Assist with preparing quotes and coordinating orders

· Conduct follow-ups with customers to ensure satisfaction and repeat business

· Maintain accurate customer and sales records

· Occasionally visit job sites or meet with customers in the local area

Qualifications

· Strong communication and interpersonal skills

· Customer service or sales experience is an asset but not required

· Basic understanding of construction or aggregates is an asset

· Self-motivated with a willingness to learn

· Valid driver’s license is required

Requirements

· Must be able to work in or commute to Hope, BC

· Local candidates only will be considered

Compensation & Benefits

· $20–$25 per hour (based on experience)

· Opportunity for growth within the company

· Hands-on experience in the construction materials industry
