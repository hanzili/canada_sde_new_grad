About the Role

Create and analyze engaging short-form social content campaigns

About LVLup Props

Toronto-based data-driven sports analytics startup

Full Description

About LVLup Props

LVLup Props is a data-driven SaaS platform designed to bring clarity, structure, and strategic insight to the rapidly growing sports analytics and betting space.

Our platform analyzes millions of data points daily to help users identify value, evaluate risk, and make more informed decisions through a centralized research experience. By transforming complex data into actionable insights, LVLup empowers users to move beyond guesswork and operate with a more disciplined, long-term approach.

Founded in Toronto, LVLup operates at the intersection of data, technology, and consumer platforms. We are focused on building a scalable, product-led business with a strong emphasis on user experience, performance, and continuous innovation.

We’re looking for a Social Media Coordinator (Internship) to help us scale our content engine across TikTok, Instagram, and X.

This is not a typical internship. You’ll work directly with the founders and own real content initiatives that drive:

  • User growth
  • Brand awareness
  • Product adoption

If you’re someone who understands internet culture, loves sports, and wants to build something from the ground up, this role is for you.

What You’ll Do

  • Create short-form content (TikTok, Reels, X) that is engaging, educational, and conversion-driven
  • Turn product insights into simple, compelling content
  • Participate in and help execute UGC-style founder content
  • Identify trends and adapt them to the LVLup brand
  • Test different hooks, formats, and angles to improve performance
  • Collaborate on content ideas, scripts, and creative direction
  • Analyze performance and iterate quickly

What We’re Looking For

  • Strong interest in sports, sports betting, or analytics
  • Deep understanding of social platforms (TikTok, Instagram, X)
  • Creative thinker with strong instincts for what makes content hit
  • Comfortable on camera OR highly skilled at editing/storytelling
  • Self-starter with a bias toward action
  • Bonus: Experience creating content or growing an account

What You’ll Get

  • Free access to the account
  • Ownership over meaningful work (not busy work)
  • Direct access to founders and real startup experience
  • Opportunity to build a portfolio of high-performing content
  • Learn how to create content that actually drives growth (not just views)
  • Potential for a full-time opportunity as we scale

Required Skills

  • Strong understanding of TikTok, Instagram Reels, and X (Twitter) — what performs, why it works, and how to replicate it
  • Ability to create short-form, attention-grabbing content (hooks, pacing, storytelling)
  • Clear and concise communication skills and able to simplify complex ideas into engaging content
  • Interest in sports, sports betting, or analytics
  • Basic video editing skills (e.g., CapCut, Adobe Premiere Pro, or similar tools)
  • Creative mindset with strong instincts for what captures attention and drives engagement
  • Self-starter with the ability to work independently and move quickly
  • Willingness to test, learn, and iterate based on performance data

What You’ll Learn

  • How to create high-performing, conversion-driven content (not just content for views)
  • How to translate data and product insights into simple, engaging narratives
  • How to identify and capitalize on trends across TikTok, Instagram, and X
  • How to test, measure, and optimize content using real performance data
  • How early-stage startups operate, including speed, ownership, and iteration

Training & Support

  • Work directly with founders on content strategy, messaging, and creative direction
  • Weekly feedback sessions focused on improving content performance and storytelling
  • Exposure to growth marketing fundamentals (acquisition, retention, conversion)
  • Access to internal frameworks, examples, and content playbooks

Career Growth

  • Opportunity to build a portfolio of real, high-performing content
  • Potential for extended internship or full-time role as LVLup scales
  • Strong foundation for careers in growth marketing, content strategy, or startups

📩 How to Apply

Send us:

  1. A short note on why you’re interested to mail@lvlupprops.ai
  2. Links to any content you’ve created (TikTok, IG, X, etc.)
  3. Follow us on Instagram & X (@LVLupProps)
  4. A sample post you would create for LVLup on X or Instagram
