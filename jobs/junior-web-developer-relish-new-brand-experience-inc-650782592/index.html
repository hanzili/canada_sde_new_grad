Full Description

Junior Web Developer

Relish – Hybrid | Winnipeg

Design That Matters.

Relish is an employee-owned creative agency with studios in Winnipeg and Calgary. Since 1998, we’ve grown from a one-person studio into a 18-person team of strategists, designers, developers, educators, and entrepreneurs.

We build brands. We design systems. We launch ideas that live in the real world.

Our clients include cultural institutions, associations, Indigenous-led organizations, non-profits, and purpose-driven brands across Canada. We are also a Certified B Corporation – which means we care deeply about the impact of our work, not just the aesthetics of it.

We believe creativity and strategy are inseparable.

Who This Role Is For

We’re looking for a Junior Web Developer who is early in their career and eager to grow within a collaborative, design-driven environment.

You are:

  • Curious, detail-oriented, and eager to learn
  • Comfortable working both independently and as part of a team
  • Interested in how design and development work together
  • Open to feedback and committed to improving your craft
  • Organized and able to manage multiple tasks at once

You understand that great digital experiences are built through both thoughtful design and solid development.

This is a hybrid role based in Winnipeg. We value in-person collaboration – but we also respect autonomy and focus time.

What You’ll Do

  • Develop both static and dynamic websites, using chosen technologies and platforms specified by Relish
  • Provide web support to clients as required
  • Interface with clients to support and provide instruction as needed
  • Develop website templates using the tools provided by Relish
  • Engage with team members on web and interactive projects
  • Offer creative ideas and solutions to complex web-related problems
  • Troubleshoot web-related issues and bugs upon request

What You Bring

Experience

  • 1–3 years of experience in a web development role (or equivalent education/projects)
  • Diploma, degree, or relevant training in web development, interactive media, or similar field

Core Strengths

  • 1-3 years in a similar position
  • Website Project Management Experience
  • Understanding of relational databases, web hosting services, domain management knowledge, an asset
  • CMS development experience (WordPress or equivalent)
  • Web application development experience is an asset
  • Please apply with your resume and a digital portfolio with relevant work samples.

Assets

  • Experience with additional CMS platforms
  • Basic understanding of web hosting, domains, and deployment
  • Exposure to accessibility standards (WCAG)
  • Familiarity with version control (e.g., Git)
  • Interest in UX/UI or digital product design

Software

  • HTML5 / CSS3 / JavaScript
  • WordPress (or similar CMS)
  • Figma or Adobe XD
  • Adobe Creative Suite (Photoshop, Illustrator, InDesign)

Why Relish

  • Hybrid work model (Winnipeg)
  • Employee-owned structure
  • Flexible schedule
  • Healthcare Spending Account
  • Disability and life insurance
  • Employee assistance program
  • Paid time off
  • Wellness program
  • Meaningful, purpose-driven work

We are committed to building a diverse and inclusive workplace. Different perspectives make stronger ideas. We actively work to create a culture where everyone feels valued and supported.
