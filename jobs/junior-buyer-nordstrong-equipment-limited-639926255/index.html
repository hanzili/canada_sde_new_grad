About Nordstrong Equipment Limited

Full Description

Junior Buyer

The Company

Founded in 1909, Nordstrong Equipment Limited is a company with a long history of providing bulk material handling equipment and solutions to agriculture and industrial customers. We are an industry leader in the design and manufacture of a wide variety of robust, high-performance bulk material handling solutions. In addition to our custom-designed product, we also design and manufacture a full line of hoisting equipment for truck dump body applications under the NORDIC trade name.

The Opportunity

Reporting to the Supply Chain Manager, this is an excellent opportunity for the supply chain professional who is passionate about leadership, technology, and working with a team driven to provide industry- best in product value and service.

Responsibilities

  • Plan and track the shipment of parts and equipment according to customer requirements.
  • Ensure timely delivery of goods and services to meet production schedules
  • Manage the return of goods that are incorrect or defective back to the vendor.
  • Negotiate prices for raw material and delivery of supplies and services.
  • Evaluate vendor performance and make recommendations for improvements
  • Analyze current inventories and procedures; suggest improvements to increase efficiency of supply chain and profitability for the company.
  • Identify optimal shipment and transportation routes with attention to consolidation of warehousing and distribution.
  • Coordinate and dispatch inbound, outbound and interbranch shipments.
  • Identify and drive continuous improvement opportunities to streamline process and improve accuracy and efficiency with operations.

The Person

  • Post-secondary education such as a College or University Degree; completion of Continuing

Education programs such as PMAC or APICS would be preferred or equivalent experience.

  • 2+ years of related experience in a supply chain role with leadership experience.
  • Proficient knowledge of ERP systems or relevant software.
  • Proven history of meeting and exceeding targets.
  • Strong interpersonal and negotiation skills with a proven ability to create and maintain positive working relationships with vendors, suppliers, shippers, and customers.
  • Experience purchasing raw materials (steel) an asset.
  • Excellent organizational skills and attention to detail.
  • Strong analytical and problem-solving skills.
  • Ability to work with little supervision and track multiple processes.
  • Ability to create and update detailed records and spreadsheets.

The Perks

  • Competitive salary
  • Extended health benefits
  • Matched pension plan
  • Annual bonus
