Full Description

Job Description Junior Buyer – Amico Accessories Richmond Hill, ON Amico Group of Companies is a leading manufacturer of Medical Equipment and Architectural Products with 9 manufacturing facilities employing over 800 people across North America.

We are currently seeking a Buyer to join our Purchasing team based out of Richmond Hill, Ontario.

Qualifications

  • Bilingual in Mandarin is a definite asset
  • Must have purchasing experience and be able to read manufacturing drawings.
  • Formal college or university education
  • Demonstrated experience managing suppliers, performance and developing long-term relationships
  • Experience in Metal fabrication, surface finishes, custom manufacturing environment is an asset
  • Highly organized, creative and self-motivated
  • Excellent verbal and communication skills.

Responsibilities

  • Preparing and executing RFQs/RFPs
  • Source, evaluate, and select suppliers to meet quality, cost and the delivery objectives
  • Collaborate with internal departments such as Production, Engineering, Quality Control, and Finance to align purchasing strategies with operational needs
  • Negotiate pricing, terms, and delivery schedules with suppliers
  • Perform daily reviews of purchase requests and the inventory management system, ensuring purchase orders are issued in a timely manner
  • Ensure all activities comply with company policies, established purchasing processes, and quality management workflows
  • Other duties as required
