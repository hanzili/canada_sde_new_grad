Full Description

Human Resources Assistant - Canada Summer Job Program

Location: 40 Samor Rd North York ON M6A 1J6

Duration: 8 weeks

Starting pay: $17.60/hour

Hours: 35 hours per week, Monday-Friday

WE ARE: Since 1979, Aptus has specialized in supporting people with complex needs, including those who have a dual diagnosis mental health disorder. To meet these needs, Aptus has a comprehensive clinical team, including Occupational Therapy and Behaviour Services teams.

YOU ARE: Passionate about supporting people and contributing to a positive workplace culture, you are creative, curious, and skilled at building strong working relationships. You are committed to continuous learning, thrive in dynamic environments, and take pride in delivering accurate, high-quality administrative support. In this role, you will have a direct and meaningful impact on supporting employees, strengthening HR processes, and contributing to the success of our organization, the people we serve, and the broader community.

Reporting to the Director, Human Resource, you are a key member of the Human Resources Department and will provide administrative support to ensure the smooth operation of the department.

*** The position is funded through Canada Summer Jobs (CSJ), as such the student will need to be between 15-30 years of age, at the start of the funded position ***

Primary duties and responsibilities include but not limited to:

Duties and responsibilities are included but are not limited to the following:

· Provide administrative support to HR processes, including recruitment coordination such as preparing interview packages, scheduling interviews, and assisting with reference and background checks. Support onboarding, offboarding, and training documentation under supervision.

· Maintain employee and departmental records, including electronic and physical filing systems, scanning, records retention, and routine file maintenance in accordance with established procedures.

· Assist with routine file audits to ensure required employee documentation is complete and properly stored.

· Coordinate meeting logistics, including scheduling, preparing materials, and taking minutes or notes as required.

· Follow up with employees and supervisors regarding outstanding documentation, training records, and compliance requirements.

· Assist in the coordination and implementation of HR projects and initiatives as assigned.

· Draft basic HR correspondence (e.g., standard letters and templates) and support data entry, tracking, and basic reporting activities.

· Perform other related duties as assigned.

Qualifications:

· Currently enrolled in a post-secondary program in Human Resources Management is required.

· General understanding of human resources practices, developmental disabilities services, and employment-related legislation.

· Strong office administration skills, with demonstrated accuracy, organization, attention to detail, and ability to manage multiple priorities.

· Demonstrated resourcefulness and problem-solving skills.

· Strong interpersonal and relationship-building skills.

· Excellent verbal and written communication skills.

· Ability to maintain strict confidentiality and privacy of information related to individuals supported, employees, and organizational business.

· Self-motivated with the ability to work both independently and collaboratively within a team environment, with appropriate supervision.

· Fast learner with strong attention to detail.

· Experience taking accurate and detailed minutes or notes.

· Must be able to move, lift or push objects up to 5 lbs

· Proficiency in Microsoft Office applications, including Outlook, Word, Excel, PowerPoint, and SharePoint.

Canada Summer Jobs Requirements

· Must be within 15 to 30 years of age (as per Canada Summer Jobs requirements)

· Must be legally entitled to work in Canada with a valid Social Insurance Number

· Must be able to commit to full 8 weeks at 35 hours per week

Aptus is committed to providing a diverse, equitable, and inclusive workplace where everyone feels safe and accepted as their authentic selves.

We welcome applications from people of all ancestries, national origins, races, creeds, colours, religions, genders, gender identities or expressions, sexual orientations, genetics, abilities, ages, body sizes, veteran status, or any other characteristics that make each of us different.

Pay: $17.60 per hour

Work Location: In person
