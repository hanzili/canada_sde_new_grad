Job Title: Junior Accountant / Administrative Clerk
Company: Alston Properties Ltd.
Location: Calgary, AB
Job Type: Full-Time
Salary: $44,000 – $50,000 per year (depending on experience)

About Alston Properties Ltd.

Alston Properties Ltd. is a vertically integrated real estate investment and development company focused on acquiring, developing, and managing high-quality commercial and residential real estate assets. Our team is involved in the full lifecycle of real estate projects—from acquisitions and development to property management and operations.

We are seeking a detail-oriented and motivated Junior Accountant / Administrative Clerk to join our growing team. This role is ideal for someone who enjoys working in a fast-paced environment and wants to gain hands-on experience in real estate finance and operations.

Position Overview

The Junior Accountant / Administrative Clerk will support the accounting and administrative functions of the company. The successful candidate will assist with day-to-day bookkeeping, financial record keeping, and general office administration.

This is a full-time, office-based position in Calgary, with some flexibility to work from home when appropriate.

Key Responsibilities

Accounting & Bookkeeping

  • Assist with accounts payable and accounts receivable processing
  • Prepare and enter invoices, receipts, and journal entries
  • Reconcile bank and credit card statements
  • Maintain organized financial records and documentation
  • Assist with monthly reporting and accounting reconciliations
  • Support the preparation of year-end materials for external accountants

Administrative Support

  • Assist with document organization and filing (digital and physical)
  • Coordinate and manage incoming correspondence and documentation
  • Provide administrative support to management as required
  • Maintain office supplies and general office organization
  • Assist with real estate operational documentation and tenant records

Qualifications

  • Minimum 2 years of relevant work experience in accounting, bookkeeping, or administrative roles
  • Diploma or coursework in Accounting, Business Administration, or a related field is an asset
  • Strong attention to detail and organizational skills
  • Proficiency with Microsoft Excel and Microsoft Office
  • Experience with accounting software (e.g., QuickBooks) is an asset
  • Strong written and verbal communication skills
  • Ability to manage multiple tasks and meet deadlines

What We Offer

  • Competitive salary of $44,000 – $50,000, depending on experience
  • Opportunity to gain experience in real estate investment, development, and property management
  • A small, collaborative team environment with exposure to many aspects of the business
  • Flexible work environment with occasional work-from-home options
