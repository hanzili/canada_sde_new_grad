About B2Gold Corp.

Full Description

The Company

B2Gold is a responsible international senior gold producer headquartered in Vancouver, Canada. Founded in 2007, today, B2Gold has operating gold mines in Mali, Namibia and the Philippines, the Goose Project under construction in northern Canada, and numerous development and exploration projects in various countries including Mali, Colombia and Finland.

Position

Contract Geologist Jr., Nunavut

Location and Reporting Structure

Reporting to the Senior Exploration Geologist, this temporary position (up to 1 year contract) is a fly-in/fly-out role on a 3x3 rotation based in Nunavut, Canada.

Responsibilities

  • Conduct detailed core logging and sampling, ensuring accurate and timely data entry into geological databases.
  • Collect, analyze, and interpret geological information from exploration programs, including diamond drilling, soil/till surveys, core preparation, and coordinating sample shipments, to support informed decision-making.
  • Work collaboratively and independently to maintain a highly organized, efficient, and safety-compliant core shack environment.
  • Prepare and deliver clear, timely reports on completed work activities to the Supervisor to support program visibility and operational planning.
  • Provide operational support to geological and geophysical programs, executing tasks safely, efficiently, and in alignment with established procedures.
  • Participate actively in daily toolbox meetings to reinforce adherence to B2Gold Nunavut’s safety standards, policies, and best practices.
  • Maintain a clean, hazard-free work area by proactively identifying and eliminating potential safety risks (e.g., tripping hazards).

Desired Competencies and Requirements

  • Two (2) years’ experience in field mapping, performing geotechnical and core logging activities, and sampling.
  • University Degree or College Diploma in Geological, Geotechnical, or other related discipline.
  • Prior experience working in a remote camp setting is considered an asset.
  • Physically able to lift a minimum of 50 lbs., walk and stand for long periods of time and perform physical labour.
  • HIMS Certification and/or Level 1 First Aid is considered an asset.

Compensation and Benefits

For informational purposes, the estimated annual earnings are $107,484.00 for 2184 scheduled hours (combined regular hourly rate of $39.00 and overtime rate of $58.50) in a work year.

B2Gold Nunavut offers a competitive compensation package, including salary commensurate with experience, knowledge, and skills, plus a group benefits package for employees and their dependents.

Application and Contact Information

Kitikmeot Inuit, Nunavut Inuit, and Residents receive priority hiring consideration, if one these categories applies, please state it after the reference number in the subject line. We thank all for their interest but are only considering applicants with working rights in Canada.
B2Gold Nunavut prioritizes local employment, supports empowerment initiatives, and continuously works towards a people profile that matches the demographics of the respective national settings in which we operate, while appreciating our inclusive culture that attracts talent from around the world. As an equal opportunity employer, B2Gold Nunavut values diversity and we encourage all qualified candidates to apply.

The B2Gold Back River Corp. owned Back River Project is a significant undertaking with contributions from both B2Gold Nunavut and valued Contractors. Although we appreciate your interest in working for B2Gold Nunavut directly; if you are not selected for employment with B2Gold Nunavut or we have no current work available that is suitable for your skill set, B2Gold Nunavut may share your application material with our contractors for positions that they are seeking to fill. If you do not want your resume shared, please indicate so in the subject line of your email and we will retain a copy of your application material for our internal records only.

We will retain application material on file for future vacancies (openings within 6-12 months) for consideration; however, you are encouraged to apply to any opening you may be suited for. If selected to move forward a member of our team will be in touch.

Closing Date

This post will remain open until 12:00AM MST February 24th, 2026, unless a suitable candidate is not found. We thank all applicants for their interest in B2Gold Nunavut; however, only those candidates selected for an interview will be contacted.
