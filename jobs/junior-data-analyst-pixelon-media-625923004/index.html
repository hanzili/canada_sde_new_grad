About Pixelon Media

Full Description

Overview
We are seeking a motivated Junior Data Analyst to join our dynamic team. This entry-level position offers an excellent opportunity to develop your data analysis skills and contribute to impactful projects. The ideal candidate will have a strong foundation in data management, analytics tools, and business analysis, with a passion for transforming data into actionable insights. You will work closely with senior analysts and project managers in a fast-paced environment that values innovation and continuous learning.

Duties

  • Assist in collecting, cleaning, and analyzing large datasets using SQL, R, Python, and other data analysis tools.
  • Develop and maintain dashboards and reports utilizing Tableau, Power BI, and Visio to visualize key metrics.
  • Support project management activities within Agile frameworks and SDLC methodologies to ensure timely delivery of analytics projects.
  • Participate in business analysis to understand client needs and translate requirements into technical solutions.
  • Contribute to database design and management using Microsoft SQL Server, Oracle, and VBA for automation tasks.
  • Perform analysis on business processes, identify improvement opportunities, and document findings for stakeholders.
  • Collaborate with cross-functional teams to implement data-driven strategies that enhance operational efficiency.

Experience

  • Bachelor’s degree in Data Science, Computer Science, Business Analytics, or related field.
  • Proven experience with data analysis skills including SQL, R, Python, and database management systems such as Microsoft SQL Server or Oracle.
  • Familiarity with visualization tools like Tableau and Power BI is highly desirable.
  • Knowledge of Agile project management practices and SDLC processes.
  • Strong business analysis capabilities combined with database design understanding.
  • Experience with VBA scripting, Bash (Unix shell), Visio, and analytics tools is a plus.
  • Excellent analysis skills with the ability to interpret complex data sets and communicate insights effectively. This role provides an exciting platform for aspiring data analysts eager to grow their technical expertise while contributing to meaningful projects within a collaborative environment.
