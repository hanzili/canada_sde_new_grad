Content Writer Intern

at Infinity Digital

📍 Location Mississauga, ON L4W 5K4 Hybrid
📋 Details Internship · 6 months · $18-20/hr · Hybrid
📅 Posted Apr 01, 2026

About the Role

Write, edit, and research content for branding

About Infinity Digital

Mississauga-based digital agency; growth-focused.

Full Description

Overview
We are seeking a talented and detail-oriented Content Writer to join our creative team. The ideal candidate will craft engaging, accurate, and compelling content across various platforms, including blogs, websites, social media, and marketing materials. This role offers an excellent opportunity to develop your writing skills while contributing to the growth and visibility of our brand. The position requires strong communication skills, a keen eye for detail, and the ability to conduct thorough research to produce high-quality content.

Responsibilities

  • Write clear, concise, and engaging content tailored to target audiences across multiple channels.
  • Proofread and edit content to ensure grammatical accuracy, consistency, and adherence to style guidelines.
  • Conduct research on industry-related topics to generate original ideas and provide factual accuracy.
  • Collaborate with marketing and design teams to develop content strategies that align with brand goals.
  • Manage multiple projects simultaneously while meeting deadlines.
  • Utilize word processing tools effectively to draft, revise, and finalize content pieces.
  • Edit and copy-edit existing content for clarity, tone, and style consistency.
  • Stay updated on industry trends and incorporate best practices into content creation.

Requirements

  • Proven experience in writing, editing, or related roles with a strong portfolio of published work.
  • Excellent writing skills with a keen eye for grammar, punctuation, and style.
  • Strong proofreading and copy editing abilities.
  • Effective research skills to gather accurate information quickly.
  • Proficiency in word processing software such as Microsoft Word or Google Docs.
  • Exceptional communication skills to collaborate effectively with team members and stakeholders.
  • Ability to manage multiple projects efficiently in a fast-paced environment.
  • Prior experience with social media content creation is a plus. This role is ideal for motivated individuals passionate about storytelling and eager to contribute meaningful content that resonates with audiences while honing their professional skills in a supportive environment.

Job Type: Internship / Co-op
Contract length: 6 months

Pay: $18.00-$20.00 per hour

Work Location: Hybrid remote in Mississauga, ON L4W 5K4
