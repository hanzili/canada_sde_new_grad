Full Description

Title: AI Centre Operations Intern

Job Description:

The AI Centre for Decision Analytics (AI4DA) is a leading research and innovation leader in artificial intelligence and data analytics for decision-making, driven by research, education, and industry partnership. Reporting to the Centre Manager of AI4DA, this role plays a key supporting role in the Centre's initiatives by contributing to administrative support, marketing, industry networking, and AI community engagement. If you are passionate about Artificial Intelligence, Decision Analytics, and building industry connections, this position offers an excellent opportunity to develop your skills while contributing to a rapidly growing and impactful field.

Responsibilities:

1. Administrative Support

  • Help with Schedule and organize meetings with internal and external stakeholders.
  • Manage day-to-day communications and maintain accurate documentation.

2. Marketing and Outreach

  • Manage the Centre’s social media accounts to engage with the AI community.
  • Maintain and regularly update the Centre’s website, ensuring all content, events, and resources are current and engaging.
  • Create promotional materials, including newsletters and posters.
  • Engage in marketing strategies to increase the Centre's visibility.

3. AI Community Engagement

  • Assist in organizing AI-focused events, conferences, and workshops.
  • Monitor and share updates on the latest in Business Analytics.

4. Funding Exploration

  • Maintain a database of funding opportunities, deadlines, and application statuses.

5. Assistance in project management

  • Serve as Scrum Master for several concurrent projects and ensuring effective adoption of Agile practices.
  • Coordinate, document, and track project tasks and progress.

Details:

Position Type: Internship

Duration: 1 year, 15 hours per week.

Compensation: $25 per hour

Location: The primary workplace for the intern is located at our office in Enterprise Square. The intern should be prepared to commute to the Campus Business Building as needed.

How to Apply:

Interested candidates are invited to apply by submitting the following materials:

● Current Resume: Highlighting your education, experience, and skills relevant to the position.

●  Cover Letter: Outlining your qualifications, interest in the role, and how you can contribute to the success of AI4DA.

● Work Samples (Optional): Include examples of relevant projects, such as marketing content, AI-related initiatives, or website management tasks.

Please send your application materials to ai4da@ualberta.ca by December 18th, 2025. The position is set to begin in January 19th, 2026, or shortly thereafter, with an expected end date of January 18th, 2027.
