Full Description

Job Overview
We are seeking a motivated Junior Data Analyst to join our dynamic team. This entry-level position offers an excellent opportunity to develop your skills in data analysis, business intelligence, and project management within a collaborative environment. The successful candidate will support data-driven decision-making processes by analyzing complex datasets, creating visualizations, and assisting with various analytics projects. Familiarity with tools such as Tableau, Power BI, R, Python, and SQL is highly desirable. This role is ideal for individuals passionate about transforming data into actionable insights and eager to grow their expertise in analytics and database management.

Responsibilities

  • Collect, clean, and analyze large datasets to identify trends, patterns, and insights that support business objectives
  • Develop interactive dashboards and reports using Tableau, Power BI, and other visualization tools to communicate findings effectively
  • Assist in the design and maintenance of databases utilizing Microsoft SQL Server, Oracle, and other database systems
  • Support project management activities within Agile frameworks and SDLC methodologies to ensure timely delivery of analytics solutions
  • Collaborate with cross-functional teams to gather requirements and translate business needs into technical specifications
  • Conduct business analysis to understand operational challenges and recommend data-driven solutions
  • Write and optimize SQL queries; perform database design tasks as needed
  • Utilize programming languages such as R, Python, VBA, and Bash (Unix shell) for data manipulation and automation tasks
  • Document processes using tools like Visio; participate in analysis sessions to improve data workflows

Experience

  • Bachelor's degree in Data Science, Computer Science, Business Analytics, or related field preferred
  • Prior experience or internship in data analysis or business intelligence is a plus but not required
  • Strong understanding of data analysis skills including statistical methods and analytical techniques
  • Familiarity with Tableau, Power BI, R, Python, SQL (Microsoft SQL Server, Oracle), and VBA is highly desirable
  • Knowledge of SDLC processes and Agile project management methodologies
  • Excellent problem-solving skills with the ability to interpret complex datasets effectively
  • Strong communication skills for presenting insights clearly to non-technical stakeholders

This position offers a comprehensive environment for aspiring data analysts to develop technical expertise while contributing to impactful projects across various departments.
