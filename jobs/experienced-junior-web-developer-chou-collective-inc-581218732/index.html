About the Role

About CHOU COLLECTIVE INC.

Family office investing in real estate and healthcare with community-driven values.

Full Description

WHO WE ARE

We are a family office — a collective of passionate individuals managing the wealth of a family and their companies. We make meaningful moves that transcend borders, blending strategic vision with a global perspective to drive growth and impact. More than just investors, we are allies, collaborators, and catalysts for change.

Through strategic collaborations, we have successfully expanded into the real estate development sector. Currently, we are overseeing and developing three projects in British Columbia. Additionally, we have ventured into the healthcare industry, with two local clinics in our portfolio.

We are also strong advocates of giving back to the community. We maintain close ties with the Vancouver Police Department (VPD) and make generous donations to various initiatives throughout the city, reflecting our commitment to supporting and enriching the communities we serve.

We envision a future where businesses and people grow together, shaped by creativity, innovation, and collaboration. At Chou Collective, we're building interconnected ecosystems, respecting and embracing change along the way.

OUR CORE VALUES

  • Strategic Focus
  • Creatively Inspired
  • Community Driven
  • Meaningful Growth
  • Compassionate Spirit
  • Globally Connected

JOIN OUR GROWING FAMILY OFFICE

Join the tribe at CHOU Collective, where we invest in our people and culture to build a strong, empowered team.

We’re looking for a passionate, community-minded individual with strong leadership skills who thrives in collaborative environments. Creativity and fresh ideas are valued here, whether you're leading or supporting a team. If you're ready to make a meaningful impact in a dynamic and supportive workplace, we’d love to hear from you.

THE OPPERTUNITY

Experienced Junior Web Developer (Fun, Creative & Collaborative!)

We’re looking for a creative, outgoing, and collaborative Junior Web Developer who thrives on variety, enjoys solving problems, and loves bringing ideas to life. If you like contributing fresh ideas, building great things with a team (and sometimes independently), and taking ownership of your projects, you’ll fit right in and grow with us. We want someone who’s excited to work on a wide range of projects, experiment with new approaches, and help shape user experiences that truly delight.

WHAT YOU'LL DO

  • Translate Figma designs into responsive, accessible front-end experiences
  • Build, test, and launch clean, functional websites and web features – including Webflow and WordPress
  • Work closely with designers, writers, and other developers to bring ideas to life
  • Contribute ideas and creative solutions during team discussions
  • Help maintain, update, and improve existing websites and platforms
  • Work on a mix of projects—from small landing pages to interactive components
  • Support simple integrations, CMS setups, and light deployments
  • Bring positivity, curiosity, and good vibes to the team

WHAT YOU BRING

Core Technical Skills

  • 1-2 years of hands-on web development experience
  • Solid foundation in HTML5 & CSS3, JavaScript (ES6+), Responsive and accessible design and Git / GitHub
  • Familiarity with a modern front-end framework (React, Vue, etc.)
  • Basic understanding of deployments (Netlify, Vercel, cPanel) or willingness to learn
  • Webflow proficiency: Able to build full sites and templates. Comfortable with interactions, animations, and responsive layouts
  • WordPress experience: Familiar with theme customization, templates, and plugin usage. Understanding of WordPress CMS structure and content workflows
  • Figma competency: Able to interpret design files, layouts, and components. Can translate Figma designs accurately into pixel-perfect builds

NICE-TO-HAVE

  • TypeScript, Next.js or Vue 3, Tailwind CSS, Experience with headless CMS (Sanity, Strapi, Contentful), Light backend exposure (Node.js, Express, REST APIs), PHP, Python, or SQL.

LIFE SKILLS & PERSONALITY

  • Creative thinker with a fun, positive attitude
  • Loves collaborating — but can also work independently like a champ
  • Self-starter who needs little to no direction (a true go-getter!)
  • Strong communication skills
  • Curious, eager to learn, and excited to try new things
  • Organized and able to juggle multiple projects
  • Not afraid to ask questions or pitch new ideas
  • Enjoys working in a supportive, dynamic team environment

WHY YOU'LL LOVE WORKING FOR US

  • A fun, energetic team that values creativity
  • A variety of exciting and ever-changing projects
  • Freedom to experiment, innovate, and grow your skills
  • A supportive environment where your ideas matter
  • Opportunities for professional development
  • A workplace that celebrates individuality, collaboration, and good vibes

READY TO JOIN US ?

If you’re a creative, outgoing, collaborative, and confident junior dev who loves coding and wants to grow with a fun team — we’d love to hear from you!

Send us your resume, portfolio, and a quick note about why you’re the perfect fit. Let’s build something awesome together!
