About Aplin

Full Description

Join a top employer supporting exciting finance transformation projects. We are currently hiring multiple  Junior Accounts Payable Associates  to join a growing finance team in Halifax, NS. This is an excellent opportunity for recent graduates or early-career accounting professionals to gain hands-on experience in a fast-paced, collaborative environment with training provided. This is a 4-month contract with potential for an extension.

Benefits & Perks • Compensation: $25 to $29/hour

• Monday to Friday (40 hours a week)

Please note, this role will be 4 days onsite at the client’s office in Halifax, NS.

Responsibilities • Process and verify vendor invoices accurately and in a timely manner

• Perform invoice matching against purchase orders and receipts

• Assist with payment processing and payment runs

• Support account reconciliations

• Respond to vendor inquiries and resolve discrepancies

• Provide ad hoc administrative and finance support as needed

Qualifications • Degree or diploma in Accounting, Finance, or Business

• Recent graduates encouraged to apply

• Internship or co-op experience in accounting/finance is an asset

• Prior exposure to Accounts Payable or invoice processing preferred

• Familiarity with ERP systems (Oracle preferred, not required)

• Strong proficiency in Microsoft Excel

• High attention to detail and ability to work in a fast-paced environment
