About Hammond Power Solutions

Full Description

**Hammond Power Solutions Inc.** is a global manufacturer headquartered in Guelph, Ontario, Canada. HPS has grown to become the leader in North America in the custom design and manufacture of dry type transformers. For the past century, HPS has grown to deliver electrical power solutions to global markets and provides solutions for complex applications like, mining, rectifier, drives, traction, wind, solar and many other electrical power applications. The world relies on HPS to continually conceive and produce state-of-the-art transformer technology. We currently have an opening for a **Co-op Student - Marketing Data & Analytics 4 to 8 month starting in May 2026**, come grow with us!

**JOB OVERVIEW:**

The position is under the general direction of the Manager for Market Research and BI. The position will focus primarily on data integration, analysis and reporting. The ideal candidate for this internship will be someone who enjoys working with numbers and has an interest in the field of data analysis, particularly within business and marketing contexts.

**KEY RESPONSIBILITIES:**

- Data integration (ETL process: Extract, Load and Transform) - Data analysis - Reporting & Dashboard - Providing accurate data that support strategic decisions

**QUALIFICATIONS:**

- Currently enrolled as a student pursuing a post grad certificate or diploma in the field of data analytics, marketing analytics, business intelligence, computer science, engineering - Preference for post grad certificate/diploma programs including mature students with some past work experience and past work with data analytics. - Knowledge of Power Bi, Tableau, Excel

**BENEFITS:**

- Casual dress - Company events - Employee assistance program - On-site parking
