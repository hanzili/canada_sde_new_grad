About the Role

Process reconciliations, AP/AR, and assist month-end close

About BMW Toronto

Global automaker with strong dealership network

Full Description

Looking for growth, stability and sense of accomplishment? Are you a proactive self starter who has a high level of attention to detail and likes working in a fast-paced environment?

At BMW Toronto, we are looking for a motivated individual with a positive attitude who loves to challenge themselves. This is an entry level role to join our accounting team as a Junior Accountant. This position is located in downtown Toronto and is required to be onsite full time five days a week.

Key Responsibilities

  • Prepare daily bank deposits and credit card payment reconciliations.
  • Accounts receivable reconciliations of customer and departmental cash clearing accounts
  • Accounts payable including coding invoices, posting and issuing payments in a timely manner.
  • Monitor floor-plan accounts to ensure payments are submitted within guidelines.
  • Review vehicle purchases and Inventory schedule maintenance
  • Administrative filing
  • Assist in month-end and year-end closing
  • Other accounting duties as required by management

Minimum Qualifications

  • Post secondary education with focus on accounting
  • 0-1 years of relevant accounting experience
  • High level of attention to detail and organizational skills
  • Proficient in MS Word, Excel and Outlook
  • Excellent oral and written communication skills
  • Experience working in a fast-paced environment
  • Ability to work independently and meet deadlines in a timely manner
  • Previous automotive retail dealership work experience preferred but not required

What we offer

  • Compensation: $42,000 - $46,000 annually
  • Medical, Dental Benefits and Life Insurance
  • Profit Sharing
  • Great working environment
  • Growth opportunities
