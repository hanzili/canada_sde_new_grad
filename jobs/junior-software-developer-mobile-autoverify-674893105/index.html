About AutoVerify

AutoVerify is a technology company born in London, Ontario. We operate remotely, hiring employees across Canada. AutoVerify is a comprehensive suite of digital solutions tailored toward automotive dealers, manufacturers, lenders, and marketplaces alike. We enable businesses to better serve their customers. Visit our website to learn more: https://www.autoverify.com/ (https://www.autoverify.com/)

About Us

Join our team and work anywhere in Canada – home, coffee shop, or even poolside, because we’re all about remote life. Enjoy perks like company-paid flexible benefits options, fun events (virtual and in-person) and half day Fridays all year round to boot. We hire incredible people and your job referrals are not just appreciated; they're rewarded. Our culture is driven by communication and trust, acknowledging and supporting your life outside of work with the flexibility you deserve. All this to say, we value your journey at AutoVerify, both personally and professionally.

About the Role

The Junior Software Developer role is dedicated to learning, developing, and maintaining our internal software systems with a focus on our business-critical mobile application, AV Capture. You will actively contribute to the Technology team by participating in code reviews, testing, debugging, and documentation. Partnering closely with our Senior Software Developer for Mobile Apps (and iOS Lead), you will develop feature requests for the Android platform and work directly with members of several groups across the business to effectively meet project requirements. You possess strong communication and problem-solving skills. You are a motivated self-starter with a passion for continuous learning who is adept at leveraging AI tools to accelerate development under the mentorship of our senior team members.

Duties

  • Write efficient code to maintain and improve the performance of our existing software systems, focusing on our Android mobile application while also building your knowledge of the iOS mobile application.
  • Develop, implement, and test small to medium sized feature requests and participate in product feature discovery for the AV Capture mobile apps.
  • Utilize assistive AI coding technology to learn new patterns, translate logic from one codebase to another, and accelerate software delivery, ensuring you rigorously review all AI output in partnership with our senior team members.
  • Create and maintain automated testing to ensure proper functionality of our mobile apps.
  • Participate in code reviews and conduct testing to ensure software is of the highest quality.
  • Detect bugs or errors within code and apply fixes in an efficient manner, particularly Android-specific issues and device variations such as screen resolutions, screen orientations, and camera capabilities.
  • Work to achieve sprint targets against fixed deadlines, changing requirements, and shifting priorities.
  • Participate in daily team stand-ups, report on progress and setbacks, and openly accept guidance or mentorship from your manager and the team’s Intermediate and Senior Software Developers.
  • Work within an Agile environment and collaborate with multiple business groups including Product Management, Growth, and Customer Experience.
  • Research and share valuable findings of relevant emerging technologies and industry best practices with the Technology team.
  • Document and maintain software functionality.
  • Fulfill developer and deployment documentation requirements.

Qualifications

  • Post-secondary degree or diploma in Computer Science or related field preferred.
  • Foundational knowledge of native Android development using Kotlin/Java.
  • Knowledge of AWS, MySQL, Node, or Python is considered an asset.
  • Dedicated team-player.
  • Strong oral, written communication and problem-solving skills.
  • Highly receptive to feedback and mentoring.
  • Detail-oriented, curious, and asks a lot of questions.
  • Self-motivated with the ability to learn new programming languages quickly.
  • Testing and debugging experience is an asset, but not required.
