Marketing / Digital Marketing Internship

Marketing Assistant & Ambassador

at Beckman's Fine Foods

📍 Location Vancouver, BC V6R 3G6 Hybrid
📋 Details Part-time · Permanent/Contract/Co-op · $22-26/hr · Hybrid (Vancouver)
📅 Posted Mar 15, 2026

About the Role

Execute social content and support brand events

About Beckman's Fine Foods

Growing BC CPG brand; founder-led mentorship

Full Description

Slow Sunday Foods is a growing BC food brand that makes less-sweet everyday sauces from whole BC fruit. Our products are expanding across Vancouver grocery stores and specialty retailers, and we’re building our brand through retail, social media, markets, and community events.

We are looking for a motivated and reliable student to help support marketing, events, and brand growth as we expand.

This role is ideal for someone studying marketing, communications, business, or entrepreneurship who wants real experience building a consumer brand.

You will work directly with the founders and gain hands-on exposure to brand building, retail marketing, and food entrepreneurship.

What You’ll Do

Marketing & Content Support

Help execute marketing initiatives and grow the brand’s online presence.

Responsibilities may include:

  • Capturing photo and video content for social media
  • Assisting with Instagram and TikTok content creation
  • Helping develop social media posts and short-form content
  • Supporting creator and influencer outreach
  • Assisting with campaigns, promotions, and giveaways
  • Tracking social media engagement metrics
  • Helping organize and manage brand content and marketing assets
  • Supporting marketing projects as the brand grows

Retail & Event Marketing

Support brand visibility and customer engagement through in-person events.

Responsibilities may include:

  • Assisting with in-store product demos
  • Helping run farmers market and community event booths
  • Setting up brand displays, signage, and sampling stations
  • Engaging customers and explaining products
  • Distributing samples and gathering customer feedback
  • Helping collect email signups and grow the customer community

Brand & Operations Support

Assist with marketing logistics and small operational tasks.

Examples include:

  • Preparing sample kits and marketing materials
  • Organizing marketing supplies and assets
  • Helping prepare materials for product launches
  • Supporting various brand-building initiatives

Who This Role Is For

You are someone who:

  • Is currently a student
  • Is interested in marketing, branding, food, and entrepreneurship
  • Enjoys talking with people and representing a brand
  • Is organized, reliable, and proactive
  • Is comfortable using social media platforms
  • Is excited about a role that you can grow in
  • Graphic design tools (Canva is okay, but illustrator or equivalent is preferred)
  • Owns a car and has a driver's license
  • Has weekend availability

Bonus Skills

  • Photography or video editing
  • Experience running social media accounts
  • Interest in food, cooking, or local food systems

What You’ll Gain

  • Hands-on experience building a growing consumer brand
  • Exposure to CPG marketing and retail strategy
  • Experience with content creation and event marketing
  • Opportunity to work directly with founders
  • Real insight into food entrepreneurship
