About the Role

Assist process engineering tasks on water projects

About Integrated Sustainability

Award-winning water, waste, and energy solutions provider

Full Description

Description:

As a Summer Student, you will support projects that span multiple disciplines within our organization in order to deliver high quality and sustainable solutions to our clients. You will have the opportunity to expand your career by working on exciting and high-profile water, waste, and energy projects.

Your experience in the industry and/or consulting, as well as your project and people skills will enable you to make a difference in our industry and inspire new and better ways to implement engineering and water management solutions.

Responsibilities:

  • Expected to undertake assignments across the business to support ongoing opportunities and to set a strong foundation for understanding the business and industries.
  • Perform site visits and conduct fieldwork with a focus on safety.
  • Support process engineering work on projects ranging from conceptual studies through to detailed design.
  • Assist with the development of engineering deliverables including:
  • Process Flow Diagrams (PFDs)
  • Piping and Instrumentation Diagrams (P&IDs)
  • Heat and material balances
  • Design basis documents
  • Equipment sizing calculations
  • Technical reports and studies
  • Assist with proposal and estimate developments as required.
  • Support the evaluation and selection of process equipment including pumps, tanks, valves, and treatment systems.
  • Grammatical/formatting review of technical documents/reports and proposals.
  • Assist with office management and any other duties as required.
  • Help organize and maintain electronic and hard copy filing systems.

Position Qualifications:

  • Working towards your Bachelor’s degree in chemical or process engineering (or equivalent) from a recognized University (master’s or other graduate degree would be considered an asset).
  • Relevant engineering experience in water treatment, mining, industrial processing, or related industries.
  • Eligibility for registration with the Association of Professional Engineers and Geoscientists of Alberta (APEGA) and/or Engineers and Geoscientists British Columbia (EGBC).
  • Strong written and verbal communication skills.
  • Legally able to work in Canada

What We’re Looking For:

  • Interest in industrial water treatment, water reuse, and environmental process engineering.
  • Basic understanding of water and wastewater treatment principles and aqueous chemistry.
  • Strong analytical and problem-solving skills.
  • Willingness to learn and develop technical engineering capabilities.
  • Ability to manage multiple tasks and priorities in a fast-paced environment.
  • Proficiency with Microsoft Office applications.
  • Familiarity with engineering software such as AutoCAD, Bluebeam, or process simulation tools is considered an asset.
  • Positive attitude, strong initiative, and eagerness to grow professionally.

Integrated Sustainability

Integrated Sustainability is an award-winning provider of world-class sustainable Water, Waste and Energy solutions. Our goal is to support clients from project conception to construction and beyond by being agile, collaborative partners.

Recognized as one of Canada’s Top 100 Small & Medium Employers for 9 years in a row, we believe that the people who work here matter and every opportunity we have to be a better employer is an opportunity to succeed. Having attracted a dynamic, multi-disciplined team of specialists, we believe that to be innovative, we must also be courageous, finding solutions and challenging conventional wisdom.

It’s About More Than Work

We are excited about what 2026 looks like for our organization and are looking for innovative, courageous professionals to join us.

We strive to build a culture where every employee is valued for their unique backgrounds, experiences, expertise, skills and passions – it’s what allows us to be leaders in innovative solutions. If you require accommodation during the selection process, please let our People & Culture team know.
