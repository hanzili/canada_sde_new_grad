About STAR Sightseeing

Small private firm; internship with real accounting tasks

Full Description

Job Summary

We are seeking an entry-level Accounting Assistant to support the day-to-day operations of the accounting department. This role is ideal for a recent graduate or someone beginning their career in accounting. The Accounting Assistant will help maintain accurate financial records, process routine transactions, and provide administrative support while gaining hands-on experience in a professional financial environment.

Key Responsibilities

  • Assist with accounts payable and accounts receivable tasks
  • Enter invoices, receipts, and payments into the accounting system
  • Reconcile bank statements and company credit card transactions
  • File and maintain accounting records in compliance with company policies
  • Assist with month-end and year-end closing activities
  • Support payroll preparation and timesheet tracking, as required
  • Respond to basic vendor and internal accounting inquiries
  • Perform general clerical and administrative duties related to accounting

Qualifications

  • Diploma, certificate, or coursework in Accounting, Bookkeeping, Finance, or a related field
  • 0–1 year of accounting, bookkeeping, or office experience (co-op or internship experience
  • welcomed)
  • Basic understanding of accounting principles
  • Familiarity with QuickBooks accounting software is an asset
  • Proficiency in Microsoft Excel and other MS Office applications

Skills & Competencies

  • Strong attention to detail and accuracy
  • Good organizational and time-management skills
  • Ability to handle confidential financial information
  • Willingness to learn and take direction
  • Strong written and verbal communication skills
  • Ability to work independently and as part of a team
