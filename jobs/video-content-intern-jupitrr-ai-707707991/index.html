Video & Content Intern

at Jupitrr AI

📍 Location Vancouver, British Columbia Hybrid
📋 Details Internship · 3 months
📅 Posted Jun 04, 2026
Apply on company site →

Direct apply link, refreshed by the daily generator.

About the Role

Create and edit own video content

About Jupitrr AI

Vancouver AI startup building video marketing tools

Full Description

About Jupitrr AI

We're building the operating system for video marketing. Think of us as the tool that makes every marketer and founder look like they have a full video team behind them. We're a small, design-obsessed team moving fast — and all three of our founders make videos ourselves. We're not outsourcing this. We want someone to come in and help us make more, and make it better.

The Role

You'll own video end-to-end — from idea to filming to editing to publishing. No handoffs, no waiting on someone else. You'll show up, point the camera at yourself, and make content that actually gets watched on YouTube and TikTok/Reels.

Half your job is thinking (what should we make and why), half is doing (go make it). This is a rare chance to build creator content at a company that makes a video product — you'll understand the craft from both sides.

What You'll Do

  • Develop video concepts and help shape our content strategy
  • Film, self-direct, and edit your own videos — soup to nuts
  • Own and grow our presence on YouTube and TikTok/Reels
  • Test formats, track what's working, and iterate fast
  • Use Jupitrr's own tools as a power user and give us real product feedback

You'd Be a Great Fit If You...

  • You're a natural when it comes to filming yourself
  • Can film, direct, and edit yourself without needing a crew
  • Curious about how and why content works — and love going down the rabbit hole
  • Are scrappy and self-directed — you don't wait to be told what to film
  • Want real startup experience building something from the ground up

Nice to Have

  • A social account, YouTube channel, or anything that shows what you've made
  • Familiarity with recording and editing tools (CapCut, Premiere, or whatever you use)
  • Interest in AI, SaaS, or creator tools

What You Get

  • Full creative ownership — your content (& product feedback) will actually ship
  • Direct mentorship from a founding team that's in the trenches with you
  • Hands-on experience building content for a live, growing AI product
  • Get embedded in the Vancouver startup ecosystem from day one

How it works

  • Flexible ~10 hrs/week over 3 months, hybrid in Downtown Vancouver
  • This is an internship role, open for university students/ new grads/who aspire to transition into content marketing.
  • Potential to transition into a paid contract role based on performance

How to apply

Drop an email to tszhoi@jupitrr.com, with your name + Video Intern, give me a short paragraph about you, and your resume + your linkedin link.

You will get 100% ahead of the game if you send us a video to our instagram btw
