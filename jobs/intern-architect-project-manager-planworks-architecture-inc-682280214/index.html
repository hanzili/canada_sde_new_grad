About PlanWorks Architecture Inc

Full Description

Overview
We are seeking a talented and detail-oriented Intern Architect/Project Manager to join our dynamic design team. The ideal candidate will possess a strong foundation in architectural design, project management, and construction processes, with proficiency in industry-standard software tools. This role offers an exciting opportunity to contribute to innovative projects ranging from renovations to large-scale developments, ensuring functional and aesthetic excellence throughout all phases of design and construction.

Duties

  • Develop comprehensive architectural designs using CAD, Revit, Bluebeam
  • Prepare detailed drawings, drafting, and construction documents aligned with client specifications and building codes.
  • Engage in project management activities including scheduling, budgeting, and coordinating with contractors and clients.
  • Conduct building code research and ensure compliance throughout project development.
  • Oversee renovation projects from conceptualization through construction administration.
  • Perform construction estimating and assist in business development efforts to attract new clients.
  • Collaborate with multidisciplinary teams to deliver innovative solutions that meet client needs and project budgets.
  • Negotiate project scopes, timelines, and budgets with stakeholders to ensure successful project delivery.

Experience

  • Proven experience as an Architect or in a similar role within architectural or construction firms.
  • Proficiency in AutoCAD, Revit, and Bluebeam is essential.
  • Strong skills in drafting, construction estimating, budgeting, and project management are required.
  • Experience with negotiation and business development is highly desirable.
  • Knowledge of building codes, renovation processes, construction methods, and cost estimation techniques.
  • Demonstrated ability to manage multiple projects simultaneously while maintaining high-quality standards. Join our team to bring visionary architectural concepts to life while working on diverse projects that challenge your creativity and technical expertise.
