Full Description

Job Title: Junior Accountant Consultant (CPA Candidate or Bookkeeper)

Organization: Magazines Canada (National Non-Profit Association)

Location: Remote (Canada-wide)

Contract Type: Independent Contractor

Compensation: $55,000

About the Organization

Magazines Canada is the national trade association representing Canadian magazine media. We support, advocate for, and strengthen the sector through programming, funding administration, and industry leadership. As a non-profit organization, we operate in a dynamic, culturally significant environment that values accountability, collaboration, and innovation.

Position Overview

We are seeking a detail-oriented and highly organized Junior Accountant (CPA candidate or experienced Bookkeeper) to support the day-to-day financial operations of a national non-profit organization. Reporting to the Executive Director, this role will play a key part in maintaining financial integrity, supporting audits, and ensuring compliance with Canadian non-profit regulations.

This is a fully remote, independent contractor position ideal for an early-career accounting professional seeking hands-on experience across a broad range of accounting functions.

Key Responsibilities

General Accounting & Financial Management

  • Maintain accurate and up-to-date financial records using accounting software (e.g., Blue Link)
  • Perform general accounting duties including journal entries, reconciliations, and financial reporting
  • Assist in the preparation of internal financial statements and variance analyses
  • Support budgeting and forecasting processes

Accounts Payable & Receivable

  • Manage accounts payable and receivable processes, including invoicing and collections
  • Process vendor payments on a bi-weekly basis
  • Monitor cash flow and ensure timely financial transactions

Banking & Payments

  • Perform daily banking activities, including reconciliations
  • Process e-transfers and EFT payments
  • Maintain accurate records of all financial transactions

Audit & Compliance

  • Prepare schedules and documentation required for annual external audits
  • Coordinate with auditors and support audit processes
  • Ensure compliance with Canadian non-profit accounting standards and CRA requirements
  • Assist with annual filings, including Corporations Canada returns

Operational Support

  • Assist with membership invoicing and renewals
  • Support program-specific accounting and reporting as required
  • Maintain proper internal controls and financial procedures

Qualifications & Experience

  • CPA designation in progress (or equivalent accounting certification) OR 2+ years of bookkeeping/accounting experience
  • Experience working with non-profit organizations is strongly preferred
  • Knowledge of Canadian non-profit accounting standards and CRA tax requirements
  • Experience supporting annual audits
  • Proficiency in accounting software (e.g., Blue Link) and Microsoft Excel
  • Strong understanding of accounts payable/receivable, payroll, and general accounting principles
  • High level of accuracy, attention to detail, and organizational skills
  • Ability to work independently in a remote environment

Contract Terms & Working Conditions

  • 1-year independent contractor agreement (April 01, 2026 to March 31, 2027)
  • 35hrs per week
  • Fully remote/work-from-home environment
  • Contractor must provide their own computer and office equipment
  • Access to organizational systems (software, shared drives) will be limited to job function
  • Compensation invoiced monthly or bi-weekly by the contractor
  • Taxes are the responsibility of the contractor
  • No additional benefits provided

Equity, Diversity & Inclusion

Magazines Canada is committed to fostering an inclusive and equitable workplace. We strongly encourage applications from individuals who identify as Black, Indigenous, and People of Colour (BIPOC), as well as candidates from diverse and underrepresented communities. Hiring with a DEI lens is a priority for our organization.

How to Apply

Please submit your resume and a brief cover letter outlining your relevant experience and interest in the role to chrisradley@magazinescanada.ca. Only candidates selected for an interview will be contacted.

Join us in supporting Canada’s magazine media industry while building your accounting career in a meaningful, national non-profit environment.
