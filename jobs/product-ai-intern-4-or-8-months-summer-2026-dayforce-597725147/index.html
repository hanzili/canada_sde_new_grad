About the Role

About Dayforce

Global HR tech leader, Dayforce/Ceridian

Full Description

Dayforce, a global leader in Human Capital Management (HCM) with headquarters in Toronto, Ontario, and Minneapolis, Minnesota, operates across North America, EMEA, and APJ regions. Our Cloud HCM platform, recognized for its unified database and continuous calculation engine, enhances efficiency, productivity, and compliance for global workforces. We are committed to "Making Work Life Better™" for employees, customers, and partners worldwide. As an Intern at Dayforce, you’ll gain real-world experience with meaningful responsibilities, access to development programs, and the chance to collaborate globally, bringing new ideas and optimizing processes across multiple business units. Job Title: Product & AI Intern

Location : Virtual

Duration: Summer 2026 – 4 or 8 months (May 2026 – August 2026) OR (May 2026 – December 2026)

Availability : *Full-time availability of 37.5 – 40 hours weekly is required to be eligible for this opportunity. Benefits For Students

  • Experience working for one of the fastest growing Human Capital Management technology companies in the world Access to Dayforce’s development programs and resources
  • Ability to work with as well as create relationships with members across the globe
  • Autonomy to bring forth new ideas and optimize existing structures within the organization
  • Meaningful responsibilities, enabling you to obtain ‘real-world’ experience
  • Dynamic hands-on exposure to multiple business-units and stakeholders across Dayforce

About The Opportunity We are seeking a technical AI Product Intern to support our GenAI and AI Agent product initiatives. In this role, you will work directly with the AI product team to develop prototypes that spark future product innovation, as well as help improve model performance, evaluate multilingual model output quality. Furthermore, you will have the opportunity to help us evolve our product analytics capabilities in Pendo, and craft meaningful content in Confluence. This internship is ideal for a Computer Science or Engineering student with a passion for machine learning, AI, experimentation, and turning messy problems into working solutions.

What You’ll Get To Do

  • Build working demos in live code to communicate product opportunities
  • Create annotated datasets to improve our LLM-based systems
  • Assist with hands-on evaluation of multilingual model output
  • Develop and refine product analytics in Pendo and analyze product usage data in Pendo and Databricks to generate insights that inform our roadmap
  • Craft and refine meaningful content and documentation (e.g. Confluence) for broad stakeholder communications
  • Support our teams’ development workflows and experimentation infrastructure

Skills And Experience We Value

  • Proven experience with machine learning, preferably GenAI or LLM applications
  • Experience with programming languages: Python and SQL, etc.
  • Comfort with tools such as (or similar to): Databricks, Pendo, Visual Studio, etc.
  • Entrepreneurial mindset: works with minimal guidance and solves their own problems
  • Ability to translate ambiguous tasks into clear outputs
  • Strong communication skills and curiosity about how product and ML intersect

What Would Really Make You Stand Out

  • Front-end experience (React, Streamlit, etc.) for prototyping
  • Familiarity with multilingual NLP or localization tools
  • This internship is a chance to work on frontier tech with real-world applications and visibility. You’ll get to experiment, deliver, and influence the future of AI at Dayforce.

We understand that no matter where you are, choosing a new employer is always one of life’s most stressful events. We encourage you to check us out at Dayforce.com or follow us on LinkedIn to learn more about how we are impacting the world of work for our customers, employees and you!

Fraudulent Recruiting Beware of fraudulent recruiting. Legitimate Dayforce contacts will use an @dayforce.com or @Ceridian.com email address. We do not request money, checks, equipment orders, or sensitive personal data during the recruitment process. If you have been asked for any of the above, or believe you have been contacted by someone posing as a Dayforce employee, please refer to our fraudulent recruiting statement found here: https://www.dayforce.com/be-aware-of-recruiting-fraud
