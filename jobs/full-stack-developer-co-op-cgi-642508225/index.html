Full Stack Developer Co-op

at CGI

📍 Location Victoria, British Columbia
📋 Details Co-op · 8mo · Summer 2026
📅 Posted Mar 13, 2026

About the Role

Develop APIs and web apps, test and debug

About CGI

Canada-based IT services firm, diverse client projects globally

Full Description

We are seeking a hard-working Full Stack Developer looking to kick-start their career through joining our growing and dynamic team. This is a great opportunity to work under the mentorship of our Project and Program Manager to assist and support the development team in all design-related tasks.

At CGI, our strength is built on our ability to work together. Our diverse set of backgrounds offer different perspectives and new ways of thinking. It encourages lively discussions, inspires thought leadership, and helps us build better solutions for our people and clients. We are seeking someone who thrives in this setting and is inspired to craft meaningful solutions through true collaboration.

Perks of Being a CGI Co-op:

  • Participate in monthly socials to meet co-ops across the country.
  • Access professional development workshops hosted by domain experts on topics ranging from how to be a top consultant, how to apply the agile methodology, data & analytics and more!

To learn more about CGI and hear from our Co-ops and New Grads, visit www.cgi.com/startmycareer

Your future duties and responsibilities

  • To join and contribute to a cross-functional software development team.
  • Develop API services and web applications as per the client specifications using coding standards and standard methodologies.
  • Perform unit testing and defect fixes.
  • Participate in project planning meetings along with all relevant agile ceremonies.
  • Co-ordinate with business analysts and testing teams for delivery.
  • Work standard and additional overtime hours to meet project outputs and pager support.

Required Qualifications To Be Successful In This Role What We're Looking for:

  • Willing to be a player of many hats
  • Takes accountability for results and exhibits a “can do” demeanor.
  • Has an appetite to continuously evolve and improve, address difficult problems and seek/give constant feedback.
  • Demonstrates flexibility and is able to move forward without having all of the answers.
  • Champions a high-performance environment and contributes to an inclusive work environment.

Your Qualifications:

  • Must be part of an accredited Co-op Program
  • Experience with Java and/or .NET and familiarity with all stages of the SDLC lifecycle.
  • Familiarity with JavaScript frameworks such as Angular, VUE and React.
  • Currently enrolled in Computer Science related program.
  • Ready to complete 8-month co-op term
  • Basic knowledge of relational databases and SQL.
  • Analytical conduct and good problem-solving skills.
  • Superb communication and interpersonal skills.Together, as owners, let's turn meaningful insights into action.

You are invited to be an owner from day 1 as we work together to bring our Dream to life. That's why we call ourselves CGI Partners rather than employees. We benefit from our collective success and actively shape our company's strategy and direction.

Your work creates value. You'll develop innovative solutions and build relationships with teammates and clients while accessing global capabilities to scale your ideas, embrace new opportunities, and benefit from expansive industry and technology expertise.

You'll shape your career by joining a company built to grow and last. You'll be supported by leaders who care about your health and well-being and provide you with opportunities to deepen your skills and broaden your horizons.
