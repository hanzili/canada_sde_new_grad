Full Description

Job Title: Junior Account Executive (International Payments)

Location: Toronto Financial District (Hybrid - 3 days onsite)

Join the Official Global FX Partner of UFC!

Ready to join a global S&P 1000 FinTech organization with ambitious growth aspirations?. We are seeking a confident, intuitive, and highly competitive Junior AE to expand our client base in Canada. This opportunity offers a unique blend of entrepreneurship, unlimited earning potential , and fantastic development at a publicly traded company.

The Mission:

Your primary objective is to generate and qualify high-quality leads that fuel the company's growth, focused on solving clients' cross-border payment challenges.

  • Prospecting: Engage prospects through outbound methods (cold calls, emails, LinkedIn).
  • Target Audience: Speak directly to C-Suite executives and finance leaders about managing foreign exchange complexity.
  • Metric Focused: Success is tied directly to the number of qualified first appointments booked .

What We Look For:

  • A positive mindset and passion for achieving goals.
  • A collaborative team player who excels at facilitating strong internal and external relationships.
  • The ability to immediately implement feedback into your sales process (Coachability).
  • A Bachelor’s Degree is required

Benefits Snapshot: 19 days PTO plus sick days, Sales/Capital Markets training, regular team socials, and the chance to qualify for an Annual Sales Conference Offsite . Medical, Dental & RRSP contribution!
