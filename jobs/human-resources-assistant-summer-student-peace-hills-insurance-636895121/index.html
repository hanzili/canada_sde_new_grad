Human Resources Assistant - Summer Student

at Peace Hills Insurance

📍 Location Edmonton, AB T5J 3N3
📋 Details Temporary · May 4, 2026 – Aug 28, 2026
📅 Posted Mar 04, 2026

About the Role

Assist HR recruitment, onboarding, and admin tasks

About Peace Hills Insurance

Stable insurer with professional development opportunities

Full Description

We currently have a temporary summer student position available for a Human Resources Assistant in our Edmonton office. The duration of this role will be from approximately May 4th, 2026 – August 28th, 2026. Working with the Senior Human Resources Advisor, this role is an excellent opportunity for a post-secondary student looking to gain some practical, hands-on working experience.

What You’ll Do:

  • Work with the Talent Acquisition and Onboarding Advisor to support the recruitment process through conducting pre-screen interviews and conducting reference and background checks;
  • Complete various administrative tasks related to the employee lifecycle;
  • Participate in the digitization of employee files;
  • Assist in the preparation for and attendance at potential job fairs and recruitment activities;
  • Assist in completing research on a variety of HR topics including salary trends, legislative changes, etc.
  • Track employee checklist and communicate any follow up needs for missing documentation;
  • Other various human resources related duties as required.

What You’ll Bring:

  • Must be currently enrolled in and returning to a related post-secondary program;
  • Excellent computer literacy and keyboarding skills;
  • Effective communication skills with an emphasis on customer service;
  • Excellent organizational and time management skills;
  • Attentive to detail;
  • Ability to work effectively within a team;
  • Demonstrates initiative and willingness to learn;
  • Must be able to work from the office in our Downtown Edmonton location.

What You’ll Experience:

  • Healthy work-life balance including a day off every 4 weeks;
  • Social events held throughout the summer;
  • Corporate experience in a stable and essential industry, at a company with an exceptional corporate culture and opportunities for professional development and advancement.

Why Peace Hills:

Peace Hills General Insurance Company has been serving Western Canada since 1982 with home, auto and business insurance products. Our head office is in Edmonton, Alberta, with branch offices in Calgary and Vancouver. We currently employ over 300 staff who work with more than 1,000 broker offices across British Columbia, Alberta, Saskatchewan, Manitoba, the Northwest Territories, Nunavut and Yukon Territory.

Peace Hills is proudly owned by the Samson Cree Nation, and it is through Wahkôtowin – a Cree word that means “kinship” and the interconnectedness of all beings – that we are and continue to be a respected insurer that provides creative and responsive solutions.
