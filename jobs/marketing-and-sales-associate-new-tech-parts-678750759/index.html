Full Description

New Tech Parts is a growing supplier of heavy-duty truck aftermarket parts in Canada. We are seeking a motivated and results-driven Marketing and Sales Associate to join our team in Mississauga.

Responsibilities:

● Collaborate with the marketing team to execute digital campaigns

● Design basic marketing materials (flyers, posts, ads)

● Proficient in Canva or Adobe tools

● Update website content (WIX platform)

● Support brand growth across Instagram, Facebook, LinkedIn, and YouTube

● Identify and contact potential customers (machine shops, dealers, parts shops)

● Conduct outbound calls, emails, and follow-ups

● Answer incoming phone calls and respond to customer inquiries

● Experience with B2B marketing preferred

● Familiar with CRM systems and MS Office

● Strong communication and organizational skills

● Must be fluent in English, Hindi, and Punjabi

What We Offer:
