About the Role

Assist software development for UI and embedded systems

About Ross Video

Canadian tech company specializing in broadcast hardware

Full Description

WHERE YOU'LL BE WORKING
Ross Video is a world leader in High Definition Video Production Equipment, designed and manufactured in Canada and sold world-wide. Our products are used by a who's who of the global entertainment industry, including top broadcast networks like NBC, ABC, CBS and Fox in the USA; Rogers, CTV and the CBC in Canada and others around the world from Fiji to Finland and everywhere in between. We are # 1 in providing behind the scenes HD production gear used in Sports Stadiums including places where the Atlanta Falcons, NY Mets, LA Lakers, Pittsburgh Penguins and Buffalo Bills play. Our equipment was used extensively at the Vancouver 2010 and other Olympic games. Our systems are used by top acts including Springsteen, Blink 182, The Red Hot Chili Peppers and the Police to name a few. Top webcasters like Revision 3 and MLB.com use Ross. Our equipment is even used on the International Space Station. Join Ross and you will have a chance to work on advanced technology used in a wide variety of very cool ways by our clients.

WHAT YOU'LL BE DOING
Working with a development team at the Ottawa Research and Development facility, and reporting to a senior developer, the successful candidate will experience first-hand the highs and lows of real software development while working with top-level developers and team leaders. Specifically, you’ll assist the Hyperconverged Solutions (HCS) Product Team with Software Development tasks, including UI and/or Embedded systems programming. A variety of different languages and technologies are used, providing opportunities for interested candidates with a variety of different interests and backgrounds.

This position is located at our office on Auriga Drive in Nepean however most teams have adopted a hybrid-work model. Our pay rates range from $24 - $35 per hour, based on experience.

WHAT YOU KNOW

  • Currently enrolled in Engineering, Computer Engineering, or Computer Science at the Intermediate level to Senior level
  • Programming experience, ideally in one or more of the following languages C & C++
  • Other desirable skills or areas of interest, considered a plus:
    • Yocto Project: BitBake, OpenEmbedded, and the Yocto build system.
  • Embedded Linux Basics – Uboot, Linux kernel, cross-compiling, boot flow.
  • Experience or working knowledge of micro-controller, micro-processor, cross-compiling, compiler, boot flow, I2C, SPI
  • Experience working hands on with hardware
  • Experience in a Linux development environment
  • Python scripting
  • Git
  • Docker

WHO YOU ARE

  • You enjoy new technical challenges and are excited at the prospect of writing code for actual, shipping products. Tasks can range from creating custom-built software applications helping to tweak existing practices and designs. Big or small, you're up for the job.
  • You are a self-starter and self-motivated. You're happy to take the reins and take initiative. When you finish something, you're already looking for the next challenge.
  • You like people. Knowledge is all around you, and you know that finding and talking to the right people is the way to get things done.
  • You recognize that communication skills are as important as technical skills.
  • You love learning new stuff. You know how to ask questions - and how to listen to answers. You absorb knowledge like a sponge. You're happy to dig through code, track down obscure issues, and solve problems other people haven't.

WHAT ARE YOU WAITING FOR?
If this sounds like you - or who you want to be - then include a few words with your application about who you are and what you want out of this position at Ross Video. Many of our current team members started their career as a co-op at Ross. What are you waiting for? We want to hear from you.

U.S. EXPORT ADMINSTRATION REGULATIONS

Ross Video complies with the U.S. Export Administration Regulations, which place limits on sharing certain technologies and source code with individuals from countries under U.S. embargo.

How this impacts our hiring…

As part of this compliance, we must consider a candidate’s citizenship and permanent residency when evaluating eligibility for roles that involve access to specific technologies. Due to these legal restrictions, we’re unfortunately unable to proceed with candidates whose current citizenship or permanent residency is in a country under U.S. embargo (currently Cuba, Iran, Syria, or North Korea).

We appreciate your understanding as we meet these important legal requirements.

EQUITY, DIVERSITY & INCLUSION
At Ross, we embrace diversity, and we want you to bring your authentic self to work. We are committed to building a team that includes a variety of backgrounds, perspectives, and skills. Inclusivity drives innovation and creativity, and that’s something we’re passionate about at Ross! We believe everyone should be able to enjoy a rewarding career at Ross, regardless of race, colour, religion, sexual orientation, gender, gender identity or expression, marital status, age, veteran status, physical or mental disability. If a disability means that you need any additional support during the recruitment process, please contact Talent Acquisition and we will make all reasonable efforts to accommodate your request.
