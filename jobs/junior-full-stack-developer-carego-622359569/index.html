About the Role

Develop, test, and maintain .NET web apps

About CareGo

Automation tech firm specializing in TELIA platform

Full Description

CareGo Process Automation Services is a high tech, rapid growth company made up of a diverse group of individuals with various backgrounds in engineering, project management, architecture / layout, software, automation, vision tech, machine learning, etc. CareGo's flagship product is the TELIA optimization and automation system. TELIA uses dynamic decision-making algorithms to help manufacturers dramatically improve the way they store, handle, and ship their products.

CareGo is advancing industrial automation and traceability through its TELIA platform. We are looking for passionate, hard-working, and self-motivated software developers who bring fresh ideas to our software engineering team. The successful applicants will be those that demonstrate comprehensive technical knowledge, superior problem-solving skills and excellent design skills.

What you will do

AI Driven Responsibilities:

• Leverage AI tools and platforms to enhance technical design, coding, debugging, and unit test creation;

• Use AI models to refactor code, generate clean and clear documentation, and troubleshoot defects.

• Stay current with emerging AI trends in software development and evaluate tools for continuous improvement.

Software Development Life Cycle Responsibilities:

• Design, develop, test, and support client facing web applications using the Microsoft Visual Studio and .NET toolset;

• Design front-end with the industry best practices;

• Build reusable and fully tested (unit tests) code;

• Optimize applications for maximum usability and function;

• Coordinate cross-functionally to ensure projects meet business objectives;

• Troubleshoot issues and solve problems as needed;

What you should have

• Degree in Computer Science or equivalent;

• 1-5+ years of experience designing and implementing robust and scalable software applications

• 1-5+ years of experience with C# .NET and Entity Framework

• Familiarity with one or more relation databases and related technologies (preferably SQL Server)

• Familiarity with the use of AI tools to develop technical design, write code, write documentation, write unit tests, review code and others

• Good understanding of object-oriented design, algorithms, data structures and databases

• Good knowledge of designing user interface and user experience

• Good knowledge of RESTFul APIs and ASP .NET Web APIs

• Familiarity of modern authorization mechanisms

• Excellent written and verbal communication skills

What would be nice to have

• Experience developing safety critical systems

• Experience interfacing with SCADA systems

• Experience with Microsoft Power BI

• Experience with cloud-hosting (Azure, AWS, Google Cloud, etc.)

What you can expect from life at CareGo:

• Competitive salary + discretionary bonus;

• Flexible work environment;

• Health and dental benefits;

• Matching RRSP program;

• Access to a Dragons Den style R&D program;

• Social committee events including BBQs, Christmas party, ski trips, pot lucks, charity breakfasts;

• A great family orientated work environment which likes to give back to the community
