About GenPark

Full Description

Company Description

GenPark leverages intelligent Brand and User Agents to help brands connect with and convert global Gen Z consumers more effectively. Through personalized AI-generated content, interactive Agents, and immersive AI video experiences, GenPark enables brands to engage with their target audience in the right context, achieving higher conversions at reduced customer acquisition costs. Beyond being a marketing tool, GenPark's AI-native system learns from user behavior and feedback to create smarter matching between products, services, digital assets, and consumers across global markets.

Role Description

This is a full-time hybrid role for a Mobile Development Intern based in Toronto, ON, with flexibility for some remote work. As a Mobile Development Intern, you will be responsible for assisting in the development, testing, and optimization of mobile applications. You will collaborate with the team to design user-friendly and efficient interfaces, debug issues, and develop innovative solutions. Additionally, you will contribute to brainstorming new mobile app features and functionalities to enhance the user experience.

  • Basic understanding of Sales concepts and their relevance to user engagement and product development
  • Eagerness to learn, adaptability, and a proactive approach to tasks
