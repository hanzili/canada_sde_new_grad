Job Description RESEARCH INSTITUTE OF THE MUHC The Research Institute of the McGill University Health Centre (RI-MUHC) is a world-renowned biomedical and hospital research centre. Located in Montreal, Quebec, the Institute is the research arm of the McGill University Health Centre (MUHC) affiliated with the Faculty of Medicine at McGill University. The RI-MUHC is supported in part by the Fonds de recherche du Québec - Santé (FRQS).

Position Summary Department / Research Program: Advancing Person-Centered DE-Prescription Practice Through Inverse Knowledge Translation

The research project "Advancing Person-Centered DE-Prescription Practice Through Inverse Knowledge Translation" is a community-driven partnership with the Inner Compass Initiative (ICI), a global non-profit organization that seeks to build an inclusive, knowledge-producing grassroots community where members share their lived experience with mental health struggles and the mental health care system. ICI is a full collaborative partner in this research, and the project is co-developed and carried out in close collaboration with ICI co-investigators and lived-experience experts. This project aims to identify salient themes and archetypal narratives of withdrawal and tapering trajectories using data from "ICI Connect user profiles," combining descriptive and qualitative thematic analysis.

The Junior Research Assistant will report to the Principal Investigator and contribute to the coding of interview transcripts and data analysis, while also serving as an intermediary between ICI and the AG-C lab. The incumbent will also have the opportunity to contribute to the dissemination of results via publications, reports, and conference presentations.

General Duties

  • Contribute to the coding of interview transcripts from the ICI user profile database, using a collectively developed coding dictionary,
  • Collaborate with the team of coders during guided coding sessions to ensure consistency and rigor in qualitative analysis,
  • Participate in research team discussions on the development and revision of the coding dictionary,
  • Contribute to thematic content analysis and the identification of common themes, patterns, and archetypal narratives in withdrawal/tapering trajectories,
  • Serve as an intermediary between ICI and the AG-C lab, facilitating communication and coordination between the two partner organizations,
  • Participate in team-building meetings and data interpretation discussions,
  • Assist in maintaining and organizing data in the qualitative analysis software,
  • Engage in administrative duties related to the project,
  • Contribute to the preparation of reports, manuscripts, and conference presentations.
