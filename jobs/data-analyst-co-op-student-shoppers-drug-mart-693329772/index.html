Full Description

At Shoppers Drug Mart, we’re innovating health and wellness in Canada. From prescriptions to walk-in clinics, and a beloved loyalty program, we’re caring and supporting our customers in new ways every day.
With more than 1,300 locally owned and operated stores from coast to coast, the Shoppers Drug Mart network is more than a great place to shop, it’s a great place to work. We’re committed to building our talented team who champion collaboration, kindness and inclusivity. Join our team, and help recognize a healthy future for your career and for all Canadians.

Job Responsibilities:

  • Data engineering, modelling and maintenance – Gather, clean, profile and transform data from large scale data environments to ensure data accuracy and integrity
  • Dashboarding - Design, build, and maintain key dashboards and reports to provide self-serve analytics to business teams
  • KPI Tracking - Trend analysis and ability to draw out insights by working with large complex datasets
  • Strategic recommendations - Translate complex data findings into clear, compelling narratives and actionable recommendations for business stakeholders
  • Ad-hoc analysis - Respond to analytical requests from business leaders, providing timely and accurate insights to support strategic decision-making

Requirements:

  • Currently enrolled in Bachelor’s or Master’s degree in a quantitative field such as Statistics, Economics, Mathematics, Computer Science, Engineering, or a related discipline.
  • Professional experience in a data analytics role preferred but not required, with a strong business focus and ability to generate actionable insights
  • High level of proficiency in Excel, SQL and Python
  • Hands-on experience developing dashboards with data visualization tools such as PowerBI
  • Understanding of databases, data modelling, and ETL Concepts
  • Experience working with large-scale data environments (Google BigQuery, Teradata) is a significant asset
  • Proven ability to transform ambiguous business questions into a structured analytical plan
  • Strong problem-solving skills with an exceptional eye for detail
  • Excellent data storytelling skills with the ability to present complex information clearly and concisely to both technical and non-technical audiences.
  • Strong written and verbal communication skills

Why work at Shoppers Drug Mart?
Acting as consultants, supporting internal customers and the Associate-Owners, you will get experience unique to the retail industry while working for an award-winning national brand. Benefit from a competitive pay program and online learning through Academy.
Take ownership of your work and find more ways to care about your work, co-workers, customers and community.
