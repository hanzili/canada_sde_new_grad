About the Role

About BBA inc.

Canada’s leading private consulting firm

Full Description

Type of position: Regular

Your future role on our team

This is a unique opportunity for you to work with experts and engineering professionals across Canada on interesting and challenging projects. You will serve as a technical resource and work closely with our experts, senior engineers and designers on stimulating heavy industrial projects in the mining and power sectors, including studies. This position will allow you to directly apply and develop your knowledge in structural design and stress analysis. You will be exposed to modern design tools, including 3D solid modelling and complex calculation software. As time progresses, project duties and responsibilities will increase, facilitating your registration as a professional engineer.

With us, you’ll get the opportunity to...

  • Prepare calculations, specifications and detail designs.
  • Design steel and concrete structures and foundations.
  • Supervise the production of design drawings and review contractor shop drawings.
  • Coordinate design activities with other disciplines (mechanical, electrical, etc.).
  • Prepare estimates, schedules and budgets, where required.
  • Plan, organize and review work (drawings, calculations, specifications).
  • Perform periodic site inspections, where required.
  • Support the preparation of formal written reports and presentations for clients.
  • Maintain cordial, close relationships with clients or their representatives.

Do your qualities and values match our corporate culture?

  • Autonomous
  • Show an aptitude for self-development
  • Result Oriented
  • Excellent communication and interpersonal skill
  • Attention to detail
  • Strong organizational skills
  • Thirst to learn and excel
  • Rigour and ethical
  • Caring mindset that puts people first

Overview of certifications and job requirements:

  • Bachelor’s degree in civil-Structural Engineering. Having a master’s degree in structural engineering will be considered as an asset.
  • 3+ years of related experience
  • A cumulative GPA of at least 3.0 on a 4.0 scale
  • Advanced knowledge of structural analysis software (SAP2000, SFrame or equal)
  • Eligible for PEO registration
  • Proficient with CSA S16 and CSA A23.3 requirements
  • Ability to read drawings
  • Proficient with computer-based tools
  • Adept in written and spoken English, required to prepare reports and presentations
  • Experience with designing steel or concrete structures for heavy industrial projects, including equipment foundations, industrial buildings, and process-related infrastructure.
  • Asset: experience in dynamic analysis and design of structures/foundations supporting vibratory and rotating equipment in heavy industrial environments.
  • Asset: fluent in French or Spanish

Additional skills: - Excellent communication skills (written and spoken) - Rigorous, excellent analytical skills and attention to detail in your work - Organized, results-oriented - Flexible, willingness to learn new things and apply them to work - Adept at teamwork, collaborative mindset - Ability to maintain positive peer relationships Mindful, aware of project objectives at all times and the drive to work with others to achieve goals

An overview of BBA’s Total Rewards

  • Annual base salary $73,000 - $100,000
  • Annual premium program for regular employees
  • Onsite mobility premium
  • Time bank
  • Cellphone fee reimbursements
  • Group insurance plan from the first day of employment, including disability insurance and a telemedicine program
  • Retirement savings plan
  • Annual vacation and sick leave Premium for employee referrals

BBA considers many factors when determining the annual base salary, including market, education, relevant and demonstrated work experience, technical assessment, qualifications, certifications and other relevant skills.

At BBA, you get many benefits:

  • Flexible schedule that combines office presence and telecommuting
  • A collaborative team that shares its ideas and knowledge
  • Committees involved in important causes: diversity, social commitment, and more
  • An inclusive culture that values diversity, respect and openness

About BBA

For over 40 years, we’ve been pushing the boundaries of engineering to serve our clients in the Energy and Natural Resources sector. At BBA, we develop innovative, flexible and sustainable solutions, from strategy to execution.

BBA is one of Canada’s leading private consulting engineering firms, focused on practice (PCB), with a network of 15 offices. Our teams work closely to deliver projects that shape tomorrow’s industry at the local, national and international levels.

Our people’s passion and excellence make BBA a Canada’s Best Employer and one of Canada’s Best Managed Companies.

We have unique talents, and we’re committed to providing a workplace where you can be yourself. Job postings are written in gender-neutral language.
