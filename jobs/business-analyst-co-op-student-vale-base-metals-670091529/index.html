About Vale Base Metals

Full Description

Ready to build a rewarding career in an industry that is transforming the future? Who We Are We are Vale Base Metals – a global supplier of the critical minerals transforming the future.

With industry-leading reserves of copper and nickel in Canada, Brazil, and Indonesia, and state-of-the-art infrastructure in the U.K., Canada, and Japan, VBM delivers a secure and responsible supply of high-quality products to customers across Europe, the Americas, and Asia.

Global demand for critical minerals is growing exponentially. From energy to aerospace, batteries to magnets, robotics to artificial intelligence, a secure and responsible supply of critical minerals is fundamental to power modern technologies. And at Vale Base Metals, we are transforming our business to provide a secure and responsible supply of these minerals.

Join our diverse team of 13,000 talented individuals committed to providing mineral security to a world in transition. We invite you to put your skills to work, driving meaningful and enduring change while enjoying a rewarding career.

The Opportunity We are currently seeking a Business Analyst Co-Op Student to join our Information Technology team in Toronto, Canada.

This is a 8 -12 month-fixed term co-op position starting in June 2026. This posting is for an existing position vacancy.

As a Co-op Student, you will work directly with the IT Director and the technology leadership team. This role is ideal for a student with an engineering foundation and a business mindset who enjoys turning complex technical information into clear business recommendations, creating executive-ready materials. You will support IT leadership team to prepare high quality steering committee materials, turning updates into rich discussion-starters and decision-ready options. The role will also build and maintain the IT Office knowledge repository.

In this position you will:

  • Design and build an Office of IT Knowledge Repository, collecting and organizing business data and markets insight in a way that is easy to consume by the IT Leadership team.
  • Prepare executive-ready materials for the IT and key governance forums (briefing notes, steering committee packs, agendas, decision memos, meeting summaries, and follow-up action logs).
  • Build and maintain lightweight reporting (dashboards, KPI summaries, status reports) using Excel/Power BI/PowerPoint/Copilot and other Microsoft 365 and AI tools.
  • Help coordinate cross-functional stakeholders (IT, Finance, Operations, Security, vendors) to ensure timely decisions and closure on actions from governance forums.
  • Draft and refine clear stakeholder communications for senior audiences (one-page updates, slides, FAQs, talking points). Audiences include the company's Executive Committee, Audits & Risk Committee, Business Process Working Groups, IT Architecture & Procurement Group and other executive forums.

What We Offer You

  • Competitive compensation
  • Flexible work arrangements
  • Work culture dedicated to safety, diversity & inclusion, and career growth
  • Online training and career development opportunities
  • Opportunities to build your network and lots of fun!

About You Education:

  • Currently enrolled in an undergraduate program in Engineering (e.g., Process, Chemical, Industrial Engineering), with a strong business orientation, or pursuing a graduate degree such as an MBA.
  • Demonstrated business exposure through coursework or experience (e.g., business electives, internships, student consulting, entrepreneurship, or case competitions).

Skills:

  • Strong analytical and problem-solving skills; able to structure ambiguous concepts and produce clear narrative and outputs.
  • Excellent written and verbal communication skills, including the ability to tailor messages to executive audiences both in terms of content and format.
  • Proficiency with Microsoft 365 (Word, PowerPoint, Excel, Teams & Copilot); comfort creating high-quality slides and concise written summaries.
  • Interest in exploring how AI-based tools can be used to augment and speed up knowledge collection and presentation.
  • Professional discretion and integrity when handling sensitive or confidential information.

Why Toronto Vale Base Metals' Toronto office serves as a pivotal hub for the company's operations. This office is central to Vale Base Metals’ finance, strategic planning, commercial (marketing and sales), and sustainability teams, driving key initiatives that support the company's global mission. Toronto’s vibrant, diverse, and dynamic environment enhances Vale Base Metals’ ability to innovate and excel in these critical areas, reflecting the city's status as a leading international business center.
