About GHY International

Full Description

GHY International has an exciting opportunity for the role of Junior Data Engineer / Junior DBA

working out of our Head Office in Winnipeg, MB.

The Junion Data Engineer/Junior DBA will support our Azure-based data platform built on Databricks, Delta Lake, and Medallion Architecture (Bronze/Silver/Gold). The role focuses on data transformations using PySpark, Spark SQL, and keeping our pipelines reliable and well-documented. We do not use on‑prem SQL - everything runs in Databricks.

Join GHY and Benefit:

  • Work Life Balance – We mean it: banked time program, paid volunteer days, social committee, cultural surveys
  • Fair Compensation – With scheduled salary assessments, salary scales, RRSP/DPSP program, Bonuses.
  • Excellent Benefits – Subsidized health, dental, short and long term disability, AD&D, HCSA, EAP and critical illness.
  • Professional Development –Employee development, external training, leadership development, career planning & advancement.

What You'll Do:

  • Maintain and enhance Medallion pipelines across Bronze, Silver, and Gold layers.
  • Build and troubleshoot transformations primarily in PySpark.
  • Leverage experience with pandas, Polars, or Koalas as a foundation to learn PySpark on the job.
  • Write, analyze, and tune Spark SQL queries in Databricks.
  • Work with Azure DevOps (repos, pull requests, branching, CI/CD pipelines).
  • Create runbooks, documentation, and improve monitoring, reliability, and operational readiness.

What You Bring:

  • Strong SQL fundamentals (Spark SQL experience is a plus, not required).
  • Experience with Python + dataframe tools (pandas, Polars, Koalas, or similar).
  • Curiosity about learning PySpark — prior PySpark experience not required.
  • Understanding of Delta Lake or Medallion concepts is valuable, but not mandatory.
  • Strong problem-solving skills and the ability to research through technical challenges.

Nice To Have:

  • Experience with Databricks or similar distributed compute platforms.
  • Exposure to Azure Data Factory, Microsoft Fabric, or other orchestration tools.
  • Basic familiarity with Azure DevOps pipelines or CI/CD.
  • Understanding of ADLS, Parquet, or Delta file formats.

Working Conditions:

  • SCHEDULE: Monday-Friday
  • START DATE: ASAP
  • LOCATION: Winnipeg, MB
