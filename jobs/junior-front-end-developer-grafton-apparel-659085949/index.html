Full Description

Grafton Apparel believes everyone deserves to look great! We fit your body, your budget, your style and with the confidence you get when you look and feel great! Grafton Apparel Ltd., owner and operator of Tip Top, George Richards & Mr. Big & Tall Menswear is a Canadian company that has been in business for over 100 years. Taking its strength from over a century of heritage, today Grafton Apparel remains committed to the retail clothing industry by responding to the ever changing demands of the market. Not only do we understand the needs of our customers, we understand the needs of our people too! We are truly committed to both our employees and our customers – a company that you will be proud to work for.Come join the team at Grafton Apparel where we are always looking for motivated, collaborative and product-driven individuals who are passionate about fashion and looking to grow and develop within our company.

We are seeking a Front-End Developer with a strong focus on Shopify and a keen eye for UI/UX design to join our team. This role is ideal for a developer who has mastered the basics of web development, utilizes modern AI-assisted coding tools, and is ready to take ownership of e-commerce experiences while implementing modern testing and automation practices. In this role, you will bridge the gap between design and technical implementation, ensuring that our Shopify storefronts are visually compelling, performant, and capable of supporting modern, agentic user interfaces.

What you will do in this role:

  • Shopify Theme Development: Build, customize, and maintain high-performance Shopify themes using Liquid, HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. Ensure compatibility with Shopify Plus features and Functions.
  • UI/UX Implementation: Translate Figma designs into pixel-perfect, responsive code, maintaining high standards for typography, spacing, and user flow.
  • Agentic UI Integration: Build resilient front-end components that interact with AI features and agents (e.g., managing streaming text states, dynamic loading indicators, and conversational interfaces).
  • Automation & Testing: Develop and help establish automated test suites (e.g., Playwright) to ensure cross-browser compatibility. Utilize AI tooling to accelerate test generation and debugging.
  • Business Central POS Config: Configuration of Point of Sales interfaces inside Microsoft Business Central
  • App Integration: Configure and style third-party Shopify apps and integrate them seamlessly into the theme architecture.
  • Performance Optimization: Monitor and improve Core Web Vitals, ensuring fast load times and smooth mobile experiences.
  • Version Control & Workflow: Manage code deployments and collaboration using Git following a structured branching strategy, while actively integrating AI-assistants (like Copilot/Claude) into your daily development workflow.

Your qualifications include:

  • Shopify Expertise: 1-2 years' experience with the Shopify platform, including Liquid templating, Schema settings, and the Shopify CLI.
  • Front-End Core: Strong proficiency in HTML5, CSS3 (SCSS), and modern JavaScript (ES6+).
  • AI Tooling & Concepts: Practical experience using AI coding assistants inside VS Code to improve efficiency.
  • Design Fundamentals: A solid understanding of UI/UX principles (visual hierarchy, accessibility/a11y, and user-centric design).
  • Testing Automation: Familiarity with automated testing frameworks and a commitment to "test-driven" front-end development.
  • Problem Solving: Ability to debug complex CSS issues and JavaScript errors across different devices and browsers.
  • Tech Stak &Tools: Figma, SCSS styled components, Vanilla JS, Vue.js or React, VS Code, Copilot or similar AI assisted environment.
