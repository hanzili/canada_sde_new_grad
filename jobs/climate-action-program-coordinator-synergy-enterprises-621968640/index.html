Job Description

This position is supported by the Pacific Institute for Climate Solutions (PICS) Climate Internship Program.

As a Climate Action Program Coordinator, you will assist in the development and execution of Synergy Academy, our climate literacy training programs, as well as other communication and education-related tasks at Synergy.

Key Responsibilities: Depending on your interests, strengths, and the business's needs, you may work on some or all of the projects below.

  • Assess and anticipate learning needs to deliver innovative solutions aligned with business priorities
  • Research and support adoption of new technologies and processes that elevate the learner experience
  • Developing and supporting the delivery of Synergy Academy and the expansion of educational offerings. Host and organize trainings, coordinate instructors, and facilitate discussions. Provide follow-up resources on climate literacy and decarbonization. Report and evaluate by compiling findings, feedback, and impact report.
  • Design and lead complex learning programs (virtual, onsite, hybrid) that meet CE requirements and deliver an exceptional participant experience
  • Writing internal and external communication - writing blogs, articles, case studies, event announcements, emails, newsletters, and website content
  • Creating educational and social content in line with Synergy's focus on climate literacy and education. Creation of educational materials (guides on food waste, climate adaptation) and supporting resources (worksheets, templates).
  • Assist in partnership development and delivery.
  • Support in compiling reports for internal and external stakeholders, summarizing performance, and providing data-driven recommendations for improvement.

Qualifications:

  • Education:
  • You're currently enrolled in a post-secondary program in environmental science, business, marketing, communications, or a related field.
  • Experience:
  • You understand the challenge posed by climate change and the key elements of climate action. Ideally, you’re interested in applying these strategies to a business context.
  • You are familiar with or interested in learning more about content management systems and learning management systems.
  • As a detail-oriented person and highly task-oriented individual, you are able to meet deadlines and take ownership of your work.
  • You're a master at creating inclusive, engaging social media content for Instagram, Facebook, Twitter, LinkedIn, Pinterest, Snapchat, and TikTok.

Skills:

  • Detail oriented who can find areas for improvement.
  • Fast-paced, sometimes ambiguous environments are no problem for you.
  • It is an asset to have a basic understanding of web design, WordPress, and HTML.
  • The ability to edit images and videos with software (Lightroom, Canva, iMovie, Adobe Creative) is an asset.
  • Proficiency with LMS platforms (preferably Thinkific) and digital collaboration tools (nice to have)

Requirements

  • Either a Canadian Citizen, permanent resident or a protected person defined by the Immigration and Refugee Protection Act.
  • Must be a currently enrolled student in an accredited Canadian post-secondary institution.
  • The student cannot be an international student with a work permit.
  • Hybrid role, there will be on-site opportunities to capture content on Vancouver Island.
  • Eligible students must be enrolled – or accepted for enrolment – in a fulltime undergraduate or graduate program at one of the four Pacific Institute for Climate Solutions (PICS) partner institutions: UVIC, UBC, SFU or UNBC.
  • Students must either be returning to complete their degree programs following the internship or have completed their programs the semester prior to the start of the internship.

How to Apply:

Please apply with your resume and cover letter to operations@synergyenterprises.ca no later than March 1, 2026.

Working at Synergy

At Synergy, we believe businesses have the opportunity to impact global change and bring the world into a new age of environmental and social responsibility. Our mission is to leverage the power of business for a regenerative economy. We believe in using business as a force for good, which is why Synergy is a Certified B Corp. We are proud of our hard-working, passionate team and our internal policies that support wellness, continuing education, and living true to our values. Our goal is to cultivate a world class team of brilliant people doing impactful work. Our team is healthy, dynamic, and collaborative.

Synergy Perks

  • Remote work-from-home option to allow you to do work in a way that is optimal for you.
  • Weekly check-ins and virtual social events to foster a collaborative, inclusive work culture.
  • Growth opportunities to learn, optimize your skills and begin achieving your career goals.
  • Opportunities to get direct experience in research, marketing, sustainability and decarbonization, and business development.

Values

  • Open-Mindedness - We approach every conversation and challenge with curiosity, empathy, and a genuine desire to understand. We believe everyone has something valuable to contribute.
  • Integrity - We follow through on our commitments and lead with honesty, accountability, and transparency to build lasting trust.
  • Curiosity and Creativity - We approach work with a proactive mindset, optimism, and courage. Curiosity and creativity are essential skills for problem-solving and driving positive change. They are the bedrock of innovation at Synergy.
  • Empowerment & Leadership- We build leadership in others by sharing knowledge, creating space, and championing growth; for our team, our clients, and our partners. Our clients become leaders in their sector, our team are leaders at work.
  • Win-Win Solutions - The concept of Win-win is where Synergy got its name. Inspired by nature’s abilities to form symbiotic relationships to create thriving ecosystems, and by 7 Habits by Stephen Covey: “Synergy is the essence of principle-centred leadership. It catalyzes, unifies, and unleashes the greatest powers within people… Win-win is not a tactic - it is a philosophy.” We drive climate action by designing solutions that create value for both businesses and the planet.
  • Collective Wellbeing - We thrive together by nurturing a culture of trust, support, fulfillment, and genuine care in which all team members, peers, and partners feel valued and empowered.
