Full Description

About KU Power Group

KU Power Group is an established electrical contractor specializing in commercial, industrial, and controls-based projects. We deliver reliable, high-quality installations with a strong focus on safety, precision, and execution.

Position Overview

This is not a traditional internship. You will be our dedicated AI champion for the entire summer. You’ll explore, test, and implement AI tools and solutions across marketing, operations, customer service, sales, data analysis, content creation, and any other area where AI can create value.

What You’ll Do

  • Audit our current tools and processes to identify high-impact AI opportunities
  • Research, test, and recommend AI solutions (ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, Midjourney, automation tools, no-code platforms, etc.)
  • Build and deploy AI-powered workflows (e.g., automated customer support, content generation, data insights, lead qualification, image/video creation)
  • Train our team on new AI tools and create simple “how-to” guides
  • Track ROI and results of every AI initiative you implement
  • Work directly with leadership — your ideas and implementations will have immediate real-world impact

Who We’re Looking For

  • Currently enrolled in a post-secondary program (college or university) in Computer Science, Data Science, Business, Marketing, or related field
  • Strong interest and hands-on experience with generative AI tools
  • Curious, self-starter, and excited to experiment
  • Excellent communication skills — you’ll be teaching others how to use AI
  • Bonus: Experience with Python, Zapier/Make, Notion, or any automation platforms

No prior professional experience required — just passion, curiosity, and the ability to learn fast.

Application Process

Qualified candidates are invited to submit their resume to careers@kupowergroup.com with the subject line: Summer Student - AI Integration

Pay: $25.00-$30.00 per hour

Benefits:

  • On-site parking

Work Location: In person
