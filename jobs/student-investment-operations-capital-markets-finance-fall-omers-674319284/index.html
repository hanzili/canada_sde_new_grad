Job Responsibilities

  • Responsible for public market securities and ensuring book of record data related to life cycle events is complete, accurate and timely.
  • Perform daily verifications between internal and external data for positions, transactions, income and cash. Analyze, explain and resolve all discrepancies on a timely basis.
  • Assist in identifying and assisting in implementing workflow improvements including automation of current processes.
  • Update existing procedures documentation to areas of responsibility. Assist in developing new documentation as required.
  • Prepare reports on public market security information as requested.
  • Assist with special projects as requested.
  • Maintain up to date knowledge of OMERS policies regarding investment authority, signing authority, and third-party agreements and apply to daily activities to ensure compliance.
  • Maintain strong understanding of investment products and customer needs in order to respond quickly and accurately to questions and issues that arise.
  • Foster effective working relationships inclusive of the other teams within CMF, OMERS investment divisions, and external counterparties, prime brokers, custodians as appropriate.

You Should Apply If

  • Current enrollment in an undergraduate Business Administration/Commerce and a demonstrated passion and capability in Investment Operations.
  • Pursuit or completion of relevant industry courses will be an asset.
  • Exposure to public markets, cash and collateral administration, investment accounting, and audit control practices.
  • Excellent interpersonal and communication skills are essential. Ability to build strong relationships with both internal and external stakeholders.
  • Ability to prioritize multiple responsibilities to meet deadlines. Needs to be flexible.
  • Detail oriented and demonstrate aptitude for quantitative and qualitative analysis and problem solving.
  • Proficient in Excel and Word. Investment accounting systems experience will be an asset (eg. Calypso, Charles River Development (CRD) and Bloomberg).
