About Jenna Yuill CPA

Boutique public accounting firm with remote work

Full Description

Join Our Growing Public Accounting Firm

We are a boutique public accounting firm serving based in Port Moody, BC serving small businesses in BC and across Canada. As our firm continues to grow, we are seeking a Junior Accountant to join our team on a fully remote basis.

This role is ideal for an accountant with public practice experience who is comfortable working independently, managing deadlines, and preparing corporate year-end files and corporate tax returns. As a member of our close-knit team, you will have direct access to firm leadership, meaningful opportunities for professional growth, and the ability to make a real impact as our firm expands.

Location: Fully Remote

Compensation: Hourly wage based on experience

Benefits:

  • Vacation pay
  • Flexible work hours
  • Professional development support
  • Growth opportunities within a growing firm
  • Fully remote work environment

About the Role

The Junior Accountant will primarily be responsible for preparing corporate year-end files and corporate income tax returns for a diverse group of owner-managed businesses. The successful candidate will be highly organized, detail-oriented, and capable of working independently in a remote environment.

Approximately 80% of this role will involve corporate year-end engagements and T2 corporate tax return preparation. Additional responsibilities will include bookkeeping support, GST/PST filings, and personal tax return preparation during tax season.

Key ResponsibilitiesCorporate Accounting and Tax

  • Prepare corporate year-end working paper files and financial statements
  • Prepare T2 corporate income tax returns
  • Assist with tax planning and tax compliance engagements
  • Identify and follow up on missing client information
  • Maintain organized and complete client files

Bookkeeping and Indirect Tax Support

  • Perform bookkeeping and account reconciliations as required
  • Assist with GST/HST and PST return preparation and filing
  • Review bookkeeping records for accuracy and completeness
  • Support clients with accounting software-related questions

Personal Tax Support

  • Prepare T1 personal income tax returns during personal tax season
  • Assist with tax return review and supporting documentation requests

Client Service and Team Collaboration

  • Communicate professionally with clients by email and phone
  • Work collaboratively with team members while managing assigned files independently
  • Meet deadlines and manage competing priorities throughout the year
  • Participate in process improvement initiatives as the firm grows

QualificationsRequired Experience

  • 1–5 years of public accounting experience
  • 1–5 years of experience preparing T2 corporate tax returns
  • 1–5 years of experience preparing corporate year-end engagements
  • 1–5 years of bookkeeping experience
  • Experience preparing GST/HST and/or PST returns

Technical Skills

Experience with the following software is considered an asset:

  • QuickBooks Online
  • Caseware Cloud
  • iFirm TaxPrep
  • Financial Cents
  • Google Workspace

Personal Attributes

We are looking for someone who:

  • Has exceptional attention to detail
  • Can work independently in a fully remote environment
  • Demonstrates strong written and verbal communication skills
  • Has excellent time management and organizational skills
  • Is comfortable managing deadlines and multiple client files
  • Takes ownership of their work and follows through on commitments
  • Is willing to ask questions and seek clarification when needed
  • Has a positive attitude and a desire to continue learning
  • Is client-service focused and professional in all interactions

Application Requirements

To apply, please submit both a resume and a cover letter by email.

Applications submitted without a cover letter will not be considered.

Your cover letter should specifically address:

  • Your experience preparing T2 corporate tax returns
  • Your experience preparing corporate year-end engagements
  • Your bookkeeping and indirect tax experience (GST/HST/PST)
  • Your experience working remotely and independently
  • Examples of how you have successfully managed your workload and deadlines with limited supervision

We are seeking candidates who can clearly demonstrate both their technical accounting abilities and their ability to thrive in a remote work environment.

We thank all applicants for their interest; however, only those selected for an interview will be contacted.

Pay: $30.00-$45.00 per hour

Benefits:

  • Flexible schedule
  • Work from home

Work Location: Remote
