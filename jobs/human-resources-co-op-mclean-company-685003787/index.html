About McLean & Company

Full Description

McLean & Company is the trusted partner of HR and leadership professionals around the world. We provide our members with everything they need to drive their organization forward and develop a thriving workforce – from full-service assessments to practical action plans, impactful training, and more.

At McLean & Company, we’re passionate about empowering our members to shape a workplace where everyone thrives. We’re also passionate about making sure our employees thrive.

McLean is a place where your voice will be heard – a place where you will see the impact of your work with our 30,000+ members. It is a place where you will find community and collaboration, flexibility, and opportunities for career advancement. You will work with diverse individuals with their own unique stories and experiences. McLean is a place where success is celebrated by the whole team – a place where you are encouraged to stretch and grow.

We Are Hiring For a Co-op Position For The Fall 2026 Term To Support Our Client-facing HR Teams. Here Are a Few Examples Of The Types Of Work The Successful Candidate May Undertake

  • Supporting our HR Services teams in online client engagements. A typical online engagement is structured as 5 days a week/4 hours a day.

  • Prior to the engagement, preparation duties include sending calendar invitations, meeting with the engagement leader to plan the agenda, and preparing presentations, documents, activities, and polls.

  • During the engagement, duties include notetaking, managing break out rooms, and preparing documentation.

  • Post engagement, duties include summarizing discussion notes, finalizing deliverables, and participating in debrief meetings.

  • Conducting short research requests on HR topics.

  • Supporting the preparation of workshop materials.
  • Note: Our co-op positions are full-time, 40 hours per week between 8:00 am – 5:00pm EST, however, there may be some client engagements that will require the ability to flex work hours and will require strong internet connectivity.

We are looking to hire a co-op student with a passion for Human Resources, who has strong listening and note taking skills; strong skills in Microsoft Office (particularly PowerPoint, Excel, Teams, and Word); who has exceptional time management skills, and who is comfortable working collaboratively with different people across multiple teams in a dynamic remote work environment. You’ll get great exposure to many different areas of HR and to people like Grace Ewles and Peter Wong, former McLean & Company co-op students who returned to make their careers with us.

As a McLean & Company co-op student, you’ll also have access to McLean Academy, a series of online research-informed courses. Courses have been pre-approved for professional development credits by the HRPA, SHRM, and HRCI.

Sound good? To apply, please send your resume through your school’s co-op program for consideration with the subject line/file name [Your name - HR Co-op Application – Spring 2026].
