In one sentence We are looking for a motivated Summer Intern to join our team and support the design and development of a GenAI-powered log mining tool. This role is ideal for a student who is passionate about AI, data, and solving real-world problems in a large-scale production environment.

You will work closely with experienced engineers and architects, gaining hands-on experience with modern tools and methodologies while contributing to a meaningful, production-oriented project.

Internship Details Term: May–August 2026 (4 months)

Work Hours: Standard business hours/30-35 hours per week

What will your job look like? You Will

  • Assist in the design, development, and testing of a GenAI log mining tool
  • Help analyze large sets of system and application logs to identify patterns and insights
  • Support data preparation, cleaning, and feature engineering activities
  • Contribute to documentation of solution design, workflows, and user guides
  • Collaborate with team members in daily standups, design discussions, and code reviews
  • Support troubleshooting, performance tuning, and continuous improvement of the solution

All you need is... Qualifications – Education Currently enrolled in Computer Science, Software Engineering, Data Science, or a related STEM program

Qualifications – Skills & Experience

  • Strong programming skills in at least one language (e.g., Python, Java, or similar)
  • Understanding of JSON and unstructured data
  • Familiarity with AI/ML concepts or frameworks (e.g., basic understanding of machine learning, NLP, or large language models) is an asset
  • Experience with data analysis, scripting, or working with APIs (FastAPI or Flask) is an asset
  • Comfortable working in a Linux or cloud-based environment and Git Workflow
  • Strong analytical and problem-solving skills
  • Good communication skills and ability to work in a collaborative team environment

What You Will Gain

  • Hands-on experience with GenAI and log mining in a real production context
  • Exposure to industry best practices in software development and data-driven solutions
  • Mentorship from experienced professionals and opportunities to build your network
  • Practical experience that can support future full-time career opportunities

Amdocs is an equal opportunity employer. We welcome applicants from all backgrounds and are committed to fostering a diverse and inclusive workforce
