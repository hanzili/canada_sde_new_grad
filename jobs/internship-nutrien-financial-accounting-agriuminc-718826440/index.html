About the Role

Assist accounting team with data modelling and improvements

About AgriumInc.

Global agribusiness leader in crop inputs

Full Description

Pay Band: UNDEFINED  

Nutrien is a leading provider of crop inputs and services, and our business results make a positive impact on the world. Our purpose, Feeding the Future, is the reason we come to work each day. We’re guided by our culture of care and our core values: We put safety first. We act with integrity. We are stronger together. We deliver with excellence.

Through the collective expertise of our nearly 26,000 employees, we operate a world-class network of production, distribution, and ag retail facilities. We efficiently serve growers' needs and strive to provide a more profitable, sustainable, and secure future for all stakeholders.  Help us raise the expectation of what an agriculture company can be and grow your career with Nutrien.  

We are looking for a full-time Summer intern to join asap with the option to work part-time during the Fall semester (Sept - Dec.31, 2026).

What You'll Do:

  • Work with various members of the Nutrien Financial team to support our expansion into offering new supplier programs to our Canadian business
  • Assist the Nutrien Financial Accounting Team with enhancements as we look to finalize the future-state allowance for doubtful accounts reporting process
  • Work with Nutrien Financial Programs analysts to gather and test sales, interest, and payment information for data modelling related to finance program pricing and returns
  • Ad-hoc projects in Power BI and excel to streamline manual processes related to input financing outcomes

What You'll Bring:

  • Currently enrolled in a 2-to-4-year Accounting or Finance degree or equivalent related program with a graduation date of May 2027 or later

Ready to make an impact with us? Apply today!
