About The Forest Hill Group

Full Description

Company Overview

The Forest Hill Group is a market leader in delivering luxury concierge, bespoke security, upscale valet parking, premium maintenance and janitorial services, as well as complete property services to condominium communities in the United States. We are committed to excellence and providing exceptional service to our clients.
Summary

Join The Forest Hill Group as a Junior Financial Analyst in our Toronto Office. In this role, you will support financial decision-making processes by analyzing data and preparing reports that help maintain our standards of excellence in property services. Your work will contribute directly to our mission of delivering top-tier services to our clients.
Responsibilities

  • Assist in preparing financial reports and analyzing financial data to support decision-making.
  • Develop and maintain financial models using Excel
  • Conduct budgeting and forecasting activities to ensure financial targets are met.
  • Support cost accounting and profit & loss analysis for various projects.
  • Collaborate with teams to collect data and improve data visualization processes.
  • Utilize ERP systems and Hyperion for financial reporting tasks.
  • Contribute to strategic planning initiatives through quantitative research and analysis.
  • Ensure compliance with financial standards and company policies.
  • Qualifications
  • Proven experience with Microsoft Excel
  • Strong understanding of financial concepts including budgeting, financial analysis, and forecasting.
  • Experience with financial modeling and data visualization tools.
  • Excellent analytical skills with attention to detail.
  • Ability to work collaboratively within a team environment.
  • Bachelor’s degree in Finance, Accounting, Economics or related field.
