Full Description

Company Description

Digital marketing intern opportunity $22-24 per hour !

Grow your career with a company that shares your passion. Entegra is a wholly owned subsidiary of Sodexo and part of a global purchasing organization. Entegra is seeking a vibrant marketing intern who is looking for a diverse experience, someone who isn’t afraid to ask too many questions and give suggestions.

At Sodexo, our purpose is to create a better every day for everyone to build a better life for all. As the global leader in services that improve the Quality of Life, we operate in 55 countries, serving over 100 million consumers each day through our unique combination of On-Site Food and FM Services, Benefits & Rewards Services and Personal & Home Services.

Job Description

As a Digital Marketing Intern (SEO & AEO Focus), you’ll get hands-on experience helping our team make our website and online content easier to find and more engaging. You’ll learn how to improve search rankings, optimize content, and support backlinking strategies that grow our digital presence. This role is all about learning by assisting with keyword research, analyzing website performance, and seeing how digital marketing drives real results. You’ll work closely with our marketing team, gain valuable insights into SEO and AEO, and help us connect with more people online. It’s a great opportunity to take classroom knowledge and turn it into real-world experience in a fast-growing digital space.

  • Assist with SEO & AEO initiatives: Support the team in optimizing website content, meta data, and structure to improve search visibility and enhance user experience. Learn and apply SEO best practices in a hands-on environment.
  • Keyword research and content optimization: Help identify relevant keywords, analyze search trends, and suggest ways to improve content for better organic reach and engagement.
  • Backlinking and outreach support: Learn how to identify backlink opportunities, support outreach to external websites, and track results to improve site authority.
  • Support content creation: Collaborate on blog posts, landing pages, and social content that aligns with SEO strategies.
  • Team collaboration & learning: Attend team meetings, participate in brainstorming sessions, and contribute ideas while gaining mentorship from marketing professionals.
  • Digital marketing research: Research industry trends, competitor strategies, and digital marketing tools to provide insights for campaigns.
