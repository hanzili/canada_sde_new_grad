About Paul Davis Restoration of the National Capital Region, VA

Full Description

About Us Paul Davis is an industry leader in property restoration, helping residential and commercial clients recover from fire, water, mold, and storm damage. We pride ourselves on professionalism, responsiveness, and delivering exceptional customer experiences during challenging times.

Position Summary We are seeking a motivated and detail-oriented Junior Estimator / Project Manager Assistant to support our restoration team. This entry-level role is ideal for someone looking to build a career in construction, restoration, or project management. You will work closely with experienced estimators and project managers, assisting with job documentation, estimating, coordination, and client communication.

Key Responsibilities

  • Assist in preparing estimates using industry software (e.g., Xactimate)
  • Support project managers with scheduling, job tracking, and coordination
  • Conduct site visits for measurements, photos, and documentation
  • Communicate with clients, subcontractors, and internal team members
  • Help maintain accurate job files, reports, and project updates
  • Track materials, labor, and job costs
  • Ensure projects are progressing on time and within scope
  • Assist with insurance documentation and claims processes

Qualifications

  • Strong organizational and time management skills
  • Excellent communication and customer service abilities
  • High attention to detail and accuracy
  • Ability to multitask in a fast-paced environment
  • Proficiency in Microsoft Office (Excel, Word, Outlook)
  • Valid driver’s license and reliable transportation
  • Previous experience in construction, restoration, or estimating is an asset
  • Experience with Xactimate is considered a strong asset
  • Willingness to learn and grow within the company

What We Offer

  • Competitive salary based on experience
  • Training and career development opportunities
  • Supportive team environment
  • Opportunity for advancement into estimator or project manager roles
  • Company vehicle or mileage reimbursement (if applicable)

How To Apply Please submit your resume and a brief cover letter outlining your interest in the role.

Compensation: $50,000.00 - $65,000.00 per year

Since 1966, Paul Davis has been an industry leader in the areas of property damage mitigation, reconstruction and remodeling. With more than 370 offices in our franchise network, the company serves residential, institutional, and commercial customers and clients across the United States and Canada.

We have built our heritage one project at a time, establishing a reputation for performance, integrity and responsibility among customers and carriers alike. Whether property damage is caused by water, fire, smoke, storms or other disasters, we deliver on our promise to deliver excellence, expertise and a customer experience that is second to none. At Paul Davis, our passion for quality drives everything we do.
