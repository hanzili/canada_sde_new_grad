Summer Student – Accounting / Finance

Location: Mississauga, ON
Term: Summer (May–August)

Role Summary

We are seeking a motivated Accounting / Finance Summer Student to support the Finance team with data analysis, reporting, and process improvement activities. This role is ideal for a high‑achieving business student with strong analytical skills and attention to detail.

Key Responsibilities

  • Support accounting and finance analysis through data collection, validation, and reporting
  • Build and maintain Excel datasets to support financial and operational decision‑making
  • Assist with basic financial analysis, modeling, and trend reviews
  • Prepare summaries and presentation materials for internal stakeholders
  • Contribute to process improvement and automation initiatives
  • Perform ad‑hoc finance support as required

Qualifications

  • Enrolled in a Bachelor of Management, Finance, Accounting, or related program
  • Strong academic performance
  • Proficiency in Excel; experience with Python or data analytics an asset
  • Strong analytical, organizational, and communication skills
  • Detail‑oriented, self‑motivated, and able to work in a team environment
