About the Role

About First Work

Notable Canadian employment services organization; strong public mission

Full Description

First Work is a leading membership organization bringing together the best providers of employment services, business leadership, academia, and government committed to advancing healthy employment ecosystems. Our membership community shapes our work and positions us as the bridge between jobseekers and employment opportunities. Our continued advocacy at all levels of government supports progressive policy development for the benefit of all jobseekers and communities. Our direct engagement with youth, newcomers and frontline practitioners ensures our services are relevant, culturally appropriate, and timely.

The Opportunity:

First Work is looking for an individual to take on the role of the Digital Marketing Coordinator. The individual will be responsible for supporting the execution of marketing campaigns, content creation and website management while ensuring brand consistency.

The successful applicant will demonstrate sound judgment, be flexible and willing to learn and adapt to different work assignments in a team setting.

Eligibility: This position is funded through the Canada Summer Jobs program. To qualify, applicants must be

  • Between 15 and 30 years of age at the start of employment.
  • Legally entitled to work in Canada.
  • Either a Canadian citizen, permanent resident, or a person with refugee protection under the Immigration and Refugee Protection Act. Please note: International students are not eligible for this opportunity.

Tasks and Responsibilities

1. Digital Marketing and Administration

  • Assist in creating and executing marketing campaigns.
  • Maintain and manage marketing systems, digital databases, and asset libraries to support campaign execution and troubleshooting.
  • Conduct keyword research, audience targeting, and monitoring analytics to identify trends and optimize performance.
  • Maintain organized marketing documentation, processes, and follow up on key actions from meetings.
  • Act as a liaison between different teams, departments, and external partners.
  • Ad-hoc tasks as assigned.

2. Website and Portals Support

  • Assist in building and maintaining website and portal content.
  • Create and optimize content while auditing for outdated information and ensuring usability.
  • Perform basic SEO tasks including keyword research, meta descriptions, alt text, and internal linking.
  • Monitor website analytics for traffic and engagement trends, report broken links or technical issues.
  • Support website optimization and enhancements to improve user experience and search visibility.

3. Email Marketing

  • Draft, format, and schedule email campaigns.
  • Assist in setting up workflows and automated emails.
  • Manage subscriber lists and segment audiences.
  • Track email performance metrics and ensure branding consistency.

4. Content Creation and Design

  • Draft, edit, and publish content for social media, website, newsletters, and blogs.
  • Assist with writing and editing marketing materials including captions, blog posts, newsletters.
  • Support development of marketing assets including graphics, videos, and other digital content.
  • Ensure all content aligns with brand voice, messaging guidelines, and accessibility standards.

5. Analytics and Reporting

  • Contribute to performance analysis and recommendations for optimization.
  • Collect and organize data from social media, website, and email campaigns.
  • Track ad performance and compile internal and external reports for stakeholders.
  • Prepare dashboards, status updates and presentations summarizing campaign results and insights.
  • Support troubleshooting by reporting broken links or technical issues and ensuring accurate data documentation.

Who you are:

  • Detail Oriented & Organized – Strong attention to detail and an ability to coordinate multiple responsibilities at once. Capable to multi-task, manage and meet tight deadlines.
  • Communicator - Strong verbal communication skills, effective and professional written communication skills.
  • Proactive Self-Starter – Independent, flexible, and adaptable learner who takes initiative to ask thoughtful questions to take action.
  • Team Player - willing to assist with multiple areas of the organization.
  • Creative thinker - Always looking for opportunities to grow and learn in a flexible and adaptive environment.

We want to hear from you if you have:

  • Personal or professional experience is preferred (co-op, projects, volunteer, etc).
  • Experience in writing, editing, and publishing written and visual content.
  • Have excellent customer services skills with both internal and external stakeholders.
  • Have strong organizational skills, with the ability to multi-task, in a fast-paced environment.
  • Working knowledge of Microsoft Office 365 (Word, Excel, PowerPoint, Outlook, SharePoint), Zoom, Canva, Mailchimp, WordPress and Adobe are considered assets.

The above statements are intended to describe the general nature and level of work applicable to this position and are not intended to be an exhaustive list of all responsibilities, duties and skills required.
