Full Description

**Company Description**

GenPark leverages intelligent Brand Agents and User Agents to help brands connect with and engage Gen Z consumers more effectively on a global scale. By integrating personalized AI-generated content, interactive agents, and immersive AI video technologies, we enable brands to reach their target audience in the most relevant contexts, achieving higher conversion rates and reducing customer acquisition costs. As an AI-native platform, GenPark's Agents continuously learn from user behavior and feedback to create smarter matches between products, services, and consumers worldwide. Our innovative solutions redefine digital marketing by delivering personalized and scalable experiences.

**Role Description**

This is a full-time hybrid role for a Machine Learning Engineer based in Vancouver, BC, with an option for partial remote work. In this role, you will design and implement machine learning models, focusing on areas like pattern recognition, neural networks, and algorithm development. Day-to-day tasks will include building scalable machine learning pipelines, analyzing complex datasets, and optimizing models for accuracy and performance. Collaborative problem-solving with cross-functional teams and maintaining the infrastructure required for machine learning projects are key components of this role.

- Proficiency in Pattern Recognition and Neural Networks - Expertise in Computer Science concepts and Statistics - Strong understanding of Algorithms and data processing methodologies - Experience implementing machine learning applications in real-world scenarios - Proficiency in Python, TensorFlow, PyTorch, or other relevant machine learning frameworks - Strong problem-solving and analytical skills - Bachelor's or Master's degree in Computer Science, Data Science, or related field - Previous experience working with AI-driven systems or products is a plus
