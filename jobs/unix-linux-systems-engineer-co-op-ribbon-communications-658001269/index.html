About the Role

Manage Unix/Linux servers and automate infrastructure tasks

About Ribbon Communications

Telecom hardware/software company

Full Description

Ribbon Communications is seeking a skilled Unix/Linux Systems Engineer to join our dynamic team. The ideal candidate will have a strong background in Unix/Linux operating systems and a solid foundation in computer science, computer engineering, or electrical engineering.

Key Responsibilities:

  • Manage and maintain Unix/Linux servers and systems.
  • Monitor system performance and ensure reliability and availability.
  • Troubleshoot and resolve system issues and outages.
  • Implement security measures and ensure compliance with best practices.
  • Collaborate with other IT teams to support infrastructure needs.

Qualifications:

  • Currently enrolled in a Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science, Computer Engineering, Electrical Engineering, or a related field.
  • Experience with Unix/Linux operating systems.
  • Strong problem-solving skills and attention to detail.
  • Excellent communication and teamwork abilities.

Preferred Skills:

  • Proficiency in programming and scripting languages (e.g., Python, Bash, Perl).
  • Experience with automation tools and frameworks.
  • AI interfaces /API development skills
  • Knowledge of network protocols and services.
