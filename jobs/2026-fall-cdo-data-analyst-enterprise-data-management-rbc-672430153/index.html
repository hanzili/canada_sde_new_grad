Full Description

Job Description What is the opportunity? The Co-op Data Analyst, Shared Data Management Services in Bedford Nova Scotia will be responsible for supporting Data Shared Services in driving business process improvement.

What will you do?

  • Gain a working knowledge of RBC’s data product and platform management
  • Work with Subject Matter Experts and stakeholders to understand business problems and create user stories and business requirements
  • Take part in business requirement gathering, solution and data model design workshops, perform UAT testing
  • Create and maintain documentation for business requirements, process and data flow diagrams, data model and data dictionary
  • Contribute content for end-user documentation and training

What do you need to succeed? Must-have

  • Passion for data
  • Critical and analytical thinker who is self-driven
  • Effective communications skills, both written and oral
  • Ability to analyze and make sense of complex data with minimal guidance
  • Proficient in Excel, Word, PowerPoint

Nice-to-have

  • Basic working knowledge of SQL and fundamental concepts of data modeling
  • Data analytics reporting capabilities and effective presentation skills
  • Familiar with SDLC lifecycle

What’s in it for you? We thrive on the challenge to be our best, progressive thinking to keep growing, and working together to deliver trusted advice to help our clients thrive and communities prosper. We care about each other, reaching our potential, making a difference to our communities, and achieving success that is mutual.

  • Leaders who support your development through coaching and managing opportunities
  • Ability to make a difference and lasting impact
  • Work in a dynamic, collaborative, progressive, and high-performing team
  • Opportunities to do challenging work and make a difference
  • Opportunities to build close relationships
