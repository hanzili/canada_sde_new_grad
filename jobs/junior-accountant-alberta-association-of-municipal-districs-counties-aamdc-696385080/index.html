About the Role

Process AP/AR, journals, and month-end close

About Alberta Association of Municipal Districs & Counties (AAMDC)

Nonprofit association serving municipalities; varied accounting

Full Description

Purpose

As part of the Finance department and reporting to the Manager of Accounting, you will be an integral part of RMA’s team supporting our insurance, procurement and employee benefits departments. You’ll be involved in full-cycle accounting. Other expectations include the ability to ask questions, take initiative, and use problem solving skills.

This is a full-time position. The position will be working directly from the RMA offices in Nisku, AB.

Responsibilities and Activities

  • Conducting day-to-day accounting tasks:
  • Accounts Payable and Receivable
  • Journal entries and general ledger updates
  • Helping with month-end and year end close
  • Keep financial records accurate and well-organized across multiple businesses
  • Help prepare reports and do some financial analysis
  • Support intercompany transactions and reconciliations
  • Work with teams across procurement, insurance and benefits to keep everything aligned
  • Pitch in on budgeting and forecasting
  • Help get things ready for audit (gathering documents, supporting info, etc.)
  • Dig into discrepancies, ask questions, and help figure out solutions
  • Look for ways to make processes smoother and more efficient

Knowledge, Skills and Abilities

  • Diploma or degree in Accounting, Finance, or something similar
  • Full-cycle accounting experience is an asset
  • Working towards a CPA (or planning to) is an asset
  • Natural curiosity and eagerness to learn
  • Someone who is organized and has high quality attention to detail
  • Confident to take ownership and follow things through
  • Curious mindset
  • Comfortable multitasking
  • Team player who can also work independently
  • Proficient Excel and Accounting software skills
  • Clear communicator
  • Practical problem solver

Contacts

Works with the Finance team, including: Financial Analysts, AP Specialists, Accounting Clerks, and the Chief Financial Officer.

RMA is an equal opportunity employer. Any applicant that meets the qualifications above is encouraged to apply. Priority will be given to Canadian Citizens and those already legally entitled to work in Canada.

To apply, please send a resume and cover letting in PDF format outlining how you meet the above qualification requirements to Janelle Lopushinsky at janelle@rmalberta.com.

We thank all applicants, but due to the high volume of responses, only those selected for an interview will be contacted. No phone calls please.

Work Location: In person
