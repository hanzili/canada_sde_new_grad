Full Description

About Ad-Wise Agency

Ad-Wise Agency is an AI-native marketing agency headquartered in Toronto, ON. We embed directly into client teams as a full marketing extension, not a vendor, not a campaign shop. Our model pairs AI-powered content, lead generation, and outreach with senior human strategy, giving growth-focused businesses full squad output at a fraction of traditional agency cost. We operate in regulated and competitive verticals where compliance, precision, and speed matter. Our team is lean, technical, and direct, and we're building fast. We're looking for a sharp, execution-oriented junior marketer who thrives in an AI-first environment and wants real ownership from day one.

The Opportunity

As a Junior Marketing Specialist at Ad-Wise Agency, you'll be embedded across multiple client accounts, supporting day-to-day marketing execution with a focus on content creation, social media management, marketing calendar coordination, lead generation and outreach campaigns. This is a hands-on role with real ownership from day one, you won't be fetching coffee. You'll be using AI tools to write copy, build calendars, launch campaigns, and help our clients show up consistently and compellingly in market.

This is a contract position with strong potential to become a permanent full-time role. We're building a team for the long term; candidates who perform and fit the culture will have a clear path to a permanent offer.

What You'll Do

Marketing Calendar Management

  • Build and maintain detailed marketing content calendars for multiple client accounts simultaneously using Ad-Wise proprietary marketing calendar tool
  • Coordinate timelines, deliverables, and approvals with internal teams and clients
  • Track campaign milestones and flag risks or delays proactively
  • Ensure content is planned, reviewed, and scheduled well in advance of publishing dates

Social Media & Content

  • Write, schedule, and publish content across platforms including Instagram, LinkedIn, TikTok, Facebook, and X
  • Develop platform-native copy and captions that reflect each client's brand voice
  • Support the creation of content briefs and collaborate with designers on visual assets
  • Monitor social channels, manage community engagement (comments, DMs, mentions)
  • Track social performance metrics and contribute to monthly reporting decks
  • Stay ahead of social media trends, algorithm updates, and emerging platform features

Outreach Campaigns

  • Use Ad-Wise AI tools to research and build targeted prospect lists based on defined ICP criteria
  • Enrich prospect data with contact details, company info, and buying signals to support personalized outreach at scale
  • Assist in planning and executing email and digital outreach campaigns from concept to deployment
  • Develop compelling outreach copy for cold email sequences, newsletters, and campaign announcements
  • Manage prospect and contact lists, ensuring data is clean and segmented appropriately
  • Track outreach performance (open rates, click-throughs, conversions) and support A/B testing efforts
  • Coordinate follow-up sequences and maintain outreach trackers in CRM or project tools

AI & Professional Development

  • Continuously develop your skills as a smarter, more efficient marketer by learning how to apply the right AI tools, the right way, across content, outreach, and lead generation

What You Bring

Required

  • 3+ years of experience in marketing, social media, or content
  • Strong writing and copyediting skills with a sharp eye for tone, grammar, and brand voice
  • Hands-on experience with social media scheduling tools such as Hootsuite, Later, Buffer, or Sprout Social
  • Familiarity with email marketing platforms (Mailchimp, Klaviyo, HubSpot, or similar)
  • Comfort using AI writing and workflow tools (ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, or similar)
  • Demonstrated ability to manage multiple projects and deadlines simultaneously without dropping the ball
  • Comfort working in a fast-paced agency environment with shifting priorities
  • Excellent verbal and written communication skills
  • Post-secondary education in Marketing, Communications, Media Studies, or a related field (or equivalent experience)

Nice to Have

  • Experience with Canva, Adobe Creative Suite, or similar design tools
  • Working knowledge of Google Analytics, Meta Business Suite, or other analytics dashboards
  • Exposure to SEO fundamentals, paid social, or influencer marketing
  • Prior agency experience or experience managing multiple client accounts
  • Familiarity with project management tools such as Asana, Monday.com, or Notion
  • Exposure to marketing automation or AI workflow tools (n8n, Zapier, Make, or similar), interest in how AI agents power lead generation and outreach is a strong plus

What We Offer

  • Competitive salary: $45K–$60K CAD
  • Hybrid work model - 4 days in the office (Markham-based)
  • Real ownership and client exposure from day one
  • Mentorship from senior creative directors and brand strategists
  • Access to industry AI tools, training & mentorship
  • A collaborative, ambitious team that has fun doing great work
