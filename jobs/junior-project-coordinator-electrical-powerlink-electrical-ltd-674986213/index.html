About Powerlink Electrical Ltd

Full Description

Company Overview

Powerlink Electrical Ltd. is a reputable electrical and construction company based in Edmonton, founded in 2016. We are committed to delivering high-quality electrical solutions and innovative project management services to our clients.

Position Summary

We are seeking a motivated and detail-oriented Junior Project Coordinator to join our team in Edmonton. In this role, you will support Project Managers in coordinating electrical construction projects, ensuring they are completed on time, within budget, and in compliance with safety and quality standards. This is an excellent opportunity to grow your career in a dynamic and hands-on environment.

Key ResponsibilitiesProject Coordination & Support

  • Assist in coordinating day-to-day project activities and schedules
  • Support Project Managers with planning, tracking, and reporting
  • Help maintain project timelines and milestones
  • Track project progress and update relevant documentation
  • Contribute to project meetings and communicate updates effectively

Documentation & Administration

  • Maintain organized project files, drawings, and documentation
  • Assist with RFIs, submittals, and change orders
  • Take meeting minutes and distribute updates to stakeholders

Procurement & Materials Coordination

  • Help source and order materials and equipment
  • Track deliveries and follow up with suppliers
  • Coordinate material shipments to site and receive materials when required
  • Maintain records of purchase orders and invoices
  • Pick up and deliver materials to job sites as needed

Communication

  • Support communication between office staff, field teams, and Project Managers
  • Relay project updates and assist in resolving minor coordination issues

Field & Site Support

  • Provide administrative support to field teams
  • Assist with scheduling site visits, inspections, and permits
  • Occasionally visit job sites to gain project exposure

Cost & Budget Tracking

  • Assist with tracking project costs and budgets
  • Support invoice processing and progress billing

Safety & Compliance

  • Ensure adherence to safety regulations, company procedures, and quality standards
  • Assist with documentation related to inspections and compliance

Technical Support

  • Read and interpret blueprints, schematics, and technical drawings
  • Support preparation of construction estimates and bids
  • Assist in implementing project management best practices

Learning & Development

  • Gain understanding of electrical systems, drawings, and project workflows
  • Develop skills in construction/project management software and tools

Qualifications

  • Knowledge or experience reading blueprints and schematics
  • Strong organizational and time management skills
  • Ability to multitask and prioritize in a fast-paced environment
  • Effective communication skills and team-oriented mindset
  • Basic understanding of Civil 3D or civil drawings is an asset

Education Requirements

  • Diploma or certificate in one of the following:
  • Electrical Engineering Technology
  • Construction Management
  • Project Management
  • Engineering (electrical or related field)
  • Valid driver’s license
