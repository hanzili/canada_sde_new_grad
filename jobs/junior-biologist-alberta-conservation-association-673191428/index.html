Environmental Science / Ecology

Junior Biologist

at Alberta Conservation Association

📍 Location Cochrane, Alberta
📋 Details Full-time
📅 Posted Apr 28, 2026

About the Role

Monitor sites and coordinate habitat projects

About Alberta Conservation Association

Conservation organization managing Alberta lands and habitats

Full Description

Central Region – Rocky Mountain House or Cochrane, Alberta

Looking to apply your skills where they truly make an impact? Join Alberta Conservation Association (ACA) and help manage and enhance over 50,000 acres of conservation land across Alberta.

This role is ideal for a motivated biologist who enjoys a mix of fieldwork and project coordination—supporting habitat conservation, restoration, and land management initiatives within our Land Management Resource Program.

What you’ll do:

• Monitor and maintain conservation sites

• Support habitat restoration and enhancement projects

• Develop land management plans and securement proposals

• Collaborate with landowners, partners, and stakeholders

• Oversee contractors and manage project deliverables

What you bring:

• B.Sc. in Biology (or related field) + relevant experience

• Strong organization, communication, and collaboration skills

• Willingness to work long field days and occasional overnight travel

• Passion for conservation, habitat, hunting, and/or fishing

🗓 Closing Date: May 13, 2026 (or until filled

Submit a cover letter and resumé online : https://www.ab-conservation.com/about/careers/

We thank all applicants in advance for their interest; however, only successful applicants will be contacted for an interview. This competition may be used to fill other vacancies in other locations.
