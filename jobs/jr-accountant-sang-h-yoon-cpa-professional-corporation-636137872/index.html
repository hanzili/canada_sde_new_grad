Full Description

Job Overview
We are seeking a detail-oriented and motivated Junior Accountant to join our finance team. This position with pervious 2 to 3 years of work experience in accounting preferrably in public practice, offers an excellent opportunity to develop foundational accounting skills within a dynamic organization. The Junior Accountant will assist with various financial tasks, ensuring accuracy and compliance across multiple accounting functions. The ideal candidate will have a strong understanding of accounting software and a keen eye for detail, supporting the company's financial health and operational efficiency.

Responsibilities

  • Assist with accounts payable and accounts receivable processes, ensuring timely and accurate transactions
  • Support bookkeeping activities using accounting software such as QuickBooks
  • Perform account reconciliation and bank reconciliation to verify financial data accuracy
  • Assist with auditing procedures and prepare documentation for internal and external audits
  • Support payroll processing activities in compliance with company policies and regulations
  • Help maintain organized financial records and ensure compliance with company standards
  • Collaborate with team members on financial projects and process improvements

Requirements

  • Basic knowledge of accounting principles and practices
  • Bilingual , Korean and English is plus
  • Experience with accounting software including QuickBooks, CaseWare and Profile.
  • Familiarity with accounts payable, accounts receivable, bookkeeping, and bank reconciliation processes
  • Strong attention to detail and organizational skills
  • Ability to analyze financial data accurately and efficiently
  • Good communication skills and ability to work collaboratively within a team
  • Prior internship or experience in financial services or corporate finance is a plus but not required
  • A degree in Accounting, Finance, or related field is preferred but not mandatory

This role provides an excellent foundation for career growth in accounting and finance. The successful candidate will gain hands-on experience across various accounting functions while contributing to the overall success of the organization.

  • Dental care
  • Extended health care
