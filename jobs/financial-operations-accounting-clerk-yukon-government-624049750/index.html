Who we are‚Ä¶

As the responsible stewards of public funds, the Department of Finance partners to ensure value for money in the delivery of programs and services for Yukoners. The Department of Finance serves as the territory‚Äôs statistical body and develops the territorial budget, corresponding supplementary budgets and Public Accounts for the territory. It is also responsible for negotiating, coordinating and representing Yukon on inter-governmental financial matters with other jurisdictions. In its capacity as a central agency, the department works with all areas of government in the creation of their annual departmental budgets and in processing operational accounts payable and accounts receivable transactions.

The opportunity‚Ä¶

The Financial Operations Unit with the Department of Finance is looking for motivated, detail-oriented people to join our team of professionals. If you enjoy working in a fast-paced environment where multiple priorities and frequent interruptions are the norm, we would like to hear from you!

The Financial Operations Accounting Clerk is responsible for the creation and maintenance of the vendor and customer systems, creating cash receipts, assists internal and external clients by providing information on payments, assists departments in resolving outstanding invoices, performing all payment functions, and is responsible for the daily balancing and monthly reconciliations of the accounts payable and accounts receivable ledgers.
