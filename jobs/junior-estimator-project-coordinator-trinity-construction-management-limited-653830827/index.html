About the Role

Estimate costs, prepare tenders, coordinate construction projects

About Trinity Construction Management Limited

Scarborough-based CM firm with growth opportunities

Full Description

Trinity Construction Management Limited specializes in delivering high-quality construction management services. Our team is dedicated to planning, executing, and completing projects efficiently and on time while ensuring the highest safety and quality standards. We take pride in building strong relationships with clients and stakeholders through effective communication and collaboration. Based in Toronto, ON, Trinity Construction is committed to fostering innovation and offering growth opportunities within a dynamic and supportive environment.

As a successful candidate, you will be responsible for :

  • Preparing estimates of probable costs of materials, labour, subcontractors and equipment for construction projects based on contract bids, quotations, schematic drawings and specifications.
  • Preparing tender documents, analyze and review tender process and results.
  • Quantity take-offs for projects and research for new projects on various bidding portals.
  • Preparing documents for pre-qualifications for various projects and clients as per the specifications.
  • Analyze and coordinate quotes from suppliers, subcontractors for tender closings.
  • Participate in site and tender meets, closings , review with the Senior Estimator and the Project Management team.
  • Track and report on the progress of the project plans, cost and schedule reporting to the Project Manager.
  • Responsible for project documentation, project execution and file management.
  • Assist in managing and coordinate construction projects.

Qualifications:

  • Candidates with Bachelor's Degree in Civil Engineering or Construction Management will be given preference.
  • Excellent verbal and written communication skills, problem solving skills and attention to details.
  • Experience and knowledge about working in a construction environment related to execution or estimating will be an asset.
  • Experience with estimating software (Planswift) will be preferred.
  • Candidates having construction estimation, project coordination will be an asset.

Job Location: Scarborough

Job type: Full time

Salary will be commensurate as per the experience.

Interested candidates can send in their resume to careers@tcmltd.ca
