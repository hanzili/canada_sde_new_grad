Overview POSITION: IT Analytics & Automation Internship (Summer 2026 Term - 4 months)

LOCATION: Mississauga, ON

COMPENSATION: $25-28 per hour

Are you hungry for an exciting and challenging opportunity in a fast-paced, diverse organization? If so, you have a future at PepsiCo. As a world class organization, we strive for excellence in everything that we do! To accomplish our goals, we need talented, innovative, creative people who know how to work hard and have fun.

Taste the success with Canada's fastest growing CPG (Consumer Package Goods) company and discover how our passion for growth creates big challenges and big opportunities: personally, professionally, and financially.

At PepsiCo, we leverage an amazing range of technologies throughout our business; from mobile apps to advanced analytics, from cloud connected sensors to complex data integrations and everything in between.

Reasons to Consider PepsiCo for your Co-op Placement:

  • Ongoing professional development to expand your business knowledge
  • Opportunities to work with a wide range of functional departments and leaders
  • Make an impactful change with your projects
  • Ability to flex work arrangements
  • To work with, and learn from business partners to gain key decision-making skills
  • Opportunities for various plant tours and visits

Responsibilities As an IT Analytics & Automation Internship Intern, you will work with various business functions across PepsiCo Foods Canada, and will be responsible for gathering requirements, building applications, modernizing business processes, and driving innovation.

Key responsibilities will include:

  • Use tools like PowerApps, Power BI, Power Automate, UiPath, and Azure Data Lake to build applications and automate processes
  • Collaborate with business and technology teams to address real-world business challenges
  • Gather and define project requirements, scope, and objectives with clients
  • Communicate project status and updates to stakeholders
  • Assist in project planning, scheduling, and issue tracking
  • Support user acceptance testing and documentation
  • Present data-driven insights through business intelligence tools to broader teams and business stakeholders

Qualifications

  • Motivated learner enrolled in a Business, Finance, Mathematics, Engineering, or IT related program
  • Previous work experience as a Business Analyst or similar role preferred
  • Exposure to Agile methodologies
  • Solid communication, organizational/time-management skills required
  • Strong skills in developing presentations and reports
  • Strong working knowledge of Microsoft Suite (Outlook, Word, Excel, PowerPoint) and SQL preferred
  • Working experience with Microsoft Power Apps, Power BI and UiPath is an asset.
  • Self-motivated, enthusiastic, and able to build collaborative relationships!

PepsiCo may use Artificial Intelligence as part of its hiring process. Specifically, we may use AI-based tools to screen, assess, or select applicants.

  • A dynamic and inclusive culture that promotes you to bring your whole-self to work
  • Inspiring positive change for people and the planet with sustainability goals
  • An opportunity to be meaningful and impactful within your work and projects
  • A supportive team that will encourage your professional growth and development
