Hatch has an opportunity for a Junior Engineer to work on several ongoing projects from our Calgary office. This person will for perform general civil engineering and design activities involving site preparation, grading, drainage, roads, paving, etc. for mining and heavy industrial projects. If you looking for an opportunity to lead projects civil projects related to mining, energy or infrastructure sectors, this is a great opportunity. At Hatch we give our engineers the opportunity to take on as much challenge they can handle. We feel that you are the driver of your career and our leaderships is here to support you in achieving your goals.

Other responsibilities include, but are not limited to:

  • Managing civil projects through to completion which are assigned in areas of bulk earthworks, site drainage, mine and plant roads and earth and mine tailings structures design.
  • QA/QC of design calculations and contract documents.
  • Assuming responsibilities of discipline practice on assigned
    project scope.
  • Working collaboratively with the project managers and multi-discipline design teams.
  • Quality control ‚Äì Drawing and calculations checking and verification.

Qualifications:

  • Experience managing and leading projects within Civil Engineering Discipline
  • Civil Engineering degree from a recognized engineering program
  • Licensed Engineer within the province of Alberta preferred
  • Up to 5 years of relevant industry experience (i.e. mining, energy or infrastructure)
  • Project Management experience with small to mid size scale projects.
  • Site grading design
  • Drainage design, storm sewers design, and storm management
  • Roads design
  • Sanitary sewers design
  • Hydrology and hydraulics
  • Thorough understanding of the design process including the application of civil design software packages.
  • Previous design experience in bulk earthworks, site grading, storm drainage, access and haul roads and earth structures for large size projects.
  • Ability to deal with competing priorities and manage time appropriately.
  • Previous experience leading, mentoring and motivating junior civil engineers
  • Dedicated team player with strong interpersonal and communication skills; will be self-motivated, quick to deliver on projects
