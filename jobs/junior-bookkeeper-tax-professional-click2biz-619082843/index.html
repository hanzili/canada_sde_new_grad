Full Description

About Click2Biz

Click2Biz is a full-service business centre providing end-to-end support to entrepreneurs and small-to-medium-sized businesses. Our services include business registration, bookkeeping, payroll, tax preparation, business consulting, and growth support. We work closely with our clients to simplify operations and help businesses operate efficiently and grow with confidence.

We are currently seeking a Junior Bookkeeper / Tax Professional to join our team in Bedford, NS.

Position Overview

This is an entry to junior-level position ideal for candidates looking to build experience in bookkeeping, payroll, and tax preparation within a professional business services environment. The successful candidate will support senior staff in maintaining accurate financial records, preparing tax filings, and assisting clients with day-to-day accounting needs.

Key Responsibilities

  • Perform day-to-day bookkeeping activities, including data entry and account reconciliation
  • Maintain accurate financial records for multiple clients
  • Assist with full-cycle bookkeeping
  • Support payroll processing and remittances
  • Assist with GST/HST filings and compliance
  • Assist in preparation of personal (T1) and corporate (T2) tax returns
  • Organize financial documents and maintain client files
  • Communicate with clients to request information and clarify transactions
  • Assist with CRA correspondence and administrative tax matters
  • Support month-end and year-end closing processes

Qualifications & Requirements

  • Diploma or degree in Accounting, Business, or related field preferred
  • Canadian bookkeeping or accounting experience preferred
  • Basic understanding of Canadian accounting and tax principles
  • Experience with accounting and tax software considered an asset
  • Proficiency in Microsoft Office, especially Excel
  • Strong attention to detail and organizational skills
  • Ability to manage multiple tasks and meet deadlines
  • Strong communication and teamwork skills
  • Must be legally authorized to work in Canada

Assets (Preferred but Not Required)

  • Experience with payroll processing
  • Experience with QuickBooks or similar accounting software
  • Experience preparing personal tax returns (T1)
  • Interest in pursuing accounting or tax certifications

What We Offer

  • Competitive salary based on experience
  • Training and mentorship from experienced professionals
  • Exposure to a wide range of industries and business types
  • Opportunity for career growth within a full-service business centre
  • Supportive and collaborative team environment
