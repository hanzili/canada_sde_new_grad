Full Description

RETAIL ASSOCIATE

Want to work in Fernie’s only year-round bike shop this riding season? At Straight Line, we are currently seeking to expand our summer retail staff team. If you live and breathe bikes and want to build a strong connection with the local riding community, we’d love to hear from you!

The role:

As a Retail Sales Associate, you will work as part of our retail team to deliver outstanding sales and rental customer experiences. You will work alongside other team members to:

  • Serve customers in purchases of bicycles, apparel, parts and accessories.
  • Assist rental customers with their booking process and bike set-up.

Key responsibilities:
Exceptional Customer Service

  • Understand the needs of each customer.
  • Provide friendly, helpful advice.
  • Guide customers through purchasing, booking and rental processes.
  • Share your knowledge and passion for bikes with the local riding community and tourists.

Retail Operations

  • Ability to use POS, rental and service softwares to deliver a seamless customer experience.
  • Complete store opening and closing duties.
  • Restocking and merchandising of items on the sales floor.
  • Cleaning, recycling and organisation.

Product Knowledge

  • Motivated to improve product knowledge in order to most effectively help customers.
  • Interested in product development and industry changes.

You have:

  • 1-2 years of experience in the bike industry or similar retail environment (preferred).
  • Strong knowledge of mountain and gravel bikes, components and gear.
  • Excellent customer service and communication skills.
  • A detail-oriented and organized approach to working in a busy retail environment.
  • The ability to work as part of a great team to ensure customer satisfaction.

Sound like a good fit? Send your resume and cover letter to info@straightlinefernie.com
