About the Role

Develop Java/Javascript components, write tests

About Flexspring

HR data integration platform

Full Description

The Opportunity

Business is booming at Flexspring. We seek a skilled R&D Full Stack Software Developer intern to join our all-star R&D team. At Flexspring, you’ll help us develop Flexspring, our cutting-edge iPaaS (Integration Platform as a Service) that integrates HR applications.

Do You Love To

  • Proactively solve problems.
  • Create cutting-edge technology.
  • Collaborate with a young, motivated, and diverse team.
  • Develop solutions to solve data integration challenges.

If “YES,” then Flexspring wants to speak with you.

What will you do in this role?

  • Design and implement software components and web applications for Flexspring’s suite of products and services.
  • Evolve our stack components by coding in Java and Javascript.
  • Write, maintain, and execute unit, integration, performance, load, and stress tests.

Essential Skills

  • Bachelor of Sciences or College degree in software engineering or computer science.
  • Excellent knowledge of the Java and/or Javascript languages.
  • Good knowledge of the Spring and JUnit framework.
  • Experience publishing and consuming web service APIs.

Nice to have

  • Software development experience with Java, Groovy, REST API, SOAP Web Service, Graph QL, ReactJS, and JMS.
  • Experience with HR or financial data integrations.

Why work for Flexspring

  • Work from home. Enjoy flexible hours.
  • Work with cutting-edge technology in the growing field of data integration.
  • Own your success: Receive attractive compensation, enjoy an empowering work culture, and apply your expertise to meaningful work.
  • Expect excellence: Collaborate, learn, and grow with a high-performance team.

About Flexspring Flexspring is the leading expert in data integration for HR applications. Any company that wants to save time and money by streamlining their HR processes to avoid manual double-data entry between HR apps benefits from Flexspring’s services. To maximize value for clients, Flexspring focuses exclusively on integrating HR applications.

For more information about Flexspring, visit: https://www.flexspring.com.
