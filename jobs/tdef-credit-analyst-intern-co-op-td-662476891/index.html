Job Description DEPARTMENT OVERVIEW Co-op and Internship opportunities allow you to gain valuable work experience across a number of the businesses at TD. You will work with experienced colleagues, receive world class training, and be part of a community of students across TD, where you will have an impact, grow as individual and experience our culture of care.

TD Equipment Finance (TDEF) is a specialty business within TD that provides financing solutions to help businesses acquire the equipment they need to grow. Supporting clients across industries such as transportation, construction, manufacturing, and agriculture, the team delivers tailored equipment loans and leases to fund capital investments. Guided by TD’s brand promise, Remarkably human, Refreshingly simple, TD Equipment Finance combines strong financial analysis, risk management, and a people‑focused approach to deliver practical, client‑centric solutions that support responsible and profitable business growth.

Job Description As a Credit Analyst Intern, you will support the Commercial Banking and Equipment Finance teams in analyzing credit risk and structuring financing solutions for business clients. You will assist with financial analysis, credit reviews, and deal support while working closely with sales and credit partners across the organization. This role offers hands‑on exposure to commercial lending and equipment finance within a collaborative environment.

Job Requirements We’re proud to work with a group of diverse colleagues. If you have relevant experience that isn’t mentioned

below, tell us about it in your resume or cover letter.

  • Currently enrolled in an undergraduate degree in Finance, Accounting, Economics, Business/Commerce or related field
  • Must be enrolled in an undergraduate degree with the intent of going back to school at the start of your work term (requirement of all co-op/intern positions)
  • Strong interest in commercial banking and credit analysis.
  • Basic understanding of financial statements (income statement, balance sheet, cash flow) and financial ratios.
  • Strong analytical and problem‑solving skills, with attention to detail.
  • Ability to communicate clearly and professionally, both written and verbal.
  • Comfortable working with Microsoft Excel, Word, and PowerPoint; ability to learn internal systems quickly.
  • Ability to manage multiple tasks and deadlines in a fast‑paced, team‑based environment.
  • Demonstrated initiative, curiosity, and willingness to learn.
  • Strong organizational and time‑management skills.
  • Team‑oriented mindset with a client‑focused and collaborative approach.
  • Bilingual (English and French)
