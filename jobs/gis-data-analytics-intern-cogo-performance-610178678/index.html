About the Role

About COGO Performance

Calgary-based geospatial and data analytics company.

Full Description

The **GIS & Data Analytics Intern** will support COGO Performance by applying geospatial analysis and data visualization techniques to better understand customer distribution, service accessibility, demographic trends, and operational performance. This role is designed as a learning-focused, hands-on internship aligned with post-secondary GIS program requirements.

The intern will work on real-world projects that involve spatial data analysis, mapping, and translating analytical results into clear insights that support business and operational decision-making. The position offers practical experience using industry-standard GIS tools in a professional environment under direct supervision.

**Key Responsibilities**

- Perform spatial analysis to assess customer geographic areas, accessibility, and service reach. - Create maps and visual products using ArcGIS Pro to support planning and analysis. - Integrate demographic, census, and open-source datasets with internal data. - Assist with data organization, geodatabase management, and quality control. (QA/QC) - Develop dashboards and visual summaries using ArcGIS Online for internal stakeholders. - Support data-driven decision-making through clear documentation and reporting. - Maintain organized records of workflows, methods, and analytical outputs.

**Work Environment**

- Hybrid or on-site work environment based in Calgary, AB. - Flexible schedule designed to meet academic placement hour requirements. - Supervised by the Owner/Director of COGO Performance with regular check-ins.

**Role Outcomes**

By the end of the internship, the successful candidate will have:

- Applied GIS skills in a real organizational setting. - Produced portfolio-ready maps, dashboards, and analytical reports. - Gained experience translating technical GIS analysis into business-relevant insights. - Developed professional workplace and documentation skills.
