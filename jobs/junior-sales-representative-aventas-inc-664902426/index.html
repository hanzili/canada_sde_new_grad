Junior Sales Representative

at Aventas Inc

📍 Location Waterloo, ON
📋 Details Full-time · Permanent · $800-$1,200/week · Permanent
📅 Posted Apr 16, 2026

About the Role

Learn sales processes, close deals, grow with mentorship

About Aventas Inc

Growth-focused agency offering mentorship and rapid training

Full Description

Are you ready to launch your career in sales and step into a role packed with growth, mentorship, and excitement?

We're on the hunt for enthusiastic, driven individuals who thrive in high-energy environments and love connecting with people! No experience? We've got you covered! With hands-on training and one-on-one mentorship, you'll hit the ground running!

▶ WHAT YOU'LL BE DOING:

  • Connect with new and existing customers to spark meaningful conversations about Cellphones, Internet, Security, and TV services.
  • Learn the sales process from start to finish, from identifying customer needs to closing the deal.
  • Attend leadership and skill-building seminars designed to foster growth.
  • Represent top brands at exciting events like the CNE and Montreal Grand Prix.
  • Master the art of communication and public speaking through real-world interactions.

▶ WHY YOU'LL LOVE WORKING WEITH US:

  • Mentorship & Training: work closely with experienced Field Managers who'll guide you every step of the way.
  • Unlimited Earning Potential: enjoy bonuses and uncapped commissions for your hard work.
  • Travel Opportunities: explore new places with a team that loves to grow together (check our social media and website for more details!).
  • Career Growth: clear pathways to leadership roles for high-performing individuals.
  • High-Energy Culture: fun, fast-paced environment where ambition is rewarded.

▶ ABOUT US:

We're an omni-channel sales and marketing agency partnering with major brands like Telus, Rogers, Bell, RBC, and BMO. Our specialty? Building authentic customer communications through face-to-face interactions and helping brands amplify their impact.

✓ WHAT WE'RE LOOKING FOR:

  • Ambitious self-starters who love challenges and rewards, and are performance driven.
  • Strong communicators with outgoing personalities.
  • People who thrive in fast-paced, team-oriented environments.
  • Experience in retail, hospitality, or customer service is AN ASSET (BUT NOT REQUIRED!).
  • Ability to commute to Kitchener.
  • Ability to work full-time from Monday to Friday from 11:30am to 8pm.
