Full Stack Developer Intern (8-Month Internship)

at Spartakus APM

📍 Location Kirkland, Quebec
📋 Details Intern · 8mo
📅 Posted Mar 17, 2026

About the Role

Develop full-stack features for Spartakus APM

About Spartakus APM

Industrial maintenance tech, APM platform

Full Description

About Us

Spartakus Technologies is dedicated to empowering the manufacturing industry through world-class maintenance and reliability consulting, along with advanced digital asset tracking solutions.

Position Summary

We are seeking a motivated and curious Full Stack Developer Intern to join our team for an 8-month internship. In this role, you will actively contribute to the development of the next generation of our Spartakus APM platform while working alongside a dynamic team passionate about innovation and continuous improvement.

You will gain hands-on experience across the full technology stack, collaborating closely with developers and cross-functional teams to build practical solutions that deliver real value to industrial users.

Key Responsibilities

  • Participate in the development, integration, and enhancement of front-end, back-end, or full-stack features within the Spartakus APM platform.

  • Collaborate with developers and internal teams to design solutions that meet the needs of industrial users.

  • Support code reviews, testing, and validation activities to ensure high-quality deliverables.

  • Write clean, maintainable, and well-structured code following best practices.

  • Stay current with emerging technologies and propose ideas to improve products, processes, and user experience.

  • Contribute to a collaborative and innovative team environment focused on continuous learning.

Required Skills

  • Currently pursuing a Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science, Software Engineering, or a related field.

  • Academic or project experience in web development (HTML, CSS, JavaScript).

  • Knowledge of or interest in React, TypeScript, PHP, Symfony, REST APIs, and GitHub.

  • Strong curiosity, autonomy, and initiative.

  • Interest in developing practical technology solutions with real industrial impact.

  • Strong communication skills in French (English is considered an asset).

What We Offer

  • Hands-on experience working on real products used in industry.

  • Mentorship and learning opportunities within an experienced development team.

  • A collaborative and innovation-driven work environment.

  • Opportunity to contribute meaningfully to impactful technology solutions.

The masculine form is used solely to simplify the text. We are committed to the principle of employment equity.

In accordance with Law 96, this position requires proficiency in English to serve customers in Atlantic Canada and interact with US suppliers to ensure effective communication and operational efficiency.
