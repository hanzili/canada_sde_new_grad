About the Role

Assist cash flow forecasting and treasury operations

About LeverageTek Staffing Solutions

Staffing specialist connecting talent with clients

Full Description

LeverageTek is actively seeking a Junior Treasury Analyst for a 6-month contract opportunity with its Ottawa-based customer.

Work Location

Remote

Key Tasks

  • Assist with the implementation and setup of Diapason, including system configuration, data entry, and testing.
  • Support the transition to a new corporate banking partner, including account setup, documentation, and internal coordination.
  • Analyze daily cash positions, cash flow forecasts, and bank reconciliations.
  • Communication with international associates (Brazil, Mexico, Colombia, Canada, US, France), managing indirect and direct cash flows, and performing bank and Diapason reconciliations.
  • Help improve internal treasury processes, ensuring alignment with Oracle system integration.
  • Provide analytical support for cash flow projections, bank fees, and funding requirements.
  • Maintain accurate and up-to-date treasury records, reports, and documentation.
  • Liaise with internal teams (finance, IT, accounting) and external banking partners as needed.
  • Identify and troubleshoot system/process issues to support a smooth transition.

Key Qualifications

  • Must have 1–3 years of experience in treasury or financial analysis, a strong grasp of cash flow management, forecasting, and financial systems
  • Must have experience with treasury management systems (e.g., Diapason) or ERP systems (e.g., Oracle).
  • Must have advanced experience and skills in Excel.

Qualifications:

  • Bachelor’s degree in Finance, Accounting, Economics, or a related field.
  • 2–3 years of experience in treasury, financial analysis, or a related finance function.
  • Strong understanding of cash flow, forecasting, and financial analysis.
  • Excellent Excel skills (advanced formulas, modeling, etc.) and comfort with financial systems.
  • Strong communication and interpersonal skills; ability to collaborate across teams.
  • Highly organized, analytical, and self-motivated with a willingness to learn.
  • Experience with improving internal treasury processes, ensuring alignment with Oracle system integration.
  • Looking for someone proactive and showing initiative in problem-solving and ownership of processes.

Assets:

  • Exposure to system implementations, process improvement initiatives, or banking transitions.
  • Familiarity with payment platforms and banking portals.

Compensation

Hourly Rate Negotiable
