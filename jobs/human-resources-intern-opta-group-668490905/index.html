Full Description

ABOUT OPTA

From our roots providing sand to local foundries in the 1880's, Opta has grown to an international group of companies committed to delivering optimal results for our customers. Opta's purpose is to create solutions that enhance productivity and set new standards for industrial performance. Our mission is to consistently deliver custom-engineered additives, specialty equipment and technical expertise that solves complex challenges, optimizes process and drives long-term success.

OPTA CORE VALUES

  • SAFETY: We put safety first on our processes, our products and our culture to protect people and ensure long-term operational success.
  • PEOPLE: We invest in our people because their expertise drives the solutions that power our success.
  • CUSTOMERS: We build lasting partnerships by understanding our customers' challenges and delivering customized solutions that meet their unique needs.
  • INNOVATION: We continually seek smarter, more effective and innovative ways to deliver solutions, blending creativity, data-driven insight and practical application.
  • SUSTAINABILITY: We engineer solutions with efficiency, responsibility, and sustainability as our foundation, optimizing performance today, while strengthening the resilience of tomorrow's operations.

POSITION SUMMARY

We are seeking an enthusiastic and detail-oriented HR Intern to join our team. This role offers a valuable opportunity to gain hands-on experience in various aspects of Human Resources, including recruitment, employee relations, and HR administration. The ideal candidate is passionate about HR, eager to learn, and ready to contribute to our organization’s success.

KEY RESPONSIBILITIES

  • Recruitment Support: Assist in drafting job postings, screening resumes, coordinating interviews, background screening and maintaining applicant tracking systems.
  • Onboarding: Help facilitate new employee orientations, ensuring a smooth integration into the company.
  • HR Administration: Maintain and update employee records, assist with payroll preparation, and ensure compliance with HR policies and procedures.
  • Employee Engagement: Support initiatives related to employee recognition, wellness programs, and team-building activities.
  • Policy Development: Assist in researching and updating HR policies to align with employment laws, such as the Employment Standards Act (ESA) or Fair Labor Standards Act (FLSA).
  • Training and Development: Help organize training sessions and track employee progress in development programs.
  • HR Projects: Participate in HR projects and initiatives.
  • All other duties as assigned.

SKILLS AND EXPERIENCE

  • Currently enrolled in, or recently graduated from a Human Resources Management program.
  • Knowledge of employment laws and standards (ESA, FLSA, OHSA, Human Rights Code) is an asset.
  • Strong organizational skills with excellent attention to detail.
  • Proficiency in Microsoft Office Suite (Word, Excel, PowerPoint) and familiarity with HRIS systems is a plus.
  • Effective communication and interpersonal skills.
  • Ability to handle confidential information with discretion.
  • A proactive attitude and eagerness to learn.
  • Note: This is a 16-week paid internship commencing May 2026
