## **Job Description**

JOB SUMMARY

The Niagara Region has a temporary full-time (12 month) paid Data Analyst Student (degree or diploma in progress) or Intern (recent graduate within 18 months) within the Pubcic Health and Emergency Medical Services department. Reporting to the Commander, EMS Technology Integration, the Data Analyst Student/Intern is responsible for assisting the EMS Data Analytics team. This encompasses many aspects, including but not limited to, the creation of PowerBI reports, redeveloping queries and reports in Microsoft Fabric, documenting data cleaning and analysis processes, creating process documentation, etc.

EDUCATION

- Completion of an Ontario High School Diploma (OSSD, SSGD, SSHGD, GED, or provincial/national high school equivalency)

- Must meet ONE of the following post-secondary requirements: - Students: Currently enrolled in a post-secondary field of study related to data collection and statistical analysis, OR - Interns: Successfully completed a post-secondary degree or diploma (within 18 months) related to data collection and statistical analysis, or equivalent combination of education, training and experience - Post-secondary degree in a health-related field is preferred (Public/Community Health, Health Informatics/Information, Health Sciences)

KNOWLEDGE

- Experience with various databases, client health care record systems and statistical reporting. - Knowledge of databases and web-based applications, Excel, SQL, computer logic and applications - Experience working within a multidisciplinary team in a health setting is preferred

SKILLS

- SQL: Querying databases, data extraction, and manipulation. - Programming: Python (Pandas, NumPy) or R for analysis. - Data Visualization: Creating dashboards in Tableau or Power BI. - Spreadsheets: Advanced Excel (Pivot tables, VLOOKUP). - Data Cleaning: Preprocessing raw data for analysis. - Statistics: Understanding of probability, mean, median, standard deviation, and regression.

SPECIAL REQUIREMENTS

- Must provide proof of successful completion of a Ontario high school diploma (OSSD, SSGD, SSHGD, GED or provincial/national high school equivalency) - Must provide proof of enrollment in or graduation (within 18 months) from a related field of study - Must maintain ability to travel in a timely manner to other offices, work locations or sites as authorized by the Corporation for business reasons. - In accordance with the Corporate Criminal Record Check Policy, the position requires the incumbent to undergo a Criminal Record Check and submit a Canadian Police Clearance Certificate. - Regional staff strive to enable the strategic priorities of council and the organization through the completion of their work. Staff carry out their work by demonstrating the corporate values.
