To achieve this, we live by our values of Integrity, Performance, Collaboration, Straightforward Communication and Respect and Care.

Our teams represent our truly international mindset, forging connections across office locations and all levels of the organization and building a network of mutual success. Working Arrangement PartnerRe operates with a hybrid work approach that balances in‑office collaboration with the flexibility of remote work. Our employees are required to be in the office 3 days a week. Our goal is to create an environment where employees can connect, innovate, and perform at their best.

This position is not for an existing vacancy within our organization but for future opportunities.

PartnerRe is committed to an inclusive, accessible recruitment experience. Reasonable accommodations for candidates with disabilities are available upon request throughout the hiring process.

Please attach your academic transcript with your application. Job Summary We are looking to recruit talented students for our Pricing, Valuation, and Project teams. The internships provide an opportunity to obtain valuable hands-on industry experience from experts in the field while participating and contributing to the teams' work and vision. The roles are as follow:

  • Experience Study
  • Projects (IFRS17, LDTI, etc.)
  • Valuation
  • Pricing

Note that the posted positions are for a 4-month work term, with the flexibility of extending to 8 months. PartnerRe also offers an opportunity for interns to return and gain experience with our various actuarial teams. This rotation opportunity is dependent of the performance of the student in their first term.

About The Role

  • AXIS Modelling - Design and build projection models for individual life reinsurance transactions.
  • Data Analysis - Work extensively with large datasets in SQL and R to perform rigorous analysis of client/enforce experience data.
  • Financial Analysis - Assist in the preparation and analysis of reserves, required capital, and financial statements.
  • Project Work - Provide support to Valuation and Pricing functions (experience analysis, IFRS17, LTDI, etc.).
  • We encourage innovations and process improvements from our staff. If you are someone who would like to leave an impact and make a difference, please apply!

Qualifications

  • Second/Third/Fourth year student in Actuarial Science.
  • A minimum of one Society of Actuaries exam.
  • Understanding of life insurance products. Previous work experience with life insurance would be considered an asset.
  • Strong technical skills including MS Excel and SQL or similar database management tool. R Programming skills are desirable.
  • Experience with the GGY AXIS actuarial system is an asset.
  • Strong analytical and problem-solving skills.
  • Excellent verbal and written communications skills.
  • Work independently and as part of a team.
