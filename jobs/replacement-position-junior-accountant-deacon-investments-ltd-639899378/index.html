Replacement Position - Junior Accounting Clerk

Are you looking for a dynamic work environment? Deacon Investments Ltd. Is looking for an exceptional accounting clerk to join our team for a 12–14-month position.

The position will include the following responsibilities:

  • Accounts receivable functions

  • Accounts payable functions

  • Support Month End and Quarter End financial close (accruals, allocations, journal entries, etc.)

  • Credit card reconciliations

  • Maintain a systematic filing system using Microsoft 365 Drop Box

  • Mail processing

  • Verify and reconcile bank statements

  • General office duties as required

The successful candidate will have some of the following experience and skills:

  • Post-secondary education in Business or Accounting; or equivalent combination of experience, skills & education.

  • Possess excellent interpersonal and communication skills.

  • Strong attention to detail and accuracy.

  • Ability to work independently within a team environment in a small office.

  • Organized with the ability to prioritize and meet deadlines.

  • Proficient with Microsoft Office suite, specifically Microsoft Excel.

  • Previous experience working with Accounting Software, preferably Sage 50.

Deacon Investments Ltd. is a locally owned commercial investment & property management company. Founded in 2004 and growing ever since. Deacon Investments provides a quality work environment with competitive salary and benefits.

The position offered is full-time, replacement for a 12-14 month period. To apply for this position, please apply with resume by March 22nd, 2026 to:

admin@deaconinvestments.ca
