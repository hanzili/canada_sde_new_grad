Full Description

At Kepler Communications

, we're not just imagining the future of on-demand space connectivity - we're leading it!

Our mission is to provide real-time Internet access for space-based assets, enabling a new era of data-driven exploration and innovation. With 33 satellites launched to date ,

Kepler operates the first commercial optical data relay constellation, enabling real-time, continuous space communications while supporting advanced on-orbit compute and hosted payload capabilities. 

Industry-leading technology is only part of the story. What sets Kepler apart is our team:

bold thinkers, skilled builders, and passionate problem-solvers who thrive on pushing the boundaries of what’s possible in space. We believe great ideas come from diverse perspectives, and we’re committed to creating an environment where you can grow, lead, and make a global impact. 

If you’re ready to reach higher, move faster, and do work that shapes the future space economy - this is your launchpad. Come build the future with Kepler!

Kepler is on an audacious mission: to bring the internet to space. Founded in 2015, our ambition is to provide internet connectivity in space, whether in LEO, MEO, GEO, or beyond. With an expanding base of early customers and our first 23 satellites in orbit, Kepler is rapidly scaling and thrives on innovation and collaboration. Kepler is now continuing to grow and expand its most important asset - the Team!

As the Finance & Accounting Intern

, you will work closely with the finance & accounting team to maintain data integrity in our ERP (NetSuite), participate in monthly/annual close processes, maintain SOPs, and perform financial analysis. This intern role is centered around supporting the day-to-day operations of the business, helping the business make data empowered decisions and being the front-line point of contact for our suppliers.

Responsibilities

  • Actively participate in month-end and year-end close processes, assisting with preparation of journal entries, maintenance of proper supporting documentation. and the preparation of financial statements, reports and variance analysis

  • Perform credit card and bank reconciliations, reviewing for policy compliance

  • Maintain finance procedures and related process documents

  • Contribute to a team effort and undertakes other special projects as requested

Requirements

  • Working towards a bachelor’s degree in commerce or business Administration (Accounting or Finance major preferred) or similar program

  • Working towards all necessary prerequisites for admission into the Chartered Professional Accountants (CPA), Professional Education Program (PEP)

  • Very high attention to detail and strong customer service skills

  • Strong communicator: oral, written, interpersonal

  • A growth mindset, self-motivated and excited to work in a fast-paced environment

  • Organized with the ability to manage multiple duties simultaneously

  • Well-developed analytical and problem-solving skills

  • Superb Microsoft Office skills; specifically Excel & PowerPoint

  • Previous accounting related office experience is an asset

  • Currently enrolled in a post-secondary program.

  • Available for a full-time internship beginning September 2026 for at least 8 months.

  • Work out of our new Toronto office (24 Ward Street) at least 4 days per week.

Hourly Rate Range: $21.00-26.00 CAD
