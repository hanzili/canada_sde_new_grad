About Park Hill Resorts

Full Description

Job Overview
We are a property management/resort management company seeking a detail-oriented and experienced Accountant to join our finance team. The ideal candidate will be responsible for managing financial records for, ensuring accuracy in bookkeeping, and supporting various accounting functions. This role offers an excellent opportunity to work with a dynamic organization that values precision, integrity, and professional growth. The Accountant will play a key role in maintaining financial health through accurate record-keeping and analysis using leading accounting software.

Duties

  • Manage and maintain accurate financial records using accounting software such as Rent Manager, QuickBooks, Sage, or Xero
  • Perform bookkeeping tasks including accounts payable and accounts receivable management
  • Reconcile bank statements and accounts to ensure consistency and accuracy
  • Conduct account analysis to identify discrepancies or irregularities
  • Prepare financial reports and assist with budgeting processes
  • Support payroll processing and related accounting activities
  • Ensure compliance with financial regulations and internal policies
  • Assist with month-end and year-end closing procedures
  • Collaborate with auditors during audits by providing necessary documentation and explanations

Qualifications

  • Proven experience in accounting, bookkeeping, or related finance roles
  • Proficiency with accounting software such as Rent Manager, QuickBooks, is required
  • Strong understanding of accounts payable, accounts receivable, and account reconciliation processes
  • Experience with bank reconciliation and account analysis is preferred
  • Knowledge of payroll processing and budgeting is a plus
  • Excellent attention to detail and organizational skills
  • Ability to analyze financial data accurately and efficiently
  • Strong communication skills to collaborate effectively with team members and external auditors

This position offers a rewarding environment for professionals passionate about accounting and finance operations. We value accuracy, integrity, and continuous improvement in all aspects of our financial management.
