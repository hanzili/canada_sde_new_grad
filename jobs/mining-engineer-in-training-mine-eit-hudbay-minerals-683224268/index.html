Full Description

MINE ENGINEER IN TRAINING (EIT)

Location: Lalor Mine, Snow Lake, MB

Schedule: 14 Days On / 14 Days Off (12-hour days)

Salary: $100,624 - $111,805 / year + Profit Sharing

THE OPPORTUNITY

Exciting challenges. Bold lifestyle. A culture of collaboration.

Hudbay Minerals Inc. is seeking a driven Mine Engineer in Training to join our team at the Lalor Mine. This is more than a desk job; it is an opportunity to fast-track your career in one of Canada’s most complex and dynamic underground mining environments.

We offer a premium 14/14 roster, allowing you to balance a high-performance career with significant time off for travel and lifestyle. You will receive direct mentorship from senior engineering leaders, guiding you toward your Professional Engineer (P.Eng.) designation while you rotate through critical mine functions.

WHO WE ARE

Hudbay is a Canadian integrated mining company with operations and exploration activities across the Americas. We focus on the discovery, production, and marketing of base and precious metals. At Lalor Mine, we are mining gold, zinc, copper, and silver using modern methods and a "safety-first" culture.

WHAT YOU WILL DO

As an EIT, you will provide technical leadership to ensure the safe and optimal extraction of mineral deposits. You will work collaboratively with safety, operations, and geology teams to execute the mine plan.

Key Responsibilities:

  • Mine Planning: Design safe and efficient short-to-mid-range extraction plans. Collaborate with geology to evaluate ore zones and minimize dilution.
  • Drill & Blast Engineering: Design long-hole blasts and stope drill layouts. Analyze cavity monitoring surveys (CMS) and perform statistical analysis to optimize blast performance.
  • Operational Support: Travel underground regularly to validate plans, assess ground conditions, and provide real-time engineering solutions.
  • Risk Management: Identify risks to the mine plan and implement appropriate mitigations.
  • Safety Leadership: Champion health and safety standards in all engineering designs and daily operations.

QUALIFICATIONS

  • Education: University Degree in Mining or Geological Engineering.
  • Registration: Eligible for registration as an EIT with Engineers Geoscientists Manitoba (EGM).
  • Technical Skills: Proficiency in mining software (e.g., Deswik, Amine, AutoCAD, Promine) is highly advantageous.
  • Attributes: Strong communication skills, ability to work in a fast-paced team environment, and a willingness to learn.

WHY JOIN HUDBAY?

  • Work-Life Balance: Even-time 14/14 rotation.
  • Compensation: Competitive base salary plus a comprehensive profit-sharing plan.
  • Housing & Travel: Option for camp accommodations during shifts, or a full relocation package (including housing assistance) for those wishing to move to the vibrant community of Snow Lake. Travel is compensated, up to the cost of driving to/from Winnipeg, MB or equivalent.

  • Benefits:

  • Company Pension & Employee Share Purchase Plan.

  • Comprehensive Health, Dental, Vision, and Disability Insurance.
  • Tuition Reimbursement & Professional Development support.
