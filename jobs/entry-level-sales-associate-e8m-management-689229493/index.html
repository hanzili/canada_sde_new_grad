Full Description

AtE8M Management Inc.we are committed to providing top-quality service to customers and clients. We believe in the principles of client satisfaction, outstanding performance, and unwavering commitment! Our team consists of visionaries, strategists, and creatives who work tirelessly to achieve remarkable results! Our clients are at the heart of everything we do, we ensure that quality is never compromised!

Our mission of excellence can be accomplished with your contributions, as a member of our team! You'll be working in a team-oriented atmosphere that values and rewards your expertise, dedication, and creativity!

Our Entry Level Sales Associate are pros at:

  • Proactively identifying and pursuing new sales opportunities
  • Building and maintaining strong, long-lasting relationships with customers through regular, personalized interactions face to face
  • Effectively presenting our comprehensive range of products/services to clients and offering tailored solutions to meet their specific needs
  • Preparing and delivering compelling and persuasive proposals, quotes, and presentations that accurately articulate the value proposition of our products/services
  • Collaborating with the sales team to develop and implement strategies to achieve overall sales objectives
  • Staying up-to-date with industry trends and competitors by consistently attending meetings and conducting research
  • Providing valuable feedback based on customer input to the product development team to continuously improve our offerings

Qualifications:

  • Experience in sales, customer service, and/or communications is an asset but not required - comprehensive training is provided to set you up for success
  • Excellent communication and interpersonal skills
  • Ability to work independently and manage your time effectively
  • Customer-focused with a commitment to delivering exceptional service
  • Adaptability and a willingness to learn about our products/services
  • Strong desire to build up your negotiation/communications/sales skills

What You Can Expect:

  • Competitive pay with additional performance-based earnings
  • Paid training and continuous skill development
  • A consistent schedule and steady workflow
  • Clear opportunities for advancement into leadership roles
  • Ongoing mentorship and support from experienced leaders
  • A positive, team-oriented work environment
  • Recognition programs and performance incentives
  • Company-sponsored travel opportunities and team events

This role is ideal for someone looking to build confidence, gain valuable experience, and grow within a company that values consistency, support, and long-term success.

Pay: $44,000.00-$54,000.00 per year

Work Location: In person
