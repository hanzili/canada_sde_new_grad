Structural Engineer - EIT

at FNG Structural Engineering Inc

About the Role

Develop structural designs and CAD drawings under supervision

About FNG Structural Engineering Inc

Timber-focused civil firm with mentorship culture

Full Description

Overview
At FNG Structural, we strive for excellence with every project, big and small. We partner with our clients through the process to ensure the best possible outcome. Our Principle has over 30 years of Structural Engineering experience and has successfully mentored young Engineers across British Columbia and Alberta. This is a company where we will grow with you and apply your talents to the benefit of our clients, industry, and our communities.

Your Opportunity

As a rapidly expanding firm, we are looking to fill the position of Structural Engineer – EIT. This position is for a Structural Engineering with at least 1 to 3 years of timber experience with a desire to build your career with a fast growing company that invests in their people. You are open to grow and improve your skills in a collaborative environment. Working autonomously and with others you will have opportunities to further develop your engineering skills,

Responsibilities

· This position is accountable for applying intensive, complex and diversified knowledge of structural engineering principles and practices in broad areas of assignments and related fields, making independent decisions to solve structural engineering problems, resolving questions and planning and coordinating work.

· Strong organizational and time management skills with the ability to work effectively in a fast-paced, high volume and deadline-driven environment.

· Provide engineering technical support to the field during construction, including responding to RFIs from

· Developing preliminary design and engineering specifications

· Establish design criteria related to area of assignment, based upon specified contractual requirements

· Handle multiple diverse assignments successfully in a consulting firm environment

· Working with CAD software such as AutoCAD, WoodWorks, etc…

· Comfortable in working to meet tight deadlines.

Education and Qualifications

· B.Sc. or M.Sc. in Civil Engineering

· 1 to 3 years of Timber Experience Preferred

· Eligible for registration as a Professional Engineer (P.Eng.) in British Columbia

· Excellent communication skills (interpersonal and written) and proven ability to collaborate with team members in a multi-disciplined environment

· Technical competence and strong attention to detail

· A strong technical capability and the ability to work in a team environment

· Experience working with BC Building Codes and City Bylaws

Job Type: Full-time

Pay: $50,000.00 per year

Application question(s):

  • When is your earliest starting date?
  • Are you currently registered, or able to be registered, as an EIT or P.Eng with EGBC?
  • Do you currently reside in the Greater Vancouver Area and is able to travel in the Greater Vancouver Area.
