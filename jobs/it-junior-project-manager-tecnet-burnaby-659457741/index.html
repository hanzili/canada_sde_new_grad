Full Description

Join Our Team

Tecnet - Delivering Best-in-Class IT Solutions Since 1974

About Us
Tecnet is Canada’s longest-standing IT services provider, offering a wide range of solutions, including hardware, software, networking, and both physical and cyber security. With major hubs in Victoria and Vancouver, and over 35 Authorized Service Centres (ASC) across British Columbia, we’re committed to supporting communities across urban and rural areas with high-quality IT solutions.

Learn more about us at www.tecnet.ca.

Role Overview
The Junior Project Manager supports the planning, coordination, and execution of technical projects with a focus on organization, communication, and customer service. This role is ideal for someone early in their project management career who is eager to learn and grow while contributing to successful project delivery.

The Junior Project Manager works closely with project managers, internal teams, and clients to ensure tasks are completed on time and project documentation is maintained accurately. A proactive attitude, attention to detail, and strong collaboration skills are essential.

Why Work with Us?

  • Legacy of Excellence: Be part of a company with over 50 years of experience and a trusted reputation in the IT services industry.
  • Career Development: We prioritize your professional growth with opportunities for training, certifications, and skill development.
  • Diverse Challenges: Work on a wide variety of projects and solve unique technical challenges for a diverse range of clients.
  • Work-Life Balance: Enjoy flexible hours, a supportive team culture, and a healthy balance between your work and personal life.

Schedule

This is a full-time permanent position with 8-hour shifts from Monday – Friday for a total of 40 hrs weekly.

  • $60,000.00-$70,000.00 per year. Salary commensurate with experience.

  • Extended Health Care
  • Dental & Vision Care

Benefits become available following the 3-month probation.

What You’ll Do

  • Coordinate and support the planning, execution, and completion of technical and operational projects
  • Assist in developing and maintaining project plans, schedules, and timelines
  • Coordinate project meetings, including kickoffs, status updates, and wrap-ups
  • Act as a point of contact for project-related communication, documentation, and coordination
  • Provide regular project status updates to internal teams and clients
  • Maintain accurate, up-to-date project documentation and ensure templates reflect best practices
  • Track tasks, deliverables, and timelines to ensure alignment with project scope
  • Support senior project managers and technical teams by monitoring progress and maintaining momentum
  • Coordinate cross-team efforts with the Service Desk and Technical teams
  • Support solution design workflows by managing requests, documentation, and timelines
  • Document lessons learned and contribute to continuous process improvement

What You Bring

  • Post-secondary education in business, project management, or a related field
  • 1–2 years of experience supporting technical or operational projects
  • Project Management certification or coursework (e.g., PMP, CAPM, Agile fundamentals) and or related experience
  • Familiarity with access control and CCTV systems is an asset
  • Strong verbal and written communication skills in English
  • Proficiency in Microsoft Office (Excel, Word, Outlook); Microsoft Project or Visio experience is an asset
  • Highly organized with a structured, systematic approach to task and time management
  • Strong attention to detail and accuracy
  • Excellent interpersonal and customer service skills
  • Self-motivated, team-oriented, and receptive to feedback
  • Ability to manage multiple priorities independently in a fast-paced environment

Key Success Metrics

  • Accurate and timely maintenance of project plans, documentation, and schedules
  • Clear, consistent project communication with internal teams and clients
  • On-time completion of project milestones and deliverables
  • Efficient tracking and coordination of solution design requests
  • Effective cross-team collaboration supporting project momentum
  • Positive internal and client feedback on coordination and communication
  • Active contribution to continuous improvement and pride in Tecnet’s mission and team culture
