Entry Level Sales Representative

Location: Greater Toronto Area
Job Type: Full-Time
OTE: $60,000–$90,000+ (based on performance)

Sell. Grow. Earn Without Limits.

We are hiring driven Sales Representatives to join our residential sales team.

This is a target based, residential sales role requiring travel within assigned territories. You will engage directly with customers, build rapport, and close sales.

If you are confident, competitive, and motivated by performance-based income, this role offers strong earning potential and advancement opportunities.

What You’ll Be Responsible For - Residential Sales Execution

  • Conduct residential sales from field, online & referral
  • Promote Telecom, Internet & Professional Monitoring Security services
  • Identify customer needs and present tailored solutions
  • Handle objections and close sales professionally
  • Accurately complete and submit customer applications

Performance & Accountability

  • Meet and exceed weekly sales targets
  • Participate in weekly team meetings and training sessions
  • Maintain accurate tracking of sales activity
  • Follow company policies and compliance standards

Professional Representation

  • Maintain professional conduct in the field
  • Represent the company with integrity and strong customer service

What We’re Looking For

  • Sales experience preferred but not mandatory
  • Strong communication and interpersonal skills
  • Goal-oriented and performance-driven mindset
  • Comfortable working outdoors
  • Ability to pass a criminal background check

What You Get

  • Uncapped commission structure
  • Performance bonuses and incentives
  • Ongoing sales coaching and mentorship
  • Clear advancement path into Team Lead and Sales Manager roles
  • Supportive and competitive team environment
