About the Role

About CIBC

Full Description

We’re building a relationship-oriented bank for the modern world. We need talented, passionate professionals who are dedicated to doing what’s right for our clients.

At CIBC, we embrace your strengths and your ambitions, so you are empowered at work. Our team members have what they need to make a meaningful impact and are truly valued for who they are and what they contribute.

To learn more about CIBC, please visit CIBC.com

What You’ll Be Doing Join the Alternate Solutions Group (ASG) as a Summer Analyst and make a real difference for our clients. Within CIBC Capital Markets, ASG was launched in 2013 with a mandate to develop innovative and creative technology-driven capital markets solutions. This group is an unconventional capital markets organization that combines the innovative spirit and agility of a FinTech startup with the financial strength and strong governance protocol of a major financial institution. ASG has been recognized as a global leader in providing disruptive technological capital markets solutions in several product areas including Cross Border Payments, Dynamic Currency Conversion, International Student Pay, Global Money Transfer, Foreign Cash and other money movement solutions.

You’ll be part of the team that focuses on understanding the FinTech industry & disruptive technologies that are revolutionizing traditional banking products and services with a particular focus on FX & payments. You’ll have an opportunity to support the ASG team with product development and growth along with sales and marketing across a number of client types including financial institutions, public sector clients, corporates, higher education institutions, small businesses, and CIBC’s retail branch network.

You may also have the opportunity to work with ASG leadership to increase Profit and Loss (PnL) and productivity and be measured on your ability to both expand product capability, distribution and grow PnL. You will be assigned to a product team where you will have the opportunity to collaborate closely with our leaders. Your role will involve presenting insights from data analysis, developing effective solutions with timely execution, as well as continuously monitoring and analyzing market activities and economic factors throughout the day to respond promptly to client requests.

At CIBC we enable the work environment most optimal for you to thrive in your role. To successfully perform the work, you’ll be on-site full-time. The expected annualized base salary for this role is $95,000.

The base salary is determined by a variety of factors, including the candidate’s skills, experience, and job-related knowledge, as well as geographic location and specific business needs. Our compensation philosophy supports ongoing growth and development, enabling progression within the salary range as team members advance in their roles.

We are committed to providing fair, equitable, and competitive compensation opportunities for all employees, and we encourage open conversations about compensation throughout the recruitment process.

How You’ll Succeed

  • Passion for financial markets – Demonstrate strong technical, quantitative, and analytical skills, with keen interest in the financial markets and proven record of academic success, teamwork and extracurricular involvement.
  • Business analysis - Formulate solution scope and objectives to meet the business need and validate requirements, testing, training and implementation of applications.
  • Organization & Execution - Demonstrate effective organizational abilities by managing tasks, following procedures, and ensuring timely completion of assignments with a sense of urgency.
  • Communication – Partner with your team to make an impact by applying a technical skillset to help generate insights and/or optimize processes. Showcase highly effective written, oral and presentation skills with an ability to explain complex concepts.
  • Reporting, analytics and research - Analyze business opportunities and present recommendations to address through analysis, reporting and research.

Who You Are

  • You have demonstrated interest in Capital Markets. You’ve built a strong knowledge of Capital Markets, FinTech and the financial services industries which allows you to analyze complex issues and identify & develop innovative opportunities, with a focus on FX & payments.
  • You give meaning to data. You enjoy investigating complex problems and making sense of information. You're confident in your ability to communicate detailed information in an impactful way. You have strong mathematical, analytical and problem-solving skills.
  • You’re digitally savvy. You are proficient in MS-office suite of tools (Word, PowerPoint, Excel, etc.) and are able to use them for financial modeling, presentations and technical project documentation. Proficiency with MS Power BI is an asset.
  • You are comfortable communicating fluently in English and Spanish. You are able to effectively synthesize and present analyses and recommendations to senior executives. You can create effective client-facing materials in both languages including RFPs, pitchbooks, presentation decks, etc.
  • You're passionate about people. You find meaning in relationships and surround yourself with a diverse network of partners. You build trust through respect and authenticity.
  • You understand that success is in the details. You notice things that others don't. Your critical thinking skills help to inform your decision making.
  • Values matter to you. You bring your real self to work, and you live our values – trust, teamwork and accountability.
