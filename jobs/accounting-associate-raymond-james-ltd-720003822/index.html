Full Description

At Raymond James, we develop, we collaborate, we decide, we deliver, and we improve together .

Raymond James Ltd. is Canada’s leading independent investment dealers offering high quality investment products and services to Canadians seeking customized solutions to their wealth management needs.

Accounting Associate How does the role impact the organization? Raymond James Ltd. is seeking an Accounting Associate to join the Finance Department of our downtown Vancouver office. The successful candidate for this position will work in a fast-paced accounting department reporting to the AVP, Finance. This role involves direct communication with external banking personnel as well as internal client groups, including Financial Advisors, management, and Operations staff.

What is the position responsible for?

  • Process day to day banking activities, including any required accounting entries and the timely preparation of bank reconciliations;
  • Assist with daily foreign exchange hedging;
  • Resolve client banking inquiries received from internal staff;
  • Assist with the monthly financial reporting process;
  • Prepare and distribute management reports;
  • Assist with monthly interest inquiries; and
  • Assist with projects and initiatives as required.

What can you expect from us? Our most important investment is in people, Raymond James Ltd offers flexible workstyles, a competitive compensation and benefits package. Our benefits range from Health Benefits, RRSP Matching Program, Employee Stock Purchase Plan, Paid Time Off, Volunteer Days, Discretionary Bonuses, Tuition Reimbursement and many more! We also support internal promotion and community involvement.

What do we expect from you?

  • Post-secondary education with knowledge of basic accounting is an asset;
  • Proficiency in Excel and Outlook;
  • Exemplary organizational skills and the ability to work quickly;
  • Excellent written and verbal communication skills;
  • The ability to demonstrate excellent client service and collaboration;
  • The ability to work effectively both independently and within a team environment;
  • An attention to detail;
  • Exemplary problem solving skills and the ability for adaptation; and
  • Fluency in written and verbal French is an asset.

We encourage our associates at all levels to:

  • Grow professionally and inspire others to do the same
  • Work with and through others to achieve desired outcomes
  • Make prompt, pragmatic choices and act with the client in mind
  • Take ownership and hold themselves and others accountable for delivering results that matter
  • Contribute to the continuous evolution of the firm

Salary Range: $50,000 - $60,000 annually. Actual compensation will be determined based on skills, experience, internal equity, and other job requirements.
