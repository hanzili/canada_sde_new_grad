About the Role

Analyze data, build dashboards, inform decisions

About Canadian Tire Corporation

Canada's leading retailer, data-driven culture

Full Description

Our Commitment to Students At Canadian Tire, we are committed to providing students with a supportive and collaborative environment that fosters knowledge sharing and offers key experiences to help them develop their capabilities through projects and objectives. We believe in setting specific and measurable goals that align with our business objectives to support learning and help students achieve their full potential. Our culture emphasizes learning from others, continuous improvement, agility, growth, and innovation, and we are invested in building a talented, diverse workforce for the future of Canada and Canadian Tire. Additionally, we are dedicated to building strong relationships with our student employees by engaging with them throughout their education and career pursuits and creating opportunities for ongoing communication and relationship building.

What You’ll Do The Operations Support and Analytics Team supports the efficient and effective movement of freight throughout CTC’s delivery network. This support is provided in a number of forms, including; research and data analysis, process improvement projects, carrier rate / contract negotiations support and reporting. The team works closely with the Canadian Dispatch and Carrier Support Team, Direct Ship, Fleet Services, the Transportation Development & Support Team and the Visibility Team, but also has frequent involvement with Planning, Logistics, Distribution, International Transportation, Marketing, Finance and IT:

  • Perform deep‑dive quantitative analysis, transforming large datasets into meaningful insights and actionable recommendations.
  • Access, validate, and extract data from multiple internal and external sources using tools such as SQL, Excel, Python, and Power BI.
  • Develop dashboards, visualizations, and automated reporting solutions to support operational decision‑making.
  • Interpret data and present insights effectively to leadership and cross‑functional partners.
  • Document current processes and support development of training and reference materials.
  • Map existing workflows, identify improvement opportunities, and support automation or simulation modelling.
  • Run recurring operational reports to gather freight, shipment, and forecast data.
  • Assist in onboarding and mentoring co‑op students supporting various reporting and analytical tasks.

What You Bring

  • Currently enrolled in a post-secondary program in Business, Data Analytics, Mathematics, Statistics, Computer Science, Engineering, or a related discipline
  • Demonstrated ability to analyze datasets, build reports, and communicate findings.
  • Experience working with Excel (advanced functions, pivot tables, Power Query).
  • Working knowledge of SQL for querying and manipulating data.
  • Experience with Power BI, Tableau, or other data visualization tools.
  • Ability to automate tasks using Python, R, or another programming language.
  • A creative, analytical thinker with strong problem‑solving skills.
  • Ability to manage multiple tasks and deliver under tight deadlines.
  • Proven ability to work both independently and collaboratively within a team.
  • Strong written and verbal communication skills.
  • High attention to detail and comfort working with large, complex data.
  • A strong communicator who can translate complex data into clear messages.

  • Career growth opportunities and product discounts

About Us Canadian Tire Corporation, Limited (“CTC”) is one of Canada’s most admired and trusted companies. With more than 90 Owned Brands, 1,700 retail locations, financial services, exemplary e-commerce capabilities, and exciting market-leading merchandising strategies. We dream big and work as one to innovate with purpose for our customers at every level of our business, investing in new technologies and products, and doubling down on top talent to drive the company forward. We offer competitive salaries and wages to CTC employees, as well as store discounts, supported learning through our Triangle Learning Academy, Canadian Tire Profit Sharing, and retirement and savings programs for eligible employees. As part of our enhanced flex benefits program, we offer mental health benefits in the amount of $5,000 per year for benefits-eligible employees and their families, including total well-being, and mental health tools and resources for all employees. Join us in helping to make life in Canada better through living and working our Core Values: we are innovators and entrepreneurs at our core, outcomes drive us, inclusion is a must, we are stronger together and we take personal responsibility. It is an especially exciting time to join CTC and its family of companies where career opportunities are wide-ranging! Join us, where there's a place for you here.
