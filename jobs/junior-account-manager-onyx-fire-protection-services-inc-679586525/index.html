About Onyx-Fire Protection Services Inc.

Growing fire protection firm, strong client service

Full Description

Onyx-Fire Protection Services Inc. is the fastest growing full-service fire protection company.

At this time, we are seeking a Junior Account Manager.

Key Responsibilities

  • Demonstrate a strong commitment to meeting and supporting customer expectations.
  • Respond to client inquiries in a timely, professional, and courteous manner, ensuring proper follow-up and follow-through.
  • Build and maintain strong client relationships through clear, consistent, and trustworthy communication.
  • Assist with the setup, monitoring, and maintenance of client portals.
  • Lead KPI tracking and monitoring for assigned clients to ensure service performance aligns with client expectations and contractual requirements.
  • Learn and understand assigned client requirements, including:
  • Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)
  • Service Level Agreements (SLAs)
  • Billing procedures
  • Reporting expectations
  • Maintain organized and up-to-date records for assigned accounts.
  • Escalate issues or service concerns to senior team members as required.
  • Support internal coordination between operations, billing, and service teams to ensure client needs are met.

Qualifications & Skills

  • Post-secondary education in Business, Administration, or a related field (or equivalent experience).
  • Strong customer service orientation with a professional and positive approach.
  • Excellent written and verbal communication skills.
  • Strong organizational skills with attention to detail.
  • Ability to prioritize tasks and manage multiple inquiries in a fast-paced environment.
  • Proficiency with Microsoft Office (Outlook, Excel, Word); experience with client portals or CRM systems is an asset

Why Join Us?

  • Join a growing, values-driven company dedicated to operational excellence and innovation.
  • Work alongside a collaborative team that supports career growth and development.
  • Competitive salary, benefits package, and learning opportunities.
  • Be part of a company that protects lives and property through world-class fire protection services

This posting is for an existing vacancy. We are actively reviewing resumes for this open role.

We’re more than just a company—we’re a community. With our headquarters in Mississauga and branches across Canada, we’re trusted by clients nationwide. We’re proud of our high-performance culture and are committed to helping our people grow and succeed.
