About the Role

About Fowler Construction

Construction leader with QC lab and field testing

Full Description

Fowler Construction is on the lookout for enthusiastic students to join our dynamic team! Fowler is seeking a Co-op QC Lab Technicians to provide technical support, involving quality control activities through our quality control lab, pits, quarries, and construction sites.

Who We Are
Fowler Construction has been a leader in infrastructure development for decades, delivering high-quality projects across Ontario. We are committed to safety, innovation, and building strong relationships with our employees, clients, and communities.

FOWLER CONSTRUCTION VALUES

  • People – We invest in our employees’ well-being, growth, and success.
  • Performance – We uphold excellence, professionalism, and hard work.
  • Community – We support local initiatives and foster strong connections.
  • Environment – We prioritize sustainability and responsible practices.

Key Responsibilities

· Complete QC sampling and testing as necessary on aggregates, asphalt and soils

· Perform work safely

· Testing involves sieve analysis, marshal and super-pave hot mix testing, mix designs, agencies correlation, aggregate analysis and compaction testing.

· Keep a record/journal of daily work site activities, materials inspected, tested and sampled.

· Provide technical support for Materials Production staff and Construction field staff.

· Provide technical support for Managers and Supervisors.

· Assist in the exploration and product development at our material sources and production facilities.

· Work with, and alongside Managers and Supervisors to ensure materials are produced and placed to meet/exceed the quality requirements of Fowler and the project owner.

· Maintain testing equipment and tools in a good state of repair.

· Complete work with attention to detail to produce a quality product.

· Communicate effectively with the field staff and report internally on quality and production activities

· Liaise with onsite inspectors, owners’ representatives and the public.

· Complete reporting for quantities, payments and regular tracking and documentation requirements.

· Conduct yourself in a professional/mature manner.

Key Skills & Qualifications

· Post-secondary student enrolled in a technical program and currently eligible for co-operative field placement.

· High level of competence in Word, Excel, Project and other.

· Strong organizational/analytical skills with clear oral and written communication abilities.

· Exceptional organizational abilities with task prioritization, multi-tasking, use of timelines and time management techniques.

· High level of personal integrity.

· Strong work ethic and the ability to work cooperatively with other staff.

· Ability to work in a fast-paced environment.

· Must be able to work with little supervision; must be self-directed.

· Maintain a valid driver’s license

· Must be able to lift 50+ lbs on occasion

Work Conditions

· Use of company vehicle to travel to various sites will be required.

· Indoor and outdoor environments in various weather conditions.

· Weekend or overtime work may be required from time to time to complete the scope of work in a timely manner and based on weather conditions.

For an opportunity to gain valuable experience and make a real impact, apply now!
