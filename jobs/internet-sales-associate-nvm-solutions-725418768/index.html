Full Description

At the core of everything we do is a simple belief: strong results are built through strong people. Our organization is driven by performance, collaboration, and continuous development, and we are proud to create an environment where motivated individuals can grow quickly and meaningfully. If you are ready to invest in your future and build skills that open doors, this is an opportunity worth exploring.

We are currently expanding our team and are excited to welcome Internet Sales Associates who bring energy, curiosity, and a results-oriented mindset. In this role, you will work directly with customers, guide conversations with purpose, and recommend solutions that genuinely improve how people stay connected. Each interaction gives you the chance to develop professionally, contribute to shared success, and build momentum toward long-term career growth.

What you will be doing as our Internet Sales Associate:

  • Connect with customers through in-person one-on-one conversations that build trust and understanding
  • Introduce and explain a range of modern solutions, including home internet services, smart routers, Wi-Fi enhancements, TV and streaming options, and wireless plans such as unlimited, prepaid, and international offerings
  • Customize recommendations based on individual needs, creating value-driven solutions
  • Manage the full sales process, from initial conversation through successful enrollment
  • Work alongside a driven, supportive team that celebrates progress and performance
  • Stay organized by tracking activity, following up consistently, and owning your results
  • Develop foundational skills in professional sales, communication, and leadership that translate across industries

What you will gain when you join our team:

  • A full-time schedule with optional weekend opportunities
  • Competitive compensation with uncapped bonuses tied directly to performance
  • One-on-one coaching and mentorship from leaders who advanced through the same path
  • A positive, achievement-focused culture that values effort, mindset, and results
  • Transferable skills in communication, negotiation, and problem-solving that support long-term success
  • Company-sponsored team-building and social events to foster connection
  • Fully-paid, company-wide travel opportunities
  • Generous company-sponsored annual trips to premium leisure destinations for well-deserved relaxation
  • And a wide range of additional perks designed for our associates’ well-being

Who thrives in this role:

  • Someone who enjoys meeting new people and working in fast-paced environments
  • A confident communicator who can adapt and connect with different personalities
  • Goal-driven, resilient, and motivated by growth and achievement
  • Open to learning, feedback, and continuous improvement
  • 18 years of age or older and legally eligible to work in Canada
  • Previous sales experience is an asset, but not required – comprehensive paid training is provided by us to all new employees

This is more than an entry-level position. It is a chance to build confidence, gain real-world experience, and prove what you are capable of in an environment where effort is recognized and success is shared. If you are ready to take that step, we would love to connect with you.

Job Types: Full-time, Permanent
