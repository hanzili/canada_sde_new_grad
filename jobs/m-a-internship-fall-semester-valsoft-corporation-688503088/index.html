Full Description

TAG GROUP – FALL SEMESTER M&A INTERNSHIP

JOB DESCRIPTION

Are you a university student or recent graduate with a passion for finance and a keen interest in Mergers and Acquisitions (M&A) looking for an internship? If so, we have an exciting opportunity for you! Join our team as an M&A Intern and gain valuable hands-on experience in the world of mergers and acquisitions.

ABOUT TAG SOFTWARE GROUP
TAG Software Group is a team of experienced investors and operators focused on acquiring and growing mission-critical software companies serving SMB, enterprise, and public sector customers. As part of Valsoft Corporation, TAG provides a permanent home for software businesses, prioritizing long-term partnerships and sustainable growth.

INVESTMENT APPROACH
Unlike private equity and venture capital firms, TAG operates as an entrepreneurial acquirer, partnering with existing management teams to enhance and grow software businesses. The company emphasizes long-term value creation and prioritizes the well-being of employees and customers over short-term results.

CULTURE
TAG fosters a collaborative, high-performance culture where people are its greatest asset. The company encourages ambition with humility, continuous growth, and celebrates shared achievements.

This is a unique opportunity for an ambitious and driven individual to develop their career in a growing and entrepreneurial environment, where they can have a clear career pathway, starting as an intern and working their way up to an M&A Leader.

Key Responsibilities:

1.Origination of Investment Opportunities (60%):

  • Independently source and identify potential software investment opportunities.

  • Build and maintain a robust investment pipeline.

2.Deal Execution Support (20%):

  • Perform financial modelling and commercial due diligence.

  • Conduct comprehensive financial analysis.

3.Business Development (20%):

  • Travel to meet and engage with businesses across Europe.

  • Foster and develop strong relationships within the industry.

Our Ideal Hire:

  • Currently enrolled in a bachelor's or master's degree program or a recent graduate in Finance, Business, Economics, or a related field.

  • Strong analytical and problem-solving skills.

  • Company valuation methodologies.

  • Excellent communication and interpersonal skills.

  • Proficiency in Microsoft Excel and financial modelling.

  • An eagerness to learn and adapt in a dynamic and fast-paced environment.

  • Strong passion for the software and investment sectors.

  • Fluent in English (additional languages are a plus).

Why Choose Us:

  • Opportunity for international travel including onboarding in our Toronto Office.

  • Hands-on experience in the dynamic field of mergers and acquisitions in a company

  • Mentorship and guidance from experienced professionals.

  • Networking opportunities with professionals in the field.

  • Opportunities for growth and career development.

  • Be a part of an entrepreneurial culture, where your innovative ideas and fresh perspectives are valued and can make a real impact on our business.

Join us in shaping the future of M&A and embark on a journey of professional growth and success. We look forward to welcoming the next generation of M&A professionals to our team!
