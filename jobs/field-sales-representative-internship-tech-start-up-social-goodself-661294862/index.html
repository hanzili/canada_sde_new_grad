Full Description

About Us:

Goodself is a clinician-referable health-tech platform that gives patients trusted, expert-led education and community support between visits. Available on iOS, Android, and desktop, it blends the familiarity of social media with structured, digital-first health education through short-form expert-led content, programs and moderated communities, and data insights. Learn more at goodself.com.

Field Sales Representative (Senior Student Role - Community Partnerships)

We’re looking for a high-performing 3rd or 4th year student to help drive early-stage growth at Goodself through in-person outreach and partnerships. Goodself is a health-focused platform built to give people access to trusted, expert-led education and support between visits. We’re now focused on scaling distribution, and this role will play a key part in making that happen. This is not a passive or “campus rep” role. You’ll be responsible for building relationships and opening doors.

What you’ll do:

  • Lead in-person outreach across community-based locations in your region (healthcare settings, campus, retail, etc.).
  • Identify, approach, and engage decision-makers to introduce Goodself.
  • Drive early partnerships and support onboarding from the first conversation to activation.

What we’re looking for:

  • A 3rd or 4th-year student currently enrolled in a STEM-related program at a Canadian post-secondary institution.
  • Strong communicator with confidence in face-to-face conversations.
  • Proactive and comfortable working in a dynamic, fast-paced environment.
  • Interested in health technology, digital media, or startup environments.
  • Experience in sales, customer-facing roles, community outreach, or healthcare is an asset (not required).

Requirements:

  • Requires access to a reliable vehicle and comfort with local travel. We provide mileage reimbursement for all local travel.
  • Must have a valid driver’s licence.
  • A 3rd or 4th-year student enrolled in a Canadian post-secondary institution.

Why this role:

  • Direct exposure to how early-stage startups build distribution and partnerships
  • High ownership and autonomy, you’re not just executing, you’re helping define the strategy.
  • Opportunity to work closely with the core team and make a visible impact.
  • Strong experience for careers in startups, consulting, healthcare, and business/sales.
