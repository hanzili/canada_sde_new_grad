Role Description

This is a on-site position based in Mississauga, ON for a Junior Staff Accountant - Contract role. The Junior Staff Accountant will handle a variety of accounting responsibilities, including preparing financial statements, assisting with audits, reconciling accounts, and maintaining accurate financial records. The role also involves managing general ledger accounts, processing payroll, preparing reports, and supporting other accounting-related tasks as needed. Collaboration with team members and effective communication are key aspects of this position.

Qualifications

  • Strong knowledge of accounting principles, financial statements preparation, and audit procedures
  • Proficient in using accounting software and tools like QuickBooks, Excel, and similar platforms
  • Skills in data entry, account reconciliations, and maintaining general ledger accounts (CASEWARE)
  • Excellent attention to detail, organizational skills, and time management abilities
  • Ability to work effectively in an on-site team environment and meet deadlines
  • Bachelor's degree in Accounting, Finance, or a related field (or in progress)
  • Previous experience or internship in an accounting or tax-related role is an asset

Please note this is a 6 month contract opportunity, with a possibility of full time as required.
