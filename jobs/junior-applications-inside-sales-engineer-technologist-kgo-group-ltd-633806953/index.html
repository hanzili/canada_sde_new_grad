About KGO Group Ltd.

Full Description

Job Summary

Build a Career in Engineered Industrial Systems

KGO Group is looking for a driven, detail-driven and technically inclined Junior Applications & Inside Sales Engineer/Technologist who wants more than a routine desk role.

This position is designed for someone early in their career who wants to develop into a senior Applications Engineer or Outside Technical Sales leader within 2–3 years.

You will work directly on engineered chemical feed, polymer preparation, pump, tank, and mixing systems serving mining, pulp & paper, municipal, and heavy industrial clients across Canada.

If you are ambitious, detail-oriented, and want real responsibility early in your career — this role is for you.

Core Responsibilities

Technical Proposal Development

  • Review engineering specifications, P&IDs, and bid packages
  • Size and select pumps, tanks, mixers, and chemical feed systems
  • Develop system concepts and preliminary layouts
  • Prepare detailed, accurate, and competitive quotations
  • Ensure designs align with fabrication capabilities and project budgets

You will not simply assemble quotes — you will learn to engineer solutions.

Inside Sales Performance

  • Actively support quotation follow-ups and order conversion
  • Communicate directly with consulting engineers and end users
  • Track opportunities in CRM and manage your proposal pipeline
  • Assist in building long-term client relationships

“A high standard of quotation accuracy, responsiveness, and support in order conversion is expected in this role.”

Supplier & Technical Coordination

  • Negotiate pricing and lead times with suppliers
  • Evaluate technical suitability of components
  • Maintain cost discipline and margin awareness

Cross-Functional Impact

  • Work daily with Mechanical, Electrical, and Fabrication teams
  • Participate in design reviews
  • Support seamless handoff from sales to production
  • Learn how engineered systems are built and commissioned

You will gain hands-on exposure to real equipment — not just drawings.

Who Will Succeed Here

  • Chemical or Mechanical Engineering Technologist (Diploma or Degree)
  • 1–3 years of relevant industrial or technical experience
  • Strong mechanical aptitude and commercial awareness
  • Comfortable speaking with engineers and plant personnel
  • Highly organized, self-motivated, and performance-driven
  • Interested in growing into Technical Sales leadership

This is not a passive support role. We are looking for someone who wants to take ownership.

What We Offer

  • Direct mentorship from senior industry professionals
  • Clear advancement path into Applications or Outside Technical Sales
  • Exposure to high-performance industrial systems
  • Performance-based growth opportunities
  • Competitive compensation with upside potential

Why KGO Group

We design and fabricate robust, high-quality chemical feed and polymer systems built for demanding industrial environments. Our culture is built on:

  • Commitment to quality
  • Reliability guaranteed
  • No, I in team

We expect high standards — and we reward performance.
