Human Resources Co-op Student

at Magna International

📍 Location Etobicoke, Ontario
📋 Details Co-op · 4 months · $19-$23/hr · 4 Month Co-op
📅 Posted Feb 26, 2026

About the Role

Coordinate HR administration; assist recruitment and training

About Magna International

Global auto supplier; Cosma HR division

What We Offer At Magna, you can expect an engaging and dynamic environment where you can help to develop industry-leading automotive technologies. We invest in our employees, providing them with the support and resources they need to succeed. As a member of our global team, you can expect exciting, varied responsibilities as well as a wide range of development prospects. Group Summary Cosma provides a comprehensive range of body, chassis and engineering solutions to global customers. Through our robust product engineering, outstanding tooling capabilities and diverse process expertise, we continue to bring lightweight and innovative products to market.

Job Responsibilities POSITION OVERVIEW:

The Human Resources Co-Op Student supports the company through various Human Resources systems to meet company goals and legislative requirements. He or She will perform all duties and promote themselves in a manner that upholds and reflects the Magna’s Employee Charter.

Duration: 4 Month Co-op

Salary Range: $19/hr - $23/hr

Qualifications Required

  • Working towards Post-secondary education in Business Administration preferably a focus in Human Resources Management
  • Excellent written and verbal communication skills
  • Possess exceptional organizational and problem solving skills
  • Strong MS Office skill
  • Able to be flexible in hours of work in order to meet internal/external customer requirements (scheduled and non-scheduled overtime, shifts, etc.

Responsibilities / Efforts / Working Conditions

  • Maintains team member personnel files
  • Assist with recruitment (job postings, scheduling interviews, etc.)
  • Coordination of internal and external training as well as maintaining training records
  • Support in the coordination of social events as required
  • Manage all Employee Communication Centers, ensuring ongoing updates and visual clarity
  • Facilitate job posting policy
  • Monitoring of performance appraisal procedures
  • Deals with team member concerns with respect to job security, fair treatment, safe and healthy Workplace, fair wages and benefits, equity participation and communication of information
  • Acts as a resource/consultant to Department Managers/Supervisors/Teamleaders with regards to policies, legislation, principles of the Employee Charter, etc.
  • Assists in the planning of key programs and activities, ie manpower, layoffs, etc.
  • Work safely and within the guidelines of the Deco Employee Handbook
  • Commitment to continuous improvement and work place safety
  • Team Player
  • TS16949/ ISO14001 awareness and adherence
  • Lean Manufacturing and 5S concepts awareness and adherence
  • Perform administrative tasks for the HR department as needed

Safety Performance Requirements

  • Accepts personal responsibility to plan and conduct their work safely in compliance with the Occupational Health & Safety Act as well as company policies and guidelines.
  • Responsible for good housekeeping in your area.
