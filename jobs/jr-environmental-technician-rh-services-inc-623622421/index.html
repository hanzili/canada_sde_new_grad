About RH Services Inc.

Full Description

RH Services Inc. is a full service Occupational Hygiene and Safety Consulting company, located in Edmonton, Alberta and established in 2002.

We are currently looking for a full time Jr. Environmental Technologist

75% field work and 25% office work

field duties include:

  • supervising hazardous materials abatement projects (Asbestos, Mould, lead etc.)

  • sample collection & analysis

  • Field level hazard assessments

  • Pre-job inspections

  • onsite document revision

Office duties include but are not limited to:

  • report writing

  • daily reporting

  • microscopy

  • housekeeping

  • general maintenance if required

Job Requirements

  • proficient English in both verbal and written

  • proficient in Microsoft office

  • strong organizational skills

  • excellent verbal and written communication skills

  • Must be willing to travel throughout province

  • extended hours and weekend work are likely

  • night shift may happen

Educational Requirements

relevant post secondary education in either Occupational Health and Safety or construction prioritized

currently either a CRSP or an ROHT or meet the requirements to obtain within 6 months of employment

  • Alberta Asbestos Ticket would be considered an asset

General Qualifications

  • Valid drivers License and Insurance

  • Reliable vehicle

  • able to pass a drug screen

  • able to pass a health assessment

  • capable of lifting 30lbs

If you are interesting in applying for the position please send your resume

Job Type: Full-time

Pay: $22.00-$27.00 per hour

Work Location: In person
