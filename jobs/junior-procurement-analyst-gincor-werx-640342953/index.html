Junior Procurement Analyst, Contract

We have an opportunity for an enthusiastic, proven Junior Procurement Analyst to join our Team.

Key Accountabilities:

  • Assist in standardizing purchased items across all Business Units.
  • Cross reference commodities for each location within the ERP system and support physical validation where required.
  • Support the cleaning and standardization of item attributes including Part Class, Purchasing or Manufacturing designation, lead times, and Min or Max levels.
  • Prepare and update DMT spreadsheets for data uploads into Epicor.
  • Help maintain and support the new Purchase Part system.
  • Assist with updating supplier listings, pricing, and approved supplier information.
  • Communicate with national suppliers to gather required information and documentation.

Process Documentation

  • Support the creation and documentation of Standard Operating Procedures for the Purchasing group.
  • Assist in identifying process gaps and opportunities for improved consistency across Business Units.

Reporting and Analysis

  • Prepare and update business reports across Business Units as required.
  • Use Excel to organize and analyze purchasing data.
  • Support leadership with accurate, timely reporting to inform decision making.

Qualifications:

  • Post secondary education in Supply Chain, Business, Finance, or a related field, or equivalent practical experience.
  • 1 to 3 years of experience in a purchasing, supply chain, finance, or analytical support role is considered an asset.
  • Working knowledge of ERP systems is preferred. Experience with Epicor is an asset.
  • Intermediate to advanced Excel skills, including pivot tables and data analysis functions.
  • Strong attention to detail and comfort working with structured data.
  • Strong organizational skills with the ability to manage priorities in a project environment.
  • Clear and professional communication skills when working with internal stakeholders and suppliers.
