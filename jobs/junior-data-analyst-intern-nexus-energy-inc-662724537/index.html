About Nexus Energy Inc.

Toronto-based energy trading company

Full Description

Position: Junior Data Analyst Intern

Department: Finance and Settlements

Location: Toronto, ON (On-Site)

Wage: $20/hour

Job Type & Duration: Internship

Note: This is a supervised, entry-level student placement designed to provide practical, hands-on experience in financial analytics.

Company Overview:

Nexus Energy Inc. is a privately held energy company headquartered in Toronto, Ontario, active in trading electricity across Eastern Canada, the Northeastern U.S., and Western markets, as well as providing power price risk management solutions to large commercial and industrial loads in Ontario. In addition, Nexus is actively working to develop a pipeline of renewable energy projects.

At Nexus, we foster a culture that values high performance, collaboration, and continuous development. You will join a passionate team that drives the business forward through innovation, accountability, and open communication.

The Junior Data Analyst will support the team in collecting, analyzing, and interpreting data to assist with business and operational decision-making. This role provides hands-on experience with data analysis tools, reporting, and real-world datasets within the energy and trading environment.

What You Will Do

  • Collecting, cleaning, and organizing data from various internal and external sources
  • Assisting in analyzing datasets to identify trends, patterns, and insights
  • Creating and maintaining reports, dashboards, and data visualizations
  • Supporting data entry and ensuring data accuracy and consistency
  • Using tools such as Microsoft Excel, SQL, and data visualization platforms (e.g., Power BI, Tableau)
  • Assisting with ad hoc data analysis requests from team members
  • Documenting data processes and maintaining organized datasets
  • Collaborating with cross-functional teams to support business decisions

What You Will Bring

Qualifications:

  • Currently pursuing or recently completed a degree/diploma in Data Analytics, Computer Science, Business Analytics, or a related field
  • Basic understanding of data analysis, statistics, and data management concepts
  • Proficiency in Microsoft Excel; familiarity with SQL or data visualization tools is an asset
  • Strong attention to detail and organizational skills
  • Good communication and time management abilities
  • Ability to work independently and as part of a team

Nice to have:

  • Previous internship or project experience involving data analysis
  • Familiarity with Power BI, Tableau, or similar tools
  • Basic knowledge of programming languages such as Python or R

Work Environment:

  • Internship or entry-level position
  • Supervised by the Senior Manager of Finance & Settlements
  • Opportunity to gain hands-on experience in accounting and financial operations
  • Work-life balance
  • Team-building events

How This Placement Relates to the Student’s Studies

This placement directly supports the student’s academic studies in data analytics, computer science, or business analytics by providing practical, hands-on experience in data collection, analysis, and visualization. The role allows the student to apply theoretical concepts such as data cleaning, statistical analysis, and data interpretation in a real-world business environment. Additionally, the position helps develop technical skills in tools such as Excel, SQL, and data visualization platforms, while strengthening analytical, problem-solving, and critical thinking skills essential for a career in data analytics.

Accommodations

Nexus Energy Inc. is committed to fostering an inclusive and accessible work environment. We are committed to providing equitable treatment and accommodation to ensure barrier-free employment by the Ontario Human Rights Code and Accessibility for Ontarians with Disabilities Act (AODA). If you require Code-protected accommodations due to disability during the recruitment process, please communicate them when contacted, and we will ensure your needs are met.
