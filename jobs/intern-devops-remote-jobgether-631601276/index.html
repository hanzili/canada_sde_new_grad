About the Role

About Jobgether

Full Description

This position is posted by Jobgether on behalf of a partner company. We are currently looking for a Remote DevOps Intern. In this role, you will have the opportunity to contribute to the automation of operational tasks using Python and Bash, while gaining hands-on experience in a leading EdTech organization. You’ll be closely supervised and will support infrastructure management in AWS, maintenance of CI/CD pipelines, and application monitoring. This is a unique opportunity to develop your skills and grow in a dynamic environment focused on innovation and learning.

Accountabilities

  • Support in managing AWS infrastructure (S3, EC2)
  • Assist in maintaining and evolving CI/CD pipelines
  • Monitor applications and alert systems
  • Document processes and create runbooks
  • Help in the triage of vulnerabilities

Requirements

  • Currently pursuing a degree in Computer Engineering, Computer Science, Systems Analysis and Development, or related fields
  • Familiarity with Python
  • Knowledge of Bash/Shell, Git
  • Experience with AWS (S3, EC2)
  • Understanding of Docker and CI/CD (GitHub Actions or similar)
  • Basic knowledge of Linux

Benefits

  • Flexible work hours: Mon-Fri 09:00-16:00 with a 1-hour break
  • Home Office working system
  • Meal voucher
  • Health insurance plan
  • Gympass and Totalpass memberships
