Full Description

Summer Internship – AI & Data Optimization (University Student)

Company: Windmill Farms

Location: Port Perry, Ontario

Duration: May – August 2026 (Flexible start/end dates)

About Windmill Farms

Windmill Farms is one of Canada’s leading mushroom producers, growing over 1 millionpounds of mushrooms per week across multiple farm sites. Operating at this scale generates a large and complex flow of data, from production metrics and environmental inputs to logistics, inventory, and sales performance.

We are looking for a highly motivated university student who wants to work hands-on with real, complex, and high-impact business problems, not theoretical ones. You will help shape how data and artificial intelligence are used across a fast-paced agricultural operation.

The Opportunity

This is not a passive internship.

You’ll work directly with senior leadership (including the CEO) and collaborate across Finance, Logistics, IT, and Sales to help us modernize how we use data and AI across the business.

If you’re excited about applying AI in the real world, this is for you!

Core Projects (You’ll Own These)

  1. Logistics Optimization

· Work with Finance & Logistics teams to improve performance and usability of a newly implemented predictive logistics software system

· Identify inefficiencies and implement data-driven improvements

  1. Data Integration (Production & Systems)

· Collaborate with IT to bridge gaps between systems

· Build or prototype solutions connecting:

Harvesting software (growing conditions + yield) with business systems (ERP, reporting, etc.)

· Focus on clean, usable data to drive decision-making on the farm

  1. Sales Forecasting vs Production

· Develop forecasting tools/models

· Align sales demand with production realities

· Improve planning accuracy and reduce waste

  1. AI Enablement for Leadership

· Help deploy practical AI tools across the company

Examples:

· AI agents for reporting / insights

· Workflow automation

· Focus on tools that get used—not ones that create noise without impact

Your Key Responsibilities

· Work cross-functionally with multiple departments

· Build, test, and iterate quickly

· Present findings and solutions to senior leadership

· Turn raw data into clear, actionable insights

· Push the organization forward in how it uses AI

Who You Are

· University student (3rd or 4th year preferred) in Computer Science, Engineering, Data Science, or a related field

· Comfortable working with messy, real-world data

· Experience or strong interest in:

· AI / machine learning

· Data analysis (Python, SQL, etc.)

· Automation tools / APIs

· Curious, proactive, and not afraid to challenge assumptions

· Able to communicate clearly with non-technical teams and stakeholders

· Experience with LLMs, agents, or prompt engineering is an asset

· Exposure to business operations, supply chain, or manufacturing is an asset

· Experience integrating multiple systems or APIs is an asset

Work Arrangement

This is a paid, on-site internship. The intern’s schedule is Monday to Thursday from 8AM to 4PM. Some projects may require travel between farm locations; access to reliable transportation is an asset.

Why This Internship Stands Out

· Direct exposure to executive decision-making

· See immediate impact of your work on real business operations

· Build experience at the intersection of AI + operations + agriculture

How to Apply

Send your resume and a short note explaining:

  1. Why this role interests you

  2. A project you’ve worked on (AI, data, or systems-related)

Pay: From $700.00 per week

Work Location: In person
