Co-op Student - Chemistry

About the Role

About Mérieux NutriSciences - North America

Full Description

Vancouver

Temporary

MERIEUX NUTRISCIENCES As a trusted partner, our Public Health mission is to make food systems safer, healthier and more sustainable. Throughout our global network in 27 countries, we offer to our customers a wide range of testing and innovative solutions dedicated to preventing health risks related to food, environment and consumer goods. External growth has been a key pillar of our history with more than 40 acquisitions completed in the last 10 years.

If you want to contribute to an inspiring long-term purpose, to be part of a fast growing company on a high-value market with significant build-up opportunities, and to grow in an entrepreneurial and warm environment, join us! YOUR DAY TO DAY LIFE We Are Looking For a Co-op Student - Chemistry In 4606 Canada Way, Burnaby, B.C. V5G 1K5 CAN. Your Mission Will Be To

  • Log in samples into the department computer
  • Prepare samples for extraction
  • Perform extraction from horse urine and blood samples for drug detection
  • Prepare supplies needed for extraction
  • Prepare solutions and reagents as required
  • Wash glassware as required
  • Dispose of completed urine and blood samples
  • Dispose of solvent waste into waste drum in storage room
  • Defrost sample storage freezers when required
  • Record temperature of freezers and refrigerator on a daily basis
  • Organize and maintains data files
  • Perform other miscellaneous duties as assigned

YOUR PROFILE

  • Obtaining the Security Screening Certificate and Briefing Form at the Protected B Reliability Status is MANDATORY
  • Dynamic, energetic, motivated, positive outlook, with a "can-do" attitude to be able to work in a fast paced environment
  • Good time-management, communication, multi-tasking and priority setting skills
  • Strong sense of urgency & the ability to adapt to a changing environment
  • Ability to work independently and as part of a team
  • Strong attention to detail & record keeping skills
  • Ability to accurately follow Standard operating Procedures (SOP’s) and instructions
  • Ability to learn quickly

Management

  • None

Work Environment

  • Students may be expected to perform repetitive somewhat physical work using a variety of machines and equipment as required and according to deadlines established by more experienced analysts & their supervisor
  • The job requires flexible working hours and overtime
  • May involve long periods of standing
  • Hazardous materials are handled using established safety procedures and appropriate equipment
  • Fast paced environment
