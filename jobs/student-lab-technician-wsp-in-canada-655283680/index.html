The Opportunity Your Impact Starts Here. As a Student Lab Technician , you will gain hands‑on laboratory experience while supporting environmental, materials, and infrastructure projects across the region. Working alongside experienced technicians and technologists, you will apply what you are learning in school to real‑world testing and analysis that helps build healthier, more resilient communities.

This role is ideal for students or recent graduates who are curious, detail‑oriented, and eager to grow their technical skills in a professional laboratory environment.

Your Impact In this role, you will:

  • Support routine laboratory testing by following established methods and quality procedures
  • Prepare, label, and process samples for chemical, physical, or geotechnical analysis
  • Operate standard laboratory equipment under supervision and assist with basic maintenance
  • Follow health, safety, and quality protocols to help ensure accurate and reliable results
  • Record observations and results clearly in logs, templates, and digital systems
  • Assist senior staff by organizing samples, maintaining clean work areas, and supporting day‑to‑day lab operations

The Skills That Set You Apart

  • Currently enrolled in a college diploma or certificate in environmental technology, chemistry, materials science, or a related program
  • Academic, co‑op, internship, or part‑time laboratory experience where attention to detail and following procedures were essential
  • Familiarity with basic lab equipment such as balances, ovens, sieves, and pH meters
  • Ability to safely lift and carry items weighing up to 50 lbs as part of routine laboratory work
  • A strong interest in hands‑on technical work, with a commitment to safety, quality, and teamwork
