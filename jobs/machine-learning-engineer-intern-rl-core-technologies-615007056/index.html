About RL Core Technologies

Full Description

**Company Description**

RL Core Technologies is a deep-tech company revolutionizing industrial automation with its flagship product, RLTune. RLTune uses advanced reinforcement learning to deliver real-time, autonomous optimization for complex industrial processes, leading to improved efficiency, lower costs, enhanced stability, and reduced environmental impact. Proven in live operations, such as water and wastewater facilities, the technology is versatile and adaptable across various industries, including energy storage, oil and gas, chemicals, food and beverage, and more. Partnering with leading engineering firms and system integrators, RL Core Technologies is shaping the future of industrial control to enable safer, cleaner, and more effective operations worldwide.

**Role Description**

We are seeking a full-time Machine Learning Engineer to join our team at our Edmonton, AB office. In this on-site role, the Machine Learning Engineer will design, develop, and deploy cutting-edge machine learning algorithms, with a special focus on continual reinforcement learning for industrial process optimization. Day-to-day responsibilities include developing custom algorithms, analyzing data, building production-grade systems, and collaborating with cross-functional teams to implement innovative solutions. The ideal candidate will play a key role in enhancing the performance, efficiency, and intelligence of RL Core Technologies' solutions.

**Qualifications**

- Strong foundation in Computer Science, including knowledge of Algorithms and programming principles - Expertise in deep learning and modern neural network modeling - Expertise in continual reinforcement learning - Experience in developing and applying advanced ML techniques - Problem-solving skills with a results-driven mindset - Master's degree or higher in Computer Science, Machine Learning, or a related field - Understanding of control systems or industrial processes is a plus
