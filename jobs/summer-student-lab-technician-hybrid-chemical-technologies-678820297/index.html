About the Role

Perform lab tests, log results, report findings

About Hybrid Chemical Technologies

Top-tier chemical services firm, hands-on training

Full Description

Hybrid is a top-tier chemical technology services practice based in Calgary. We provide specialized laboratory and technical solutions to innovative organizations around the world.

We are looking for a Summer Student Lab Technician to join our vibrant team composed of multi-disciplinary professionals over this summer months. As an organization, we demand the best from our employees while providing a fun and flexible work environment and a respect for work-life balance. We encourage initiative and creativity in getting the job done and are committed to the professional growth of our employees.

This role involves hands on laboratory work supporting projects in hydraulic fracturing, acidizing, cementing and coiled tubing operations. You will work within a team under the guidance and supervision of Technical Specialists and Operations Manager.

Responsibilities

  • Understand and comply with all of Hybrid’s policies and safety procedures
  • Follow standardized test procedures and equipment operation
  • Perform general lab tasks such as housekeeping, equipment calibration, and maintenance
  • Receive samples and log in accurately
  • Interface with clients, suppliers, and colleagues in a professional manner
  • Perform tests, write up results, interpret key findings and report them as required
  • Organize and maintain testing databases
  • Deliver and transport samples as directed

Requirements

  • Minimum: Currently enrolled in or completed BSc in Chemistry or Chemical Engineering, or Chemistry Laboratory Technology Diploma
  • Preferred: Enrolled in Bachelor’s degree in Chemistry or Chemical Engineering
  • Strong attention to detail
  • Comfortable working in a time-sensitive, project driven, team-based environment
  • Valid driver’s license with clean abstract
  • Excellent communication skills with an ability to work well within all levels of the organization

What Hybrid Offers

  • Full training provided within a supportive team environment
  • Competitive student remuneration

Hybrid is an equal opportunity employer.

Please submit your cover letter and resume to info@hybridchemtech.com by May 13th, 2026.

Only local candidates will be considered. We thank all interested applicants, however, only those under consideration will be contacted.
