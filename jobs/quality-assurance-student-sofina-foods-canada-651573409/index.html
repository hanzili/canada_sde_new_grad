About the Role

Support QA operations, audits, data entry, SOP updates

About Sofina Foods Canada

Canadian food manufacturer, QA-focused

Full Description

GENERAL DESCRIPTION OF ROLE: Reporting to the QA Manager, the Student -QA is primarily responsible for maintaining the facility’s food safety and quality program. This includes all necessary onsite and record verifications as well as facility audits, product sampling and associate training. It may also include testing requirements, pre-operational inspections, data entry and record keeping as well as other projects as deemed necessary by the QA Manager.

Key Accountabilities

  • Primarily responsible for the day-to-day functions of maintaining the food safety and quality system in the facility
  • Assist with HACCP system Reassessment and SOP updates under direct QA Manager /FSSS supervision.
  • Record keeping of monitoring and verification documents
  • Associate training (production, supervision, maintenance, engineering, etc)
  • Data entry of test results into electronic spreadsheets
  • Other duties as assigned.

Qualifications

  • Education: Enrolled in a college or university program related to Food Science.
  • Skills: Proficient in MS Word and MS Excel.
  • HACCP: Completed or currently enrolled in an HACCP course.
  • Communication: Excellent oral and written communication skills.
  • Attention to detail: Strong eye for detail.
