Full Description

New Jr. Makreting Coordinator Role Open! - 5 Month Contract

Agilus is recruiting for a Jr. Marketing Coordinator in the Real Estate Industry in a hybrid work environment in Toronto, Ontario. Are you a creative, hands-on marketer who loves seeing ideas come to life? This Junior Marketing Coordinator role is perfect for an early-career professional who wants real exposure to event marketing, digital campaigns, and creative execution. You’ll work on exciting placemaking initiatives, support large-scale events, and produce marketing assets that are seen across Toronto and beyond.

Total Rewards

  • $25.00 - $30.95 hourly
  • Real ownership of marketing assets and event campaigns
  • Hands-on experience with digital ads, design, and events
  • Exposure to a fast-paced, professional corporate environment
  • Creative freedom within established brand guidelines
  • Opportunity to build a diverse marketing portfolio
  • Collaboration with experienced marketing and communications leaders
  • Hybrid work model (mostly in-office, Fridays WFH)
  • 5 Month Contract opportunity with a possibility of extention based on performance.

Duties

  • Execute marketing plans for events, including digital and print assets
  • Design and resize creative assets for social, ads, email, and events
  • Support organic and paid social campaigns using Meta Ads Manager
  • Coordinate event marketing logistics, vendors, and print production
  • Assist with onsite events, including setup, teardown, and content capture
  • Draft short, engaging copy aligned to brand tone and voice
  • Manage marketing files, approvals, and asset organization
  • Support basic reporting, documentation, and administrative coordination

You Bring

  • 2+ years of experience in marketing, communications, or a related field
  • Hands-on experience with Adobe Creative Suite (Illustrator, InDesign, Photoshop, etc.)
  • Understanding of digital and social media marketing fundamentals
  • Strong organization and project coordination skills
  • Creative mindset with high attention to detail
  • Comfortable working in a corporate, people-focused environment
  • Ability to adapt to flexible hours for events when needed
  • Curiosity and eagerness to learn and grow
