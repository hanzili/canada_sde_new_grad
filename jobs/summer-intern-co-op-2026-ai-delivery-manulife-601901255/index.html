About the Role

Coordinate AI delivery, track initiatives, report progress

About Manulife

Global insurer pursuing AI-driven business transformation

Full Description

The AI Delivery Co-op will support the advancement of AI initiatives across Group Functions. This position offers hands-on experience in project coordination, processes improvement, and stakeholder engagement within a dynamic, innovation-driven environment. The successful candidate will collaborate with diverse teams—including data scientists, IT professionals, and business leaders—to ensure initiatives are delivered efficiently and aligned with strategic priorities. This role provides a unique opportunity to gain exposure to both the technical and operational dimensions of AI delivery, including strategy development, governance, and enablement practices.

Position Responsibilities:

  • Support AI Delivery:

  • Assist in coordinating and tracking AI and GenAI initiatives across Group Functions, ensuring accurate documentation, reporting, and timely updates

  • Assist in identifying gaps and opportunities for enablement, designing and documenting processes, templates, and standards that improve how we deliver AI initiatives across Group Functions, ensuring scalability and consistency
  • Collaborate with multi-functional teams (project leads, managers, data scientists, IT, Data Office, function partners) unblock deliverables to accelerate results where possible

  • Contribute to Group Functions AI Strategy and Roadmap Development:

  • Help gather insights and stakeholder inputs to refine the AI strategy roadmaps for different Group Functions

  • Prepare summaries and visualizations to communicate priorities effectively
  • Assist in monitoring progress of the AI roadmaps for different functions, including AI benefits/ value tracking

  • Reporting and Stakeholder Engagement Support

  • Create and maintain initiative trackers, dashboards, and reporting templates

  • Support preparation of status reports, quarterly reviews, and delivery health checks for leadership
  • Support AI initiatives intake and prioritization process

Required Qualifications:

  • Currently enrolled in a program in a quantitative field (e.g., Computer Science, Engineering, Mathematics) or Business, or a related discipline
  • Strong interest in AI, GenAI, digital transformation and their business applications
  • Familiarity ( experience or coursework) with project management methodologies (both waterfall and agile)
  • Proficiency in PowerPoint and Excel for creating presentations and reports
  • Strong communication and organizational skills
  • Comfortable working in a fast-paced environment with multiple stakeholders

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Experience or coursework in d ata analysis and dashboard building, preferably with familiarity with PowerBI
  • Notions or experience with workflow automation tools (e.g., Power Automate)
  • Familiarity with AI/ML concepts and emerging trends in Generative AI

When you join our team:

  • We’ll empower you to learn and grow the career you want.
  • We’ll recognize and support you in a flexible environment where well-being and inclusion are more than just words.
  • As part of our global team, we’ll support you in shaping the future you want to see.
