Full Description

Title: Data Analytics Intern (Co-op)

Location: Toronto, ON (Hybrid)

Duration: 4–8 months

Hours: 35/week

Compensation: Paid

About Havas CX Canada

Havas CX Canada is part of the global Havas network, focused on building meaningful digital experiences for leading brands. Our Toronto team works across strategy, design, technology, and analytics in a collaborative, fast-paced environment.

Role Overview

We are looking for a Data Analytics Intern (Co-op) to support our Strategy and Analytics team. This is a hands-on role where you will work on real client projects across reporting, data analysis, analytics implementation, and SEO.

What You Will Do

  • Support weekly and monthly reporting across digital platforms
  • Analyze data and support ad hoc client requests
  • Assist in building dashboards and data visualizations
  • Help QA web and app analytics implementations
  • Support tracking documentation and tagging requirements
  • Assist with SEO research and audits
  • Prepare presentation materials and summaries for clients

What You Bring

  • Strong Excel skills (PivotTables, VLOOKUP, SUMIFS)
  • Working knowledge of SQL
  • Experience using Python for data analysis (Pandas, data cleaning, basic scripting)
  • Strong attention to detail and organization
  • Clear communication skills
  • Ability to manage multiple tasks and deadlines

Nice to Have:

  • Exposure to Google Analytics or Adobe Analytics
  • Experience with Tableau, Power BI, or Looker Studio
  • Basic understanding of HTML/CSS/JavaScript

Additional Information

This role represents an active vacancy at Havas. You will be joining a collaborative, growth-oriented environment where ideas are encouraged and career development is supported. The base hourly range for this position is CAD $20 to $26, with final compensation determined based on factors such as work location, skills, experience, and background.

Havas uses artificial intelligence-enabled tools to support certain aspects of the recruitment process. All hiring decisions are made by our recruiting and hiring teams.
