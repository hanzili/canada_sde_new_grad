Job Description:

We’re looking for 3rd+ year students for our Workplace Services Coordinator Co-op position. In this role, you'll be responsible for retrieving and issuing lunch vouchers, assisting with space planning drawings in AutoCad and Revit, and coordinating interoffice moves. You'll also handle various facility requests and miscellaneous projects. This position offers the opportunity to work in a dynamic environment, collaborate with different departments, and leverage your organizational skills to ensure smooth operations.

Why Work With Us?

  • We value integrity, respect, and teamwork, encouraging a unique and inclusive culture.
  • Enjoy exclusive discounts at our stores, available to you and eligible family members.
  • Comprehensive training and development resources designed to help you learn, grow, and succeed.
  • Support for you and your family through our Associate and Family Assistance programs, offering helpful resources and services to enhance your well-being.
  • Discover innovative amenities at our eco-friendly corporate office, along with the flexibility and balance of our hybrid work model.

What You’ll Do:

  • Focus on construction-related project and space planning projects.
  • Support projects for new office builds and offsite locations.
  • Review drawings, understand stakeholder requirements, and assist with space planning in AutoCAD and Revit.
  • Opportunity to coordinate interoffice moves and support multiple projects simultaneously.
  • Work both onsite and offsite as needed, including support for facility requests and miscellaneous projects.

About You:

  • Proficient in AutoCAD and Revit.
  • Education and/or experience in Facilities Management – preferably a corporate environment.
  • Strong Computer skills (spreadsheets, word processing; e-mail), and Database experience an asset.
  • Organizational skills including prioritizing and organizing tasks; time management (i.e. completing projects on time; working with short time frames).

Posting Details:

  • Posting End Date: May 15, 2026
  • What to expect: Qualified applicants will be required to complete a 1-way video interview and assessment no later than May 19th, 2026. Successful candidates to proceed with a formal 1:1 interview with the hiring teams.
  • Work Term Duration: 4 month contract

If you’re ready to bring your energy and passion, we’d love to hear from you. Join us and be part of a place where every day is a chance to make a difference.
