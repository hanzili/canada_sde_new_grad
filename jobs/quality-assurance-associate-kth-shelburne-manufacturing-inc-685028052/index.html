About the Role

About KTH SHELBURNE MANUFACTURING INC

Automotive frame components leader in Shelburne.

Full Description

KTH Shelburne is now accepting applications for a Quality Assurance Associate. KTH Shelburne is a leader in automotive frame components. We have built a team environment where all of our associates contribute to our overall success. Successful candidates for this position must be team players and able to work in a fast paced manufacturing environment.

Quality Assurance Associate Job Duties:

  • Support the overall quality level of stamped and welded automotive assemblies
  • Root cause analysis
  • Attend line calls for issues related to the accuracy of parts and equipment
  • Daily reporting to Management on process of projects
  • Data trend analysis
  • Other duties as assigned

What We Look For:

  • One to two years’ experience working in a fast-paced manufacturing environment
  • Mechanical Engineering Technology Diploma or related discipline
  • Hand tool experience
  • Willing and able to support overtime and weekends as required
  • Exhibit the ability to work in a challenging environment
  • Willing and able to be a self-starter
  • Ability to work a two-shift rotation (days and afternoons)
  • Steel toe CSA approved boots that cover the ankle are required

The Perks:

  • Competitive wages
  • Group benefits – vision, medical and dental
  • Work boot and safety glasses allowance
  • Attendance incentives and annual performance bonus
