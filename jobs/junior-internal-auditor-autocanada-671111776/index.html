Full Description

Join the Ride! Position: Junior Internal Auditor

Department: Internal Audit

Reports to: VP, Internal Audit

About The Role We’re looking for a motivated Junior Internal Auditor to join our Internal Audit team. This entry-level position offers hands-on experience in internal controls, process reviews, and risk management.

Key Responsibilities

  • Support internal audits by participating in process walkthroughs, control testing, and fieldwork (including transaction reviews and compliance checks)
  • Prepare audit work papers, document findings, and assist with summary memos
  • Coordinate with control owners and external auditors on testing requirements
  • Identify and document audit issues with recommended improvements
  • Communicate findings and discuss remediation plans
  • Collaborate with business units across the organization and complete ad hoc projects as assigned

Required What You’ll Bring

  • Bachelor’s degree (or currently working towards one) in Business Administration, Accounting or a related field.
  • Strong attention to detail and ability to follow work programs accurately
  • Good verbal and written communication skills
  • Proficiency in Microsoft Office Suite
  • Ability to work effectively in a fast-paced, collaborative team environment
  • High commitment to ethics, quality, and confidentiality

Assets

  • 1–2 years of relevant experience (auditing, accounting, process mapping, business analysis, or quality control)
  • Knowledge of internal auditing principles, risk management, or control frameworks

Compensation Salary Range: $55,000 – $65,000 CAD per year (depending on experience and qualifications)

This role provides strong professional development opportunities, including training and exposure to a wide range of business processes.

The Perks.

  • Competitive Compensation and Benefits Package
  • Employee Vehicle Purchase & Service Plans
  • Employee and Family Assistance Programs
  • Company-wide appreciation events and contests throughout the calendar year
  • Professional development and the opportunity to grow your career
