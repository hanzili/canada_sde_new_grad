Full Description

Junior Production Controller – Come Join Our Team!

Location: Olds, AB | Shift: Full-Time | Monday–Thursday |6:00am – 4:30pm

We are looking for a Junior Production Controller to join our team and play a key role in supporting the production team with planning, scheduling, and monitoring manufacturing operations. This position helps to ensure materials, equipment, and resources are available to meet production targets, while maintaining accurate production records and assisting with inventory control, warehousing and work orders. This is an entry-level role ideal for individuals seeking to grow their career in production planning and manufacturing operations.

What’s In It for You?

  • Career Growth: Training and advancement opportunities.
  • Work-Life Balance: Monday to Thursday, have a long weekend, every weekend!
  • Positive Team Culture: Supportive, collaborative environment.
  • Dynamic, Fast-Paced Environment: No two days are the same.
  • Inclusivity: All are welcome with initiatives like Women@Terex.
  • Competitive Pay & Benefits: Great wages + up to 5% RRSP matching, 15% Matching Stock Purchase Plan.
  • Safety is our top priority: We ensure proper PPE, training, and procedures to keep our team protected.

What You’ll Do

  • Assist in creating and maintaining production schedules to meet customer orders and production targets.
  • Monitor daily production activities and report any deviations or delays to the Production Control Manager.
  • Coordinate with purchasing and inventory teams to ensure materials and components are available when needed.
  • Update and maintain production records, including work orders, inventory movements, and production reports.
  • Support the team in identifying and resolving production bottlenecks or inefficiencies.
  • Assist in analyzing production data to provide insights into process improvements.
  • Collaborate with cross-functional teams including manufacturing, quality, and logistics to ensure smooth operations.
  • Follow company safety procedures and quality standards in all activities.
  • Crosstrain and provide back-up for Warehouse, Inventory and Work Order personnel.
  • Perform all other duties as assigned and light duty tasks when assigned.

What We’re Looking For

  • Diploma or certificate in Manufacturing, Supply Chain, Production Management, or related field.
  • Basic understanding of manufacturing processes and inventory management.
  • Strong organizational and time management skills.
  • Proficient in Microsoft Office; experience with ERP systems is an asset.
  • Ability to communicate clearly with team members at all levels.
  • Detail-oriented, proactive, and willing to learn.

Why ProAll?
At ProAll, we value culture and inclusion and offer a supportive environment where you can grow. As part of Terex, you’ll enjoy opportunities to advance your career while making a real impact. Plus, Olds, AB is a great place to live and work!
