About Dear Born Baby & Kids - Toronto - Thornhill

Established baby gear retailer with 45+ years

Full Description

About Us:
With over 45 years in the Baby Business, at Dear Born Baby and Kids, we are passionate about helping parents prepare for their little ones with high-quality baby gear, furniture, and essentials. Our goal is to offer an exceptional shopping experience by providing a range of trusted products and expert advice to our customers If you love working with people, have a passion for helping parents, and want to be part of a growing team, we would love to hear from you! Join our friendly and supportive team, and help make every parent’s journey a little easier!

Position Overview:
We are looking for a friendly, enthusiastic, and customer-oriented Sales Associate to join our team. In this role, you will assist customers in selecting the best baby gear, furniture, and essentials for their needs, while delivering an excellent customer experience and maintaining a welcoming store environment.

Key Responsibilities:

  • Greet and assist customers with product selection and offer personalized recommendations.
  • Maintain knowledge of our full product line, including baby gear, furniture, and essential items.
  • Answer customer questions, provide product demonstrations, and explain product features and benefits.
  • Help customers find the perfect items for their baby’s needs and provide guidance on how to use them.
  • Process transactions accurately using our POS system.
  • Maintain store cleanliness, organization, and product displays.
  • Assist with inventory management, restocking, and product placement.
  • Work as part of a team to meet store goals and provide a great customer experience.
  • Stay updated on the latest baby products and trends to ensure expert-level advice.
  • Drive Sales through exceptional customer service, product demonstrations and selling the complete package offering relevant add on items.
  • Communicate Customer Feedback and Product inquiries to Management.

Qualifications:

  • Previous retail or customer service experience (baby or children’s products a plus).
  • Passion for babies and an interest in baby gear, furniture, and essentials.
  • Excellent communication skills and a friendly, approachable demeanor.
  • Strong problem-solving skills and the ability to work in a fast-paced environment.
  • Ability to work flexible hours, including weekends and holidays.

What We Offer:

  • Competitive hourly wage.
  • Employee discounts on all baby products.
  • Training and development opportunities.
  • A positive, supportive work environment.
  • Opportunities for career growth within the company.

Job Types: Full-time, Part-time

Pay: $18.00-$19.00 per hour

Benefits:

  • On-site parking
  • Store discount

Experience:

  • Retail sales: 2 years (required)
  • Customer Service: 2 years (required)

Work Location: In person
