Full Description

Digital Marketing & Social Media Intern (Edmonton)

The Digital Marketing & Social Media Intern plays a key role in building HonestDoor’s digital presence and brand awareness. This is an entry-level opportunity designed for someone eager to gain hands-on experience in content creation, social media management, and marketing execution within a fast-growing real estate tech company.

Working closely with the HonestDoor Team, this role supports the development of engaging content across platforms, helps execute campaigns, and contributes to storytelling that educates consumers and promotes HonestDoor’s innovative approach to real estate. This position offers exposure to both B2C and B2B marketing initiatives, with opportunities to grow skills in content strategy, analytics, and brand development.

Content Creation & Social Media

  • Create and publish engaging content across social platforms (Instagram, TikTok, Facebook, LinkedIn, Reddit)
  • Assist with short-form video creation, editing, and posting
  • Support the development and maintenance of a content calendar aligned with marketing priorities
  • Monitor social media channels, respond to comments/messages, and support community engagement
  • Stay up to date on trends and contribute creative ideas for content and campaigns
  • Support email marketing, blog content, and website updates

Content Production & Coordination

  • Support photo and video shoots, including planning, coordination, and editing
  • Edit images and video content using tools like Canva
  • Maintain organized digital asset libraries (images, videos, templates)
  • Ensure all content aligns with HonestDoor’s brand voice and visual standards
  • Proofread and review content for accuracy, clarity, and consistency
  • Ensure brand consistency across all platforms and materials
  • Assist in updating marketing templates

We’re looking for someone who is currently enrolled in or has recently completed a diploma or degree in Marketing, Communications, Digital Media, or a related field, with a strong interest in social media, digital marketing, and content creation. The ideal candidate is familiar with major platforms such as Instagram, TikTok, Facebook, Reddit and LinkedIn, and has basic experience using content creation tools like Canva, CapCut, or Adobe Suite. They bring strong written communication skills with a high attention to detail, along with a creative mindset and a willingness to test ideas and learn quickly. They should also be comfortable being on camera, as they may be featured in video content. Being organized, proactive, and able to manage multiple tasks in a fast-paced environment will be key to success in this role.

Pay: $15.00-$23.00 per hour

Benefits:

  • Flexible schedule

Work Location: In person
