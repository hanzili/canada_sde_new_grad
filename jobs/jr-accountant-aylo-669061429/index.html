Full Description

Established in 2004, we are a tech pioneer offering world-class adult entertainment and games on some of the internet's safest and most popular platforms. With the support of an international team of dynamic and collaborative innovators, we are on a mission to enable safe user experiences and empower our communities by celebrating diversity, inclusion, and expression — all while maintaining robust trust-and-safety protocols.

*A select number of positions require full-time in office attendance*

As an Jr. Accountant, you will be a key member in the accounting team. Overseeing various transactions processing such as bank reconciliations, interco entries, managing AR and collection cycles. You will be responsible for month-end and year-end close processes for multiple companies including financial reporting and analysis. Also, will have the opportunity to lead the transformation and improvement of business revenue generating online platform to achieve high accounting and taxation standards.

What you'll be doing:

  • Process bank entries and prepare bank reconciliations
  • Issue invoices based on agreement with customers
  • Manage Accounts Receivable & collections processes including related reporting and analysis
  • Assist with month-end and year-end close processes for multiple companies including accruals and financial reporting
  • Process journal entries and perform account reconciliations for various accounts
  • Assist with annual audits
  • Maintain various schedules including intercompany reports
  • Assist in the preparation of quarterly sales tax filings
  • Drive process improvement initiatives, ensuring process changes are appropriately implemented and documented
  • Support the accounting team and management with ad hoc projects and tasks as needed
  • Support the operations team by providing accounting treatment and tax insights

What you'll need to be successful:

Must Haves:

  • College degree in accounting
  • 2-4 years of experience in Accounting
  • Excellent communication and interpersonal skills
  • Good knowledge of Microsoft Excel
  • Strong analytical thinking and problem-solving skills
  • Ability to work in a dynamic and fast changing environment
  • Excellent time management, organizational and multitask skills
  • Ability to be proactive and lead the processes
