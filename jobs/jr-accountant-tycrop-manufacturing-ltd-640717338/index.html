The Junior Accountant will report to the Accounting Manager and support the Senior Accountant with day-to-day accounting operations. This role is responsible for maintaining accurate financial records, assisting with reconciliations, and supporting accounts payable and accounts receivable activities. The ideal candidate is detail-oriented, organized, and able to work in a fast-paced environment while ensuring financial transactions are processed accurately and in accordance with company policies.

Responsibilities

  • Reconcile bank accounts on a daily and monthly basis by reviewing and balancing financial transactions.
  • Apply customer payments accurately in the ERP system and maintain up-to-date accounts receivable records.
  • Prepare and reconcile entries related to bank and other financial transactions.
  • Assist Accounts Payable with processing invoices and support payment runs.
  • Reconcile financial transactions to ensure accurate and timely posting in accordance with company accounting policies.
  • Maintain, update, and organize financial documentation to support audit requirements and internal recordkeeping.
  • Prepare the weekly working capital report and key performance indicators (KPIs).
  • Assist with month-end and year-end closing activities as required.
  • Assist with ad hoc tasks and analysis as required.
  • Conduct additional tasks and duties as assigned by management or Senior Accountant

Education & Experience:

  • Bachelor’s degree in Accounting, Finance, or a related field.
  • 1 year of accounting or bookkeeping experience (internship experience considered an asset).
  • Basic knowledge of accounting principles and financial reporting.
  • Experience with ERP or accounting systems is preferred

Specific Skills:

  • Proficiency in Microsoft Excel and other Microsoft Office applications
  • Strong attention to detail and accuracy
  • Strong organizational, time management, and communication skills
  • Experience with financial reconciliations and reporting
  • Familiarity with ERP systems and financial reporting tools
  • Progress toward CPA designation or interest in pursuing professional accounting certification
  • Ability to prioritize
