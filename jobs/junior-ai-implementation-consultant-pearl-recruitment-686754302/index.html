Full Description

Job Title: Junior AI Implementation Consultant

Job Type: Full-time

Experience Level: Junior / Early Career

Location: Calgary, AB

Company Overview

Our client is a well-established Calgary-based consulting firm with a background in EPCM, advisory, and implementation services across the energy industry. The firm is known for delivering practical, high-quality solutions through a lean structure that supports close collaboration, flexibility, and strong client outcomes.

In response to growing client demand, the firm is expanding its service offering to include a dedicated AI Implementation Consulting practice. This group will help organizations assess, adopt, implement, and sustain artificial intelligence solutions in a responsible, scalable, and value-driven way.

Job Overview

The Junior AI Implementation Consultant will support AI consulting and implementation engagements through research, analysis, documentation, solution support, and client-facing project work.

This is an early-career role suited to someone with a strong interest in artificial intelligence, technology, data, business improvement, and consulting. The successful candidate will work under the guidance of senior consultants and project leads while building practical skills in AI use case development, process improvement, implementation support, and client delivery.

Key Responsibilities

  • Support AI implementation projects through research, analysis, documentation, and project coordination
  • Assist with identifying business problems, process gaps, and opportunities where AI solutions may add value
  • Gather, organize, and analyze client information to support recommendations and project deliverables
  • Support solution design activities, including process mapping, workflow documentation, and use case definition
  • Conduct research on AI tools, technologies, implementation methods, and industry trends
  • Assist with preparing reports, presentations, meeting materials, and client deliverables
  • Participate in client meetings, workshops, and internal project discussions
  • Document requirements, decisions, risks, action items, and project progress
  • Learn and apply consulting methods, AI implementation frameworks, and responsible AI practices
  • Contribute to internal templates, knowledge materials, and reusable project resources

Core Competencies

  • Interest in AI, technology adoption, and business improvement
  • Research and analysis skills
  • Clear written and verbal communication
  • Attention to detail
  • Curiosity and willingness to learn
  • Comfort working with new tools and concepts
  • Practical problem-solving ability
  • Project coordination and documentation skills
  • Ability to work in a consulting or client-service environment
  • Collaborative working style

Education and Experience

  • Bachelor’s degree or diploma in business, technology, engineering, computer science, data, or a related field
  • Exposure to data analysis, automation tools, AI platforms, or business process improvement is considered an asset
  • Experience preparing documentation, presentations, spreadsheets, or other business materials is considered an asset
  • Experience in an EPCM, engineering, energy, industrial, or consulting environment is considered an asset but is not mandatory

Keywords AI Implementation Consultant, Junior AI Consultant, Artificial Intelligence, AI Consulting, Business Process Improvement, Data Analysis, Technology Adoption, Process Mapping, Project Coordination, Responsible AI
