About Integrated Strategic Partners

Integrated Strategic Partners (ISP) brings together five industry leaders: zag (marketing and communications), ThinkHQ (research and polling), P3, Hark Strategic Insights (government relations) , and Hal Danchilla Consulting (government relations) to deliver a seamless, results-driven approach to shaping conversations, influencing decisions, and driving meaningful change. Learn more about ISP on our website.

Position Overview

Integrated Strategic Partners is seeking a motivated Junior Consultant to join our team. The ideal candidate is a strong writer and critical thinker with a keen interest in public policy, government, and strategic research. As a Junior Consultant, you will work closely with partners and senior consultants to support high-impact client work through research, analysis, and drafting. This role offers hands-on experience across a wide range of sectors, including developers, municipalities, non-profits, post-secondary institutions, defence and education clients, professional associations, and more. This is an excellent opportunity to gain practical experience in research, policy analysis, and strategic advisory work within a collaborative, fast-paced environment.
