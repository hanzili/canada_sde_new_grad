Full Description

About Integrated Strategic Partners

Integrated Strategic Partners (ISP) brings together five industry leaders: zag (marketing and communications), ThinkHQ (research and polling), P3, Hark Strategic Insights (government relations) , and Hal Danchilla Consulting (government relations) to deliver a seamless, results-driven approach to shaping conversations, influencing decisions, and driving meaningful change. Learn more about ISP on our website.

Position Overview

Integrated Strategic Partners is seeking a motivated Junior Consultant to join our team. The ideal candidate is a strong writer and critical thinker with a keen interest in public policy, government, and strategic research. As a Junior Consultant, you will work closely with partners and senior consultants to support high-impact client work through research, analysis, and drafting. This role offers hands-on experience across a wide range of sectors, including developers, municipalities, non-profits, post-secondary institutions, defence and education clients, professional associations, and more. This is an excellent opportunity to gain practical experience in research, policy analysis, and strategic advisory work within a collaborative, fast-paced environment.

Key Responsibilities

· Conduct in-depth research on policy, legislation, political developments, and industry trends.

· Draft and edit briefing notes, policy summaries, and strategic documents for internal and client use.

· Monitor legislative and regulatory activity to identify emerging issues and opportunities.

· Provide analytical and administrative support to partners and consultants.

· Assist in preparing presentations, proposals, and client communications.

· Conduct grant research and provide support to clients at the municipal, provincial and federal levels.

Preferred Qualifications and Experience

· Bachelors degree or higher

· Strong research, analytical, and writing skills

· High attention to detail and ability to synthesize complex information

· Interest in government, public affairs, and legislative or business processes

· Ability to thoughtfully use artificial intelligence (AI) tools to enhance productivity and research quality, where appropriate

What We Offer

· Paid, full-time employment with a competitive salary based on candidate qualifications and experience

· Eligibility for benefits following the initial probationary period

· A challenging and intellectually stimulating work environment

· Mentorship and hands-on experience working with senior professionals across government relations, research, and communications

· An opportunity to build practical skills and professional experience at the intersection of government and industry
