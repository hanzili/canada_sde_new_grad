About Nutemp Mechanical Systems Inc.

Full Description

Nutemp Mechanical Systems Ltd. is a multi-trade mechanical contractor that focuses on the industrial, commercial, and institutional markets. With over 40 years of experience, Nutemp has evolved into a vertically integrated, industry-leading mechanical organization in southern Ontario.

We are currently seeking a highly organized and detail-oriented Junior Project Managerto join our dynamic team. If you're passionate about working with a talented group of professionals and managing complex projects, we would love to hear from you!

YOUR Responsibilities

  • Support Project Managers in planning and executing projects by coordinating schedules, budgets, staffing, and resource allocation
  • Assist in developing and tracking project timelines, identifying risks, and helping implement mitigation strategies
  • Coordinate procurement activities including researching specifications, sourcing vendors, processing purchase requisitions, and tracking equipment lead times
  • Monitor project progress, maintain accurate cost tracking, and support budget control efforts
  • Manage and update project documentation, including logs, drawings, change orders, meeting minutes, and status reports.
  • Liaise with clients, vendors, consultants, and contractors to ensure clear communication and timely project delivery
  • Support contractor coordination, review performance, and assist with billing and invoice verification
  • Attend site meetings, provide progress updates, and help resolve routine project issues to maintain workflow efficiency
  • Assist in preparing closeout documentation and ensure all project deliverables are completed and submitted on time
  • Utilize internal systems and tools to track project milestones, documentation, and reporting requirements

YOUR Qualifications

  • Degree/Diploma in Mechanical Engineering, Project Management, or related field
  • 2+ years of experience in mechanical project coordination or a similar role
  • Strong understanding of mechanical systems and project specifications
  • Strong attention to detail and commitment to producing high-quality work.
  • Technically proficient in Microsoft Office and Adobe. Experience with Sage300 is considered an asset
  • Proven ability to manage multiple projects and work in a fast-paced environment
  • Exceptional communication and interpersonal skills

YOUR benefits

  • Competitive salary and health benefits package
  • Enjoy paid time off, including sick leave and vacation days
  • External training reimbursement
  • Deferred Profit-Sharing Plan
  • Group Retirement Saving Plan
