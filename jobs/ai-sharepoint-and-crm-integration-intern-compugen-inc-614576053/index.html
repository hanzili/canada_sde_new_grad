### **Position Overview:**

Successful adoption of the new Compugen Catalogue is largely dependent on adoption of it as a crucial Sales Enablement tool. We have identified that to do this successfully depends on two things: 1) seamless two-way link with CRM, and 2) AI enabling the Catalogue so sellers can dynamically and intelligently engage with it. Sales Ops (CRM) is already engaged in both of these. The AI, Sharepoint, and CRM Integration Intern will support initiatives to integrate Compugen Catalogue with our CRM (Microsoft Dynamix) leveraging AI capabilities, enhancing automation, user experience, and operational efficiency. Additionally, to streamline catalogue content administration and ensure content relevancy, convert catalogue static content into dynamic (CMS). - **Please note:** **This role will be full-time hours (37.5 hours per week), for a duration of 4 months (May 4 to August 21, 2026) with a rate of $22.00/hour.**

### **Key Responsibilities:**

- Assist in AI-enablement of CRM workflows using Microsoft Copilot and related technologies - Collaborate with the Service Portfolio Management and Sales Operation teams to design and implement integration strategies between CRM and Compugen Catalogue - Conduct data mapping and validation to ensure seamless connectivity between systems - Support testing and troubleshooting of integrated solutions, documenting issues and resolutions - Develop user guides and training materials for new AI-driven features - Participate in requirements gathering sessions with stakeholders to understand business needs - Research and recommend best practices for AI-driven CRM enhancements - Provide status updates and progress reports to the SPM team and project stakeholders

### **Skills & Qualifications:**

- Familiarity with CRM platforms (e.g., Dynamics 365 or similar). - Basic understanding of AI tools and Microsoft Copilot. - Strong analytical and problem-solving skills. - Excellent communication and documentation abilities. - Ability to work independently and in a team environment. - You must currently be a post-secondary student, enrolled in a Computer Science, Data Science, Business Technology, or related program - You must be comfortable working in a hybrid work model with a combination of remote plus onsite work
