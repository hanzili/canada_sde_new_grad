About GEMTEC Consulting Engineers and Scientists Limited

Full Description

Are you looking for the opportunity to make a difference and work in the fastest growing Region in Ontario. We are looking for a junior Environmental Engineer / Geoscientist to join our team in Clarington.

At GEMTEC, we offer something different. We’re an employee-owned, 400-person multidisciplinary consulting firm—not too big, not too small. We’re large enough to compete head-to-head with publicly traded engineering giants, but still small enough to maintain the culture and flexibility of a close-knit team.

Our size and hands-on management style let us move quickly, embrace new opportunities, and empower our people to shape their own careers—without getting lost in corporate bureaucracy.

You’re an ideal candidate if you have:

  • A demonstrated ability to work both independently and as part of a team in a fast paced, multidisciplinary environment
  • Ability to present technical information, both written and verbally, in a concise and professional manner
  • Adaptability to represent GEMTEC professionally with clients, developers, or contractors
  • Ownership mentality that treats each project as if it's building your own home

A typical day on the job might look like:

  • Supporting multidisciplinary infrastructure and greenfield development projects through application of project management, problem solving skills and knowledge of Ontario Regulations (O.Reg 153/04, O.Reg 406/19, etc.).
  • Contributing to proposals supporting GEMTEC pursuits.
  • Assisting in the completion of Phase One and Phase Two Environmental Site Assessments, including preparation of Records of Site Condition.
  • Assisting the team and participating in the preparation of excess soil management plans.
  • Completing field investigations pertaining to brownfield redevelopments, including development and implementation of sampling and analysis plans.
  • Completing, tabulation and interpretation of field and laboratory samples, preparation of borehole logs and report figures, data management and report preparation.
  • Preparing reports as necessary to meet client needs
  • Adhering to all company policies.
  • Performing other duties as required.

The good stuff you get:

  • Full time salaried position.
  • Fair pay plus extra: competitive salary with year-end profit-sharing bonus
  • Complete health & dental coverage including vision, paramedical services, and Teladoc
  • Retirement planning made easier with a 5% employer match on your contributions
  • Paid time off (vacation and sick days) plus Employee & Family Assistance Program when you need it
  • Office year-end closure (paid time off between Christmas and the New Year)

Your background and what you bring:

  • Bachelor’s degree in environmental engineering, environmental science or geoscience from an accredited institution.
  • Eligible for Professional (e.g., P.Eng., P.Geo.). in an environmental consulting environment.
  • Eligible for Qualified Person (QP) designation is an asset
  • Valid driver’s license

Salary Range: $54,000 – $62,000 CAD Annually
