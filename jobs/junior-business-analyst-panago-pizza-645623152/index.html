About the Role

Translate business needs into clear requirements

About Panago Pizza

Canadian pizza brand; growing tech-enabled operations

Full Description

WHO WE ARE?

For more than 33 years, Panago Pizza Inc. has built a reputation as one of Canada's premiere delivery and takeout pizza franchises. We are a Canadian-owned franchise business that invests in our relationships - with our customers, our franchisees, and our employees. We are committed to being market leaders in customer service, franchise engagement, and sustainability.

With more than 150 stores across the country, serving over 8 million pies per year, we believe in the power of remarkable customer service and are committed to delivering only the highest quality products to our customers.

Mission

You will report to the Director of Solutions Delivery and work closely with the Senior Business Analyst to support the planning, analysis, and delivery of technology solutions across Panago. You will help translate business needs into clear, actionable requirements and contribute to the design and implementation of solutions that improve operational efficiency and customer experience. This role provides an excellent foundation for growth in business analysis and software delivery within the Quick Service Restaurant (QSR) industry.

Impact

  • Work collaboratively with the Solutions Delivery team of IT and cross-functional Panago teams to document and clarify business needs.

  • Develop detailed business requirements and software product specifications that address customer and business objectives.

  • Research and analyze information, supporting and facilitating discussions among business and IT stakeholders to document agreements and findings.

  • Draw insights from analysis activities and produce high-quality documentation using appropriate techniques (e.g., BRD, Use Cases, UML diagrams, Business Case Analysis, Process Maps, etc.).

  • Provide clarification and support to project management, development, testing, operations, and support teams to ensure correct interpretation of requirements and successful solution delivery.

  • Assist in elaborating features and user stories for the development team.

  • Assist in triaging reported issues and documenting enhancement requests, documenting clear, actionable enhancement request.

  • Participate in business process analysis and re-engineering initiatives to identify opportunities for improvement.

  • Contribute to testing and implementation efforts to ensure acceptance criteria are met and solutions meet business needs. Support testing and implementation activities to ensure requirements are understood and acceptance criteria are met.

  • Develop understanding of business processes, systems and data to support analysis activities.

Competencies

  • Bachelor’s Degree in Information Systems Management, Computer Science, Business, Engineering, or a related field; or an equivalent combination of education and experience (experience in food or retail industries is an asset). A high GPA is mandatory.

  • Up to 2 years of experience in software development, implementation initiatives, or similar technology-related projects.

  • Strong analytical and problem-solving skills with high attention to detail. Ability to analyze information, identify patterns, and support problem-solving efforts.

  • Effective written and verbal communication skills; proficient in Microsoft tools and familiar with visualization or documentation tools (e.g., Visio or similar).

  • Strong written and verbal communication skills, with the ability to translate business needs into clear, actionable requirements and documentation.
  • Proficiency in Microsoft Office tools (e.g., Excel, Word, PowerPoint) and familiarity with diagramming tools (e.g., Visio, Lucidchart, or similar).

  • Ability to produce clear, structured, and well-organized documentation.

  • Ability to work collaboratively with other business and technical team members.

  • Motivated and proactive, with a demonstrated interest in continuous learning and professional growth.

  • Organized able to manage multiple tasks while maintaining quality and attention to detail.

  • Familiarity with Agile software development methodologies is an asset.

  • Progress toward IIBA ECBA certification or equivalent is an asset.

Technical Skills

  • Basic understanding of business and system requirements and exposure to documenting them using techniques such as user stories, use cases, or process models.
  • Foundational knowledge of the software development lifecycle (SDLC) and how requirements support delivery.
  • Familiarity with Agile methodologies (e.g., Scrum, Kanban) and exposure to backlog activities such as documenting or refining user stories.
  • Proficiency with Microsoft 365 tools (Word, Excel, PowerPoint, Visio, Teams) for documentation and communication.
  • Familiarity with collaboration and requirements tools (e.g., Jira, Confluence or similar).
  • Exposure to process modeling or visualization techniques (e.g., basic UML or BPMN concepts).
  • Comfortable working with data in spreadsheets; basic data analysis or querying is an asset.
  • Basic awareness of system concepts such as APIs, integrations, and databases is an asset.

Additional Details

  • This is a remote position with occasional in-person attendance required for team evenings and meetings at our Abbotsford Head Office and on-site store visits when needed.
  • Please provide academic transcript or academic grade report along with resume.

Why Join Panago?

Joining Panago means having the opportunity to make a meaningful national impact with one of Canada’s most recognized and trusted pizza brands. You’ll have the creative liberty to help shape how Canadians experience Panago, bringing bold ideas to life alongside a supportive, passionate, and innovative marketing team. We offer a competitive salary, comprehensive benefits, and opportunities for growth, on a team where your work can truly make an impact.

For a rewarding career, we also include:

  • Competitive salary and vacation benefits
  • Comprehensive dental and extended health benefits
  • Corporate support for paid community volunteering
  • Learning opportunities, including support for training, self-development, and well-being
  • Support for the work-life balance, including a flexible schedule
  • A personal pizza allowance to share the pizza love with your friends and family

Diversity, Equity, Inclusion & Belonging

At Panago, we passionately embrace diversity, equity, inclusion, and belonging as cornerstones of our culture and business practice. We believe that a variety of backgrounds, perspectives, and talents is not just a strength but a necessity for our growth and innovation. To us, fostering an environment where everyone feels valued and empowered is more than a commitment—it's a reflection of who we are. Joining our team means being part of a community that celebrates differences, champions equity, and thrives on inclusivity. Together, we create a space where everyone belongs and every voice matters.
