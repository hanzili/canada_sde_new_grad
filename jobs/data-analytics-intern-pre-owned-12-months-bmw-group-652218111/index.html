Full Description

A GOOD INTERNSHIP IS NEVER HANDS OFF. SHARE YOUR PASSION. We believe in creating an environment where our interns really can learn by doing and where they are given their own areas of responsibility right from the start of their time with us. That’s why our experts will treat you as part of the team from day one, encourage you to bring your own ideas to the table – and give you the opportunity to really show what you can do.

The Data Analytics Intern, Pre-Owned role at BMW Group Canada offers an exciting opportunity to be part of a dynamic team. You will help develop and steer advanced analytical capabilities by designing, analyzing, and implementing innovative data solutions, directly impacting critical aspects of the business. In addition, you will provide operational support within the Remarketing (wholesale) and Pre-Owned (retail) operations, offering a unique learning experience across the entire used car business. The tentative start date for this role will be May 2026.

Key Accountabilities Of The Function.

  • Build, analyze and maintain Tableau and Quick Sight dashboards and reports.
  • Work with MySQL to build and maintain key data sets to empower operational and exploratory analysis.
  • Conduct analytical tasks to improve processes, reporting systems and results.
  • Administrative support including month-end processes.
  • Ad-hoc data and reporting requests as required.

Qualifications.

  • Recent graduate in Business, Mathematics, Statistics, Computer Science or related quantitative field.
  • Minimum one year experience within a technical role utilizing data and reporting systems.

Additional skills: special skills / technical ability etc.

  • Experience with data visualization and discovery tools (Tableau and Quick Sight strongly preferred).
  • Fluency in MySQL and Microsoft Office, especially Excel and Access.
  • Analytical thinking, gathering, analyzing, and visualizing data.
  • Ability to communicate results of analysis in a clear and effective manner.
  • Strong teamwork and communication skills.
  • Ability to thrive in a fast-paced work environment.
  • Motivated to drive change with hands-on mentality.

Compensation. The compensation range for this role is $46,000 - $48,000, with variations based on factors like experience, education and skills. Experienced candidates are encouraged to apply.

Benefits of working at BMW Group Canada include:

  • An award-winning culture.
  • Cutting edge innovation and creativity.
  • Flexible working models. **
  • World-class office space.
  • Fresh & nutritious meals in our amazing fully staffed, and subsidized, onsite cafeteria.
  • Fully stocked coffee/tea bar.
  • We encourage interested candidates who may legally work in Canada to apply for the role. Our organization follows a hybrid work structure. To be eligible for this role, candidates are expected to work from our Head Office in Richmond Hill, Ontario three times in a week.

At the BMW Group, we place great importance on equal treatment and equal opportunities. Our recruiting decisions are based on the personality, experience, and skills of the applicants.
