Full Description

About This Intern Opportunity At Black & McDonald, you’re not just an Intern, you’re part of the family. Our Internship Program is centrally supported, fully leveraged paid summer employment offering you the chance to bridge theory to practice in a supportive and dynamic environment. Our goal is to provide you with engaging, meaningful, and impactful experience that will launch your career in the right direction.

Here are just a few benefits you can look forward to in your placement with us:

  • A customized, structured work plan and skill development library
  • A dedicated mentor to offer regular coaching, feedback, and support
  • An internship program manager to help you stay on track
  • Access to a large library of online training and e-learning through top-notch learning platforms
  • Collaboration with a vast network of interns across North America
  • Opportunity to network with subject matter experts and seasoned industry professionals
  • The chance to contribute to the growth and success of our diverse team of 6,000 employees

As a Quality Assurance intern, some of your roles and responsibilities may include:

  • Support the adoption, optimization, and training for Procore construction software, ensuring that project teams efficiently transition to new workflows and maximize the platform's capabilities
  • Support Procore change management - assist in planning, coordinating, and executing change initiatives, ensuring smooth transitions
  • Organizing project documentation
  • Tracking progress against milestones
  • Support user engagement activities
  • Research and evaluate Phase 2 opportunities for Procore
  • Other administrative duties as assigned

Requirements

  • A great attitude and enthusiasm for learning
  • Available May to August 2026
  • Strong academic record with a passion for your field of study
  • Previous work or volunteer experience highlighting your attitude, collaboration, and enthusiasm to continually grow

  • Completed or pursuing a degree or diploma in a related field, including but not limited to:

  • Business / commerce

  • Construction management
  • Nuclear/Power Generation
  • Project management
  • Supply chain

Expected compensation for this role is a starting pay range between $21-24/hour dependent on education level, skills and experience. Please be advised that a Criminal Background Check and/or Professional Reference Check will be required as part of our employment screening and selection process. Additionally, all applicants must be legally entitled to work in Canada, unless otherwise stated in the job posting.

Black & McDonald welcomes and encourages applications from persons with accessibility accommodation requirements. Accessibility accommodations are available upon request for candidates taking part in all aspects of the recruitment and selection process.
