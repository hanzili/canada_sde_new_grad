Full Description

Company Overview

LawLabs Inc. is a dynamic team born in London, Ontario, comprised of law and tech professionals dedicated to enhancing the property closing experience. Our mission is to streamline the property conveyancing process for lawyers and clerks, ensuring it is optimized for today's needs and future innovations. We pride ourselves on our values of trust, integrity, and delivering a world-class product.

About the Role

As our Junior Recruiter, you will be the first point of contact for the talent that will build the future of LawLabs. You’ll work closely with our Human Resources Coordinator and leadership team to identify, attract, and hire top-tier talent across product, development, sales, client services and legal operations for multiple provinces throughout Canada. This isn't just about filling seats, it's about finding the right candidates who share our vision for a modern legal landscape.

Duties & Responsibilities:

  • Full-Cycle Recruitment:  Support the end-to-end hiring process, from drafting job descriptions and screening resumes to conducting initial screenings and scheduling final interviews.
  • Sourcing Strategy:  Proactively find candidates through LinkedIn, Indeed, local tech communities, and London-based networks.
  • Candidate Experience:  Ensure every applicant, whether they get the job or not, has a professional, transparent, and outstanding experience with LawLabs.
  • Onboarding Support:  Assist in transitioning new hires from "signed offer" to "productive team member," ensuring they feel welcomed into our culture of integrity.

Qualifications:

  • Educational Background: Degree or Diploma in Human Resources or a related field (or equivalent practical experience).
  • Regulatory Knowledge & Compliance: A working knowledge of provincial and federal employment laws, specifically regarding hiring mandates.
  • Master of Prioritization: You can juggle multiple open roles and candidate pipelines without letting the "human element" slip through the cracks.
  • Communication Excellence: You possess the ability to write and speak confidently with high-level legal and tech professionals.
  • Adaptability: You are comfortable with ambiguity and ready to pivot as our team’s needs evolve.
  • Curiosity: You have a genuine interest in how technology is changing the legal landscape.

Nice-to-Haves:

  • Professional Experience:  1–2 years of experience in recruitment or talent acquisition is preferred, but not required.
  • Industry Knowledge:  Experience working within a Law Firm or a Tech (SaaS) environment is considered a significant asset.
  • Training and Development:  Experience creating training and development plans specific to multiple roles.

This position is for an existing vacancy.
