About the Role

Support UK market entry via research & analysis

About Allseating

Global office furniture maker; strong analytical exposure

Full Description

Position Title: International Business Analyst (Project-Based, Summer Internship)

Reports to: President, Product Quality Manager, and Regional Sales Director, UK

Work Schedule: 4 Month – May 2026 to August 2026 - Regular, Full-Time, Days

Position Summary: The International Business Analyst – UK Market is a project-based summer internship designed for MBA students or senior commerce undergraduates. Reporting to senior leaders including the President, Product Quality Manager, and Regional Sales Director UK, the role provides an opportunity to gain hands-on experience in international business development, operational planning, and strategic analysis. The analyst will support Allseating’s preparation for entry into the United Kingdom by assisting with documentation and analysis of internal processes, regulatory requirements, logistics, and financial considerations. A key deliverable of the project is a comprehensive business case outlining a path to profitability for UK operations.

Position Responsibilities:

  • Assist the Regional Sales Director UK with research and supporting documentation related to the UK office furniture market, including industry trends, customer segments, and competitive landscape, as required;
  • Collaborate with internal subject matter experts across Allseating to identify, document, and analyze internal business processes required to support UK operations both in Canada and in the UK;
  • Research and document UK regulatory, compliance, and trade requirements applicable to Allseating’s products and operations;
  • Evaluate freight, shipping, customs, and logistics considerations related to exporting products and distributing them within the UK;
  • Assist with the creation of Allseating’s legal and financial entity in the UK, collaborating with internal teams and external advisors as required;
  • Contribute to the development of a comprehensive business case for UK market entry, including cost estimates, operational requirements, and financial assumptions;
  • Support financial and operational analysis to help outline a clear path to profitability for UK operations;
  • Identify and document operational gaps, risks, and compliance considerations associated with UK market entry;
  • Prepare reports, summaries, and supporting materials for the Regional Sales Director UK and project stakeholders; and
  • Other duties as may be required or assigned.

Position Qualifications:

  • Currently enrolled in an MBA program or in the final year of a commerce/business undergraduate degree;
  • Strong analytical and problem-solving skills, with attention to detail; Ability to collaborate effectively with cross-functional teams and internal subject matter experts;
  • Excellent written and verbal communication skills; and
  • Proficiency in Microsoft Excel, PowerPoint, and process documentation tools.

Key Skills:

  • Business process documentation;
  • Financial and operational analysis;
  • Collaboration with subject matter experts; and
  • Business case development and reporting.

Work Environment:

  • Office setting; and
  • Prolonged sitting at a computer workstation
