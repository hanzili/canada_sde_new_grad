About Hatch

Full Description

Requisition ID: 99232

Job Category: Campus

Location: Vancouver, BC, Canada

Join a company that is passionately committed to the pursuit of a better world through positive change. With more than 70 years of business and technical expertise in mining, energy, and infrastructure, our 10,000 colleagues in 150 countries tirelessly work toward bettering the industries in which we operate. With practical solutions that are safe, innovative and sustainable, we think globally while acting locally.

Join our Project Delivery Group (PDG), a dynamic team of professionals passionately committed to world-class project delivery across the globe. With expertise in sustainable studies, asset building and operations, and industry-leading efficiencies, our best-in-class team leverages methodologies, governance and systems that are unparalleled in the engineering space. Are you looking for an opportunity to join a diverse group of professionals who are committed to remaining exceptional? We look forward to hearing from you.

Looking to get the most out of your upcoming internship?

Hatch is currently seeking a Geotechnical Engineering Student for a position in the Geotechnical department in our Vancouver, BC office. The Vancouver office specializes in infrastructure, renewable power, and mining projects. If you have strong communication and interpersonal skills, a positive attitude, and a desire to learn and innovate, we want to hear from you.

What Will an Internship at Hatch Offer You:

You will:

  • Be given opportunities to contribute to complex projects around the world in meaningful ways.
  • Work on problems and gain experience relevant to your field of study in an office environment.
  • Collaborate with diverse teams locally and globally.
  • Work in an exciting environment where you can apply your academic learning on a daily basis.
  • Benefit from formal and informal mentorship from some of the leading minds and authorities in the industry.
  • Participate in local Lunch and Learns, Hatch Charity Events, the triannual Student Showcase, and more to develop your skills.
  • Join in charitable, social, and sports activities with other Hatch employees.

We’re entrepreneurs with a technical soul. Our flat organizational structure creates an atmosphere of openness and participation. We build practical solutions that are safe, innovative, and sustainable. Our ultimate goal is to achieve no harm for our people, our clients, and our communities.

As the successful candidate, you will:

  • Work closely with our engineers, geoscientists, and designers.
  • Act as a technical resource on challenging projects.
  • Apply analytical, conceptual, research, and planning skills to support geotechnical investigations, geological characterization, and geotechnical design and analysis as well as construction.
  • Be exposed to modern design tools including 3D modeling, geotechnical analysis, and complex calculation software.

The student will be assigned to work closely with experienced professional engineers and geoscientists who will provide guidance and mentoring. There will be opportunities to gain experience in a variety of technical areas.

You bring to the role:

  • 3rd or 4th year student enrolled in a Geological Engineering or Earth Sciences program from an accredited University, after which graduates will meet the academic requirements for licensure as a Professional Engineer or Geoscientist.

  • Keen attitude towards learning and an openness to try many different projects across industries, as available during your work term.

  • Attention to detail and dedication to quality.
  • Flexibility for field and office work (role is office-based but site opportunities may arise).
  • Experience with RocScience, GeoStudio, PLAXIS, and/or Leapfrog an asset but not required.
  • Previous experience with management and processing of large data sets considered an asset.
  • Proactive approach to taking on independent tasks and seeking out work.

Competencies (knowledge, skill, attributes):

  • Good understanding of engineering and business fundamentals as they apply to engineering problems and projects.
  • Ability to learn quickly and to take on new challenges.
  • Collaborative team player with excellent interpersonal skills.
  • Excellent organizational skills and attention to detail.
  • Strong written and verbal communication skills.
  • Shows critical and creative thinking and problem solving and brings forward solutions for our clients' toughest challenges.
  • Adapts and embraces change.

Compensation:
