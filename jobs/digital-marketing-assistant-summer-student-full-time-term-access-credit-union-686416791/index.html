About Access Credit Union

Manitoba's Top Employer since 2014

Full Description

End Date: May 22, 2026

Grow your career with one of Manitoba's Top Employers since 2014.

At Access Credit Union, we support a professional and innovative work environment with a corporate culture that uniquely embraces empowerment, accountability, and trust. We recognize and value each employee's contributions to our success, which is why we offer competitive compensation, 100% company paid health benefits, employer-matched pension plan, continuing education, and career advancement opportunities within our credit union.

At Access Credit Union, our values are straightforward:

  • Do good
  • Be better
  • Own it
  • Move forward

The Marketing Assistant is responsible for providing administrative support to the Marketing & Communications Department.

Key Responsibilities and Accountabilities

  • Implements deliverables across a variety of channels outlined in tactical marketing plans.
  • Remain current on credit union products and services and participate in identifying methods to elevate members understanding through marketing and communications programs.
  • Coordinate, write, and post or distribute internal communications, acting as a liaison between corporate and branches for events, campaign collateral, and project communications.
  • Coordinate, write, and distribute external member/vendor communications, develop working relationships with media outlets and outside vendors.
  • Assist with the research, organization, and acquisition of various agreements, providing cost analysis comparisons and recommendations.
  • Coordinate and assist with web and digital property changes and updates, including website, social media, and intranet platforms as required.
  • Attend credit union sponsored events, including evening and weekends, as a representative and volunteer of the credit union, as required.
  • Research, analyze, and compile information relative to marketplace, competition, members, and industry trends and provide recommendation(s) for growth opportunities in support of organizational objectives.
  • Actively participate in meetings, planning and training initiatives, sharing information and ideas to build organizational achievement and success.
  • Maintain thorough knowledge of and adhere to credit union policies and procedures on risk management, confidentiality, security, and fraud prevention.
  • Maintain familiarity with the credit union’s history, vision statement, objectives, and its role within the community.
  • Ability to follow standardized policies and procedures.

This list is not inclusive and other tasks and/or responsibilities may be assigned by your supervisor.

Essential Qualifications

  • Post-secondary education and/or practical experience in marketing, graphic design, creative communications
  • Financial Services experience is beneficial
  • Knowledge of Financial Services products and services
  • Moderate understanding and working knowledge of brand building and brand management
  • Experience in graphic design using a variety of programs in Adobe’s Creative Suite including Photoshop, InDesign, Illustrator, Adobe Acrobat Programs is considered an asset
  • Experience and proficiency in Microsoft Office Suite
  • Excellent interpersonal and communication skills, both verbal and written
  • Ability to work in a fast-paced team environment
  • Attention to detail with high degree of accuracy
