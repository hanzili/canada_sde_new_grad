About Rolls Right Industries Ltd.

Small logistics firm with mentorship and growth

Full Description

We are currently seeking a motivated and detail-oriented Logistics Coordinator Intern to join our team. This is an excellent opportunity for a recent graduate or student looking to gain hands-on experience in logistics and supply chain operations within a fast-paced environment.

As a Logistics Coordinator Intern, you will support the team in day-to-day logistics activities, while learning how to manage shipments, coordinate with partners, and maintain accurate records.

Responsibilities:

  • Assist with coordinating daily logistics operations, including shipping and receiving
  • Support the team in tracking shipments and updating delivery status
  • Help maintain accurate inventory records and assist with basic audits
  • Communicate with internal teams and external partners (carriers, suppliers) as needed
  • Assist in preparing logistics reports and updating system data
  • Help identify and escalate any shipment or inventory discrepancies
  • Support process improvement initiatives and general administrative tasks

Requirements:

  • Recent graduate or currently pursuing a diploma/degree in Logistics, Supply Chain, Business, or a related field
  • Strong attention to detail and willingness to learn
  • Good organizational and time management skills
  • Basic knowledge of Microsoft Excel and Office tools
  • Strong communication skills and a team-oriented mindset
  • Ability to work in a fast-paced environment and adapt quickly

Nice to Have (Not Required):

  • Basic understanding of logistics or warehouse operations
  • Exposure to any logistics or inventory systems
  • Co-op or internship experience in a similar field

What We Offer:

  • Hands-on training and mentorship from an experienced team
  • Exposure to real-world logistics operations
  • Opportunity to transition into a full-time role based on performance and business needs

Rolls Right is an equal opportunity employer. We value diversity and encourage candidates from all backgrounds to apply. Only selected candidates will be contacted for further evaluation.
