About School Milk Foundation of NL

Full Description

SUCCESSFUL APPLICANT MUST BE A STUDENT (funded by Canada Summer Jobs)

The School Milk Foundation of Newfoundland and Labrador is an organization which exists to enhance the health and well-being of school aged children in this province through milk promotion. The goals of the School Milk Foundation of Newfoundland and Labrador are: 1) to increase milk consumption by school children; 2) to help school children develop positive, lifelong attitudes and habits for proper nutrition; 3) to make milk available at the lowest possible price to children Grades K-12.

The School Milk Foundation of NL is seeking a student (preferred post-secondary) to serve as a Marketing Assistant for an 8-week term. Position funded by Canada Summer Jobs.

Tasks and Responsibilities

The Marketing Specialist serves as an assistant to the Sales and Marketing Lead. This position will play a crucial role in supporting the marketing team in various tasks and activities to enhance the overall efficiency of the department. Position responsibilities will include assisting with the execution of marketing campaigns, assisting with the managing social media platforms, including developing a plan and content for the upcoming school year, and providing general administrative support.

Responsibilities: Social Media Management - Develop a plan for the 2026-27 school year, create engaging content. Content Creation - Assist in the creation of marketing material, including copywriting and content development. Assist in designing visually appealing materials. Administrative Support:Provide general administrative support to the marketing team, including scheduling meetings, maintaining files, and managing communication. Event Coordination: Assist in organizing and coordinating marketing events, trade shows, and promotional activities.
