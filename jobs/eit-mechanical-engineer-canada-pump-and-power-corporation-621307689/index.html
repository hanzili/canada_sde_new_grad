Full Description

Engineer in Training (EIT) - Industrial loT Integration

Industrial loT Control Platform Deployment Project

Mission:

The Engineer-in-Training (EIT) – Industrial IoT Integration is responsible for supporting the rapid deployment of a new industrial IoT control platform. Assigned to a dedicated 90-day project, this role focuses on integrating Modbus-based field devices with cloud infrastructure, supporting edge hardware and gateway development, and enabling remote monitoring and control functionality. The position involves hands-on field integration, system testing, troubleshooting, and commissioning activities, with an emphasis on delivering reliable, well-documented solutions in a compressed timeline. The EIT works closely with internal engineering and operations teams to ensure successful implementation and knowledge transfer.

Key Responsibilities:

  • Assist with the integration of Modbus RTU and TCP sensors and controllers into a cloud-based IoT platform
  • Support the design, prototyping, configuration, and testing of edge hardware and gateway solutions
  • Configure and validate remote monitoring capabilities, including pump start/stop control
  • Participate in system testing, troubleshooting, and commissioning activities in the field and lab
  • Support cloud connectivity using platforms such as AWS, Azure, or similar environments
  • Collect, analyze, and validate system data to ensure reliable performance and functionality
  • Assist in diagnosing and resolving communication, hardware, and control system issues
  • Document system architecture, configurations, test procedures, and commissioning results
  • Collaborate with internal stakeholders to understand operational requirements and integration constraints
  • Support continuous improvement of system reliability, performance, and deployment processes
