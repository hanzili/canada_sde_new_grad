Job Description:

DEPARTMENT OVERVIEW

Job Family: Campus

Role Type: Undergraduate

Work Location: Toronto, Ontario

Hours: 37.5 hours per week

TD Enterprise Finance is responsible for developing and delivering financial strategy, insights, and management support across TD Bank Group. This includes financial planning and analysis (FP&A), performance management, regulatory reporting, financial control, and the ongoing evolution of Finance systems and processes.

Our vision is to position Finance as a world-class, insight-driven function that enables better business outcomes through analytics, innovation, and technology transformation.

JOB DESCRIPTION

The CPA Associate Program, Finance & Accounting is a 30-month rotational program designed to develop future finance leaders pursuing the Chartered Professional Accountant (CPA) designation. Candidates will be supported in following a work plan that is in the process of being approved by CPA Ontario, to ensure you meet the Professional Work Experience requirement.

This program provides a strong foundation in financial reporting, accounting, and FP&A, while also building capabilities in business partnering, data analysis, and finance transformation.

We are seeking candidates who demonstrate intellectual curiosity, strong analytical thinking, a commitment to accuracy and quality, and a willingness to challenge the status quo.

What You’ll Do

Deliver Core Finance Value:

  • Support financial planning, forecasting, and performance management (FP&A)
  • Analyze financial and operational data to generate insights and identify trends
  • Apply structured problem-solving to support decision-making and business performance

Enable Decision Support:

  • Provide data-driven insights to business partners and leadership
  • Evaluate risks and opportunities
  • Communicate complex financial insights clearly

Contribute to Finance Evolution:

  • Improve processes and reporting efficiency
  • Support finance systems and data initiatives
  • Contribute to transformation efforts

WHAT YOU WILL EXPERIENCE

  • Rotational exposure across Finance functions
  • CPA designation support (exam coverage and training through the new CPA program)
  • Mentorship from Finance leaders
  • Performance feedback and development planning
  • Cohort networking opportunities

JOB REQUIREMENTS

We are seeking candidates who are committed to pursuing a career in Finance and Accounting and demonstrate both financial acumen combined with a strong orientation toward curiosity, innovation, and problem-solving.

Core Capabilities:

  • Strong analytical and problem-solving skills
  • Attention to detail and accuracy
  • Business acumen and judgment
  • Curiosity and continuous improvement mindset

Professional Skills:

  • Strong communication and storytelling skills
  • Proven ability to collaborate and influence stakeholders
  • Ability to manage ambiguity and solve problems creatively
  • Passion for continuous learning, innovation, and emerging technologies

Technical Skills:

  • Proficiency in Excel
  • Financial analysis experience
  • Exposure to Power BI or analytics tools is an asset

ADDITIONAL INFORMATION

Educational Requirements

  • You are graduating in Spring 2027 with an undergraduate degree
  • You must be registered with CPA Ontario and have met the CPA Ontario admission requirements including:
  • A recognized degree or equivalent
  • At least 120 credit hours (or equivalent)
  • Meet the minimum GPA across those 120 credit hours, as outlined on the CPA Ontario website
  • Successful completion of the Knowledge Assessment or CPA Qualifying Courses

Program Location and Start Date

This is a Toronto-based program starting in the Fall of 2027.
