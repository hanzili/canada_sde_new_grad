About Realigned Construction

Full Description

Overview
We are seeking a detail-oriented and proactive Junior Project Coordinator to join our management team. This entry-level role offers an excellent opportunity to develop your skills in project coordination, construction processes, and management software. The ideal candidate will support project teams by assisting with planning, documentation, and communication to ensure successful project execution. Candidates should have a strong interest in construction and a willingness to learn industry-specific tools and techniques.

Key Responsibilities
Project Coordination

  • Assist with project execution including document control, coordination, quality control, logistics, and project status tracking
  • Manage project documentation flow
  • Track sub trades, schedules, and crew time on site
  • Follow up with outside trades and consultants
  • Handle reporting submissions and drawings
  • Prepare document transmittals
  • Maintain registers for site instructions, change orders, and drawings
  • Provide project updates including approvals, design mockups, and installation details
  • Occasionally travel to project sites for measurements, photos, and site checks

Office Administration

  • Support Operations Manager with monthly reports, ordering supplies, freight shipping, and trade communications
  • Answer and direct phone calls
  • Maintain internal database of ongoing projects
  • Assist accounting with receivables and payables, invoicing, payment processing, and payroll time sheets
  • File receipts for bookkeeping

Logistics and Procurement

  • Source materials and request pricing from vendors
  • Arrange transportation and delivery schedules
  • Coordinate communication across departments including Sales, Admin, Painting, Shipping and Receiving, Assembly, Installation, and Electrical teams

Cross Functional Support

  • Work with Project Coordinator, Sales Manager, and Accountant
  • Liaise between departments to ensure a positive customer experience

Desired Skills and Qualifications

  • College, vocational, or technical training preferred, engineering, construction, or related field
  • Minimum 2 to 3 years experience in coordination or administration
  • Advanced MS Office and Excel skills
  • Strong organizational and communication skills
  • Fast typing skills
  • Blueprint reading and basic math skills required
  • Ability to multitask and work under pressure
  • Team oriented mindset
  • Self starter with strong motivation
  • Strong problem solving and decision making skills

Assets

  • Adobe Acrobat or Illustrator experience
  • AutoCAD knowledge
  • Accounting knowledge

Additional Requirements

  • English proficiency, written and oral
  • Flexible working hours depending on project schedules
  • Position involves measurement based tasks and mathematics
