About Delta Resources Limited

Full Description

Company Description

Delta Resources Limited is a Canadian mineral exploration and project development company focused on advancing its Eureka gold discovery and the broader Delta-1 Project in Ontario. The Delta-1 Project is located approximately 50 kilometres west of Thunder Bay. Backed by a strong technical team and an active exploration program, Delta offers early-career geologists the opportunity to gain hands-on experience in a dynamic, field-oriented environment, contributing to all stages of the mineral exploration process.

Role Description

This is a full-time Junior Geologist role based in Thunder Bay, Ontario. The Junior Geologist will report directly to the Senior Geologist and Vice President of Exploration and support ongoing exploration programs. This role is well suited to a recent graduate or early-career professional looking to develop practical skills in mineral exploration while working closely with experienced geologists in a field-focused setting.

Key Responsibilities

  • Assist with geological mapping, trenching, sampling, and core logging
  • Support drill programs, including drill supervision and core handling
  • Conduct QA/QC procedures for sampling and data collection
  • Compile, manage, and interpret geological data
  • Assist with report writing and technical documentation
  • Work safely and efficiently in both field and office environments
  • Travel to exploration sites as required
  • Supervise students and geotechnicians in the field

Qualifications

  • Must reside within 100km of Thunder Bay, ON
  • BSc in Geology, Geological Engineering, or Earth Sciences
  • Registered as a GIT or P.Geo. with the Professional Geoscientists of Ontario (PGO)
  • At least 2 years of mineral exploration experience (field school, internships, and other relevant experience considered)
  • Strong interest in exploration for orogenic gold deposits in Archean greenstone belts; previous experience in gold exploration is a plus
  • Familiarity with mapping, core logging and sampling techniques
  • Proficiency in Microsoft Office; experience with QGIS & Leapfrog is an asset
  • Valid driver’s license
  • Ability to work outdoors in remote settings and variable weather conditions

Personal Attributes

  • Strong work ethic and attention to detail
  • Willingness to learn and take initiative
  • Good communication and teamwork skills
  • Physically capable of conducting fieldwork

What Delta Offers

  • Competitive entry-level compensation
  • Hands-on field experience with active exploration projects
  • Mentorship from experienced geologists
  • Opportunity for career growth within the company
  • Work from home and in the field as required ( based in Thunder Bay)
