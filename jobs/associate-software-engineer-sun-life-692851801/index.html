About Sun Life

Full Description

You are as unique as your background, experience and point of view. Here, you’ll be encouraged, empowered and challenged to be your best self. You'll work with dynamic colleagues - experts in their fields - who are eager to share their knowledge with you. Your leaders will inspire and help you reach your potential and soar to new heights. Every day, you'll have new and exciting opportunities to make life brighter for our Clients - who are at the heart of everything we do. Job Description:

Associate Software Engineer – Launch Your Engineering Career at Sun Life

Your Career Starts Here

You're ready to make an impact. At Sun Life, you won't just code—you'll build solutions that improve lives for millions worldwide. If you're a recent graduate or early-career professional with passion and ambition, we're ready to invest in your success.

Join us and discover why thousands of engineers choose Sun Life to accelerate their careers.

What You'll Do

  • Design & Develop enterprise software that powers real business impact
  • Solve Complex Problems working alongside experienced engineers and innovative teams
  • Build & Automate with cutting-edge tools in a true Agile environment
  • Support & Optimize production systems while mastering enterprise-scale technology
  • Collaborate Across Teams with product owners, engineers, and business leaders
  • Own Your Growth through diverse, challenging projects and dedicated mentorship
  • Participate in On-Call Rotation for production systems (with support and training)

Who Are We Looking For?

Education and Experience

  • Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science, Engineering, or related discipline (or equivalent experience)
  • 0–2 years of relevant internship or co-op experience; or, 3–5 years of relevant experience in software development or application support

Technical Skills

  • Foundational knowledge of programming languages (e.g., Java, Python, C#, or similar)
  • Basic understanding of SQL and relational databases
  • Exposure to scripting, automation, or API development through coursework or projects (e.g. PowerShell, shell scripting)
  • Familiarity with software development lifecycle concepts (Agile, DevOps)
  • Experience working with APIs, integrations, and enterprise systems is an asset
  • Familiarity with CI/CD pipelines, cloud platforms, or modern DevOps practices is an asset

Professional Skills

  • Strong problem-solving and analytical thinking
  • Willingness to learn and adapt to new technologies and tools
  • Effective communication and collaboration skills
  • Ability to work in a team-oriented environment

What’s In It For You?

  • Accelerated Learning – Work on enterprise-scale platforms with mentorship from senior engineers.
  • Real-World Experience – Build solutions that impact millions of clients globally
  • Clear Career Path – Grow skills and advance in a company committed to your development.
  • Inclusive Culture – Bring your authentic self and thrive in a supportive environment
  • Continuous Development – Access to learning opportunities and cutting-edge technology.
  • Flexibility & Balance – Work in an environment designed for your success

Nice to Have

  • Exposure to financial systems or enterprise applications
  • Knowledge of cloud technologies or modern data platforms
  • Experience with automation frameworks or workflow tools
  • Familiarity with IT service management processes (incident, change, problem)
  • Experience working in an onsite–offshore delivery model

Unique Requirements:

As a condition of the role, the successful candidate must obtain a Government of Canada Reliability Status security clearance through Sun Life.
