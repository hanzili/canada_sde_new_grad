Investment Banking & M&A Advisory Internship – Toronto (Summer 2026)

Location: Toronto, ON

Program Length: 4 Months (May–August)

Application Deadline: March 15, 2026

Overview

We are seeking a highly motivated Investment Banking Analyst Intern to support our advisory team. This role provides hands-on exposure to live transactions, financial modeling, valuation analysis, and client interaction. The intern will work closely with senior bankers and other team members on active deals and strategic advisory assignments.

This position is ideal for candidates pursuing careers in investment banking, private equity, corporate development, or strategic finance.

About The Fairing Group

The Fairing Group is an independent investment bank focused on mergers and acquisitions (M&A) advisory for North American middle-market corporations. Based in Toronto, the firm provides buy-side and sell-side M&A advisory and execution services to independent businesses and private equity firms (both fund and platform levels), helping structure and execute transactions that unlock value.

Collectively, our team has advised on mergers, acquisitions, divestitures, and capital raises exceeding $400 million across a range of industries.

As a growing firm, we offer the opportunity to contribute meaningfully while experiencing the entrepreneurial culture of an independent advisor.

Program Overview

This internship is designed for students ideally in the final two (2) years of their undergraduate studies. The intern will participate in an initial orientation and training program before joining senior bankers on active and prospective advisory assignments. The objective is to provide meaningful exposure to live deals and client initiatives while building a strong foundation in financial analysis and transaction execution.

The intern will support the deal team on both buy-side and sell-side mandates, as well as other advisory assignments, through responsibilities such as compiling industry research, developing presentations, conducting target screening and identification, and performing financial modeling.

While expected to bring a foundational level of technical proficiency, the intern will receive training in research, presentation design, market mapping, and financial modeling. The intern will be paired with senior bankers who will act as mentors, providing direct feedback and guidance.

As an industry-agnostic investment bank, the intern will gain exposure to a wide range of industries.

What You’ll Do

  • Research and Analysis

The intern will conduct industry and company research to identify market trends and generate insights for client pitches and deal execution. This may include reviewing financial statements and regulatory filings, compiling comparable companies and precedent transaction analyses, and monitoring news and sector developments.

  • Financial Modelling

The intern will build and maintain detailed financial models and supporting workbooks to assist with internal analyses, transaction recommendations, and valuations of target companies.

  • Client Materials

The intern will prepare pitch books, management presentations, and other marketing materials for clients and prospective clients.

Candidate Profile

We seek a highly motivated student who demonstrate strong analytical ability, teamwork, and a genuine interest in finance with the following key qualifications:

  • Education | Enrolled in an undergraduate program (business, finance, economics, engineering or a related discipline) with an expected graduation date in 2027 or 2028.

  • Technical Skills | Strong financial and quantitative aptitude, including proficiency with Microsoft Excel, PowerPoint, and Word. Prior experience with financial modelling or valuation through coursework, competitions, or prior internships is an asset.

  • Communication | Excellent written and verbal communication skills and the ability to clearly articulate complex concepts to colleagues and clients.

  • Work Ethic and Adaptability | Ability to manage multiple projects and deadlines, thrive in a fast‑paced environment and remain attentive to details.

  • Teamwork and Integrity | Strong collaborative skills, professionalism, and commitment to ethical behaviour.

  • Eligibility | Must be legally eligible to work in Canada and fluent in English.

Compensation

The Fairing Group offers a competitive salary commensurate with market rates for mid-market investment banking internships in Toronto. In addition, we offer:

  • performance bonus, subject to individual and firm performance.
  • the possibility of a return offer for a full‑time analyst role upon successful completion of the internship.

How to Apply

Applications will be accepted until the deadline indicated above. Please submit the following documents via LinkedIn:

  1. Resume (1 page maximum) summarizing education, work experience, and extracurricular involvement.
  2. Unofficial Transcript showing all university coursework to date.
  3. Work Samples (optional) outlining your interest in investment banking, relevant experience, and career goals.

Please note, short‑listed candidates will be contacted for interviews, which may include a combination of technical questions, behavioural interviews, and a case study. We encourage candidates to apply early as roles may close before the stated deadline.

Recruitment and Communication Disclaimer

All official correspondence regarding this internship will be conducted exclusively through LinkedIn. The Fairing Group does not manage or monitor job postings on third-party websites or platforms. Any listings appearing outside of LinkedIn are unofficial and are not authorized or administered by The Fairing Group.

Please note, only candidates selected to proceed in the interview process will be contacted directly through LinkedIn. Due to the anticipated volume of applications, we are unable to respond individually to all applicants.

We appreciate the time and effort each candidate invests in their application and their interest in The Fairing Group.
