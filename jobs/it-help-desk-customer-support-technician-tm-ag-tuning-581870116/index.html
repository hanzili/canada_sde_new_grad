About the Role

Provide first-line support via tickets and remote tools

About TM Ag Tuning

Canadian tractor tuning company with growing support team

Full Description

TM AG Tuning is one of North America’s leading tractor, construction, forestry, and automotive tuning companies. With nearly 400 dealers, and a rapidly growing customer base, we’re expanding our support team to keep delivering fast, reliable, and friendly service.

We’re looking for a Help Desk & Customer Support Technician who can assist customers through our ticketing system and remote-access tools like TeamViewer. If you enjoy solving problems, communicating clearly, and working in a fast-paced environment, this role is for you.

Key Responsibilities

  • Provide first-level customer support via ticketing system, email, and phone.
  • Use TeamViewer to remotely assist customers and dealers with software installations.
  • Troubleshoot basic computer, tuner, and connectivity issues.
  • Document all work clearly inside the help desk system.
  • Guide customers through the tuning process and escalate technical cases when needed.
  • Maintain professional, friendly communication that reflects TM AG Tuning’s brand.

Requirements

  • Basic IT knowledge and troubleshooting skills.
  • Experience with TeamViewer or similar remote-desktop tools.
  • Experience with a help desk ticketing system (Freshdesk is an asset).
  • Strong communication skills and ability to explain steps clearly.
  • Reliable internet connection and a quiet home workspace.
  • Ability to learn quickly and follow internal processes.

Preferred (Not Required)

  • Knowledge of diesel engines, tractors, construction equipment, or diagnostics.
  • Experience with tuning tools, cables, or automotive software.

Work Environment

  • Training is completed on-site in Redcliff, Alberta.
  • After training, the position becomes fully remote.
  • Support hours are flexible but may include some evenings or weekends depending on demand.
  • Competitive pay based on experience

Benefits

  • Health Spending Account
  • Computer and monitors supplied
  • Paid time off
  • Work from home after training

Why Join TM AG Tuning?

  • Work with a rapidly growing, family-run Canadian company.
  • Remote work after training.
  • Supportive, knowledgeable team.
  • Opportunities to grow into advanced technical roles.
  • Be part of the leading name in tuning equipment “bit by bit.”
