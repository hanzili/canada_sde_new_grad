Full Description

Job Title: Supply Chain Coordinator Co-op

Location: Cambridge, ON | On-site

Job Type: Co-op

Compensation: $21 - $27

Get to Know Us:
Eclipse Automation delivers cutting-edge custom automated manufacturing solutions across multiple industries. We combine advanced automation expertise with digital innovation to create smart, efficient, and sustainable manufacturing systems. Our global network includes facilities in Canada, the United States and Hungary ensuring regional expertise and global strength.

The Position:
A Supply Chain Coordinator at Eclipse Automation is accountable to facilitate the purchase, scheduling and delivery of materials on schedule and on cost, while collaborating cross-functionally with a variety of other Eclipse employees. Within this fast-paced environment, supporting other team members in daily dashboard deliverables along with facilitating the internal processes are key to success.

What You’ll Be Doing:

  • Facilitate the release of direct purchases of materials utilizing MRP functionality
  • Work with the project management and manufacturing teams to ensure production requirements are maintained while limiting on hand inventories
  • Ensure continuity of supply while escalating constraints as appropriate
  • Develop and maintain effective business relationships with all supplier partners
  • Implement cost reduction strategies and maintain cost baselines
  • Management and implementation of all engineering change requests
  • Manage reverse logistics for failed, suspect, or non-required materials
  • Maintain all dashboard metrics daily
  • Develop a good understanding of the products, the supply base and the associated technologies within your commodity
  • Keep abreast of current and emerging technologies as well as their business implications
  • Ability to travel globally, not to exceed 20% of time

What We’re Looking For:

  • Post-secondary diploma or degree in a related discipline considered an asset (materials management, supply chain management or business)
  • Industry Certifications considered an asset (CSCMP, CPIM, CSCP, CCLP, etc.)
  • Minimal supply chain management experience in a manufacturing environment required
  • Excellent negotiation skills a must
  • MRP/ERP experience an asset (SAP, Oracle, etc.)
  • Intermediate level skills required in MS Office (Word, Excel, PowerPoint).
  • A strong team-player with experience working in cross-functional teams is preferred
  • Strong communication and interpersonal skills
  • Good problem analysis and resolution skills
  • Committed to continuous improvement within the supply chain
  • The ability to multitask and to prioritize those tasks
  • Students should be enrolled in a post-secondary academic program requiring a co-op/work term.

Why Join Us:
Here at Eclipse Automation, creation and innovation are at our core, and our culture reflects that. Fast-paced and continuously fostering growth, we encourage the entrepreneurial spirit that our teams embody, and leverage each of their strengths to do better for our people, our clients, and our communities. Our teams are made up of innovators, thinkers, and doers, with the know-how and expertise to exceed expectations, and the relentless spirit to create and reinvent. Our people are what enable us to live up to our name - to eclipse all others, inspiring and creating outcomes that change the world.
