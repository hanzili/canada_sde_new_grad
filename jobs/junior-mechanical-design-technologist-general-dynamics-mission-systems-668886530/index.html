About the Role

Create mechanical designs and drawings for rugged systems

About General Dynamics Mission Systems

Defense contractor focused on mission-critical systems

Full Description

Company Description

Build bold. Deliver impact. Win together.

At General Dynamics Mission Systems–Canada, we’re not just engineering technology — we’re shaping the future of defence and security. Our teams design and deliver advanced, mission-critical solutions that enhance national security, strengthen communities, and protect the people we serve.

If you’re looking for meaningful work that contributes to a safer, more secure world, you’ll find purpose here. Every day, your skills will directly impact programs that matter.

Job Description

  • Create mechanical designs and drawings mechanical systems for use in rugged environments using Computer Aided Design (CAD) software.
  • Support the full design life cycle from requirements definition, concept through to successful production ramp-up, including support for prototype procurement, assembly, design verification and qualification testing, and on-going manufacturing support.
  • Review part and assembly drawings and parts lists in support of the release of design activities.
  • Work with other mechanical designers and engineers, system engineers and human factor engineers in the development of installation designs of military vehicles.
  • Work closely with other design professionals, systems engineers, Supply chain management personnel, and manufacturing engineers to ensure a smooth flow of information throughout a project.
  • Perform 3D scanning and process data in support of vehicle platforms.
  • Perform vehicle cable routing per Company Standards
  • Responsible for the successful completion of project goals according to General Dynamics Mission Systems-Canada (GDMS-C) standard processes and procedures.
  • Support Program management in the creation of reports for technical reviews and task process updates.
  • Travel to Department of National Defence (DND) locations in Canada to review vehicles for installation design, AND to install the final design for system qualification and verification.

Qualifications

Education and Experience

  • Mechanical Engineering Technology Diploma
  • 2 to 3 years of related experience.

Familiarity with the following areas:

  • Design for vibration and shock environments.
  • Design for harsh climatic environments, corrosive environments.
  • Design of sheet metal, machined and welded parts.
  • Familiarity with Siemens NX or other comparable computer aided design (CAD) software.
  • Familiarity to Commercial or Military specifications.
  • Familiarity with ASME drafting standards.
  • Experience working with or familiarity with AI/ML models is preferred

It is a requirement that General Dynamics Mission Systems-Canada be registered with the Canadian Controlled Goods program and that all of its workforce be security assessed. Successful applicants must meet all applicable security requirements, including but not limited to the ability to obtain and maintain a Canadian government security clearance. Applicants may be required to meet additional security requirements in order to gain access to technical data, classified areas or information that is subject to international regulations. You must be eligible to work in Canada.
