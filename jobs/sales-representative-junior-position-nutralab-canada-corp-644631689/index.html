About NutraLab Canada Corp.

Full Description

Position: Sales Representative

NutraLab Canada Corporation is one of Canada’s premier contract manufacturers of natural health supplements. We are seeking a motivated sales professional to help drive new business and deliver outstanding service to our private label clients.

Key Responsibilities:

  • Develop and manage sales accounts for natural health supplement private labelling and contract manufacturing
  • Generate new business and cultivate long-term client relationships
  • Provide responsive support to clients throughout the manufacturing process
  • Collaborate with internal teams to ensure timely and efficient project execution
  • Monitor and report on sales activities and market trends

Qualifications:

  • Minimum 1 year of B2B sales experience
  • Prior experience in the health supplement or nutraceutical industry is a strong asset
  • Strong interpersonal and communication skills
  • Proven track record in business development, sales, and customer service
  • Knowledge of contract manufacturing is a plus
  • Highly organized, self-motivated, and results-oriented

What We Offer:

  • Competitive salary (negotiable based on experience) and sales commission package
  • Comprehensive company health and dental package
  • Opportunity to work in a growing and innovative industry

Why Join NutraLab:

  • Be part of a trusted Canadian contract manufacturer with over 20 years of industry leadership
  • Capitalize on the rising demand for local, high-quality nutraceutical production
  • Thrive in a collaborative, fast-paced, and growth-focused work environment

How to Apply:

If you're ready to take your career to the next level and contribute to a vibrant team, send your resume to tracy@nutralabcorp.com.

Join us at NutraLab and be a part of a company that values innovation, quality, and customer satisfaction!
