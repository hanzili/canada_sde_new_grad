About the Role

Create TikTok/Instagram content; test trends; collaborate.

About Bprotect Inc.

Founder-led, early-stage brand; hands-on growth.

Full Description

Are you drawn to themes around nightlife, partying responsibly, dating culture, and the social pulse of life in your 20s?

We’re preparing to launch a new consumer product and are looking for a junior or emerging social content creator to help build our presence on TikTok and Instagram.

This role is ideal for a college/university student or recent graduate who already creates content on their own social channels and wants hands-on experience building a brand from the ground up.

This is not an agency role and not a senior marketing position.

What you’ll be doing:

• Creating short-form video content for TikTok and Instagram

• Filming raw, authentic, user-generated style content

• Staying on theme around confidence, social culture, and personal awareness

• Keeping up with trends and testing new content ideas

• Collaborating directly with the founder

What we’re looking for:

• Gen-Z or Millennial creator

• Student or recent graduate preferred

• Comfortable on camera

• Familiar with TikTok & Instagram trends

• Interested in building a portfolio and real-world experience

Compensation:

Flat monthly fee (part-time, flexible hours)

How to apply:

Send a short note introducing yourself and links to your TikTok & Instagram (personal or portfolio accounts are fine).

Shortlisted candidates will receive full details during the interview process.
