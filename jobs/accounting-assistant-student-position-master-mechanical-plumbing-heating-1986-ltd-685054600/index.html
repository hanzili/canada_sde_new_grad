Full Description

Company Description

Master Mechanical Plumbing & Heating (1986) Ltd. is a well-established company based in Calgary, Alberta, Canada. Since its founding, the company has built a reputation for reliable and professional service. Specializing in a range of plumbing and heating services, Master Mechanical has been committed to quality craftsmanship and client satisfaction. With decades of expertise, the organization has consistently delivered top-notch solutions for residential and commercial projects.

Position Overview

We are seeking a motivated post-secondary student pursuing studies in accounting, business, or a related field to join our team for a summer work placement. This role is designed to provide hands-on experience in day-to-day accounting processes, financial administration, and business operations, while supporting the student’s academic and career development.

Key Responsibilities

·        Assist with data entry and organization of financial records

·        Support accounts payable and receivable processes

·        Help with expense tracking and basic reconciliations

·        Participate in maintaining and improving internal systems (Excel-based workflows, tracking tools, etc.)

·        Assist with general administrative and project-based tasks

·        Contribute to process improvements and documentation

Learning Objectives

During this placement, the student will:

·        Gain practical experience applying accounting concepts in a real-world business setting

·        Develop proficiency in tools such as Excel and accounting systems

·        Build an understanding of financial workflows and internal controls

·        Strengthen organizational, analytical, and problem-solving skills

·        Receive mentorship and feedback to support career development in accounting/business

Supervision & Mentorship

The student will work closely with experienced team members and receive:

·        Ongoing guidance and task-specific training

·        Regular check-ins and feedback

·        Exposure to broader business operations and decision-making processes

Qualifications

·        Currently enrolled in a post-secondary program (accounting, business, or related field)

·        Strong attention to detail and willingness to learn

·        Basic knowledge of Excel (or willingness to develop skills)

·        Interest in accounting, finance, or business operations

Work Term Details

·        Duration: May 27 to August 28

·        Hours: Full or Part-Time

·        Location: Edmonton, in-office
