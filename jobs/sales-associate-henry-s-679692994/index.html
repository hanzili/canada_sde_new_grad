About the Role

Assist customers, drive sales, promote products

About Henry's

Canadian camera retailer with strong customer service culture

Full Description

Job description:

Henry’s is a great place to work. The loyalty of our employees and their passion for photography and customer service sets us apart from the competition. Henry’s employs principled, dedicated, and passionate people who work hard at what they do.

Our success is based as much on our creative and talented teams as the selection of products, competitive prices, and industry-leading services we offer. If you want to join Canada’s greatest camera store and be part of our talented team, then this might just be the right opportunity for you.

What Your Day Looks Like

You arrive fifteen minutes before your shift to get yourself ready for the day, check the whiteboard to see how you and your team did yesterday, and catch up on the latest promotions and sales updates from your manager. You scroll through Instagram to feel inspired before shifting into sales mode and heading to the floor.

If things are quiet, you grab some product to put away, clean the glass on the showcases, and try out the demo model of the latest 4K video camera that just came out.

You love this stuff, and it shows. Your first customer of the day is looking for a DSLR. You warmly welcome them into the store and begin asking insightful questions to understand where they are in their purchasing journey and what their research has taught them already.

You demonstrate some awesome accessories and sign them up for a Henry’s Learning Lab Course. You know that the Henry’s Extended Life Plan saves lives, so you tell them all about it. You offer to stay in touch by keeping them informed with the latest Henry’s news by signing them up for the Henry’s newsletter.

After helping a few customers, researching new products, and practicing your sales presentation with your manager, you’ve had a great day. You can’t wait to do it all over again tomorrow.

Who You Are

Successful Sales Associates are inspiring and motivated professionals with the following skills:

  • Sales: “Closing” is your middle name.
  • Customer Service: You have standards, and your customers know it.
  • Retail: Your store is your office, and it’s where you do your best work.
  • Technology and Gadget Knowhow: You’re passionate about technology and can’t live without it.
  • Photo and Video: You nerd out on camera gear.

Your previous managers and favourite teachers describe you as:

  • Self-Starter: You take initiative and aren’t afraid to dive in.
  • Team Player: You wrote the book on why there is no “I” in team.
  • Communicator: You know your audience and how to carefully explain complex things to match different levels of understanding.
  • Information Junkie: You’ve mastered the art of Googling, and you like knowing things.

Why Join Henry’s?

We believe Henry’s culture supports individuals to pursue their passion and achieve great things. This opportunity will offer a career that will be rewarding, interesting, sometimes challenging, but mostly fun.

Join the Henry’s family and be part of our exciting future.
