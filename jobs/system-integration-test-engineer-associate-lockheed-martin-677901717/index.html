About the role

The successful candidate will contribute to the River Class Destroyer (RCD) Land Based Test Facility (LBTF) project as a member of the Integration Test and Evaluation (IT&E) team conducting the following activities:

  • Development and/or review of shipboard/Shore-based Test and Trials (T&T); including Set-To-Work (STW), Integration Testing, Harbour Acceptance Trials (HATs), Sea Acceptance Trials (SATs), and Operational Test & Evaluation
  • Conduct post installation inspections and commissioning of equipment
  • Develop and execute test documentation to verify the functionality of the integrated software/systems
  • Document test results in the Test Management System
  • Managing defect reporting and documentation
  • Support in Test Report reviews to include data collection analysis results and report findings as necessary to key stakeholders
  • Support planning and the execution of the Acceptance activities
  • Assist with the development of Contract Data Requirement Listings (CDRLs)
  • Support the development and maintenance of Test Sequence Network (TSN) and Time Phased Test Sequence Network (TPTSN)
  • Participate in training activities and acquire knowledge of complex warfare systems

What you bring to the role

  • Post-secondary education (degree or diploma) is preferred, or an equivalent combination of education and relevant military/civilian technical experience of 0-2 years experience.
  • Ability to accommodate a flexible work schedule, including evenings and weekends, as required
  • Must have an aptitude for solving challenging problems in a team environment
  • Must have a high degree of initiative and autonomy
  • Demonstrate an ethical, respectful, and collaborative approach, contributing positively to a team environment
  • Ability to communicate effectively both orally and in writing
  • Must be able to obtain a North Atlantic Treaty Organization (NATO) Secret security Clearance in a timely manner

Additional skills desired for the role

  • Experience in Combat Management System 330 (CMS 330) operation is considered an asset
  • Integration and Test (I&T) experience including understanding of Verification Cross Reference Matrix (VCRM)
  • Familiarity with IBM Dynamic Object-Oriented Requirements System (DOORS) is considered an asset
  • Familiarity with I&T processes
  • Familiarity with Agile Scrum methodology is considered an asset
  • Familiarity with software development and test
  • Familiarity with Microsoft Office suite
  • Familiarity with Linux
  • Familiarity with Cameo
  • Familiarity with JIRA / Confluence
  • Familiarity with SharePoint and Windchill
  • Familiarity with Scheduling
  • Experience in reviewing technical documentation

About us

Headquartered in Ottawa, Lockheed Martin Canada is the Canadian unit of Lockheed Martin Corporation, a global defense technology company driving innovation and advancing scientific discovery. Our all-domain mission solutions and 21st Century Security® vision accelerate the delivery of transformative technologies to ensure those we serve always stay ahead of ready. We operate major facilities in Ottawa, Montreal, Halifax, Calgary and Victoria and work on a wide range of programs from advanced naval technology products, aircraft sustainment, and unmanned systems software. This position is part of the Rotary and Mission Systems business area, where employees across Canada support engineering, systems integration, training, sustainment and in-service support programs for Canadian and international military customers across land, air and sea.
