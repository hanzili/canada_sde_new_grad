Role Reports To Manager Analytics and Operations

Location: North America, Canada, Ontario, Markham

Work Location: Hybrid

What You Will Do The Supply Chain Intern is responsible for working closely with the Supply Chain Ecosystems Analytics and Operations team to ensure that we are meeting customer expectations. This role reports into the Ecosystems Analytics Manager and is responsible for analyzing and reporting on key supply chain information such as forecasts and service levels. You will also have the opportunity to lead efforts on process improvements that will contribute to the success of the department. Such process improvement projects may involve analytical Excel work, database querying, and development of Power BI dashboards.

Key Responsibilities

  • Work with local, regional and global business partners to report key performance metrics with

high degree of accuracy and reporting frequency

  • Collaborate cross-functionally to ensure related systems are maintained and improved
  • Participate in various projects such as new system integrations, product discontinuations and

other supply chain improvement initiatives

  • Support the Deliver and Planning teams on their daily responsibilities
  • Participate in ad-hoc projects both within Supply Chain and cross-functionally

Required Qualifications

  • Enrolled in a Bachelor of Business Administration, Engineering, Math, or Science degree program
  • Co-op Program that allows 8-month work term
  • Advanced knowledge in MS Excel using large data sets, pivot tables, and formulas
  • Foundational knowledge of Power BI and SQL
  • Proficiency in MS Office software
  • Strong written and verbal communication skills
  • Attention to detail

Desired Qualifications

  • ERP system knowledge such as SAP
  • Experience with Tableau
  • Exposure to Supply Chain Management through work experience or coursework
  • Interest in process improvement and automation
