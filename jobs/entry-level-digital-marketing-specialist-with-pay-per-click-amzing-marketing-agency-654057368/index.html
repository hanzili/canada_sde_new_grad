About the Role

Create Amazon listings, run PPC campaigns, analyze data

About AMZing Marketing Agency

Marketing agency focusing on ecommerce ads and PPC

Full Description

You MUST have a Permanent Resident or Canadian Citizenship!

This position is funded by the BC Government with the FOLLOWING REQUIREMENTS

  1. Candidate MUST have Permanent Resident or Canadian Citizenship

The best way to apply is to SEND YOUR RESUME and COVER Letter (Mandated) to our company email:
jobs@amzingmarketing.com

Who Are We Looking to Hire?
Are you a marketing person WITH strong analytical skills have salescopy writing skills ? AND passionate about E-commerce (selling products online)? Do you love to create online advertising campaigns to sell products? Then keep reading...

This is an Entry Level Position. For just graduates or someone with a MAXIMUM 1 or 2 years experience in Pay Per Click Advertising

IMPORTANT: We receive hundreds of resumes per week!
so READ THIS CAREFULLY before you APPLY

You MUST have the following skills or your application will be automatically rejected:

  • Excellent English writing skills (we will test your writing skills)
  • 6 Months experience with Google Advertising
  • at LEAST 6 months E-commerce experience
  • Basic experience with Graphic Designs using Canva

Your MUST HAVE Personality:

  • Strong Analytical Skills (you love number and keywords)
  • Problem Solver with a "can do" and "will do" attitude
  • Go-getter, someone that likes challenges
  • Self-starter who can work to deadlines
  • Fast Self-Learner, to keep up to date with the latest changes of online tactics and Amazon policies
  • Organized and details oriented
  • Team player, you inform team mates and manager about important changes

What we are NOT looking for :
NO Social media!

Your Goal:
Your goal is to increase the Amazon store sales of our clients. How? Write product description with keyword optimization and create pay per click advertising

What You Will Do:

· Create, Improve all Amazon Product Listings

· Create perfect sales writing than convince the customer to buy

· Increase the organic AMAZON SEO Ranking using keyword optimization

· Create and monitor over 100 different Amazon Advertising campaigns

· Analyze the web traffic data and decide with product needs to be improved

· Report results, activities, and Amazon updates to marketing manager daily

From time to time you will need to change the marketing strategies due the policy changes of Amazon market places.

You will be working closely with another Amazon consultant and report daily to the Marketing Manager. NO Virtual work. Not working at home. We work at the office together that creates an awesome team spirit.

Skills Required

  1. Minimum 6 months E-commerce experience (selling at Ebay, Shopify or Amazon)

  2. Excellent English writing skills to sell a product (we are not talking about boring essay)

  3. Amazon Pay Per Click Advertising skills or 6 Months Experience with Google Adwords

  4. Excellent Verbal Communication Skills

  5. Intermediate level in Microsoft Excel

Optional, but VERY NICE TO HAVE:
1. Experience in SEO, Google Adwords, Facebook Advertising, A/B Split testing
2. Video Editing Skills

About AMZing Marketing Consultancy

We are a digital marketing agency helping clients to sell more on Amazon by providing Amazon marketing Service.

www.amzingmarketing.com

How to Apply Please send resume and cover letter (include your references) to:
jobs@amzingmarketing.com:

AMZing Marketing Culture

  1. Be open minded, accept new idea’s that works

  2. Anticipate change and adapt to change quickly

  3. Make decisions based on data, not on your gut feeling

  4. Open management culture ( no slow bureaucratic hierarchy)

We have a culture where diversity is valued and where you have an opportunity to make a difference. You have the freedom to implement your ideas that are aligned with our company strategy, but we need to see results.
