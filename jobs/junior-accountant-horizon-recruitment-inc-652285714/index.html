About Horizon Recruitment Inc

Recruitment firm specializing in finance roles

Full Description

Horizon Recruitment is recruiting for a Junior Accountant to support day-to-day accounting operations and ensure accurate financial records across billing, collections, and reporting. This role helps maintain strong cash flow, supports month-end close, and improves visibility into accounts receivable and project-based revenue.

Responsibilities:

  • Review sales orders, invoices, and contracts to ensure accurate billing and revenue recognition
  • Prepare and issue invoices, including those related to training and consulting services
  • Follow up on overdue accounts and manage collections to improve cash flow
  • Track and report on accounts receivable aging and collection activity
  • Apply incoming payments and resolve discrepancies or unidentified receipts
  • Perform monthly bank and account reconciliations
  • Support month-end close activities related to AR and revenue
  • Prepare journal entries tied to receivables, revenue, and project work
  • Maintain clear documentation of billing and AR procedures
  • Assist with audit requests by providing supporting documentation and analysis
  • Coordinate consulting project administration including contract setup, cost tracking, and billing
  • Track timesheets and assist with commission calculations
  • Contribute to regulatory reporting such as Statistics Canada surveys
  • Provide backup support for accounts payable and fixed assets as needed

Requirements:

  • Diploma or degree in accounting or finance
  • 2-3 years of relevant accounting experience
  • Experience with NetSuite is an asset
  • Strong Excel skills at an intermediate to advanced level
  • Proficiency with Microsoft Office including Word and Outlook
  • Strong attention to detail and accuracy
  • Able to manage multiple priorities and meet deadlines
  • Clear written and verbal communication skills
  • Self-starter who can work independently and within a team

Remuneration:

  • $30 - $35/hr

Only candidates eligible to work in Canada will be considered

Horizon Recruitment Inc. is a specialized recruitment and search firm with two complimentary practice areas: Finance & Supply Chain.
