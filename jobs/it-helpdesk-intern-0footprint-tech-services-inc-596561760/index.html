Full Description

About the Opportunity

0footprint Tech Services Inc. is looking for an motivated IT Helpdesk Intern to join our team in Vancouver. This is a 12-week internship (with the possibility of an extension for the right candidate) specifically designed for current IT students or recent graduates looking to gain hands-on experience in the Managed Service Provider (MSP) industry.

We provide a structured learning environment where you will gain the knowledge and professional experience needed to kickstart your career in Information Technology.

Your Impact & Learning Journey

As an intern, you will handle IT support for end-user technology and gain exposure to high-level technical operations:

  • Support & Troubleshooting: Provide first-level support for IT-related queries and assist with troubleshooting hardware/software issues.
  • Infrastructure Tasks: Complete desk setups, equipment moves, and decommissions.
  • Deployment: Assist with the installation, configuration, and updating of hardware (PCs, printers, monitors) and software.
  • Team Collaboration: Work alongside senior engineers on Microsoft 365, server maintenance, backups, and security tasks.
  • Documentation: Maintain IT inventory and system documentation, including user guides and configuration records.

Qualifications & Requirements

Required:

  • Education: Currently enrolled in or a recent graduate of an Information Technology degree or diploma program.
  • Communication: Fluent in English with excellent verbal communication and professional phone manners.
  • Technical Basics: Fundamental understanding of computer systems (Windows, macOS), networking (IP, DNS, VPN), and mobile devices.
  • Soft Skills: Customer-oriented, cool-tempered, and a strong problem-solver with excellent attention to detail.

Assets (Nice to have):

  • Relevant certifications: CompTIA A+, Network+, or Security+.
  • Microsoft Certifications: MS-900 or AZ-900.
  • Experience with ticketing systems.

Working Conditions

  • Work Location: In-person (Vancouver).
  • Autonomy: Ability to work independently while managing multiple tasks in a fast-paced environment.
  • Growth: An eager drive to learn, adapt, and grow within the IT field.

Job Type: Internship / Co-op
Contract length: 3 months

Expected hours: 40 per week
