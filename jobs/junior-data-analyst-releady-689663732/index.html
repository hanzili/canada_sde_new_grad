About Releady

Full Description

OVERVIEW

We are seeking an experienced Junior Data Analyst with a strong background in data analysis, reporting, and business management support within Capital Markets, Commercial Banking, or enterprise technology environments. The successful candidate will support a Business Management team by delivering data-driven insights, maintaining reporting accuracy, and assisting with operational and portfolio management activities across a large banking technology organization.

This role is ideal for a detail-oriented and analytical professional who thrives in collaborative environments and enjoys working with large datasets, reporting tools, and business stakeholders to improve operational efficiency and reporting quality.

Location: Toronto, ON (Hybrid – 2 days in office)

Contract Length: 6 Months (Extension Possible)

Hours: 8:30 AM–5:00 PM EST

Pay: T4: $30 / IC: $$34.5

RESPONSIBILITIES

  • Perform data validation, analysis, and reporting activities to support business management and portfolio reporting requirements.
  • Analyze large datasets to identify trends, inconsistencies, and opportunities for process improvement.
  • Develop and maintain recurring reports, dashboards, and presentations for leadership and stakeholders.
  • Support record keeping, documentation management, and reporting governance processes to ensure data integrity and accuracy.
  • Collaborate with cross-functional teams to gather requirements, document findings, and support business initiatives.
  • Prepare executive-ready reporting materials using Excel, PowerPoint, and Power BI.
  • Assist with process improvement initiatives and reporting automation opportunities.
  • Support stakeholder management activities by responding to reporting requests and providing analytical insights.
  • Ensure reporting standards, controls, and documentation practices are maintained across business management activities.
  • Learn and adapt quickly to new tools, systems, and internal business processes.

QUALIFICATIONS

  • 2–3 years of experience in a Data Analyst, Reporting Analyst, Business Analyst, or similar analytical support role.
  • Strong analytical, numerical, and problem-solving skills with the ability to interpret and validate complex datasets.
  • Experience working with large data sets, reporting processes, and operational analysis.
  • Advanced proficiency in Microsoft Excel, including formulas, pivot tables, data manipulation, and reporting functions.
  • Experience with Microsoft Office 365, PowerPoint, and Power BI.
  • Strong attention to detail with a focus on reporting accuracy, consistency, and data integrity.
  • Excellent communication and collaboration skills with the ability to work effectively across teams and stakeholders.
  • Strong organizational and documentation skills.
  • Demonstrated eagerness to learn new systems, tools, and business processes.
  • Understanding of how business operations, reporting, and technology functions align within enterprise organizations.

Preferred

  • Experience within banking, financial services, Capital Markets, or Commercial Banking environments.
  • Experience with MIS reporting, reporting automation, or dashboard development.
  • Exposure to process improvement initiatives or operational optimization activities.
  • Familiarity with enterprise reporting environments and governance practices.

EDUCATION

  • Post-secondary degree or diploma in Business, Finance, Data Analytics, Information Systems, Mathematics, or a related discipline.
