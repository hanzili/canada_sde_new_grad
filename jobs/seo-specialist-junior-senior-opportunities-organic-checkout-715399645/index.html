About the Role

Execute SEO campaigns, conduct keyword research, optimize content

About Organic Checkout

Growing eCommerce SEO agency with AI focus

Full Description

Location: Remote

Company: Organic Checkout

Organic Checkout is growing, and we’re looking for both Junior and Senior SEO Specialists to join our team. We work with a wide range of eCommerce and lead generation brands, helping them scale through SEO, AI search optimization, content strategy, technical SEO, and GEO (Generative Engine Optimization).

We’re looking for people who are analytical, organized, proactive, and genuinely passionate about the future of search.

What You’ll Be Doing

  • Executing SEO campaign strategies across multiple client accounts
  • Conduct keyword research, search intent analysis, and competitor research
  • Optimize on-page SEO elements including metadata, headings, internal linking, and content structure
  • Perform technical SEO audits and identify growth opportunities
  • Analyze SEO performance using GA4, GSC, Ahrefs, Semrush, and other tools
  • Support AI/AO/GEO initiatives and help optimize brands for AI-driven search experiences
  • Assist with content planning, optimization, and topical authority strategies
  • Monitor rankings, traffic, conversions, and AI search visibility
  • Collaborate with strategists, developers, and content teams
  • Assist with reporting and identifying actionable insights
  • Stay current on algorithm updates, AI search trends, and evolving SEO best practices

Junior SEO Specialist Requirements

  • Basic understanding of SEO fundamentals
  • Familiarity with Google Search Console and Google Analytics
  • Strong willingness to learn and grow
  • Organized and detail-oriented
  • Good written and verbal communication skills
  • Ability to manage tasks and deadlines independently
  • Interest in AI search, GEO, and the future of organic search

Senior SEO Specialist Requirements

  • 3+ years of hands-on SEO experience
  • Strong understanding of technical SEO, content strategy, and SEO reporting
  • Experience managing and executing SEO campaign strategies across multiple clients or websites
  • Ability to identify issues, prioritize opportunities, and execute strategy
  • Experience with tools such as Ahrefs, Semrush, Screaming Frog, GA4, and Looker Studio
  • Strong analytical and problem-solving skills
  • Ability to lead strategy discussions and mentor junior team members
  • Strong understanding of AI/AO/GEO strategies and modern search behavior

Strong Assets

  • Experience with eCommerce SEO (Shopify/WooCommerce)
  • Experience with AI search optimization, GEO, and Answer Optimization (AO)
  • Strong client communication and account management skills
  • Experience presenting reports and explaining SEO strategy to clients
  • Familiarity with ClickUp, Notion, or project management systems
  • Content optimization and SEO copywriting experience
  • Experience using AI tools and workflows within SEO processes

What We’re Looking For

  • Someone who wants to grow long term with the company
  • Strong communication and organizational skills
  • Self-motivated and proactive mindset
  • Ability to work in a fast-paced environment
  • Someone who takes ownership and genuinely cares about results
  • Passion for staying ahead in the evolving AI/search landscape
