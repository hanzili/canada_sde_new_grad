Full Description

Craft Recruiter - Entry Level

At EllisDon Industrial, we're constantly pushing the limits of what we've done in the past - propelling ourselves toward bigger and better opportunities, while exploring new market sectors and services. So, it only seems natural that we'd hire people who have their own voice and aren't afraid to use it. These are people who are simultaneously critical, creative and captivated by construction. They make our business their business and follow a path that with continuous learning and an innovative spirit leads them to be recognized as leaders in the industry. We hire these types of people knowing they are as diverse as we are, and they come on board because they share our core values. Then we simply give them the freedom to take their career further.

Key Information:

  • Hours: Based on a 40-hour week. (Monday-Friday) Overtime when required.
  • Hourly rate is dependent on experience.
  • Health benefits and vacation pay.

Job Description:

  • Communicate with the Lead Workforce Coordinator regarding manpower needs.
  • Pre-Screen and organize applicants in our tracking system.
  • Conduct interviews and facilitate on-boarding and orientations as required.
  • Track and maintain safety tickets and records of the employees.
  • Coordinate and register employees into necessary training and re-certifications.
  • General Administrative duties that include but may not be limited to; answering the phone, directing email, and onboarding documents.

Experience and Certification Requirements:

  • Must have a basic understanding on computer programs, such as Microsoft Excel, and Google drive.
  • An outgoing and optimistic personality.
  • Must have strong communication skills.
  • Must be willing to work overtime, when necessary.
  • A reliable means of transportation is required.
  • Proficient English language skills required.
  • A reliable means of transportation.

Education: Completion of high school or equivalent is required.

Go ahead and be yourself. We'll pay you for it!

We are an equal opportunity employer. While only qualified candidates will be contacted for an interview, be sure to continually check our website for other related positions as they are posted.
