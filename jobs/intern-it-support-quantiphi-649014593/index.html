Role:Intern - IT Support

Experience Level:1-2 years of experience (Internship or entry-level experience welcome)

Work Location:Toronto, ON

Role Overview:

We are looking for a motivated and customer-focused IT Support Intern to join our team. In this role, you will be the first point of contact for technical support, helping to ensure a seamless experience for end users across hardware, software, and network systems. This is a great opportunity to gain hands-on experience in a fast-paced, technology-driven environment while developing your troubleshooting and communication skills.

Key Responsibilities:

  • Provide timely support by addressing user tickets related to hardware, software, and networking issues
  • Assist users with installing applications, configuring systems, and setting up peripherals (e.g., printers, scanners)
  • Diagnose technical issues by asking targeted questions and identifying root causes
  • Guide users through solutions with clear, step-by-step instructions
  • Perform remote troubleshooting and escalate issues when necessary
  • Test and implement alternative solutions to resolve technical problems effectively
  • Customize and configure desktop applications based on user requirements
  • Accurately document technical issues, resolutions, and processes in internal systems
  • Contribute to the creation and maintenance of technical documentation and user guides

Basic Qualifications:

  • Strong written and verbal communication skills
  • 1–2 years of experience in IT support, desktop support, or a similar role (including internships/co-ops)
  • Familiarity with Windows, Linux, and/or Mac OS environments
  • Working knowledge of office productivity tools and computer peripherals
  • Basic understanding of network security practices and antivirus tools
  • Ability to troubleshoot technical issues remotely and explain solutions clearly
  • Strong problem-solving skills with the ability to multitask and prioritize
  • Customer-first mindset with a proactive and positive attitude
  • Bachelor’s degree (or currently pursuing) in Computer Science or a related field

What’s in it for YOU at Quantiphi:

  • Make an impact at one of the world’s fastest-growing AI-first digital engineering companies.
  • Upskill and discover your potential as you solve complex challenges in cutting-edge areas of technology alongside passionate, talented colleagues.
  • Work where innovation happens - work with disruptive innovators in a research-focused organization with 60+ patents filed across various disciplines.
  • Stay ahead of the curve immerse yourself in breakthrough AI, ML, data, and cloud technologies and gain exposure working with Fortune 500 companies.

If you like wild growth and working with happy, enthusiastic over-achievers, you'll enjoy your career with us!
