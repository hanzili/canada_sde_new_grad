About the Role

About Adamson Systems Engineering

Manufacturing firm with global distribution and growth

Full Description

Adamson Systems Engineering manufactures products for the global market, based in Port Perry. Our distribution network extends to all parts of the globe. We source raw materials both domestically and internationally, and we employ both a local and remote workforce that represent the brand.

The team driving the day-to-day operations of Adamson must be well-versed in the mechanics of running a global business. Come join the Adamson team and help us make an impact.

We are seeking a detail-oriented professional with an accounting background and strong data skills to support our finance and operations team. The ideal candidate will combine solid accounting knowledge with practical experience in Power BI, SQL, account reconciliations, and inventory counts.

Key Responsibilities

  • Perform monthly account reconciliations and resolve variances in a timely manner
  • Assist in preparation of financial statements and supporting schedules
  • Support inventory management, including organizing and conducting physical inventory counts
  • Reconcile inventory records to general ledger and investigate discrepancies
  • Develop and maintain dashboards and reports in Power BI for finance and operations
  • Support month-end and year-end close activities
  • Collaborate with operational and field teams to ensure accurate financial and inventory reporting
  • Identify process improvement opportunities using data analytics

Qualifications

  • Diploma or degree in Accounting , Finance , or a related field
  • 1–4 years of experience in accounting or finance (entry to intermediate level)
  • Solid understanding of general ledger , account reconciliations , and financial reporting
  • Practical experience with Power BI (building reports/dashboards)
  • Working knowledge of SQL (writing queries, pulling data from databases)
  • Experience with inventory accounting and/or physical inventory counts
  • Strong proficiency in Excel (pivot tables, VLOOKUP/XLOOKUP, formulas)
  • High attention to detail and strong analytical skills
  • Good communication skills and ability to work with both finance and operations teams

Asset:

  • Familiarity with the M1 ERP system
  • Experience in manufacturing

Benefits

  • Company events
  • Dental care
  • Disability insurance
  • Extended health care
  • Vision care
