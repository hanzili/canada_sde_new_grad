About YSK Media

Full Description

**MUST BE a 2026 Graduate from a univeristy or college program

Job Summary:

Storybox is a leading social media content creation agency in London Ontario. We are seeking a creative and results-driven Social Media Coordinator to join our dynamic team. The ideal candidate will be responsible for developing and implementing effective social media strategies that align with client's brand's goals. This role requires a strong understanding of social media marketing trends, content creation, and communication skills.

Duties

  • Develop and execute comprehensive social media strategies to increase brand awareness and engagement across various platforms.
  • Create, curate, and manage published content video content that aligns with the clients voice and objectives.
  • Monitor social media channels for trends, customer feedback, and engagement opportunities.
  • Conduct research on industry trends, competitor activities, and audience preferences to inform content creation and strategy adjustments.
  • Collaborate with the marketing team to integrate social media efforts with broader digital marketing campaigns.
  • Analyze performance metrics to assess the effectiveness of social media initiatives and make data-driven recommendations for improvement.

Skills

  • Proficiency in social media video content creation techniques.
  • Video capabilities (on iphone)
  • Knowledge of social media trends and best practices
  • Ability to conduct thorough research to stay updated on industry trends and audience preferences.
  • Excellent communication skills with a knack for engaging storytelling through social media.
  • Experience in managing multiple social media accounts simultaneously
  • A passion for staying current with emerging social media trends and technologies.

Join us as we elevate our brand's presence in the digital landscape!

Pay: Up to $65,000.00 per year

  • Paid time off
