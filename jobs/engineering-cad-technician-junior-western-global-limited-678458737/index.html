About the Role:

This position assists our Engineering team in creating and updating technical drawings, models, and documentation. This internship offers hands-on experience in CAD software, engineering design, and collaboration with cross-functional teams to support ongoing projects.

Key Responsibilities:

  • Support senior drafters and engineers in developing detailed mechanical designs and drawings in accordance with Western Global standards and procedures using SolidWorks.
  • Utilize SolidWorks PDM workflow process to maintain internal engineering files.
  • Model containerised self-bunded fuel storage tanks and produce shop fabrication detail drawings in accordance with relevant standards (UL 142, ULC S601, UL 2085).
  • Work through all phases of production design from concept development to production drawings.
  • Interprets PIDs and model piping and pumping packages to produce shop fabrication detailed drawings and production drawings complete with Bill of Materials (BOM).
  • Communicate effectively and collaborate with engineering team, BDMs and production.
  • Assist production with all design-related troubleshooting, test fitment and inquiries.
  • Provide accurate timely designs and drawings through quotation and project phases.
  • Assist in ensuring Western Global products and design maintains the highest level of safety, quality, efficiency, cost effectiveness and customer satisfaction.
  • Be responsible, own and drive the engineering drawing production, from start to finish ensuring all engineering drawings are fully compliant with relevant codes, standards, client specifications, while meeting tight deadlines.
  • Reviewing, analyze, and interpret customer and supplier data and specifications.

What will give you the edge:

  • High proficiency in SolidWorks and PDM (Version 2025 – SP5 minimum), with experience using AutoCAD or similar software.
  • Previous experience with 3D CAD design and manufacturing processes is considered an asset.
  • Understanding of fuel dispensing systems or related industry experience (e.g., mining, oil & gas, pumping systems, fuel tanks, fabrication).
  • Ability to read, analyze and interpret technical procedures, specifications, governmental regulations, drawings and job specifications.
  • Strong understanding of drafting principles, basic manufacturing principles, schematics, documentation, and applicable technical standards.
  • Ability to interpret technical sketches and translate them into precise digital drawings.
  • Excellent attention to detail and strong organizational skills, with the ability to manage multiple projects simultaneously.
  • Ability to work effectively both independently and as part of a team in a fast‑paced environment.
