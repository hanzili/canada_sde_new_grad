Junior Cloud Engineer

at Applicantz

📍 Location Vancouver, British Columbia
📋 Details Full-time

About the Role

Maintain CI microservices, troubleshoot, and deploy features

About Applicantz

Fortune Technology client; globally acclaimed employer

Full Description

On-site work from the Vancouver office ( V7X 1L4)

We are looking for a Cloud Engineer (1–3 years experience) to support and enhance our CI microservices platform. This role blends system operations, cloud infrastructure, CI/CD, and AI feature integration . If you enjoy working with cloud platforms, troubleshooting systems, and building scalable microservices, this is a great opportunity to grow.

Minimum Qualifications:

  • Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science or related field
  • 1–3 years of experience in software engineering or system operations
  • Working knowledge of Linux and scripting
  • Hands-on experience with Docker and CI/CD pipelines
  • Basic understanding of databases
  • Familiarity with cloud platforms such as AWS or Azure

Responsibilities:

  • Maintain and operate CI microservice systems in production
  • Troubleshoot technical issues and support internal users
  • Apply security patches to servers and databases
  • Enhance existing features and develop new capabilities
  • Support AI-related components (MCP server, AI agents)
  • Investigate and resolve system bugs to ensure reliability

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Exposure to microservices architecture
  • Experience supporting production environments
  • Understanding of AI-based applications or integrations.

Our Fortune Technology client is ranked as one of the best companies to work with, in the world. As a global leader in 3D design, engineering, and entertainment software, they foster a progressive culture, creativity, and a flexible work environment using cutting-edge technologies.
