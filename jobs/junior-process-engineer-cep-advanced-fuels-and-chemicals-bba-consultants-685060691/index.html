About the Role

About BBA Consultants

Full Description

Type of position: Regular

Your future role on our team BBA is currently seeking a candidate to the engineering profession, Process, for our Montréal team. Join a team that offers high-quality expertise and consulting services and help develop and strengthen client relationships. You’ll also contribute to proposals, engineering, technology selection and project delivery for plant services, including biofuels manufacturing processes, carbon capture and utilization, green hydrogen, water treatment, liquefied natural gas, specialty chemicals, lithium processing and more.

With us, you’ll get the opportunity to...

  • Prepare simple plans and basic calculations
  • Apply established design and calculation methods
  • Develop mass and energy balances using simulation software
  • Select and size process equipment
  • Draft specifications, process design criteria, PFDs, P&IDs, plant layouts, technical specifications and assessments as well as reports
  • Where required, participate in various work phases at the office, on site or in a lab
  • Prepare proposals
  • Participate in the firm’s development and growth
  • Carry out commissioning and equipment performance testing
  • Perform routine inspections or technical surveys

Do your qualities and values match our corporate culture?

  • Entrepreneurial spirit
  • Strong commitment to health and safety
  • Thirst to learn and excel
  • Willingness to work together while having fun
  • Ability to look beyond established standards
  • Rigorous and ethical
  • Ability to share and pass on knowledge

Summary Of Certifications And Job Requirements

  • Bachelor's degree in chemical engineering
  • Up to two years of relevant experience in the advanced fuels and chemicals or oil and gas industry
  • Preferably registered, or eligible to register, with the Ordre des ingénieurs du Québec (OIQ) as a CEP
  • Basic knowledge of process simulation
  • Sound knowledge of statistics and effective analytical skills
  • Excellent knowledge of fluid mechanics and heat transfer
  • Interest in and knowledge of process control
  • Interest in the environment
  • Proficient with Microsoft Office Suite
  • Advanced English skills to facilitate collaboration with other BBA offices and/or clients in English-speaking Canadian provinces and internationally; you may need to use English occasionally or daily, depending on current projects
  • Advanced French skills to facilitate collaboration with other BBA offices and/or clients in Québec

About BBA BBA is one of Canada’s leading private consulting engineering firms, with over 45 years of experience serving the energy and natural resources industry.

Our people are the foundation of our success. Their passion and excellence have earned us recognition as one of Canada’s Best Employers and Best Managed Companies , and we’re committed to fostering a workplace where everyone feels empowered to grow, lead and be themselves.

Our teams bring together engineering, environmental and commissioning expertise to deliver practical, innovative and sustainable solutions—from strategy to execution. With 20 offices across Canada, the U.S. and Latin America, we combine local presence with international reach, offering clients close collaboration and field-ready support.
