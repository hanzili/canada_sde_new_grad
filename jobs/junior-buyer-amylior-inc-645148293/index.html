About Amylior Inc.

Mobility devices innovator, strong procurement and supply chain

Full Description

Company Description

Amylior specializes in the design, development, and production of premium power wheelchairs, customized seating and positioning systems, and related mobility accessories. The company also distributes high-quality products like electric scooters, rollators, manual wheelchairs, and therapeutic air cushions across North America and internationally. With an innovative R&D team comprised of skilled engineers and specialists, Amylior continuously develops solutions that cater to evolving customer needs. Employing nearly 200 dedicated professionals, Amylior is committed to enhancing mobility and improving quality of life with superior products and exceptional customer service.

Role Description

This is a full-time, on-site role for a Junior Buyer located in Vaudreuil-Dorion, QC. The Junior Buyer will be responsible for supporting the procurement team by managing purchase orders, maintaining supplier relationships, analyzing inventory levels, and ensuring timely delivery of materials. Other responsibilities include negotiating with vendors, maintaining accurate purchasing records, and collaborating with internal departments to meet company objectives.

Qualifications

  • Strong procurement and negotiation skills, with experience in sourcing and managing supplier relationships.
  • Proficiency in inventory management, purchase order processing, and record maintenance.
  • Analytical skills and attention to detail for managing budgets and monitoring deliveries.
  • Effective communication and organizational skills for cross-departmental collaboration.
  • Ability to work effectively in a fast-paced environment and multitask efficiently.
  • Proficiency in MS Office Suite; experience with ERP systems is an asset.
  • Post-secondary education in Supply Chain Management, Business Administration, or a related field.
  • Experience in manufacturing or distribution industries is an added advantage.
  • Languages: Bilingualism (English and French) is considered an asset.
