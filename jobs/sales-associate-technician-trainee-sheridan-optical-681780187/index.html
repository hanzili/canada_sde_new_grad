About the Role

Assist customers, sell frames, learn lens edging

About Sheridan Optical

Local optical retailer with hands-on training

Full Description

Job Summary

While this is a Sales Associate position, we are looking for a candidate that not only is efficient in providing customer/patient service but someone who is willing to learn new technical skills. The ideal candidate will excel in providing exceptional customer service, demonstrating strong communication skills, welcoming customers/patients, assisting opticians in navigating frame selection for patients, maintain day-to-day organization and cleanliness, and be willing to develop lens edging skills. The ability to utilize various software is appreciated.

Duties

  • Greet customers warmly and assist them in selecting frames
  • Develop and demonstrate relevant product knowledge
  • Develop lens edging skills after a training period
  • Operate POS systems accurately for transactions, cash handling, and receipts
  • Maintain merchandising standards to ensure attractive product displays
  • Provide excellent customer service by addressing inquiries, resolving issues, and offering technical support when necessary
  • Handle cashiering duties with professionalism, including processing payments via cash, credit/debit cards, and mobile payments
  • Keep the sales floor organized and clean, ensuring a positive shopping environment
  • Use effective communication skills to inform customers about promotions, new products, and store policies
  • Manage time efficiently to serve multiple customers during busy periods

We are seeking a candidate that is interested in the above line of work, at a full or part-time position, is self-confident and is looking to be a part of a small team of eye care professionals. Applicants who already have the noted experience are also welcome.

Pay: $17.50-$23.00 per hour

Benefits:

  • Flexible schedule
  • Paid time off
  • Store discount
  • Vision care

Experience:

  • Retail or Customer Service: 1 year (preferred)
