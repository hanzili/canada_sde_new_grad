Full Description

Great student job opportunity!

Arnts the Landscape Supplier Inc. is looking for hard-working, energetic students who enjoy working in a fast-paced environment.

We offer:

  • Starting wage is $18.00 (above minimum wage!)
  • Consistent and great hours - no late evenings
  • Saturday half-day shifts (as required)
  • Work with great people + other student team members
  • Build your resume and business relationships with us - we provide full support training
  • Opportunities for part-time work during the school year
  • Fun, interactive company events

... and more!

GREAT HOURS - NO LATE EVENINGS - TRAINING PROVIDED

The primary responsibility of a sales associate is to:

  • Provide customers with appropriate information and assistance to complete their product purchase / landscape plan;
  • Process sales quickly and accurately;
  • Perform POS and cash register operations;
  • Answer customer phone calls and assist with the calculation of quotes, orders, and sales;
  • Complete general housekeeping duties.

For the full job description and additional details, visit our website: www.arnts.ca/employment

Safety shoes or work boots are required for this position.

Arnts the Landscape Supplier Inc. is committed to a diverse and inclusive workplace for all. We are able to accommodate applications with disabilities in the recruitment process. For accommodation requests, please email or call and ask to speak with the Human Resources department.

We thank all applicants in advance for their interest in this position, however, only those selected to move forward in the interviewing process will be contacted.

  • Store discount

  • POS: 1 year (preferred)
