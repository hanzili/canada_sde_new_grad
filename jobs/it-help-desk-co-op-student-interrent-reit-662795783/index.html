About the Role

Provide IT helpdesk support and device provisioning

About InterRent REIT

Real estate REIT with tech-enabled assets

Full Description

The Opportunity

InterRent REIT is looking for an IT Help Desk Assistant CO-OP Student to join its team on a full-time basis for the summer term.

You will have the opportunity to learn and be involved in all aspects of IT Helpdesk Support across a wide range of platforms (Windows, OSX, IOS, Android).

What will make you successful in this role is your desire to learn in a fast paced environment while providing excellent customer service skills to all of your teammates across every department.

Responsibilities will include, at a minimum:

  • Assist with all aspects of Desktop/Laptop device provisioning
  • Assist with User Account Management in a Active Directory environment
  • Assist with Inventory Management
  • Assist with repair/service of devices
  • Assist with all aspects of mobile device provisioning
  • Setting up new PC workstations, printers and other peripherals;
  • Diagnosing and supporting all client applications
  • Troubleshooting PC hardware and operating system software in a network/AD domain environment.
  • Troubleshooting low voltage equipment such as Access Control and Intercom systems
  • Support of wireless telecom devices including user support, hardware provisioning (IOS, Android)
  • Maintaining asset tracking.
  • Assuming additional responsibilities and completing special projects as needed or directed

Qualifications

  • Must be enrolled in a college or university CO-OP program of which is in a related field
  • Experience with all variations of Windows desktop OS
  • Understanding of IP networking
  • Experience with Low Voltage devices
  • Outstanding people skills and customer service skills orientation
  • Able to handle rapidly changing priorities, fast pace situations
  • Excellent interpersonal and telephone skills
  • Excellent research skills

Additional Information

  • Corporate hours are 8:30am-5pm and you will be required to be on-call
  • This is a co-op role for May 2026.
  • In-office position only. Hybrid not an option.

Why we ‘love it here.’

We work hard to provide the proper training and development based on your career objectives and areas of interest. Your journey at InterRent does not follow a prescribed path, but is rather tailored to how you learn, where you want to go and what you want to achieve. We also take time to celebrate our successes and socialize as a team. Through a variety of team events and other special engagements, we truly live up to the ‘Work Hard. Play Hard’ philosophy.

About InterRent

Formed in 2006 as an unincorporated, open-ended real estate investment trust, we have steadily grown our portfolio and market presence in our core markets which includes the Greater Toronto and Hamilton area, Ottawa, Montréal, and Vancouver. More importantly, we have repositioned dozens of our communities and improved the lives of countless Canadians. With a reputation for innovation and a best-in-class management platform, we continue to play a leadership role in the multi-residential space.
