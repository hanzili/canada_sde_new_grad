Junior Accountant

About the Role

Process bookkeeping, tax returns, and financial statements accurately.

About Perfektax

Calgary-based public practice; client exposure and growth.

Full Description

Perfektax is a Calgary-based accounting practice providing corporate accounting, tax, and advisory services to small and medium-sized businesses. Our work includes bookkeeping, corporate and personal tax preparation, and Notice to Reader (Compilation) financial statements for owner-managed companies.

We are looking for a Junior Accountant who wants to gain hands-on experience in public practice and work closely with small business clients. This role offers exposure to corporate tax (T2), personal tax (T1), bookkeeping, and year-end file preparation.

Key Responsibilities

  • Maintain client bookkeeping and accounting records using QuickBooks or similar accounting software
  • Reconcile bank, credit card, and balance sheet accounts
  • Assist with preparation of corporate tax returns (T2) and personal tax returns (T1)
  • Assist with preparation of Notice to Reader (Compilation) financial statements and working papers
  • Prepare GST/HST filings for clients
  • Support the preparation of financial statements and management reports
  • Communicate with clients to obtain accounting records and supporting documentation
  • Assist senior accountants with year-end engagements and tax season work

Qualifications

  • Diploma or degree in Accounting, Finance, or related field
  • 0–2 years of accounting or bookkeeping experience
  • Experience with QuickBooks, Sage, Xero, or similar accounting software is an asset
  • Strong Excel skills and attention to detail
  • Strong organizational and communication skills\

Perfektax offers a supportive environment where junior team members gain exposure to a wide variety of clients and accounting work while developing practical skills in tax and small business accounting.

Pay: $40,000.00-$60,000.00 per year

Work Location: In person
