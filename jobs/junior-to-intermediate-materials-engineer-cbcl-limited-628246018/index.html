About CBCL Limited

Full Description

We have an exciting opportunity for a Junior to Intermediate Materials Engineer to join our growing team in Bedford, NS. The successful candidate will support the delivery of materials engineering services and develop technical expertise across a wide range of projects. Our geotechnical and materials division specializes in geotechnical and materials engineering consulting.

Your Key Responsibilities:

  • Provide materials engineering consulting services for sectors such as industrial, commercial, municipal, highway, and residential projects;
  • Assist with condition assessments and materials testing of concrete structures and support the development of concrete repair strategies, including structures affected by Alkali-Aggregate Reactivity (AAR);

  • Support consulting services related to:

  • Thermal control measures for mass concrete applications;

  • Shrinkage control measures to minimize cracking of concrete structures;

  • Protection of concrete structures in chemical exposure environments;

  • Contribute to the preparation of proposals, technical reports, drawings, and specifications for materials engineering works; and

  • Support input into CCIL laboratory certification.

Your Capabilities and Credentials:

  • A degree in Civil or Materials Engineering with a focus on infrastructure construction materials (concrete, asphalt pavement, aggregates). A post‑graduate degree (master’s degree or higher) would be a strong asset;
  • At least two (2) years of experience in the Materials Engineering field;
  • Eligibility for registration as an Engineer‑in‑Training (EIT) or P.Eng. with the provincial association; and
  • Experience in concrete engineering including assessment, design and construction support for concrete elements is preferred;
  • Experience or interest in asphalt pavement mix design, pavement condition assessment, and laboratory testing of physical and volumetric properties of asphalt would be considered an asset.

About Us At CBCL our purpose is to create a positive and lasting impact on people and our planet. As an employee-owned firm, at CBCL, our people are everything. We are committed to promoting inclusivity and diversity for a workplace where everyone can thrive, and we build long-term relationships with our clients while providing them with innovative solutions.

We have earned recognition as one of Canada’s Top 100 Employers and one of Canada’s Best Managed Companies. Our multitalented group of professionals offer consulting in the Infrastructure, Water and Wastewater, Buildings, Industrial, and Earth and Environment sectors. We value our people, our communities, and our history and look forward to continuing our steady growth.

Along with our commitment to reducing our carbon footprint corporately, CBCL develops and implements sustainable engineering solutions that respect the current social and environmental impacts, as well as future implications of our designs.

CBCL operates from its offices in Halifax and Sydney, NS; Charlottetown, PE; Saint John, Fredericton and Moncton, NB; St. John's, NL, and Ottawa, ON.
