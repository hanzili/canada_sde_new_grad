About the Role

Perform QA testing, data logging, SOP compliance

About Sofina Foods

Leading Canadian food company with QA training

Full Description

Sofina Foods is one of Canada's leading manufacturers of primary and further processed protein products for retail and food service customers as well as international markets. We manufacture pork, beef, turkey, chicken, and fish products. Our family of brands includes Cuddy, Lilydale, Janes, Mastro, San Daniele, Fletcher's, Vienna, Riserva, and Zamzam. We are also the exclusive distributor of Italy’s finest Rio Mare, Lavazza, and San Benedetto products in Canada.

Position Summary:
Responsible for the administration of Quality Assurance functions as they relate to raw material and finished product testing to achieve customer product specifications inclusive of routine microbiological testing and results analysis and general administrative duties. Provide coverage for sick and vacation days to both plant locations as needed.

Key Accountabilities:

  • Inspection/collection of incoming raw materials, dry ingredients and finished product for microbiological, chemistry tests and other tests, sending samples to internal or external accredited laboratory.
  • Inspection/audit of incoming raw materials, dry ingredients and packaging by conducting weight / count measurements and organoleptic analysis for conformance as per Food Safety and Quality Manual.
  • Monitor SOP’s (Standard Operating procedures), personal hygiene, product handling (raw materials, in process or finished) for temperatures, weights, shapes, injection, cook up and other product specifications.
  • Observe and coach plant employees to comply with GMPs.
  • Update and maintain the Visual Quality Standards database for raw materials.
  • Perform monitoring and verification of various tasks based on HACCP program and Food Safety and Quality Manual
  • Verify production and HACCP reports for accuracy and follow up with production department when needed.
  • Investigate the customer complaints as required and provide this information to QA Manager.
  • Maintain the lab, work areas and equipment clean at all times.
  • Assist QA Manager and HACCP Coordinator in special projects updating programs and procedures.
  • The duties and responsibilities outlined above are representative but not all inclusive.

Requirements:

  • Currently enrolled in College education in food related field of study
  • Computer literacy: Basic level spreadsheets, word processing, and statistics
  • Basic Knowledge of safe food handling, GMP’s
  • Communication skills (both verbal and written)
  • Building and maintaining relationships (both internal and external relationships)
  • Detail-oriented, observant
  • Effective Time Management
  • Organizing and planning

Hourly wage is $22.11 per hour for this role, with the ability to work 40 hours per week. Weekends and overtime may be required based on the needs of the business and production. This job posting is for a position which is currently vacant.
