Job Title: Accounts Receivable Administrator (Summer 2026 - Internship Opportunity)
Job Summary:
As an Accounts Receivable Administrator, you will be supporting the posting of all payments to QBO, exporting, sending invoices, and uploading them to the customer portals. The AR position will support the collection of outstanding accounts receivable by contacting customers when invoices are past due to collect monies owed to the company. As a service-based organization, our customers need to receive the support they need via phone calls and emails.
Job Responsibilities:
Accounts Receivable:

  • Support export and send invoices to customers as required (Daily, Weekly, etc.)
  • Support receiving and recording payments from customers on account
  • Support for all EFT’s, e-transfers, and cheques
  • Support processing credit card payments
  • Help identify when customers are paying invoices for multiple companies and work with the person posting payments to coordinate the appropriate journal entries and bank transfer.
  • Help send statements every two weeks.
  • Upload invoices into Customer portals
  • Monitor emails on the AR inbox.

Collections:

  • Support in collecting overdue accounts to achieve established ratios as set by management, with a particular focus on the invoices 31 days past due or greater.

Customer Service:

  • In all aspects of our company, staff must be aware of how they influence the customer experience and be prepared to serve the customers with a positive and enthusiastic attitude.
  • Respond to customer queries and requests in a timely and courteous manner.
  • Proficient data entry with a high degree of accuracy.
  • Document management and filing.
  • Ability to stay organized and re-prioritize work as needed each day.

Other Duties:

  • Any other ad hoc-related duties required by the management.

Qualifications/Skills:

  • Being able to multitask
  • High Attention to Detail
  • Invoicing/Billing and collections experience
  • Payment Processing
  • Organized and strong time management
  • Able to work in a busy Team environment.
  • Excellent communication & problem-solving skills

Preferred Education and Experience :

  • An accounting degree or diploma is an asset
  • Fresh and Graduate students are welcome to apply
  • Microsoft Office 365
  • Understanding Accounting software and programs (QuickBooks Online)
  • Transportation Management Systems, i.e., Rose Rocket
  • Data Entry
