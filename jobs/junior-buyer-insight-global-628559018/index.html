Full Description

Role: Junior Buyer

Type: 6 months contract

Location: Onsite 5x/week in Cambridge, ON

Rate: $19-20/hr

DAY TO DAY

Insight Global is looking for a Junior buyer to join a leading automation company in Cambridge on a contract basis. This position will be within the Supply Chain Management department. Responsibilities include processing purchasing documents, tracking orders, and supporting sourcing strategies, all while adhering to ethical, safety, and ISO standards. The successful candidate should have a postsecondary diploma in purchasing or equivalent, one to three years of relevant experience and advanced skill in MS Office with a strong focus on excel.

MUST HAVES:

  • 1-3 years Supply Chain experience
  • Excellent with excel
  • Administrative task support (order confirmations, past dues, expedites etc. )
  • ERP system experience (JDE a plus)
