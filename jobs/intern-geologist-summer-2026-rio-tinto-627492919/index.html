About the role

We are offering a 4-month (May to August 2026) Geology internship as part of the Rio Tinto Exploration team. This opportunity will allow you to be involved in several geoscience activities. Please note that relocation to Vancouver is not required for this role; however, this role includes several field rotations at grassroots field programs in Canada, as well as a hybrid arrangement of remote and office work between home and Rio Tinto’s Thunder Bay and Vancouver offices.

In this role:

  • Field-based exploration, field reconnaissance.
  • Assist with geological and geotechnical core logging and sampling to collect pertinent data and ensure timely processing of samples.
  • Conduct field work related to geological mapping, prospecting and diamond drilling.
  • Work to high standards for quality assurance, quality control and data validation of for all geological data.

What you’ll bring:

  • At least one year completed* of Geology, Earth Sciences or Geological Engineering
  • Safety mindset
  • Leadership skills, eagerness to learn and ready for the challenge ahead
  • Flexibility and autonomy
  • Good communication skills and ability to work in a team
  • Valid driving license
  • To be eligible for this internship, you must have the right to work in Canada.

What we offer

  • A work environment where safety is always the number one priority
  • A competitive salary
  • Ambitious, exciting and rewarding experience
  • Opportunity to build relationships with the Rio Tinto team and community
  • A great chance to prepare yourself to join our Graduate Development Program

Where will you be working

This role includes several field rotations in the Northwest Territories and Eastern Canada; the team also offers flexibility with a hybrid arrangement of remote and office work between home and Rio Tinto’s Thunder Bay and Vancouver offices. All travel expenses incurred as a result of this internship will be covered by Rio Tinto.

About Rio Tinto

Rio Tinto is a leading global mining and materials company. We operate in 35 countries where we produce iron ore, copper, aluminium, critical minerals, and other materials needed for the global energy transition and for people, communities, and nations to thrive.

We have been mining for 150 years and operate with knowledge built up across generations and continents. Our purpose is finding better ways to provide the materials the world needs – striving for innovation and continuous improvement to produce materials with low emissions and to the right environmental, social and governance standards. But we can’t do it on our own, so we’re focused on creating partnerships to solve problems, create win-win situations and meet opportunities.

À propos de Rio Tinto

Rio Tinto est un chef de file mondial du secteur des sociétés minières et des matériaux. Nous sommes établis dans 35 pays où nous produisons du minerai de fer, du cuivre, de l’aluminium, des minéraux critiques et d’autres matériaux nécessaires à la transition énergétique mondiale et à la prospérité des personnes, des communautés et des nations.

Nous exerçons nos activités depuis 150 ans, en nous appuyant sur les connaissances accumulées au fil des générations et sur les différents continents. Notre mission – trouver de meilleures façons de fournir les matériaux dont le monde a besoin – nous guide dans notre quête d’innovation et d’amélioration continue, dans le but de fabriquer des produits à faibles émissions et répondant aux bonnes normes environnementales, sociales et de gouvernance.
