Full Description

Come Grow With Us at Massload

Massload Technologies is a well-established Canadian manufacturer and exporter of high-precision electronic weighing products, serving customers across North America and around the world. With over 40 years of experience and a reputation for quality, innovation, and reliability, we design and manufacture solutions that perform in demanding industrial environments.

Our continued growth has created an exciting opportunity for a Junior Mechanical Engineer to join our close-knit, high-performing engineering and manufacturing team. This role is ideal for someone who enjoys hands-on engineering, practical problem-solving, and seeing their designs move from concept through to finished product on the production floor.

This position reports to a Professional Engineer, who will actively support your Engineer-in-Training (EIT) development and progression toward P.Eng. designation.

Your Opportunity

As a Mechanical Engineer at Massload, you will be exposed to the full engineering lifecycle in a manufacturing environment. You will work on both R&D initiatives and standard product lines, collaborating closely with engineering, production, and machining teams.

This role offers meaningful responsibility early in your career, supported by mentorship, structured processes, and a culture that values learning, accountability, and continuous improvement.

Duties and Responsibilities

  • Assist with the design and development of load cells, load pins, and industrial weighing systems, including standard and custom design applications
  • Create and maintain accurate engineering drawings using SolidWorks under the supervision of a Professional Engineer
  • Support custom engineering projects by reviewing application requirements, load conditions, and environmental considerations to provide proposed solutions
  • Provide engineering support to CNC machining and fabrication processes, including drawing clarification, and part validation
  • Contribute to R&D and continuous improvement initiatives related to product performance, quality, and manufacturing efficiency
  • Work collaboratively with engineering, production, and sales teams to support project execution and delivery

  • Exposure to manufacturing, machining, or fabrication environments
  • Familiarity with SolidWorks, CNC machining, and LEAN manufacturing principles.
