About MASSLOAD TECHNOLOGIES INC

Canadian manufacturer; strong EIT progression toward P.Eng

Full Description

Come Grow With Us at Massload

Massload Technologies is a well-established Canadian manufacturer and exporter of high-precision electronic weighing products, serving customers across North America and around the world. With over 40 years of experience and a reputation for quality, innovation, and reliability, we design and manufacture solutions that perform in demanding industrial environments.

Our continued growth has created an exciting opportunity for a Junior Mechanical Engineer to join our close-knit, high-performing engineering and manufacturing team. This role is ideal for someone who enjoys hands-on engineering, practical problem-solving, and seeing their designs move from concept through to finished product on the production floor.

This position reports to a Professional Engineer, who will actively support your Engineer-in-Training (EIT) development and progression toward P.Eng. designation.

Your Opportunity

As a Mechanical Engineer at Massload, you will be exposed to the full engineering lifecycle in a manufacturing environment. You will work on both R&D initiatives and standard product lines, collaborating closely with engineering, production, and machining teams.

This role offers meaningful responsibility early in your career, supported by mentorship, structured processes, and a culture that values learning, accountability, and continuous improvement.

Duties and Responsibilities

  • Assist with the design and development of load cells, load pins, and industrial weighing systems, including standard and custom design applications
  • Create and maintain accurate engineering drawings using SolidWorks under the supervision of a Professional Engineer
  • Support custom engineering projects by reviewing application requirements, load conditions, and environmental considerations to provide proposed solutions
  • Provide engineering support to CNC machining and fabrication processes, including drawing clarification, and part validation
  • Contribute to R&D and continuous improvement initiatives related to product performance, quality, and manufacturing efficiency
  • Work collaboratively with engineering, production, and sales teams to support project execution and delivery

Qualifications & Attributes

Required

  • Bachelor’s degree in Mechanical Engineering (new graduates encouraged to apply).
  • Strong analytical and practical problem-solving skills in a hands-on design and manufacturing environment
  • Ability to take concepts from initial design through to finished product
  • Self-motivated, able to work independently, and eager to learn
  • Strong verbal and written English communication skills
  • Ability to work within established quality standards, processes, and protocols
  • Valid Saskatchewan Class 5 driver’s license
  • Must be a Canadian Citizen or Permanent Resident. Temporary Residents will not be considered for this full-time permanent position.

Assets

  • Demonstrated mechanical aptitude through hands-on projects, hobbies, or practical work (e.g., farm, agricultural, or real-world problem-solving)
  • Exposure to manufacturing, machining, or fabrication environments
  • Familiarity with SolidWorks, CNC machining, and LEAN manufacturing principles.

What We Offer

  • Full-time, permanent employment in a stable and growing company.
  • Direct mentorship and support from a Professional Engineer toward P.Eng. certification.
  • Competitive compensation commensurate with education and experience.
  • Comprehensive benefits package, including: Health, drug, dental, and vision care. Life insurance and short- and long-term disability, Group RRSP with employer matching contributions.
  • A collaborative, respectful, and values-driven workplace.
  • Opportunities for long-term career growth in engineering, R&D, and manufacturing leadership.

Our Values

At Massload, our work is guided by Integrity, Honor, and Stewardship. We believe in doing things right, taking responsibility for our work, and supporting one another as a team. Communication, accountability, and professionalism are central to how we operate—on the shop floor, in engineering, and with our customers.

About us

With over 40 years of experience, Massload Technologies is a well-established and respected manufacturer and exporter of cutting-edge electronic weighing products, serving customers across North America and around the world, as we consistently deliver high-quality solutions that meet the evolving needs of our customers.

At Massload, our bedrock is built on three core values: INTEGRITY, HONOR, and STEWARDSHIP. These principles are the compass guiding our interactions with both our dedicated employees and valued customers. We hold honesty, faithfulness, and professionalism as our cornerstones, ensuring that every interaction reflects our unwavering commitment to ethical business conduct and integrity.

Communication is at the heart of our operations, where transparency, timeliness, and effectiveness are not just ideals but living practices. We are a team, working together to achieve our goals, fostering a positive and upbeat atmosphere where enthusiasm and positivity are embraced. Personal responsibility and accountability are championed, underlining our belief that each member of our team plays a pivotal role in our collective mission.

We believe in honoring the uniqueness of every individual who contributes to our company's success. At Massload Technologies, you are more than an employee; you are part of a community where your contributions are recognized and valued.

We invite you to join us in our new, purpose-built manufacturing facility and be a vital part of our journey as we continue to innovate and excel in the field of electronic weighing products.
