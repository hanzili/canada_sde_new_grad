About Superasia Global Inc

Full Description

Job Overview
We are seeking a detail-oriented and organized Assistant Bookkeeper to join our team. The ideal candidate will support our financial operations by maintaining accurate financial records, processing transactions, and providing excellent customer service. This position is essential to ensuring the smooth and efficient functioning of our accounting processes.

Responsibilities

  • General administrative support (filing, data entry, answering calls/emails)
  • Assist with day-to-day bookkeeping tasks
  • Maintain accurate records and documents
  • Help prepare invoices, receipts, and reports
  • Support other team members as needed

Qualifications

  • Basic knowledge of Microsoft Office (Word, Excel, Outlook)
  • Familiarity with bookkeeping or accounting software is an asset (e.g., QuickBooks)
  • Strong attention to detail and organizational skills
  • Good communication and multi-tasking skills
  • Bilingual in Mandarin and English (spoken and written)
  • Willingness to learn and take initiative

If you enjoy working with numbers, take pride in organization, and like supporting a collaborative team, we would love to hear from you. This is a great opportunity to grow your skills in a supportive and professional environment.
