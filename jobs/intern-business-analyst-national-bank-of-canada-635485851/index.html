About the Role

Map processes, document requirements, support implementation

About National Bank of Canada

National Bank of Canada; reputable financial institution

Full Description

Attendance: Hybrid

Employment Category: Professional

Type of Contract: Internship

Benefits for vendors:

A career as a Business Analyst within the Hyper-Automation team at National Bank means acting as a liaison between the Bank’s various business teams and delivery teams. More specifically, you will have the opportunity to develop your business analysis skills by working on existing processes in order to understand them, analyze them, and propose innovative solutions tailored to their realities, while ensuring that current ways of working are optimized and challenged.

As part of your internship, you will also become familiar with the various hyper-automation tools used at the Bank (Intelligent Document Processing (IDP), Low Code, ServiceNow, etc.).

In this role, your process expertise, curiosity, and interpersonal skills (influence, collaboration, leadership) become concrete levers to accelerate your growth and impact.

Your Role

  • Perform high-level and detailed impact analyses covering all aspects of processes, with the support of subject-matter experts (people, processes, technology, and data)
  • Map and validate business processes
  • Document functional and non-functional requirements (role management, permissions, data access, availability, recovery)
  • Work closely with IT teams by providing detailed requirement documentation for implementation by development teams
  • Define, identify, and execute user test scenarios
  • Know and apply best practices for identifying, articulating, classifying, and writing business requirements, stakeholder requirements, and solution requirements (functional, non-functional, informational, transitional); understand the relationships between them and ensure traceability

Your Team

Within the Corporate IT sector, you will report to the Lead Product Owner. Our team stands out for its dynamism and strong focus on innovation and autonomy. We aim to offer maximum flexibility to support your quality of life, notably through a hybrid work environment and flexible, adaptable working hours.

Prerequisites

  • Currently enrolled in a university program
  • Knowledge of the business analyst role
  • Ability to support, influence, and mobilize partners
  • Ability to think strategically, with strong analytical, synthesis, and communication skills
  • Knowledge of business process improvement and mapping: modeling, Lean approach, and impact analysis
  • Knowledge of Agile methodologies (Scrum, Kanban, SAFe) and Atlassian tools (JIRA, Confluence, etc.) an asset

Your benefits
In addition to competitive compensation, you’ll be eligible for a wide range of flexible benefits to help promote your wellbeing and that of your family.

  • Health and wellness program, including many options
  • Group insurance
  • Generous pension plan
  • Employee and Family Assistance Program
  • Preferential banking services
  • Opportunities to get involved in community initiatives
  • Telemedicine service
  • Virtual sleep clinic

These are a few of the benefits available to you. We have an offer that keeps up with trends as well as your needs and those of your family.

Our dynamic work environments and cutting-edge collaboration tools foster a positive employee experience. We actively listen to employees’ ideas. Whether through our surveys or programs, regular feedback and ongoing communication is encouraged.

We're putting people first
We're a bank on a human scale that stands out for its courage, entrepreneurial culture, and passion for people. Our mission is to have a positive impact on peoples' lives. Our core values of partnership, agility, and empowerment inspire us, and inclusivity is central to our commitments. We offer a barrier-free workplace that is accessible to all employees.

We want our recruitment process to be fully accessible. If you require accommodation, feel free to let us know during your first conversations with us. We welcome all candidates! What can you bring to our team?
