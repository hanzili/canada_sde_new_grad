Full Description

This job is with Fitch Group, an inclusive employer and a member of myGwork – the largest global platform for the LGBTQ+ business community. Please do not contact the recruiter directly. As one of the world’s top three credit ratings agencies, Fitch Ratings plays a critical role in global capital markets by providing credit analysis, ratings, research, and commentary to financial market participants. For over 100 years, Fitch Ratings has been creating value for global markets through its rigorous analysis and deep expertise, which have resulted in a variety of market-leading tools, methodologies, indices, research, and analytical products. Fitch Ratings is part of Fitch Group, a global leader in financial information services with operations in more than 30 countries, which also includes Fitch Solutions. With dual headquarters in London and New York, Fitch Group is owned by Hearst.

Fitch Ratings is currently seeking an AI Associate, based out of our London office. About The Team You will join our innovative Ratings AI team, which focuses on ideating, planning, and testing AI solutions to enhance Fitch Ratings’ workflows and processes. The team is highly collaborative and partner-facing, with strong visibility across the organization as we scale practical AI capabilities that drive measurable business outcomes.

How You’ll Make An Impact

  • Develop and refine AI prototypes and lightweight interfaces, with mentorship from senior team members
  • Explore and apply machine learning algorithms and data analytics to real-world business problems and datasets
  • Code, test, and debug AI prototypes to ensure robust, reliable, and efficient performance
  • Engage with stakeholders to understand needs, translate requirements, and deliver tailored AI solutions
  • Monitor and enhance AI systems using performance metrics, evaluation methods, and feedback loops
