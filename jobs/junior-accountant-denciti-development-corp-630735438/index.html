About DenCiti Development Corp.

Full Description

Company
Denciti Development Corp. is seeking a Junior Accountant to join our finance team. This is a full-time, in-office position based in our Downtown Vancouver office, offering strong potential for growth and advancement.

Job Summary
If you are detail-oriented, organized, proactive, and enjoy working with numbers, this is an excellent opportunity to build your career in a supportive and fast-paced environment. This role is integral to maintaining the financial accuracy and operational stability of the organization. We are seeking a dedicated professional who values accuracy, confidentiality, and continuous improvement in accounting practices.

In this role, you will support day-to-day financial operations, including full-cycle accounts payable and general bookkeeping functions. From invoice processing and vendor setup to bank and general ledger reconciliations, you will play a key role in ensuring smooth and reliable financial processes while working closely with internal teams and external partners.

Duties

Accounts Payable

  • Receive, sort, and enter invoices and supporting documents into accounting software
  • Review and enter employee expense claims
  • Maintain vendor database and communication
  • Prepare payment reports and monthly payment runs
  • Support improvements in processes and systems

Bookkeeping

  • Prepare journal entries
  • Assist in maintaining supporting schedules to financial statements
  • Prepare monthly bank reconciliations
  • Maintain intercompany invoices
  • Assist in monthly GST filing and payments Manage cheque deposits
  • Assist with month end and year end duties
  • Assist with preparation of audit information and support
  • File and maintain financial documents
  • Handle special projects, responsibilities and other duties as assigned by management

Requirements

  • Basic experience in accounts payable and bookkeeping, ideally with 0-1 years of experience
  • Bachelor’s or Diploma degree in accounting, finance, or a related field. CPA candidate or interest in pursuing the CPA designation is preferred
  • Proficiency in Microsoft Office 365 (Word, Excel, PowerPoint, Teams)
  • Strong attention to detail with a high level of accuracy
  • Strong numerical aptitude and efficient data entry skills
  • Ability to prioritize and manage multiple tasks with competing deadlines
  • Ability to interact regularly with staff and management to provide or obtain information
  • High level of professionalism, discretion, and confidentiality
  • Strong decision-making, communication (written and verbal), and analytical skills
  • Organized self-starter with the ability to work independently
  • Strong interpersonal skills and ability to contribute effectively within a team environment

Compensation and Benefits

  • Salary Range of $45,000 - $55,000 (commensurate with experience), plus discretionary bonus
  • Benefit and vacation package
  • Opportunities for career growth and advancement
  • Engaging and collaborative work environment
  • Meaningful work on impactful projects
  • Continuous learning and professional development opportunities

Please submit your resume, and two references to careers@denciti.ca. Please indicate "Junior Accountant" in the subject line. All applicants must have valid work permits for Canada, and pass a background check, and be available to start within a month.
