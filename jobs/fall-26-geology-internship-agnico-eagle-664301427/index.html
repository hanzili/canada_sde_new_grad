WHAT DOES AGNICO EAGLE VALUE?

At Agnico Eagle, our values never waver. We believe in trust, respect, equity, family and responsibility. Why? Because they express who we are, and they have helped us succeed in business for over 60 years. We value you – your unique set of skills and experience. We value your willingness to build those skills and make a meaningful contribution to our company. And, we value your commitment to treat every person on our team with respect, helping us to operate safely, protect the environment and make a positive contribution to our local communities.

Unlock Your Potential with Agnico Eagle's Internship Program

An internship at Agnico Eagle is more than a learning opportunity—it's a defining experience.

Here, we nurture your individual growth, encouraging you to develop your skills and carve out your unique path within our organization. Your dedication to excellence is not just valued; it's celebrated as you make a tangible impact on our operations, sustainability initiatives and communities we operate in.

ABOUT OUR OPERATION

Committed to Responsible Mining Practices

Agnico Eagle stands at the forefront of the Canadian and global gold mining industry, as the third largest gold producer in the world, with operating mines in Canada, Australia, Finland, and Mexico. Our commitment to sustainability has garnered global recognition for our efforts and practices in environmental stewardship, health and safety, social responsibility and governance. Join us and be a part of shaping a greener future where our core values of Family, Trust, Respect, Responsibility and Equity continue to be at the heart of everything we do.

YOUR NEXT CHALLENGE:

Reporting to the Senior Exploration Geologist, the Geology Intern is part of the Geology Department. You will ensure that the goals and objectives are achieved while promoting and respecting Agnico Eagle’s values, Health & Safety Code of Conduct and the environment.

  • Drill, blast and excavation monitoring in regard to the mine geology grade control standards;
  • Logging core
  • Any other tasks that the senior grade control and/or production geologist might identify.

WHAT YOU NEED TO SUCCEED

  • Student must be currently enrolled in an undergraduate or technical program in any kind of Geosciences, Geological Engineering or related fields;
  • Students must be enrolled in a formal Co-op program.
  • A strong ability to work independently on assigned tasks as well as a member of a team is required.
  • Strong work ethic with the ability to handle change.
  • Demonstrate a high level of energy, self-motivation, and strong organizational skills.
  • Strong communication skills including verbal and written as well as presentation skills required.
  • Promote a safety mindset and approach to ensure that all projects and activities carried out comply with safety regulations and AEM standards.

Your Work Schedule:

  • Schedule: 14 days of work, followed by 14 days for transportation and rest;
  • Accommodations and meals are provided on site at no cost to the employee. Agnico Eagle provides charter flights to and from the mine site and will accommodate to cover other travelling expenses according to your location of residence in Canada.

Be a part of it. Be proud of it. Be Agnico Eagle.

We look forward to having you onboard, and while we sincerely appreciate all applications, only those candidates selected for an interview will be contacted directly by a member of our team.
