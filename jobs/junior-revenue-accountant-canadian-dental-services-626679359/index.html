About the Role

About Canadian Dental Services

Growing Canadian dental services firm; strong growth

Full Description

Corporate Head Office, Downtown Toronto (Bloor and Yonge) Visit us:

https://www.canadiandentalservices.ca/

Who we are?

Canadian Dental Services (CDS) proudly supports some of the best dental offices and professionals across Canada in Alberta, Ontario and Saskatchewan!

CDS offers employees a wealth of challenging and rewarding career opportunities. We are a successful and fast-growing company that values diversity and innovative thinking and fosters a culture of performance and accountability. We invest in the growth and development of our employees by offering a wide range of career paths, comprehensive training, and career development opportunities.

What are we looking for?

We are looking for a passionate and driven Junior Accountant with at least 2 years of full cycle accounting experience. If you are looking to grow your career with a rapidly growing organization and work collaboratively with a team of well-experienced finance professionals, you will thrive in this role. You will be focusing on processing accounts payable and other general accounting duties.

What’s in it for you?

  • Safe and comfortable office environment with a focus on health and safety
  • Career enhancement opportunities (Certifications and professional development)
  • Diverse and Inclusive team of expert, talented and dedicated professionals

Key accountabilities

Accounts Payable:

  • Prepares monthly PMS reports
  • Prepares weekly clinic deposit reconciliation, monthly Production, AR, DR, Refund, SPP, Align Tech DSP reconciliations
  • Maintains a record of all relevant backups associated with revenue cycle, bank reconciliation, PAD bills and scan logs
  • Follow up with clinics for outstanding deposits on a weekly basis and alert accounting manager on unresolved issues
  • Prepares accurate monthly bank reconciliations and coordinate with treasury analyst and AP to ensure complete posting of intercompany transfers and PAD payments
  • Maintains accounting controls and exercise due diligence on potential fraud and misappropriation of funds
  • Reconcile financial discrepancies by collecting and analyzing account information
  • Completes assigned audit tasks within prescribed due dates
  • Supports Edge Ortho 2 migration

Other General Accounting Duties

  • Assist with post acquisition accounts payable cycle transition and integration
  • Reconciling vendor statements,
  • Work with Treasury Analyst to prepare weekly disbursement and payable requirement
  • Assist AP supervisor on associate pay booking and reconciliation
  • Assist with processing contractor timesheet and payments
  • Other special projects assigned from time to time,

What do you bring?

  • Degree in Accounting / Business or a related field
  • 2+ years’ experience in a relevant role
  • Strong account reconciliation skills
  • Excellent communication abilities and organizational skills
  • Proficient in problem solving
  • Intermediate MS Excel skills
  • Knowledge of MS Office Suite and NetSuite
  • Adaptable to new Practice Management Systems

Job details and work days:

This position located in Downtown Toronto. This is an amazing opportunity to support an environment of collaboration, care and success and as such the successful incumbent should be enthusiastic about working in the office with flexibility.
