Job description

Canuck Door Systems is a Mississauga-based commercial door systems company. We are a premier supplier and servicer of glass aluminum storefront door systems, metal doors, overhead doors and dock systems. Canuck Door Systems is a one-stop shop for all our clients’ door needs.

RESPONSIBILITIES:

Canuck Door Systems is looking for a Coordinator/ Estimator /Customer Service Representative who will work closely with customers, salespersons and lead technicians to coordinate job orders, create sales estimates, source materials and parts required for ongoing jobs:

Estimating and Purchasing

  • Prepare a detailed cost estimate of materials and labour for the Salespersons based on the customer’s requests, take-offs, or/and tenders' requirements.
  • Prepare accurate quotes on spare parts and materials for Service Calls
  • Prepare quotation requests for suppliers and obtain quotations.
  • Prepare production specifications for final approval
  • Prepare shop drawings for final approval
  • Prepare and submit purchase orders
  • Follow up on purchase orders and estimated delivery time

Project Coordination and Customer Service

  • Receive, schedule and dispatch Job Orders
  • Coordinate jobs with customers, installaers and service technicians
  • Obtain customer feedback and review
  • Collect payments
  • Prepare Installation hardware
  • Receive shipment
  • Maintain Warehouse

REQUIREMENTS:

All Candidates Must

  • Have a Civil Engineering Technology or Construction Management diploma
  • Have excellent organizational skills
  • Be able to understand project documents and blueprints to create accurate estimates quickly
  • Have strong attention to details
  • Have methodical skills in filing and record-keeping with all relevant project documentation
  • Be able to work under high pressure to meet deadlines
  • Have proficient computer skills. Having experience working with G Suite applications (Gmail, Hangouts, Google Docs and Google Sheets) is an asset.
  • Be Self-motivated, professional and flexible; able to work both independently and as part of a team
  • Have basic AutoCAD knowledge and skills
  • Have excellent communication skills and customer servivce expirience

Education

  • Bachelor's Degree or Advanced Diploma in Civil Engineering or Construction Management. University or College.

COMPENSATION

  • Salary will be commensurate with experience, and Canuck Door Systems offers competitive salaries and benefits.

BENEFITS:

  • Vacation & paid time off
  • Bonus scheme
  • Subsidized commuter solution
  • Benefits
