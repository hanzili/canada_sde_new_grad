Full Description

Northern Roads Auto Group is more than just a business, a collection of dealerships or rooftops, it’s a place where our family has the privilege to serve yours.

Location: 658 Dundas Street West, Belleville, ON

Availability: Open - Monday to Friday

Compensation: $20-25 per hour

When you join Northern Roads, you’ll enjoy:

  • Trusted relationships – We’re a work family and you’ll become a part of the team.
  • Many employee engagement activities – We want you to want to come into work every day.
  • Employee Health and Wellness Initiatives – We truly care about your well-being.
  • Career path planning and training – We want you to succeed and grow alongside us.
  • There are plenty of other perks and incentives, you’ll have to join us to find out more!

You’ll love your role as a Junior Accountant & Administrative Assistant because no two days are the same and you’ll be right at the heart of the action.

What you’ll be doing (aka where you shine):

  • Owning accounts payable postings, reconciliations, and payments for your assigned location
  • Making sure all deal paperwork is accurate, complete, and properly posted
  • Keeping month-end on track by ensuring all deals are received before cut-off
  • Preparing and reconciling balance sheet schedules like a pro
  • Organizing and filing financial documents so everything is exactly where it should be
  • Being a friendly first point of contact—answering phones and greeting customers
  • Jumping in on other accounting and administrative tasks as needed (teamwork makes the dream work)

We’d love to hear from you if you:

  • Have a Bachelor’s degree in Accounting, Finance, Commerce, or Business Administration
  • Bring 2+ years of hands-on accounting experience
  • Have dealership experience (nice-to-have, not a must)
  • Are pursuing—or planning to pursue—a CPA designation
  • Have strong MS Excel skills and aren’t afraid of spreadsheets
  • Work well independently and thrive in a team environment
  • Are flexible and understand that month-end and year-end can mean extra hustle
  • Are detail-oriented, organized, and quick on your feet
  • Hold a valid G driver’s license with a clean abstract

More About Us:

At Northern Roads Auto Group, we’ve proudly operated successful, family-owned automotive businesses for over 30 years in the heart of the Niagara Region. We’re passionate about growth, innovation, and delivering exceptional customer experiences.

As one of the first Kia dealers in Canada and a multi–Kia President’s Award-winning group, we continue to expand our footprint with the addition of Lindsay Kia (2023), Belleville Chrysler Dodge Jeep RAM FIAT (2024), and most recently, Fort Erie Toyota (2025).

Our success is built on integrity, innovation, and community—a commitment that drives everything we do.
