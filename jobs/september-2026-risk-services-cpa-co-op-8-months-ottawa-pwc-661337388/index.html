Meaningful work you’ll be part of

Throughout your co-op term, you will support the delivery of internal audit and internal controls (including SOX) services for clients across a variety of industries. Working alongside experienced team members, you’ll help teams understand business processes, identify risks, document controls, assist with controls testing and data analytics . You’ll gain exposure to how strong governance, risk management, and controls help organizations protect value, navigate disruption, and make confident decisions , using evolving methods and enabling technologies.

Your Responsibilities Will Include:

  • Performing your work efficiently while meeting established timelines and budgets.

  • Supporting process and controls work across the 3 lines (e.g., process documentation, walkthrough support, risk and control identification).

  • Assisting with internal control testing procedures (design and operating effectiveness), including preparing testing documentation and organizing evidence.

  • Clearly and concisely documenting work performed and evidence gathered; validating completeness, relevance, and quality of support.

  • Flagging potential control gaps, exceptions, or documentation issues to senior team members and helping track follow-up items.

  • Maintain ongoing communication with engagement leaders to ensure clarity, quality, and proper follow ‑ up of deliverables.

  • Building and maintaining collaborative relationships with internal teams and clients.

  • Staying up to date on trends in governance, risk management, internal audit, regulation, and emerging technologies/analytics as they relate to controls and assurance.

  • Supporting the creation of client-ready materials (e.g., process narratives/flowcharts, test plans, status trackers, and summary slides).

  • Identifying opportunities to promote the firm’s services to new clients or deepen existing client relationships.
