Full Description

At Manulife John Hancock, we believe in investing in the future – starting with you. Our Accounting & Finance Co-op is your launchpad to success, offering the support, structure, and exciting opportunities you need to thrive and make your mark.

Are you on the path to earning your CPA designation? Our Accounting and Finance Co-op Program, available at our Toronto office, provides invaluable hands-on experience and serves as a direct pathway to our CPA Development Program. You'll gain technical expertise, business insight, and leadership skills while collaborating with dedicated accounting professionals in a supportive and nurturing environment. This opportunity is ideal for undergraduate students eager to acquire real-world experience in accounting and finance.

Available Roles for the Fall 2026 Term: Accounting Analyst, General Accounting, Corporate Tax, Financial Planning & Analysis Transformation, Private Markets, and WAM Legal Entity Reporting.

Please note that your application will be considered for multiple positions within this job posting.

Position Responsibilities:

  • Provide support and handle aspects of reporting while understanding monthly and quarterly deliverables.
  • Prepare monthly reports including analysis related to job functions.
  • Meet with leaders to review submissions and highlight possible risks and opportunities while taking initiative to escalate issues impacting reporting functions.
  • Support Month-end production and reporting work, including journal entries, budgets, adjustments, Actuals to Budget analysis, consolidating Reporting packages.
  • Gain proficiency in investigating financial ledgers, statements, and journals using various software tools and Excel.
  • Acquire expertise in accounting practices, including reconciling, and analyzing balance sheet and income statement accounts, all while adhering to corporate standards.
  • Lead the continuous improvements of existing reporting processes.

Required Qualifications:

  • Currently pursuing an undergraduate degree in Business or Accounting, with a strong interest in Finance and Economics and a solid foundation in general Business principles.
  • Strong proficiency in Microsoft Excel and Power BI, along with exceptional mathematical, analytical, and problem-solving skills.
  • Flexible and adaptable, capable of working both independently and as part of a team while exercising sound judgment.
  • Exhibits meticulous attention to detail, effectively prioritizing tasks in a deadline-driven environment.
  • Ability to identify and recommend improvements to streamline existing processes.
  • Supports the development and implementation of new processes.
  • Responds to challenges and opportunities with a positive and productive attitude.
  • Excellent interpersonal, organizational, and communication skills.
  • Knowledge of Access and Visual Basic is required for some roles.

When you join our team:

  • We’ll empower you to learn and grow the career you want.
  • We’ll recognize and support you in a flexible environment where well-being and inclusion are more than just words.
  • As part of our global team, we’ll support you in shaping the future you want to see.
  • As part of our co-op program, you will gain early access to Manulife John Hancock’s premier early talent program, GRO, tailored to graduating students! This program is designed to launch your career with the support, structure, and opportunities you need to thrive.
