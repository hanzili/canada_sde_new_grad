Business Analyst (Summer Student)

at University Health Network

📍 Location Toronto, Ontario
📋 Details Temporary Full-time · 16 weeks · $20/hour · May–August 2026
📅 Posted Mar 07, 2026

About the Role

Analyze marketing, outreach; translate insights into actions

About University Health Network

Canada's #1 hospital, world-leading research

Full Description

Company Description UHN is Canada’s #1 hospital and the world’s #1 publicly funded hospital. With 10 sites and more than 44,000 TeamUHN members, UHN consists of Toronto General Hospital, Toronto Western Hospital, Princess Margaret Cancer Centre, Toronto Rehabilitation Institute, The Michener Institute of Education and West Park Healthcare Centre. As Canada's top research hospital, the scope of biomedical research and complexity of cases at UHN have made it a national and international source for discovery, education and patient care. UHN has the largest hospital-based research program in Canada, with major research in neurosciences, cardiology, transplantation, oncology, surgical innovation, infectious diseases, genomic medicine and rehabilitation medicine. UHN is a research hospital affiliated with the University of Toronto.

UHN’s vision is to build A Healthier World and it’s only because of the talented and dedicated people who work here that we are continually bringing that vision closer to reality.

www.uhn.ca

UHN Medical Solutions is a division of Altum Health focused on commercial revenue generation. It includes a number of revenue generating programs for UHN, including the UHN International Patient Program, Patient Ambassador Program, Virtual Urgent Care Services, and other private pay patient services. UHN MS supports UHN’s Vision of a Healthier World while generating ancillary revenue that is reinvested back into UHN for the benefit of OHIP patients and Ontario health system stakeholders.

Job Description Union: Non-Union

Number of Vacancies: 1

New or Replacement Position: New

Site: Krembil Discovery Tower (Toronto Western Hospital)

Department: UHN Medical Solutions

Reports to: Senior Program Manager

Wage Range: $20 per hour

Hours: 35 Hours Per Week

Shifts: Days, Monday-Friday

Status: Temporary Full-time (16-week contract, May - August 2026)

Closing Date: March 20, 2026 Reporting to the Senior Program Manager, the Business Analyst (Summer Practicum) will support a comprehensive needs assessment focused on strengthening marketing, business development, and patient‑facing engagement strategies. This role is ideal for a student with interests in health communication, digital experience, service design, and strategic planning.

The student will analyze current marketing and outreach practices, assess gaps, review competitor positioning, evaluate website content and structure, and contribute to developing a new concierge‑style patient support model. The role requires strong analytical skills, curiosity, and the ability to translate qualitative and quantitative insights into actionable recommendations.

Qualifications Applicants must be currently enrolled in an accredited Master's program to be considered for this position.
