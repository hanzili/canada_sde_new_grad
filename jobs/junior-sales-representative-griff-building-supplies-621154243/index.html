Full Description

JUNIOR CONTRACTOR SALES REPRESENTATIVE

Job purpose

The Junior Contract Sales Representative supports senior outside sales representatives in maintaining a high level of customer service through the accurate preparation of quotes and sales orders. For individuals interested in sales advancement, the role can serve as a foundation for potential future growth into an outside sales role.

Duties and Responsibilities

  • Preparing quotes for assigned outside sales representative.

  • Entering orders as requested by the assigned sales representative.

  • Assisting store staff with incoming calls/customers.
  • Providing coverage for store staff on the sales counter when required.
  • Monitoring and responding to emails in a timely manner.
  • Maintaining a tidy, organized work area.
  • Processing cash sales and charge account orders.
  • Client satisfaction:
  • Striving for 100% prompt customer satisfaction and service
  • Increasing/improving account relationships through direct phone and in person communication
  • Problem solving & dispute resolution.
  • Participation in product knowledge sessions, supplier tours and other forms of training for continued development of industry and product knowledge.
  • Reviewing and processing returns/refunds.
  • Assisting with procurement of specialty products.
  • Managing a diverse clientele with the utmost respect and superior customer service.
  • Responding to requests from outside sales representatives.
  • Updating stock-owed spreadsheet as required.
  • Providing written and verbal quotes in a professional and timely manner (with guidance from the assigned rep).
  • Strong organizational skills
  • Ability to work independently and as part of a team
  • A basic understanding of building materials and their applications
  • Strong written and verbal communication skills

Competencies

  • Strong organizational skills
  • Ability to work independently and as part of a team
  • A basic understanding of building materials and their applications
  • Strong written and verbal communication skills

Working Conditions

This is a full time, in-office position.
