Full Description

Job Title: Sales Associate (FinTech)

Location: Downtown Toronto, Canada

Empower Businesses with Global Financial Solutions

We are a global FinTech leader that helps over 800,000 business clients effectively manage foreign exchange and cross-border payments. We are seeking an extremely driven, strategic thinker to join our team in the heart of the Toronto financial district. This is a merit-based structure where your potential can truly unfold.

Key Responsibilities & Impact:

You will be engaging with high-level contacts to identify opportunities where our expertise in currency risk management can provide comprehensive tools and strategies.

  • Lead Generation: Generate new leads using cold outreach methods.
  • High-Level Dialogues: Engage finance leaders to articulate customized global payment solutions.
  • Career Building: Utilize the Sales/Capital Markets training and career progression plans to move into more senior roles.

Why Choose This Path?

  • Top Earnings: Base salary of CAD $62.5K with uncapped commission .
  • Culture: Experience a unique blend of collaboration, resources, and opportunity.
  • Prestige: Work for a publicly traded, S&P 1000 member company .
  • Benefits : 19 days PTO . Sales/Capital Markets training. Annual Sales Conference Offsite . Medical, Dental & RRSP contribution!

Qualifications:

  • Education : Completion of a Bachelor's Degree is a prerequisite.
  • Performance Mindset : You are highly competitive and intensely focused on achieving results. You are success-hungry and extremely driven.
  • Essential Sales Skills : You are articulate, persuasive, and possess excellent negotiation skills. Crucially, you are coachable —you understand and quickly implement feedback to refine your sales process.
  • Industry Interest : Demonstrate a keen interest in diving into the technical field of cross-border payments and FX.
