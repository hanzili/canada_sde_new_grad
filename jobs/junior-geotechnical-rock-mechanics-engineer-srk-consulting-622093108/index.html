About the Role

Conduct geotechnical logging and rock mechanics analyses

About SRK Consulting

Geotechnical consulting leader in mining projects

Full Description

We are seeking a Junior Geotechnical/Rock Mechanics Engineer to be based in our Vancouver, BC office.

The successful candidate will consult on projects for North American and international clients, requiring travel. Assignments will include geotechnical characterization and applying rock mechanics principles to both surface and underground mine design issues. This work will involve full project scopes from conceptual studies to the detailed design and construction stage, as well as to the mine closure stage, liaising with other technical specialists.

  • A degree in geological, mining or geotechnical engineering.
  • 1-3 years of relevant experience.
  • Practical experience in geotechnical logging, mapping and rock mass characterization will be a benefit.
  • Some understanding of basic operating skills in 2D/3D geological/geotechnical numerical modelling packages will be advantageous.
  • The ability to work in a team under the leadership of a Senior/Principal Consultant.
  • Valid driver’s license
  • Based in Vancouver or ability to relocate.

This position involves working in multi-disciplinary teams on a range of projects and may include international travel.

The estimated base pay range for this position is $75,000 to 85,000. The salary offered will be commensurate with relevant skills, knowledge, and experience.
