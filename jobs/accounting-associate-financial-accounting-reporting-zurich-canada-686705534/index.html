Full Description

The Opportunity

Are you looking for a caring, collaborative, values-driven workplace with inspiring teammates and leaders? Do you have the ambition and desire to be the best and thrive at the most impactful global insurance provider in the world? Look no further than Zurich Canada.

If you have experience in accounting, finance, or financial reporting and are looking for a new challenge, we would love to hear from you.

Zurich Canada is currently looking for an Accounting Associate, Financial Accounting and Reporting to support the preparation, analysis, and reporting of financial information while contributing to key accounting operations and reporting activities.

Reporting to the Financial Accounting and Reporting Manager, you will support day-to-day accounting and financial reporting processes, assist with general ledger activities, prepare reconciliations and journal entries, and contribute to the timely and accurate completion of financial reports. You will collaborate with internal stakeholders across finance and business functions to ensure compliance with accounting standards, company policies, and reporting requirements. This role offers an excellent opportunity to build foundational accounting and reporting expertise within a global insurance organization while working in a collaborative and supportive environment.

This is a unique opportunity to build your knowledge and experience for the future in a supportive environment where your voice matters. This posting is for an existing vacancy.

Zurich Canada uses artificial intelligence–enabled tools to support certain aspects of the recruitment process, including the initial review and screening of applications. Artificial intelligence is not the sole basis for candidate shortlisting or selection. All hiring decisions are reviewed and made by qualified hiring professionals.

Zurich follows a hybrid work model requiring three days per week of in-person presence, which may include time in the office or market-facing engagements.

What you will do

  • Support monthly, quarterly, and annual financial close activities
  • Prepare journal entries, account reconciliations, and supporting documentation
  • Assist with financial reporting and general ledger maintenance
  • Review and analyze financial data to identify discrepancies and ensure accuracy
  • Collaborate with internal teams to gather information and resolve accounting-related inquiries
  • Support internal and external audit requests by preparing required documentation
  • Assist with process improvement initiatives and financial reporting projects
  • Maintain compliance with accounting standards, internal controls, and company policies
  • Utilize financial systems and reporting tools to support accounting operations
  • Perform additional accounting and reporting responsibilities as assigned

Job Qualifications - What You Bring To The Table Required:

  • Bachelors Degree and no prior experience required in the Accounting or Finance area

OR

  • High School Diploma or Equivalent and 2 or more years of experience in the Accounting or Finance area

Preferred:

  • Knowledge of accounting principles
  • Basic general ledger transactional experience
  • Intermediate Microsoft Office skills
  • Technology experience with general ledger and financial reporting systems
  • Strong verbal and written communication skills
