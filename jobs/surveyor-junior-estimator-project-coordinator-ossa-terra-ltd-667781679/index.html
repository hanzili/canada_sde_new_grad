About the Role

Survey, estimate, and coordinate civil projects

About Ossa Terra Ltd

Local earthworks firm; diverse field & office roles

Full Description

Ossa Terra Ltd. is an earthworks company based in Lethbridge, Alberta.

We are seeking a Surveyor / Junior Estimator / Project Coordinator to take on a multi-faceted role supporting survey, estimating, and project coordination functions within our civil and earthmoving operations.

This hands-on role is suited to someone with practical field survey experience who is looking to expand their career. This position involves a mix of office and field responsibilities.

If you are looking to grow and develop your capabilities within an earthworks company, Ossa Terra offers the opportunity to build those skills in a diversified team-oriented environment.

Key Responsibilities

Survey & Field Support (Core Function)

  • Set up and localize job sites from control files or coordinate lists
  • Perform layout for grades, utilities, and control points
  • Grade checking and verification of elevations and alignments
  • Work directly with operators to achieve required depths and final surfaces
  • Read and interpret drawings, plans, and specifications
  • Prepare and manage survey data (including AutoCAD/Civil 3D)
  • Conduct topographical surveys and quantity measurements
  • Maintain and care for survey equipment, including minor troubleshooting
  • Communicate with site personnel and clients as required

Estimating (Earthworks Focus)

  • Assist in identifying and evaluating project opportunities
  • Obtain and compare pricing from suppliers and subcontractors
  • Review quotes, terms, and conditions
  • Support quantity takeoffs and production-based estimating
  • Research haul distances, material sources, and production rates
  • Assist with tender preparation, submissions, and prequalification requirements
  • Support internal coordination and communication during bid preparation

Project Coordination (Supporting Role)

  • Assist with document control and project documentation
  • Support scheduling, progress tracking, and cost monitoring
  • Assist with contract and change order documentation
  • Support procurement, submittals, and progress tracking
  • Participate in internal and external project meetings
  • Maintain communication with project teams, consultants, and clients

Requirements

  • Bachelor’s degree or diploma in Civil Engineering Technology, Construction, or related field
  • 2+ years of hands-on experience in earthworks or civil construction
  • Experience with Topcon, AutoCAD Civil 3D, and Microsoft Office
  • Comfortable working in a fast-paced, deadline-driven environment
  • Working knowledge of grading, excavation, and site development practices with exposure to surveying and grade control.
  • Strong attention to detail and ability to work accurately under tight deadlines
  • Strong critical thinking, problem-solving, and organizational skills
  • Effective written and verbal communication skills
  • Ability to work independently, in a team environment, and overtime if required
  • Comfortable working in both office and field settings
  • Valid driver’s license

Team & Work Environment

Ossa Terra is a family-oriented company that believes its success comes from supporting a diverse team of people with varied backgrounds, skills, and life experiences.

We value:

  • Respectful communication across crews, office staff, and management
  • A willingness to support others and step in where needed
  • Accountability and follow-through
  • A practical, non-ego approach to problem solving
  • A positive, team-driven environment where people take pride in their work

The right fit is someone who takes ownership of their work, communicates directly, and contributes to a steady team environment built on mutual reliability — both on and off the job site.
