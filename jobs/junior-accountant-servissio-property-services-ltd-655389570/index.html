Full Description

About the Role
We are looking for a detail-oriented and motivated Junior Accountant to join our finance team. This is a great opportunity for someone early in their accounting career to gain hands-on experience and grow within a supportive environment.

Key Responsibilities

  • Providing day-to-day support to the Assistant controller and Senior Accountant on multiple entities
  • Receivables and payables duties:
  • Receiving, assembling, organizing, coding, and posting invoices, requesting adjustments where necessary
  • Weekly cheque run and making online payments
  • Monthly billing or ad-hoc billing
  • Depositing cheques, and recording payments
  • Following up with vendors as required
  • Performing bank reconciliation and making journal entries
  • Reconciling intercompany transactions
  • Reconciling credit card transactions and creating chargeback invoices
  • Preparing invoice summary for project updates
  • Assisting in Year end Close and Trust audit
  • Filing Bank statements and Cheque images
  • Handle some GST Filings and prepare accounts receivable reports
  • Other accounting related duties as requested from time to time
  • Other Ad hoc duties

Qualifications

  • Bachelor’s degree or diploma in Accounting, Finance, or related field
  • 0–2 years of relevant accounting experience (Work experience in real estate finance, development, or property management is a plus)
  • Basic knowledge of accounting principles and practices
  • Proficiency in Microsoft Excel and QuickBooks
  • Strong attention to detail and organizational skills
  • Excellent communication and problem-solving abilities

What We’re Looking For

  • A motivated and detail-oriented individual with a strong interest in accounting and finance
  • Solid understanding of basic accounting principles and willingness to continue learning
  • Strong analytical and problem-solving skills with high attention to accuracy
  • Good organizational and time management abilities, with the ability to meet deadlines
  • Proficiency in Microsoft Excel and familiarity with accounting software is an asset
  • Effective written and verbal communication skills
  • Ability to work both independently and as part of a team
  • A positive attitude, reliability, and a proactive approach to tasks
  • Commitment to maintaining confidentiality and handling sensitive financial information responsibly

Why Join Us

  • Career Growth: We invest in your development with ongoing training, mentorship, and opportunities to advance within the company.
  • Supportive Team Environment: Work alongside experienced professionals in a collaborative and inclusive workplace where your contributions are valued.
  • Hands-On Experience: Gain practical exposure to a variety of accounting functions and build a strong foundation for your career.
  • Competitive Compensation: Enjoy a fair salary, benefits package, and performance-based growth opportunities.

Job Type: Full-time

Pay: $45,000.00-$50,000.00 per year

  • Extended health care
