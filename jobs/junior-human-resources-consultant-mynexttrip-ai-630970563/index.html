Role Description

This is a full-time remote role for a Junior Human Resources Consultant. In this position, you will assist in implementing HR policies, managing employee relations, and ensuring compliance with labor and employment law. Your daily responsibilities will include helping with recruitment processes, supporting employee engagement initiatives, updating policies, and offering HR guidance to team members. Collaborating closely with other departments, you will contribute to fostering a positive work environment and providing HR solutions tailored to organizational needs.

Qualifications

  • Knowledge of Human Resources fundamentals, including HR Management and HR Policies
  • Experience in Employee Relations and fostering positive workplace environments
  • Understanding of Labor and Employment Law, ensuring compliance with legal frameworks
  • Strong interpersonal, communication, and problem-solving skills
  • Bachelor’s degree in Human Resources, Business Administration, or a related field
  • Ability to work collaboratively in a virtual setting and manage tasks autonomously
  • Familiarity with HR tools and software is a plus
