Full Description

Company Description

GS Professional Corporation, CPA's is a client-focused accounting firm dedicated to fostering meaningful relationships with our clients and providing tailored solutions that align with their business and industry needs. Our commitment to excellence motivates our team to pursue ongoing professional growth and development. We prioritize delivering the most relevant services and insights to empower our clients and their communities to thrive. Through these efforts, we aim to establish trust and long-lasting partnerships with those we serve.

Role Description

This is a full-time, on-site role for a Junior Staff Accountant located in Mississauga, ON. Responsibilities include preparing financial statements, reconciling accounts, performing data entry, and assisting with payroll and tax filings. The role also involves maintaining general ledger accounts, supporting periodic audits, and collaborating with the team to ensure timely and accurate financial reporting.

Qualifications

  • Proficiency in financial reporting, preparing financial statements, and general accounting principles
  • Experience with reconciliation, data entry, and account management
  • Knowledge of tax filings, payroll processing, and audit preparation
  • Strong analytical and problem-solving skills
  • Proficiency with accounting software and Microsoft Office Suite, especially Excel
  • Effective communication, organizational, and time management skills
  • Ability to work well both independently and collaboratively in a team environment
  • Bachelor’s degree in Accounting, Finance, or a related field
  • CPA designation or working towards obtaining a CPA is an asset
  • Softwares: QBO, Profile, Caseware
