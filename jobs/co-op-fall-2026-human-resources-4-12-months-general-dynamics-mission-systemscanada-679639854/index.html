About the Role

Support HR functions across recruitment, total rewards, analytics

About General Dynamics Mission Systems–Canada

Global defense tech leader, diverse HR exposure.

Full Description

Build bold. Deliver impact. Win together.

At General Dynamics Mission Systems–Canada, we’re not just engineering technology — we’re shaping the future of defence and security. Our teams design and deliver advanced, mission-critical solutions that enhance national security, strengthen communities, and protect the people we serve.

If you’re looking for meaningful work that contributes to a safer, more secure world, you’ll find purpose here. Every day, your skills will directly impact programs that matter.

Job Description

We are seeking a Human Resources Co-op Student to join our growing team. This is an exceptional opportunity for a student to collaborate with our supportive and knowledgeable team of Talent Acquisition, Total Rewards, HR Strategic Partnerships, Labour Relations, Talent Development, and HR Systems professionals. Gain exposure to a variety of HR disciplines while contributing to our mission of achieving a more secure world through innovation.

What You’ll Be Doing

Depending on business needs and your interests, you’ll support and gain exposure in areas such as:

  • Talent Acquisition – assisting with job postings, resume screening, interview coordination, and employer branding initiatives
  • Total Rewards – supporting compensation and benefits administration, and research
  • HR Data, Analytics & Reporting – helping prepare reports, dashboards, and insights that inform decision-making
  • HR Administration – supporting employee lifecycle activities including onboarding, documentation, and HR systems
  • Projects & Initiatives – contributing to a variety of people-related projects, process improvements, and special initiatives across the HR team

What You’ll Learn

  • How HR supports business strategy and organizational effectiveness
  • Practical experience working with HR systems, data, and processes
  • Exposure to multiple HR specialties to help you explore your career interests
  • How to work with stakeholders, manage priorities, and deliver results in a professional environment

Qualifications

  • Currently enrolled full-time in a postsecondary co-op program in Business Administration, Commerce, or equivalent
  • Enthusiastic contributor with strong interest in Human Resources
  • Excellent communication, interpersonal, and teamwork skills

This position is open for a 4-month, 8-month, or a 12-month term beginning September 2026, with a preference for an 8- or 12-month term. Please indicate your availability in your application.

It is a requirement that General Dynamics Mission Systems–Canada be registered with the Canadian Controlled Goods program and that all of its workforce be security assessed. Successful applicants must meet all applicable security requirements, including but not limited to the ability to obtain and maintain a Canadian government security clearance. Applicants may be required to meet additional security requirements in order to gain access to technical data, classified areas or information that is subject to international regulations. You must be eligible to work in Canada.

Additional Information

Compensation

The expected hourly rate for this position is $21.66 – $27.08. Final compensation will be determined based on your relevant work experience to date. As part of student placement, you will be eligible for quarterly pay increases.

Why Students Love Working Here

  • Purposeful Innovation & Problem Solving: You’ll work alongside experienced professionals and some of the brightest minds in the defence sector, gaining hands-on experience with next-generation technologies. This is a place where your ideas are encouraged, creativity is valued, and you’ll be supported as you learn and help drive modern, practical solutions.
  • Connection & Mentorship: Through onboarding to your career journey with us, you will work with teammates that are excited to share their experience and develop your skills. Have meaningful interactions, ask questions, and build relationships that can lead to lasting industry connections.
  • Perks & Highlights:
  • Eligibility for future co-ops or full-time roles
  • Flexible work hours and compressed workweek options
  • Hybrid work model (where applicable)
  • Networking opportunities across teams and disciplines
  • Onsite amenities and social events (gym, bike storage, trivia, appreciation events, etc.)
  • Corporate discounts

Accommodation

If you require accommodation during any stage of the application process, please contact Human Resources via recruitment@gd-ms.ca.
