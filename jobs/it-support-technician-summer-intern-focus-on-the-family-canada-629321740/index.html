About The Opportunity We are seeking motivated and passionate individuals as interns, working with various teams performing roles in the following areas below . Department Overview

The IT Department plays a critical role in supporting our mission by ensuring the reliability, security, and efficiency of our technology infrastructure. This internship program is designed to provide individuals interested in pursuing a career in Information Technology with meaningful, hands-on experience. The intern will gain exposure to real-world IT operations, contribute to ongoing projects, and develop both technical and professional skills in a supportive learning environment.

Key Responsibilities

  • Assist with troubleshooting hardware and software issues across Windows, macOS, and Microsoft 365 environments.
  • Support end-users by responding to help desk tickets and providing technical guidance and assistance .
  • Participate in system maintenance tasks, including updates, backups, and documentation.
  • Contribute to IT projects, including system upgrades, automation scripts, and network improvements.
  • Document processes and create user guides for common IT tasks.
  • Collaborate with team members on cross-functional initiatives.

About You

  • Currently p ursuing ( ideally 3rd or 4th year student ) or have recently completed a post-secondary degree, preferably in Computing, Sciences, Engineering, or a related field .
  • Ability to adapt to shifting priorities in a dynamic environment.
  • Strong understanding of Microsoft Windows, macOS, and Microsoft 365.
  • Experience or training in user support, scripting, or coding is an asset.
  • Highly organized and detail ‑ oriented, capable of managing multiple tasks while maintaining attention to detail and a high standard of execution.
  • Strong verbal and written communication skills with ability to connect well with staff
  • Enthusiastic about learning and applying new technologies with a curious, proactive, and resourceful mindset in identifying issues and developing practical solutions.
  • Eagerness to learn and develop skills, particularly in technical areas such as programming
