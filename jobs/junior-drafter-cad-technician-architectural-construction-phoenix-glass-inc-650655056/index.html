About the Role

Produce and revise architectural shop drawings in AutoCAD

About Phoenix Glass Inc

Architectural glazing contractor focusing on building envelopes

Full Description

Phoenix Glass is a leading commercial glazing contractor specializing in high-performance architectural glass and aluminum systems. We work on a wide range of projects including commercial, institutional, and mixed-use developments across British Columbia.

We are looking for a detail-oriented drafter to support our team with production drawings. This is a support-focused role where you will work closely with senior team members to develop and revise drawings based on direction, markups, and project requirements. You’ll be supporting real-world construction projects from concept through fabrication and installation.

What you’ll be working on:

  • Architectural and construction drawings related to building envelope systems
  • Layouts, elevations, sections, and shop drawings
  • Revisions and updates based on internal and consultant feedback

Responsibilities:

  • Produce and revise drawings using AutoCAD
  • Translate sketches, redlines, and instructions into accurate drawings
  • Assist with drawing packages for fabrication and installation
  • Maintain drawing standards and file organization
  • Support project teams with ongoing revisions and coordination

Requirements:

  • Proficiency in AutoCAD
  • Strong attention to detail and organization
  • Ability to follow direction and work efficiently
  • 0–3 years experience in drafting or related field

Nice to have:

  • Experience with architectural, construction, or building systems drawings
  • Familiarity with Revit

We’re looking for someone reliable, coachable, and comfortable working in a fast-paced, production-driven environment.
