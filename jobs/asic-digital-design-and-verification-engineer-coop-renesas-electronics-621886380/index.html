About the Role

About Renesas Electronics

Global embedded semiconductor leader

Full Description

Job Description Summer COOP (40 hours/week) position for 16 weeks from May 4, 2026 - September 4, 2026 Must be currently enrolled full time in a university degree program pursing a bachelor. Must not have graduated before September 4, 2026. Local candidates only Hybrid role To further strengthen the team at our Ottawa location, we are looking for a ASIC Digital Design and Verification Engineer COOP who will work on the development of state of the art mixed-signal PLL (phase-locked loop) integrated circuits. During the COOP term in the Digital Verification team, you will learn about our verification environment, test bench, and test-case writing. You will be assigned a number of defined tasks, which will allow you to contribute to our verification team and grow your knowledge and gain experience in ASIC verification at the same time.

Qualifications

  • Must be currently enrolled full time in a university degree program pursing a bachelor degree. Must not have graduated before September 4, 2026
  • Good academic standing
  • Object oriented coding skills
  • Strong problem solving skills
  • Fast learner with ability to work under minimal supervision
  • Strong organization and communication skills

Preferred Skills & Knowledge

  • Understanding Standard Specifications/functional specifications/feature enhancements for the product to create and execute detailed verification plans
  • Knowledge of Verilog, SystemVerilog and UVM is an asset
  • Experience with JAVA programming is an asset
  • Knowledge of scripting languages such as Python and Tcl is an asset

Additional Information This posting is for a new vacancy. The expected hourly pay range for this position is $25/hr - $33/hr. This position is also eligible for bonus opportunities. Please note that the final offer amount, including any applicable bonuses, will be dependent on geographic location, relevant experience, and skillset of the candidate.

Renesas is an embedded semiconductor solution provider driven by its Purpose ‘ To Make Our Lives Easier .’ As the industry’s leading expert in embedded processing with unmatched quality and system-level know-how, we have evolved to provide scalable and comprehensive semiconductor solutions for automotive, industrial, infrastructure, and IoT industries based on the broadest product portfolio, including High Performance Computing, Embedded Processing, Analog & Connectivity, and Power.

With a diverse team of over 22,000 professionals in more than 30 countries, we continue to expand our boundaries to offer enhanced user experiences through digitalization and usher into a new era of innovation. We design and develop sustainable, power-efficient solutions today that help people and communities thrive tomorrow, ‘ To Make Our Lives Easier .’
