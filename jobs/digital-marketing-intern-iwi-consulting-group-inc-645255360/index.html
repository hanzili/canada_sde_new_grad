About the Role

Execute outbound campaigns, build lead lists, optimize campaigns

About IWI Consulting Group Inc

Canadian ERP consulting group; growth-focused marketing exposure

Full Description

We’re looking for a Digital Marketing Intern with a strong interest in growth, demand generation, and outbound marketing.

This is not a “watch and learn” internship — you’ll be hands-on from day one, supporting real campaigns that generate pipeline and business opportunities.

If you’re curious, data-driven, and want to learn how modern B2B marketing actually drives revenue, this role is for you.

Key Responsibilities

  • Support execution of outreach strategies for company prospecting
  • Assist in building and enriching targeted lead lists (ICP, industry, company size, intent signals)
  • Help write and optimize outbound email sequences (cold emails, follow-ups, A/B testing)
  • Support content creation with focus on SEO and inbound lead generation
  • Assist in optimizing website content for search rankings and conversions
  • Support email marketing campaigns and automation workflows
  • Work within CRM systems to manage leads, lists, and campaign tracking
  • Help monitor campaign performance (open rates, reply rates, conversions)
  • Collaborate with marketing and sales teams on pipeline generation initiatives

Requirements

  • Currently pursuing or recently completed a degree in Marketing, Business, or related field
  • Strong interest in digital marketing, outbound, and growth strategies
  • Basic understanding of SEO, content marketing, or email marketing
  • Interest in lead generation and prospecting strategies
  • Organized, detail-oriented, and eager to learn
  • Analytical mindset with willingness to test and improve

Nice to Have

  • Familiarity with tools like HubSpot, 6sense, Clay, or LinkedIn Sales Navigator
  • Interest in copywriting for outbound campaigns
  • Exposure to CRM systems or marketing tools
  • Interest in B2B marketing environments

About the Company

IWI Consulting Group is a Canadian consulting firm specializing in the implementation, integration, and optimization of ERP systems. With more than two decades of experience, IWI delivers tailored business management solutions that empower organizations to improve efficiency, financial visibility, and collaboration across all departments.

As a certified partner of Sage and Acumatica, we provide full-service consulting, from system selection and process mapping to implementation, training, and ongoing support. Our team has extensive expertise with Sage Intacct, Sage X3, Sage 300 and Acumatica Cloud ERP, helping companies modernize their financial and operational workflows with flexible, cloud-based solutions.

IWI serves clients across Canada in multiple industries, including manufacturing, distribution, food and beverage, professional services, non-profit, and financial services. We understand that every business has unique needs, which is why our approach focuses on aligning ERP functionality with strategic objectives — ensuring measurable results and long-term value.

Our services include:

  • ERP consulting and system evaluation
  • Cloud ERP implementation and migration
  • Data integration and automation
  • Multi-entity financial management
  • Custom reporting and dashboards
  • End-user training and post-implementation support

Whether you are upgrading from a legacy system or adopting your first cloud ERP, IWI Consulting Group provides the experience and insight needed to ensure a smooth transition. We take pride in building lasting partnerships, delivering personalized solutions, and supporting Canadian organizations as they grow with Sage and Acumatica technologies.
