About Johnson Controls

Full Description

Build Your Best Future With The Johnson Controls Team As a global leader in smart, healthy and sustainable buildings, our mission is to reimagine the performance of buildings to serve people, places and the planet. As a member of the Johnson Controls Future Leaders Internship Program, you will learn to use your valuable skill set, knowledge, and passion to excel in a dynamic and competitive global environment. Our Internship Program is a unique opportunity to put classroom theory into practice while bolstering your resume and professional network. By joining a program that is rooted in community, connection, and memorable experiences, you will have an opportunity to transform your future. We are proud to share that our Intern Program has been recognized by Yello x WayUp as one of the Top 100 Internship Programs in the United States for 2025.

Join hundreds of interns nationwide for a summer of learning, growth, and connection, including:

  • Innovation Challenge
  • Volunteering opportunities
  • Executive engagement
  • Development + career workshops
  • Inclusive and diverse environment with in-person and/or hybrid work options
  • Comprehensive benefits package including 401(k), Medical, Dental, and Vision

What You Will Do Johnson Controls invites you to apply for an in‑person Project Analysis Accounting Internship supporting our Silent‑Aire business, located in Nisku, Alberta. Silent‑Aire is a global leader in mission‑critical cooling solutions for data centers.

This internship is designed to provide hands‑on exposure to project accounting and financial analysis in a complex, project‑based manufacturing and services environment. You will support accounting and finance professionals who partner closely with project managers and operations teams.

How You Will Do It

  • Assist with project financial tracking, including cost analysis, revenue recognition support, and variance review
  • Support month‑end and period‑end accounting activities related to project reporting
  • Help maintain accurate project documentation and financial records
  • Analyze project data to support forecasting, budgeting, and financial decision‑making
  • Prepare basic financial reports, summaries, and presentations for internal stakeholders
  • Collaborate with cross‑functional teams including Project Management, Operations, and Finance
  • Participate in process improvement initiatives to enhance reporting accuracy and efficiency
  • Other duties and learning opportunities may be assigned based on business needs

Required Qualifications

  • Currently enrolled as a full‑time student at an accredited college or university
  • Pursuing a degree in Accounting, Finance, Business, or a related field
  • Strong analytical and organizational skills
  • Proficiency with Microsoft Excel and basic financial tools
  • Ability to manage multiple tasks while maintaining attention to detail
  • Strong written and verbal communication skills
  • Legal authorization to work in Canada

HIRING HOURLY RANGE: $20.00 – $24.00 CAD/hour

(Hourly rate to be determined by the education, experience, knowledge, skills, and abilities of the applicant, internal equity, location and alignment with market data.) This position includes a competitive benefits package. For details, please visit the About Us tab on the Johnson Controls Careers site at About Johnson Controls Careers
