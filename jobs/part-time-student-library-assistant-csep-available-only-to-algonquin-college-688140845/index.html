Job Description:

PLEASE READ EMPLOYMENT STIPULATIONS FOR POSITION PRIOR TO APPLYING

Students applying to this position must be in receipt of OSAP o r out-of-province assistance during the current or prior academic year or must have a demonstrated financial need determined using Financial Aid and Student Awards standardized needs and assessment budget calculation tool.

Reporting to the Manager, Student Learning Centre & Libraries , the incumbent provides assistance to library patrons using the campus library and online services. Assists, as required, in the maintenance of library materials.

Duties and Responsibilities:

  • Maintains the integrity of patron records in the integrated library system;
  • Provides guidance to patrons in the use of resources;
  • Assists clients with technical issues (computers, printers, scanners, photocopier);
  • Provides general information and problem-solving;
  • Performs duties such as straightening furniture, shelving materials, as required;
  • Provides front desk services and telephone responses.

Requirements for this position include:

  • This position is funded through the College Student Employment Program (CSEP). Applicants must be eligible for CSEP.
  • You must be currently enrolled in a full-time program. The following students are not eligible : Second Career, Apprenticeship Programs, International students, Algonquin College employees paying a reduced tuition, Collaborative programs with the University of Ottawa or Carleton University and all programs that are not Ministry-approved;
  • You must be a Canadian citizen, a Permanent Resident, or a Protected Person/Convention Refugee within the meaning of subsection 95(2) of the Immigration and Refugee Protection Act (Canada) prior to the application deadline date;
  • You must demonstrate a financial need by successfully applying for OSAP or out-of-province financial aid funding

For more information on CSEP:
