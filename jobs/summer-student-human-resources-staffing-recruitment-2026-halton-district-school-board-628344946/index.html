Full Description

The Halton District School Boards’ Staffing and Recruiting Team supports 108 schools in the Halton Region (Halton Hills, Milton, Burlington and Oakville). The team contributes to a supportive learning environment where more than 65,000 students learn, grow and succeed.

The Halton Region has been growing fast and growth is projected to remain strong for the foreseeable future. The region's population is anticipated to almost double in size by 2051, from a current population o f to a projected population of by 2051.

Position: Summer Student, Human Resources - Staffing and Recruitment

Location: J.W. Singleton Education Centre – Human Resources

2050 Guelph Line, Burlington, ON

Term : May 4, 2026 up to and including August 28, 2026

Hours: 7 hours per day, 5 days per week

Pay Rate: $20.00 - $22.60 per hour (depending on year of study) plus 4% vacation

Position Summary:

The successful candidate will work in the Human Resources Department – Staffing and Recruitment.

Duties will include but are not limited to:

  • Assist by leveraging technology to improve current staffing processes (i.e. create electronic forms);
  • Assist with data cleansing and standardization;
  • Categorizing/organizing terminated employee files for HR and Staffing
  • Boxing and labeling files in preparation for off-site storage;
  • Prepare files for shredding in accordance with retention policy;
  • Assist with annual processes;

Qualifications:

  • Enrollment in a post-secondary Human Resources Management program and will be returning to school in September 2026 on a full time basis
  • Currently enrolled in Records Management, Office Administration or a related program would be an asset;
  • Excellent written and oral communication skills;
  • Effective team player;
  • Excellent attention to detail;
  • Analytical, resourceful, articulate and computer-savvy;
  • Committed to maintaining highly confidential information;
  • Ability to lift heavy items (approximately 30 lbs/14 kg or more).

Applications, along with your resume and other supporting documents must be submitted through Apply to Education – 4:00 pm on Wednesday, February 25, 2026

Job Category:- Job Code# 4010487

Applications submitted directly to the Human Resources Department or the School will not be considered or processed.
