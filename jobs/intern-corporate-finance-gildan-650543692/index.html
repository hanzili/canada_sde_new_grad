About the Role

Support FP&A, consolidation, and finance transformation

About Gildan

Leading apparel manufacturer with strong training programs

Full Description

Who We Are Gildan is a leading manufacturer of everyday basic apparel, with a strong portfolio of brands, including Gildan®, Hanes®, Comfort Colors®, American Apparel®, ALLPRO™, GOLDTOE®, Peds®, Bali®, Playtex®, Maidenform®, Bonds®, as well as Champion® which is under an exclusive licensing agreement for the printwear channel in the U.S. and Canada. Together with our global team of dedicated employees, Gildan is united in its vision of Making Apparel Better®. Discover the full scale of Gildan and prepare to be surprised at gildancorp.com .

The opportunity As a Corporate Finance intern, you will support the Corporate Finance team with the preparation of monthly deliverables and other process improvement projects. The intern candidate will have an opportunity to work across different finance sub-functions such as but not limited to: Consolidation, FP&A, Finance Transformation in order to build their knowledge and expertise in these fields to complement their areas of study.

The role FP&A Related

  • Assist in preparation of weekly/monthly presentations for various operating rhythms
  • Perform closing procedures and variance analysis

Consolidation Related

  • Assist in preparation of process maps and standard operating procedures

Finance Transformation

  • Assist with IT related initiatives that impact financial forecasting and reporting
  • Assist with integration of recently acquired company

The Requirements

  • Studies in Finance or related fields (accounting, commerce, economics, business administration)
  • Relevant course work/projects/experience in the field of interest, or relevant leadership or community involvement experience.
  • This position requires proficiency in a language other than French to support customers, employees or markets located outside the province of Quebec or requiring services in a language other than French, mainly English.

What's In It For You

  • Join a leader in apparel manufacturing with strong safety and environmental standards
  • Be part of a diverse workplace with fun workplace activities
  • Benefit from continuous training opportunities
  • Take advantage of our attractive benefits packages
