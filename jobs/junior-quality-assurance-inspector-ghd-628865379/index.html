About GHD

Global engineering firm specializing in infrastructure

Full Description

Job Description Help us build the future and we’ll help you build a rewarding and purposeful career. Our global network is made up of architects, designers, planners, engineers, and environmental scientists all working towards the same goal.

Join a team that brings inspirational architecture, landscapes, townscapes and places to our world, and we’ll provide you unlimited space to grow.

Who are we looking for? Our Construction Phase Services group assigned to Waterloo, ON construction projects is looking for a Graduate Quality Assurance Inspector to complete inspections as part of a team on Highway, Road and Bridge construction projects. Apply theory and principles of civil engineering in planning, designing, and overseeing construction and maintenance of structures and facilities under the direction of engineering staff or physical scientists.

Be part of the dynamic culture! Our workplace thrives on a hybrid model, combining in-office collaboration and flexibility. Join us in office, three days a week to contribute, connect and excel in our vibrant environment. Working with an energetic and high performing team, this position offers a variety of work and will see you involved in:

  • Personal Capability Building: Develop and maintain excellent procedural or technical skills by participating in assessment and development planning activities as well as formal and informal training and coaching.
  • Testing: Perform a variety of standard tests requiring a moderate level of judgment.
  • Data Collection and Analysis: Perform basic data entry tasks, including data verification.
  • Solutions Analysis: Assess compliance with established standards and protocols for parts of routine inquiries.
  • Project Management: Carry out defined project tasks.
  • Equipment Installation: Carry out simple installation tasks, following detailed instructions and working under close supervision to complete the allocated tasks.
  • Insights and Reporting: Perform basic data reporting tasks to support others by using established procedures.
  • Work Scheduling and Allocation: Organize own work schedule each day in line with changing priorities.
  • Plant and Machinery Maintenance and Repair: Maintain and repair plant and machinery under close supervision to ensure that operational requirements are met, including conformance with regulatory and organizational health, safety, and environmental standards.
  • Product and Solution Development: Support others by carrying out simple product development or engineering tasks, such as gathering data or preparing documents. Involves following established procedures.

What You Will Bring To The Team Education

  • Completion of Civil Engineering Technician or Technologist diploma, or completion of Civil Engineering degree.

Experience

  • General Experience: 0-2 years of experience in related field.
  • Past experience in construction field work is considered an asset;
  • Valid Ontario Class “G” driver’s licence;
  • Team player, cooperative with other staff with open sharing of information and knowledge.
