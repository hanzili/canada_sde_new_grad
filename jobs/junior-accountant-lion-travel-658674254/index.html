About the Role

Record journal entries, close monthly books, analyze accounts

About LION TRAVEL

Travel company with growth and global footprint opportunities

Full Description

Join Lion Travel — Connect the World, Share the Culture!

With 40+ years of experience and 36 global offices, Lion Travel is Taiwan’s leading travel brand, serving over one million travelers each year.

A People-First Culture

We value trust, clarity, and human connection. At Lion Travel, you’re not just filling a role—you’re bringing your whole self to work.

We are committed to fostering an inclusive, respectful, and diverse workplace where different backgrounds, cultures, and experiences are welcomed and valued.

Responsibilities of the job:

  • Daily cost and revenue Auditing
  • Record Inter-Company accounting transactions with other locations
  • Trial balance maintenance, including Cash receipts and disbursement activities, and general journal preparation etc.
  • Perform tasks associated with monthly financial statement closing
  • Analyze select Balance Sheet and P&L accounts
  • Research and respond questions from Head Quarters or other offices

Required Skills and Qualifications:

  • The ideal candidate will have at least 1 year of experience in a similar role
  • Strong knowledge of MS Office & email applications
  • Intermediate Excel skills, working with multiple spreadsheets is required
  • Ability to work independently and take initiative while working collaboratively as part of the team
  • Well-developed problem solving and trouble shooting skills with strong analytical abilities, numbers and detail oriented with focus on accuracy
  • Effective time management skills with the ability to prioritize and complete tasks within tight timelines
  • Ability to work flexible hours with some weekends and evenings
