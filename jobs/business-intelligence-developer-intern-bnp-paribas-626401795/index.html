About BNP Paribas

Full Description

In a changing world, unprecedented challenges require unmatched talent. Join one of Montreal’s Top Employers in 2025. We are a dynamic and growing organization having its main establishment located in downtown Montreal and part of a leading international banking institution fully committed to building a more sustainable future. Note that the position may be in the Canadian Branch of BNP Paribas or in one of its subsidiaries based in Montreal.

Internship program As an intern, you will benefit from a 4 month program that aims to give you the tools to develop yourself and discover your potential through customized trainings, networking sessions and transversal projects.

More information about the program available on our website:

BNP Paribas in Canada Students & Young graduates

The position at a glance We develop and support advanced Business Intelligence (BI) solutions that drive data-driven decision-making across BNP Paribas CIB. Our BI platforms empower teams worldwide with real-time insights, optimized reporting, and scalable analytics to enhance operational efficiency and strategic planning.

We work in an Agile environment with regular deployments, ensuring continuous improvement and alignment with business needs. As part of a collaborative, international team, you’ll have the opportunity to refine your technical expertise while contributing to high-impact projects in a dynamic financial setting.

In detail The team members are responsible of the following activities:

  • Develop, implement, and optimize BI solutions to improve data accessibility, reporting, and decision-making.
  • Design and enhance dashboards, reports, and data models to deliver actionable insights.
  • Maintain and optimize existing BI systems, ensuring data accuracy, integrity, and performance.
  • Collaborate with business stakeholders to gather requirements and deliver tailored BI solutions.
  • Document BI processes and solutions to ensure knowledge sharing and system clarity.
  • Validate and troubleshoot BI solutions to guarantee data quality and reliability.
  • Train and support end-users to maximize adoption of BI tools.

Work conditions: This position provides for standard working conditions in an office and a normal work schedule from Monday to Friday.

*The Strengths And Skills That Will Help You Succeed*

  • Oracle Database:

  • Strong experience with SQL, query optimization, and complex queries.

  • Hands-on expertise in performance tuning and database management.

  • Power BI:

  • Data modelling following best practices (star schema, relationships, performance optimization).

  • Advanced mastery of DAX (measures, calculated columns, technical optimizations).

  • SSRS (SQL Server Reporting Services):

  • Proven experience in designing and delivering paginated reports.

  • Handling parameters, subscriptions, and report optimization.

  • Strong understanding of data warehousing concepts (ETL/ELT, dimensional modelling, OLAP).

  • Version control (Git) and Agile methodologies (Scrum/Kanban).
  • Jira/Confluence for project tracking and documentation.
  • Strong analytical skills to translate business requirements into technical solutions.

  • Nice to Have:

  • CI/CD tools (Jenkins, Bitbucket, Artifactory, Ansible).

  • Containerization & orchestration (Kubernetes).

  • Experience with cloud BI platforms (Azure Synapse, AWS Redshift).

  • Professional knowledge of the English language*

  • Given the vast majority of our clients, both internal and external, are based outside of Quebec and Canada, specific language requirements may apply. Professional working proficiency in English language is required.

*What’s In It For You* In addition to competitive compensation, hybrid work arrangements are available for most positions. In-office presence is required a minimum of 3 days per week, one of which must be on a Monday and/or a Friday. BNP Paribas provides excellent training and personal development programs, as well as opportunities for career development within the company and internationally.

  • BNP Paribas is committed to accessibility and inclusion. During the recruitment process, accommodation needs are available at all times for candidates. You will have the chance to make a request for an accommodation during your application.
  • You must be legally eligible to work in the Greater Montreal area and, if applicable, hold a valid work or study permit. Physical presence in BNP Paribas’ office(s) is an essential function of this position;
  • If you are applying and accepted to a position which requires working in/for the U.S. securities industry, you will be required to provide your fingerprints and undergo additional background checks by the FBI.
