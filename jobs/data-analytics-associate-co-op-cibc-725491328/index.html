About the Role

Analyze data, transform datasets, deliver insights

About CIBC

Canadian bank with global financial services

Full Description

We’re building a relationship-oriented bank for the modern world. We need talented, passionate professionals who are dedicated to doing what’s right for our clients.

At CIBC, we embrace your strengths and your ambitions, so you are empowered at work. Our team members have what they need to make a meaningful impact and are truly valued for who they are and what they contribute.

To learn more about CIBC, please visit CIBC.com

What You'll Be Doing Join CIBC’s Business Banking Product Analysis and Insight team, where a diverse group of analytics professionals come together to support multiple areas across the bank. As a Data Analytics Associate, you’ll contribute to data transformation and analytics projects that impact products such as lending, mortgages, wealth management, Wood Gundy, and private banking. You’ll work closely with business stakeholders to understand their requirements and deliver actionable insights. This role is highly collaborative, involving cross-functional efforts with peers, directors, and independent business units. You’ll have the opportunity to learn from experienced leaders. If you enjoy working in a dynamic environment, solving complex business challenges, and networking across teams, this is the place for you.

At CIBC we enable the work environment most optimal for you to thrive in your role. You'll have the flexibility to manage your work activities within a hybrid work arrangement where you'll spend core days on-site, while other days will be remote. How You'll Succeed Data Analytics & Transformation – Analyze and transform data to support business objectives across lending, mortgages, wealth management, and commercial banking.

Stakeholder Engagement – Collaborate with business partners and stakeholders to understand requirements and deliver tailored solutions.

Reporting & Presentation – Export data and insights into Excel and PowerPoint, creating clear and impactful presentations for internal audiences.

Collaboration & Teamwork – Work closely with peers, directors, and independent business units, contributing to a culture of shared success.

Continuous Learning – Gain exposure to different coding styles and data sets, and learn directly from hands-on leaders who mentor and support your growth.

Who You Are You’re a strong communicator. You can clearly articulate complex ideas and insights to both technical and non-technical audiences.

You have relevant technical skills. You bring proficiency in SQL, Excel, and MS Office, with experience in exporting and presenting data. Python or other coding experience, SaaS tools, and business intelligence reporting are assets.

You’re detail-oriented. You notice patterns, inconsistencies, and opportunities within large datasets, ensuring accuracy and quality in your work.

You thrive in collaborative environments. You enjoy working as part of a team and networking across different groups within the bank.

You’re self-managed and adaptable. You take initiative, manage your own tasks, and embrace new challenges with flexibility.

Values matter to you. You bring your real self to work, and you live our values - trust, teamwork, and accountability.

What CIBC Offers At CIBC, your goals are a priority. We start with your strengths and ambitions as an employee and strive to create opportunities to tap into your potential.

  • We work to recognize you in meaningful, personalized ways including a competitive compensation, a banking benefit*, wellbeing support and additional offers such as employee and family assistance programs and MomentMakers, our social, points-based recognition program.
  • Our spaces and technological toolkit will make it simple to bring together great minds to create innovative solutions that make a difference for our clients.
  • Subject to program terms and conditions

  • You need to be legally eligible to work at the location(s) specified above and, where applicable, must have a valid work or study permit
  • We may ask you to complete an attribute-based assessment and other skills test (such as simulation, coding, French proficiency).
  • We use artificial intelligence tools during the recruitment process. Our goal for the application process is to get to know more about you, all that you have to offer, and give you the opportunity to learn more about us.
