Full Description

About SalesPilot 365

SalesPilot 365 is an innovative talent solutions platform committed to connecting businesses with exceptional candidates. As part of our expansion into Data Analytics, we’re offering a structured internship program for motivated individuals eager to build a career in data-driven decision-making.

Internship Summary:

Join our analytics team and gain practical experience working on real business datasets. You’ll develop dashboards, conduct data analysis, and help generate actionable insights that contribute to business growth in a dynamic tech environment.

Key Responsibilities:

  • Collect, clean, and preprocess data to ensure accuracy and usability
  • Conduct exploratory data analysis (EDA) to identify trends and patterns
  • Create interactive and visually compelling dashboards using Power BI, Tableau, or Excel
  • Perform SQL queries and support basic statistical analyses
  • Maintain comprehensive documentation of processes and workflows to ensure data reliability

Ideal Candidate Profile:

  • Current student or recent graduate in fields such as Data Science, Statistics, Computer Science, or Business Analytics
  • Proficient with Excel or Google Sheets, SQL, and basic programming skills (Python or R)
  • Strong analytical thinking, problem-solving ability, and keen attention to detail

Additional Advantages:

  • Familiarity with business intelligence tools (Tableau, Power BI, Looker)
  • Exposure to cloud platforms, machine learning concepts, or participation in hackathons
  • Relevant industry certifications (e.g., Google Data Analytics Certificate, Microsoft Data Analyst Associate)

What You Will Gain:

  • Real-world project experience and structured mentorship
  • Personalized one-on-one guidance from industry professionals
  • Internship Completion Certificate and Letter of Recommendation
  • Opportunity for pre-placement offer (PPO) based on performance

Selection Process:

  1. Online Application Submission
  2. Aptitude Assessment
  3. Technical & Managerial Interviews
  4. Offer Confirmation & Onboarding

Note:

This is a paid internship opportunity. SalesPilot 365 is an equal opportunity employer and values diversity, welcoming applications from all qualified candidates regardless of age, gender, ethnicity, religion, disability, or other protected status.
