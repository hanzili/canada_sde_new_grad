About the Role

Conduct QA/QC testing and analyze results

About Aurora Nutrascience

Quality-focused manufacturer with QA-driven culture

Full Description

Overview
We are seeking a dedicated and detail-oriented Quality Control Technician to join our team. The ideal candidate will play a crucial role in ensuring that our products meet the highest quality standards through rigorous testing and inspection processes. This position requires a strong understanding of quality management principles and the ability to analyze data effectively. If you have a passion for quality assurance and are committed to maintaining excellence in production, we encourage you to apply.

Responsibilities

  • Conduct thorough quality inspections of raw materials, in-process components, and finished products to ensure compliance with specifications.
  • Analyze test results and data collection to identify trends, discrepancies, and areas for improvement.
  • Collaborate with production teams to implement quality control measures and corrective actions as needed.
  • Fabricate test samples as required for various quality assurance processes.
  • Maintain detailed records of inspections, tests, and results in accordance with QA/QC protocols.
  • Assist in the development and refinement of quality management systems to enhance overall product quality.

Requirements

  • Proven experience in Quality Control or Quality Assurance roles within a manufacturing is preferred.
  • Strong knowledge of quality management principles and practices.
  • Excellent analytical skills with the ability to interpret complex data sets effectively.
  • Familiarity with QA/QC methodologies and best practices is highly desirable.
  • Attention to detail and strong organizational skills are essential for maintaining accurate records.
  • Ability to work collaboratively within a team environment while also being self-motivated.

If you are passionate about ensuring product quality and have the necessary skills, we invite you to become an integral part of our team as a Quality Control Technician.
