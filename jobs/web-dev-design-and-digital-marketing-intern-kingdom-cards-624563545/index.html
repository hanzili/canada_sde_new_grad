About the Role

Develop WordPress sites, optimize SEO, support marketing

About Kingdom Cards

Founder-run ecommerce brand; hands-on web projects

Full Description

Kingdom Cards

Kingdom Cards is a Winnipeg based ecommerce brand focused on unique and collectible playing cards ( kingdomcards.ca ). In addition to the online storefront, work includes a variety of web and digital projects with customers and partners. I am seeking a student intern with a primary interest in web development and some experience in digital marketing and SEO. Kingdom Cards is a one-person, founder-run business, and you will be working directly with me on all projects.

This role is ideal for someone seeking hands-on experience working on real WordPress projects across multiple sites.

Role details

  • 18-week internship starting May 4, 2026
  • Part-time position, approximately 20-26 hours per week | Hourly wage of $18-$20
  • Hybrid remote, with occasional in-person meetings and events in Winnipeg
  • Ability to set most of your schedule, with some set meetings.
  • Must have flexible scheduling, with some availability on evenings or weekends as needed.

What you will work on

  • WordPress website development and ongoing improvements
  • Building and refining layouts using Oxygen Builder
  • Front-end styling with HTML/CSS, and light PHP updates and template adjustments
  • Working across multiple WordPress-based web projects as needed
  • Technical and non-technical SEO improvements
  • Copy updates for websites and landing pages
  • Occasional support for social media and digital marketing initiatives

Most of your time will be spent on web development and site optimization

, with marketing tasks supporting the web work rather than leading it.

What skills are needed:

  • Hands-on experience with WordPress development (3+ completed projects)
  • Comfort working with Oxygen Builder
  • Strong understanding of HTML and CSS with a basic PHP understanding
  • Interest in SEO and website performance
  • Ability to switch between different web projects and priorities
  • Comfort working independently with minimal supervision
  • Experience in e-commerce or client-facing work is a plus but not required.

Eligibility requirements

Applicants must:

  • Be a full-time or part-time student enrolled in an accredited Canadian college or university
  • Be studying any discipline at any diploma or degree level
  • Hold Canadian citizenship, Permanent Residency, or refugee status
  • Be enrolled in their program for at least the first 30 days of the internship
  • International students are not eligible

Equity statement

In alignment with the values and goals of the internship program, priority may be given to candidates from underrepresented communities

, including but not limited to:

  • Indigenous students
  • Visible minorities
  • Women studying in STEM
  • Students with disabilities
  • Newcomers to Canada
  • First-year students

All qualified candidates are encouraged to apply.

*Final hiring is conditional on the successful candidate completing a short third-party application process after acceptance.

How to apply

Please send:

  • Your resume
  • A short cover letter (0.5-1 page)
  • Portfolio links (optional but encouraged)

Email applications to admin@kingdomcards.ca

All applicants must hold Canadian citizenship, Permanent Residency, or refugee status

Applications are due by February 18th, 2026; however, interviews will begin immediately, so submit as soon as you can.
