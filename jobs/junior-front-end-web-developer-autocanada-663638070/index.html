Full Description

Join the ride! Position : Junior Front-End Web Developer

Location : AutoCanada Home Office

Classification : Full-Time, Immediate Hire

About The Role Are you a passionate web developer ready to grow your career in a fast-paced, collaborative environment? AutoCanada is looking for a Junior Front-End Developer to join our in-house digital team and help shape the online presence of 80+ dealership websites across Canada.

What You’ll Do Website development

  • Build and maintain responsive websites with HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and PHP
  • Utilize AI coding tools such as Claude and Gemini.
  • Translate design files (Figma, Adobe XD, Photoshop) into clean front-end code
  • Ensure brand consistency across all devices and browsers

Content & CMS

  • Manage and update WordPress and other CMS content
  • Publish promotions, specials, hours, and staff updates
  • Troubleshoot CMS issues and plugin conflicts

Performance & QA

  • Audit sites using Lighthouse and PageSpeed Insights
  • Optimize for Core Web Vitals, load time, and mobile-first experience
  • Perform cross-browser and cross-device testing

Campaigns & collaboration

  • Build campaign-specific landing pages and micro-sites
  • Integrate third-party tools, analytics scripts, and marketing pixels
  • Participate in sprint planning, project briefs, and team check-ins

What We’re Looking For

  • Proficiency in HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, and basic PHP
  • Experience with Bootstrap, jQuery, and front-end frameworks
  • Strong familiarity with WordPress, including themes and plugin management
  • Ability to work from design files in Figma, Canva, or Photoshop
  • Understanding of responsive design, UX best practices, and accessibility standards
  • Familiarity with Google Tag Manager, GA4, and performance tools (Lighthouse, PageSpeed)
  • Diploma or certification in Web Design, Web Development, or a related field
  • Up to 2 years of front-end experience, or a strong portfolio and co-op experience welcome

The Perks.

  • Competitive Compensation and Benefits Package
  • Employee Vehicle Purchase & Service Plans
  • Employee and Family Assistance Programs
  • Company-wide appreciation events and contests throughout the calendar year
  • Professional development and the opportunity to grow your career
