About the Role

About Avanade

IT services firm focused on data and AI

Full Description

Avanade's North American internship program was launched to revolutionize how we create and deliver engaging experiences for our clients and their customers.

Our data engineering interns leverage open-source applications to build custom solutions that are both creative and agile for our clients.

Working in this market segment is cutting-edge, large-scale, and game-changing.

Through these aspects, Avanade interns experience our unique culture of collaboration and growth mindset, while building professional networks and gaining real-world experience.

Join us Become part of a team dedicated to innovation and analytics excellence, where you will contribute to delivering robust analytical solutions and valuable insights. Our mission is to transform data into action for our clients across various industries, providing you with a platform for learning and professional development.

About The Role As a Data Engineering Intern, you will:

  • Support passionate project teams developing customer solutions through exploratory data analysis.
  • Use your knowledge of statistics, programming, data modeling, and advanced mathematics to recognize patterns, identify opportunities, ask business questions, and make valuable discoveries that lead to better business decisions.
  • Provide feedback on data visualization techniques and opportunities.
  • Stay up-to-date on current trends in AI, cloud computing, and big data within the data community.
  • Be introduced to implementing big data architectures with tools such as Hadoop, Spark, Databricks, Azure Synapse Analytics, and Kafka.

Your Profile

  • Currently pursuing a bachelor's degree in a technology-related field.
  • Current title: Junior, with an expected graduation date of 2026 and 2027.
  • Proven ability to apply a creative mindset to projects.
  • Passion for technology.
  • Excellent verbal and written communication skills, with the ability to work in English.
  • You excel in direct contact with external clients and as a project consultant.
  • Proven leadership experience in an academic, extracurricular, or professional setting.
  • Strong critical thinking skills and the ability to reflect and make timely decisions.
  • Ability to learn in a fast-paced environment.
  • Interest in starting a career in technology consulting upon graduation.

Qualifications Your Skills

  • Experience with at least one key analytics tool (R, SAS, SPSS, Tableau, Power BI, SQL, etc.).
  • Knowledge of data mining, analysis, and modeling.
  • Knowledge of statistical analysis, quantitative analysis, and forecasting/predictive analysis.
