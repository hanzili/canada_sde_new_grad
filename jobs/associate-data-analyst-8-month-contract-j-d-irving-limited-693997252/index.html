Full Description

Job Description We are seeking a detail-oriented and business-focused Data Analyst to join our Finance team on an 8-month contract. This role is ideal for someone who thrives on transforming financial data into actionable insights, enjoys collaborating with stakeholders, and is passionate about enabling data-driven decision-making while upholding strong data governance standards.

Responsibilities

  • Partner with finance and business stakeholders to understand challenges and deliver data-driven solutions.
  • Act as a trusted advisor, educating internal customers on analytics tools, financial data literacy, and best practices to empower self-service and informed decision-making.
  • Guide Finance teams through their data journey—helping them identify, access, and leverage financial data.
  • Collaborate with stakeholders to define and establish Key Performance Indicators (KPIs) that accurately reflect financial health and uncover key cost drivers.
  • Apply predictive analytics techniques to anticipate trends, identify risks, and support proactive financial strategies.
  • Lead requirements gathering sessions to define business needs and translate them into effective reporting and visualization solutions.
  • Design, develop, and maintain dashboards and reports using tools such as Power BI, Tableau, and Alteryx for financial performance monitoring.
  • Analyze complex financial datasets to uncover trends, patterns, and insights that solve real business problems.
  • Drive continuous improvement by identifying opportunities to automate and streamline financial data processes.
  • Support and enhance data governance practices, including data quality, integrity, and stewardship across Finance systems and processes.
  • Provide mentorship and guidance to colleagues on interpreting financial data and applying analytical techniques.

Qualifications

  • Bachelor’s degree in information technology, data analytics, finance, or related field.
  • 2+ years of experience in data analytics, data management, finance, or business analysis.
  • Strong communication and interpersonal skills with a customer-centric mindset.
  • Proficiency in Power BI, Tableau, Alteryx, SQL, and Excel.
  • Microsoft PL-300 certification is considered an asset.
  • Experience with ERP tools (e.g., JD Edwards E1) is an asset.
  • Ability to explain complex data concepts in a clear and engaging way to non-technical audiences.
  • Strong problem-solving skills and an innovative approach to analytics.
  • Background in statistics, data modeling, or Six Sigma is considered an asset.
  • CPA designation is considered an asset.
