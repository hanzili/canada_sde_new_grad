About NXT Accounting and Tax Services

Full Description

Job Summary

We are a growing accounting firm seeking a motivated and detail-oriented Trainee Accounting Technician to support our team in providing bookkeeping, payroll, and basic accounting services to a diverse client base.

This is an excellent opportunity for a recent graduate or early-career candidate to gain hands-on experience working with multiple clients across various industries, while developing practical skills in bookkeeping, financial reporting, and taxation within a professional accounting environment.

Key Responsibilities

  • Assist with bookkeeping for multiple client accounts (data entry, journal entries, reconciliations)
  • Maintain accurate financial records using accounting software such as QuickBooks
  • Support accounts payable and receivable for client files
  • Assist with bank and credit card reconciliations
  • Help prepare monthly and quarterly financial reports for clients
  • Support payroll processing and related filings for client businesses
  • Assist in gathering and organizing information for GST/HST and tax filings
  • Communicate with internal team members regarding client file updates and deadlines
  • Ensure client records are accurate, complete, and compliant with basic accounting standards

Job Type & Schedule

  • Full-time, permanent position
  • 40 hours per week
  • Monday to Friday ( and weekends as needed)

Compensation

  • $16–$20 per hour, depending on education and experience
  • Performance-based increases as skills and responsibilities grow

Qualifications

  • Diploma or degree in Accounting, Finance, or related field (or currently in progress)
  • Recent graduates and junior candidates are encouraged to apply
  • Interest in pursuing CPA or other accounting designation is an asset

Skills & Requirements

  • Basic understanding of accounting and bookkeeping principles
  • Familiarity with QuickBooks or similar accounting software preferred
  • Proficiency in Microsoft Excel and other Office applications
  • Strong attention to detail and accuracy
  • Good organizational and time-management skills
  • Ability to manage multiple client files and deadlines
  • Strong communication skills and willingness to learn in a team environment

What We Offer

  • Exposure to a variety of clients and industries
  • Hands-on training and mentorship from experienced accounting professionals
  • Opportunity to build practical experience in bookkeeping, payroll, and tax support
  • Supportive and collaborative team environment
  • Growth opportunities within the firm
  • Extended Healthcare

About the Role

This position reports to the Branch Manager, Edmonton Office, and is designed to develop foundational skills in public practice accounting while supporting client bookkeeping and financial reporting needs.

Company Profile

NXT Accounting is one of the fastest‑growing accounting firms in North America, proudly serving clients across Canada with accuracy, integrity, and unmatched professionalism. Our firm delivers full‑cycle accounting solutions for individuals, small businesses, and organizations who rely on us for clarity, compliance, and strategic financial guidance.

We specialize in full‑service bookkeeping, payroll administration, GST/HST processing, financial reporting, business planning, and CRA representation. Our team works directly with the Canada Revenue Agency on behalf of our clients, ensuring issues are resolved quickly and confidently.

As a modern accounting firm, we embrace technology, efficiency, and continuous improvement. We are looking for team members who are proficient with leading accounting software, advanced Excel functions, and CaseWare. The ideal candidate is detail‑oriented, adaptable, and committed to delivering high‑quality work in a fast‑paced environment.

At NXT Accounting, we take pride in offering a supportive workplace, opportunities for growth, and a comprehensive extended health‑care package. We believe in building a team that feels valued, empowered, and equipped to succeed.

Join us as we continue to expand and redefine what exceptional accounting service looks like in Canada.
