About the Role

Provide desktop, network, and user IT support

About Ontario College of Technology

Ontario college with campus IT support services

Full Description

Job Overview
We are seeking a detail-oriented and proactive Administrative Assistant / Junior IT Support to join our dynamic team. This role offers an excellent opportunity for individuals interested in developing their IT support skills while providing essential administrative assistance. The successful candidate will support daily operations, troubleshoot technical issues, and assist with a variety of IT-related tasks across multiple operating systems and platforms. Strong communication skills and a customer-focused attitude are essential to excel in this position.

Duties

  • Provide technical support to end-users, including troubleshooting software and hardware issues on Windows, macOS, and Linux operating systems
  • Assist with desktop support tasks such as installing, configuring, and maintaining computer hardware and peripherals
  • Support network connectivity issues, including LAN setup and troubleshooting, firewall configurations, and VPN connections
  • Utilize Teams, Amps Jane app systems to document and resolve support requests efficiently
  • Manage user accounts and permissions within operating systems and applications
  • Support the setup and maintenance of computer networks, including LANs and wireless connections
  • Assist in the configuration and management of security tools such as firewalls
  • Perform routine maintenance on computers and network devices to ensure optimal performance
  • Provide help desk support via phone or email, delivering excellent customer service with clear communication skills
  • Collaborate with IT team members on projects related to software troubleshooting, operating system updates, and network improvements
  • Provide assistants to Admin Team and Admission Team for IT support.
  • Provide IT support for Reception Desk.

Qualifications

  • Basic knowledge of computer networking concepts including LAN, VPN, firewalls, and operating systems (Windows, macOS, Linux)
  • Strong understanding of desktop support procedures, software troubleshooting, and hardware maintenance
  • Proficiency in Microsoft Office Suite (Word, Excel, PowerPoint)
  • Excellent communication skills with the ability to assist users effectively and professionally
  • Ability to learn quickly about new technologies and operating systems
  • Customer service-oriented mindset with a proactive approach to problem-solving
  • Prior experience in help desk or technical support roles is desirable but not required

This position provides an engaging environment for those eager to grow their IT skills while supporting administrative functions. We value dedication to quality service and continuous learning in our team members.

Pay: $18.50-$21.00 per hour

Benefits:

  • Casual dress
  • Dental care
  • Flexible schedule
  • On-site parking

Work Location: In person
