About Omland Heal LLP

Local CPA firm with strong CPA development

Full Description

Omland Heal is a Penticton-based accounting firm providing accounting, tax and advisory services to business owners and their families. Our firm has a long-standing history providing high quality service for small to medium-sized businesses and individuals.

If you aspire to be a Chartered Professional Accountant and love living in the sunny Okanagan, Omland Heal is the firm for you. We are currently seeking motivated individuals to begin a CPA career. With our firm you will receive the experience you want and need to be a well-rounded CPA. Please note that this is a full-time, permanent position.

What we have to offer:

We invest in our people, supporting their growth through training, mentorship, and professional development. Our firm will provide you with hands on experience to really accelerate your growth that will translate to on-the-job success as well as strong achievement in your CPA PEP courses.

Here are some of the benefits you can expect as part of our team:

· Competitive compensation package

· Education reimbursement

· Continued professional development

· Opportunities to grow and learn from partners and managers

· Close knit office culture

· Comprehensive health, medical and dental benefits

Key Responsibilities

· Preparation of Compilation and Review engagement working papers

· Preparation of corporate, individual and trust income tax returns

· Detailed analysis of revenue, expense, and investment transactions

· Other types of special engagements as necessary

Qualifications

· Plans to pursue a CPA designation

· Academic achievement - completed or near completion of an undergraduate degree and CPA pre-requisite courses

· Strong client/customer service skills

· Excellent interpersonal relations and demonstrated ability to work with others effectively in teams

· Superior verbal and written communication skills

· Strong problem solving and analytical skills
