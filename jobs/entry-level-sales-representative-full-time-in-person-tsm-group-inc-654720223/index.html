Full Description

About TSM Group .

TSM Group Inc. is an Ottawa-based company focused on direct sales and customer acquisition .

We partner with leading telecommunications providers and represent their services through face-to-face interactions with customers .

Our environment is structured, performance-driven, and team-oriented , with a strong focus on developing communication, discipline, and consistency.

Description

We are currently hiring Entry Level Sales Representatives to join our team.

This role is designed for individuals who want to:

  • build real-world communication skills
  • develop confidence and discipline
  • grow in a performance-based environment

This is a full-time, in-person, field-based role .

What You’ll Be Doing

  • Engaging with customers face-to-face
  • Presenting service solutions clearly and professionally
  • Working toward measurable daily and weekly goals
  • Applying coaching and improving consistently
  • Tracking progress and performance

Who We’re Looking For

  • Coachable and self-motivated individuals
  • Comfortable in a performance-driven environment
  • People who prefer action over routine
  • Strong communication skills

If you are looking for a routine or low-pressure role, this may not be the right fit.

What You’ll Gain

  • Hands-on experience from day one
  • Ongoing coaching and mentorship
  • Real-world skill development
  • Advancement based on performance

Important

This is an in-person, field-based role involving daily interaction with customers.

How to Apply

Apply through LinkedIn or send your resume directly.

Qualified candidates will be contacted for the next step in the hiring process.
