We believe that every candidate brings something special to the table, including you! We currently have an existing vacancy within our organization, so, even if you feel that you’re close but not an exact match, we encourage you to apply.

Overview We are seeking an actuarial student to join our team as a Summer Intern in our Retirement Practice (Actuarial and Investment Consulting). This dual-track internship provides hands-on experience in supporting pension actuaries and investment consultants fulfil all aspects of their mandates related to employer sponsored pension plans.

How You'll Make An Impact

  • Assist with year-end regulatory filings and data preparation for pension statements and actuarial valuations
  • Assist with assets and plan member reconciliation
  • Assist with pension plan administrative tasks, such as termination/retirement and death benefit calculations and preparation of related forms and correspondence
  • Support analysis of investment portfolios, including performance measurement and risk metrics
  • Assist in preparing client presentations and investment reports
  • Assist with monitoring investment managers
  • Assist with miscellaneous client deliverables as appropriate

About You

  • Currently in 3rd or 4th year of a bachelor’s program in Actuarial Science, Mathematics, Statistics, Finance, Economics, or a related field
  • Must have at least 2 preliminary actuarial exams with the SOA/CAS
  • At least one internship in a related field

Strong Quantitative And Analytical Skills

  • Proficiency in Microsoft Excel and Word; knowledge of VBA is an asset
  • Broad understanding of financial markets and investment principles
  • Excellent written and verbal communication skills
  • Strong attention to detail and ability to manage multiple tasks
  • Ability to manage multiple deadlines
