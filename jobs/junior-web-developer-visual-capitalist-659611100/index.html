Full Description

About Visual Capitalist

Visual Capitalist is on a mission to Make the World’s Data More Discoverable. We started out in 2011 with the belief that art, data, and storytelling can be combined to make complex issues more digestible. Using infographics, data visualizations and charts, we create visually-intuitive content on business, investing and technology. 

15 years later we’re

a leading media company with over 15 million monthly site visitors, 350k email subscribers and over 1.5 million social followers. We’ve partnered with many Fortune 500 companies and senior leaders often share or reference our work.

About the role

We're growing our web team and looking for a Junior Web Developer to support our Senior Developer across the Visual Capitalist platform. This role is an opportunity to work on a high-traffic, globally recognized media brand and develop real skills across WordPress development, WooCommerce, and front-end maintenance, with direct mentorship from an experienced lead.

You'll be responsible for keeping our sites running smoothly and handling day-to-day maintenance tasks, bug fixes, and platform support work. You’ll also support the Senior Web Developer as necessary on other strategic projects.

We operate a lean team structure where we seek out people who enjoy taking a lot of responsibility from day one and who want to make a big impact. There is the opportunity to quickly grow and develop in this role.

Note: While this is a standard business-hours role, occasional after-hours availability may be required for urgent site issues or production incidents. These situations are infrequent but part of working on a high-traffic platform.

What you'll do

WordPress maintenance & support

  • Investigate and resolve front-end and back-end bugs on WordPress properties
  • Apply plugin and theme updates in staging, test for regressions, and coordinate production deploys
  • Assist with PHP template edits, hook/filter modifications, and ACF field adjustments under guidance
  • Respond to and triage internal requests (e.g. user access issues, 2FA resets, broken layouts)

WooCommerce & store support

  • Manage product listings, subscriptions, pricing updates, and basic order/customer administration
  • Assist with minor WooCommerce configuration changes (shipping zones, tax settings, coupon setup)
  • Investigate checkout or order-related issues and escalate complex cases to the Senior Developer

Front-end updates & QA

  • Implement approved front-end changes: CSS/layout adjustments, template updates, content zone edits
  • Perform cross-browser and cross-device QA testing across staging and production environments
  • Document bugs clearly with steps to reproduce, environment details, and screenshots

General platform assistance

  • Support the Senior Developer on larger projects with scoped, well-defined tasks
  • Help maintain internal documentation for common procedures and recurring fixes
  • Assist with basic SEO tasks: meta field updates, image alt text audits, sitemap checks

What you bring

  • 1–2 years of experience working with WordPress (themes, plugins, the admin panel)
  • Solid HTML and CSS fundamentals; basic JavaScript
  • Familiarity with PHP at a read-and-edit level (you don't need to architect, but you need to not be afraid of it)
  • Basic understanding of how WooCommerce works (products, orders, customers)
  • Strong attention to detail and clear written communication
  • Ability to follow instructions precisely and ask good clarifying questions

Nice to have

  • Experience with ACF (Advanced Custom Fields)
  • Exposure to staging/production deployment workflows
  • Familiarity with browser DevTools for debugging layout and JS issues
  • Any experience with a managed host like WP Engine or SiteGround

Salary & Benefits

The salary range for this role is $60,000 - $70,000 CAD, depending on experience and scope alignment.

In addition to base salary, we offer:

  • 3 weeks annual vacation + birthday day off
  • Extended health benefits
  • Annual Health Spending Account
  • Flexible work environment
  • Dog-friendly Gastown office with free drinks and snacks
  • Summer Friday half-days
  • Weekly ‘Happy Hour’ and team social events
