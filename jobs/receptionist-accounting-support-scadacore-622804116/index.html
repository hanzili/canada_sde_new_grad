Full Description

Company description

SCADACore is a fast-growing SaaS technology company delivering fully-managed Cloud SCADA / IIoT solutions to customers in Energy, Aerospace, Environmental, and Health & Safety industries.

Job description

SCADACore is seeking a reliable and detail-oriented Receptionist / Accounting Support to assist with front-desk operations, customer invoicing, and shipping coordination.

This role focuses primarily on invoicing and shipping tasks. The ideal candidate has strong computer skills, proficiency in Microsoft Office, and experience with ERP systems such as SAP or Microsoft Dynamics.

As a growing technology company, we offer meaningful opportunities for professional growth and expanded responsibilities over time for individuals who demonstrate reliability, initiative, and strong performance.

Role and Responsibilities:

  • Prepare and issue monthly customer invoices through SAP
  • Manage customer-specific invoicing, adhere to unique instructions, and handle multi-provincial sales taxes
  • Support purchasing and receiving processes through SAP
  • Package and prepare items for shipment
  • Utilize UPS, Purolator, and FedEx portals to manage and dispatch shipments
  • Ensure accurate and timely shipment delivery through careful review and adherence to customer-specific shipping requirements
  • Maintain accurate records for invoicing, inventory, and shipments
  • Provide general administrative and operations support as needed

Job Requirements:

  • Reliable and consistent in daily responsibilities
  • Knowledge or experience working with SAP or Microsoft Dynamics
  • Strong attention to detail with excellent organizational skills
  • Comfortable working independently on routine, process-driven tasks
  • Proficient with Microsoft Office, especially Excel
  • Strong written and verbal communication skills in English
  • Able to adapt quickly to changing priorities in a fast-paced environment
  • Experience using courier shipping portals (Purolator, FedEx, UPS)
  • Experience with invoicing and accounting portals (OpenInvoice, JobuTrax, Ariba, etc.)

Nice To Haves:

  • Experience with website updates, social media management, and marketing
  • Experience with staffing or recruitment process

Job Type: Full-time

Pay: From $50,000.00 per year

Benefits:

  • Dental care
  • Paid time off
  • Vision care

Work Location: In person
