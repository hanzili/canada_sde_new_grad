About the Role

Assist in SEO, ads, content, analytics

About OSiM

Small digital agency with growth-focused culture

Full Description

Are you a digital person who loves data as much as a well-designed Instagram post? We are looking for a versatile, proactive Digital Marketing Associate to join our team. This is a "Swiss Army Knife" role; you’ll handle everything from administrative tasks that keep the office humming to getting your hands dirty in SEO, Paid Ads, and content strategy.

﻿If you are eager to learn the inner workings of a digital agency and aren't afraid to wear multiple hats, this is your home.

Key Responsibilities

  • The Foundation (Admin):  Support the senior team with scheduling, client reporting preparation, and basic project management.
  • The Creative (Content & SEO):  Draft engaging copy for website blogs and social media. You will learn to optimize this content for search engines using keyword research and on-page SEO best practices.
  • The Visibility (SEO Support):  Assist with "under-the-hood" website tasks, such as updating meta tags, fixing broken links, and performing basic SEO audits to help our clients rank higher on Google.
  • The Strategy (Paid Ads):  Assist in the setup, monitoring, and optimization of Google Ads and Social Media ad campaigns (we will train you!).
  • The Brains (Data):  Pull weekly performance metrics from Google Analytics and Search Console, identify trends, and help translate "numbers" into "stories" for our clients.
  • The Support:  Hop in where needed, whether it’s researching a new industry trend or proofreading a website before launch.

What You’ll Need to Succeed

  • Curiosity:  You don’t need to be an expert yet, but you must be a quick study who asks great questions.
  • Technical Literacy:  Comfortable with Google Workspace. Bonus points if you’ve played around with Canva, WordPress, or SEO tools like Ahrefs, Siteguru or Semrush.
  • Attention to Detail:  You’re the person who catches the typo in a text message and notices when a link is broken.
  • Analytical Mindset:  You enjoy finding the "why" behind the data and have a basic understanding of how search engines work.
  • Writing Chops:  Ability to adapt your voice to different brands while keeping search intent in mind.

Why Join Us?

  • Holistic Mentorship:  You won't just learn one niche; you'll see the full 360-degree view of digital marketing, from SEO to Paid Social.
  • Career Path:  This role is designed to help you discover what you love, whether it’s data analysis, creative content, or technical SEO.
  • Culture:  We are a small firm, getting our virtual team a bit more back into the office. Your role will be mostly office-based but we value flexibility.
