We Are Teva

We’re Teva, a leading innovative biopharmaceutical company, enabled by a world-class generics business. Whether it’s innovating in the fields of neuroscience and immunology or delivering high-quality medicine worldwide, we’re dedicated to addressing patients’ needs now and in the future. Here, you will be part of a high-performing, inclusive culture that values fresh thinking and collaboration. You'll have the room to grow, the flexibility to balance life with work, and the opportunity to better health worldwide, together.

Our Team, Your Impact

Teva Canada is happy to announce the continuation of the Summer Student Intern Program for 2026. Summer employment opportunities will be from May to August 2026. This program is eligible for students enrolled in a post-secondary program and who will return to full-time studies in September 2026.

Power BI Support Intern at 30 Novopharm Court in Toronto

Business Hours: 9:00am – 5:00pm, Monday to Friday

Required to be on site for up to five (5) days

Duties Include:

  • Develop, maintain, and enhance Power BI reports and dashboards.
  • Leverage data from existing enterprise data lakes and internal systems to support reporting needs.
  • Collaborate with business users to gather, clarify, and document reporting requirements.
  • Design intuitive and visually effective dashboards using data visualization best practices.
  • Apply creative approaches to presenting information and solving reporting challenges.
  • Perform basic testing and validation to ensure data accuracy and reliability.
  • Maintain documentation for reports, data sources, and assumptions.
  • Act as a technical contact between business teams and global IT support groups to help develop or refine data requirements when needed.

Next Steps:

  • Applicants who meet the criteria may apply here, online.

Your Skills and Experience

The following general criteria must be met in order to apply:

  • Legally eligible to work in Canada and 18 years of age.

  • Registered as a full-time, post-secondary student in 2026-2027, and returning to full-time studies in fall 2026 – must provide proof of enrollment.

  • Available to work from May 2026 through to August 2026.
  • Satisfactory completion of criminal background checks.

Consideration Preference:

  • All children of regular, full-time or regular part-time employees, who meet functional requirements, will be provided first consideration.
  • External Summer Student applicants are considered if a Summer Student position cannot be filled from internally referred candidates.
  • Positions require specific academic focus and/or related work experience, and preference will be given to candidates who best meet these qualification requirements.
  • Candidates who do not meet specific education and/or experience requirements will only be considered for roles that do not have function-specific limitations.
  • Applications for Summer Student positions will ONLY be accepted between (no exceptions) posting period from March 11 to March 20, 2026.

Please Note:

  • Students may work at Teva Canada for a maximum of two (2) years.
  • If selected for a Summer Student opportunity, the student must work the full duration of the contract and achieve a Successful rating in final performance review to be eligible to return to Teva Canada.
  • Applications from multiple family members will be accepted, however, only one (1) hire will be considered per family. This practice is in place to provide greater opportunity to the children of Teva Canada employees.

Next Steps:

  • Applicants who meet the criteria may apply for positions, starting March 11, 2026.
