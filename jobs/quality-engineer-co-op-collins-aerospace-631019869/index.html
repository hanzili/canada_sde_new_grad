About the Role

Support shop-floor quality, investigate defects, review drawings

About Collins Aerospace

Global aerospace leader, structured co-op program

Full Description

Date Posted: 2026-02-23

Country: Canada

Location: CA-ON-OAKVILLE-1400 ~ 1400 S Service Rd W ~ 1400 SERVICE

Position Role Type: Onsite

What You Will Do

  • Support daily quality activities on the shop floor
  • Help investigate defects and support root cause analysis
  • Review drawings and work instructions
  • Track, analyze and automate quality data
  • Work with Engineering, Manufacturing, and Quality teams

What You Will Learn

  • How quality systems work in a manufacturing environment
  • Basics of root cause and corrective action
  • How to read engineering drawings and specifications
  • Practical experience with continuous improvement

Qualifications You Must Have

  • Currently pursuing a degree in Engineering (Aerospace, Mechanical, Industrial, Manufacturing, or related field).
  • Please be advised that the successful candidate must meet all requirements under International Traffic in Arms Regulations (ITAR) & Controlled Goods Directorate (CGD) and be eligible to work in Canada. Securing CGD Clearance, as required by Canadian law, may take 1-2 weeks for Canadian citizens and permanent residents and up to 5 months or longer for international students.

Qualifications We Preferred

  • Strong problem-solving skills
  • Basic knowledge of engineering drawings
  • Good communication skills
  • Comfortable using Microsoft Office & Programming Languages

Our Advanced Structures team leverages advanced materials and additive manufacturing to develop complex interior and exterior solutions. These solutions ensure structural integrity of the aircraft, help aircraft take off and land, stay trim in the air, move forward, carry cargo and conduct rescues. We delight our customers with superior products and best-in-class service. Our global team is committed to continuous improvement – we work hard to make our solutions lighter-weight, stronger and more technically advanced, so that plane travel can be safer, more affordable, and more sustainable in the years to come. We are looking for the best and brightest to fly and land with us!

WE ARE REDEFINING AEROSPACE. Please ensure the role type (defined below) is appropriate for your needs before applying to this role.

Onsite: Employees who are working in Onsite roles will work primarily onsite. This includes all production and maintenance employees, as they are essential to the development of our products.

At Collins, the paths we pave together lead to limitless possibility. And the bonds we form – with our customers and with each other -- propel us all higher, again and again.
