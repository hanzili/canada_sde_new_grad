IT Field Technician / Desktop Support TechnicianJob Overview

We are seeking a dependable and technically skilled IT Field Technician / Desktop Support Technician to join our growing technology services team. This role primarily focuses on break/fix services, desktop support, and on-site technical troubleshooting across client environments.

The ideal candidate has hands-on experience in diagnosing and resolving hardware issues, supporting end-user systems, and delivering high-quality field-based IT services.

Key Responsibilities

  • Provide on-site support for desktops, laptops, POS systems, printers, and peripherals
  • Perform break/fix services, including part replacements and device swaps
  • Deliver desktop support for Windows-based systems
  • Diagnose and resolve hardware and software issues
  • Install and configure Windows operating systems
  • Support POS devices, receipt printers, scanners, and payment terminals
  • Install and troubleshoot basic networking equipment
  • Follow ticket instructions and meet SLA timelines
  • Communicate with dispatch and technical teams
  • Submit detailed service reports upon job completion

Requirements

  • Minimum 1 year of experience in Break & Fix and Desktop Support
  • Practical experience troubleshooting desktops, laptops, and peripherals
  • Working knowledge of Windows OS and basic networking concepts
  • Experience with printers, POS systems, or enterprise hardware (preferred)
  • Reliable transportation and ability to travel locally
  • Smartphone and/or laptop for ticket updates
  • Strong troubleshooting and problem-solving skills
  • Excellent communication and customer service abilities
  • Legally authorized to work in Canada

Preferred Qualifications

  • Experience in Break & Fix environments
  • Experience in Desktop Support / End-User Support roles
  • CompTIA A+ / Network+ certifications
  • Familiarity with hardware from Dell, HP, Lenovo, Zebra, NCR, Honeywell, or Cisco
  • MSP or enterprise field services experience

Why Join Us?

This position offers an excellent opportunity to work in dynamic field environments, gain exposure to diverse technologies, and expand your technical expertise. You will play a critical role in maintaining system reliability while delivering professional IT support services.
