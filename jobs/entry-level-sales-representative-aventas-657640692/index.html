🚀 About Aventas

Aventas is a Omni-channel marketing agency that has worked along side brands like Telus, Rogers, Bell, RBC & BMO. What we do is we help brand unleash their unique point of view by conducting all our sales and marketing face-to-face.

We are on the HUNT for someone to join as an Entry Level Sales Representative! This individual will be someone who likes to work in a fun and dynamic environment with the opportunity to learn and grow within the role. We are not looking for someone with a ton of experience because full training will be provided for the role. Every candidate will also be paired up with a Seasoned Field Manager for a duration of 6 weeks.

💡👨‍💼 The ideal candidate will be someone who is a motivated self-starter, has OUTSTANDING communication skills and an outgoing personality. A successful representative will be sourcing new sales opportunities, navigating customers through our sales cycle and utilizing our CRM.

✅ Why join Aventas?

  • Personal and professional development
  • 1 on 1 mentorship
  • Opportunities to grow into leadership roles
  • Opportunity for travel - we have travelled around the world with our team (Check out our website/social media)
  • Bonuses and un-capped commissions

📝 Description

  • Engage with new customers by having educated conversations around Cellphones, Internet, Security and TV
  • Attend sales and leadership seminars
  • Develop public speaking skills
  • Learn the art of the sale
  • Identify a customers need and deliver presentations
  • Opportunities to work at events like the CNE, Montreal Grand Prix !

📝 Qualifications:

  • Ambitious self starter
  • Motivated by reward
  • Wants to learn and grow
  • Likes high energy, fast-paced environments
  • Wants to be paid for performance (commissions)
  • Outgoing personality with strong interpersonal skills
  • Competitive and values a great work culture
  • 1-2 years experience in retail, hospitality or customer service is an asset
  • Must be able to commute to ETOBICOKE
