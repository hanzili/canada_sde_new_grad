Summer Student (IT Support/ Helpdesk / Co-op)

at Levitt-Safety

📍 Location Oakville, ON L6H 5T5
📋 Details Co-op · 4mo · Summer 2026
📅 Posted Apr 01, 2026

About the Role

Set up equipment and accounts; provide IT support.

About Levitt-Safety

Century-old safety partner in Canada.

Full Description

Making Canada a safer place to live and work for over 90 years.

As Canada's trusted safety partner since 1935, we work alongside customers coast to coast to help ensure people return home safely at the end of every shift.

We’re hiring a Summer IT Student to support our team.

Based in Oakville, this is a great opportunity for someone who already has a foundation in IT and wants hands-on experience in a fast-paced environment.

What you’ll be doing:

  • Set up equipment and accounts for new hires
  • Provide basic IT support (password resets, troubleshooting, access issues)
  • Help manage IT equipment and inventory
  • Support onboarding/offboarding tasks
  • Escalate more complex issues as needed

What we’re looking for:

  • Someone with a baseline IT background (school, co-op, or experience)
  • Able to jump in and contribute with minimal training
  • Strong communicator with a customer-first mindset
  • Availability: May-August (flexible)

This role, while designed as a summer support position, may offer potential for future opportunities. It is best suited for someone with a foundational understanding of IT support who can troubleshoot common issues independently.

Application & Hiring Process

All AI-supported results are reviewed by a member of our recruitment team before any hiring decisions are made.
