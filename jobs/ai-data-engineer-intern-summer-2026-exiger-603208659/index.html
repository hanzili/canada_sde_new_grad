### **Role Summary**

Exiger is seeking a highly motivated **AI Data Engineer Intern** to join our **Tech Enabled Services (TES): Research & Delivery** team for Summer 2026. ### **Key Responsibilities**

As an AI Data Engineer Intern, you will contribute to applied AI research and delivery projects with a focus on automation and intelligent workflows:

- Support the development and evaluation of **agentic AI workflows** that automate research and operational tasks - Work with **natural language processing (NLP)** techniques to enable AI systems to search, retrieve, and synthesize information from structured and unstructured data sources - Assist in designing and implementing **data pipelines, retrieval mechanisms, and APIs** that support AI-driven workflows - Collaborate with data scientists and engineers to operationalize research concepts into repeatable, scalable processes - Experiment with large language models (LLMs) to perform higher-order tasks such as:
