About the Role

Design and build AI agents and tooling

About Huawei Canada

Global tech giant with AI research focus

Full Description

Huawei Canada has an immediate co-op opening for an Engineer. About the team: The Technology Planning and Cooperation Department promotes strategic innovation across all of Huawei Canada. Working with fellow experts, the focus is researching new and upcoming areas of technology as well as strategic planning to help achieve long term vision of Huawei. This department offers a unique opportunity to leverage both technical and business skills.

About the job:

  • Design, build, and optimize AI Coding Agents capable of autonomous planning, code writing, and debugging.
  • Proficiently utilize and deeply integrate cutting-edge AI coding tools such as Claude Code, Codex, and Antigravity, exploring their limits in complex engineering scenarios.
  • Design complex task workflows (Agentic Workflows) based on LangGraph or similar agent frameworks to address long-chain task automation.
  • Track the latest advancements in the LLM field and quickly translate them into usable tool features.
  • Establish an evaluation system for AI tools to improve the accuracy of agents in code generation and logical reasoning.

The total target annual compensation (based on 2,080 hours per year) ranges from $58,000 to $104,000 depending on education, experience, and demonstrated expertise.

Job requirements

About the ideal candidate:

  • An undergraduate student majoring in Computer Science (CS), with proficiency in programming languages such as Python, TypeScript, and Node.js.
  • You must be a "super user" of AI programming tools. You should be able to demonstrate how to complete the process from requirement to deployment in an extremely short time using tools like Claude Code.
  • Have a deep understanding of the operational mechanisms of large language models (LLMs), including token limits, hallucination handling, RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation), and the principles of semantic search.
  • Possess good code craftsmanship, able to write code architectures that are easy for AI to understand and maintain. Be familiar with technical components such as vector databases, AI Agents, and Skills.
  • Be able to adapt to the daily update pace of technological changes in the AI field, with a strong sense of self-motivation and curiosity.

Additional Information: Huawei Canada is committed to a fair, inclusive, and accessible recruitment process. If you require accommodation during any stage of the hiring process, please let us know and we will work with you to meet your needs.

All applications for this position are reviewed directly by our hiring team, we do not use artificial intelligence tools to screen or select candidates.
