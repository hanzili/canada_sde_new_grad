Full Description

Company Description

Afflo is a configurable transplant platform that accelerates your move to advanced transplant patient management, matching and allocation, and donor management. The leading transplant platform, Afflo is highly configurable and can hyper-scale to meet the unique demands of large, complex jurisdictions. Afflo's workflows were built together with clinical experts to capture the latest in medical science and best practices. Afflo’s automated ingestion of referral forms, configurable policy rules, patient acceptance criteria, and case management are all designed to help transplant professionals put their patients at the center of the process.
