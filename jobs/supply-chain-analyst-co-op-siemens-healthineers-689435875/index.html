About the Role

Develop supply chain metrics; support logistics and Kanban

About Siemens Healthineers

Global healthcare leader; strong operations exposure

Full Description

Join us in pioneering breakthroughs in healthcare. For everyone. Everywhere. Sustainably.

Our inspiring and caring environment forms a global community that celebrates diversity and individuality. We encourage you to step beyond your comfort zone, offering resources and flexibility to foster your professional and personal growth, all while valuing your unique contributions.

At Siemens Healthineers, we offer you a flexible and dynamic environment with opportunities to go beyond your comfort zone in order to grow personally and professionally. Sound interesting? Then come and join our global team as a Supply Chain Analyst Co-op.

This is a role well suited for a highly motivated student looking to build their skill set and gain hands-on experience. As a Supply Chain Analyst Co-Op, you will be responsible for:

  • Develop periodic scorecard for direct materials that monitors Purchase Price Variance (PPV) trends.
  • Track and trend price stability impact based on currency transfer rates (CTR).
  • Verify pricing model and quantities in costed bill of materials for finished goods is accurate to current pricing.
  • Assist in cycle counting activities for all direct materials.
  • Assist logistics team with warehouse space optimization planning via Value Stream Mapping of Current State versus Future State
  • Forecast space requirement that includes introduction of Mega, Check and current on Market Supply.
  • Monitor and record freight method and costs for all incoming materials
  • Assess stocking levels - planning parameter for Kanban pull system triggers
  • Confirm Kanban best practices are recognized and in place by all participants
  • Assist with overall supplier related compliance requirements that include facilitating Non-Disclosure Agreements, Code of Conduct, and general onboarding activities.

Required skills to have for the success of this role:

  • Completion of 2 years of a Post Secondary Business Program
  • Accredited University or College Program is Coop Eligible
  • Knowledge of general business concepts related to Quality, Cost and Delivery
  • Mid-level competency with Microsoft Office tools
  • General knowledge of activities/input that drive Cost of Goods Sold
  • Understanding of Managerial and Cost Accounting

The pay range for this position is $20-$32 hourly; however, base pay offered may vary depending on education, skills, and experience. Siemens Healthineers offers interns paid time off.
