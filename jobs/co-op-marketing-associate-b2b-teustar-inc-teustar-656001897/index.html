Full Description

Co-op Marketing Associate (B2B) – Teustar Inc.
Location: Toronto, ON (Hybrid / Field Work)
Job Type: Co-op / Internship (Full-time)
Duration: 3–6 Months (Flexible)

About Teustar

Teustar is a fast-growing startup that connects customers with local businesses through a smart digital platform. We help businesses increase visibility, generate real sales, and build strong customer relationships through modern marketing solutions.

Role Overview

We are seeking motivated and confident Co-op Marketing Associates to gain hands-on experience in B2B marketing and business development. This role includes both fieldwork and digital marketing, with a focus on onboarding local businesses onto the Teustar platform.

Key Responsibilities

  • Identify and approach local businesses (restaurants, services, retail)
  • Conduct in-person meetings and pitch Teustar services
  • Generate leads and convert them into active partners
  • Support B2B and digital marketing initiatives
  • Build and maintain client relationships
  • Meet weekly targets and report progress

Requirements

  • Enrolled in a Business / Marketing / Management program (Co-op eligible)
  • Strong communication and interpersonal skills
  • Confident in face-to-face business interactions
  • Self-motivated and target-driven
  • Basic digital marketing knowledge (preferred)
  • Own transportation is an asset

What You’ll Gain

  • Real-world experience in B2B sales & marketing
  • Startup exposure and business growth insights
  • Skill development in communication, negotiation, and closing
  • Opportunity for full-time role based on performance

Compensation

  • Performance-based incentives + commission
  • Travel allowance (if applicable)
