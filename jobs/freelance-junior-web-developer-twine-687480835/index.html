About the Role

Customize Shopify themes; launch and optimize store.

About Twine

Freelance marketplace connecting tech professionals and clients.

Full Description

This role involves providing expert consultation and hands-on support to prepare a Shopify store for launch. The ideal candidate will have a strong background in web development, with specific expertise in Shopify platform adjustments and optimizations. The work is fully remote and is expected to be completed within a short timeframe, making it suitable for professionals who can deliver high-quality results efficiently.

Deliverables

  • Review the current Shopify store setup and identify areas for improvement or adjustment
  • Implement necessary changes to ensure the site is fully functional and optimized for launch
  • Troubleshoot and resolve any technical issues related to Shopify themes, plugins, or integrations
  • Provide recommendations for best practices in e-commerce site management and user experience
  • Communicate progress and deliverables clearly and promptly

Requirements

  • Proven experience in web development, with a focus on Shopify store setup and customization
  • Strong understanding of Shopify themes, Liquid templating, and app integrations
  • Ability to work independently and deliver results within tight deadlines
  • Excellent problem-solving skills and attention to detail
  • Effective communication skills for remote collaboration
  • Availability for a short-term, remote project

About Twine Twine is a leading freelance marketplace connecting top freelancers, consultants, and contractors with companies needing creative and tech expertise. Trusted by Fortune 500 companies and innovative startups alike, Twine enables companies to scale their teams globally.

Our Mission Twine's mission is to empower creators and businesses to thrive in an AI-driven, freelance-first world.
