About the Role

End-to-end motor control panel design

About DV8 Energy Inc

Oilfield electrical design firm in energy sector

Full Description

Based in Calgary, DV8 Energy has leveraged a deep understanding of the physics of rod pumping, advanced mathematics, computer science, cloud and IIoT technologies to bring Operators, Production Engineers, and Data Science teams together to unlock their combined potential.

At DV8 Energy, we cultivate a workplace where innovation thrives, collaboration is encouraged, and every team member plays a vital role in shaping the future of energy technology. Our values, culture, and commitment to excellence create an environment where individuals can grow, contribute, and succeed.

Your Background

  • We are looking for an Electrical Designer Engineer to be part of our team, specializing in the end-to-end design of industrial motor control panels for 480VAC, 120VAC, and 24VDC systems supporting oil well and production applications. This role takes ownership of technical execution—from schematics and layout to component selection—while actively collaborating across technical teams to support the delivery of practical, high-performance field-ready solutions.

Responsibilities

  • Create detailed 480VAC, 120VAC, 24VDC Motor Control panel schematics using AutoCad Electrical and/or E-Plan
  • Size Appropriate heating and cooling solutions
  • Design control panel layout
  • Work with vendors to achieve product customization and quality
  • Collaborate with the Applications team on product design
  • Collaborate with the Supply Chain team on component selection to achieve cost and delivery KPIs
  • Create documentation for wiring, mounting, installation and programming

Skills

  • End-to-end design of industrial motor control panels (480VAC, 120VAC, 24VDC), including schematics, layout, and thermal considerations
  • Proficiency with AutoCAD Electrical and/or EPLAN for production-ready control designs
  • Strong understanding of oil well / production environments and the practical requirements of field-deployed equipment
  • Ability to collaborate cross-functionally and take ownership from concept through release, including component selection and documentation

Qualifications

  • Degree in Electrical Engineering or related field (EIT welcome to apply)
  • 1-5 years experience in oil well and field production applications is preferred
  • Proven ability to excel in demanding technical environments, demonstrated through academic or professional distinction
  • Experience designing industrial control panels or motor control systems
  • Hands-on experience with AutoCAD Electrical and/or EPLAN
  • Familiarity with oil & gas, well operations, or industrial field environments
  • Exposure to IIoT, automation, or remote monitoring systems

Requirements

  • Valid drivers license
  • Criminal record check
  • Fluency in English is required

What We Offer

  • Paid Vacation
  • Company paid Benefit package
  • A team culture built on technical excellence, accountability, and continuous growth, where strong ideas are valued and individual contributions make a real impact
