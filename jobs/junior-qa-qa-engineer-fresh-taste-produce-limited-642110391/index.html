Full Description

Summary

We are looking for a Junior QA / QA Engineer to join our team. This role is ideal for someone starting their QA career with 2+ years of experience in software testing.

Location: Halton Hills, ON

Work Model: Hybrid – 3 days on-site at our Halton Hills office and 2 days remote

Hours of Work: Monday to Friday from 7am to 3pm

Wage: $28.85 - $30.00/hr. Contract role for 8 months.

Responsibilities

  • Prepare, maintain, and monitor QA test suites and test plans.
  • Execute manual test cases for applications, system patches, and application upgrades.
  • Perform functional, regression, smoke, sanity, and service-level testing.
  • Validate transactions, screen flows, and error scenarios to ensure accuracy and system stability.
  • Test releases from development to ensure they meet quality standards and specifications.
  • Work with developers to reproduce issues and verify bug fixes.
  • Write clear bug reports, test documentation, and maintain test evidence.
  • Perform defect retesting and post-deployment validation.
  • Translate business requirements into practical test scenarios.
  • Conduct peer reviews and overall QA quality checks.
  • Collaborate with project managers and cross-functional teams to support timely and high-quality releases.
  • Identify clear reproduction steps for issues reported by customers.
  • Verify builds for quality prior to production deployment.
  • Ensure compliance with company policies and procedures and perform other QA-related duties as required.

Requirements

  • 2+ years of experience in QA/testing or internship experience preferred.
  • Degree in Computer Science or Software Engineering.
  • Strong communication and collaboration skills.
  • Understanding of manual testing processes, test management, defect lifecycle, and STLC.
  • Experience or exposure to Data Quality Management (DQM).
  • Familiarity with issue or defect tracking systems such as JIRA or similar platforms used for managing and tracking bugs.
  • Strong attention to detail and problem-solving skills.

Nice to Have (Optional)

  • Basic SQL knowledge for testing or data validation.
  • Exposure to automation testing concepts or tools.

We are looking for someone who is detail-oriented, eager to learn, and interested in building a career in manual and automation of software testing and quality assurance.
