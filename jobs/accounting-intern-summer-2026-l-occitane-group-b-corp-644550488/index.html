About the Role

Prepare journal entries; reconcile GL; assist audits

About L'OCCITANE Group (B Corp)

Global beauty brand with CSR focus

Full Description

JOB SUMMARY: The Accounting Intern will gain hands-on experience with preparing journal entries, support documentation, analyzing and reconciling GL Accounts. The intern will receive exposure and experience using our SAP (ERP) system and will be exposed to our financial close processes and internal and external audit requirements.

JOB RESPONSIBILITIES:

  • Assist with Preparation of journal entries and maintenance of proper supporting documentations
  • Assist the accounting team with the monthly, quarterly, and yearly closings
  • Assist with bank and other balance sheet reconciliation process
  • Assist with internal and external audit requirements
  • Assist with preparation of government surveys and annual reports
  • Assist with ordering of deposit slips for stores
  • Ad hoc analysis and special projects as required

REQUIREMENTS: EDUCATION:

  • Pursuing an Accounting/Finance or Business degree

EXPERIENCE:

  • Previous experience in Accounting or Finance

SKILLS:

  • Proficiency with excel a must
  • Excellent oral, written, presentation and communication skills needed
  • Planning and organization skills with the ability to manage multiple duties simultaneously
  • Ability to manage deadlines

PHYSICAL & TRAVEL REQUIREMENTS:

  • Must be able to travel to and work from our Toronto Office following a hybrid schedule
  • Must be able to work up to 28 hours per week

BENEFITS INCLUDE:

  • Competitive compensation
  • School credits offered
  • A warm, open and friendly working environment
  • Generous employee discounts
