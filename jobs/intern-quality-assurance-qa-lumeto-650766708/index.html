About Lumeto

Lumeto’s immersive simulation lab, InvolveXR, aims to meaningfully reduce the social and financial cost of medical error. Through flexible, virtual simulated environments, we help improve clinician preparedness, confidence, and decision-making across the healthcare ecosystem.

The Role

Reporting to the QA leadership team, the QA Intern will support testing efforts across Lumeto’s multi-platform product ecosystem. This is an opportunity to gain hands-on experience in a fast-growing XR company, working on complex systems spanning Web, VR, Desktop, and Pixel Streaming environments. You’ll play a key role in ensuring product quality, stability, and performance, while building foundational QA and testing skills in a real-world environment.

This is a full-time, paid internship (12-16 weeks minimum), operating on an EST schedule.

Responsibilities

  • Execute daily functional testing across Web, VR, Desktop, and Pixel Streaming environments
  • Perform structured end-to-end test flows with defined scope and completion criteria
  • Conduct regression testing and routine validation to maintain product stability
  • Identify, document, and track bugs with clear reproduction steps
  • Support and maintain existing test coverage within automation frameworks
  • Perform repeatable QA operations to improve efficiency and turnaround time
  • Collaborate with developers and product teams to resolve issues

Qualifications

Must-Haves

  • 1–2 years of experience or academic exposure in QA, testing, or programming
  • Familiarity with Python or another programming language
  • Strong attention to detail and problem-solving mindset
  • Clear communication skills (written and verbal)
  • Currently pursuing or recently completed post-secondary education (2+ years / CEGEP or equivalent)

Nice-to-Haves

  • Experience with software testing (manual or automated)
  • Exposure to AI tools (e.g., ChatGPT, Claude, etc.)
  • Interest in gaming, playtesting, or immersive technologies (VR/XR)
  • Previous experience with complex or multi-platform products

What You’ll Gain

  • Hands-on experience testing cutting-edge XR healthcare technology
  • Exposure to real-world QA processes across multiple platforms
  • Mentorship and collaboration with experienced engineers and product teams
  • Opportunity to contribute to meaningful, high-impact products
