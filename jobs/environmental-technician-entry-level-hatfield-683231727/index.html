Full Description

Location: New Westminster or North Vancouver, BC.

About Hatfield Hatfield, a proudly Canadian company, has been serving our clients and our community for over 50 years. We have been recognized as one of Canada’s Top SME Employers in 2025. With 250 team members and growing, Hatfield provides high-quality environmental and social services for private and public sector clients around the world. When you join Hatfield, you choose a career that makes a difference in the lives of all those who share our planet.

We are seeking an Entry Level Environmental Technician to join our Infrastructure Environmental Management (IEM) team. This is primarily a field-based position.

Responsibilities

  • Primarily participate in field investigations and construction monitoring programs and reporting locally with flexibility to support our offices in Fort St John, Terrace, and Fort MacLeod.
  • Work long hours during busy months and on weekends when required.
  • Assist with data analysis and interpretation.

Education and Experience:

  • Recent graduate of a 2-year accredited diploma or a degree program in applied science, engineering technology, environmental science, or a related discipline.
  • Previous training, coursework and work experience (e.g., co-op’s) in environmental construction monitoring, soil and water sampling, and reporting in BC.
  • Eligibility to apply for Applied Science Technologist (AScT) designation within 6-12 months.

Other Assets/Skills:

  • A valid Canadian driver’s license for the past three years, experience driving a pickup truck and the ability to meet the requirements for our third-party automobile insurance policy.
  • Willingness to work long hours and in inclement outdoor weather conditions.
  • Vehicle is required to drive to various sites.
  • Strong communication and problem-solving skills.
  • Comfortable working on or near water and in variable weather conditions.
  • Ability to thrive in remote or field-based environments.
  • Team-oriented with a positive and adaptable mindset.
  • Detail-oriented with a commitment to data integrity and safety.

What's In It For You ?

  • Friendly and down-to-earth work culture
  • Matching group retirement plan
  • Comprehensive health insurance – medical/dental/vision coverage
  • Competitive vacation and special leave
  • Professional development
  • Relocation allowance

Diversity, Equity, Inclusion & Belonging: *Hatfield is committed to employment equity and workplace diversity. We strive for equity, inclusiveness, and diversity, irrespective of race, color, sex, age, national origin, religion, sexual orientation, gender identity and/or expression, or disability.

Hatfield acknowledges that our offices are located on the traditional and unceded territories of Indigenous Peoples. We also recognize and acknowledge the rights, cultures, interests, and aspirations of the many other Indigenous Peoples who have lived since time immemorial in the places we live and work. Hatfield is committed to building an inclusive workforce that is representative of the populations we serve, and to provide and support career opportunities for Indigenous Peoples. We warmly invite all Indigenous Peoples to apply.

How To Apply To apply, please submit your CV in confidence through the online form or email: resumes@hatfieldgroup.catsone.com. Please indicate “Entry Level Environmental Technician” in your subject line. Only short-listed candidates will be contacted.

Salary range: $52k. We extend an invitation to applicants of all levels to submit their candidacy for this role. Please be advised, that in the course of our assessment of your qualifications, we retain the right to adjust the seniority classification of the position accordingly.

Please note that we will only consider candidates who are authorized to work in Canada at the time of application. Proof of eligibility to work in Canada may be required prior to employment.*
