Job Purpose

The Accounting Clerk performs accounting-related tasks utilizing knowledge of the entire accounting cycle. This position performs accounts payable and accounts receivable tasks.

Responsibilities

  • Prepare the cash deposits and cash clearing schedule
  • Maintaining the funding/Chattel/Payouts for Audi Downtown and Audi York.
  • Posting and maintaining the licensing schedule.
  • Posting and verifying costs for the vehicle purchases- 180 and CDK.
  • Posting Deals- New, Used, Wholesale.
  • Managing external warranty, schedule review and payment submission
  • Managing rebates and submissions.
  • Perform other tasks based on management requirements and instructions.

Qualifications

  • Minimum of one year of related industry experience.
  • Background in the automotive or related industry is an asset.
  • College Diploma or University Degree.
  • Excellent communication, interpersonal and time management skills.
  • Ability to adapt to a changing environment and meet deadlines.
  • Proficient with CDK and/or Reynolds systems; ability to operate PC-based software, e.g. Excel, PowerPoint, Word.
  • Strong attention to detail.
  • Dealership Experience Required

Personal Attributes

  • Passion, motivation, focus and leadership skills.
  • Results focused approach.
  • Team player.
  • Strong communication skills both written and verbal.
  • Ability to influence, negotiate and mediate effectively.
  • Strong attention to detail
  • Ability to maintain confidentiality and professionalism.
  • Excellent interpersonal and team building skills.
  • Strong time management and organizational skills.
  • Honest, courteous and able to demonstrate strong work ethics.
  • Driven and ability to work with minimum supervision.
