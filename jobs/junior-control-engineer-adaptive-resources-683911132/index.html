Company Description

Adaptive Resources leverages scientific principles to innovate process control for complex manufacturing environments. The company offers advanced control technology solutions, process consulting, and systems integration to enhance operational efficiency and minimize variability. With a four-tier system for process control design—including instrument selection, systems integration, advanced control loops, and model-based predictive controls—Adaptive assists clients in optimizing performance and reducing waste and energy usage. They serve diverse industries such as food, power, metals, and manufacturing, providing tailored technology and expertise to meet unique challenges. Learn more about Adaptive Resources at adaptiveresources.com.

Junior Controls Engineer

We are seeking a motivated and detail-oriented Junior Controls Engineer to join our growing team. This role is ideal for someone who is technically capable, eager to learn, and interested in developing a career in industrial automation and control systems.

The successful candidate will work closely with senior engineers, technicians, and customers to support automation projects in industrial environments.

Key Responsibilities

  • Assist with the development, troubleshooting, and support of industrial control systems
  • Read and interpret basic electrical drawings, control schematics, and P&IDs
  • Identify common electrical and control devices including:
  • Motors
  • Valves
  • Breakers
  • Relays
  • Sensors and other field devices
  • Support commissioning and startup activities both in-office and onsite
  • Perform basic electrical measurements using a multimeter
  • Assist with configuring and assigning IP addresses for industrial devices and networks
  • Install and configure software applications and operating systems
  • Complete assigned tasks within project schedules and deadlines
  • Communicate effectively with colleagues, management, contractors, and customers
  • Escalate issues appropriately to ensure timely resolution during project execution
  • Follow all company procedures, safety requirements, and operational policies

Qualifications & Skills

  • Basic understanding of electrical systems, wiring practices, and voltage concepts
  • Familiarity with digital and analog inputs/outputs (I/O)
  • Understanding of wire gauge sizing and electrical fundamentals
  • Basic computer proficiency, including:
  • Microsoft Excel
  • Microsoft Word
  • Microsoft PowerPoint
  • Strong problem-solving skills with the ability to learn quickly
  • Positive attitude and willingness to learn new technologies and systems
  • Ability to work independently and as part of a team
  • Reliable and punctual with the ability to report to the office and job sites as required

Preferred Qualifications

  • Diploma or degree in Electrical Engineering Technology, Automation, Mechatronics, or a related field
  • Exposure to PLCs, HMIs, VFDs, or industrial networking is considered an asset
  • Previous co-op, internship, or field experience in industrial automation is an asset

What We Offer

  • Hands-on experience with real industrial automation projects
  • Mentorship from experienced controls engineers
  • Opportunities for technical growth and career advancement
  • Collaborative and fast-paced work environment
