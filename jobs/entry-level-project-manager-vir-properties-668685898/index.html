About the Role

About VIR Properties

Full training and mentorship; clear progression opportunities.

Full Description

We are looking for a motivated and organised Entry-Level Project Manager to join our growing marketing team. This is an excellent opportunity for someone at the beginning of their career who is eager to gain hands-on experience managing marketing projects, coordinating campaigns, and supporting successful project delivery.

You will assist with planning, organising, and executing marketing initiatives while working closely with team members to ensure projects run smoothly and objectives are achieved.

Key Responsibilities

  • Assist in managing marketing projects and campaign timelines
  • Support project planning, coordination, and deliverables
  • Collaborate with team members to execute marketing initiatives
  • Help organise events, promotions, and brand activations
  • Track project progress and support performance reporting
  • Communicate updates between teams to ensure smooth delivery
  • Support day-to-day project coordination and marketing operations

What We’re Looking For

  • Strong organisational and time management skills
  • Excellent communication and interpersonal abilities
  • Positive attitude and willingness to learn
  • Ability to multitask in a fast-paced environment
  • Attention to detail and proactive mindset
  • Interest in marketing, project management, or campaign coordination

What We Offer

  • Full training and ongoing mentorship
  • Supportive and collaborative team environment
  • Clear progression opportunities within the company
  • Hands-on experience managing marketing projects
  • Opportunity to work with a variety of brands and clients

Ideal Candidate This Role Is Perfect For Someone Who Is

  • Starting their career in project management or marketing
  • Organised, motivated, and eager to learn
  • Confident working with teams and coordinating tasks
  • Interested in long-term career development
