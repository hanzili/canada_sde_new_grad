Job Overview:

Ernie's TV Furniture Mattress & Appliance has been around for over 52 years!

In September, we moved to a larger, fully renovated location in Kingsville!

We are seeking a motivated and customer-oriented Sales Associate to join our team. As a Sales Associate, you will be responsible for providing exceptional customer service, promoting and selling products, and ensuring a positive shopping experience for our customers.

This position includes regular Weekend hours.

Duties:

  • Collaborate with team members to achieve sales goals
  • Greet customers and assist them in finding the right products

  • Provide product information and answer any questions or concerns

  • Process sales transactions accurately using the Point of Sale (POS) system

  • Maintain a clean and organized sales floor
  • Assist with inventory management, including restocking shelves and monitoring stock levels
  • Stay up-to-date with product knowledge and industry trends

Requirements:
- Previous experience in retail sales or customer service is preferred
- Strong time management skills to prioritize tasks effectively
- Excellent customer service skills with the ability to build rapport with customers
- Basic math skills for cash handling and calculating discounts
- Proficient in using technology, including POS systems and other sales tools
- Ability to upsell and cross-sell products to maximize sales opportunities
- Professional phone etiquette when assisting customers over the phone
- Ability to work in a fast-paced environment and adapt to changing priorities

We offer competitive pay and opportunities for career advancement. Join our team of dedicated people and contribute to our success in providing exceptional customer service.
