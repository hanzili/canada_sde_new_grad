Full Description

Site Based Civil Engineer (EIT) – Construction Seasonal Term

Why West Horizon?

Are you looking for a great place to build your career, after you complete your civil engineering degree?

Are you driven to find solutions?

Are you a team player looking to be part of building a great company, which has outstanding and interesting projects to construct?

We are seeking a highly organized and detail-oriented individual to join our Project Team as a Site Civil Engineer (EIT) for Heavy Construction for our Road and Bridge projects, during the construction season (generally April to October).

As part of the Project Team, you will play a crucial role in supporting the successful completion of heavy construction projects. This position comes with responsibility and accountability, and the opportunity for long term growth and development within our Company, after you have graduated with your civil engineering degree.

Project Team members must be dedicated to the success of West Horizon's projects. You must be confident, capable and show initiative in the tasks that you will be given. Our Project Team members are focused to ensure Client standards/specification and contractual requirements are met.

West Horizon aspires to be respected and renowned for delivering innovative infrastructure solutions and projects in challenging conditions. We do this by combining our experience, equipment and perseverance while continuously improving, innovating and modernizing.

Our values form the foundation of our company. Adhering to our values is critical in the execution of our company mission and vision, which is to:

· Continue to demonstrate perseverance in the face of adversity.

· Approach all business relationships in a professional, fair, and reasonable manner.

· Continue to improve as a team in all our activities.

Who you are?

You have a love and a passion for delivering and completing complex and diverse construction projects, and you want to be part of building innovative infrastructure in challenging conditions.

You are currently working on an undergraduate degree in civil engineering from a recognized Canadian University. You are confident in your current technical civil engineering university knowledge and abilities and you are able to take direction when required. A strong knowledge of English is required for verbal and written communications.

What you bring to West Horizon?

Detail Orientation. You pay close attention to the fine details of everything in Project scope of work, Client standards/specifications, documents, drawings and contractual requirements. You give tasks undivided attention, and you catch mistakes. You will obtain the ability to understand contract design standards/specifications and thereby ensure compliance details are met. You can review and understand Project drawings and ensure all contract and Client standards/specification and all other Project related documents are thoroughly reviewed on a continuous basis, as changes to may occur through the Project lifecycle.

You are Solutions Orientated. Meeting the design intent/Client standards and specifications and contract requirements is what’s important.

Team Player. You should get along with everyone. You understand that everyone is on the same team, and there to work "together" towards the same goals. You communicate excellently and can work with any member of the Project Team. Be an active champion to promote West Horizon in applicable company sponsored and private social events.

Display High Natural Intelligence Level with Sound Judgement. New concepts and ideas are understood and picked up quickly. You listen and don’t need regular reminders. You can be creative to help develop solutions and make sound judgement calls.

Confident and Assertive. You can stand up for what needs to be stood up for. Your task is to make sure everything is completed correctly but in a collaborative manner.

Reporting. To the West Horizon Engineering Manager for mentorship and technical guidance/support/assistance for Project issues and to the Project Manager for specific Project task assignments.

Typical Project/Daily Responsibilities, but not limited to:

· Assisting in the development of the monthly progress billing to the Client, as directed by the Project Manager.

· Participate in the daily/weekly progress/production meetings and offer ideas for any improvements.

· Consult with the Engineering Manager on an ongoing basis as it relates to Project drawings and Client standards/specifications and other documentation, where support is required.

· Participate in field engineering decisions and manage the documentation process in collaboration with the Engineering Manager and Project Manager.

· Support the Project Quality Manager in the preparation of documentation to support the deliverables within the Project Quality Plan.

· Update and management of the change order log and participate in the development of change trends/orders as it relates to the revised scope of work and associated quantity/schedule changes. This may require interfacing with material suppliers and subcontractors to obtained revised or updated pricing with any associated schedule inputs.

· Updating, red-lining and managing the development and final turnover of all as-built drawings, to the Client upon project completion.

· Assist, maintain and prepare accurate project documentation and turnover records as specified in the Project contract.

· Meet with the Engineering Manager on a weekly or ad hoc basis, to discuss Project technical issues than need resolution and/or to discuss any other questions/issues/concerns.

· Attend the daily safety meeting and bring any safety concerns to the attention to the Project Manager for resolution.

· Assist in the development of bid preparation, specifically around the generation of quantities, and in the preparation of material requisitions and subcontractor packages to obtain pricing to support the overall bid/execution strategy.

Minimum Requirements:

· Working on an undergraduate degree in civil engineering from a recognized Canadian University.

· Excellent time management skills to meet deadlines.

· Strong attention to detail and accuracy in work.

· Ability to work in remote sites.

· Effective communication skills to collaborate with other Project Team members.

· General computer proficiency, including basic knowledge of Microsoft products such as Excel, Power Point, Word, etc.

We offer competitive compensation packages based on experience and qualifications. This is an excellent opportunity for individuals looking to grow their career, with West Horizon and in the civil/structural construction industry, in general. If you are motivated, organized, and have a passion for building civil/structural based construction projects, we would love to hear from you.
