About Vanguard Consulting Inc.

Vanguard Consulting: dynamic, growth-oriented sales firm

Full Description

Overview
We are seeking a motivated and dynamic Junior Sales Representative to join our sales team. This entry-level position offers an excellent opportunity to develop your skills in outside sales, customer service, and marketing within a fast-paced environment. The ideal candidate will be passionate about building relationships, expanding client portfolios, and contributing to the growth of our company. Bilingual abilities, especially in Spanish, are highly valued to serve our diverse customer base effectively.

Duties

  • Engage in outside sales activities to identify and pursue new business opportunities
  • Assist with retail sales efforts by providing excellent customer service and product knowledge
  • Conduct telemarketing campaigns to generate leads and schedule appointments
  • Analyze market trends and customer feedback to develop targeted sales strategies
  • Manage sales administration tasks including updating client records and preparing reports
  • Negotiate terms and close sales deals with clients, ensuring mutual satisfaction
  • Collaborate with marketing teams to promote products and services effectively
  • Maintain ongoing communication with clients to foster long-term relationships and repeat business

Requirements

  • Previous experience or interest in outside sales, retail sales, or insurance sales is preferred
  • Strong communication skills, both verbal and written; bilingual abilities in Spanish are a plus
  • Excellent analysis skills to interpret market data and customer needs
  • Proven negotiation skills with a focus on customer satisfaction
  • Ability to work independently and as part of a team in a fast-paced environment
  • Customer service orientation with a professional attitude
  • Knowledge of sales administration tools and marketing principles is advantageous
  • Multilingual skills are highly desirable for serving diverse markets

This role offers an engaging environment for individuals eager to grow their career in sales while contributing to the success of our organization.

Job Type: Full-time

Pay: $3,000.00-$5,000.00 per month

Benefits:

  • Company events
  • On-site parking

Work Location: In person
