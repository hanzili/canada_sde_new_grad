About Flamingo+ Cannabis

Full Description

Company Overview
Join the Flamingo+ Cannabis Family!
At Flamingo+ Cannabis, we are more than a retailer. We are a growing community focused on quality products, fair pricing, and a welcoming customer experience. With fresh inventory arriving weekly and competitive pricing across Winnipeg, we continue to grow both our in store and digital operations.

Position Summary
We are seeking a Full Stack Developer to join our team and support the development and maintenance of our internal systems, website, and retail technology. This role is based out of Saint James in Winnipeg, Manitoba, with flexibility for work from home when appropriate.

You will work closely with our team to help maintain and improve the systems that support our website, data management, and point of sale operations. This role is well suited for someone with entry to intermediate web development experience who is comfortable working with existing systems and is interested in learning and growing in a practical retail environment.

Responsibilities

  • Assist in building, updating, and maintaining web based tools and internal applications
  • Support systems used for retail operations, reporting, and POS platforms
  • Help manage databases, background data, and system organization
  • Assist with website updates, performance improvements, and troubleshooting
  • Implement front end features using JavaScript and related technologies
  • Collaborate with team members on planning, testing, and problem solving
  • Maintain clean, organized code using version control tools such as Git

Qualifications

  • Entry to intermediate experience in web or software development
  • Working knowledge of JavaScript and SQL
  • Familiarity with at least one back end language such as Java, C#, or Python
  • Basic understanding of databases such as MySQL
  • Willingness to learn new tools, systems, and technologies
  • Strong attention to detail and problem solving skills
  • Ability to work independently and collaboratively within a team
  • Education or training in web development, computer science, or a related field is an asset

Nice to Have

  • Exposure to cloud platforms such as AWS or Azure
  • Familiarity with REST APIs
  • Experience supporting or working with POS or retail systems
  • Previous experience in a retail or customer focused environment

What We Offer

  • Flexible work arrangements including work from home options
  • A supportive, team focused work environment
  • Opportunities to develop practical, hands on technical skills
  • The chance to contribute to real systems used in daily retail operations
  • Competitive compensation based on experience

Join Our Team
If you are interested in building your technical skills, enjoy learning by doing, and want to be part of a growing Canadian cannabis retailer, we would love to hear from you. Join Flamingo+ Cannabis and help support the technology behind our stores and community.
