Junior Bookkeeper

About Insight Advantage Inc

Small firm offering training and growth opportunities

Full Description

About the Role

Insight Advantage is looking for a Junior Bookkeeper to join our team at our Leamington office. This is a hands-on, in-office position suited for someone with foundational bookkeeping knowledge and 1–2 years of practical experience who is looking to grow their skills in a supportive environment.

You'll work alongside our team to maintain accurate financial records, process payroll, and support day-to-day accounting operations. If you're detail-oriented, comfortable with numbers, and ready to take the next step in your bookkeeping career, we'd love to hear from you.

What You'll Do

  • Calculate and prepare cheques for payroll processing
  • Maintain financial records using both manual and computerized bookkeeping systems
  • Establish, maintain, and balance various accounts and general ledgers
  • Post journal entries and prepare trial balances
  • Reconcile accounts on a regular basis
  • Prepare financial statements and other statistical/accounting reports
  • Support process improvements within the accounting function

What We're Looking For

Education & Experience:

  • College diploma, CEGEP certificate, or equivalent combination of education and experience in bookkeeping or accounting
  • 1–2 years of hands-on bookkeeping or accounting experience

Technical Skills:

  • Proficiency with accounting software (experience with Sage is a strong asset)
  • Comfortable working with Microsoft Office (Excel, Word, Outlook, PowerPoint)
  • Familiarity with spreadsheets and general office technology

Personal Qualities:

  • Strong attention to detail and accuracy
  • Organized, dependable, and able to work independently
  • Effective communicator — both written and verbal
  • Comfortable in a fast-paced environment with shifting priorities and deadlines
  • Team player with good interpersonal skills and sound judgement

Requirements

  • Must have own reliable transportation and a valid driver's licence
  • Must be able to pass a criminal record check and basic security clearance
  • Must be bondable
  • Must be authorized to work in Canada (Canadian citizen, permanent resident, or valid work permit holder)

What We Offer

  • Employer-paid learning and training opportunities
  • Variable/compressed work week options
  • Free on-site parking
  • Team-building opportunities and a collaborative work culture

Only candidates authorized to work in Canada will be considered. We thank all applicants for their interest; however, only those selected for an interview will be contacted.
