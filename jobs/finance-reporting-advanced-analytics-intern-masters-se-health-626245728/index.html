About SE Health

Full Description

As a master’s student at SE Health, you’ll gain hands-on experience contributing to real-world projects that improve lives, strengthen communities, and shape the future of health care and social innovation.

Project Summary

The Finance Reporting & Advanced Analytics Intern will support a focused 12–16 week sprint aimed at strengthening Financial performance measurement and planning capabilities, and advanced analytics insights.

This role combines operating model redesign, AI exploration, performance benchmarking, and applied analytics to drive measurable impact across key service lines.

The intern will work closely with Finance leadership and cross-functional partners to:

  • Design data-driven, measurable performance scorecards aligned to strategic priories
  • Explore AI capabilities within Finance functions and identify automation opportunities
  • Apply advanced analytics to pinpoint cost and outcome drivers and translate insights into program recommendations to improve financial impact
  • Design data-driven performance scorecards that enable proactive financial management and decision-making

This placement is ideal for a student interested in finance transformation, health system performance, AI in operations, and data-driven decision-making.

Responsibilities

Advanced Analytics & Service Model Insights

  • Apply advanced analytics to OHaH bundles, SDC Transitions, and Transitions models
  • Contribute to redesigning the financial planning model, enabling a shift to driver‑based planning
  • Pinpoint key drivers of cost, throughput, and outcomes
  • Translate quantitative insights into actionable program recommendations to improve financial performance and sustainability
  • Support development of executive-ready insights, dashboards, and decision-support materials
  • Design integrated, data-driven performance scorecards to monitor financial and operational impact
  • Explore and assess AI-enabled tools, predictive analytics models, and automation opportunities within Finance

What You’ll Gain

  • Direct exposure to senior Finance and operational leadership
  • Opportunity to apply advanced analytics to real-world healthcare service models
  • Experience integrating AI exploration into core finance operations
  • A strategic, high-impact sprint with measurable outcomes

Qualifications

  • Currently enrolled in a Master’s program in MBA, Finance, Data Analytics, Health Informatics, or related field
  • Strong analytical and quantitative skills (experience with Excel, Power BI, Python, R required, Adaptive Planning is an asset)
  • Experience conducting benchmarking, financial modeling, operating model analysis, or performance measurement design
  • Interest in AI applications in finance and business operations
  • Ability to synthesize complex data into clear strategic insights and executive-ready recommendations
  • Strong communication skills and comfort presenting to senior stakeholders
  • Curious, future-forward thinker who thrives in structured, high-impact projects

About SE Health

SE Health is a not-for-profit social enterprise advancing health with heart. With 115+ years of impact, we bring hope, happiness and exceptional care to people and communities across Canada. We lead with empathy, dignity and purpose while building a future where everyone can realize their full health and well-being potential. We’re also an inclusive, supportive workplace offering competitive compensation, strong benefits and real opportunities to grow. We’re All In Together.

COVID-19: To protect the health of our clients, teams, and communities, all SE Health employees must be fully vaccinated (two doses, 14+ days since the final dose).
Accessibility: If you require accommodations due to illness or disability, please contact Talent Acquisition at careers@sehc.com.

AI and compensation details: We use AI to take notes during our interview. All applications and interviews are reviewed by our Talent Acquisition team. This role is a new addition to our team. The hiring pay range is $26.00 per hour.
