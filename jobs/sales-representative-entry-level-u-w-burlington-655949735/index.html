About the Role

About U W Burlington

Motorsports/home improvement sales, strong growth opportunities.

Full Description

Overview
UW Burlington is looking for motivated, people-oriented individuals to join our sales team representing premium brands in the NASCAR and motorsports industry as well as the home improvement industry. This is a hands-on, entry-level role with real growth potential for the right person. This role offers an excellent opportunity for individuals looking to develop their sales skills, build professional relationships, and grow within a supportive environment. The ideal candidate will be eager to learn, possess strong communication skills, and have a passion for helping customers find solutions that meet their needs.

What You'll Do

  • Represent and demonstrate home improvement and FW1 products at events and outreach sites in Burlington and surrounding areas
  • Engage and connect with customers in a professional and energetic manner
  • Support promotional campaigns and marketing initiatives
  • Work toward individual and team targets
  • Participate in ongoing training and team development sessions

What We're Looking For

  • Strong communicator who enjoys meeting new people
  • Positive attitude, professional presentation, and a competitive drive
  • Ability to work on-site in Burlington and at local events
  • Legally authorized to work in Canada
  • 1–2 years in sales, customer service, or hospitality is an asset — motivated entry-level candidates are welcome

What We Offer

  • Full training and mentorship from day one
  • Fast-track advancement opportunities
  • Team events and weekly meetings
  • Exposure to professional networking in the motorsports industry
  • Inclusive, high-energy team environment

Pay: $700.00-$1,000.00 per week

Work Location: In person
