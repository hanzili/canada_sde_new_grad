Position Overview: We take pride in the quality of our work and provide a safe work environment for our people. Safety is not just a culture; it is a founding principle of our business. From trucks with 100,000 running hours to trucks that can run themselves, there is endless opportunities to carve out your future with Finning.

This summer student position is responsible for carrying out standardized tests on used petroleum products, data entry and other lab related duties as required. This is a Monday to Friday position based in Edmonton at our Regional Distribution and Field Service Centre.

Job Description: Collective Agreement Details:

  • Collective Agreement
  • Carrying out standardized tests on used petroleum products, data entry and other lab related duties as required.

Qualifications:

  • The successful candidate will be in progress working toward a chemical/science degree or diploma
  • Detail orientation with excellent interpersonal, organizational, verbal and written communication skills
  • Team player able to work with minimal supervision in a fast paced environment
  • Previous oil lab experience, and advanced computer skills would be assets
  • The successful candidate will be required to stand for prolonged periods of time with repetitive movements and the ability to lift 50lbs
  • Possess advanced keyboarding skills and have sufficient knowledge of computers and software programs.
  • **Candidate selection will be in compliance with the terms of the current collective agreement**
