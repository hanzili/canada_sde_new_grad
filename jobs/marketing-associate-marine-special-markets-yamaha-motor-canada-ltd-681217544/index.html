About YAMAHA MOTOR CANADA LTD.

Global motor brand with marine product leadership

Full Description

Yamaha Motor Corporation (“YMC”) is a publicly listed Japanese Corporation in the business of manufacturing and distributing recreational product to dealers and customers around the world. YMC is a Kando creating company; “offering more excitement and a more fulfilling life for people”.

Yamaha Motor Canada Ltd (“YMCA”) is a subsidiary of YMC, responsible for distributing Yamaha Motor product to Canadian dealers and consumers. YMCA’s Vision is to “Create Lifelong Memories by Connecting People through Unique Canadian Outdoor Experiences”. YMCA executes its day-to-day activities in reference to its defined values, “Be Inclusive”, “Think Customer”, “Own it!”, “Innovate Now!”, “Trust & Integrity”, “Be Agile”, and “Drive results”.

Position Information

  • Department: Marketing & Brand Development

  • Location: 480 Gordon Baker Road

  • Hybrid

  • Status: Full Time

  • Reports to: Team Lead, Product Marketing

Summary Of Responsibilities Oversee marketing projects for Outboard, Sport Boat and WaveRunner product lines and accessories, including promotional materials, content development and event support. Support Yamaha’s OEM, commercial and government initiatives. Grow the Yamaha brand in Canada through influencers, boat builders and industry stakeholders. Evaluate marketing ROI and recommend actions. Manage budgets for marine sponsorship, promotion and public relations for products, rigging and accessories. Partner with product planning, accessories, sales and technical teams to align marketing with positioning and sales priorities. Communicate strategies and results to the broader marketing team and key stakeholders, and model Accelerate Together values daily.

Key Duties And Responsibilities

  • Develop and produce corporate and dealer showroom promotional materials for print, TV, radio and/or digital platforms, including opportunities for cross promotion to expand Yamaha’s presence in the market.
  • Develop and produce marine content to support digital marketing efforts and marketing campaigns, promoting products, rigging, accessories, lifestyle and experiences.
  • Work closely with the Marine OEM & Special Markets sales team to develop Yamaha’s strategy to ensure it is well represented with OEM, commercial and government partners, as well as identify opportunities to grow this area of the business. Develop content and marketing materials in support of these efforts.
  • Manage media, influencer and industry outreach, including creation and distribution of news releases, coordination of product testing, and planning of media events. Provide recommendations on partners and deliverables to ensure business value and marketing impact.
  • Work with events associate on consumer shows and corporate events, including unit logistics support.
  • Plan and administer annual marketing budget(s) for Outboard, Sport Boat and WaveRunner (products & accessories).

Education Requirements And/or Experience Requirements

  • Degree/Diploma: Post-Secondary, University preferred
  • Program of Study: Marketing, journalism, communications or related
  • Years of Experience: 1-5 Years
  • Type of Experience: Marine required; marketing preferred

Skills And Abilities

  • A comprehensive understanding of the industry and our customer.
  • Understanding of marketing concepts and experience with managing digital media and other marketing communication channels.
  • Outstanding time management, organization and communication skills
  • Proficient in business and creative writing, specifically copywriting.
  • Ability to multi-task and work independently with a high degree of initiative
  • Excellent in MS Office and basic understanding of Adobe Creative Cloud, social media savvy
  • Outgoing personality
  • Bilingualism in French/English an asset

Travel And Overtime Requirements

  • Occasional travel in support of conferences, shows and new product launches.
  • Occasional overtime required to support conferences, launches and shows.
