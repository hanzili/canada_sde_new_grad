Summer Student, Human Resources Assistant

NOC #12101

External Posting #2026-06

280 hours total - 35 hours/week for 8 weeks

One (1) Temporary Full-time Student Position Available

This is a vacant position

Barrie, Ontario

Camphill Communities Ontario is a not-for-profit organization that provides support services to adults and seniors in Simcoe County who have an intellectual disability. We support people in directing their own lives and in promoting partnerships, opportunities, well-being, freedom and contributions in their community.

SUMMARY

The summer student “Human Resources Assistant” will support the HR Team in day-to-day administrative and people-centered tasks. Under the guidance and direction of the HR Manager, the student/assistant will learn about inclusive employment practices, confidentiality requirements, and the principles of equitable workplace support. This position offers an interested student the opportunity to learn foundational HR skills, develop strong administrative and communication abilities, and gain experience working collaboratively within a diverse team. The Assistant will work in a welcoming, community-focused environment.

DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES

  • Data entry into BambooHR and Inclusion (time and attendance) system.
  • Complete personnel file audits, Scanning personnel folders
  • Minutes/note taking, at supervisor meetings and staff investigations as needed
  • Helping to summarize data and create reports
  • Aid with form creation
  • Aid with creation of training modules
  • Create job postings and upload to BambooHR
  • Manage internal/external postings, Review resumes
  • Assist HR with Onboarding of new staff as needed
  • Phone screen candidates, arrange for in person or virtual interviews as needed
  • Prepare Onboarding packages
  • Handle confidential information with utmost care and discretion
  • Ad hoc projects with Payroll, Finance and HR
  • Experience with BambooHR, Inclusion (time and attendance system) considered an asset
  • Ability to provide excellent customer service and working with others in a culturally diverse environment

EDUCATION AND SKILL REQUIREMENTS

  • Per funding requirements, these opportunities are open to candidates between 15 and 30 years of age (inclusive) at the start of employment;
  • Must be a Canadian Citizen, permanent resident, or person on whom refugee protection has been conferred and is legally entitled to work in Canada.
  • Previous experience and/or education in Business Administration, Human Resources, or a related field would be an asset but is not required
  • Able to communicate well, and work well with others
  • Positive attitude and willingness to learn and work with individuals with developmental disabilities
  • Must be able to commit to full contract duration

Able to provide a current (within 6 months’) satisfactory Criminal Background Check with Vulnerable Sector Screening.

Hourly Rate: $18.60 per hour, plus Employee Family Assistance Plan, plus more.

Date of Posting: April 29, 2026

  • If you are interested in this opportunity please submit your resume via BambooHR by 9:00am on May 11, 2026.

Camphill Communities Ontario is an equal opportunity employer. We welcome resumes from all qualified candidates. Only candidates selected for an interview will be contacted.

Accommodations are available on request for candidates taking part in all aspects of the recruitment and selection process.
