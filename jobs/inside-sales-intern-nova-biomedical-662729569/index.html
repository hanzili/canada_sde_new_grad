What You’ll Do

  • Account & Customer Intelligence

  • Conduct in-depth research on biopharma accounts, including: -depth research:

  • Company background, size, focus areas, and pipeline
  • Drug portfolios, therapeutic areas, and development stages
  • Funding status, acquisitions, mergers, or bankruptcies
  • Site locations and operational footprint
  • Identify accounts that are obsolete, inactive, closed, or no longer relevant and flag them for cleanup.
  • Map key stakeholders, decision-makers, and influencers within target organizations.

Market & Drug Research

  • Support market research initiatives related to biopharma trends, therapeutic focus areas, and customer segments.
  • Assist with drug-level research to help contextualize accounts and understand how customers may use Nova
  • Biomedical solutions.
  • Compile research findings into concise summaries for Account Managers and leadership.

CRM Data Management & Cleanup

  • Audit and validate CRM data to ensure accuracy and completeness.
  • Clean up outdated, duplicate, or incomplete account and contact records.
  • Add missing company, contact, and site information where available.
  • Ensure proper account hierarchies and naming conventions are followed.

Sales & Account Manager Support

  • Partner with Account Managers to:
  • Research missing contact or account detail
  • Identify additional sites or contacts within existing accounts
  • Prepare background information ahead of meetings or account planning sessions
  • Provide administrative and operational sales support as needed.

General Administrative & Team Support

  • Assist with data analysis, reporting, and internal documentation.
  • Support ad-hoc projects related to account planning, territory optimization, or portfolio expansion.
  • Collaborate cross-functionally with Inside Sales, Marketing, and Sales Operations.

Professional Contribution

  • Represent Nova Biomedical professionally with customers, peers, and internal stakeholders.

  • Contribute to a positive, high-performance sales culture through collaboration, accountability, and continuous improvement.
