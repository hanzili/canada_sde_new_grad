About the Role

Assist in architecture research and documentation tasks

About Co-operators

Leading Canadian financial services co-operative

Full Description

Company: CGL
Department: IT-Solutions Delivery P/C
Employment Type: Student
Work Model: Hybrid (2 days in office)
Language: English is required, French is an asset.
Additional Information: This/these role(s) is/are currently vacant

Location(s) opportunity is to be posted: Guelph (preferable), Toronto (preferable), Cambridge, Waterloo, Kitchener, Burlington

The Opportunity:

We are a leading Canadian financial services co-operative committed to being a catalyst for a sustainable and resilient society and our team is essential to deliver on this strategy. That’s why we prioritize our people, to ensure we provide a strong culture and development opportunities which enables our team to thrive and to live our purpose. The best part is that you will work with people that care passionately about you, our clients and our communities.

Our Information Technology team aspires to be a leader in applying technology to power business strategies. We connect concepts with solutions to create value and efficiencies for our clients, employees, and communities. Our success is driven by our skilled and diverse team who are passionate about excellence, innovation, and agility.

How you will create impact:

  • Research, gather, and organize information about AI applications and emerging technologies.
  • Contribute to the creation of roadmaps and transition plans around AI.
  • Help create and maintain architectural diagrams and documentation.
  • Support the team in maintaining the enterprise architecture repository.
  • Assist in the development of architectural standards and guidelines.
  • Potential to present information and reports to stakeholders.
  • The creation of enterprise content and communication

To join our team:

  • You are currently enrolled in post-secondary education in a tech-related field.
  • Strong analytic and critical thinking skills, with attention to detail.
  • Excellent communication (written and verbal) and collaboration skills.
  • A desire to learn.
  • Previous experience, an asset.

How you will succeed:

  • You influence change and are committed to continuous improvement, in order to exceed client expectations.
  • You leverage critical thinking skills to identify problems and proactively propose solutions.
  • Your strong communication skills allow you to clearly convey messages.
  • You’re an effective team player who shares knowledge to support your peers.

What you need to know:

  • You will be subject to a Background check as a condition of employment, in the event you are the successful candidate.

What’s in it for you?

  • Training and development opportunities to grow your career.
  • Gain practical experience that complements your academic studies and helps you stand out to future employers
  • Work with enterprise architecture platforms and tools (e.g., application inventory and modeling tools) to understand how organizations track, assess, and evolve their technology landscape
  • Mentorship from experienced professionals
  • Gain exposure that prepares you for roles in Enterprise Architecture, Solution Architecture, IT Strategy, or Technology Consulting
  • Discover what you enjoy (and what you don’t) to better define your future career path
  • A holistic approach to your well-being, with physical and mental health programs and a supportive workplace culture.

Expected hourly range $22/h -$28/h
