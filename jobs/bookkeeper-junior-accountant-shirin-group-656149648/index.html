About Shirin Group

Full Description

Position Summary

The Bookkeeper provides vital support to the daily financial management of our hotel portfolio. This role is responsible for the accurate recording, classification, and verification of numerical data to ensure the integrity of our financial records. This position requires steadfast accuracy and a commitment to maintaining professional bookkeeping standards across all aspects of our operations. Additionally, the Bookkeeper is responsible for generating billings and assisting the Directors with the broader financial activities of the hotels.

Key Responsibilities

Daily Financial Operations

  • Maintain accounting databases by entering data accurately into the accounting program.
  • Perform cash handling functions and reconcile all cash dropped at the front desk.
  • Reconcile bank statements and prepare wire transfer confirmations for reimbursable invoices.
  • Review and approve all reconciliation and audit work papers.
  • Assist with G/L account reconciliation, budget preparations, inventories, and monthly closings.

Accounts Receivable & Revenue Audit

  • Review deposit ledgers daily to validate proper payment and revenue posting.
  • Post city ledger payments in the PMS; reconcile and bill all city ledger accounts.
  • Process credit card billings (AMEX, Diners, etc.) and maintain accurate logs for all transactions.
  • Set up new accounts in accordance with credit policy and assign unique AR numbers in both PMS and Back Office software.
  • Process and follow up on all returned checks.
  • Ensure correct taxation is applied across all billing software (PMS, POS, and SPA software).

Reporting, Analysis & Compliance

  • Analyze financial statements on a monthly basis and report variances to the Director.
  • Analyze revenues, commissions, and expenses to ensure appropriate monthly recording.
  • Assist with the preparation of tax returns and corporate reporting requirements.
  • Support the Director and Financial Controller with financial/tax audits and the year-end audit process.
  • Conduct rotating monthly audits of inventories and enter final totals into tracking spreadsheets.

Prerequisites & Skills

  • Organizational Excellence: Strong organizational skills with a high level of attention to detail and the ability to compile complex facts and figures.
  • Communication: Strong command of the English language, both written and verbal.
  • Technical Proficiency: Expert-level proficiency with QuickBooks.
  • Advanced skills in MS Office (Excel and Word).
  • Experience with Property Management Systems (PMS) and financial software such as Opera, Room Master, Protel, Visual One, Delphi, Sun Finance, Oracle Finance, or SAP.

Education & Experience

  • Education: Bachelor’s degree, Master of Commerce, BS in Accounting, or currently on an accounting designation track.
  • Experience: 3–5 years of professional bookkeeping/accounting experience, ideally within a Hotel or Hospitality Finance environment.

Mission Alignment

  • Accomplishes accounting and organizational missions by completing related results as needed.
  • Performs any other job-related duties as assigned by the Director to ensure the health of the financial operations.
