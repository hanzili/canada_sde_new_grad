About the Role

Assist clients with eyewear selection and orders

About Sight Care Optometry and Optical

Growing optometry practice offering mobile eye care

Full Description

From the very beginning, Sight Care Optometry and Optical has been built on providing exceptional service and earning the trust of everyone we serve. This commitment has led to steady growth every year since we opened our doors 6 years ago.

2025 was a year of growth, and 2026 trending the same. Right now, we are seeking an ambitious Optician/Optical Salesperson to join our practice in East Gwillimbury. The primary focus for this person will be our growing mobile practice – Sight Care on Site – that brings comprehensive eye examinations and eyewear to retirement homes and businesses throughout the GTA.

  • Everything you do is meant to ensure that people leave their appointment or visit having had an amazing experience.
  • Greet them warmly and introduce yourself by name.
  • Register people when they arrive for eye examinations.
  • Assist clients with choosing the best glasses and/or contact lenses for their needs.
  • Check all incoming orders from the lab to ensure that they were correctly prepared.
  • Support the mobile business by creating patient records, validating OHIP eligibility and ensuring that there is a great selection of frames ready for a mobile day.
  • If possible, we will have you attend mobile days throughout the GTA, and setting/attending dispensing days post examination.

The person we are seeking:

  • Has their own reliable transportation – this location does not have reliable public transportation. In addition, if you are part of a mobile day, you could be working in a different location for some shifts - some areas we serve do not have regular transit service.
  • Has ideally completed a recognized Opticians program – however, we are open to some who has at least 2 years’ solid industry experience and has worked as an Optometric Assistant or Eyewear Stylist.
  • Is focused on providing superior patient care.
  • Is detail oriented and able to keep the team organized.

All we ask from you is that you take exceptional care of each patient you serve.

This position will offer 4 days per week (Wednesday to Saturday). If you are able to participate in mobile, you could work Monday as well.

We are offering a competitive compensation package that includes performance incentives based on revenue generated. If being part of a growing and professional team appeals to you, please send us a message and we will get back to you quickly.

Job Types: Full-time, Part-time

Benefits:

  • Dental care
  • On-site parking
  • Store discount
  • Vision care

Work Location: In person
