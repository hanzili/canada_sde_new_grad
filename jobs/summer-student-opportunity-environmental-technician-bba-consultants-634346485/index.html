About the Role

Assist environmental fieldwork, data collection, and reporting

About BBA Consultants

Leading private environmental consulting firm

Full Description

Type of position: Étudiant

Your future role on our team The Environmental Technician will interact with a multi-disciplinary team of environmental professionals on a number of diverse projects that could be occurring concurrently. The Technician will be responsible for assisting the team in conducting environmental assessments and providing support to regulatory applications. The ability to work with Project Managers and the team is critical to the position. Both office and field work will be required to support project needs. The position may require some overnight travel and possibly shift work, depending on project schedules.

With us, you’ll get the opportunity to... Assist with field work and assessments

  • Field planning and preparation
  • Support for vegetation, soil, wetland and wildlife assessments
  • Support for soil and groundwater sampling and monitoring
  • Support for fish salvage and construction monitoring

Work Plans and Reporting

  • Assist with compilation and analysis of field data
  • Report writing
  • Assist with work plans and cost estimates

Business Support

  • Marketing and business development
  • Proposal preparation
  • Support to Safety Management program

Do your qualities and values match our corporate culture?

  • Results-driven
  • Thirst to learn and excel
  • Willingness to work together while having fun
  • Constantly seeking sustainable and agile solutions
  • Desire to be in the thick of the action
  • Rigour and ethical

Summary Of Certifications And Job Requirements

  • Enrolled in a college or university program related to the environmental field
  • Eligible for professional designation in an Alberta Professional College
  • Highly motivated and self-starting work ethic
  • Excellent communication, interpersonal, and teamwork skills
  • Must possess a valid Alberta driver’s license
  • Asset: Intermediate computer skills in MS Office Suite and knowledge of GIS software

At BBA, You Get Many Benefits

  • Access to many experts and coaching
  • A collaborative team that shares its ideas and knowledge
  • Custom training and development plans

About BBA BBA is one of Canada’s leading private consulting engineering firms, with over 45 years of experience serving the energy and natural resources industry.

Our people are the foundation of our success. Their passion and excellence have earned us recognition as one of Canada’s Best Employers and Best Managed Companies , and we’re committed to fostering a workplace where everyone feels empowered to grow, lead and be themselves.

Our teams bring together engineering, environmental and commissioning expertise to deliver practical, innovative and sustainable solutions—from strategy to execution. With 20 offices across Canada, the U.S. and Latin America, we combine local presence with international reach, offering clients close collaboration and field-ready support.

Learn more about our culture and projects on LinkedIn or connect with our talent team.
