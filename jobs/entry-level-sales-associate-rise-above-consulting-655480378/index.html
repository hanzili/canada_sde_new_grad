About Rise Above Consulting

Full Description

Are you looking for something more stable and structured, but still want the opportunity to grow, develop your skills, and increase your income over time?

We are currently hiring an Entry Level Sales Associate to join our team in a role that combines customer interaction, professional development, and a consistent, structured work environment. This is an excellent opportunity for someone who enjoys working with people and is looking to build long-term skills in communication, sales, and customer service.

In this position, you will work directly with customers who are already interested in learning more about our services. Your focus will be on guiding conversations, providing clear information, and helping individuals make confident, informed decisions.

You will receive full training, ongoing mentorship, and daily support from experienced team members to help you succeed from day one.

What Our Entry Level Sales Associates Do:

  • Engage with customers through in-person conversations, phone, and digital communication
  • Understand customer needs and provide thoughtful, solution-based recommendations
  • Present services in a clear and approachable way, helping customers feel confident in their choices
  • Maintain a high level of professionalism and attention to detail in every interaction
  • Keep accurate records and follow up with customers as needed
  • Work closely with your team to maintain consistent performance and service standards
  • Continue developing your communication and sales skills through ongoing training

Qualifications:

  • Strong communication skills and a people-focused mindset
  • A reliable and self-motivated approach to work
  • Willingness to learn and grow in a structured environment
  • Ability to work both independently and as part of a team
  • Interest in developing long-term, transferable skills
  • No previous sales experience is required. Full training and ongoing support are provided.
  • Experience in customer service, retail, hospitality, or communications is considered an asset.

What You Can Expect:

  • Competitive pay with additional performance-based earnings
  • Paid training and continuous skill development
  • A consistent schedule and steady workflow
  • Clear opportunities for advancement into leadership roles
  • Ongoing mentorship and support from experienced leaders
  • A positive, team-oriented work environment
  • Recognition programs and performance incentives
  • Company-sponsored travel opportunities and team events

This role is ideal for someone looking to build confidence, gain valuable experience, and grow within a company that values consistency, support, and long-term success.

Job Types: Full-time, Permanent

Pay: $45,000.00-$59,000.00 per year

Work Location: In person
