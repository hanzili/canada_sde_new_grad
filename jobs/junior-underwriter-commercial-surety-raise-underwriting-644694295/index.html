About RAISE Underwriting

Full Description

RAISING THE BAR

RAISE Underwriting is an independently owned, Canadian Managing General Agent (MGA), dedicated to being an industry-leading supplier of commercial insurance and surety products through transparency, honesty, and integrity.

JOB RESPONSIBILITIES

  • Conduct financial analysis and analyze existing data and reports to find areas of improvement

  • Create ad hoc reports and perform miscellaneous projects, as needed

  • Develop and maintain effective relationships with departments to facilitate the planning/reporting process.

  • Work closely with the underwriting team to ensure that account management expectations are met

  • Accurate and timely preparation of account security documents

  • Review active files to ensure all essential underwriting and security information is current and updated

  • Setup up new account files in underwriting/accounting system

  • Invoice all fees, bonds/policies and endorsements in accounting system

  • Ensure all correspondence and underwriting information is properly filed

  • Follow-up with brokers for outstanding underwriting information

  • Other ad hoc tasks and reporting as requested

EDUCATION, EXPERIENCE, AND QUALIFICATIONS

  • Minimum of two years of relevant experience preferred

  • Bachelor’s degree or College Diploma in accounting, finance, or a related field

  • Strong analytical ability, very well organized and rigorous

  • Prior experience with Applied/EPIC is an asset

  • Must be technologically savvy and data-driven, including proficiency in Excel

  • Commitment to ongoing learning and professional development including insurance courses and certifications

JOB PERKS

  • Competitive compensation package

  • Paid vacation and personal time

  • Health benefits

  • Ongoing professional development and training

  • Flexible working arrangements under a hybrid model in our downtown Toronto office

  • Salary Range: $55,000 - $65,000 based on experience and skill set (does not include annual performance review bonuses)

APPLICATIONS
