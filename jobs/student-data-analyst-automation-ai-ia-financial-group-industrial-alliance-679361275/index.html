Full Description

Description Student position / Full-time during summer and possibility of working part-time during school terms / BI / Artificial Intelligence and Automation / Hybrid / Location: Quebec City or Montreal / Start as soon as possible Build your future with us Are you driven by data, BI, DevOps, and artificial intelligence and eager to contribute to the purpose of a company that aims to help its clients feel confident and secure about their future? As a Student – Data Analyst (Automation & AI) , you will play a key role in implementing data test automation to improve the reliability and robustness of BI and data solutions.

This position is a great opportunity to showcase your skills and fully realize your potential within a caring and trustworthy company. Here, people and their development are at the heart of our priorities, fostering an environment that encourages collaboration and innovation.

What You Will Accomplish With Us As a Student – Data Analyst (Automation & AI) , you will be at the core of our mission. Here are the main responsibilities:

  • Designing and developing automated tests for data quality and BI pipelines;
  • Integrating these tests into CI/CD pipelines (DevOps);
  • Exploring the use of artificial intelligence agents for test generation and anomaly detection;
  • Collaborating with data analysts and engineers to understand business rules;
  • Documenting and standardizing automated testing practices.

What Could Accelerate Your Success In This Role We are looking for someone who:

  • Is currently a student in computer science, data science, artificial intelligence, or a related field;
  • Is recognized for their curiosity, rigor, strong communication skills, autonomy, and team spirit;
  • Stands out for their interest in DevOps, BI tools, data quality, and artificial intelligence;
  • Demonstrates strong programming and data manipulation skills (SQL, Python, or equivalent);
  • Is available to work full-time during the summer and 15 to 20 hours per week during academic terms (daytime schedule, Monday to Friday).

Why you will love working with us

  • A work environment where learning and development merge with a collective pursuit of excellence.
  • A healthy, safe, fair, and inclusive environment where potential can be freely expressed and developed.
  • The possibility to work in a hybrid environment supported by flexibility and access to inspiring, innovative workspaces.
  • Student benefits: Access to internal training, mentoring, involvement in real projects, flexible schedules adapted to studies, and a rewarding experience for your professional journey.

Apply now and get ahead of your career, where your talent really belongs! Still unsure about applying? At iA, we believe in potential and value diverse experiences. If this role inspires you, go ahead and apply – your place might be with us, and we want to get to know you!
