Full Description

About the Role

We are looking for a creative and driven Digital Marketing Intern to support Enabled Talent’s growth and outreach initiatives. This role is ideal for someone passionate about digital storytelling, community building, and making an impact through inclusive hiring and accessibility-focused campaigns. You will gain hands-on experience working on real campaigns across events, platform growth, and global markets.

Responsibilities

  • Plan and execute digital marketing campaigns to support platform growth, events, and global expansion
  • Create, schedule, and manage content across social media platforms and email campaigns
  • Support lead generation through targeted outreach, partnerships, and audience engagement
  • Build and nurture online communities through consistent and creative communication strategies
  • Track and analyze campaign performance (engagement, reach, conversions) and suggest improvements
  • Optimize marketing funnels to improve user acquisition and conversion rates
  • Collaborate with internal teams to promote workshops, hiring fairs, and initiatives like the Enable Canada Tour
  • Conduct market research to identify trends, opportunities, and new growth channels
  • Assist in managing CRM tools, email marketing platforms, and outreach databases

Requirements

  • Currently pursuing or recently completed a degree/diploma in Marketing, Business, Communications, or related field
  • Strong interest in digital marketing, social media, and content creation
  • Basic understanding of marketing analytics and performance metrics
  • Excellent written and verbal communication skills
  • Creative mindset with attention to detail
  • Ability to work independently and in a fast-paced, collaborative environment
  • Passion for social impact, accessibility, and inclusive hiring initiatives

Eligibility Requirements

  • Must be legally eligible to work in Canada
  • Candidates must be Canadian Citizens or Permanent Residents
  • Must be based in or around Sudbury, Ontario
  • Final semester student or recent graduate

Why Join Enabled Talent?

  • Contribute to a mission-driven organization advancing inclusive hiring and equitable workforce opportunities
  • Gain hands-on experience in recruitment, onboarding, and talent operations
  • Develop transferable skills in communication, coordination, and project management
  • Collaborate with a passionate and supportive team focused on impact and innovation
  • Build your professional network with partners across academia and industry
  • Take ownership of your work and contribute ideas in a growing organization
  • Be part of shaping programs that influence the future of inclusive employment

Accessibility & Inclusion

Enabled Talent is committed to equitable and inclusive hiring practices. We encourage applications from individuals of all backgrounds and abilities. Accommodations are available throughout the recruitment process upon request.
