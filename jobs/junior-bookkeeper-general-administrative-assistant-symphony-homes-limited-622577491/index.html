Full Description

Job Title: Junior Bookkeeper & General Administrative Assistant

Job Summary:

Family run Land Development & Construction company. We are seeking a detail-oriented Junior Bookkeeper & General Administrative Assistant to support daily financial record-keeping and office administration. The ideal candidate is organized, reliable, and comfortable handling bookkeeping tasks alongside general office duties in a dynamic environment.

Key Responsibilities:

Bookkeeping Duties:

  • Record daily financial transactions and maintain accurate financial records
  • Assist with accounts payable and accounts receivable
  • Reconcile bank and credit card statements
  • Prepare invoices and process payments
  • Maintain organized financial files and documentation
  • Assist with payroll support and expense tracking
  • Support month-end and year-end financial reporting

Administrative Duties:

  • Answer phone calls and respond to emails professionally
  • Maintain office filing systems (digital and paper)
  • Schedule appointments and manage calendars
  • Prepare documents, reports, and correspondence
  • Order office supplies and support general office operations
  • Assist management with ad-hoc administrative tasks

Qualifications & Skills:

  • Basic knowledge of bookkeeping principles
  • Experience with accounting software (e.g., Sage & QuickBooks) preferred
  • Proficiency in Microsoft Office (Excel, Word, Outlook)
  • Strong organizational and time-management skills
  • Attention to detail and accuracy
  • Good written and verbal communication skills
  • Ability to work independently and as part of a team

Education & Experience:

  • Diploma or coursework in accounting, bookkeeping, or business administration preferred
  • Previous administrative or bookkeeping experience is an asset
  • Entry-level candidates with relevant training are encouraged to apply

Growth & Development Opportunities:

This role offers strong opportunities to learn, grow and expand professional skills. The successful candidate will be supported in developing their bookkeeping and administrative expertise, with opportunities to take relevant courses and training to advance their career withing the company.

Job Type: Full-time

Pay: $45,000.00-$55,000.00 per year

Benefits:

  • Dental care
  • Extended health care
  • On-site parking
  • Vision care

Work Location: In person
