About the Role

Assist pricing, renewals, and underwriting analyses

About MSH International Canada

Global insurer/broker with Canada focus

Full Description

At MSH, passion meets profession. We're a team of dedicated enthusiasts who find genuine fulfilment in what we do, and we're looking for someone who feels the same way.

The Opportunity:

We are looking for a Junior Underwriter (for 12 months) that will assist with augmenting the sales and account management function by pricing, evaluating and preparing renewal analyses and new business.

What You'll Own:

New Business

  • Pricing of new accounts and submission/negotiation/approval by underwriters.
  • Pricing of plan changes to existing accounts.
  • Collating of rate, plan, and carrier summaries from all participants in market.
  • Production of initial proposal draft for submission to sales for review.

Renewal Business

  • Pricing of renewals and submission/negotiation/approval by underwriters.
  • Production of internal renewal memos in advance of confirmation of terms by underwriters, outlining renewal recommendations.

Rate Development

  • Analysis of market adequacy of Lloyd’s rates in relation to markets and plan terms quoted for coverage options.
  • Development of ASO pricing model for large accounts.
  • Development of alternate funding arrangements and negotiation with underwriters on jumbo accounts.
  • Rate and plan pricing for new product development through in-house plan arrangements

What We're Looking For

  • College/University diploma/degree or equivalent work experience.
  • 1-2 years’ experience in group underwriting capacity.
  • Exposure to pooled, experience rated, ASO, and refund accounted funding arrangements.
  • Prior experience underwriting groups of 10 to 100 lives required.
  • Advanced knowledge of Microsoft Office applications – specifically Excel and PowerPoint.
  • Experience working within a domestic insurance broker/consulting firm would be a definite asset.
  • Ability to communicate (oral and written) in English. French would be an asset but not required.

What's In It for You

  • Salary Range: $45-50k CAD annually (Final compensation will reflect the candidate's experience, skills, and qualifications)
  • Hybrid work environment
  • Comprehensive health benefits + Employee Assistance Program
  • Wellness spending account
  • Minimum 3 weeks' vacation + generous sick and flex days
  • Clear pathways for vertical and horizontal growth, pursue what excites you
  • Inclusive, supportive leadership that genuinely invests in your success
