About the Role

Create content, manage social media, analyze performance

About Hydro Solution

Local Quebec marketing firm, growing community brands

Full Description

Role

As a Marketing Intern, you will leverage your creativity and digital skills to support the team on a variety of marketing-related projects. Your role will primarily focus on social media, content creation, and engaging our online community. You will also contribute to the planning, execution, and monitoring of publication calendars.

Your goal for this internship? To collaborate closely with the marketing team to ensure the dynamism and performance of our social media platforms, while providing concrete suggestions for improvement based on results analysis. With your creative flair and technical skills, you will actively contribute to our brand's growth and the achievement of our objectives.

Responsibilities

  • Be responsible for publications and community management across all our social media channels;
  • Create engaging content, both static and video, for our various platforms;
  • Plan, update, and monitor the publication calendar;
  • Write blog articles; • Create and update reports related to customer satisfaction surveys (InputKit);
  • Analyze the competition and monitor pricing;
  • Analyze the performance of digital initiatives to make recommendations to the team.

Ideal Requirements

  • College or university degree in a relevant field;
  • 1 to 2 years of experience in marketing;
  • Proficiency in creating content for social media and a good understanding of Facebook, Instagram, and LinkedIn;
  • Meticulous attention to detail to ensure high-quality posts;
  • Autonomy, initiative, and the ability to manage multiple projects simultaneously;
  • Ability to meet deadlines in a constantly evolving environment;
  • Excellent written and oral communication skills in both French and English;
  • Full-time availability, Monday to Friday (37.5 hours) from May to August 2026;

Here, we take care of our heroes by offering:

  • Competitive salary based on experience;
  • Hybrid work/teleworking;
  • A dynamic team with stimulating projects;
  • Free covered parking at the office;
  • And much more!

Do you have the right profile and would you be proud to contribute to the growth of a local company? You can apply now!

Description

At HydroSolution, everyone's work is a source of inspiration and pride. The sum of our efforts allows us to offer peace of mind to Quebecers and contribute to their well-being. It's true we don't save lives… but we help people through tough times! That's why we consider our employees to be everyday heroes to thousands of Quebecers who are looking for a turnkey solution.

Desired Superpowers

  1. Adapt easily to different situations or unforeseen events

  2. Be open to change

  3. Know how to work as part of a team (and not go it alone)

  4. Serve each customer fairly

  5. Demonstrate diligence.
