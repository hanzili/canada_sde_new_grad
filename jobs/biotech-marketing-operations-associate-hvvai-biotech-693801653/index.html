About the Role

About HVVAI Biotech

Biotech startup blending AI and biology

Full Description

Overview
We combine artificial intelligence, bioinformatics, and laboratory science to accelerate therapeutic and diagnostic innovation.

We are looking for a motivated, adaptable, and tech-savvy team member to grow with our company.

This is a multidisciplinary role ideal for someone who enjoys both marketing and biotechnology. The successful candidate will help manage our company’s marketing efforts, social media presence, office operations, and occasionally assist with laboratory and research-related activities.

This position is perfect for someone who enjoys startups, innovation, AI, biotech, and wearing multiple hats in a fast-growing company environment.

Duties

  • Manage and grow company social media accounts
  • Create marketing content for LinkedIn, Instagram, X, Facebook, and website updates
  • Assist with digital marketing campaigns and branding
  • Help maintain and update company websites
  • Support office administration and day-to-day operations
  • Assist with customer communication and outreach
  • Help organize marketing materials, presentations, and documents
  • Perform basic Python scripting and automation tasks
  • Support data handling and simple bioinformatics workflows
  • Assist with light laboratory work when needed
  • Coordinate with leadership on new projects and business development

Experience

  • Strong Python skills required
  • Experience with social media management and digital marketing
  • Strong communication and organizational skills
  • Comfortable working in a startup environment
  • Ability to multitask and learn quickly
  • Interest in biotechnology, AI, or life sciences
  • Proficient with Microsoft Office / Google Workspace
  • Self-motivated and detail-oriented

Preferred Qualifications

  • Background in biotech, biology, bioinformatics, or computer science
  • Experience with AI tools or automation
  • Familiarity with website management, React, or web development
  • Laboratory experience is an asset
  • Experience with Canva, Adobe tools, or video editing is a plus

Pay: From $17.85 per hour

Work Location: In person
