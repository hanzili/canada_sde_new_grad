About the Role

Perform fieldwork; prepare ESAs and reports

About Ballast Environmental Consulting Ltd.

Calgary-based environmental consulting with remediation focus

Full Description

We are a growing environmental consulting company based in Calgary, AB, that offer employees work-life balance with a flexible work schedule, health benefits and a close team environment. Ballast Environmental Consulting Ltd. is comprised of a diverse team of scientists that offer reclamation and remediation services for commercial, industrial and energy clients.

Who you are:

You want a career to be proud of where you can make a difference in the environment and see real results while you follow projects through from start to finish. You are passionate about the environment, possess a strong work ethic and sense of accountability. You would like the opportunity to grow and advance your career with us.

The Position:

We’re looking for a full-time, junior Environmental Scientist. The pay is hourly and will be based on prior experience.

  • Responsibilities include:
  • Field work as required, must be willing to travel and work out of town
  • Report writing and field work for Phase 1 Environmental Site Assessments (ESAs), Phase 2 ESAs, Phase 3 ESAs, Detailed Site Assessments, reclamation and remediation for commercial/industrial and oil and gas clients
  • Project administration

Minimum Requirements:

  • Degree or diploma in the environmental sciences, engineering, biology or related sciences
  • Member in good standing with the AIA, ASPB, APEGA, ASET, ACPA or AAFMP
  • Minimum if 2 years continuous employment
  • Organized, detail-orientated and proficient in MS office
  • Exceptional communication skills (written and verbal)/ strong scientific writing aptitude
  • Strong problem-solving aptitude, resourceful and flexible
  • Alberta Class 5 Driver’s licence with a clean Driver’s Abstract
  • Ability to work efficiently across multiple software platforms and adapt quickly to changing tools, systems, and workflows

Additional assets:

  • Experience in both the commercial/industrial and energy sectors
  • Standard First Aid with CPR, Ground Disturbance for Supervisors, Bear Aware, TGD and WHMIS

The Location:

The office is in the Shepard Business Park of Calgary. There is no public transportation servicing the area, so candidates must be able to provide their own mode of transportation.

Why You Should Apply:

  • Health and dental benefits
  • Savings program (RRSP or TFSA)
  • Flexible work schedule

How to Apply:

Applicants must be legally entitled to work in Canada and able to provide a criminal record check.
