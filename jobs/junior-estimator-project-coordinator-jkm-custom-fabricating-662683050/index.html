About the job

Company Description

JKM Custom Fabricating Inc. specializes in providing high-quality, customized metal fabrication solutions for a wide range of industries. Our team brings years of expertise, precision, and craftsmanship to every project, ensuring we meet and exceed our clients' expectations. Located in Cambridge, ON, JKM is committed to delivering innovative and durable solutions tailored to our clients' specific needs. We pride ourselves on fostering a collaborative and dynamic work environment that encourages growth and excellence.

Role Description

This is a full-time, on-site role located in Cambridge, ON, for a Junior Estimator / Project Coordinator. In this role, you will support project execution by preparing detailed estimates and coordinating resources for fabrication projects. The key responsibilities of the position include reviewing project specifications, liaising with clients and suppliers, generating cost estimates, creating timelines, and assisting in the management of project deliverables. You will also ensure projects are executed on time and within budget while maintaining open communication with stakeholders. Collaboration with other departments and maintaining accurate project documentation will be integral to your role.

Qualifications

Proficiency with estimating and project coordination tools, and familiarity with CAD software

Strong organizational and multitasking skills for managing multiple projects and deadlines

Understanding of manufacturing processes and materials used in custom fabricating

Effective communication skills for collaborating with team members, clients, and suppliers

Problem-solving skills with an ability to think critically and address project challenges

Experience in metal fabrication or construction project management is an asset

A diploma or degree in engineering, construction management, or a related field is desirable

Proficiency with Microsoft Office applications, including Excel and Word
