Full Description

Responsibilities:
- Greet and assist customers in a friendly and professional manner
- Provide product information and answer customer inquiries
- Recommend and upsell products based on customer needs and preferences
- Process sales transactions accurately and efficiently
- Maintain a clean and organized sales floor
- Stock shelves and merchandise products as needed
- Collaborate with team members to achieve sales goals
- Stay up-to-date with product knowledge and industry trends

  • Unload Trucks and warehouse Work (on occasion)

  • Building of new products in store

-Pay PLUS Commission (to be discussed upon hire)

Skills:
- Excellent time management skills to prioritize tasks effectively
- Experience in furniture sales is a plus
- Strong organizational skills to maintain inventory and displays
-Fluent in English and French with excellent communication skills

We are seeking a motivated bilingual Retail Sales Associate to join our team. As a Sales Associate, you will be responsible for providing exceptional customer service, promoting products, and driving sales. This is a great opportunity for individuals with a passion for sales and customer satisfaction.

In this role, you will interact with customers on a daily basis, assisting them in finding the right products to meet their needs. You will also be responsible for processing sales transactions accurately and efficiently. Additionally, you will help maintain the appearance of the sales floor by restocking shelves, organizing merchandise and light cleaning of the sales floor/products.

To succeed in this position, you should have excellent time management skills to prioritize tasks effectively. Experience in furniture sales is a bonus. Strong organizational skills are also important to ensure inventory is properly managed and displays are well-maintained. Fluency in English and French is required to effectively communicate with customers.

If you are a dedicated individual with a passion for sales and providing exceptional customer service, we would love to hear from you. Join our team today and be part of a dynamic retail environment!

Please email resume to decoambience1050@gmail.com.
