Celestica is a global electronics manufacturing services company, headquartered in Toronto, Canada, with over 28,000 employees across 40 sites in North America, Europe, and Asia.

Working with the world’s leading technology companies across a broad range of industries, Celestica delivers a full suite of services – from design and engineering, to manufacturing and supply chain management – to help its customers develop and manufacture products that are transforming the global economy. These products span key areas such as data centre technology, green energy, healthtech, and autonomous vehicles.

Working at Celestica is more than a job. It’s about being part of something bigger and shaping the future with your work. You’ll take on exciting and challenging projects, get hands-on experience, collaborate with a global team, and learn from some of the greatest minds in the industry. And just as we aspire to imagine, develop and deliver a better future with our customers, we’re just as committed to delivering the best employee experience.

About this Opportunity

The Global Quality & Reliability Intern position is ideal for a person interested in developing operation systems in a worldwide manufacturing organization. The candidate will have the opportunity to learn and contribute to several key enabling functions in Global Quality organization. We are looking for a team player who possesses exceptional communication skills (written and verbal), is able to learn quickly, and is self-motivated.

Preferred Skills:

  • Proficiency in Microsoft Office Applications (Excel, PowerPoint, Word)
  • Programing skills in VBA or Python for data processing automation
  • Familiarity with Generative AI and Large Language Models (LLMs) to enhance data analysis, problem-solving, and process automation
  • Relational database knowledge and application
  • Statistical knowledge for data processing and decision making
  • Knowledge of Smartsheet and PowerBI
  • Understanding of Google Applications
  • Critical thinking on how to leverage existing resources and technology to bring value to the business
  • Strong analytical skills to present data in an innovative way to business leaders
  • Time and project management, ability to prioritize and re-assess schedules and tasks quickly and effectively

Activities:

  • Collect and organize product quality and reliability data from manufacturing and engineering teams, construct databases.
  • Analyze data and derive conclusions via statistical analysis.
  • Leverage Generative AI tools to accelerate routine analytical workflows, such as script generation (VBA/Python) and visualization.
  • Create operation reports for Celestica's operations and engineering teams.
  • Collaborate with Global Quality operations worldwide to achieve all objectives.
  • Participate in Global Quality programs deployment and support.
  • Participate in teleconferences, meetings with global counterparts.
  • Participate in volunteering activities within Celestica volunteer programs.
  • Create and update website content and produce quality communications for the sites.

Physical Demands, duties of this position are varied and will include:

  • Duties of this position are performed in a normal office environment.
  • Duties may require extended periods of sitting and sustained visual concentration on a computer monitor or on numbers and other detailed data.
  • Repetitive manual movements (e.g., data entry, using a computer mouse, using a calculator, etc.) are frequently required.
