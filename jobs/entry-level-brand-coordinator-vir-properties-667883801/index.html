Entry-level Brand Coordinator

at VIR Properties

📍 Location Toronto, Ontario
📋 Details Full-time
📅 Posted Apr 21, 2026

About the Role

Coordinate brand campaigns and events; track performance

About VIR Properties

Training-focused marketing team; clear growth path

Full Description

We are looking for a creative and organised Entry-Level Brand Coordinator to join our growing marketing team. This is an excellent opportunity for someone at the beginning of their career who is eager to gain hands-on experience in brand coordination, campaign execution, and marketing strategy.

You will support the delivery of brand campaigns, help maintain brand consistency, and contribute to engaging marketing initiatives.

Key Responsibilities

  • Assist in coordinating brand campaigns and marketing initiatives
  • Support the team in maintaining brand consistency across campaigns
  • Help organise promotional events and brand activations
  • Collaborate with team members on creative marketing ideas
  • Assist with campaign timelines and project deliverables
  • Track campaign performance and gather customer insights
  • Support day-to-day brand and marketing operations

What We’re Looking For

  • Strong organisational skills and attention to detail
  • Excellent communication and interpersonal abilities
  • Creative mindset with an interest in branding and marketing
  • Positive attitude and willingness to learn
  • Ability to work in a fast-paced, team-oriented environment
  • No prior experience required – full training provided

What We Offer

  • Full training and ongoing mentorship
  • Supportive and collaborative team environment
  • Clear progression opportunities within the company
  • Hands-on experience in brand coordination and marketing campaigns
  • Opportunity to work with a variety of clients and industries

Ideal Candidate This Role Is Perfect For Someone Who Is

  • Starting their career in marketing or branding
  • Organised, motivated, and eager to learn
  • Confident working with teams and supporting projects
  • Interested in long-term career growth
