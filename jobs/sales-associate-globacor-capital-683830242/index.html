About Globacor Capital

Full Description

Safe Storage Depot is seeking a motivated and enthusiastic Sales Associate to join our dynamic team. The ideal candidate will have a passion for sales and customer service, with the ability to engage customers effectively. As a Sales Associate, you will play a crucial role in driving sales, providing tours and product demonstrations, and ensuring an exceptional experience for our customers. Self Storage experience is an asset but not a requirement.

Responsibilities

  • Greet customers warmly and provide assistance in a friendly manner.
  • Engage with customers to understand their needs and recommend suitable storage solutions.
  • Maintain knowledge of current promotions, products, and services.
  • Manage inventory levels of moving supplies and assist with restocking shelves as needed.
  • Handle transactions accurately using basic math skills for cash handling.
  • Provide excellent phone etiquette when responding to customer inquiries.
  • Collaborate with team members to achieve sales goals and enhance customer satisfaction.
  • Ensure the facility is clean, safe, and secure
  • General office management including opening and closing, balancing, making daily deposits, and report generation

Requirements

  • Previous experience in retail sales or technical sales is preferred.
  • Strong communication skills with the ability to engage multilingual customers is a plus.
  • Basic math skills for handling transactions and inventory management.
  • Excellent time management skills to prioritize tasks effectively.
  • A positive attitude with a strong desire to learn and grow within the company.
  • Must be able to work flexible hours, including evenings and weekends as needed.

· Self Storage experience, knowledge of Self Storage software, and collections experience are an asset, but not a requirement

Schedule & Details

  • Full-time position: 30 hours per week
