About the Role

About OTTAWA SPORTS & ENTERTAINMENT GROUP

Sports/entertainment group supporting community programs

Full Description

Reporting to the Senior Manager, Community Impact and Engagement, the Summer Student will support the OSEG Foundation’s mandate to leverage the power of sport to help children and youth develop critical life skills, improve their mental and physical wellbeing and reach their fullest potential.

  • This is a paid full-time position for eight (8) weeks from June 29, 2026 to August 21, 2026.
  • Candidates must be a youth aged 15 to 30.
  • This position is part of Canada Summer Jobs (CSJ). Candidates must not hold another CSJ position.

This is a replacement position and offers the flexibility to work on-site or in a hybrid work arrangement. The hourly pay for this full-time student position is $20.00.

What you’ll do:

Program Support

  • Support OSEG Foundation community impact programs and initiatives, assisting in the development, facilitation, and implementation of youth sport programming for marginalized communities in Ottawa.
  • Work alongside Foundation team to collaborate and ensure programs are delivered effectively.
  • Support volunteer recruitment and recognition.

Event Management & Communications

  • Develop overall event plans and critical paths for signature events.
  • Coordinate logistics for events, including vendors and event production teams to ensure successful event execution.
  • Coordinate program logistics, including organizing materials and equipment for programs and events.
  • Communicate key event information with event guests, sponsors, donors, and speakers.
  • Assist with the promotion and digital marketing of the event to increase media exposure and volunteer interest.

Community Relations Coordinator

  • Help maintain, grow, and enhance content for OSEG Foundation web pages and social media to promote and increase exposure of signature programs and events.
  • Capture and prepare social media content.

What’s needed:

  • Motivated, self-starter, team player.
  • Excellent inter-personal skills – skillful at building and fostering relationships.
  • Strong written communication skills.
  • Strong organizational and time management skills.
  • High level of proficiency in Word, PowerPoint, Excel, etc.
  • Ability to multi-task, work under pressure, and work in a high energy environment.
  • Valid drivers license and access to a vehicle.
  • Able to provide a clear Vulnerable Sector Check.
  • Bilingualism (English and French) is an asset.

What’s in it for you:

Founded in 2017 as the charitable arm of the Ottawa REDBLACKS and Ottawa 67’s, the OSEG Foundation leverages the power of sport to help children and youth reach their fullest potential. The 50/50 raffle is held at each home-game for the REDBLACKS, 67’s, Atlético, Blackjacks and other special events at TD Place. Revenue raised through charitable gaming is invested in positive youth development programs for children and youth facing barriers

Located at Lansdowne Park, the Ottawa Sports and Entertainment Group (OSEG) is the largest sports and entertainment company in the capital. We are proud owners of the Ottawa REDBLACKS, and Ottawa 67’s. We are also home to the Ottawa Charge, Atletico Ottawa, Ottawa Blackjacks, and Ottawa Rapid FC, and live events at TD Place. OSEG and its related Foundation were founded on the vision of its partners - to create year-round world-class guest experiences and give back to the Ottawa community.

TD Place is an advocate for equity, diversity, and inclusion and invites and encourages all candidates to apply for this role, including women, Indigenous peoples, members of racialized communities, persons with disabilities and persons of minority sexual orientations and gender identities. Additionally, we are happy to accommodate any individual needs in keeping with the Ontario Human Rights Code and the Accessibility for Ontarians with Disabilities Act. If you require an accommodation in order to participate in the hiring process, please contact us to make your needs known in advance.

Please note this role will require interacting with the public, including in crowded spaces. TD Place is committed to providing a safe and enjoyable experience for all employees and guests. We encourage you receive a full dose of the COVID-19 vaccination (subject to applicable exceptions).

We appreciate the time and effort it takes to apply and thank all applicants for their interest. Our selection process utilizes AI to compliment application screening. We’ll reach out to candidates whose applications best match the role's requirements.
