About the Role

Coordinate campaigns, manage social media, craft content

About GBS TECHNOLOGIES

Telus dealer with 23 locations, growth-focused team

Full Description

GBS Technologies is an independently owned and operated Wireless & IT Solutions powerhouse. We are proudly rooted in Atlantic Canada, with over 20 years of service to our communities across 23 retail locations.

Our best-kept secret, you ask? It's our people, of course! Over 100 passionate professionals who bring energy, expertise and a whole lot of heart in everything we do.

Being one of the fastest-growing Telus dealers in the country, we have partnered with industry leaders to deliver managed IT solutions that keep you and your business connected, protected and ahead of the curve.

Professional IT services, cloud-based phone systems via Telus Business Connect, vehicle and asset tracking, cybersecurity and security awareness training, amongst others, are a short list of the services and products we provide.

We are recruiting for a Marketing Co-OP Coordinator to join our Accounting & Operations team, based at our Joseph Howe Head Office. This role will function to coordinate marketing activities for retail and B2B divisions. The successful candidate should have an intimate understanding of how marketing campaigns work with a proven ability to seamlessly convert ideas into images, layouts and other designs across different sectors.

We are looking for an individual who has a strong background working in collaborative environments. A willingness and ability to continuously learn new things and retain what has been learned is preferred. Experience in working with corporate design guides/ principles and designing with other professionals. Additionally, they are creative and strong communicator.

About the Job:

Own the Brand: A self-starter that can take the lead on ensuring our brand looks sharp and our message stays consistent across 23 retail locations from Yarmouth, NS, to Gander, NL.

Digital Storytelling: Manage our social media platforms. Finding the perfect balance between consumer retail excitement and B2B tech expertise.

Content Creator: Draft engaging copy for web and print that makes complex IT solutions feel accessible, helpful, and local.

Empower Our Stores: Act as the strategic partner for our retail managers, providing them with the signage, promotional materials, and local campaign support they need to shine.

Campaign Architect: Plan and execute seasonal promotions for the latest product launch, from the initial "big idea" to the final rollout.

Maintain inventory of sales support materials, ensuring all are current and accurate, and coordinate the creation of new materials when necessary

Qualifications Include:

  • Currently participating in an integrated co-op undergraduate or graduate degree program or a recent graduate in Marketing or Public Relations.
  • Previous experiences are considered an asset:

o Previously held a role in marketing or public relations.

o Experience in planning and executing corporate events.

o Knowledge of basic website updates and graphic file editing.

o Experience in the Telecommunications Industry.

o An understanding of the retail industry.

  • Good working knowledge of Adobe Creative Cloud, Microsoft 365 apps (PowerPoint, etc), Google Ads, and Google Business platforms
  • Good working knowledge of common social media apps, along with adaptive creativity for social media posts, Meta for Business advertising and Google Ad content.
  • Ability to absorb and apply constructive criticism from peers and clients.
  • Legally able to work in Canada

Working with GBS:

We work hard, but we keep the atmosphere light and the coffee hot! Our team dynamic values initiative, willingness to learn, but is not afraid to speak up and ask for help. An easygoing person who can build relationships/camaraderie with ease. We are huge when it comes to teamwork and collaboration. A “can-do” attitude, organized with excellent communication skills in both oral and written. Work for a company deeply invested in the Atlantic provinces with a tight-knit, supportive culture.

Please note that only qualified candidates will be contacted. Thank you for your interest in this position.
