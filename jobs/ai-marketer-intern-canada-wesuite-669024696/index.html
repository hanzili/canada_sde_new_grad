Full Description

Description The Opportunity: AI Marketer Intern

Internship

  • Remote or Hybrid
  • Summer / Fall 2026

At WeSuite, a Fluent Software Group company We're building AI-native workflows across a portfolio of vertical software companies — and we need someone who thinks in both prompts and pipelines. You won't just use AI tools; you'll help design how we use them. This is a hands-on role where your work ships, not just sits in a deck.

We're building AI-native workflows across a portfolio of vertical software companies — and we need someone who thinks in both prompts and pipelines. You won't just use AI tools; you'll help design how we use them. This is a hands-on role where your work ships, not just sits in a deck.

What You'll Own

AI-Powered Content Creation

  • Draft, iterate, and publish marketing content (emails, landing pages, case studies, social posts) using AI tools like Claude, ChatGPT, and others
  • Build and refine prompt libraries that make content production faster and more consistent across portfolio companies
  • Test multi-model workflows to identify where different AI tools outperform each other

Campaign Support

  • Support outbound and inbound campaigns for portfolio companies in vertical SaaS markets
  • Help design and execute A/B tests on messaging, subject lines, and CTAs
  • Analyze campaign performance and surface insights with a clear point of view

AI Workflow Development

  • Document and systematize AI-assisted marketing processes so they're repeatable and scalable
  • Research and evaluate emerging AI marketing tools — and make recommendations backed by real rationale
  • Partner with cross-functional teams to identify manual marketing tasks that AI can accelerate

Research & Competitive Intelligence

  • Run market and competitor research to inform positioning across portfolio verticals
  • Synthesize findings into clear, actionable summaries for business leaders

Who You Are

Qualifications

  • Currently pursuing a degree in Marketing, Communications, Business, or a related field
  • Genuinely excited about AI — you use it daily, you think about it, and you have opinions about it
  • A strong writer who can communicate complex ideas clearly and without fluff
  • Comfortable with ambiguity — you figure things out before asking for help
  • Curious and resourceful, with a bias toward action

Nice-to-Haves

  • Hands-on experience with AI tools in a professional or personal project context
  • Familiarity with B2B SaaS or vertical software markets
  • Experience building or documenting workflows and SOPs
  • Basic knowledge of SEO, paid media, or marketing analytics
  • Experience with marketing tools such as email platforms, CMS, or analytics dashboards

Why Fluent?

  • High Autonomy, High Impact: Your work ships from Day 1 — real content, real campaigns, real results
  • Serious Career Growth: Direct exposure to a multi-company software portfolio and how growth strategy works at scale
  • Learning-Driven Culture: Mentorship from operators who've built and scaled B2B software businesses
  • Entrepreneurial Energy: A front-row seat to how AI is actually changing how companies operate — not in theory, in practice
  • Competitive Offer: Competitive internship compensation
  • Team-First Mindset: Regular events, collaboration, and global connections

Join Us

The best marketers of the next decade will be the ones who know how to think alongside AI — not just use it. If that's you, help us scale incredible software companies without losing their entrepreneurial edge.

At Fluent and Valsoft, you'll make a real impact from Day 1.
