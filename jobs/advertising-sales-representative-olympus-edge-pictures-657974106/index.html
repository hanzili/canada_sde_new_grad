Full Description

Advertising Sales Representative (Remote) The Edge Magazine x Olympus Edge Pictures Company Description

Olympus Edge Pictures, in partnership with The Edge Magazine , is a dynamic media studio focused on helping founders, brands, and creators grow through powerful storytelling and revenue-driven strategy.

We operate at the intersection of media and business—producing investor decks, social-first content, cinematic campaigns, and sales systems designed to build trust and drive measurable growth.

Our team is made up of experienced entrepreneurs who understand what it takes to turn vision into execution. We specialize in working with brands across sports, hospitality, and creator-led industries.

As part of our expansion, we have recently launched The Edge Magazine , a new platform highlighting top talent, businesses, and cultural trends across Toronto. We are currently building a sales team to support our advertising and brand partnership efforts.

Role Description

This is a remote internship opportunity for an Advertising Sales Representative.

In this role, you will be responsible for identifying and engaging potential advertisers, understanding their business goals, and presenting tailored advertising and media solutions through The Edge Magazine and Olympus Edge platforms.

Day-to-day responsibilities include:

  • Prospecting and reaching out to potential clients across Toronto and surrounding markets
  • Building and maintaining strong client relationships
  • Pitching advertising placements and integrated media packages
  • Managing inbound inquiries and responding to client needs
  • Collaborating with the marketing and content teams to align campaigns
  • Supporting ongoing sales initiatives and contributing to team strategy

This role offers hands-on experience in media sales, brand partnerships, and client development within a growing platform.

Qualifications

  • Strong communication and interpersonal skills
  • Confidence in sales conversations and relationship building
  • Basic understanding of marketing and advertising principles
  • Self-motivated with the ability to work independently in a remote environment
  • Willingness to learn, adapt, and participate in ongoing training
  • Interest in media, entrepreneurship, sports, hospitality, or creator-driven businesses

Preferred:

  • Background in Marketing, Business, Communications, or related fields
  • Previous sales or customer-facing experience is an asset

Compensation

  • Commission-based structure: 20%–30% on all advertising deals closed

This commission structure will be in place for the initial 3–6 months , with the opportunity to transition into a salary + commission model based on performance and growth.

Why Join Us

  • Opportunity to be part of a growing media brand from the ground up
  • Direct exposure to entrepreneurs, brands, and creative projects
  • Real sales experience with uncapped earning potential
  • Pathway to long-term role within Olympus Edge and The Edge Magazine
  • Flexible, remote work environment

How to Apply

Please submit your resume along with a short message outlining:

  • Why you’re interested in this role
  • Any relevant sales, marketing, or communication experience
  • Your availability
