Full Description

Company Description

NextGen Consulting Inc. is an IT consulting and staffing firm specializing in addressing the complex demands of today’s evolving market. Our mission focuses on delivering professional excellence and ensuring the highest level of satisfaction for both enterprise clients and talented candidates. We are dedicated to fostering innovation, integrity, and collaborative success. Join our team to contribute to dynamic projects and grow your career in a supportive environment.

Role Description

These are contract-based Junior Finance/Accounting/AP role, suitable for candidates with 1-5 years of experience. The roles involve supporting financial and accounting operations, preparing and analyzing financial statements, maintaining accurate records, reconciliation, audits and assisting with accounts payable processes.

Qualifications

  • Strong Finance and Accounting knowledge, including experience with accounts payable and financial operations
  • Proficiency in analyzing financial statements and applying Analytical Skills to assess financial data
  • Excellent Communication skills for effective collaboration with team members and stakeholders
  • Ability to work accurately and efficiently under tight deadlines
  • Bachelor's degree in Accounting, Finance, or a related field (or equivalent experience)
  • Proficiency in accounting software and tools is an asset
  • Previous experience with Canadian financial regulations and practices is a plus
