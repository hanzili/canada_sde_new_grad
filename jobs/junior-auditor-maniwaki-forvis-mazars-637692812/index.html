Full Description

✨ About the Role

Are you a passionate audit professional looking for meaningful challenges within a dynamic firm that combines a human-scale environment with global reach? If so, this opportunity is for you.

As a Junior Auditor, you will play a pivotal role in delivering audit and assurance engagements for a diverse and engaged client base. This role is ideal for someone looking to broaden their professional experience, take part in high-impact projects, and gain national exposure through engagements across Quebec and throughout Canada.

Who We Are

Forvis Mazars is a leading global audit & assurance, tax and advisory network, bringing together two independent partnerships and more than 40,000 professionals across 100+ countries.
In Canada, our firm has been supporting clients for more than 50 years through the expertise of over 200 professionals specializing in Audit & Assurance, Accounting, Tax, and Financial Advisory Services. We are a fast‑growing team with offices in Montréal, Gatineau, Maniwaki and Toronto.

At Forvis Mazars, we are convinced of one thing: every day is a human adventure. This adventure comes to life through our employer promise:

  • . You will be supported as you learn, develop and explore new perspectives throughout your journey with us.
  • . You will be part of a strong collective, benefit from hands‑on management, and celebrate your successes as a team.
  • . You will feel confident sharing your point of view and bold ideas that help shape our future.

Our core values guide us every day:

Gather to grow – Excel in client service – Commit to results – Embody change

️ Your Responsibilities

  • Participate in certification engagements, collaborating with senior auditors and directors;
  • Prepare Canadian tax returns for individuals, corporations, and other entities, demonstrating solid tax knowledge.
  • Contribute to special mandates by delivering innovative, tailored solutions to meet client needs.

Your Profile

  • Enrolled in a bachelor's degree program in business administration or equivalent;
  • Intermediate knowledge of Office 365;
  • Basic knowledge of financial reporting and certification standards;
  • Basic knowledge of taxation (an asset);
  • Advanced level of French (spoken and written);
  • Student member of the professional order in your province (an asset);
  • Experience in accounting or certification (an asset);
  • Knowledge of CaseWare and TaxPrep software (an asset);
  • Willingness to travel for work.

Your Strengths

  • Strong technical expertise and attention to detail.
  • Exceptional organizational skills and methodical approach.
  • Excellent communication skills with a proactive and anticipatory mindset.
  • Results-driven, with a strong ability to meet budgets and deadlines.
  • High level of integrity and accountability.
  • Flexibility and willingness to travel across Canada for client engagements.

Language Requirements

  • Fluency in French, both spoken and written.
  • Proficiency in English is required due to the nature of the position: the selected candidate will be required to communicate regularly, both verbally and in writing, with clients and colleagues located outside of Quebec whose working language is English. (In compliance with the Charter of the French Language)

What We Offer
We provide a dynamic and structured environment where people are truly supported. Here’s what you can expect:

  • Competitive salary
  • Vacation based on your experience
  • Casual dress code
  • Personal days bank
  • Virtual healthcare with Dialogue
  • Annual wellness allowance
  • Flexible remote work depending on your role
  • Comprehensive group insurance
  • Retirement savings plan with employer contributions
