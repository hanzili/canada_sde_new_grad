Full Description

We are actively hiring an Entry Level Customer Account Manager to join our growing team at Exceler8 Marketing in Victoria, BC. This Entry Level Customer Account Manager position is ideal for motivated individuals looking to launch a long-term career in sales, customer relations, and account management.

If you are driven, competitive, and ready to build professional skills in a fast-paced environment, this Entry Level Customer Account Manager opportunity offers hands-on training, mentorship, and clear advancement pathways.

As an Entry Level Customer Account Manager, you will represent service solutions directly to customers, build relationships within the community, and contribute to territory growth. Our team develops every Entry Level Customer Account Manager through structured coaching and performance-based promotions.

Responsibilities Of The Entry Level Customer Account Manager

  • Conduct face-to-face customer outreach in Victoria and surrounding areas, promoting high-speed internet solutions tailored to the customers needs.
  • Introduce and explain internet service options clearly and confidently
  • Assist customers with enrollment and account setup
  • Track daily outreach activity, leads, and performance metrics
  • Maintain accurate client records and follow-up communication
  • Contribute to territory expansion and account growth initiatives

Skills Needed For The Entry Level Customer Account Manager

  • Strong communication and interpersonal skills
  • Self-motivated, competitive, and goal-oriented mindset
  • Ability to work independently and manage time effectively
  • Positive attitude and professional presentation
  • Professional attitude and desire for career growth as an Entry Level Customer Account Manager

What We Offer Our Entry Level Customer Account Manager

  • Paid training for every Entry Level Customer Account Manager
  • Ongoing mentorship and leadership development
  • Performance-based advancement opportunities
  • Supportive, team-oriented work environment
  • Opportunity to build transferable skills in sales and account management
