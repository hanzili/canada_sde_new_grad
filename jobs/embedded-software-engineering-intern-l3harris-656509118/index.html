Job Title: Embedded Software Engineering Intern

Job Location: Ottawa, QC

Job Code: 35767

Job Schedule: Day Shift

Essential Functions:

  • Design and implementation of software algorithms;
  • Develop automation scripts, code and processes to achieve faster workflow;
  • Interact with systems engineering on technical issues;
  • Debugging software issues;
  • Conduct independent research and provide new solutions to problems;
  • Conduct and prepare presentations and demonstrations;

Qualifications:

  • Completed 2nd year of Computer Science/Computer or Software Engineering, or Systems, Electrical Engineering with software knowledge;
  • Experience with C/C++ and object-oriented design and programming;
  • Commitment to work well done, ensuring follow-ups and seeing that deadlines are respected;
  • Ease of working with people (customer teams, several internal services and external suppliers);
  • Strong English written and spoken skills.

Preferred Additional Skills:

  • Experience with Linux or embedded Linux;
  • Strong organizational, analytical, and problem-solving skills;
  • Able to work independently and in a team-based environment.

Eligibility Criteria:

  • Must be eligible for registration with the Controlled Goods Program;
  • Must be eligible to obtain and maintain a government of Canada Reliability status and Secret Level 2/NATO Secret security clearance;
  • Must be eligible to meet the requirements for U.S. International Traffic in Arms Regulations (ITAR).
