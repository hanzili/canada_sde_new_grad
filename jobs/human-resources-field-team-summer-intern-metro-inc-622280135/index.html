This position is an existing temporary, full-time position until September 2026. Summary Metro Inc. is seeking a dynamic and motivated Summer Intern to join our HR Field team for the summer. This internship offers a unique opportunity to gain hands-on experience in various HR functions and support the HR field team in delivering exceptional service to our operational clients.

Specific Responsibilities

  • Provide administrative support to HR Field Managers, including generating weekly reports, reviewing employee information, composing registered letters, scheduling meetings, etc.
  • Update employee information and track grievance activity
  • Support the onboarding process for new hires, including preparing orientation materials and coordinating and conducting orientation meetings
  • Facilitate communication of bargaining unit promotions to stores
  • Conduct exit interviews
  • Reorganize and digitize files
  • Provide support to the HR Field Management Team on a variety of projects and initiatives as needed

Qualifications

  • Currently enrolled in a Bachelor's degree program in Human Resources or a related field
  • Strong organizational and time management skills
  • Excellent communication and interpersonal skills
  • Proficiency in Microsoft Office Suite (Word, Excel, PowerPoint)
  • Ability to handle confidential information with discretion
  • Ability to work independently and within a team environment
  • A proactive and positive attitude with a willingness to learn
