About the Role

About Rodan Energy Solutions

Energy tech leader in grid optimization

Full Description

About Us:

Rodan Energy is a North American leader in distributed energy resources and energy intelligence, enabling a more reliable, cost-effective, and decarbonized grid. We partner with large energy users, utilities, and grid operators to design, finance, and operate high-impact DER solutions, including asset optimization, demand response, metering, and power system engineering. Managing over 1,400 MW of flexible capacity across Ontario, NYISO, PJM, MISO, and AESO, we make sustainable attainable.

Why Join Rodan:

  • Thriving Industry: Join a rapidly growing sector at the forefront of innovation and sustainability, shaping the future of energy management.
  • Product Market Fit: With a 98% client retention rate and consistent YOY growth, Rodan has built a reputation for delivering trusted, long-term partnerships with industry leaders.
  • Top Talent & Strong Culture: Collaborate with high-performing, passionate individuals who are committed to excellence and sustainability.

What Makes This Role Great:

  • As a Co‑op Software Developer, you’ll work alongside experienced software development professionals to build, test, and support client facing SaaS applications used by real customers. You’ll contribute across the full software development lifecycle while working in an Agile team environment. This role provides exposure to building software that meets high standards for accuracy, reliability, and availability of energy data, helping you develop practical skills and prepare for future full‑time software engineering opportunities.

Key Responsibilities:

  • Help build, enhance, and maintain SaaS software tools used to visualize energy market data and or facility information, and support customers
  • Contribute to the full software development lifecycle, including coding, testing, deployment, and ongoing support
  • Work within an Agile development team, participating in sprint planning, stand‑ups, code reviews, and retrospectives
  • Support testing and quality assurance efforts to ensure reliable, high‑quality software
  • Assist with troubleshooting and issue resolution, including ad‑hoc client or internal support when needed

Required Skills and Qualifications:

  • Currently enrolled in a Software Engineering, Computer Science, or related program
  • Have completed 2 or more co‑op terms
  • Experience contributing to software development projects, including building, testing, and maintaining applications
  • Working knowledge of software development best practices, including version control, testing/QA, and clean code design
  • Programming skills in 1 of 2 technology stacks:

Market Stack:

  • Python
  • Django
  • Vue JS
  • HTML/CSS
  • JavaScript
  • RESTful APIs
  • MySQL
  • Linux CLI / SSH

Facility Stack:

  • C#
  • .NET Framework4.8, .NET 8-10
  • RESTful APIs
  • Entity Framework
  • SQL Server
  • Azure ecosystem

  • Blazor/ Razor pages

  • Familiarity with Agile development, including sprint planning, stand‑ups, and code reviews

  • Strong problem‑solving, debugging, and collaboration skills, with the ability to communicate effectively with technical and non‑technical stakeholders
  • Experience supporting AI‑driven solutions, with exposure to modern development tools such as GitHub, Copilot.

Compensation & Work Term

  • Hourly Rate: $30 - $38/hour
  • Work Term: 4 months, with the opportunity for an extension
