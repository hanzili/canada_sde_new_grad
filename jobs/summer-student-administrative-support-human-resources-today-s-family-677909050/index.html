About Today's Family

Full Description

Starting Wage: $17.20 to $23.45, based on experience

Region: Hamilton

Employment Type: Full-Time, (3-month contract)

At Today’s Family, we believe in the power of strong partnerships to help children and families thrive. We’re a not-for-profit organization serving over 7,500 children and families across Hamilton, Halton, Haldimand-Norfolk, Oxford County and Peel Region.

This job posting is for an opening vacancy and reflects a current opening within the agency.

Our mission is to ensure everychild experiences the joy of learning through play. We value Caring, Collaboration, Accountability, Innovation, and Inclusion, and we’re committed to delivering high-quality programs that evolve to meet the needs of our growing communities.

Who We Are

At Today’s Family, we believe in creating strong communities where children and families thrive. Our work is grounded in relationships, quality, and a commitment to belonging. As we continue to grow, we are looking for team members who are eager to contribute and learn within a purpose-driven environment.

Your Opportunity

As a Summer Student supporting our Human Resources team, you will contribute to the administrative systems that support our staff and organizational practices. This role offers an opportunity to gain experience in HR processes while working in a collaborative and values-driven environment.

What You Will Do

  • Complete data entry and maintain accurate HR records
  • Support digital filing and documentation management
  • Assist with preparation and organization of HR documentation
  • Contribute to project-based work related to HR initiatives
  • Handle confidential employee information with a high level of discretion

What You Bring

  • Strong attention to detail and organizational skills
  • Ability to manage sensitive and confidential information
  • Comfort using Microsoft Office or similar tools
  • Strong communication and ability to work collaboratively
  • Interest in Human Resources or people-focused work is an asset

Eligibility Requirements (Canada Summer Jobs)

To be eligible, applicants must:

Be between 15 and 30 years of age at the start of employment

  • Be a Canadian citizen, permanent resident, or person with refugee protection
  • Be legally entitled to work in Canada

What You Will Gain

  • Practical experience in Human Resources within a mission-driven organization
  • Exposure to HR systems, processes, and people practices
  • Opportunity to contribute to work that supports staff and strengthens community

At Today’s Family, we celebrate diversity and are committed to fostering an inclusive environment. We welcome applicants from all backgrounds and are dedicated to accommodating any needs during the hiring process, in accordance with the Accessibility for Ontarians with Disabilities Act (AODA). If you need any accommodation throughout the recruitment process, please do not hesitate to contact our Human Resources Department at hr@todaysfamily.ca
