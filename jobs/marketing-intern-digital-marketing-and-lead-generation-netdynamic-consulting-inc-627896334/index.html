Full Description

*About NetDynamic Consulting*

NetDynamic Inc. is a certified *SuiteSuccess partner and official Oracle NetSuite Alliance partner* . We deliver high-touch, customized services that enable businesses to scale effectively using cloud-based solutions. Our team comprises a sophisticated blend of technical professionals, certified accountants, and MBAs. Operating within a small, fast-moving, and dynamic environment.

*Role Overview*

NetDynamic Inc. is seeking a motivated and detail-oriented *Marketing Intern to support our marketing and lead generation initiatives. This position is designed for a high-potential individual who wishes to gain hands-on experience in B2B SaaS marketing and enterprise software consulting . The incumbent will provide operational support to the leadership team, assisting in the execution of campaigns that drive awareness for our ERP solutions and our proprietary ATS360* product.

*Duration: Four (4) months, with the possibility of extension based on performance and organizational needs. You MUST be an active student for being considered for this role and also a Canadien Citizen.*

*Key Responsibilities*

  • *Campaign Support: Assist in the execution of marketing initiatives to promote core service lines, including NetSuite Implementation , Optimization , and Managed Support* .
  • *ATS360 Product Support: Contribute to lead generation efforts for ATS360* , the only native NetSuite recruitment solution, through digital outreach and database management.
  • *Content Assistance: Collaborate with technical experts and CPAs to draft and format high-quality case studies and technical documentation concerning Order to Cash (O2C) , Procure to Pay (P2P) , and Record to Report (R2R)* .
  • *Market Research: Conduct ongoing industry analysis and competitive benchmarking to identify emerging trends within the mid-market B2B sector* .
  • *Collateral Maintenance: Organize and update marketing collateral to ensure all materials accurately reflect current system capabilities and client Return on Investment (ROI)* .
  • *Cross-Functional Coordination: Support Solutions Consultants and Sales Engineers* in preparing presentations and materials for prospective clients.

*Required Qualifications*

  • *Educational Status: Recent graduate (within the past 18 months ) with a University or College degree in Marketing , Business Administration , Information Systems* , or a related field.
  • *Technical Aptitude:* A demonstrated ability to rapidly master complex enterprise software concepts and translate technical information into professional communication.
  • *Communication Excellence:* Exceptional written and verbal communication skills; the incumbent must be capable of interacting professionally with senior leadership.
  • *Learning Agility:* A proven track record of being a "quick study" regarding new systems and professional service methodologies.
  • *Core Behaviors: Must exhibit high levels of Ownership and Proactivity* , demonstrating the initiative required to succeed in a deadline-driven environment.

*Performance Success Metrics*

  • *Execution Accuracy:* The successful delivery of assigned marketing tasks with minimal errors and high attention to detail.
  • *Operational Momentum:* Consistent adherence to project timelines and the proactive identification of next steps in the campaign lifecycle.
