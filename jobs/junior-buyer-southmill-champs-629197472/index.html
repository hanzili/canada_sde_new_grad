Full Description

Company Overview

Champ's Fresh Farms Inc. is the largest mushroom distributor in Western Canada, located in the Fraser Valley of British Columbia. As a leader in the mushroom marketing sector, we pride ourselves on our innovative distribution methods and commitment to quality control.

Summary

We are seeking a motivated and detail-oriented Junior Buyer to support our Procurement Department. This is an excellent opportunity for a student or recent graduate in Supply Chain Management, Business Administration, or a related field to gain hands-on experience in a dynamic, multi-plant manufacturing environment.

Responsibilities

  • Assist in day-to-day purchasing activities, including placing purchase orders and ensuring timely delivery.
  • Support procurement strategies for categories such as packaging, PPE, and raw materials.
  • Daily and week tonnage report to the farms
  • Daily receiving of compost raw materials
  • Day to day management of Limble to include inventory management and maintenance planning
  • Monitor supplier performance and help resolve delivery or quality issues.
  • Analyze inventory and consumption data to support demand forecasting.
  • Collaborate with internal departments to ensure procurement aligns with business needs.
  • Help maintain accurate procurement documentation and support ERP system updates (Sage X3 will be added advantage).
  • Participate in cost-saving initiatives and supply chain improvement projects.

Requirements

  • Bachelor's degree in Business Administration, Supply Chain Management, or a related field is preferred.
  • Strong analytical skills with attention to detail.
  • Being able to work under pressure
  • Basic knowledge of stock & inventory management
  • Excellent communication and negotiation abilities.
  • Ability to work collaboratively in a team environment.
  • Proficient in Microsoft Office Suite; experience with procurement software is a plus.

If you're ready to embark on an exciting career in the food industry and contribute to our success at Champ's Fresh Farms Inc., we invite you to apply today!
