Company Description

GenPark leverages intelligent Brand Agents and User Agents to enable brands to effectively engage and convert Gen Z consumers worldwide. Through personalized AI-generated content, interactive agents, and immersive AI video experiences, GenPark helps brands reach the right audience in the right context, leading to improved conversion rates and reduced customer acquisition costs. As an AI-native platform, GenPark continuously learns from user behavior and feedback, delivering smarter matches between products, services, and consumers in global markets. GenPark is at the forefront of innovation, redefining how brands connect with new-generation audiences.

Role Description

This is an internship role for a Data Scientist Intern located in Vancouver, BC, offered as a hybrid position with the flexibility to work remotely part-time. As a Data Scientist Intern, you will assist in analyzing and interpreting complex datasets to provide actionable insights. Your responsibilities will include designing data models, creating data visualizations, generating predictive analytics, and collaborating with cross-functional teams to support AI-driven initiatives. You will also have the opportunity to contribute to the improvement of AI algorithms and explore innovative data-driven solutions.

Qualifications

  • Proficiency in Data Science and Data Analysis
  • Strong background in Statistics and Data Analytics
  • Skilled in Data Visualization tools and techniques
  • Programming knowledge in Python, R, or similar languages is a plus
  • Strong problem-solving skills and attention to detail
  • Excellent communication and collaboration abilities
  • Pursuing or recently completed a degree in Data Science, Statistics, Computer Science, or a related field
