About Black Fly Beverage Co.

Full Description

Who We Are

At Black Fly Beverage Co., we don’t just make drinks - we craft experiences. As a proudly Canadian, fast-growing producer of spirit-based beverages and a trusted partner in the beverage industry, we’re all about quality, innovation, and teamwork. Our facility features cutting-edge canning, bottling, and injection molding technology - but it’s our people who make us truly exceptional.

We’re looking for team players who care about what they do, take pride in their work, and want to grow with a company that’s expanding nationwide and raising the bar for what a great beverage brand can be!

Role Overview

Black Fly Beverage Company is looking for a highly organized and detail-oriented Accounts Payable Associate to support our growing Finance Team. This role is responsible for processing invoices, reconciling employee expenses, maintaining accurate financial records, and supporting day-to-day accounts payable operations. The ideal candidate is dependable, organized, and able to manage multiple priorities while maintaining a high level of accuracy and confidentiality. This position plays an important role in supporting smooth financial operations across our fast-paced beverage manufacturing environment.

Key Responsibilities

Accounts Payable & Expense Administration

  • Process vendor invoices accurately and within established timelines
  • Reconcile employee credit card transactions and expense reports using Concur
  • Review expense submissions for compliance with company policies and procedures
  • Follow up on missing receipts, approvals, and supporting documentation
  • Perform invoice matching against purchase orders and receiving documentation (3-way matching)
  • Maintain organized electronic and physical financial records and documentation
  • Assist with vendor account reconciliations and payment tracking
  • Support payment processing activities and administrative finance functions
  • Communicate professionally with vendors and internal departments regarding invoice or payment inquiries
  • Ensure compliance with internal accounting controls, procedures, and confidentiality requirements
  • Assist with identifying process improvements and administrative efficiencies within Accounts Payable

Team & Operational Support

  • Support Finance team members with administrative and operational tasks
  • Assist with month-end activities, reporting, and audit preparation as required
  • Contribute to a professional, collaborative, and team-oriented work environment
  • Perform other related duties as assigned

Qualifications & Requirements

Education & Experience

  • College diploma in Accounting, Finance, Business Administration, or related field preferred
  • Minimum 2 years of experience in Accounts Payable, finance administration, or similar accounting support roles preferred
  • Manufacturing, food, beverage, or distribution industry experience is considered an asset

Knowledge, Skills & Abilities

  • Exceptional attention to detail and accuracy
  • Strong organizational and time-management skills
  • Intermediate Microsoft Excel skills required
  • Experience with ERP systems; Microsoft Dynamics 365 Business Central experience preferred
  • Concur experience is considered an asset
  • Strong communication and administrative skills
  • Ability to manage multiple priorities and deadlines effectively
  • Ability to work independently and collaboratively within a team environment
  • High degree of professionalism, discretion, and confidentiality

Working Conditions

  • Primarily office-based within a beverage manufacturing environment
  • Frequent computer use, data entry, and document review
  • Occasional interaction with warehouse and production departments
  • Fast-paced environment requiring strong attention to detail and organization
