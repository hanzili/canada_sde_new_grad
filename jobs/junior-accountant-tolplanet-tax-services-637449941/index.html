Full Description

Job Overview
At Tolplanet Tax Services, we take the complexity out of numbers. Based in vibrant Vancouver, we are a dynamic firm dedicated to providing personalized tax and accounting solutions to individuals and small businesses. We believe in building careers, not just filling positions. If you are a detail-oriented numbers person looking for mentorship and real growth, Tolplanet is the place for you.

We are seeking a motivated Junior Accountant to join our close-knit team. You will work alongside experienced professionals, supporting day-to-day accounting functions and tax preparation. This is an excellent opportunity to gain hands-on experience across a variety of client files while developing your technical skills in a supportive environment.

Duties

  • Assist with the preparation of financial statements, working papers, and supporting documentation .
  • Prepare personal and corporate tax returns (T1, T2) under the guidance of senior staff.
  • Perform account reconciliations, bank reconciliations, and general ledger maintenance .
  • Process payroll, GST/PST returns, and other compliance filings.
  • Respond to client inquiries and maintain organized client files.
  • Support the team with administrative tasks and special projects as needed.

Requirements

  • Bachelor's degree or diploma in Accounting, Finance, or a related field.
  • Working towards a CPA designation (or willingness to enroll) is considered a strong asset.
  • 0-2 years of experience in a public practice or accounting role; recent graduates are encouraged to apply .
  • Basic understanding of Canadian accounting standards and tax regulations.
  • Proficiency with accounting software (QuickBooks Online, Sage, or similar) and MS Excel.
  • Strong attention to detail, organizational skills, and a willingness to learn.

Why Join Tolplanet Tax Services?

  • Mentorship: Learn directly from experienced professionals committed to your growth.
  • CPA Support: Financial support and study flexibility for employees pursuing their designation .
  • Variety: Work on diverse client files—no two days are the same.
  • Location: Conveniently located in Vancouver with a collaborative office culture.
