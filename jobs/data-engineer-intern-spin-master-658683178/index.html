Full Description

Please Note: If you are a current Spin Master employee with access to Workday, apply to this job via the Workday application.

Are you a kid at heart looking to build a career with a leading global children's toy, entertainment and digital gaming company?

At Spin Master, our unwavering commitment to open mindedness, integrity and innovation is a great part of what has made us an industry leader. How do we stay ahead of the pack? By hiring the best and brightest minds—and that’s why we want you!

Job Description What will you work on? As an Intern at Spin Master, you will gain hands-on experience working with innovative teams and world‑class brands in a fast‑paced, collaborative environment. You’ll be paired with a dedicated manager and mentor who will support your development, introduce you to the organization, and help you build the skills needed to succeed in your role.

How will you create impact?

  • Automate data processes and help up build data foundations
  • Assist with key projects, analysis, and initiatives aligned to team goals
  • Gain exposure to diverse functions and collaborative processes across the organization to understand how different areas contribute to company success.
  • Provide support on day-to-day tasks to help drive team efficiency and business outcomes

How will you fit into the team?

  • Bring a positive, proactive approach to working with teammates across the organization
  • You’re a self-starter willing to bring energy and ideas

What are your skills and experience?

  • Exposure to Power BI or other display tech
  • Some knowledge of Data Pipelines
  • Can write code in Python or other scripting languages

The anticipated pay range for candidates who will work in Ontario is $25 Per Hour. The offered pay to a successful candidate will be dependent on several factors that may include but are not limited to years of experience within the job, years of experience within the required industry, education, etc. Spin Master Inc. is a multi-state employer, and this salary range may not reflect positions that work only in other states.
