Full Description

Company Overview

Resnick Partnership LLP is a dedicated accounting firm committed to delivering exceptional financial services. We value integrity, professionalism, and continuous learning to serve our diverse client base effectively.

Summary

The Junior Accountant role is vital in supporting our senior staff by managing essential accounting functions. Located in Vaughan, Ontario, this position offers an excellent opportunity to grow within a reputable firm dedicated to professional development.

We are seeking a CPA student to start immediately.

Junior staff accountants will be responsible for assisting and completing a variety of engagements including audits, reviews, and compilations. We also provide experience in filing corporate and personal tax returns. Employees will essentially learn all aspects of public accounting.

We are a designated training firm accredited by CPA Ontario.

Must have completed substantially all of the required courses for the CPA Program.

Previous work experience in Public Accounting and use of Caseware and Profile are beneficial, but not required.

Candidates must be a Canadian resident, have a valid driver's license in Ontario and access to a vehicle. Candidate must also be fluent in speaking and writing English.

Please submit your most recent transcript when applying.

This position is your gateway to a rewarding career in accounting. Join us today and be part of a team that values your growth and expertise.
