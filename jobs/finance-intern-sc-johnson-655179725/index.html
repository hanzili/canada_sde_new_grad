About SC Johnson

Full Description

SC JOHNSON IS A FIFTH-GENERATION FAMILY COMPANY BUILT ON THE SPIRIT OF OUR PEOPLE. We have been leading with purpose for over 130 years, building iconic brands that win the hearts and minds of consumers – such as Raid®, Glade®, Ziploc® and more, in virtually every country around the world. Together, we are creating a better future – for the planet, for future generations and for every SCJ team member. Join our winning team of Wave Makers and Go Getters and help us write the next chapter in the SCJ story.

The Company's Total Rewards package is at or above industry levels. The expected base salary range for this position is between 40,820.00 CAD - 54,080.00 CAD. Job related skills, experience, education, and location will be considered in setting actual starting base salary. In addition to your base salary and depending on job level, eligibility, and performance, a total package may include profit sharing, a short-term incentive and/or long-term incentives. As a family company, benefits are a key piece of our Total Rewards package as well and we’re proud to provide a comprehensive, competitive, and differentiated benefits program that our people and their families value.

What if you didn’t have to wait to make an impact? At SC Johnson, we believe everyone can make an impact, including recent graduates and interns.Our university programs are a vital part of SC Johnson's growth and plans for the future. Internships at SC Johnson are designed for maximum impact and reward. Though each department is tailored to the field of study, we’ve built all our programs to be hands-on, challenging, meaningful and fun. An SC Johnson internship prepares you for working here and the world beyond.

Join SCJ Canada – recognized 12 times as a “Great Place to Work®”. We are seeking a high potential student who wants first-hand experience working with top brands such as Windex®; Ziploc®; Glade®; Fantastik®; OFF! ®, Raid® and Scrubbing Bubbles®.

At SC Johnson, your career success is defined by you. We nurture and develop the highest standards in employee development, while maintaining a lifestyle-friendly environment. This opportunity may be your first step in a very rewarding career with us.

This opportunity is based in the Canadian head office in Brantford Ontario, which is an easy commute from Kitchener/Waterloo, Hamilton, Burlington areas. You will be a part of a dynamic corporate Finance division and will get the opportunity to work on and support projects with the Finance teams for each of the functional areas of Sales & Marketing, Manufacturing & Logistics, and Tax & Treasury.

Your primary responsibilities will involve special projects, process improvements, reporting and analysis, with the overriding goal to create and implement innovative solutions to financial problems. During your work term, you also will be responsible for several month-end closing requirements including monthly reporting and analysis, journal entries, and account reconciliations. Additional specific assignments will include profitability analysis on product merchandising displays, advertising pre/post launch examinations, sales variance analysis and trade spend analysis. Throughout your work term, you will also gain valuable SAP experience, which is a marketable skill to add to your resume.

Interested in hearing first hand what an internship at SCJ is all about? Ask one of our former interns! We receive outstanding feedback from our interns on their experiences and seek to hire our top performing interns after graduation.

Job Requirements / Skills Required

  • Strong analytical skills with precise attention to detail
  • Demonstrated ability to work in a team environment
  • Demonstrated problem solving skills – ability to develop innovative solutions to complex problems
  • Ability to think strategically, analyze various business situations and make actionable recommendations
  • Software Packages (essential): Microsoft Office (particularly Microsoft Excel)
  • Additional Software Packages (experience is an asset): SAP, SAP Business Warehouse
  • CPA aspirations is a definite asset
  • Consumer Packaged Goods industry experience is also an asset

Advantages of a Career at SCJ

  • Unmatched career growth
  • Well known and highly reputable brands
  • Autonomy from day one
  • Exposure to senior leaders
  • Lifestyle benefits such as our on-site gymnasium & fitness center (with classes during lunch hour), flexible work hours, subsidized cafeteria
  • FUN, collaborate work environment
  • Pride – we are leaders in environment, diversity, and community responsibility. As a privately owned company SC Johnson is able to focus on sustainable growth for the business, our employees and the communities in which we live and work
