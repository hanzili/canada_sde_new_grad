About Francis Winspear Centre for Music

The Francis Winspear Centre for Music is seeking a dynamic individual as a Junior Data Analyst. This is a 12-week role working 35 hours/week from May 25-August 14, 2026, to support the Business Intelligence Team.

Who you are:

You love music, you enjoy working on a team and bringing out the best in yourself and your colleagues, you are creative, and you are not afraid to try new things. You are aligned with these values and want us to succeed together.

The position requires candidates to meet the following criteria:

  • Between 15 and 30 years of age (inclusive) at the start of employment
  • Canadian Citizen, permanent resident, or person on whom refugee protection has been conferred under the Immigration and refugee Protection Act
  • Legally entitled to work in Alberta and Canada

What you will do:

  • Analyze data from multiple sources to identify trends, patterns, anomalies, and insights that support business decisions
  • Apply analytical thinking to understand business questions and translate them into structured data analyses
  • Extract, transform, and prepare data for analysis, ensuring datasets are accurate, well‑structured, and fit for purpose
  • Investigate data quality issues, identify root causes, and recommend improvements to data collection, processes, or definitions
  • Review, understand, and document existing data systems, data flows, datasets, and analytical outputs to build knowledge of the current environment
  • Maintain clear documentation for data definitions, business rules, assumptions, calculations, and analytical logic
  • Develop and maintain analytical views, queries, and supporting visuals; creation of reports and dashboards (Power BI experience is an asset) supports analysis rather than drives it
  • Assist with historical and ad‑hoc analysis to answer business questions and support planning and process improvement initiatives
  • Collaborate with business users and the Business Intelligence team to clarify requirements, validate findings, and communicate insights
  • Perform other related duties as assigned by the Business Intelligence Manager

Qualifications:

  • Education related to or with a focus on Data Analytics, Data Science, Data Management
  • Intermediate skills using MS Office
  • Knowledge of data management processes
  • Organizational skills and ability to work with large amount of data and information
  • Strong communication and problem-solving abilities
  • Experience working with many details and being organized and able to prioritize work
  • Positive attitude with motivation to learn and grow professionally
  • Takes initiative and demonstrates proactiveness
  • Able to work evening and weekends when required

Hourly Wage: $19/hour

The position will remain open until a suitable candidate is found.
