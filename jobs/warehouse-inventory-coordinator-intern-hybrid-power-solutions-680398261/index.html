Full Description

ABOUT US:

Hybrid Power Solutions was founded in 2015 in response to a pressing need for safer, cleaner and more effective portable power systems. Today, Hybrid Power Solutions produces patent-pending industrial battery systems and turnkey hybrid solutions for a variety of industrial sectors such as mining, construction, railway and military.

Driven by a commitment to performance, we believe that real change occurs at the intersection of innovation and sustainability. Our products not only help our customers save on operational costs but they also happen to be better for the planet that’s a win-win in our eyes.

We are a family of smart, innovative and collaborative thinkers and doers. We put our employees first and strive to foster a community based on transparency and inclusion. We are growing quickly and looking for creative, out of the box problem solvers to challenge and support us as we continue to innovate in the industrial portable power and clean energy space.

Job Overview:

We are seeking a Warehouse Inventory Coordinator to oversee and optimize our warehouse inventory and organization. The successful candidate will be responsible for managing inventory levels, organizing stock, conducting regular counts, and handling basic shipping and receiving tasks. This role is ideal for someone who thrives in a hands-on environment and is passionate about process improvement and operational efficiency. You will act as the inventory gate-keeper for R&D and production, meaning all inventory requests in and out will run through you.

Duties

  • Inventory Cycle counts: Count and record the inventory for our fiscal year end audit
  • Stock Organization: Ensure all inventory is properly stored, labeled, and easily accessible and organized in shelves
  • Documentation: Manage records of inventory movements while encountering during the counting process

To succeed in this job, you will need:

  • Attention to Detail: Strong organizational skills and the ability to identify parts if misplaced and put back in the bins
  • Communication Skills: Ability to work collaboratively and communicate effectively for the issues encountered
  • Physical Ability: Comfortable working in a warehouse environment, including standing for extended periods and lifting up to 20 kg.

Education & Experience:

  • Education: University student or a fresh graduate willing to work for one month.

Work Environment:

  • Fast-Paced Manufacturing: The role involves working in a dynamic manufacturing environment with frequent interactions with production teams.
  • Physical Demands: Must be comfortable with standing for extended periods and working in a fast-paced setting.
