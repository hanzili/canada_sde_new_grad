Bookkeeper/Junior Accountant

at POWERGROUP Resources

📍 Location Hamilton, ON
📋 Details Full-time · Permanent · $50,000–$65,000/year · N/A
📅 Posted May 01, 2026

About the Role

Process P2P, close books, prepare financial reports

About POWERGROUP Resources

Construction-focused firm; ERP implementation and growth

Full Description

All Pro Door & Hardware Ltd. is a leading supplier of architectural doors, frames, and hardware for construction projects, serving residential, commercial, and institutional clients across Hamilton, ON and beyond. Our team supports both new-build and retrofit work, delivering reliable solutions for a diverse range of construction needs.

As a Bookkeeper/Junior Accountant, you will play a key role in maintaining financial accuracy and supporting operational excellence. You will collaborate closely with project management, procurement, and sales teams to ensure seamless financial processes and contribute to the company's ongoing growth.

This posting reflects a current hiring need for our client. We use AI-assisted tools to help review resumes and identify potential candidate matches, however all hiring decisions are made by our team and the client directly.

Key responsibilities include:

Managing daily accounts payable (A/P) and accounts receivable (A/R) transactions Preparing, reviewing, and reconciling purchase orders and vendor/customer invoices Tracking and maintaining inventory and cost records Supporting month-end, quarter-end, and year-end close and financial reporting Assisting with financial analysis and project/operations reporting Collaborating with internal teams to ensure financial accuracy Maintaining organized financial documentation Identifying and resolving discrepancies in financial records Assisting with implementation and optimization of a new ERP system and related accounting software Providing additional finance-team support as assigned

Requirements

Required Qualifications: Post-secondary education in accounting, finance, or business administration 2–4 years of bookkeeping or junior accounting experience Hands-on experience with accounts payable (A/P), accounts receivable (A/R), purchase order processing, and invoice reconciliation Experience supporting month-end close and preparing basic financial reporting Hands-on experience with construction progress billing Proficiency with QuickBooks Online and experience using an ERP system for accounting workflows Experience in construction, building materials, or trade-related industries Experience communicating with customers and vendors regarding invoices, collections, and payment issues English proficiency for day-to-day customer and vendor communication Legally authorized to work in Canada Preferred Qualifications: Certified Professional Bookkeeper (CPB) designation Experience with Microsoft Dynamics ERP

Benefits

Health Care Spending Account Competitive salary of $50,000–$65,000/year based on skills and experience, plus bonus pay Dental, disability, extended health, life, and vision coverage On-site parking 8-hour shifts, Monday–Friday (no weekends)
