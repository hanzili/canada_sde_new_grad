Full Description

The future you want is within reach.

At PCL, we build the places where life happens and find camaraderie in the process. We’re a community of builders committed to building better communities. That’s why we’re always looking ahead, and not just to the next project or what’s next in our industry.

We’re also looking at what’s next for you and how we can help you build a career you’re proud of. As a student at PCL, you will get the opportunity for challenging work assignments in a supportive work environment which promotes personal and professional growth.

We are seeking a Financial Systems Analyst Student for our North American Headquarters (NAHQ) in Edmonton, Alberta , that is available for 8 months starting in May 2026 . This position will be under the direct supervisor of the Business Systems Support Manager.

As a Financial Systems Analyst Student And Depending On Your Assigned Project, You Will Have The Opportunity To Contribute To Our Team By

  • Provides user support for enterprise business development.
  • Responds to related service requests, troubleshoot incidents, and investigate problems.
  • Assists with, create or update process, training and system documentation.
  • Assists with/deliver web-based training to end users.
  • Assists with documentation of requirements for new system.
  • Assists with, create or update use cases that support requirements analysis, functional designs, user acceptance testing, and training material.
  • Participates in pilots and rollouts.
  • Develops and deliver stakeholder communications.
  • Assists with the coordination of subject matter experts for requirements refinement and user acceptance testing.
  • Assists with/perform system configuration and testing.
  • Liaises with business, technology, and support teams to ensure systems meet business requirements.
  • Assists with new and ongoing department initiatives.
  • Assists with/perform analysis of system and data usage.
  • Configures navigation, dashboards, analytics, and reports in solutions such as Power BI.

What You Will Bring To The Role

  • Excellent communication and interpersonal skills
  • Ability to prioritize multiple tasks and meet deadlines
  • Ability to work both independently and as a team

  • Assets:

  • Basic understanding of SQL

  • Construction experience
  • Support experience
  • Data analysis experience
  • Training experience

  • Technical writing experience

  • Students from the following fields are encouraged to apply:

  • Business systems analysis

  • Information management, information technology, computing science or a related discipline
