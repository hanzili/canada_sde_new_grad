Full Description

About Road to Rail Construction (RTRC)
Road to Rail Construction is a growing, Alberta based contractor delivering a wide range of services across civil construction, rail maintenance, oilfield construction, and trucking and material supply. we’ve built a reputation for being a reliable, one stop solution for our clients handling everything from earthworks and site development to ongoing project support services.

At RTRC, we pride ourselves on running a modern, well-maintained fleet, backed by experienced crews and a commitment to getting the job done safely and efficiently. Our team operates with a strong focus on quality, accountability, and keeping projects moving forward.

We’re built on our core values:
Resilience. Trust. Reputation. Commitment.

As we continue to grow, we’re looking for team members who want to be part of a company that values hard work, supports development, and takes pride in delivering results across every project.

Location: Camrose, AB
Employment Type: Full-time

About the Role: We’re looking for a motivated Junior Project Manager to support our civil earthworks projects. This role is ideal for someone early in their construction or project management career who wants hands-on experience in the field and the office.

What You’ll Do

  • Assist project managers with planning, scheduling, and coordinating earthworks projects
  • Help track project progress, budgets, quantities, and documentation
  • Communicate with site supervisors, subcontractors, and suppliers
  • Support quality control, safety documentation, and daily reporting
  • Attend site meetings and help resolve day-to-day project issues
  • Learn and grow into more responsibility as you gain experience

Qualifications

  • Post‑secondary education in engineering technology or a related field
  • Strong organizational and communication skills
  • Ability to work on-site and in the office
  • Basic computer skills (Excel, Word; project management software is an asset)
  • A positive attitude and willingness to learn
  • Valid driver’s license

What We Offer

  • Training and mentorship
  • Opportunities for career growth
  • Competitive wages and benefits
  • A supportive team environment

How to Apply
Send your resume and a brief introduction to our hiring team. We look forward to meeting candidates who want to build their career in civil construction.
