About the Role

Record transactions, manage AR/AP, assist month-end closes

About E.B. Box Company

Small manufacturing firm; 1-year contract with growth potential

Full Description

We are a fast growing manufacturing business located in Richmond Hill, ON. We are looking for someone who is a motivated self starting individual to help take on day to day operational roles within our organization.

This is a one year contract role with the possibility of turning permanent at the end and you may be offered to join our benefits program after successfully completing the probationary period. You are expected to work in the office 5 days a week.

We are looking for an ambitious Junior Accountant to provide support to the financial department by managing daily accounting tasks. You will be part of a team of professionals working to maintain order and transparency for the company’s finances. The Junior Accountant will support the Controller with ongoing compliance activities, including reporting, audit and management of the Company’s books and records.

The ideal candidate will be well-versed in accounting principles and able to work comfortably with numbers and attention to detail.

Responsibilities

  • Record day-to-day financial transactions and complete the posting process
  • Process accounts receivable, including customer invoicing and payment collection, in a timely manner
  • Process accounts payable, including inputting and verifying supplier invoices and payments as well as expense reports, in a timely manner
  • Assist in researching, tracking, and resolving discrepancies
  • Assist in the processing of balance sheets, income statements and other financial statements according to legal and company accounting and financial guidelines
  • Update financial data in databases to ensure that information will be accurate and immediately available when needed
  • Manage the filing of returns and payments within prescribed timelines
  • Prepare and submit weekly/monthly reports
  • Assist in the preparation of monthly/yearly closings
  • Maintain appropriate files, reports, and documentations
  • Maintain and update customer/vendor files and communicate with vendors for discrepancies.
  • Assist the controller with month-end and monthly GL reconciliations, including bank recs.
  • Entering and posting credit card activities and reconciling the credit card accounts
  • Assist with preparation of Canadian tax filings; HST, corporate tax, etc
  • Assist with interim and year-end audit reporting requirements
  • Process weekly timesheet and bi-weekly payroll
  • Other special projects assigned from time to time,

Requirements

  • MUST have college diploma or degree in accounting, business, finance, or a related field
  • Must have 3-4 years of experience in similar role
  • CPA designation or working towards it will be considered an asset
  • Demonstrated knowledge of clerical accounting and bookkeeping procedures
  • Computer literacy, including MS Word, Excel, Outlook - Knowledge of Sage 300 is an asset
  • Excellent organizing abilities
  • Great attention to detail
  • Good with numbers and figures and an analytical acumen
  • Good understanding of accounting and financial reporting principles and practices

Personality & Attitude:

  • Warm, friendly, and great under pressure.
  • Motivated, Self Starter, Go-Getter, Organized

Skills:

  • Exceptional listening skills with the ability to understand and learn from what you are being told and apply that to your role
  • Punctual, responsible, with excellent interpersonal skills.

Follow instructions on multiple tasks, stay organized, and meets deadlines.

  • Independent worker, able to stay on task for assignments.
  • Team player who takes initiative, and solves problems. Goes the extra mile.

Great attention to details and can see "the bigger picture"
