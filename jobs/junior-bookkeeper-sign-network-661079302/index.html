About the Role

About Sign Network

Full Description

Overview:

We are seeking a detail-oriented and organized Junior Bookkeeper to join our finance team. The primary function of this position is to support the Accounting department in meeting monthly deadlines and ensuring the accuracy of financial data.

Primary Responsibilites:

  • Take ownership for maintenance of the permanet accounting records of assigned entities.
  • Conduct monthly account and bank reconciliations
  • Maintain AR and AP
  • Balance inter-company accounts
  • Issue payment or invoices as required
  • Prepare and post journal entries as required
  • Process payroll on a timely basis
  • Produce monthly financial statements
  • File taxes and assist on source remittances on a timely basis
  • Assist on general bookkeeping and generate reports
  • Perform other duties as assigned

Personal & Orgaizational Developement:

  • Able to work independently & in a team setting
  • Excellent attention to details and organizational skills to ensure timeliness and quality of work
  • Work well under stress and tight deadlines
  • Basic accounting knowledge
  • Basic excel, word and outlook skills

Personal Attributes:

  • Dedicated to continuous improvement of self and team
  • Willing to assist the team on meeting deadlines
  • Strong communication and organizational skills
  • Ability to creatively and proactively problem-solve
  • Motivated to continally develop technical and soft skills

Qualifications:

  • Some formal education in accounting or finance
  • Experience with Quick Books is an asset

Tasks:

  • Prepare payroll
  • Maintain general ledgers and financial statements
  • Post journal entries
  • Prepare other statistical, financial and accounting reports

Computer and technology knowledge:

  • MS Excel
  • MS Word
  • Quick Books
  • MS Office
  • Invoicing

Experience:

  • 1 year to less than 2 years

This position is an excellent fit for candidates seeking a professional role in bookkeeping that emphasizes accuracy, efficiency, and the use of leading accounting tools.

Preference to those living in the Upper York Region area
