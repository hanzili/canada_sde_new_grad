Full Description

About Joveo:

Every company says they're "AI-first." We actually are. Joveo's recruitment

advertising platform processes millions of hiring decisions through machine

learning, real-time bidding, and predictive analytics — helping the world's

largest employers find the right people, faster and fairer. But we're not

done. Not even close.

Role: Junior Software Engineer

Location: Remote

Role Overview:

We are hiring a Junior Software Engineer to contribute real code to

Joveo's production platform while growing your skills alongside

experienced engineers. You will own small features end-to-end,

participate in team rituals, and build the engineering foundation

that will carry your career for years.

Key Responsibilities:

  • Build, test, and ship features with increasing levels of autonomy

  • Fix bugs, resolve production issues, and contribute to technical debt work

  • Participate actively in code reviews, giving and receiving feedback

  • Learn and apply modern engineering practices — testing, CI/CD, observability

  • Collaborate with product and design on feature scoping and delivery

  • Develop deeper expertise in your team's primary tech stack

Required Skills & Qualifications:

  • 1–2 years of professional software engineering experience

  • Strong working knowledge of at least one production language (Python, Java, JavaScript, Go)

  • Experience shipping code in a team-based environment with version control

  • Familiarity with writing tests, reviewing code, and basic debugging practices

  • Understanding of databases, APIs, and web application fundamentals

  • Growth mindset with a willingness to take on stretch assignments
