Full Description

Job Summary:
As an Junior Accountant/ Bookkeeper at Dunlevy Food Equipment Ltd., you will play a crucial role in maintaining day to day records, preparing daily reports, and ensuring compliance with company rules and regulations. You will work closely with the management staff to support the company's financial operations and contribute to its overall success.

Responsibilities:
- Manage accounts payable and accounts receivable processes to ensure timely payments and collections
- Perform account reconciliations to verify accuracy of financial transactions
- Process small payroll accurately and in a timely manner
- Utilize accounting software, including Spire Business Systems and Excel to record and process transactions
Requirements:
-Opportunity for a accounting student with some experience in full cycle accounting in a small company environment.
- Proven experience working in accounting roles
- Strong understanding of financial services and accounting principles
- Proficiency in using accounting software such as Spire Business Systems and Excel
- Knowledge of accounts payable, account reconciliation, bank reconciliation, payroll processes, and budgeting
- Excellent attention to detail and organizational skills
- Ability to work effectively in a team environment and collaborate with cross-functional teams

Join Dunlevy Food Equipment Ltd. where you will have the opportunity to contribute to a dynamic team that values excellence in service and commitment to providing quality products to our customers.
