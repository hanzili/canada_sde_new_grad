Full Description

About Us

We are seeking motivated candidates who aspire to embark on a rewarding career within the sales domain. Our primary emphasis lies in brand cultivation and large scale market outreach. Prospective applicants should demonstrate positivity, diligence, and a collaborative spirit conducive to a team environment . We recently expanded to Kelowna and are excited to continue growing our team.

Responsibilities

  • Develop and execute sales strategies that maximize acquisition for our partners
  • Connect directly with customers and bring awareness and value to client brands
  • Initiate meaningful connections with customers through direct engagement.
  • Actively pursue sales
  • Achieve sales targets and meet or exceed sales quotas
  • Answer customer inquiries with patience, care, and empathy
  • Provide exceptional customer service throughout the sales process
  • Assist in data collection
  • Take part in daily team meetings and brainstorm sales strategies

What We Offer

  • Monday to Friday schedule and flexible time-off
  • Paid training
  • Weekly compensation
  • Competitive compensation that includes a guaranteed base wage + bonus pay.
  • Average weekly compensation for an entry-level position: $800-1,400/week
  • Ability to earn daily cash bonuses
  • Career advancement opportunities based on performance and skill set, not seniority
  • An energetic and fun office culture
  • Opportunities to attend networking conferences, and annual company retreats

Must Haves

  • At least 18 years of age.
  • Previous experience is an asset, but not required.
  • Ability to work Monday through Friday, 1:00 pm to 8:00 pm.
  • Able to establish rapport and connect with clients, customers and colleagues.
  • Enjoys working in a team environment

Think you’re the right fit? We’d love to hear from you! Applicants who are selected for an interview will be contacted by our team
