About the Role

About NE Analytics & Consulting

Analytics consulting for finance and strategy decisions

Full Description

NE Analytics & Consulting is building capabilities that help organizations make better financial and strategic decisions through Deal Analytics, FP&A intelligence, and data-driven insights .

We are currently looking for a Deal Analytics Associate to support the development of financial analytics solutions used in investment, diligence, and operational decision-making.

This is not generic dashboard development work. You will be working on analytics that help evaluate businesses, understand performance drivers, and support real investment decisions.

What will you work on:

  • Deal evaluation & due diligence analytics
  • Portfolio performance tracking and reporting
  • EBITDA, revenue, and margin driver analysis
  • Deep dives into customer, employee and investment returns
  • KPI frameworks for investment and operational decisions
  • Power BI dashboards built from financial and operational datasets

What we are looking for:

Required:

  • 2+ years of experience in data analytics, BI, or data consulting
  • Strong experience with Power BI and/or Microsoft analytics stack
  • Understanding of financial statements and business KPIs
  • Strong analytical and problem-solving skills
  • Ability to translate data into actionable insights

Preferred:

  • Experience in FP&A, consulting, or deal advisory
  • Knowledge of SQL, DAX, Python, or similar tools
  • Exposure to Microsoft Fabric, Azure, or data pipelines
  • Experience working with financial or investment-related datasets

Certifications:

Microsoft certifications such as DP-600, DP-700, PL-300, AZ-203, AZ-305, etc. are considered an asset.

Other Details:

  • Location: Toronto (hybrid/remote flexibility considered)
  • Engagement: Full-time, contract, or project-based opportunities may be considered depending on fit

If you are interested in working at the intersection of finance, analytics, and investment decision-making , please send your resume and a brief summary of relevant experience to: careers@ne-analytics.com. Please include a cover letter mentioning your current active certifications and 2 relevant project experiences.
