About Cavendish Farms

Full Description

Job Description Digital Marketing is more than ads and social posts- it is about how content, data, and technology work together to create great customer experiences. As our Digital Marketing Summer Student , you’ll work closely with our Digital Marketing, Brand, and Product Marketing teams on meaningful projects that shape how our marketing platforms and website operate every day.

This role is ideal for a marketing student who enjoys problem-solving, organization, and understanding how digital experiences are built and improved. You’ll gain hands-on experience with enterprise marketing platforms, collaborate with business partners across the organization, and see how marketing strategy turns into execution.

Responsibilities

  • Working on the New Website development- By coordinating action items between marketing, IT and external partners.
  • Helping in improving how marketing assets (images, videos, documents) are organized and used.
  • Work on improving how marketing work gets planned and executed by supporting project coordination for marketing workflows.
  • Support ongoing improvements to our marketing product content platforms by assisting with the build and launch of new Brand stores.

What You’ll Learn

  • How enterprise websites and marketing platforms are built, launched, and maintained
  • How content strategy, technology, and process come together in modern marketing
  • How to work with cross‑functional teams, stakeholders, and real business requirements
  • How data and dashboards are used to improve marketing effectiveness
  • Practical experience that builds a strong foundation for a career in marketing operations, digital marketing, or marketing technology

Qualifications

  • Currently enrolled in a post‑secondary program in Marketing or a related field
  • Strong organizational skills with the ability to manage multiple tasks and deadlines
  • Analytical mindset with an interest in data, structure, and process improvement
  • Clear communicator who is comfortable working with different teams
  • Able to work independently while also being a strong team player
  • Working knowledge of Microsoft Office (Excel, PowerPoint, Word)

What Sets You Apart

  • Interest in websites, content management, or marketing technology
  • Curiosity about how digital experiences are designed and improved
  • Desire to learn tools that are widely used in professional marketing environments
