The Role

We are seeking an aspiring Valuation Associate to join our team. You will work directly with the valuation partner, gaining exposure to diverse industries and valuation methodologies. This is an ideal role for a CPA looking to transition from audit/tax into a CBV career.

  • Research: Conduct industry research; identify and analyze guideline companies, and public/private transaction multiples.
  • Support: Review and critique third-party valuation reports on behalf of audit/law firms.
