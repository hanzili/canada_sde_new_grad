About the Role

Support M&A deals, analyze target software companies

About Volaris Group

Subsidiary of Constellation Software; acquisitive growth

Full Description

Job Summary Business development analyst.

Job Description THE COMPANY Volaris Group (www.volarisgroup.com) acquires, strengthens and grows vertical market technology companies enabling them to be clear leaders within their focused industry. Our operating companies provide mission-critical software and technology solutions to a number of vertical markets. Volaris seeks out long term investment opportunities in companies, technologies or ideas that add value to existing core markets, or allow entry into new ones.

The Volaris Group is a subsidiary of Constellation Software Inc. (www.csisoftware.com), listed on the Toronto Stock Exchange.

THE POSITION You Will Drive Our Acquisition Activities In a Number Of Industries Globally By Sourcing And Supporting Acquisitions Of Vertical-market-software Companies By

  • Creatively identifying and analyzing mid-market software businesses meeting our strategic criteria and specific financial/operational metrics
  • Maintaining and building a proprietary database of key industry contacts
  • Conducting industry research to support business development activities
  • Growing our prospect database by identifying businesses meeting our investment criteria
  • Identifying relevant research organizations, advisory groups, and trade conferences
  • Supporting the larger team on other business development related projects and with ad-hoc analysis

You will have the freedom and responsibility to work autonomously but also directly with a highly engaged team building out a global business. Our team and culture are high growth oriented and include a mix of seasoned founders, CEOS, sales & marketing, and product leaders as well as M&A and business development professionals who work hard and have fun doing it.

Qualifications

  • Have or pursuing a relevant post-secondary degree in Business
  • Strong verbal and written communication skills
  • Confident and professional presentation skills regardless of the stakeholder level
  • Self-motivated individual with an ability to perform independently in a fast-paced environment
  • Proficient in Microsoft Excel
  • High degree of professionalism and integrity

Worker Type Student (Fixed Term) (Trainee)
