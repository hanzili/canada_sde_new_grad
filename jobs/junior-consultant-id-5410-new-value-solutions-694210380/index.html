About New Value Solutions

Full Description

New Value Solutions, a national IT consulting company, is seeking a Junior Consultant to work for our client in Richmond, BC. This 4-month contract will support the Technology Enablement team with Microsoft Copilot adoption, knowledge management maturity, user education, and evaluation of end-user technologies.

The successful candidate will help gather business requirements, apply user research methods, develop documentation and learning materials, support Copilot and Copilot Chat education, assist with agent building and testing, and contribute to the growth of self-serve knowledge resources. This role requires strong communication skills, comfort with Microsoft 365 tools, analytical thinking, and the ability to support digital adoption and knowledge management initiatives in an enterprise environment.

Responsibilities:

  • Support Microsoft Copilot and Copilot Chat adoption through documentation, education sessions, and user learning resources.
  • Assist with Copilot agent building and testing, including pilot coordination, outcome documentation, and baseline metrics.
  • Gather business requirements and apply user research methods to understand end-user needs.
  • Develop and maintain knowledge articles, job aids, FAQs, and other self-serve learning materials.
  • Evaluate new and existing end-user technologies and communicate business value to stakeholders.
  • Contribute to Technology Enablement’s knowledge management practice and Digital First service maturity.
  • Use analytics and feedback to identify knowledge gaps and support continuous improvement.
  • Draft, publish, and refresh Technology Enablement content for channels such as the Technology and Services HUB, Technology Bulletins, TechTalks, and TechTips.
  • Support basic reporting, insight generation, and recommendations related to technology adoption and knowledge management.
  • Perform other related duties as assigned.

Requirements:

  • 2+ years of relevant experience in an IT-related role within a public organization.
  • Experience in end-user enablement, digital adoption, knowledge management, or business analysis.
  • Minimum 1 year of working knowledge with relevant end-user technologies.
  • Working knowledge of Microsoft 365 tools and collaboration capabilities, including Teams, SharePoint, and OneDrive.
  • Comfort learning new Microsoft 365 and end-user technology features quickly.
  • Working knowledge of Microsoft Copilot and Copilot Chat.
  • Experience or exposure to Copilot agent design, prototyping, and testing.
  • Strong written and verbal communication skills.
  • Ability to synthesize inputs into actionable summaries and recommendations.
  • Analytical skills to support basic reporting, insight generation, and continuous improvement work.
  • Ability to create clear documentation and learning materials, including job aids, FAQs, and knowledge articles.
  • Ability to facilitate basic learning or education sessions under guidance.
  • Undergraduate degree in Information Technology, Business Management, or a related discipline. (Alternative studies in knowledge management, behavioral science, or a related field may be considered).
