About Us

Moe’s Home Collection - Design made simple for the modern home. Our passion lies in furniture and design and it is the fuel that drives our business. We started out as a family-run company and aim to maintain this as we continue to rapidly grow. We are entrepreneurial. We challenge the status quo, are not afraid to try new ideas, and we leverage our agile structure to make smart decisions and find new opportunities. We are a people-first company and what we do wouldn’t be possible without our customers. For this reason, we like to say that our collections are always customer curated.

Sundays – Founded on the expertise of Moe’s, Sundays was created with a focus on comfort, quality, and ease. We create beautiful, durable pieces designed for real life, crafted from solid wood, performance fabrics, and materials chosen to last. With a cohesive collection that feels effortless and lived-in, Sundays is available online and through retail locations across Canada and the US, offering free shipping and complimentary assembly on large items.

Hetta - Launched as a sister company to Sundays, Hetta creates furniture to grow with, gather around, and pass down. Grounded in tradition, the collection balances timeless forms with modern comfort and function. Defined by craftsmanship and high-quality materials, Hetta pieces are made to feel warm and enduring for generations. Hetta is available online, with free delivery and complimentary assembly on large items.

Field & Social – We take salads seriously, which means we consider every detail: from the greens we cut fresh daily; to the dietitian we consult for our recipes; to the dressings we make from scratch; to the local herbs and meats we source; to the thoughtful and contemporary design we put into our retail spaces. Ride Cycle Club – RIDE is an innovative boutique concept that is challenging the status quo of indoor cycling. RIDE combines an intense cardiovascular sweat, micro bike movements and full body toning reinforced by the authority of music.

We are looking for a Junior Accountant to join our finance team. Reporting to the Group Controller, the Junior Accountant will support the preparation of timely, accurate, and relevant financial information while learning and applying best practices in accounting. This is a great opportunity for someone early in their career who is eager to develop their technical skills within a collaborative, results-driven team.

RESPONSIBILITIES & DUTIES

  • Support month-end close procedures, including journal entries and account reconciliations
  • Assist in maintaining the trial balance and ensuring accuracy of financial records
  • Support the preparation of monthly financial statements under the guidance of the Controller
  • Assist the AP team with treasury-related tasks, including bi-weekly payment runs and bank template updates
  • Support regulatory filings to ensure compliance with deadlines (GST, BC-PST, BC-EHT, WSIB, Source Deductions, and Corporate Tax)
  • Assist with payroll processing and related reconciliations
  • Prepare and distribute weekly KPI reports for internal stakeholders
  • Assist with year-end processes, including support for external accountants (NTR)
  • Reconcile and maintain assigned balance sheet accounts
  • Contribute to process improvements and support other finance projects as needed

SKILLS & ABILITIES

  • 1–2 years of accounting experience (co-op or internship experience welcomed)
  • Diploma or degree in Accounting, Finance, or related field
  • Interest in pursuing a CPA designation is considered an asset
  • Strong attention to detail and commitment to accuracy
  • Organized and able to manage multiple priorities in a fast-paced environment
  • Clear and confident communication skills
  • Experience in a multi-entity or retail/e-commerce environment is an asset

WHAT WE OFFER:

  • Competitive salary plus bonus.
  • Comprehensive benefits plan (health, dental, vision) with enhanced coverage for mental health resources.
  • Three weeks of vacation in your first year.
  • Hybrid environment
  • Casual and open office with views of downtown Vancouver and the North Shore mountains.
  • Stocked kitchen with snacks, fresh fruit, coffee, and tea.
  • Ergonomic workstations with sit-stand desks.
  • Free electric vehicle charging at our head office.
  • Generous product discounts for yourself, friends, and family.
  • Professional development opportunities.
  • Office gym and free weekly spin classes at Ride Cycle Club (locations in Vancouver and Toronto).

Salary range for this role is budgeted at $55,000 - $60,000

We appreciate hearing from all candidates, however, only those under consideration will be contacted. Moe’s is proud to be an equal opportunity employer who is committed to inclusive hiring practices.
