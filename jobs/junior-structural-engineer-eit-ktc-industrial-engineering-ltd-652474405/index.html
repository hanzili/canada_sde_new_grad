Junior Structural Engineer EIT

at KTC Industrial Engineering Ltd.

📍 Location Surrey, British Columbia
📋 Details Full-time
📅 Posted Mar 27, 2026

About the Role

Design and review industrial structural systems and drawings

About KTC Industrial Engineering Ltd.

Industrial engineering firm in forestry/mining sectors

Full Description

About Us

KTC Industrial Engineering Ltd., based in Surrey, British Columbia, provides engineering and project management expertise across the forestry, mining, petrochemical, and power generation industries in North America and Europe. We are committed to delivering innovative, safe, and cost-effective solutions for complex industrial projects.

The Opportunity

We are seeking a Junior Structural Engineer to join our growing team. This is a full-time, on-site role where you will collaborate with multidisciplinary engineering teams to design and review structural steel and foundation systems for industrial equipment and facilities.

In this role, you will prepare conceptual and detailed designs, perform structural calculations, draft comprehensive drawings, and produce technical reports. You will also review shop and technical drawings while coordinating with project managers, contractors, and site personnel to deliver high-quality industrial projects.

Key Responsibilities

  • Design structural steel and foundation systems for industrial projects.
  • Perform structural calculations and ensure compliance with applicable codes and standards.
  • Prepare and review shop drawings, technical drawings, and detailed engineering documents.
  • Draft comprehensive structural drawings and reports for project deliverables.
  • Collaborate with multidisciplinary engineering teams, project managers, and contractors.
  • Contribute to both conceptual and final design stages of industrial projects.

Qualifications

  • Bachelor’s degree in Civil or Structural Engineering (or equivalent education and experience).
  • Minimum 2 years of experience as a Structural Engineer, preferably in industrial projects.
  • Proficiency in RISA 3D, AutoCAD, Advance Steel, Navisworks, Revit, and Microsoft Office.
  • Strong knowledge of structural steel, reinforced concrete, foundation design, and industrial construction systems.
  • Experience working in multidisciplinary teams and coordinating with site personnel.
  • Excellent written and verbal communication skills, with the ability to clearly present technical information.
  • Strong attention to detail, accuracy, and ability to manage multiple projects.
  • Quick learner with adaptability to different drawing standards and specifications.
  • Experience in forestry, mining, petrochemical, or power generation industries is considered an asset.

Why Join Us?

  • Work on diverse, large-scale industrial projects across North America and Europe.
  • Join a collaborative team of professionals in a supportive work environment.
  • Gain valuable hands-on experience and career growth opportunities in structural engineering.
