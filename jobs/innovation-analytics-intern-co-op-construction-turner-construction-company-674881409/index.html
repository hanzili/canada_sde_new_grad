BUILD YOUR FUTURE WITH TURNER CONSTRUCTION

Turner Construction Company is a North America–based construction services firm with operations across Asia, India, and Europe. Founded in 1902, we are guided by values of active caring, teamwork, integrity, and commitment, with more than 120 years of impact on skylines and the industry. With over 16,000 employees, we deliver approximately $29 billion in annual construction across 1,500+ projects. Turner specializes in large, complex builds while advancing innovation and new technologies, with work spanning healthcare, education, commercial, aviation, and infrastructure sectors.

In Canada, where Turner has operated for more than 50 years, we employ nearly 1,000 people and generate close to $1 billion in annual revenue. With over 930 professionals, we play a key role in the industry while supporting communities through local projects, partnerships, and volunteer efforts. Turner is committed to quality, safety, inclusion, and building lasting value for the communities it serves.

WHAT WE HAVE TO OFFER YOU

We are committed to developing the next generation of construction professionals by combining academic support with hands on experience. As an Intern or Co-op, you will gain meaningful experience that supports your goals and career development and may be eligible for educational credit, where applicable. This work term also includes:

  • access to practical tools and resources including, a work phone, laptop, and safety gear,
  • paid statutory holidays,
  • Summer Fridays (early dismissals during the summer months on Fridays where applicable),
  • invitations to participate in company events and activities,
  • and CA$20 to CA$23 per hour, depending on academic year and prior experience.

THE INNOVATION & ANALYTICS CO OP / INTERN OPPOURTUNITY

Turner Construction’s Vancouver office is currently seeking students, recent graduates, or individuals interested in a 4- or 8- month Innovation & Analytics Co-Op/Internship opportunity for the upcoming Summer 2026 semester. This is a full-time position (Monday to Friday, 40 hours per week) based at our Vancouver office, with occasional on-site work at active construction projects, depending on the assignment.

The Innovation & Analytics Co-op/Intern will work with the Procurement team, focusing on translating conceptual ideas into practical outputs with structured feedback and oversight. The role includes data analysis, dashboard development, and the use of applied AI tools. As an Innovation & Analytics Co-op/Intern, you will:

  • Translate high-level concepts into actionable plans and deliverables
  • Conduct data analysis to support decision-making and operational insights
  • Design and maintain dashboards using Power BI
  • Utilize AI tools (e.g., ChatGPT) to improve workflows, documentation, and analysis
  • Iterate on solutions based on feedback and stakeholder input
  • Identify process improvements and opportunities for automation
  • Prepare clear summaries, reports, and visualizations
  • Deliver functional dashboards and analytical outputs
  • Advance ideas independently from concept to implemented solutions
  • Contribute to improved workflows through data and digital tools
  • Other activities, duties, and responsibilities as assigned

Is this the right role for you:

  • Enrolled in a relevant program (e.g., Data Analytics, Business, Engineering, Computer Science)
  • Working knowledge of data analysis techniques and tools (Excel, SQL, or similar)
  • Experience or familiarity with Power BI or other visualization tools
  • Exposure to AI tools such as ChatGPT or similar platforms
  • Strong problem-solving and critical thinking skills
  • Self-starter with the ability to work independently
  • Comfortable taking initiative with minimal direction
  • Able to move projects from concept to completion
  • Receptive to feedback and able to iterate quickly
  • Organized with strong attention to detail
  • Experience with process automation or scripting (Python, Power Automate, etc.) is preferred
  • Prior internship or project experience involving dashboards or analytics is preferred
