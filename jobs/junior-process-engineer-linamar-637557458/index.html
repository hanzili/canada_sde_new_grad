Junior - Process Engineer

at Linamar

About the Role

Assist process engineering tasks and continuous improvement

About Linamar

Global manufacturing leader in auto parts

Full Description

Job Title: Engineer – Process, Junior

Job Summary

Work as a trainee process engineer, under guidance of a process / project engineer or a program manager assigned to a task, program or project

Responsibility

  • Assist project / process engineer with all program elements like documentation, attending meetings, as instructed
  • Participate APQP / CFM teams and record all activities for action trackers
  • Participate in continuous improvement efforts based on the feedback from external (CAT, SAT) and internal related performance indicators
  • Develop and monitor strategy for procuring special tools and fixtures where required
  • In collaboration with manufacturing engineers, estimate cycle times for each operation in the process
  • Participate in reviewing Process FMEA on regular basis and work with engineering and core teams to reduce high RPN numbers.
  • Support core teams to reduce lines scrap against plant targets Assist in the preparation and monitoring of SRED (Scientific Research and Experimental Development) and WET (Waste Elimination Tracker) activities

Academic/Educational Requirements

  • A college technical diploma in engineering or an equivalent experience working as a process or manufacturing technician in a manufacturing or industrial environment Completion of a Program Management Course as an asset

Required Skills/Experience

  • A minimum of two (2) year experience as a process or manufacturing technician in a machining or manufacturing environment.
  • Knowledge of SPC methods, process and machine capability requirements, blueprint reading and Geometric Dimensioning & Tolerancing
  • Working knowledge of APQP, CFM and PPAP processes
  • Practical knowledge of cutting tools, machining processes, materials & equipment Basic to Intermediate proficiency in all applicable software applications (i.e. Microsoft Office including Project, AutoCAD, SolidWorks)

What Linamar Has to Offer

  • Competitive Compensation
  • Employee Benefits package includes but not limited to, Drug, Dental & Vision etc.
  • Opportunities for career advancement.
  • Sustainability Council
  • Community based outreach supporting both local and global initiatives and charities. Discounts for local vendors and events, including auto supplier discounts.

About Us

Linamar Corporation is a Canadian-founded global manufacturer, renowned for its advanced engineering and innovative product development across diverse industries and markets. Our journey started in 1966 under the visionary leadership of our founder, and today, we remain committed to cultivating a culture of innovation and collaboration.

With access to state-of-the-art tools and resources, you'll have the opportunity to make a meaningful impact alongside a team of driven and passionate professionals. Join us and be part of a company where innovation, collaboration, and growth are at the heart of everything we do.
