*Group Summary:* Cosma provides a comprehensive range of body, chassis and engineering solutions to global customers. Through our robust product engineering, outstanding tooling capabilities and diverse process expertise, we continue to bring lightweight and innovative products to market.

*Job Responsibilities: Job Requirements:*

  • Post-secondary education in Information Systems (IS)
  • Technical/user knowledge of Windows 10/11, MS Office 365, Network Printing, File Access, and Client/Server applications
  • Excellent written and verbal English communication skills
  • Trained to operate the AWP (sky lift), must be comfortable working in heights
  • Good organizational, interpersonal, and problem-solving skills
  • Solid Knowledge and understanding of Active Directory
  • Demonstrated ability to work in a team environment
  • Ability to uphold confidentiality
  • Ability to work overtime to meet customer requirements

*Job Responsibilities:*

  • Administers user accounts
  • Maintains file and folder security
  • Diagnoses and trouble-shoots desktop software, hardware, or network connectivity problems at an entry level
  • Takes technical direction from senior staff
  • Handles the evaluation, procurement, installation and provides ongoing support of PCs, printers and peripherals
  • Evaluates and installs software, and trouble-shoots hardware problems
  • Writes technical documentation and manuals, and assists with end user training
  • Maintains hardware inventory control, asset tagging and software licensing.
  • Assists new computer users with Windows basic training on programs such as Microsoft Outlook, Word and Excel, network file storage, etc.
  • Performs MAC (Move/Adds/Changes) on telephone system
  • Enforces Sarbanes Oxley IT Standards\Best Practices
  • Maintain helpdesk metrics (Key Performance Indicators [KPIs])
  • Follows and complies with all Operational Procedures and Policies as outlined in the Employee Handbook, Quality and Environment programs (i.e., IATF16949, ISO14001)
  • Understands, promote and live by Magna’s philosophies and Employee Charter
  • Adheres to Modatek’s core values; integrity, respect, trust, honesty, fairness and loyalty
  • Follows Magna’s Code of Conduct and Ethics and related compliance policies.
  • Performs all duties in an environmentally responsible manner in accordance with the Modatek Systems Environmental Policy
  • Performs other duties as required

*Worker Health And Safety Responsibilities:*

  • Maintains safety standards of equipment and work area and perform all duties in a safe manner in accordance with the safety policies of Modatek Systems
  • Performs all duties in a safe manner in accordance with Section 28 of the Occupational Health & Safety Act and Regulation 851 for Industrial Establishments

*WHAT YOU CAN EXPECT FROM US:*

  • A great work experience
  • An environment that focuses on Modatek’s Core Values; Integrity, Respect, Trust, Honesty, Fairness and Loyalty

*Work And Physical Environment:*

  • Office and manufacturing environment, but may be exposed to elements such as temperature and noise and sitting for extended periods of time
  • Dexterity of hands and fingers to operate a computer keyboard, mouse, and to handle other computer components
  • Works rotating shifts or static shifts dependent on business requirements
  • On-call availability and occasional evening and weekend work to meet deadlines
  • Physically able to perform the essential duties of the position
  • Lifting and transporting of moderately heavy objects, such as servers, computers and peripherals.
  • Able to participate in training sessions, presentations, and meetings
  • Some travel may be required for the purpose of off-site installations and configuration
  • Required to wear all personal protective equipment for job/area
  • May be exposed to climbing and heights
