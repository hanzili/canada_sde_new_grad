Full Description

Venture outside the ordinary - TMX Careers The TMX group of companies includes leading global exchanges such as the Toronto Stock Exchange, Montreal Exchange, and numerous innovative organizations enhancing capital markets. United as a global team, we’re connecting cross-functionally, traversing industries and geographies, moving opportunity into action, advancing global economic growth, and propelling progress. Through a rich exchange of ideas, meaningful collaboration, and a nimble operating model, we're powering some of the nation's most critical systems, fueling capital formation and innovation, bringing increased opportunity to business visionaries, product ingenuity to consumers, and career exploration to our team.

Ready to be part of the action? You will assist the DevOps team in day-to-day tasks as outlined in the Job Description below. In addition, you will be assigned a special project to complete during the internship. The special project will likely involve heavy use of Python and Cloud Infrastructure. You'll also get a chance to work with the development teams working on these services, and learn the technologies behind these services as well.

This role is hybrid (2-3 days/week in the office) - based in Montreal, QC. Key Accountabilities

  • Work as part of a DevOps team and collaborate with developers
  • Learn our existing tools and offerings
  • Gain understanding of the problems we are trying to solve
  • Play a meaningful part in the build and release processes of our current solutions portfolio

Must Haves

  • Passion for technology and a desire to automate everything
  • Ability to work well on your own and in teams
  • Knowledge of and the ability to learn various development and scripting languages, specifically Python, as well as workflow automation
  • Passion for solving complex problems; ability to create or identify solutions that are well-tested and maintainable
  • Knowledge of the UNIX or Linux | Windows operating system

Nice To Haves

  • Ability to graphically represent concepts and architectures in documentation
  • Experience working in agile software development teams
  • Any experience with Cloud hosting providers (Google, Amazon, or Azure)
  • Kubernetes / Docker Knowledge

Notes

  • 4 months internship : May 4, 2026 - August 28, 2026
  • In order to be considered for the position, you are required to submit your Knockri Assessement
