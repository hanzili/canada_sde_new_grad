That means no hidden fees, no predatory interest rates - just financial products designed to help our users spend smart, save more, and build real wealth. We’re a performance organization with a strong heart: we care deeply about outcomes, and everything ties back to our mission - to financially empower a generation of Canadians.

About The Role We are seeking a motivated and detail-oriented Junior Data Engineer to join our Data Platform team for a remote role based in Canada.

The successful candidate will support the development and maintenance of data pipelines, assist with data integration tasks, and help ensure data quality across our fintech platform. This role provides an excellent opportunity to learn from experienced data engineers while working with modern cloud technologies and contributing to real-world data solutions. We value curiosity and eagerness to learn, so if you're passionate about data engineering, we'd love to hear from you.

Reporting to the Data Platform Development Manager, you will be part of a team that maintains and expands KOHO's data engineering platform.

What You'll Be Doing

  • Assisting in the development and maintenance of batch data pipelines using AWS Glue, Lambda, and Python
  • Writing and optimizing SQL queries for data transformation in Amazon Redshift
  • Supporting data quality checks and monitoring data pipeline health
  • Collaborating with Data Analysts and Data Scientists to understand data requirements
  • Documenting data pipelines, workflows, and technical processes
  • Learning and implementing best practices for code quality and data engineering standards
  • Participating in code reviews and team knowledge-sharing sessions
  • Troubleshooting data issues and assisting with incident resolution under guidance

Who You Are

  • Someone who has 0-2 years of professional experience working with data, or relevant internship/co-op experience, as well as:

  • Basic proficiency in Python and SQL

  • Familiarity with at least one AWS service (S3, Glue, Lambda, or Redshift)
  • Understanding of relational databases and data modelling concepts
  • Exposure to version control systems (Git)
  • Basic understanding of data pipeline concepts (ETL/ELT)
  • Ability to write basic scripts (Python or Bash)
  • Nice to have: Exposure to Apache Spark, Airflow, or Terraform
  • Nice to have: AWS Certified Cloud Practitioner or working towards AWS Certified Data Engineer - Associate
  • Nice to have: Familiarity with AI-powered development tools (GitHub Copilot, Claude, Cursor, etc.) to improve coding productivity or troubleshooting

  • Nice to have: Experience with financial data or understanding of fintech concepts

  • Someone who is curious, eager to learn, and passionate about building a career in data engineering

  • A strong communicator who asks thoughtful questions and seeks feedback
  • Detail-oriented with strong problem-solving skills
  • Comfortable working in a collaborative, fast-paced environment
