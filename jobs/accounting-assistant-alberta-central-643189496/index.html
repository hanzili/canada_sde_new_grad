Accounting Assistant

13 Month Full Time Co-op Position

Starting August 2026

The Opportunity

Reporting to the Assistant Controller, this position is responsible for providing support to the accounting department. This role typically performs entry level accounting functions, assists with maintaining the general ledger, month-end preparation, assistance in preparing financial statements and reconciliations, variance analysis and budget work for Alberta Central and two joint venture organizations. This position works under the supervision of CPA designated accountants.

What You’ll Be Doing

  • Assist in the preparation and production of month end financial reports and analysis.
  • Prepare and post journal entries.
  • Assist with external and internal audits.
  • Assist in the preparation of year-end financial statements.
  • Participate in special projects as required.

How You’ll Succeed

  • Minimum 3 years university completed in Business Administration or Commerce specializing in Accounting.
  • Highly organized and detail-oriented with the ability to set priorities and effectively manage multiple tasks.
  • Ability to work independently and in a team environment.
  • Strong analytical skills with proven quantitative problem-solving and decision-making skills.
  • Possess good communication and organizational skills.
  • Proficiency Level of Microsoft Office Suite: Word - intermediate, Excel - intermediate, PowerPoint - intermediate and Outlook – intermediate.
  • Must be bondable.

About Alberta Central

Alberta Central is the central banking facility for Alberta’s credit unions. We are owned by 12 credit unions across Alberta that together have more than $35 billion in system assets and serve more than 700,000 members at 190 branches across Alberta.

At Alberta Central, credit unions come first in everything we do. We work to connect credit unions to the products, services, knowledge and partnerships they need to be successful and to grow.

Alberta Central is focused on ensuring the long-term success of all Alberta credit unions and positioning ourselves as leaders in the future of the credit union system, and we want you to join us on this exciting journey.
