Power Platform & AI Development Co-op Student

at Compass Group

📍 Location Mississauga, ON Hybrid
📋 Details Co-op · 4mo · $22–$30/hr
📅 Posted Apr 28, 2026

About the Role

Build Power Apps, AI-driven in-app features

About Compass Group

Global food services leader embracing enterprise tech

Full Description

Compass Technology Canada is hiring a Co-op Student to support the development of internal business applications using the Microsoft Power Platform and AI technologies.

You will help design and build an AI-driven help agent embedded within applications, enhance existing Power Apps, and contribute to interactive Power BI dashboards used across the organization. This role offers hands-on experience with real enterprise solutions and modern low-code and AI tools.

Responsibilities

  • Build and enhance Power Apps (Canvas Apps) for internal users

  • Research and develop an AI-based in-app help agent

  • Create Power BI dashboards and reports

  • Work with technical and business stakeholders

  • Test, document, and iterate on solutions

Required Qualifications

  • Enrolled in Computer Science, Software Engineering, IT, or related program

  • Interest in Power Apps, Power Platform, or application development

  • Strong problem-solving and communication skills

  • Ability to learn new technologies quickly

Assets

  • Knowledge of Power BI, Power Automate, SharePoint, Dataverse, or SQL

  • Interest in AI, copilots, or intelligent assistants

Salary & Hours

  • Hourly Rate: $22 – $30 per hour (based on experience and academic level)

  • Hours: Full-time, approximately 37.5 hours per week

Work Environment

Hybrid / Remote: 3 days in office (Mississauga)
