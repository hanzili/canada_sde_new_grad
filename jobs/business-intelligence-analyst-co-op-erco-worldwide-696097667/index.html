About ERCO Worldwide

Full Description

ERCO Worldwide has operated for over 100 years, focused largely on the production and supply of in-organic products along with technology for the production of chlorine dioxide. Today, ERCO’s major business areas include sodium chlorate, chlor-alkali products, sodium chlorite, and chlorine dioxide technology.

The ERCO business serves a number of key customer segments including pulp and paper, food, energy, agriculture, water treatment, airport de-icing, fertilizers and specialty chemicals.

Business Intelligence Analyst Co-Op Employment Type: Temporary, Contract (September - April )

Location: 101 Chemin Donaldson Gatineau, Quebec J8L 3X3

Get To Know Us! ERCO Worldwide’s century-long tradition of excellence has firmly established its reputation for providing reliable, intelligent, and environmentally responsible manufacturing on a global scale. As one of the largest suppliers of Chlorine Dioxide technology in the world, we are devoted to building genuine customer relationships and delivering innovative, sustainable chemical solutions that improve lives and the health of our planet. Grounded in our commitment to sustainability, we serve multiple industries such as pulp & paper, oil & gas, agriculture, food processing, water treatment and more! It’s our goal to make everyone’s lives more productive, convenient, enjoyable, and safe.

Our Vision: To be a leader in delivering innovative and sustainable chemical solutions that improve lives and the health of our planet.

Our Mission: We endeavour to provide unmatched value and expertise through sustainable, intelligent, and reliable products and solutions, creating genuine customer relationships.

Our Values: Consistent and Dependable: Customers can expect excellence from ERCO. We deliver reliably , operate safely, and care about our customers’ success .

Knowledgeable and Innovative: ERCO helps evolve and improve our industry through thought leadership, manufacturing and product innovation , and proven expertise .

Proactive: ERCO makes decisions that ensure sustainable health for our employees, customers, and environment.

To learn more click here . Join The Team: ERCO Worldwide is looking for a Business Intelligence Analyst Co-op to join our team! During your term, you will actively contribute to the activities of the IT Business Intelligence team, supporting with transforming data into insights by designing and maintaining Power BI dashboards and reports, ensuring data quality, and supporting both daily operations and special projects. Additional tasks and special assignments may be provided as needed to further support the team’s objectives.

The Role:

  • Collect, clean, and analyze data from multiple sources to generate actionable reports using Power BI.
  • Design, build, and maintain interactive dashboards and visualizations to present data in an accessible manner.
  • Collaborate with business users and stakeholders to identify analytical needs and translate them into Power BI solutions.
  • Perform regular data validation to ensure data accuracy, consistency, and integrity.
  • Develop and maintain comprehensive documentation for reports, dashboards, data models, and workflows.
  • Support users with daily BI reporting needs

The Person:

  • Pursuing a Bachelor’s degree in Business Administration, Information Technology, Computer Science, Statistics, Mathematics, or a related field
  • Bilingual in French & English
  • Experience with Power BI, including designing, developing, and optimizing dashboards and reports is preferred
  • Additional BI tools such as Firetran, Databricks, and Business Objects is an asset
  • Proficient experience with MS Office suite, specifically MS Excel
  • Demonstrates a strong analytical mindset and attention to detail
  • Ability to leverage Power BI and other analytical tools to interpret complex data, transforming it into actionable insights that empower stakeholders to recognize trends, enhance operational efficiency
  • Showcases strategic decision-making skills
  • Proactive – takes initiative, creative, stays ahead of the curve, enthusiastic, inspirational
  • Excellent analytical, communication and problem-solving skills
  • Strong relationship builder – collaborative, team-builder
  • Assist Infrastructure team with specific tasks requiring physical access to systems/equipment

The Value You Bring:

  • Strong health and safety mindset
  • Strong relationship builder – collaborative, team-builder
  • Focused and results driven, transparent, and articulate
  • Drives continuous improvement
  • Resilient – can adapt to changing environments/technology
  • Proactive – takes initiative, creative, stays ahead of the curve, enthusiastic, inspirational
