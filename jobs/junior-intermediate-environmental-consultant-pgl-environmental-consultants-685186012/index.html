Full Description

Advancing People, Environment and Business! Does that statement resonate with you? Do you enjoy working with an engaged team towards a common goal? Do you regularly question the norm and come up with creative solutions to problems? If you’ve answered yes to any of these questions, we might have the perfect role for you!

PGL Environmental Consultants’ is seeking an Junior / Intermediate Level Environmental Consultant

to join our team. This role is

based out of Whitby, ON

. PGL employees work a 37.5-hour work week, and all employees have the option to work from home or either office as and when needed.  

Responsibilities:

  • Soil, groundwater, surface water, and soil vapour sampling;
  • Drilling investigations (Phase II ESAs);
  • Environmental monitoring, including water quality sampling, construction supervision, and liaising with site personnel, clients, and regulatory agencies;
  • Site remediation;
  • Budgeting, reporting;
  • Reviewing policies and systems; and
  • Contributing to PGL’s corporate initiatives.

Comfort working on active construction sites is necessary as you will be expected to supervise remediation and drilling contractors. Hands-on experience in the field will progress towards project management, directing others in the field, and preparing reports. As your career develops, report writing, and client contact will become an important component of the position. Ideally, we are seeking candidates with strong people skills and the ability to work in a team environment with a strong foundation of consulting skills.

Job Requirements:

  • Undergraduate or Graduate Degree in Engineering (chemical, geological, geotechnical, environmental), Geology, Soil Science, Environmental Science, related engineering discipline or Toxicology.
  • 2 - 5 years of consulting experience with exposure to contaminated site assessments .
  • Ability to register as a professional (in-training) in the Province of ON is an asset, such as P.Eng, P.Geo, or equivalent.
  • Comfortable working on job sites (including but not limited to construction sites, gas stations, around traffic and heavy equipment).
  • Experience with soil and groundwater testing and hydrogeology is an asset but not a requirement.
  • Experience in contaminated site investigations including Phase I and Phase II Environmental Site Assessments, remediation, risk assessments, and fieldwork appropriate to the level of experience.
  • Proficient with computers and Microsoft Office suite products. Knowledge of EsDat is an asset but not a requirement.
  • Excellent internal and external client service skills.
  • Strong problem-solving and decision-making skills.
  • Strong mentoring and coaching abilities and team spirit attitude.
  • Valid Driver’s license required.
  • Ability to travel and work in potentially remote locations, generally in the province of ON.
  • Ability to work hours based on project requirements.
  • Ability to lift and carry equipment and materials weighing up to 25 lbs. for short durations.

PGL Perks:

  • 100% Employee-owned organization;
  • Employee and Owner Profit Share Program;
  • Flexible work arrangements and daily work hours;
  • Annual Company Retreat in British Columbia;
  • Allowances: Cell phone, Wellness, and Costco memberships;
  • Annual training and development budget;
  • Maternity and Parental leave top-up program;
  • Salary range $63,000 – $90,000;
  • 3 weeks’ vacation and paid sick time; and;
  • Competitive benefits: Extended Health, Dental, STD, LTD, and Life insurance.

Visit our website at www.pggroup.com/join_team.htm to apply online. We will be reviewing resumes and successful candidates will be contacted via email .

About PGL

At PGL, we are committed to fostering a sense of belonging for every member of our team and promoting equity, diversity, and inclusion. We recognize that our strength lies in embracing a diverse range of perspectives and backgrounds. We aim to provide equal growth opportunities for all employees and celebrate differences in race, religion, age, gender, sexual orientation, and physical and mental abilities.

We are a full-service environmental consulting firm with offices in Vancouver, Victoria, and Langley (BC), Whitby (ON), and Calgary (AB). Since 1991, PGL has provided environmental services such as contaminated site assessments, remediation, environmental impact assessments, environmental permitting, and sustainability assessments. PGL is an employee-owned company with 110+ employees, including biologists, environmental scientists and engineers, geologists, hydrogeologists, agrologists, and a dynamic and customer-oriented support team.
