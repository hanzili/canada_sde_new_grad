About the Role

Designs and coordinates structural projects; drafts on-site

About SHELLEX GROUPE CONSEIL

Regional civil/structural consulting firm; growing practice

Full Description

Shellex Groupe Conseil is currently seeking a Junior Structural Engineer to join its team at the Toronto office .

As a Structural Engineer within our engaged and collaborative team, you will contribute to large-scale projects, benefit from enriching professional development opportunities, and work in a stimulating environment with competitive benefits.

Responsibilities

The Structural Engineer will be responsible for:
Performing technical design and project management activities;

Supporting the team of technicians/drafters in the execution of various projects (design and construction);

Coordinating projects and communicating with external stakeholders;

Coordinating projects with internal engineering disciplines (process, building mechanics, civil, electrical);

Ensuring compliance with fee budgets and delivery schedules;

Ensuring the quality of deliverables in accordance with firm standards, applicable codes, and regulations;

Identifying client needs and proposing innovative solutions;

Monitoring construction work, both from the office and on-site.

Required Qualifications

Bachelor’s degree in Civil / Structural Engineering from an accredited university, with focus on reinforced concrete, structural steel, masonry, dynamic analysis, and finite element analysis. Master’s degree preferred;

Eligible to work in Canada without further authorization;

Currently working towards Professional Engineering designation (P.Eng.) in Ontario;

Experience in utilizing codes and standards – NBCC, OBC, A23.3, S16, etc.;

Experience in structural analysis software – SAFI, STAAD.Pro, etc.;

Experience in calculation software – MathCAD, Excel, etc.;

Experience in drafting and modelling software – AutoCAD, Revit, etc.;

Ability to work independently and efficiently;

Ability to communicate ideas clearly and effectively;

Open and driven to learn and develop;

Maintains a valid driver’s license.

Working Conditions

Annual salary between $85,000 and $100,000 , based on experience (salary scale corresponding to a junior engineer with approximately 1 to 3 years of experience);

Flexible work schedule between 37.5 and 40 hours per week ;

Vacation policy of 3 to 5 weeks per year, depending on experience.

Two-week closure during the holiday season to fully enjoy this period;

Comprehensive group benefits (full insurance coverage, telemedicine, RRSP, 3 sick days, mileage reimbursement for business travel, and more—providing peace of mind);

Numerous team activities fostering a friendly and dynamic work atmosphere;

Opportunities for professional development and training based on the candidate’s aspirations.
