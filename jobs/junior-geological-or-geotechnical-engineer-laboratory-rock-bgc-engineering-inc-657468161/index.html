Full Description

About Us BGC Engineering Inc. (BGC) is an international applied earth sciences professional services firm. We are proud of our talented team of over 800 employees, spanning the fields of Engineering, Geoscience, Software, Geomatics, Data Science, Accounting, Administration, Health & Safety and Human Resources.  Alongside our clients and industry partners, BGC works collaboratively as ‘One Team’, dedicated to solving the world’s most critical applied earth science challenges. BGC has offices located across Canada, the United States, Chile, the Dominican Republic, and Australia.

Our flat management structure creates a culture of innovation, excellence and transparency and a casual, dynamic and inclusive work environment. BGC prides itself on offering training and mentoring opportunities to further our employees’ professional growth as well as social and wellness activities that contribute to the connection and wellbeing of our team.

We believe in One Team, Excellence, Curiosity, Common Sense and Clarity.

The Role

BGC is seeking a Junior Geological or Geotechnical Engineer with a strong interest in rock mechanics and laboratory rock testing to join our advanced soil and rock testing facility in Fredericton, New Brunswick (BGC Engineering | Fredericton, NB (matterport.com)).

BGC’s laboratory is a state-of-the-art facility which specializes in advanced testing of soil strength, compressibility and permeability alongside rock strength and deformation testing. Our rock testing equipment includes a servo-controlled fully automated uniaxial/triaxial load frame and automated direct shear system. The Junior Engineer will work closely with intermediate and senior engineers to conduct advanced rock testing, assist with test planning and execution, interpret results, and develop a strong foundation in applied rock mechanics and geotechnical laboratory practices.

Responsibilities

  • Perform advanced geotechnical tests on rock specimens to determine their physical and mechanical properties
  • Collect, analyze, and interpret data from rock tests to support engineering projects and research.
  • Work closely with senior and intermediate engineers to support laboratory testing programs with varying deadlines and responsibilities
  • Prepare laboratory test reports
  • Follow lab procedures for safety and quality
  • Train and mentor laboratory staff and laboratory visitors

Required Education, Experience & Competencies

  • Bachelor’s degree in Civil, Geological, or Geotechnical Engineering; coursework in rock mechanics is preferred
  • 0–5 years of relevant industry experience
  • Eligible for professional registration as an EIT or PEng
  • Desire to work in a lab and learn advanced rock testing
  • Strong written and verbal communication skills

Travel and Vaccination BGC is committed to maintaining a safe work environment to ensure the health and safety of employees while on business travel and during field assignments. To meet travel and client requirements, employees may be required to receive vaccinations such as yellow fever, COVID-19, malaria, etc. If you require an accommodation or wish to discuss your situation, please contact Human Resources.
