About the Role

We are hiring two AI Engineer Interns

to join a high-impact initiative focused on building multiple AI agents and chatbots

on Google Cloud Platform (GCP)

using Vertex AI

.

You will work on real-world, enterprise-grade AI systems that will be used across large-scale production environments.

This is a hands-on role for recent Canadian graduates

who want to build production AI systems

, not just academic projects.

What You Will Do

  • Build and customize AI agents and chatbots

using Google Vertex AI - Develop prompt flows, embeddings, and conversational workflows - Integrate AI agents with APIs, cloud services, and enterprise applications - Work with senior engineers to deploy and optimize models on GCP - Perform testing, tuning, and monitoring of AI agent performance

Required Qualifications

  • Bachelor’s or Master’s degree in Computer Science, AI, Data Science, Engineering, or related field
  • 0–2 years of experience

or fresh graduate - Exposure to Google Cloud Platform (GCP) - Familiarity with: - Vertex AI - Python - LLMs, chatbots, or AI agents - Strong interest in Generative AI

and applied machine learning - Legally eligible to work in Canada

Nice to Have

  • Experience with chatbots, LangChain, RAG, or AI workflows
  • Knowledge of cloud APIs, REST services
  • Internship or project experience in AI/ML

Why Join Us

  • Work on 9 enterprise AI agent projects
  • Hands-on experience with Google’s Vertex AI
  • Exposure to real client production systems
