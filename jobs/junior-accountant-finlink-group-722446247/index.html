Full Description

We’re Hiring: Junior Accountant

We’re partnering with a well-established accounting firm in Mississauga that’s looking to add a Junior Accountant (CPA designated) to their team.

This is a great opportunity for someone early in their career who wants to build strong technical accounting skills, gain hands-on experience across a variety of clients, and grow within a supportive public practice environment.

What you’ll be doing:

  • Assisting with day-to-day accounting and month-end processes
  • Supporting the preparation of financial statements and working papers
  • Assisting with tax compliance and client file management
  • Working closely with senior accountants and managers on client deliverables
  • Gaining exposure to a range of industries and accounting functions

What we’re looking for:

  • CPA designation (required)
  • 1–3 years of accounting experience (public practice preferred)
  • Strong attention to detail and willingness to learn
  • Good communication and team collaboration skills
  • Able to work full-time in-office in Mississauga

Preference will be given to candidates who are immediately available.

If you’re looking to grow your career in a professional firm environment where you can develop your skills and gain meaningful experience, we’d love to hear from you.

If you or someone in your network would be interested in learning more, feel free to reach out directly or apply today. We’d love to connect!
