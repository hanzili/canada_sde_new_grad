Civil / Structural

Junior Civil Engineer

at Philrobben Group

📍 Location Paradise, NL A1L 0A7
📋 Details Full-time · Permanent
📅 Posted Mar 28, 2026

About the Role

Assist civil designs, draft drawings, inspect sites

About Philrobben Group

Residential subdivision focus; strong CAD and fieldwork exposure

Full Description

Overview
We are seeking a motivated Junior Civil Engineer to join our dynamic engineering team. This entry-level position offers an excellent opportunity to develop foundational skills in civil engineering projects, including infrastructure design, construction management, and technical drafting. The ideal candidate will have a strong interest in civil engineering principles for residential subdivision. Water and sewer, blueprints and interpret grading plans. Candidates should be familiar with industry-standard software and tools, and possess a solid understanding of construction processes and project management fundamentals.

Duties

  • Assist in the development of civil engineering designs using AutoCAD, Civil 3D, Revit, MicroStation, and Schematics.
  • Support project management activities including scheduling, resource allocation, and documentation.
  • Conduct site inspections and construction observation to ensure compliance with design specifications and safety standards.
  • Prepare detailed drawings, schematics, and system designs utilizing CAD, CAD-based software, and drafting tools.
  • Utilize GIS tools such as ArcGIS and GIS software like MicroStation or System Design platforms for spatial analysis and mapping.
  • Perform construction estimating and assist in project cost analysis.
  • Apply technical knowledge of HEC protocols, Mathcad calculations, and construction inspection procedures.
  • Collaborate with senior engineers on project planning, design development, and schematic reviews.
  • Support the use of Bluebeam for document review and markup processes.
  • Assist in the integration of mechanical engineering principles where applicable to civil projects.

Requirements

  • Bachelor’s degree in Civil Engineering or related field.
  • Familiarity with AutoCAD, Civil 3D, Revit, MicroStation, ArcGIS, HEC software, Mathcad, and Schematics is highly desirable.
  • Knowledge of construction estimating, project management practices, system design, drafting techniques, and construction inspection processes.
  • Experience with CAD systems and GIS tools such as ArcGIS or MicroStation preferred.
  • Strong mathematical skills with an understanding of civil engineering calculations.
  • Excellent communication skills and ability to work collaboratively within a team environment.
  • Basic knowledge of mechanical engineering concepts relevant to civil projects is a plus.
  • Ability to prioritize tasks effectively while managing multiple responsibilities. This position provides valuable hands-on experience in civil engineering projects across various sectors including infrastructure development, transportation systems, and construction management—ideal for candidates eager to build their careers in civil engineering through practical application of their technical skills.

Job Types: Full-time, Permanent

Benefits:

  • Dental care

Work Location: On the road
