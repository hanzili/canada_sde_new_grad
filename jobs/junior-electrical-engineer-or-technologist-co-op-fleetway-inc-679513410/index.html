About Fleetway Inc.

Full Description

Job Description Are you an Electrical Engineer or Technologist looking for an opportunity in the marine industry? Join Fleetway Inc.’s engineering team and be a part of Canada’s National Shipbuilding Strategy and the Halifax Class Design Agent contracts. Through these contracts Fleetway’s engineers and technologists provide a full range of technical data management and engineering support services to the Royal Canadian Navy. Be part of a team that produces complex designs to support new structures and systems, that will ensure the Royal Canadian Navy is “ready to help, ready to lead and ready to fight”.

If we sound like a team you’d like to be a part of, and make a difference with, then we’d love to hear from you! We are looking for a qualified Jr. Electrical Engineering/Technology student for our Ottawa, ON office.

Responsibilities During your internship, you can expect to be part of an outstanding group of Naval Architects, Mechanical, and Electrical engineers and technologists.

We tailor your work experience to your skills and abilities. If you choose to work on one specific project and hone your skills in a particular area, we can make that happen. If you prefer broader project exposure across a variety of client projects, we can make that happen too.

Supported by your peers, you have exposure to a wide variety of Government, Military, Commercial, Oil and Gas and Industrial based project work.

You can expect as a co-op student/intern to gain valuable work experience and broad project exposure by:

  • Assist with technical studies, single‑line diagrams, schematics, specifications, and 3D models
  • Support preparation of Technical Reports, ECIDs, ECIPs, and engineering change proposals
  • Perform engineering analysis, including electrical calculations (load, voltage drop, short‑circuit), cable sizing, and basic finite element analysis
  • Assist with equipment selection and systems integration activities
  • Develop 3D models of ship equipment and compartment arrangements
  • Prepare cable run sheets and cable pull sheets
  • Perform quality control reviews of drawings and technical deliverables
  • Support naval electrical system design under senior engineer guidance

Qualifications

  • Enrolment in Electrical Engineer Bachelor's Degree or Electrical Engineering Technology Diploma.
  • Familiar with AutoCAD
  • Interest in Rhino or other 3D modeling software
  • Proficient in MS Office Suite
  • Strong interpersonal and organizational skills
  • We encourage applicants to apply even if you do not have all of the above qualifications or experience requirements.

About The Team We are Fleetway ! We believe Canada’s maritime fleets need to be reliable and fit for purpose, with crews that are fully trained and supported. We work as a trusted technical partner during design, construction, in-service, and end of life. We are proud to deliver quality professional technical services including Engineering, Integrated Logistics Support, Technical Editing, Configuration Management, Project Management, Training, Technical Translation, and Marine Technical Services.

Over the past 40+ years, we have built an outstanding team of people and partners, invested in the tools, and earned an exemplary track record servicing our customers. Everything we do is aimed at developing our people and improving our use of innovation to be the leading professional services provider in the marine industry. We are proud to have earned national recognition as a Nova Scotia and Atlantic Canada’s Top Employer.

All successful applicants must meet requirements for Canadian Controlled Goods Program (CGP), and Canadian Government Security Clearance.
