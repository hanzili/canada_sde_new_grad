About Ryzuk Geotechnical

Full Description

We are looking for a Junior Geoscientist/Geotechnical Engineer to join our Victoria Ryzuk team!

Ryzuk Geotechnical and Materials Testing is an experienced, medium sized organization, currently located in Victoria, BC. Our mission is providing creative, innovative, and practical geotechnical services with the best team possible. We began serving Greater Victoria in 1987, forging us to have an incredibly loyal client base. We are ever expanding, with locations in Victoria, Sunshine Coast, Comox Valley

We focus on:

  • Mentorship
  • Career growth
  • Team building
  • Diversity
  • Excellence
  • Communication
  • Corporate culture

As a Junior Geoscientist/Geotechnical Engineer, you will be working on a wide variety of projects with the support and mentorship of your senior engineers and peers. Ryzuk believes in helping you achieve your career goals, and offers the opportunity for continued education, association membership meetings and luncheons, and in-house seminars/training.

If you are:

  • Looking for a fast-paced environment
  • Team orientated
  • Driven
  • Enthusiastic
  • Love learning
  • Seeking diverse experiences
  • Considering/currently living in Victoria

Then we would love to hear from you!

Benefits:

  • Industry leading salary
  • Paid time off
  • Comprehensive benefit plan, including critical illness
  • Banked overtime
  • Company vehicle
  • Company phone
  • RRSP Matching Program

Qualifications:

  • Minimum of a bachelor’s degree in civil/geotechnical engineering or geoscience
  • 2 years of post-education experience in engineering or geoscience
  • Eligible to be registered as an Engineer in Training (EIT) or Geoscientist in Training (GIT) with EGBC
  • Able to obtain a criminal record check during employment for possible high level security projects
  • BC drivers license

You will be working on a team of 65 amazing co-workers and some of the best geotechnical engineers in British Columbia!

Some of the things you will be working alongside on:

  • Geotechnical Assessments and Reporting
  • Shallow and Deep Foundation Engineering
  • Slope Stability Assessments
  • Earth Retention Structures
  • Braced Excavations and Anchored Wall Support Systems
  • Ground Improvement and Settlement Analysis
  • Micro-Tremor Surveys and Earthquake Engineering
  • Soil Instrumentation and Monitoring
  • Shoreline revetments
  • Rockfall Hazard Assessment and Protection Measures
  • Materials Engineering and Testing

We use cutting edge technologies such as TIP, Plaxis, and PDA. Giving you the opportunity to learn new geotechnical modernizations with us. The sky is the limit for us, or rather the foundation is the limit!

Thank you for your consideration regarding this position, we will only be reaching out to those applicants who are shortlisted.
