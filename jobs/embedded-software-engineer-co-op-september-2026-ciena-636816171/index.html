About the Role

Develop embedded real-time software and test features

About Ciena

Ottawa-based networking hardware leader

Full Description

As the global leader in high-speed connectivity, Ciena is committed to a people-first approach. Our teams enjoy a culture focused on prioritizing a flexible work environment that empowers individual growth, well-being, and belonging. We’re a technology company that leads with our humanity—driving our business priorities alongside meaningful social, community, and societal impact.

How You Will Contribute Our team design and implement real-time embedded software for Ciena’s flagship product – optical coherent modem which is to be integrated into multiple internal and external platforms. Functional areas range from register level bit drivers to high level software state machines for interfacing with external applications:

  • Drivers - to configure and maintain in-house and 3rd party ASICs, FPGAs and other hardware components.
  • HSI (hardware/software interface) – to provide bridging of various abstraction layers.
  • Fault and alarms subsystems - to detect, report and maintain defects and manage their hysteresis and co-relations.
  • System application such as traffic/equipment protection, performance monitoring, etc.
  • System provisioning / configuration in conformance of telecom standarda such as OTN, Ethernet, etc.

The Opportunity 4, 8, or 12 Month Term: May 2026 - April 2027

Key Responsibilities

  • Development of embedded, real-time software for telecommunications systems
  • Coding and testing of software
  • Support of testing activities in the release where feature is delivered
  • Working closely with Senior Designers who will provide mentoring and coaching
  • Collaborating with other design teams and support organizations

Areas of impact

  • Problem Solving and analysis
  • Contributing to your team
  • Innovation
  • Communication
  • Scripting and Embedded C programming

Educational And Experience Requirements

  • 2nd year or greater in an Engineering or Computer Science Bachelor’s degree, preferably with prior co-op experience in related fields.

Assets One or more skills in the following areas would be an asset:

  • Embedded C, C++, Python
  • Real-time OS and embedded systems
  • Agile development methodologies
  • Messaging interfaces Netconf, REST/HTTP, gRPC
  • Design and development tools such as: Git, Eclipse, JIRA, Confluence, GDB, Yocto
  • YANG modeling language and toolchains
  • Linux development environment
  • Automation and Test
