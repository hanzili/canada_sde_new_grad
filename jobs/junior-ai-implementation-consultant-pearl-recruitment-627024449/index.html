Job Title: Junior AI Implementation Consultant

Job Type: Full Time

Workplace Type: Hybrid

Experience Level: Entry Level, Early Career

Location : Calgary, AB

Company Overview:

We are recruiting on behalf of our client, a well established EPCM consulting firm supporting organisations with advisory and implementation services across multiple industries. They are expanding into a dedicated AI Implementation Consulting practice focused on helping teams adopt, implement, and sustain AI solutions in a responsible, scalable, value driven way.

Job Overview:

The Junior AI Implementation Consultant will support AI and data driven consulting engagements through research, analysis, documentation, and project coordination. This is a strong entry point for someone building a career in AI consulting, technology implementation, and change enablement. Experience in the energy sector is an asset but not mandatory.

Key Responsibilities:

  • Support AI implementation and technology enablement projects through research, analysis, and clear documentation
  • Assist with requirements gathering, process mapping, and documenting workflows
  • Support data analysis and insights development using common business tools
  • Prepare reports, presentations, meeting notes, and other client ready materials
  • Research AI tools, platforms, and industry trends to support recommendations
  • Support senior consultants and managers across multiple client engagements
  • Learn and apply consulting methodologies and AI implementation frameworks

Core Competencies :

  • Strong analytical skills and attention to detail
  • Clear written and verbal communication
  • Comfortable working across multiple priorities in a consulting environment
  • Curious, adaptable, and motivated to learn new tools and concepts
  • Able to translate research and analysis into practical, well structured outputs
