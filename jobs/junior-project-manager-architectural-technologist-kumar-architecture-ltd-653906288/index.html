Junior Project Manager/Architectural Technologist

at Kumar Architecture ltd.

📍 Location West Vancouver, British Columbia Hybrid
📋 Details Full-time · Permanent · Not specified · Ongoing
📅 Posted Mar 30, 2026

About the Role

Collaborate to develop designs, draft docs, coordinate delivery

About Kumar Architecture ltd.

BC architecture firm with design-focused practice

Full Description

As a Junior Project Manager/Architectural Technologist , you will be integral to the design and execution of architectural projects, reporting directly to the lead architect. Your core skills in drafting and construction will be essential in creating detailed plans using Revit. You will leverage premium skills in project management and software like Bluebeam to ensure timely and accurate project delivery. With a focus on commercial construction, your expertise in time management and drafting will help streamline processes and enhance collaboration within the team, contributing to the overall success of our architectural endeavours.

Responsibilities

Collaborate with architects and engineers to develop detailed design drawings and specifications - Utilize Revit to create accurate and precise architectural drawings - Prepare construction documents, Conduct site visits, Assess project progress, Review and analyze blueprints and technical documents - Assist in the coordination of project activities with clients, contractors, and consultants - Ensure compliance with building codes, regulations - Provide technical support to other staff members Requirements: -. Proven experience as a Junior Project Manager/Architectural Technologist - Proficiency in Revit - Strong time management skills to meet project deadlines - Experience in commercial construction projects is preferred. As a Junior Project Manager/Architectural Technologist, you will have the opportunity to work on exciting projects and contribute to the success of our team.

Qualifications

  • The candidate should have Excellent verbal and written communication skills. You are a resident of British Columbia, Canada, with a minimum of 8 years  of Local architectural experience in the building construction industry, a degree in Architecture and/or interior design is required. A positive, friendly, professional attitude and a good sense of humour is must.
