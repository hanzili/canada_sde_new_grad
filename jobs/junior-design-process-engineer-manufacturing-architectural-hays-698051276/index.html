****** Calling Fresh graduates with co-op experience in Product Design ******

Our client is a leading manufacturer of custom-built architectural panels doors and windows and have a job open for Junior Design & Process Engineer in the Brampton- Mississauga, ON area . Candidates will work closly with design and process improvement teams in manufacturing engineering. This is on site 5 days a week.

Junior Design & Process Engineer will ideally have a degree in Mechanical Engineering and co-op experience in Product Design, establishing BOM and improving existing processes , using time studies in manufacturing and have a process improvement mindset. Excellent English communication skills are a MUST ; an ability to work independently / in teams is a MUST. Clear Criminal check is required.

Qualified applicants already Brampton- Mississauga, ON area willing to work 5 days on site may please send resumes as word DOC to Preeti.Dayal@hays.com . Please write “ Junior Design & Process Engineer” in the subject line of your e mail.

While we thank all for applying, only the most qualified candidates will be contacted due to high volume of applications
