About GenPark

Full Description

**Company Description**

GenPark leverages intelligent Brand Agents and User Agents to empower brands to effectively connect with global Gen Z consumers. Through personalized AI-generated content, interactive Agents, and immersive AI video experiences, GenPark enables targeted engagement, resulting in higher conversion rates and reduced customer acquisition costs. Functioning as more than just a marketing tool, GenPark offers an AI-native system where Agents continuously learn and adapt based on user behavior and feedback. This approach ensures enhanced matching between products, services, digital assets, and audiences in global markets.

**Role Description**

This full-time hybrid role as an AI Product Intern is based in Vancouver, BC, with flexibility for some remote work. Day-to-day responsibilities include supporting the development and improvement of AI-driven products, conducting data analysis to enhance product performance, and collaborating with various teams to gather and address user insights. The intern will also play a role in assisting customers with inquiries and ensuring that their experience with GenPark's solutions is seamless.
