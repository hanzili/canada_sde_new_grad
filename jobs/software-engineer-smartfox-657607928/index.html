Full Description

Job Title: Entry-Level Software Engineer

Employment Type: Full-time

Location: Remote (Canada)

Overview We are seeking a motivated and curious software engineer to join our team. This role is ideal for someone early in their career who is eager to learn, values thoughtful engineering, and wants to contribute to meaningful products. You will work closely with experienced engineers, gain hands-on experience across the stack, and grow through mentorship and structured feedback.

Responsibilities

  • Develop, test, and deliver features across both front-end and back-end systems with guidance from senior engineers
  • Participate in code reviews by incorporating feedback and providing constructive input to others
  • Investigate and resolve production issues, contributing to post-incident analysis to improve system reliability
  • Write clear, maintainable, and well-documented code with attention to performance and scalability
  • Collaborate with product managers and designers to translate requirements into effective technical solutions
  • Contribute to internal tools, automation efforts, and improvements in developer experience
  • Engage in agile practices including sprint planning, stand-ups, and retrospectives
  • Learn and participate in the full software development lifecycle, from design through deployment and monitoring

Qualifications

  • Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science, Software Engineering, or a related field, or equivalent practical experience (such as bootcamps, self-study, or open-source contributions)
  • 0–2 years of experience in software development, including academic or personal projects
