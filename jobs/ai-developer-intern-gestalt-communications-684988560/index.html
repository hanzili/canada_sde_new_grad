About the Role

Develop AI tools for advocacy and research

About gestalt communications

Public-interest communications firm applying AI tools

Full Description

This role is suited for a student interested in artificial intelligence and technology for public interest work. The intern will support the development of AI-enabled tools that assist with advocacy, research, and strategic communications. The position provides hands-on exposure to practical applications of AI in real-world policy and communications environments.

Experience & Skills

  • 1+ Years Experience
  • Basic experience with AI tools, machine learning, or automation
  • Familiarity with Python or other programming languages
  • Interest in civic technology, policy, or advocacy applications
  • Ability to problem-solve and experiment with new tools
  • Strong attention to detail

To qualify, you must:

  • Be 15 to 30 years old on your first day of work.
  • Be a Canadian citizen, permanent resident, or official refugee.
  • Have the legal right to work in your province or territory.
  • Have a valid SIN (if your SIN starts with a "9", you must provide proof of eligible status).

You do NOT qualify if you are:

  • An international student or temporary visa holder (visitor, temporary worker, or pending refugee).

Interested candidates should submit a resume and a cover letter outlining their experience and qualifications to hr@gestaltcomms.com with the subject AI Developer Intern

Pay: $18.00 per hour

Work Location: In person
