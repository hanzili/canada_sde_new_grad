About the Role

About TribalScale

Toronto-based AI-driven digital transformation agency

Full Description

Shape the Future with TribalScale

At TribalScale, we're not just participating in digital transformation—we're driving it. As global leaders in innovation, we combine Agile methodologies and cutting-edge AI, including Large Language Models (LLMs), to create digital solutions that propel enterprises into the future. Based in Toronto with a hybrid workforce across the Americas, we're on an ambitious mission to "Right the Future." If you're a bold innovator ready to push the boundaries of what's possible, we invite you to join our team of digital pioneers.

Our Digital Expertise

  • Transformation Experts: We transform traditional business models into agile, AI-powered ecosystems.
  • Strategic Visionaries: We navigate the uncharted waters of technological evolution.
  • Product Virtuosos: We orchestrate the creation of world-class digital solutions.
  • Code Optimizers: We leverage AI to refine and perfect digital systems.

Our Technological Toolkit

  • Mobile & Web Development: (iOS, Android, React Native, React, Node.js)
  • Voice-Activated Platforms: (Amazon Alexa, Google Home)
  • Connected Ecosystems: (Cars and Homes)
  • Streaming Platforms: (Roku, Fire TV, Android TV, tvOS)

The TribalScale Professional

You thrive in challenging environments where innovation is key. Your passion for technology is matched only by your drive to redefine its limits. You seek more than a job; you seek a calling—a chance to make a lasting impact on the digital landscape.

Your Responsibilities

  • Design and execute test plans for web and backend applications
  • Work on real client products from your first day instead of internal training environments
  • Identify, document, and track bugs across applications and systems
  • Build and maintain automated test suites (UI, API, and integration testing)
  • Collaborate with engineers through pair testing and AI-assisted QA workflows
  • Use AI tools (Copilot, ChatGPT, etc.) to:
  • Generate test cases and edge scenarios
  • Improve test coverage and regression efficiency
  • Debug and analyze failures faster
  • Test AI-powered and agent-driven features, including:
  • LLM outputs (accuracy, consistency, edge cases)
  • Multi-step agent workflows and automations
  • Context-aware and intelligent user experiences
  • Validate systems where outputs are non-deterministic and require probabilistic thinking
  • Contribute to building scalable QA systems that combine traditional testing with AI-driven validation
  • Participate in Agile ceremonies including standups, sprint planning, and retrospectives

What You Will Do

  • Gain hands-on experience in modern quality engineering from day one
  • Learn how to test AI-powered systems and agent-driven applications
  • Work on real-world client products across different industries
  • Develop skills in test automation, exploratory testing, and AI-assisted QA
  • Collaborate closely with experienced engineers, product leaders, and teams
  • Understand how modern teams ensure quality in fast-moving, AI-augmented environments
  • Get exposure to how leading companies are testing and shipping AI-driven products
  • Build practical experience with tools and frameworks used in automation and QA
  • Learn how to think in edge cases, failure modes, and system behavior rather than just pass/fail tests
  • Be part of a high-performance, collaborative team with strong mentorship
  • Accelerate your learning in a space that blends QA, automation, and AI
  • Ideal for students who are curious, detail-oriented, and excited about AI-driven software development
