About the Role

QA automation for Salesforce, UI, and API tests

About CGI

Global IT and business consulting firm

Full Description

This role is hybrid and requires you to be at our / client office at a minimum 2 days per week - subject to change at any time.

We are seeking a QA Automation Engineer to join our team. The ideal candidate will have strong experience in Salesforce testing, Web UI and API testing, and proficiency with modern automation tools. This role requires collaboration, attention to detail, and a commitment to delivering high-quality software solutions.

Your future duties and responsibilities

  • Perform Salesforce, Web UI, and API testing to ensure functionality and performance.
  • Design, develop, and maintain automated test scripts using tools such as Selenium, TestNG, AccelQ, and Playwright.
  • Collaborate with development and product teams to identify and resolve defects.
  • Participate in test planning, execution, and reporting to ensure comprehensive coverage.
  • Contribute to continuous improvement of testing processes and frameworks.

Required qualifications to be successful in this role

  • Hands-on experience with Salesforce testing, Web UI, and API testing.
  • Strong proficiency in automation tools: Selenium, TestNG, AccelQ, Playwright.
  • Solid understanding of software testing principles and best practices.
  • Excellent problem-solving and communication skills to work effectively with cross-functional teams.

Nice to Have

  • Experience in the banking domain or financial services industry.
