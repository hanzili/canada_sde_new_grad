Position Title: Marketing Coordinator (Summer Student)

Company Description

The Active Adult Centre of Mississauga (AACM) is a registered charitable organization dedicated to enhancing the well-being of adults aged 50 and older. Founded in 1992, AACM provides a welcoming environment for over 1,000 members, offering diverse programs that promote physical and mental health, social connection, and cultural enrichment. The Centre runs 80 weekly social, recreational, wellness, and multicultural activities to ensure participants remain active and engaged in their community. Guided by the vision of fostering a vibrant community of healthy and involved older adults, AACM plays a vital role in enriching lives and fostering a sense of belonging.

Role Description

This is a full-time, on-site role as part of Canada Summer Jobs located in Mississauga, ON. The Marketing Coordinator will support marketing operations by conducting market research, creating and maintaining promotional materials, assisting with outreach to community members, and supporting marketing campaigns. The role includes providing excellent customer service to members and collaboration with the sales and marketing team to drive engagement and participation in the Centre’s programs.

The AACM is excited to offer an emerging professional the opportunity to work within a vibrant grass-roots organization to make a meaningful difference in the lives of local seniors this summer.

Tasks and Responsibilities:

Working with the Centre’s staff, the successful applicant will be trained and mentored in the following areas, and demonstrate job specific and transferable skills such as digital content creation, communication vehicle development, administration and customer service, event management, program development, team and relationship building, and project management skills – namely:

  • Digital media outreach & campaign development - including social media strategy, e-mail creation, and website content development. The candidate will collaborate with our Communications and Marketing Coordinator to develop strategy and explore new and creative ways to enhance seniors’ experience of the Centre.
  • Event planning and implementation - candidate will experience first-hand how community events come together and will participate in planning, promotion, execution and success with direction and ongoing support from the AACM Programming and support staff.
  • Graphic design and streaming - candidate will be given creative range to practice and further develop their experience in the creation of visually compelling brand content, as well as help in the streaming of various virtual classes/sessions.
  • Client Service and Administrative support - will be trained in addressing the needs and questions of seniors who attend classes and purchase memberships, as well as researching community resources, conducting phone outreach to engage seniors, and practicing problem solving and organizational skills within an office environment.
  • Participate in and report at staff meetings, and complete other tasks and projects as assigned to support the AACM staff and volunteers.

Examples of Projects:

  • Writing content for communication channels, including website, blogs, social media, and Annual Report
  • Capturing photos, videos, and designing graphics for use on Centre’s social media platform
  • Assist with the creation of the Centre’s Annual Impact Report for FY 2025-2026 through analyzing member survey responses
  • Conducting vital community research and outreach through the development, distribution, and data analysis of our Annual Members’ Survey
  • Work with the AACM team to organize and implement summer events, including helping to develop timelines, logistics, etc.
  • Assist in the development, streaming, and hosting of ongoing seniors’ programming and social events to engage and better the well-being of seniors

Qualifications & Assets:

  • Between 15 and 30 years of age at start of employment
  • Strong Communication and Customer Service skills, including the ability to interact effectively with diverse populations.
  • Experience in Market Research to identify trends and insights that support marketing strategies.
  • Proficiency in Sales and Sales & Marketing to assist in promoting programs and community initiatives.
  • Organizational and time management skills to handle multiple projects and tasks efficiently.
  • Tech-savviness, including familiarity with social media platforms and basic marketing tools, is an asset.
  • Previous experience in a non-profit or community-based organization is an advantage.
  • Previous experience working with Seniors is an asset.
  • Studying Marketing, Communications, or a related field is preferred.
  • A Canadian citizen, permanent resident, or persons to whom refugee protection has been conferred under the Immigrant and Refugee Protection Act
  • Legally entitled to work in Canada according to the relevant provincial legislation and regulations

Please attach a resume and cover letter to your application.

Address cover letters to: Saba Khan, Communications and Marketing Coordinator
