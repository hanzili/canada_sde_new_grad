About the Role

Support month-end close; collections and general accounting

About myGwork - LGBTQ+ Business Community

ABB global leader; inclusive employer with diverse teams

Full Description

This job is with ABB, an inclusive employer and a member of myGwork – the largest global platform for the LGBTQ+ business community. Please do not contact the recruiter directly. At ABB, we help industries run leaner and cleaner—and every person here makes that happen. You’ll be empowered to lead, supported to grow, and proud of the impact we create together. Join us and help run what runs the world.

This Position Reports To Marketing Sales&Service Finance Manager

The internship is for students in their last year of Accounting and Management DEC followed by a summer job until the end of August 2026. The internship lasts 5 weeks, with an extension into a summer job within the customer claims team.

Your Role: Own the Challenge

  • Assist the collections team with customer payments
  • Participate in month‑end journal entries with the Sales & Marketing Accounting team as well as General Accounting
  • Follow up on consignment inventory
  • Follow up on company vehicles
  • Analyze and record transportation expenses
  • Work on customer claims related to open billing disputes

What It Takes To Run What Runs The World

  • Enrolled in a DEC – Accounting and Management (final year of DEC)
  • Strong analytical and accounting skills
  • Ability to work collaboratively in a team environment
  • Must be able to commute to the Iberville area office in Saint‑Jean 5 days a week
  • French is required. English is also required to communicate with customers outside Quebec on a regular basis

Why ABB? We empower you to take initiative, challenge ideas, and lead with confidence. You’ll grow through meaningful work, continuous learning, and support that’s tailored to your goals. Every idea you share and every action you take contributes to something bigger.
