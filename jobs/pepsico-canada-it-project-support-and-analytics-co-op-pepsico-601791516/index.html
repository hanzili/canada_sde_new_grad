**Overview** **POSITION:** IT Project Support and Analytics Internship (Winter 2026 Term - 4 months) **LOCATION:** Mississauga, ON **COMPENSATION:** $25-28 per hour **Are you hungry for an exciting and challenging opportunity in a fast-paced, diverse organization?** If so, you have a future at PepsiCo. Taste the success with Canada's fastest growing CPG Company, and discover how our passion for growth creates big challenges and big opportunities: personally, professionally, and financially. - Support project plan development, monitoring and tracking project schedule, and tracking issues and risks. - Oversee creation and maintaining comprehensive project documentation, plans and reports - Liaise with clients to identify and define requirements, scope, and objectives - Consult with development team and vendors during development phase to provide requirements and feedback - Support the development of UAT test cases with the use of a Traceability Matrix - Support project Go-Live, troubleshoot issues and help develop solutions

Analytics (Secondary Focus)

- Consult with Business Partners to provide key data insights using various Business Intelligence tools

**Qualifications**

- Motivated learner enrolled in a Business, Finance, Mathematics, Engineering, or IT related program - Previous work experience as a Business Analyst or similar role preferred - Exposure to Agile methodologies - Solid communication, organizational skills, including multitasking and time-management required - Strong skills in developing presentations and reports - Strong working knowledge of Microsoft Suite (Outlook, Word, Excel, PowerPoint) and Knowledge of SQL required - Experience in Analytics from conception to delivery an asset - Working experience with dashboarding (e.g., Business Objects, Power BI) is an asset. - Self-motivated, enthusiastic, and able to build collaborative relationships!
