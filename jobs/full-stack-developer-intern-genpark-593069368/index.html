**Company Description**

GenPark is a cutting-edge platform dedicated to introducing emerging Oriental direct-to-consumer brands to Gen Z consumers around the globe. Leveraging a multi-agent intelligence system, GenPark delivers personalized, AI-driven marketing that creates culturally relevant, localized, and engaging shopping experiences. From retail products to real-world assets, GenPark provides an innovative, AI-driven approach to marketing and distribution. Join us in reshaping global retail with technology and creativity.

**Role Description**

This is a full-time hybrid role for a Full Stack Developer Intern, based in Vancouver, BC, with flexibility for remote work. The intern will assist in developing and maintaining both front-end and back-end aspects of web applications, implementing responsive designs using technologies like CSS, and contributing to a seamless user experience. You will also collaborate closely with developers, designers, and product teams to develop software solutions that align with business goals, troubleshoot technical issues, and improve application quality.
