DESCRIPTION: Junior Account Manager – Edmonton, AB – Full-Time Permanent

As a Junior Account Manager at CARO Analytical Services, you will play a key role in supporting client projects and managing work orders to ensure a seamless client experience. Acting as a primary point of contact, you will help coordinate technical requirements, resolve client inquiries, and ensure timely and accurate delivery of services.

Key responsibilities include:

Client Project Support

  • Act as the primary point of contact for client inquiries, including project status, work orders, and analytical questions
  • Set up and maintain client projects in LIMS (Laboratory Info Management System)
  • Respond to client feedback, troubleshoot concerns, and escalate issues as needed, including ICARs
  • Communicate special analytical requirements clearly to operations teams
  • Initiate and follow up on data verification with clients and resolve COC (Chain Of Custody) discrepancies
  • Enter bottle order requests in the system

Work Order Management

  • Follow up on work orders requiring additional analysis or revisions
  • Support client-requested data verification
  • Proactively communicate delays to clients
  • Perform data entry for sublet work
  • Ensure reports are issued accurately and on time
  • Review invoicing details, including pricing, bids, and applicable surcharges
  • Conduct work order reviews as needed

QUALIFICATIONS:

  • Energetic, self-motivated individual who thrives in a fast-paced environment
  • Strong client service mindset with the ability to build relationships across diverse client groups
  • Effective problem-solving skills with a proactive approach
  • High attention to detail and strong record-keeping abilities
  • Excellent interpersonal, communication, and organizational skills
  • Professional, friendly, and diplomatic demeanor

EDUCATION AND EXPERIENCE:

  • Post-secondary education in a related discipline or an equivalent combination of education and experience
  • Industry experience is considered an asset

What we offer:

  • Competitive base salary
  • Comprehensive benefits package including RRSP matching and company DPSP contributions
  • Competitive vacation, additional holidays, and a volunteer day
  • Ongoing learning and development opportunities
