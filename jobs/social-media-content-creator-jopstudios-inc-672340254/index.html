Position Overview

Euphoria, a creative arm of JOPStudios, is seeking a motivated, creative, and digitally-savvy Social Media & Content Creator for a 9-week full-time, in-person summer internship. This role merges two essential creative functions; social media management and content creation, into a single, impactful position designed to grow and energize the JOPStudios and Euphoria brands across all digital platforms.

The selected intern will spend the majority of their time creating, editing, and publishing engaging content across Instagram, TikTok, YouTube, LinkedIn, and other key platforms; including behind-the-scenes footage, brand storytelling reels, and daily social posts. They will also manage community engagement, respond to audiences, and help build a consistent, compelling online presence that converts followers into real clients.

This internship is built around mentorship, skills development, and real-world creative experience; designed to empower the next generation of digital storytellers with practical, career-building opportunities in social media and content production.

Core Responsibilities

PRIMARY: Content Creation & Brand Storytelling

Capture behind-the-scenes footage during shoots, events, meetings, and creative sessions

  • Document day-to-day activities across the Euphoria and JOPStudios brands
  • Create short-form video content (Reels, TikToks, Stories) that showcases process, culture, and creativity
  • Develop content that highlights the personality, values, and visual identity of both brands
  • Support storytelling around client projects, launches, and creative workflows
  • Edit videos and photos for social media platforms using tools such as CapCut, Canva, Adobe, or similar
  • Write engaging, on-brand captions and copy to accompany published content
  • Design and seek approval for a minimum of 4 cover graphics per week
  • Select and seek approval for a minimum of 4 images and 4 video clips per shoot or event

PRIMARY: Social Social Media Management

  • Manage JOPStudios and Euphoria’s presence across Instagram, TikTok, YouTube, LinkedIn, Pinterest, and Threads
  • Ensure a minimum of 1 post per day on all active platforms, published by 12:00 PM EST
  • Create and maintain a content editorial calendar to plan campaigns and ensure consistent posting
  • Monitor engagement across platforms; respond to comments, DMs, and mentions within 10 minutes during working hours
  • Respond to online enquiries and mentions within 3 hours on weekends and during off-hours
  • Oversee profile and account presentation; covers, bios, thumbnails, and visual branding
  • Promote content across social networks and track performance metrics (reach, saves, shares, comments)
  • Send newsletters at least once per month and blog post notifications at least once per week
  • Proactively monitor and manage community feedback, complaints, and brand reputation across all platforms

SECONDARY: Growth & Engagement Targets

As part of this internship, the intern will work toward the following platform growth benchmarks:

  • Instagram: +1,000 new followers/month
  • LinkedIn: +1,000 new connections/month
  • YouTube: +100 new subscribers/month
  • TikTok: +100–200 new followers/month
  • Threads & Pinterest: meaningful follower growth month-over-month
  • Target engagement per post: minimum 1,000 likes, 50 comments, and 50 reshares on edited photo content
  • Convert online engagement into a minimum of 30 leads per month
  • Identify 1–2 cost-effective and viable lead generation sources per month.

Learning & Program Participation

  • Participate in structured mentorship sessions with the Creative Lead and Workflow Manager
  • Engage in skills development activities aligned with CSJ program learning objectives
  • Attend team creative reviews, content planning sessions, and project debriefs
  • Complete the mandatory Canada Summer Jobs participant feedback questionnaire at program end

SKILLS & QUALIFICATIONS

Required:

  • Experience creating short-form content for social media platforms (Instagram, TikTok, YouTube)
  • Comfort filming and editing with a smartphone or camera in fast-paced, real-world settings
  • Familiarity with editing tools such as CapCut, Canva, Adobe Premiere, or similar software
  • Strong understanding of social media best practices, trends, and platform algorithms
  • Excellent written communication skills for captions, responses, and community engagement
  • Creative, observant, and able to capture authentic, compelling moments naturally
  • Strong attention to detail, ability to manage a content calendar, and meet daily posting deadlines
  • Comfortable working in a collaborative, in-person team environment.

Assets / Nice to Have:

  • Prior experience managing a brand or business social media account
  • Familiarity with social media analytics tools (Meta Business Suite)
  • Interest in photography, videography, wedding industry, or lifestyle content
  • Experience with email marketing platforms for newsletter distribution
  • Ability to work flexible hours on occasion, including select weekends for booked events or shoots.

HOW TO APPLY

Please submit the following:

• Your resume

• A portfolio, demo reel, or sample edits (link or attachment)

• A brief cover letter describing your interest in video production and what you hope to gain from this internship

• Confirmation that you meet Canada Summer Jobs program eligibility requirements:

◦ Must be a Canadian Citizen or Permanent Resident (as required by the Canada Summer Jobs program)

◦ As per federal program requirements, must be between 15 and 30 years of age at the start of employment

◦ Must hold a valid Social Insurance Number (SIN) and be legally entitled to work in Canada.
