About the Role

Analyze data, build dashboards, reveal insights

About Kensington

Travel experiences company with global reach

Full Description

Kensington delivers the world’s most personal travel experiences. We bring to life each client’s desire to travel in a way that’s tailored specifically for them, in the company of local private guides who ensure that they enjoy the authentic best of their destination. The result is a unique journey rich in memories that last a lifetime.

We achieve this because of our extraordinary people, experts with deep knowledge of their destinations. For over 120 countries worldwide, and across each of our brands – Tours, Cruises, Villas, Yachts, Jets, and Air.

To learn more about Kensington, visit kensingtontours.com.

Are you curious, analytical, and eager to apply your skills to real business challenges? Join us for a hands-on internship where data meets unforgettable travel experiences.

What You’ll Do As a BI/Data Analyst Intern, you'll work with our Analytics team to uncover insights that drive business growth and optimize client experiences in the luxury travel space. You’ll dive into data using tools like SQL , Power BI , and Excel , and get the chance to contribute directly to dashboards, KPIs, and decision-making.

You Will

  • Extract and manipulate data using SQL and/or Python
  • Build visualizations and interactive dashboards in Power BI Desktop and Microsoft Fabric
  • Support performance tracking by developing KPIs and report automation
  • Conduct ad hoc analyses to answer business questions and uncover trends
  • Translate business needs into analytical solutions through critical thinking and hypothesis testing
  • Learn how to work with stakeholders across departments to understand goals and communicate insights

What You Bring

  • Interest in data analytics and solving real business problems
  • Comfortable working with SQL for data extraction and Excel for analysis
  • Exposure to Power BI or other data visualization tools (academic or personal projects welcome)
  • Willingness to learn Python, data modeling, and Microsoft Fabric if not already familiar
  • Strong attention to detail, problem-solving mindset, and curiosity
  • Excellent communication and collaboration skills

What You’ll Gain

  • Hands-on experience with enterprise BI tools and real-world datasets
  • Mentorship from experienced analysts in a fast-paced, supportive environment
  • Understanding of how data drives decisions in a high-end service business
  • Exposure to a data strategy function in a growing, client-first organization
  • Development of business acumen, technical skills, and storytelling with data

This is a great opportunity for students who are excited to roll up their sleeves, dive into data, and grow their analytics skillset in a unique and inspiring industry.
