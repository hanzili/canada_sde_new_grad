About Weiler Smith Bowers

Full Description

We are seeking a highly organized, personable, and motivated Junior Administrative & Marketing Assistant to join our team. This entry-level role is ideal for someone looking to gain experience in both office administration and marketing within a professional environment. This role will support daily office operations, assist with client communications, and contribute to marketing initiatives such as social media content, project materials, and visual media.

Administrative Assistant

  • Perform general office tasks including data entry, filing, and clerical duties to maintain organized records.
  • Assist with front desk responsibilities, including greeting visitors and answering phone calls in a professional manner.
  • Manage appointment scheduling and coordinate meetings for staff members.
  • Maintain and update databases, ensuring accuracy and confidentiality of information.
  • Collaborate with team members to streamline administrative processes and improve efficiency.
  • Write, edit, and format proposals and RFP responses for engineering projects

Marketing Assistant

  • Manage the company’s social media accounts (LinkedIn, Instagram, Facebook)
  • Create marketing materials, including brochures, graphics, and promotional content
  • Maintain and update the company website and marketing assets
  • Assist with project photography organization and portfolio development
  • Edit and enhance project photographs for marketing materials, presentations, and social media
  • Coordinate marketing campaigns and company announcements
  • Track social media engagement and assist with content planning

Skills:

  • Proficiency in office administration and clerical tasks.
  • Comfortable working independently and in a team environment
  • Confidence in taking the lead on marketing projects and initiatives
  • Ability to multitask and manage competing deadlines
  • Excellent organizational skills and detail-oriented
  • Professional demeanor with strong interpersonal skills

This role is an excellent opportunity for individuals looking to develop their administrative skills in a supportive environment while contributing to the success of our organization.

Job Types: Full-time, Permanent
