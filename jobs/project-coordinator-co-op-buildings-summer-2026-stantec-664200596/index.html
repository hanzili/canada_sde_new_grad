Project Coordinator Co-Op – Buildings (Summer 2026)

at Stantec

📍 Location Vancouver, BC V5Y 2E2
📋 Details Internship/Co-op · Summer 2026 · $23.37-$32.07/hr · Temporary
📅 Posted Apr 15, 2026

About the Role

Coordinate project tasks, documents, and schedules

About Stantec

Global design/engineering firm; strong project exposure

Full Description

Learning begins in the classroom but there’s no better place to gain real-world experience than with Stantec. Interns are an integral part of our teams, working to solve some of the world's most complex challenges.

As an intern, you’ll work alongside our experienced practitioners and with our clients and communities. This hands-on experience will equip you with practical skills, build your network, and provide opportunities that set you up for success.

Your Opportunity

Stantec’s Buildings team is on a mission to become the world’s leading integrated design practice. Our architects, engineers, interior designers, consultants, sustainability specialists, and technologists are passionate about the power of design. Our collaborative culture and our innovative, sustainable approach to projects help us create buildings that matter to our world. Together, we are enhancing the quality of life globally through design. Join us and design your place with Stantec.

We have an exciting opportunity for a Project Coordinator Co-Op in our Vancouver office. In this role you will be assisting Project Managers and Senior Project Coordinators on one or more projects. This will allow growth potential as you will be exposed to design and construction team members, and project management processes. You will have the support and mentorship of our Project Management team.

Your Key Responsibilities

  • Assist with the preparation of cost, schedule and document management functions on project(s).
  • Assist with project tracking and communications with the clients, project teams, and other project stakeholders.
  • Prepare and distribute project correspondence and meeting notes.
  • Conduct general QA/QC activities in Project Document Control (document numbering, document register, version control, etc.) Support the preparation of regular Progress Reports (internal and external).
  • Participate and coordinate project team activities and interact effectively with Architects, Engineers, Project Managers, and other consultants or stakeholders.
  • Perform additional duties of a similar nature and level as assigned.

Your Capabilities and Credentials

  • Demonstrated skill at organizing and planning work within tight deadlines.
  • Detailed attention to requirements and ensuring quality work.
  • The ability to work independently and maintain productivity, unsupervised.
  • The discipline to maintain required safe work procedures in any environment.
  • Strong communication skills are essential.
  • Ability to contribute positively to and flourish in a team focused environment.
  • Good interpersonal skills
  • Excellent organizational skills and problem-solving abilities.
  • Strong computer skills and literacy in software including MS Power Point, MS Word, MS Outlook and MS Excel.
  • Experience with specialty software such as MS Project, Primavera, and document control software will be considered an asset.

Education and Experience

  • Currently enrolled in a university degree program, preferably Engineering or Architecture.

This description is not a comprehensive listing of activities, duties or responsibilities that may be required of the employee.

Stantec is a place where the best and brightest come to build on each other’s talents, do exciting work, and make an impact on the world around us. Join us and redefine your personal best.

About Stantec

Stantec is a global leader in sustainable engineering, architecture, and environmental consulting. The diverse perspectives of our partners and interested parties drive us to think beyond what’s previously been done on critical issues like climate change, digital transformation, and future-proofing our cities and infrastructure. We innovate at the intersection of community, creativity, and client relationships to advance communities everywhere, so that together we can redefine what’s possible.
