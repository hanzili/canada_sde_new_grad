About the Role

About BBA Consultants

Full Description

Type of position: Trainee

Your future role on our team Within the Digital Power Systems group, you’ll join a multidisciplinary team composed of electrical, automation and telecommunications specialists. Equipped with a lab and test equipment, the group is centred around executing digitalization and modernization projects in the control, protection and automation sectors for electrical substations. You’ll be designing innovative solutions with the market’s latest technologies.

With us, you’ll get the opportunity to...

  • Participate in engineering projects based on IEC 61850 standards for digital power systems
  • Perform testing in the BBA digital power systems lab
  • Configure smart electronic devices (relays, substations controllers, etc.)
  • Design substation HMI/SCADA
  • Participate in detailed engineering (protection and control diagrams, lists, technical documents)

Do your qualities and values match our corporate culture?

  • Entrepreneurial mindset
  • Thirst to learn and excel
  • Enthusiasm for collaborating in a fun environment
  • Constantly seeking sustainable and agile solutions
  • Desire to be in the thick of the action
  • Strong sense of rigour and ethics

Overview Of Certifications And Job Requirements

  • Undergraduate student in Electrical Engineering
  • Ability to read plans and schematics
  • Asset: student registered with the Institut en g é nie de l ’é nergie é lectrique (IGEE)
  • Intermediate level of English to collaborate effectively with BBA offices and/or clients outside Québec. The frequency of English use may range from occasional to daily, depending on ongoing projects

At BBA, You Get Many Benefits

  • Access to many experts and coaching
  • A collaborative team that shares its ideas and knowledge
  • Growing business with many opportunities

About BBA For over 40 years, we’ve been pushing the boundaries of engineering to serve our clients in the Energy and Natural Resources sector. At BBA, we develop innovative, flexible and sustainable solutions, from strategy to execution.

BBA is one of Canada’s leading private consulting engineering firms, focused on practice (PCB), with a network of 18 offices. Our teams work closely to deliver projects that shape tomorrow’s industry at the local, national and international levels.

Our people’s passion and excellence make BBA a Canada’s Best Employer and one of Canada’s Best Managed Companies .
