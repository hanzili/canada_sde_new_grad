Full Description

Job Title

Junior Accountant / Bookkeeper (QuickBooks Desktop) — Part-Time

Work Locations (Split Role)

  • Client site (in-person): Avenue Road & Hwy 401 area, Toronto — 1–2 days per week (must be able to drive)
  • Our office: Oshawa — 1–2 days per week (part-time office work)

Schedule

Part-time (approximately [15–30] hours/week). Must be available to attend the Toronto client site 1–2 days/week and work from our Oshawa office 1–2 days/week.

Role Summary

We are hiring a detail-focused Junior Accountant/Bookkeeper with 2–3 years of QuickBooks Desktop experience who can manage bookkeeping for multiple companies. The role includes monthly bank, investment, and full balance sheet reconciliations, accounts payable/receivable processing, document handling, and preparing organized working papers to send to the Accountant.

Key Responsibilities

  • Use QuickBooks Desktop on Windows to maintain accurate books for multiple companies
  • Enter and manage bills, invoices, payments, deposits, and adjustments
  • Print cheques from QuickBooks Desktop with proper support and cheque sequence controls
  • Prepare and post journal entries with clear descriptions and supporting documentation

Reconciliations (Core Requirement)

  • Complete monthly bank reconciliations and resolve discrepancies
  • Perform full balance sheet reconciliations for all balance sheet accounts, including:
  • Bank and credit card accounts
  • Accounts receivable / accounts payable
  • Loans and lines of credit
  • Accrued liabilities and prepaid expenses
  • Other balance sheet accounts as assigned
  • Perform monthly investment reconciliations including:
  • Investment bank accounts
  • Investment brokerage statements (matching activity to books)
  • Track lines of credit and interest statements, maintain schedules, and ensure accurate postings

Payments & Online Banking

  • Help set up and process bill payments through online banking (as required)
  • Ensure payments are properly recorded and supported

Working Papers & File Management

  • Prepare and send organized working papers to the Accountant (clear schedules, supporting documents, consistent folder structure)
  • File personal and corporate statements in the office filing system
  • Scan documents and save accurately to the correct client/company folders using standard naming conventions

In-Person Requirements

  • Must be able to drive 1–2 days/week to the client’s office near Avenue Road & Hwy 401 (Toronto)
  • Must be able to work 1–2 days/week in our Oshawa office

Required Qualifications (Must-Haves)

  • 2–3 years experience with QuickBooks Desktop
  • 2–3 years experience with bank reconciliations and balance sheet reconciliations
  • Must be comfortable working with multiple companies in QuickBooks Desktop
  • Must be able to work on Windows and use QuickBooks Desktop confidently
  • Strong English communication skills (written and spoken)
  • Strong attention to detail, organization, confidentiality, and deadline management

Nice to Have

  • Experience with investment/brokerage reconciliations
  • Experience in a bookkeeping/accounting firm environment
  • Familiarity with creating schedules for loan/LOC tracking and interest allocations
