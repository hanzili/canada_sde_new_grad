Full Description

Job Title: Entry-Level Accountant - Join Our Successful Team!

Company: Part Time CFO Services

Are you an ambitious Accountant that is a recent graduate, or with 1-2 years of experience looking for an exciting opportunity to work in a growing business? Join our team as an Entry-Level Accountant and contribute to the financial success of our diverse clients. This full-time position offers the chance to apply your expertise while gaining valuable experience.

About Us:

Part-Time CFO Services is a leading financial consulting firm specializing in providing expert financial services to businesses across various industries. We partner with our clients to improve their financial health and drive success.

Responsibilities:

  • Prepare and help prepare company financial statements, month-end journal entries, and accruals
  • Conduct key account reconciliations
  • Support customer invoicing process
  • Help maintain an accurate financial system, including posting invoices and reviewing reports to check account coding is correct
  • Create and distribute financial and operational reports, as well as performance metrics
  • Learn and utilize existing software and recommend system and process improvements where relevant
  • Assist clients with annual budget preparation and quarterly forecast updates
  • Calculate and analyze variances between actuals and budget on a monthly basis
  • Conduct research on accounting treatment for routine transactions
  • Look to continuously improve processes that you are working on or are affected by
  • Perform ad-hoc financial analysis
  • Support the team with projects and improvement initiatives, such as system implementations

Key Technical Skills:

  • Interest in pursuing CPA certification.
  • Bachelor's degree or diploma in business, finance, accounting, or a related field.
  • Proficiency in Microsoft Excel.
  • Experience with accounting software (e.g., Quickbooks, Sage, SAP, or another) is advantageous.

Key Interpersonal Skills:

  • Thrives in a collaborative team environment, working effectively with non-financial employees.
  • Embraces learning opportunities within an evolving business.
  • Customer-focused mindset.
  • Strong organizational skills and ability to prioritize effectively.
  • Exceptional attention to detail.

Perks and Benefits:

  • Competitive salary and comprehensive benefits package.
  • Opportunities for professional development and growth.
  • Collaborative and supportive work environment.
  • Recognition and rewards for outstanding performance.

  • Company events and team-building activities.
