About The Acquisition Group

Recruiting firm; TELUS client, growth-focused culture

Full Description

Spring is a season of fresh starts, new opportunities, and growth — and we believe your career should bloom the same way. As the weather warms up and the city comes alive, we’re looking for motivated and enthusiastic individuals to join our Edmonton downtown team as *Sales Representatives representing TELUS Communications* , one of Canada’s most trusted telecommunications providers.

If you enjoy meeting new people, and building skills that grow with you, this opportunity is the perfect way to start something new this season.

*Why Join Us?*

  • Growth-Focused Environment

Just like spring brings new growth, we invest in your development through hands-on training and ongoing learning opportunities.

  • Mentorship & Support

Work alongside experienced mentors who are committed to helping you succeed every step of the way.

  • Positive Team Culture

Be part of an energetic, collaborative team where encouragement, teamwork, and positivity thrive.

  • Enjoy the Outdoors

Take advantage of sunny days while talking with customers in local Edmonton communities.

  • Uncapped Commission Earnings

Uncapped commission-based pay structure, dependent on commission rates associated with a successfully sold product.

*What You’ll Do* As a Sales Representative, you’ll connect with households across Edmonton, introducing customers to TELUS’s reliable products and services. You will:

  • Build friendly and professional relationships through direct residential interactions
  • Share product knowledge confidently and answer customer questions
  • Help clients discover solutions that best fit their needs
  • Strengthen communication, confidence, and problem-solving skills through real-world experience

*What We’re Looking For*

  • Clear communicators who enjoy working with people
  • Positive self-starters eager to learn and grow professionally
  • Team players who thrive in a supportive environment
  • No previous sales experience required — attitude and work ethic matter most
  • Must be 18+ and legally eligible to work in Canada
  • Based in Edmonton and available for full-time, in-person work

*Why This Role?* This role is perfect for someone ready to step outside, enjoy the sunshine, meet new people, and build valuable career skills while growing toward long-term professional success.

*Job Type:* Full-Time

*Location:* Edmonton, AB
