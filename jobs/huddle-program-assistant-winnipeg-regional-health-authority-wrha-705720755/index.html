Full Description

NORWEST SUMMER STUDENT JOB POSTING Huddle Program Assistant Paid Term Position | 15 hours/week |Term for 8 weeks Department: Huddle NorWest

Position: Huddle Program Assistant

Responsible To: Community Development Coordinator

Approximate Start Date : July 6, 2026

Salary: $16.00/hour

Anticipated Shift: 15 hours per week (Tuesday to Friday from 12:30-4:00 PM)

Summary Of Job Description The Huddle Program Assistant will be located at NorWest at Notre Dame, 945 Notre Dame Ave. This is an integrated site that houses a few different programs. The assistant will support a variety of summer activities, programs, outreach and engagement and front desk supports. Additional support to other programs based at NorWest on Notre Dame will be required.

Employment is subject to eligibility under the Manitoba Green Team Program, including confirmation that the applicant is between 15–29 years of age, legally entitled to work in Canada, and possesses a valid Social Insurance Number.

Qualifications

  • High school diploma would be preferred.
  • Currently or plans to be enrolled in a post-secondary education program preferred.
  • Experience working with youth aged 12-29 years of age.
  • Strong communication and problem-solving skills.
  • Empathetic, non-judgmental approach.
  • Knowledge and experience with diverse cultures and economic backgrounds.
  • Experience in recreation is an asset.
  • Experience with program delivery and facilitation is an asset.
  • Experience or knowledge of program development.
  • Valid driver’s license and access to a vehicle is an asset.

Please Submit Your Application In Confidence To Rupsi Parajuli, HR Assistant

NorWest Co-op Community Health

785 Keewatin Street, Winnipeg MB

Email: rparajuli@norwestcoop.ca

Application deadline: June 17, 2026 We thank everyone who expresses interest but only those selected for an interview will be contacted.
