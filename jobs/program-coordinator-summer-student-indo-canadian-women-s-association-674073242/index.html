About the Role

About Indo Canadian Women's Association

Community NGO promoting equity and inclusive programming

Full Description

Overview
ICWA is seeking an enthusiastic and team-oriented summer student to support the planning, coordination, and delivery of community programs. This role is ideal for someone interested in community development and program management. The Indo-Canadian Women’s Association (ICWA) works to promote equity and inclusion through impactful community programming and engagement.

Duties

  • Update the website with upcoming events and create promotional graphics
  • Assist in developing and managing programs for youth, seniors, and diverse community groups
  • Support ongoing programming and activities
  • Assist in writing project proposals and identifying funding opportunities
  • Help facilitate meetings, workshops, and events
  • Ensure programs align with organizational goals and mission
  • Collaborate with community partners and stakeholders

Qualifications

  • Aged 15–25 and legally entitled to work in Canada
  • Strong communication and interest in community programming
  • Ability to work independently and in a team environment
  • Proficiency in Microsoft Office, Windows, and Canva
  • Graphic design skills are considered an asset
  • Fluency in English required, and proficiency in South Asian languages considered an asset

Job Types: Full-time, Temporary
Contract length: 16 weeks

Pay: $20.00 per hour

Work Location: In person
