Full Description

Social Media Content Creator and Account Manager Internship (Instagram, YouTube, Quora & Medium)

CANYON ENTERTAINMENT GROUP

Location: Remote / Greater Toronto Area and Mississauga

About Us:

Canyon Entertainment Group represents a roster of high-caliber artists who inspire their fans every day. By constantly learning from our talented array of singers, songwriters, and producers, we strive to create music and live events that spur positive change. Our mission is to empower artists by providing innovative marketing solutions and driving their music to new heights.

Position Overview:

We are seeking a creative and motivated intern for our Social Media Content Creator and Account Manager Internship. This unpaid internship is ideal for College or University students interested in social media marketing, content creation, digital publishing, and audience growth within the entertainment industry. The intern will gain hands-on experience managing content across Instagram, YouTube, Quora, and Medium while helping expand the online presence of artists and brands.

Responsibilities:

• Create, schedule, and publish engaging content across Instagram, YouTube, Quora, and Medium.

• Develop content ideas aligned with artist branding and marketing objectives.

• Write captions, articles, video descriptions, and audience-focused content.

• Research industry trends, keywords, and content opportunities.

• Manage account engagement by responding to comments and interacting with audiences.

• Assist with content planning, campaign execution, and community growth initiatives.

• Monitor analytics and provide recommendations to improve performance and reach.

• Repurpose content into platform-specific formats to maximize visibility and engagement.

Requirements:

• Currently enrolled in a College or University program in Marketing, Communications, Media, Journalism, Business, English, or a related field.

• Strong writing, communication, and content creation skills.

• Familiarity with Instagram, YouTube, Quora, and Medium platforms.

• Understanding of social media trends, content marketing, and basic SEO principles.

• Experience with Canva, video editing tools, or content creation software is an asset.

• Ability to work independently and collaboratively in a remote environment.

Key Competencies:

• Social media marketing

• Content creation and storytelling

• Digital publishing and audience engagement

• Research and content strategy

• SEO and content optimization

• Organization and time management

Benefits:

• Gain practical experience in social media management and content marketing.

• Build a professional portfolio featuring published content and campaigns.

• Develop skills in content strategy, audience growth, and digital marketing.

• Flexible internship schedule to accommodate academic commitments.

• Opportunity to contribute to creative projects within the entertainment industry.

How to Apply:

If you are passionate about social media, content creation, digital publishing, and entertainment marketing, please apply! Once your application is received and reviewed, we will contact you to discuss further details.

Job Type: Internship (Unpaid)

Contract Length: 3–6 months

Part-time Hours: 10–20 per week

Important Details

• This training is similar to that which is provided in a vocational school.

• This training is for the benefit of you personally, to develop your skills, resume, and professional portfolio.

• Your training supervisor will ensure that the activities you work on benefit your learning and development.

• This position is only for learning purposes and does not displace any employees in this company; additionally, it is not an opportunity to become an employee of this company.

• This is an unpaid internship to support the individual obtaining a university/college credit.
