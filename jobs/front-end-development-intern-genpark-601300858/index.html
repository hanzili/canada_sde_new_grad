About GenPark

Full Description

**Company Description**

GenPark leverages intelligent Brand Agents and User Agents powered by AI to help global brands effectively engage and convert Gen Z consumers. Through personalized AI-generated content, interactive Agents, and immersive video experiences, GenPark empowers brands to connect with their target audience in meaningful ways. The platform ensures smarter matching between products, services, and consumers, based on continuous AI learning from user behavior and feedback. This innovative approach enhances marketing efficiency by increasing conversion rates and lowering customer acquisition costs.

**Role Description**

This internship opportunity is for a Front End Development Intern at GenPark. The intern will assist in designing, developing, and debugging front-end components and interfaces to enhance user experiences. Key responsibilities include collaborating with the development team, implementing responsive web designs, testing and troubleshooting applications, and writing clean and maintainable code. This is a hybrid role based in Vancouver, BC, allowing flexibility with some remote work.

**Qualifications**

- Strong foundation in Programming and Software Development, including proficiency in front-end technologies such as HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. - Excellent Analytical Skills to assess performance, solve problems, and optimize user interfaces. - Effective Communication skills to collaborate within the team and articulate ideas clearly. - A basic understanding of Sales concepts and user behavior to align development with business goals. - Experience with version control systems (e.g., Git) and frameworks like React or Angular is a plus. - Currently enrolled in or recently graduated from a degree program in Computer Science, Web Development, or a related field. - Ability to work in a hybrid environment with a proactive and adaptable mindset.
