Environmental Science / Ecology Internship

Laboratory Technician (Co-Op)

at Alliance Technical Group

📍 Location Mississauga, Ontario
📋 Details Co-op · 0-12 months · Competitive pay · Internship
📅 Posted May 01, 2026

About the Role

Perform lab analyses and field sampling with GC

About Alliance Technical Group

Oil and gas lab; field sampling exposure

Full Description

Mississauga Oil and Gas Laboratory – Laboratory/Field Sampling Technician Alliance Technical Group North inc. (Alliance) is seeking a dynamic individual to join its Oil and Gas Laboratory

group as a laboratory and field sampling technician for the Mississauga, ON office. This is a dual purpose role

consisting of working in a laboratory setting as well as occasional field work collecting air samples. Only those

candidates selected for interviews will be contacted. Interviews are expected to be held in March-April 2026

Duties And Responsibilities

  • Will be working as part of a team performing field evaluations of source emissions in industrial settings
  • Working at heights and some heavy lifting is required
  • Perform calibrations of measurement and analytical instrumentation, and maintain calibration records
  • Perform analysis of gas samples using several gas chromatography applications
  • Log, ship and receive sample containers
  • Keep track of lab supplies and gas cylinder inventories
  • Perform equipment maintenance and prepare test equipment for field work
  • Assist in other laboratory/shop duties as needed

Qualifications

  • Must have valid Class G drivers license
  • Travel and occasional overnight stays at hotels will be required to conduct field testing programs
  • Must be able to work at heights
  • Experience with data processing (Excel), report writing (Microsoft Word), and data acquisition systems
  • Mechanical aptitude and electronics background is an asset
  • Experience with analytical instrumentation is helpful but not essential

Attributes

  • Ability to foster and maintain excellent working relationships with co-workers
  • An aptitude for problem solving in the lab and field
  • Ability to work in and contribute to project teams
  • Excellent written and verbal communication skills
  • Ability to multi-task
  • Exceptional organizational skills

Key Benefits Include

  • Medical, Dental, and Vision Insurance
  • Flexible Spending Accounts
  • 401(K) Plan with Competitive Match
  • Continuing Education and Tuition Assistance
  • Employer-Sponsored Disability Benefits
  • Life Insurance
  • Employee Assistance Program (EAP)
  • Paid Time Off (PTO), Paid Holidays, & Bonus Floating Holiday (if hired before July 1st)
  • Profit Sharing or Individual Bonus Programs
  • Referral Program
  • Per Diem & Paid Travel
  • Employee Discount Hub
