Your future duties and responsibilities

  1. Full-Stack Development

  2. Develop features across the stack using technologies such as JavaScript/TypeScript, React, Node.js, Python, Java, or similar.

  3. Build and maintain REST APIs, UI components, and lightweight tools or utilities.

  4. Write clean, efficient, and testable code following best practices.

  5. AI/ML Implementation

  6. Support development of AI-driven features using libraries and APIs such as TensorFlow, OpenAI APIs, or computer vision frameworks.

  7. Assist in training, testing, and tuning ML models.

  8. Integrate AI-based capabilities into applications or prototypes.

  9. Data Processing & Analytics

  10. Work with data pipelines, real-time data sources, or analytics platforms.

  12. Contribute to research and analysis tasks as needed.

  13. Prototyping & Innovation

  14. Build proof-of-concepts for new ideas or product enhancements.

  15. Experiment with modern tools, frameworks, and APIs.

  16. Document prototypes and present findings to the team.

  17. Collaboration & Communication

  18. Participate in Agile ceremonies, team discussions, and code reviews.

  19. Collaborate with cross-functional teams (engineering, product, design).
  20. Prepare clear technical documentation, diagrams, and demo walkthroughs.

Required qualifications to be successful in this role

  • Currently enrolled in a Computer Science, Software Engineering, or related program.
  • Strong programming experience in Python, Java, or C++.
  • Hands-on experience with full-stack technologies (React, Node.js, JavaScript/TypeScript, etc.).
  • Exposure to AI/ML or computer vision (TensorFlow, OpenCV, MediaPipe, etc.).
  • Familiarity with version control (Git) and common development tools.
  • Strong analytical, problem-solving, and debugging skills.

Preferred Skills

  • Experience with mobile or desktop applications (Android Studio, JavaFX, etc.).
  • Knowledge of cloud platforms or backend services (e.g., Firebase).
  • Experience participating in team-based engineering or robotics projects.
  • Interest in AI integration, automation, and emerging technologies.

What the Student Will Gain

  • Opportunity to work on real applications and AI-driven features.
  • Exposure to software engineering best practices.
  • Experience building prototypes, tools, and end-to-end technical solutions.
  • Mentorship from experienced engineers and project leads.
  • A strong portfolio of completed technical work.

Additional Requirements

  • Open to students in 2nd, 3rd, or 4th year.
  • Prior technical project or internship experience is an asset.
  • Students with a technical background or demonstrated programming experience will be preferred.
