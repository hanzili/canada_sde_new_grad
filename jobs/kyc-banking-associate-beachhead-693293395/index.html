Full Description

Interested in a vital financial operations role? Apply Now!

Working with one of our top financial clients, this role calls for a KYC Banking Associate. This position involves providing essential operational support within a busy banking environment, ensuring high accuracy in transaction processing, compliance, and customer service excellence.

Pay rate range (CAD): $17.60/hr – $24.87/hr Responsibilities

  • Process high-volume banking transactions, including bank drafts and everyday banking activities
  • Conduct KYC checks supporting compliance standards and regulations
  • Support daily operational activities to meet Service Level Agreements (SLAs) and performance metrics
  • Review and support adherence to bank policies, procedures, and compliance requirements
  • Assist with process improvements and contribute to quality assurance efforts
  • Maintain accurate documentation and ensure data integrity for all transactions
  • Collaborate with internal teams and external partners, ensuring efficient communication
  • Contribute to audit readiness by following established guidelines and controls
  • Handle customer data with confidentiality and in accordance with privacy standards
  • Participate in daily team meetings to discuss workflow and address operational issues

Desired Skill-Set

  • Post-secondary certification or equivalent experience in a banking or financial services environment
  • 4 years of experience handling high-volume transaction processing within a banking or financial institution
  • Proven expertise in conducting KYC checks and supporting compliance processes
  • Strong understanding of bank policies, procedures, and draft handling
  • Excellent organizational skills and meticulous attention to detail
  • Proficiency in Microsoft Office applications, especially Excel, Word, and Outlook
  • Ability to learn new software quickly and adapt to changing systems
  • Analytical mindset with strong problem-solving capabilities
  • Ability to work independently and collaboratively in a team setting
  • Strong interpersonal skills to interact effectively with internal teams and external partners

Nice to Have

  • Experience with global and WSS platforms or similar financial software systems
  • Knowledge of foreign exchange products, money market deposits, loans, and swaps
  • Previous experience in a back-office banking or trade processing role
  • Bilingual skills are an asset but not required
