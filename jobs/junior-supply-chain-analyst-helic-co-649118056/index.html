About the Role

Analyze supply chain data; optimize inventory and logistics

About Helic & Co.

Remote entry-level role; mentorship toward senior analyst

Full Description

This is a remote position.

Overview

We are seeking a detail-oriented Junior Supply Chain Analyst to support the optimization of supply chain processes, inventory management, and logistics. This entry-level role involves data analysis to improve efficiency and reduce costs.

Key Responsibilities

  • Analyze supply chain data, including inventory levels, demand forecasts, and supplier performance.
  • Monitor KPIs, generate reports, and identify opportunities for process improvements.
  • Assist in inventory planning, procurement tracking, and order fulfillment coordination.
  • Support vendor management, contract reviews, and compliance checks.
  • Use tools like Excel and ERP systems to model scenarios and forecast trends.
  • Collaborate with operations, procurement, and logistics teams on daily activities.

Qualifications

  • Bachelor’s degree in Supply Chain Management, Logistics, Business, or related field.
  • Basic proficiency in Excel, SQL, and ERP software (e.g., SAP); analytical mindset.
  • 0-2 years of experience or internships; strong organizational skills.
  • Knowledge of supply chain fundamentals.

Preferred Attributes

  • Familiarity with Tableau or Power BI for visualization.
  • Certifications like CSCP or interest in sustainable logistics.

What You’ll Gain

  • Practical experience in end-to-end supply chain operations.
  • Mentorship toward senior analyst or planner roles.
