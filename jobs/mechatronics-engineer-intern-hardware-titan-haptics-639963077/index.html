Full Description

Mechatronics Engineer Intern - Hardware

We are seeking a hands-on Mechatronics Engineering Intern to join our engineering team. This role focuses on the design, assembly, testing, and debugging of electromechanical prototypes, PCBs, fixtures, and development hardware. The intern will work across mechanical, electrical, and embedded hardware tasks, supporting product characterization, validation, and day-to-day engineering build and test activities for our haptic technology.

About TITAN Haptics

TITAN Haptics is a startup dedicated to developing touch technologies (haptics) for smartphones, consoles, and touchscreen devices. Working at TITAN Haptics is dynamic, innovative, and solution-driven. We appreciate individuals who are risk-takers, creative problem solvers, and those who are self-motivated to tackle significant challenges.

Role Type

4 - 8 Month Contract

Responsibilities

  • Build, assemble, and test electromechanical prototypes, subsystems, fixtures, and development hardware.
  • Conduct characterization and validation testing on motors, PCBs, and integrated hardware systems, then document findings clearly.
  • Support PCB inspection, bring-up, soldering, rework, and basic bench-level debugging.
  • Assist with mechanical design tasks including CAD updates, fixture design, 3D printed parts, fit checks, and assembly improvements.
  • Support embedded hardware testing by flashing boards, running test code, and helping debug hardware-software interactions.
  • Work across mechanical, electrical, and embedded hardware tasks to troubleshoot issues and implement practical fixes.
  • Help develop test setups, QA procedures, reliability testing, and engineering documentation.

Qualifications

  • Currently pursuing a degree in Mechatronics Engineering, Electrical Engineering, Mechanical Engineering, or a related field.
  • Hands-on skills in CAD, 3D printing, soldering, prototyping, circuit design, and electronics testing.
  • Strong analytical and problem-solving skills, with attention to detail.
  • Basic understanding of motor mechanics, electronic circuits, and control systems.
  • Experience using measurement and diagnostic tools to test and troubleshoot equipment.
  • Ability to read and interpret technical documents, such as schematics and mechanical drawings.
  • Comfortable working across mechanical, electrical, and embedded hardware tasks in a prototyping environment.
  • Portfolio of personal and academic projects demonstrating hands-on experience in hardware, embedded systems, or electromechanical design.

Nice to Haves

  • Interest in consumer electronics technology, gaming, AR/VR, and tech trends.
  • Proficiency in other languages, especially Chinese, is considered an asset.

Location

  • Mississauga (On-site)

Compensation and Benefits

  • CAD $25/hour
  • Catered lunches in office

TITAN Haptics Company Culture

  • An open, collaborative environment that fosters the exchange of ideas across Engineering, Marketing, Sales, and Strategy.
  • A high-energy, fast-paced team that prioritizes speed and iteration.
  • A flat management structure that values ideas. Be creative, be heard.
  • A flexible work schedule that values quality work product rather than time spent.

Application Requirement

  • Applicants should include a portfolio, project links, or examples of past technical work with their application.
