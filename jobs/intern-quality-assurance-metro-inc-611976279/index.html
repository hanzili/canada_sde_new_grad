Full Description

Position Title: Intern Quality Assurance

Job Requisition ID: 45484

Banner: Metro

Employment Type: Internship and New Graduates

The typical salary range for this position is $41 300.00 - $61 900.00 annually. The base salary offered will be determined based on the candidate’s job-related knowledge, skills, education, experience, and internal equity considerations.

This posting is for an existing temporary, full-time position.

Summary This position is responsibility for assisting and supporting the QA Specialists with their daily best before date audits, product checks and completions of Vendor Return documents.

Specific Responsibilities

  • Random product checks - Produce quality check, based on random checks and requests from Merchandising and Procurement
  • Daily quality audits - Organic quality checks
  • Complete "Return to Vendor" documents - RTV documents to facilitate product returns
  • Assist CFIA and Third-party inspection agencies - gather samples and assist cleaning up the inspection area
  • Complete daily Dates and Alert audits - a morning report checking Best Before Date of salads and processed product
  • Complete daily logs and reports - record daily rejection and inspections into a QA report
  • Inbound quality and short weight inspections - Assist QA with inbound inspections
  • Communicate with Buyers, Toronto and Laval QA, QA Manager and Director
  • Assist Full Time QA Specialist with daily duties

Qualifications

  • Produce experience an asset, Grocery retail experience necessary
  • Able to work in different temperature areas (3C to 23C)
  • Able to lift 50 lb.
  • Able to commit to the following work schedule: 6:00am-2:00pm, Monday-Friday
  • Available to work weekends or holidays as needed, flexibility with work hours required
  • Some Excel and Word experience
  • Able to work independently

The responsibilities and relationships assigned to this role may change as is necessary to achieve Metro's business objectives.
