Full Description

About The Role

Not just another entry-level career opportunity: receive coaching, mentorship, and support at every step of your career journey. We invest in you so you can thrive and succeed.

Launch your tech career with FDM – any degree welcomed.

Your degree got you here—your passion can take you further. Start a tech career that’s dynamic, analytical, and impactful. As an FDM Quality Engineering Consultant, you’ll work with global companies across banking, consulting, retail, insurance, and more. You’ll add big names to your resume while gaining hands-on experience from day one.

Serving a wide variety of industries, we offer a defined, hands-on pathway into Quality Engineering. You’ll build cross-functional skills, learn leading testing frameworks, and help deliver high‑quality digital products. Through industry‑relevant coaching and real-world assignments, we help you grow into a confident, business-ready Quality Engineer.

We match your strengths to the right role. Opportunities could include, but are not limited to:

Quality Engineer

Ensure world-class product quality by combining manual testing, automation frameworks, and systemic thinking. You’ll:

  • Test applications manually and with industry tools like Selenium, Cucumber, and REST-Assured
  • Work across QA, staging, and production environments
  • Analyze risk, design test strategies, and collaborate with developers and product teams
  • Build automated test suites that integrate into CI/CD pipelines
  • Support releases, monitor quality metrics, and help prevent defects before they occur

A great Quality Engineer proactively designs systems and strategies to ensure holistic quality across the entire product lifecycle. Strong QEs combine manual and automation testing, understand environment flows, and excel in collaboration and ownership—often overlapping with SDETs to build quality in from the start.

Career progression often moves from Quality Engineer → Quality Architect or SDET or Software Engineer, with top profiles demonstrating expertise in automation frameworks, API & integration testing, DevOps tooling, and strong communication skills.

About You

What’s in it for you?

  • Fast-track your career with 6–12 weeks of initial upskilling facilitated by expert coaches, followed by practice-based learning that matches industry demand
  • Opportunity to work on exciting, business-critical projects with top-tier companies in banking & finance, insurance, retail, and more
  • Contribute to delivering high‑impact solutions across the full application lifecycle
  • No tech experience? No problem. If you’re analytical, detail-oriented, and love problem‑solving, we’ll equip you with the skills to succeed in Quality Engineering
  • With hands-on support and real client projects, you’ll be assignment-ready—able to grow into senior QE, SDET, or QA leadership roles in just a few years
  • Build a diverse and rewarding career path with ongoing coaching through our Skills Lab
  • Consultant Experience Team dedicated to supporting your wellbeing, health, and happiness
  • Potential eligibility for relocation support

What we look for

  • You hold a university degree level (bachelor or higher)
  • Able to commit to at least two years of employment with FDM as a consultant following the initial training period
  • Strong problem-solving and analytical skills, paired with great interpersonal and communication skills
  • Eligible to work in Canada
  • Please note that all client assignments will require on-site work in client offices. Office locations may include, but are not limited to, Toronto, Montreal, Halifax, Waterloo, Calgary and Vancouver

During the initial period of upskilling, all hours worked will be compensated at the rate of $17.85/hour. Once on client assignment, the compensation for this position is $50,000 annually and this role may also be eligible for participation in FDM's bonus scheme.
