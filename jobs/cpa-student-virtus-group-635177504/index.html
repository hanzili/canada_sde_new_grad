About Virtus Group

Full Description

Support the dreams of those who have boldly ventured into entrepreneurship. Believe in those who have faced a business challenge or taken a step into the unknown. Help local entrepreneurs experience the full extent of success. This is what Virtus Group is all about.
If you’re a recent or soon to be university accounting graduate, this might be the workplace you’re looking for. At Virtus Group, our employees are accustomed to an environment where ideas are embraced and can make an immediate impact. We see every day as an exercise in learning and an opportunity for growth.
As a CPA Student, you’ll gain experience in a variety of accounting and assurance services as well as personal and corporate tax. Our comprehensive training program and hands-on work experience will fast track your development, putting you on the path to becoming a professional accountant.
We’re looking for students who can start full-time in May 2026.
You’ll gain experience with:

  • Compilations, reviews, audits;
  • Personal and corporate tax;
  • Payroll; and
  • General bookkeeping, reconciliations, and business advisory.

What you’ll bring to our team of vibrant professionals

  • Keen interest in continual learning and taking initiative;
  • Strong verbal and written communication skills;
  • Excellent attention to detail;
  • Positive attitude;
  • Appreciation of entrepreneurship;
  • A post-secondary degree in accounting; and
  • Desire to pursue a CPA designation.

At Virtus Group, you’ll find yourself working alongside a fun, collaborative, and innovative team of professionals dedicated to maintaining an engaging and rewarding work environment. We offer an exceptional rewards package that includes competitive compensation, overtime that can be banked as paid time off or paid out, healthcare benefits (including a wellness plan), pension plan, vacation and personal days, parking, Fridays off in July & August, and ongoing professional development to support you in achieving your goals. Intrigued? Apply directly through our careers page or contact hr@virtusgroup.ca with questions. About Virtus GroupVirtus Group’s purpose is to help people. Our story started with one person, one spark, and a commitment to virtue and helping Saskatchewan businesses and the community for over half a century. As the largest independent accounting and business advisory firm in Saskatchewan, with offices in Saskatoon, Regina, and Estevan, we offer comprehensive financial services tailored to meet the needs of our diverse clientele. We have resources to manage the most challenging, complex client work while maintaining the flexibility and agility to make all decisions quickly and in-house.
