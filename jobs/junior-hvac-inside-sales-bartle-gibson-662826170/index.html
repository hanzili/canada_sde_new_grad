About the Role

About BARTLE & GIBSON

HVAC supplier with career training and growth

Full Description

Are you successful at building instant rapport and achieving customer satisfaction?

Do your organizational skills shine in your workflow?

Are you looking for a career opportunity with great potential?

If you’ve answered yes to these questions, then come join our team at Bartle & Gibson!

We are currently in search of a career-orientated person to join our team. We are looking for the right candidate to train and develop for a career in Hydronics & HVAC. This position handles Inside Sales, and Quotations for our customers. The position operates in a fast-paced, dynamic, and rewarding environment.

DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES

  • Responding to inquiries and processing incoming calls and orders
  • Contacting current customers and identifying new products and potential up-selling
  • Entering orders into the system
  • Sourcing materials for customers when currently not in stock
  • Maintaining knowledge base on current products and learning about new products
  • Providing product information, pricing quotations, and follow-up on quotes
  • Developing and maintaining positive customer relationships
  • Ensuring that customers are satisfied with order taking, product, pricing, deliveries, and overall service
  • Supporting Counter Sales and handling returns as required
  • Working closely with others to ensure customer needs are met
  • Other duties as required

SKILLS AND COMPENTENCIES

  • Exceptional customer service
  • Ability to warm up and develop instant rapport with potential customers
  • Ability to develop long-term relationships and generate referrals from your customers
  • Excellent interpersonal & communication skills, both verbal and written
  • Impeccable organizational skills with attention to detail
  • Time management & multitasking skills to serve several customers at the same time
  • Strong networking skills, outgoing personality
  • Excellent computer skills (Word, Excel, Email)
  • Self-motivated; able to function effectively both independently and as a member of a team
  • Proven ability to learn quickly and work effectively in a fast-paced work environment

EXPERIENCE AND EDUCATION

  • Minimum grade 12 education required
  • Candidates should possess a minimum of two years’ sales experience (counter sales, inside sales or outside sales) preferred
  • Post-Secondary courses in Hydronics or HVAC preferred
  • Skilled in serving and negotiating with institutional and industrial purchasers, Hydronics & HVAC contractors, and the public preferred
  • Knowledge of the Hydronics or HVAC industry or a background in Hydronics and HVAC products preferred

Final candidates will be required to undergo an assessment and security screening, including a criminal record check. Employment is subject to screening completion that is satisfactory to Bartle & Gibson.
