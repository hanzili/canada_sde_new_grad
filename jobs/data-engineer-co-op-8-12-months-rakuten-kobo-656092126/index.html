Data Engineer Co-op (8-12 Months)

at Rakuten Kobo

📍 Location Toronto, ON Hybrid
📋 Details Co-op · 12mo · $45K/yr · Summer 2026
📅 Posted Apr 02, 2026

About the Role

Build data pipelines, write SQL and Python

About Rakuten Kobo

Global e-book platform, part of Rakuten.

Full Description

Job Description:

Here at Rakuten Kobo Inc. we offer a casual working start-up environment and a group of friendly and talented individuals. Our employees rank us highly in terms of commitment to work/life balance. We realize that for our people to be innovative, creative and passionate they need to feel valued and supported.

If you’re looking for a company that inspires passion, personal, and professional growth – join Kobo and come help us on our mission of making reading lives better.

The Role:

Rakuten Kobo Inc. is looking for a Data Engineer Co-op to join our Big Data Team for 8-12 months starting Summer 2026 in Toronto! The team is comprised of many highly talented enthusiastic individuals who ensure the services and offerings from Kobo are available to both internal and external customers. This position reports to our Manager, Tolino Business Intelligence.

The Person:

  • A pragmatic generalist who tackles problems end-to-end and automates manual work.
  • Self-motivated, creative, detail-oriented; collaborates well and learns fast.
  • Thrives in a fast-paced environment and isn’t afraid to dive into any layer of the stack.
  • Eager to learn and contribute to cutting-edge big data and potentially AI initiatives.

Responsibilities include:

  • Design, build, and document efficient ETL/ELT data pipelines and models.
  • Write and optimize SQL and Python for large-scale datasets.
  • Visualize data and deliver BI dashboards/reports using our BI tools (e.g., Domo, Tableau) for internal and external partners.
  • Prepare and ingest data into Google Cloud Platform (GCP).

The Skillset

Required:

  • Experience with Python and SQL.
  • Ability to prioritize competing requests and handle multiple tasks in a fast-paced environment.
  • Initiative in communicating, asking questions, and learning.
  • Excellent oral and written communication skills in German and English.
  • Proactive, creative problem solver with strong time management.
  • Works independently with a structured approach.
  • Comfortable working with very large datasets.
  • Currently enrolled in a relevant Bachelor's or Master's degree program (e.g., Computer Science, Data Science, Engineering).

Nice to Haves

  • Experience with CI/CD tools.
  • Experience with GCP and BI tools (Domo, Tableau).
  • Experience working with REST APIs and Python data libraries (Pandas, NumPy).
  • Experience building ETL/big data pipelines with workflow tools (e.g., Airflow).

The Perks:

  • Flexible working environment (Hybrid work model after initial in-office period)
  • Free Kobo device + free weekly e-book or audiobook
  • Weekly Kobo Tech University sessions
  • Employee Purchase Program & corporate partner discounts

About Rakuten Kobo Inc.
Rakuten Kobo Inc. is the world’s digital bookseller created by and for booklovers. Owned by Tokyo-based Rakuten Group, Inc. and headquartered in Toronto, Rakuten Kobo’s millions of worldwide users can read anytime, anywhere, and on any device. With a mission to make reading lives better for all, Rakuten Kobo connects readers to stories using thoughtful and personalized curation of eBooks and audiobooks, and the best dedicated eReaders and apps for reading. With the singular focus of making reading lives the best they can be, Kobo’s open platform allows people to fit reading into more moments in their busy lives. To learn more about Rakuten Kobo, visit www.kobo.com.
