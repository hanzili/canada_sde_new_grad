About the Role

Assist HR functions: recruitment, onboarding, admin

About Lakes District Maintenance

Local contractor focused on people and safety

Full Description

At Volker Stevin, our People & Culture team plays an important role in supporting our workforce and helping our projects succeed. Reporting to the People & Culture team, this role offers exposure to recruitment, onboarding, HR administration, and learning coordination. It is an excellent opportunity for a student who is eager to learn, detail oriented, and interested in building a career in Human Resources.

How You’ll Add Value:

  • Review resumes, assist with candidate screening, conduct phone interviews for hourly and field-based roles, and complete reference checks
  • Support applicant tracking and recruitment administration in ADP Workforce Now
  • Assist with generating offer letters and new hire documentation
  • Track and follow up on pre-employment requirements to ensure timely onboarding completion
  • Coordinate training enrollment, track completion records, and assist with learning session logistics
  • Maintain accurate employee files and shared HR resources
  • Assist with document management, template updates, and team communications
  • Support HR projects including SharePoint updates and policy research

What You Bring:

  • Enrolled in a post-secondary institute focused on Human Relations, Business Administration, or a related field Returning to school in September 2026 or January 2027
  • Prior HR or administrative experience is an asset but not required
  • Strong attention to detail and organization skills
  • Excellent communication and follow-through
  • Positive attitude, curiosity, and willingness to learn

Criteria for position:

To be considered, applicants must meet the following criteria:

  • Currently enrolled in a post-secondary institution (college, university, or technical program)
  • Returning to post-secondary studies after completing the work term

Please note that only applications that meet these requirements will be considered.

How We Make It Count

The Volker Stevin Advantage

Spend your summer building meaningful roads, bridges, and projects that people rely on every day. At Volker Stevin, you’ll work in a supportive environment with a focus on teamwork and safety. It’s more than a job; it’s an opportunity to try something new and make a difference.

  • Earn Good Money: Competitive pay while gaining hands-on experience.
  • Learn New Skills: Develop in-demand skills for construction and beyond.
  • Be Part of a Team: Work on real projects and see the impact of your efforts.
  • No Experience? No Problem: We’ll teach you everything you need to know.

Our Commitment to You

Connecting People with Progress

Safety Isn’t Just a Priority—It’s a Value. Safety is woven into the fabric of who we are and how we operate. It’s not a box we check off; it’s a commitment that guides every decision we make, every process we implement, and every project we undertake. Because at the end of the day, when we say safety is a value, we mean it’s a promise we make to our people, families, and the communities we serve.

True to Our Word, True to Our People. Our culture is grounded in humility and authenticity, with a deep commitment to family. We believe in supporting each other, no matter the role or title. When we say we care, we mean it—our actions match our words. Everyone is empowered, valued, and respected, because being 'true to our word' means being 'true to our people.'

Interesting Projects and Skill-Building Work. Volker Stevin is known for its ability to take on complex projects of all sizes, from large-scale infrastructure builds to specialized niche developments to highway maintenance and more. This diversity in project scope and complexity means that our employees have access to new opportunities across our family of companies that challenge them to think creatively and develop new skills.
