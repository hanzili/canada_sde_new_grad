About Rates.ca

Full Description

Rates.ca Group Ltd. | Toronto (Hybrid)

Reporting to: Manager, Finance

At Rates.ca Group Ltd., we take pride in doing things the right way—thoughtfully, accurately, and as a team. As a growing organization, we rely on people who are dependable, curious, and take ownership of their work, while also supporting those around them.

We’re looking for a Junior Accountant who is detail-oriented, reliable, and eager to learn. This is a great opportunity to build a strong foundation in accounting within an IFRS reporting environment, while being part of a team that values both performance and people.

What You’ll Do

  • Process vendor invoices end-to-end, ensuring accurate coding, documentation, and adherence to internal controls
  • Prepare and process payments (EFTs/wires) in line with company policies
  • Assist with vendor inquiries and collaborate with internal teams on invoice-related matters
  • Assist with journal entries (accruals, prepaids, adjustments) to support accurate period-end close
  • Contribute to audit support and preparation of reconciliations and schedules
  • Perform monthly bank reconciliations and investigate discrepancies
  • Follow internal policies and contribute to maintaining strong financial controls
  • Support process improvements and help identify opportunities for efficiency
  • Assist with various finance projects and cross-functional initiatives

What You Bring

  • Bachelor’s degree in Accounting or Finance
  • 1–2 years of relevant accounting experience (co-op or internship experience counts)
  • Strong attention to detail and a high level of accuracy
  • Proficiency in Excel and comfort learning new systems
  • Strong communication skills and ability to work collaboratively
  • Experience with NetSuite, SAP Concur, or similar tools is an asset

Who You Are

  • Takes pride in being dependable and following through on commitments
  • Notices the details and understands that they matter
  • Enjoys being part of a team and contributes positively to the environment
  • Is curious, asks questions, and is motivated to learn and improve
  • Brings your own personality while maintaining professionalism

Why Join Rates.ca Group Ltd.?

  • Be part of a growing company where you can learn and develop quickly
  • Work with a supportive, collaborative finance team
  • Gain exposure to real-world IFRS reporting and controllership processes
  • Contribute to meaningful work that impacts the business
  • Join a culture that values accountability, respect, and people

If you’re looking for a place to start or grow your accounting career—where expectations are clear, support is real, and your work matters—we’d love to hear from you.

Our Perks

  • You get your Birthday off!
  • This role requires two in‑office days per week (Tuesday and Thursday), with an additional Wednesday in the office every other week
  • 4 weeks of 'Work from Anywhere Program' every year
  • Full employer-paid benefits
  • RRSP Matching Program with Wealthsimple
  • Annual holiday festivities, free lunches and so much more
  • Helping you help others with our paid volunteer programs
  • Employee satisfaction survey for you to voice your opinion on working at Rates.ca Group Ltd.
  • Free access to LinkedIn Learning
  • Lounge and games room - ping-pong, pool, foosball, and more!

The compensation package consists of a competitive salary, benefits, and an incentive bonus.
