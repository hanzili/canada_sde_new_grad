Full Description

Junior IT Support Engineer | Help Drive Innovation at Curaleaf International!

Location: Brampton, Ontario (Hybrid - 4 days on-site)

Salary: $55,000 - $65,000 CAD (Depending on experience)

Hours: Monday to Friday, 8:30 AM - 5:00 PM

Are you an aspiring IT professional looking to level up your career with a global leader?

Curaleaf International is searching for a proactive and curious Junior IT Support Engineer to join our team in Brampton. If you have a passion for problem-solving, a knack for Microsoft 365, and a desire to learn from seasoned experts, this is the perfect launchpad for you.

In this role, you won't just be "fixing computers"-you'll be a vital link in our global operations, collaborating closely with our IT Manager and European teams to support over 700 users.

About Us:

At Curaleaf International, we're not just participating in the cannabis industry-we're leading it.

As part of the world's largest cannabis company, Curaleaf Holding, Inc., we are powered by a strong presence at all stages of the supply chain. Our unique distribution network throughout Europe, Canada and Australasia brings together pioneering research with cutting-edge cultivation, extraction, and production. Amidst a rapid growth trajectory, our emphasis on quality and expertise aims to deliver safe and legal cannabis.

Curaleaf Clinics: Curaleaf Clinic is the largest medical cannabis clinic in the UK and remains one of the highest rated services. Offering consultations for a range of conditions where there is evidence for the use of medical cannabis, Curaleaf Clinic is dedicated to improving access to medical cannabis for patients.

Our Vision: To shape the future of cannabis through our commitment to research and product excellence

Our Mission: To be the world's leading cannabis company consistently delivering superior products and services and driving the global acceptance of cannabis.

Our Values:

Lead & Inspire

Commit to Win

Driven to Deliver Excellence

Customer Obsession

One Curaleaf

What You'll Be Doing:

  • The Go-To Problem Solver: Provide first-line support via phone, email, and service desk, tackling everything from hardware hiccups to software snags.
  • Systems Specialist: Administer our Microsoft 365 environment, manage user accounts, and handle password resets or access permissions.
  • Hardware Hero: Set up and configure equipment for new hires and ensure all on-premises hardware is patched, updated, and running smoothly.
  • Documentation Pro: Maintain clear records of IT procedures and manage our digital and physical assets.
  • Global Collaborator: Coordinate with our UK and EU teams to provide seamless support across time zones.

What We're Looking For:

  • Experience: At least 1+ years in a first-line support role within a busy office environment.
  • Tech Savvy: Strong knowledge of Windows 10/11 and experience troubleshooting within the Microsoft 365 suite.
  • Adaptability: The ability to support a diverse workforce of 700+ users with patience and excellent customer service.
  • Bonus Points: If you've worked with RMM tools (like Atera) or have a basic understanding of network equipment (routers/switches), we want to hear about it!
  • Communication: Fluent English is essential for supporting our local and international colleagues.

Why Join Us?

We don't expect you to know everything on day one. We value curiosity and a "can-do" attitude. This is a unique opportunity to sharpen your technical skills under the mentorship of experienced IT leaders while earning a competitive salary.

The Perks:

Competitive Salary - Get rewarded for your hard work.

Excellent Holiday Allowance - Time to recharge is vital.

On-site Parking - Stress-free commuting.
