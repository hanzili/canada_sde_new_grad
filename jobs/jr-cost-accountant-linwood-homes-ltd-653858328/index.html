Jr. Cost Accountant

at Linwood Homes Ltd.

About Linwood Homes Ltd.

Construction firm; hands-on job costing environment

Full Description

About the Role

The Junior Cost Accountant supports the accounting team in maintaining accurate financial records related to accounts payable, job costing, bank reconciliations, and office administration. This role is ideal for an individual beginning their accounting career who is detail‑oriented, organized, and eager to learn within a construction environment. The Junior Cost Accountant will play a key role in ensuring vendor invoices, job cost data, and daily financial activities are processed accurately and efficiently.

Responsibilities

  • Accounts Payable (AP)
  • Enter vendor invoices into the accounting system with accuracy.
  • Match vendor invoices against purchase orders and receiving documents.
  • Reconcile accounts payable subledger to the general ledger (AP to GL).

  • Scan, upload, and electronically file vendor invoices.

  • Job Costing Support

  • Enter construction job change orders and assign them to the correct job cost category.

  • Assist with maintaining accurate job cost records.

  • Cash Management & Banking

  • Deposit customer cheques as needed.
  • Apply customer deposits in the accounting system.
  • Perform daily bank statement reconciliations.

  • Assist with month-end bank reconciliations.

  • Office & Administrative Support

  • Pick up, sort, and dispatch daily mail.
  • Manage office stationery and supply inventory.
  • Support general administrative needs.

Qualifications

  • Diploma or certificate in Accounting, Bookkeeping, or related field.
  • Strong attention to detail.
  • Basic understanding of accounting principles.
  • Proficiency with Microsoft Excel.
  • Strong organizational skills.

Required Skills

  • Experience with accounting or ERP systems.
  • Prior AP or bookkeeping experience.
  • Knowledge of construction job costing is an asset.

Preferred Skills

  • High accuracy and attention to detail.
  • Strong communication and teamwork.
  • Ability to work in a fast-paced environment.
  • Professionalism and reliability.
