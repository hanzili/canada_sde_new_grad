About Sibwest

Concrete forming contractor with growth and learning

Full Description

Geo Concrete Group is seeking a motivated and detail-oriented Junior Estimator / Project Coordinator to join our growing team. This is an excellent opportunity for someone with 1–2 years of experience in construction who is looking to develop their career in estimating, project coordination, and office administration within the concrete forming industry.

This role offers a mix of estimating, project support, and administrative responsibilities, making it ideal for a candidate who wants hands-on exposure to multiple areas of the business.

Key Responsibilities:

Estimating

  • Assist in preparing quantity take-offs, cost estimates, and bid submissions for concrete forming projects
  • Review drawings, specifications, and tender documents
  • Request and compare supplier and subcontractor pricing
  • Maintain organized records of bids, estimates, and project files
  • Support senior estimators with pricing and tender deadlines

Project Management Support

  • Assist Project Managers with scheduling, document control, and project coordination
  • Prepare and track purchase orders, change orders, and site documentation
  • Communicate with clients, suppliers, and field staff regarding project updates
  • Help monitor project progress, budgets, and timelines

Administrative / Office Duties

  • Maintain filing systems, spreadsheets, and project databases
  • Prepare reports, correspondence, and meeting notes
  • Support daily office operations and general administrative tasks
  • Ensure documents are accurate, organized, and submitted on time

Qualifications

  • 1–2 years of experience in construction, estimating, project coordination, or a related field
  • Diploma or degree in Construction Management, Civil Engineering, Engineering Technology, or similar
  • Strong organizational and time management skills
  • Ability to read construction drawings
  • Proficiency in Microsoft Office (Excel, Word, Outlook)
  • Strong communication skills and attention to detail
  • Positive attitude and willingness to learn

What We Offer

  • Competitive salary based on experience
  • Career growth opportunities within a growing company
  • Exposure to estimating, project management, and operations
  • Supportive team environment
  • Valuable experience in the concrete forming industry

Location

Office-based position with occasional site visits as required.
