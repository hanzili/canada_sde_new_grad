About Soucy Group

Full Description

At Soucy, we believe in the leverage effect of a great internship experience on the start of a career. That's why, every year, we open our doors to dozens of interns in all fields so they can venture into the world of work with us and explore their potential.

Our team is made up of curious minds and creators who are always pushing boundaries, and we need an intern who can bring a new dimension to our already amazing world.

Benefits designed to take you far!

  • Attractive salary (you can pay off your student loans or fund your next trip—the choice is yours!)
  • “My Flexibility” program (That's right, you can finish at noon on Fridays to enjoy your favorite activities)
  • Real projects that match your strengths and interests (We want your internship to live up to YOUR expectations!)
  • Social activities (Yes, we like to have fun here too!)

What does a fun internship look like?

Want an internship where you can have a real impact on the quality of products delivered to customers? By joining our team, you'll be at the heart of the supply chain and play a key role in ensuring that every part meets the highest quality standards.

As a quality assurance intern, you will participate in the implementation and maintenance of the quality system at our suppliers while contributing to its continuous improvement.

During this internship, you will have the opportunity to:

  • Verify and approve received components by following the PPAP process
  • Inspect parts upon receipt to ensure compliance with end customer requirements
  • Write and follow up on non-compliance reports and work with suppliers to implement effective corrective solutions
  • Actively participate in resolving issues related to customer complaints
  • Coordinate and follow up on necessary actions during engineering changes (parts replacement, inventory management, etc.)

With the following skills and qualities, you will be successful:

  • Bachelor's degree student in mechanical engineering
  • You have a good command of English, mainly for communicating by email
  • Knowledge of reading plans and geometric tolerancing

Ready to join us? We are waiting for you for an internship that will run from May 4 to August 14, 2026. The schedule is Monday to Friday, 40 hours per week, and you will enjoy flexible hours!

We sincerely want to learn more about you!

Working with the world's leading manufacturers requires us to be the best at what we do. I like to think that my team is dynamic, competent, and effective, truly making a difference. We enjoy our collaborative efforts, and if you're eager to take on responsibilities, we're ready to provide them, as we're open to new ideas!

Life at Soucy Track Systems

Working at Soucy Track Systems means helping to design and manufacture our flagship product: Soucy track systems. Our 320 employees work hard every day with other Soucy subsidiaries to design, produce, and assemble our track system components which are renowned for their high quality.

We rely on big machines and advanced technology to carry out our activities. But our greatest asset is our people. People who are dedicated to their work and who move forward as one.

Our is a working environment that reflects our values: a collaborative space where you can find your place, where you can take on challenges, and where your ideas will come to life.
