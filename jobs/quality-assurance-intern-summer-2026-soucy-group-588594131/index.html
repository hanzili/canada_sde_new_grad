**What does a fun internship look like?**

Want an internship where you can have a real impact on the quality of products delivered to customers? By joining our team, you'll be at the heart of the supply chain and play a key role in ensuring that every part meets the highest quality standards.

As a quality assurance intern, you will participate in the implementation and maintenance of the quality system at our suppliers while contributing to its continuous improvement.

**During this internship, you will have the opportunity to:**

- Verify and approve received components by following the PPAP process - Inspect parts upon receipt to ensure compliance with end customer requirements - Write and follow up on non-compliance reports and work with suppliers to implement effective corrective solutions - Actively participate in resolving issues related to customer complaints - Coordinate and follow up on necessary actions during engineering changes (parts replacement, inventory management, etc.)

**With the following skills and qualities, you will be successful:**

- Bachelor's degree student in mechanical engineering - You have a good command of English, mainly for communicating by email - Knowledge of reading plans and geometric tolerancing
