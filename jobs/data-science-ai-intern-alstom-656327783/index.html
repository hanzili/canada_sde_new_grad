Data Science & AI Intern

at ALSTOM

📍 Location Saint-Bruno, QC
📋 Details Intern
📅 Posted Apr 02, 2026

About the Role

Develop and deploy predictive models, data pipelines

About ALSTOM

Global rail leader applying AI solutions

Full Description

Req ID:507436

At Alstom, we understand transport networks and what moves people. From high-speed trains, metros, monorails, and trams, to turnkey systems, services, infrastructure, signalling, and digital mobility, we offer our diverse customers the broadest portfolio in the industry. Every day, 80,000 colleagues lead the way to greener and smarter mobility worldwide, connecting cities as we reduce carbon and replace cars.

Could you be the full-time Data Science & AI Intern in St-Bruno-de-Montarville, QC we’re looking for?

Your future role

Take on a new challenge and apply your data science and artificial intelligence expertise in a cutting-edge field. You’ll work alongside innovative and collaborative teammates.

You'll help implement advanced data science and AI techniques to solve business challenges and create actionable insights. Day-to-day, you’ll work closely with teams across the business (such as engineering, operations, and IT), develop predictive models and algorithms, and support the deployment of machine learning models, and much more.

You’ll specifically take care of ensuring the quality and integrity of data within AI solutions, but also contribute to documenting methodologies and best practices for AI applications.

We’ll look to you for:

  • Implementing data science and AI techniques to address business problems
  • Developing predictive models and algorithms
  • Supporting the creation and deployment of machine learning models
  • Ensuring data quality and integrity in AI solutions
  • Documenting methodologies and best practices for AI applications
  • Providing training on AI tools and techniques

All about you

We value passion and attitude over experience. That’s why we don’t expect you to have every single skill. Instead, we’ve listed some that we think will help you succeed and grow in this role:

  • Currently enrolled in a degree program in Computer Science, Engineering, Mathematics, Operations Management, Data Science, or a related field
  • Understanding of data science and AI techniques
  • Experience in developing predictive models and algorithms
  • Knowledge of machine learning deployment processes
  • Familiarity with ensuring data quality and integrity in AI solutions
  • Strong documentation and communication skills
  • Ability to collaborate effectively within a team

Things you’ll enjoy Join us on a life-long transformative journey – the rail industry is here to stay, so you can grow and develop new skills and experiences throughout your career. You’ll also:

  • Enjoy stability, challenges, and a long-term career free from boring daily routines
  • Work with new security standards for rail signalling
  • Collaborate with transverse teams and helpful colleagues
  • Contribute to innovative projects
  • Utilise our flexible working environment that accommodates your academic schedule

You don’t need to be a train enthusiast to thrive with us. We guarantee that when you step onto one of our trains with your friends or family, you’ll be proud. If you’re up for the challenge, we’d love to hear from you!

Important to note

As a global business, we’re an equal-opportunity employer that celebrates diversity across the 63 countries we operate in. We’re committed to creating an inclusive workplace for everyone.

Knowledge of a language other than French is an asset to facilitate communication with our partners, suppliers, colleagues, and international clients. In Quebec, French remains the primary working language of our company.
