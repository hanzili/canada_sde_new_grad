About bri-steel

Industrial steel manufacturer with established regional presence

Full Description

Bri-Steel Manufacturing is a unique large-diameter seamless steel pipe manufacturer located in Edmonton, Alberta. We are the only manufacturer in the Western Hemisphere producing 14” to 36” seamless pipe. Bri-Steel has built a strong reputation for producing high-quality products while maintaining exceptional ethical and professional standards.

We are currently seeking a detail-oriented and dependable Junior Accountant to join our accounting team.

Reporting directly to the Controller, the Junior Accountant plays an important role in supporting accurate financial reporting and day-to-day accounting operations. The ideal candidate is organized, reliable, and able to work effectively both independently and as part of a team.

Key Responsibilities

  • Receive, process, verify, and reconcile purchase invoices
  • Assist with cheque runs and payment processing
  • Reconcile and process employee expense claims
  • Maintain accurate vendor and customer information within the accounting system
  • Respond to vendor and customer inquiries in a professional and timely manner
  • Investigate and resolve invoice discrepancies and payment issues
  • Reconcile month-end supplier statements
  • Assist with bank deposits
  • Maintain organized electronic and physical filing systems
  • Provide support with additional accounting and administrative functions as required

Qualifications & Skills

  • Diploma or post-secondary education in Accounting, Finance, or a related field
  • Minimum 5 years of accounting or bookkeeping experience preferred
  • Strong attention to detail and accuracy
  • Ability to prioritize tasks and meet deadlines in a fast-paced environment
  • Strong organizational and communication skills
  • Reliable, honest, and professional with a high degree of confidentiality
  • Experience with payroll and Microsoft Navision is considered an asset

Pay: From $65,000.00 per year

  • Disability insurance
  • Employee assistance program
  • Extended health care
  • Life insurance
  • On-site parking
