About the Role

About Aventas Inc

Omni-channel agency; brands, sales, marketing focus

Full Description

▶ ABOUT US

We are an Omni-channel sales and marketing agency that has worked alongside brands like Telus, Rogers, Bell, RBC, & BMO. Our company specializes in helping brands unleash their unique point of view through conducting all our sales and marketing FACE-TO-FACE to build customer relationships and amplify impact.

▶ WHY JOIN OUR TEAM?

  • Real-world experience you can actually use - build communication, confidence, and sales skills employers look for across all industries
  • Hands-on 1-on-1 mentorship from day 1 with experience leaders who support your growth
  • Clear path to leadership - advance based on performance, not seniority
  • Bonuses and uncapped commissions - your effort directly impacts your earnings, no earning ceiling
  • Travel opportunities - team trips and events across North America and internationally (check out our social media & website)

▶ WHAT YOU WILL BE DOING

  • Connect with new and existing customers through confident, friendly conversations about cellphones, internet, security, and TV services
  • Learn how to identify customers needs and deliver clear, engaging product presentations
  • Learn how to communicate, influence, and build trust in real-world sales situations
  • Develop strong public speaking and presentation skills
  • Opportunities to represent our brand at exciting live events and promotions, including the CNE, Montreal Grand Prix, and more

▶ WHO WE ARE LOOKING FOR

  • A motivated, positive self-starter who’s excited to learn new skills and grow
  • Driven by goals and rewarded for performance (commission-based pay)
  • Comfortable starting conversations and building rapport with customers
  • Competitive in a healthy way and values being part of a supportive team culture
  • Someone who enjoys high-energy, fast-paced and team-oriented work environments
  • Previous experience in retail, hospitality, customer service, or volunteering is a plus - but is not required
  • ABILITY TO COMMUTE TO ETOBICOKE LOCATION

Job Type: Full-time

Pay: $800.00-$1,200.00 per week
