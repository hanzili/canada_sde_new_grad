Full Description

Overview
We are seeking a detail-oriented and motivated Tax Trainee to join our dynamic financial services team. This entry-level position offers an excellent opportunity to develop foundational skills in accounting, tax preparation, and financial analysis within a professional environment. The ideal candidate will possess a strong interest in finance and be eager to learn about various accounting software and tax processes. This paid internship position provides valuable hands-on experience, supporting the growth of future accounting professionals.

Duties

  • Assist with preparing and reviewing tax documents and returns under supervision
  • Support the team in conducting account reconciliations and audits
  • Utilize accounting software QuickBooks to maintain accurate financial records
  • Support the preparation of financial reports and statements for clients
  • Participate in data entry, document organization, and filing related to tax and accounting projects
  • Collaborate with senior team members on special projects related to investment analysis and account management

Qualifications

  • Basic understanding of accounting principles and financial services industry practices
  • Experience or familiarity with accounting software QuickBooks.
  • Strong analysis skills with attention to detail and accuracy
  • Excellent organizational skills with the ability to manage multiple tasks effectively
  • Eagerness to learn about tax regulations, investment strategies, and financial analysis techniques
  • Ability to work collaboratively within a team environment
  • Prior coursework or experience in accounting or finance is a plus

This position offers a comprehensive learning experience for individuals interested in building a career in accounting or financial services.
