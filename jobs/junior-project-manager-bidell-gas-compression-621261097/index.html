About Bidell Gas Compression

Full Description

Company Description

Bidell Gas Compression, a division of Total Energy Services Inc., is a premier supplier and service provider of reciprocating and rotary screw natural gas compressors ranging from 20 to 7,800 horsepower. With an in-house engineering and design team, Bidell specializes in comprehensive natural gas compression solutions and provides certified OEM equipment such as Caterpillar, Waukesha, Ariel, and Sullair. The company operates through multiple branches across Western Canada and is supported by robust rebuild facilities, extensive parts inventory, and 24/7 factory-certified technicians. Bidell’s innovative NOMAD trailer-mounted compressor, with over 1,000,000 operating hours, is a game-changing solution for gas compression in the industry. The company is committed to exceeding customer expectations and continually setting new standards for performance and reliability.

Role Description

This is a full-time, on-site role for a Junior Project Manager based in Calgary, AB. The Junior Project Manager will be responsible for supporting project management activities, coordinating logistics, expediting processes, scheduling inspections, and ensuring efficient project execution. This role involves close collaboration with cross-functional teams to monitor project timelines, resolve challenges, and ensure adherence to company standards and client requirements.

Qualifications

  • Experience with Expediting and Expeditor-related tasks to manage workflow and ensure project timelines are met.
  • Knowledge of Project Management principles and processes, with the ability to coordinate across teams effectively.
  • Expertise in Inspection processes and quality assurance to maintain project and regulatory standards.
  • Skills in Logistics Management, including coordinating resources, scheduling, and ensuring efficient delivery of materials.
  • Strong problem-solving and organizational skills, with the ability to work under deadlines.
  • Proficiency in communication tools and project management software.
  • Experience in the natural gas or energy sector is an asset but not required.
