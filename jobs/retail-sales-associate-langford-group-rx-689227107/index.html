About the Role

Sell mobility products, assist customers, manage orders

About Langford Group Rx

Established home health retailer with training programs

Full Description

Brant Arts Home Health Care & Mobility has been proudly serving the Burlington and surrounding communities for over 60 years! We pride ourselves on our dedication to customer service and will always strive to go the extra mile to help our clients on their journey to better health and mobility.

Job Summary:

We are currently looking for a full-time Retail Sales Associate to join our Home Health Care & Mobility team . This position plays a vital role as the primary point of contact with our valued customers. If you are a self-starter, have a passion for helping people, and are looking to join a growing organization, we strongly welcome your application!

Job Description:

Work in conjunction with the Home Health & Mobility teams to provide exceptional sales and service of mobility and home health products, including, but not limited to: ostomy care, mobility aids, bathroom safety, first aid and wound care, bracing, compression, mastectomy products, and much more.

Provide advice on proper use and fit of products and equipment. Listen to customer needs and preferences, offering personalized recommendations and solutions that best suit their requirements, ensuring a positive experience.

Identify opportunities to recommend additional products or accessories that complement customers' purchases, contributing to increased sales.

Prepare customer deliveries, pick ups, and online orders, and process transactions using the POS.

Maintain accurate and organized records of customer interactions, transactions, and inquiries using our CRM software.

Perform a variety of administrative functions including maintaining customer files, generating reports, completing funding applications, responding to emails, etc.

Order products, maintain sufficient inventory levels, and anticipate customer demand.

Ensure the store's appearance is welcoming and organized, including arranging displays and shelves, assembling and organizing products, and participating in routine cleaning tasks.

Contribute to a healthy, safe and inclusive work environment and follow all SOPs as they relate to our various services.

Other duties as assigned.

Qualifications include:

Previous experience in health care, including roles such as Occupational Therapy Assistant/Physiotherapy Assistant (OTA/PTA) or Personal Support Worker (PSW), is considered a strong asset.

Excellent communication skills, both verbal and written, with a strong ability to actively listen and empathize with customers.

Attention to detail and strong organizational skills are required for critical tasks like order processing, inventory management, product fittings, funding applications, etc.

Basic computer proficiency, including experience with point-of-sale systems and customer relationship management (CRM) software.

Passion for assisting people and a genuine desire to help improve their quality of life through home health and mobility products.

Willingness to learn in a team environment and an aptitude for development.

Flexibility to work days, evenings and some weekends during hours of operation (Monday to Friday 9am-6pm, Saturday 9am-3pm).

Must be able to lift up to 20 lbs and stand and walk for extended periods as well as sit, reach, and bend throughout the day.

Why work for us? We proudly offer:

  • Training with an experienced team
  • Healthcare spending account
  • Paid personal/sick days
  • Store discount
  • Company events
  • Development programs
  • and much more!

We thank all candidates who apply but only those selected for interviews will be contacted. We are an equal opportunity employer. Please advise in your application if you require accommodation during the hiring or interview process.

This role is an existing vacancy and is open to qualified applicants.
