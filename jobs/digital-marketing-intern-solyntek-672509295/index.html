About Solyntek

Full Description

Company Description

Solyntek is Motreal based innovative startup leveraging advanced artificial intelligence (AI) technology to transform ordinary security cameras into 24/7 workplace safety and security solutions. Our cutting-edge system employs computer vision algorithms to detect and alert unusual activities, ensuring a safe environment for employees and customers. Seamlessly integrating with existing security infrastructures, our AI-powered solutions simplify monitoring and enhance workplace safety. Join us at Solyntek to be part of a transformative journey towards creating safer workplaces for businesses everywhere.

Role Description

This is a remote internship role for a Digital Marketing Intern. The Digital Marketing Intern will assist with executing digital marketing strategies, managing social media accounts, analyzing web performance, and implementing online marketing campaigns. Additional responsibilities include content creation and effective communication to promote Solyntek’s mission.

Qualifications

  • Familiarity with Social media marketing and digital marketing techniques
  • Basic knowledge of web analytics and online marketing
  • Strong Communication skills with the ability to adapt tone and style based on the target audience
  • Proactive attitude, creativity, and a willingness to learn and contribute to team efforts
  • Assist in optimizing website pages for SEO performance
  • Monitor website traffic and SEO metrics using analytics tools
  • Currently pursuing a degree in Marketing, Communications, Business, or a related field
  • Experience with content creation tools or platforms is a plus
  • Strong organizational skills and ability to meet deadlines in a remote work environment

What You Will Gain

• Hands-on experience in AI technology marketing

• Exposure to startup growth and B2B technology commercialization

• Experience working directly with founders and leadership

• Opportunity to contribute to real marketing campaigns and strategy

• Networking opportunities with industrial and technology professionals
