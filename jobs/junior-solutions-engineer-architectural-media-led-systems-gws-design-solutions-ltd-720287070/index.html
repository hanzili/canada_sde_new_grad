Full Description

About ClearLED

ClearLED is a global leader in transparent LED displays, media architecture, and custom digital display solutions. Our technology has been deployed in airports, mixed-use developments, retail destinations, cultural institutions, sports venues, and landmark architectural projects across North America, the Middle East, Europe, and Asia.

As demand for our custom-engineered solutions continues to grow, we are seeking a Junior Solutions Engineer to support our sales, engineering, and project teams in developing innovative LED display and media architecture solutions.

This position is ideal for someone with a background in Architectural Technology, Architecture, Building Engineering, Industrial Design, AV Systems, or a related field who wants to build a career at the intersection of architecture, technology, and large-scale project delivery.

About the Role

As a Junior Solutions Engineer, you will work closely with our sales, engineering, and project management teams to develop technical solutions for some of the world's most unique LED and media architecture projects.

You will help create technical drawings, support proposal development, coordinate with architects and consultants, and work directly with our engineering and manufacturing teams in China to bring custom solutions to life.

Because this position works closely with both North American project teams and our engineering and manufacturing partners in China, candidates who are fluent in both English and Mandarin Chinese will have a significant advantage.

This is a growth-oriented role with a clear career path toward Solutions Engineer, Sales Engineer, Solutions Architect, or Project Manager positions.

Key Responsibilities

Technical Design & Drafting

  • Create and maintain AutoCAD drawings for proposals, project submissions, and client presentations.
  • Develop technical layouts, elevations, sections, mounting details, and installation concepts.
  • Assist with Revit and BIM coordination when required.
  • Support the creation of technical diagrams, schematics, and project documentation.

Sales Engineering Support

  • Support the sales team during the pre-sales process by developing technical solutions and layouts.
  • Assist in preparing quotations, equipment schedules, and bill of materials (BOMs).
  • Review project requirements and recommend appropriate display technologies and Clconfigurations.
  • Participate in client meetings and technical discussions as needed.

Proposal & RFP Support

  • Assist with the preparation of large-scale RFP and tender submissions.
  • Coordinate technical content, compliance documentation, and product specifications.
  • Support presentation and proposal development efforts.

Project Coordination

  • Coordinate with architects, consultants, fabricators, contractors, and internal teams.
  • Work closely with our engineering and manufacturing teams in China to communicate project requirements.
  • Review shop drawings, fabrication drawings, and engineering documentation.
  • Support project managers during project execution and installation phases.

Qualifications

Required

  • Diploma or Degree in Architectural Technology, Architecture, Building Engineering, Mechanical Engineering, Industrial Design, AV Technology, or a related discipline.
  • Strong proficiency in AutoCAD, Microsoft Office Suite, and Adobe Acrobat.
  • Excellent communication and organizational skills.
  • Ability to manage multiple projects and deadlines simultaneously.

Preferred

  • Experience with Revit, SketchUp, Rhino, Bluebeam, and BIM workflows.
  • Familiarity with construction documentation, architectural detailing, AV systems, digital signage, and LED display technology.

Bonus Qualifications

  • Fluent in both English and Mandarin Chinese (spoken and written) is a strong asset.
  • Experience communicating with overseas engineering, manufacturing, or supplier teams.
  • Experience working with international teams and cross-cultural communication.
  • CTS or AVIXA training/certification.
  • Experience supporting architectural, construction, AV, lighting, or technology projects.

What You'll Learn

  • Media Architecture
  • Transparent LED Technology
  • Large-Scale Digital Displays
  • Airport & Transportation Projects
  • Retail & Mixed-Use Developments
  • International Project Delivery
  • Sales Engineering
  • Technical Consulting
  • Project Management

Compensation & Benefits

Salary: CAD $70,000–85,000 per year

Additional Compensation:

  • Annual performance bonus
  • Career growth opportunities
  • International project exposure
  • Flexible hybrid work environment
  • Professional development support
  • Opportunity to travel to project sites, industry events, and manufacturing facilities

Why Join ClearLED?

You'll be joining a specialized global team that sits at the intersection of architecture, technology, engineering, and digital media. Our projects are often one-of-a-kind, requiring creativity, technical excellence, and close collaboration with some of the world's leading architects, designers, developers, and integrators.

If you're excited about building the future of media architecture and want to grow your career beyond traditional drafting or design roles, we'd like to hear from you.
