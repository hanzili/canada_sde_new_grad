About Kruger Products | Produits Kruger

Full Description

At Kruger Products we have a shared vision for greatness. We manufacture some of North America’s most popular tissue brands - Cashmere®, Purex®, Scotties®, SpongeTowels®, Bonterra®, White Cloud®, as well as products for use away from home.

We are leaders in our industry, leaders in our communities, and leaders in sustainability. Our headquarters is in Mississauga, ON, and we proudly have nearly 3,000 employees across North America, including 10 manufacturing plants in Quebec, British Columbia, Ontario, and Tennessee.

Kruger Products is investing in our manufacturing facilities with the latest technology. As an industry leader in this space, we are pushing innovation through Operational Excellence and the integration of digital & automation solutions. We also continue to invest in ways to further our commitment to a culture of health and safety - for everyone, by everyone, and in everything we do.

We are looking for a BI & Data Analytics Co‑Op Student to help build Power BI dashboards that support our Operations team. You will work with real operational data, create data models, and develop clear visual reports using Power BI, DAX, and M Language. This role is ideal for a student who enjoys data, problem‑solving, and building practical reporting tools.

What You Can Expect…

  • Build and maintain Power BI dashboards for operational reporting.
  • Use DAX and M Language for calculations and data transformations.
  • Clean, prepare, and validate data to ensure accuracy and quality.
  • Automate data tasks using Python or JavaScript when needed.
  • Collaborate with teams to understand reporting requirements.

What We Expect…

  • Enrolled in a post‑secondary program in Data Analytics, Computer Science, Business Technology, or similar.
  • Experience with Power BI, DAX, and M Language.
  • Strong skills in data visualization, data preparation, and data quality.
  • Working knowledge of Python and basic JavaScript.
  • Strong analytical thinking and attention to detail.
  • Excellent communication and organizational skills.
  • Work On-Site at 1625 5th Avenue New Westminster V3M 1Z7
  • Contracted 4-month term May – September

Pay

  • $21-24/hour
  • Boot allowance, reimbursement up to a maximum of $225
