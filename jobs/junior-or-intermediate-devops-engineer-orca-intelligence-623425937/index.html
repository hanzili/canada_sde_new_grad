Full Description

Company Description

Orca Intelligence specializes in supply chain optimization, combining advanced technologies and efficient processes to deliver cost-saving solutions tailored to the unique needs of each client. By automating freight payables and consolidating data, Orca provides real-time supply chain analytics that enable businesses to make faster, informed decisions. Focused on innovation and customer-centric services, Orca offers a holistic view, reliable data, and intelligent recommendations to optimize logistics and streamline operations. With no upfront or monthly fees, Orca is committed to adding value for its clients and stands out as a leading freight audit company in the industry.

Role Description

This is a full-time hybrid role for a Junior or Intermediate DevOps Engineer based in London, Ontario, with some flexibility for remote work. The successful candidate will assist in designing, deploying, and maintaining cloud-based infrastructure and automation pipelines. Responsibilities include supporting continuous integration/continuous deployment (CI/CD) processes, monitoring system performance, troubleshooting issues, improving system reliability, and collaborating with cross-functional teams to enhance development and operations workflows.

Qualifications

  • Knowledge of cloud platforms (e.g., AWS, Azure, GCP) and infrastructure as code tools (e.g., Terraform, CloudFormation).
  • Proficiency in CI/CD tools and pipelines (e.g., Jenkins, GitLab, GitHub Actions).
  • Experience with scripting languages (e.g., Python, Bash) for automation and task optimization.
  • Familiarity with containerization tools (e.g., Docker) and orchestration platforms (e.g., Kubernetes).
  • Understanding of system monitoring tools, logging, and performance metrics analysis.
  • Strong problem-solving skills and ability to troubleshoot complex systems effectively.
  • Excellent communication and collaboration aptitude to work within cross-functional teams.
  • Degree in Computer Science, Software Engineering, or a related field is preferred.
  • Experience with version control systems (e.g., Git) is an advantage.
