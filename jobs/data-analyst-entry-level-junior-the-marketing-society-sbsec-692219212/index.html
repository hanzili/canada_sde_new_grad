Data Analyst (Entry-Level / Junior) – Role Description & Qualifications

The Data Analyst (Entry-Level / Junior) is responsible for supporting data-driven decision-making by collecting, organizing, analyzing, and interpreting data from various sources. This role focuses on transforming raw information into meaningful insights that help improve business performance, operational efficiency, and strategic planning.

Key responsibilities include cleaning and validating datasets, preparing reports and dashboards, and monitoring key performance indicators (KPIs). The analyst assists in identifying trends, patterns, and anomalies within data while ensuring accuracy and consistency across reporting processes. The role also involves supporting data visualization efforts and presenting findings in a clear and structured manner to stakeholders.

A core aspect of this role is maintaining reliable and organized data that supports business operations and analytical reporting. The analyst collaborates with different teams to understand reporting needs, improve data processes, and contribute to continuous improvement initiatives through analytical insights.

Qualifications include a foundational understanding of data analysis, statistics, and reporting concepts. Familiarity with spreadsheets, SQL, and data visualization tools is beneficial. Basic knowledge of data cleaning, data structures, and analytical problem-solving is important. Strong attention to detail, logical thinking, and organizational skills are essential for handling data accurately and efficiently.

The ideal candidate is curious, analytical, and eager to develop technical and business analysis skills in a fast-paced environment. Strong communication skills, willingness to learn new technologies, and the ability to work with structured and unstructured data are important for success in this role.

  • This position is suited for individuals who are motivated to build a strong foundation in analytics while contributing to data-driven business decisions and operational improvements.
