Junior Sales Coordinator

at Longhouse Branding & Marketing

Full Description

About Longhouse

Founded in 2018, Longhouse Branding & Marketing helps business and community leaders win back their time and focus on what needs them most. We dedicate the time to taking Branding, Web Design, Advertising, SEO, and AEO off of our Partners to-do lists. Today, we've worked with over 850 businesses across 92+ industries, contributing an estimated $785 million in revenue.

Our Vision: A world where leaders can focus on making their impact.

Our Mission: To win time back for business and community leaders by simplifying to-do lists, allowing them to focus on what needs them most.

About the Role

Job type: Part-Time with opportunity for Full-Time (in-person)

Location: Langley, British Columbia

Compensation: $21.11 - $28.67 per hour

The Junior Sales Coordinator will often be the first voice many prospective partners hear from Longhouse. The first interaction sets the tone, builds trust, and shapes whether someone feels ready to take the next step.

This role supports the sales team (who we call Growth Consultants) by keeping our pipeline organized, qualifying leads, preparing documents, and making sure Growth Consultants walk into every session with the context they need. The person in this role is a self-starter, they’re motivated to learn, and they have a passion for helping others.

This role provides an opportunity for career growth, with a path to broader sales responsibilities and leadership.

What You'll Do

Pipeline & Activity Organization

  • Maintain Pipedrive so all deals are in the right stage and organized
  • Monitor the leads inbox and qualify incoming leads
  • Move deals to the next stage when a trigger occurs, like a signed agreement or a completed meeting

Connecting With Future Partners

  • Possibly taking the first call from incoming leads and assessing how Longhouse can help.
  • Ask the right questions to help us understand their timeline, budget, and goals.

Communication and Follow-Up

  • Send follow-up emails to potential partners in the pipeline
  • Prepare intro and project handoff emails when deals move between stages or team members

Prepare Documents & Conduct Research

  • Prepare authorization forms, draft agreements, and create intake material
  • Draft emails on behalf of the Growth Consultant
  • Compile pre-meeting research on incoming leads, covering their website, current marketing activity, and relevant background

Daily Preparation

  • Review Growth Consultants' calendars to help prepare for upcoming sessions
  • Confirm phones are on and ready before the day's first call
  • Identify anything from the previous day that needs attention before the team dives in

What We're Looking For

Skills & Experience

  • Familiarity with CRM tools like Pipedrive, or similar platforms
  • Strong written communication, including the ability to write professional emails
  • Comfortable and confident speaking to potential partners on the phone
  • Ability to manage a shared inbox with attention to detail
  • Comfortable doing research and creating summaries
  • Ability to be pro-active and support the sales team with upcoming meetings, RFPs and questions coming in from potential partners.

How to Apply

Apply directly through LinkedIn, or send your resume and a short note about why this role interests you, your experience, and how you feel you can make an impact to careers@longhouse.co with the subject line: Junior Sales Coordinator Application.

We'll be in touch with qualified candidates to schedule a phone screen.

Our Hiring Process

Here's what to expect after you apply:

  1. Application review. We review every application and reach out to candidates whose experience and working style look like a strong fit.
  2. Phone screen. A short call (15 to 20 minutes) with a member of our team to learn more about you and share more about the role.
  3. Interview. An in-person conversation at our Langley office with the hiring team.
  4. Reference check. We'll ask for two to three professional references before extending an offer.
  5. Offer. If it's a fit on both sides, we'll put together an offer and walk you through the details.

We aim to keep the process straightforward and respectful of your time. If you have questions at any stage, don't hesitate to ask.
