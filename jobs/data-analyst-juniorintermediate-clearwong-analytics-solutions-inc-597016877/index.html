Job Summary

Why Join ClearWong Analytics & Solutions Inc.?

Are you passionate about turning data into clear, actionable insights? ClearWong Analytics & Solutions Inc. is a growing analytics-focused company supporting research, business, and technology teams across multiple industries. We’re looking for a Data Analyst who enjoys working with data, collaborating with stakeholders, and contributing to real-world decision-making.

This role is ideal for candidates who want stability, skill growth, and flexibility while working in a professional and supportive environment.

What You’ll Do

  • Collect, clean, and analyze structured datasets from multiple sources
  • Create reports, dashboards, and summaries for internal teams
  • Identify trends, patterns, and insights to support business decisions
  • Work with stakeholders to understand data requirements
  • Maintain data accuracy and documentation
  • Support ad hoc analysis and ongoing reporting needs

What We’re Looking For

  • Experience with data analysis, reporting, or analytics (entry to intermediate level)
  • Proficiency in tools such as Excel, Google Sheets, SQL, or BI tools
  • Strong attention to detail and problem-solving skills
  • Ability to explain data insights clearly to non-technical audiences
  • Comfortable working independently and meeting deadlines

We welcome candidates with diverse educational and professional backgrounds. Training and onboarding support are provided.

Work Arrangement

  • Hybrid or remote options available (within Canada)
  • Standard business hours with some flexibility
  • Collaborative and inclusive team culture

Why Work With Us

  • Clear role expectations and realistic workloads
  • Opportunities to grow your analytics and reporting skills
  • Supportive management and structured processes
  • Long-term stability in a professional environment

Apply Today

If you’re ready to build your analytics career with a growing, data-driven organization, we encourage you to apply today. Qualified candidates will be contacted for next steps.
