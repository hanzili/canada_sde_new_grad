About the Role

Assist procurement, due diligence, vendor compliance

About BGIS

Global facility management and real estate services

Full Description

Who We Are

BGIS is a leading provider of customized facility management and real estate services. With our combined team of over 6,500 globally, we relentlessly focus on enabling innovation through the services we deliver, while actively looking for new opportunities that will enable innovation for our clients’ businesses. Globally, we manage over 320 million square feet of client portfolios across 30,000+ locations in North America, Europe, Middle East, Australia and Asia. Further information is available at www.bgis.com

Contract Dates: May-August 2026

Work Arrangement: hybrid, office in Ottawa

SUMMARY

The Accounting/Reporting Assistant reports to the Procurement Manager and provides support in areas of procurement including but not limited to: Anti-Bribery and Corruption, Financial Due Diligence, process compliance and governance, purchasing card and vendor compliance.

KEY DUTIES & RESPONSIBILITIES

  • Facilitate Anti-Bribery and Corruption due diligence checks where and when required
  • Facilitate Financial Due Diligence
  • Perform FDD on potential new vendors on a where and when required basis
  • Perform FDD on existing new vendors annually
  • Prepare savings Glidepath spreadsheet from procurement logs
  • Collaborate with the Procurement team to track and report RP1 vendor compliance.
  • Assistance in responding to inquiries from vendors/Procurement regarding vendor compliance
  • MERX Admin support
  • Reporting for RP1 dashboard
  • Vendor modification
  • User additions/inactivation’s
  • Dashboard – provide support to prepare and distribute RP1 dashboard KPI
  • Coordinate Purchasing Cards for RP1 account; maintain cardholder profiles
  • Complete audit on Purchasing Card activity
  • Responsible for responding to inquiries made by internal and external stakeholders, including Facility Managers, Financial Controllers, Sourcing managers and vendors.
  • Support the RP1 Procurement Manager with ongoing Audits of the Procurement process(es)
  • Support the RP1 Procurement Manager as and when required
  • Other duties as assigned

KNOWLEDGE & SKILL

  • College diploma in business or other relevant program
  • 1 – 3 years related experience
  • Computer proficiency in MS Word, Excel, PowerPoint and Outlook.
  • Proficiency with Oracle application is an asset.
  • Bilingualism (French) is an asset
  • Strong interpersonal skills.
  • Excellent communication skills (both written and verbal).
  • Strong customer focus.
  • Ability to work both independently and in a team environment.
  • Strong organizational skills, with the ability to multi-task under deadline pressure.
  • Attention to detail.

Licenses and/or Professional Accreditation

None required

This is a full-time, contract position with a salary range of $19 - $23 per hour. The starting salary will be based on the successful candidate’s competencies, including but not limited to experience, education and performance related to this role.
