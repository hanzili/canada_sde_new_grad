About Sobeys

Full Description

Ready to Transform Retail? Embark on a rewarding career with Sobeys Inc., celebrated among Canada’s Top 100 employers where your unique contributions drive success.

Sobeys is full of exciting opportunities, and we are always looking for bright new talent to join our team! We currently have a full-time opportunity for a Financial Analyst, Supply Chain to join our Finance team.

The Financial Analyst, Supply Chain will provide financial analysis and business decision support to our partners. We are looking for someone who will build strong relationships with our stakeholders and who loves to crunch numbers and tell stories.

This role can be based out of one of our Stellarton offices, and the candidate must be Bilingual, French.

Here’s Where You’ll Be Focusing

  • Build and maintain monthly financial reporting to present actuals versus budget/forecast and provide meaningful and relevant variance analysis and commentary to business partners
  • Build and maintain weekly reporting on key financial and operational metrics and effectively communicate results to business partners
  • Prepare and post accruals and journal entries.
  • Identify risks and opportunities to address, to drive savings
  • Perform complex financial analysis on key metrics, effectively communicating insights and financial recommendations to business partners to drive decision making.
  • Build financial models to understand implications of decisions and against which to measure results.
  • Drive continuous improvement in finance processes and reporting activities. Put forward suggestions to improve functional area processes while providing excellent level of customer service to all levels of the business
  • Adhere to GAAP/IFRS, Sobeys Accounting Standards & policies and CEO/CFO Certification requirements
  • Prepare various ad-hoc reporting requests, as needed

What You Will Bring To The Table

  • An Undergraduate Degree and a minimum of 2 years of relevant accounting or finance experience
  • Holding a recognized professional accounting designation, such as CPA would be an asset
  • Advanced Excel skills (comfortable working with large data sets and using pivot tables, v-lookup, etc.)
  • Working knowledge of SAP would be an asset
  • Effective communication skills, both orally and in writing
  • Ability to establish and maintain solid interpersonal relationships and to work well within a team
  • Analytical mind, with the ability to analyze large data sets and tell stories from the data
  • Takes initiative, while working autonomously and resourcefully
  • Bilingual French/English.
