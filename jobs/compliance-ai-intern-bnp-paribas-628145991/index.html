The position at a glance

The Compliance Function is an integrated function in BNP Paribas. This internship is in the Compliance Nearshoring Platform, specifically within the team in charge of large-scale strategic Transformation projects of the Americas Region across all types of Compliance risks.

In detail

What You’ll Do (4 Month Internship)

Support AI enabled projects by helping structure business problems into clear analytical questions

Participate in client’s problem solving, notably conducting exploratory analyses (e.g. on datasets, processes, etc.), identifying patterns, and drawing actionable insights

Define relevant AI / analytics approaches applicable to defined use cases

Contribute to decision support deliverables (slides, documents) for different audiences

Translate technical findings into clear, concise messages for non technical stakeholders

Participate in project governance meetings and support tracking of milestones and risks

What You’ll Learn

How AI and data science are applied in real business contexts with real life constraints

How to move from ambiguous problems to structured, decision ready insights

How multidisciplinary teams (project, SMEs, IT, etc.) collaborate in practice

How analytical work informs strategic and operational decisions

Looking Ahead

This 4 month internship is designed as a focused, hands on experience. High performing interns may be considered for full-time opportunities.

Work conditions: This position provides for standard working conditions in an office and a normal work schedule from Monday to Friday.

The strengths and skills that will help you succeed

Required

Enrolled in a Bachelor’s or Master’s program (Computer Science, Data Science, Information Systems, Statistics, or related).

Experience with AI/ML models.

Strong analytical mindset and attention to detail.

Good written & verbal communication (English; French is a plus)*.

Appetite to work in a project-based mode.

Nice to have

Prior experience in a financial services or regulatory setting

Experience with AI/ML models for Financial Services

Introductory Python (pandas, scikit learn).

Experience with data management (data analysis, data quality, or governance).

Basic SQL (SELECT, JOIN) and familiarity with relational databases.

Familiarity with SharePoint site management.

  • Given the vast majority of our clients, both internal and external, are based outside of Quebec and Canada, specific language requirements may apply. Professional working proficiency in English language is required.

What’s in it for you

In addition to competitive compensation, hybrid work arrangements are available for most positions. In-office presence is required a minimum of 3 days per week, one of which must be on a Monday and/or a Friday. BNP Paribas provides excellent training and personal development programs, as well as opportunities for career development within the company and internationally.

What you need to know

We will review candidates as they apply, so don’t wait to submit your application;

BNP Paribas is committed to accessibility and inclusion. During the recruitment process, accommodation needs are available at all times for candidates. You will have the chance to make a request for an accommodation during your application.

You must be legally eligible to work in the Greater Montreal area and, if applicable, hold a valid work or study permit. Physical presence in BNP Paribas’ office(s) is an essential function of this position;

If you are applying and accepted to a position which requires working in/for the U.S. securities industry, you will be required to provide your fingerprints and undergo additional background checks by the FBI. BNP Paribas Securities Corporation is required to maintain a supervisory program over the conduct of its Associated Persons; some of your personal data will be transmitted to the United States of America and made available to US regulators. About us

BNP Paribas is the top bank in the European Union and a major international banking establishment. Present in 63 countries, with more than 180,000 employees, the bank holds key positions in several areas of banking and financial services.

BNP Paribas’ mission is to contribute to a responsible and sustainable economy by financing and advising its clients according to the highest ethical standards, while striving to respond to essential concerns in terms of the environment, regional development and social inclusion.

Since 1961, BNP Paribas has supported large Canadian companies and institutions in their business development by offering a full range of specialized financial services and investment products.

With over 1,400 employees, BNP Paribas in Canada continues to attract experts from diverse fields as well as ambitious young talent from around the world. We are proud to offer our employees a rewarding and international workplace where they can build their professional careers by honing their skills, meeting challenges and enriching their knowledge of the financial industry.
