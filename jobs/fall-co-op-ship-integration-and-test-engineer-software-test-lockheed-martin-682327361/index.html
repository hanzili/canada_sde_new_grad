About the role

The successful candidate contributes to our key projects as a member of our Integration, Test, and Evaluation (IT&E) Cross Product Team (CPT) as follows:

  • Support functional testing of combat system software
  • Support regression testing of combat system software.
  • Develop test plans and procedures to verify software requirements in an Agile environment (DOORS/Jira/Redmine)
  • Develop and execute tests using the Lockheed Martin Canada Inc. (Lockheed Martin) Automated Test Tool (Python/Linux)
  • Document, assess, and test deficiencies using the Lockheed Martin defect tracking tool
  • Participate in Release and Sprint planning, as required.

What you bring to the role

  • Proceeding in a bachelor’s degree from an accredited university in Computer Science or related engineering discipline
  • Willing to support scheduling including evenings and weekends, as required
  • Aptitude for solving challenging problems in a team environment
  • High degree of initiative and autonomy
  • Able to obtain North Atlantic Treaty Organization (NATO) Secret security clearance in a timely manner.

Additional skills desired for the role

  • Software test automation experience
  • Familiarity with Microsoft Office suite
  • Familiarity with Linux
  • Familiarity with BASH and Python
  • Familiarity with Subversion and/or Git
  • Familiarity with Java and C++ is considered an asset
  • Familiarity with Dynamic Object-Oriented Requirements System (DOORS) is considered an asset
  • Experience with Agile Scrum methodology is considered an asset.

About us

Headquartered in Ottawa, Lockheed Martin Canada is the Canadian unit of Lockheed Martin Corporation, a global defense technology company driving innovation and advancing scientific discovery. Our all-domain mission solutions and 21st Century Security® vision accelerate the delivery of transformative technologies to ensure those we serve always stay ahead of ready. We operate major facilities in Ottawa, Montreal, Halifax, Calgary and Victoria and work on a wide range of programs from advanced naval technology products, aircraft sustainment, and unmanned systems software. This position is part of the Rotary and Mission Systems business area, where employees across Canada support engineering, systems integration, training, sustainment and in-service support programs for Canadian and international military customers across land, air and sea.
