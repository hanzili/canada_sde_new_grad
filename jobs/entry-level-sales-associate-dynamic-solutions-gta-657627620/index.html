About Dynamic Solutions GTA

Full Description

Dynamic Solutions

is seeking ambitious people to join our structured Graduate Sales Training Program.

This full-time role provides hands-on experience in B2C sales

, equipping you with the skills and knowledge needed to thrive in a fast-paced commercial environment while representing leading brands.

Our approach is practical and growth-focused. We believe sales is the foundation of a successful business career. You’ll gain direct client exposure, structured mentorship, and a clear progression pathway.

Key Responsibilities

  • Learn the fundamentals of B2C sales through structured training and mentorship
  • Develop skills in client engagement, negotiation, and account development
  • Take responsibility for your own sales pipeline and results
  • Exceed performance targets in a professional, team-driven environment
  • Progress into leadership and management roles within 12–18 months

Candidate Requirements

  • Strong communication and interpersonal skills
  • Competitive and resilient personality
  • No prior sales experience required — full training provided

Benefits

  • Comprehensive training program with personal mentorship
  • Performance based pay
  • Clear progression into leadership and management roles
  • Supportive, team-oriented environment that recognizes achievement
  • NOC A/B Opportunities

This program offers a unique opportunity for anyone looking to be rewarded for their hard work and to accelerate their professional development in sales and beyond.

Apply today to begin your journey with Dynamic Solutions GTA.

Dynamic Solutions is an equal-opportunity employer. All roles are full-time.
