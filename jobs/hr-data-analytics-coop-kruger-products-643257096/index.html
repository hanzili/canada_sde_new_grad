HR Data Analytics Coop

at Kruger Products

📍 Location Mississauga, ON L5W 0G8
📋 Details Co-op · 4mo · $23-$24/hr
📅 Posted Mar 13, 2026

About the Role

Analyze HR data, build dashboards and reports

About Kruger Products

Canadian tissue manufacturer, strong HR analytics focus

Full Description

At Kruger Products we have a shared vision for greatness. We manufacture some of North America’s most popular tissue brands - Cashmere®, Purex®, Scotties®’, SpongeTowels®, Bonterra®, White Cloud®, as well as products for use away from home.

We are leaders in our industry, leaders in our communities, and leaders in sustainability. We proudly have close to 3,000 employees across manufacturing plants in Quebec, British Columbia, Ontario, and Tennessee, as well as our headquarters in Mississauga, ON and Sales offices in North America.

What you will do

The HR & Data Analytics Co-op will support the Human Resources team by helping to manage employee data, assist with reporting, and contribute to process improvements. The role will focus on collecting and analyzing HR information to help the team make informed decisions and improve daily operations. This is a 4-month work term.

In this role, you can expect to:

  • Assist in updating HRIS, reporting, and maintaining accurate employee records
  • Collect, analyze, and report HR data to support decision-making and process improvements
  • Assist in developing dashboards and visualizations for HR metrics and trends
  • Design, build, and maintain regular and ad-hoc HR reports for leadership and stakeholders
  • Identify data patterns, trends, and insights to inform HR strategies and initiatives
  • Collaborate with HR and business teams to define analytics requirements and deliver actionable reports
  • Support data integrity and quality assurance processes for HR systems and analytics
  • Contribute to projects focused on HR process automation and data-driven improvements

What you will bring

  • Currently pursuing a College Diploma, or Bachelor's Human Resources, Data Analytics, or related field
  • Experience or coursework in data analytics, HR, or related areas
  • Strong analytical skills and proficiency with Excel, Power BI, or similar tools
  • Ability to handle confidential information with professionalism. Excellent communication and interpersonal skills
  • Interest in pursuing a career in HR and/or Data Analytics

At Kruger Products, we see greatness in everyone and what they bring to the organization. We monitor the market to ensure our total compensation practices are competitive. The starting range for this role is $23-$24hr – or higher, depending on your experience.
