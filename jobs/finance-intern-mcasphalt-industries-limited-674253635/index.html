About McAsphalt McAsphalt is the industry leader in Canada specializing in asphalt products and services. Founded in 1970 by Leo McArthur and John Carrick, the company has since grown to over 500 employees spanning 26 terminals across Canada. McAsphalt offers a wide range of asphalt products, including emulsions, cutbacks, polymer-modified, performance-graded, and roofing, and specialized transportation of those products by rail, land and water. Additionally, McAsphalt provides comprehensive services and technology solutions, encompassing paving, testing, research and development, engineering, and consulting.

Position Summary

The Accounting Intern supports our finance team by assisting with day-to-day accounting operations.

Accounting Intern – Duties & Responsibilities

  • Assist with data entry and maintenance of financial records in ERP or accounting systems
  • Support the team with reconciliations (intercompany accounts, expense reports, inventory or balance sheet accounts)
  • Help prepare monthly financial reports, variance analysis, and supporting schedules
  • Assist with updating and maintaining Excel tracking files and financial models
  • Support accounts payable and receivable processes, including invoice verification and documentation
  • Help organize and maintain financial documentation and audit support files
  • Assist in reviewing cost coding, expense reports, and basic financial transactions for accuracy
  • Provide general administrative support to the office team as needed
  • Participate in process improvement initiatives, including improving Excel templates and reporting tools
  • Work closely with operations teams to help gather basic operational and financial information

Qualifications

  • Recent graduate or currently completing a diploma/degree in Accounting, Finance, or Business
  • Strong Excel skills (pivot tables, formulas such as SUMIFS, VLOOKUP/XLOOKUP preferred)
  • Basic understanding of accounting principles and financial statements
  • Good business acumen and interest in understanding how operations impact financial results
  • Strong attention to detail and accuracy when working with numbers and data
  • Organized with the ability to manage multiple tasks and meet deadlines
  • Willingness to support administrative and routine tasks when required
  • Strong communication and teamwork skills
  • Positive attitude and willingness to learn in a fast-paced environment

Nice to Have (Not Required)

  • Exposure to ERP systems (SAP, JDE, Oracle, etc.)
  • Experience with Power BI or data visualization tools
  • Internship or part-time experience in accounting, finance, or business operations
