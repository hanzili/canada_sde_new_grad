About the Role

Engage customers, demo products, close flooring sales

About Pure Floors

Regional flooring retailer with design-focused sales

Full Description

We are seeking a dynamic and motivated Flooring Sales Representative to join our team. The ideal candidate will be responsible for engaging with customers, demonstrating flooring products (hardwood, laminate, vinyl, tile, carpet, etc.), and driving sales growth across our retail showroom and job sites. This role offers an exciting opportunity to develop flooring sales expertise, enhance customer service skills, and contribute to the company’s success through effective communication and product knowledge.

Duties

  • Engage with customers to understand their flooring needs (room usage, foot traffic, subfloor conditions, moisture concerns, design preferences) and recommend suitable products
  • Conduct product demonstrations by showing flooring samples, explaining wear layers, durability, acoustics, maintenance requirements, and installation methods
  • Upsell additional products or services such as underlayment, transition strips, moisture barriers, premium padding, or professional installation packages
  • Provide excellent customer service by addressing inquiries about warranties, product care, delivery timelines, and resolving any post-purchase concerns
  • Utilize strong communication skills to build rapport with homeowners, contractors, property managers, and interior designers
  • Manage time effectively to meet monthly sales targets, follow up with leads, and complete daily showroom responsibilities efficiently
  • Demonstrate proficiency in flooring technology, including explaining differences between SPC/WPC vinyl, engineered vs. solid hardwood, low-VOC carpets, and locking vs. glue-down systems
  • Perform basic math calculations accurately (e.g., measuring room square footage, calculating waste factors, converting between square feet and square yards, and preparing estimates)

Requirements

  • Bilingual or multilingual abilities are highly desirable to serve diverse customer bases
  • Prior experience in flooring sales, retail home improvement sales, merchandising flooring displays, or construction materials sales is preferred
  • Strong communication skills, including phone etiquette (handling quote requests and installation follow-ups) and active listening
  • Organizational skills to manage multiple customer estimates, sample checkouts, and order tracking in a fast-paced showroom environment
  • Ability to perform product demos confidently and clearly explain technical details (e.g., AC rating for laminate, plank thickness, radiant heat compatibility)
  • Customer service orientation with a friendly, professional demeanor while helping customers compare hundreds of flooring options
  • Basic math skills for measuring rooms and calculating material quantities
  • Time management skills to prioritize walk-in traffic, scheduled appointments, and phone/email inquiries throughout shifts
  • Experience with cashiering, cash handling, and POS systems (including creating work orders and purchase orders) is advantageous
  • Knowledge of upselling techniques such as offering premium padding, waterproof upgrades, or whole-home vs. single-room projects to enhance sales performance

Benefits

  • On-site parking
  • Store discount (e.g., on flooring remnants, tools, or installation supplies)

Work Location: In person
