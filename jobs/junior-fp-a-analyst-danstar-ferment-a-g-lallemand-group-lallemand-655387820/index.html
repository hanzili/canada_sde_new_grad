JUNIOR FP&A ANALYST - DANSTAR FERMENT A.G (LALLEMAND GROUP)

MAIN FUNCTIONS

As part of its growth, Danstar, an entity of the Lallemand Group, is creating a Junior FP&A Analyst position.

Reporting to a Senior FP&A Analyst, the position holder will be involved in all reporting, financial analysis, and budget planning activities, in support of Business Unit controllers and the consolidation team.

This role offers broad exposure to operations, industrial challenges, and an international multi‑entity environment, with genuine opportunities for learning and development.

Responsibilities of the Junior FP&A Analyst:

  • Prepare monthly financial closings and analyze results (vs. budget and prior year).
  • Ensure monthly inventory valuation and production cost calculations, as well as variance analysis.
  • Contribute to Net‑OPEX (SG&A) cost control.
  • Actively participate in the full budgeting process (sales, costs, transfer pricing, SG&A, etc.).
  • Develop and enhance profitability and performance analyses for the supported entities and BUs.
  • Participate in ad hoc analyses, cross‑functional projects, and continuous improvement initiatives.

Required Qualifications

  • 1 to 3 years of experience in accounting, FP&A, or management control.
  • Strong understanding of cost accounting, standard costs, and variance analysis.
  • Excellent command of Excel (advanced functions, pivot tables); Power BI is an asset.
  • Initial experience in budget preparation or monitoring.
  • Experience in a multi‑entity and multi‑currency environment is an asset.
  • Knowledge of an ERP and/or reporting tool is an asset.

Key Skills

  • Excellent command of both French and English, spoken and written.
  • Autonomy, proactivity, and strong priority management.
  • Team spirit and a business partner mindset.
  • Rigor, reliability, and strong attention to detail.
  • Ability to adapt with agility in a dynamic and constantly evolving environment.
  • Strong interest in financial analysis.

This position represents an excellent opportunity for a junior analyst seeking to develop solid FP&A expertise within a fast‑growing, international industrial group.

ORGANIZATION

Lallemand is a privately held Canadian company founded in the late 19th century, which develops, produces, and markets microorganisms for various markets. The administrative offices of the parent company are in Montreal, Canada. Today, Lallemand employs more than 5,000 people working in more than 45 countries on 5 continents.

Lallemand's corporate division encompasses all of the support services offered to our branches. Its goal is to optimize internal processes, ensure that your operation is running smoothly, and that your strategy is well thought-out.

Our corporate branch's head office is located in Montreal, Canada, and relies on a team that specializes in: Management; Finance and accounting; Engineering; Research and Development (R&D); Human Resources (HR); and Information Technology (IT). If you want to help bring enthusiasm, collaboration and innovation to our workplace, we'd love to get to know you!
