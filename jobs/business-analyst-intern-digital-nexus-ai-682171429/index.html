About Digital Nexus AI

AI software firm; global client base

Full Description

Digital Nexus AI is a software development company specializing in AI, Machine Learning, and emerging technologies, serving global clients across Hospitality and Finance.

Role Overview

We are looking for a Business Analyst Intern to support requirement gathering, process analysis, documentation, and user acceptance testing while working with cross-functional teams.

Key Responsibilities

  • Analyze business processes and gather requirements
  • Create documentation and UML use case diagrams
  • Participate in client meetings and presentations
  • Collaborate with diverse, cross-functional teams and actively share ideas and insights.
  • Work in an inclusive environment with team members from varied backgrounds and perspectives.
  • Assist in User Acceptance Testing (UAT)
  • Prepare reports and communicate insights

Requirements

  • Strong analytical and communication skills
  • Basic knowledge of business analysis/SDLC
  • Familiarity with UML (preferred)
  • Ability to explain concepts to non-technical stakeholders

What You’ll Gain

  • Hands-on experience in real-world AI/software projects
  • Exposure to international clients
  • Experience working in a diverse and collaborative team environment
  • Practical business analysis skills
