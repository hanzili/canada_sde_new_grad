About the Role

Create content, execute campaigns, analyze markets

About Ainger Group

Tech-driven roofing company with growth mindset

Full Description

Overview

We’re hiring a Marketing Specialist to support the execution of high-impact marketing initiatives across content, campaigns, and brand development.

This is not a passive role. You’ll be responsible for producing content that drives traffic, leads, and conversion—while working closely with the Marketing Manager to implement a structured, scalable marketing system.

We are a fast-growing roofing company taking a tech-first approach to an otherwise traditional industry. Our focus is on building a modern, process-driven, and data-backed marketing engine that can scale.

What You’ll Be Responsible For

Content Production (Core Function)

  • Create written content across blogs, landing pages, website copy, email campaigns, and paid ads
  • Translate messaging into high-converting formats tailored to each channel
  • Maintain consistency in tone, clarity, and brand voice across all assets

Campaign Execution Support

  • Assist in launching and optimizing marketing campaigns (email, paid media, organic content)
  • Support funnel development (top → mid → bottom) with relevant content
  • Help manage content calendars and publishing schedules

Market & Competitive Research

  • Research industry trends, customer behavior, and competitor positioning
  • Identify content opportunities and gaps that can drive qualified traffic
  • Contribute insights that improve messaging, offers, and campaign performance

Brand Implementation

  • Help execute brand strategy across all touchpoints
  • Ensure consistency in messaging, positioning, and visual direction
  • Support ongoing refinement based on performance data and market feedback

What We’re Looking For

Baseline Competence

  • Strong writing skills (clear, structured, persuasive—not fluffy)
  • Ability to take a concept and turn it into usable marketing output quickly
  • Comfortable working across multiple content formats and channels

Mindset

  • Execution-focused: you ship work consistently and improve through iteration
  • Curious: you actively research, test, and learn what works
  • Process-oriented: you can follow systems and help improve them over time

Technical Comfort

  • Comfortable using modern marketing tools (CMS, email platforms, basic analytics)
  • Open to leveraging AI tools to increase speed and output quality

Nice-to-Have (Not Required)

  • Experience writing conversion-focused content (ads, landing pages, emails)
  • Basic understanding of SEO or paid media principles
  • Exposure to CRM or marketing automation tools

Why Join Us

  • Real Growth Environment: You’ll be part of a company scaling aggressively, not maintaining status quo
  • Tech-First Approach: We actively use AI, automation, and modern tools to move faster and smarter
  • Structured Marketing System: Clear processes, not chaos—built for repeatable performance
  • Industry Disruption: We’re modernizing roofing with a focus on customer experience and efficiency
  • Team Environment: Collaborative, high-energy, and execution-driven
  • Benefits: Comprehensive health benefits included

What Success Looks Like in This Role

  • You consistently produce content that gets used (not shelved)
  • Your work contributes to measurable outputs (traffic, leads, engagement)
  • You improve speed and quality over time through feedback and iteration
  • You become a reliable execution arm for the marketing function

Pay: $20.00-$25.00 per hour

Benefits:

  • Company events
  • Dental care
  • Extended health care
  • Life insurance
  • On-site parking
  • Vision care

Work Location: In person
