What is the Mercedes-Benz Canada Make Your Mark Internship (MYMI) Program?
Mercedes-Benz is the world’s first automotive brand, and remains at the cutting-edge of sustainability, design, digitalization, connectivity, technology, fashion, and popular culture. The Make Your Mark Internship (MYMI) Program is a Mercedes-Benz Canada internship program where students in their final year of studies or who have completed their studies in the past 12 months, can gain experience with an exciting brand and organization. Interns in the program have the opportunity to make an impact and apply their skills and ideas, supporting the team on a variety of projects and initiatives.

Mercedes-Benz Canada needs stars like you!
The iconic three-pointed star is always looking for the best and brightest. We are seeking individuals who want to be in the driver’s seat of transformation, working alongside talented colleagues who represent the Mercedes-Benz values.

Do you want to be part of writing the next chapter of the Mercedes-Benz story?

A day in the life:

  • Build lightweight workflow apps using PowerApps to improve team efficiency and automation.
  • Use Microsoft Copilot to accelerate analysis, documentation, and AI assisted productivity.
  • Support building and evaluating machine learning models to generate predictions, insights, and data driven decisions.
  • Experience with LLMs to prototype intelligent use cases like summarization, classification, and automated insight generation.
  • Build prototype Agentic AI workflows using Databricks Agents to automate data tasks, model training, and reasoning based operations.

Mercedes-Benz Canada is committed to fostering an inclusive, equitable, and accessible environment where all employees and customers feel valued, respected, and supported. While we thank every applicant for their interest in joining our iconic brand, we will only contact applicants selected for an interview.

If you require accommodation because of a disability, please reach out to mbcrecruitment@mercedes-benz.ca and we will work with you to meet your needs throughout the recruitment, assessment, and selection process as applicable.

Vacancy Status: This posting is for approved internship positions that are current vacancies and will be filled as part of the Mercedes-Benz Canada Make Your Mark Internship (MYMI) Program for 2026.

Qualifikationen

General Eligibility and Qualification Requirements:

  • Eligibility to Work in Canada: Candidates must be eligible to work in Canada, as this role does not support work visa/permit sponsorship.
  • Graduation Requirement: Candidates must have graduated in 2025 or expect to graduate by April 2026 with a specialization in Information Technology. As part of the onboarding process, the selected candidate will be required to submit a copy of their transcripts.
  • Communication Skills: Excellent oral and written communication skills are required.
  • MS Office Proficiency: Proficiency in MS Office (Outlook, Excel, and Word) is required. Skills testing will be part of the recruitment process.
  • Technical Skills:
  • Must have
    • Basic knowledge of Microsoft Power Platform (including PowerApps, Power Automate, and Copilot)
    • Foundational understanding of SQL (Postgres or Databricks SQL) with the ability to write simple queries for data analysis.
  • Nice to have
    • Basic Python programming skills for data analysis or simple automation tasks.
    • Exposure to Databricks or similar cloud data platforms (e.g., Azure Synapse, Snowflake) is an asset.
    • Introductory knowledge or academic exposure to AI/ML concepts, including large language models (LLMs) or common machine learning frameworks.
  • Full-Time Contract Role: This position is a full-time contract role requiring 40 hours per week.
  • Recruitment Process: The recruitment process will begin immediately and may include a combination of an online skill testing questionnaire, and a panel interview (either in person or via Teams).
  • Work Arrangement: This is a hybrid role requiring employees to work on-site 2-3 days per week.
