Your work environment at EXP

Kick-start your career with hands on field experience!

As a Geotechnical Engineering Student (Co-op) at EXP, you’ll gain practical exposure to geotechnical and environmental fieldwork while supporting a wide range of construction and infrastructure projects. This is a great role for students studying civil engineering, environmental engineering, geoscience, or related fields looking to gain practical experience and apply their academic knowledge to real world projects.

What a day at EXP has in store for you

  • Conduct soil compaction testing using a soil density gauge
  • Perform concrete field tests (slump, air content) and cast concrete test cylinders
  • Observe asphalt placement and conduct sampling and compaction testing
  • Fieldwork related to environmental and geotechnical investigations, including soil, groundwater, and environmental sampling
  • Environmental monitoring and data collection in accordance with project requirements and regulations
  • Assisting with site assessments, inspections, and remediation activities
  • Recording and organizing field data and observations
  • Supporting report preparation, data analysis, and documentation
  • Maintaining field equipment and following health and safety procedures Collaborating with project teams and supporting day to day project needs

What your experience looks like

  • Currently enrolled in Civil Engineering, Environmental Engineering, Earth Sciences or related program
  • Interest in construction, geotechnical testing, or environmental fieldwork
  • Ability to problem-solve and think proactively on site
  • Comfortable reading and interpreting drawings/specifications (training provided)
  • Strong communication skills and ability to interact professionally with contractors and team members
  • Solid report writing and attention to detail
  • Valid driver’s license and access to a reliable vehicle for travel to job sites Physically able to lift/carry equipment such as soil density gauges and concrete cylinders

What We Offer

  • Hands on experience on real geo environmental engineering projects
  • Mentorship and support from experienced professionals
  • Exposure to field and office based work
  • A supportive and collaborative team environment Opportunity to build technical skills and industry experience

Our story

With a mission to understand, innovate, partner and deliver, EXP provides engineering, architecture, design and consulting services to the world’s built and natural environments.

Our heritage dates back to 1906, when the earliest of EXP’s predecessor companies started its engineering infrastructure practice. Today, thousands of creative professionals across EXP work together to deliver extraordinary experiences year after year.
