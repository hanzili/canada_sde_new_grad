Full Description

At Trojan Technologies, we believe success for all comes from the contributions of every single one of us. Trojan is proud to be bringing water and environmental businesses together under the unified purpose of safeguarding the world’s most vital natural resources. Want to ensure the world has the clean water it needs? Then become part of the team that’s making it happen.

POSTING

In this role, you will:

  • Assist with the design of power electronics for UV light sources, working under the supervision of a senior power electronics designer.
  • Research, analyze and apply conversion topologies and control circuits for power circuit designs.
  • Prototype, test, and troubleshoot power electronics assemblies.
  • Support thermal design, analysis, and testing.
  • Create and maintain documentation including schematic, bills of materials, drawings, and technical reports.
  • Support full development cycle from concept through volume manufacturing
  • Assist with design, construction, and deployment of test fixtures.
  • Use programming tools to program and debug micro-controller devices
  • Apply systematic problem-solving process.

Are you qualified? The essential requirements of the job include:

  • Enrolled in post-secondary education, Electrical / Electronics / Computer Engineering or Technology program with interest in the following technology areas:
  • ADC/DAC power conversion circuit design
  • Linear and switching power supply design
  • Application of microcontrollers
  • Design for safety and EMC compliance
  • PCB design
  • Selection and application of magnetic devices.
  • Technical documentation skills (for Design Specifications, Block Diagrams and Test Plans.

It would be a plus if you also possess previous experience in:

  • Effective written and verbal communications skills.
  • Attention to detail and commitment to quality.
  • Able to work collaboratively with a team where striving for continuous improvement is expected and valued.
