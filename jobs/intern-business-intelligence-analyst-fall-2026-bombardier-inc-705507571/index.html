About the Role

Build PowerBI dashboards; analyze data for insights

About Bombardier Inc.

Global aviation leader; strong analytics culture

Full Description

Innovation is in our DNA… is it in yours?
Bombardier is a global leader in aviation, creating innovative and game-changing planes. Our products and services provide world-class experiences that set new standards in passenger comfort, energy efficiency, reliability and safety. We are a global organization focused on working together with a team spirit.

Your boarding pass will include…

Several conferences, including:

  • Meet An Executive
  • Women Taking Flight

Learning more about Bombardier, including:

  • Bombardier Products conference
  • Visits of the Bombardier sites
  • Bombardier Academy of Learning

Many social/networking opportunities, including:

  • Volunteering
  • Networking for Success
  • 5 à 7, Potluck, and much more!

What are your contributions to the team?

  • Design, implement and maintain dynamic PowerBI dashboards to transform complex data into compelling visual stories, driving actionable insights and strategic decision-making
  • Conduct competitive intelligence to monitor industry trends, benchmark against competitors, and support strategic decision-making
  • Strategic projects to grow Bombardier's offering including:
  • Planning, coordinating and preparing presentations on behalf of senior leaders
  • Enabling Leadership team to make data driven decisions by developing forecasting models, performing analytics and making insightful recommendations
  • Supporting the Services Sales team before and after sales campaigns with customers
  • Leading the gathering of content and sending out the Daily Intel Alerts newsletter
  • Providing industry updates on a weekly basis
  • Analyze and draft summaries on economic data
  • Improving and/or automating the team’s tools and processes to increase quality and productivity

During your internship, you will learn:

  • Develop your analytical and presentation skills
  • Grow your Aviation network
  • Work with PowerBI and Alteryx

How to thrive in this role?

  • You are currently completing a Bachelor’s degree or Master’s degree in Business Administration, Business Analytics/Intelligence, Economics, Management, Marketing or related field of study in a recognized University
  • You have completed a minimum of 2 year of studies in this program (for Bachelors)
  • You can work autonomously by being well organized and planning your time
  • You can take the initiative to do things without it being asked
  • You have good working knowledge of the Microsoft Office Suite, including very strong skills in PowerPoint and Excel
  • You know some Business Intelligence tools like PowerBI, Alteryx, Tableau, etc
  • You have a good level of written and speaking French and/or English
  • Knowledge of building forecasting model is an asset
  • You are a good team worker and are highly reliable
  • You have a clear drive for results

​
Boarding Information:

  • Location: Administrative Centre (CA)
  • Duration: 8 months
  • Flexible workplace-Hybrid

It is important to note that our internship opportunities are open to students only, not new graduates. All our interns may be required to occasionally travel outside of Canada for training/work purpose.
