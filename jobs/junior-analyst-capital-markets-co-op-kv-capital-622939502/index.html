About KV Capital

At KV Capital, we have established ourselves as industry leaders in real estate debt and investment, driven by our unwavering commitment to excellence, trusted partnerships, and innovative solutions. Since our founding in 2006, we have expanded our business across three connected areas of service: real estate private equity, real estate lending, and private equity investments in real estate supply chain businesses. Managing a robust portfolio of +$1.8 billion in funded investments, accompanied by +$750 million in assets under management, our dedicated team of finance experts is driven by a shared goal to cultivate prosperity for our clients, investment partners, and communities. We have office locations in Edmonton, Calgary, and Toronto, and continue to expand and extend our reach nationally.

Capital Markets

The objective of the Capital Markets team is to drive organizational growth by successfully interfacing with diverse sources of capital to grow our three core business divisions: Real Estate Debt, Real Estate Equity, and Building Products Private Equity. Our Capital Markets team touches all facets of capital markets and aspires to create long-term relationships and deliver value for our clients, partners, and team members. This team allows the successful candidate to have impact and learning across KV Capital‚Äôs various operations to ensure the long-term growth of the firm is supported by fostering existing capital relationships and growing new ones

The Opportunity

The successful Co-op student will play a pivotal role in supporting the Capital Markets team in strategy execution and capital raising objectives for KV Capital (KVC). They will work closely with internal stakeholders to contribute to the overall success of KVC‚Äôs capital-raising efforts. The successful candidate will have an interest in the Capital Markets field, be a critical thinker, have strong financial acumen, and have an understanding of macroeconomic themes.

Your Key Responsibilities

  • Assist the Capital Markets team with the creation of pitch books and client presentations.
  • Undertake research projects for various strategic and tactical initiatives.
  • Analyze market conditions and economic data to forecast and assess the impact of external factors on capital markets strategies.
  • Monitor fundamental economic, industrial, and corporate developments relevant to KVC.
  • Support the execution of capital markets raises and transactions.
  • Maintain databases of capital raise transactions, CRM and compile regular investor lead activity reports.
  • Liaise with the Investor Relations team to gather investor feedback and contribute to the distribution of transactions.
  • Collaborate across KVC to understand the capital-raising needs of each business unit.
  • Support the execution of strategic initiatives, including tracking metrics and milestones.
  • Gather and format data into regular and ad-hoc reports and dashboards.

  • A willingness to embrace learning in a dynamic environment that encourages new ideas and embraces change.
  • Personal interest, working knowledge, and/or experience in the capital markets, real estate or commercial mortgage industry.
  • Familiarity with local (Alberta and Canadian) economic conditions and indicators is considered an asset.
