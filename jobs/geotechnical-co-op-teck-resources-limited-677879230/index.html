Full Description

Location: Calgary, AB, Canada
Employment Type: Temporary Full Time
Duration: 8 months

About our Calgary Office

Teck’s Calgary office serves as a key hub supporting our operations across North America.

Located in the heart of downtown, it offers easy access to Calgary’s vibrant culinary scene, striking architecture, and a wide range of cultural and urban experiences.

Role Overview

We are seeking motivated students currently enrolled in an accredited Canadian engineering program, with a focus in geological, geotechnical, civil engineering, or mineral extraction-related disciplines.

This opportunity is ideal for individuals who bring strong communication and interpersonal skills, an analytical mindset, effective time management, and a genuine eagerness to learn and grow in a hands-on environment.

Candidates must be returning to their academic program following the co-op term. A valid study permit and studentship letter will be required where applicable.

Key Responsibilities

  • Be a courageous safety leader, adhere to and sponsor safety and environmental rules and procedures
  • Participate in Teck’s health and safety programs to help ensure everyone on site returns home safe and healthy each day.
  • Support the team by monitoring, managing, and coordinating data and information
  • Assist in implementing governance requirements in alignment with established guidelines (e.g., Tailings Management Protocol and internal standards)
  • Coordinate activities and facilitate information sharing with consultants, site teams, and other stakeholders as needed
  • Contribute to continuous improvement initiatives and bring forward innovative ideas related to tailings and dam management

Qualifications

  • Working towards a degree or equivalent experience in Geological, Geotechnical, Civil or Mining Engineering with soil mechanics, tailings, or rock mechanics background
  • Motivated self-starters with the ability to manage tasks simultaneously
  • Comfortable working in a multifaceted work environment with a willingness to work outdoors and able to operate vehicles at our sites, in various weather conditions and terrain. Valid Canadian drivers license is a requirement.
  • Strong written, verbal and interpersonal communication skills with a capability to communicate complex ideas.
  • Proficient in Microsoft Office applications (Word, Excel, PowerPoint)
  • Understanding of geotechnical investigation and analyses, soil mechanics, instrumentation/monitoring systems, or tailings/dam develop principles would be an asset

Why choose a Co-op with Teck?

At Teck, we offer more than just a job – we provide a comprehensive student experience comprised of relevant, hands-on work, vibrant social activities, extensive networking opportunities, and rich professional development.

With captivating projects set against stunning backdrops, a culture of inclusivity and collaboration, and boundless opportunities to learn, grow, and have fun, joining us means embracing a fulfilling and dynamic co-op adventure.

Pay Range: CAD $4,833 - $6,125 per month

The actual amount offered is determined based on the successful candidate’s relevant experience, skills, and competencies and considers internal equity.

In addition, our Co-ops receive a variety of advantages including:

  • Financial assistance to help with travel costs and co-op fees
  • Opportunity to develop technical skills and gain practical experience while applying academic knowledge to real-world mining operations and projects
  • Enjoy mentorship and guidance from experienced specialists in their respective fields
  • Receive primary consideration for professional positions upon graduation
