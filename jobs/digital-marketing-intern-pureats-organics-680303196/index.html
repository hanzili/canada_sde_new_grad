at PurEats Organics

About the Role

Own social channels; create content; run campaigns

About PurEats Organics

Startup, DTC baby food, founder mentorship

Full Description

We’re hiring a Marketing Intern for PurEats Organics - a baby food startup for parents who read the label twice.

We’re pre-launch, which means there’s no “playbook” yet - we’re writing it as we go. If you want to see how brand, content, and growth come together from day one, this might be your role.

What you’ll actually do (not just “help the team”):

• Set up and own our presence on Instagram, TikTok, and LinkedIn

• Turn ideas into real posts: reels, stories, carousels, hooks, and captions

• Build a living content plan: weekly schedule, themes, and experiments

• Help define what we test each month: concepts, angles, and campaigns

• Try different approaches, look at performance, and suggest what we do next

• Support ad launches and simple performance tracking (what did we run, what worked)

What you’ll get:

• A front-row seat to building a DTC brand from zero

• Direct feedback, context, and mentorship from the founder

• The freedom to propose ideas - and actually see them go live

Who this might be perfect for:

• You’re early in your career and want real, practical experience

• You live on social media (Instagram/TikTok), but also care about strategy

• You’re curious about startups, marketing, and the parenting / baby space

• You’re not looking for “perfect systems” - you’re excited to build them

Location:

• Ideally U.S. or Canada, or strong familiarity with these markets

• Remote-friendly, with async work and simple check-ins

No long CV required. If you’re interested, send a short message:

• Who you are

• Why this caught your attention

• A link or screenshot of content you like (yours or someone else’s)

Let’s see what we can build together for modern parents
