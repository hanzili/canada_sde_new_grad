About the Role

Build data pipelines, maintain ETL workflows

About League

Canadian health benefits platform, strong benefits program

Full Description

Who you are

  • Exposure to data engineering projects and technologies
  • Hands on software development
  • Previous engineering co-op experience

What the job involves

  • You'll join our team and gain hands-on experience in building and maintaining data pipelines
  • You'll learn the fundamentals of ETL processes, data warehousing, software development, working with large datasets, and AI systems
  • Participate in designing and implementing data solutions, contributing to data quality initiatives, and exploring cutting-edge data technologies
  • This co-op is your chance to build a strong foundation in a rapidly growing field
  • Some things the data team works on:
  • You'll be learning about real-time and batch pipelines with Python and SQL
  • Software Development, Terraform Deployment for fault-tolerant, scalable systems
  • Root cause analysis of production issues, performance tuning and optimization
  • Some of the technologies we use on the data engineering team include:
  • GCP, python/SQL, Airflow, Terraform, Looker
  • Security-related Responsibilities:
  • Compliance with Information Security Policies
  • Compliance with League’s secure coding practice
  • Responsibility and accountability for executing League's policies and procedures
  • Notification of HR, Legal, Compliance & Security of any incidents, breaches or policy violations

The application process

  • As part of your application, please record a 1-minute video answering:
  • 👉 “Why do you want to work at League?”
  • Upload your video to Google Drive or YouTube, and share the link in a clickable PDF with your application
  • Applying to a role you love can be exhausting, and understanding the next steps can feel vague and uncertain. You have done the hard part of submitting your application; let's do ours by sharing potential next steps
  • You should receive a confirmation email after submitting your application
  • A recruiter (not a computer) reviews all applications at League
  • If we see alignment with League's needs, a recruiter will reach out to learn more about your goals. The recruiter will also share the team-specific interview process depending on the roles you are exploring
  • The final step is an offer, which we hope you will accept!
  • Prior to joining us, we conduct reference and background checks. Additional checks could be required for US Candidates, depending on the role you are exploring

Benefits

  • Flexible medical and dental plans
  • 401(k) and RRSP matching programs
  • Health, lifestyle, learning and family spending accounts
  • Employee stock option program (ESOP)
  • Generous leaves (including parental and sabbatical)
  • Flexible time off
