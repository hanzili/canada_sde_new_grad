Junior Software Developer

at Convverge, Inc

About the Role

Develop software components, test, and support client solutions.

About Convverge, Inc

Azure-focused software consultancy delivering client solutions.

Full Description

What We Offer At Convverge, we build business-level applications on Microsoft Azure, SharePoint, and the Power Platform that solve complex, high-stakes problems for our clients. We’re regional leaders in a growing cloud technology space — and we’re growing with it. You’ll work alongside developers, architects, and designers who take quality seriously, on projects that challenge you to think beyond the task in front of you.

About Our Team We’re a close-knit, high-trust team that moves quickly, supports one another, and takes pride in delivering quality work. We lead with quality as a design principle, not an afterthought.

We work in small, empowered teams where junior developers are given meaningful opportunities to contribute, ask questions, learn from experienced team members, and build confidence through real project work.

We value diverse backgrounds and experiences because the best solutions come from teams that don’t all think the same way.

About You We are seeking a Junior Software Developer who is curious, motivated, and excited to grow in a collaborative technical environment. You enjoy learning new technologies, solving problems, and turning business requirements into practical software solutions. You may be early in your career, but you bring strong fundamentals, a willingness to ask thoughtful questions, and a commitment to building reliable, high-quality work.

Key Responsibilities

  • Contribute to the design, development, testing, and support of client solutions in collaboration with senior developers and project teams.
  • Work within an Agile development environment, following team processes, coding standards, and delivery best practices.
  • Break down problems into smaller tasks and seek guidance when needed to prioritize and move work forward.
  • Create and maintain as-built documentation for client solutions.
  • Build and update technical support documentation for internal teams and client solutions.
  • Support testing and quality assurance activities by reviewing functionality, documenting issues, and helping validate fixes.
  • Develop software components and solutions using Microsoft technologies, including the Power Platform, with support from experienced team members.
  • Communicate questions, blockers, risks, or delays early so the team can support successful project delivery.

Qualifications And Experience

  • Post-secondary education in Computer Science, Software Development, Information Technology, or a related program, or equivalent practical experience.
  • Approximately 0–2 years of software development experience, including internships, co-op placements, academic projects, personal projects, or professional experience.
  • Foundational experience with at least one programming language, such as C#, JavaScript, TypeScript, Python, or a similar language.
  • Basic familiarity with version control tools such as Git.
  • Interest in Microsoft cloud technologies, including Power Platform, Azure, Microsoft 365, SharePoint.
  • Ability to work both independently and collaboratively within a team environment.
  • Clear verbal and written communication skills, with a willingness to ask questions and share progress openly.

Nice to Have

  • Exposure to Agile development practices through work, school, or project experience.
  • Experience with Microsoft Azure, Azure DevOps, Microsoft 365, SharePoint, or Power Platform.
  • Experience with C#, PowerShell, Bash, APIs, databases, or web development frameworks.
  • Previous experience working on client-facing, consulting, or team-based software projects.

Benefits

  • Competitive salary and benefits package.
  • Opportunity to learn from experienced developers, consultants, architects, and project teams.
  • Career advancement, mentorship, and professional development opportunities.
  • Hands-on exposure to Microsoft cloud technologies and meaningful client projects.

What We Expect Our team shows up ready to do great work, and we expect the same from you. We collaborate, take ownership seriously, ask for help when needed, and hold each other to a high standard. In return, you’ll find a team that supports your growth and clients who genuinely value what we build together.

What We Value Our company leads with a values-based mindset, and we believe the best career matches happen when personal values align with ours. Before you apply, take a moment to review our five core values — we think it’s worth your time.

Application Process If this sounds like the right fit, we’d love to hear from you. Submit your resume and a cover letter telling us about your experience and why Convverge is the right next step.
