About Market 49 Fine Foods

Local gourmet foods company with community focus

Full Description

Farmers Markets, Pop-Ups & Events

We are a growing local gourmet foods company based in Langley, BC, and we are looking for a full-time Sales Associate to join our team. This is a dynamic, hands-on role that blends customer-facing sales with behind-the-scenes kitchen and event support.

This position is ideal for someone who enjoys variety in their workday — from engaging with customers at pop-ups and trade shows, to assisting in the kitchen, to serving food and representing our brand in the community.

Position Details

  • Full-time role
  • 4 × 10-hour shifts per week
  • Three-day long weekend every week
  • Mix of weekday and weekend shifts depending on events and schedules
  • Must have a valid Drivers License

What You’ll Do

  • Represent the company at pop-ups, farmers markets, trade shows, and special events
  • Greet customers, explain products, and provide excellent customer service
  • Process sales using POS systems, cash, and card
  • Serve food samples and prepared items in a safe and professional manner
  • Assist with booth and event setup, teardown, loading, and unloading
  • Support kitchen operations including basic prep, packaging, and organization
  • Maintain cleanliness, food safety, and presentation standards
  • Help with inventory tracking, restocking, and event preparation
  • Work closely with the team to ensure smooth day-to-day operations

What We’re Looking For

  • Friendly, confident, and comfortable speaking with customers
  • Reliable, punctual, and able to handle a fast-paced environment
  • Comfortable switching between customer service, kitchen work, and event support
  • Able to lift up to 40–50 lbs
  • Comfortable working evenings, weekends, and events as required
  • Food service, retail, sales, or market experience is an asset (but not required)
  • Food Safe certification is an asset (or willingness to obtain)
  • A genuine interest in food, local businesses, and customer experience

Why Join Us

  • Consistent full-time hours with a 4-day work week
  • Long weekends every week
  • Varied work — no two weeks are the same
  • Opportunity to grow with a local company
  • Staff discounts on products
  • Supportive, hands-on team environment

If you’re someone who enjoys working with people, likes being involved in events, and doesn’t want a desk job, we’d love to hear from you.

Pay: $18.50-$21.00 per hour

Benefits:

  • Discounted or free food
  • On-site parking
  • Store discount

Work Location: In person
