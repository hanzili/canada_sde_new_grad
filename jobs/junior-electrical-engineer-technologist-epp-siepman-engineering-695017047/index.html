About the Role

Prepare electrical design drawings; support site duties

About Epp Siepman Engineering

Winnipeg-based engineering firm offering mentorship and growth

Full Description

Are you an aspiring professional in the field of electrical engineering or technology, eager to apply and enhance your technical skills while contribute to impactful projects? Epp Siepman Engineering invites individuals like you to step into a pivotal role as our Junior Electrical Engineer / Technologist. This role is your opportunity to a dynamic career, offering hands-on experience and mentorship within a team committed to driving advancements in energy efficiency and sustainability.

At Epp Siepman Engineering, we are one of the largest independent mechanical and electrical consulting engineering firms in Winnipeg, specializing in the building construction industry. Our project experience includes a wide range of projects in the educational, commercial, health care, industrial, and hospitality sectors. We also provide engineering services such as feasibility studies, life-cycle costing reports, energy modeling, and systems commissioning. Our portfolio of projects offers an environment that fosters collaboration, fuels creativity, and facilitates personal and professional growth. Our open and collaborative work culture is designed to inspire innovation and teamwork.

As a Junior Electrical Engineer / Technologist with us your responsibilities will include aiding in electrical design systems, ensuring alignment with industry standards and best practices. This role provides a platform for you to apply your skills, learn, and grow within a collaborative team focused on achieving excellence in engineering solutions.

Your Role

Project Design:

  • Preparing preliminary and detail working design drawings for power, distribution, lighting, life safety, communication and security systems using Revit MEP

  • Coordinate with other design disciplines.

  • Preparation of contract documents and specifications.

  • Working alongside contract administration team members to conduct site visits and oversee contract administration.

  • Providing on-site support for inspections, equipment start-up, and commissioning.

Learning & Development:

  • Actively participating in professional associations and committees

  • Staying current with industry trends and advancements by attending educational workshops and fostering personal networks.

Qualifications: Your Path to Success:

  • Possession of a degree in electrical engineering or a diploma in electrical engineering technology.

  • Registered or eligibility to register as an Engineer-in-Training (EIT) with EGM, or as a Certified Engineering Technologist (C.E.T.) / Technologist-in-Training with CTTAM

  • Demonstrated ability to work within a team in a collaborative environment.

  • Ability to apply critical thinking and reasoning to decision making process;

  • Ability to effectively communicate and work as part of a team is essential;

  • An understanding of Canadian commercial construction methods and design processes;

  • Desire to develop skills and expand career;

The following qualifications and skills will be considered an asset

  • Strong organizational, communication, and interpersonal skills.

  • Experience with Revit;

  • Experience with AutoCAD;

  • Demonstrated experience with office software such as MS Office (Outlook, Word, Excel, Adobe, etc.)

  • Experience working in design for commercial or industrial settings, preferably in a consulting environment.

  • An understanding of Canadian commercial construction methods.

  • Field Experience in the construction industry

In addition to a supportive work environment, Epp Siepman Engineering offers a range of benefits designed to support our team members in both their professional and personal lives. These include:

Flexible Work Schedule: Enjoy the flexibility of a shortened work week during the summer months, allowing you to make the most of the season.

Work from Home Capabilities: Embrace the convenience of remote work options, to provide you with flexibility when life needs.

RRSP Matching: We invest in your future by matching your contributions to your Registered Retirement Savings Plan, helping you plan for financial security.

PHSP Benefits Plan: Access a Personal Health Spending Plan, ensuring you have the coverage you need for your well-being.

Epp Siepman Engineering understands the importance of work-life balance and is committed to the development of team culture. This role also offers opportunities for professional development and advancement within our organization. If you're a self-motivated professional with a proven track record in electrical engineering or technology, and if you're seeking a role that allows you to showcase your leadership skills and technical skills, this opportunity is tailored to you. Join Epp Siepman Engineering as a Junior Electrical Engineer / Technologist and be part of a team committed to pursuing engineering innovation.
