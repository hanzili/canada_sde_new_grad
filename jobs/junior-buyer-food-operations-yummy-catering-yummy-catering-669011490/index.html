About the Role

About Yummy Catering

Full Description

About Yummy
Yummy Catering is a fast-growing childcare catering company serving thousands of meals daily across the GTA. We operate in a high-energy, fast-paced environment where organization, teamwork, and accountability are key to our success.

The Role
We are looking for a Junior Buyer to support our purchasing and inventory operations. This is a great opportunity for someone who is organized, motivated, and eager to learn.

No prior purchasing experience is required — we will train the right candidate.

What You’ll Do

  • Assist with daily and weekly ordering of food and supplies
  • Work with the kitchen and operations team to understand needs
  • Help monitor inventory levels and avoid shortages
  • Coordinate deliveries and check incoming products
  • Communicate with suppliers and resolve basic issues
  • Support cost tracking and purchasing records

What We’re Looking For

  • Strong organization and attention to detail
  • Positive attitude and willingness to learn
  • Comfortable working in a fast-paced environment
  • Good communication skills
  • Basic computer skills (Excel, email, etc.)
  • Reliable and takes ownership of tasks

What You’ll Get

  • Full training provided
  • Opportunity to grow within the company
  • Work directly with leadership
  • Competitive salary based on experience
  • A key role that supports daily operations
  • Free meals, snacks, and beverages provided daily
  • On-site gym (currently under construction)

Why Join Yummy?
At Yummy, we build strong teams. If you’re looking to learn, grow, and be part of a company where your work matters every day, this is the role for you.

To Apply
Apply through Indeed or send your resume directly. We look forward to meeting you.

Pay: $45,000.00-$55,000.00 per year

  • Casual dress
  • Discounted or free food
  • On-site gym
  • On-site parking
