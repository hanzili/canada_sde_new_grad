Junior Accountant

at C.Ebert Professional Corp

📍 Location Edmonton, AB

About the Role

Post journal entries, reconcile accounts, assist audits

About C.Ebert Professional Corp

Small firm; CPA-friendly growth opportunities

Full Description

We are seeking a detail-oriented Junior Accountant to join our finance team. In this role, you will support the accounting department by maintaining financial records, preparing reports, and assisting with daily accounting operations. This position is ideal for a recent graduate or early-career professional looking to develop their accounting skills and gain hands-on experience.

Key Responsibilities

  • Assist with daily accounting tasks and financial record keeping
  • Prepare and post journal entries in the general ledger
  • Maintain and reconcile accounts payable and accounts receivable
  • Perform bank and account reconciliations
  • Assist with month-end and year-end closing processes
  • Prepare basic financial reports such as income statements and balance sheets
  • Maintain organized accounting records and documentation
  • Support audits, tax preparation, and compliance reporting
  • Assist senior accountants with various accounting projects

Qualifications

  • Bachelor’s degree in Accounting, Finance, or related field
  • 0–2 years of accounting or bookkeeping experience
  • Basic knowledge of accounting principles and financial reporting
  • Proficiency in Microsoft Excel and accounting software (e.g., QuickBooks, Sage, SAP)
  • Strong attention to detail and analytical skills
  • Good communication and organizational abilities

Preferred Skills

  • Experience with financial reporting or reconciliations
  • Knowledge of payroll or tax processes
  • Progress toward CPA or other accounting certification

Pay: $22.00-$26.00 per hour

Expected hours: 30.0 – 40.0 per week

Education:

  • Bachelor's Degree (required)

Work Location: Hybrid remote in Edmonton, AB
