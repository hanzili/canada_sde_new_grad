Full Description

Company Description PartnerRe is a leading, privately owned, multi-line global reinsurer with a reputation of financial stability and strength, and a commitment to rebuilding businesses and communities after risk events around the world.

Our mission is to continue to be a financially stable and predictable business partner, supporting our clients with solutions tailored to their needs, delivering superior returns to our owner, providing rewarding opportunities for our people and making a positive contribution to society. To achieve this, we live by our values of Integrity, Performance, Collaboration, Straightforward Communication and Respect and Care.

Core to The PartnerRe Experience is our global nature. No matter where you sit in the company, you are part of something bigger. Our teams represent our truly international mindset, forging connections across office locations and all levels of the organization and building a network of mutual success. PartnerRe is an equal opportunities employer. Please join our company LinkedIn Page for all updates on new positions that are coming live.

Compensation The salary range for this role is CAD 53,100 - 64,900 per year. This amount would be prorated to the length of the contract.

We are seeking for our Toronto office, a Business Data Analyst – Co-op (May - August 2026) , who is a highly skilled, self-motivated individual. The Business Data Analyst will be responsible for either processing business in PartnerRe systems or working on NAL Operations and Administration projects.

About The Job

  • Support PartnerRe projects by processing transactions in the administration system
  • Support the PartnerRe monthly billing process as needed
  • Adhere to all IT controls and security established by IT management.
  • Ensure the Team Lead is informed of ongoing work activities and any unusual circumstances to ensure quality and timely deliverables.
  • Assist other teams within North American Life Operations as required.
  • Keep the Team Lead informed of ongoing work activities and any unusual circumstances to ensure quality and timely deliverables.

About You

  • Demonstrated analytical, problem-identification and resolution skills with attention to detail
  • MS Office, Access, VBA, Python or SQL skills
  • Demonstrated ability to follow instructions clearly, and ask for clarification when needed
  • Demonstrated ability to communicate with management and peers
  • Demonstrated ability to promote effective teamwork and accountability
  • Ability to handle multiple assignments communicating prioritizations to required levels
  • Highest degree of professional ethics
  • Strong interpersonal skills & effective communicator (written and oral)
  • High degree of personal competence (self-awareness, self-management) and social competence (social awareness, relationship management)
  • Time management, prioritization, organization, flexible, adaptable while working in a fast paced environment
