About Infinite Investment Systems

Full Description

**Role Summary**

We are seeking a Junior Software Developer to support the design, development, testing, and maintenance of financial software systems. This role works closely with senior developers, analysts, and business stakeholders to deliver reliable, secure, and high-quality solutions in a regulated environment where accuracy and data integrity are critical.

This position is ideal for someone early in their career who wants to build strong foundations in enterprise development, financial systems, and modern Microsoft technologies.

**Duties and Responsibilities**

- Review business and technical requirements to support development of new and existing applications - Write, test, and debug code using established development standards - Maintain and enhance existing software applications - Assist in troubleshooting production issues and implementing fixes - Participate in unit testing, system testing, and validation of new features - Write and maintain technical and process documentation - Document programming issues and resolutions for future reference - Follow secure coding practices and protect confidential financial and client data - Collaborate with developers, analysts, and stakeholders in an iterative development environment - Adapt to evolving tools, technologies, and business requirements - Other duties as assigned

**Requirements**

- Diploma or degree in Computer Science, Information Systems, Software Engineering, or equivalent practical experience - 2 to 3 years of professional programming experience, including internships or co-op placements - Experience developing applications using C# and .NET - Experience working with relational databases such as Microsoft SQL Server - Experience maintaining or enhancing existing software applications - Experience working in a team-based development environment - Experience participating in testing, debugging, or release activities

**Skills and Competencies**

- Strong analytical and problem-solving ability - High attention to detail - Good time management and organizational skills - Ability to communicate clearly with both technical and non-technical team members - Ability to work effectively in a collaborative environment - Comfortable working in a fast-paced environment with evolving priorities
