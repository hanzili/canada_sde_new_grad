About Evans Tedham Building Products

Full Description

Job Description:

Evans Tedham Building Products is looking for a summer student to join our team in a Marketing Assistant role. This is a hands-on position that blends online selling and advertising with face-to-face customer outreach — a great fit for someone who is outgoing, entrepreneurial, and not afraid to put themselves out there.

A big part of this role involves managing our presence on Facebook Marketplace — listing products and helping drive sales through the platform. The successful candidate will also assist with paid online advertising through Facebook Ads and Google Ads, helping us reach contractors and builders in our market. Beyond the digital side, this role involves getting out into the field — visiting job sites and meeting directly with contractors and builders to build relationships and promote our products.

Our lumber yard is located on the east side of Stoney Trail and is not easily accessible by transit, so a vehicle is required. Hours are Monday to Friday, 8 hours per day.

Successful candidates will have:

  • A naturally outgoing personality with a genuine comfort talking to new people — whether online or face-to-face
  • The confidence to walk onto a job site, introduce themselves, and represent our company professionally
  • Excellent communication skills, both written and verbal
  • Experience with Facebook Marketplace and an understanding of how Facebook Ads and Google Ads work
  • A valid Class 5 driver's licence
  • Strong organizational skills and the ability to manage multiple tasks
  • Self-motivated with the ability to work independently in the field
  • Post-secondary education in Marketing, Business, or a related field is considered an asset, but not required — we're more interested in drive and personality than credentials
  • Familiarity with the construction or building products industry is considered an asset

This position is supported by a government youth employment grant. Applicants under the age of 30 will receive preference in accordance with program eligibility requirements.

Please note that all applications will be considered, but only shortlisted candidates will be contacted for interviews.

Pay: $18.00-$21.00 per hour

Work Location: In person
