Full Description

About Warrior Engineering

Warrior Engineering is a Northern Ontario–based, multidisciplinary engineering and consulting firm headquartered in Thunder Bay . We provide civil, environmental, geotechnical, and specialty engineering services to clients across Northwestern Ontario, with a strong focus on community-driven, resource, and infrastructure projects .

We work closely with Indigenous communities, municipalities, and private-sector clients to deliver practical, buildable solutions that reflect local context, regulatory requirements, and long-term sustainability. Our team is hands-on and collaborative, and we pride ourselves on being responsive, transparent, and grounded in the realities of working in northern and remote environments.

As a growing firm, Warrior offers engineers the opportunity to gain broad exposure across multiple disciplines , take on real responsibility early, and learn directly from experienced professionals. We value buildable solutions; accountability and ownership; responsiveness; safety and quality; and a growth mindset focused on continuous learning.

About the Opportunity

We are building a candidate pool of Civil / Environmental Engineers-in-Training (EITs) for current and upcoming work in Thunder Bay and across Northwestern Ontario.

This is an excellent opportunity for early-career engineers who want broad exposure across civil and environmental engineering , rather than being siloed into a single discipline. Successful candidates will gain hands-on experience across site development, water and wastewater, environmental monitoring, permitting, geotechnical interfaces, and construction support.

What You’ll Be Involved In

  • Support civil and environmental design and analysis under the supervision of licensed engineers
  • Assist with site development, grading, drainage, and servicing works
  • Support environmental programs including monitoring, reporting, and regulatory compliance
  • Participate in field work, site inspections, and data collection
  • Contribute to technical memos, drawings, calculations, and cost estimates
  • Assist with permitting, approvals, and coordination with regulators and Indigenous communities
  • Work closely with multidisciplinary teams and Project Managers

Who We’re Looking For

  • Bachelor’s degree in Civil or Environmental Engineering
  • Registered or eligible for registration as an EIT in Ontario
  • Located in Thunder Bay
  • Interest in gaining experience across a wide range of civil and environmental work
  • Comfortable with field work and working in northern or remote environments
  • We value self-starters — people who bring ideas to the table, take initiative, and want to help shape how we work.

Why Join Our Candidate Pool

  • Opportunity to be considered for current and upcoming roles
  • Broad, hands-on exposure early in your career
  • Mentorship toward P.Eng. licensure
  • Meaningful work supporting infrastructure, resource, and community projects in Northwestern Ontario
