About the Role

Triage Salesforce issues; document requirements; support stakeholders

About Bombardier

Global aviation leader; strong internship training program

Full Description

Innovation is in our DNA… is it in yours?
Bombardier is a global leader in aviation, creating innovative and game-changing planes. Our products and services provide world-class experiences that set new standards in passenger comfort, energy efficiency, reliability and safety. We are a global organization focused on working together with a team spirit.

Your boarding pass will include…

Several conferences, including:

  • Meet An Executive
  • Women Taking Flight

Learning more about Bombardier, including:

  • Bombardier Products conference
  • Visits of the Bombardier sites
  • Bombardier Academy of Learning

Many social/networking opportunities, including:

  • Volunteering
  • Networking for Success
  • 5 à 7, Potluck, and much more!

What are your contributions to the team?

  • Work with the Salesforce.com Product Owner Team to triage and categorize daily support queries related to user access, data transformation, analytics, new functionalities, bugs, and project related tasks.
  • Deliver Salesforce.com solutions: implement assigned change requests submitted by users; document requirements, participate in testing and liaise between the technical teams and the business.
  • Interact, and support business stakeholders through written and verbal communications.
  • Deliver weekly and month reports for management.
  • Keep up with changes in the user community and administrate focal management processes.
  • Investigate operational needs, problems and opportunities, contributing to the recommendation of improvements.
  • Maintain and /or create procedural documentation related to the department’s processes.
  • Plan required activities that will contribute to your assigned tasks and / or projects; organize your day in order to meet deadlines and work in a very dynamic environment.

How to thrive in this role?

  • You show initiative and enjoy working in a fast-paced environment.
  • You can multitask and adapt to changing priorities.
  • You are a strong team player with good communication skills.
  • You are analytical, and a problem solver with strong attention to detail.
  • You speak and write both English and French.
  • You are familiar with Salesforce.com and have knowledge of Excel.

Boarding Information:

  • Location: Administrative Centre (CA)
  • Duration: 8 months
  • Flexible workplace-Hybrid

It is important to note that our internship opportunities are open to students only, not new graduates. All our interns may be required to occasionally travel outside of Canada for training/work purpose.
