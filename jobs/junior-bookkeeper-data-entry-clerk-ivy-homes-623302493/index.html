About Ivy Homes

Full Description

Qualifications:

  • Secondary School (required)
  • College diploma in Accounting, Business Administration or related field (recent graduates encouraged to apply)

Job Description:

Ivy Homes Ltd., a growing construction & property management company, is looking to expand our team and seeking a motivated, detail-oriented candidate who is eager to learn.

This entry-level position is ideal for a recent graduate looking to build a long-term career. Full onsite training will be provided, along with direct mentorship under an experienced Controller.

Responsibilities:

  • Verify invoices received and discussing discrepancies with vendors
  • Data entry/inputting invoices into Sage 50 (45 sub-trades)
  • General accounts receivable duties including entering revenue and following up with bad debts
  • Maintaining general ledgers and an organized workspace
  • Working closely with the controller to ensure the financial records are current
  • Performing general administrative tasks, filing and other tasks as requested

Job Requirements:

  • Attention to detail
  • Strong data entry skills
  • Ability to prioritize work based on the business needs
  • Desire to be a part of a team environment
  • Excellent communication skills
  • Proficient with excel and MS office products

Schedule/Location:

  • Monday to Friday 9 am to 5 pm
  • 40 hours per week
  • On site (no remote work)
