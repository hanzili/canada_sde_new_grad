Position Overview:

The Junior Accounts Payable Clerk supports the finance team by processing invoices, maintaining accurate financial records, and assisting with vendor payments. This role also serves as a backup receptionist, providing front desk coverage when needed and ensuring a professional and welcoming experience for visitors and callers. The ideal candidate is organized, detail-oriented, and comfortable multitasking in both administrative and accounting functions.

Key Responsibilities:

Accounts Payable Duties

  • Process and enter vendor invoices into the accounting system accurately and in a timely manner
  • Match purchase orders, receipts, and invoices to ensure proper documentation
  • Assist with preparing payment runs (cheques, EFTs, or other payment methods)
  • Maintain vendor files and ensure records are complete and up to date
  • Respond to vendor inquiries regarding invoices and payment status
  • Reconcile vendor statements and resolve discrepancies
  • Assist with month-end closing activities related to accounts payable
  • Ensure compliance with company policies and accounting procedures

Reception / Administrative Support (Backup Coverage)

  • Provide front desk coverage during receptionist absences, breaks, or peak periods
  • Greet visitors and direct them to the appropriate person or department
  • Answer, screen, and route incoming phone calls
  • Manage incoming and outgoing mail and courier deliveries
  • Maintain a clean and organized reception area
  • Provide general administrative support to various departments as required

Qualifications:

  • Diploma or certificate in Accounting, Finance, Business Administration, or related field preferred
  • 1–2 years of administrative or accounting experience is an asset (co-op or internship experience acceptable)
  • Basic understanding of accounts payable processes
  • Proficiency in Microsoft Office, particularly Excel
  • Experience with accounting software is an asset
  • Strong attention to detail and accuracy
  • Excellent organizational and time-management skills
  • Professional communication and customer service skills
  • Ability to handle confidential information
  • Ability to multitask and adapt in a fast-paced office environment
  • Reliable and team-oriented
