About the Role

About Smartrend Manufacturing Group (SMG)

Manitoba's fast-growing manufacturing group.

Full Description

Smartrend Manufacturing Group (SMG) Inc. is one of Manitoba’s fastest-growing companies, committed to increasing safety in the school bus industry and reducing emissions in the electric vehicle charging sector through our diverse divisions. We design and develop exciting, patented, and innovative technologies that make a real and lasting impact across multiple industries.

At the heart of our success are the values that guide everything we do:

  • People – our team is what makes us special
  • Passion – from our hearts and minds, demonstrated through our actions
  • Priority – we put the customer first, every day, in everything we do
  • Partnership – with each other, our customers, and our suppliers

We provide a supportive environment where hard work, passions, and goals are recognized. Innovation and collaboration are at the heart of what we do. Share your drive and aspirations with us, and we’ll ensure that your co-op experience opens doors to new opportunities and growth.

THE POSITION This well-rounded marketing student co-op role will expose you to a wide range of marketing activities supported by our team across multiple business functions. You will assist with document branding, content updates, and the organization of departmental resources, while developing and updating presentations and marketing materials. The role also includes supporting social media planning and management, generating creative content ideas, and identifying opportunities to improve digital channels such as websites and social platforms. You’ll contribute to industry and competitive research, data management and analysis, asset library development, and photo/video projects. In addition, you will support internal marketing initiatives, events, trade shows, sponsorships, inventory coordination, logistics, budget tracking, and general administrative tasks—gaining hands-on experience across the full marketing mix.

How You Will Be Making An Impact

  • Gaining hands-on experience supporting brand consistency through document branding and process updates
  • Learning how to organize and manage marketing documents, resources, and digital asset libraries
  • Developing practical skills by creating and updating presentations and marketing materials
  • Contributing creative ideas and draft content for social media channels to help grow engagement and followers
  • Learning how to evaluate social media and website performance and identify areas for improvement
  • Assisting with website updates and issue identification to improve the user experience
  • Building research skills by supporting industry research and competitive market analysis
  • Developing analytical skills through data management, reporting, and analysis
  • Supporting photo and video projects to learn how visual content is planned and executed
  • Assisting with internal and external testimonial initiatives to understand storytelling and brand voice
  • Gaining exposure to internal marketing initiatives, events, and social committee activities
  • Learning how trade shows, sponsorships, and memberships are tracked and coordinated
  • Supporting inventory management, shipping, and logistics to understand marketing operations behind the scenes
  • Assisting with invoicing and budget tracking to gain insight into marketing financial processes

What Success Looks Like

  • You quickly learn our brand standards and apply them consistently across documents and marketing materials
  • You stay organized and reliable, keeping marketing documents, assets, and resources easy to find and up to date
  • You take initiative by contributing thoughtful ideas for social media content and digital improvements
  • You support social media channels by drafting posts, tracking performance, and helping grow engagement and followers
  • You accurately identify website issues and follow through to ensure updates or fixes are completed
  • You deliver research and data analysis that is clear, well-organized, and useful for decision-making
  • You communicate clearly, ask questions, and collaborate effectively with marketing and cross-functional teams
  • You manage multiple tasks and deadlines while maintaining attention to detail
  • You actively participate in photo, video, and testimonial projects, meeting timelines and expectations
  • You contribute positively to internal marketing initiatives, events, and team activities
  • You support trade shows, sponsorships, and inventory coordination in a timely and dependable way
  • You track invoices, budgets, and materials accurately and responsibly
  • You show curiosity, a willingness to learn, and steady growth in your marketing skills throughout the term

What Skills And Experience You Require

  • Currently enrolled in a post-secondary program in Marketing, Communications, Business, or a related field
  • Strong written and verbal communication skills, with attention to detail
  • Basic understanding of marketing principles, social media platforms, and digital content
  • Comfortable creating and updating presentations (PowerPoint or Google Slides)
  • Organized and detail-oriented, with the ability to manage multiple tasks and deadlines
  • Creative mindset with an interest in social media, content creation, and digital marketing
  • Willingness to learn and take initiative in a fast-paced, team-based environment
  • Basic research and analytical skills, with an interest in market and industry research
  • Comfortable working with data, spreadsheets, and basic reporting
  • Familiarity with Microsoft Office or Google Workspace tools
  • Ability to work collaboratively while also managing tasks independently
  • Positive attitude, curiosity, and eagerness to learn new tools and skills
