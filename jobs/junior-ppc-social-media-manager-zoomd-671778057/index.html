About the Role

Manage PPC campaigns, optimize social ads

About Zoomd

MarTech firm with global campaigns

Full Description

We are seeking a motivated Junior PPC & Social Media Manager to join our media division in Herzliya.

The Role

This is a hands-on position designed for a marketer at the start of their career who is eager to master the MarTech landscape. You will work alongside our senior media buying team to build your ad operations skills, managing and scaling social and search campaigns for leading global brands.

About Zoomd

Zoomd technologies (TSXv:ZOMD) (OTC: ZMDTF) is an award-winning innovating MarTech company helping advertisers achieve their key performance indicators (KPIs) through our performance-based advertising platform. The platform unifies many media channels Such as Social, programmatic, affiliate, and much more all under one unified strategy, providing advertisers with a powerful partner for managing new customer acquisition campaigns and comprehensive data insights. The company operates globally with 6 offices worldwide, serving clients in over 70 countries.

Job Description

You will be responsibility for the setup and management of new campaigns across our ad serving platforms, as well as monitoring performance, building reports, analyzing results and daily optimizations.

Key Responsibilities

  • Develop and execute data-driven performance marketing strategies across diverse clients and industries.
  • Manage end-to-end PPC campaigns across Google Ads, Meta (Facebook & Instagram), Microsoft Ads, TikTok Ads, DV360, and other channels
  • Continuously optimize budgets, bids, targeting, and creatives to maximize ROI and exceed campaign goals.
  • Lead real-time analysis, A/B testing, and ongoing optimizations based on actionable insights
  • Act as a strategic advisor to clients, building strong relationships and ensuring satisfaction through proactive communication
  • Stay ahead of industry trends and platform updates, applying innovative strategies to campaigns.
  • Deliver detailed performance reports, insights, and recommendations to clients and internal stakeholders.
  • Collaborate cross-functionally with creative, technical, and analytics teams to ensure campaign execution aligns with client objectives

Required Skills & Experience

  • 1+ year of hands-on PPC experience (agency or brand-side) with a proven track record of delivering measurable results
  • Expertise in Google Ads, Meta Ads, TikTok, Snap and other performance marketing platforms.
  • Strong analytical skills, comfortable working with campaign data, attribution models, and optimization strategies.
  • Proficiency in tools such as Google Analytics, Tag Manager, and other performance measurement platforms.
  • Language: Fluent English (written and verbal) is required for managing our global client portfolio.
  • Excellent organizational and project management skills, with the ability to handle multiple accounts and priorities simultaneously
  • Multitasking
  • Strategic mindset with a passion for digital marketing innovation, performance growth, and paid media excellence.

Location: Herzliya (Hybrid: 4 days in-office, 1 day remote).

Opportunity: A fast-track role for a social-savvy marketer looking to break into the global AdTech space.
