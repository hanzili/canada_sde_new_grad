About the Role

Collaborate on experiments, analyze data, build dashboards

About LawDepot

Leading Canadian online legal documents provider

Full Description

What The Role Does Turn data into better product decisions. This is a high visibility analytics role within the Revenue Operations Department. As a Business Data Analyst, you will partner directly with Product Owners and business stakeholders to shape product decisions through rigorous experimentation and behavioural analysis.

You won’t just report results. You will influence roadmap priorities, challenge assumptions, and proactively identify opportunities that improve user experience and drive measurable outcomes.

You will help teams understand what is happening, why it is happening, and what to do next. Your work will influence user experience, engagement, renewals, conversions, and overall product performance.

This role is best suited for someone who takes initiative, follows through, and cares deeply about the quality and impact of their work.

This role is based in Edmonton, Alberta, with opportunities for a hybrid work arrangement.

What You’ll Do

  • Collaborate closely with Product Owners and business stakeholders to design and plan experiments, define requirements, assumptions, and limitations
  • Explore and analyze experiment data to understand customer behaviour, usage patterns, and performance drivers
  • Propose, design, and evaluate experiments and causal analyses to answer well-scoped business questions
  • Create new projects and manage multiple analytical initiatives informed by experiment results and emerging insights
  • Conduct deep dive analyses to uncover trends, friction points, and opportunities
  • Build dashboards and automated reports to improve accessibility and insight quality
  • Translate business questions into measurable metrics
  • Deliver data based recommendations and clearly identify actionable next steps
  • Improve tracking plans, event quality, and attribution logic to ensure accurate and reliable measurement
  • Write and optimize SQL queries and notebooks to support analysis
  • Use AI assisted tools to support data exploration, query development, and analytical workflows
  • Communicate data analysis results, insights, and recommendations clearly to stakeholders
  • Collaborate with fellow analysts to review results, refine methodologies, and strengthen analytical approaches

What You Bring The Essentials

  • Post-secondary education in Business, Economics, Mathematics, Data Science, Computer Science, or other related programs
  • Experience in data analysis or product analysis
  • Strong communication skills and ability to explain complex analysis clearly
  • Solid understanding of statistics and experiment analysis
  • Proficiency in SQL, including writing efficient queries with joins, aggregations, and filtering logic
  • Knowledge of data mining and segmentation techniques
  • Familiarity with Python for data analysis and visualization (ex. pandas, Matplotlib)
  • Ability to manage multiple experiments and analyses simultaneously
  • Experience analyzing and designing A/B experiments
  • Proficiency using Microsoft Excel (Pivot Tables, Visualizations, Formulas) and Project Management software
  • Ability to work independently and manage complex projects
  • Strong interest in data analysis and a natural tendency to dig deeper into unexpected results

What Sets You Apart

  • Ability to structure ambiguous problems into analytical plans
  • Confidence challenging assumptions while maintaining strong working relationships
  • Proactive mindset that goes beyond answering questions to identifying new opportunities
  • Comfortable presenting insights to Product Owners and leadership
  • Experience optimizing existing systems and reports used for key Revenue Operations initiatives
  • Experience analyzing web traffic, engagement, or Search Engine Optimization (SEO) related metrics to inform performance improvements
  • Familiarity with AI assisted tools for data analysis or productivity (ex. Cursor, Copilot, ChatGPT)

Why You Should Join The LawDepot Team

  • Excellent total compensation package
  • Comprehensive health and dental benefits, plus an additional Health Care Spending Account
  • Great work life balance (37.5 hour work weeks, flexible schedules, ability to bank hours)
  • Three weeks paid vacation, plus bonus personal days
  • Continuous learning opportunities, including LinkedIn Learning subscriptions and training budgets
  • Company share plan
  • Free catered lunches for the entire office
  • Monthly social events for team members to enjoy (think axe throwing, rock climbing, board games, food trucks, trivia contests, and charitable activities)
  • Casual work environment
  • Personal offices for focused work and to have your individual space (plus collaborative workspaces for when you want to meet with the team)
  • Free and easily accessible parking
  • Southgate Mall, Whyte Ave, and Calgary Trail restaurants are only minutes away
  • Hybrid working arrangements for that balance between getting work done at home and engaging with others in the office

Who We Are Join one of the fastest growing companies in Canada. LawDepot is proud to be a seven time Growth 500 ranked organization and a major player in the global legal solutions industry. Our mission is to modernize the legal document creation experience by empowering people with fast, easy to use, and professional solutions. With over ten million satisfied customers worldwide and millions of monthly website visitors, you will be joining a team that is truly making a difference. At LawDepot, we foster a collaborative and inclusive learning culture that values excellence and continuous improvement.
