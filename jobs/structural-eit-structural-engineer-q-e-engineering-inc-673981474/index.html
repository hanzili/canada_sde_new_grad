Full Description

Overview

We are seeking a highly skilled and detail-oriented Structural EIT / Structural Engineer to join our dynamic engineering team on a 1-year contract with the potential for extension.

The successful candidate will be responsible for designing, analyzing, and supporting the construction of structural systems across a variety of projects. This is an excellent opportunity to contribute to innovative infrastructure and building projects while ensuring safety, durability, and compliance with industry standards.

Key Responsibilities

  • Develop detailed structural designs using AutoCAD and other CAD drafting tools
  • Perform structural analysis and calculations using industry-standard engineering software
  • Prepare comprehensive project documentation, including drawings, specifications, and reports
  • Collaborate with project managers, architects, contractors, and clients to coordinate deliverables
  • Support and/or oversee construction phases to ensure adherence to design specifications and safety standards
  • Utilize Bluebeam for document review, markups, and project collaboration
  • Manage multiple projects while maintaining quality, timelines, and budgets
  • Conduct site visits to assess existing structures and support construction activities as required

Qualifications & Requirements

  • Bachelor’s degree in Civil or Structural Engineering (Master’s degree considered an asset)
  • Registered as an EIT or Professional Engineer (P.Eng.) (or eligible for registration in Ontario)
  • Minimum experience in structural design (co-op/internship experience acceptable for EIT level)
  • Proficiency with structural design software (e.g., AutoCAD, STAAD, or similar)
  • Strong understanding of construction practices and applicable building codes
  • Experience with project coordination and working in multidisciplinary teams
  • Proficiency in CAD drafting and modeling tools
  • Strong analytical and problem-solving skills
  • Ability to manage multiple priorities in a fast-paced environment

Why Join Us

This role offers an engaging and collaborative environment where innovation meets practical application. You’ll work on diverse structural engineering projects across multiple sectors, contributing to solutions built for long-term performance and safety.
