Jr. Manufacturing Engineer

at Royal Laser MFG

📍 Location Toronto, ON
📋 Details Full-time · Permanent · $19-32/hour · N/A

About the Role

Coordinate manufacturing projects across teams and vendors

About Royal Laser MFG

Mid-sized laser manufacturing and fabrication specialist

Full Description

The top priorities of this position will be coordinating among customer, vendor and in house engineering and production team in order to complete the project on time.

The ideal candidate for this position will be able to work closely and effectively with ovaries departments and possess excellent verbal and written English skills.

Due to the nature of this fast-paced position, a people-oriented person that is dedicated to accuracy would be best suited. Performance will be measured by productivity, decision making, completion of objectives and on time release of project information. Opportunities for advancement and growth could be to supervisor of manufacturing engineering, design engineering or product specialist.

Skills requirement:

· Experience in AutoCAD and ERP system

· Proficiency in Microsoft Office

· Collaborative and professional work ethic

· Ability to problem solving while under pressure, within a fast paced environment

· Ability to take direction and collaborate with team members

· Strong communication and interpersonal skills

· Excellent time management and multi-tasking skills

· Highly organized and self-motivated.

· Able to work independently and as a part of team.

***Salary based on experience and relevance

Job Types: Full-time, Permanent
