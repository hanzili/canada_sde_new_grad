About the Role

Coordinate influencer campaigns, manage social content

About Rouge Care

Wellness brand with influencer marketing focus

Full Description

We're looking for a Marketing Coordinator to join our team at Rouge Care and support our social media, influencer partnerships, and community growth initiatives.

You'll work closely with our Social Media & PR Manager on everything from coordinating influencer campaigns to engaging with our community across platforms. This is a hands-on role where you'll gain real experience in digital marketing, content strategy, and relationship management for a growing wellness brand.

What you'll do

Influencer & Partnership Management

  • Coordinate influencer outreach, contracts, and creative briefings
  • Organize content review and follow-up with brand partners
  • Assist in scripting and planning UGC campaigns
  • Support affiliate partner communication and relationship management

Community & Content

  • Manage day-to-day community engagement on Instagram, TikTok, Facebook, and Reddit
  • Participate in relevant wellness forums and conversations to build brand awareness
  • Assist in social media content creation and scheduling
  • Write first drafts of blog posts on red light therapy and wellness

SEO & Growth

  • Research and identify link-building opportunities to improve organic reach
  • Support content strategy with keyword research and topic ideation

General Marketing Support

  • Help coordinate marketing campaigns and track performance
  • Stay up-to-date on red light therapy and wellness industry trends
  • Collaborate with the team on creative projects and brand initiatives
  • Assist with In-person events and Photoshoots on a need-basis

Who you are

  • Organized, detail-oriented, and able to manage multiple projects simultaneously
  • Able to work autonomously and collaborate in a team setting
  • Genuinely interested in wellness and beauty communities
  • A strong communicator who's comfortable reaching out to influencers and partners
  • Social media savvy with an understanding of what makes content resonate - better yet, if you're a TikTok or YouTube lover!
  • Proactive and resourceful - you take initiative and find solutions independently

Requirements

  • Relevant experience or education in Marketing, Communications, Business, or a related field
  • Strong written and verbal communication skills in English
  • Familiarity with social media platforms (Instagram, TikTok, Facebook, Youtube and Pinterest)
  • Understanding of content creation and digital marketing fundamentals
  • Short-form video editing skills (Capcut, Edits and Canva)
  • Experience with influencer outreach or content
  • Comfortable learning new platforms quickly

Internship details

Start date: May 1st, 2025

  • Contract until the end of August 2025
  • Remote work with occasional meetings in Montréal
  • Possibility of a full-time role at the end of the internship based on performance and company needs

Why join us

  • Work on a premium, wellness brand
  • Learn directly from marketing experts in a hands-on, collaborative environment
  • Network with influencers and brands in the wellness industry
  • Gain real experience in influencer marketing, social media, and content strategy
