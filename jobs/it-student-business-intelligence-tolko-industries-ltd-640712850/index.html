Full Description

IT Student

4-month or 8-month work term starting May 2026 Vernon, British Columbia

Our IT students use their passion for data and technology to develop data pipelines and solve business problems. As a student, you will have the opportunity to work with seasoned professionals to build your skills and gain meaningful IT work experience. If this sounds like your kind of opportunity, read on to join and make a difference!

We’re looking for an IT Student to join our head office located in Vernon, British Columbia for a 4-month or 8-month work term. Our head office houses Tolko’s corporate services groups including Sales, Marketing & Logistics, Finance, Accounting & Credit, IT, Procurement and Human Resources.

Our ideal candidate has the following qualifications:

  • Currently enrolled in a post-secondary Information Services/Information Technology program or have graduated in the last 12 months in this field.
  • Strong desire to expand their learning within data and analytics.
  • Ability to communicate effectively both verbally and in writing.
  • Strong organizational and analytical skills.
  • Ability to work independently.
  • An introduction with at least one of the following is required:
  • SQL
  • Azure
  • Python
  • SSIS / Visual Studio
  • SSAS / Tabular Models
  • Data Warehousing Strategies and Methodologies
  • ETL Methodologies
  • Microsoft Power BI
  • Machine Learning / Predictive Analytics

We value diversity at Tolko and know that experience and knowledge come in many forms. Even if you don't have all the qualifications above, please apply. We would love to hear your story and have you grow with us.

In this role you will:

  • Work as a team member on the Business Intelligence and Analytics Team to support all facets of Tolko’s business.
  • Investigate and resolve issues submitted by the business.
  • Support the modification and build of new data pipelines on various platforms including SQL Server, and Azure.
  • Support various internal teams including Woodlands, Manufacturing, Export, and Logistics, Sales and Finance users to deliver excellent customer service.
  • Gain insight on multiple facets of Tolko’s business.

So, what’s in it for you?

  • You’ll take on an exciting new challenge with supportive team members.
  • You'll earn a competitive salary based on your skills and experience. The expected base salary range for this position is $46,000 to $56,000 per year.
  • Returning students may be eligible for a signing bonus.
  • You may also be eligible for relocation support.
  • You’ll be supported in continued development through on-the-job training.
  • You’ll be part of a positive, innovative, and inclusive work environment.

If you want to work in a sustainable, future focused industry, there is no better place than Tolko. Our family has been producing innovative forest products for over 65 years. Our focus remains on sustainably managing our forests and our business for upcoming generations.

We want YOU to join our family. Come Grow Here! Apply today at www.tolko.com/careers.
