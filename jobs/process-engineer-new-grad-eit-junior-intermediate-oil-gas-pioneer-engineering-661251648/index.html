Process Engineer (New Grad / EIT / Junior / Intermediate) – Oil & Gas
Pioneer Engineering Inc. – Calgary, AB
Full-Time

About Us

Pioneer Engineering Inc. is a growing, multi-disciplinary EPCM firm delivering high-quality engineering, procurement, and construction management services across Western Canada’s oil and gas sector.

We are known for our practical, cost-effective designs, strong client relationships, and efficient project execution. Our team values collaboration, accountability, and technical excellence.

About the Role

We are seeking a Process Engineer to join our Calgary team. This role is ideal for someone who enjoys working on a variety of upstream oil & gas projects and wants exposure to the full project lifecycle, from concept through detailed design.

You will play a key role in process design, simulation, and project execution while collaborating across multiple disciplines. You will also have the opportunity to work directly with senior engineers and clients across Western Canada.

We are open to candidates at the new grad, EIT, junior, and intermediate levels. Responsibilities, level, and compensation will be aligned with the candidate’s experience and qualifications.

Experience Levels

We are open to candidates at multiple experience levels. The scope of responsibilities and level of independence will be aligned with experience:

  • New Graduates / EITs (0–2 years): Support senior engineers, develop technical skills, and assist with simulations and process deliverables
  • Junior Engineers (2–5 years): Contribute to design work, perform simulations, and prepare deliverables with moderate supervision
  • Intermediate Engineers (5+ years): Take ownership of process design deliverables, coordinate across disciplines, and support project execution with minimal supervision

Key Responsibilities

  • Perform process simulations using Aspen HYSYS, flare system modelling, and EDR
  • Develop and review process deliverables, including:
  • PFDs, P&IDs, and process design criteria
  • Line lists, control narratives, and shutdown keys (SDK)
  • Equipment sizing, line sizing, and PSV sizing
  • Process data sheets for equipment and instrumentation
  • Coordinate with mechanical, electrical, and instrumentation teams
  • Ensure deliverables are accurate, on schedule, and within budget
  • Support project engineers and managers in executing multiple projects
  • Participate in design reviews and provide technical input to clients and vendors

Qualifications

  • Degree in Chemical Engineering
  • Please provide an unofficial transcript
  • Experience levels ranging from new graduate to intermediate; experience in upstream oil & gas facilities in Western Canada
  • Experience with HYSYS
  • Registered (or eligible for registration) with APEGA
  • Experience in natural gas processing, including:
  • Separation, compression, dehydration, refrigeration, and sweetening
  • Working knowledge of applicable codes and standards (ASME, CSA, API, ABSA, etc.)
  • Understanding of AER and/or BCER regulatory processes is an asset
  • Experience with construction, commissioning, or operations is considered an asset
  • Legally entitled to work in Canada. If you are not a Canadian citizen or permanent resident, you must maintain a valid work permit at all times.

Skills & Attributes

  • Strong analytical and problem-solving skills
  • Ability to manage multiple projects and priorities
  • High attention to detail and ability to self-check work
  • Effective written and verbal communication skills
  • Proficiency in core Microsoft 365 Applications

Why Join Pioneer Engineering?

  • Opportunity to work on diverse and technically challenging projects
  • Exposure to the full project lifecycle and direct client interaction
  • Collaborative team environment with direct access to senior leadership
  • Growing company with opportunities for career development
