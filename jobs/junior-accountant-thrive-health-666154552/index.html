Junior Accountant

at Thrive Health

📍 Location British Columbia Remote
📋 Details Full-time · Permanent · $60-66k ·
📅 Posted Apr 17, 2026

About the Role

Manage full-cycle accounting, payroll, and reporting

About Thrive Health

AI-first healthcare startup with growth opportunities

Full Description

At Thrive Health , we’re on a mission to make healthcare work better for everyone. Our digital care coordination platform connects people, data, and care across the entire health journey – empowering individuals and health professionals alike.

As an AI-first company , we’re building products that place people at the centre of care by enhancing care delivery, improving patient experiences, and helping clinicians focus on what matters most.

If you’re passionate about improving the healthcare system, bring an ambitious and creative spirit to complex tasks, and “thrive” in a fast-paced, start-up environment, you’ve come to the right place.

The Opportunity

We’re seeking a Junior Accountant to manage full-cycle accounting and support financial operations as we scale. You will be responsible for managing accounts payable and receivable, processing transactions, reconciling accounts, running payroll, maintaining and provisioning inventory, travel booking, and assisting with financial reporting in a fast-paced environment. The ideal candidate will possess a strong knowledge of Canadian accounting standards (ASPE and/or IFRS), excellent attention to detail, exceptional communication skills, the ability to work within deadlines, and the ability to work both collaboratively and independently.

📍 Remote-first | Based in BC or Alberta

🗓 Full-time | 💼 $60-66K

Responsibilities

Accounting and Bookkeeping

  • Maintain accurate bookkeeping records using QuickBooks Online;
  • Manage the full accounting cycle, including GST and PST calculations and filings, accruals, reconciliations, and financial statement preparation;
  • Process accounts payable and receivable transactions;
  • Assist with year end working papers and liaise with external accountants;
  • Support budgeting, forecasting, and cash flow management.

Payroll Administration

  • Process payroll efficiently using ADP, ensuring timely and accurate compensation for all employees;
  • Address payroll-related inquiries and resolve discrepancies promptly;
  • File and track Employer Health Taxes and Worksafe BC filings.

Financial Operations

  • Oversee invoicing and expense management, ensuring compliance with company policies;
  • Create, manage, and maintain Accounting SOPs.

Other Operations

  • Manage the submission of Statistics Canada surveys;
  • Handle the maintenance and provisioning of our laptop fleet;
  • Ensure compliance with Lobbying regulations.

Skills & Qualifications

  • Bachelor's degree in Finance, Accounting, or a related field with a strong preference for CPA designation (in progress or completed);
  • 1-2 years experience in full-cycle accounting (Canadian GAAP (ASPE)) and Canadian payroll administration (experience in ADP an asset);
  • Proficiency in QuickBooks Online and advanced Microsoft Excel skills;
  • Solid understanding of GST and PST regulations and compliance;
  • Excellent attention to detail and organizational skills;
  • Superior communication skills in English, both written and verbal;
  • Ability to meet deadlines and manage multiple tasks effectively;
  • Proficiency in remote tooling (Slack, Gsuite, Meet, Zoom etc);
  • Experience in the healthcare technology or SaaS industries is a plus;
  • Demonstrated ability to work independently and collaboratively within a team.

📩 Apply by sending your resume to careers@thrive.health — even if you don’t tick every box! We value curiosity, collaboration, and courage over perfection. Join us and help shape the future of AI-driven healthcare in Canada.
