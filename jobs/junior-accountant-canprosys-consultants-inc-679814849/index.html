Full Description

Job Overview
We are seeking a detail-oriented and motivated Junior Accountant to join our finance team. This entry-level position offers an excellent opportunity to develop your accounting skills within a dynamic corporate environment. The ideal candidate will support various financial functions, including bookkeeping, account reconciliation, and financial reporting, utilizing leading accounting software. This role is perfect for individuals passionate about finance and eager to grow their career in accounting and financial services.

Duties

  • Assist with accounts payable and accounts receivable processes to ensure timely payments and collections
  • Maintain accurate records using accounting software such as QuickBooks, Xero, Sage, PeopleSoft, and Accountancy software
  • Perform bank reconciliations and account analysis to verify financial data accuracy
  • Support bookkeeping activities, including data entry and ledger management
  • Aid in account reconciliation and auditing processes to ensure compliance with company policies
  • Help prepare financial reports and assist with budgeting tasks
  • Support payroll processing and related activities as needed
  • Collaborate with team members on financial projects and administrative tasks related to corporate finance
  • Ensure adherence to internal controls and financial regulations

Requirements

  • Basic understanding of accounting principles and practices
  • Experience or familiarity with accounting software such as QuickBooks, Xero, Sage, PeopleSoft, or similar platforms
  • Knowledge of accounts payable, accounts receivable, bank reconciliation, and account analysis
  • Strong organizational skills with attention to detail
  • Ability to work effectively within a team environment
  • Excellent communication skills—both written and verbal
  • Prior experience in bookkeeping or financial services is a plus but not required
  • A degree in Accounting, Finance, or related field is preferred but not mandatory for entry-level candidates

This position provides an excellent foundation for those looking to build a career in accounting or finance. We are committed to supporting professional growth through ongoing training and development opportunities.
