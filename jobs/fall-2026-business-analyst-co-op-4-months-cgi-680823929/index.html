About the Role

About CGI

Canada's largest independent IT services firm

Full Description

We are Canada's largest independent information technology services firm, and after 45 years, we're still growing. CGI is a leading services provider to major Financial Institutions in the Greater Toronto Area. We are offering the opportunity to work with our team of dedicated consultants, and we are looking for you. This is an excellent opportunity to learn about financial modelling and business analytics while working with Canada’s largest IT consulting firm. We are offering a chance to work with our team of experience professionals, who are responsible for maintaining the standards of project management and providing high-value support to our Business Unit. This is your opportunity to learn about business operations and project management in a sophisticated business environment. You will work with CGI Consultants, Directors and Executives on a full range of business operations, program management and resourcing activities/tasks.
In an effort to foster collaboration and partner with clients, co-op students will be required to be in the office multiple times a week.

Start Date: September 2026
Application Deadline: May 25th, 2026
Work term length: 4 months

Perks of Being a CGI Co-op:

  • Participate in monthly socials to meet co-ops across the country.
  • Access professional development workshops hosted by domain experts on topics ranging from how to be a top consultant, how to apply the agile methodology, data & analytics and more!

To learn more about CGI and hear from our Co-ops and New Grads, visit www.cgi.com/startmycareer

Your future duties and responsibilities

Daily activities include data analysis, refine and deliver Business Operational Reports for these areas – Resourcing, Business Development, Financial, Utilization and Communication.

  • Build and manipulate reports from raw data using sophisticated Excel spreadsheets, graphs, formulation, conditional formatting, etc.
  • Assist with various projects and help set up initiatives.
  • Maintain and follow-up on action items, issues, and status reporting.
  • Conduct business research and information management.
  • Project control and organization, including project governance, communication and issue management.
  • Create or assist in the creation of presentations.
  • Responsible for meeting minutes, distribution of agendas, meeting logistics and running Live meetings.
  • Work directly with VPs to detail and build strategy materials.
  • Develop financial models and perform analysis on data to drive hypothesis’s and conclusions.
  • Conduct industry research and summarize information for general consumption.
  • Coordinate workshops and meeting Logistics.

Required qualifications to be successful in this role

  • Must be in a Co-op Accredited program
  • Strong desire to learn about business operations.
  • Strong communications skills (verbal & written).
  • Excellent organizational and time management abilities.
  • Consulting and data analysis skills.
  • Business and financial background with experience using financial modelling.
  • Ability to efficiently multi-task.
  • Ability to work on own with minimal direction, as well as in a team environment.
  • Sophisticated Microsoft Office tools: Outlook, Excel (Macros, Pivot Table, VLOOKUP, Conditional formatting, Graphs), PowerPoint, SharePoint, Word.
