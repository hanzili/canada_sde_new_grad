Geoscience / Mining Internship

Geology Summer Student

at Kinross Gold Corporation

📍 Location Red Lake, Ontario
📋 Details Internship · Summer · $23.92/hour · Seasonal
📅 Posted Apr 23, 2026

About the Role

Assist geology team; sampling, fieldwork, data support

About Kinross Gold Corporation

Kinross Gold: Global gold mining leader

Full Description

The Great Bear project is a development-stage asset located in the renowned and prolific Red Lake mining district, and represents one of the most exciting recent gold discoveries globally. The project has excellent potential to become a top-tier deposit that could support a large, long-life mine complex. Kinross’ comprehensive exploration and development program will aim to support the vision of a quality, high-grade, open-pit mine and a longer-term, sizeable underground mine.

Job Summary The Geology Summer Student will be responsible for assisting the Geology team at the Great Bear project. This position will require training within the department while continuously aligning daily functions to reflect Kinross’ values.

Key Responsibilities and Duties

  • Assist in the execution of exploration programs.
  • Provide geological support to technical & geological staff.
  • Photo/RQD/RMR/Sample Core as needed.
  • Undertake sampling activities in the field and core yard.
  • Work in a healthy work environment with full support, cooperation, and commitment to satisfy our operation’s goal of being accident-free.
  • Support a work environment that is aligned with Kinross’ Safeground principles.
  • Comply with federal and provincial acts and regulations, and legal requirements.
  • Adhere to all relevant Great Bear policies and procedures.
  • Perform other duties as assigned.

Qualifications And Skill Requirements This position will be offered to a student going into their 2nd, 3rd, or 4th year of a Geology Program. Must provide proof of enrollment in a post-secondary institution on a full-time basis and be returning to the school in the fall.

  • Demonstrates strong administration, organization and technical skills.
  • Comfortable knowledge working with Microsoft Excel (data entry, basic formulas, filtering, etc.)
  • Comprehends and capable or interpreting technical language.
  • Possess a valid G2 or G Drivers License.
  • Ability to work outdiirs and in inclement weather when required.

Work Conditions

This position is based in Red Lake, Ontario. In this role you will work a 5X2 schedule.

Compensation And Total Rewards The hourly rate for the Geology Student starts at $23.92 per hour.

The hourly rate is in accordance with our established compensation framework for the role. This rate is fixed for the role and reflects requirements and benchmark market data for similar positions.

Use of AI in Our Hiring Process We use AI-enabled tools to help sort and review applications based on job-related criteria. All hiring decisions, including who moves forward in the process, are made by a human.

Existing Vacancy This job is for an existing vacancy.

Kinross is a Canadian-based global senior gold mining company with operations and projects in the United States, Brazil, Mauritania, Chile, and Canada. Our focus on delivering value is based on our core principles of responsible mining, operational excellence, disciplined growth and balance sheet strength. Kinross maintains listings on the Toronto Stock Exchange (symbol:K) and the New York Stock Exchange (symbol:KGC).
