Full Description

Position: Sales Associate – Luigi & Sons

Reports to: Store Manager

Luigi & Sons is a boutique retail space in Vancouver's Yaletown district that provide freshly cut meats, grab-and-go selections, as well as house-made provisions – an extension of the Elisa Steakhouse family.

The Sales Associate role is the first point of contact our customers have when they come to Luigi & Sons. We are looking for individuals who are friendly, outgoing, organized and have a genuine passion for hospitality and a desire to make a memorable customer service experience.

Some of the Benefits of working at Luigi & Sons:

  • Flexible work schedules (can flex with school schedules)
  • Group medical plan offered after 6 months of continuous employment, subject to minimum hours
  • A family/team culture with long term career opportunities

Essential Duties and Responsibilities:

(Other duties may be assigned as required)

  • Actively engage with guests, cultivating relationships and ensuring guest satisfaction
  • Provide expert and detailed information on our product line
  • Assist customers with orders; anticipating their needs, offering suggestions and answering questions
  • Handling customer transactions
  • Maintain a fully stocked, tidy and clean environment
  • Process orders and pick ups
  • Close down and set out cash till at the end of the night
  • Assist with product packaging and refresh display products

Required Experience & Qualifications:

  • Previous cashier or customer service experience is an asset
  • Completion of Secondary Education
  • Excellent English communication skills, both verbal and written
  • Ability to multi-task and have an eye for detail
  • Organized and able to work under pressure in a very busy environment
  • Ability to work flexible hours as required (mornings, weekends, nights and/or holidays)
  • Must be legally entitled to work in Canada

This position will remain open until filled.
