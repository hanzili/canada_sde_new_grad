About the Role

Perform organic synthesis, purification, and analyses

About OTI Lumionics Inc.

Innovative OLED materials company; advanced synthesis

Full Description

About Us

OTI Lumionics is developing advanced materials—by design—using quantum simulations, machine learning and real-world testing in pilot production. We are currently focused on key enabling materials for OLED displays that will be used in next-generation consumer electronics and automotive. Our advanced electrode materials, and associated manufacturing technology, are being used to build transparent displays and lighting. We work closely with our partners and customers to design new materials that are mass-production ready. Using our computational Materials Discovery Platform, we are able to rapidly iterate new materials, allowing fast turn around time to meet our customers’ needs.

Workplace Type

On-Site

Primary Responsibilities

OTI Lumionics is looking for a Junior Organic Chemist to join its Synthesis team. The Junior Organic Chemist will carry out organic synthesis and produce molecules using various chemical and instrumental techniques, in accordance with SOPs, and health & safety standards, while working under minimal supervision. Responsibilities include, but are not limited to:

  • Following established method procedures, SOPs, and safety guidelines for setting up and organizing laboratory equipment and reactions to ensure accurate, efficient and in a safe fashion
  • Performing laboratory separation of complex mixtures using advanced and fundamental purification techniques such as normal phase chromatography, reversed phase chromatography, ion-exchange, in accordance with established procedures
  • Conducting laboratory analysis of raw materials, in-process and finished products by using analytical techniques such as NMR, MS, TGA and DSC
  • Evaluating physical properties of raw materials, in-process and finished products such as melting point and solubility
  • Reporting experimental results and any deviations encountered during synthesis and suggesting appropriate improvements to methods or procedures
  • Presenting data clearly and accurately in both written documentation and verbal discussions
  • Ensuring compliance with environmental policies, procedures, and objectives, and actively participate in the identification and reduction of environmental impacts related to the role
  • Adhering to health and safety protocols, reporting hazards, and contributing to maintaining a safe and healthy work environment for all employees
  • Performing other ad hoc duties as assigned

Profile

  • PhD or MSc in Organic Chemistry

Required Skills

  • Strong knowledge in organic reactions and their corresponding mechanisms
  • Ability to conduct chemical literature research
  • Knowledge of Microsoft Office (Word, Excel, PowerPoint) and relevant data processing program
  • Experience with laboratory setup and maintenance is desirable
  • Knowledge and understanding of GMP/GLP is considered an asset
  • Excellent verbal and written communication skills
  • Strong attention to detail
  • Fast learner who is flexible, proactive, and demonstrates a strong initiative and a can do attitude
  • Willingness to work extended hours when required
  • Ability to work all shifts, including morning, evening, weekend, and holiday shifts
