About KGHM International Ltd.

Full Description

The Company

KGHM International Ltd., headquartered in Vaughan, Ontario, is the Canadian subsidiary of KGHM Polska Miedź S.A., one of the world’s largest producers of copper and silver. KGHM International has operations across North America and engages in exploration and development projects to expand its resource base, reinforcing its role as a global leader in mining and metallurgy.

With a strong commitment to sustainability and community engagement, KGHM International, integrates environmental stewardship with advanced mining technologies and collaborates closely with Indigenous communities through impact and benefit agreements. These partnerships ensure respect for Indigenous rights and cultural heritage while promoting shared economic benefits.

With investments in infrastructure, exploration, and social responsibility programs, KGHM International continues to strengthen its role as a responsible mining leader committed to innovation, efficiency, and long-term value creation.

Our culture is underpinned by our values - zero harm, teamwork, results-driven, accountability, and courage. These values are deeply rooted convictions which guide our day-to-day behaviours and form the organisation's DNA.

Our values are a lodestar for all our action and facilitate the decision-making process of today and build long-term strategies of the future.

The Opportunity

The Geology Summer Student – Core Logging position will report in to the Area Geologist. The postion is on site at the Core Shack located in Whitefish, Ontario. This position is for a period of 4 months, on a Monday to Friday work schedule (subject to change if required for operational needs). The start date for this position is May 1, 2026 with and end date of August 31, 2026.

What You’ll Do

  • Committing to a safe and healthy work environment for self, and colleagues;
  • Logging of diamond drill core. Following all QA/QC standards;
  • Handling and transferring of drill core from drill site to core facility via truck;
  • Assisting geologists and other technicians with handling of drill core within the core facility;
  • Responsible for keeping well organized log of all drill core samples;
  • Labelling of samples and core boxes and documenting un-sampled core;
  • Miscellaneous paperwork and filing;
  • Follow departmental safety program and maintain safety standards;
  • Maintaining a clean work environment;
  • Such other duties as may be assigned.

What You’ll Bring

  • Previous Core Logging work would be an asset but will train.
  • Geology Student – 2nd year university/college or better preferred.
  • Ability to safely use small hand tools.
  • Basic MS Office functionality.
  • Driver’s license required.
  • First Aid training would be an asset.

At KGHM International, we value diverse perspectives and experiences. If you’re excited about this role but don’t meet every listed requirement, we encourage you to apply. Skills can be developed, and we believe in supporting growth and potential. Your unique background could be exactly what we need.

What we offer

Compensation for this position is $28.70 per hour.
