Full Description

About the Role:

We’re looking for an enthusiastic and driven Entry-Level Event Marketing Associate to join our dynamic direct marketing team. This is an excellent opportunity for someone looking to start a career in events, marketing, and brand promotion. You’ll play a key role in supporting the setup, coordination, and delivery of marketing events that help connect brands with customers face-to-face.

Key Responsibilities:

  • Assist in planning, organising, and executing marketing events, pop-ups, and promotional stands.
  • Represent clients’ brands professionally while engaging with customers at events.
  • Coordinate event logistics, including equipment setup, promotional materials, and staffing.
  • Support the marketing team in developing creative event strategies to attract and retain customers.
  • Track event performance and provide feedback to improve future campaigns.
  • Work collaboratively with team members to ensure smooth event delivery and customer satisfaction.
  • Maintain accurate records of daily activities and report results to management.

Requirements:

  • Excellent communication and interpersonal skills.
  • Positive, outgoing, and professional attitude.
  • Strong organisational and multitasking abilities.
  • A team player who thrives in a fast-paced, customer-focused environment.
  • No previous experience required, full training and ongoing support provided.
  • A genuine interest in marketing, events, and brand development.

What We Offer:

  • Comprehensive training and mentorship to develop your marketing and event coordination skills.
  • Clear opportunities for career progression within marketing, events, or management.
  • A lively and supportive work culture.

If you’re passionate about events, love working with people, and are eager to kick-start your marketing career, apply now to become an Entry-Level Event Marketing Associate and be part of a growing team that brings brands to life through direct marketing.
