Professional Intern - Business Analyst

Professional Intern– Business Analyst at Birchwood Used Vehicle Services Full-Time , TERM Summer Internship Position

Start date: May (Flexible), End Date: End of August

Closing date: March 11st, 2026

The Professional Intern – Business Analyst will support ongoing initiatives for both the Birchwood Used Vehicle Services and Business Analyst teams. Reporting to the Asset Manager, the Business Analyst will act as a resource in support of a number of business operations. The successful candidate will play a key role in defining trends and areas of opportunities for the Birchwood Automotive Group.

Professional Interns have been an integral part of Birchwood for over 25 years. The professional intern program has evolved to include an immersive experience that focuses on personal and professional development. Our program is designed to create a network of empowered students who gain valuable skills while supporting Birchwood’s purpose. Our internship includes professional development workshops, unique social events, and an opportunity to network with our executive team.

At Birchwood, experience is everything, and we want you to be a part of our team.

Project Opportunities

  • Analyze monthly client financial results using financial analysis models and tools.
  • Take part in a team environment, while providing real-time collaborative feedback on asset procurement and reallocation.
  • Gain exposure to the used vehicle marketplace at an operational level, thus being strategically placed to influence decision making.
  • Research & develop key reports to assess accuracy & effectiveness in KPI reporting by utilizing analytics software including Power BI.
  • Assist in inventory risk assessment and mitigation activities.
  • Develop initiatives to reduce inventory inefficiencies and to improve inventory Return on Investment (ROI).

Qualifications

  • Participating in the Asper School of Business Co-op program, preferably in their third or fourth year of studies.
  • Majoring in Business Analytics is preferred.
  • Creative ability to conceptualize and articulate new approaches and solutions.
  • Demonstrates an understanding of Return on Investment, micro-economics and business acumen.
  • Basic experience using Power BI is required.
  • Strong interpersonal and collaboration skills as you will be dealing with many stakeholders within the industry.
  • Thrive in a collaborative environment where team support is encouraged.
  • A commitment to learning and professional development.
  • Comfortable leveraging technology for most day-to-day functions.
  • A valid class 5 driver’s license and reliable transportation to work is required.

Birchwood is Manitoba’s largest automotive retailer. Our 24 franchises are in addition to four state-of-the-art collision repair centres and four Birchwood Credit Solutions locations. We offer sales, services, and parts to meet our customers’ automotive needs. We’re a recognized leader in the retail automotive industry, not only for our diverse workforce reflecting our community, but also for our unparalleled commitment to team members, customers, partners, and neighbourhoods. At Birchwood, our days begin and end with gratitude, personal leadership, trust, respect, continuous improvement, and generosity.
