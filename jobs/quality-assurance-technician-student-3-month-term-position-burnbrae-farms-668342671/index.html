Full Description

Quality Assurance Technician – Student (3 month Term Position)

JOB DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES

  • Perform required microbiological, physical and chemical analysis on ingredients, in-process products, finished products and shelf-life samples
  • Accurately record results
  • Obtain raw and finished product samples from production line
  • Prepare media
  • Perform equipment calibrations
  • Perform HACCP and quality systems verifications and inspections
  • Assist with problem solving when line performance is not optimal
  • Take appropriate corrective actions when deviations are found
  • Take GMP, HACCP and quality systems training, as required
  • Working shifts in afternoon and evenings

REQUIRED KNOWLEDGE, SKILLS, AND ABILITIES

  • Strong oral and written communication skills
  • Excellent organizational skills and ability to work in a fast-paced environment
  • Ability to follow instructions and strong attention to detail
  • Ability to work as a team or independently with minimal Supervision
  • Working proficiency with MS Office applications
  • Must be legally entitled to work in Canada
  • Must possess and maintain a valid Manitoba class 5 driver's license
  • Must be willing to work various hours, including evening, nights, weekend and holidays

MINIMUM EDUCATION / EXPERIENCE

  • Degree or Diploma in Food Science, Food Technology or related field
  • Previous food manufacturing or lab experience would be an asset

Burnbrae Farms; Mission – A Canadian egg business dedicated to quality, health and innovation.

Burnbrae Farms’ Vision is to grow into a world-class egg business using the strength of our people to develop leading edge products and processes. Through our commitment to quality, health and innovation, we will provide healthy lifestyle solutions, exceed customer expectations and build highly trusted brands.

We offer our employees the opportunity to grow personally and professionally, to contribute to the success of a dynamic organization, and to serve others in a manner that exceeds expectations.

Burnbrae Farms is committed to Employment Equity. Accommodations during the recruitment process are available upon request for candidates with disabilities.
