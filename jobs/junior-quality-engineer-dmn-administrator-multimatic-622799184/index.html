About Multimatic

Full Description

Position Overview Multimatic Niche Vehicles North America (MNVNA) is the exclusive production facility of the Ford GTD Mustang.

MNVNA is seeking a Junior Quality Engineer & DMN Administrator who will be responsible for creating, tracking, administrating and following up on all Internal and External DMNs (Defective Material Notifications) and Quality Alerts. As well as following the 24-hour containment, 14-day permanent corrective action and 30-day corrective action verification and closure rules. They will assume ownership of the External DMN log, Internal DMN & Cost of Quality log. The Junior Quality Engineer & DMN Administrator will be the primary point of contact for all supplier quality issues.

Duties & Responsibilities

  • Issue/initiate DMNs for supplier quality issues discovered in all areas of the manufacturing and warehouse floor
  • Administer DMN Log
  • Issue/initiate Quality Alerts for supplier quality concerns discovered in all areas of the manufacturing and warehouse floor
  • Administer Quality Alert Log
  • Issue/initiate internal DMNs to track (COQ) Cost of Quality due to MNV caused defective material
  • Administer Internal DMN/COQ Log
  • 24-hour, 14-day and 30-day follow-up on all issued supplier DMNs
  • Participate in Quality training functions as required
  • Maintain customer, company and supplier confidentiality
  • Must comply with all ISO9001/TS16949, ISO 14001 (Environmental) and OHSA (Health and Safety) systems and standards
  • Adhering to Multimatic‚Äôs quality-focused attitude and work culture
  • All other duties as assigned

Education & Experience

  • Engineering Technologist Diploma or Engineering Degree
  • A minimum of 1 to 2 years of experience in Tier 1 Automotive Quality Engineering
  • Sound knowledge of 8D Process & solid knowledge of Root Cause Analysis
  • Working experience with Automotive Customer Specifics including AIAG requirements
  • Sound understanding of SPC, Engineering Drawings, CAD & GD&T
  • Experience in supplier quality assurance is an asset
