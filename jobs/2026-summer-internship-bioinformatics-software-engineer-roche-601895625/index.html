The Position

*The Opportunity*

  • The Summer at Roche Intern Program has been developed to provide students with a fun yet rewarding summer through hands-on experience. Interns enjoy opportunities to network with other interns as well as employees throughout our organization. Additionally, we help our students meet their career development and educational goals while potentially building a future with the Roche organization
  • By leveraging the education you have already received, we hope to give you an opportunity to grow and develop with us!
  • As a Bioinformatics Software Engineering intern, you will be part of a computational science and informatics team building cutting edge bioinformatics and software tools for Next Generation Sequencing informatics solutions

*Who You Are:*

  • You hold an undergraduate degree in Bioinformatics, Biomedical engineering, Data Science Engineering, Computer Science or closely related fields
  • You are currently pursuing a Master's Degree (enrolled student) in Bioinformatics, Biomedical engineering, Data Science Engineering, Computer Science or closely related fields
  • You have experience building or running bioinformatics algorithms and pipelines
  • Proficiency in Python programming skills
  • Excellent problem-solving and analytical skills
  • Experience with version control systems like Git
  • Familiarity with software development methodologies and tools such as SCRUM, agile or similar.

*Preferred:*

  • Excellent communication, collaboration, and interpersonal skills
  • Complements our culture and the standards that guide our daily behavior & decisions: Integrity, Courage, and Passion

*Additional Information:*

  • Duration: (4 months), work term (Full time) and will begin in May, 2026
  • Location: Based in Mississauga, Ontario
  • Our team follows a hybrid work structure (minimum 3 days per week in the office)
