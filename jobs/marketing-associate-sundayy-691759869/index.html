Full Description

About The Company At Baxter, we are dedicated to making a meaningful difference in healthcare by providing innovative solutions that improve patient outcomes worldwide. Founded in 1931, Baxter has established itself as a global leader in medical products and services, committed to redefining healthcare delivery. Our mission is to save and sustain lives through the development of high-quality, reliable products and a patient-centric approach. Our culture fosters courage, trust, and collaboration, empowering our colleagues to take ownership and make impactful contributions. We believe that every individual, regardless of background or location, deserves the opportunity to live a healthy life. Join us in our journey to transform healthcare and make a positive impact on communities around the globe.

About The Role This position offers an exciting opportunity for a marketing professional to contribute to Baxter’s mission by supporting various digital marketing initiatives and customer engagement strategies. The role involves working closely with cross-functional teams to develop, analyze, and optimize marketing campaigns, portals, and digital platforms. You will be instrumental in managing content updates, supporting market research, and executing education and engagement campaigns. Your efforts will help enhance Baxter’s digital presence, foster stronger relationships with healthcare professionals and customers, and ensure the effective delivery of marketing messages. This role demands a proactive individual with strong communication skills, a strategic mindset, and a passion for healthcare innovation. You will have the chance to develop your skills through collaboration with diverse teams and exposure to global marketing strategies, making a tangible impact on healthcare delivery and patient care.

Qualifications

  • Bachelor’s degree or equivalent experience in Marketing, Communications, Business Administration, or related field
  • Minimum of 3 years of proven experience in marketing support, digital marketing, or related roles
  • Experience with market research, customer engagement, and digital campaign management
  • Strong knowledge of marketing tools, platforms, and analytics
  • Excellent communication and presentation skills
  • Strong organizational and collaboration skills
  • Ability to analyze data and generate insights to inform marketing strategies
  • Experience working with third-party vendors and managing projects from inception to completion
  • Familiarity with marketing technology platforms and content management systems is preferred

Responsibilities

  • Provide marketing support for market research initiatives and customer events to gather insights and enhance engagement
  • Assist in developing and analyzing customer surveys, competitor research, and clinical practice studies to inform strategic decisions
  • Collaborate with the Marketing team to manage and refresh content on the Education Portal, ensuring relevance and accuracy
  • Coordinate with Commercial Perfection and Global Corporate Marketing to review digital marketing metrics and report on performance across Canadian assets
  • Manage Baxter Canada's LinkedIn pages, including content updates, community engagement, and advocacy for Baxter’s initiatives
  • Lead the execution and scaling of customer-facing portals, micro-sites, and digital campaigns
  • Partner with various departments and third-party vendors to develop and maintain digital assets and campaigns
  • Support planning and execution of education campaigns, webinars, and promotional activities
  • Conduct training sessions for marketing teams on event management, digital publications, and marketing tools
  • Monitor campaign performance, analyze data, and provide insights for iterative improvements
  • Stay informed about marketing technology platforms and best practices to optimize digital marketing efforts

Benefits

  • Comprehensive health and dental insurance plans
  • Paid time off and flexible work arrangements
  • Opportunities for professional development and continuous learning
  • Collaborative and inclusive work environment
  • Employee wellness programs and resources
  • Participation in impactful healthcare initiatives that make a difference
