Overview

Do you want to be a part of an organization where management appreciates and recognizes your hard work and values your input? If you are ready for a challenging position that promotes personal and professional growth, keep reading!

Harbour Foundations is a local mid-sized structural formwork company with job sites located throughout Halifax Regional Municipality.

Our team has experienced tremendous growth in the last few years, and we continue to grow. We are currently looking to hire a junior project coordinator for current and upcoming projects. In this position you will work with and report to the Senior Project coordinator.

Job Description:

As Jr Project Coordinator, you will play a role in managing multiple projects in conjunction with the site supervisors and senior project coordinator.

Key Responsibilities:

  • Review and interpret blueprints, schematics, and construction drawings to facilitate accurate project execution
  • Collaborate with architects, engineers and client to facilitate effective communication and problem solving
  • Manage documentation including contracts, change orders, RFIs submittals to maintain organized project records
  • Identify and mitigate project issues
  • Logging project drawings and keeping the site up to date with current drawings
  • Support the supervisors and senior project coordinator in day-to-day operations
  • Job documentation management
  • Administrative tasks
  • Other duties as assigned

Qualifications:

  • Strong knowledge of blueprint reading, schematics and construction drawings
  • Proven experience as a Project Coordinator or similar role
  • Experience in coordination on multiple projects
  • Strong organizational and time-management skills
  • Excellent communication and interpersonal abilities
  • Proficient in word, excel, outlook and teams
  • Knowledge of AutoCAD an asset
  • Estimating concrete formwork an asset
