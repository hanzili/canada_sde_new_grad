Full Description

Ajopro Financial Inc. | Remote (Canada) About the Role Ajopro is building infrastructure for a new financial system — one designed to enable structured access without relying on traditional debt. As a Junior DevOps Analyst, you will support the reliability, scalability, and security of the systems powering Ajopro. This role is ideal for someone early in their career who is eager to work at the intersection of cloud infrastructure, automation, and fintech systems. You will collaborate closely with engineering and product teams to ensure our platform operates efficiently as we scale. What You’ll Do Support deployment, monitoring, and maintenance of Ajopro infrastructure Assist in managing cloud environments (AWS or equivalent) Monitor system performance, uptime, and incident response Troubleshoot issues across applications, APIs, and payment integrations Collaborate with engineering teams to improve CI/CD pipelines Maintain logs, alerts, and system documentation Support security, compliance, and operational best practices Who You Are 0–3 years of experience in DevOps, IT, or Cloud Engineering Basic understanding of cloud platforms (AWS preferred) Familiarity with Linux environments and scripting (Bash, Python, etc.) Understanding of CI/CD pipelines and deployment workflows Strong problem-solving mindset with attention to detail Eager to learn and grow in a fast-paced environment Nice to Have Experience with Docker and/or Kubernetes Exposure to fintech, payments, or regulated systems Familiarity with monitoring tools (Datadog, CloudWatch, etc.) Understanding of APIs and system integrations Why This Role Matters Ajopro operates at the intersection of finance, compliance, and infrastructure. Reliability is not optional — it is foundational. This role directly impacts how users trust, access, and experience financial systems built on Ajopro. What You’ll Gain Hands-on experience building real fintech infrastructure Exposure to payment systems and regulatory environments (RPAA) Opportunity to grow into a senior DevOps or platform engineering role Experience working on live, scalable systems — not just internal tools A front-row seat to building a category-defining financial platform Join Us If you want to work on systems that actually move money — not just code — this role is for you.
