About the Role

Assist audits, prepare tax returns, gain CPA experience.

About Wilde Advisory Group

Mid-sized CPA firm with mentorship and training.

Full Description

Location : Vegreville, Alberta (just under an hour from Edmonton)

Start Date : Flexible, based on graduation

Apply to : Renée Senko – careers@wildeag.ca

Advance Your Career, Make An Impact

At Wilde & Company, we’ve been helping clients and developing professionals for over 60 years. As a mid-sized public accounting firm, we offer articling students something unique: the opportunity to work directly with experienced CPAs, gain exposure to a wide range of industries, and be part of a training model that has consistently produced successful, well-rounded professionals.

We’re proud to be a firm that’s large enough to offer variety and challenge, but small enough that you’ll never be just a number. Our articling students are trusted with real responsibility and supported every step of the way.

What You’ll Be Doing

  • Compilation, review, and audit engagements
  • Corporate and personal tax preparation
  • Exposure to a wide range of industries and organization types

What Sets Us Apart

We know you have options when it comes to where you article. Here’s why Wilde & Company stands out:

  • Proven Training Model:  Our students consistently succeed in the CPA program and beyond, thanks to structured mentorship and real-world experience.
  • Depth of Experience:  With decades of service across multiple sectors, we offer exposure to a wide range of client needs and business challenges.
  • Direct Access to Senior Staff:  You’ll work closely with partners and senior managers who are invested in your development.
  • Balanced Workload:  Our team enjoys a steady, manageable workflow year-round.

What We Offer

  • Competitive salary
  • CPA module fees and travel are covered
  • Moving bonus
  • Flexible benefits plan
  • Fridays off in July & August
  • Work-from-anywhere flexibility and adaptable scheduling

Grow Your Skills, Live Your Passion

We take pride in our work, but we also know how to enjoy the job. Whether it’s team lunches, themed office days, or community events, we make time to connect. Our team supports each other through busy seasons and celebrates milestones together. You’ll be part of a workplace that values professionalism, balance, and time to enjoy what you are passionate about along the way.

How to Apply

Send your  cover letter, resume, and transcripts  to:

Renée Senko  – careers@wildeag.ca

APPLICATION DEADLINE: March 31, 2025

We’ll also be at the Meet Your Employer Event in Edmonton on March 16, 2026 -come meet us and learn more about what it’s like to work at Wilde & Company, CPAs.
