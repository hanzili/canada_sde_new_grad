About the role

Launching your career at Intact means joining a diverse community of experts who value your skills in an ever-changing industry. Here, you will receive coaching in a dynamic environment where the work is concrete. You will innovate and drive our organization forward, for the benefit of customers and communities. You will grow both personally and professionally through state-of-the-art learning platforms and develop lasting connections throughout the organization. Above all, you will see how stimulating the world of work can be.

Here, your career will take off

We are looking for a Procurement Analyst our growing team!

What you’ll do here:

Third-Party Risk Management

  • Help maintain and improve third-party risk management tools and databases.
  • Prepare reports, presentations, and supporting materials for internal reviews and discussions with key stakeholders.
  • Download supplier financial rating documents and analyze trends and fluctuations.

Data and Analytics

  • Collect, clean, and analyze procurement data to identify trends and insights.
  • Assist with dashboard creation and updates using tools such as Power BI, Jira, and other procurement platforms.
  • Prepare periodic reports and presentations to communicate findings and recommendations under guidance.

Business Analysis and Documentation

  • Document business requirements and translate stakeholder discussions into actionable plans and tasks.
  • Collaborate across departments to gather inputs and support project coordination.

AI and Tools

  • Contribute to identifying, prioritizing, and delivering AI use cases that improve procurement performance and operational efficiency.
  • Support the team in defining data strategies that enable AI use cases and the broader AI roadmap.
  • Research industry trends and emerging technologies

What you bring to the table:

  • Bachelor’s degree in business administration, procurement & supply chain management, data science or a related field.
  • Technical skills: Strong Excel, proficient in PowerPoint and Visio; familiarity with Oracle, Power BI, and Jira.
  • Data/AI: understanding of AI technologies and data strategy; ability to derive insights from complex datasets
  • Act as a change agent to promote new ideas, processes, and tools
  • Excellent communication and interpersonal skills to work effectively with diverse teams.
  • Analytical and problem-solving skills
  • Autonomous and responsible
  • Bilingualism (French and English) Requirement to interact on a regular basis with colleagues across the country
  • Available 35 hours a week from September 8 to December 18, 2026
  • Must be actively enrolled in a post-secondary institution during the internship
