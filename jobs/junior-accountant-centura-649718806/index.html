Junior Accountant

at Centura

📍 Location Dartmouth, NS B3B 1H3
📋 Details Full-time
📅 Posted Mar 24, 2026

About the Role

Process GL, reconcile accounts, support month-end close

About Centura

Mid-size firm with growing accounting practice

Full Description

Job Overview
We are seeking a detail-oriented and experienced Junior Accountant to join our finance team. The ideal candidate will have a strong background in accounting principles, with expertise in various accounting software. This role requires excellent analytical skills, attention to detail, and the ability to work collaboratively within a team environment.

Responsibilities

  • Verify, allocate, post and reconcile monthly recurring entries, accrual, prepaids etc.
  • Process HST/GST monthly returns.
  • Assist in managing Accounts Receivables.
  • Calculate and post monthly depreciation on fixed assets.
  • Processing bank reconciliations on monthly basis.
  • Produce error-free accounting reports and present their results
  • Analyze financial information and summarize financial status
  • Assist to prepare financial statements
  • Support month-end and year-end close process
  • Liaise with other departments to improve financial procedures

Requirements and skills

  • Proven experience as an book keeper/accountant with at least 1-2 years of experience.
  • Thorough knowledge of basic accounting procedures.
  • Familiarity with financial accounting statements
  • Experience with general ledger functions and the month-end/year-end close process
  • Advanced MS Excel skills .
  • Accuracy and attention to detail
  • Aptitude for numbers and quantitative skills
  • Degree in Accounting, Finance or relevant.

Job Type: Full-time

  • Extended health care
  • Paid time off

  • Bachelor's Degree (preferred)

Language:

Work Location: In person
