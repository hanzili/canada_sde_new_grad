About Turner & Townsend Pty Limited

Full Description

Company Description

Turner & Townsend is a global professional services company with over 22,000 people in more than 60 countries.

Working with our clients across real estate, infrastructure, energy and natural resources, we transform together delivering outcomes that improve people’s lives. Working in partnership makes it possible to deliver the world’s most impactful projects and programmes as we turn challenge into opportunity and complexity into success.

Our capabilities include programme, project, cost, asset and commercial management, controls and performance, procurement and supply chain, net zero and digital solutions.

We are majority-owned by CBRE Group, Inc., the world’s largest commercial real estate services and investment firm, with our partners holding a significant minority interest. Turner & Townsend and CBRE work together to provide clients with the premier programme, project and cost management offering in markets around the world

Who are you?

The ideal candidate is self-motivated, driven, and capable of working both independently and as part of a team. In this impactful role, you will be providing leadership and direction, actively embedding and promoting the purpose, values, and vision of Turner & Townsend.

Job Description

As Junior Project Controller you will

  • Support the project controls team with inputting estimates and maintaining project data
  • Assist in tracking project costs and schedules
  • Support the project management team in keeping estimates and forecasts up to date.
  • Help with administrative duties related to project controls
  • Opportunity to support on execution projects and grow into more senior roles
  • Entry to mid-level experience in project controls, with familiarity with project management tools

Qualifications

  • Bachelor’s degree in Engineering, Finance, Accounting, Business Administration, or a related field
  • Proficiency in project controls tools, including advanced spreadsheet use, Microsoft Project, and ERP systems
  • Strong analytical skills with a high level of attention to detail, including the ability to interpret financial data, perform variance analysis, and support accurate cost forecasting
  • Effective written and verbal communication skills, with the ability to prepare clear reports and collaborate closely with project managers, team members, and stakeholders

Additional Information

What's in it for you...

  • 100% covered health and dental benefits for you and your dependents (including paramedical services)
  • Health spending account, to top up expenses not covered in the benefits program
  • RRSP option with company matching
  • Annual learning and development budget
  • Access to a range of online learning tools, and support for career development and growth
  • Coverage of one professional membership or license fee per year, if directly related to your role
  • We believe in work-life balance, so you can recharge and take care of what matters most with vacation, flex and sick days
  • A flexible, hybrid work approach that allows colleagues to find balance between their professional and personal lives
  • Commitment to creating a diverse, equitable and inclusive culture that promotes a sense of belonging
