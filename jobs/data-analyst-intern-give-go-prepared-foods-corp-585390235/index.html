About the Role

About Give & Go Prepared Foods Corp.

Mondelēz-backed baker, North American market

Full Description

Give & Go Prepared Foods Corp.is a proud member of the Mondelēz family. We are a leading, and growing, North American manufacturer of baked goods for over 30 years. If you're looking for a company that will provide you with opportunities for career growth and development, while working with an energetic and team with deeply ingrained values of respect, direct and open communication, quality, and accountability, we’d love to have you join our team. Our Consumer Brands: two-bite® brand, including two bite-brownies®, Kimberley's Bakeshoppe™, Mason St. Bakehouse™, and The Worthy Crumb Pastry Co™

It’s an exciting time to part of the Give & Go team as we are growing rapidly and want you to become a part of our growth and extraordinarily bright future!

Job Overview:
To own the company’s rich data sets and generate valuable insights (using SQL, PowerBI) through partnership with various departments (Marketing, Finance, HR, Operations, Engineering). This is a special opportunity to develop subject matter expertise and grow your technical skill set towards enabling a data-driven culture, deploying best practices and enhancing the way we leverage data.

Key Responsibilities:

  • Assist with tasks around data type questions; respond to data or analytics related inquiries in real-time to support both business and technical teams
  • Capture requirements and collaborate with BI developers to deliver new metrics and reports through various analytics platforms
  • Work with our technology, data engineering, MDM, and other functional/operational teams to help build data solutions (BI reporting packages, scorecards and dashboards) that allow the business to operate effectively

The Recipe for Success:

  • Currently enrolled in an academic degree in Data analytics, Business Intelligence, data science or IT
  • Microsoft PowerBI certified
  • Experience using Power BI as a Power user
  • Strong proficiency with Excel; additional tool expertise such as SQL is a bonus
  • Detail oriented, incredibly organized and process driven
  • Communicate effectively and timely with Team and Stakeholders

What we can offer:

  • As we grow, we are committed to preserving the parts of our diversity & culture that is unique, people-focused and are core to our success at every size. Our values of Quality, Direct Communication and Respect mean something.
  • Learning and growth; we are working to create an environment where sharing new ideas, experimenting and learning from each other is strongly encouraged.
