About the Role

Design and build Power Platform apps; automate processes.

About CGI

Global IT consulting firm

Full Description

CGI offers an excellent opportunity for students who are passionate about developing digital solutions. As a Power Platform Developer Intern, you will collaborate with multidisciplinary teams and contribute to real projects for our clients. You will learn how to design, develop, and enhance modern applications using the Microsoft Power Platform suite (Power Apps, Power Automate, Power BI, Dataverse, etc.).

Perks of being a CGI Co-op:

The co-op program was designed to help students improve their skills and explore their professional interests through hands-on experience and professional guidance, while also helping them develop core competencies. As an intern, you have access to:

  • Professional development sessions
  • Professional development platform
  • Networking events with colleagues and leaders
  • Mentorship from leaders and colleagues aimed at setting and achieving your career goals

Start Date: September 2026
Application Deadline: May 31st, 2026

CGI is providing a reasonable estimate of the pay range for this role. The determination of this range includes factors such as skill set level, geographic market, experience and training, and licenses and certifications. Compensation decisions depend on the facts and circumstances of each case. A reasonable estimate of the current range is $18-32/hour. This role is an existing vacancy.

Your future duties and responsibilities

  • Work with analysts and architects to understand business and functional needs.
  • Design and develop applications using Power Apps.
  • Automate business processes with Power Automate.
  • Build and optimize reports and dashboards with Power BI.
  • Contribute to data integration using Dataverse and connectors.
  • Perform testing, debugging, and ensure quality deliverables.
  • Draft technical and functional documentation.
  • Contribute to the management and continuous improvement of the technical knowledge library.

Required qualifications to be successful in this role

  • Student in computer science, software engineering, information systems, or a related field.
  • Strong interest in application development and process automation.
  • Basic knowledge or hands-on experience with Power Platform or similar technologies (e.g., .NET, SQL, advanced Excel, data visualization tools).
  • Ability to analyze needs, propose solutions, and work collaboratively in a team.
  • Rigor, curiosity, and eagerness to learn quickly.
