About Websdepot Technology Partners Inc.

Full Description

We have an opening in our small development team for an Junior Web Developer to work out of our growing Markham office. A very ambitious recent graduate will also be considered as we believe in internal skill development. The successful candidate should be a well rounded individual who is familiar with a wide range of business IT technologies and principles from education and/or experience, who likes to be hands on as we tackle internal IT services web app projects to serve our internal automation workflows goals and client web development projects.

This is a great opportunity to work in a challenging and rewarding work environment in a forward thinking company where your feedback is valued as we are truly a smaller company that has had giant success last 20 years plus. We are looking for someone who is a good fit with our existing web development team and the technical services group, who has excellent written and verbal communication skills and who can work both as part of a team or independently. Working knowledge of a variety of business IT systems like CRM and methodologies is an asset.

Be excellent problem solving skills and you are looking for a job where you no one limits what you can work on or where you can apply what you know and grow as technology changes, then we want to hear from you.

Access to a vehicle is required as light travel around the GTA may be required from time to time to visit clients onsite for meetings. Your mileage will be fully reimbursed.

This is not a dead end development junior role only working on WordPress for example - come grow with us! You will work out of a the Markham office with a talented group of like-minded professionals who have fun. We involve all eager folks in projects and among entire client base. Lots of room for growth internally. We are always looking for bright intelligent people to join our family like team for a long term career.

Opportunity to work on large projects with some of Canada's best brands and not be limited to what you can learn or get hands-on with. Our development team is like a start-up within our well established IT services organization. We hire for long-term advancement. We also offer a comprehensive group benefits package.

Qualifications:.

-A diploma or degree in computer science/information technology or an equivalent combination of skills and experience.

Responsibilities:

-Develop and maintain web applications using PHP 8+, MySQL, and JavaScript (ES6+)

-Build responsive user interfaces using CSS3 and React

-Collaborate using Git for version control

-Write and maintain scripts using PowerShell and/or Bash

-Troubleshoot, optimize, and maintain existing codebases

Required Skills:

-PHP 8+, MySQL

-JavaScript (ES6+), jQuery

-CSS3, React

-Git

-Experience interfacing with and developing RESTful web services

-PowerShell and/or Bash scripting

-Experience implementing unit and integration tests

Nice to Have:

-Node.js and asynchronous programming experience

-Linux and/or Windows system administration skills

Job Type: Full-time

Pay: $40,000.00-$50,000.00 per year

Benefits:

  • Dental care
  • Extended health care
  • Vision care

  • Driving Licence (required)
