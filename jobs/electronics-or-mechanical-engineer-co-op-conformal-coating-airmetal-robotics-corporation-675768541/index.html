About Airmetal Robotics Corporation

Full Description

About AirMetal

AirMetal Robotics is a Canadian defense-tech company building the next generation of unmanned aerial systems, autonomous robotics, and related technologies for defense, law enforcement, first responder, security, and industrial customers. We develop novel UAV concepts that push the boundaries of what unmanned systems can do — and we move fast. We are a small, technical team that values engineering depth, ownership, and the ability to go from idea to hardware quickly.

The Role

We are looking for an engineering co-op student to lead the development and validation of a conformal coating and resin waterproofing process for drone electronics. This is hands-on work involving silicone resin formulation, application process development, contractor management, and rigorous environmental testing of coated and uncoated electronics. You will take the process from early prototype through a validated, repeatable production method.

What You Will Work On

  • Manage external contractors in silicone resin conformal coating processes for drone flight control and power electronics (FCU, ESC boards), including selection and characterization of materials
  • Define and execute baseline testing of uncoated electronics to establish reference performance metrics including operating temperature range and IMU noise characteristics
  • Write and maintain test protocols for waterproofing validation, defining acceptance criteria, test methods, and pass/fail standards for coated assemblies
  • Identify, qualify, and manage external contractors to apply and test conformal coatings, including preparation of sample boards and clear technical briefs for contractor use
  • Coordinate sample shipment, tracking, and return logistics for boards sent to coating contractors
  • Execute internal testing of returned coated assemblies against defined protocols, document results, and identify process improvements
  • Produce revision reports and technical documentation summarizing findings and process recommendations

What We Are Looking For

  • Working towards a degree in materials engineering, electrical engineering, mechanical engineering, or a related field
  • Experience writing and executing hardware test protocols
  • Comfortable managing external contractors and communicating clear technical requirements
  • Detail-oriented with strong documentation habits
  • Able to work full-time on-site in Ottawa

Nice to Have

  • Hands-on experience with conformal coating, potting, or encapsulation processes for electronics
  • Familiarity with silicone-based resin systems and their mechanical and environmental properties
  • Background in environmental testing of electronics (thermal cycling, humidity, immersion)
  • Experience with UAV or other mobile robotics hardware where size, weight, and reliability constraints apply
  • Familiarity with IMU performance characterization

What We Offer

  • Paid contract engagement with clearly defined scope and milestones
  • Real ownership of meaningful work in a fast-growing drone technology company
  • The opportunity to work at the frontier of drone technology
