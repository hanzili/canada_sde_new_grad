Junior Digital Marketing Generalist

at zag

📍 Location Edmonton, Alberta
📋 Details Full-time · Permanent · Competitive pay ·
📅 Posted Apr 07, 2026

About the Role

Own technical marketing tasks; learn by doing

About zag

Edmonton-based full-service agency with growth focus

Full Description

Most job postings list 30 different tools and hope the right person happens to check every box. This isn’t that.

zag is a full-service Edmonton-based marketing agency, and we’re looking for someone to support the technical side of the work we do for our clients.

Some of the work will be routine. Most of it will be moments where something breaks and no one else in the room knows how to fix it. You’re the person we will turn to.

The work

Websites, email platforms, analytics, integrations, tracking, DNS, coding and automations — all of the systems that keep marketing actually working. Figure things out, make them work, make them better.

Potential day-to-day:

• Website updates

• Content swaps

• Email campaigns

• Analytics

Potential challenges:

• Solving problems you’ve never seen before

• An integration that broke overnight and needs to be fixed asap

• A client who needs something built by Thursday that doesn’t exist yet

On AI & Tools

We don’t care how you get to the answer. We care that you get there fast and it’s excellent.

If you’re already using AI tools to move at 2–3x, building with Cursor, debugging with Claude, prototyping with v0, you’ll fit right in.

We’ll never tell you to stop using AI. We’ll wonder why you’re not using it more.

Who you are

You’re the kind of person who builds things in your spare time, not because anyone asked, but because you’re genuinely curious and learn by doing.

You take ownership of your work. When something’s broken, you’d rather fix it than flag it. When something’s good enough, you want to make it better.

You’re independent but not isolated. You communicate clearly, flag what matters and make the people around you better.

Do apply if

• You want ownership of technical work from day one

• You want to be surrounded by people who are the best at what they do: Technology, Strategy, Creative, Accounts

• You love to learn and learn by doing

Don't apply if

• You hear “figure it out” and feel anxious instead of excited

• You do what’s asked and stop there

• You’d describe yourself as someone who’s casual about their work

• You think AI tools are a shortcut, not a skillset

Comp & Environment

• Competitive Pay

• Health and dental benefits

• Vacation, PTO and bonus days off

To apply

This role is for someone who’s optimizing for what they can become. No standard cover letter, no “Dear Hiring Manager”.

Tell us about something cool you’ve built, a side project, a site, a tool, a hack. Tell us why you built it, what you learned and what you would do differently if you built it again.

Shortlisted applications will receive an email from us requesting a screening interview.

Following the screening interview, successful applicants will receive a request for a formal interview.
