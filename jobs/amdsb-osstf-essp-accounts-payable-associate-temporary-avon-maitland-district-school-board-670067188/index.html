Full Description

OSSTF-ESSP ACCOUNTS PAYABLE ASSOCIATE FINANCIAL SERVICES TEMPORARY POSITION Avon Maitland District School Board (AMDSB) provides first class education to students and families throughout Huron and Perth Counties. We aim to engage, inspire, and innovate, in all that we do! We are always learning! Our work is guided by a Strategic Plan and highlights 3 main areas of focus that enable our students and staff to say: I am prepared. I am well. I am engaged.

Our vision is to engage , inspire , innovate , because we are always learning .

We are seeking qualified applicants for a temporary OSSTF-ESSP Accounts Payable Associate position at the Education Centre in Seaforth, Ontario . Hours of Work: 35 hours per week, 12 months per year

Start Date: May 1, 2026

End Date: June 25, 2027

Rate of Pay: $24.86 per hour, Level 1as outlined in the OSSTF-ESSP Collective Agreement

Vacancy Status: current vacancy

Essential Functions And Responsibilities

  • Process invoices for payment in accordance with standard procedures and payment schedules, including but not limited to:

  • Organize and prepare invoices, expense claims and internal payment requests for payment

  • Confirm transactions are in accordance with Board procedures (i.e. proper authorization, itemized receipts, etc.).

  • Calculate and code tax rebate where applicable.

  • Assign general ledger account code where applicable.

  • Input invoice data into the Board’s accounting system.

  • Prepare and balance invoice batches for payment, including AP batch audits of payment items entered by another member of the Financial Services team.
  • Generate cheques and electronic funds transfers (EFT) for payment and assist in the accounting for preauthorized distribution payments (PAD).
  • Distribute cheques and remittance advices to vendors including external, schools, and departments.
  • Initiate stop payment as required (i.e. due to lost cheque or incorrect payee).

  • File completed transactions.

  • Communicate with all Board sites and departments regarding payments to vendors, employees, host families, etc.

  • Liaise with vendors regarding outstanding or incorrect invoices, payments, etc.
  • Perform related general office duties including filing, archiving, processing department mail, e-mail and internal courier, etc.

Job Specifications And Requirements

  • Completion of a one year community college business program plus up to six (6) months related experience; or a combination of experience, education and training that is considered equivalent.
  • Proficiency with Microsoft Office (Excel, Word, Power Point) and computerized financial systems (e.g. Sparkrock).
  • Proficient and accurate keyboarding skills.
  • Demonstrated belief in customer service excellence.
  • The capacity to problem-solve, and multi-task in a busy environment.
  • Ability to self-motivate and to work both independently and within a team.
  • Proven professional oral and written communication skills and a high degree of confidentiality.

Note - A criminal background check will be part of the recruitment process.
