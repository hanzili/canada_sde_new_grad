Role Description

This is a full-time, on-site role for a Junior Recruiter based in North York, ON. The Junior Recruiter will support recruitment activities, including sourcing candidates, screening resumes, scheduling interviews, and facilitating communication between candidates and hiring teams. The role involves maintaining an updated talent pipeline, building relationships with potential candidates, and contributing to the overall recruitment strategy. The Junior Recruiter will also assist in administrative tasks associated with recruitment processes, ensuring a seamless and efficient experience for candidates and team members.
