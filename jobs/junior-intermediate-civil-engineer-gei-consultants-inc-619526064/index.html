Full Description

Description Your role at GEI. GEI Consultants is seeking a Junior / Intermediate Civil Engineer (Water & Wastewater) to join our Guelph, Ontario office. This role will support the planning, design, and delivery of municipal water and wastewater infrastructure projects serving communities across Guelph and Southwestern Ontario.

This position offers the opportunity to work on technically meaningful projects that protect public health, support sustainable growth, and enhance the resilience of municipal infrastructure. You will collaborate with experienced professionals in a multidisciplinary environment while continuing to build your technical and project delivery expertise within an employee-owned consulting firm.

Essential Responsibilities & Duties

  • Assist with the planning and design of municipal water distribution and wastewater collection systems, including watermains, sanitary sewers, forcemains, pumping stations, and related linear infrastructure.
  • Support preparation of engineering calculations, drawings, technical memoranda, and design reports.
  • Apply applicable design standards and guidelines, including MECP and municipal standards.
  • Assist with regulatory approvals and coordination with agencies such as conservation authorities and MECP.
  • Support construction administration activities, including site reviews and contract administration tasks.
  • Collaborate with multidisciplinary teams including land development, infrastructure planning, and environmental professionals.
  • Assist with preparation of cost estimates, tender packages, and project documentation.
