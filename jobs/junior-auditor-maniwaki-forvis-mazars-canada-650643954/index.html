✨ About the Role Are you a passionate audit professional looking for meaningful challenges within a dynamic firm that combines a human-scale environment with global reach? If so, this opportunity is for you.

As a Ju nior Auditor , you will play a pivotal role in delivering audit and assurance engagements for a diverse and engaged client base. This role is ideal for someone looking to broaden their professional experience, take part in high-impact projects, and gain national exposure through engagements across Quebec and throughout Canada.

🌍 Who We Are Forvis Mazars is a leading global audit & assurance, tax and advisory network, bringing together two independent partnerships and more than 40,000 professionals across 100+ countries. In Canada , our firm has been supporting clients for more than 50 years through the expertise of over 200 professionals specializing in Audit & Assurance, Accounting, Tax, and Financial Advisory Services . 🎓 Your Profile

  • Enrolled in a bachelor's degree program in Business Administration (Accounting profile) or a related field, or holding a Diploma of College Studies (DEC) in Accounting;
  • Intermediate knowledge of Office 365;
  • Basic knowledge of financial reporting and certification standards;
  • Basic knowledge of taxation (an asset);
  • Advanced level of French (spoken and written);
  • Student member of the professional order in your province (an asset);

  • Knowledge of CaseWare and TaxPrep software (an asset);
  • Willingness to travel for work.

  • Strong technical expertise and attention to detail.
  • Exceptional organizational skills and methodical approach.
  • Excellent communication skills with a proactive and anticipatory mindset.
  • Results-driven, with a strong ability to meet budgets and deadlines.
  • High level of integrity and accountability.
  • Flexibility and willingness to travel across Canada for client engagements.

🌐 Language Requirements
