About the Role

Support finance projects; document workflows and data

About SE Health

Not-for-profit health social enterprise improving care

Full Description

SE Health is one of Canada’s largest social enterprises, working to improve health care and home care services for people across the country. We are using technology, innovation, and creative problem-solving to make health care more personalized, safe, and effective.
We are looking for a Junior Business Analyst (Jr. BA) who is excited to learn, work with a team, and gain real-world experience in enterprise-wide transformation.

This is a great opportunity for a high school or early post-secondary student interested in gaining exposure to large-scale technology and Enterprise Resource Planning (ERP) transformation initiatives within a healthcare environment.
The student will support initiatives related to finance process improvement, implementation, reporting, documentation, and project coordination while working alongside finance, business, and technology professionals.
This role is ideal for students interested in finance processes, technology, business, project management, data, or innovation.

What You’ll Do

  • Learn About Business & Technology within the Finance workstream
  • Help teams understand problems and find better ways of working
  • Participate in meetings related to finance transformation initiatives
  • Help document ideas, workflows, and project requirements

Work With Data

  • Help review and organize information and data
  • Support creation of simple reports, charts, or dashboards using Excel and other tools
  • Look for trends or patterns that may help improve services

Support Projects

  • Take meeting notes and track tasks or action items
  • Help different teams stay organized and connected
  • Assist with testing new systems and tools before they are launched

Help With Training & Communication

  • Support creation of training guides or presentations
  • Help staff learn about new systems or processes

What We’re Looking For

  • A student interested in business, technology, healthcare, finance, or problem-solving
  • Strong interest in finance, technology, business transformation, or project management
  • Strong communication and teamwork skills
  • Organized and willing to learn new things in a fast-paced environment
  • Comfortable using Microsoft Office programs like Word, Excel, and PowerPoint
  • Curiosity about how technology supports business operations

Why This Role Is Great for Students

  • Gain hands-on experience in a professional healthcare environment
  • Exposure to enterprise finance and transformation projects
  • Learn about business analysis, finance, projects, and digital transformation
  • Work with experienced professionals across different departments
  • Build skills in communication, teamwork, technology, and problem-solving
  • Make a meaningful impact by helping improve healthcare services for Canadians

Why Join SE Health?

  • Competitive Total Rewards: So much more than a paycheque! Enjoy comprehensive benefits, pension, flexible pay options, car-loan support, housing solutions and exclusive staff perks.
  • Flexibility & Belonging: Thrive with hybrid work, flexible scheduling and a supportive, inclusive culture that puts people first.
  • Purpose & Impact: Join a national social enterprise where your voice matters. Every role helps advance health, spark innovation and strengthen communities across Canada.
  • Growth That Meets Your Ambition: Access tuition support, training and meaningful career pathways across a growing, future-focused organization.

About SE Health

SE Health is a not-for-profit social enterprise advancing health with heart. With 115+ years of impact, we bring hope, happiness and exceptional care to people and communities across Canada. We lead with empathy, dignity and purpose while building a future where everyone can realize their full health and well-being potential. We’re also an inclusive, supportive workplace offering competitive compensation, strong benefits and real opportunities to grow. We’re All In Together.

Accessibility: If you require accommodations due to illness or disability, please contact Talent Acquisition at careers@sehc.com.

AI and compensation details: We use AI to take notes during our interview. All applications and interviews are reviewed by our Talent Acquisition team. This role is a new addition. The hiring pay range is $18 per hour based on experience.
