Full Description

Job Title: Junior Accountant (Vendor Relations & AP Support)
Location: Vancouver, BC (Hybrid)
Reports to: Operations Manager

Role Overview

We are looking for a detail-oriented Junior Accountant to join our finance team as a primary support pillar for our vendor network across Canada and the U.S. In this role, you will bridge the gap between accounting and operations, ensuring our cross-border vendor relationships remain healthy through timely payments, accurate reconciliations, and professional communication. This is a perfect entry-level opportunity for someone with 0–3 years of experience looking to master North American accounts payable in a hybrid environment.

Key Responsibilities

  • Vendor Support & Inquiry Management: Act as the main point of contact for vendor inquiries, resolving payment discrepancies, and maintaining positive relationships with suppliers in both CAD and USD markets.
  • Accounts Payable Processing: Review, code, and process high-volume invoices, ensuring compliance with both Canadian and U.S. tax requirements (e.g., GST/HST/PST and U.S. Sales/Use Tax).
  • Cross-Border Reconciliations: Perform weekly and monthly reconciliations of vendor statements across multiple entities, managing foreign exchange (FX) impacts between CAD and USD.
  • Payment Coordination: Assist in the preparation of weekly payment runs via EFT, ACH, and wire transfers for domestic and international vendors.
  • Vendor Onboarding: Maintain the vendor master file, ensuring all documentation (such as W-8BEN or W-9 forms for U.S. entities) is accurate and up to date.
  • Month-End Support: Assist with accruals and aging reports to ensure all vendor liabilities are accurately reflected for month-end closing under U.S. GAAP and ASPE.

Required Qualifications

  • Education: Degree or Diploma in Accounting or Finance
  • Experience: 0–3 years of accounting experience. Previous exposure to U.S. accounting practices or cross-border transactions is highly preferred.
  • Software Skills: Proficiency in MS Excel (v-lookups and pivot tables). Experience with ERPs like NetSuite, Sage Intacct, or QuickBooks Online is an asset.
  • Communication: Exceptional verbal and written communication skills to effectively support vendors and internal stakeholders.
  • Organization: Strong ability to manage multiple deadlines and high-volume data entry with a high degree of accuracy.

Why Join Us?

  • Hybrid Flexibility: Enjoy a balance of in-office collaboration in downtown Vancouver and remote work.
  • Growth Potential: Mentorship from senior CPAs and exposure to complex international accounting structures.
  • Competitive Compensation: Salary range of $45,000 – $55,000 (depending on experience) plus benefits.
