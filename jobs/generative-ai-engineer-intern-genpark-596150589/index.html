About GenPark

AI-driven branding startup crafting personalized experiences

Full Description

**Company Description**

GenPark leverages intelligent Brand Agents and User Agents to help brands better understand, engage with, and convert global Gen Z consumers. By combining AI-generated personalized content, interactive Agents, and immersive AI video experiences, we enable brands to connect with the right audience in the right context for improved conversion rates and reduced acquisition costs. More than a marketing tool, GenPark's AI-native system continuously learns from user behaviors and feedback to create smarter matches between products, services, digital assets, and consumers globally. Our mission is to empower brands with cutting-edge technology to reach their audiences more effectively and efficiently.

**Role Description**

This is a full-time, hybrid internship role as a Generative AI Engineer at our Vancouver, BC location, with flexibility for some remote work. As a Generative AI Engineer Intern, you will assist in the development and optimization of AI algorithms, design and implement models for generating personalized content, and collaborate with the engineering team to enhance the performance of our AI-native system. You will also contribute to data preprocessing, model fine-tuning, and improving the scalability of AI systems.

**Qualifications**

- Proficiency in programming languages such as Python, and experience with AI/ML frameworks like TensorFlow or PyTorch. - Strong understanding of machine learning, deep learning, and natural language processing concepts and techniques. - Experience in building and fine-tuning Generative AI models (e.g., GPT, GANs) or language models. - Familiarity with data preprocessing, feature engineering, and handling large datasets, as well as basic knowledge of cloud platforms (e.g., AWS, Google Cloud, or Azure). - Strong analytical and problem-solving skills with attention to detail and the ability to work independently. - Excellent communication skills to collaborate effectively within a cross-functional team. - Enrollment in or recent completion of a degree program in Computer Science, Artificial Intelligence, Data Science, or a related field. - Passionate about emerging AI technologies and their application in real-world scenarios.
