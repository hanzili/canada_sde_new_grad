About Mavcor Inc.

Full Description

Company Description

Mavcor is a general contracting, design-build, and construction firm servicing the industrial, commercial, and institutional construction market. With over three decades of industry experience, Mavcor has earned a stellar reputation for delivering exceptional construction solutions. We specialize in pharmaceutical, industrial, and institutional projects of various sizes and scopes. Fully licensed, insured, and bonded, we operate throughout Canada on projects up to $50 million. Join us as we transform the future of construction.

Role Description

This is an entry level role for a Project Coordinator located in Mississauga, ON. The Project Coordinator will support project managers in organizing ongoing projects, maintain project documentation and handle communication within project teams. Responsibilities include scheduling and coordinating meetings, updating project timelines, assisting with project budgeting, and ensuring compliance with safety regulations. Career path can progress to project manager for the right candidate.

Qualifications

  • Organizational and Time Management skills
  • Strong Communication and Interpersonal skills
  • Familiarity with Project Management Software and Tools
  • Basic knowledge of Construction Processes and Terminology
  • Problem-Solving and Critical Thinking abilities
  • Ability to work collaboratively in a team environment
  • Degree in Engineering, Construction Management, or a related field from a Canadian University.
  • Previous internship or work experience in construction is a plus

Compensation

  • Salary will begin at 50000 with bonuses/commission based on performance. Annual increases thereafter.
  • Health and Dental Benefits will begin after 3 months probationary period
  • Group RRSP and Profit Share will be be available after probation
  • 2 Weeks vacation progressing to 3 after 5 years.
  • Phone and Boot Allowance
  • Full time employment
  • Office work 4 days per week
