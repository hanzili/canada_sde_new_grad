Rainy River Mine

About The Role

Schedule: 5x2, 4x3 9hr Days

Position Reports To: Procurement & Contracts Supervisor

Position Overview

The Buyer plays a critical role in managing the daily procurement of goods, services, and materials essential for New Gold’s operations. This position ensures that purchasing activities are aligned with organizational requirements, budgets, and timelines, contributing to the efficiency and cost-effectiveness of operations. The Buyer is responsible for sourcing suppliers, negotiating quotes, maintaining supplier relationships, and ensuring that all materials and services are procured and delivered on time and at the best possible cost. This role will have a direct impact on the costs and profitability of New Gold Inc.’s Rainy River mine location.

Key Responsibilities

  • Create and manage purchase orders in ERP systems (e.g., S4 Hana). Track and expedite orders, resolve overdue deliveries, and ensure compliance with terms. Maintain accurate records of transactions and vendor communications.
  • Review, audit, and prioritize purchase requisitions to ensure that specifications, budgets, and delivery deadlines are clearly defined and met. Ensure that procurement activities support the strategic goals and operational needs of the organization.
  • Act as liaison between suppliers, internal departments, and end users. Assist with vendor setup and maintenance in procurement systems. Develop and maintain relationships with both internal customers and external suppliers.
  • Coordinate with inventory team, management, and warehouse. Monitor the stock quality and escalate any discrepancies to suppliers and management
  • Reconcile shipping and payment documents and resolve billing issues.
  • Other duties to support the team as required to ensure the Rainy River Procurement Teams success.

Qualifications

  • Proven experience of 2 years in administration position, preferably in an industrial or mining environment.
  • Demonstrated understanding of Supply Chain operations, and sourcing within the supply operations of a large resource company.
  • Procurement or Supply Chain certification.
  • Excellent mathematical and evaluation skills are required with strong commercial acumen.
  • Excellent verbal and written communication skills are essential.
  • Analytical, negotiation and communication skills
  • Knowledge and experience using S4 Hana would be a strong asset.
  • Computer literacy in Microsoft office and high level of competency in Excel spread sheet creation.
  • Ability to work under pressure and produce high quality work within time constraints.
  • Good judgment, critical thinking, and problem-solving skills with the ability to work in a team environment.
  • Demonstrated success in building trusting relationships with internal and external stakeholders including the local community, indigenous groups, employees, colleagues, and service providers/contractors
