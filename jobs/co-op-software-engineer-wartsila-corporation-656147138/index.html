Co-op Software Engineer

at Wärtsilä Corporation

About Wärtsilä Corporation

Global maritime technology leader.

Full Description

Co-op Software Engineer

Wärtsilä is an innovative technology company known for being a forerunner in the Marine and Energy markets. Every day, we – Wärtsilians - put our hearts and minds into turning Wärtsilä's purpose of enabling sustainable societies through innovation in technology and services into reality. Our ultimate aim is to provide increased value to both our customers and society. New, game-changing ideas and continuous improvement have been part of our DNA since 1834. Together, we can create new business opportunities and more sustainable future that we can all be proud of.

We want to transition the world towards a decarbonised and sustainable future. By pushing the boundaries of engineering and technology, we can make it work. We are always on the lookout for future-oriented talent – want to join the ride?

Join Wärtsilä Voyage, a leading technology company in the Marine and Energy markets, on a journey towards a cleaner, more sustainable future. We are seeking a Co-op Software Engineer to contribute to the development of our port management information system. This role offers the opportunity to work with a talented team, gain hands-on experience, and make a real impact in the maritime industry.

You will have the opportunity to work with systems and ideas used across the globe, and in an environment that fosters ideas and innovation. Our teams blend diverse personalities and interests, united by a collaborative and fun-loving culture, creating a relaxed and welcoming environment.

Grow your skills in C#, .net, SQL and Typescript, learn popular frameworks/libraries such as LINQ and Telerik, in a modernized N-tier web application designed for the world's leading ports.

Responsibilities:

  • Collaborate with team members through SCRUM to discuss, and design agile solutions for real world problems. Translate this into implementation and build upon software.
  • Learn about the maritime world and incorporate this understanding into code.

Qualifications:

  • Currently pursuing a degree in Computer Science, Software Engineering, or related field.
  • Basic understanding of C#, .Net, SQL, and TypeScript.
  • Problem-solving skills and attention to detail.
  • Communication skills.

Additional Information:

  • The position is based in Vancouver, BC, at 570 Granville Street. General start time is 8:00AM-8:30AM, 1 hour lunch, 8-hour days. Monday to Friday in the office besides Thursday which is remote.
  • Possibility of full-time position offer after co-op.
  • Duration: 8-month contract starting in August 2025.
  • Pay: $22/hour
