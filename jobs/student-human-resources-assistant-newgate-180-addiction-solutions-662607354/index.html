Full Description

Human Resource Student Position – Summer Contract

Newgate 180 is seeking Human Resource Personal

Responsibilities
Newgate 180 is looking for someone to Administer, Develop and oversee regulations, policies and procedures in place, while being encouraged to suggest and organize new ideas in the area of Human Resources. This position will report directly to the Operations Manager.

Skills Required

  • Administer, Develop and oversee regulations, policies and procedures.
  • Develop, evaluate and ensure continuous improvement of processes, templates and monitoring tools to maintain the integrity of all HR systems data and the accuracy of reports;
  • Efficiency and effectiveness in all our practices.
  • Students are encouraged to take initiative and gain hands-on work experience.
  • Provide support to staffing processes, such as, creating, organizing and completing staffing files;
  • Prepare staffing processes documents, including candidate files, interview guides, letters of offer, security clearance documents, competition results notices, etc.;
  • Participate in data entry in corporate software for resource planning (Retain) and perform data validation;
  • Adaptability / Reliability
  • Innovation and ability to guide change
  • Excellent organizational skills
  • Complete any other required tasks, if required, in a timely manner.

LOCATION: Merrickville, Ontario Duration: May – mid August 2026 Government Funded Summer Jobs

- Must meet following criteria:

  • is between 15 and 30 years of age (inclusive) at the start of employment;
  • was registered as a full-time student during the preceding academic year;
  • intends to return to school on a full-time basis during the next academic year;
  • is a student in a secondary, post-secondary, CEGEP (Quebec only), vocational or technical program;
  • is a Canadian Citizen, permanent resident, or person on whom refugee protection has been conferred under the Immigration and Refugee Protection Act* and;
  • is legally entitled to work according to the relevant provincial / territorial legislation and regulations

Newgate 180 is an equal opportunity employer. All applicants will be considered for employment without attention to race, color, religion, sex, sexual orientation, gender identity, national origin, veteran or disability status.”
