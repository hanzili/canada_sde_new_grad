About IKO Industries

Full Description

IKO Industries Ltd. is a market leader in the manufacturing of roofing and building materials. IKO is a Canadian owned and operated business with production facilities worldwide and has many years of unparalleled success in the roofing materials industry. Quality, integrity, and trustworthiness are the values that underlie this success, and we have built this company by hiring people who hold these values. People like you!

Job Description

BI Developer Co-Op

Location: Mississauga, ON

Type: Full Time Co-Op / Paid Intern Department: Corporate

Our Purpose

At IKO, we’re more than a roofing manufacturer—we’re innovators, transforming raw materials into products that shelter families, businesses, and communities. Our Analytics team is integral to this process, turning raw data into actionable insights that drive efficiency, reduce waste, and enhance product quality. Just as innovation drives our production line, our Analytics team powers IKO’s success by enabling strategic decisions that keep us at the forefront of the industry.

By living our purpose, we bring data to life and help shape IKO’s future, ensuring we remain a global leader in roofing, insulation, and waterproofing solutions.

WHAT’S IN IT FOR YOU?

  • Competitive compensation/pay package: $22.00 - $24.00 per hour
  • Start Date: 05/04/2026 for a 4‑month term, with the possibility of extension
  • Be part of a long-standing and stable industry leader
  • Hands-on experience with an industry leader supporting our manufacturing facilities.
  • Work with a company committed to eco-friendly practices and sustainable production methods.
  • Exposure to different roles or departments to help you clarify your career interests and path.
  • Potential for future Internship/Co-Op placements or full-time roles upon graduation.

WHEN YOU JOIN US, YOU WILL BE;

  • As a BI Developer Co-Op, you’ll design and develop interactive dashboards that provide actionable business insights. You will work closely with the data team and stakeholders to understand business requirements, transforming complex data into easy-to-understand visuals that empower decision-makers across the company.
  • You will also be the lead BI developer helping to drive consistency in utilizing industry best practices and acting as a mentor for the data team and stakeholders.
  • Develop and maintain Power BI reports and dashboards for key business areas using Power BI
  • Perform detailed analysis on tested and deployed Power BI report and scripts and utilize them
  • Continuously optimize Power BI solutions to enhance performance and usability
  • Collaborate with various teams to ensure data is integrated seamlessly from multiple sources
  • Create technical documentation, design wireframes for dashboards, and develop semantic models
  • Participate in System Integration Testing with architects and developers, while also supporting User Acceptance Testing (UAT) by resolving defects promptly during the process
  • Stay updated with the latest Power BI features and innovations to deliver cutting-edge solutions
  • Conduct user training to maximize adoption and empower teams to use self-serve analytics
  • Log defects/issues in Azure DevOps and follow up with the development team
  • Collaborate with business stakeholders to understand their data needs and transform data into engaging, user-friendly reports

OUR IDEAL CANDIDATE;

  • Enrolled in a university-level program with a focus in Computer and Information Science, Data Science/Engineering, Computer Engineering, Information Technology, or equivalent program.

  • Project or Coop experience in developing Power BI dashboards and reports

  • An understanding of data visualization best practices to create clear and actionable insights

  • Strong analytical skills and the ability to translate business requirements into technical solutions

  • Proficiency in DAX, SQL, and data modeling in Power BI

  • Experience in developing and maintaining Row-level security (RLS), and object-level security (OLS) a plus
  • Experience in data transformation, integration (cloud-based solutions), and the Microsoft Fabric stack (e.g., Azure Data Lake, Data Factory Azure Synapse, Synapse Data Warehouse) a plus
  • Knowledge of the software development lifecycle (SDLC) and applied experience with data-related projects
  • Experience in communicating with both business and technical teams
  • Self-motivated, adaptable, and able to manage multiple priorities in a fast-paced environment
  • A team player, and initiator, with the ability to manage complex tasks and priorities
  • Bilingual proficiency in spoken and written French is an asset

Benefits of Employment: IKO recognizes that its success is due to the strength of its employees. A primary goal of IKO is to promote individual employee's sense of accomplishment and contribution so that employees enjoy their association with IKO.
