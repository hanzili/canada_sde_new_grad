About the Role

Assist with structural analysis and design

About HKA Enterprises

Global engineering consultancy; semiconductor/industrial focus

Full Description

We’re hiring a Structural Engineer (EIT) to support innovative industrial and semiconductor projects in a primarily remote role based in Canada!

In this role, you’ll assist with structural analysis and design for steel and concrete structures, collaborate with multidisciplinary teams, and support the development of engineering solutions for advanced industrial facilities. This is an excellent opportunity to grow your technical skills and advance your career in a collaborative environment.

Pay: $45.00- $50.00 per hour DOE

Qualifications:

• Bachelor’s degree in Civil Engineering (Structural emphasis preferred)

• Engineer in Training (EIT) certification required

• 2+ years of structural engineering experience

• Experience with US building codes including IBC, ASCE 7, ACI, and AISC

• Strong communication and interpersonal skills

Preferred Qualifications:

• Master’s degree in Structural Engineering

• Experience with structural analysis software, Navisworks, and Bluebeam

• Experience designing steel and concrete structures

• Semiconductor or industrial facility experience

What You’ll Do:

• Assist with structural analysis and design for industrial facilities

• Support development of calculations, drawings, and design documents

• Collaborate with engineers, designers, and project teams

• Review work for quality and code compliance

• Support project schedules, deliverables, and coordination efforts

Additional Details:

• Remote position — candidates must be located in Canada

• Home internet service required

• Opportunity to work on advanced semiconductor and industrial projects

If you’re looking to grow your engineering career while working on exciting projects, we’d love to connect!
