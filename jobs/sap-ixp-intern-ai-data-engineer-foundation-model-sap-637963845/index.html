What you’ll do

Position title: SAP iXp Intern - AI & Data Engineer Foundation Model

Location: Montreal, QC

Expected start date: April 2026 (8 months)

  • Collaborate with Applied AI Researchers and AI Engineers to support data needs for various research projects.
  • Develop opportunities to shape and have an influential impact on the future of SAP’s AI portfolio and its suite of enterprise software products.
  • Work with cloud platforms for data processing, data lakes, and model training providers.
  • Gain hands-on experiences with Python, Machine Learning/Deep Learning libraries & Knowledge Graphs.
  • Develop and maintain data pipelines for structured data powering the training of Foundational Models for Tabular Data.
  • Build innovative tools to enrich and scale the training of large machine models on enterprise software data

What you bring

We’re looking for someone who takes initiative, perseveres, and stay curious. You like to work on meaningful innovative projects and are energized by lifelong learning.

  • Currently pursuing or recently completed a degree in Computer Science, Software Engineering, Data Science or a related field.
  • Good communication and teamwork abilities.
  • Strong understanding of Machine Learning algorithms and Deep Learning pre-training & fine-tuning techniques, particularly in the context of structured data.
  • Experience in Python for Data Science and experience with Machine Learning framework for Deep Learning
  • Experience with cloud-based Data Lakes & data processing pipeline on large volumes of data (e.g., Azure ML, Databricks, Apache Spark).
  • Knowledge or experience working with SAP ERP Software (such as S/4HANA or ECC) and SAP BTP is a plus.
  • Knowledge of version control systems like Git and familiar with using Unix or windows-based command lines.
  • Bachelor's or Master's degree (ongoing or recently completed)

Meet your team

Language foundational models, like LLMs, have been pivotal in driving generalization across various NLP tasks. Our SAP Foundation Model Team for structured data aims to establish a centralized foundation with graph and generative intelligence that can effectively support cross-SAP AI use cases, particularly for tabular AI tasks.

The success of this foundation model depends heavily on several factors, including the quality, type, format, volume, and scheduling of the pre-training data. This makes the data pipeline and overall data strategy critical to its success.
