Data & AI Intern

at Insight

📍 Location Calgary, Alberta Hybrid
📋 Details Intern · Summer 2026
📅 Posted Feb 25, 2026

About the Role

Build AI solutions, write SQL, deploy prototypes quickly

About Insight

Global tech provider; data & AI focus

Full Description

Insight is excited to announce our 2026 Summer Internship program ! We are looking for motivated rising college seniors to join our 10-week, full-time, paid internship starting on June 1, 2026.

Are you ready to kickstart your career with a company that empowers innovation in the Data & AI space? Join our Microsoft + Solution Line and work alongside industry experts to help clients harness the power of AI, automation, and modern data solutions. Be part of a team that transforms ideas into intelligent outcomes.

Basic Qualifications

  • Currently enrolled in an accredited Undergraduate degree program in Computer Science, Data Science, Information Technology, or a related field
  • Available for the full internship duration from June 1 to August 7, 2026
  • Self-motivated, eager to learn, and detail-oriented
  • Ability to work a hybrid schedule on-site at our Charlotte office

Responsibilities Interns will gain hands-on experience with Microsoft AI and Data technologies while supporting client-facing and internal projects. Activities may include:

  • Exploring and building AI solutions using Microsoft Copilot and Prompt Engineering best practices.
  • Designing and automating workflows with the Power Platform (Power Apps, Power Automate, Power BI).
  • Writing and optimizing SQL queries and basic programming scripts to support data analysis and integration tasks.
  • Applying Azure AI Fundamentals knowledge to develop proof-of-concept solutions.
  • Using Git version control to collaborate on code and manage project files.
  • Participating in structured problem-solving sessions to address technical challenges.
  • Engaging in proactive learning to stay current with emerging AI and data technologies.

Required Skills

  • Understanding of core programming concepts and SQL.
  • Familiarity with Microsoft Copilot and AI-assisted productivity tools.
  • Experience or coursework in the Power Platform.
  • Knowledge of Azure AI Fundamentals concepts.
  • Strong problem-solving skills and ability to work through structured solution approaches.
  • Proactive learner with curiosity for new technologies.
  • Ability to use Git version control for collaborative development.
  • Strong team player with the ability to collaborate and work independently.

Areas Of Study

  • Computer Science
  • Data Science
  • Information Technology
  • Data/Business Analytics

Join us for an enriching internship experience where you can develop valuable skills and contribute to impactful projects. Apply now and be a part of our dynamic team at Insight! What You Need

  • Bachelor's degree in progress in Computer Science, Data Science, IT, or related field of study.
  • Strong attention to detail and organizational skills.
  • Proficient in Microsoft Office Suite and basic coding tools.
  • Excellent interpersonal and communication skills.
  • Ability to adapt to changing priorities in a calm and professional manner.
  • Ability to tactfully handle confidential documents and information.
