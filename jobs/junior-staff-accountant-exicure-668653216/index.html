Full Description

Junior Staff Accountant

We are looking for a detail-oriented Junior Staff Accountant to join our team.

Key Responsibilities:

  • Perform day-to-day bookkeeping activities, including journal entries, account reconciliations, and general ledger maintenance

  • Support month-end and year-end closing processes

  • Assist with financial reporting and audit preparation

  • Manage AP/AR functions and ensure timely processing of transactions

  • Maintain accurate financial records in accounting systems

Qualifications:

  • Hands-on experience with QuickBooks and NetSuite

  • Solid understanding of bookkeeping and basic accounting principles

  • Strong attention to detail and organizational skills

  • Ability to work independently and manage multiple tasks

  • Fluency in both Korean and English (written and verbal)

Preferred:

  • Prior experience in a similar role

  • Experience supporting financial reporting or audits
