About the Role

Analyze client requirements, translate to proposals, support deployments

About Ortec Finance

Global client servicing, PEARL software

Full Description

Turn complex business needs into smart software for investment clients. Join Ortec Finance’s Global Client Servicing team and use your analytical skills, data affinity and curiosity to make real impact.

Investment Performance Solution The Investment Performance department within Ortec Finance focuses on the development, sales, implementation and support of PEARL, our performance measurement and attribution software. PEARL enables our clients to conduct in-depth analysis to gain insights into the results of their investment decisions. With colleagues based in Toronto, London, Melbourne, Amsterdam and Rotterdam (our headquarters), our Investment Performance solution continues to attract a growing number of leading investment organizations worldwide. We work with large pension funds, asset managers and other global financial institutions, to provide insight on 4 trillion Euros of assets.

What do we have to offer? As a consultant in the Global Client Servicing team, you will handle a variety of projects with clients based in Canada, and the UK. Working in collaboration with our institutional client base, communicating our expertise and complex industry concepts, you will assist our clients in conducting analysis using our performance measurement & attribution system, PEARL. This software, tailored to the specific needs of each client, generates investment performance results to enhance their strategic decision making. PEARL also offers in-depth reporting capabilities and workflow support to manage the performance process.

We offer you a challenging and rewarding role in a growing company, while being part of an inspirational global business with a lot of opportunities for personal and professional career development.

In addition to a competitive salary, we offer you excellent benefits, including:

  • Opportunities to develop yourself with a training program as well as weekly research and knowledge sharing meetings
  • Collaboration with different international teams
  • Business travel allowance, working from home allowance
  • 20 vacation days
  • Participation in a group RRSP with matching contributions
  • Basic life, healthcare, and dental insurance - including a person (health) spend account

Ortec Finance is an equal opportunity employer. All qualified applicants will receive consideration for employment without regard to race, color, national origin, sex, religion, sexual orientation, age, disability, citizenship status, veteran status or any other basis protected by applicable law. If and as required by applicable law, all persons hired to work at Ortec Finance will be required to verify identity and eligibility to work in the location for which they have an offer to work and to complete the necessary employment eligibility verification documents upon hire.

Who are you? You are a strong communicator, able to interact easily with both colleagues and clients. You possess the drive, skills and creativity needed to analyze clients’ requirements to their core, placing them into the correct context and translating them into accurate development proposals. Also, you understand use cases and the working processes of our clients. With your strong analytical skills, you can get to the core of their requirements and place them in the correct context within the total solution. And of course, you recognize yourself in our core values.

In Addition, The Following Aspects Are Important

  • You hold a bachelor’s degree, preferably in an exact science (e.g. Econometrics, Computer Science, Applied Mathematics, or Physics) or business-related field (e.g. Finance or Accounting)
  • You have excellent verbal and written communication skills in English
  • Good working knowledge of MS Office tools (Word, Excel, PowerPoint). Affinity with databases, financial modeling and/or design of web applications is considered an advantage
  • The same holds for experience of up to 2 years within the investment industry and with performance measurement or risk management

Who are we? Ortec Finance is the leading provider of technology and solutions for risk and return management. It is our purpose to enable people to manage the complexity of investment decisions. We do this through delivering leading technologies and solutions for investment decision-making to financial institutions around the world. Our strength lies in an effective combination of advanced models, innovative technology and in-depth market knowledge. More at www.ortecfinance.com

Diversity & Inclusion One of the core values of Ortec Finance is personal initiative and helping each other. Our D&I policy reflects exactly that: showing personal initiative to be who you want to be and being open to people with a different background or idea. We have respect for the opinions of others and appreciate the differences that make us all unique individuals with our own strengths and challenges. You can find our full D&I Statement here.

More information or do you want to apply? For more information regarding this position please contact the HR department at +31 10 700 5000 or send an email to recruitment@ortec-finance.com.

We look forward to receiving your application and we hope to meet you soon!
