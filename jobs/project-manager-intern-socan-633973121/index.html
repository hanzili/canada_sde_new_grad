ABOUT THE ROLE

SOCAN is seeking a Project Manager Intern to join our growing Data Analytics and Strategic Solutions team. As a Project Manager Intern at SOCAN, the intern will be supporting the BI team by optimizing our DevOps environment, coordinating agile rituals, maintaining a healthy and welldefined backlog, and translating team progress into clear business insights. They will streamline workflows, facilitate sprint ceremonies, ensure user stories and acceptance criteria are complete, and build dashboards that track key performance metrics such as lead time, sprint velocity, and burndown trends. Through this work, they will help improve team efficiency, transparency, and overall delivery effectiveness.

WHAT YOU‚ÄôLL DO / KEY RESPONSIBILITIES

  • DevOps Environment Optimization. To conduct an audit of the current board structure and lead the migration to a standardized workflow. This includes mapping out state transitions (e.g. "In Progress" to "Peer Review"), automating ticket movements, and ensuring the board reflects the actual lifecycle of a BI project.
  • Sprint Coordination & Rituals. To act as a "Junior Scrum Master," facilitating daily stand-ups, sprint planning, and retrospectives. The goal is to keep the team focused on the sprint goal, document blockers, and ensure that action items from retrospectives are actually implemented in the next cycle.
  • Backlog Grooming & Requirement Mapping Working closely with the team, the intern will ensure the backlog is "healthy." This involves chasing down missing details for user stories, ensuring acceptance criteria are clearly defined, and flagging tickets that have been stagnant for too long to keep the pipeline moving.
  • Data Analysis & Progress Reporting. The intern will be responsible for translating technical progress into business insights. They will build and maintain dashboards that track key metrics like: Lead Time: How long it takes for a feature to go from idea to production. Sprint Velocity: How much work the team can realistically handle. Burndown Charts: Real-time tracking of work remaining in the sprint.

  • Understanding of data analysis tools such as Python or R
