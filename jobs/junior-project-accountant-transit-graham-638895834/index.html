About Graham

Full Description

Build What Matters At Graham, one of North America’s largest general contractors, we don’t just build projects, we connect communities and create pathways for progress. As part of our Infrastructure Division, you’ll contribute to transformative work across the region, including roads, highways, bridges, interchanges, light rail transit systems, bus rapid transit systems, and airports.

Here, your work has purpose. Your safety and growth are supported. And your impact is lasting.

Join a team that builds with commitment, reliability, and integrity. Apply today and help us build what matters. Your Impact Starts Here Graham is actively recruiting for a Junior Project Accountant or Accounting Clerk to support the Infrastructure Division. Reporting to the Assistant Regional Operations Controller, this role provides accounting and transactional support for transit and heavy civil construction projects. The Accounting Clerk ensures compliance with contractual terms, union agreements, and tax legislation.

Your Role in Building What Matters – From Office to Site

  • Support Accounting Processes – Assist with transactional accounting, month-end procedures, and project cost controls.
  • Manage Contracts & Billings – Administer subtrade contracts and ensure accurate, timely progress billings.
  • Handle Timekeeping & Reporting – Process time entries, generate LEMS reports, and maintain compliance with labor standards.
  • Assist with Receivables – Support collections, deposits, and maintain accurate financial records.
  • Ensure Compliance – Uphold contractual, tax, and internal control requirements across projects.

The Right Tools for the Job

  • Education: Degree or diploma in Accounting or Finance; CPA designation is an asset.
  • Experience: Minimum 5 years in accounting, project administration, or contract administration, with at least 2 years in a general contracting environment.
  • Skills: Strong knowledge of accounting and job costing principles; proficiency in SAP and advanced Excel

Compensation

  • Salary Range $70,000 to $80,000 per annum
  • Compensation aligned to education and experience, ensuring fair and competitive placement
  • Three to five weeks of vacation, with additional credit for prior industry experience
  • Flexible, comprehensive Health & Dental benefits plan designed to support your well‑being
