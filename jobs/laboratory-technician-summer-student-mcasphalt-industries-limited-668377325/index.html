Job Information

Job Title: MCASPHALT | Lab Technician – Summer Student

Status: Full-time Temporary Position

Expected Start Date: May 1, 2026

Location: 600 Arcola Avenue, Regina, Saskatchewan S4R 5K6

Reporting to: Lab Supervisor

About McAsphalt McAsphalt is a company that specializes in asphalt products and services. Founded in 1970 by Leo McArthur and John Carrick, it has since grown to become the industry leader in Canada, with 26 terminals across the country. McAsphalt offers a wide range of asphalt products, including emulsions, cutbacks, polymer-modified, performance-graded, roofing, and industrial. Additionally, McAsphalt provides comprehensive services and technology solutions, encompassing paving, testing, research and development, engineering, and consulting.

Position Summary

We are seeking a motivated summer student to join our dynamic laboratory team and play an essential role in QA/QC testing for asphalt emulsions in our Canada-wide industry. This position is ideal for students pursuing studies in Chemical Engineering, Civil Engineering, Chemistry, Materials Science, or related technical fields.

Key Responsibilities

  • Perform routine quality control testing on asphalt emulsions following standardized procedures (e.g., viscosity, sieve tests, residue testing, stability tests).
  • Prepare, label, and organize samples for testing and recordkeeping.
  • Maintain accurate and detailed laboratory data sheets and electronic records.
  • Assist with calibration, cleaning, and general maintenance of laboratory equipment.
  • Follow all safety protocols, including proper handling of chemicals and PPE requirements.
  • Support laboratory staff with additional testing and project work as required.

Qualifications

  • Currently enrolled in a post secondary program in Chemical Engineering, Civil Engineering, Chemistry, or a related technical/scientific field.
  • Strong attention to detail and commitment to quality and accuracy.
  • Ability to follow standardized test procedures and work independently once trained.
  • Comfortable working in a laboratory environment with chemicals and heated materials.
  • Strong communication and organizational skills.
  • Previous laboratory experience is an asset but not required—training will be provided.

Shift Information

Day shift (hours may vary depending on facility production schedule)

What We Offer

  • Hands on experience with real world laboratory testing and quality control processes
  • Training in industry recognized asphalt emulsion test methods
  • Supportive team environment with experienced mentors
  • Competitive student wage
  • Opportunity to gain experience in the asphalt and materials engineering industry
