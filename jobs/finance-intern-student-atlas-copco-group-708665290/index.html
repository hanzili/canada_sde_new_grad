Full Description

Finance Intern

CPC Pumps is looking for a talented professional to join our team to fill a new vacancy in the role of Finance Student / Intern (June to August, approx 3-4 months) .

Purpose

The Finance Student / Intern provides support to the accounting department with finace project-related tasks.

Duties and Responsibilities

  • Support finance-related projects including:
  • Finance Policy updates and documentation
  • Maintain and update budget tracking files (Excel):
  • Work closely with department managers and Business Controller
  • Validate and maintain accurate and up-to-date budget data
  • Assist in preparing budget communication materials
  • Support vendor-focused finance initiatives, including:
  • Updating banking information
  • Maintaining business partner master data

To succeed, you will need

Qualification Requirements

  • Good organizational and communication skills (written and verbal)
  • Strong attention to detail, enjoy data entry and commitment to accuracy
  • Ability to manage multiple tasks and work collaboratively
  • Comfortable working in Excel and data-driven tasks

Education and/or Experience

  • Bachelor’s Degree and/or Diploma in business, finance or accounting is preferred
  • Preference will be given to candidates enrolled in a qualifying co-operative education program
  • Strong Microsoft office (word and excel) skills
  • ERP experience (e.g., ACCPAC and SAP knowledge) is an asset
  • Previous office administrative and finance/accounting working experience is an asset

CPC Pumps International Inc. is an equal opportunity employer committed to diversity, inclusion, and accessibility. We welcome applications from all qualified individuals. The hiring process may use AI tools to assist with candidate screening and assessment. If contacted for an interview, please inform us of any accommodation that you may require during the hiring process.

In return, we offer

  • Culture of trust and accountability
  • Lifelong learning and career growth
  • Innovation powered by people
  • Health and well-being

The expected rate for this position is $21.47 per hour. The final compensation may vary due to factors such as job-related knowledge, skills, and experience.
