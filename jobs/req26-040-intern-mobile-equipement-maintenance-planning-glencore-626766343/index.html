INTERN – MOBILE EQUIPMENT MAINTENANCE PLANNING

1 INTERSHIP – 4 MONTHS

Service : Infrastructures and Operations support
Location: Raglan Mine Site
Reference number: REQ26-040

INTRODUCTION
Working as an Intern – Mobile equipment maintenance planning at Raglan Mine means living in the best of all worlds: giving your all at work, with both feet in the Far North, while enjoying long vacations thanks to a rotating schedule.

Our people's well-being is a priority. We offer you a safe working environment and a pleasant atmosphere in a close-knit team. What's more, you can have fun, both in your career and in your free time, thanks to our out-of-the-ordinary lifestyle and competitive salaries.

OUR BEST ADVANTAGES

Period: May to August 2026

High-quality accommodation complex (with gym, training rooms, relaxation areas, virtual golf and more).

Hearty and delicious meal service.

Single room with private bathroom.

14/14 rotation (2 weeks on, 2 weeks off)

11 working hours per day.

Private air transportation to and from cities such as Montreal, Quebec City, Rouyn-Noranda, Mont-Joli and Inuit communities, depending on where you live.

Competitive salary based on level of education completed.

HOW YOU CONTRIBUTE TO OUR WORLD

Reporting to the Planning Coordinator, you will have the opportunity to support planners and schedulers in the maintenance of Raglan Mine's mobile equipment. Your main duties will be:

Receive and plan project requests and work requests, considering available human and material resources.

Establish work schedules in accordance with company deadlines and standards, as well as environmental standards.

Develop tools to facilitate project planning and scheduling to improve efficiency in terms of preparation, execution, and quality.

Ensure that planning is consistent with appropriate work methods and safety procedures in place.

PROFILE REQUIRED

Be a student in a college program in industrial maintenance, mechanical engineering, industrial engineering, or a bachelor's degree in mechanical engineering.

Be eligible to complete a recognized internship as part of your program of study.

Hold a valid driver’s license

Within our organization, inclusion and diversity are essential values. We see everyone as an asset to our team.

Please note that only those selected for an interviewwill be contacted.
