At Kalesnikoff, we honor our heritage by investing in our future. Role and Objectives:

The Junior QA Technician supports quality assurance activities by performing routine product testing, completing administrative and data entry tasks, and assisting in maintaining company quality and safety standards.

Responsibilities:

  • Understand and comply with all company policies and procedures.
  • Follow established safe work procedures (SWP) and practices. Ensure you are signed off annually.
  • Report identified safety hazards to supervisors or safety.
  • Help identify areas where we may be exposed to safety hazards and assist in the development of new SWP’s.
  • Ensure that Company quality standards are effectively implemented.
  • Ensure operational excellence in quality control, data management, inventory control and manufacturing.
  • Conduct daily product testing according to established procedures.
  • Maintain quality records and documentation.
  • Observe systems and processes and provide recommendations for improvements.
  • Identify and communicate key indicators of performance.
  • Assess and ensure we are maximizing value back to the company by performing regular checks and provide recommendations on improvement opportunities.
  • Perform other duties as assigned or required.
  • Once fully trained, this role may provide temporary coverage for QA Technician absences to support operational continuity.

Requirements:

  • Post-secondary studies in forestry, wood science, or manufacturing are an asset.
  • Prior experience in manufacturing, quality or inspections an asset.
  • Strong attention to detail and accuracy.
  • Working knowledge of regulatory agency quality assurance/control activities.
  • Strong English communication skills, both verbal and written.
  • Receptive to change – can adapt to changing circumstances.
  • Proactive and focused on continuous improvement.

Work Conditions

  • Location: KMT – 3050 South Slocan Station Road, South Slocan, BC
  • Overtime as required.
  • Occasional exposure to dust and fumes.
  • Requires frequent stooping/bending/crouching, reaching, handling, finger dexterity, sitting, standing, walking, and lifting 20 or more lbs.
  • Constant finger dexterity and ability to hear, and talk, and possess depth perception.
  • Occasional climbing and carrying.
  • Moderate noise intensity level.
