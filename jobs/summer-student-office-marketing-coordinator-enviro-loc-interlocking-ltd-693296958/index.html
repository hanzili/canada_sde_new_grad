About Enviro-Loc Interlocking Ltd.

Full Description

About Us

Enviro-Loc Interlocking is a premium landscaping and construction company specializing in high-end patios, driveways, and outdoor spaces. We take pride in quality craftsmanship, strong teamwork, and delivering exceptional results for our clients.

Location: Remote (Work From Home)
In-Person Requirement: One team meeting per month in Milton, ON
Position Type: Seasonal Summer Position (Part-Time or Full-Time)

About the Role

Enviro-Loc Interlocking is seeking an organized, detail-oriented Summer Student to support our office administration and marketing efforts. This is a primarily remote position, offering the flexibility to work from home while collaborating virtually with our team. The successful candidate will attend one in-person team meeting per month in Milton and will gain hands-on experience in administration, marketing, customer relationship management, reporting, and project coordination.

The successful candidate will play an important role in keeping our office systems organized, supporting day-to-day administrative functions, and assisting with marketing initiatives. This role is ideal for someone who enjoys staying organized, learning new skills, and contributing to a growing business.

Responsibilities

  • Maintain and update customer information within our CRM system
  • Assist with production scheduling and project tracking
  • Create and distribute company newsletters and email campaigns
  • Pull and organize reports related to sales, marketing, and operations
  • Assist with data entry, record keeping, and document management
  • Support daily administrative and office tasks as needed
  • Coordinate internal communications and follow-up activities
  • Assist with marketing initiatives, social media planning, and content organization
  • Update spreadsheets, dashboards, and tracking documents
  • Conduct research and compile information for management projects
  • Assist with special projects and process improvement initiatives
  • Provide general administrative support to the leadership team

Qualifications

  • Currently enrolled in a college or university program in Business, Marketing, Communications, Administration, or a related field
  • Strong organizational skills and attention to detail
  • Excellent written and verbal communication skills
  • Proficient in Microsoft Office and Google Workspace
  • Comfortable working with spreadsheets and data management
  • Strong problem-solving skills and ability to multitask
  • Self-motivated with the ability to work independently
  • Positive attitude and willingness to learn
  • Experience with CRM software, email marketing platforms, Canva, or social media management is considered an asset

What We’re Looking For

  • A proactive individual who enjoys staying organized
  • Someone who takes initiative and isn't afraid to tackle new tasks
  • Strong attention to detail and accuracy
  • Excellent time management and communication skills
  • Ability to adapt to changing priorities in a fast-paced environment
  • A team player with a positive attitude and strong work ethic

What You'll Gain

  • Flexible work-from-home environment
  • Hands-on experience with office administration, marketing, and business operations
  • Exposure to CRM management, reporting, scheduling, and customer communication
  • Opportunity to work closely with leadership and contribute to meaningful projects
  • Valuable experience that can be applied to future careers in business, administration, marketing, or project management
