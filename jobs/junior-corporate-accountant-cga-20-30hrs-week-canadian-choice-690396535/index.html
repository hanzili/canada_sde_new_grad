Job description:

On-Site Junior Corporate Accountant CGA – Hamilton
Solange Medical Aesthetics
Location: Hamilton, ON
Compensation: Starting at $28-32/hour + Clinic Perks!

About Us

Founded in 2020, Solange Medical Aesthetics delivers a unique, European boutique-style experience rooted in Parisienne flair. We offer premium products, industry-leading expertise, and a dedicated team culture across our clinical locations. As we continue to scale our footprint across the GTA and Ottawa, we are looking for a sharp, detail-oriented Junior Corporate Accountant to manage our daily financial ecosystem and act as a vital strategic partner to our leadership.

The Role

In this position, you will own the day-to-day full-cycle accounting functions for a growing multi-location business. You will bridge the gap between financial precision and strategic business planning; managing everything from payroll to cash flow reconciliation, while collaborating directly with ownership on high-level budgeting.

Key Responsibilities

  • Daily Financial Operations: Manage Accounts Receivable (AR), Accounts Payable (AP), and routine ledger entries.
  • POS & Revenue Tracking: Efficiently reconcile daily sales, transactions, and merchant reports through our Point of Sale system (Square).
  • Payroll & Compliance: Process regular team payroll, manage accrued vacation pay (4%), track source deductions, and process Records of Employment (ROEs) when necessary.
  • Taxation & Remittances: Prepare and file timely HST remittances, manage corporate reporting timelines, and coordinate annual corporate tax returns.
  • HR Operational Support: Collaborate on the administrative infrastructure required to roll out and support our upcoming employee benefits program.
  • Executive Advisory: Support the business owner with personal financial filings and administrative corporate tasks.
  • Strategic Planning: Collaborate closely with management to establish annual budgets, build financial forecasts for new expansion, and actively identify operational cost-saving strategies.

What You Bring

  • Enrolled in or working towards a relevant accounting designation (CPA/CGA pathways)..
  • Solid baseline knowledge of full-cycle accounting, Canadian payroll compliance, and provincial tax regulations.
  • High proficiency with digital tools, modern cloud accounting platforms, and POS software (experience with Square data integration is a major asset).
  • High organizational grit; you enjoy the puzzle of balancing multiple clinic balance sheets and keeping data exceptionally clean.
  • Strong communication skills and a collaborative mindset; you can translate financial metrics into actionable insights for management.

What We Offer

  • Competitive Compensation: $28.00 – $32.00/hr based on experience, with 4% vacation pay.
  • Wellness Perks: We believe a healthy team drives a healthy business. Enjoy dedicated Paid Wellness Days to rest and recharge.
  • Exclusive Team Benefits: Generous internal perks, including access to our premium medical aesthetic treatments and medical-grade skincare products.
  • Room for Growth: As a fractional, part-time role in a rapidly expanding company, this position offers the unique opportunity to grow into a more senior corporate leadership position.

How to Apply

If you are an operational problem-solver ready to bring financial structure to our beautiful, fast-growing aesthetic space, we want to hear from you. Please submit your resume along with a brief introduction detailing your experience with payroll, platform reconciliation, and why you are excited to join the Solange team!

Pay: $28.00-$32.00 per hour

  • Casual dress
  • Dental care
  • Employee assistance program
  • Flexible schedule
  • On-site parking
  • Paid time off
  • Vision care
  • Wellness program

Work Location: In person
