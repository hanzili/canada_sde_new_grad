Full Description

About the Opportunity:

At Excel Homes, we believe that great communities are built on strong foundations – and that includes the financial systems behind the scenes. As a Junior Accountant, you’ll play an important role in supporting the accuracy, integrity, and efficiency of our accounting operations as we continue to grow.

This is an exciting opportunity for someone early in their accounting career who’s eager to learn, contribute and develop within a collaborative team. If you’re looking for an innovative company that’s focused on growth and is on a mission to create an unforgettable customer experience, please read on.

What You’ll Do:

Reporting to the Senior Corporate Accountant, you will play an active role in supporting the accounting team and contributing to the organization’s financial operations. This is an excellent opportunity for a recent graduate who is eager to learn, develop their skills, and grow within a collaborative environment.

You will be encouraged to think critically, ask questions, and continuously improve processes. We’re looking for someone driven, detail-oriented, and excited to build a strong foundation in accounting.

  • Responsible for bank reconciliations
  • Responsible for various clearing account reconciliations
  • Prepare monthly journal entries
  • Assist in preparing monthly and quarterly working papers, management reporting packages and financial statements
  • Analyze variances between budget and actual
  • Support operations by improving business processes and providing financial information to various departments, as required
  • Assist the accounting team where needed on special projects
  • Other duties as assigned

What We're Looking For:

We're seeking an enthusiastic, outgoing, and motivated individual with a degree in Accounting or Finance who is eager to begin their career in an entry-level role. This position is designed for those starting out in the field, and no prior work experience is required. Co-op placements, internships, or relevant coursework are considered an asset but are not mandatory.

Our ‘Must Haves’:

  • Degree in Accounting, Finance, or related field
  • Strong proficiency in Microsoft Excel and Word
  • Strong analytical and problem-solving skills
  • High attention to detail
  • Strong organizational and communication skills
  • A positive attitude and willingness to learn

About Us:

For more than three and a half decades, Excel Homes has been helping Albertans find their perfect place to call home. Since opening our doors in 1988, we’ve built over 15,000 homes across 80+ communities and earned more than 75 awards for design, innovation and industry excellence.

We don’t just build houses, we create vibrant communities where families can grow, connect and thrive together. Our mission is simple: to create a homebuying experience that delivers the most joy and value to our homeowners.

At Excel, we are proud to be recognized as a Certified Best Employer by Mercer – three years running. This recognition reflects the passion, responsibility and commitment of our incredible team, whose ideas and energy drive us to challenge the status quo and continually raise the bar for what homebuilding can be.

If you are ready to build a career where everyday brings new opportunities, we’d love to have you on our team. Apply today and help us continue simplifying the journey home – one family at a time.

We would like to thank all applicants for their interest in Excel Homes, however only those who qualify for an interview will be contacted. No phone calls please.
