Intern Toronto Digital Marketing + Content Producer Position - Health + Fitness Studio

Be Sparked To Be at Your Best!

(This is a high-level, accredited University/College Internship Placement and Career Development mentorship program. Spring Into Action (InsideOut Studio) has partnered with top school progams by providing mentorship over the past 21-years to provide the necessary skills and backdrop to reaching best potential and skills. This internship is a critical step leading immediately to a part-time and full-time career)

Intern Digital Marketing + Content Producer Position with Skills In:

English Communication, Marketing, Social Media, Website Editing, Production, Event Planning and/or Graphic Design Internship Position:

*Internship objective is to grow and develop with a leadership focus on some of the following areas:

  • Routinely Use Creativity, Build Out Ideas and Seek Solutions
  • Assist Teammates
  • Lead Various Tasks and Some Projects
  • Produce Content
  • Marketing Campaigns
  • Optimize use of platforms ie IG, Facebook, YouTube, LinkedIn Other
  • Graphic Design ie Canva, ect
  • Social Media posting + strategies to increase followers + engagement
  • Ad campaigns
  • Media Content ie Video, Photography
  • Help Organize Events Planning Calendar (Google)
  • Produce monthly newsletter
  • Contribute To Website Development
  • Podcast Project Development
  • Virtual Events Planning and Production ie Classes, WorkshopsWebinar, Podcast ect
  • Review metrics for successful campaign tracking and steady business growth
  • Regular Updating + Check-in re Tasks/Project Status
  • Identify Target Market(s) - Help Tailor/Position Marketing Channels
  • Running Live Stream Virtual Classes (Optional)

Your Recognized Key Skill Set:

Willingness to grow and develop. Team player, upbeat, positive person, excellent communicator, strong organizational and interpersonal skills, good writing skills, attention to detail, organized, multi-tasker, love a challenge of problem solving, resourceful self-starter, ability some weekends for special events.

You are ready to grow, assist and gain invaluable mentorship! You are passionate about what you do and making a difference for others - the InsideOut chief value! You want to be sparked in guidance on future direction and opening career possibilities.

Spring Into Action's chief mission is to empower others to reach their full potential and to make a difference for individual and our community.
