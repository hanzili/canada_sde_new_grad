at Athena Club

📍 Location Montreal, Quebec Remote
📋 Details Full-time

About the Role

Build Shopify themes and apps, integrate APIs

About Athena Club

DTC wellness brand focused on women's health

Full Description

Hi there, we're Athena Club! When it comes to women's health and body care, we've been given "good enough" for decades. But we're here to change that. Our mission is to create the highest-quality products that address women's health from a 360-degree perspective. We are growing rapidly and are looking for someone with an entrepreneurial mindset who thrives in a fast-paced environment.

The Role:

Athena Club is looking for a Junior Full Stack Developer (Shopify) to join our Engineering team remotely (or at our New York HQ). Reporting directly to the Engineering Manager, this Team Member will bridge the gap between design and development, building beautiful, high-performance e-commerce experiences and utilizing AI tools to craft intuitive, pixel-perfect user interfaces.

Responsibilities:

  • Build & Maintain: Develop custom Shopify themes and apps using Liquid, React, Node.js, and modern front-end frameworks.
  • Design Engineering: Take high-fidelity designs and implement them with an obsession for detail, spacing, typography, and motion.
  • AI-Driven Development : Utilize LLM-based coding tools (Cursor, Claude, etc.) to rapidly prototype, debug, and implement features.
  • Full Stack Execution : Work on everything from front-end UI adjustments to back-end API integrations, obsessing over site speed and conversion rates.

Requirements:

  • Experience: 1–2 years of experience with the Shopify ecosystem (Liquid, Storefront API, App development); experience at a DTC or subscription-based company is preferred.
  • The Tinkerer: You have a portfolio of side projects and build things because you love to create.
  • The AI Pro: You are proficient with Cursor, Claude Code, or similar AI-assisted development environments.
  • Tech Stack: Proficiency in HTML, CSS, JavaScript (ES6+), React/Vue, and Node.js.
  • Self-starter with excellent organizational and prioritization skills.
  • Demonstrated ability to collaborate cross-functionally across technical and non-technical teams.

The (+) Extras

  • Medical, Dental, Vision insurance
  • Life + Disability
  • HSA or FSA
  • Commuter benefits
  • 401K
  • Weekly team lunches
  • Optional Bi-Weekly Team Workouts
  • Annual Offsite “Camp Athena Club”

Join us in our journey to revolutionize women's health and body care products. If you're eager to make a tangible impact in a thriving startup environment, Athena Club awaits you.

How to Apply: Please submit your resume and a link to your GitHub or Portfolio. Note: In your cover letter, please include a link to a project where you are proud of the UI/UX polish, and a brief mention of how you utilize AI tools in your workflow.
