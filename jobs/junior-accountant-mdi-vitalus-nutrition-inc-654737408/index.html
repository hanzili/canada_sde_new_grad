About the Role

Prepare journal entries, reconciliations, and month-end close

About Vitalus Nutrition Inc

Plant-focused private manufacturer; strong accounting function

Full Description

Keep plant finances accurate, clear, and trusted.

At MDI (part of Vitalus Nutrition), we turn milk into ingredients people rely on—so the numbers behind our Winnipeg plant need to be right, on time, and easy to act on.

As a Junior Accountant, you’ll partner closely with the Plant Controller to keep financial reporting accurate, support month-end and audit needs, and provide dependable cost and production insights that help the plant run smoothly.

IS THIS YOU?

  • Your reconciliation skills + steady attention to detail mean our month-end closes stay clean and predictable.
  • Your analytical mindset + practical problem-solving help you spot discrepancies early and fix them fast.
  • Your calm, service-oriented style + clear communication make you a trusted partner to operations and cross-functional teams.

WHAT YOU’LL DO

Close with Confidence

  • Prepare journal entries and balance sheet reconciliations for review.
  • Support organized month-end/year-end close with solid documentation.

Support Audits, Keep It Clean

  • Respond to audit requests with complete, accurate backup.
  • Help maintain controls and reporting integrity for the plant.

Run Day-to-Day Accounting & Compliance

  • Process invoicing, AR reconciliations, inventory adjustments, and banking transactions accurately and on schedule.
  • Complete statutory reporting (GST returns, PST self-assessments, Statistics Canada reporting) on time.

Turn Production Data into Useful Insights

  • Support standard cost updates and monthly transfer price maintenance.
  • Contribute to budget prep and ad-hoc analysis that supports plant decisions.

WHAT YOU’LL BRING

  • Bachelor’s degree in Accounting, Finance, or related field.
  • 3+ years of relevant financial accounting experience (AR experience preferred).
  • Strong Excel skills and comfort with accounting software; D365 (or similar ERP) is a strong asset.
  • Working toward CPA is an asset.
  • High accuracy, strong organization, and the ability to prioritize in a fast-paced plant environment.
  • Professional discretion when handling sensitive financial and employee information.

CALL TO ACTION

If you want your work to directly support plant performance—and you take pride in accurate, reliable numbers—we’d love to hear from you. Apply and bring your accounting skills to a team that values clarity, follow-through, and practical impact
