Full Description

Financial Analyst Intern — Co-op / Internship

On-site Etobicoke, ON · Reports to: Founder

ABOUT JITTO

The wholesale produce sector is stuck in the past, costing grocers and restaurants billions due to inefficiency, waste, and hidden markups. At Jitto, we're rethinking the entire system from the ground up. From sourcing to delivery, we're using technology to modernize every step of the supply chain.

Every year, over $4 billion worth of fresh produce moves across Ontario and it all happens overnight. By sunrise, it's on shelves, in kitchens, and on plates. Most people never think about how that happens.

We've already moved over $15 million in fresh produce across Ontario, and we're scaling fast. As we prepare for our next financial raise, we need someone who can build the financial backbone of this company — clean books, real dashboards, and systems that flag issues before they become problems.

If you're an Excel wizard who loves clean data, sharp dashboards, and squeezing the most out of AI tools, keep reading.

ROLE OVERVIEW

This is a build-it-from-the-ground-up role. You'll start by cleaning up and reorganizing our books and accounting setup in QuickBooks — auditing the chart of accounts, fixing categorizations, reconciling, and building the clean systems we need for AR, AP, payroll, and grant tracking. The goal of this first stretch is a financial foundation we can trust and a clean audit trail that gets us raise-ready.

Once that's solid, the role shifts into ongoing management: owning the weekly and monthly close, building dashboards, monitoring cash flow, and putting alerts and automated reports in place to flag issues early. You'll work alongside our external accountants on the technical accounting side and own the day-to-day rhythm internally.

You'll also play a key role in communicating status and success back to our government and investors who support Jitto's growth — keeping reporting clean, timely, and accurate so we maintain strong relationships with the institutions backing us.

This is not a remote position; our office is in Etobicoke (39 Advance Road).

WHAT YOU'LL DO

* Clean up and reorganize Jitto's books in QuickBooks — chart of accounts, categorizations, reconciliations

* Set up clean systems for AR, AP, payroll, and financial tracking

* Own the weekly and monthly close

* Build and maintain financial dashboards — cash flow, P&L by route, AR aging, burn, margin

* Monitor cash flow daily and put alerts and automated reports in place to flag issues early

* Work alongside our external accountants — feed them clean data, manage handoffs, and own follow-through

* Manage reporting back to our government and grant partners — track milestones, prepare status updates, and ensure we communicate progress clearly to the institutions supporting us

* Support raise prep — clean financial package, projections, investor-ready data room

* Run deep-dive analytics on what needs answering — pricing, route profitability, customer cohorts

* Leverage AI tools (Claude, Gemini, ChatGPT) to maximize speed and quality on every deliverable

* Build in automations to highlight KPIs and alerts for different aspects of the business

WHO YOU ARE

* Currently enrolled in a Canadian college or university (required)

* Canadian citizen or Permanent Resident (required)

* Excel wizard — pivot tables, lookups, complex formulas, clean dashboards

* Hands-on QuickBooks experience — you've cleaned up books before, not just observed it

* Detail-obsessed — you can't sleep if a reconciliation doesn't tie out

* Strong analytical mind — you spot patterns in numbers and ask the next question

* Avid user of AI tools — Claude, Gemini, ChatGPT are part of your daily workflow

* Comfortable owning ambiguous, messy work and building structure where there isn't any

* Strong communicator — comfortable preparing reports and updates for external stakeholders

* Open background — accounting, finance, business analytics, or self-taught with the chops to prove it

STRONG ASSETS

* Prior co-op or internship experience in accounting, bookkeeping, or financial analysis

* Familiarity with QuickBooks Online beyond the basics

* Experience building investor-ready financial models or dashboards

* Comfort with Stripe, HubSpot, and other business tools

* Curiosity about how AI is changing finance and accounting work

WHY JITTO

You won't be one of fifty interns running variance reports for a corporate FP&A team. You'll be the finance person on the inside of a venture-backed startup that's preparing for its next raise. The systems you build, the dashboards you design, and the reporting you own will become the financial foundation of this company. Founders, accountants, and government partners will all be looking at your work.

If you want to learn how a real growth-stage startup runs its numbers — from cleanup to close to capital raise — this is the seat.

THE DETAILS

* Compensation: $18 / hour + end-of-term performance bonus

* Term: 4–8 month full-time co-op / internship (8 months preferred)

* Schedule: Business hours

* Start date: ASAP — rolling applications

* Location: On-site, Etobicoke ON (39 Advance Road)

ELIGIBILITY

* Canadian citizen or Permanent Resident

* Currently enrolled in a Canadian college or university

* In-person at our Etobicoke office (not remote)

OUR INTERVIEW PROCESS

* Round 1: 10-Minute Intro Call — Quick fit and motivation check.

* Round 2: In-Person Interview — Group / role-specific session at our Etobicoke office.

* Round 3: Paid Challenge — We'll give you a messy data set to clean up, structure, and turn into a dashboard. We'll evaluate the work, the speed, and how well you leveraged AI tools to get there.

We move fast for the right people.
