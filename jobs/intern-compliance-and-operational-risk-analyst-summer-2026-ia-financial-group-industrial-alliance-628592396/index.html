Full Description

Description Summer Internship 2026 / Full-time / Location: Québec City or Montreal / Hybrid – minimum 1 day per week in the office Build the future with us Are you driven by compliance and operational risk management activities and eager to contribute to the purpose of an organization that aims to help its clients feel confident and secure about their future? As a Compliance and Operational Risk Analyst (Intern) , you will play a key role in the analysis, monitoring, and execution of essential activities that help maintain a rigorous and compliant environment.

This position is a great opportunity to showcase your skills and fully realize your potential within a caring and trustworthy company. Here, people and their development are at the heart of our priorities, fostering an environment that encourages collaboration and innovation.

What You Will Accomplish With Us As a Compliance and Operational Risk Analyst (Intern) , you will be at the core of our mission. Your main responsibilities will include:

  • Participating in conflict of interest management processes, including the review of employees’ personal transactions (StarCompliance);
  • Collaborating in the monitoring of investment sector employees’ activities on social media (Global Relay);
  • Contributing to compliance and operational risk controls, including the completion of risk assessments;
  • Participating in portfolio monitoring processes;
  • Supporting the team in the delivery of various compliance and operational risk projects.

What Could Accelerate Your Success In This Role We are looking for someone who:

  • Is currently pursuing a bachelor’s degree in administration (finance or accounting) or another relevant field;
  • Is recognized for strong attention to detail, organizational skills, and the ability to manage multiple tasks simultaneously;
  • Demonstrates a strong ability to learn quickly, work under pressure, and meet tight deadlines;
  • Stands out for autonomy, initiative, and professionalism;
  • Has excellent interpersonal skills and the ability to collaborate effectively within a team;
  • Has an intermediate level of English proficiency, as the role requires daily verbal and written communication with unilingual English-speaking colleagues, partners, and suppliers;
  • Is comfortable with the Microsoft Office suite, and ideally with Bloomberg, StarCompliance, and Global Relay (assets).

Why you will love working with us

  • A work environment where learning and development merge with a collective pursuit of excellence;
  • A healthy, safe, equitable, and inclusive environment where potential can be freely expressed and developed;
  • The opportunity to work in a hybrid environment, supported by flexibility and access to inspiring and innovative workspaces;
  • Student benefits: access to internal training, mentorship, involvement in real projects, flexible schedules adapted to studies, and a rewarding experience that supports your professional journey.

Apply now and get ahead of your career, where your talent really belongs! Still unsure about applying? At iA, we believe in potential and value diverse experiences. If this role inspires you, go ahead and apply – your place might be with us, and we want to get to know you!
