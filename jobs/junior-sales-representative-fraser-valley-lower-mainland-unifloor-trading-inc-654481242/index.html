About Unifloor Trading Inc

Full Description

Location: Greater Vancouver Area (Fraser Valley & Lower Mainland East)

Job Type: Full-Time

Compensation: Base Salary + Commission Bonus

About the Role

We are seeking a motivated and energetic Junior Sales Representative to join our growing team. This role is ideal for someone who is eager to learn, enjoys working independently, and thrives in a fast-paced, customer-facing environment.

You will be responsible for developing and maintaining relationships with clients across the Fraser Valley and Lower Mainland East, representing our product lines and supporting business growth within the territory.

This is a field-based role, requiring regular travel and in-person visits to customers.

Key Responsibilities

  • Develop and maintain strong relationships with existing customers

  • Actively identify and pursue new business opportunities within the assigned territory

  • Conduct regular in-person visits to dealers, retailers, and project sites

  • Present products, provide recommendations, and support customers with their needs

  • Follow up on quotes, orders, and customer inquiries in a timely manner

  • Coordinate with internal teams (order desk, warehouse, and management) to ensure smooth operations

  • Stay informed on product knowledge, market trends, and competitor activities

Qualifications

  • Energetic, self-motivated, and willing to learn

  • Strong communication and interpersonal skills

  • Ability to work independently and manage time effectively

  • Comfortable with a road-based / outside sales role

  • Valid driver’s license and reliable transportation

Preferred (but not required):

  • Experience in flooring, construction materials, or related industries

  • Background in distribution or wholesale sales

What We Offer

  • Competitive base salary + commission bonus structure

  • Training and mentorship to support your growth

  • Opportunity to build a long-term career in sales

  • Dynamic and supportive team environment

How to Apply

If you are driven, eager to grow, and ready to build your career in sales, we would love to hear from you.

Please apply with your resume send to hiring@unifloor.ca

Job Type: Full-time

Pay: From $65,000.00 per year

Benefits:

  • Dental care
  • Extended health care

Work Location: In person
