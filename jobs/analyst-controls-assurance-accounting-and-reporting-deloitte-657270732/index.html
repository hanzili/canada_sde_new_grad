What will your typical day look like? Your role at Deloitte will include, but not be limited to, the following:

  • Serve clients and/or support local office teams around the country, as opportunities arise
  • Review and contribute to the planning and execution of Controls Assurance and Accounting Reporting Advisory (ARA) projects
  • Focus on applying a deep knowledge of financial reporting requirements, controls and processes to the operational aspects of Finance – “How” Finance does what it needs to do and where it can enhance the value it brings to the organization
  • Develop and lead timely communications - both internal and external - relevant to our professionals and clients
  • Participate in current state findings workshops in order to understand and document key financial processes (i.e., procure to pay, order to cash, record to report, etc.)
  • Understand and document companies’ IT architectures, as well as conduct research into financial systems
  • Develop materials for, and participate in the delivery of, finance effectiveness and visioning workshops
  • Document companies’ key performance metrics, as well as visualize these metrics using visualization tools

About The Team Our teams make a commitment to each other to foster a collaborative and supportive environment with quality, professional development, apprenticeship, mentorship, and wellness at the forefront of team delivery. Our commitment to helping each other grow and develop is strong and intentional.

At Deloitte, your career path is flexible. You will support organizations of all sizes and in different industries to help them structure business transactions and perform better. Your initial role will provide you with a variety of experiences. You will work strategically with your team members to develop an understanding of the firm’s business and the clients you are serving. You will support organizations in different industries of all sizes to help them perform better and adapt to an ever-evolving future.

Enough About Us, Let’s Talk About You In these roles, the expected qualifications are:

  • Actively pursuing or holding a CPA designation is preferred, but not required
  • Must possess strong technical skills (GAAP, control certification, securities and regulatory, assurance) and experience in, and knowledge of, Public Sector Standards, IFRS, ASPE, and internal controls and risk
  • Ability to approach engagements with a strategic perspective (options available, pros / cons of different options) and with project problem diagnosis, solution development, client communications, facilitation of decision making, documentation, managing client expectations and team leadership
  • Strong facilitation, presentation skills, communication skills (both written and verbal) and problem solving (ability to “find it” and “fix it”)
  • Experience with financial systems and tools (i.e. Blackline, Workiva, Concur, Tableau, etc.) is an asset
  • Preferred areas of academic study/background: Accounting/Business/Finance, or a related discipline
  • Quebec office locations: We require basic English language skills to meet the needs of our clients. In the context of mandates, you could work for clients with activities outside of Quebec and where the first language is English.
