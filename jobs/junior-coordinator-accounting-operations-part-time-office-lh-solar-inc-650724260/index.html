About LH SOLAR Inc.

Markham-based small company; hands-on accounting role

Full Description

Job Summary

We are seeking a detail-oriented and motivated Junior Coordinator to support both Accounting and Operations functions. This role is ideal for someone looking to gain hands-on experience in financial processes, reporting, and operational coordination within a growing organization.

You will assist with bookkeeping, data tracking, system monitoring, and reporting while ensuring accuracy and efficiency across daily operations. This position offers an excellent opportunity to build a strong foundation in accounting and operational workflows. The ideal candidate exercises sound judgment, takes initiative, resourceful, and proactively identifies and resolves issues with minimal supervision.

Key Responsibilities

  • Enter and track hydro/electricity statements and operational data
  • Assist with accounts payable and receivable processing
  • Perform account reconciliation and basic financial analysis
  • Conduct bank reconciliations and maintain accurate financial records
  • Prepare reports for budgeting and operational tracking
  • Collect, organize, and manage documentation
  • Monitor ECU connections and system performance
  • Support field inspection reporting and operational updates
  • Assist with bookkeeping tasks (data entry, journal entries, ledger updates)
  • Support payroll documentation and audit preparation
  • Collaborate with team members on reporting and special projects

Requirements

  • High school diploma required; post-secondary in Accounting, Business, or Finance is an asset
  • Basic knowledge of accounting principles and corporate finance
  • Experience with accounting software (Quickbooks) is an asset
  • Proficiency in Microsoft Excel, Smartsheet, and Zoho
  • Strong attention to detail and organizational skills
  • Ability to work independently and manage multiple tasks
  • Strong communication and problem-solving skills

Why Join Us

  • Flexible part-time office hours
  • Gain hands-on experience in Accounting & Operations
  • Opportunity to grow within a dynamic and supportive team
  • Exposure to real-world financial systems and reporting
