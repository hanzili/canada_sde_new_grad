Full Description

R10094130 Human Resources Coordinator, Summer Student (Open)

Location:

Mississauga, ON - Creditview Rd (VA HO) - Support functions

At Air Liquide Home Healthcare Canada, we believe in patient-centered care and innovation. We’re on a mission to help transform healthcare for the better. From advancing and digitizing our tools, products, and systems, to forging meaningful relationships with our patients.

Air Liquide Home Healthcare Canada is a leading provider of sleep apnea tests, treatments, and home oxygen services. We partner closely with the healthcare community to deliver an exceptional patient experience that supports health, wellness, and collaborative medicine.

Ready to be part of this global family of companies making a difference in the quality of life for patients around the world?

How will you CONTRIBUTE and GROW?

The Incumbent will support the Human Resources team by providing comprehensive administrative, analytical, and project-based support across multiple HR pillars, including Total Rewards, Talent Development, Talent Acquisition, and general HR operations. Central to this role is the ability to collaborate effectively and build relationships with HR and cross-functional stakeholders. In this role, you will help translate workforce data into actionable insights while supporting programs, policies, and initiatives that directly enhance our ability to deliver exceptional care.

Total Rewards & HR Analytics

  • Data Integrity & Collection: Support gathering and cleaning workforce data from HR systems (Workday) to ensure accuracy, completeness, and privacy compliance.
  • Compensation Support: Assist with compensation administration, market benchmarking studies, and the preparation of salary reports and equity analyses.
  • Dashboard Maintenance: Assist in developing and updating HR dashboards (using Google Sheets, Workday Analytics, or Power BI) to track key performance indicators (KPIs) such as turnover and compliance.
  • Reporting & Insights: Prepare regular and ad-hoc reports for the HR team, translating complex datasets into clear, visual, and easy-to-understand narratives.

Talent Development Support

  • Training Design & Delivery: Support the design, build, and delivery of comprehensive training plans and instructional presentations to enhance employee skills.
  • Needs & Resource Assessment: Participate in the review of existing manager resources, identifying knowledge gaps, and updating the centralized library of learning assets, in partnership with the Talent Development team.
  • Metrics Tracking: Utilize HR systems and spreadsheet functions to track training completion rates, assessment scores, and participant feedback.
  • Performance & Engagement Support: Assist with the coordination of the performance review cycle, goal-setting tracking, and employee engagement initiatives.

Talent Acquisition Support

  • Recruitment Administration: Administer aspects of the recruitment and selection process, including job postings, creative sourcing, and coordinating interviews.
  • Tracker Management: Utilize advanced spreadsheet skills to assist in maintaining recruitment trackers and analyze metrics to help optimize "time to fill" results.
  • Onboarding Coordination: Assist in onboarding and orientation activities, processing new hire packets, and addressing first-day inquiries.

Administrative and Technical Support

  • Filing support:  Assist with archiving our filing system.
  • Project Coordination: Support HR team projects and ad-hoc initiatives.
  • Process Improvement: Review current HR data collection and admin methods to recommend efficiencies or automation opportunities to streamline workflows.

__________________

Are you a MATCH?

  • Currently enrolled in a post-secondary program (University or College) with a focus on Human Resources, Business Administration, Data Analytics, or a related field.
  • Strong proficiency in Google Workspace (Sheets, Slides, Docs) and/or Microsoft Office (Excel, PowerPoint) is essential. Experience with VLOOKUPs, Pivot Tables, and conditional formatting is required.
  • Fluent in written and spoken English (French is an asset).
  • A strong ability to spot patterns, anomalies, and stories behind numbers, coupled with a natural curiosity to improve existing processes.
  • Discretion & Confidentiality: Deep understanding of the sensitive nature of HR data, with a strict commitment to maintaining privacy and security.
  • Strong organizational skills with excellent attention to detail, the ability to multitask effectively, and strong interpersonal communication skills.
