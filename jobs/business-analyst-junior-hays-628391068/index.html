Full Description

TITLE: BUSINESS ANALYST (JUNIOR)

LOCATION: CALGARY, AB (HYBRID)

ROLE: BUSINESS ANALYST

EMPLOYMENT TYPE: CONTRACTOR, FULL TIME

Responsibilities :

• Support in carrying out Inter-Affiliate compliance requirements, primarily with the Canadian Gas Pipelines Code of Conduct and the U.S. Federal Energy Regulatory Commission (FERC) Standards of Conduct (SOC).

• Conduct and support analysis of routine business processes, assist with regulatory submissions, and improve processes.

o Work closely with other departments for data gathering and compliance canvassing.

o Coordinate and support processes for daily, monthly, quarterly and annual compliance requirements.

o Provide compliance analysis and advice to internal stakeholders.

• Analyze personnel changes and manage role classifications to ensure appropriate permissions are implemented.

• Database and document management, including use of SharePoint and Workday.

Qualifications and Education:

• Relevant post-secondary education (e.g., Undergraduate degree in Business, Policy Studies, Legal/Regulatory or other related field).

• Minimum 2 years of relevant experience.

• Strong verbal and written communication skills;

• Strong planning and organizational skills, as well as attention to detail;

• Adaptable and able to handle multiple priorities and manage projects to successful completion on tight timeframes;

• Experience following and continuously improving business processes;

• Experience analyzing problems and recommending solutions;

• Strong interpersonal skills and proven ability to work efficiently and effectively independently, as part of a team and/or with stakeholders;

• Enthusiasm and willingness to learn, problem solve, share information, meet deadlines and accomplish tasks;

• Exhibits integrity and accountability in all work responsibilities; and

• Intermediate working knowledge of Word, PowerPoint, Excel, and SharePoint.
