About the Role

Build CI/CD pipelines, automate cloud infrastructure

About Dawn InfoTek Inc.

IT consulting firm serving financial sector clients.

Full Description

Dawn InfoTek Inc. is a professional IT consulting team that partners with major financial institutions, investment firms and government sectors. We have been dedicated to delivering cutting-edge consulting services and recruiting all levels of IT positions for our clients.

We are currently seeking a Junior DevOps Engineer to join our DevOps and Automation team supporting one of our major financial clients.

You will support the development and maintenance of CI/CD pipelines, automation tasks, and cloud infrastructure using tools such as Ansible, OpenShift, ArgoCD, Azure DevOps (ADO), and AWS. This role is ideal for someone with 1–3 years of hands-on experience who is eager to learn, grow, and contribute to reliable, automated delivery workflows alongside senior team members.

Key Responsibilities

  • Assist in creating and maintaining pipelines and templates using Ansible , Azure DevOps (ADO) , and other CI/CD tools based on established standards.
  • Support the development and refinement of automation tasks using Ansible playbooks .
  • Work with ArgoCD to implement GitOps deployment patterns, assist with application sync and drift-detection workflows.
  • Support OpenShift environments by helping manage build configurations, deployments, and image streams.
  • Participate in maintaining and troubleshooting AWS resources used in build and deployment workflows.
  • Help monitor and maintain the objects created on these platforms to ensure reliability and compliance.
  • Collaborate with senior engineers to troubleshoot issues and learn best practices.
  • Contribute to documentation of pipelines, configurations, and deployment processes for training and operational reference.

Required Skills & Experience

  • 1–3 years of experience working with DevOps, automation, or cloud technologies.
  • Hands-on experience or familiarity with:
  • Ansible (playbooks and basic automation patterns)
  • OpenShift (deployment basics and understanding of pods/builds)
  • ArgoCD (GitOps workflows)
  • Azure DevOps ( ADO ) for pipelines and templating
  • AWS fundamentals (cloud services usage & deployment support)
  • Basic understanding of CI/CD principles and automation workflows.
  • Willingness to learn new tools, processes, and DevOps practices in a team environment.

Preferred Qualifications

  • Experience working in a collaborative DevOps team with senior engineers.
  • Familiarity with Git, version control workflows, and branching strategies.
  • Strong desire to grow in cloud and automation technologies.

Education

  • Bachelor’s degree or diploma in Computer Science, Information Technology, Engineering , or a related field
  • Equivalent professional experience may be considered in lieu of formal education

Salary Range

$65K - $80k

Must reside in Toronto area.
