Entry Level Sales Representative

at The Acquisition Group

📍 Location Abbotsford, British Columbia
📋 Details Full-time · Permanent · Uncapped commissions · N/A
📅 Posted Apr 22, 2026

About the Role

Represent TELUS, engage customers, drive sales results

About The Acquisition Group

Big brand TELUS client, strong training and growth

Full Description

New to sales? Perfect. We’ll train you. Ready for more? We’ll grow with you.

At The Acquisition Group, we represent TELUS Communications, one of Canada’s most trusted brands, and we’re hiring sales representatives to join our Abbotsford team right now.

No previous sales experience? No problem. Many of our top performers started with zero background in sales. We train you from day one, coach you as you grow, and promote from within. If you already have experience in sales, customer service, or leadership, this is also a place where you can sharpen your skills, earn more, and take the next step in your career.

What You’ll Be Doing

  • Represent TELUS directly in Abbotsford
  • Attend local events and engage with residents
  • Build strong customer relationships through genuine conversations
  • Help people find trusted products and services that fit their needs
  • Turn your effort into results and earnings

What’s In It For You

  • Uncapped commission-based pay structure, dependent on commission rates associated with a successfully sold product
  • Full training and support from your very first day
  • A clear path to take on more responsibility and move into leadership
  • Work at engaging local events and within Abbotsford residential communities
  • A motivated, supportive team that genuinely wants to see you win
  • Valuable experience in sales, communication, and business growth

You’re a Great Fit If You

  • Enjoy meeting new people and building genuine connections
  • Are motivated and open to feedback
  • Are legally eligible to work in Canada
  • Want a career where growth is based on performance

Spring is a great time to start fresh. Our Abbotsford team is growing, and we’re looking for people who are ready to build something bigger for themselves. Whether you’re starting your career or looking for your next opportunity, this is a role where hard work is rewarded and your potential is never capped.
