MUST - SPEAK ARABIC

Paid Internship — Social Media Game Changer (Content Creator & Editor)

PregnaWell Inc. | Ottawa

We’re not looking for a typical social media intern.

We’re looking for someone on fire. Someone who can shoot, edit, and turn ideas into content that actually captures attention.

PregnaWell is a fast-growing company helping women improve fertility, hormone health, and pregnancy outcomes. Our content reaches thousands of women globally — and we are scaling aggressively.

You will work directly with the founder and core team.

Your responsibilities:

• Shoot high-quality video content (Reels, TikTok, YouTube Shorts)

• Edit videos with fast turnaround

• Come up with content ideas, hooks, and concepts

• Turn raw footage into finished, ready-to-publish content

• Help maintain a consistent, high-level content output

This role is for you if:

• You know how to shoot and edit professionally

• You are fast, reliable, and proactive

• You understand attention and storytelling

• You take initiative and care about quality

• You want real experience, not a passive internship

Bonus if you use CapCut, Premiere Pro, or Final Cut.

This is a paid internship with potential to grow into a full-time role.
