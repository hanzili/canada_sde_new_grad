Full Description

Junior Power BI Developer

Location: Toronto

Experience: 2+ Years

Employment Type: Full-Time

Role Overview

We are looking for a motivated Junior Power BI Developer to join our data analytics team. In this role, you will be responsible for developing and maintaining reports and dashboards that help our business units make data-driven decisions. You will work closely with senior developers and business analysts to understand data requirements and translate them into visual stories.

Key Responsibilities

  • Report Development: Design and build intuitive, interactive Power BI dashboards and reports based on business requirements.
  • Data Transformation: Use Power Query (M language) to clean, transform, and load (ETL) data from various sources (Excel, SQL Server, SharePoint).
  • Data Modeling: Assist in developing and maintaining functional data models, including defining relationships and creating basic calculated columns and measures.
  • DAX Writing: Write and troubleshoot basic to intermediate DAX formulas for time-intelligence and aggregation.
  • Maintenance: Perform regular updates to existing reports and ensure data accuracy and refresh schedules are functioning correctly.
  • Collaboration: Participate in requirements-gathering sessions with stakeholders and document report specifications.

Required Skills & Qualifications

  • Education: Bachelor‚Äôs degree in Computer Science, Data Analytics, Information Technology, or a related field.
  • Technical Experience: 2+ years of hands-on experience using Power BI Desktop and Power BI Service .
  • SQL Knowledge: Fundamental understanding of SQL (Select statements, Joins, Filters) to query relational databases.
  • Data Concepts: Basic understanding of data warehousing concepts like Star Schemas and Snowflake Schemas.
  • Attention to Detail: Strong ability to validate data to ensure reports reflect "one version of the truth."
  • Communication: Ability to explain technical findings to non-technical team members.

Preferred Skills

  • Experience with Power Automate or Power Apps.
  • Familiarity with Excel (Advanced formulas, Pivot Tables).
  • Certification in Microsoft Power BI (e.g., PL-300).
  • Exposure to cloud platforms like Azure Synapse or Snowflake.
