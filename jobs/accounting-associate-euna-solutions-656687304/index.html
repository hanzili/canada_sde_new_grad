Accounting Associate

at Euna Solutions

📍 Location Oakville, Ontario Hybrid
📋 Details Co-op/internship · May-Aug 2026 · $20-$23/hr · 4 months
📅 Posted Apr 03, 2026

About the Role

Assist AP, reconciliations, close, and reporting

About Euna Solutions

GovTech 100-listed software provider to public sector

Full Description

The Opportunities

Euna Solutions is looking for an Accounting Co-Op Associate to join our dynamic Accounting. If you're a proactive student who thrives in a fast-paced, collaborative environment, this role offer hands-on experience in real business operations, exposure to cross-functional teams, and opportunities to own impactful projects.

You’ll be part of a group that values accuracy, initiative, and curiosity. We’re looking for a team player who wants to learn, contribute meaningfully, and isn't afraid to speak up when something can be done better.

This position is for the May - August term. Anticipated start date is May 5, 2026.

What You'll Do In this hands-on role, you'll assist with accounts payable, treasury, and month-end accounting tasks. It’s ideal for students interested in the day-to-day workings of corporate finance.

Key Responsibilities

  • Support monthly GL account reconciliations
  • Assist with vendor invoice reviews and payment processing
  • Help with month-end close and finance reporting
  • Maintain data accuracy and help improve finance file organization

What You Bring

  • Enrolled in a Co-op program and working toward a diploma or degree in Accounting, Finance, Legal Studies, or Business Administration
  • Basic understanding of accounting principles
  • Organized, accountable, and comfortable working independently or on a team
  • Strong communication skills and a high attention to detail
  • Adaptable to changing priorities—comfortable in a fast-paced, tech-driven environment
  • Intermediate Excel skills and strong Microsoft Office proficiency

Core Competencies We Value

  • Accuracy & attention to detail
  • Collaboration & teamwork
  • Initiative & problem-solving
  • Time management & accountability
  • Communication & adaptability
  • Integrity and ethical judgment

Location:

This position will be hybrid with 3 days/week in our Oakville, ON (or ATL, GA) office.

Compensation Range: $20-$23/hr

We believe in openness, so we publish our range from the start. Where an offer will land depends on your background, strengths, and how you’ll contribute to the work ahead.

This role fills an existing vacancy. Euna uses AI responsibly to assist in screening, assessing, or selecting applicants. AI helps us work smarter, but people lead the process.

AI Mindset at Euna Solutions

We believe the future of work is human + AI. At Euna Solutions, we encourage our team members to leverage AI tools to enhance creativity, efficiency, and decision-making. We’re looking for people who are curious about emerging technologies, eager to experiment, and committed to using AI responsibly to augment—not replace—their expertise.

If you enjoy exploring new ways to solve problems, learning continuously, and applying AI to make your work smarter and more impactful, you’ll thrive here.
