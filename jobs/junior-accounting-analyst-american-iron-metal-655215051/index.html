About American Iron & Metal

Global metal recycling leader, Honeycomb division of AIM

Full Description

Company Description

Honeycomb is a division of American Iron & Metal (AIM), a family-owned company and recognized global leader in the metal recycling industry with more than 125 sites and 4000 employees worldwide. We have continued to prosper for the last eight decades thanks to the dedication of our employees and the ongoing trust and support of our customers.

Honeycomb purchases and processes catalytic converters to recover and refine PGMs (platinum group metals).

Why? To give a converters a second life and to strive to reduce the environmental impact associated with metal extraction. We work hard to keep our partners happy, and our business clean.

Job Description

As the Junior Accounting Analyst at Honeycomb, you will play a key role in ensuring the reliability of financial data within the organization. You will maintain accurate and up-to-date records and ensure that processes are followed and adhered to. You will serve as a technical resource within the team and actively contribute to the continuous improvement of accounting practices.

What you’re responsible for:

  • Enter financial data: sales, purchases, expenses, and other routine transactions.
  • Perform monthly bank reconciliations and account monitoring.
  • Reconcile and manage corporate credit cards.
  • Prepare client invoicing, follow up on payments, and issue reminders as needed.
  • Monitor accounts payable and follow up on outstanding balances.
  • Prepare GST/QST returns and other tax obligations.
  • Manage payroll and group insurance entries.
  • Assist with month-end and year-end close processes.
  • Collaborate with other finance team members to ensure accurate and high-quality data.
  • Participate in improving accounting processes and implementing internal controls.
  • Ensure compliance with company policies and accounting standards.
  • Perform any other related tasks.

Qualifications

To join our team:

  • You have a bachelor’s degree in accounting, finance, or related field.
  • You have 2 years of accounting experience.
  • You have knowledge of the entire accounting cycle.
  • You have a commitment to excellence, autonomy and initiative.
  • You have strong attention to detail and organizational skills.
  • You have the ability to meet deadlines and manage multiple priorities.
  • You have excellent communication and problem-solving skills.
  • You are bilingual in French and English.

Additional Information

What we offer!

  • A competitive wage, vacation, benefits and a RRSP matching program
  • Annual AIM tuition scholarship program up to $8,500 per eligible dependents
  • The tools and support needed to be successful in your career and professional development
  • A dynamic & rewarding work environment that is also a lot of fun!
