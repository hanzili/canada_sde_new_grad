Full Description

About StudyPug.com

StudyPug is an innovator in the online math and science help space. We provide 3M+ students, tens of thousands of parents, and thousands of schools globally with comprehensive math and science video tutorials, instant practice and a dynamic study plan to fit their study needs. Our content is engaging and changing how education is being done.

We are a small, creative and fast-moving team in Richmond, BC, designing innovative products and educational programs by responding quickly to feedback from our students, their parents and educators.

You can learn more about us at www.studypug.com

About the Role

Full-time, hybrid position (3 days in-office, 2 days remote) for a junior to intermediate full stack developer to work with our senior development team on marketing conversion pages, landing pages, and CRM systems. You will work primarily with Node.js, Next.js (React), WordPress, and SuiteCRM while learning to leverage AI tools to accelerate development.

What You'll Do

  • Develop and optimize marketing landing pages and conversion funnels using Next.js and React
  • Work on StudyPug's main platform built with Node.js and Next.js
  • Maintain and update StudyPug Learning's WordPress websites
  • Work with SuiteCRM for customer relationship management features
  • Collaborate with senior developers on code reviews and best practices
  • Use AI coding tools to accelerate development and problem-solving
  • Debug and fix issues across web and mobile platforms
  • Implement A/B testing variations for conversion optimization
  • Write clean, maintainable code following team standards

Requirements

  • 1-3 years professional development experience in Canada
  • Proficiency in JavaScript/TypeScript and Node.js
  • Experience with React (Next.js experience preferred)
  • Understanding of HTML, CSS, and responsive web design
  • Experience with WordPress development (themes, plugins, customization)
  • Familiarity with CRM systems (SuiteCRM, SugarCRM, or similar)
  • Basic understanding of SQL and databases
  • Experience with Git version control
  • Comfortable learning and using AI coding tools (GitHub Copilot, Cursor, or similar)
  • Strong problem-solving skills and ability to work with senior developers
  • Legally authorized to work in Canada
  • Based in Richmond or Vancouver - hybrid schedule: 3 days in-office, 2 days remote
  • Professional English communication skills

Preferred

  • Next.js and server-side rendering experience
  • WordPress theme and plugin development
  • SuiteCRM customization experience
  • Experience with conversion optimization and A/B testing
  • Understanding of SEO and web performance optimization
  • Experience with testing frameworks (Jest, Cypress)
  • Chinese language ability (Mandarin or Cantonese)

What We're Looking For

  • Eagerness to learn and grow with guidance from senior developers
  • Demonstrated aptitude with AI tools and modern development workflows
  • Ability to work independently on assigned tasks
  • Strong communication skills for in-person collaboration
  • Interest in EdTech and helping students succeed
  • Portfolio showing production-quality code, not tutorial projects
  • Attention to detail (hint: include the word "pineapple" somewhere in your application to show you read this carefully)

Details

  • Small collaborative team environment
  • Work directly with senior developers and Director of Product
  • Opportunity to work on high-traffic educational platform serving 3M+ students
  • Learn modern AI-assisted development practices

Qualified candidates will be invited for technical assessment and in-person interview.
