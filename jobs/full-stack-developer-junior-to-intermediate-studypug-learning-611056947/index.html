**About StudyPug.com**

StudyPug is an innovator in the online math and science help space. **About the Role**

Full-time, hybrid position (3 days in-office, 2 days remote) for a junior to intermediate full stack developer to work with our senior development team on marketing conversion pages, landing pages, and CRM systems. You will work primarily with Node.js, Next.js (React), WordPress, and SuiteCRM while learning to leverage AI tools to accelerate development.

- 1-3 years professional development experience in Canada - Proficiency in JavaScript/TypeScript and Node.js - Experience with React (Next.js experience preferred) - Understanding of HTML, CSS, and responsive web design - Experience with WordPress development (themes, plugins, customization) - Familiarity with CRM systems (SuiteCRM, SugarCRM, or similar) - Basic understanding of SQL and databases - Experience with Git version control - Comfortable learning and using AI coding tools (GitHub Copilot, Cursor, or similar) - Strong problem-solving skills and ability to work with senior developers - Legally authorized to work in Canada - Based in Richmond or Vancouver - hybrid schedule: 3 days in-office, 2 days remote - Professional English communication skills

**Preferred**

- Next.js and server-side rendering experience - WordPress theme and plugin development - SuiteCRM customization experience - Experience with conversion optimization and A/B testing - Understanding of SEO and web performance optimization - Experience with testing frameworks (Jest, Cypress) - Chinese language ability (Mandarin or Cantonese)
