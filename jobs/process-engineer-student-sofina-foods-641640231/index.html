About Sofina Foods

Full Description

Join Us:
Sofina Foods is one of Canada's leading manufacturers of primary and further processed protein products for retail and foodservice customers as well as international markets. Join our family of diverse, talented, and dedicated people and become part of an industry leading company as well as striving to be the most successful food company in the world.

Position Summary:
Process Engineer Student Opportunity: From April 01,2026 – September 4, 2026

Location: Abbotsford, BC

Pay rate: 20.15 per hour

Provide Support to Team to help optimize maintenance inventory, purchasing activities, and vendor relationships using Limble CMMS software. This role is critical in ensuring the availability of spare parts, minimizing equipment downtime, and supporting proactive maintenance strategies and to identify the opportunities on the floor and with the process and any inefficiencies.

Requirements:

  • Currently enrolled in a college or University program related to engineering.
  • Experience in a food manufacturing environment is an asset.
  • Excellent communication skills both oral and written.
  • Computer Literacy (intermediate MS Word, MS Excel) and asset to have proficiency in computerized systems like Limble CMMS or similar platforms for inventory tracking and vendor management
  • Strong attention to detail

Work Conditions and Physical Capabilities

  • Fast-paced environment
  • Physically demanding – lifting to 50lbs
  • Attention to detail.
  • Work under pressure.
  • Hand-eye co-ordination
  • Standing and walking for extended periods
  • Production deadlines
  • Cold/refrigerated.

Sofina Foods Inc.is a privately owned Canadian company headquartered in Markham, Ontario, Canada dedicated to providing great-tasting, high-quality food products for consumers.

Our workforce is exceptionally diverse with professional opportunities across many functional areas of our business.
