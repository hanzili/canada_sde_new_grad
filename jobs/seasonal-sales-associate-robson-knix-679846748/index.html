About the Role

Assist customers, drive sales, deliver brand experience

About Knix

Award-winning intimate apparel brand with inclusive culture

Full Description

As a Sales Associate at Knix, you play a key role in creating a warm, inclusive, and confidence-building experience for every customer who walks through our doors. From first hello to finding the perfect fit, you bring the Knix experience to life through genuine connection, thoughtful listening, and personalized product guidance. Join a high-performance team where your contribution is measured by your impact. At Knix, we don't just sell; we empower through results. You will thrive here if you are self-motivated, initiative-driven, and love the thrill of hitting a goal.

Knix is an award-winning intimate apparel and activewear brand that is re-inventing intimates for real life. Through our innovative products and our engaged community, we are empowering people everywhere to be unapologetically free. Launched in 2013, we are one of the fastest-growing intimate apparel brands in North America and are globally recognized as an innovator within the apparel space. We reach over 3 million customers through our online stores, Knix retail locations across North America and channels such as wholesale and Amazon. We are revolutionizing the apparel industry with our best-in-class customer experience, innovative products and brands Knix and Kt by Knix. Come join us if you are looking for a purpose-driven and authentic career!

We are looking for a Part-Time Seasonal Sales Associate who will create a comfortable and uplifting environment for customers as they explore the store and Knix’s product line. Actively listening to their needs and preferences, you expertly guide the customer experience from greeting to fit to purchase. You are intuitive, enthusiastic, and able to leverage your love for Knix to drive sales goals and enhance customer loyalty. Position type: Contract Part-Time Knix Store: 1150 Robson St, Vancouver BC, V6E 1B5 Contract End: September 11th, 2026

What's in it for you (Benefits & Perks)

  • Competitive Pay - Highly competitive compensation plus monthly bonus!
  • Product Discounts - Generous Discounts on your favourite Knix products and Gratis Program
  • Health - Access to a mental health & wellness platform that offers therapy and coaching sessions
  • Work Environment - A one-of-a-kind culture that is committed to fostering an inclusive and diverse workplace where all our team members can bring their whole selves to work
  • Career Advancement - Knix keeps growing and we love to promote from within!

About You

  • You’re a Knix Ambassador - inspired by our mission, and driven to translate that passion into measurable brand growth.
  • You’re energized by a fast-paced and sales-driven environment, adapt quickly to change, and consistently strive to meet and exceed sales goals.
  • You are a self-starter who doesn't wait for a task list. You see the 'open space' on the floor and fill it—whether that’s connecting with a client or optimizing a display to drive UPT.
  • You know how to read customer cues and tailor each interaction to create a welcoming, memorable experience.
  • You understand the vulnerability of fitting rooms and naturally make others feel comfortable, confident, and supported.
  • You treat feedback as a tool for success, proactively applying coaching to hit your targets and support the store’s success.
  • You’re a team player who strives to keep the floor positive, productive, and customer-focused.
  • You’re comfortable handling difficult customer interactions and objections.

The Opportunity

  • Create a comfortable, inclusive, and uplifting end-to-end experience for all Knix customers.
  • Actively listen to customers' unique needs and guide them to the right Knix products, ensuring they leave confident and with the perfect fit.
  • Comfortable working in a sales-driven environment. Adopt a 'lean in” mindset toward sales targets. You understand that every KPI (AOV, CVR, UPT) is a direct reflection of how well we are serving our community.
  • Stay knowledgeable on our growing product assortment and current promotions to confidently support customer decisions.
  • Maintain high energy and engagement on the floor, contributing to a positive in-store experience regardless of traffic levels.
  • Support visual standards, organization, and general operational needs to keep the store customer-ready.
  • Escalate any issues or opportunities as required to the Manager on Duty.

Qualifications

  • Previous experience in customer service or retail is considered an asset, but above all else, you’re great with people and are passionate about delivering exceptional customer experiences.
  • You love being part of a team and are a great collaborator.
  • Ability to stay flexible, take initiative, and handle multiple customer interactions with care and attention.
  • Experience in a sales-driven environment, working with KPIs and sales targets is considered an asset.

Eligibility Requirements

  • Must have the necessary work authorization to be legally employed in Canada and be 18 years of age or older.
  • Ability to lift and handle packages weighing up to 25lbs.
  • Ability to move efficiently through the store for long periods of time and as needed for an entire shift.
  • Available to work a flexible schedule to meet the needs of the business, including holiday, evening, and weekend shifts.

Hourly Salary

  • $19.70 per hour
