About the Role

Capture, edit, and publish social media content

About Accencis Group

Creative marketing agency with hospitality client focus

Full Description

Accencis Group is looking for a creative and energetic Part-Time Content Creator to help bring our portfolio of restaurant, café, and retail brands to life across social media.

Working directly with the Marketing Team and VP of Operations & Marketing, you will capture, create, and edit engaging content for Instagram, TikTok, RedNote, and other digital platforms.

What You'll Do

  • Capture photos and videos across multiple brand locations
  • Create and edit Reels, TikToks, and short-form content
  • Develop creative content ideas and social media concepts
  • Coordinate and execute content shoots with restaurant teams
  • Stay on top of social media trends and new content tools
  • Deliver content quickly while maintaining brand standards

What We're Looking For

  • Strong photography, videography, and editing skills
  • Proficiency with CapCut, Canva, and modern AI editing tools
  • Adobe Creative Cloud experience is an asset, not a requirement
  • Excellent communication and interpersonal skills
  • Comfortable directing staff and encouraging participation in content shoots
  • Self-starter who can work independently
  • Valid driver's license and reliable vehicle required

Position Details

  • Part-Time / Freelance
  • Flexible schedule
  • Travel between GTA locations required
  • Hourly compensation
  • No benefits

If you're passionate about creating content that gets views, engagement, and results, we'd love to hear from you.

To apply, please send your resume, portfolio, and social media work samples.

Pay: $18.00-$20.00 per hour

Benefits:

  • Casual dress
  • Company events
  • Discounted or free food
  • Flexible schedule
  • On-site parking
  • Store discount
  • Work from home

Work Location: Hybrid remote in Richmond Hill, ON
