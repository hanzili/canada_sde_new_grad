About the Role

Reconcile ledgers, process AP/AR, assist reporting

About Everison International Group Inc.

Global commodities firm; growth-focused, internal promotion.

Full Description

Who We Are

Everison International Group Inc. was founded overseas in 2001. In 2005, we expanded our operations to North America, with our Toronto office acting as the central hub overseeing global operations. Through the years, Everison International Group Inc. has grown from dealing mostly in recycled metal and steel pipes to dealing with many different types of commodities from all over the world.

Job Summary

The Junior Account Coordinator is responsible for assisting the Accounting Department with account reconciliations, updating various ledgers, preparing financial reports, ensuring transactions are supported with adequate documentation, ensuring financial statements are accurate on an ongoing basis among other accounting duties as assigned. The Junior Account Coordinator will also be responsible for assisting in general administrative duties as assigned. These duties can include acting as the designated client contact and follow-up with clients and maintain ongoing communications with clients among other administrative duties as assigned.

Main Responsibilities

  • Complete account reconciliations on various general ledger and sub-ledger accounts.
  • Reconcile accounts payable transactions and vendor statements; resolve discrepancies promptly.
  • Must have experience with QuickBooks and bookkeeping.
  • Collaborate with other departments to streamline processes and improve efficiency.
  • Handle day-to-day accounting tasks, including accounts payable, accounts receivable, and general ledger entries.
  • Ensure all transactions are supported by adequate supporting documentation, in accordance with official policy and properly approved.
  • Initiate all transactions on time.
  • Ensure transactions are reflected in a proper accounting period.
  • Assist with year-end and month-end processes.
  • Act as the designated client contact and follow-up with clients from multiple countries.
  • Communicate with tenants and vendors as needed regarding payment status or discrepancies
  • Assist in preparing financial reports, budgets, and forecasts.

Qualifications and Education Requirements

  • Bachelor’s degree in Accounting, Finance, or a related field
  • Proficiency with Microsoft Excel, Google Workspace, and general IT troubleshooting.
  • Excellent organizational and communication skills.
  • Excellent organizational skills with the ability to manage multiple tasks effectively.
  • CPA or Working Towards designation.

What We Offer

  • Modern Workplace : Enjoy our newly renovated, open-concept office with an upbeat vibe. On-site parking and a business casual dress code mean a comfortable, hassle-free workday.
  • Supportive Culture: Work alongside friendly colleagues in a fun, team-oriented atmosphere. Open communication and collaboration are the norm – we’re all about helping each other succeed!
  • Career Growth: We love promoting from within. You’ll have access to professional development resources and fast-track promotion opportunities, helping you grow your career right here at Everison.
  • Benefits & Perks: We provide 100% company-paid extended health, dental, vision, and life insurance coverage for you and your family . In addition, we offer a wide range of benefits designed to enhance your overall well-being and improve your daily quality of life. We believe that taking care of our team means supporting you both at work and at home.
  • Stability: This is a permanent, full-time position (37.5 hours per week, Monday–Friday) offering solid job security and work-life balance.

Join Us: If you’re a proactive, organized professional excited about career advancement and diverse responsibilities, Everison could be the perfect fit. Come thrive in our fast-paced yet supportive environment – we can’t wait to grow with you!
