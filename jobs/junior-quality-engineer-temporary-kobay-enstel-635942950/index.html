About the Role

Support stamping QA on plant floor

About Kobay Enstel

Automotive stamping and welding manufacturer

Full Description

Kobay Enstel Ltd., an automotive manufacturing company specializing in stamping and welding, is seeking a Junior Quality Engineer (Temporary) to support plant-floor quality activities and ensure compliance with IATF 16949 standards.

This role supports quality assurance, problem-solving, and continuous improvement within a stamping and welding production environment.

Key Responsibilities

  • Support quality assurance activities in a stamping and welding environment
  • Monitor production processes and perform in-process and final inspections
  • Assist with APQP, PPAP submissions, and control plan updates
  • Participate in root cause analysis using 8D and 5 Why methodologies
  • Support FMEA and SPC activities
  • Conduct layered process audits and internal audits
  • Investigate internal and customer quality concerns
  • Support corrective and preventive action implementation
  • Maintain quality documentation and reports

Qualifications

  • Diploma or Degree in Engineering (Mechanical, Industrial, or related field)
  • 1–3 years of experience in automotive manufacturing
  • Experience in stamping and welding operations required
  • Working knowledge of IATF 16949 standards
  • Basic understanding of APQP, PPAP, FMEA, and SPC
  • Strong analytical and problem-solving skills
  • Good written and verbal communication skills
  • Proficient in Microsoft Office (Excel preferred)

Pay: $43,909.31-$52,000.00 per year

  • Extended health care
  • Life insurance
  • On-site parking
  • Paid time off
