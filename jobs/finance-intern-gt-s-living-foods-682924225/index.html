About the Role

About GT'S Living Foods

Leading health beverage brand; culture-driven, fast-growing

Full Description

Company & Culture:

At GT’s Living Foods, we’re more than just a company – we’re a community passionate about creating a healthier, happier world. Over the last 30 years we’ve built a culture of inclusivity and authenticity, where our shared values create an environment that inspires you to do your best while achieving meaningful results. Our mission to transform health and happiness through potent, living foods has established us as the #1 Kombucha brand and a leader in the Health & Wellness space.

Join GT’s Living Foods and be part of a company that is passionate about creating a healthier, happier world. As a member of our team, you’ll contribute to a mission focused on transforming health and happiness through potent, living foods, while gaining meaningful, hands-on experience in a fast-paced, growth-driven environment.

Summary

The GT’s Living Foods Internship Program is a paid opportunity designed to provide real-world experience, build foundational skills, and expand your professional network. We are seeking currently enrolled college students interested in gaining exposure within the Finance Department at a leading health and wellness organization.

This internship offers the opportunity to:

  • Gain hands-on experience supporting key finance functions in a dynamic, high-growth environment
  • Develop analytical and problem-solving skills through project-based work
  • Build business acumen by partnering cross-functionally with teams across the organization
  • Strengthen communication and presentation skills through exposure to various levels of leadership
  • Contribute to meaningful projects that support the continued growth and financial health of the company

Internships at GT’s Living Foods are paid and do not require course credit.

Basic Qualifications

  • Currently enrolled in an undergraduate or graduate program at an accredited university, pursuing a degree in Finance, Accounting, Business, or a related field.
  • Authorized to work in the United States without sponsorship
  • Ability to commute to and work onsite for the duration of the program

Preferred Qualifications

  • Demonstrated leadership experience through academics, extracurricular activities, or prior work experience
  • Strong written and verbal communication skills
  • Detail-oriented, organized, and able to manage multiple priorities
  • Ability to work both independently and collaboratively in a team environment
  • Proficiency in Microsoft
  • Strong time management and follow-through

This role description is intended to outline primary responsibilities but is not exhaustive. Additional duties may be assigned as needed.

Job Details:

  • Position Type: Full Time or Part Time
  • Work Hours: Business Hours (M-F Onsite)
  • Location: Onsite – Los Angeles (Vernon, CA)
  • Pay Range: $20/hr. - $25/hr.
