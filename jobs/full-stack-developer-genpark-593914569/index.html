About GenPark

Full Description

**Company Description**

GenPark is an innovative platform that connects emerging Oriental direct-to-consumer brands with Gen Z consumers through AI-driven personalized marketing. With a multi-agent intelligence system, GenPark curates culturally resonant and engaging experiences that enhance the shopping journey. The platform empowers brands with next-generation AI marketing tools that optimize campaigns for social media and beyond. From digital retail to real-world assets, GenPark creates a unified and dynamic ecosystem for both brands and consumers. Join GenPark to be part of a transformative approach to marketing, shopping, and brand engagement.

**Role Description**

This is a hybrid Full-Stack Developer Internship located in Toronto, ON, with the flexibility of partial work-from-home options. The intern will contribute to designing, developing, and maintaining both the front-end and back-end components of the GenPark platform. Daily tasks include coding in various programming languages, collaborating with cross-functional teams, and troubleshooting issues to ensure optimal performance. The role offers an excellent opportunity to gain hands-on experience working with cutting-edge AI-driven systems in a dynamic and innovative environment.

**Qualifications**

- Proficiency in Front-End Development, including knowledge of HTML, CSS, and JavaScript - Experience in Back-End Web Development and understanding of server-side programming - Fundamental knowledge of Full-Stack Development and Software Development principles - Familiarity with Cascading Style Sheets (CSS) for styling and responsive design - Strong problem-solving skills and ability to work collaboratively in a team environment - Interest in AI-driven technologies and innovative marketing tools - Pursuing a degree in Computer Science, Software Engineering, or a related field
