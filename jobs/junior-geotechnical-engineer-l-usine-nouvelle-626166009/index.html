About the Role

Assist geotechnical investigations and field testing

About L'Usine Nouvelle

International engineering group; infrastructure and mobility services

Full Description

The successful candidate will assist in the planning, design, and execution of geotechnical investigations and provide technical support, design and reporting for a variety of foundation and pavement engineering projects, including transportation, infrastructure, and major project developments.

  • Assist in conducting site investigations, soil sampling, and laboratory testing.
  • Analyze soil, rock, and groundwater data to support geotechnical design recommendations.
  • Prepare technical reports, drawings, and specifications under the guidance of senior engineers.
  • Support the design and assessment of foundations, roadways and pavements, retaining structures, slope stability, and ground improvement solutions.
  • Participate in field work, including site supervision, drilling operations and construction monitoring, and support laboratory testing operations.
  • Collaborate with multidisciplinary teams to deliver project objectives on time and within budget.
  • Ensure compliance with relevant industry standards, codes, and health & safety regulations.

,

  • Bachelor’s degree in Geotechnical, Civil, or Geological Engineering from an accredited institution.
  • Registered as a Professional Engineer (P.Eng).
  • 2-5 years of relevant experience, with demonstrated involvement in pavement design, evaluation, and/or foundation engineering projects.
  • Strong knowledge of geotechnical engineering principles, soil mechanics, foundation design, and pavement structure analysis.
  • Experience with pavement materials testing, subgrade assessment, and foundation system recommendations is an asset.
  • Familiarity with geotechnical and pavement design software (e.g., GeoStudio, Slope/W, CIRCLY, AASHTOWare) is an advantage.
  • Strong written and verbal communication skills, with the ability to prepare technical reports and presentations.
  • Willingness to travel and perform fieldwork, including site investigations and construction monitoring, as required.

,

Egis est un groupe international de conseil et d'ingénierie de la construction, des transports et des services à la mobilité.

Notre vocation ? Créer et exploiter des infrastructures et bâtiments intelligents au service de la transition écologique en permettant un aménagement du territoire plus équilibré et durable.

Pour accompagner notre dynamique de croissance, nous offrons des opportunités de carrières variées dans près de 120 pays.

Rejoindre notre Groupe et nos 18 000 collaborateurs, c'est avant tout partager nos valeurs : une entreprise humaine, impliquée dans le développement durable et dans l'innovation.
