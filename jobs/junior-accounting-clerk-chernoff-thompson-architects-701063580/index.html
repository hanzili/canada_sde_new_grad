About the Role

About Chernoff Thompson Architects

Local architecture firm; stable, supportive environment

Full Description

Busy architectural office is looking for a responsible and reliable person to join our Accounting Team.

Junior Accounting / Bookkeeping / Data Entry – full-time, permanent.

37.5 hours per week.

Specific Duties include, but are not limited to:

  • Data entry
  • Accounts payable & accounts receivable
  • Credit card and expense reconciliations
  • Set up of new projects; close-out of completed projects
  • Provide assistance and support to Partners and staff that require accounting and clerical tasks
  • Clerical work, including both general and accounting filing.
  • General administrative support, including typing, photocopying, phone coverage, mail preparation, etc.

Note that this is not a position that will provide direct work experience for a CGA program.

This position is an office-based position with no remote working ability.

You Have:

  • Canadian or equivalent post-secondary education in the field of Accounting or Business Management, or the equivalent combination of experience and education in a related field.
  • Strong aptitude for numbers.
  • Extreme accuracy and a high attention to detail.
  • Microsoft Suite skills; note we use a custom accounting system so knowledge of other accounting systems is not required.
  • The ability to work in a fast-paced, busy, creative, open office area.

Salary to commensurate with experience and education level (in range of $23-$26 per hour).

Please provide the following to info@cta.bc.ca:

  • Cover Letter indicating interest in the position (PDF)
  • Resume (PDF)
  • Salary expectations
  • Current Work Visa status and dates of such or confirmation that you are a Canadian citizen or Permanent Resident

No phone calls, please. Only those applicants selected for an interview will be contacted.

Benefits:

  • Company events
  • Dental care
  • Extended health care
  • On-site gym
  • Paid time off
  • Vision care

  • French not required
