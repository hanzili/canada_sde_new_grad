Job Summary

The Team
The Biometric team is an established group of three professionals dedicated to maintaining and improving Variable Density Yield Projection system (VDYP7), a key tool for updating forest inventory and generating yield tables for Timber Supply Review. You'll work closely with a Forest Mensurationist and contribute to impactful projects that shape sustainable forestry management. We value creativity and attention to detail, and we're excited to welcome someone who shares our passion for innovative, high-quality work.

The Role
As a Forest Inventory Data Analyst, you'll be responsible for testing the newly rewritten VDYP7, helping to improve the accuracy of its projections. This is an exciting opportunity to deepen your understanding of the provincial stand-level model while enhancing your analytical skills. Your work will have a direct impact on ensuring reliable data for sustainable forest management in British Columbia.

Qualifications:
Education and Experience Requirements

  • Currently completing graduate studies in Forestry, Natural Resources Management or Computer Science.
  • Currently registered in a recognized co-op program at a participating post-secondary institution.

Preference may be given to applicants with:

  • Experience using R or SAS programming.
  • Experience in data compilation.

For questions regarding this position, please contact Yong.Luo@gov.bc.ca.
