About the Role

About Capcrete

Hands-on R&D in coatings and polymers

Full Description

Role Overview:
We are seeking an Entry-Level Industrial Chemist with a passion for coatings and polymer formulations to join our R&D and product development team. This role is ideal for recent graduates or early-career chemists looking to gain hands-on experience in specialty materials research.

Key Responsibilities:

  • Assist in the development and testing of coatings and cement polymer products.
  • Support R&D initiatives and product optimization projects.
  • Conduct lab experiments, analyze results, and prepare technical reports.
  • Collaborate with the team to ensure product quality, performance, and compliance.
  • Participate in problem-solving and formulation improvement activities.

Qualifications:

  • Bachelor’s degree in Chemistry, Materials Science, or Chemical Engineering.
  • Basic knowledge of polymers, coatings, and cement-based materials.
  • Strong analytical, laboratory, and documentation skills.
  • Enthusiasm for research, product development, and innovation.
  • Excellent teamwork and communication abilities.

What We Offer:

  • Hands-on experience in specialty R&D roles in coatings and polymer formulations.
  • Opportunity to grow into senior R&D or product development positions.
  • Supportive team environment with mentorship from experienced chemists.
  • Competitive entry-level salary with potential for performance-based growth.

How to Apply:
Interested candidates should submit their resume and a brief cover letter outlining their interest to info@cap-chem.com

Salary : Forty – Fifty thousand per year

What You’ll Do:

  • Assist in formulation, testing, and optimization of coatings and cement-based polymers.
  • Conduct lab experiments and analyze results.
  • Collaborate with a dynamic team to improve product performance.

What We’re Looking For:

  • Bachelor’s in Chemistry, Materials Science, or Chemical Engineering.
  • Interest in R&D, coatings, and polymers.
  • Strong lab, analytical, and teamwork skills.

Why Join Us:

  • Hands-on experience in specialty product development.
  • Clear growth path into senior R&D roles.
