About the Role

The Junior Accountant supports the Finance team in the day-to-day processing of accounts payable and accounts receivable, as well as month-end close activities for ZoomerMedia and its operating entities.

What You'll Do

Accounts Receivable

  • Review incoming cheques for completeness and accuracy and complete daily deposits to the office scanner at least twice weekly.
  • Apply payments — cheque, cash, e-transfer, EFT, credit card, and inter-company — to customer accounts in WideOrbit, ensuring correct invoice number, date, customer, and amount.
  • Process adjustments, NSFs, returned cheques, and credit card chargebacks across MZMedia and ZoomerMedia accounts.
  • Review daily bank deposits for EFT, wire, and e-transfer activity and reconcile to the AR sub-ledger.
  • Generate and send monthly invoices and customer statements.
  • Respond to customer and agency emails on invoicing and payment queries; escalate unresolved items to the Collections Manager.
  • Provide collections support on an as-required basis (first-touch reminders, account research) under the Collections Manager's direction.

Accounts Payable

  • Review vendor invoices for appropriate documentation, coding, and approval before posting.
  • Perform invoice and general-ledger data entry; obtain approvals; enter into the system; and process payments.
  • Obtain and print signatures on cheques and prepare EFT/wire payment runs.
  • Perform credit card and bank account reconciliations.
  • Resolve vendor inquiries and reconcile vendor statements as needed.

Month-End Close

  • Prepare and post month-end journal entries.
  • Reconcile AR, AP, bank (CAD and USD), prepaid expenses, capital assets, accrued liabilities, and intercompany balances.
  • Assist with the preparation of monthly financial schedules in accordance with the Financial Statement Close Process and timeline.
  • Provide supporting documentation and schedules to the Senior Accountant and Controller as requested.

General

  • Cross-cover the other Accountants during absences and at month-end peak.
  • Maintain organized, audit-ready documentation for all transactions and reconciliations.
  • Perform other related duties as required.

What You Have

  • Post-secondary diploma or degree in accounting, finance, or a related field.
  • One to three years of relevant AP and/or AR experience, including invoice posting, payment application, and account reconciliation.
  • Enrolment in the CPA program is an asset.
  • Working knowledge of an ERP system (Microsoft Dynamics GP / Great Plains) and a billing/traffic system (WideOrbit) is an asset; willingness to learn both is required.
  • Solid Excel skills (lookups, pivots, basic formulas).
  • Strong attention to detail and accuracy.
  • Analytical and problem-solving ability; comfortable researching and resolving discrepancies.
  • Clear written and verbal communication; able to interact professionally with customers, vendors, and internal business leaders.
  • Self-starter who can work independently and as part of a team, and meet recurring close deadlines.
  • Discretion and integrity in handling financial information.

What We Offer

  • Flexible paid time off policy to ensure you're able to get the break you need and work at an optimized level.
  • Opportunity for both professional and personal growth.
  • Hybrid work environment.
  • Benefits package including dental, vision and extended health starting after 3 months.
