Full Description

Location – Abbotsford, British Columbia, Canada

JOB DESCRIPTION

About Wellness Extract
Wellness Extract is the #1 Vitamin E brand in North America and one of the fastest-growing supplement companies globally. With four international offices and a 40+ person marketing team, we are on a mission to make science-backed wellness accessible across the US, Canada, UK, Australia, and beyond. We are not just building a supplement brand — we are building a global lifestyle brand.

Our strategies include programmatic marketing campaigns, influencer partnerships, podcasts, PR, TikTok series, Instagram reels, LinkedIn thought leadership, and live events. We’re also integrating AI-driven marketing into everything we do.

We are looking for a creative, outgoing, and ambitious Marketing & Communications Associate to join our team and become one of the faces of our brand.

RESPONSIBILITIES

1. Brand Voice & Content Creation

  • Develop and publish content across TikTok, Instagram, YouTube Shorts, LinkedIn, and other platforms
  • Appear on camera for podcasts, reels, interviews, and event recaps
  • Shape how younger audiences perceive and experience the brand

2. Marketing, PR & Events

  • Identify and explore PR opportunities including podcasts, media coverage, and influencer collaborations
  • Assist CEO and COO in executing local events, brand activations, and thought leadership campaigns
  • Research and recommend new marketing opportunities within the health and wellness space

3. Project Coordination & AI Integration

  • Coordinate with the global marketing team to manage timelines and deliverables
  • Use project management tools such as Zoho Projects, Jira, and Microsoft Project to track tasks
  • Design workflows and mind maps in Miro to support campaigns and initiatives
  • Learn and implement AI tools and prompt engineering to enhance campaign effectiveness

QUALIFICATIONS

  • Outgoing, creative, and comfortable on social media
  • Confident on camera and able to represent the brand in public settings
  • Strong written communication skills (blogs, captions, scripts, posts)
  • Highly organized with the ability to manage multiple projects simultaneously
  • Eager to learn and apply marketing technologies, including AI
  • Interested in a fast-paced role with direct access to executive leadership

WHY JOIN US

  • Work with the leading Vitamin E brand in North America
  • Join a rapidly growing international wellness company
  • Gain hands-on experience in influencer marketing, AI-powered campaigns, public relations, and content strategy
  • Receive direct mentorship from the CEO and COO
  • Enjoy a creative, public-facing role with clear growth potential to full-time and leadership positions
