About the Role

About Asset Strategy Inc.

Regina-based AI-driven asset management firm

Full Description

Role Overview

We are seeking a detail-oriented Junior AI Data Technician for a 1-month evaluation placement. This role is foundational to our AI development; you will be responsible for "teaching" our models how to recognize infrastructure components and identify structural defects within massive image libraries.

Trial-to-Hire Structure

  • Evaluation Period: 1 month.
  • Compensation: $50/day flat rate.
  • Transition: Upon successful completion of the first month, high-performing candidates will be considered for a transition into a permanent paid part-time or full-time role, if they desire this, based on accuracy and project needs.

Key Responsibilities

  • Visual Data Analysis: Systematically review large-scale image libraries to identify and tag specific structural components.
  • Defect Classification: Examine images to detect and label specific conditions (e.g., corrosion, cracking, spalling, or mechanical wear) based on our engineering standards.
  • Quality Control: Maintain high "labeling integrity" to ensure the AI model doesn't learn incorrect patterns.

Qualifications & Skills

  • High Attention to Detail: You have a "detective’s eye" for noticing small structural inconsistencies or hairline cracks.
  • Technical Curiosity: An interest in how Computer Vision and Machine Learning are applied to real-world engineering and asset management.
  • Analytical Mindset: Ability to follow technical manuals to distinguish between different types of material degradation.
  • Reliability: Strong time-management skills and the ability to work consistently through high volumes of visual data.
  • Background: Ideally suited for students or recent graduates.

Why Join ASI Engineering?

  • Real-World AI Impact: See firsthand how raw data is transformed into a functional AI model for infrastructure inspection.
  • Professional Mentorship: Work alongside our engineering team to learn the standards of asset management.
  • Direct Career Path: We are looking for a long-term team member to grow with our AI division.

Job Type: Fixed term contract
Contract length: 1 month

Pay: From $50.00 per day

Work Location: In person
