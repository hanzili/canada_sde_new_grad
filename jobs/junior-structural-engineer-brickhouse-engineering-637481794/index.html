About the Role

Assist senior engineers with design, drafting, and reviews

About Brickhouse Engineering

Small Calgary structural firm with mentoring and growth

Full Description

Brickhouse is seeking an Junior Structural Engineer to join its team. Working in a small office environment, the successful candidate will participate in many new and ongoing projects from inception through completion, including all stages from proposal development to project close out.

Projects:

We handle a wide variety of projects, including car dealerships, commercial spaces, warehouses, lift stations, tenant fit-ups, concrete buildings, golf clubhouses, twenty-story concrete towers, cultural centers, multi-use developments, multi-family residential properties, child care facilities, pickleball centers, column removals, and much more.

What will you do?

· Assist the senior engineers and partners in all aspects of the project. Proposal preparation, design, drafting mark ups, drafting, shop drawing review, project administration, project close out, other day to day office item.

· Carry out structural calculations, simulations, and modeling for construction and renovations.

· Design with materials such as steel, concrete, masonry, cold-formed steel, and wood.

· Collaborate to create drawings, technical reports, and specifications.

· Review construction for compliance and conduct investigations for renovations.

· Coordinate with clients, architects, and contractors throughout all project phases.

Requirements

· We require 2 to 4 years of experience, preferably in a structural engineering consulting office.

· Demonstrated experience in analysis, concrete design, steel design, masonry design, steel stud design, and wood design.

· Possession of Engineer in Training status in the Province of Alberta.

· Thorough understanding of relevant building codes.

· Project management skills, with a history of delivering projects within established timelines and budgets.

· Proficiency in Microsoft Word, Excel, and structural analysis software such as S-frame, Structure Point Suite, Autocad, Revit.

· Good verbal and written communication abilities, with the capacity to collaborate effectively with builders, architects, engineers, and clients at all levels.

· Ability to efficiently manage multiple tasks, priorities, and deliverables on an ongoing basis.

· A portfolio showcasing work from previous projects that illustrate the required competencies.

Brickhouse

At Brickhouse, we provide a small office setting with flexible hours, telework, and an emphasis on work-life balance. Our family-oriented management encourages staff to prioritize family and achieve success in ways that suit their lifestyles. You'll handle diverse projects of all sizes, ensuring each day is different. We offer ongoing learning through mentoring, technical courses, peer discussions, and valuable partnerships with other firms for growth and experience.

Compensation, Benefits, & Total Rewards

We value the skills and commitment that fuel our success, and we demonstrate this through comprehensive rewards.

· Competitive base salary: Ranges from $65,000 to $85,000, determined by your experience, qualifications, and education.

· Winter and Summer Bonus: Annual discretionary bonuses are offered.

· Retirement: We provide RRSP matching up to 5% of your annual salary.

· Health & wellness: Enjoy employer-paid health insurance, a health spending account, and participation in our wellness program.

· Time off: Benefit from a Winter shutdown period and a vacation plan tailored to your experience level.
