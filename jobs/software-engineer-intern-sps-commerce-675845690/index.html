About the Role

Design, write, test, and ship production software

About SPS Commerce

Global cloud-based supply chain software provider

Full Description

Description SPS Commerce is a leading provider of cloud-based supply chain management solutions, serving a global network of retail trading partners. We foster a collaborative and inclusive work environment where innovation and continuous improvement are highly valued. Join SPS Commerce and be part of a dynamic team that's transforming the global retail supply chain!

Position Summary As a Software Engineering Intern at SPS Commerce, you’ll work alongside a collaborative engineering team to design, build, and deliver real software that ships to production. You won’t be assigned isolated “intern projects.” Instead, you’ll contribute directly to the same work as full-time engineers, gaining meaningful, hands-on experience while helping deliver features and improvements that impact customers.

In this role, you will partner closely with your team to understand requirements, design thoughtful solutions, and write high-quality code. You will participate in daily Scrum ceremonies to share progress, align on priorities, and plan your work. Throughout the term, you will develop, deploy, and verify your own code in production environments, embracing a DevOps mindset with ownership across the full lifecycle—from design through delivery and support. Along the way, you’ll gain exposure to the full technology stack, modern cloud-based tools, and engineering best practices that help you grow into a well-rounded developer.

We’re looking for someone who is curious, eager to learn, and excited to collaborate. You should be comfortable working in a team environment, open to feedback, and motivated to contribute ideas and explore new technologies. This is a great opportunity for students pursuing Computer Science, Software Engineering, or a related field who want practical experience building and shipping real software in a professional environment.

If you’re looking for a role where you can make an impact, learn quickly, and work on meaningful problems every day, you won’t be bored here.

Location:

This role is open to remote locations in Canada.

What We Offer At SPS Commerce, we are committed to ensuring that each employee's compensation reflects their unique experiences, performance, and skills in their role.

The hourly pay rate for this role is: $34.00 CAD/hr.

Commitment To Our Employees At SPS we power connections that drive the world of commerce forward, and our success depends on making strong decisions, fostering innovation, delivering unparalleled customer solutions, and driving outstanding business performance. We achieve this by creating an environment where every employee feels a true sense of belonging. We embrace diversity, equity, and inclusion, ensuring everyone feels accepted, valued, and empowered to make a meaningful impact.
