Full Description

JOB TITLE: Junior Business Analyst

ABOUT NORTH SUN ENERGY

North Sun Energy™ is a co-ownership between North Atlantic and Petro-Canada™ (the fuel retail arm of Suncor Energy™), overseeing 111 gas stations and convenience stores across Newfoundland and Labrador, Nova Scotia, and PEI. Customers enjoy top-tier loyalty rewards, including Petro-Points™ and Yay!™ Rewards, alongside their Orangestore™ convenience store brand, now located at 36 locations. North Sun Energy is committed to long term growth and delivering exceptional customer experiences, expanding its Orangestore™ brand across Nova Scotia and strengthening its network through strategic partnerships with leading quick-service restaurants like Little Caesars® and A&W™.

Position Overview

Reporting to the Business Analyst (BA) and working closely with the Chief Operations Officer, the Junior Analyst will support the development and maintenance of a variety of reports used to run the business, identify opportunities, and improve best practices.

The Junior Analyst will assist the BA in organizing and prioritizing initiatives, contributing to data collection, basic analysis, and routine reporting. The ideal candidate brings a positive, enthusiastic energy and a strong “can‑do” attitude. If you are motivated, eager to learn, and looking for an opportunity to grow within a diverse and expanding company, please submit your cover letter and resume to North Atlantic.

Duties and Responsibilities:

  • Assist in preparing routine reports for the management team (daily, weekly, monthly, quarterly)
  • Help maintain and track KPIs across various business areas
  • Gather and organize information from multiple internal data sources
  • Support basic data analysis to identify trends, issues, or opportunities
  • Assist with creating PowerPoint presentations that summarize findings
  • Help monitor, track, and update fuel and c‑store inventory reporting
  • Assist in preparing c-store gross margin and royalty summaries
  • Support forecasting activities under the direction of senior analysts
  • Conduct data validation to ensure accuracy and reliability of reporting
  • Perform general administrative and analytical support tasks as required
  • Other duties as required

Qualifications:

  • University Degree or College Diploma in Business, Accounting, Economics, Finance, or a related field (or equivalent combination of education and experience)
  • 1-2 years of experience in a business, accounting, or analytical role
  • Proficient with Microsoft Excel, Word, and PowerPoint
  • Working knowledge of Power BI and T-SQL is an asset
  • Strong attention to detail and accuracy
  • Positive attitude with a willingness to learn and take initiative
  • Ability to work both independently and collaboratively
  • Strong communication and presentation skills
  • Demonstrated discretion and professionalism when handling confidential information
  • Experience with business analysis and report writing is required

North Sun Energy offers a dynamic, fulfilling, and high energy environment where each day brings new challenges and opportunities for growth. We take teamwork seriously and we'll make you feel right at home. We are committed to Employment Equity, accommodations during the recruitment process are available upon request for candidates with disabilities.

We offer a competitive compensation and benefits package which include Health and Dental, Wellness Program • Short-Term and Long-Term Disability • Life Insurance • Company Pension Plan with employer contribution• Employee and Family Assistance Program• Free on-site parking • Employee engagement activities

If you are a highly motivated team player, have the qualifications, and are prepared to meet the challenges as outlined, apply by submitting your resume and cover letter to careers@northsunenergy.ca
