Finance Intern (Cost Accounting) (Fall 2026)

at Soucy Group

📍 Location Drummondville, QC J2E 1A9
📋 Details Internship · Sep 8 - Dec 18, 2026 · Attractive salary · Fall 2026
📅 Posted Apr 30, 2026

About the Role

Assist inventory costing and month-end accounting tasks

About Soucy Group

Private manufacturer of specialized rubber tracks.

Full Description

At Soucy, we believe in the leverage effect of a great internship experience on the start of a career. That's why, every year, we open our doors to dozens of interns in all fields so they can venture into the world of work with us and explore their potential.

Benefits designed to take you far!

  • Attractive salary (you can pay off your student loans or fund your next trip—the choice is yours!)
  • “My Flexibility” program (That’s right, you can finish at noon on Fridays to enjoy your favorite activities)
  • Real projects tailored to your strengths and interests (We want your internship to meet YOUR expectations!)
  • A team of experts at your disposal (The ideal environment for learning!)

What does a fun internship look like?

Are you looking for an environment where every contribution counts and where your expertise is put to good use to tackle major challenges? At Soucy, we value authenticity and commitment on a daily basis. As part of our finance team, you will participate in the management of our financial activities. Here, every day is an opportunity to collaborate, analyze, and innovate, while contributing to an environment where your voice is not only heard, but also encouraged. We are ready to welcome you for an internship experience off the beaten path, where your talent and rigor will make a difference!

In this internship, you will have the opportunity to:

  • Participate in inventory management.
  • Actively participate in the annual/semi-annual price list review process.
  • Open and track costs for various development projects
  • Actively participate in the month-end process
  • Track supplier costs
  • Analyze and monitor ongoing production jobs
  • Perform any other tasks requested by your immediate supervisor

Is this the internship you've been waiting for? Apply now.

With the following skills and qualities, you will be successful:

  • Bachelor's degree in accounting or finance (in progress)
  • Good knowledge of MS Office software, mainly Excel
  • Ability to perform well in an environment where meeting deadlines is essential
  • Good judgment and rigor in performing work
  • Interpersonal skills and good team spirit

Ready to join us? We are waiting for you for an internship that will run from September 8 to December 18, 2026. The schedule is Monday through Friday, 40 hours per week.

We sincerely want to learn more about you!

Life at Soucy Rubber Tracks

Working at Soucy Rubber Tracks means designing and manufacturing a unique yet complex product: Soucy rubber tracks.

We rely on big machines and advanced technology to carry out our activities. But our greatest asset is our people. People who push the limits of tech to design track systems that can brave even the harshest of terrains.

Our 300 employees are a tight-knit group who value collaboration. At Soucy, you'll be able to see the impact of your work and you’ll share in the company's success.
