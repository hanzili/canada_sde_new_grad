Full Description

Job Overview

We are seeking a proactive and detail-oriented Junior Marketing Associate to join our growing marketing team. This paid position offers an excellent opportunity to gain hands-on experience across digital marketing, content development, and campaign coordination.

The successful candidate will support the execution of marketing strategies, contribute to brand awareness initiatives, and assist with analysing campaign performance. This role is ideal for an early-career professional or recent graduate eager to develop their skills within a collaborative and fast-paced environment.

Key Responsibilities

  • Assist in planning and executing marketing campaigns across digital channels, including social media, email marketing, and B2B platforms
  • Manage social media accounts by creating content, scheduling posts, and monitoring engagement metrics
  • Conduct keyword research and support SEO optimisation efforts
  • Update website content using WordPress and assist with basic visual edits using Adobe Photoshop
  • Coordinate marketing projects and liaise with internal teams to ensure deadlines are met
  • Draft and edit marketing copy for blogs, newsletters, advertisements, and promotional materials
  • Monitor campaign performance using Google Analytics and prepare performance summaries
  • Assist with managing Google Ads campaigns and tracking key performance indicators
  • Maintain marketing databases and reporting documents using Microsoft Excel and PowerPoint
  • Support B2B marketing initiatives by preparing presentations and proposal materials

Qualifications

  • Post-secondary education in Marketing, Business, Communications, or a related field is preferred
  • Strong written and verbal communication skills in English
  • Proficiency in Microsoft Office (Word, Excel, PowerPoint)
  • Familiarity with WordPress, Adobe Photoshop, and email marketing platforms
  • Basic understanding of SEO principles and digital marketing best practices
  • Experience with Google Analytics and Google Ads is an asset
  • Strong organisational and time-management skills
  • Analytical mindset with attention to detail
  • Ability to manage multiple projects and work collaboratively within a team
  • Understanding of B2B marketing concepts is desirable

What We Offer

  • Competitive entry-level salary
  • Supportive and team-oriented work environment
  • Hands-on experience across multiple digital marketing channels
  • Opportunities for professional development and career progression
