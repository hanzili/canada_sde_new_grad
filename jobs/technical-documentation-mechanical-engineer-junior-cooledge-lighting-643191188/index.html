About the Role

Create drawings; prototype testing; document processes.

About Cooledge Lighting

Lighting solutions company; strong early-career program.

Full Description

Overview:

This role combines technical documentation ownership with hands-on mechanical engineering support. The primary focus of this position is the creation, improvement, and long-term maintenance of global technical documentation. This position reports to the Mechanical Lead and works closely with Marketing and the Process & Quality team.

Responsibilities – technical Documentation:

· Create and maintain installation manuals

· Develop and maintain website technical content

· Create and update sales technical guides

· Generate drawings, diagrams and schematics

· Assist in creation of installation and training videos

· Manage document revisioning and audits

· Support document control and revision processes

· Build documentation templates and standards

· Maintain the technical documentation database

Responsibilities – Mechanical Engineering:

· Create manufacturing drawings in SolidWorks and AutoCAD

· Support maintenance-of-line product updates

· Build and test prototypes in workshop

· Provide 3D models and visuals for documentation

· Support Mechanical Engineering team overflow

Success After 12 Months

The successful candidate will have spearheaded the creation of a complete installation manual from concept to release and established documentation processes.

Requirements

  • Degree or Diploma in Mechanical Engineering or related field
  • Early career engineering experience
  • Strong written and visual communication skills
  • Proficiency in SolidWorks and AutoCAD
  • Microsoft Office proficiency
  • Strong attention to detail and organization

Assets

  • Adobe InDesign / Creative Suite

  • Lighting or architectural products experience

Growth Opportunity

Growth path toward Engineering, Product Management or Process & Quality roles.
