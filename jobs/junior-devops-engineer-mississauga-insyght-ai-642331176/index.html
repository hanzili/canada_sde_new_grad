Company Description

InsyghtAI is an IT and business process outsourcing provider based in Oakville, ON. We offer tailored services and solutions for each customer, ensuring that all individuals are in the right place and know their job. Our team focuses on analyzing business needs and developing cost-efficient solutions to help our customers excel in their field of expertise.

Role Description

This is a full-time on-site role for a Junior DevOps Engineer at InsyghtAI Inc. The Junior DevOps Engineer will be responsible for tasks such as Infrastructure as Code (IaC), software development, continuous integration, system administration, and working with Linux systems on a day-to-day basis.

Qualifications

  • AWS Cloud and EKS
  • Software Development and Continuous Integration skills
  • Strong Experience with Linux systems
  • Strong problem-solving and analytical skills
  • Excellent communication and collaboration abilities
  • Ability to work well in a team environment
  • Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Engineering, or related field

MUST HAVE:

  1. At least 1 AWS certification
  2. Hands on experience with Kubernetes and Terraform
