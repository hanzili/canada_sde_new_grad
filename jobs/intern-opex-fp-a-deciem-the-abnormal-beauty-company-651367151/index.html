Intern, OPEX - FP&A

at DECIEM | THE ABNORMAL BEAUTY COMPANY

About the Role

Support FP&A: budgeting, forecasting, reporting

About DECIEM | THE ABNORMAL BEAUTY COMPANY

Global beauty brand; strong growth, supportive culture

Full Description

WHAT ON EARTH IS DECIEM? We are a collective of disrupters and doers, of creatives and scientists, with a mission to reimagine the world of beauty.

Since inception, our vertically integrated structure, including our own laboratories, manufacturing, retail stores, and brand infrastructure, have enabled us to rapidly identify opportunities and deliver quality. Home to The Ordinary, NIOD, AVESTAN, and other emerging brands, our goal is to create with purpose. We were founded on the principle of doing everything that others don’t. Because normal gets you nowhere.

We are DECIEM, The Abnormal Beauty Company.

We are growing rapidly and we’re looking for someone who shares this vision and wants to grow with us!

WHAT WE'RE LOOKING FOR We are seeking a detail-oriented and motivated Intern, OPEX – FP&A to join our Financial Planning & Analysis team. This role will support the operating expense (OPEX) management processes and provide hands-on experience in financial reporting, planning, and analysis in our Toronto , Fraser Location.

PAY RANGE The anticipated base salary for this position is $22/hour

Exact salary depends on several factors such as experience, skills, education, and budget. Salary range may vary based on geographic location.

WHAT YOU'D BE DOING IN THIS ROLE

  • Support the FP&A team in planning and tracking operating expenses by preparing regular financial reports and reconciling data for accuracy.
  • Assist in maintaining and enhancing financial templates and simple models, helping to streamline forecasting and budgeting activities.
  • Contribute to the budget and forecast cycles by coordinating the collection of inputs from various departments, ensuring timely and complete submissions.
  • Apply foundational knowledge of finance and accounting to support data entry, analysis, and reporting.
  • Collaborate with team members across finance and operational departments to gather and validate data used for planning and reporting.
  • Utilize Microsoft Excel to manipulate, analyze, and present financial data, including the use of formulas, pivot tables, and charts.
  • Learn and follow internal processes for budgeting and forecasting, with exposure to basic financial systems or templates used by the FP&A team.
  • Report to a designated FP&A Manager or Senior Analyst, receiving regular feedback and guidance on tasks and deliverables.
  • Work under close supervision, with the opportunity to build independence over time as familiarity with processes grows.
  • Participate in team meetings and finance check-ins to stay informed on priorities and contribute updates on assigned responsibilities.

SKILLS AND QUALIFICATIONS NEEDED TO GET THE JOB DONE

  • Strong attention to detail and comfort working with numerical data.
  • Proficiency in Microsoft Excel, with familiarity in formulas, pivot tables, and basic data visualization.
  • communication skills and the ability to collaborate in a team-oriented environment.
  • proactive, organized, and eager-to-learn mindset with a willingness to ask questions and take initiative.
