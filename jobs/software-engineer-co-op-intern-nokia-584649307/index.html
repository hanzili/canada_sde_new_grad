Job Description Position: Software Engineer Co-op/Intern

Number of Position(s): 1

Duration: 4 Months

Date: May to August, 2026

EDUCATIONAL RECOMMENDATIONS Currently a candidate for a Master’s, bachelor's degree or Diploma in Computer Science, Computer Systems Engineering, Software Engineering or a related field with an accredited school in Canada.

How You Will Contribute And What You Will Learn As part of our team, you will: Collaborate with software designers and testers to refine requirements, develop, and test product features.

Investigate and fix product defects.

Enhance automated test suites.

Key Skills And Experience

  • Solid software engineering fundamentals (data structures, OOP, etc)
  • Programming skills (Java, Python, React, Go)
  • Experience with docker, k8s and rest APIs.
  • Strong problem-solving abilities
  • Strong teamwork and communication skills

It would be nice if you also had:

  • Experience with CI/CD tools: Jenkins, Gitlab.
  • Understanding of the cybersecurity landscape, and the concepts of red/blue team operations
