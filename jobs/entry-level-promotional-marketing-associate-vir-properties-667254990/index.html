About the Role

Execute promotional campaigns and engage customers

About VIR Properties

Brand activations and client engagement at events

Full Description

We are looking for an enthusiastic and motivated Entry-Level Promotional Marketing Associate to join our growing marketing team. This is an excellent opportunity for someone at the start of their career who enjoys working with people and wants to gain hands-on experience in promotional campaigns, brand activations, and customer engagement.

You will support promotional marketing initiatives, represent client brands, and help drive brand awareness and customer acquisition.

Key Responsibilities

  • Assist in the execution of promotional marketing campaigns
  • Represent client brands at events, activations, and marketing locations
  • Engage with customers and communicate key brand messaging
  • Support lead generation and customer acquisition activities
  • Help set up promotional displays and campaign materials
  • Gather customer feedback and campaign insights
  • Collaborate with the team to achieve campaign objectives

What We’re Looking For

  • Strong communication and interpersonal skills
  • Outgoing and confident personality
  • Positive attitude and willingness to learn
  • Customer-focused mindset
  • Ability to work in a fast-paced, team-oriented environment
  • No prior experience required – full training provided

What We Offer

  • Full training and ongoing mentorship
  • Supportive and energetic team environment
  • Clear progression opportunities within the company
  • Hands-on experience in promotional marketing campaigns
  • Opportunity to work with a variety of brands and clients

Ideal Candidate This Role Is Perfect For Someone Who Is

  • Starting their career in marketing or promotions
  • Confident engaging with customers face-to-face
  • Energetic, motivated, and people-oriented
  • Interested in long-term career development
