About the Role

Assist design, drafting, and site inspections

About Alpha Engineering

Local civil firm, mentorship and growth

Full Description

Responsibilities:
- Assist senior engineers in the design and development of civil engineering projects
- Conduct research and gather data to support project planning and decision-making
- Prepare and review technical drawings, schematics, and specifications using Civil 3D software
- Collaborate with project teams to ensure timely completion of deliverables
- Perform construction inspections to ensure compliance with design specifications
- Assist in the management of project schedules, budgets, and resources
- Conduct site visits to assess project progress and identify potential issues

Experience:
- Bachelor's degree in Civil Engineering
- Experience and proficiency with drafting software, such as AutoCAD or Civil 3D, for creating technical drawings
- Familiarity with project management methodologies and tools
- Strong analytical and problem-solving skills
- Excellent communication and teamwork abilities

Note: This is a junior-level position, so candidates with limited work experience are encouraged to apply. We provide mentorship and opportunities for professional growth within our organization.
