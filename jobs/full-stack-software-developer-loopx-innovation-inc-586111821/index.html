Company Introduction

LoopX, founded in 2022, is headquartered in Waterloo and Greater Sudbury, Ontario. We specialize in AI-driven autonomous systems for mining equipment, serving:

  • Mining Operators
  • Mining Contractors
  • Mining Equipment Manufacturers

We actively collaborate with institutions such as NORCAT and Waterloo Innovation Park on real-world pilot programs.Core Product Lines

  • Situational Awareness System (SAS):

Camera + LiDAR + IMU for real-time perception and collision warning

  • Autopilot Control System (ACS):

Automated control such as emergency braking

  • Autonomous Operation System (AOS):

Fully autonomous navigation and task execution

  • Safe & Smart Mining Platform (SMP):

Vision + LLM-based analysis for safety and productivity insights
