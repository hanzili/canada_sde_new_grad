Full Description

Job Summary:

We are currently looking for an Electrical Engineer / Electrical Designer to join our Electrical Engineering Group. This is an in-office role based out of Burlington, ON. In this role you’ll be responsible for designing and developing electrical power and control systems for refrigeration and cooling industry. The successful candidates will be hands-on, electrically and mechanically inclined with a degree in Electrical Engineering or Mechatronics with some electrical engineering experience.

Role Responsibilities

  • Participate in kick off meetings to determine project scope, requirements, deliverables, budget and schedules
  • Design and develop electrical circuits for motor controls using MCC’s, VFD’s and Soft Starters
  • Perform calculations and size electrical components using Canadian and American standards and codes
  • Design control circuits for temperature and pressure controls using programmable controllers and relay logic
  • Prepare electrical drawings and panel layouts using AutoCAD Electrical
  • Coordinate and integrate starter panels and MCC’S design with PLC’s panels
  • Prepare bills of material and issue material requisitions to purchasing department using SAP software
  • Provide technical support to Panel Shop and electricians during manufacturing and testing process
  • Assist Service Department with remote troubleshooting and equipment commissioning when required
  • Liaise with project engineering and mechanical department, customers, and suppliers
  • Participate in Continuous Improvement /Lean Manufacturing meetings

Required Professional Knowledge, Skills & Qualifications

  • Post-Secondary Degree in Electrical Engineering or Mechatronics
  • Experience with electrical engineering design process for manufacturing
  • Proficiency in AutoCAD Electrical
  • Knowledge and experience of CSA & UL Standards and control panels certification process
  • Good knowledge of AC motors protection and controls, VFD’s, Soft Starters, PLC’s
  • Good knowledge of electrical and electronic instrumentation for process control
  • Knowledge of control strategies for refrigeration equipment and systems would be an asset
  • Familiarity with SAP software would be an asset
  • Experience with control panels manufacturing processes
  • Good team player, ability to work independently and accept responsibilities
  • Ability to schedule work, deal with conflicts and meet deadlines
  • Ability to document engineering designs, prepare record files
  • Ability to troubleshoot electrical problems
  • Ability to coordinate with panel shop personnel & electricians to ensure successful project completion

We thank all applicants for their interest, however, only those selected for an interview will be contacted.

Company Profile

CIMCO specializes in the engineering, design, manufacture, installation and service of complex thermal building solutions for industrial, process cooling and recreational refrigeration and mechanical systems. For more than 100 years, CIMCO’s leadership and unparalleled research, development and manufacturing programs has put us at the forefront of industry advancements and technological breakthroughs. Our professional engineers, technicians and designers apply innovative state-of-the-art equipment and refrigerants to develop systems that meet even the most complex thermal challenges.

We offer our employees excellent compensation, medical and dental benefit programs, retirement savings options, career development opportunities, mentoring and continuing education.

CIMCO is committed to creating an inclusive environment where people from all backgrounds can thrive. Together, we embrace diversity of thought, experience, and perspective, as we combine our unique skills and capabilities to drive successful outcomes for all stakeholders.
