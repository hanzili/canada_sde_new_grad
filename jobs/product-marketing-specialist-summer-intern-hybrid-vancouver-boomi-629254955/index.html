AI Product Marketing Intern **Location: Vancouver, BC

Worktype: Hybrid - 3 days a week in office, 2 days at home.

Duration: 4 months (Full-Time), May 11, 2026 - Aug 28, 2026 The impact you’ll make:** As a Boomi AI Product Marketing Intern, you will support the product marketing team in planning and executing marketing strategies for the Boomi. You will have the opportunity to gain hands-on experience in market research, content creation, campaign planning, and competitive analysis. This role is ideal for someone passionate about technology, marketing, and eager to learn about exciting topics like agentic AI, AI governance, data, integration, and much more!

What you’ll do:

  • Support product and solution messaging by helping develop content used across web, sales enablement, events, and campaigns
  • Conduct competitive analysis to identify opportunities for growth and differentiation.
  • Contribute to product releases by supporting messaging alignment, asset creation, and cross-functional coordination
  • Collaborate with Digital and Field teams to understand performance metrics, learn how PMM informs demand programs, and surface insights
  • Collaborate with team members to brainstorm new ideas and initiatives to enhance the company's marketing efforts.
  • Track industry, AI, and enterprise software trends to help inform messaging and point of view development

What you’ll need:

  • Demonstrated curiosity, ownership, and willingness to learn quickly in a fast-paced environment
  • Creative thinking and a passion for technology are essential. A working knowledge of the enterprise software market is a plus.
  • Excellent written and verbal communication skills.
  • Ability to self-learn quickly, ask questions and operate with autonomy within a team environment.
  • Proficient graphic design skills to create visually appealing brochures, presentations, and other materials. Video editing skills are a plus (e.g., Google Slides, Canva, Adobe).
  • Currently enrolled in a bachelor’s degree program, preferably in Marketing, Business, Computer Science or a related field.
  • Availability to commit to 2-3 days per week in person in Boomi’s Vancouver office for the duration of the internship period.

You can look forward to:

  • Hands-on experience in product marketing for a market leading software company.
  • Developing a fundamental understanding of Boomi’s agentic AI, governance, and data solution offerings and their impact on businesses around the world.
  • Exposure to cross-functional teams, including product management, sales, marketing, and an opportunity to work with creative agencies.
  • Opportunity to contribute to real-world projects and initiatives.
  • Vibrant community activities and snacks in the office kitchen.

Boomi is committed to fair and equitable compensation practices. The hourly rate of pay will range from CAD $17.40 - CAD $23 / Hour. Final compensation will be determined by various factors including the candidate’s knowledge, skills, experience, and geographic location.
