Junior Data Engineer

About Skip

Full Description

Ready for a challenge?

Then Just Eat Takeaway.com might be the place for you. We’re a leading global online food delivery platform, and our vision is to empower everyday convenience.

Whether it’s a Friday-night feast, a post-gym poke bowl, or grabbing some groceries, our tech platform connects tens of millions of customers with hundreds of thousands of restaurant, grocery and convenience partners across the globe.

About This Role As a Data Engineer at Just Eat Takeaway.com, you will be instrumental in advancing our data-driven initiatives for the Logistics organisation. In this role, you'll focus on building out Data & Analytics solutions alongside Data Scientists, Business Analysts and other Data Engineers, ensuring efficient data management and contributing significantly to our e-commerce success. You will play a key role in enhancing our data analytics infrastructure, directly impacting decision-making and customer satisfaction.

Location: Remote Canada Reporting to: Team Lead - Logistics Data Engineering These are some of the key ingredients to the role:

  • Leading, driving and supporting the delivery of global logistics tech infrastructure initiatives with data engineering, in close cooperation with Product, Operations, and other departments within JET
  • Establishing cross functional collaboration between different teams
  • Making sure Logistics Engineering and other global teams understand local business needs, the required improvements, and the priorities for the systems for which you function as a subject matter expert
  • Providing in-depth analysis of our network of logistic systems to equip our stakeholders with the right insights in their decision making process
  • Organisational learning on Logistics innovation, in and outside your subject matter expertise, to help form a holistic vision on how Logistics can contribute to JET’s mission
  • Supporting Operations, Product and other global teams on projects, questions and improvements in relevant tech domains

What will you bring to the table?

  • Prior experience in a Data Engineering position
  • Advanced skills in SQL and experience with relational databases; desired proficiency in data transformation tools like dbt
  • Familiarity with Python or similar programming languages
  • Experience with cloud platforms (preferably GCP)
  • Excellent interpersonal and communication skills, capable of working effectively in a dynamic team environment and interacting with various stakeholders

At JET, This Is On The Menu Our teams forge connections internally and work with some of the best-known brands on the planet, giving us truly international impact in a dynamic environment.

Fun, fast-paced and supportive, the JET culture is about movement, growth and about celebrating every aspect of our JETers. Thanks to them we stay one step ahead of the competition.

Inclusion, Diversity & Belonging

No matter who you are, what you look like, who you love, or where you are from, you can find your place at Just Eat Takeaway.com. We’re committed to creating an inclusive culture, encouraging diversity of people and thinking, in which all employees feel they truly belong and can bring their most colourful selves to work every day.

What else is cooking?

Want to know more about our JETers, culture or company? Have a look at our career site where you can find people's stories, blogs, podcasts and more JET morsels.
