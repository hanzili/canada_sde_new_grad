About TYM Consulting

Full Description

Overview
About the roleWe are a growing fractional CFO and accounting services firm seeking a detail-oriented Bookkeeper / Junior Accountant to join our team. You will handle day-to-day accounting operations for a portfolio of clients, working closely with our Controller and senior finance leadership to ensure accurate, audit-ready books and timely compliance deliverables.

Key responsibilities

  • Process accounts payable, accounts receivable, and general ledger entries on a daily basis
  • Post daily cash transactions and import bank data into accounting software
  • Prepare monthly bank and account reconciliations
  • Process payroll and remit payroll source deductions to CRA on schedule
  • Prepare and file HST returns in compliance with CRA deadlines
  • Prepare T4 slips and year-end payroll summaries
  • Assist with monthly close procedures, including posting adjusting journal entries
  • Maintain and update rolling cash flow reports and flag liquidity concerns
  • Support preparation of financial statements under ASPE
  • Organize documentation to support external audits and auditor requests
  • Follow and help maintain internal controls, approval workflows, and filing standards

Qualifications

  • 2–4 years of bookkeeping or accounting experience (public practice or multi-client experience is an asset)
  • Diploma or degree in Accounting, Business, or a related field; CPA pursuit is a plus
  • Proficiency in QuickBooks Online and/or Xero
  • Solid understanding of Canadian payroll rules, HST, and CRA filing requirements
  • Working knowledge of ASPE financial reporting standards
  • High attention to detail and strong organizational skills
  • Ability to manage multiple client files simultaneously with professionalism

Pay: $43,604.34-$55,000.00 per year

Application question(s):

  • This position requires daily on-site work at our office located in Vaughan, ON (L4K 4J3). Are you able to reliably commute to this location every day?

Experience:

  • bookkeeping or full-cycle accounting: 1 year (preferred)
  • QuickBooks Online: 1 year (preferred)

Work Location: In person
