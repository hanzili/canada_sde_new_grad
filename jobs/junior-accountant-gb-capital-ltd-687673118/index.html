Role Description - Junior Accountant

This contract Junior Accountant role supports the finance team across core accounting operations, month-end close support, reconciliations, and reporting preparation. Working closely with the Senior Accountant and Controller, you’ll help ensure accurate financial records, timely processing of payables and receivables, and well-organized supporting documentation. This role is ideal for someone early in their accounting career who is looking to build hands-on experience in a fast-paced, professional finance environment.

Key Responsibilities

Financial Reporting & Month-End Close

  • Assist in preparing and posting basic journal entries and routine adjustments
  • Perform routine reconciliations for cash, accounts receivable, and accounts payable
  • Support month-end close by gathering, organizing, and validating financial data
  • Help prepare internal financial reports under supervision

Accounts Payable & Accounts Receivable

  • Review and process vendor invoices for accuracy, coding, and documentation
  • Assist with customer invoicing and follow up on outstanding balances as needed
  • Maintain accurate and up-to-date vendor and customer records

General Ledger & Audit Support

  • Ensure general ledger entries are accurate and supported with proper documentation
  • Prepare basic supporting schedules for audit purposes
  • Respond to straightforward audit requests and follow internal control procedures
  • Assist with compliance and adherence to applicable accounting standards

Financial Analysis & Management Reporting

  • Assist in preparing variance reports comparing actual results to budget
  • Compile data for management reporting and key performance metrics
  • Support cash flow tracking by updating short-term forecasts

Systems & Process Improvement

  • Learn and work within accounting systems such as QuickBooks Online and JobBOSS²
  • Help identify incremental process improvements to increase efficiency
  • Maintain organized digital files and records in accordance with company standards

Collaboration & Professionalism

  • Work closely with the Senior Accountant and Controller to complete assigned tasks
  • Communicate clearly regarding issues, risks, or delays in deliverables
  • Maintain confidentiality and uphold professional ethics at all times

Qualifications

  • Foundational knowledge of accounting principles and financial reporting
  • Experience (or strong exposure) to AP/AR processing, reconciliations, and journal entries
  • Strong attention to detail and ability to work accurately with numerical data
  • Organized, dependable, and able to manage priorities in a deadline-driven environment
  • Proficiency in Microsoft Excel and MS Office applications
  • Comfort learning new systems and working with digital documentation standards
  • Effective communication and ability to collaborate across a small finance team
  • Bachelor’s degree (or nearing completion) in Accounting, Finance, or a related field preferred
  • Knowledge of US GAAP accounting standards and tax basics is an asset
  • Prior internship or junior accounting experience is beneficial

Compensation & Schedule

This role is based on a standard 37.5-hour work week and is in-person/on-site during the probationary period. Following successful completion of the probation period, there may be an opportunity to work partially remote, subject to role requirements and performance.

The expected salary range is $50,000–$70,000 per year, commensurate with experience, qualifications, and overall fit for the role. Compensation will be discussed in more detail with shortlisted candidates.
