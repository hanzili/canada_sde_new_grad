Full Description

Job Summary

As a Digital Marketing Intern at Upzoids, you will support our marketing team in planning, executing, and measuring digital campaigns across our website, email, and social channels. You will help improve our digital presence through analytics, optimization, and ongoing experimentation, gaining hands-on experience with real campaigns in a Microsoft-focused consulting environment.

About Upzoids

Upzoids is a Microsoft solutions partner that helps organizations modernize their workflows and deliver services efficiently. We design and build practical, user-friendly solutions using Microsoft Dynamics 365, Power Platform, and other Microsoft technologies, always with our client's needs in mind. At Upzoids, we believe technology should feel intuitive, adaptable, and purposeful, and we build with the future in mind.

About the Role

Upzoids is looking for a Digital Marketing Intern who is analytical, curious, and proactive. This role is ideal for someone interested in how digital channels drive awareness, engagement, and growth. You will work closely with our marketing team to support digital campaigns, website performance, email marketing, and marketing analytics to help ensure our digital presence is thoughtful, effective, and continuously improving.

What You’ll Be Doing

  • Support and create digital marketing campaigns across website, email, and social channels
  • Assist with website updates, content optimization, and basic SEO initiatives
  • Help manage email marketing campaigns, including audience segmentation and performance tracking
  • Track, analyze, and report on marketing performance and engagement metrics
  • Research digital marketing trends, tools, and best practices
  • Assist with the execution of webinars, events, and digital promotions
  • Help improve and grow Upzoids’ digital reach and visibility

What We’re Looking For

  • Currently enrolled in a Marketing or related program (3rd–4th year preferred)
  • Strong writing and communication skills, both verbal and written
  • Strong analytical and problem-solving skills with attention to detail
  • Interest in digital marketing, analytics, and performance optimization
  • Comfortable working with data, dashboards, and learning new tools
  • Strong organizational skills with the ability to manage multiple tasks and deadlines
  • Familiarity with tools such as Google Analytics, CRM platforms, or marketing automation tools is an asset
  • Basic understanding of SEO, email marketing, or analytics is an asset
  • Previous marketing experience is an asset

How Success Will Be Measured

  • Support the successful execution of digital marketing campaigns across multiple channels
  • Provide accurate tracking, reporting, and insights on campaign performance
  • Demonstrate steady improvement in understanding digital marketing tools and analytics
  • Meet deadlines and manage digital marketing tasks with minimal supervision
  • Apply insights and feedback to optimize campaigns and digital experiences
  • Collaborate effectively with the team and incorporate feedback constructively
  • Show curiosity, initiative, and a willingness to learn and improve
