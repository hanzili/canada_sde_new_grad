About the Role

About VIR Properties

Full Description

As an Entry-level Marketing Assistant, you will support the planning and execution of marketing campaigns and promotional initiatives. You will work closely with the marketing team to help coordinate campaigns, assist with customer engagement activities, and ensure marketing operations run smoothly. This role is ideal for a motivated individual looking to begin their career in marketing within a fast-paced and dynamic environment.

Key Responsibilities

  • Assist with planning and executing marketing campaigns
  • Support brand awareness and customer acquisition initiatives
  • Engage with customers during promotional activities and campaigns
  • Conduct basic market research and competitor analysis
  • Maintain accurate records of campaign activities and performance
  • Assist with preparing marketing materials and campaign resources
  • Collaborate with team members to achieve campaign objectives
  • Contribute ideas to improve campaign effectiveness and engagement

What We’re Looking For

  • Strong communication and interpersonal skills
  • Organised with good time-management abilities
  • Positive, proactive, and eager-to-learn attitude
  • Ability to work effectively in a fast-paced team environment
  • Interest in marketing, branding, and business growth

What We Offer

  • Structured training and ongoing mentorship
  • Clear progression opportunities within marketing and management roles
  • Supportive and collaborative team culture
  • Hands-on experience working with live marketing campaigns and client accounts
  • Opportunities for professional and personal development

If you’re motivated, creative, and ready to start your marketing career within a dynamic marketing company, we’d love to hear from you. Apply today and take the first step towards building your future with us.
