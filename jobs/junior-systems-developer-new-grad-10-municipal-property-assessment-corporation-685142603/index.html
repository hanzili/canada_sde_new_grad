Description Do you want to be part of a team helping to strengthen every community across Ontario? Are you interested in seeing the importance of your work firsthand? Do you have a passion for big data and cloud computing – and know the benefit of using data to improve business practices?

Become a Junior Systems Developer (New Grad)(10) with MPAC The Municipal Property Assessment Corporation (MPAC) is made up of a team of experts who understand local communities and assess every property in Ontario. What we do provides the very foundation that municipalities use to base the property taxes needed to pay for the services we use every day.

Equity, Diversity, Inclusion and Anti-Racism MPAC is committed to Equity, Diversity, Inclusion and Anti-Racism. We are taking important steps towards ensuring that all voices are respected, valued and being heard. MPAC is dedicated to fostering an environment where employees can bring their full, unique and authentic selves and are inspired to do their best work.

Our Information Technology team works closely with each department across the business to design, build and deliver innovative solutions that help make MPAC an international leader in the property assessment industry. The way we think about technology is different, and our teams are proud to share their knowledge and contribute to meaningful, technology-enabled success stories that enhance operational intelligence and customer service delivery. We tackle complex problems collaboratively in an agile environment, and are committed to ensuring MPAC technology is consistently available, scalable, performance-based and secure. We’re a group of forward-thinking and talented professionals who stay ahead of the game by using open source technology, cutting-edge cloud computing, advanced analytics and predictive and machine learning models.

Job Description What makes you great for this role? You’ve recently completed a degree in Computer Science, Software Engineering, or a related field, and you’re ready to start building real-world solutions that make an impact.

In this role based in Pickering, Ontario, you’ll work at the intersection of modern software development and enterprise-scale systems, supporting the Information Technology branch in delivering new and enhanced systems and services.

Unlike many junior roles focused on a single stack or narrow scope, this opportunity gives you exposure across a broad range of technologies and challenges. You’ll gain hands-on experience with modern applications and cloud-based solutions, working with technologies such as Oracle DB, Docker, Python, .NET (C#), Node.js, and AWS Cloud services.

If this sounds like the right next step in your career, we’d love to hear from you—apply today!

We Have

  • A team of highly skilled, dedicated and collaborative staff
  • Leadership that supports you
  • An enterprise that embraces change

You Have

  • Recent graduate within the last year or enrolled in a Computer Science program or related field in IT with a focus on Programming and Systems Development
  • Knowledge of computer science, data structure, design frameworks, programming languages
  • The ability to write well documented and quality code, with the use of source version control
  • Front-end web development experience with Angular or ReactJS, JavaScript, TypeScript, HTML and CSS
  • Strong knowledge of Object-Oriented Programming (OOP) and Java, with working familiarity in PHP, Python, web services, AJAX, Hibernate, Visual Basic, and related technologies.
  • Ability to build dynamic single page applications
  • Open communication – an ability to clearly convey thoughts, both written and verbally and proactive in asking questions to seek clarification and understanding
  • Self-motivation, ability to manage competing priorities in a fast-paced environment and work with minimal supervision
  • A strong passion for technology by keeping up-to-date with trends and upcoming technological advancements

Nice To Have

  • An understanding of agile methodology

What You Will Do

  • Support staff within the Information Services department, by documenting simple business problems in draft and reviewing existing code, and related programs and/or process
  • Assist with systems analysis on the impact of proposed maintenance or feature enhancements requests
  • Assist with User Interface coding and debugging based on given technical requirements
  • In collaboration with IT staff, write high quality, well documented and tested code, based on inputs and direction from the Manager, Information Technology
  • Assist with the creation of simple prototypes and proof-of-concept efforts including mock demos, ensuring that quality attributes (performance, scalability, extensibility) are met
  • Participate and assist, when required, in the review of detailed designs to ensure that they meet functional and nonfunctional requirements and comply with architectural standards
