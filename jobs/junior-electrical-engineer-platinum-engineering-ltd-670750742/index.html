About the Role

About Platinum Engineering Ltd.

Calgary electrical firm focused on LRT and rail

Full Description

Platinum Engineering Ltd (PEL) is a vibrant, independent, electrical engineering firm located in Calgary, Alberta. With a strong reputation for delivering innovative design, project management, and construction solutions, we serve the Light Rail Transit (LRT), Rail Systems, Industrial and Utility Sectors. We’re on the lookout for a talented engineer to join our dynamic Electrical Engineering Team as we take on exciting new engineering projects and advance existing ones. This is a great opportunity where you’ll have the opportunity to shape the future of transportation and energy infrastructure. From designing catenary plans and profile diagrams to creating support structures such as poles, cantilevers, and foundations, you’ll be at the heart of impactful projects that make a difference.

We are looking for new graduates and motivated engineers registered with APEGA with Co-op experience, or up to 5 years of experience, who is passionate about collaborating with engineers, technical teams, consultants and contractors. An ideal candidate will contribute taking projects from conceptual design through to construction completion while learning from seasoned industry professionals.

This is your chance to make your mark in a high-energy, fast-paced environment where your skills will directly impact transformative projects. If you're ready to elevate your career and work with a passionate team dedicated to engineering excellence, we want to hear from you!

Responsibilities:

As a key member of our team, you’ll take ownership of impactful projects and tasks, ensuring timely and quality delivery. You’ll collaborate with a talented engineering team and contribute to shaping the future of infrastructure and technology. Here’s what you’ll be diving into:

  • Drive Project Success: Help execute the development, management, and coordination of project scopes, deliverables, and designs to meet schedules.
  • Innovate Through Design: Create detailed engineering designs and calculations to meet project requirements, standards, and industry best practices.
  • Share Your Expertise: Contribute to in-depth technical and engineering studies and offer peer reviews for team members.
  • Be a Communication Dynamo: Collaborate with internal teams, external clients and vendors to resolve technical challenges and ensure project alignment.
  • Stay Hands-On: Monitor construction progress, review field changes impacting designs, and support testing and commissioning phases.
  • Shape the Future: Play a pivotal role in all aspects of a project including design, procurement, construction, and testing and commissioning.
  • Collaborate with Vendors: Coordinate technical clarifications and design updates with suppliers.
  • Quality Assurance Specialist: Review design drawings, material specifications, and support QA/QC processes during design and construction.

You’ll also enjoy occasional travel to meet project needs and see your work come to life on-site!

This role is perfect for someone ready to step up, make an impact, and grow alongside a team passionate about engineering excellence. Let’s build the future together!

Qualifications:

As the ideal candidate you possess a bachelor’s degree from an accredited Canadian university and have 2 to 5 years of applicable experience. You are a licensed Professional Engineer in Canada and/or are eligible for APEGA membership.

Your experience includes demonstrated skills in the following:

  • Canadian codes and design standards.
  • Knowledge and experience with electrical power systems.
  • Ability to utilize CAD software to design and produce complete electrical drawing packages.
  • Knowledge of MS Office, power system design software (SKM Power Tools, ETAP, SES, EasyPower, CYME), and relay programming software (SEL, GE, Basler, etc.) Experience with BlueBeam PDF software is an asset.
  • Able to work effectively with engineering and construction groups to ensure that project delivery and quality goals are met.
  • Experience in multi-disciplinary coordination (integration of other disciplines such as civil, mechanical, etc.)
  • Interpersonal communication and ability to work in a team environment.
  • Strong communication and self-motivation.
  • Possess a high attention to detail, with organizational and time management skills.
  • You possess strong English communication skills, both written and verbal, along with excellent interpersonal abilities and willingness to work in a team atmosphere. Problem solving and decision-making skills are essential.

Personal Qualities

  • Strong problem-solving and decision-making skills are essential.
  • Speaks and writes clearly, listens effectively and conveys messages with transparency.
  • Acts with integrity and takes accountability for actions.
  • Have a willingness to take on responsibilities and challenges.
  • Ability to interface and collaborate effectively with internal and external customers.
  • Cooperate well with a variety of team members on day-to-day, project critical, and time sensitive issues.
  • Noticing a problem and figuring out the best way to solve it.
  • Reliable, responsible, and dependable and fulfill obligations.
  • Strong self-starter, with ability to work independently, and possess organizational and teamwork skills.

In addition to hands-on learning, we offer a range of training and mentorship opportunities to help employees develop their skills and advance their careers.
