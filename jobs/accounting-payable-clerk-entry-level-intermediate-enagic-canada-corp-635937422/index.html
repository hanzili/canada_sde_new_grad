Job Title: Accounts Payable Clerk (Entry Level - Intermediate)

About the Role:

We are seeking an entry-level Accounts Payable Clerk to support our finance team in Burnaby. This role offers hands-on experience processing invoices, preparing payments, and maintaining accurate financial records. Training will be provided.

Responsibilities:

  • Process and verify vendor invoices
  • Prepare payment runs (EFT, cheques)
  • Reconcile vendor statements
  • File and maintain AP documentation
  • Respond to vendor inquiries
  • Support month-end closing tasks
