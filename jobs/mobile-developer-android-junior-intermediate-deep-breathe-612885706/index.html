About Deep Breathe

Medtech startup, AI-enabled ultrasound platform.

Full Description

Mobile Developer (Android, Junior/Intermediate)

Location: Hybrid from London or Toronto

Experience: 1–3 years

Employment Type: Full-time

About Us

We are building ATLAS, a mobile platform that delivers real-time ultrasound diagnostics for trauma assessment across the chain of care, from the hospital to the front lines. Our mission is to bring advanced imaging and AI-driven insight directly to the point of care – empowering medics and clinicians to make faster, better decisions when every second matters. You will join a small, multidisciplinary team at the intersection of ultrasound imaging, AI, and mobile systems, where your work will directly shape how life-saving tools are used in real-world emergencies.

The Role

We are looking for a Mobile Developer with 1-3 years of experience building consumer Android applications who wants to work on medical devices and hardware-integrated software for real-time, AI-enabled applications. You will contribute to Android apps that interface with ultrasound hardware and cloud services, working with a team of engineers across mobile, backend, and hardware teams. No prior healthcare or medical device experience is required – we value strong Android fundamentals, curiosity, and a willingness to learn.

Responsibilities

● Design, develop, and maintain Android applications using Kotlin.

● Build intuitive user interfaces for real-time data display.

● Integrate existing Android apps with hardware SDKs and backend services.

● Apply consumer-grade UX and performance best practices to specialized workflows.

● Write clean, readable, and testable code.

● Participate in code reviews and team design discussions.

● Debug issues and help improve app stability and performance.

● Work closely with iOS, backend, and data engineers to ensure a consistent cross-platform experience, and when needed, support iOS development to maintain platform continuity.
