About the Role

QA technician performing inspections, testing, reporting, and GMP/HACCP training

About Global Gourmet Foods Inc.

Global Gourmet Foods, a food manufacturer under Premium Brands Holdings

Full Description

Global Gourmet Foods is a division of Premium Brands Holdings Corporation (TSX: PBH) which owns a broad range of leading specialty food manufacturing and differentiated food distribution businesses with operations. Global Gourmet Foods is a federally registered food manufacturer who supplies premium kettle products to key accounts in both domestic and export markets.

We are seeking dynamic individuals that can work with the Quality Assurance department, ensuring that the facility is in compliance with regulatory and food safety standards.

**DESCRIPTION OF DUTIES:**

- Perform the daily monitoring and verification tasks according to the food safety program, such as temperature, weight check and visual inspections and other quality inspections etc. - Conduct environmental testing and product checking - Prepare QA inspection reports with document the QA findings and appropriate corrective action - Report the foods safety and quality issues to the QA Manager and the floor operations Supervisors - Conduct employee training on GMP, HACCP or as required - Other duties as assigned

**QUALIFICATIONS AND REQUIREMENTS:**

- Have a strong interest in food safety and quality assurance practices - Have relevant working experiences in food processing industry is an asset - Ability to work independently and cooperatively with all employees and contractors and to provide technical help when needed - Strong interpersonal and communication skills, problem solving, time management skills and detail oriented - Proficiency in Microsoft Office software (Excel, Word and PowerPoint)

If interested, please send in a detailed resume and cover letter with the subject title, "Quality Assurance Technician". While we greatly appreciate all the replies and applications we receive, regretfully only those selected for an interview will be contacted. Thank you.
