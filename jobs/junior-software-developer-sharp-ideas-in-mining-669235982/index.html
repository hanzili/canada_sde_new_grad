Full Description

ABOUT US

Sharp Ideas in Mining (SIIM) is a small mining consultancy firm supplying technical support to the mining industry.

THE OPPORTUNITY

We are hiring two (2) developers join our Vancouver team. You will work directly with the founders, gaining exposure to mining terminology, complex problem spaces, and production-grade software development.

While you will collaborate as a team, we are looking for two distinct skill sets. However, you are more than welcome to apply for both roles, just be sure to speak to your qualifications for each role in distinct sections of your cover letter:

Role A: AI & Data Science Engineering Student

Focus: Cost modeling, Python workflows, and Data Analysis.

  • Core Task: Design, implement, and refine mining cost models that feed into our core computational engine.
  • Data Ops: Extract, clean, and analyze complex mining datasets using Python, PostgreSQL, Excel, and AI LLM Querying.
  • Workflow: Turn experimental analyses into repeatable, documented workflows, and become deeply knowledgeable about the underlying mining data.
  • Math: Apply advanced statistics to reason about complex datasets.

Role B: Full Stack Engineering Student

Focus: Refactoring, System Architecture, and GUI Development.

  • Legacy Refactoring: Refactor our legacy expert-system Kernel from Fortran to Rust, improving modularity, testability, and performance.
  • Interface Development: Design, develop, and enhance in-house GUI programs using Tauri (Rust + Web Frontend).
  • System Architecture: Contribute to the design of a scalable software ecosystem that ensures long-term extensibility.

Shared Responsibilities

Both students will contribute to general engineering and operational tasks:

  • Security & Ops: Maintain and refine the system's security posture and development workflows.
  • Web Presence: Update the SIIM corporate website to better display capabilities for client outreach.
  • Learning: Rapidly acquire knowledge of mining terminology, the problem space we are in, and the specific software stack we run.

QUALIFICATIONS

  • Eligibility: Must be currently enrolled in or graduated from a recognized software engineering, computer science, or related program at a Canadian post-secondary institution and available for full-time, on-site work in Vancouver. Secondly, for funding reasons, the applicant must be a permanent resident or citizen of Canada.

Required Skills (Both Roles):

  • Languages: Proficiency in Python is required.
  • Communication: Excellent English communication skills (written and verbal).
  • Mindset: A self-starter who works efficiently independently but is a team player; who can pivot and adapt to failure but be unafraid to experiment.

Role-Specific Skills:

  • For AI & Data Science:
  • Proven experience in Data Analysis and Excel.
  • Strong grasp of calculus, statistics, and data modeling.
  • Understanding of AI fundamentals, including LLMs and agentic workflows.
  • For Full Stack:
  • Experience with Rust and React is highly preferred.
  • Strong understanding of software architecture, design patterns, and code maintainability.
  • Experience working with legacy codebases is a bonus.

Job Type: Full-time

Pay: From $25.00 per hour

Expected hours: 40 per week

Work Location: In person
