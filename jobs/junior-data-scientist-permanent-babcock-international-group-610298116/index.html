Full Description

Victoria, BC / Ottawa, ON / Halifax, NS Expected Salary: $57,508 to $71,885

To determine final salary, Babcock considers a variety of factors including the successful Candidate’s skills and experience and internal equity. The final base salary offer is at the Company’s sole discretion and presented as part of a competitive total compensation package.

What You Do Here Matters In a complex world marked by the threat of war, natural disasters, and geopolitical instability, national security has never been more important.

Babcock is an international defence company providing support and product solutions to enhance our customers’ defence capabilities and critical assets.

Our Canadian team combines their talent and expertise with deep global knowledge and experience from our operations in the United Kingdom, Europe, Australasia and Africa.

Working for us, you’ll help ensure that Canada’s critical services and assets are readily available, reliable, and capable for our defence and civil customers and contribute to Babcock’s Purpose, ‘Creating a safe and secure world, together.’ Be Part of Something Big. Babcock is a people business. Our people and customers matter most to us. Here you will experience a challenging and rewarding work environment with the support you need to deliver and thrive in your career alongside inclusive teams you respect that value your skills and dedication.

We encourage you to learn more about Babcock Canada by exploring our website: babcockcanada.com .

In this role, you will:

  • Work with business users and subject matter experts (SME’s) throughout the Business Intelligence and data science lifecycle.
  • Develop Business Intelligence and data science solutions to support business processes within the organization and support updates to existing solutions.
  • Provide analytical and technical support for complex requests, projects, or initiatives.
  • Capture and interpret business requirements in the form of user stories.
  • Write technical documentation for new and existing solutions.
  • Work with the business to ensure appropriate governance is applied and adhered to in accordance with Business Intelligence and Data Science best practices.

The ideal candidate brings:

  • A post-secondary degree in Engineering, Computer Science, Mathematics, Statistics, or 2 years relevant experience in a similar role
  • Experience conducting data analysis with one or more of the following (Power BI, R, Python)
  • Proficient with the use of data visualization tools (Power BI or Tableau)
  • Technical writing skills with experience in gathering and documenting user requirements and the ability to produce documents ranging from specifications to user manuals.
  • You are eligible for a Canadian Secret Security and ITAR Clearance

Desired (An Asset):

  • Knowledge of Database Architecture and delivering solutions within Database Systems (SQL & NOSQL)
  • Implementation of Extraction, Transformation and Load (ETL) flows.
  • Implementation and deployment of machine learning algorithms
  • Advanced knowledge of statistical models
  • Knowledge of Agile (Scrum/Kanban) methodologies and practices.
