Full Description

D2L is a cloud company that is modernizing education and building the Future of Work. The old models of teaching and learning are in the midst of the largest transformation in history, and D2L is at the heart of that fundamental shift.

New models of teaching and learning enable a personalized, student-centric experience – and deliver improved retention, engagement, satisfaction, and results for learners of all ages – in schools, campuses, and companies.

D2L is disrupting the way the world learns, by providing the next generation learning environment and solutions to engage and inspire learners. And most importantly, by giving customers a platform that is easy, flexible, and smart. No other company provides a solution as robust and innovative as D2L.

D2L has had a singular mission for 25 years and is dedicated to that same mission in the years ahead: to transform the way the world learns – and by doing so, we will help improve human potential globally.

Every application we receive is personally reviewed by a member of our Talent Acquisition team - yes, a real person looks at your resume! While we use AI tools internally to streamline tasks like meeting notes, summaries, and administrative work, these tools never rank resumes, make hiring decisions, or influence candidate evaluations.

We may continue to review applications on a rolling basis, but recommend applying by March 13th to ensure you are included in initial application reviews. The Social Media Co-op Student will assist with content creation, management, and strategy across various social platforms for the Social Media team. The successful candidate will support social media content planning, creation, and post-production, including engaging with stakeholders and assisting with administrative tasks. They will work alongside a creative and dynamic marketing team to help execute social media campaigns and contribute to the success of the organization.

What would my main duties be?

  • Collaborate with Marketing and Creative teams to support social media campaigns and media generation.
  • Assist in content creation, including short-form video and visual media tailored for social channels.
  • Create and edit content for various platforms, including Instagram, TikTok, LinkedIn and Facebook.
  • Collaborate with stakeholders to ensure content aligns with brand goals and audience preferences.
  • Assist with social media strategies to increase engagement and brand presence across platforms.
  • Engage with online communities and track social media trends to inform future content creation.

Work Experience

  • Understanding of social media dynamics, especially the role of video content in a business-to-business environment.
  • Experience with social media platforms and how video and multimedia content can drive engagement.
  • Ability to create engaging content that resonates with different audience demographics.
  • Strong written and verbal communication skills for crafting posts, captions, and engaging with followers.
  • A team player with a proactive, adaptable mindset to keep up with the fast-paced, ever-evolving social media landscape.
  • Comfortable with tools for creating and editing social media content, with emphasis on short-form video for platforms like TikTok, Instagram, and YouTube.

Other Requirements And Assets

  • Strong attention to detail and a keen eye for visual design elements.
  • Strong analytical skills for tracking social media metrics and performance.
  • Creativity and a passion for staying on top of the latest social media trends, and willingness to experiment with new content formats and strategies.
  • Strong communication, collaboration, and interpersonal skills.
  • Excellent writing and copywriting skills for various platforms.
  • Highly organized, with effective time management skills to manage multiple projects.
  • Familiarity with tools like the Adobe Creative Suite (Premiere Pro, After Effects, Photoshop) or similar video editing software.
  • Experience with social media management tools and an understanding of analytics platforms is an asset.
  • Familiarity and interest in AI tools (e.g. ChatGBT, Copilot, etc.)

Education Recommendations

  • Student enrolled in social media marketing, digital marketing, communications, or a related field.
  • Eligible candidates must be currently enrolled in a post-secondary program.
