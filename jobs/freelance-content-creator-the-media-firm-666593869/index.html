About the Role

About The Media Firm.

Luxury real estate media firm with strong network

Full Description

Company: The Media Firm.
Location: Greater Toronto Area (In-Person Required | East End Preferred)
Job Type: Part-Time Internship (3–4 Days/Week | 3-Month Term with Potential Extension)

About The Opportunity

This is not a typical internship.

This is an opportunity to work directly alongside a growing personal brand built within the luxury real estate, automotive, and interior design space, backed by:

  • A luxury real estate media company
  • A content marketing agency
  • A strong network of builders, entrepreneurs, and industry professionals

You will be involved in creating content with:

  • Real estate developers
  • Builders and contractors
  • Interior designers
  • Successful entrepreneurs

This role is designed for someone who wants to learn how to build a high-level personal brand while actively creating content that performs.

Role Overview

You will work closely with the founder to capture, create, and support content production across:

  • Podcasts
  • Vlogs
  • Short-form content (TikTok, Instagram Reels, YouTube Shorts)

This role requires someone who already understands:

  • Viral content formats
  • Storytelling structure
  • How to identify and replicate high-performing content

You are not just filming, you are learning how to:

  • Think like a content creator
  • Identify what works and why
  • Execute content designed to perform

Core Responsibilities

Content Creation & Production

  • Assist in filming:
  • Podcasts
  • Vlogs
  • Short-form content
  • Capture behind-the-scenes and lifestyle content
  • Help structure shots, angles, and content flow
  • Ensure content is captured cleanly and consistently

Viral Content Research & Replication

  • Study and analyze high-performing content across TikTok and Instagram
  • Identify:
  • Hooks
  • Formats
  • Trends
  • Storytelling patterns
  • Help replicate and adapt viral content ideas to fit the personal brand
  • Contribute ideas based on what is currently working in the market

Storytelling & Content Thinking

  • Understand how to:
  • Structure engaging content
  • Capture attention in the first few seconds
  • Maintain viewer retention

Support the development of:

  • Short-form concepts
  • Vlog ideas
  • Podcast content angles

Editing Support (Bonus)

  • Assist with editing short-form content (CapCut or similar)
  • Help identify strong clips, hooks, and highlights
  • Support basic editing for vlogs or podcasts

On-Site Execution & Reliability

  • Be available for 3–4 days per week
  • Attend shoots across the Greater Toronto Area
  • Show up on time, prepared, and ready to execute
  • Be proactive and engaged during all shoots

Growth & Leadership Potential (Bonus)

  • Take initiative in suggesting content ideas and improvements
  • Support planning and coordination of content shoots
  • (Bonus) Assist in directing or leading smaller content segments

What We’re Looking For

  • Strong interest in content creation and social media
  • Understanding of viral content and storytelling (required)
  • Ability to identify and replicate high-performing content

Interest in:

  • Luxury real estate
  • Automotive
  • Interior design
  • Entrepreneurship
  • Based in the Greater Toronto Area (East End preferred)
  • Reliable, punctual, and organized
  • Strong work ethic and willingness to learn
  • Comfortable being on-site and working in fast-paced environments

Bonus If You Have

  • Video editing experience (CapCut, Premiere, etc.)
  • Experience filming content
  • Interest in building your own personal brand
  • Experience directing or leading content shoots

What You’ll Gain

  • Hands-on experience building a real personal brand
  • Exposure to high-level entrepreneurs and industry professionals
  • Deep understanding of:
  • Viral content
  • Social media growth
  • Content strategy
  • Real-world experience in content creation and media production
  • Opportunity to grow into a full-time role after the 3-month internship

Internship Structure

  • Duration: 3 months
  • Schedule: 3–4 days per week
  • Location: In-person (GTA-based shoots required)
  • Opportunity for extension based on performance

Why This Role is Different

You are not just assisting.

You are learning:

  • How viral content is created
  • How storytelling drives attention
  • How personal brands are built in real industries
  • How content translates into real opportunities

We’re looking for someone who wants to be in the room, in the process, and in the action, not just watching from the outside.

Pay: $16.60-$22.00 per hour

Benefits:

  • Company events
  • Flexible schedule

Ability to commute/relocate:

  • Greater Toronto Area, ON: reliably commute or plan to relocate before starting work (required)

Application question(s):

  • What creators do you study consistently, and what have you learned from them?

Experience:

  • Content creation: 1 year (required)
  • Video editing: 1 year (required)
  • real estate videography: 1 year (preferred)

Location:

  • Greater Toronto Area, ON (required)

  • 75% (required)
