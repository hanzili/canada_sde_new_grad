Full Description

Now Hiring: Summer Boutique Assistant
Love local? Obsessed with jewelry and handcrafted goods? Ready to spend your summer in a shop full of stories and sparkle? This might be your dream job.

We’re on the hunt for a full-time Summer Boutique Assistant to join our team at Selkie - our bridge-side locally-loved shop that celebrate handcrafted treasures, coastal charm, and artisan goods. If you're someone who loves meeting new people, telling stories, and being part of a supportive, creative team, we’d love to meet you!

What We’re All About:

We’re more than just a retail shop- we’re a community. Each product has a story, and we’re passionate about connecting our customers with pieces that inspire joy, nostalgia, and meaning. Our summer team plays a key role in that magic.

You’ll Love This Role If You:

  • Are outgoing, friendly, and genuinely love talking to people
  • Are curious and love learning the stories behind handmade products
  • Thrive in creative, upbeat spaces with strong team vibes
  • Are looking to build real-world skills in retail, sales, and small business life
  • Are able to work independently.

What You’ll Be Doing:

  • Welcoming customers and helping them find pieces they’ll love
  • Ringing through sales and keeping the cash accurate
  • Learning (and sharing!) the stories behind our products and artisans
  • Keeping our displays beautiful and our shop tidy
  • Restocking shelves, stickering inventory, and helping with day-to-day tasks
  • Supporting the manager and helping the shop run smoothly
  • Helping us hit our daily sales goals in a way that feels natural and positive

Availability:

  • Must be available June 1 – August 31
  • Evenings and weekends are a must (our latest shift ends at 8:15pm)

Perks:

  • Mentorship from experienced small business owners and creatives
  • A glowing reference for future school or job applications
  • A fun, supportive team that actually cares
  • Room to grow - past summer team members have gone on to launch businesses, return season after season, and build lasting friendships here

What You Bring:

  • A love for jewelry, home decor, the value of handcrafted, and/or interior design
  • Great communication skills and a warm, upbeat personality
  • Responsible cash-handling and attention to detail
  • High school diploma or equivalent
  • You’re bondable and willing to complete a criminal record check

Pay: $17–$18/hour depending on experience.

Apply Now:

Ready to join our creative little world? Send us your resume and a short note telling us why you’d be a great fit and what your favourite local brand or product is! Bonus points if you make us smile!

Job Types: Full-time, Seasonal
Contract length: 4 months

Pay: $17.00-$18.00 per hour

Benefits:

  • Store discount

Application question(s):

  • Are you currently residing on Prince Edward Island and available for an in-person interview in Charlottetown or Borden?
  • Do you need certain dates booked off for special occasions? (weddings, pre-planned trips, etc.) Please list them.

Work Location: In person
