About the Role

Develop data pipelines and BI dashboards

About Kinaxis

Kinaxis: Ottawa-based data-driven supply chain software

Full Description

About Kinaxis In 1984, we started out as a team of three engineers. Today, we have grown to become a global organization with over 2000 employees around the world, with a brand-new HQ based in Kanata North in Ottawa. As one of Canada’s Top Employers for Young People (2024), we want to help you take the first step in crafting your career journey.

At Kinaxis, we power the world’s supply chains to help preserve the planet’s resources and enrich the human experience. As a global leader in end-to-end supply chain management, we enable supply chain excellence for all industries, with more than 40,000 users in over 100 countries. We are expanding our team as we continue to innovate and revolutionize how we support our customers.

About The Team The Data & Analytics team at Kinaxis drives transformation into a data-driven organization. By designing and governing scalable, modern data ecosystems, we ensure reliable, high-quality, and accessible data that empowers decision-making across the company. Our expertise spans data integration, engineering, governance, business intelligence, and cloud cost optimization.

As a Data & Analytics Intern/Co-op Student, you will have the opportunity to gain hands-on experience and develop skills in data integration, engineering, modeling, business intelligence, and observability. Working alongside experienced team members, you will help transform legacy systems into a cloud-native, modern data stack and create innovative data products that drive Kinaxis’ decision-making. By contributing to the innovation of Kinaxis' data ecosystem, you will gain practical experience while playing a part in the company’s growth and success.

Location: This is a hybrid position. You must be in the Ottawa, Canada office, at least three days a week

Term Duration: This is a full-time, 8, month position, starting May 2026

Co-op or Intern: This position is open to co-ops and interns. To be eligible for a Co-op or Intern position at Kinaxis, you must either be currently enrolled in full-time education or, if you are a recent/upcoming graduate, your graduation date must be within 12 months of the placement end date.

Compensation range: $24.15 - $35.42 hourly rate. The final offer within this range will reflect the candidate’s skills, year of education and experience.

Canadian Citizenship or Permanent Residency is required to obtain CGRP Security Clearance, a requirement of the role. Vacancy Status: This is an existing job vacancy.

What You Will Do Modernization & Development

  • Assist in modernizing data systems with cloud-based tools.
  • Learn and apply modern data engineering practices and tools under guidance.
  • Develop basic data pipelines and integrations.
  • Participate in data validation, cleansing, and error-handling mechanisms to maintain data quality.
  • Stay updated on the latest technology trends relevant to your role.

Collaboration & Solution Design

  • Work with team members to gather requirements and support small-scale data-driven solutions.
  • Contribute to team planning and the execution of data projects.
  • Troubleshoot issues while focusing on learning and skill development.
  • Work in an agile environment to deliver tasks collaboratively.

Insights & Enablement

  • Create simple data models and dashboards to provide actionable insights to stakeholders.
  • Build and maintain visually appealing BI reports/dashboards.

Storytelling & Influence

  • Learn to deliver compelling presentations to engage and inform stakeholders.

What We Are Looking For

  • Currently enrolled in a Bachelor’s degree program in Computer Science, Software Engineering, or related fields.
  • Exposure to SQL, Python, or data pipeline tools (e.g., Airflow, dbt) through coursework or previous projects.
  • Exposure to BI tools such as PowerBI and/or Looker is a plus
  • Basic understanding of software engineering principles, including CI/CD pipelines, version control (Git), and RESTful APIs.
  • Basic knowledge of database concepts and different types of data models.
  • Interest in infrastructure-as-code (Terraform) and/or configuration-as-code (Ansible) is a plus.
  • A passion for learning and solving technical challenges.

Things that would definitely help

  • Learn how to modernize legacy systems using cutting-edge cloud-native technologies.
  • Gain hands-on experience in developing data products that create a tangible impact.
  • Collaborate with cross-functional teams to deliver innovative data solutions.

We’re accepting applications now through end of day on Friday, March 20, 2026. Please note that we may begin reviewing applications before the posting closes, so early submission is encouraged.

Work With Impact: Our platform directly helps companies power the world’s supply chains. We see the results of what we do out in the world every day—when we see store shelves stocked, when medications are available for our loved ones, and so much more.

Work with Fortune 500 Brands: Companies across industries trust us to help them take control of their integrated business planning and digital supply chain. Some of our customers include Ford, Unilever, Yamaha, P&G, Lockheed-Martin, and more.

Social Responsibility at Kinaxis: Our Diversity, Equity, and Inclusion Committee weighs in on hiring practices, talent assessment training materials, and mandatory training on unconscious bias and inclusion fundamentals. Sustainability is key to what we do and we’re committed to net-zero operations strategy for the long term. We are involved in our communities and support causes where we can make the most impact.

  • Flexible work options
  • Physical and mental well-being programs
  • Regularly scheduled virtual fitness classes
  • Mentorship programs and training and career development
  • Recognition programs and referral rewards
  • Hackathons
