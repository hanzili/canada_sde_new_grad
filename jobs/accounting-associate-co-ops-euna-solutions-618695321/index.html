Full Description

The Opportunities

Euna Solutions is looking for 3 Accounting Co-Op Associates to join our dynamic Accounting and Legal teams. If you're a proactive student who thrives in a fast-paced, collaborative environment, these roles offer hands-on experience in real business operations, exposure to cross-functional teams, and opportunities to own impactful projects.

You’ll be part of a group that values accuracy, initiative, and curiosity. We’re looking for team players who want to learn, contribute meaningfully, and aren’t afraid to speak up when something can be done better.

These positions are for the May - August term. Anticipated start date is May 5, 2026.

Position 1: Accounting Associate Co-Op – Projects Focus

This role focuses on supporting accounts receivable, billing, and cross-departmental initiatives. You’ll be involved in customer communication, financial documentation updates, and process clean-ups. It’s a great role if you want variety and enjoy jumping into new challenges.

Key Responsibilities:

  • Assist with customer communications about company updates (e.g., name changes, banking info)
  • Update vendor forms, customer portals (US & Canada)
  • Complete tax-related projects including exemption certificates and sales tax updates
  • Ensure billing data integrity and collaborate with internal teams

Position 2: Accounting Associate Co-Op – Operations Focus

In this hands-on role, you'll assist with accounts payable, treasury, and month-end accounting tasks. It’s ideal for students interested in the day-to-day workings of corporate finance.

Key Responsibilities:

  • Support monthly GL account reconciliations
  • Assist with vendor invoice reviews and payment processing
  • Help with month-end close and finance reporting
  • Maintain data accuracy and help improve finance file organization

Position 3: Legal & Finance Associate Co-Op – Projects Focus

This role offers a hybrid of legal operations and finance project work. You’ll support a large-scale internal contract system update while gaining exposure to compliance and vendor management processes.

Key Responsibilities:

  • Help locate and upload contracts into our CMS
  • Support renewal tracking and document audits
  • Ensure compliance with vendor registration processes
  • Participate in data clean-up and reconciliation across Salesforce and other platforms

What You Bring (Requirements For All Roles)

  • Enrolled in a Co-op program and working toward a diploma or degree in Accounting, Finance, Legal Studies, or Business Administration
  • Basic understanding of accounting principles
  • Organized, accountable, and comfortable working independently or on a team
  • Strong communication skills and a high attention to detail
  • Adaptable to changing priorities—comfortable in a fast-paced, tech-driven environment
  • Intermediate Excel skills and strong Microsoft Office proficiency

Core Competencies We Value

  • Accuracy & attention to detail
  • Collaboration & teamwork
  • Initiative & problem-solving
  • Time management & accountability
  • Communication & adaptability
  • Integrity and ethical judgment

Location:

This position will be hybrid with 3 days/week in our Oakville, ON (or ATL, GA) office.

Compensation Range: $20-$23/hr

We believe in openness, so we publish our range from the start. Where an offer will land depends on your background, strengths, and how you’ll contribute to the work ahead.
