About the Role

About 4L Communications Inc

TELUS dealer with growth and training programs

Full Description

Join the 4L Communications Team!

4L Communications is a family-owned and operated TELUS dealer with 21 locations across Manitoba, Saskatchewan, and Alberta. We're looking for an energetic, motivated individual to join our Thompson team as a Full-Time Sales Associate.

Why You’ll Love Working With Us

At 4L Communications, we live by our core values:

  • Growth is a must
  • Happy customers
  • Just like family
  • Make it fun!
  • Count on us
  • Driven to win

You're a Great Fit If You:

  • Enjoy helping others discover the best products and services to improve their lives
  • Thrive in a fast-paced environment
  • Are excited about new and ever-changing technology
  • Are competitive by nature, self-motivated and enjoy the challenge of achieving sales targets
  • Work well independently and as part of a team to reach a common goal

What's In It For You?

  • Competitive average earnings: $18–$25+ per hour (hourly wage + uncapped commissions)
  • Monthly bonuses, contests, TELUS rewards programs and employee discounts
  • Full benefits package (dental, health, vision, RSP)
  • Career growth opportunities with training and mentoring programs
  • A fun, supportive team environment
  • Join a dependable dealer with 25+ years of success and 100+ team members

What You'll Do

As a 4L Sales Associate, you will:

  • Help our customers choose the best TELUS products and services that fit their needs
  • Become an expert on the complete TELUS lineup: smartphones, connected devices, accessories, rate plans, home security and automation, business solutions as well as TV and internet products
  • Engage customers in-store, online and by phone
  • Meet and exceed sales targets and metrics

Schedule: Full-time, 32-40 hours per week (Monday - Saturday)

4L Communications is an equal opportunity employer. We celebrate diversity and we are committed to ensuring every team member feels respected, valued, and supported. Should you require any accommodations throughout the recruitment and selection process, please inform our hiring team and we will work with you to ensure your needs are met.

Ready to start your journey with us? Apply today!

We thank all applicants for their interest; only those who have been selected for an interview will be contacted.
