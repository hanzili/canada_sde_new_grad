SSR Mining – Seabee Gold Operation (SGO) is hiring for a Mine Geologist, Junior (Resource Development Beat Geologist) .

About Your New Role Reporting to the Supervisor, Resource Development, the Resource Development Beat Geologist plays a vital role in supporting both Engineering and Mine Operations teams with a focus on resource integrity, geological oversight, and field execution.

Your Key Responsibilities

  • Provide geological input for grade control, stope design, and stockpile management.
  • Conduct geological mapping and geotechnical data interpretation to guide production decisions.
  • Participate in key geological projects, including specialized mapping and integration of structural data into the geological model.
  • Maintain strong communication with the Mining Department to align geology with operations.
  • Conduct daily grade control activities, including chip, channel, and muck sampling
  • Map underground headings and development faces with a focus on vein geometry, structure, and mineralization
  • Assist in identifying ore/waste boundaries to support mining decisions
  • Log diamond drill core (lithological, structural, and alteration logging), both underground and on surface
  • Communicate geological conditions and risks to operations teams in a timely manner
  • Assist with underground drillhole planning and targeting
  • Monitor drilling progress and ensure adherence to geological objectives
  • Support QA/QC protocols and sample handling procedures
  • Assist in updating geological interpretations of narrow vein systems
  • Contribute to stope design inputs and orebody understanding
  • Enter and validate geological data in company databases to maintain accurate and organized geological records
  • Support generation of daily and weekly production geology reports
  • Maintain safe work practices and procedures, including following communication requirements, both on surface and underground
  • Any other duties as assigned by the department.

Your Knowledge And Skills

  • Strong leadership, communication, and problem-solving skills.
  • Work both independently and within a team environment that is fast-paced, and a rapidly changing environment.
  • Positive and proactive attitude towards safety.

Your Education And Experience The successful candidate will have:

  • B.Sc. in Geology, with preference given to candidates registered as a Professional Geoscientist in Saskatchewan.
  • Minimum of one (1) or more years of relevant experience in mining.
  • Exposure to narrow vein mining is considered an asset.
  • Experience with Leapfrog, MX Deposit, and Imago are an asset.

About Our Working Conditions This position has a schedule of 2 weeks on, 2 weeks off at our remote camp which you will be required to fly in fly out with pick up points in Saskatoon, Prince Albert and La Ronge.
