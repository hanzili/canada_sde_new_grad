About the Role

Conduct field geology, core logging, QAQC activities

About Uranium Energy Corp.

Uranium energy company exploring northern Saskatchewan

Full Description

UEC is seeking a curious and motivated Junior Geologist to join our team. In this role, you will work with our Saskatoon-based Exploration team to advance our portfolio of Uranium projects in northern Saskatchewan. You will participate in and contribute to exploration programs through a combination of office and field-based work. During active exploration programs, field work is completed on a rotational schedule.

Responsibilities:

  • Engage in and supervise geological work including core logging, sampling, and QAQC to ensure best practices are maintained
  • Create and digitize cross-sections and maps
  • Collect and process reflectance spectroscopy data
  • Complete downhole probes with geophysical tools
  • Spot drill collars and trails
  • Conduct safety inspections around the Project(s)
  • Assist with geological compilations, interpretations, and reporting of geological data
  • Other field duties as assigned by the Project Geologist

In addition, there is opportunity to work on geoscience-focused initiatives as time permits throughout the year.

Requirements:

  • Bachelor’s degree in geology, or equivalent, and eligible for registration with APEGS
  • Comfortable working in remote locations for extended periods of time
  • Proficient in Microsoft Office applications
  • Strong communication, interpersonal and analytical skills
  • Eager to work in a team environment
  • Valid Class 5 driver’s licence

Recommended:

  • First Aid, ATV and/or snowmobile training

Job Types: Full-time, Permanent

Benefits:

  • Dental care
  • Employee assistance program
  • Extended health care
  • Life insurance
  • Stock options

Education:

  • Bachelor's Degree (required)

Work Location: In person
