About the Role

About Photo Studio Calgary

Full Description

Location:
Primary: Sunpark Plaza, Calgary
Others: Royal Oak NW, Downtown Calgary, Falconridge NE

About Us
We are a busy photography studio specializing in ID photos, photo printing, and photo gifts. Our environment is fast-paced, customer-focused, and detail-oriented.

We’re looking for a reliable, full-time Sales Associate who enjoys working with people, shows up consistently, and takes pride in doing things properly.

Job Responsibilities

  • Assist customers with passport/ID photos, headshots, and photo printing
  • Take photos using a DSLR camera (training provided)
  • Basic photo editing using Adobe Photoshop (training provided)
  • Guide customers through products and upgrades in a friendly, helpful way
  • Handle in-store sales and payments
  • Keep the studio clean, organized, and running smoothly
  • Occasionally cover shifts at our other location when needed

Requirements (PLEASE READ BEFORE APPLYING)

  • Open availability for full-time work (including weekdays and weekends)
  • Able to work at Sundance (Sunpark Plaza) and other locations if needed (a consistent schedule is given)
  • Flexible to cover shifts at other locations when needed (not frequent)
  • Comfortable using a computer and typing at a reasonable speed
  • Strong customer service skills and clear communication
  • Comfortable talking to people throughout the day
  • Reliable, punctual, and professional — showing up on time matters

Job Type: Full-time

Pay: From $15.00 per hour

Application question(s):

  • What is your availability? Please share your availability. Are you able to work full-time (30–40 hours) with open availability, including weekends?

Experience:

  • Customer service: 1 year (preferred)
