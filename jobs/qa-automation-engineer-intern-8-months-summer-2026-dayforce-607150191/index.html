About Dayforce

Full Description

Dayforce, a global leader in Human Capital Management (HCM) with headquarters in Toronto, Ontario, and Minneapolis, Minnesota, operates across North America, EMEA, and APJ regions. Our Cloud HCM platform, recognized for its unified database and continuous calculation engine, enhances efficiency, productivity, and compliance for global workforces. We are committed to "Making Work Life Better™" for employees, customers, and partners worldwide. As an Intern at Dayforce, you’ll gain real-world experience with meaningful responsibilities, access to development programs, and the chance to collaborate globally, bringing new ideas and optimizing processes across multiple business units. Job Title: QA Automation Engineer Intern (Playwright)

Location : Hybrid – Dayforce Toronto Office – 3 days per week

Duration: Summer 2026 - 8 Months (May 2026 – December 2026)

Availability : *Full-time availability of 37.5 – 40 hours weekly is required to be eligible for this opportunity. Benefits For Students

  • Experience working for one of the fastest growing Human Capital Management technology companies in the world Access to Dayforce’s development programs and resources
  • Ability to work with as well as create relationships with members across the globe
  • Autonomy to bring forth new ideas and optimize existing structures within the organization
  • Meaningful responsibilities, enabling you to obtain ‘real-world’ experience
  • Dynamic hands-on exposure to multiple business-units and stakeholders across Dayforce

About The Opportunity The QA Automation Engineer Intern will work closely with product engineering teams to help ensure the quality, reliability, and release readiness of customer-facing applications. In addition to building and maintaining automated UI and API tests, the intern will be responsible for creating and maintaining clear QA documentation, including test plans, test cases, automation guidelines, and release readiness notes. The role will also focus on improving CI test signal and partnering with engineers to document testing strategies that make features more testable and resilient. This is an excellent opportunity to join a fast-moving team within Dayforce, gain hands-on experience with modern test automation and documentation practices, and contribute directly to production-quality software by reducing regressions, improving knowledge sharing, and increasing confidence in releases.

What You’ll Get To Do

  • Build and maintain end-to-end UI tests using Playwright.
  • Add API and integration tests for Node.js services.
  • Improve CI signal: reduce flaky tests, speed up runs, tighten reporting.
  • Partner with engineers to make features testable (better hooks, stable selectors, better logging).
  • Help define what “doneˮ means: test plans, coverage, and release checks.

What You’ll Learn And Experience

  • Real ownership of a test suite that ships to production.
  • Mentorship on automation patterns, debugging, and CI reliability.
  • A clear output: measurable increases in coverage and fewer regressions.

Skills And Experience We Value

  • Experience coding in JavaScript or TypeScript (academic and personal projects are welcome).
  • Familiarity with automated testing frameworks, or the ability to quickly learn one (Playwright is preferred).
  • A solid understanding of core web concepts such as the DOM, selectors, HTTP, and JSON.
  • Ability to analyze logs, reliably reproduce issues, and write clear, well-documented bug reports.
  • Strong attention to detail, with a thoughtful approach to investigating and resolving flaky test failures.

What Would Really Make You Stand Out

  • CI experience (GitHub Actions or similar)
  • SQL basics and comfort working with relational data.
  • Experience testing SPAs (Vue/React) and REST APIs.
