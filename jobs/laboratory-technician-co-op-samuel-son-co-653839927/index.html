Laboratory Technician Co-op

at Samuel, Son & Co.

📍 Location Oakville, Ontario
📋 Details Co-op · Pay: $23-$24/hour
📅 Posted Mar 30, 2026

About the Role

Perform materials testing, QC checks, and data logging

About Samuel, Son & Co.

Co-op at Burloak AM materials lab

Full Description

About Burloak Technologies

Burloak, a division of Samuel, Son & Co is a pioneering additive manufacturing (AM) metal technology company with an expectation of significant growth in the short term as it transforms from a research & development stage to a commercial and production focused company within the AM environment. If you're looking for a rewarding career opportunity with a family run, industry leading organization, bring your experience and motivated attitude to Burloak Technologies.

Role Responsibilities:

  • Performing mechanical/material properties characterization under ISO/ASTM standards. This includes but is not limited to powder testing, metallography, chemical analysis, mechanical testing and Computed Tomography.

  • Support and perform quality control inspections and checks.

  • Create documentation based on quality records and quality inspections.

  • Support Burloak’s activities for research and development programs on materials and process/quality development for additive manufacturing

  • Support Burloak’s activities with regard to AS9100, ISO17025 and NADCAP accreditations for quality, materials and mechanical testing lab

  • Perform engineering/quality functions under AS9100 quality management system requirements

Qualification Requirements

  • Pursuing a college degree or BS in Material Science and Engineering, Mechanical Engineering, or related technical degree with a GPA of 3.5 or higher

  • Eligible for the co-op program for the duration of the contract

  • It is imperative that the successful candidate can quickly learn technical procedures as they will be trained by the Burloak Engineering team on the specific equipment and standardized testing that is being preformed

  • Demonstrated computer knowledge with proficiency in Microsoft Office Suite (i.e Word, Excel, etc.).

  • Demonstrated ability to manage routine correspondence, multiple tasks/projects, diversified workload and rapidly changing priorities and challenging deadlines

  • Ability to multitask, teamwork and schedule management.

  • Demonstrated strong written and oral communication skills; problem-solving / troubleshooting skills.

  • Ability to be comfortable moving and working around the lab for multiple hours.

Work Environment:

  • The work environment characteristics described here are representative of those an employee encounters while performing the essential functions of this job. Reasonable accommodations may be made to enable individuals with disabilities to perform essential functions. Normal office environment/Laboratory.

The expected compensation range for this position is $23--$24 hourly.

At Samuel we believe in a culture of equality. Respect and integrity are at our core. We are committed to building and creating an environment that is open and welcoming for everyone. Samuel is an Equal Opportunity Employer.
