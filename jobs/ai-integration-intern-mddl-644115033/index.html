AI Integration Intern

at MDDL

About the Role

About MDDL

Full Description

Job Title: AI Integration Intern

Company: mddl Ltd.

Location: Calgary, AB (Hybrid — Room 332, 906 8th Ave SW)

Job Type: Part-Time, Contract (Term)

Pay: $26.00 per hour

Schedule: 20–25 hours per week, flexible scheduling

Duration: 4 months (with possibility of extension)

Job Description

mddl is a housing advocacy and delivery organization working to accelerate middle housing across North American communities. We combine planning, policy, design, education, and technology to expand housing choice and we're building an AI-forward team culture to do it faster and better.

We're looking for an AI Integration Intern to join our small, collaborative team. You'll work hands-on with our core team to identify, test, and implement AI-powered tools and workflows that improve how we operate day to day. This is a role for someone who is curious about applied AI, comfortable experimenting with new tools, and excited to help a small team punch above its weight. You'll touch everything from document automation and data analysis to program delivery and grant writing support, all through the lens of making work smarter with AI.

What you'll do:

AI Workflow Development & Automation

  • Research, evaluate, and recommend AI tools and platforms to streamline internal operations
  • Design and implement AI-assisted workflows for recurring tasks such as document formatting, data entry, report generation, email drafting, and scheduling
  • Build and maintain prompt libraries, templates, and SOPs for AI tool usage across the team
  • Develop automated pipelines for content production, including proposals, presentations, and program materials

Content & Document Support

  • Assist with preparing branded documents, reports, proposals, and presentations using AI tools
  • Support the creation and maintenance of document templates, ensuring brand consistency
  • Help draft, edit, and refine content for grant applications, program descriptions, marketing materials, and internal communications

Data & Research

  • Use AI tools to gather, organize, and analyze data related to housing policy, program performance, grant opportunities, and market research
  • Support the development of dashboards, summaries, and visualizations for leadership decision-making
  • Conduct research on emerging AI trends, tools, and best practices relevant to small organizations

Program & Operations Support

  • Support mddl U program delivery by assisting with course materials, student communications, and logistics coordination
  • Help optimize internal processes such as project tracking, file management, and team communications
  • Assist with event coordination, stakeholder outreach, and meeting preparation

Knowledge Sharing & Documentation

  • Document all AI workflows, integrations, and SOPs so the team can adopt and sustain them independently
  • Provide informal training and support to team members on AI tools and best practices
  • Track and report on time savings, quality improvements, and measurable outcomes from AI integration efforts

What we're looking for:

  • Currently enrolled in or recently completed a post-secondary program in business, technology, communications, design, or a related field
  • Demonstrated interest in and familiarity with AI tools (e.g., Claude, ChatGPT, Midjourney, automation platforms, LLM APIs)
  • Strong written communication skills, comfortable drafting professional documents, emails, and reports
  • Detail-oriented with the ability to manage multiple tasks and priorities in a fast-paced environment
  • Self-starter who is comfortable working independently and taking initiative on projects
  • Familiarity with Microsoft Office Suite (Word, Excel, PowerPoint) and cloud-based collaboration tools
  • Bonus: experience with Python, APIs, no-code/low-code automation tools (Zapier, Make, n8n), or data visualization

What you'll gain:

  • Hands-on experience embedding AI into a real organization's operations — not just theory
  • Exposure to housing policy, urban planning, education delivery, grant writing, and business development
  • Mentorship from mddl's leadership team and the opportunity to shape how a growing organization works
  • A portfolio of tangible AI integration projects you can point to
  • Flexible, hybrid work environment with a small, collaborative team
  • May be eligible for academic credit depending on your program
