Full Description

Junior Financial Analyst – Finance & Accounting (Back Office Operations)

Location: Montreal, Quebec

Hybrid work model: 2 days onsite (Wednesday & Thursday)

Contract – 2 years

Start Date: ASAP

Conversion to Full-Time: Yes, potential

Work Schedule

  • Monday to Friday
  • 9:00 AM – 5:00 PM
  • 37.5 hours per week
  • Overtime: Not required

Position Overview

The Global Market Data Services team is seeking a Junior Financial Analyst to support back-office operations focused on Brokerage, Commissions, and Executions (BCE) activities. This role is an excellent entry point into the financial services and capital markets environment , offering strong learning and long-term growth opportunities.

The successful candidate will support invoice reconciliation, validation, and payment processing activities related to executed trades across multiple lines of business. Training will be provided, and the team promotes a collaborative and supportive culture.

Key Responsibilities

  • Log and track incoming invoices in the master inventory database
  • Review invoices to ensure accuracy of:
  • Cost centers
  • Service descriptions
  • Billing rates and amounts
  • Validate invoice details against expected trade and brokerage data
  • Communicate with internal business partners to:
  • Confirm billing details
  • Obtain approvals for invoice processing
  • Process approved invoices through procurement/payment systems
  • Ensure timely processing of high volumes of invoices while meeting strict deadlines
  • Maintain strong documentation and audit-ready records

Team & Environment

  • Team Size: 4 members
  • Structure:
  • Manager and Analysts based in Montreal
  • Analyst support based in Toronto
  • Work Style:
  • Collaborative team culture
  • Regular team meetings and one-on-one sessions
  • Cross-location teamwork with Montreal and Toronto teams

Required Skills & Qualifications

Must-Have

  • Proficiency in Microsoft Office (Excel, Outlook, Word, SharePoint, Teams)
  • Strong attention to detail and accuracy
  • Willingness to learn and adapt in a fast-paced environment
  • Excellent written and verbal communication skills ( English required )
  • Strong analytical, financial, and judgment skills
  • Ability to work effectively in a complex, multi-stakeholder organization
  • Proven ability to handle large volumes of transactional data within tight timelines

Nice-to-Have

  • Experience or knowledge of Market Data Management (MDM) tools
  • Familiarity with Coupa / SmartProcure or similar invoice processing platforms
  • Prior experience in banking or financial institutions
  • Bilingual in English and French

Education

  • Bachelor’s degree in Finance, Accounting, Business, Economics, or equivalent experience

Additional Requirements / Disqualifiers

  • Candidates must be based in Montreal
  • Must be able to work onsite in the Montreal office as required
  • Remote-only candidates will not be considered

Candidate Value Proposition

  • Strong entry-level opportunity into financial services and capital markets
  • Hands-on exposure to real trading and brokerage operations
  • Skill development in financial operations, invoice reconciliation, and systems
  • Potential pathway to long-term growth and full-time employment
