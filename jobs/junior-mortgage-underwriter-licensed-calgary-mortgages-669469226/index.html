Junior Mortgage Underwriter (Licensed)

at Calgary Mortgages

📋 Details Full-time · Permanent · $35,000-$60,000 CAD · Calgary, AB

About the Role

Underwrite mortgage files, ensure compliance

About Calgary Mortgages

High-volume mortgage broker with client focus

Full Description

Overview

We are a high-volume, top-producing mortgage broker seeking a licensed Junior Mortgage Underwriter who can independently manage files from application to funding with minimal oversight.

This is not a training role. You must already understand the full mortgage lifecycle.

Responsibilities

  • Input deals and manage files in the CRM
  • Collect and review documents for accuracy
  • Communicate with clients throughout the process
  • Work with lenders to move approvals and conditions forward
  • Ensure compliance and clean submissions
  • Track files and proactively resolve issues
  • Support underwriting operations as needed

Requirements

  • Active Alberta mortgage license
  • Experience with full deal lifecycle
  • Strong communication skills (client & lender-facing)
  • Highly organized and detail-oriented
  • Ability to work independently and take initiative

What We’re Looking For

  • Self-starter who takes ownership
  • Professional and client-focused
  • Accountable and solution-oriented
  • Comfortable in a fast-paced, high-volume environment
  • Experience with Velocity an asset

Not a Fit If You:

  • Need constant direction
  • Avoid accountability
  • Prefer non-client-facing roles
  • Are looking for short-term employment

Pay: $35,000.00-$60,000.00 per year

Benefits:

  • Work from home

Work Location: Hybrid remote in Calgary, AB
