About Vertex Resource Group Ltd.

Leading North American environmental services provider

Full Description

VERSATILITY. EXPERTISE.

With these two words, Vertex makes the promise that we will always be versatile in developing solutions to our clients’ unique needs and that we will always demonstrate expertise in our designated fields.

Since 1962, Vertex has been a leading North American provider of environmental services. With over 60 years of expertise, Vertex helps clients achieve their development goals through our versatile suite of services. From the initial site selection, consultation and regulatory approval, through the construction, operation and maintenance phases, to conclusion and environmental cleanup, Vertex offers a diverse range of services throughout the life cycle of our clients’ projects.

Overview Vertex is currently accepting applications for a full-time, permanent Junior Environmental Scientist to join our team. In this role, you will support a variety of oil and gas assessment and remediation projects, helping deliver high-quality environmental solutions that meet project objectives, regulatory requirements, and client expectations. This role will focus on developing the core field skills for environmental assessments and remediation.

The successful candidate will be mostly involved in the field stage of project execution; however, there will be the opportunity to learn how to support the development of work plans and cost estimates, project coordination, data interpretation, and reporting. This position is well suited to a motivated environmental professional who enjoys being outdoors, takes pride in executing quality work and cares about the environment.

As part of the Vertex team, you will play an important role in delivering safe, efficient, and high-quality work while contributing to successful outcomes for our clients and the environment.

CANDIDATE QUALIFICATION

  • Diploma, degree, or higher education in environmental science, biology, or a related discipline.
  • Is qualified to obtain a professional designation or in-training designation applicable for conducting professional sign-off in Alberta.
  • 1 to 2 years of environmental consulting or related field experience, with demonstrated experience in:
  • Phase I and Phase II program execution; and
  • Remediation programs
  • Willingness and ability to complete field work for extended periods throughout Western Canada (predominantly Alberta).
  • Excellent written and verbal communication skills.
  • Strong time management, organizational, and problem-solving skills.
  • Hydrogeology experience or background would be considered an asset.

Responsibilities

  • Field work is a requirement, approximately 80% or more of time will be allocated to the field
  • Provide support during environmental site assessments projects (Phase I and II ESAs).
  • Provide support during remediation activities
  • Provide support during groundwater monitoring projects
  • Provide support during reclamation activities
  • Data management (data tables, photographs, inputting field data sheets, analysis and interpretation)
  • Reporting (all types, styles and formats that Vertex produces)
  • Keep up-to-date on industry guidelines and regulatory requirements as it relates to reclamation and revegetation in Western Canada
  • Create high-quality content that conveys technical concepts
  • Other duties as directed by your direct supervisor or manager

LOCATION

  • This position will be based out of our Sherwood Park office.

Additional Information

  • Compensation is commensurate with education and experience
  • A comprehensive benefit package is provided

APPLY NOW

With a track record of success, Vertex provides a fast-paced environment where people thrive. We recognize that each member brings a valuable skill set to the Vertex team and we make health, safety and community well-being a priority by supporting numerous endeavors at each of our locations across North America.

At Vertex, we value teamwork through collaboration and provide support across the entire organization. If you think you have what it takes to play for the Vertex team, please apply.
