Full Description

Job Summary
We are seeking a detail-oriented and proactive Junior Marketing Coordinator to support our sales efforts. This entry-level position offers an excellent opportunity to develop foundational skills in marketing, administrative support, and client communication within a dynamic environment. The ideal candidate will possess strong organizational skills, proficiency in office software, and a customer-focused attitude to assist with various marketing and administrative tasks.

Responsibilities

  • Development and execution of marketing campaigns and initiatives
  • Manage data entry related to marketing activities, client databases, and project tracking
  • Support the creation of marketing materials using Canva, Microsoft Office Tools and various other software
  • Coordinate with team members to organize promotional events and outreach efforts
  • Maintain accurate filing systems for marketing collateral, client records, and correspondence
  • Assist with the coordination and execution of trade-show initiatives and attend the shows as required (some travel may be required)
  • Provide customer support by responding to inquiries via phone or email with professional phone etiquette
  • Proofread marketing content for accuracy and clarity before publication
  • Perform clerical tasks such as filing, data management, and document organization

Requirements

  • Prior office experience or administrative background preferred
  • Proficiency in Microsoft Office Suite (Word, Excel, PowerPoint), Canva and other applications
  • Strong computer skills including data entry, typing, proofreading, and file management
  • Excellent organizational skills with attention to detail and accuracy
  • Customer service experience with professional communication skills and phone etiquette
  • Ability to handle multiple tasks efficiently in a fast-paced environment

This role provides an excellent platform for growth within the marketing field while supporting essential administrative functions across the organization.
