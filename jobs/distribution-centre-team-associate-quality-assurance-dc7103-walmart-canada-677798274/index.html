About Walmart Canada

Full Description

Position Summary... Responsible of ensuring inventory and order filling accuracy throughout the DC.

Obtain and maintain Order picker license

What You'll Do... Obtain and maintain Order picker license

  • Perform quality audits and verify reports for DC inventory and order-filling accuracy.
  • Adhere to all Quality Assurance policies and procedures.
  • Operates power equipment safely and under control at all times.
  • Review and maintain item-profiling requests from Operations Supervisors.
  • Administer Warehouse Management System item/slot management, including new item setup, existing item maintenance (sequence, module, marshalling), slot label maintenance, slot creation/deletion, frozen slot tracking and maintenance, item replenishment maintenance, product recalls.
  • Process damaged product in a timely manner using proper procedures.
  • Research and justify larger inventory adjustments for Supervisor approval
  • Accurately and efficiently prepare/process the TPR report daily band maintain integrity through enforced timelines for Walmart finance departments
