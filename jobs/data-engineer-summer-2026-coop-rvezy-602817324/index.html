About RVezy

RVezy (https://www.rvezy.com/) is an innovative travel experience company that brings aspiring vacationers and RV owners together. Founded in 2016, we provide a platform for RV rentals available across most of Canada and the United States.

RVezy serves thousands of hosts and guests across North America, and you will be helping ensure they have a safe, secure, and enjoyable experience throughout each RV rental. We have a tight-knit team that has meaningful relationships with one another. To see how we started, check out our pitch on Dragon’s Den (https://www.cbc.ca/dragonsden/pitches/rvezy). Since then, we have grown exponentially, and our three-year revenue increased 286%, as mentioned in the Globe and Mail’s: Canada's Top Growing Companies 2023 (https://www.theglobeandmail.com/business/rob-magazine/article-upwardly-mobile-six-of-canadas-top-growing-companies-on-how-they-keep/).

We were also named one of Ottawa’s Best Places to Work (https://obj.ca/here-are-ottawas-best-places-to-work-in-2023/?no_cache=1692725825)! If you want a sneak peek at what it’s like to work here, check out our Life at RVezy Instagram! (https://www.instagram.com/lifeatrvezy)

About the role

As a Data Engineer Co-op Student, you’ll work closely with our Engineering and Data teams to help build, maintain, and improve the data foundations that support decision-making across RVezy. This role is ideal for a student who enjoys working with data from end to end, including ingestion, transformation, analysis, and ensuring data reliability, and who is excited to tackle real-world data challenges in a growing marketplace business.

You’ll gain hands-on experience working with production data systems, collaborating with engineers, product managers, and business stakeholders, and contributing to data initiatives that have a direct impact on how the company operates and grows.

Key responsibilities

  • Data Pipeline Development: Assist in building, maintaining, and improving data pipelines that ingest, transform, and load data from various internal and external sources.
  • Data Quality & Reliability: Help monitor data quality, investigate data issues, and support improvements to ensure accuracy, consistency, and reliability across datasets.
  • Analytics Enablement: Support the creation and maintenance of datasets, tables, or views used for reporting, dashboards, and analysis by business teams.
  • Collaboration Across Teams: Work closely with Data Engineers, Software Engineers, and stakeholders to understand data needs and translate them into scalable solutions.
  • Documentation & Knowledge Sharing: Contribute to documentation of data flows, models, and processes to help improve team knowledge and onboarding.
  • Continuous Improvement: Identify opportunities to optimize data workflows, performance, or tooling, and contribute ideas to improve how data is used across the organization.
  • Learning & Growth: Actively learn new tools, technologies, and best practices related to data engineering and analytics in a production environment.

Qualifications

Competencies, skills, and attributes:

  • Co-op Enrollment: You must be enrolled in a university or college co-op program to be eligible for this opportunity.
  • Availability: You must be available for a full-time co-op term from May 4, 2026 – August 28, 2026.
  • Foundational Technical Skills: Experience with or coursework in areas such as data structures, databases, data processing, or software development.
  • Programming Experience: Comfortable writing code in at least one programming language commonly used for data work (e.g., Python, SQL, or similar).
  • Analytical Mindset: Curious, detail-oriented, and interested in understanding how data flows through systems and supports business decisions.
  • Problem-Solving Skills: Ability to break down problems, ask good questions, and work through ambiguous data challenges.
  • Collaboration & Communication: Comfortable working in a team environment and explaining technical concepts at a high level.
  • Curiosity & Willingness to Learn: Motivated to learn new tools, technologies, and best practices, and open to feedback and mentorship.

Bonus points if you:

  • Have experience with SQL, data modeling, or data visualization tools.
  • Have worked with cloud platforms, APIs, or ETL/ELT workflows.
  • Have completed personal, academic, or open-source projects involving data.
  • Are interested in how data supports product, operations, or business strategy.
  • Have experience or interest in working at a startup or scale-up environment.

Location

The role will require you to work from our office in Centretown 5 days per week.

RVezy perks

  • Employee recognition platform (https://bucketlistrewards.com/) to reward employees’ hard work.
  • Frequent social events and a steady supply of coffee and snacks in the office.
  • Dog-friendly work environment in the Ottawa office.
  • Rotating work hours and a relaxed dress code.
  • Company laptop and equipment provided.

How to apply

Want to stand out? At RVezy, we’re looking for someone who is willing to go that extra mile, so we’re asking you to do a little more than just click the “Apply Now” button.

  1. Go to RVezy.com and create an account.

  2. Click “Rent an RV” and search Yellowknife. Using the filters at the top of the page, select “Drivable” as the RV type and find the RV with the title RVezy Elite.

  3. When you're on the listing, scroll toward the bottom and find "Ask a Question."

  4. In the message to the owner, introduce yourself and tell us about a time you went camping (or share another outdoor or travel experience) and tell us why you would be a great fit for the position. We want to get to know you! Enter whatever dates and location you please.

  5. Then just submit your resume with the rest of the application as normal.

RVezy is committed to providing an accessible recruitment process. If you require accommodations at any stage of the hiring process, please let us know and we will work with you to meet your needs.
