Full Description

Requisition ID: 252027

Join a purpose driven winning team, committed to results, in an inclusive and high-performing culture.

The Role The Collateral Management and Funding team is looking for a result-oriented Associate Software Engineer who can work on applications that integrate with different platform and technologies The successful candidate will need to work closely with customers, business Analyst and other team members. Solid hands on experience in building real-time applications and batch processes. Must possess good analytical and problem-solving skills.

Is this role right for you? In this role you will:

  • Independently develop and implement solutions to meet business need
  • Provide third level application support when required
  • Deliver the assigned task(s) as per project plan

Skills Do you have the skills that will enable you to succeed in this role? We’d love to work with you if you have:

  • 1--3years’ experience with OOP Programming, Java, Enterprise Edition Java - J2EE
  • Good working knowledge in databases such as Oracle and Sybase:SQL queries, PL/SQL, SQLLoader

  • Must be familiar with Unix/Linux environment and proficient in shell scripting

  • Bash, ksh

  • Experience with Messaging Bus technologies

  • MQ, JMS

  • Experience with DevOps

  • Bitbucket, Jenkins

  • Experience working in capital markets would be an asset
