Full Description

Jeans Jeans Jeans has been established since 1974.

We are a retail denim store (JEANS JEANS JEANS) that is based on exceptional customer service in the heart of the Mile-End. About 12 minutes' walk from Laurier Metro.

We are currently in search of sales representatives to join our friendly team.

Tasks consist of:

  • Building friendly relationships with customers

  • Fulfill requested assignments

  • Willing to take on challenges
