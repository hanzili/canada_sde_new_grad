About the Role

Support HR operations: data entry, recruitment, onboarding

About Acclaim Health

Healthcare nonprofit; inclusive culture and growth opportunities

Full Description

PRIMARY RESPONSIBLITIES:

  • Assist in with the day-to-day efficient operation for the HR Department
  • Data entry for Administration and HR databases
  • Record keeping and maintaining employee records and files
  • Assisting with recruitment
  • Creating and modifying forms and documents
  • Assist with the on-boarding of new employees including the preparation of new hire documents
  • Assisting in developing and modifying PowerPoint presentations
  • Sending out and receiving mail
  • Providing customer service
  • Other tasks relating to projects

QUALIFICATIONS, EDUCATION, EXPERIENCE & SKILLS:

  • Persons who are between 15 to 30 years of age are eligible.
  • Be a Canadian citizen, permanent resident, or person to whom refugee protection has been conferred under the Immigration and Refugee Protection Act for the duration of the employment
  • Have a valid Social Insurance Number at the start of employment and be legally entitled to work in Canada in accordance with relevant provincial or territorial legislation and regulations.
  • Enrolled in full time studies, Human Resources or Business Administration
  • Strong written and verbal communication skills
  • Proficient with Microsoft Office (Word, Excel & PowerPoint)
  • Demonstrated ability to work independently and collaboratively as a team member

SALARY & COMPENSATION:

$17.60 per hour

Must be legally eligible to work in Canada
