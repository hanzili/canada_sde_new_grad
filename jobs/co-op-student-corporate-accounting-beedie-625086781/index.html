Position Overview

Join our energetic Finance team as we complete our period-end reporting and various other projects. The objective of the finance team is to accurately capture, report, and analyze financial and non-financial information to provide the business with exceptional decision support.

This role is open to students available for a 4-month co-op term starting in May 2026.

Key Responsibilities

  • Recording daily deposits and cash transactions into the accounting system.
  • Preparation of routine and ad-hoc journal entries.
  • Preparing bank reconciliations and other general ledger reconciliations.
  • Assisting with the month-end and quarter-end close processes.
  • Assisting in compiling financial reporting packages, including assisting in preparation of financial statement files and working papers.
  • Supporting various ad-hoc accounting projects.
  • Performing clerical and administrative duties to support the finance function.
