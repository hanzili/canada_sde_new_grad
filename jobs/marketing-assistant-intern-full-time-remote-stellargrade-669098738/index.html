Full Description

About the Company

We are a growing, fast-paced company focused on building our brand and expanding our digital presence. Our mission is to create meaningful connections with our audience through high-quality content, authentic storytelling, and impactful marketing strategies.

We operate in a fully remote environment, valuing autonomy, creativity, and collaboration. As part of our team, you’ll have the opportunity to contribute directly to our visibility and growth while gaining hands-on experience in modern marketing practices.

About the Role

We are looking for a motivated and creative Marketing Assistant Intern to join our team. This internship is designed for students seeking hands-on experience in digital marketing, content creation, and brand visibility.

  • ️ Important: This internship must be completed as part of a recognized academic program and requires formal approval or academic credit from the candidate’s school.

Key Responsibilities

  • Create and schedule engaging content for platforms such as LinkedIn
  • Develop customer stories, testimonials, and case studies
  • Support the execution of social media strategies to increase brand visibility
  • Conduct research on industry trends and competitors
  • Assist in drafting marketing materials (posts, newsletters, light copywriting)
  • Track and report on content performance and engagement metrics
  • Collaborate with the team on marketing campaigns and growth initiatives

What We’re Looking For

  • Currently enrolled in a Canadian college or university program (marketing, communications, business, or related field)
  • Internship must be approved or credited by the school
  • Must be legally eligible to work in Canada
  • Must be based in Canada for the duration of the internship
  • Strong writing and communication skills (English required; French is a strong asset)
  • Interest in social media marketing and storytelling
  • Basic familiarity with LinkedIn and content creation tools
  • Self-starter with the ability to work independently in a remote setting

What You’ll Gain

  • Real-world experience in digital marketing and brand building
  • Portfolio-worthy projects (content, campaigns, case studies)
  • Mentorship and feedback from experienced professionals
  • Exposure to startup/growth marketing environments

Location: Fully Remote

Duration: 3–6 months (Full-Time)

Start Date: Spring 2026
