Junior Project Coordinator –CDN Concrete Inc

Location: Delhi, Ontario

Compensation: $28–$38/hour based on experience

Full-Time Position

We are a growing concrete construction company looking for a reliable, organized, and motivated Junior Project Coordinator to join our team.

This role is ideal for someone who enjoys a mix of office work, job site coordination, customer interaction, and hands-on problem solving. No two days are the same.

Responsibilities:

  • Assist with small job quoting and pricing

  • Coordinate materials, suppliers, and subtrades

  • Pick up and deliver materials/tools as needed

  • Assist with scheduling and job coordination

  • Communicate with customers regarding smaller projects

  • Help track project costs and basic job profitability

  • Support invoicing and project administration

  • Visit job sites to ensure operations are running smoothly

  • Solve day-to-day operational issues quickly and professionally

What We’re Looking For:

  • Strong communication and organizational skills

  • Basic Spreadsheet skills

  • Construction experience is an asset

  • Comfortable using cell phones, email, and basic computer programs

  • Ability to multitask and work independently

  • Mechanically inclined and willing to learn

  • Valid driver’s license with clean driving record

  • Positive attitude and strong work ethic

  • Not afraid to get your hands dirty

This position has strong opportunities for growth within the company for the right individual. We are looking for someone who wants to build a long-term career in construction/project management.

To apply, send your resume and a brief introduction outlining your experience and why you’d be a good fit.

Pay: $28.00-$38.00 per hour

Benefits:

  • Dental care

Experience:

  • Construction: 5 years (preferred)
  • Construction management: 2 years (preferred)

Licence/Certification:

  • Driving Licence (required)

Work Location: In person
