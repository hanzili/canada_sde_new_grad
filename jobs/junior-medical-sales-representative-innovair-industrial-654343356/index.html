About the Role

About Innovair Industrial

Medigas Manitoba: medical gases and equipment supplier

Full Description

Medigas Manitoba is a proudly Canadian, Winnipeg-based independent distributor of compressed gases and medical supplies and equipment. We are a dynamic organization, recognized for our leadership, community involvement and commitment to provide our employees with a great work environment, opportunities for growth and competitive rewards.

Now Hiring a Junior Medical Sales Representative

  • Excellent Salary (commensurate with experience)
  • Full Benefits Package
  • Growth and Development Opportunities

As a Junior Medical Sales Representative, you enjoy building relationships and helping healthcare providers deliver better care to their patients. You take pride in connecting with clients, understanding their needs, and providing solutions that support patient health and comfort. In this role, you will help grow Medigas’ client base while supporting existing customers. You thrive in a fast-paced environment where no two days are the same. Whether you are visiting clients, following up on opportunities, or supporting current accounts, you stay organized, manage your time effectively, and keep multiple priorities moving forward. You enjoy being out in the field, meeting new people, and building trusted relationships with customers. You are a team-oriented professional who takes pride in delivering excellent service. Detail-oriented and reliable, you are comfortable balancing client interactions with documentation and reporting. Most importantly, you find purpose in knowing the work you do helps improve patient care and quality of life.

As a Junior Medical Sales Representative, you will:

  • Build and maintain strong relationships with healthcare providers, clinics, and referral partners, supporting the growth of Medigas’ respiratory therapy services and medical equipment offerings.
  • Identify and pursue new business opportunities within the assigned territory by prospecting, meeting with potential clients, and developing partnerships with healthcare professionals.
  • Promote and educate customers on Medigas’ products and services, including sleep apnea therapy equipment, medical oxygen solutions, and related respiratory supplies.
  • Conduct regular client visits to support existing accounts, strengthen relationships, and ensure customers receive reliable service and timely product support.
  • Respond to customer inquiries, provide product information and recommendations, and coordinate with internal teams to ensure client needs are addressed efficiently.
  • Maintain accurate records of sales activities, customer interactions, and opportunities within CRM systems and internal reporting tools.
  • Collaborate with internal teams including operations, clinical staff, and customer service to ensure a seamless experience for healthcare partners and patients.
  • Support product demonstrations, training sessions, and educational opportunities for healthcare providers and partners.
  • Contribute ideas and feedback to continuously improve the sales process, customer experience, and the way Medigas delivers services to its clients.
  • Perform other duties and participate in projects as required.

The successful candidate will have the following qualifications:

  • Post-secondary education in Business, Marketing, or a related field is preferred and a minimum of two (2) years of experience in sales, business development, or a customer service-oriented role, with a demonstrated ability to build strong client relationships.
  • Excellent communication and interpersonal skills, with a natural ability to develop trust and rapport with healthcare professionals, partners, and customers.
  • Strong organizational and time management skills, with the ability to manage multiple priorities and maintain accurate documentation in a fast-paced environment.
  • Demonstrated problem-solving abilities with a proactive approach to identifying client needs and delivering effective solutions.
  • Proficiency in Microsoft Office applications (Outlook, Word, Excel, PowerPoint). Experience working with CRM systems or databases is considered an asset.
  • Ability to work independently while also collaborating effectively within a team-oriented environment.
  • Valid Class 5 Manitoba Driver’s License and a clear driving abstract.
  • Physically capable of lifting, pushing, and pulling equipment weighing up to 25 lbs.

At Innovair Group, we recognize the profound impact our people have in fostering a work environment that is inclusive, innovative and ensures a sense of belonging for all. As such, we are intentional and self-reflective about the team we are building by seeking talented individuals who bring both incredible skills and a desire to support the success of others. We strive to create an equitable and accommodative recruitment process. We welcome all applications from individuals who would contribute to further diversification of our company. If you require accommodation during any stage of the recruitment process, please let our recruitment team know.

We thank all applicants for their interest, but only selected candidates will be contacted for an interview. Short-listed candidates may be asked to take a physical capabilities, psychometric and/or a relevant duties test.
