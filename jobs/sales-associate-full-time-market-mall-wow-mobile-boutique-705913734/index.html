About WOW! mobile boutique

Full Description

Job Title Full Time Sales Associate Reports To Store Manager Location Market Mall, Calgary AB Salary $15.00/hr + commission Requirements 32-40hr/week availability We are actively hiring for this position Our team at WOW! mobile boutique is passionate about providing the best-in-class wireless retail experience - focusing on excellence in every aspect of our business. We focus on a personalized customer experience, providing solutions from Canada’s top wireless carriers, TELUS, Rogers, Koodo, Fido and Chatr. Our exceptional line-up of wireless carriers, latest devices and a wide-array of accessories means we can offer an unbiased solution as unique as our customers. Our team makes an impact every day by offering quality advice to customers across Canada - making WOW! mobile boutique Canada’s premiere wireless retailer.

What you will accomplish

  • Actively engage mall shoppers and follow each step of our sales process.
  • Consistently network, creating and maintaining a list of prospective clients using our CRM tool
  • Generate hype and awareness within a mall environment by being an ambassador for WOW! and the brands we represent
  • Deliver on our brand promise by offering personalized, unbiased, advice.
  • Consistently achieve all key business metrics
  • Offer a best-in-class customer experience
  • Participate in documented coaching conversations
  • Responsible for inventory management receiving, signing, and securing inventory
  • Follow merchandising and daily store cleanliness standards
  • Complete daily operations tasks, such as opening & closing the kiosk
  • Complete all required training modules and tasks
  • Stay current on promotions, products, services and policies
  • Follow our policies and procedures and maintain a high level of integrity
  • Managing cash, point of sale and inventory systems
  • Available to work during mall hours (evenings and weekends) based on operational needs
  • Manage multiple priorities with a demonstrated sense of urgency
  • Ability to easily adapt to change in a fast-paced environment

What we’re looking for

  • Be Ready

  • To initiate conversations with mall traffic

  • To change and adapt in a fast-paced environment

  • Be Real

  • Be your genuine self

  • Approach each customer in a unique and personalized way

  • Be Great

  • Invested in personal and professional growth and development

  • Open to feedback and coaching

  • Customer-facing experience in retail or hospitality using a point-of-sale system

  • Available to work during mall hours including evenings and weekends
  • Wireless retail experience or commission sales experience is an asset

What we offer

  • Full time hours (32-40hrs/week)
  • Competitive salary AND uncapped commission structure
  • 50% of your monthly wireless plan
  • Rewards & Recognition, based on monthly and quarterly performance
  • Group benefits package
  • Dedicated training and coaching to help you reach your goals

Physical Job Requirements

  • Ability to lift 30-50 pounds
  • Ability to stand for extensive periods of time

How We Hire The team at WOW! mobile boutique believes in a fair, transparent and people-first hiring process . Here's what to expect when you apply

  • Application Review We may use AI-assisted tools to help screen resumes for key skills and minimim requirements relevant to the role. All results are reviewed by our hiring team to ensure thoughtful and balanced decision making.
  • Interview Process Our hiring process includes two interview stages. Qualified candidates will be invited to complete an initial interview, by phone or virtually. Those candidates that meet role requirements will then be asked to participate in an in-person Store Visit.
  • Background Check As a part of our hiring process, successful candidates will be required to participate in a backgrounnd check, in accordance with applicable laws and regulations.

We are committed to providing a inclusive and equitalbe hiring expereince and encourage all qualified candidates to apply.
