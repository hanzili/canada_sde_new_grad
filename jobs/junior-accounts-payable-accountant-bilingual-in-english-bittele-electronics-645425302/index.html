Junior Accounts Payable Accountant (Bilingual in English & Mandarin)

at Bittele Electronics

About the Role

Process payables, reconcile accounts, support accounting

About Bittele Electronics

Electronics contract manufacturer with growth in Canada

Full Description

Company Overview
Bittele Electronics has been a trusted provider of Turn-Key PCB assembly services since 2003, specializing in prototype and low-volume electronic contract manufacturing. With facilities strategically located, we deliver comprehensive solutions including PCB fabrication, parts procurement, and final circuit board assembly to meet diverse client needs.

Overview
We are seeking a motivated Junior Accounts Payable Accountant who is bilingual in English and Mandarin to join our finance team. This role offers an exciting opportunity to develop your accounting skills within a dynamic environment, supporting accurate financial operations and ensuring timely processing of vendor payments. Your contributions will help maintain the integrity of our financial records and streamline our accounts payable processes.

Essential Duties and Responsibilities
1. Majorly accounts payable and some accounts receivable activities.
2. Credit Card transaction reconciliation.
3. Office reception and administration.
4. Financial Analysis (Entry Level)

Knowledge, Skills and Abilities

  • Behaves consistently, responsible and dependable in fulfilling obligations;

Diligently checks work to ensure that all essential details have been considered.

  • Notices errors or inconsistencies, and takes prompt, thorough action to correct errors.
  • Enters data into a variety of computer programs quickly, with an acceptable degree of consistent accuracy; double checks data entry carefully; notices when data are missing or look wrong and takes steps to ensure computer files are complete and accurate.
  • Adapts quickly to change and work under tight deadlines, and be cooperative, willing to share knowledge, cross-train, and take on additional responsibilities.

Requirements

  • Diploma or degree in accounting program.
  • Bilingual proficiency in English and Mandarin, with excellent verbal and written communication skills
  • Experience with a variety of computer programs to include Microsoft Office and QuickBooks.
  • Strong verbal and written communication skills, strong organizational/time management skills, and ability to prioritize and multi-task.
  • Strong attention to detail for account reconciliation and journal entries

Join us as a Junior Accounts Payable Accountant to grow your career while supporting our financial operations with enthusiasm and precision. We’re committed to fostering a collaborative environment where your skills can flourish!
