About the Role

Prototype hardware, test sensors/actuators, iterate experiments

About Sanctuary AI

Robotics AI startup, humanoid control systems

Full Description

Your New Role and Team

Sanctuary, a world leader in developing AI-based control systems for humanoid robots, is seeking a 4- or 8-month Hardware R&D Intern starting May 2026, with a preference for candidates available for the full 8-month term. Reporting to the Hardware R&D Lead, you’ll gain a comprehensive understanding of hardware systems, sensors and actuators, instrumentation, and control systems.

The best candidates for this role have excellent mechanical/mechatronics engineering or engineering physics or equivalent background and hands-on experience with fast prototyping, machining, sensors and actuators, mechanical mechanisms, fluid mechanics and heat transfer. Ultimately, you’ll be a valued resource as you contribute to the development and optimization of the actuation systems, sensors, and other instrumentations.

Our Success Criteria

  • Bring hands-on experience in machining, fast prototyping methods, structural simulation, sensors and actuator design
  • Gain cutting-edge career experience as you build and monitor the performance of new prototypes, resolve issues and iterate to improve the performance and resolve the issues of the previous versions
  • Achieve company-wide visibility as you partner with design and mechanical teams using test equipment and assisting with experiments
  • Expand your R&D experience as you develop, prototype and manufacture new parts and systems

Your Experience

Qualifications:

  • Pursuing a bachelor’s degree in mechanical engineering, mechatronics engineering, engineering physics, a related technical field, or equivalent practical experience
  • Knowledge in fluid mechanics, hydraulics and heat transfer
  • Knowledge of CAD, mechanical design, mechanisms and actuators
  • Hands-on experience and deep technical understanding of prototyping methods, machining, and 3D printing

Skills:

  • In-depth knowledge of mechanical design, fluid mechanics (including hydraulics) and heat transfer, sensors and actuators, and mechatronics systems
  • CAD experience (OnShape, SolidWorks, etc.), machining and power tools experience, and hands-on experience with fast prototyping methods
  • Excellent problem-solving and communication skills
  • Excellent organizational and process documentation skills

Traits:

  • Above all else, a consistently positive attitude and a willingness to do whatever it takes to create robust solutions to complex problems
  • Optimistic listening and conflict resolution capabilities
  • Demonstrated ability to influence others without authority
  • Eager to take on new challenges with tenacity and positivity
  • Patience, persistence, and attention to detail when resolving performance issues
  • Obsession with bringing human-like intelligence to machines

About Sanctuary AI

Founded in 2018, Sanctuary builds humanoid robots and a novel control system for them that integrates symbolic logic and reasoning with data-driven robot foundation models. We use our robots to collect vision, audio, touch, and proprioception data from the perspective of the robot while they perform real-world work tasks. We use that data to train multimodal robot foundation models. Because our systems are vertically integrated, we can design, deploy, and refine at scale. Our mission is to create the world's first human-like intelligence in general-purpose robots.
