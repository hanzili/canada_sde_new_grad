About the Role

Process AR invoices and maintain accounts receivable

About BioScript Solutions

Specialty pharmacy; fast-growing Canadian firm

Full Description

At BioScript Solutions, we’re committed to simplifying access to specialty care for people with chronic health conditions and rare diseases.

Your future starts here

We are looking for a first, second or third year Student to join us at one of our specialty pharmacies across Canada for the summer term. The scope of this role will allow for you to support the pharmacy team in a variety of ways.

Why BioScript Solutions?

At BioScript, we're not just a company—we're a fast growing company always putting patients first. As pioneers in pharmaceuticals, we believe in pushing boundaries, setting trends, and crafting experiences that captivate and inspire. Our vibrant team is made up of brilliant minds who are passionate about driving success and making an impact. Ready to be a part of this exhilarating journey?

Your mission:

  • Manage general administrative duties including maintaining effective communication channels with pharmacies, partners and patients via email/phone while maintaining detailed documentation of all encounters.

  • Work with financial information, including entering data into computer software, preparing invoices, and maintaining patient AR accounts.

  • Participating in responding to communication from various stakeholders, including monitoring email accounts and answering phones. Exemplary patient centered care in all interactions with both internal and external stakeholders.

  • Ensure documentation is completed and submitted according to the timeline outlined in the Standard Operating Procedure (SOP) including Incident Reports, Adverse Event Reports, and Product Quality Complaints.

  • Future development of this role may also include remote support of other pharmacy activities.

  • Responsible for reporting any patient safety information as per adverse event reporting requirements.

Job Status: Summer Employment. You must be currently enrolled in a post-secondary institution and returning in the fall of 2026.

Job Location: Remote, but must be located near one of our specialty Pharmacies.

Application Deadline: May 13, 2026

Compensation: At BioScript, your base pay is one part of your total compensation package and is determined within a range. Our pay ranges are based on the local cost of labor benchmarks for each specific role, level, and geographic location.
