About BlueDot

Full Description

Description

If you are interested in a high-agency role building modern interfaces that can help make humanity healthier and safer, this could be the role for you.

BlueDot is a globally recognized infectious disease intelligence provider, using AI combined with human expertise to anticipate, understand, and respond to threats. BlueDot provides infectious disease intelligence that empowers enterprise organizations and governments to rapidly detect, understand, and respond to infectious disease threats, and ultimately strengthen readiness and resilience. We work with governments at all levels, globally-leading private organizations and multinational health organization—our products contribute to the protection of over 700 million people.

We are Diversio certified, and previously recognized as a Best Workplace for Technology, Women and for Giving Back by Great Place to Work. In 2023, BlueDot ranked no. 25 on Globe & Mail's Top Growing Companies and as one of Canada's most impactful companies by Power 50.

Responsibilities

  • Learn our frontend stack — its patterns, performance considerations, and structural choices — and apply them in your work
  • Translate well-scoped tickets into shipped frontend work; ask good questions of PM and design when requirements are unclear
  • Contribute to our transactional and lifecycle email infrastructure under guidance
  • Build primarily in TypeScript and modern CSS
  • Make occasional C#/.NET changes where the frontend meets the backend, with support
  • Participate in CI/CD improvements for the frontend
  • Use AI coding tools effectively and keep evolving your skills as the tools change
  • Actively unblock yourself by seeking feedback, context, and pairing with teammates
  • Apply accessibility (WCAG 2.1 AA) and performance (Core Web Vitals, render perf) practices in your work

Required

  • Experience delivering well-scoped features end to end
  • 1–2 years of professional frontend experience (internships, bootcamp projects, or substantial personal projects count)
  • Direct experience with TypeScript and modern CSS
  • React experience (or equivalent depth with a component framework)
  • Experience thoughtfully experimenting with and using the right AI tools for your coding practice

Strongly preferred

  • Experience delivering self-directed projects end-to-end (personally or professionally)
  • Accessibility (WCAG 2.1 AA) familiarity

Not required, but an added bonus if you have some of the following:

  • A track record of improving efficiency of releases
  • Comfortable making C#/.NET changes
  • Experience shipping or improving a transactional or lifecycle email system
  • Exposure to performance, observability, or deliverability platforms
  • Cloud architecture experience, especially Azure (AWS and GCP count too)
  • Python

What we offer:

  • Meaningful work that truly has purpose
  • A remote-first work environment
  • Direct mentorship from experienced senior team members
  • Experience shipping real code used daily by a growing company
  • As a smaller, agile team, we offer roles with impact
  • Your contributions are integral, your voice will be heard
  • A generous, well-rounded benefits package

Together let’s create a healthier, safer, and more prosperous world.

For more information, visit us at: bluedot.global
