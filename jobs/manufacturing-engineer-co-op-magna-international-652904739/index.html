Group Summary The Mechatronics, Mirrors and Lighting group specializes in automotive technologies that are driving the future of mobility. Combining a deep systems knowledge to develop unique vehicle access experiences, intelligent visions systems and advanced automotive lighting technologies, MML's expertise light the path to innovation, safety and styling.

Job Responsibilities

  • Salary: $21.00-$23.00/Hour
  • Assist engineers in developing and defining concepts and manufacturing processes for all new product introductions and product enhancement programs using advanced engineering techniques.
  • Assist in evaluating and implementing innovative, cost effective, safe, manufacturing processes and techniques which will improve the effectiveness of the manufacturing operations and business systems.
  • Ensure all product validation testing requirements are completed per the DVP&R
  • Assist in developing manufacturing planning, initial tooling requirements and costs, capability requirements, control plans, and assessments, which facilitate the definition and specification of the manufacturing processes.
  • Serve as a resource for cause, effect analysis, investigations, statistical studies and corrective action planning.
  • Assist in developing and implementing manufacturing flows, work station layouts and organization, which optimize value added operations, operating costs and product/process quality.
  • Comply with defined Business Management and Environmental Management Systems requirements.
  • Provide support for the continuous improvement initiatives within Techform.
  • Provide support in the attainment of Techform' s operating system goals as outlined by the key graphs.
  • Carry out all duties focusing on the goal of internal and external customer satisfaction.
  • Other duties as may be assigned by the supervisor or designate.

Qualifications And Skills

  • Enrolled in post-secondary degree or diploma in Mechanical Engineering or equivalent is required
  • Manufacturing experience is an asset.
  • Strong written and verbal communication skills.
  • Attention to detail is key;
  • Ability to use Outlook, Word, Excel, 3D viewers, and PowerPoint is an asset.
