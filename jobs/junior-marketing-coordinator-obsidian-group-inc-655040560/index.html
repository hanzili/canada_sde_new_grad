Junior Marketing Coordinator

at Obsidian Group Inc.

📍 Location Mississauga, ON
📋 Details Full-time · Permanent · $50k-$55k per year · N/A

About the Role

Execute social campaigns, content, and email programs

About Obsidian Group Inc.

Canadian hospitality group expanding franchise outlets nationwide

Full Description

Company Overview

Obsidian Group Inc. is a management company focused on developing, marketing, and managing hospitality and real estate interests. We are one of Canada's leading, fastest growing, and most progressive hospitality companies. We love hospitality, and as a restaurant operator, we are rapidly expanding our restaurant outlets across Canada. Our passion for hospitality is reflected in our franchise outlets such as Roadhouse, Coffee Culture, and others. Join us to be part of a passionate team of professionals who share an entrepreneurial spirit and are dedicated to providing exceptional service while driving profitability for our franchisees.

Responsibilities

  • Collaborate with influencers and other content creators to build brand recognition on all social platforms.
  • Set and implement social media and communication campaigns to align with marketing strategies.
  • Develop compelling graphics and visual assets using Adobe Creative Suite.
  • Assist in generating, editing, publishing, and share daily content that fosters community engagement.
  • Assist in monitoring and optimizing social media accounts across platforms such as Google, Facebook, Instagram, Twitter, Snapchat, Yelp, TikTok, YouTube, etc.
  • Assist in executing social media strategies through research, benchmarking, messaging, and audience targeting.
  • Design and implement all public-facing marketing materials.
  • Develop and create email blasts and execute with hubspot and other direct email campaigns.
  • Trade show set up, and coordination of all material required.
  • Assist in all marketing and advertising in support of franchise business development.

Our ideal Marketing Coordinator will have:

· Excellent communication, interpersonal, and relationship skills.

· Good project management skills.

· Ability to adapt techniques based on requirements, being a fast learner.

· An open mindset and will seek out feedback to refine and develop their skills.

· The ability to problem solve.

  • must have experience with word press and elementor.

· Good understanding of digital marketing platforms.

· BA in marketing, communications, or equivalent.

· At least 1 year of work experience in a marketing-related position.

Job Types: Full-time, Permanent

Pay: $50,000.00-$55,000.00 per year

Benefits:

  • Dental care
  • Extended health care
  • On-site parking
  • Paid time off
