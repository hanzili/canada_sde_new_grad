Full Description

Content Creator — Short-Form Video & AI

Remote | Full-time | TikTok · Reels · Shorts

ABOUT THE ROLE

We're looking for a content creator who doesn't wait for trends — they set them. You'll be the creative engine behind our short-form video strategy across TikTok, Instagram Reels, and YouTube Shorts. You get the algorithm, you love AI tools, and you can make a SaaS product feel exciting to watch.

WHAT YOU'LL OWN

  • Concept, shoot, edit, and publish short-form videos (TikTok, Reels, Shorts) — multiple times per week

  • Use AI tools to accelerate production: scripting, voiceover, visuals, subtitles, B-roll

  • Develop original series formats that build audience and brand affinity over time

  • Stay ahead of TikTok/Reels trends and remix them into on-brand content fast

  • Script and produce short cinematic or storytelling-style videos that showcase the product and its impact

  • Monitor analytics weekly — views, watch time, follower growth, saves — and double down on what works

  • Collaborate with the team on launches, campaigns, and reactive content moments

YOU'RE A FIT IF YOU HAVE

  • A portfolio of short-form video work with real engagement data (not just follower count)

  • Deep understanding of TikTok's algorithm — you know why videos go viral and can replicate it

  • Hands-on experience with AI content tools — not just curious, actually using them daily

  • Comfortable on camera OR skilled enough in editing/AI to produce without being on screen

  • Strong storytelling instinct — you can turn a feature demo into a compelling 30-second narrative

  • Self-directed: you pitch your own ideas, not just execute briefs

AI & TOOLS WE EXPECT YOU TO KNOW

  • CapCut / Descript

  • ElevenLabs / HeyGen

  • Runway / Kling

  • Captions.ai

  • Claude / ChatGPT

  • Midjourney / Flux

BONUS POINTS

  • Own TikTok / YouTube channel

  • Short film or cinematic background

  • Motion graphics (After Effects)

  • Podcast or long-form experience

  • Grew a brand from 0 to 10k+

  • SaaS or tech content niche

COMPENSATION

Competitive salary + equity. We give full creative freedom — no approval hell. If a video flops, we learn. If it blows up, we reward it.

Pay: $17.20-$40.03 per hour

Work Location: In person
