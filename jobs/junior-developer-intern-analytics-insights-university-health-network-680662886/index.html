Full Description

Company Description At The Princess Margaret Cancer Foundation, our mission is to create a world free from the fear of cancer. Cancer remains the number one cause of death for Canadians and our top priority is to accelerate cancer research and care.

Our role is to garner support for Princess Margaret Cancer Centre, one of the world’s leading cancer research and treatment centres, through philanthropy, fundraising events, and our world-renowned lottery program. The work we accomplish drives groundbreaking research, relentless innovation, and a deep commitment to improving the lives of cancer patients and their loved ones.

Now, we have an opportunity to blaze an even brighter future for everyone affected by cancer.

We will Carry The Fire for cancer patients everywhere, lighting the way forward through discovery, reigniting our commitment to transform cancer outcomes and ease the profound suffering cancer causes patients and their loved ones.

At the PMCF, every one of us carries the fire for someone else. Join us as we inspire a movement and help us achieve our vision to change the way we understand, diagnose, and treat cancer.

Job Description Union: Non-Union

Department: Customer and Business Operations

Reports to: Manager, Business Intelligence Reporting & Development

Hours: 35 hours

Hourly Rate: $22.50/hour

Status: Temporary, Full Time (12-month contract)

Vacancy: Existing Vacancy - Backfill

Posted Date: May 8, 2026

Closing Date: May 24, 2026

We are seeking a Junior Developer who is passionate about utilizing data and analytics to help us deliver on our mission. We are looking for someone who will proactively drive improvements in performance across our businesses: philanthropy, lottery, events, and marketing.

In this role, you will be a key member of the team focused on creating supporter contact lists and delivering data requests, as well as supporting analysis and reporting initiatives. You will work with teams to ensure data solutions deliver value by deepening supporter engagement, driving results, and creating efficiencies. You are a resourceful problem solver that is motivated to learn and improve. This role will report to the Manager of Reporting & Development.

You Will

  • Develop, test, and deploy data solutions using a high level of expertise in querying data from our data warehouse.
  • Implement effective list management practices; automate/optimize the list build process, validate the data quality, track list usage, report on results, and recommend improvements.
  • Partner with Data Engineer to create standard views in the data warehouse that integrate multiple data sources (CRM, MailChimp, Finance) and maintain clear, up-to-date documentation outlining data definitions, transformation logic, source mappings, and usage guidelines to support consistency and knowledge sharing across teams.
  • Support various stakeholders to extract relevant data for analysis and work with the Manager of Reporting in building dashboard solutions (Power BI) that deliver key performance metrics and forecasting.
  • Support the Foundation’s data quality efforts by partnering with Business Operations to fix and maintain the integrity of our data.

Qualifications

  • Currently enrolled undergraduate or graduate student, preferably in computer science, mathematics, engineering, or related field. Co-op accepted.
  • Experienced with SQL – able to write structured and efficient queries.
  • Proficient with Python – strong understanding of core programming principles, able to transform data, and maintain automation scripts and internal utility libraries.
  • Proficient with Git – able to version control scripts and collaborate using branching and pull requests.
  • Strong documentation skills – able to clearly capture business processes and code logic to support collaboration and future maintenance.
  • Experience in dashboard development, data analysis, research, or similar field considered an asset.
  • Familiarity with machine learning and AI concepts is considered an asset
  • Strong communication skills, both written and verbal.
  • Ability to shape and develop requirements into practical solutions.
