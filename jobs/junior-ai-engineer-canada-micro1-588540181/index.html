Job Title: Junior AI Engineer

Job Type: Full-time

Location: Canada (Remote)

Job Summary:

Join our team as a Junior AI Engineer and help us push the boundaries of Generative AI applications. You‚Äôll collaborate with passionate colleagues to design, build, and scale advanced GenAI systems that power innovative solutions. This is an exciting opportunity for a hands-on engineer eager to grow their skills while building impactful, production-grade AI features.

Responsibilities:

  1. Contribute to the development of GenAI applications such as answering engines, entity extraction tools, and summarization systems.

  5. Version Control: Git fundamentals and collaborative development.

Good-to-have Skills:
