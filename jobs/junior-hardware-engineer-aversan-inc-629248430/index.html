Full Description

Junior Hardware Engineer

Aversan Inc. (www.aversan.com) is a trusted multi-service engineering and electronics manufacturing company. Aversan delivers leading-edge and reliable safety-critical electronics and software systems to the aerospace, defence, and space industries.

We are currently seeking a qualified Junior Hardware Engineer to join our team. The Junior Hardware Engineer will design new electronic circuits based on product requirements, perform circuit analysis, and create detailed hardware schematics. You will be responsible for preparing design review materials, and collaborating with systems, software, hardware, and mechanical teams to analyze and generate design ideas.

Location: Mississauga, ON

Job Type: On-site

Responsibilities

• Create detailed hardware schematics.

• Perform circuit analysis using LTSPICE and PSPICE.

• Design new electronic circuits using suitable components and equipment including product prototypes, test interface, and test equipment.

• Prepare materials (Block Diagram, Schematic, Design Description, Tolerance Analysis) for design reviews (PDR, CDR).

• Work with software, hardware, and mechanical team members to analyze and generate design ideas.

Basic Qualifications

• A Bachelor’s Degree in Electronics or Computer Engineering, or equivalent.

• 0-1 year of experience in electronics design involving Microcontrollers, Microprocessors, Memory Devices, Ethernet, ADC, SPI, I2C, USB2.0, RS232, RS422, A429, Discrete, Switching Power Regulators.

• Experience with schematic design entry in OrCAD is an asset not mandatory.

• Experience with LTSPICE and PSPICE are preferred.

• Experience with Root Cause Analysis and Debugging experience is an asset

• Experience with embedded C is an asset.

• Prior working experience in the Aerospace industry is an asset.

Please note:

Interested applicants must apply directly to the employer to be considered for this position: apply@aversan.com.

The subject of the email should be “Junior Hardware Engineer Application”. The name of the file for the resume should be the applicant’s full name. The resume format should be PDF.
