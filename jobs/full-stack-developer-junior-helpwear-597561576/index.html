About HelpWear

Clinical-stage medical device startup.

Full Description

Background

HelpWear is an exciting clinical-stage startup who is pioneering a new type of heart monitoring system that will identify patients who are at a high risk of suffering a massively debilitating event such as a stroke or syncopal episode (faint/fall due to loss of consciousness). Many of these patients only have random and sporadic cardiac abnormalities, so we are developing the first device that can be comfortably worn as long as needed to ensure that the next actionable event is recorded. After being placed on the patient's upper arm, the HeartWatch continuously records medical-grade electrocardiogram (ECG) signals that give doctors the information they need to make the best treatment decisions to protect their patients. HelpWear was founded out of (and is still based in) St. Michael’s Hospital in Toronto, Ontario and has a second office based in the University of Calgary’s Life Science Innovation Hub. We’re currently a team of ~25 and are looking to find more talented individuals who share our passion for improving patient lives!

Job Description

The HeartWatch is a continuous, clinical-grade ECG monitor worn on a patient’s arm to enable the prevention and treatment of heart-related illness. In addition to novel hardware, the HeartWatch has several interconnected software systems: a smartphone app, desktop software, and cloud computing system to collect, store and transfer information to patient’s healthcare providers. As HelpWear’s Full-Stack Developer you will be responsible for developing the HeartWatch product that is rolled out to customers. You will be reporting to the VP of Engineering. You will be responsible for:

  • Develop and maintain Django backend services.
  • Develop and maintain the Python Qt application used to review ECG data and generate a customer facing summary report.
  • Develop and maintain the React frontend web application to deliver customers clinical summary reports.
  • Support the development and maintenance of AWS infrastructure.
  • Follow HelpWear’s cybersecurity policies and participate in HelpWear’s regulatory discussions to ensure the HeartWatch’s regulatory approvals
  • Collaborate with the business teams (including the CEO) to develop system requirements for the HeartWatch.
  • Work in a team of enthusiastic developers keen on growing their knowledge.

Requirements

  • Bachelor's Degree in Computer Science, Software Engineering, Computer Engineering, or a related field.
  • 1+ years of work experience, ideally on a medical device/software that has gone to be approved for use in the US or Canada.
  • Proficient in Python and JavaScript.
  • Experience with AWS or a similar cloud service provider.
  • Excellent attention to detail and problem-solving skills.
  • An excellent team player who can work efficiently with minimal supervision.
  • Must be based in Toronto.

Preferences

  • Experience with Django or a similar Python web framework.
  • Experience with React or a similar JavaScript framework.
  • Experience with Qt or a similar application-development framework.
  • Experience with Docker.
  • Experience with PostgreSQL or any major RDBMS.
  • Experience developing and/or maintaining CI/CD pipelines.
  • Experience working with time series data at scale (e.g., IoT).
