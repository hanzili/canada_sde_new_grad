Junior Accountant / Bookkeeper – Fast-Paced, Real-World Experience

at Volleybox Sports Centre Ltd.

📍 Location Surrey, BC
📋 Details Full-time · Permanent · $28.00-$38.00 per hour · N/A
📅 Posted Mar 27, 2026

About the Role

Turn receipts into clean books; reconcile accounts daily.

About Volleybox Sports Centre Ltd.

Small firm; real client exposure, growth potential.

Full Description

Tired of repetitive, corporate accounting work?

This is not a typical bookkeeping role.

You will work directly with an experienced accountant handling 50 to 100+ client files. Many of these come in messy, incomplete, and high-volume, especially during tax season.

If you want real experience, not just ticking boxes, this is where you get it.

What You Will Be Doing

  • Turning boxes of receipts and disorganized records into clean, accurate financials
  • Cleaning up and reconciling messy or incomplete books
  • Full-cycle bookkeeping including AR, AP, and bank and credit card reconciliations
  • Preparing files for tax reporting and year-end
  • Working across multiple businesses and industries
  • Using QuickBooks and/or Sage regularly
  • Communicating with clients to clarify missing or unclear information

What We Are Looking For

  • 1 to 3 years of accounting or bookkeeping experience
  • Experience with QuickBooks and/or Sage (both is a big plus)
  • Strong attention to detail and problem-solving ability
  • Comfortable working in a fast-paced, sometimes chaotic environment
  • Excellent verbal and written English communication skills
  • You will be working directly with clients and need to communicate clearly and confidently
  • Someone who takes pride in turning disorganized information into something clean and usable

This Role Is NOT For You If

  • You want predictable, repetitive work
  • You prefer highly structured, corporate environments
  • You are not comfortable working through incomplete or messy records
  • You are looking for a low-responsibility, low-growth role

Why This Role Is Different

  • Direct mentorship from an experienced accountant
  • Exposure to real client work, not just one part of accounting
  • Rapid skill development. You will learn more here in months than most roles offer in years
  • High ownership and responsibility early on

Long-Term Opportunity (For the Right Person)

This role has serious upside.

For someone ambitious, there is a potential path to:

  • Taking on more client responsibility
  • Growing into a senior role
  • Eventually stepping into a partnership or ownership position

This is a rare opportunity to work closely with an established accountant who is looking to gradually step back and transition client relationships over time.

Who This Is Perfect For

  • Someone early in their career who wants to accelerate their growth
  • Someone leaving or considering leaving a large firm environment
  • Someone who wants real responsibility, real exposure, and real upside

Job Type: Full-time

Pay: $28.00-$38.00 per hour

Expected hours: 40 per week
