Human Resources Assistant

at Quinte Health

📍 Location Belleville, Ontario
📋 Details Temporary · 12 months
📅 Posted Apr 01, 2026

About the Role

Coordinate HR onboarding and maintain employee records

About Quinte Health

Regional health system, strong HR operations

Full Description

Position Summary The Human Resources Administrative Assistant provides administrative and operational support to the Human Resources department and contributes to the efficient delivery of HR services across the organization. This role serves as a key point of contact for employees, managers, and external partners, providing professional and responsive administrative support.

The Human Resources Administrative Assistant supports a variety of HR functions including onboarding and orientation, employee records management, HR reporting, benefits and pension administration, and general departmental operations. The role requires strong organizational skills, attention to detail, and the ability to manage multiple priorities while maintaining a high level of confidentiality.

This temporary full-time role will be approx. 12 months.

Required

  • Diploma or certificate in Human Resources, Business Administration, or Office Administration.
  • 1-2 years of administrative experience, preferably within Human Resources or a healthcare environment.
  • Experience working with HR information systems and Microsoft Suite applications including Outlook, Excel, and Word.
  • Strong organizational and time management skills with the ability to prioritize work and manage multiple competing demands.
  • Excellent interpersonal, communication, and customer service skills.
  • Demonstrated accuracy and attention to detail.
  • Ability to maintain confidentiality and handle sensitive information with discretion.
  • Ability to work both independently and collaboratively within a team environment.

Duties

  • Provide front desk coverage for the Human Resources department and act as a first point of contact for employee and manager inquiries.
  • Respond to general HR inquiries in person, by phone, and through the HR email inbox.
  • Maintain accurate employee information within HR systems, including updating employee records as required.
  • Process employee information changes such as name changes, direct deposit updates, and employment status changes.
  • Support the administration of new hire onboarding including system setup, tracking documentation, and assisting with orientation preparation.
  • Generate and distribute HR reports including probationary status reports and other recurring departmental reports.
  • Support the preparation of employment verification letters and respond to documentation requests from employees and external organizations.
  • Assist with recruitment processes including issuing candidate testing and providing candidate support when required.
  • Maintain electronic personnel files through scanning, electronic filing, and document management.
  • Support records management processes including maintaining terminated employee files and assisting with records retention activities.
  • Coordinate orientation materials required for new employees.
  • Order and maintain HR office supplies and coordinate business card requests.
  • Coordinate courier pick-up and delivery and manage internal mail distribution.
  • Maintain HR administrative areas and meeting rooms to ensure they remain organized and stocked.
  • Provide general administrative support for HR initiatives, meetings, and departmental activities.
  • Perform other related duties as assigned.

At Quinte Health, our four hospitals unite under the core values of Imagine It’s You, Value Everyone, We All Make a Difference , and Stronger Together . We work as one team to enhance lives, improve access to high-quality care, and build healthier communities—right where people need it most.
