CPA Co-Op Student - Summer

at Baker Tilly Canada

📍 Location Winchester, Ontario

About the Role

Assist audits, tax returns, and bookkeeping

About Baker Tilly Canada

Reputable mid-size Canadian accounting firm

Full Description

Be part of something bigger. Build with us today — Now, for tomorrow.

At Baker Tilly, we care about the impact you make. That’s why we’re invested in your growth from day one—because the steps you take today define your tomorrow.

We are looking for a CPA Student to join our team—a role where your expertise will drive meaningful outcomes and your ambition will help shape what comes next. Here, you’ll collaborate with a dynamic team of professionals, united by a shared commitment to making a difference—for our clients, our communities, and one another.

Baker Tilly is a leading advisory and accounting firm dedicated to building better futures. With global reach and local roots, we combine the scale of a network with the trust of strong relationships. Your role

  • Will include preparation and development of skills under the guidance of a manager/senior staff member in a wide variety of areas relevant to your studies including but not limited to:
  • Preparation of personal, corporate, non-profit organization information and charitable tax returns
  • Execution and completion of a wide range of Audit, Review and Compilation engagements, both independently and as part of a team
  • Bookkeeping basics including entry and reconciliation of monthly and quarterly bookkeeping assignments including but not limited to payables, receivables, bank statements, credit card statements and journal entries, GST/HST and source deductions using manual and computerized bookkeeping systems
  • Accounting basics including preparation and analysis of general ledgers and monthly financial statements, calculation of fixed assets and depreciation as well as preparation of other statistical, financial, and accounting reports

What you bring to the table

  • Enrolment in an accounting program
  • High proficiency in MS Office (Word, Excel, Outlook, Teams) and technical competencies to execute work at the direction received up front from other experienced staff using Caseware/Caseview, TaxPrep, QuickBooks Online and/or any other tax preparation software. Any prior experience with these programs is an asset
  • Excellent attention to detail, interpersonal, communication and organizational skills
  • Sound judgement regarding confidential information and sensitive matters
  • Professional manner, strong work ethic and ability to work both independently and as part of a team
  • Strong understanding of a client-focused fast paced work environment including related deadlines and the ability to work to meet those deadlines
  • The ability to accept feedback and apply feedback to future engagements

What’s in it for you?

  • wellness and fitness incentives,
  • flex time, and
  • a comprehensive benefit package
