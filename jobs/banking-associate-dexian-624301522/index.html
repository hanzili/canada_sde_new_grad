Full Description

Job Title: Banking Support Associate

Location: Toronto, ON (Hybrid)

Duration: 12 months contract (with possibility of extension/conversion)

Key Responsibilities:

  • Deliver superior client service to Private Wealth clients, ensuring professionalism, courtesy, and timely support.
  • Assist Private Wealth Advisors by managing credit, non-credit, and related administrative activities.
  • Respond to client inquiries, resolve concerns, and escalate issues appropriately.
  • Process transactions accurately and ensure adherence to internal policies, regulatory requirements, and risk guidelines.
  • Maintain detailed knowledge of personal and commercial banking products, operational processes, and systems.
  • Support workflow process improvements to increase efficiency and service levels.
  • Collaborate with internal teams and external stakeholders to meet business objectives.
  • Participate in audits, compliance reviews, and other regulatory activities as assigned.
  • Analyze data and provide insights or recommendations to support client service and business growth.

Must-Have Qualifications & Skills:

  • 2‚Äì3 years of experience in banking, financial services, or private wealth support.
  • Post-secondary education in finance, business, or related field (or equivalent experience).
  • Working knowledge of personal (primarily) and commercial credit and non-credit products.
  • Familiarity with banking operational processes and lending policies.
  • Excellent verbal and written communication skills.
  • Strong organizational skills and ability to prioritize in a fast-paced environment.
  • Exceptional collaboration, problem-solving, and analytical abilities.
  • Commitment to compliance, risk management, and professional conduct.
