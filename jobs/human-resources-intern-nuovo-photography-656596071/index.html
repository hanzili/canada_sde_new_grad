About the Role

Support HR operations, onboarding, and policy docs

About Nuovo Photography

Creative photography studio; fast-growing, people-focused culture

Full Description

Nuovo Artistic Studios is the premier destination for all things self-love. A by-women company and one of the largest artistic photography companies in North America, Nuovo is on a mission to empower individuals by fostering confidence and self-expression while celebrating intimacy and individuality.

As a Human Resources Intern, you will gain hands-on experience supporting our People & Culture manager in day-to day operations and contribute to HR projects that impact the employee experience. This internship is designed to provide real-world experience and allow you to develop practical skills in HR administration and make an impact on our company culture.

This is a great opportunity for someone eager to learn, grow, and explore a career in Human Resources.

This Internship will take place from May 4th, 2026 to August 28th, 2026

What You’ll Do:

  • Maintain and update HR documentation, policies, employee records, and support day-to-day administrative tasks.
  • Draft HR documents, including contracts, letters, and official communications.
  • Track and manage incoming HR requests, providing timely and accurate responses.
  • Assist in the development, review, and implementation of HR policies, ensuring compliance with internal standards and legal requirements.
  • Support internal HR communications, employee onboarding/offboarding, and initiatives to enhance the employee experience.
  • Contribute to HR projects and process improvements, helping to structure workflows and optimize efficiency.
  • Handle sensitive employee information with discretion and support the HR team on employee-related matters.

Requirements

About You:

  • Currently completing a Bachelor’s degree in Human Resources, Labour Relations, or a related field.
  • Knowledge in Labour Relations in Canada & USA is an asset
  • Strong ability to maintain confidentiality and exercise sound judgment.
  • Comfortable working in a fast-paced environment.
  • Strong organizational and communication skills.
  • Fluent in English, French and Spanish is considered a strong asset

Benefits

What We Offer:

  • Inspiring Work Environment: Immerse yourself in a creative, collaborative company where artistry and empowerment converge.
  • Growth Opportunities: Be part of a fast-growing company committed to nurturing talent and fostering career development.
  • Recognition and Support: A team that values your contributions and celebrates your success.
  • Exclusive Perks: Access to Nuovo’s services and products, ensuring you, too, can experience the power of portraiture. We also provide you with a 50% Family & Friends discount.

About Us:

Nuovo Artistic Studios is not just a photography studio; it’s a movement designed to uplift, empower, and transform. Our mission is to provide every guest with a luxury experience that inspires confidence and celebrates individuality. With studios across North America, we are redefining the art of modern portraiture, one empowered story at a time.

This is your opportunity to join a team dedicated to excellence and artistry. Become part of the Nuovo legacy and make an impact, one unforgettable moment at a time.

This is your moment. Apply today!
