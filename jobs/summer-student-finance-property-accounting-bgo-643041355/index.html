Summer Student – Finance (Property Administration)

Toronto, ON | 1 York (Hybrid)

Join Us to Grow, Connect, and Make an Impact.

At BGO Properties, we’re dedicated to enhancing personal and professional growth, fostering meaningful connections, and giving back to the communities where we operate.

As one of Canada’s premier providers of commercial property management and leasing services, BGO Properties manages a diverse portfolio of over 400 properties, totaling more than 67 million square feet of residential, office, industrial, and retail space. For over 30 years, our clients have trusted us to deliver innovative real estate solutions that create value and support their success.

As part of the global BGO family, we leverage international expertise and local market insights to provide high-quality services. Our experienced team works across a variety of commercial real estate sectors, which ensure every property we manage is a welcoming and functional destination for tenants.

Whether you’re launching your career or advancing as a seasoned professional, BGO Properties offers opportunities for professional growth, continuous learning, and leadership development.

Join us and help shape the future of commercial real estate.

The Opportunity: Are you a driven individual eager to gain practical experience in accounting, lease management and financial administration? BGO is offering an exciting Accounting Internship opportunities where you'll collaborate with our accounting and lease administration group to tackle diverse tasks, from administrative duties, lease reviews to the preparation of vital financial reports.

Available Opportunities

  • Property Accountant
  • Banking Administrator

What you will do:

The Accounting Intern will report to the Controller and will be responsible for some or all the tasks listed below:

  • Assist with the preparation of property financial statements.
  • Assist with the preparation of property budgets.
  • Provide support to Property Accountants relating to daily, monthly, and annual accounting activities:
  • Maintain various Excel spreadsheets for reconciliation purposes.
  • Complete analytical reconciliations as required.
  • Preparation of bank reconciliations
  • Preparation of monthly GST/HST reconciliations
  • Realty Tax Refunds
  • Document filing and merging mainly using PDF Converter
  • To take on additional work as required and as delegated.

Sustainability

  • Embrace and proactively support BGO's culture of environmental responsibility and social responsibility.

Who You Are:

  • At a minimum, completion of first year of Business/Commerce Program at a recognized university
  • Relationship Skills- ability to communicate effectively, both oral and written
  • Organizational/Multi-task Skills- ability to allocate one’s time effectively, work under pressure, and meet tight deadlines.
  • Computer Skills- intermediate to advanced skills in Excel, Word, and other Microsoft Office programs; knowledge of Yardi an asset
  • Police Clearance – A criminal verification check is required for employment.

The Expected Base Pay Range: $20 - $25/ per hour. The Base Pay range is for the primary location for which the job is posted.
