TekStaff's Client has a current vacancy for a Junior Business Analyst.

Pay Rate Range: $35 - $43

Job Description

This is a 10 months contract located in Toronto/Ontario, Hybrid

(3 days on site, 2 days work from home - 4 days on site come 2026.)

Job Requirements:

• Demonstrated expertise in identifying and organizing complex project requirements across multiple stakeholders.

• Proven ability to advocate for team requirements and priorities throughout project planning and execution phases.

• Experience in setting targets, defining clear objectives, and developing detailed work-back schedules to meet deadlines.

• Support process documentation, through capturing and documenting processes and procedures to support different teams within Merchant Solutions Group.

• Exceptional self-motivation and a strong work ethic, consistently driving projects forward.

• Track record of effectively influencing and impacting individuals at all organizational levels.

MUST-HAVE Hard Skills:

1.) Excel and MS Office/Projects proficiency

2.) Strong requirement gathering skills

SOFT SKILLS:

1.) Strong documentation skills

2.) Strong organization skills

3.) Strong time management skills

4.) Strong communication skills (written and verbal)

NICE-TO-HAVE

1.) Vendor management experience, considered an asset

2.) Prior banking or financial institution experience

3.) Experience with "point of sale" projects
