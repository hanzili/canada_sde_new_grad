Company description

ABSOPULSE Electronics Ltd. is an original equipment manufacturer (OEM) specializing in the design, development and manufacture of heavy-duty, switching power supply solutions for the railway, industrial automation, automotive, power utility, mining, marine and telecommunications sectors. Founded in Ottawa in 1982 to meet a market requirement for custom designed, not-off-the-shelf power supplies, ABSOPULSE has designed and built thousands of power conversion products that operate in the field with high reliability. The company’s line of professional quality power conversion products includes AC-DC power supplies, rectifiers and battery chargers, DC-DC power converters, DC-AC sine wave inverters, AC-AC phase and frequency converters (single and three-phase), complete power systems in 19″ and 23″ racks, DC-input backlight inverters and electronic ballasts.

Positions open

We are looking for a Junior Project Coordinator to join in our team, which is at the west end of Ottawa (110 Walgreen Rd, Ottawa, ON K0A 1L0). Training will be provided by ABSOPULSE.

Key Responsibilities:

· Monitor processing of the project documentation (electrical, mechanical, bill of materials).

· Ensure that all relevant information from the quote binder is loaded into the project binder.

· Co-ordinate the work of clerks, assemblers and technicians in the following areas:

production, purchasing, inventory of materials (components and metal parts), receiving, shipping and finished goods stock.

· Ensure adherence to deadlines, maintain realistic project schedule, report expected delays to Operations Manager and propose recovery plans to get schedule back on track.

· Ensure that any changes inquired during the production process are reported to Engineering for approval and properly documented in the appropriate Project binders.

· Report to shipping Administration as early as possible (2-3 days) in advance when shipments are expected to be completed and ready for shipping.

· Prepare and submit progress and other reports on a weekly basis.

· Participate in daily coordination meeting.

Requirements/Qualifications:

· Ability to follow work instructions.

· Ability to work independently or as a part of a team.

· Effective communication, verbal and written.

· Fast Learner with strong motivation for self-improvement.

· Detail-orientation with excellent organization and time management skills.

How to apply: Please send your resume to Yao@absopulse.com
