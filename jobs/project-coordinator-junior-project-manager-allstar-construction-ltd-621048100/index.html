Full Description

Project Coordinator / Junior Project Manager

Shallow Utility Construction

Edmonton, AB

We are a Leading shallow underground utility construction contractor operating throughout the Edmonton area. We install power, street lighting, telecom, and gas infrastructure. We primarily operate in the Land Development Industry but also have a portfolio of work completing rehab/renewal work. We are looking to add a Project Coordinator or Junior Project Manager to support our senior project management team and help drive projects from start to finish.

This is a role designed for someone with 1–5 years of experience who wants real responsibility, exposure to field operations, and a clear path for growth.

Key Responsibilities

  • Order and track materials to support active projects
  • Coordinate between field crews, suppliers, and office staff
  • Support senior Project Managers with scheduling, documentation, and project tracking
  • Review drawings and assist with estimating and cost tracking
  • Conduct site checks to monitor progress and identify potential issues
  • Maintain organized project records and ensure deadlines are met

Required Skills & Experience

  • 1–5 years of experience in construction, civil, or underground utility work
  • Strong Excel skills and general comfort with numbers
  • Ability to read and interpret construction drawings
  • Exposure to estimating or cost tracking is a strong asset
  • Highly organized and detail-oriented
  • Valid driver’s license and ability to travel to job sites across Edmonton

Qualifications

  • Post Secondary degree or diploma will be an asset
  • 1-5 years experience in construction or construction related fields
  • Class 5 License

  • Motivated to learn and grow into increased responsibility over time
  • Someone willing to put in extra hours as required
  • Comfortable attending industry events after hours

Work Environment

  • No remote work
