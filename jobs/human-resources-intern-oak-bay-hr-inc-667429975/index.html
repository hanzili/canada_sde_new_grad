About Oak Bay HR

Oak Bay HR Inc. is a Halifax-based recruitment and HR consulting firm focused on helping growing organizations build strong, sustainable teams. We partner with companies across Atlantic Canada in industries such as green energy, manufacturing, and emerging sectors to deliver practical, people-first HR solutions.

The Opportunity

We are looking for a motivated and detail-oriented HR Intern to join our team for the summer. This is a great opportunity for a student or recent graduate interested in gaining hands-on experience in recruitment, HR operations, and consulting.

You will work directly with the Founder and gain exposure to real client projects, candidate engagement, and HR strategy work.

What You’ll Do

  • Support recruitment projects
  • Assist with job posting creation and posting across platforms (LinkedIn, Indeed, etc.)
  • Support HR policy and handbook development
  • Assist with employer branding initiatives
  • Provide administrative support including scheduling interviews and maintaining candidate records
  • Contribute to special projects as needed

What You Bring

  • Currently enrolled in or recently graduated from a program in HR, Business, or a related field
  • Strong interest in recruitment and HR consulting
  • Excellent communication and organizational skills
  • High attention to detail and ability to manage multiple tasks
  • Comfortable using tools like Microsoft Office, LinkedIn, and Google Workspace
  • Self-starter with a proactive and positive attitude

Nice to Have

  • Previous work experience in HR or recruitment
  • Familiarity with applicant tracking systems (ATS)

What You’ll Gain

  • Hands-on experience in recruitment and HR consulting
  • Exposure to a variety of industries and clients
  • Mentorship and direct coaching from an experienced HR leader
  • Opportunity to build your professional network in Atlantic Canada
