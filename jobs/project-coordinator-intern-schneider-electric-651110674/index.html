Project Coordinator - Intern

at Schneider Electric

📍 Location Milton, Ontario Hybrid
📋 Details Intern · CAD 21.00/hour
📅 Posted Mar 26, 2026

About the Role

Assist project tasks; update orders

About Schneider Electric

Global electrical solutions leader; strong training

Full Description

What will you do? The intern will be introduced to project management by performing project management activities and will be exposed to the technical side of the electrical distribution field by doing changes to existing orders.

What qualifications will make you successful for this role? We are looking for a current student or a recent graduate (within the last 12 months) from a university program such as Electrical Engineering or a related field.

To be successful in this role, you should bring:

  • A foundational understanding of electrical distribution systems (low and medium voltage).
  • Exposure to or coursework in project management (considered an asset).
  • Well-developed critical thinking, problem-solving, and decision-making abilities.
  • The ability to plan, organize, and work independently with general guidance.
  • Clear and effective communication and collaboration skills.
  • Attention to detail and the ability to manage multiple tasks efficiently.
  • Proficiency in MS Office tools (Excel, Word) and Adobe Acrobat.

What's in it for you? The base pay compensation for this full-time position is starting at CAD 21.00 per hour for candidates who are O.N. residents.
