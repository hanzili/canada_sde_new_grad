Full Description

Job Description & Summary Ready to grow your career and make a real impact from day one?

At PwC, you’ll join a team that helps organisations meet their tax and accounting obligations in a world that’s constantly changing. You’ll learn from experts, work on meaningful projects and develop the skills to shape your future — and ours.

Key Responsibilities: As a Tax Accountant Associate, you'll work as part of a team of problem solvers, helping to solve complex business issues from strategy to execution. Specific responsibilities include but are not limited to:

  • Proactively assist the team in various aspects of the project
  • Prepare deliverables
  • Contribute to the development of your own and team’s technical acumen
  • Ensure you are adhering to compliance matters
  • Work on developing internal relationships and your PwC brand

We are looking for candidates that have the following qualifications and skills:

  • Bachelor/Master degree, preferably in finance, accounting, economics, math, statistics
  • 2-3 years of experience in accounting and tax compliance
  • Very good knowledge of English
  • Good knowledge of any accounting software (preferably SAP and/or Navision)
  • Registration in ACCA, SCAAK (for Kosovo applicants) or other relevant qualifications will be considered a plus.

If you want to advance your career in tax and accounting and want a place where you can learn, grow and make an impact, we’d love to hear from you. Grow here. Go further.
