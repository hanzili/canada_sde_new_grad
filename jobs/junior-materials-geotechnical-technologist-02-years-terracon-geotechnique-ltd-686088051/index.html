About the Role

Perform field geotechnical QA/QC testing and inspections

About Terracon Geotechnique Ltd

Field-focused geotech firm with strong safety culture

Full Description

Why Choose Terracon?

We know you have choices when it comes to your career. At Terracon, we focus on building a culture where employees feel supported, challenged, and empowered to grow. Our core values guide everything we do:

  • A collaborative, inclusive, and safety-focused work environment
  • A comprehensive, cost-shared benefits program supporting physical and mental well-being
  • Innovative savings and investment programs to help you reach your financial goals

About the Role

Terracon is seeking Junior Technologists to support a variety of field-based projects including geotechnical and civil engineering, QA/QC construction monitoring, drill monitoring, geo-environmental monitoring, site investigations, foundation inspections, and soil and concrete laboratory testing.

This full-time, field-based role is ideal for recent graduates or early-career professionals looking to build practical, hands-on experience. Working alongside experienced technologists and engineers, you will develop strong technical and field competencies. An April or May start date is anticipated.

Location & Work Environment

  • Primarily based in Fort McMurray
  • Fieldwork includes 12-hour day and night shifts
  • Work environments range from active mining sites to municipal infrastructure projects

Key Responsibilities

  • Perform soil and concrete QA/QC testing and monitoring under supervision
  • Assist with field inspections, earthworks monitoring, and concrete testing
  • Verify construction activities meet project specifications and standards
  • Accurately complete field reports and documentation
  • Communicate effectively with project team members using two-way radio systems
  • Drive company vehicles in active construction and mining environments
  • Adhere to all safety policies, procedures, and site-specific requirements
  • Support positive client and team relationships

Compensation

$31.00 – $33.00 per hour + overtime, depending on education and experience

What We Look For

  • Diploma in Civil Engineering Technology, a degree in Civil Engineering or related field
  • 0–2 years of relevant field or construction-related experience
  • Legal authorization to work in Canada
  • Valid driver’s license and clean driver’s abstract
  • Ability to perform physical fieldwork and pass a fitness-for-work assessment
  • Willingness to work in safety-sensitive environments and meet drug and alcohol testing requirements
  • Strong work ethic, eagerness to learn, and a team-focused mindset

Additional Assets

  • Winter driving or off-road driving exposure
  • Safety certifications (BSO/CSO, H2S)
  • Interest in professional membership (ASET or APEGA)
