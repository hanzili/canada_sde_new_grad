About Softworks Group Inc.

Full Description

Company Overview

Softworks Group Inc. is a Canadian software company specializing in purpose-built registrant management software for professional regulatory authorities. Our flagship product, Alinity™, is the leading registrant management system for healthcare professions in Canada and is quickly expanding into other regulated professions. We pride ourselves on providing an energetic, respectful, and inclusive work environment where every team member is valued.

Summary

Are you early in your software development career and eager to grow your skills in a collaborative, real-world environment? Softworks Group Inc. is looking for Associate Software Engineers to join our Implementation Team. This is a great opportunity for individuals with a strong foundation in software development who are enthusiastic about expanding their expertise - particularly in database technologies - while working on a large-scale application used by hundreds of thousands of users.

In this role, you will work closely with clients to configure and customize Alinity™ to meet complex business needs. Your contributions will directly impact the success of our clients and the evolution of our platform.

To be eligible for this position, applicants must meet the following criteria:

  • Be a Canadian citizen or permanent resident, and legally authorized to work in Canada as of the application date.
  • Demonstrate proficiency in SQL/T-SQL, JavaScript, and HTML by successfully completing two online technical assessments as part of the application process.
  • Reside in Edmonton and be available to work onsite at our office full-time during the initial training period, and then on a hybrid schedule of 3 days in-office and 2 days remote per week.

Role description:

  • Create complex queries and stored procedures in SQL/T-SQL.
  • Configure custom web forms in XML and JavaScript.
  • Develop custom reports in Power BI™.
  • Convert data from other systems into Alinity™.
  • Collaborate with clients and team members to understand requirements.
  • Conduct testing and debugging to ensure high-quality software delivery.

Required qualifications:

  • You must be eligible to work in Canada now, without requiring sponsorship.
  • You must be living in Edmonton or willing to move to Edmonton without requiring a relocation allowance.
  • Degree or diploma in IT or a related field.
  • Intermediate skills in SQL/T-SQL, HTML, and JavaScript.
  • Excellent written and verbal communication skills in English.
  • Strong problem-solving skills and high attention to detail.
  • Ability to work collaboratively in a team environment.

Our interview process:

  • Chat Q&A (15-30 minutes)
  • Technical assessments (1-2 hours)
  • Initial interview (30 minutes)
  • Take-home assignment (1-2 hours).
  • Final interview (1-2 hours)

We offer:

  • Work-from-home 2 days per week.
  • Exceptional healthcare and dental benefits.
  • Company supplied high-performance workstations.
  • Exceptional learning opportunities and professional development program.

If you're ready to kick start your career in software engineering and join a rapidly growing Canadian company, we invite you to apply today to Softworks Group!

At Softworks, we respect and celebrate the unique attributes, characteristics, and perspectives that make each person who they are. We also believe that bringing diverse individuals together allows us to build better products and a better overall company. Softworks is an Equal Opportunity Employer.
