About Graham

Full Description

Build What Matters At Graham, one of North America’s largest general contractors, we don’t just build projects, we enable the systems and services that make construction possible. As part of our Corporate Services team, you’ll support impactful work across the region through expertise in HR Advisory, Communications, Supply Chain, Finance, and more.

Here, your work has purpose. Your growth is supported. And your impact is lasting.

Join a team that builds with commitment, reliability, and integrity. Apply today and help us build what matters.

Your Impact Starts Here Graham is actively recruiting for a Jr. FP&A Analyst supporting Corporate Services. Reporting to the Financial Planning and Analysis Manager, the role plays a key role in leading moderately complex initiatives that span departments and support mid-sized systems or process changes.

Your Role in Building What Matters – From Office to Site

  • Maintain budgets and forecasts by entering, updating, and ensuring accuracy of annual budgets and quarterly forecasts in the enterprise planning system
  • Monitor and analyze financial performance, including monthly variance analysis against budget and forecast with clear explanation of business drivers
  • Prepare financial reports and materials for recurring, ad hoc, monthly, forecast, and budget review presentations
  • Support business partnering by responding to financial questions and assisting stakeholders with financial data and processes
  • Ensure data accuracy and integrity by reconciling financial information, maintaining consistency, and troubleshooting data or reporting issues
  • Provide planning and reporting system support, translating planning discussions into accurate financial data within integrated financial systems

The Right Tools for the Job

  • Education: Bachelor’s degree or diploma in Business, Finance, Accounting, or a related field
  • Experience: 0–4 years of relevant experience in Financial Analysis, Accounting, or FP&A (or an equivalent combination of education and experience)

  • Software & Technical Tools:

  • Experience with integrated financial systems (EFM / ERP); SAP and Microsoft preferred

  • Advanced Microsoft Excel and familiarity with Power BI for reporting and data visualization

  • Exposure to Power Query and DAX (considered an asset)

  • Certifications: CPA, CFA, or similar professional designation or in-progress
