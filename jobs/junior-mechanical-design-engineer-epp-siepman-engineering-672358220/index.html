About Epp Siepman Engineering

Full Description

Epp Siepman Engineering invites individuals like you to step into a key role as a Junior Mechanical Design Engineer.

Epp Siepman Engineering is one of Winnipeg’s largest independent mechanical and electrical consulting engineering firms, specializing in the building construction industry. Our project experience includes a wide range of projects in the educational, commercial, health care, industrial, and hospitality sectors. We also provide engineering services such as feasibility studies, life-cycle costing reports, energy modeling, and systems commissioning. Our portfolio of projects offers an environment that fosters collaboration, fuels creativity, and facilitates personal and professional growth. Our open and collaborative work culture is designed to inspire innovation and teamwork.

Our firm's goal is a flexible design approach that will assist clients in meeting their project objectives. We value integrated design solutions that minimize environmental impacts through sustainable design. Our priority is to provide high-quality service to our clients through clear communication, dedicated personnel, and a team approach.

As a Junior Mechanical Design Engineer, you will play a key role in our projects, bringing your experience and technical expertise to the table. Your responsibilities will include working within our internal project team of engineers and support staff, supporting the mechanical design process and ensuring compliance with codes, industry standards, and best practices.

Your Roles

Project Management & Design:

  • Work within our mechanical and electrical team to deliver great service to our clients.

  • Design building mechanical systems including HVAC, plumbing, and sprinkler systems; collaborate with the engineers and designers in the office and with architects, other consultants, owners, and contractors on building designs.

  • Produce construction documentation using REVIT and our related design programs, coordinate design with other disciplines.

  • Evaluate building heating and cooling loads, energy modeling, size and select equipment.

  • Assist in project delivery throughout construction in conjunction with our construction administration team, including but not limited to, review shop drawings, attend meetings, and issue contract amendment documentation.

  • Coordinate with other design disciplines and project partners.

  • Assist in the development and coordination of specification documents.

  • Conduct site visits and reviews for design and contract administration.

  • Responsibilities will be scaled based on experience level.

Learning & Development:

  • Actively participating in professional associations and committees.

  • Staying current with industry trends and advancements by attending educational workshops and fostering personal networks.

  • Playing an integral role in advancing technical and professional leadership within our firm.

Qualifications: Your Path to Success:

  • Possession of a bachelor’s degree in mechanical engineering from a recognized Canadian (or equal) university.

  • Experience in mechanical building systems design (co-op or internship experience considered).

  • Ability to apply critical thinking and reasoning to decision making process.

  • Ability to effectively communicate and work as part of a team is essential.

  • Desire to develop skills and expand career.

  • Candidate must be self-motivated and be able to manage multiple priorities.

  • Candidate must have extremely strong general computer skills and must be able to learn new programs quickly.

  • Demonstrated experience with office software such as MS Office (Outlook, Word, Excel, Adobe, etc.);

  • Registration or eligibility to register with APEGM as an E.I.T.

  • This position is suited to both recent graduates (EITs) and candidates with early-career experience.

The following qualifications and skills would be considered an asset:

  • Experience with Autodesk Revit.
  • Hands on construction experience would be considered a significant asset.
  • Experience with design and modeling software such as Carrier HAP, Trane Trace, Energy Plus.

In addition to a supportive work environment, Epp Siepman Engineering offers a range of benefits designed to support our team members in both their professional and personal lives. These include:

Flexible Work Schedule: Enjoy the flexibility of a shortened work week during the summer months, allowing you to make the most of the season. Flexible workday; start/stop times.

Work from Home Capabilities: Embrace the convenience of remote work options, to provide you with flexibility when life needs.

RRSP Matching: We invest in your future by matching your contributions to your Registered Retirement Savings Plan, helping you plan for financial security.

PHSP Benefits Plan: Access a Personal Health Spending Plan, ensuring you have the coverage you need for your well-being. This plan covers extended health care claims including eyecare and dental.

As well as other benefits like Group Life Insurance, continued education support and covering the cost of professional dues and other registrations.

Epp Siepman Engineering understands the importance of work-life balance and is committed to the development of team culture. This role also offers opportunities for professional development and advancement within our organization. If you're a self-motivated professional looking to develop your technical and career skills in building systems design, this opportunity is tailored to you. Join Epp Siepman Engineering as a Mechanical Design Engineer and be part of a team committed to pursuing engineering innovation.
