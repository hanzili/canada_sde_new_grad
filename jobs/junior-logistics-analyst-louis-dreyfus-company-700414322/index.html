About the Role

Support rail logistics data, billing, and reporting

About Louis Dreyfus Company

Global commodity trader with extensive logistics network

Full Description

Louis Dreyfus Company is a leading merchant and processor of agricultural goods. Our activities span the entire value chain from farm to fork, across a broad range of business lines, we leverage our global reach and extensive asset network to serve our customers and consumers around the world. Structured as a matrix organization of six geographical regions and ten platforms, Louis Dreyfus Company is active in over 100 countries and employs approximately 18,000 people globally.

Job Description The Junior Logistics Analyst supports the transportation team by assisting with the day‑to‑day execution, data management, and reporting of rail logistics activities serving LDC’s processing plants, terminals, and trading desks. This role provides foundational support across shipment tracking, railcar billing, rate data maintenance, and performance reporting, helping ensure accurate information flow and timely execution. The position is designed to develop core logistics, rail, and commercial knowledge while supporting continuous improvement, data quality, and operational discipline across transportation operations.

Primary Responsibilities/Essential Functions

  • Support day‑to‑day transportation execution by assisting with railcar tracking, shipment status updates, and coordination activities in support of plants, terminals, and trading desks.
  • Maintain transportation data accuracy and integrity, including shipment records, rate data, billing information, and performance metrics used by the transportation team.
  • Assist with transportation reporting and analytics, supporting KPI tracking, performance summaries, and ad‑hoc analysis to improve visibility and decision‑making.
  • Provide foundational operational support to the transportation team, enabling timely execution, consistent processes, and effective information flow across stakeholders.

Additional Responsibilities Functions

  • Administer railcar track‑and‑trace activities, including monitoring movements, identifying exceptions, and escalating issues as needed.
  • Assist with railcar billing and invoice processing, including validation against contracts, rate tables, and shipment details.
  • Support maintenance and upkeep of rail rate databases and tariff files, ensuring current and accurate pricing information is available.
  • Compile and maintain transportation performance reports, including service metrics, dwell time, cycle time, and exception tracking.
  • Assist with demurrage and accessorial charge tracking, supporting documentation, reporting, and issue resolution.
  • Coordinate shipment documentation and record keeping, including billing data, customs-related information, and internal reporting files.
  • Support transportation system updates and data cleanup efforts, including improvements to reporting tools, spreadsheets, and dashboards.
  • Assist with special projects and process improvement initiatives aimed at increasing efficiency, data quality, and operational discipline within the transportation team.

Qualifications

  • Must have post-secondary in a related field of study (Supply Chain, Logistics, etc)
  • Must have 0-3 years of previous work experience in data analysis, operations, or a relevant field.
  • Strong analytical and data management skills, with the ability to accurately enter, maintain, and interpret transportation data, rate tables, and KPI reports using Excel and logistics systems.
  • Attention to detail and financial discipline, particularly when reviewing freight invoices, monitoring ancillary charges (e.g., demurrage), and ensuring data accuracy for reporting and accounting purposes.
  • Effective communication and collaboration skills, enabling clear interaction with senior analysts, traders, plant operations, rail carriers, and accounting partners.
  • Ability to prioritize and adapt in a dynamic operating environment, responding to service disruptions, changing shipment plans, and time‑sensitive requests.
  • Demonstrated learning agility and curiosity, with the ability to quickly build knowledge of rail networks, systems, and internal processes.

Additional Information What We Offer We provide a dynamic and stimulating international environment, which will stretch and develop your abilities and channel your skills and expertise with outstanding career development opportunities in one of the largest and most solid private companies in the world.

  • Retirement Savings Plan with Employer contributions and matching
  • Paid vacation, paid sick time and paid statutory holidays
