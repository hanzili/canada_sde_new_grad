Full Description

About Peregrine Professional Services

Peregrine Professional Services is a modern accounting firm serving entrepreneurs and growing businesses across Canada.

Our primary focus is Client Accounting Services, including bookkeeping, financial reporting, and accounting support. We leverage technology and efficient processes to help clients maintain accurate financial records and make informed business decisions.

We are looking for a Client Accounting Associate to join our team and gain practical experience working with real clients, real businesses, and real accounting engagements.

Students and recent graduates are encouraged to apply.

The Opportunity

This role is ideal for someone interested in building a long-term career in accounting.

You will work directly with experienced professionals while developing practical skills in bookkeeping, financial reporting, accounting software, client service, and business operations.

Successful candidates will have opportunities to expand their responsibilities as they develop their technical and professional skills.

Responsibilities

  • Review and categorize financial transactions
  • Assist with bank and credit card reconciliations
  • Maintain client financial records
  • Support month-end bookkeeping procedures
  • Prepare supporting schedules and working papers
  • Organize client documents and records
  • Assist with bookkeeping and accounting projects
  • Support client information requests
  • Maintain accurate records within accounting software

QualificationsRequired

  • Strong attention to detail
  • Strong written and verbal communication skills
  • Strong organizational and time management skills
  • Ability to manage multiple priorities
  • Professional attitude and willingness to learn
  • Proficiency with Microsoft Excel or Google Sheets

Preferred

  • Currently enrolled in or recently completed a program in Accounting, Commerce, Business, Finance, Economics, or a related field
  • Interest in pursuing the CPA designation
  • Experience with QuickBooks Online or similar accounting software
  • Previous bookkeeping, administrative, customer service, or office experience

What We Offer

  • Flexible scheduling
  • Practical accounting experience
  • Training and mentorship
  • Exposure to a variety of businesses and industries
  • Professional development opportunities
  • Experience working directly with business owners and entrepreneurs

Application Requirements

Please submit:

  • Resume
  • LinkedIn profile (optional)
