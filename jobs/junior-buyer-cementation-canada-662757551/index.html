GENERAL PURPOSE OF THIS JOB (Summary)

Reporting to the Procurement Supervisor, the Junior Buyer is responsible for the sourcing and procurement of goods and services for all departments and site projects within the company, negotiating and analysing prices, establishing delivery times and methods, verifying purchase orders, reviewing backorders and performing related duties and ensuring coverage during their absence from office.

ESSENTIAL DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES

HEALTH AND SAFETY

  • Work in compliance with all Health and Safety rules and regulations for Cementation
  • Be aware of any health and safety hazards or infractions and report same to Health and Safety Department

GENERAL RESPONSIBILITIES

  • Respond to telephone, e-mail, and in-person inquiries from clients, business partners, and other parties.
  • Locate appropriate suppliers via yellow pages, internet, and current database.
  • Prepare, submit and evaluate requests for quotes.
  • Place orders with suppliers.
  • Determine delivery type.
  • Prepare requisitions/quotes for purchase order entry by clerks.
  • Review purchase orders for accuracy.
  • Pull purchase orders for matching with receipts.
  • Process receipts and identify items on back order.
  • Finalize (close-out) purchase orders.
  • Prepare and analyse spreadsheets.
  • Meet with supervisor to discuss issues (areas for improvementother requirements).
  • Provide, file and ensure back-up support is entered in our document management system.
  • Provide back up (when required) for receipting functions and P-Card month end balancing when Receipting and Expediting Admin person is absent
  • Other duties as assigned.

SUPERVISORY RESPONSIBILITIES

  • None

TRAVEL REQUIREMENTS

  • This position would not normally have the requirement to travel.

EDUCATION AND EXPERIENCE

  • Relevant College or University Education.
  • 3+ Years purchasing related experience.
  • Experience in Mining Industry is considered an asset.
  • Certified Supply Chain Management Designation or working towards or enrolled in program would be consider an asset.

SKILLS, KNOWLEDGEABILITIES

  • Able to build and maintain lasting relationships with other departments, key business partners, and other stakeholders.
  • Able to effectively prioritize workload to ensure deadlines are met.
  • Working knowledge of purchasing terms and practices.
  • Superior keyboarding skills and accuracy with attention to detail.
  • Strong knowledge of MS Office software applications including Word, Excel, Power Point, Project, and Outlook.
  • Strong knowledge of Adobe.
  • Proficient in JD Edwards would be considered an asset.
