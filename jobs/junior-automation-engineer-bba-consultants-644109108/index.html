Junior Automation Engineer

at BBA Consultants

📍 Location Québec, Quebec Hybrid
📋 Details Full-time
📅 Posted Mar 15, 2026

About the Role

Program automation systems, perform site surveys, document findings

About BBA Consultants

Canada-based engineering firm serving energy and resources

Full Description

Type of position: Regular

Your future role on our team Are you passionate about automation and the fast-paced world of work sites and industrial plants? Do you want to see the direct impact your work has in the field while working closely with clients?

If so, we’ve got an exciting opportunity for you!

As a CEP in Automation, you'll be joining the Process Control and Operational Technologies department at our Québec City office. This multidisciplinary team will support your career growth, whether you choose to specialize in a niche area or develop a broader, generalist profile. You'll work with a motivated, dynamic group, ready to coach you on projects and guide you throughout your professional journey.

With us, you’ll get the opportunity to...

  • Program automated systems (PLC, SCADA, DCS, operator interfaces)
  • Develop and maintain solid client relationships
  • Participate in executing detailed engineering projects
  • Draft technical documents, calculation notes and study reports
  • Participate in project management (execution, costs, schedules)
  • Attend internal or client coordination meetings
  • P erform site surveys
  • Assist with equipment commissioning, both onsite and remotely

Do your qualities and values match our corporate culture?

  • Results-driven
  • Willingness to work together while having fun
  • Rigour and ethics
  • Interest in solving highly complex challenges
  • Autonomy and adaptability

Summary Of Certifications And Job Requirements

  • Bachelor’s degree in electrical or automated production engineering or any other equivalent training
  • Member of a Canadian professional engineering association
  • Available for short- and long-term work assignments at client locations or sites
  • Valid driver’s licence
  • Advanced level of English to collaborate effectively with BBA offices and/or clients outside Québec. The frequency of English use may range from occasional to daily, depending on ongoing projects
  • Asset: interest or relevant experience in automation and instrumentation; participation in detailed engineering of a project in the mining or energy sectors
  • Asset: experience in the mining sector
  • Asset: ASP Construction card

An overview of BBA’s total rewards

  • Annual base salary
  • Annual premium program for regular employees
  • Onsite mobility premium
  • Time bank
  • Cellphone fee reimbursements
  • Group insurance plan from the first day of employment, including disability insurance and a telemedicine program
  • Retirement savings plan
  • Annual vacation and sick leave
  • Premium for employee referrals

BBA considers many factors when determining the annual base salary, including location, market, education, relevant and demonstrated work experience, technical assessment, qualifications, certifications and other relevant skills.

At BBA, You Get Many Benefits

  • A collaborative team that shares ideas and knowledge
  • Access to many experts and coaching
  • Growing business with many opportunities
  • On site and at the office: Health, safety and the environment are a priority
  • Involvement in innovation projects

About BBA For over 40 years, we’ve been pushing the boundaries of engineering to serve our clients in the Energy and Natural Resources sector. At BBA, we develop innovative, flexible and sustainable solutions, from strategy to execution.

BBA is one of Canada’s leading private consulting engineering firms, focused on practice (PCB), with a network of 18 offices. Our teams work closely to deliver projects that shape tomorrow’s industry at the local, national and international levels.

Our people’s passion and excellence make BBA a Canada’s Best Employer and one of Canada’s Best Managed Companies .
