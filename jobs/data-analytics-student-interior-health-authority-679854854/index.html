About Interior Health Authority

Full Description

Interior Health is hiring a Data & Analytics Student to join the Data Governance & Advanced Analytics Team.

This position qualifies for flexible work location (within the Interior Health region only). This opportunity is available from approximately May to August 2026.

What will you work on? Interior Health is hiring a Data & Analytics Student to support the Data Governance & Advanced Analytics team. The team plays a key role in ensuring data across the organization is well‑managed, trusted, and used responsibly to support clinical care, operations, planning, and analytics.

The Data Governance & Advanced Analytics team supports data stewardship, data literacy, metadata management, and data access through a structured data management and lifecycle approach. This student role will contribute to foundational data governance activities while also supporting more technical work related to metadata, data cataloguing, and documentation that enables analytics and reporting across the organization.

In alignment with Interior Health’s vision and values, the student will work as part of the Data Governance & Advanced Analytics team, collaborating closely with team members and partners across Health Technology & Clinical Services and Data & Analytics. The student will gain hands‑on experience applying academic learning to real‑world data governance and analytics initiatives within a complex healthcare environment.

Some Typical Duties And Responsibilities

  • Assist in the development and documentation of data governance policies, standards and guidelines.
  • Support data lifecycle processes, including archiving, retention, and documentation of data management practices.
  • Maintain and update metadata repositories and data catalog information to support data discovery and understanding.
  • Develop and maintain documentation related to data processes, standards, and definitions.
  • Participate in team meetings, working sessions, and knowledge‑sharing activities, and provide updates on assigned data initiatives.
  • Performs other related duties as assigned.

Salary Range Salary range for the position is $22.66 to $25.75. Interior Health establishes salaries within the minimum and maximum of the salary range based on consideration of the qualifications, experience of the applicant, and an internal equity review of the salaries of other employees.

How will we help you grow? We provide you with an orientation customized to meet your needs, and immediate support and access from excellent colleagues. You will have access to work in an interdisciplinary environment where patients are at the centre of care.

Reasons to Apply at Interior Health... What we can do for you We offer a work environment conducive to growth and development of strong technical skills. The opportunity to have a balanced lifestyle that allows you to maintain the quality of life you desire.
