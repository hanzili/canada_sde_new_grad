Full Description

Position Title: Financial Analyst Summer Student (Analytics & Business Intelligence Focus) – Summer 2026 Position reports to: Director, Finance Position status: Temporary – May 2026 – August 2026 Position category: Full Time Location: Calgary, Alberta Work hours: Monday – Friday 8am – 5pm (40 hours/week)

Calvert Home Mortgage (CHMIC) is a full-service alternative mortgage investment firm that supports homeowners, investors, and brokers to achieve their goals by seeing the person behind every mortgage. We are on a mission to be the most trusted lending solution in Canada by putting people first, focusing on their success, and always doing the right thing.

Simply put, our team is the best in the business. Our expertise and experience are unrivalled -but it’s our caring approach that really sets us apart.

Calvert’s longevity and continued success is due to our shared sense of purpose and the understanding that relationships are far more important than transactions. We were founded on the principle of “kitchen table lending” -taking the time to build trust by listening to our customers, understanding their unique needs and goals, educating them on their options, and finding a solution that’s right for them. That might sound obvious but, in our experience, it’s not the norm.

We are looking for a Financial Analyst Summer Student (Analytics & Business Intelligence Focus) to be a part of an exciting growth-focused company who truly cares about its employees. At Calvert Home Mortgage we are champions of a culture that creates a positive work environment, which will allow you to thrive and be a contributor to the long-term success of the company.

At Calvert Home Mortgage we offer competitive salaries, a bonus program, share ownership, extended health and dental benefits, a free on-site fitness facility, a location that avoids the downtown commute and free parking.

WHAT’S THE BIG IDEA?

As our Financial Analyst Intern (Analytics & Business Intelligence Focus), you’ll help bring our financial and operational data to life. You’ll work alongside our Finance and BI teams learning to analyze trends, track key performance metrics, and build dashboards that help the business understand what’s happening and where we’re headed. From supporting budgeting and forecasting to validating data and improving reports in Excel and Power BI, you’ll gain hands-on experience turning raw data into meaningful insights. Along the way, you’ll contribute to business intelligence initiatives, learn how financial and operational KPIs drive decision making, and get real exposure to how a modern finance team uses analytics, automation, and AI tools to support the business. By the end of the summer, you will leave your role with real-world experience analyzing business performance, building data-driven reports, and a stronger understanding of how analytics and finance work together to guide strategic decisions.

HOW DO WE WORK?

As an organization we aim to be trusted advisors, offering an effortless experience in providing short-term lending solutions for the personal and financial success of our clients. Offering the best solutions is highly dependent on the management of risk and compliance within our industry. This role will be a key contributor with the ability to manage and analyze risk. We value a productive, proactive, and fast-paced office environment.

HOW DO YOU FIT IN?

The successful individual will have the following experience, education, and ability to develop these demonstrated skills:

Currently pursuing a degree in Finance, Accounting, Business Analytics, Economics, or a related field

Strong Excel skills and analytical mindset

Interest in financial analysis, analytics, and BI

Exposure to Power BI, SQL, or analytics tools is an asset

Detail-oriented with strong communication skills

WHAT YOU WILL BE DOING

Support financial analysis, reporting, and performance tracking
Analyze trends, variances, and key financial and operational KPIs
Build and maintain dashboards and reports (Power BI, Excel)
Assist with data validation, reconciliation, and documentation
Support budgeting, forecasting, and ad-hoc financial analysis
Contribute to BI initiatives, including standardized metrics and reporting

WHY CALVERT HOME MORTGAGE?

Calvert Home Mortgage's mission is to be the most trusted mortgage lender in Canada so that our customers, investors and partners succeed and grow in thriving communities. We are a team of focused individuals who care about both our internal and external clients, and who serve each other by delivering the most timely and competitive service.

Imagine building a career you can be proud of, where doing things right is always the right thing to do and where everyone you work with will both support and inspire you. At Calvert, you will be respected for your unique skills and experience, encouraged to show up authentically every single day, and surrounded by some of the best in the industry to help you learn and grow.

WHAT WE OFFER

An opportunity to be a part of a culture committed to truly making a difference in the personal and financial success of others. We are an equitable employer offering a wide range of employee benefits, including a competitive salary, bonus program, opportunity for share ownership and extended health and dental benefits. Full training and support for the individual to be successful in this role will be provided.

For more information about Calvert Home Mortgage Investment Corporation, please visit our website at www.chmic.ca.

Please note that candidates applying for openings must be authorized to work in Canada.

We thank all applicants in advance for their interest, however, only those selected for an interview will be contacted.
