at Cloud Resourcing

📍 Location Toronto, ON
📋 Details Full-time · Permanent · Negotiable pay · N/A

About the Role

Coordinate client onboarding and project tasks

About Cloud Resourcing

Staffing firm for IT/digital health projects

Full Description

Must Haves:

  • 2+ years of experience as a Project Coordinator in IT or digital health environments.
  • Hands-on experience with client support, client integration, and business process implementation.
  • Strong stakeholder engagement and communication skills.
  • Experience supporting product integration projects.
  • Knowledge of Identity and Access Management (IAM) framework.

Desired Skills:

  • Experience in the digital health ecosystem
  • Proven ability to work within IAM framework
  • Strong analytical, problem-solving, and communication skills
  • Ability to work in a high volume, fast-paced environment
  • Experience with business intelligence tools, dashboards, or reporting systems
  • Knowledge of ITSM practices

Required Skills:

  • Solid understanding of data integrity principles and quality assurance.
  • Strong customer service orientation and interpersonal communication skills.
  • Ability to interpret and update technical/process documentation.
  • Strong organizational skills with the ability to multitask and meet SLA timelines.

Required Experience / Evaluation Criteria:

  • Project coordination, business operations or technical onboarding experience. 20 points

  • Experience supporting identity and access operations or similar authorization processes. 35 points

  • Proven customer service, client support and stakeholder engagement experience. 35 points

  • Experience assisting with business process improvement and documentation initiatives. 10 points

Total 100 points

Deliverables

Deliverables Include:

  • Process Implementation: Support implementation of business processes and provide input to process improvements.
  • Operations Support: Coordinate client integration, with focus on client engagement, client support, and end-user authorization.
  • Project Support: Provide project coordination support for product integration initiatives.
  • Documentation: Provide input to process documentation, user guides, and operational workflows.

Responsibilities:

  • Operational responsibilities:
  • Coordinate operational activities including client support, stakeholder requests, incidents and escalations within Service Level Agreement timelines.
  • Execute IAM processes including end-user authentication and authorization.
  • Support communication and engagement plans for initiatives.
  • Provide input to process improvements and process documentation aligned with evolving business needs.
  • Project Responsibilities:
  • Provide project coordination support for product integration initiatives, including IAM activities and Business Acceptance Test (BAT) case execution.
