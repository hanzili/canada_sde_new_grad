About the Role

Research AI use cases; implement automation solutions.

About Sopra Steria

Global IT consulting and digital services leader

Full Description

Sopra Steria, a major Tech player with 51,000 employees in nearly 30 countries, is recognized for its consulting, digital services and solutions. Sopra Steria provides end-to-end solutions to support large enterprises and boost competitiveness by combining in-depth knowledge of a wide range of business sectors and innovative technologies with a collaborative approach. We are committed to achieving sustainable results and placing people at the heart of technology. In 2024, the Group generated revenues of $9.4 billion. The world is how we shape it. For more information, visit us at www.soprasteria.ca.

Are you ready to take on the challenge with us? Sopra Steria Infrastructure & Security Services (I2S) is a subsidiary of Sopra Steria dedicated to infrastructure, cloud, and cybersecurity activities. It plays a key role in Sopra Steria's end-to-end services. With 3,000 employees, Sopra Steria I2S stands out for the flexibility of its delivery model, combining client proximity from our 8 agencies with the advanced industrial capabilities of our offshore service centers.

Job Description We are seeking a passionate AI Solutions Engineer (Co-op/Internship program) to help drive automation and efficiency across our business operations. This internship offers hands-on experience in researching processes, identifying automation opportunities, and collaborating with experts to implement AI solutions. Are you adaptable, a strong critical thinker, and have a keen interest in artificial intelligence? Then this may be the role for you.

  • Start date: ASAP / Flexible
  • Co-op/Internship Length: 4-8 months+, with possible full-time offer based on performance
  • Location: Toronto

Key Responsibilities

  • Research, analyze, and identify opportunities to streamline and improve internal and external processes on a large scale
  • Work with experts to put the provided use cases into action and foster innovation
  • Support in implementing process improvements and tracking their impact on team productivity and customer experience
  • Document research findings, proposed solutions, and implementation steps
  • Stay updated on the latest AI and machine learning tools, frameworks, and trends

Qualifications

  • Currently enrolled in or recently graduated from a Bachelor’s or Master’s program in Computer Science, Data Science, Cybersecurity, or a related field
  • Familiarity with AI and machine learning fundamentals
  • Proficiency in Python (preferred) or other programming languages
  • Deep understanding of LLM & AI tools (Copilot, Claude, Gemini, etc)
  • Deep understanding of IDE (VSCode, Antigravity, etc)
  • Strong analytical and problem-solving skills
  • Demonstrated interest in cybersecurity

Additional Information Bonus Skills

  • Experience with machine learning frameworks (e.g. TensorFlow, PyTorch, scikit-learn)
  • Familiarity with data analysis and visualisation techniques
  • Basic knowledge of cybersecurity principles
  • Exposure to automation tools (e.g. RPA, scripting)

Soft Skills

  • Critical thinking and creative problem-solving
  • Adaptability to evolving AI technologies and project requirements
  • Strong communication and documentation skills
  • Team collaboration and proactive engagement
  • Self-motivation

Internship Benefits

  • One-on-one mentorship from cybersecurity professionals
  • Exposure to real-world AI applications in a cybersecurity/consulting context
  • Cross-functional collaboration across departments
  • Global professional development and networking opportunities
  • Potential for full-time employment based on performance

Perks

  • Competitive hourly wage with paid statutory holidays
  • Monthly transportation allowance
  • Monthly social events
  • Free in-office coffee/snacks
  • Excellent training, mentorship, and career advancement opportunities in a caring, collaborative, and inclusive culture
  • Hybrid work environment with provided equipment to ensure optimal productivity
  • Sopra Steria is certified as a “Great Place to Work”

*Sopra Steria is an equal opportunity employer. We value diversity and are committed to creating an inclusive work environment. We thank all applicants for their interest in this position. Only shortlisted candidates will be contacted for the next steps in the selection process.*
