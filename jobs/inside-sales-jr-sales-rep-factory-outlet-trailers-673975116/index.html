About Factory Outlet Trailers

Growing trailer upfitting leader in Western Canada

Full Description

Factory Outlet Trailers is Western Canada’s largest trailer and truck upfitting dealer, known for unbeatable prices and exceptional customer service. We’re growing fast and looking for driven individuals to join our dynamic team.

Position Overview

We are seeking an enthusiastic and passionate Jr Sales Representative to join our high-energy team. This role is perfect for a hungry, go-getter personality who thrives in a fast-paced environment and loves helping customers find the perfect trailer for their needs.

Key Responsibilities

  • Qualify incoming leads and build strong relationships with prospective customers
  • Coordinate and assist with trailer hook-ups and ensure safe, professional delivery
  • Deliver exceptional in-person and phone-based customer service
  • Conduct proactive follow-up calls to qualify prospects and build lasting customer relationships
  • Work closely with the sales team to meet and exceed monthly targets
  • Maintain accurate records using CRM and other sales tools

What We’re Looking For

  • A quick thinker who stays calm and effective under pressure
  • Self-motivated, driven, and results-oriented mindset
  • Passion for sales and delivering outstanding customer experiences
  • Strong communication and problem-solving skills
  • Ability to work collaboratively in a supportive team environment and independently
  • Willingness to learn and continuously improve
  • Previous sales experience (automotive, trailer, or similar industry) is an asset
  • Mechanical aptitude or comfort with trailer hook-ups is an asset

Why Join Us?

  • Be part of a growing, energetic, and supportive team
  • Competitive compensation and bonus opportunities
  • Training and growth potential within a thriving industry
  • Work with an industry leader that values passion and hard work

Apply today and bring your energy and passion to Factory Outlet Trailers — where we don’t just sell trailers, we create loyal customers for life!

  • Dental care
  • Extended health care
