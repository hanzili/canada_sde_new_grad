Jr. Accountant

at Penfolds Roofing & Solar

📍 Location Coquitlam, BC V3K 6X3
📋 Details Full-time · Permanent · $55,000-$60,000 CAD · N/A
📅 Posted Apr 13, 2026

About the Role

Process AP, reconcile records, analyze costs, support close

About Penfolds Roofing & Solar

Regional roofing and solar company in growth mode

Full Description

Are you driven by innovation in roofing and renewable energy, and excited to make a real impact in a rapidly growing industry? Join Penfolds Roofing & Solar — a leader in roofing and solar installations — as we expand our Accounting team to support increasing demand and evolving project complexity. We’re seeking a knowledgeable and motivated professional to join our company as a Jr. Accountant and help shape the future of roofing and sustainable energy solutions.

Position Overview:

The Jr. Accountant supports the integrity of Penfolds’ financial operations by managing the full accounts payable cycle, ensuring sales tax compliance, and performing project cost analysis. This role is key to streamlining financial processes and supporting monthly closing duties to ensure accurate reporting and operational efficiency.

Key Responsibilities:

  • Review and process urgent requests and distribute documents for approval
  • Match and post job invoices to purchase orders from multiple departments
  • Calculate and verify sales tax collection and remittance
  • Perform payment runs and enter employee expense forms
  • Ensure all vendor statements are received, reconciled and processed
  • Financial Statement Variance analysis and project cost allocation
  • Improve accounts payable processes through conducting and reviewing both weekly and monthly duties
  • Run quick cheques, as needed
  • Other Tasks as assigned

What you bring:

  • Business Degree or Diploma
  • Knowledge of sales tax regulation and compliance
  • Relevant work experience considered an asset
  • Strong English speaking and writing skills
  • Excellent communication, organizational and interpersonal skills
  • Ability to work in a fast-paced, time-sensitive environment
  • Effective problem solver
  • Computer literacy
  • Experience using accounting software

What do we offer:

  • Competitive Pay: $55,000-$60,000 based on experience
  • Training and Growth Opportunities: Learn and grow with us.
  • Flexibility: Including remote work possibilities.
  • Supportive Work Environment: Join a diverse, collaborative, and inclusive team committed to building a sustainable future.
  • Company Perks: Regular team events and recognition for your contributions.
  • RRSPs
  • Extended Health and Dental Benefits

This role offers a unique opportunity to contribute directly to the company's growth and long-term success. If you are a visionary Jr. Accounting with a passion for driving business performance, we’d love to hear from you.

Penfolds is an equal opportunity employer. We celebrate diversity and are committed to creating an inclusive environment for all employees.
