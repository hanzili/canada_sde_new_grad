About the Role

Promote and sell Bell products to customers

About Bell

Canada's largest telecom, strong growth and benefits

Full Description

We are looking for enthusiastic, outgoing people to join our retail sales team! We welcome individuals with previous sales and/or management experience, who understand the importance of customer service. If you enjoy using the latest smartphone or gadget, and love working with people, we might be a good fit for you!

Qualifications:

The ideal candidate should possess:

  • A solid competitive drive and commitment to sales
  • A strong focus on building positive rapport with customers
  • A willingness to receive feedback to further personal development

Compensation:

  • Group medical benefits
  • Competitive commissions
  • Free* voice & data wireless plan
  • Numerous incentives and rewards
  • Discounts on products and Bell home services·
  • Opportunities for growth and internal upward mobility

Roles and Responsibilities:

  • Be prepared to work in a fast-paced, team-oriented environment
  • Demonstrate an ability to promote and sell our Bell products and services
  • Show a commitment to meeting and exceeding store sales performance targets
  • Strive to obtain a high degree of knowledge about the products and services we have to offer

This role offers a base hourly wage complemented by commission, performance bonus and other incentives with an opportunity to earn ranging from $18-$22 per hour or more when combining base and supplemental pay.

Bell is Canada's largest telecommunications company, providing Mobile phone, TV, high speed and wireless Internet, and residential Home phone services.
