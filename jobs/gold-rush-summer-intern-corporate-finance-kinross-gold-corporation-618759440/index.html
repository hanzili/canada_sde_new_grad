This program provides:

  • Opportunities working closely with various departments in Kinross‚Äô Corporate Office in Toronto, Ontario gaining real-world experience in an exciting, dynamic industry.

Job Responsibilities

The Corporate Finance Intern will assist the Corporate Accounting and Accounts Payable functions of the Kinross Finance department:

  • Participate in Kinross‚Äô vendor verification program by facilitating independent calls to vendors to confirm banking details to prevent fraud and maintain integrity in the payments process
  • Assist with the month-end and quarter-end close processes through preparation of routine journal entries
  • Support the intercompany and external gold sales process through preparation and management of internal invoices
  • Assist in performing trade confirmations with external financial institutions
  • Review incoming invoices from vendors determining system accuracy of capturing relevant details
  • Perform routine activities to process invoices and enable payments ensuring timeliness and accuracy
  • Assist with other ad-hoc requests and analysis across the greater Corporate Finance team including the Payroll function as necessary
