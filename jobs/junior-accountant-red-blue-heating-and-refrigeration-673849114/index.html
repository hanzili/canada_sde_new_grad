About the Role

Process AP/AR, payroll, and monthly reporting

About Red Blue Heating and Refrigeration

CPA-friendly, growth-focused accounting role in SMB

Full Description

About RedBlue

Founded in 2013 by passionate industry professionals, RedBlue has grown to a team with over 100 skilled technicians serving Greater Victoria. Our team is made up of highly skilled and experienced professionals who are committed to providing our customers with the best possible service in the HVAC, sheet metal, plumbing and electrical industries.

The Position

Red Blue Heatpumps and Refrigeration is currently experiencing high growth and looking to add additional accounting support to our existing team. We are currently looking to hire a Junior Accountant in our Langford office to start immediately. This is a new position and will work directly with the Senior Accountant and Controller to provide support to the accounts payable, accounts receivable, payroll and accounting functions of the organization. This role will also be integral in helping to put in place processes and systems to support scalability of the business. This onsite role is ideal for an individual pursuing their CPA designation as it will offer the ability to work with and learn from a number of experienced CPAs; provide exposure to all facets of the business; and offer opportunities to enhance existing accounting practices, and implement new policies and procedures.

The expected salary range for this position is between $25-$32/hour depending on experience, and includes benefits and education assistance upon successful completion of a probationary period.

Candidate Requirements

· At least 2 years of experience in a full-cycle accounting environment.

· Currently enrolled or planning to enroll in the CPA program.

· A bachelor’s degree or equivalent in accounting, commerce or business is not required but preferred.

· Proficient computer skills, including Sharepoint and Microsoft Office Suite (Outlook, Word, and Excel).

· Experience with Quickbooks is an asset.

· Knowledge of Accounts Payable and Expense Reporting systems is considered an asset.

· Ability to manage workflow efficiently and prioritize in a dynamic environment.

· Experience with process redesign and implementation.

Ideal Candidates will possess the following:

· Knowledge of ASPE or IFRS

· Experience with multi-divisional accounting environments.

· Understanding of percentage of completion and completed contract revenue recognition.

· Excellent time management

· Strong written and verbal communication skills, and the ability to relate to professionals in multiple industries

· Desire for continuous learning and the willingness to grow with the company.

· Excellent customer service and a strong team player.

Key Job Responsibilities:

· Working with AP to ensure all payables recorded and processed in a timely manner.

· Working with AR to ensure all deposits and revenues recorded correctly.

· Help to resolve any AP or AR related issues as they arise.

· Prepare and remit all GST, PST, WCB and EHT filings.

· Assist in the preparation of monthly financial reporting package. Including, preparation of month-end working papers.

· Investigate and participate in the implementation of accounting-related software solutions.

· Assist with year-end audit.

· Assist in remittances and reporting related to bi-weekly payroll.

Company Benefits:

All employees are entitled to company benefits after three months of employment.

Highlighted benefits include:

· Employer paid health benefits

· CPA education costs reimbursed

· Mentorship through the CPA process

· Ergonomic workstations

· Supportive team

· Interesting, challenging, and rewarding work

· Paid time off
