Chemistry / Lab Sciences Internship

Laboratory Analyst, Summer Student

at Redpath Sugar Ltd

📍 Location Toronto, Ontario
📋 Details Internship · Summer · Pay negotiable · Term: Summer
📅 Posted Apr 15, 2026

About the Role

Perform sugar QC tests and document results

About Redpath Sugar Ltd

Legacy sugar company with QA and safety focus

Full Description

Redpath Sugar is located on Toronto’s historic downtown waterfront and has been a part of Canada’s heritage for more than 160 years. Our brand is the oldest food-related trademark in Canada and is recognized across the country. Redpath Sugar is part of ASR Group, the largest cane sugar refiner in the world.

Overview The Laboratory Analyst, Summer Student is a member of the Toronto Redpath Quality Group and reports directly to the Lab Supervisor. The role is primarily responsible for performing laboratory and quality and food safety functions as assigned by the Lab Supervisor and/or the Quality Assurance Manager.

DETAILED ROLES AND RESPONSIBILITES

  • Wide array of testing for granulated sugar, liquid sugars, icing sugars and soft sugars as needed
  • Testing of in-process samples and other samples as assigned
  • Sugar Loss samples collection and analyses as needed
  • Laboratory equipment verification and troubleshooting
  • Documentation and analysis of results
  • Assists in preparation of necessary reports and trending information
  • Participates in all quality, safety, and environmental training
  • Practices and promotes GMPs and plant safety goals
  • Interacts with Process and Packaging Supervision on issues related to sampling and documentation
  • Ensures optimal performance of various testing equipment and accuracy of generated results
  • Updating databases as needed
  • Organizing and filing documents as needed
  • Other quality and food safety tasks as assigned

Work Experience And Education

  • Enrolled in college or university program with chemistry or food science (or equivalent) related discipline preferred
  • Capable of demonstrating good laboratory skills and techniques
  • Proficient computer skills in standard Microsoft applications (Word, Excel, Access, Visio)
  • Knowledge of Quality Assurance/Control practices and procedures preferred

Essential Capabilities (knowledge, Skills, Abilities And Personal Attributes

  • Able to follow instructions
  • Excellent communication skills - both verbal and written in English
  • Must be capable of troubleshooting and resolving issues in a fast-paced manufacturing environment
  • Provides feedback on trends to quality management
  • Works closely with other laboratory staff to maintain efficient testing, and clean working environment

LOCATION Toronto, Canada
