Full Description

About Peregrine Professional Services

Peregrine Professional Services is a modern accounting firm serving businesses across Canada and the United States.

We combine accounting expertise with AI-powered technology to deliver bookkeeping, tax, and advisory services more efficiently and effectively. Our team leverages technology to reduce repetitive work, improve accuracy, and create a better experience for both clients and employees.

We’re looking for an Accounting & AI Associate who is interested in accounting, technology, and continuous improvement.

Responsibilities

  • Review and categorize financial transactions
  • Assist with bank and credit card reconciliations
  • Support bookkeeping and month-end processes
  • Review outputs generated by internal technology tools
  • Assist with financial record maintenance
  • Communicate with clients regarding bookkeeping requests
  • Support document collection and organization
  • Identify opportunities to improve processes and workflows
  • Assist with various accounting and operational projects

Qualifications

Required

  • Strong attention to detail
  • Strong written and verbal communication skills
  • Excellent organizational skills
  • Comfortable learning new software and technology
  • Ability to manage multiple tasks and deadlines

Preferred

  • Experience with QuickBooks Online
  • Accounting, bookkeeping, finance, or business education
  • Previous bookkeeping or administrative experience
  • Experience using AI productivity tools such as ChatGPT, Claude, or Microsoft Copilot

What We Offer

  • Salary of $35,000-$50,000 annually
  • Performance-based bonus opportunities
  • Ongoing training and professional development
  • Exposure to modern accounting technology and automation tools
  • Career growth opportunities
  • Flexible and collaborative work environment
  • Direct mentorship from experienced accounting professionals

Career Development

This role is designed for individuals who are interested in building a long-term career in accounting and professional services.

Successful candidates will have opportunities to develop technical accounting skills, client service experience, operational knowledge, and expertise in emerging technologies used within the profession.

We value team members who demonstrate initiative, reliability, strong communication skills, and a commitment to continuous learning.

Job Types: Full-time, Internship / Co-op

Benefits:

  • Dental care
  • Profit sharing
  • Stock options

  • Work from home
