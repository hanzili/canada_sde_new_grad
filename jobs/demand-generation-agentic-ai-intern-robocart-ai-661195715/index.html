About the Role

Build AI workflows, automate enterprise outreach

About Robocart.ai

AI-driven startup, enterprise automation focus

Full Description

We are seeking a  technical and sales-oriented intern  who is passionate about  Agentic AI, automation, and enterprise demand generation .

This role combines  AI technology development and enterprise outreach , making it ideal for candidates who want to work at the intersection of:

  • Artificial Intelligence
  • Automation and AI agents
  • Enterprise technology
  • Demand generation and lead development

The intern will work on building  AI workflow tools and automation agents , while also helping generate  qualified enterprise leads and meetings  for SAPSOL platforms and services.

Key Responsibilities

1. Demand Generation & Lead Development

  • Identify and research companies that match SAPSOL’s Ideal Customer Profile.
  • Conduct targeted outreach using  LinkedIn, SEO insights, CRM systems, campaigns, and email outreach .
  • Engage enterprise decision makers including  CIOs, CTOs, IT leaders, and transformation executives .
  • Support development of qualified leads and meetings for SAPSOL solutions.
  • Maintain lead tracking and outreach records within CRM platforms.
  • Assist with  campaign development, lead funnels, and demand generation programs .

2. Agentic AI Development

  • Assist in developing  AI workflow builders and automation tools .
  • Help design and configure  AI agents used for research, outreach, and enterprise intelligence .
  • Build or configure  multi-step automation workflows  using AI tools and platforms.
  • Support experimentation with  complex AI agents and intelligent automation frameworks .
  • Contribute to development of  AI-driven productivity and outreach systems  used internally by SAPSOL.

Candidate Profile

The ideal candidate has both technical and sales curiosity  and wants to work deeply in  AI automation and enterprise technology .

Candidates should be:

  • technically capable and comfortable working with  AI tools and automation
  • interested in enterprise demand generation and digital outreach
  • motivated to build real AI systems and enterprise workflows
  • willing to work independently and learn quickly

Preferred Skills

  • AI tools, automation platforms, or workflow builders
  • Software development or technical configuration experience
  • LinkedIn outreach or B2B demand generation
  • CRM tools and campaign platforms
  • SEO and digital demand generation knowledge
  • Interest in  enterprise software such as SAP

Compensation

Base Compensation

  • CAD  $18 per hour start

This role begins as an internship engagement .

The position may transition to  full-time employment based on performance, contribution, and business requirements with Variable pay, incentive and ESOPS.
