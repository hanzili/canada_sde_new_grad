ROLE & RESPONSIBILITIES
(including but not limited)

Buying Execution & Replenishment

  • Own in-season replenishment across assigned categories and locations to maintain strong in-stock levels and visual density.
  • Prepare and execute purchase orders aligned with sales performance, forecasts, and Open-to-Buy targets.
  • Monitor sell-through, inventory levels, and seasonal trends to support proactive buying decisions.
  • Manage substitutions, transfers, and in-season adjustments to reduce stockouts and missed sales.
  • Support clearance and markdown execution in alignment with buying strategy.

Inventory Planning & Operational Support

  • Partner with Inventory and Operations teams to support: Auto-PO Setup & Management, Min/Max Adjustments, Inventory Accuracy & Flow
  • Track and follow up on purchase orders, ETAs, backorders, and delivery issues, escalating risks proactively.
  • Support store openings, seasonal transitions, and micro-retail projects through timely product coordination.

Vendor & Product Coordination

  • Maintain ongoing communication with vendors regarding product availability, pricing, timelines, and substitutions.
  • Support vendor negotiations related to MOQs, timelines, and cost efficiencies.
  • Assist with vendor onboarding, product setup, and sample management.
  • Gather and share product feedback from stores and guests to inform future buying decisions.

Store Support & Visual Merchandising

  • Partner with Store Managers to support incoming product, category growth, and execution on the sales floor.
  • Assist with rollout of planograms, merchandising standards, and visual guidelines
  • Provide on-site support during key periods (openings, resets, peak season) to ensure stores are guest-ready and on standard. Support product knowledge and visual merchandising training alongside the Senior Buyer and Operations team.

Collaboration & Growth

  • Work closely with the Senior Buyer, Operations Manager, Inventory team, and Store Managers.
  • Provide regular updates on PO status, replenishment needs, and product performance.
  • Participate in seasonal planning, assortment reviews, and post-season analysis.
  • Actively develop buying, commercial, and merchandising skills with a clear pathway toward future growth.
