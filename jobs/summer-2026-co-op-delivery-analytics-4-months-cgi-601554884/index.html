About the Role

About CGI

Global IT services firm with diverse client base

Full Description

Position Description

We are looking for a motivated and detail-oriented Delivery Analytics Co-op to join our service delivery team. In this role, you will analyze operational data across ITSM functions—Incidents, Problems, Changes, Service Requests, and Timesheets—to uncover patterns, trends, and actionable insights. Your insights will help improve service quality, reduce operational friction, and support continuous improvement initiatives across multiple workstreams.

This is an excellent opportunity for an analytical thinker who enjoys solving real-world service management challenges through data.

Start Date: May 2026
Application Deadline: January 30 2026

Perks of Being a CGI Co-op:

  • Participate in monthly socials to meet co-ops across the country.
  • Access professional development workshops hosted by domain experts on topics ranging from how to be a top consultant, how to apply the agile methodology, data & analytics and more!

To learn more about CGI and hear from our Co-ops and New Grads, visit www.cgi.com/startmycareer

Your future duties and responsibilities

Operational Data Analysis

  • Analyze Incident, Problem, Change, and Service Request data to identify trends, recurring issues, potential root causes, and improvement opportunities.
  • Review timesheet and effort data to validate accuracy, track capacity, and identify areas to optimize resource utilization.
  • Create dashboards, visualizations, and summary reports to communicate findings clearly.

Service Delivery Improvement

  • Recommend process and operational improvements backed by quantitative and qualitative insights.
  • Monitor KPIs/SLAs and support the development of performance baselines and forecasts.
  • Support continuous improvement initiatives related to ITSM, AMS, and governance processes.

Stakeholder Support

  • Provide analytical support to delivery leaders, Service Managers, and project teams.
  • Translate complex data into simple, business-ready insights and recommendations.
  • Participate in review meetings to present analysis and track ongoing actions.

Tools & Technologies

(You can tailor these to your environment)

  • Familiarity with ITSM platforms (e.g., ServiceNow, JIRA, Remedy).
  • Experience with Excel/Sheets, BI tools (Power BI / Tableau), or basic SQL/Python for analysis.
  • Exposure to ticketing, timesheet, or capacity planning systems is an asset.

Required qualifications to be successful in this role

Qualifications

  • Currently enrolled in a post-secondary program in Data Analytics, Computer Science, Business Technology Management, Information Systems, or a related field.
  • Strong analytical and problem-solving skills with an ability to draw insights from complex datasets.
  • Comfortable working with large data exports and conducting structured analysis.
  • Strong communication skills, with the ability to present insights to technical and non-technical audiences.
  • Curious, detail-oriented, and excited to explore how data can improve service delivery.

What You Will Gain

  • Hands-on experience with enterprise ITSM data and real operational delivery environments.
  • Exposure to service delivery governance, incident/problem/change processes, and performance management.
  • Opportunity to influence how services are measured, tracked, and improved.
  • Experience collaborating within a global delivery organization.
