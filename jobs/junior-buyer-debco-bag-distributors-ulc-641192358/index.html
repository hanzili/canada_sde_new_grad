Junior Buyer

at Debco Bag Distributors ULC

📍 Location Concord, ON L4K 4R3
📋 Details Full-time · Permanent · $45,000-$50,000/year · On-site
📅 Posted Mar 11, 2026

About the Role

Process POs, manage suppliers, track deliveries

About Debco Bag Distributors ULC

Ontario-based distributor with stable benefits

Full Description

Debco, a Toronto‑based subsidiary of HPG, is part of one of the most awarded and respected brand families in the promotional products industry. As a top‑10 supplier in a $25B market, we continue to lead through innovative products, exceptional service, and strong, authentic relationships.

We’re looking for an energetic and detail‑oriented Junior Buyer to join our Supply Chain team. This role plays a key part in day‑to‑day purchasing operations, inventory management, and ensuring timely communication across internal teams. If you thrive in a fast‑paced environment and want to contribute to an award‑winning organization, this is an exciting opportunity to grow your career.

This position is on-site.

Key Responsibilities

  • Monitor weekly purchase order reports and track the status of incoming materials.
  • Create and place purchase orders with domestic suppliers for inventory, consumables, and MRO items.
  • Obtain weekly updates from suppliers on open POs to maintain accurate ship and arrival dates in the system.
  • Expedite or defer deliveries as needed to support operational requirements.
  • Collaborate with receiving and accounting teams to ensure timely and accurate PO billing.
  • Create and manage transfer orders within the MRP system.
  • Communicate quality issues to suppliers and coordinate compensation or corrective actions.
  • Identify opportunities for process improvements and support implementation.
  • Perform additional responsibilities and special projects as assigned.

Experience & Qualifications

  • Minimum 1 year of procurement or purchasing experience.
  • Proficiency with MS Excel, MS Word, and MRP systems.
  • Exceptional attention to detail and accuracy.
  • Proactive approach to issue resolution.
  • Bachelor’s degree or equivalent work experience.
  • Strong analytical and problem‑solving skills.
  • Demonstrated integrity, creativity, and adaptability.
  • Ability to work effectively in a fast‑paced environment and collaborate with all levels of the organization.
  • A positive, team‑focused mindset with a strong “can‑do” attitude.

If you’re that person who thrives in being able to juggle a lot at one time, pay attention to detail, provide clear communication, and have a cheerful can-do attitude, we have an opportunity for you!

No phone inquiries, please. We will contact selected candidates directly.
