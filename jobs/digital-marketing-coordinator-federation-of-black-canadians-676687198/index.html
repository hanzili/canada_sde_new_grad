About Federation of Black Canadians

Full Description

Organization: Black Innovation Zone Location: Greater Toronto Area (Work from home with travel as required) Duration: 8 weeks (35 hours per week) Compensation: $17.60/hour

About the Black Innovation Zone

The is dedicated to empowering Black youth and entrepreneurs by fostering innovation, creativity, and leadership, alongside our allies in the broader community. We support initiatives that drive positive change in our communities.

Position Overview

The Digital Marketing Coordinator will support the Black Innovation Zone’s digital presence by managing social media platforms, creating compelling graphic designs (both digital and print), and supporting website design and marketing initiatives.This person will report to the BIZ Executive Team, made up of a rep from the Federation of Black Canadians, Nobellum, and Black Founders network and or a BIZ lead designate. This is an exciting opportunity for a creative and tech-savvy individual passionate about innovation and community.

Key Responsibilities

  • Social Media Management:
  • Schedule, publish, and monitor content across platforms (Instagram, LinkedIn, Facebook, Twitter, etc.)
  • Engage with followers, respond to comments, and grow the digital community
  • Analyze metrics and prepare reports on engagement and campaign performance
  • Graphic Design:
  • Create visually compelling graphics for social media, flyers, posters, and other print materials
  • Collaborate with the team to ensure brand consistency across all marketing assets
  • Website Support:
  • Assist in creating and updating web pages, banners, and other digital content
  • Ensure website updates align with campaigns and promotions
  • Support SEO and digital marketing best practices

Qualifications & Requirements

  • Canada Summer Jobs Federal Program requirements: Canadian Citizen or permanent residence and or refugee status with a valid SIN #
  • Proven experience (or strong interest) in digital marketing and graphic design
  • Proficiency in design tools (e.g., Adobe Creative Suite, Canva)
  • Experience with social media management and content scheduling tools
  • Basic understanding of website platforms (e.g., WordPress, Squarespace)
  • Strong creative vision and attention to detail
  • Excellent written and verbal communication skills
  • Self-motivated, organized, and able to meet deadlines in a fast-paced environment
  • Alignment with the values and mission of the Black Innovation Zone

Additional Assets

  • Bi-linugal
  • Own a Car
  • G level Licence

What You’ll Gain

  • Hands-on experience managing digital marketing in a community-driven organization
  • Opportunity to expand your portfolio with impactful, purpose-driven campaigns
  • Professional growth in digital marketing and design within a vibrant nonprofit sector
