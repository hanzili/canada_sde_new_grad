Full Description

There is something powerful about work that puts you in a position to make a real difference for people every single day. When customers are treated with professionalism, clarity, and care, their experience changes — and so does their confidence in the company behind it.

We are currently hiring a Junior Account Manager – Customer Solutions to join our growing team full-time. This opportunity is ideal for someone who enjoys working with people, values strong communication, and wants to build a long-term career in a professional, team-driven environment. You do not need years of experience to succeed here. What matters most is your attitude, your willingness to learn, and your ability to connect with others in a genuine and confident way.

In this role, you will represent industry-leading telecommunications services, speak with customers directly, help them understand their options, and support them in making informed decisions that fit their needs. If you are motivated by personal growth, enjoy a fast-moving atmosphere, and want to be part of a company that invests in its people, this could be the right next step for you.

Primary Responsibilities of the Junior Account Managers at our company:

  • Meet with customers personally to introduce available telecommunications solutions
  • Build trust through professional, informative, and customer-focused conversations
  • Understand customer needs and recommend suitable service options
  • Answer questions clearly and handle concerns with confidence and care
  • Support account growth by creating positive customer experiences from start to finish
  • Work closely with team members and leadership to strengthen performance and results
  • Stay informed on products, promotions, and service updates
  • Maintain a high standard of representation, communication, and professionalism in the field

Skills and Qualifications:

  • Strong people skills and a positive, professional attitude
  • Confidence communicating with customers in person
  • A coachable mindset and genuine willingness to learn
  • Self-motivation and a strong work ethic
  • Ability to work well independently and as part of a team
  • Customer service, sales, retail, hospitality, or similar experience is an asset, but not required
  • Availability for a full-time schedule
  • Ability to start within 1–2 weeks is considered an asset

What We Offer:

  • Full-time, stable opportunity
  • Paid training and continuous guidance
  • Clear path for career development and advancement
  • Supportive team environment with hands-on leadership
  • Valuable experience in customer relations, account support, and business development
  • The opportunity to grow your communication, presentation, and professional skills

This is a full-time, in-person position and requires daily attendance at our office and assigned work locations. If you are looking for a role where you can grow professionally, develop valuable experience, and be recognized for your effort, we would be pleased to hear from you.

Job Types: Full-time, Permanent
