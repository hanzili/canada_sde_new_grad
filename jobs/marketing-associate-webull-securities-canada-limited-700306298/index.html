About the Role

Execute multi-channel campaigns; analyze performance; optimize.

About Webull Securities (Canada) Limited

Fintech marketing in Canada; data-driven growth

Full Description

Webull is a leading digital brokerage platform built on next generation global infrastructure. At Webull, we believe that everyone should have an equal opportunity to control their own financial future. Our vision has always been to empower individual investors with the tools and resources they need to succeed financially. This commitment remains at the heart of everything we do. From low-fee trading and robust market data to advanced analytical tools, we are committed to providing a seamless, top-tier trading experience.

About The Role & Team

Webull Securities (Canada) Ltd. provides a mobile-first investing platform designed for today’s retail investors. As a global fintech company entering an exciting new phase of growth in Canada, our mission goes beyond launching a product, it is to build a trusted financial partner for Canadian investors.

We are seeking a Marketing Associate to join our growing team in Toronto. This individual will play a key role in supporting Webull’s continued growth in the Canadian market by driving data-informed marketing initiatives and delivering measurable business impact. The ideal candidate is a curious, data-driven marketer who thrives in fast-paced environments, isn’t afraid to roll up their sleeves, and can balance strategic thinking with hands-on execution across campaigns, channels, and content.

Reporting to the Director of Marketing, this role will work across both strategy and execution — building campaigns, analyzing performance, optimizing results, and identifying opportunities to drive growth and engagement.

In This Role, You Will

  • Translate strategic insights and customer data into actionable marketing plans, including audience segmentation, channel strategy, campaign execution, and measurement frameworks.
  • Develop and execute integrated marketing campaigns across multiple channels, including email, app banners & push notifications, paid media, social, and digital marketing ecosystems.
  • Write and edit compelling, benefit-driven marketing copy for landing pages, email campaigns, customer communications, digital ads, and promotional content.
  • Monitor campaign performance and business metrics, delivering insightful weekly reporting, recommendations, and executive-ready updates to stakeholders and leadership.
  • Collaborate cross-functionally with teams across Product, Analytics, Compliance, Tech, Design, PR, and Brand to ensure campaigns are aligned, compliant, and customer-centric.
  • Develop workback schedules, coordinate campaign deliverables, and manage multiple projects simultaneously in a fast-moving environment.

The Skills You Bring

  • 3 - 4 years of experience in Marketing, Growth or Communications, preferably within fintech, financial services, banking, or a highly regulated industry.
  • Strong understanding of customer lifecycle marketing, digital acquisition, engagement, retention, and conversion strategies.
  • Hands-on experience executing multi-channel campaigns across CRM, email, paid digital, social, referral, and digital platforms.
  • Proven ability to turn data into actionable insights and make informed, performance-driven marketing decisions.
  • Exceptional written and verbal communication skills, with the ability to simplify complex concepts into clear, compelling messaging.
  • Strong project management and organizational skills with exceptional attention to detail.
  • Proficiency with marketing analytics and reporting tools including BI, Google Analytics and social listening tools.
  • Experience with marketing automation, campaign reporting, and content development.
  • Working knowledge of tools such as Microsoft Office, PowerPoint, Excel, Adobe Creative Suite, and Canva.
  • University degree in Marketing, Communications, Business, or a related field, or equivalent practical experience.

What Makes You Stand Out

  • A self-starter with a strong bias for action and ownership.
  • Naturally curious, analytical, and always looking for ways to improve results.
  • Comfortable operating both strategically and tactically – from campaign planning to hands-on execution.
  • Adaptable and resourceful, with the ability to thrive in a fast-paced, evolving environment.
  • Collaborative, proactive, and energized by solving complex business and customer challenges.

Why Webull?

Webull is more than a fintech company—we’re a global community of innovators, collaborators, and trailblazers. Headquartered in St. Petersburg, FL, Webull operates in 15 regions worldwide, serving over 20 million users. At Webull, your ideas matter, your voice is heard, and your work makes a real impact.

Investing in our people is a top priority for us, which is why we have a comprehensive benefits package that includes:

  • Comprehensive Health Coverage: Extended healthcare for you and your dependents, basic life insurance, AD&D coverage, and dental care.
  • Generous Paid Time Off: Enjoy 2 weeks of paid time off.

This role is based in our Canada Operations (Toronto, CA), where in-person collaboration fuels growth, mentorship, and innovation. At Webull, we’re committed to fair, equitable, and transparent compensation. The base salary range is $75,000 - 85,000 CAD annually, plus a discretionary bonus and benefits. Final offers consider experience, location, and relevant qualifications.

EEOC Statement

Webull is an equal opportunity employer. We’re committed to building a diverse and inclusive team where all backgrounds, perspectives, and talents are valued.
