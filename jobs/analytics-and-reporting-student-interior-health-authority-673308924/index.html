About the Role

Assist data analysis, prepare reports, support analytics tasks

About Interior Health Authority

Provincial health authority, BC

Full Description

Interior Health is hiring an Analytics and Reporting Student to join the Data and Analytics Services Team.

This position qualifies for flexible work location within the Interior Health region only. This opportunity is available for approximately four months.

What We Offer

  • Competitive salary and an attractive remuneration package
  • Career Growth
  • Employer paid training/education
  • Balanced lifestyle

What will you work on? The Interior Health Analytics and Reporting department includes Community & Clinical Service Analytics, Acute Care & Quality Analytics, Corporate & Support Service Analytics and Data Products and Services teams. The student will be part of the A&R department and participate in team functions. The student will work closely with the Manager for the assigned area as well as internal stakeholders. The student will have opportunity to engage in tasks relevant to their education such as: supporting business cycles, performance monitoring, and analysis and reporting requirements.

Some Typical Duties And Responsibilities

  • Participate and engage in project work. May include project coordination, communication with stakeholders, implementation and evaluation depending on student’s skill set. Assist with data gathering, analysis and reporting as directed by the Manager.
  • Create and/or update analytic documentation in alignment with departmental standards.
  • Participates in team meetings.
  • Other related duties and responsibilities assigned by the Manager.

Salary Range Salary range for the position is $22.66 to $25.75. Interior Health establishes salaries within the minimum and maximum of the salary range based on consideration of the qualifications, experience of the applicant, and an internal equity review of the salaries of other employees.

How will we help you grow? We support your growth through a personalized orientation tailored to your goals, along with ongoing guidance, mentorship, and easy access to a collaborative team of experienced and supportive colleagues who are invested in your success.

Reasons to Apply at Interior Health... What we can do for you At Interior Health, we create a vibrant work environment that supports your growth and helps you build strong skills essential for your future career. We prioritize psychological health and well-being, ensuring a supportive space where you can thrive. Additionally, we offer flexible opportunities that allow you to maintain a balanced lifestyle, making it easier to juggle your studies, work, and personal interests.

Come live, work and play where others only vacation. Make a difference. Love your work. Apply today!
