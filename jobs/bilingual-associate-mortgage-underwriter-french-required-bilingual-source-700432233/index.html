Full Description

Bilingual Mortgage Underwriter and Associate Mortgage Underwriter (French required / français requis)

Introduction

Our client is experiencing strong growth and is actively hiring both a Bilingual Mortgage Underwriter and a Bilingual Associate Mortgage Underwriter. Que vous soyez en début de carrière ou déjà expérimenté(e), cette opportunité vous permet de bâtir une carrière solide en souscription hypothécaire résidentielle. French and English are required, as you will collaborate with partners and clients across Canada in a high performance, growth driven environment with clear advancement opportunities.

What You’ll Do

You will review and assess residential mortgage applications submitted through broker channels, ensuring sound credit decisions while delivering an exceptional partner experience. Selon votre niveau d’expérience, vous prendrez en charge des dossiers de complexité progressive et développerez votre autonomie décisionnelle dans un environnement structuré et collaboratif.

Responsibilities

  • Review and analyze mortgage applications for completeness, accuracy, and risk
  • Évaluer le crédit, les revenus, les actifs et les propriétés des emprunteurs
  • Apply underwriting guidelines to approve, decline, or structure mortgage solutions
  • Communicate decisions clearly and professionally to broker partners in both English and French
  • Établir et maintenir des relations solides avec les partenaires internes et externes
  • Manage a pipeline of files ranging from straightforward to more complex cases
  • Maintain turnaround times and service standards in a fast paced environment
  • Stay current on underwriting policies, lending guidelines, and market developments

Qualifications

  • Bilingual in French and English, both written and spoken
  • Experience in mortgage underwriting or financial services is an asset
  • Strong analytical thinking and sound judgment
  • Capacité à interpréter et appliquer des politiques de crédit avec rigueur
  • Excellent communication and relationship building skills
  • Ability to manage multiple files and deadlines effectively
  • Post secondary education in business, finance, or a related field is an asset

Benefits

  • Associate Mortgage Underwriter salary: $55,000 to $62,000 plus approximately 10 percent bonus
  • Mortgage Underwriter salary: $65,000 to $80,000 plus approximately 15 percent bonus
  • Hybrid work model, 2 days in office on Thursdays and Fridays and 3 days remote after training
  • 37.5 hour work week, Monday to Friday from 8:30 AM to 5:00 PM
  • Three weeks of vacation starting on day one
  • 80 percent employer paid medical and dental coverage
  • RRSP program with employer contributions up to 3 percent in the first two years, increasing to 5 percent thereafter, with vesting after one year
  • Discretionary annual bonus paid in January
  • Additional paid personal and sick days
  • Ongoing training, mentorship, and career development opportunities
  • Clear progression from Associate Mortgage Underwriter to Mortgage Underwriter and beyond

Why Work with Us

Our client offers a high growth environment where your career development is a priority. Vous aurez accès à du mentorat, à de la formation continue et à des opportunités concrètes d’avancement. Whether you are launching your underwriting career or advancing to the next level, this role provides the structure, exposure, and support to help you succeed and grow long term.

Disclosure: "Bilingual Source uses artificial intelligence (AI) technology to assist in screening and assessing applicants for this position. This technology helps us match your unique language skills and professional experience with the specific needs of our clients. Final hiring and interview decisions continue to be made by our human recruitment experts."
