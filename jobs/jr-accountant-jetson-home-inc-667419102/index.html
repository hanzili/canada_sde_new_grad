Jr Accountant

at Jetson Home Inc.

About the Role

Process AP/AR, reconcile books, support month-end close

About Jetson Home Inc.

Canadian startup focusing on sustainable home electrification

Full Description

About Jetson:

Jetson is on a mission to accelerate the transition of 100 million homes across North America away from fossil fuels toward sustainable energy use. We believe in a future that is 100% electric and 100% better.

Homes are one of the largest sources of carbon emissions, yet adoption of solutions like heat pumps remains slow due to cost and complexity. Jetson is solving this by building the first fully vertically integrated home electrification company — making clean energy simple, transparent, and affordable.

We rely heavily on technology, automation, and data to scale this impact. At Jetson, we value people who are excellent at their craft, curious about new tools (including AI), and motivated to continuously improve how work gets done.

The Opportunity:

We are looking for a detail-oriented, motivated, and passionate junior accounting professional who wants to be part of an exciting company fighting climate change by disrupting the residential HVAC industry. This is an onsite role based in our North Vancouver, Canada office where you will work closely with our Controller and AR/AP team to support day-to-day transactional accounting operations across our North Vancouver and Denver offices. This is a clerical, high-volume role built for someone early in their career who takes real pride in accuracy, consistency, and getting it right every time.

What You Will Do:

  • Process and maintain accurate financial transaction records on a timely basis
  • Process high-volume AP/AR transactions accurately and on time
  • Match invoices to purchase orders and verify billing accuracy
  • Follow up on outstanding and past-due invoices with vendors and customers
  • Utilize NetSuite as the primary system for entering & maintaining accurate transaction data across multiple entities
  • Perform bank and general ledger reconciliations
  • Support month-end close by preparing journal entries and reconciling sub-ledgers
  • Maintain supporting documentation for all transactions in an organized and audit-ready manner
  • Assist with GST/PST remittances and basic tax documentation in accordance with local requirements
  • Assist the Controller with budgets, forecasts, and quarterly reports as directed
  • Contribute to team efforts by taking on related tasks as needed

What You Bring:

  • Knowledge of NetSuite and ability to learn essential accounting software.
  • 2-5 years experience with at least 2 years in industry.
  • Full-cycle AP/AR experience and comfortable owning the transactional workflow end to end.
  • Familiarity with spreadsheets, formulas, and data analysis tools.
  • A general understanding of the main accounting principles, such as accruals and revenue.
  • Strong attention to detail and level of accuracy.
  • Effective English verbal and written communication skills.
  • Capable of spotting and resolving discrepancies.
  • Knowledge of relevant tax requirements and how to prepare basic tax documentation.
  • Bachelor’s degree in accounting, finance, or related field with previous bookkeeping experience preferred
  • Comfortable working onsite daily in a collaborative team environment.
