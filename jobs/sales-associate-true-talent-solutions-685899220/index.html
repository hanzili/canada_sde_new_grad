About the Role

About True Talent Solutions

GTA field sales role with comprehensive training

Full Description

Job Summary We are seeking dynamic and motivated Sales Associates to join our field sales team on behalf of our client, a Food Service and Supplier business headquartered in Scarborough, ON. In this role, you will visit local restaurants within your assigned territory to promote and sell our client's app services, food delivery solutions, and product offerings directly to restaurant owners and managers. The ideal candidate is personable, self-driven, and comfortable engaging decision-makers face-to-face in the food service industry.

Responsibilities

  • Visit local restaurants and food service businesses within your assigned territory to generate new sales and build relationships
  • Present and promote app services, delivery solutions, and product offerings clearly and confidently to business owners and managers
  • Conduct on-site product demonstrations and walk prospects through features, benefits, and pricing to support informed decision-making
  • Engage in consultative selling identify each client's needs and match them with the right service or solution
  • Follow up with prospects and existing clients to maintain relationships and close outstanding opportunities
  • Manage your daily schedule independently, planning an efficient route through your assigned territory
  • Track client interactions, visit notes, and sales progress accurately
  • Represent the company professionally in all client-facing interactions

Experience

  • Previous experience in field sales, B2B sales, retail sales, or customer-facing roles is preferred
  • Comfortable approaching and engaging business owners or managers in a direct, professional manner
  • Strong communication and interpersonal skills with the ability to build rapport quickly
  • Self-motivated with excellent time management this role requires independent daily planning and execution
  • Organized and detail-oriented with the ability to manage a territory and track multiple prospects simultaneously
  • Experience with product demonstrations or service-based selling is an asset but not required full training is provided
  • Bilingual or multilingual abilities are an asset when serving diverse, multicultural business communities across the GTA

Compensation & Schedule Details

  • $20.00/hr base pay, biweekly (~$1,400 per pay period at base)
  • Commission structure on top you are paid whichever is higher between hourly and commission earnings
  • 7-hour shifts, generally 10:00 AM – 5:30 PM (downtown locations may run 1:00 PM – 8:00 PM)
  • Monday – Friday; some positions run Tuesday – Saturday depending on territory
  • Approximately 12–17 positions available across the GTA
  • Online training (~3 hours) followed by shadowing a top performer before going independent
  • Reliable personal vehicle required candidates must have access to their own transportation daily
  • Please note: gas, mileage, and parking costs are not reimbursed

This role is ideal for someone who thrives working independently, enjoys meeting new people, and wants a compensation structure that rewards performance. If you are energetic, reliable, and motivated to earn above your base, we encourage you to apply.

Pay: $20.00-$27.59 per hour

Work Location: On the road
