About the Role

About PISE

Non-profit sport education institute; community-focused organization.

Full Description

Job Summary
We are seeking a dynamic and results-driven Event Marketing Specialist to join our team. In this role, you will be responsible for planning, promoting, and executing marketing strategies for various events aimed at increasing brand awareness and customer engagement.

Why PISE?

PISE (Pacific Institute for Sport Education) is more than just a fitness centre — we’re a community hub for movement, inclusion, and sport development. Since 2008, we’ve supported people of all ages and abilities across South Vancouver Island by promoting active, healthy lifestyles. We’re proud to be a non-profit leader in physical literacy and accessibility, transforming lives through education and sport. Learn more at pise.ca.

About the Role:

The Event Marketing Specialist Intern will be responsible for planning, promoting, and executing events that build brand awareness, generate leads, and create memorable customer experiences.

As an Event Marketing Specialist, you will:

  • Develop summer events calendar that aligns with organizational marketing goals.
  • Plan and coordinate logistics for promotional events.
  • Create timelines and schedules to ensure smooth execution.
  • Design and implement marketing campaigns to promote events across digital, print, and
  • social channels.
  • Develop event collateral such as brochures, signage, presentations, and branded
  • giveaways.
  • Act as a brand ambassador during events, representing the organization professionally.
  • Gather and respond to attendee feedback to improve future events.
  • Prepare post-event reports with insights and recommendations.

What You Bring:

  • Excellent verbal and written communication skills.
  • Strong understanding of social media platforms and analytics tools.
  • Experience with WordPress, email platforms (e.g., Constant Contact), and scheduling tools.
  • Creative mindset with the ability to multitask and work independently.
  • Proficiency with Microsoft Office programs.
  • Experience with Adobe Creative Suite is an asset.
  • Ability to work collaboratively within a team environment.

Qualifications

  • Post-secondary student in marketing, communications, public relations, or a related field.
  • Experience with content creation and administrative work.
  • Prior experience in marketing or social media is an asset.

What We Offer:

  • Free membership to our Fitness Centre
  • Discounts on PISE-led programs
  • Supportive and inclusive team culture
  • Free on-site parking
  • Fun staff events and community involvement

This role offers an engaging environment where your marketing talents can shine while creating memorable experiences for our guests. We value professionalism, enthusiasm, and a proactive approach in all our team members.
