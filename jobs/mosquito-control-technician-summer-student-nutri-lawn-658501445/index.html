About Nutri-Lawn

Full Description

Mosquito.Buzz, one of our departments @ Nutri-Lawn is preparing for the start of another great season! Love to work outside, are a team player, and want to be part of our continuously growing company in town? We are looking to hire Mosquito Control Technicians.

Our season starts mid May and ends late August / early September. For students, this is a great opportunity for summer employment at a leading company. We are offering Full time summer employment. Earlier start dates and later end dates also available.

Mosquito.Buzz is Canada's leading mosquito/tick control company. We allow our customers to stay outside and enjoy their backyard all season long. We professionally treat properties with our proven solution in all the areas mosquitoes/ticks live, rest and hide. We give people their yard back!

Our company is growing every year and it’s time to hire a few more technicians. We take pride in our work and promise our clients quality and reliability. We have a safe, fun and respectful teamwork environment, with positive managers and co-workers.

Responsibilities:

  • Apply mosquito/tick control products with a powered backpack sprayer to customer’s properties.
  • Use and maintain assigned Mosquito.Buzz van.
  • Interact with our customers daily.
  • Complete daily production reports.

Requirements/Qualifications:

  • Must have valid "G" drivers license.
  • Proven time management and organizational skill.
  • Able to walk 10 to 20 km per day.
  • Frequently move or lift up to 55lbs.
  • Typical work week consists of 40-55 hours per week including some Saturdays during our peek weeks.

Job Types: Full-time, Seasonal

Pay: $18.00-$22.00 per hour

Ability to commute/relocate:

  • Sault Ste. Marie, ON: reliably commute or plan to relocate before starting work (required)

Licence/Certification:

  • Class G Licence (required)

Work Location: In person
