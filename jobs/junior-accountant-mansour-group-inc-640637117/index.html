Junior Accountant

at Mansour Group Inc.

📍 Location Azilda, ON
📋 Details Full-time · Permanent · $22-28/hr · TBD
📅 Posted Mar 10, 2026

About the Role

Process AR/AP, reconcile accounts, prep monthly statements

About Mansour Group Inc.

Canadian industrial supplier with diverse product lines

Full Description

As a top Canadian industrial supplier, we provide comprehensive solutions with our diverse range of products. In addition to lime products and other bulk chemicals, we offer a wide selection of industrial consumer products. We partner with top vendors in the mining, construction, commercial, transportation, and energy industries to deliver quality products. We provide the highest quality products and services to help our customers succeed.

Mansour Chemicals & Commodities is currently seeking a motivated and dynamic individual to join our team as a Junior Accountant. The ideal candidate will be someone who enjoys working in a fast-paced environment, possesses excellent attention to detail, and is willing to take on a wide variety of tasks.

Responsibilities & Duties:

  • Assisting with accounts receivable process, including preparing and processing sales invoices in a timely manner
  • Assisting with accounts payable process, including issuing purchase orders and processing vendor invoices in a timely manner
  • Reconciling monthly bank statements
  • Preparing, entering, posting, and recording journal entries including various month-end and year-end accruals
  • Reconciling account balances and preparing various working papers to support the preparation of monthly financial statements
  • Prepare and analyze reports, including budget preparation, for the CFO and Divisional Managers
  • Preparing monthly HST remittances
  • Various administrative tasks such as invoicing, entering and coding payables, filing, billing, and doing payroll (oversee role)
  • Documenting and implementing Standard Operating Procedures (SOP)
  • Conduct inventory audits and perform inventory analysis to support effective inventory management practices
  • Assisting staff with system and accounting related questions
  • Perform other related duties as required

Key Competencies:

  • Ability to coordinate and prioritize multiple tasks and deadlines, while proving accurate information on a timely basis
  • Ability to work independently, while contributing to the overall team success
  • Ability to develop, recommend, and implement new accounting business processes and internal controls
  • Ability to understand business operations and provide business improvement ideas throughout the organization
  • Ability to anticipate problems and proactively resolve situations before they become larger scale issues
  • Ability to maintain confidentiality, exercise good judgement, and use discretion in dealing with confidential information
  • Excellent analytical skills and attention to detail

Requirements & Qualifications:

  • Minimum of a two-year Diploma or a Bachelor’s Degree in Accounting from an accredited post-secondary institution
  • Great Plains (Microsoft GP) experience is an asset
  • Intermediate to advanced Microsoft Excel Skills
  • Knowledge of Enterprise Resource Planning software is considered an asset
  • Knowledge of Accounting Standards for Private Enterprises
  • Excellent written and verbal communication skills
