Jr. Manufacturing Engineer - Test Facilities

at MDA

📍 Location Brampton, ON

📅 Posted Mar 12, 2026

About the Role

Operate and optimize environmental test facilities daily

About MDA

Aerospace leader with advanced test equipment

Full Description

ABOUT MDA SPACE

A robotics, satellite systems and geointelligence pioneer with a 55-year+ story of world firsts and more than 450 missions, MDA Space is a global leader in communications satellites, Earth and space observation, and space exploration and infrastructure. For more information, visit www.mda.space.

The Position

As a Manufacturing Test Facilities MTS, you will be part of a team that supports the operation, maintenance, function and documentation surrounding Capital Test Equipment in the application of Environmental Space Simulation Testing.

This is a 12-month contract TERM.

Responsibilities

  • Operation, maintenance, upgrading and troubleshooting of Environmental Test Facilities (including Vibration, TVAC, Thermal and Structural Test Equipment)
  • Organize and Optimize Environmental Test Lab Spaces
  • Participate in Risk Assessments, Workplace Safety Inspections and implement safety-driven changes in lab spaces
  • Collaborate with other disciplines to prepare and document Training Plans for equipment
  • Write and Review Technical Documents pertaining to Test Equipment including standard operating procedures
  • Preform 6S and apply of Lean Manufacturing principles in Lab Spaces
  • Review Engineering Test Procedures and test requirements, validate and make recommendations for feasibility of testing
  • Assist in execution of projects related to Test Equipment
  • Day-to-day reoccurring task for lab operation (kanban, tactical meetings, schedule adherence)
  • Liaise with cross-functional team members to resolve technical issues

Qualifications

  • Bachelor’s degree (or equivalent) in Mechanical/Manufacturing Engineering/Industrial Engineering or a Technologist diploma in a related field with 5+ years of relevant experience
  • Minimum 1 years of experience in manufacturing and test equipment (aerospace preferred)
  • Strong technical writing skills with experience writing documents, manuals and procedures
  • Independent and collaborative worker
  • Experienced in 6S, TPM, Lean Manufacturing, TPM
  • Demonstrated multi-tasking and task-prioritizing capability in a high-pressure environment
  • Experience in Environmental Test Equipment(Vibration, TVAC, Thermal and Structural Test Equipment) is an asset

Comments/Special Considerations:

Successful candidates must obtain and hold security clearance at the reliability status level, and pass security assessment for the Controlled Goods Program (CGP).
