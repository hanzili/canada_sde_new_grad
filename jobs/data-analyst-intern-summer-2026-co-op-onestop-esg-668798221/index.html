About the Role

Analyze ESG data, build dashboards, generate insights

About OneStop ESG

Global ESG marketplace, 80k+ visitors monthly

Full Description

Company: OneStop ESG
Location: Fully remote. Core collaboration hours will partially overlap with Gulf Standard Time (UTC+4)
Work Term: 12 weeks starting from the first week of May (exact start date to be decided)
Hours: 35 hours per week
Compensation: This role is offered exclusively to students completing a registered co-op or for-credit internship through their post-secondary institution.
Number of Openings: 2
Application Deadline: April 30, 2026 (rolling; early applications encouraged)
Reports to: Core team working at the intersection of product, data, and growth

About OneStop ESG:

One Stop ESG is a global ESG and sustainability marketplace and media platform that brings the entire sustainability ecosystem into one place. We help organisations communicate real impact, reach decision-makers, and connect with a highly engaged global community working across climate, sustainability, responsible business, and governance.

Our platform reaches 80,000+ monthly visitors and a 50,000+ subscriber community across 100+ countries.

Our audience includes professionals from financial institutions, energy, manufacturing and heavy industry including steel and cement, real estate and construction, transport and logistics, FMCG and retail, technology, agriculture and food systems, consulting, healthcare, climate tech, academia, and policy teams who rely on One Stop ESG to stay informed and discover credible sustainability solutions.

The business is built around five products that work together:

OneStop ESG Marketplace: A curated directory of ESG solution providers across categories like environmental consultancy, clean technology, sustainable finance, and ESG data platforms.
OneStop ESG News: Independent editorial coverage and analysis on how organisations are responding to real sustainability challenges.
OneStop ESG Educate: A growing library of ESG learning resources and certifications.
OneStop ESG Events: Coverage of global ESG conferences, forums, webinars, and workshops.
OneStop ESG Jobs: A specialized job board for ESG and sustainability roles worldwide.

We are a global team working across geographies, collaborating closely to build one of the most trusted platforms in the sustainability ecosystem.

About the Internship

We are hiring two Data Analyst Interns to join our Data and Research team for Summer 2026. This is a hands-on role where you will work on real datasets, contribute to platform insights, and present your findings directly to senior team members and founders.

We value interns who ask questions, challenge assumptions, and bring a point of view to the data.

What You'll Do

Marketplace and platform data

  • Maintain and improve datasets of ESG solution providers, including categorization, tagging, and structuring
  • Identify gaps, duplicates, and inconsistencies in data and help improve overall data quality
  • Build simple queries and internal tools to make data more usable across teams

Research and insights

  • Support research initiatives by organizing and analyzing sustainability-related data
  • Translate datasets into clear insights that can be used across product, content, and growth
  • Contribute to internal notes, summaries, and insight-driven outputs

Jobs and ecosystem analytics

  • Analyze trends across the ESG jobs ecosystem including roles, geographies, and hiring patterns
  • Support dashboard creation and tracking of ecosystem activity

Audience and content analytics

  • Analyze engagement across newsletters and platform content
  • Identify patterns in what drives engagement and suggest improvements

Data and reporting

  • Work with SQL, Python, or spreadsheets to clean, structure, and analyze datasets
  • Build dashboards or reports to support decision-making
  • Document workflows and datasets for internal use

Hiring Scope

We are hiring globally and welcome applications from students across geographies who can work in a remote, collaborative environment.

Tools and Tech Stack

You will get exposure to and work with:

Languages and querying: SQL, Python (pandas, requests, BeautifulSoup)
Visualization: Tableau, Power BI, or Looker Studio
Spreadsheets: Google Sheets and Excel for collaborative work and quick analysis
Cloud and storage: AWS or GCP (basic exposure)
Collaboration: Slack, Notion, GitHub, Google Workspace

You do not need experience with all of these. You should be comfortable picking up new tools quickly.

Required Qualifications

  • Currently enrolled in a Bachelor's program in Data Science, Computer Science, Statistics, Finance, Economics, Environmental Studies, or a related field (3rd or 4th year preferred)
  • Currently registered in a co-op or for-credit internship program at an accredited post-secondary institution
  • Returning to full-time studies after the work term (Fall 2026)
  • Working knowledge of SQL (joins, aggregations, subqueries) and Python (pandas, basic scripting)
  • Comfortable with Excel and Google Sheets, including formulas, pivot tables, and lookups
  • Strong analytical and problem-solving skills with attention to detail
  • Genuine interest in ESG, sustainability, climate data, or responsible investing
  • Clear written and verbal communication in English

Nice to Have

  • Experience with Tableau, Power BI, or Looker Studio
  • Exposure to cloud platforms such as AWS, Azure, or GCP
  • Web scraping or API experience (BeautifulSoup, Selenium, requests, REST APIs)
  • Version control with Git or GitHub
  • Personal or academic projects involving data analysis, scraping, or visualization (please link in your application)
  • Coursework or prior internship in finance, accounting, environmental science, or climate-related fields

What You'll Gain

  • Exposure to real ESG and sustainability datasets and how they are structured and used in practice
  • Experience across the data analytics workflow including cleaning, analysis, visualization, and insight generation
  • Mentorship from senior team members with regular feedback
  • A capstone project that can become a strong portfolio piece
  • Potential LinkedIn recommendation on successful completion
  • Potential consideration for future opportunities based on performance

How to Apply

Submit the following to karan.hooda@onestopesg.com with the subject line:
"Data Analyst Intern, Summer 2026, [Your Name]"

  • Resume (maximum 2 pages, PDF)
  • A short note (300 words or less) on why you are interested in ESG data
  • Link to GitHub, portfolio, or a sample data project (optional but strongly encouraged)
  • Confirmation from your institution that you are eligible for a credit-based internship

Selection Process:
After resume shortlisting, candidates will go through two interview rounds:

  • Round 1: Interview with the Technical Lead
  • Round 2: Interview with the Head of Product
  • A hands-on exercise may be included in either one or both of the interview rounds.

Equal Opportunity

OneStop ESG is an equal opportunity employer. We welcome applications from all qualified candidates regardless of background, identity, or experience.

Work Location: Remote
