Accountabilities User support/ End User Support

  • Review Article listing packages in share point
  • Respond to e-mails from different parts of the business
  • Create and expire PIR
  • Working with new vendor set up
  • Communicate with supply chain/BU on any issue that arise
  • Manage Vendor transition/ DC Transition
  • Update shipping location/set new location
  • Review sourcing issue( Internal and external sourcing issue)
  • May lead some training and huddle discussions
  • Analyze, explain and resolve system, business and process related inquiries from users
  • Network transition.

Role Requirements:

  • Post secondary education at either the college or university level
  • Strong working knowledge of legacy systems to support in the coexistence of IPFR and Legacy
  • Strong computer skills in Microsoft Excel, Microsoft Access and Micro Strategy
  • Demonstrated in-depth process knowledge of the particular Business Organization and Business Unit they are representing
  • Above average analytical skills and problem solving skills
  • Proven ability to respond to new situations and work in a constantly changing environment
  • Able to effectively manage multiple projects and prioritize work in order to meet deadlines
  • Strong communication, interpersonal, facilitation and presentation skills required for communicating to all levels within the organization.
  • Team player with a passion for learning and training and working in collaboration with others
  • A self-starter with demonstrated ability to thrive in a fast paced work environment
  • 2 to 3 years’ experience in retail and supply chain
  • Work weekend when needed
  • Knowledge of JDA/IPFR and SQL
