Quality Assurance Analyst: Co-op Opportunity

at Real Estate Webmasters

📍 Location Nanaimo, British Columbia Remote
📋 Details Co-op · $50,000/yr
📅 Posted Mar 27, 2026

About the Role

Perform testing, document results, support releases.

About Real Estate Webmasters

Real Estate Webmasters; growth-oriented tech team.

Full Description

Department: Product / IT

Reports to: Head of Product

Location: Nanaimo BC - Remote possible for those eligible to work in Canada.

Overview We are seeking a QA Analyst Co-op student who is motivated to build a long-term career in Quality Assurance .

This role is best suited for someone who is specifically interested in QA as their primary career path and is looking to develop strong, practical experience in software testing and product quality.

This opportunity is designed for candidates who have a strong interest in Quality Assurance and are looking to build depth and long-term growth within this field. You will join a collaborative product team working on web-based applications, reporting to the Head of Product and working closely with a Senior QA Analyst for onboarding and mentorship.

Our Core Values At REW, we live and breathe these values. We expect our everyone on our team to embody them every day:

  • Innovation: Staying curious, open-minded, and always looking for ways to do things more efficiently.
  • Customer Success: Caring deeply about delivering great service to both internal and external clients.
  • Always be Awesome: Remaining positive and helpful even when days are tough; supporting colleagues in a way that elevates the team.

How We Work

  • We operate in an agile, product-focused environment with regular releases
  • Emphasis is placed on clear communication, accountability, and practical documentation
  • Testing insights and results are captured directly within our workflow tools (JIRA)

What You’ll Do

  • Develop a strong understanding of our product, users, and workflows
  • Execute functional, regression, and exploratory testing across web applications
  • Contribute to test coverage by designing and maintaining effective test cases
  • Document testing activities including: scope of testing, results, and supporting evidence
  • Collaborate with developers, designers, and product stakeholders to identify and resolve issues
  • Assist in troubleshooting UI/UX, browser, and performance-related issues
  • Participate in agile ceremonies and support release validation

What We’re Looking For

  • A co-op student who is intentionally pursuing a career in Quality Assurance
  • Demonstrated interest in QA beyond academic requirements (e.g., personal learning, projects, or prior exposure)
  • Strong attention to detail and analytical thinking
  • Ability to identify edge cases and think through user scenarios
  • Clear written and verbal communication skills
  • Proactive mindset and willingness to take ownership of tasks

Nice to Have

  • Basic understanding of web technologies (HTML, CSS, JavaScript)
  • Exposure to QA concepts such as test cases, bug tracking, or regression testing
  • Interest in test automation (self-driven learning is encouraged)

What Success Looks Like

  • Consistent, high-quality documentation of testing within JIRA
  • Strong understanding of product functionality and user workflows
  • Ability to independently execute testing and clearly communicate findings
  • Reliable contribution to product quality and release readiness

Why This Role This is an opportunity to gain hands-on experience in a product-driven QA environment , working closely with experienced team members and contributing to real product releases.

We are looking for someone who views this role as the foundation of a career in Quality Assurance .

Compensation

  • Base Salary: $50,000 annually (equivalent)
  • For the right candidate, this could lead to a permanent, full time position!
