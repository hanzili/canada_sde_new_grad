About the Role
We’re looking for a motivated and detail-oriented student to join our team as a Bookkeeper through the Canada Summer Jobs program. This is a great opportunity to gain hands-on experience in a professional (but fun) office environment, with the potential for continued employment after the program.

What You’ll Be Doing

  • Manage day-to-day bookkeeping tasks using Sage and/or QuickBooks Online (QBO)
  • Accurately record and categorize financial transactions
  • Reconcile bank and credit card accounts
  • Assist with accounts payable and accounts receivable
  • Maintain organized and compliant financial records
  • Support month-end and year-end processes

What We’re Looking For

  • Basic understanding of bookkeeping principles (AP, AR, reconciliations)
  • Strong attention to detail and organizational skills
  • Ability to work with numbers accurately and efficiently
  • Good communication and time-management skills
  • Willingness to learn and continue professional development within the company
  • Reliable and able to work independently and as part of a team

Eligibility Requirements (Canada Summer Jobs Program)

  • Must be between 16 - 30 years of age
  • Must be eligible to work under the Canada Summer Jobs program
  • Must be able to work on-site in Lunenburg, NS

Why Join Us?
At Books N’ More, you’ll gain real-world experience in a supportive, team-focused environment where accuracy and growth matter. This role is perfect for someone looking to build a strong foundation in bookkeeping and finance.
