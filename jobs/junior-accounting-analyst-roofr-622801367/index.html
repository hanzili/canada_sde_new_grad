About the Role

About Roofr

Growing SaaS startup with strong growth and equity

Full Description

At Roofr, we’re obsessed with our customers. We constantly gather feedback to shape, prioritize, and launch the products they truly need. That’s what makes Roofr’s CRM special. We started by building essential sales tools like aerial roof measurements and digital sales proposals. But when our customers asked for a simple, affordable way to manage and scale their entire businesses, we listened. So, we created a CRM that connects these solutions—along with payments, material ordering, and more—into a seamless, powerful platform. With a clear roadmap ahead, we’re excited to continue expanding and leading the market with innovative products.

We have an amazing culture, strong financials, and best-in-class company metrics. It’s an exciting time to be part of an extraordinary startup that is already successful, yet still early enough to offer its team significant growth, equity, and the opportunity to make a real impact.

This position is for an existing vacancy.

We are looking for a Junior Accounting Analyst to support the accounting team with day-to-day transactional accounting, reconciliations, and close activities. This role is ideal for someone early in their accounting career who wants to develop a strong foundation in a fast-growing SaaS company.

What You’ll Get to Do:

  • Support monthly close activities, including:
  • Assist with:
  • Support revenue and billing processes, including:
  • Maintain schedules for key balance sheet accounts
  • Support audit and compliance requests by preparing documentation and support schedules
  • Help improve documentation of accounting processes and controls
  • Participate in process improvement initiatives as the team scales

What You’ll Bring to the Role:

  • Bachelor’s degree in Accounting, Finance, or related field
  • 1–2 years of relevant accounting or finance experience (internships included)
  • Strong attention to detail and accuracy
  • Solid Excel / Google Sheets skills
  • Ability to follow established processes and meet deadlines
  • Willingness to learn and grow in a fast-paced environment
  • Strong organizational and communication skills

Bonus Points:

  • Exposure to SaaS or technology companies
  • Experience with accounting systems (NetSuite, QuickBooks, or similar)
  • Experience with financial tech stacks (Ramp, Brex, Floatcard, Bill.com, etc.)
