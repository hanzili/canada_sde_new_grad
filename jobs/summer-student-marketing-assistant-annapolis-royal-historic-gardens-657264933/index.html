Marketing / Digital Marketing Internship

Summer Student - Marketing Assistant

at Annapolis Royal Historic Gardens

📍 Location Annapolis Royal, NS
📋 Details Full-time, Seasonal · 14 weeks · $16.85 per hour · Seasonal
📅 Posted Apr 04, 2026

About the Role

Assist marketing projects to raise visibility

About Annapolis Royal Historic Gardens

Historic garden promoting Nova Scotia tourism and heritage

Full Description

Position Title: Marketing Assistant

Area of Specialty: Marketing & Promotion

Immediate Supervisor: Manager

Position Summary:

  • Assisting with a series of marketing projects designed to raise the profile of the Historic Gardens, increase visitation, lengthen visitor stays and encourage repeat visitation.

Duties:

  • Assist management with innovative ways to attract more visitors from target markets in Nova Scotia and Atlantic Canada..
  • Increase cross promotion with other regional attractions and businesses in order to intercept visitors already in the region and encourage them to visit the Historic Gardens while they are in the area.
  • To assist staff in carrying out all protocols for visitor safety within the Historic Gardens.
  • All other duties as relating to the operation of the Historic Gardens as required for the overall good of the Gardens, as instructed by the Manager.

General Expectations on the Job:

  • must interact well with co-workers
  • positive interaction with visitors and potential visitors and responding to their inquiries
  • able to work independently with minimal supervision
  • practice proper and safe use of tools and equipment
  • be vigilant about all safety protocols

Skills Required (prerequisite):

  • excellent interpersonal skills
  • strong organizational abilities, self-starter
  • interest in tourism and visitor service (interest in gardens and heritage an asset)
  • able and willing to work in a variety of conditions, flexible hours
  • must have valid drivers license

Equipment Used:

  • Office equipment: computers, copiers, fax, etc
  • Customer Service equipment: cash register, interac, etc

Hours of Work:

  • Work Term: 14 weeks
  • Hours: 35 hours per week. Half hour unpaid lunch.

Job Types: Full-time, Seasonal
Contract length: 14 weeks

Pay: $16.85 per hour

Expected hours: 35 per week

Benefits:

  • Casual dress
  • On-site parking

Application question(s):

  • Please tell us why you would like to join our team at the Annapolis Royal Historic Gardens.
  • This is a funded student position. Are you enrolled in university or college for September 2026?
  • This job is in Annapolis Royal, Nova Scotia. Do you live close enough to commute to Annapolis Royal daily for work? And if not, please explain your plan.

Work Location: In person
