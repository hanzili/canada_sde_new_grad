About Enverus

Full Description

Description Associate Consultant Why YOU want this position At Enverus, we’re committed to empowering the global quality of life by helping our customers make energy affordable and accessible to the world.

We are the most trusted energy-dedicated SaaS company, with a platform built to maximize value from generative AI, and our innovative solutions are reshaping the way energy is consumed and managed. By offering anytime, anywhere access to analytics and insights, we’re helping our customers make better decisions that help provide communities around the world with clean, affordable energy.

The energy industry is changing fast. But we’ve continued to lead the way in energy technology, creating intelligent connections across the entire energy ecosystem, from renewables, power and utilities, to oil and gas and financial institutions. Our solutions create more efficient production and distribution, capital allocation, renewable energy development, investment and sourcing, and help reduce costs by automating crucial business operations. Of course, this wouldn’t be possible without our people, which is why we have built a team of individuals from a diverse range of backgrounds.

Are you ready to help power the global quality of life? Join Enverus, and be a part of creating a brighter, more sustainable tomorrow.

We are currently seeking an Associate Consultant to join our Business Automation team. This role offers the opportunity to join a rapidly growing company delivering industry-leading solutions to customers in the world’s most dynamic and fastest-growing sector.

Performance Objectives

  • Responsible for driving customer success by helping customers get the most out of the Minerals products they have purchased.
  • Partners with customers to manage their mineral portfolio. Focus is primarily on MineralSoft but will include other products such as EnergyLink, MiQ, Prism, or DrillingInfo as needed.
  • Works with customers to review existing business processes and metrics. Makes process improvement recommendations based on established best practices and conducts product training for customers related to the processes being reviewed.
  • Gathers information necessary to make decisions; anticipates and resolves problems in a timely manner.
  • Works on projects of moderate complexity with general direction from team manager.

Competitive Candidate Profile

  • Bachelor’s degree or equivalent in education and experience.
  • 2+ years related experience in designing and implementing business processes.
  • Works independently with general supervision to manage project timelines and risk. Receives general instruction on managing risk, scope, and change control.
  • Applies practical knowledge of job area typically obtained through advanced education and/or work experience.
  • Excellent organizational and time management skills.
  • Excellent communication, presentation, and facilitation skills.
  • Strong understanding of the mineral management business processes, from the perspective of the mineral owner/mineral investor.
  • Basic abilities in data analysis with the ability to understand trends and identify anomalies.

Physical Demands

  • Ability to sit for long periods, work on a computer with repetitive motions and utilize devices typically found in an office environment.
  • Ability to communicate effectively with another person.
  • Regular and predictable attendance is required.
  • Ability to work in an office environment as required.
  • Travel requirements – up to 10% or as required for company needs.
