Job Profile

Join Our Team as a Retail Sales Associate & Ringside Cardtender at TKO Toy Co.!

At TKO Toy Company, we believe collecting and playing should be an experience. We are more than just a hobby store. We are a fast-growing, community-driven destination for trading cards, collectibles, toys, and entertainment.

Recently voted again as, Guelph’s #1 Hobby, Toy & Game Store and recognized as a TCGplayer Hobby Shop of the Year Finalist (2nd Place), TKO Toy Company continues to push the boundaries of what modern hobby retail can look like.

As part of our expansion into Stone Road Mall, we have launched Ringside, a unique bar-style trading card experience where customers can select and open packs and boxes in a highly social, interactive environment. Think hospitality-style customer engagement combined with the trading card industry. At Ringside, employees are expected to actively host, guide, and enhance the customer experience by creating a welcoming and memorable atmosphere around trading cards and collectibles.

We are seeking a Retail Sales Associate & Ringside Cardtender who is passionate about customer experience, community engagement, and creating memorable moments for customers. This is not a traditional retail position. The ideal candidate is personable, confident communicating with customers, comfortable in fast-paced environments, and capable of balancing hospitality-style interaction with strong retail fundamentals.

In this role, you will help deliver an elevated in-store experience while supporting day-to-day retail operations in a fast-paced environment.

Why Join Us?

At TKO Toy Co., we are building more than a retail store. We are building a destination for collectors and community members alike.

Here’s why you should consider joining our team:

We value professionalism, reliability, and strong customer service, with opportunities to grow alongside a rapidly expanding company.

Work within Ringside, a first-of-its-kind trading card experience that blends retail, entertainment, and hospitality-style customer interaction.

Be part of a welcoming and energetic environment where customer relationships and memorable experiences are a core part of the business.

Opportunities for compensated travel to represent TKO Toy Co. at trade shows, conventions, and special events.

  • Employee discount on store products
  • Company-sponsored team meals and events hosted periodically throughout the year
  • Access to exclusive employee discount programs through CFIB, including savings with brands such as Shell, Telus, Avis, Budget, Canon, LG, Dell, and more
  • Flexible scheduling based on availability and business needs
  • Opportunities for advancement within a growing company

Key Duties & Responsibilities

  • Provide customers with a high level of service and engagement to create a memorable in-store experience
  • Guide customers through trading card, collectible, and product selections
  • Actively support the Ringside experience by engaging customers in conversation and creating a welcoming atmosphere around pack and box openings
  • Build rapport with repeat customers and contribute to a strong community-focused environment
  • Process purchases, returns, and exchanges accurately using POS systems
  • Support merchandising, restocking, and overall store presentation standards
  • Assist with inventory handling and basic fulfillment support when required
  • Maintain cleanliness, organization, and safety standards throughout the store
  • Support team members and contribute positively to daily operations

Qualifications

  • Previous experience in retail, hospitality, serving, bartending, or customer-facing environments preferred
  • Strong communication and interpersonal skills
  • Comfortable working in a highly interactive, fast-paced environment
  • Ability to multitask while maintaining professionalism and customer focus
  • Reliable, punctual, and team-oriented
  • Ability to stand, walk, bend, and lift items for extended periods
  • Interest or familiarity with trading cards, sports, collectibles, or hobby products is considered an asset

Application Requirements

Applicants are encouraged to submit a resume along with a cover letter outlining their interest in the role and relevant experience.

Applicants are also requested to answer the screening questions below within their cover letter or attached application document. Responses to these questions will be considered as part of the overall candidate evaluation and interview selection process.

Screening Questions (Please utilize one experience per question/asset when responding.)

1. This role has aspects that share similarities with hospitality and bartending-style service, where employees are expected to confidently engage with customers, maintain conversation, and create a welcoming atmosphere.

Please describe a previous role or experience where your personality, communication skills, and ability to engage with people contributed to a positive customer experience.

2. Describe a time you worked in a fast-paced or high-pressure environment with large volumes of customers, competing priorities, or demanding situations. How did you remain organized, maintain professionalism, and ensure customers still received a positive experience?

3. A major part of this role involves building rapport with repeat customers and contributing to a strong community-focused environment.

Please describe a situation where you successfully built a relationship with a customer(s), remembered returning guests or clients, or created an experience that encouraged repeat business or positive feedback.
