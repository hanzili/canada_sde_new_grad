Company

MORO Technologies Inc

Job Type

Paid Co op Internship 20 to 30 hours/week

Hybrid, minimum two days per week in office

About MORO

MORO Technologies Inc is a Toronto based startup building a mobile app that supports families and caregivers of non speaking individuals. Our focus is on communication, routine sharing, and care coordination. We are early stage, fast moving, and purpose driven.

Role Overview

We are looking for a co op student who is currently enrolled in a recognized school co op program and eligible for government grants. This role is ideal for someone who enjoys creating short form content, understands social media platforms, and is comfortable managing content from idea to post.

Most of your time will be spent creating, editing, and preparing content that supports MORO’s online presence and growth.

This is a hybrid role and requires someone who is a self starter, organized, and able to consistently deliver quality content.

Key Responsibilities

Create short form content for Instagram, TikTok, Facebook, and YouTube Shorts

Edit videos using Canva and CapCut or Instagram Edits

Prepare content calendars and schedule posts

Support daily management of MORO’s social media accounts

Assist with basic paid ad setup and monitoring on social platforms

Repurpose content across platforms

Collaborate with the founders on content ideas, messaging, and campaigns

Help maintain a consistent brand voice and visual style

Required Qualifications

Currently enrolled in a school co-op or internship program

Eligible for wage subsidy or government co-op grants

Strong interest and experience in social media and content creation

Experience using Canva and CapCut or similar tools

Basic understanding of Instagram, TikTok, and short form video trends

Comfortable working independently and meeting deadlines

Willing to work in office at least two days per week in our Scarborough office

Nice to Have

Experience running or assisting with social media ads

Basic understanding of analytics and engagement metrics

Interest in startups, tech, or social impact work

Experience managing or growing a social media account

What You Will Gain

Hands on experience in a real startup environment

Direct mentorship from founders

Ownership over content and creative direction

Exposure to marketing, branding, and growth strategy

A strong portfolio of real world work

Opportunity to grow with the company beyond the internship

How to Apply

Please send your resume and a short note explaining your co-op program and availability.

If you have examples of content you have created, portfolios, or social accounts you manage, include them.
