About the Role

Are you looking to grow your accounting or finance career in a professional, supportive environment? M. Zafar & Co. Consulting Inc. is seeking a Junior Accountant to assist with bookkeeping, reporting, and financial analysis for our clients.

This role is ideal for detail-oriented individuals who enjoy working with numbers, learning new financial software, and supporting clients’ business operations.

What You’ll Do

  • Assist with bookkeeping, accounts payable/receivable, and general ledger maintenance
  • Prepare financial reports and reconcile accounts
  • Support clients with tax filings and documentation
  • Maintain accurate and organized financial records
  • Collaborate with senior accountants and management on client projects

What We’re Looking For

  • Basic accounting or finance knowledge (degree or coursework preferred)
  • Proficiency in Excel and accounting software (QuickBooks, Sage, or similar)
  • Strong attention to detail and organizational skills
  • Good communication and teamwork skills
  • Ability to manage multiple client accounts

Why Work With Us

  • Hands-on experience in a professional accounting firm
  • Supportive and collaborative team environment
  • Opportunities for professional development and growth
  • Stable full-time position with consistent schedule

Schedule

  • Monday to Friday
  • 8-hour shifts
  • Day shift

About the Company

M. Zafar & Co. Consulting Inc. is a professional services firm based in Saskatoon, Saskatchewan. We provide accounting, bookkeeping, and business consulting services to a range of clients across Canada, helping businesses streamline operations and make informed financial decisions.
