Full Description

About Niche:

Niche Technology designs, develops, supports, and implements an advanced police Records Management System (RMS). Our current offering, NicheRMS, covers the full breadth of police operations as standard, out-of-the-box functionality that is available now and in live production use. It is a single, integrated operational police information management platform—all police data is organized into a single, accessible source with a set of core building blocks. This is our sole focus and line of business.

Niche Technology Inc. is a privately-owned Canadian corporation, established in 1992, that is owned and managed by its founders

Niche has subsidiaries in the UK and the US serving projects in the UK, Canada, Australia, and the US

Our development office is based in Winnipeg, MB. We have had next to zero turnover in our development group, resulting in a stable and highly experienced team.

We are looking for an innovative Junior Quality Assurance Analyst, reporting to the Lead QA Analyst and Lead Developer to join this team.

Skills:

  • Analytical with a strong attention to detail.

  • Problem-solving skills to find solutions for various quality issues.

  • Strong multitasking skills to transition efficiently between projects.

  • Ability to work independently or within a team.

  • Time management and organizational proficiency.

  • Experience testing API’s and web services interfaces for integrations.

  • Experience testing web, mobile and client/server applications.

  • Prior experience with SQL, SQL Server, and Microsoft Visual Studio.

  • Experience with accessibility testing or willingness to learn.

Characteristics and Responsibilities:

  • Ambitious, eager to learn with a strong aptitude for technology.

  • Excellent written and oral communication.

  • Open to asking for assistance to solve problems.

  • Testing execution and reporting.

  • Maintain the company’s quality assurance practices and procedures.
  • Other duties as assigned.

Education/Experience:

  • College Diploma or University Degree in Computer Science or related field.
  • Previous experience a plus.

Job Requirements:

Because of the nature of the work and our clients, you must be able to meet the following requirements:

  • Obtain a pre-employment criminal background search
  • Are eligible to attain Enhanced reliability status, and willing to complete additional
  • security clearances as required
  • Be a Canadian Citizen or have PR status

Benefits:

  • Hybrid working option available after training is completed.
  • Modern office with state-of-the-art equipment.
  • Catered lunches on Fridays in lunchroom with espresso machine, free pop and gaming consoles.
  • Full on-site gym.
  • Parking or transit pass provided.
  • Full benefits.
  • Generous compensation package.
