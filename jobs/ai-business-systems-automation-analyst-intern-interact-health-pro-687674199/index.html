Full Description

AI BUSINESS SYSTEMS & AUTOMATION ANALYST INTERN

(Tech + Strategy Integration)

About Us

Interact Health PRO (IHP) develops AI-supported operational systems focused on rehabilitation coordination, medical documentation, communication tracking, and healthcare workflow optimization.

We combine technology, automation, and operational strategy to improve the coordination of care and the management of medical-legal processes.

Role Overview

As an AI Business Systems & Automation Analyst Intern, you will work between technical teams and operational teams to improve business workflows, systems integration, communication structures, and automation processes.

You will help analyze:

  • workflow inefficiencies
  • operational bottlenecks
  • communication systems
  • reporting structures
  • administrative workflows

This role combines:

  • business analysis
  • systems thinking
  • automation planning
  • operational optimization

Key Responsibilities

Business Systems Analysis

  • Analyze current operational workflows
  • Identify process inefficiencies and optimization opportunities
  • Support workflow mapping and documentation
  • Assist with systems planning and operational improvements

Automation & Workflow Support

  • Support automation projects using Airtable and Make.com
  • Assist with trigger structures and workflow logic
  • Help improve reporting and communication systems
  • Support onboarding and operational automations

Operational Coordination

  • Collaborate with Developers, Analysts, Marketing, and Administration Teams
  • Support project tracking and operational reporting
  • Participate in workflow planning meetings
  • Contribute recommendations for process improvements

AI Integration

You will actively use:

  • Claude AI workflows
  • Claude.md systems
  • AI-assisted workflow mapping
  • Airtable automations
  • Make.com operational systems

AI will support:

  • systems analysis
  • operational documentation
  • workflow optimization
  • automation planning

Technical Skills

  • Workflow mapping
  • Airtable
  • Process documentation
  • CRM systems
  • Business analysis fundamentals

Assets

  • Make.com
  • Dashboard/reporting systems
  • Automation planning exposure

Soft Skills & Professional Traits

  • Analytical and organized mindset
  • Strong communication skills
  • Professional accountability
  • Systems-thinking abilities
  • Team collaboration mindset

Learning Outcomes

  • Gain business systems analysis experience
  • Learn operational workflow design
  • Support real-world automation projects
  • Participate in healthcare operational optimization

Why Join Us?

Become part of a 25+ IHP Intern Team and collaborate closely with Web Developers, Full Stack App Developers, AWS Engineers, Business Analysts, IT & Security Networking, Medical-Health, Social Media, Video Editors, and Administrative Groups. Together, we are launching Interact Health PRO’s innovative platforms to transform healthcare, patient outcomes, and virtual care technology.
