About WOLF

At Wolf Advanced Technology (WOLF), we’re redefining what’s possible on the battlefield and beyond. Our ultra-rugged, field-deployable systems don’t just capture and process embedded video—they supercharge it with real-time AI insights, neural-network deep learning, and hyper-efficient encoding. Powered by the latest NVIDIA, Xilinx, and AMD processors, our gear thrives where others fail, meeting the uncompromising demands of glob-al defence and aerospace pioneers.
Born from a relentless drive to innovate, WOLF’s solutions set the bar for mission-critical performance—trusted by top-tier contractors and program leads worldwide. We’re a high-octane, collaboration-first squad that thrives on exponential growth and breakneck development cycles. If you live for cutting-edge tech, rapid R&D sprints, and making a life-or-death impact, your next breakthrough belongs at WOLF. Join us—and help build the future of mission-critical video systems.

For more information, please visit our website: wolfadvancedtechnology.com

Your Mission Brief

WOLF is seeking a motivated and curious Junior Software Engineer to join our special projects team. This role is ideal for a recent graduate with a strong foundation in software development and a focus on artificial intelligence. You will contribute to end-to-end design and development of innovative solutions, working across front-end, back-end, and system-level programming in a fast-paced, dynamic environment. in this exciting role you will:

  • Contribute to the end-to-end design, development, and deployment of software applications as part of a special project team
  • Write, test, and maintain code across front-end web/app interfaces and back-end services
  • Develop back-end services and APIs using .NET 8 and C#
  • Design and implement Linux and Windows drivers and applications using C and C++
  • Identify, troubleshoot, and resolve software bugs; deploy enhancements and updates
  • Support AI-related development, including inference and agent-based systems
  • Write and maintain scripts to improve automation and operational efficiency
  • Participate in version control and code management using GitHub
  • Perform administrative and coordination tasks related to software project management
  • Collaborate effectively with peers, colleagues, and leadership while maintaining strong working relationships
  • Take ownership of assigned projects with minimal oversight
  • Contribute to additional tasks and projects as required

Your Edge

You are a recent graduate in Software Engineering, Computer Science, or a related field with a focus on AI, bringing a solid foundation in programming languages such as C, C++, and C#, along with experience in .NET development and familiarity with both Linux and Windows environments. You have exposure to AI concepts such as inference or agent development, and ideally some background in electronics. You pride yourself in being detail-oriented, highly organized, and an effective communicator who can manage multiple priorities in a fast-paced environment. You demonstrate strong initiative, take ownership of their work with minimal oversight, and are eager to learn and adapt, especially when working with new or proprietary systems. A collaborative team player with a positive attitude and sense of humour, you are comfortable contributing to shared codebases using tools like GitHub, proficient in common business and design software, and flexible to meet project demands, including occasional overtime.

Wolfpack Perks

At WOLF, we invest in you – not just through a competitive base salary, tailored to your skills and experience, but with growth, and work-life balance. This role is a one-year contract, with opportunity for extension to a permanent position. Here’s what you can look forward to:

  • Total Compensation & Growth: Negotiable salary plus profit-sharing after your first year.
  • Flexible Work Model: Balance in-office collaboration with time at home - whatever helps you perform at your best.
  • Professional Development: Support for industry conferences, professional memberships, and ongoing learning to keep your career trajectory soaring.
  • Team Culture: Regular social events - from bi-weekly wellness mornings and food-truck lunches to holiday parties and sports outings—so you can build real connections with your teammates.

Ready to Unlock Your Mission?

At WOLF, we thrive on innovation—but we also play by strict defence rules. To join our pack, applicants must be able to provide valid documentation to show Canadian or US citizenship or Canadian Permanent Residency and undergo a police check with no criminal background.

Join WOLF and be part of a culture that rewards your talents, champions your growth, and ensures you’re set up to thrive—both on and off the production floor. Apply today!
