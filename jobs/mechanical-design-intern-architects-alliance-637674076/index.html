Job: Mechanical Design Intern

Job ID: REINEMI

Start Date: April 2025

Duration: 12-16 months 40 hours/week

Compensating: $20/hr

We are seeking a Mechanical Design Intern to join our Mechanical Design team. In this role you will be responsible to produce mechanical drawings in accordance with project requirements under the supervision of the Mechanical Designer.

As part of our internship program you will be exposed to and trained in all aspects of the job ranging from HVAC and P&D drafting to engineering design, project management and team management.

We offer competitive compensation packages, flexible working conditions, and most importantly an open-door policy where everyone from all levels of seniority can participate in decision making

Novatrend Engineering Group LTD is a Mechanical and Electrical Engineering firm with a combined experience of over 50 years in the residential and commercial building sector.

Though we have a broad range of specialties, our focus in the high rise residential builds has made us one of the key contributors to this sector.

Our philosophy is very simple: focus on customer satisfaction while maintaining a harmonious workplace environment.

To achieve these goals, it is essential to understand the client’s needs which can only be accomplished through clear consistent communication. To maintain this standard, our principals participate in design meeting for all projects. We recognize the importance of coordination and the necessity for timely responses to clients, architects and fellow members of the design and construction team.

Ours is an open office culture. All employees are encouraged to participate in establishing office policies and their feedback is included in much of the decision making. Understanding our staff’s needs and providing support to make them feel like they’re a valued member of our corporate family is our main goal in operating a successful business.

  • Reporting to Mechanical Designer
  • Drafting of mechanical systems and components
  • Coordinating with the engineering team and other design teams
  • Transferring hand marked sketches of design drawings to AutoCAD
  • Maintaining and improving visual and functional standards of CAD drawings and final product
  • Performing load calculations using engineering software
  • Using Microsoft Excel to assist in various engineering calculations
  • Assisting mechanical designers in various design duties

  • Supporting the engineering team's analysis, evaluations, designs, and implementation

  • Currently Completing a Mechanical Engineering Technologist diploma/degree or equivalent

  • Basic of proficiency in MS Office and AutoCAD; REVIT considered an asset
  • Ability to work both independently with little supervision and in a team environment
  • Attention to detail
  • Strong problem-solving skills and initiative
  • Commitment to ongoing professional growth

Please email rei@architectsalliance.com and include:

  • Cover letter indicating the Job Position you are applying for

  • CV/Resume

You will be asked to confirm your TCHC tenancy prior to interviewing
