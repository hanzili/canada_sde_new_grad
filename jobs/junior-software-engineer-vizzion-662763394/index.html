Full Description

JUNIOR SOFTWARE ENGINEER

ABOUT VIZZION

Vizzion aggregates live road imagery and provides data and services to major brands in the mapping, media, safety & security, ITS (Intelligent Transportation Systems), and traffic & weather industries. Our imagery is used by millions of users every week, saving drivers time, money, and frustration, while also helping the environment by reducing vehicle emissions.

OUR CORE VALUES

  • Cares deeply about customer satisfaction

  • Detail-oriented with a relentless drive for quality and accuracy

  • Unwavering moral compass that puts integrity ahead of profits and personal gain

  • Brings energy and solutions; helps move things forward with a calm, constructive approach even in challenging situations

  • Believes rewards should follow meaningful results, with engineers who make a real impact on the product and the company sharing in that success

  • Prioritizes solving real problems for our customers, and working collaboratively as a team

  • Focuses on the value created rather than the cost incurred

  • Takes a long-term view, prioritizing sustainable growth and lasting impact over short-term wins

  • Acts with empathy, humility, and generosity, placing team and purpose above self-gain

WHAT WE OFFER

  • Competitive hourly salary of $28- 40, based on experience
  • Flexible work hours to support work–life balance
  • Health, dental, and vision coverage for employees and their families
  • Wellness initiatives, including a company wellness program, ergonomic workspace setup, and healthy snacks with catered weekly lunches
  • Professional development opportunities, including Lunch & Learn sessions, mentorship, dedicated development time, and professional development funding
  • Collaborative and social workplace culture, with team lunches, job shadowing, monthly social events, and employee recognition programs

Please note: This position is a full-time, on-site role based in North Vancouver, requiring in-person attendance five days a week.

WHAT WE ARE LOOKING FOR

We are seeking a motivated software engineer to contribute to internal projects such as image analytics, web services, Windows applications, websites, and web apps. You will be involved in designing, coding, testing, and introducing new tools and technologies. This role provides a comprehensive view of a small software engineering business and opportunities to grow your skills. You’ll work with big data and AI to process and analyze large-scale road imagery, helping transportation agencies monitor and manage infrastructure.

KEY ACCOUNTABILITIES

· Iteration planning and estimation

· Design, code, test, release

· Assist account managers with customer support issues

REQUIRED EDUCATION, SKILLS AND EXPERIENCE

· Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science

· Excellent understanding of data structures and algorithmic design.

· High proficiency in OOP

· Proficiency in C#, .NET, ASP.NET, SQL

· Proficiency with version control, preferably Git

· Working knowledge of HTML and JavaScript

· Excellent communicator fluent in spoken and written English

EXPERIENCE WITH THE FOLLOWING IS CONSIDERED AN ASSET

· RESTful APIs

· Developing and optimizing multithreaded applications

· Optimizing application performance, including caching strategies and load balancing

· Microservices architecture

· Cloud platforms (e.g. Azure, AWS)

· CI/CD pipelines

· PHP

· DevOps

· Object-Oriented Design

· Experience with code optimization

· Agile methodologies

HOW TO APPLY

Submit your resume and cover letter in PDF format. Instead of a cover letter, you can include a short video (up to 3 minutes and 25MB) telling us why you’d like to join Vizzion and what excites you about the role

Email your application to Head of Culture & Talent, at careers@vizzion.com

We'd like to thank all applicants for their interest in our company, but only selected candidates will be contacted for further consideration.
