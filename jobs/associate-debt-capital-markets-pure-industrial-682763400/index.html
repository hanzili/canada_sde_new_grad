About the Role

Underwrite debt, build models, prepare financing materials

About Pure Industrial

Industrial real estate debt specialist with diverse financing

Full Description

Description Role Summary Pure Industrial is seeking an Associate to join their Debt Capital Markets team. As a key member of the Debt Capital Markets team, you will assist in the underwriting, structuring and execution of various debt financing solutions to support our investments, acquisitions, and ongoing debt portfolio strategies. You will collaborate with internal and external stakeholders—including lenders, sponsor, and other corporate teams—while driving analytical rigor and process efficiency.

*Core Responsibilities*

  • Asist in origination of large volume of bank balance sheet, CMBS and other loans associated with Pure Industrial’s acquisitions, dispositions, development, and refinancing activities.  A typical year at Pure Industrial involves multi-million dollars of borrowing activities.
  • Have experience underwriting lending transactions, perform detailed financial modeling and analysis to evaluate financing, including cash flow projections and/or cost estimation. Adept at performing various scenario analyses, covenant stress testing, and valuation sensitivities.
  • As a deal team member, you will be required to prepare marketing materials, conduct due diligence, and coordinate documentations as well as assist with the lender and/or investor due diligence process on new financings, including collaboration with multiple Pure Industrial teams and outside counsel to review and gather all required information.
  • Help produce Pure Industrial management/BX debt reporting packages.
  • Acts as a resource person and coordinates transaction activities with other internal and external stakeholders of Pure Industrial. Collaborate with Pure Industrial investments, operations, construction and development teams to draft marketing materials, manage loan covenants and submit loan draw requests, among other responsibilities.
  • Monitor capital market conditions, including interest rates, spreads and bond market trends, to identify and recommend optimal financing strategies.

Qualifications And Experience

  • Bachelor’s degree in finance, Economics, Business, Real Estate, or a related field; advanced degree or relevant certification (MBA, CFA) a plus.
  • 2–4 years of experience in real estate finance, investment banking, debt capital markets, or related roles.
  • Strong understanding of various debt instruments (including mortgages, revolving credit facilities, CMBS, bonds, and syndicated loans).
  • Demonstrated proficiency in financial modeling, data analysis, and credit underwriting.
  • Familiarity with real estate industry metrics, valuation methods, and deal structures.
  • High degree of competence in Microsoft Office Suite (Excel, PowerPoint) and relevant financial software.
  • Strong analytical mindset with proven interest in commercial real estate finance

Key Skills And Competencies

  • Analytical Problem-Solving: Ability to dissect complex financial structures and provide clear, data-driven recommendations.
  • Detail Orientation: Meticulous diligence, especially in financial modeling and documentation review.
  • Communication & Presentation: Strong written and verbal communication skills; able to articulate complex concepts to both technical and non-technical audiences.
  • Collaboration & Stakeholder Management: Collaborator with a track record of building positive relationships across diverse groups.
  • Initiative & Adaptability: Initiative-taker with a proactive mindset; comfortable working in a fast-paced environment with shifting priorities.
  • Purposeful: Ability to balance multiple assignments under tight deadlines without compromising quality.
