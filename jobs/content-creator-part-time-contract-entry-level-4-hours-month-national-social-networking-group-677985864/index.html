🎥 Social Media Content Creators Wanted – Ottawa

Do you love creating Reels or TikToks? Want real-world experience while building your creator portfolio?

National Social Networking Group is looking for content creators to capture the energy of our social events and turn them into engaging short-form videos.

This role is ideal for aspiring creators, film students, or anyone looking to grow their presence in social media.

What you'll do:

• Capture video at select social events

• Edit engaging Reels/TikToks

• Post content on Instagram and/or TikTok

• Help showcase the fun, welcoming atmosphere of our events

What we're looking for:

• Active Instagram or TikTok account

• Comfortable filming in social settings

• Basic editing skills

• Reliable and professional

Nice to have:

• Experience creating short-form content

Time Commitment:

  • Approximately 4–20 hours per month depending on event schedule.
