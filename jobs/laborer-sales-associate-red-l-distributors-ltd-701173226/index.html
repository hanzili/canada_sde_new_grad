About the Role

Assist customers, operate forklift, support production operations.

About Red-L Distributors Ltd.

Canadian distributor, industrial hoses, safety-focused.

Full Description

Job Summary
We are seeking a motivated and detail-oriented individual to join our team. The ideal candidate will play a key role in supporting sales operations, maintaining a clean and safe work environment This position offers an excellent opportunity to develop skills in manufacturing hoses while contributing to the overall success of our company.

Responsibilities

  • Assist customers by providing product information and support during the sales process
  • Operate Forklift safely and efficiently
  • Perform heavy lifting tasks as required
  • Maintain cleanliness and sanitation of the work area to ensure a safe environment
  • Work efficiently in the production of building and testing hoses
  • Follow safety protocols
  • Ensure proper documentation of activities and adherence to company policies

Qualifications

  • Prior experience or training in forklift operation is an asset
  • Ability to work with various computer programs
  • Ability to perform heavy lifting safely and efficiently
  • Experience in manufacturing or related industrial environments is a plus
  • Strong attention to safety standards
  • Ability to work effectively in a team environment
  • Physical stamina for prolonged periods of standing and lifting
  • Knowledge of hydraulic fittings and hose parts would be an asset
  • Basic knowledge of cleaning and sanitation procedures is desirable.
  • Customer service skills are required as that is a huge part of this position.

This role is essential for maintaining operational efficiency and safety within our facility. We value candidates who are eager to learn, reliable, and committed to upholding high standards of safety and cleanliness.

Job Type: Full-time

  • Dental care
  • Extended health care
  • Life insurance
  • RRSP match

Work Location: In person
