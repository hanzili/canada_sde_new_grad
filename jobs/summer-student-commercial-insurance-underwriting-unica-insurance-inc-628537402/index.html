About the Role

Assist underwriting operations; manage data and logs

About Unica Insurance Inc.

Insurance company; growth opportunities for interns

Full Description

Official Internal Job Title Student

Status Student (Fixed Term)

Job Description As the successful candidate you will be a naturally enthusiastic team player who is able to handle administrative duties efficiently and accurately within defined service levels. You will be required to have a strong attention to detail and organization; and an ability to prioritize and successfully manage all accountabilities within a fast paced and high-volume environment.

Vacancy: 1 Temporary

Salary Range: $19.84/hour

What We Offer

  • Award winning culture
  • Flexible hybrid working conditions (1 day in the office per week)
  • Growth opportunities

Responsibilities

  • Maintain the Commercial mailbox to ensure accurate updating of the new submission and outstanding documents logs
  • Operate system data input for commercial package & automobile transactions
  • Complete Confirmations of Insurance
  • Review and clear IBC Errors
  • Order inspections and maintain system log
  • Review inspection requests for accuracy and completion
  • Attend occasional inspection with Senior Risk Control Consultant
  • Operate department phone queue

Qualifications

  • One year of post-secondary completed in the summer of 2026 and students must be returning in fall of 2026.
  • Ability to handle large volume of transactions and to meet defined service levels
  • Self-motivated, detail oriented and innovative
  • Exceptional phone etiquette with strong communication skills (written & verbal)
  • Proficient in Microsoft Office products
  • Must be flexible with hours of work as required

L'Unique and Unica are equal opportunity employers, so we encourage all Women, persons with disabilities, Indigenous people as well as visible and ethnic minorities to apply. Follow us on LinkedIn !
