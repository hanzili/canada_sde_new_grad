About the Role

Assist month-end close, reconciliations, budgets, reporting

About Primaris REIT

Toronto-based real estate investment trust

Full Description

Head Office - Toronto, ON

JOB TYPE

Internship - 8 months

Position Summary

Primaris REIT is seeking an Accounting Intern to join our team in downtown Toronto for an 8-month internship, to aid and gain practical experience in the accurate accounting, and will report to the Manager, Accounting.

Vacancy Status: Internship

Role

  • Support monthly accounting activities including month-end close, GST & QST returns, bank reconciliations, etc.
  • Help to prepare operating and capital budgets/reforecast and perform variance analysis
  • Aid in the maintenance of actuals and budget CAM/Tax rates and recoverable operating costs spreadsheets
  • Support reconciliations of balance sheet accounts and investigate discrepancies
  • Assist with audit requests and updating internal controls
  • Assist with preparing presentation materials
  • Participation general administrative support in projects and other duties as assigned

What You’ll Gain

  • Exposure to a dynamic corporate environment and accounting processes in commercial real estate
  • Networking opportunities with professionals to build your industry knowledge
  • Mentorship from experienced team members
  • Possibility of future career opportunities within the company

Requirements

  • Currently enrolled in a Bachelor’s Degree in Accounting, Finance or related field
  • Proficiency in MS Office (Excel, Word, PowerPoint) and accounting software is an asset
  • Strong analytical skills and attention to detail
  • Pro-active and a self-starter with demonstrated organisational capabilities.
  • Excellent communication skills (verbal & written) to work in a team environment
  • Curiosity with an eagerness to learn and take on new challenges

Join our public and growing REIT, hone your skills and provide invaluable hands-on experience in the dynamic world of Commercial Real Estate. Contribute to meaningful projects, gain real-world expertise, and build a foundation for a successful career!

Compensation This is a 8 month paid internship starting May 2026 until end of December 2026. Internship hourly wage ranges are dependent on your year of study: 1st Year – 5th Year ranges between CAD $22.00 - $25.00

AI Use & Accommodation We appreciate all applications and will be reviewing them on a rolling basis over the next couple of months. To manage the large volume of applications, AI may be used in the recruitment process. If your qualifications align with the requirements of this role, you may be contacted for next steps.

Competencies

  • Communicate clearly, concisely, professionally and convey active listening and openness
  • Build positive relationships and demonstrate commitment, effort and a "take charge" attitude
  • Produce accurate work with attention to detail and consistently meet deadlines
  • Take ownership for actions, accept constructive criticism and make the necessary changes to behaviour
  • Overcome obstacles to get the job done, identify problems and recommend solutions
  • See change positively and adapt quickly to change in work requirements

Values

  • Respect: Value diverse perspectives, experiences and talents; treat others the way you would want to be treated
  • Empowerment: Use the means provided to carry out responsibilities to the best of your ability and to implement creative ideas
  • Integrity: Do the right thing, deliver on commitments and admit mistakes
  • Teamwork: Work together as one team toward common goals
  • Accountability: Hold yourself and others accountable for decisions and results
