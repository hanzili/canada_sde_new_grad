About Delta Resources Limited

Active mineral exploration company with field-focused program

Full Description

Company Description

Delta Resources Limited is a Canadian mineral exploration and project development company focused on advancing its Eureka gold discovery and the broader Delta-1 Project in Ontario. The Delta-1 Project is located approximately 50 kilometres west of Thunder Bay. Backed by a strong technical team and an active exploration program, Delta offers early-career geologists the opportunity to gain hands-on experience in a dynamic, field-oriented environment, contributing to all stages of the mineral exploration process.

Role Description

Delta Resources is seeking a full-time Junior Exploration Geologist and motivated geology students for the 2026 summer field season. This role is ideal for recent graduates or students looking to build practical skills in mineral exploration through direct involvement in field programs. Both roles will report directly to the Senior Geologist and Vice President of Exploration and support ongoing exploration programs. The job site is near Thunder Bay with lodging available in Thunder Bay (if required) at a company staff house.

Key Responsibilities

  • Conduct/assist with geological mapping, prospecting, trenching and sampling, and possible core logging
  • Collect and document rock samples and maintain accurate field notes
  • Data entry
  • Conduct/assist with QA/QC procedures for sampling and data collection
  • Help compile and interpret geological data and contribute to reports
  • Follow all safety protocols in the field

Qualifications

  • Must reside within a 100km radius of Thunder Bay, ON (Junior Geologist) or within Canada (Student Assistants)
  • Currently enrolled in or recently completed a B.Sc. in Geology, Earth Sciences, or Geological Engineering
  • Registered as a GIT or P.Geo. with the Professional Geoscientists of Ontario (PGO)
  • Physical capability for fieldwork, including hiking and working outside in variable weather conditions
  • 2 years of mineral exploration experience (Junior Geologist) and any previous field experience (field school, co-op, or internships) is an asset for Student Assistants
  • Interest in mineral exploration and orogenic gold systems
  • Basic understanding of prospecting, sampling, and mapping
  • Proficiency in Microsoft Office and familiarity with GIS software
  • Valid driver's license
  • Must live in Canada

Personal Attributes

  • Strong work ethic
  • Attention to detail
  • Willingness to learn and take initiative
  • Positive attitude and ability to work in a team
  • Physical capability for demanding field conditions

What Delta Offers

  • Hands-on field experience on an active exploration project
  • Mentorship from experienced geologists
  • Competitive entry-level compensation
  • Opportunity for future roles with the company
  • Work from home and in the field as required (based in Thunder Bay)
