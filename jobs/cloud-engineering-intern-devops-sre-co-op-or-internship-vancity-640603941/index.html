Cloud Engineering Intern - DevOps & SRE (Co-op or Internship Opportunity - 4 Months)

at Vancity

📍 Location Vancouver, British Columbia Hybrid
📋 Details Co-op · 4mo · $27.85/hr
📅 Posted Mar 10, 2026

About the Role

Assist cloud engineering tasks and automation

About Vancity

Canadian member-owned credit union focused on inclusion

Full Description

Our Story & Purpose:

Vancity is a member-owned credit union built on the principles of inclusion, social justice, and community impact. Since 1946, we’ve worked to challenge the status quo, break down barriers, and build a more equitable future. With bold commitments—including becoming net-zero by 2040 across all mortgages and loans—we are championing financial resilience, climate action, and belonging for our members and communities.

As the largest private sector Living Wage Employer in Canada and home to a team of 2,300 passionate employees, Vancity offers competitive rewards and benefits, diverse career pathways, and the opportunity to be part of something bigger.

The Opportunity:

We’re looking for two (2) interns to join our Cloud Engineering team for a 4-month opportunity. The Cloud Engineering team at Vancity builds and operates the cloud and platform foundations that support banking products and services. These roles will report to the Sr Manager SRE (or Sr Manager DevOps) and work closely with SRE, Observability, Product and ITSM teams. These roles will receive additional mentorship from senior Cloud and SRE engineers.

A typical day would involve:

  • Supporting backlog grooming and prioritization in Azure DevOps under guidance from senior engineers
  • Tracking project deliverables, dependencies & assist with status reporting
  • Participating in meetings with SRE, Observability, Product and ITSM teams
  • Gathering and documenting user feedback on tooling usability and operational workflows
  • Creating clear documentation and summaries to support decision-making
  • When required, developing code with PowerShell, Python or NodeJS

You are:

  • Results-Oriented - You have a demonstrated ability to engage stakeholders to elicit requirements gathering, analyze findings, generate appropriate solutions, and build deliverables all while securing team's commitment to these solutions.
  • Technology Savvy - You have education and/or experience supporting technology development/ implementation efforts.
  • Critical Thinker - You seek to facilitate data-driven decision making, including "rolling up your sleeves" to elicit requirements, research, analyze data
  • Curious & innovative - You always do your research and analyze it to assess and recommend a course of action considering industry best practices
  • A team player - you can engage with members of your team as well as cross-functional teams and are willing to collaborate across projects and initiatives.

You have:

  • Current enrollment in an undergraduate program within an applicable field and eligible for co-op/internship opportunities
  • An interest in Cloud or Infrastructure engineering
  • Experience with process improvement and workflow optimization
  • Exposure to data analysis, reporting and stakeholder feedback synthesis
  • Knowledge of DevOps, SRE or Observability practices
  • A willingness to work in a highly flexible environment with multiple competing priorities.
  • A high level of attention to detail

Bonus points, if you have:

  • Familiarity with Azure DevOps, Jira or similar work management tools
  • Exposure to cloud platforms (Azure preferred, GCP or AWS is nice to have)
  • Basic understanding of SRE, monitoring or IT operations concepts
  • Documentation and presentation skills
  • Coding Experience

What You’ll Earn:

This role offers a pay range of $27.85/hr.
