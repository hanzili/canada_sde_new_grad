Junior Project Coordinator (Summer Intern)

at FlatironDragados

📍 Location Montréal, QC H3E 1N3
📋 Details Internship · 4 months · Paid · Summer
📅 Posted Apr 10, 2026

About the Role

Support project docs, data, and cost tracking

About FlatironDragados

Construction firm focusing on civil engineering projects

Full Description

Overview:

We are looking to hire a summer intern to support our team in Montreal for 4 months.

What you will be doing:

  • Record and track the internal and external distribution of project documentation
  • Ensure compliance with the standards of construction designs, drawings and diagrams
  • Maintain spreadsheets and documents in appropriate folders in the system
  • Manage data collection in all required fields for the applicable project documentation
  • Prepare work breakdown structures and quantity takeoffs to assist with estimates
  • Assist in completing performance monitoring for constructability review
  • Prepare the reports, sketches (related with the scope of work) needed to fulfill the requirements of the contract
  • Support daily and weekly cost tracking and analysis
  • Assist the project manager and construction team reporting when help is needed
  • Perform all other related tasks that will help complete the project

What we are looking for:

  • Enrolled in a Bachelor's degree program in Civil Engineering or relevant practices
  • Enrolled in an official co-op program
  • Experience in construction and civil engineering is considered an asset
  • Proficient in Microsoft Office (MS Excel, MS Word, MS Project)
  • Excellent verbal and written communication skills are required
  • Ability to take initiative
  • Ability to pay attention to details
  • Ability to work in a team environment

Why work for us:

Some of the benefits you may be eligible for as an employee are:

  • Comprehensive compensation package and paid time off program
  • Industry leading 401(k)/RRSP
  • Medical/Extended Health Care, Dental, Vision and/or Provincial Medical
  • Wellness benefits & Employee Assistance Program
  • Tuition Reimbursement Program

We are an EEO/ADA/Veterans employer.
