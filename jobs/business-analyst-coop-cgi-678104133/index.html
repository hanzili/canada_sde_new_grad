About the Role

Liaise between IT and business; document requirements clearly

About CGI

Global IT consulting leader; strong learning culture

Full Description

Ready to join an outstanding culture that cares about diversity and inclusion, corporate social responsibility and gives the freedom to innovate, influence decisions, and chart an exciting career? CGI is more than just an IT company; be part of a team that supports the local community with the ability draw on global best-in-class talent. Benefits include a share purchase program, profit sharing, wellness credits, training and development programs, and flexible work schedules and locations. This is a superb opportunity to become an employee of CGI, based in Regina, to provide leading business and information technology consulting services to guide our client through their major business changes. If you are an experienced business consultant (or equivalent professional) with an ambitious spirit and a passion for creating outstanding business results for clients, then CGI may be the place for you. We are looking for confident Coop Business Analyst who has an interest in understanding of how organizations function, perform and change, with an eagerness to grow their skills in gathering and analyzing data, analyzing and solving complex business problems, writing and presenting, facilitating and coaching. Grounded in business, you have a willingness to learn technology – able to assess a client's current technical environment and develop technology requirements and conceptual solutions to meet business objectives. You are looking to gain confidence working with all levels of client organizations – from the executive suite to the front line.

Your future duties and responsibilities

Liaison between information technology teams and business teams
Identify, validate, and prioritize clients’ business needs and requirements
Participate in workshops/JAD sessions to identify business needs and requirements
Document current business processes, business flows, rules and artifacts, and propose business processes, business flows, rules and artifacts
Produce detailed requirements documents and requirements traceability matrices
Review and analyze existing application effectiveness and efficiency
Develop strategies for improving and demonstrating systems
Ensure new software integration meets functional requirements, systems compliance and interface specifications
Create and implement through test strategies and plans

Required qualifications to be successful in this role

Strong written and verbal English communication skills, demonstrated through the ability to develop clear, structured documentation, present findings and recommendations across business and technical audiences
Experience documenting business rules, data models, business cases and business case summaries
Experience business process modeling
Post-secondary education in relevant discipline

Attributes
Communicates ideas in both technical and user-friendly language
Excellent listener with good interpersonal skills
Self-motivated and directed
Quick learner
Problem solver
Team player

Nice to Have:
Relevant certifications or training such as IIBA or PMP
Working knowledge of ITIL-aligned service management practices, gained through hands-on experience and/or formal training
JAD exposure

Together, as owners, let’s turn meaningful insights into action.

Life at CGI is rooted in ownership, teamwork, respect and belonging. Here, you’ll reach your full potential because…

You are invited to be an owner from day 1 as we work together to bring our Dream to life. That’s why we call ourselves CGI Partners rather than employees. We benefit from our collective success and actively shape our company’s strategy and direction.

Your work creates value. You’ll develop innovative solutions and build relationships with teammates and clients while accessing global capabilities to scale your ideas, embrace new opportunities, and benefit from expansive industry and technology expertise.

You’ll shape your career by joining a company built to grow and last. You’ll be supported by leaders who care about your health and well-being and provide you with opportunities to deepen your skills and broaden your horizons.

At CGI, we value the strength that diversity brings and are committed to fostering a workplace where everyone belongs.
