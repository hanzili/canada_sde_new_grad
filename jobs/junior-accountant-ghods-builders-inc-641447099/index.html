Junior Accountant

at Ghods Builders Inc.

📋 Details Full-time · Permanent · $35,000-$40,000 CAD · Permanent

About the Role

Review journal entries, support financial statements, reporting

About Ghods Builders Inc.

Private Canadian construction company with growing finance team

Full Description

  • Review of journal entries for all activity and transactions to ensure in accordance with accounting policies and internal control requirements

  • Assist with the preparation of financial and operational information along with analysis and commentary to support decisions for senior management

  • Review monthly reports, variance analysis, year-end files, financial statements and budgets to ensure accuracy and completion in accordance with established timelines

  • Review financial reports for accuracy and completeness in accordance with policies and timelines

  • Monitoring of intercompany balances and working with head office teams to resolve any issues

  • Assist with ensuring that key controls over financial reporting are operating effectively for areas of responsibility

Requirements

  • Minimum 1 years experience in accounting

  • Strong interpersonal and communication skills

  • Strong analytical, organizational and administrative skills

  • Exceptional attention to detail and ability to produce high quality reports

  • Ability to work with minimal supervision and to interact at all organizational levels

  • Strong time management skills and ability to manage multiple tasks at one time.

Job Types: Full-time, Permanent
