About the Role

Prepare journal entries; close month-end; support reports

About CaraCo Group of Companies

Kingston-based property management firm with growth

Full Description

We are growing again!

CaraCo has an exciting opportunity in our Finance Team for a Junior Accountant.

The Junior Accountant supports the Finance team by performing day-to-day accounting tasks, maintaining accurate financial records, and assisting with month-end and year-end processes to name a few!

This role is ideal for an early-career accounting professional who is detail-oriented, eager to learn, and committed to maintaining high standards of accuracy, ethics, and compliance.

Who you are

You’re a detail‑loving number enthusiast who enjoys bringing order to complexity. As a Junior Accountant, you’re excited to build a strong foundation in accounting, learn from experienced finance professionals, and contribute to accurate, timely financial reporting. You take pride in your work, ask thoughtful questions, and bring a positive, can‑do attitude to everything you do. You’re motivated by growth, value teamwork, and are eager to see how your work directly supports the success of the business.

Who are we

CaraCo is a proudly Canadian, Kingston‑based Property Management, and Development company with a strong reputation for quality, integrity, and long‑term community impact. We believe that great places start with great people. At CaraCo, collaboration, respect, and continuous improvement are core to how we work. For this role, you’ll be joining a supportive Finance team that values learning, mentorship, and doing things the right way—while contributing to projects that shape the communities where we live and work.

Duties & Responsibilities

· Prepare journal entries and maintain general ledger for a small portfolio of companies or in support of larger entities

· Participate in month end close processes, ensuring completeness and accuracy

· Review and prepare month end journal entries, identifying trends and variances

· Process intercompany transactions as required

· Reconcile balance sheet accounts and investigate discrepancies

· Identify, analyze, and resolve accounting irregularities

· Ensure all financial data is accurate and properly recorded

· Assist with the preparation of financial statements

· Prepare variance analysis reports and provide insights as requested

· Identify issues, analyze root causes, and recommend appropriate actions

· Assist with year end audit and engagement requirements

· Coordinate with the accounting team to ensure deadlines are met

· Support accounts payable and receivable functions as needed

· Coordinate and work with accounting team to ensure deadlines are met

· Support accounts payable/receivable team from time to time

· Perform additional duties as assigned

Skill Set, Education and Experience

· High level of accuracy and attention to detail, completing data sets and information

· Strong accounting and numerical skills

· Ability to prioritize, has good judgment and critical thinking skills

· Timeliness of work and adherence to deadlines

· Excellent written and verbal communication skills

· Strong interpersonal skills and ability to work collaboratively

· Financial reporting prepared in accordance with generally accepted accounting principles

· College Diploma in accounting or finance

· Min two years combined experience in accounting or finance

· Progress towards a CPA designation is preferred

· Strong proficiency in Microsoft Office Suite, bonus points for Excel

Core Competencies

  • High degree of professionalism, integrity, and confidentiality
  • Strong analytical and problem-solving skills
  • Willingness to learn and accept coaching
  • Ability to work both independently and collaboratively

Working Conditions

  • Office-based or hybrid work environment (based on organizational policy)
  • Standard business hours, with flexibility during month-end or year-end close periods

Please note that CaraCo is an inclusive employer and should you need accommodation throughout this process at any time please let us know. CaraCo supports a violence and harassment free workplace.
