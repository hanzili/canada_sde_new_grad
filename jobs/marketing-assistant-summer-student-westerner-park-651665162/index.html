About the Role

Support social media, content, research, events

About Westerner Park

Regional event venue with community marketing focus

Full Description

Ignite a meaningful career at Westerner Park — where opportunity, growth, and purpose converge.

Westerner Park is seeking a creative and motivated Marketing Assistant (Canada Summer Jobs) to join our Marketing & Communications team. This temporary, full-time role provides hands-on support for a variety of marketing activities, including social media management, content creation, market research, and event support.

As part of the Marketing team, the Marketing Assistant helps ensure the Westerner Park brand is represented consistently and effectively while supporting day-to-day marketing operations and event initiatives.

Position Overview

Reporting to the Assistant Marketing Manager, the Marketing Assistant supports marketing and communications initiatives across multiple platforms and departments. This role requires creativity, organizational skills, and adaptability in a fast-paced, major events environment.

Key Areas of Responsibility

1. Social Media & Digital Marketing

  • Assist with management of social media platforms (Facebook, Instagram, Twitter) for multiple brand accounts.
  • Create, schedule, and post engaging content, tracking results and reporting metrics.
  • Update website content regularly to reflect current events, promotions, and organizational news.

2. Market Research & Event Support

  • Conduct market research to support campaigns and initiatives.
  • Support events, including the Westerner Days Fair & Exhibition, with marketing-related activities.
  • Ensure consistent execution of brand standards across all channels and collateral.

3. Communication & Collaboration

  • Communicate directly with clients to build trusting relationships.
  • Liaise with other business units to identify and support internal marketing needs.
  • Assist the Marketing team in coordinating projects and maintaining workflow.

4. Administrative Support & Department Operations

  • Undertake daily administrative tasks to ensure smooth department operations.
  • Assist with organization, scheduling, and tracking of marketing projects.
  • Perform additional duties as assigned.

Key Deliverables

  • Engaging social media posts and updated website content.
  • Market research summaries and insights to support campaigns.
  • Brand-consistent marketing materials across all platforms.
  • Efficient support of events and internal marketing projects.

What You’ll Bring

  • Currently attending a business-related post-secondary program, preferably with a Marketing focus.
  • Previous marketing experience or familiarity with social media management (an asset).
  • Proficiency with Microsoft Office 365.
  • Strong organizational, communication, and teamwork skills.
  • Positive, professional, and courteous attitude.
  • Ability to adapt to changing priorities and work flexible hours, including evenings and weekends.
  • Eligibility for the Canada Summer Jobs Program.

Why Join Westerner Park?

At Westerner Park, you’ll be part of a team driven by purpose and community. We offer:

  • Competitive compensation of $18.00/hr.
  • Hands-on experience in marketing, digital media, and event support.
  • Exposure to a fast-paced, dynamic major events environment.
  • A supportive and inclusive culture that encourages learning, creativity, and collaboration.

Explore working at Westerner Park, where your contributions help showcase our brand and create memorable experiences for the community. Realize your potential and apply now to start your extraordinary story with us.
