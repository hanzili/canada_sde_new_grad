Job Description

Summer COOP (40 hours/week) position for 16 weeks from May 4, 2026 - September 4, 2026

Must be currently enrolled full time in a university degree program pursing a bachelor. Must not have graduated before September 4, 2026.

Local candidates only

Hybrid role

To further strengthen the team at our Ottawa location, we are looking for a ASIC Digital Design and Verification Engineer COOP who will work on the development of state of the art mixed-signal PLL (phase-locked loop) integrated circuits. During the COOP term in the Digital Verification team, you will learn about our verification environment, test bench, and test-case writing. You will be assigned a number of defined tasks, which will allow you to contribute to our verification team and grow your knowledge and gain experience in ASIC verification at the same time.

Qualifications

  • Must be currently enrolled full time in a university degree program pursing a bachelor degree. Must not have graduated before September 4, 2026
  • Good academic standing
  • Object oriented coding skills
  • Strong problem solving skills
  • Fast learner with ability to work under minimal supervision
  • Strong organization and communication skills

Preferred Skills & Knowledge

  • Understanding Standard Specifications/functional specifications/feature enhancements for the product to create and execute detailed verification plans
  • Knowledge of Verilog, SystemVerilog and UVM is an asset
  • Experience with JAVA programming is an asset
  • Knowledge of scripting languages such as Python and Tcl is an asset

Additional Information

This posting is for a new vacancy. The expected hourly pay range for this position is $25/hr - $33/hr. This position is also eligible for bonus opportunities. Please note that the final offer amount, including any applicable bonuses, will be dependent on geographic location, relevant experience, and skillset of the candidate.
