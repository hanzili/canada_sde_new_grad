Responsibilities

The Project Coordinator internship is to support the Go Expansion Senior Management at Turnkey level on the activities related to Project Management Office, as:

  • Reporting internal and external KPIs.
  • Coordinating transversal internal and external reports.
  • Overarching needs for production on plans and procedures.
  • Coordinating meetings and diareis and project governance issues across subsytems, partners and customer.
  • Develops and maintains details project schedule of regular reportings, following Alstom rules for internal reports and customer requirements for external reports.
  • Support Senior Management in managing key information.
  • Prepare and/or edit meeting minutes and presentations.
  • Apply analytical skills to compile summary documents, e.g Monthly Progress Report, Management Plans, Project Delivery Reviews. Collect and include contributions of the Team.

Organisational Reporting

Report to the Alstom E&M PMO Manager

Network and Links

The role will support the Systems S&TC, Electrification, Systems, Vehicle Suppliers and transversal support functions.
