About the Role

About ProServeIT

IT solutions provider with 20+ years experience

Full Description

ProServeIT is a leading IT solutions provider that specializes in delivering innovative technology solutions to help businesses thrive. With over 20 years of experience, we've established ourselves as a trusted partner in the ever-evolving world of technology.

We’re seeking a Digital Marketing Intern (SEO and AI Search Optimization) for the summer season. This opportunity is perfect for a student or recent graduate who understands the foundations of SEO and is eager to learn more through hands‑on experience, mentorship, and real‑world projects. Don’t worry — if you’re motivated and curious, we’ll teach you a lot along the way. SEO is evolving quickly, especially in the age of AI and answer‑based search. At ProServeIT, we want to stay ahead of these changes. That’s why we’re looking for a highly motivated intern who is passionate about SEO in the AI era, enjoys writing, and loves turning research into compelling stories that help people find the right answers. Beyond your core projects, you’ll gain exposure to a variety of marketing initiatives, giving you hands-on experience in a dynamic, multidisciplinary environment. By the end of the internship, you’ll have a strong understanding of how different marketing functions work together to drive real business impact.

Life at ProServeIT is fast paced, performance-driven, rewarding, and fun! We value and support our team members' career growth and ongoing professional development; and we recognize their achievements and outstanding results on a regular basis. We work hard and play hard. "People Matter. Be like gumby. Do it right." These are the three values we follow every day. These truly represent who we are and what we care about. Excited? Read on and apply! Looking forward to hearing from you.

What you will do

Your main duties will include:

  • Content marketing (blogs, landing pages, website copy, and thought leadership content)
  • SEO and on-page optimization activities (content updates, metadata, internal linking, audits)
  • Website audits and performance reviews
  • Researching and helping improve our visibility across search engines and AI-driven platforms
  • Supporting backlink-building activities (such as submitting content to relevant directories and partner platforms)
  • Outbound marketing support
  • Market and keyword research and competitor analysis
  • Website and social media management
  • Other administrative duties as assigned

We would love to hear from you if you have

  • A degree (or are enrolled in) Marketing, Digital Media, Design, or other related programs
  • Experience using HubSpot
  • Experience with website content management systems
  • Basic understanding of SEO principles (on-page and off-page)
  • Awareness of AI tools and their role in digital content creation
  • Graphic design and video editing skills (experience with Adobe Photoshop, Illustrator, and Premiere)
  • Strong content writing and research skills
  • Strong organizational, communication, and interpersonal skills
  • Strong attention to detail
  • Excellent MS Office skills
  • A team spirit

What you will get

As an intern at ProServeIT, you will enjoy:

  • Remote/Hybrid work
  • Career development and progression
  • Opportunities to work with talented and diverse teams, whilst shaping the future of work
