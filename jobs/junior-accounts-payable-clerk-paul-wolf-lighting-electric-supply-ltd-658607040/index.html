Full Description

Job Overview
We are seeking a detail-oriented Junior Accounts Payable Clerk to join our finance team. This role provides essential support in managing accounts payable processes, ensuring accurate data entry, account reconciliation, and timely processing of invoices. The ideal candidate will have a foundational understanding of accounts payable.

Responsibilities

  • Filing of packing slips
  • Matching invoices and packing slips
  • Entering of invoices into the system
  • Problem solving discrepancies
  • Assist in month-end closing activities related to accounts payable.

Requirements

  • Basic experience with AS400 Capp software
  • Familiarity with accounts accounts payable.
  • Strong data entry skills with high accuracy and attention to detail
  • Good organizational skills and the ability to prioritize tasks in a fast-paced environment
  • Team player
  • Must be able to read write and speak English fluently.
  • Must have strong communication skills as well as strong problem solving skills.

Job Type: Full-time

Pay: $42,000.00-$48,000.00 per year

Benefits:

  • Dental care
  • Extended health care
  • RRSP match
  • Vision care

Experience:

  • Accounts payable: 1 year (required)

Work Location: In person
