Full Description

Content Creator & Social Media Intern — Co-op / Internship

On-site Etobicoke, ON · Reports to: Founder & Business Development Lead

ABOUT JITTO

The wholesale produce sector is stuck in the past, costing grocers and restaurants billions due to inefficiency, waste, and hidden markups. At Jitto, we're rethinking the entire system from the ground up. From sourcing to delivery, we're using technology to modernize every step of the supply chain.

Every year, over $4 billion worth of fresh produce moves across Ontario and it all happens overnight. By sunrise, it's on shelves, in kitchens, and on plates. Most people never think about how that happens.

We've already moved over $15 million in fresh produce across Ontario, helping small communities and independent operators win in a market that's stacked against them. Food prices are rising, supply chains are getting tighter, and people deserve to understand why — and what better looks like.

If you want to change the way food moves and tell the story of how it happens, keep reading.

ROLE OVERVIEW

This is the storytelling seat at Jitto. You'll help us flip the funnel — getting customers, suppliers, and the broader food community to come to us — by telling the story of how food really moves across Ontario, why prices are doing what they're doing, and how we're reaching the underserved small communities the rest of the industry overlooks.

This is a content-first role. You'll live on Instagram, TikTok, YouTube, LinkedIn, our blog, and newsletter. You'll shoot, edit, write, and post. You'll know what's trending and know how to adapt it for Jitto. SEO, email, and paid ads support the work — but social and content are the engine.

This is not a remote position; our office is in Etobicoke (39 Advance Road).

WHAT YOU'LL DO

* Tell Jitto's story across Instagram, TikTok, YouTube, LinkedIn, our blog, and newsletter

* Capture content at the terminal, on the trucks, with customers, and with suppliers — the parts of the food system most people never see

* Stay on top of trending formats, hooks, sounds, and video styles — and translate them into Jitto's voice

* Educate Canadians on why food prices are doing what they're doing and spotlight the small communities winning with Jitto

* Run our content calendar end-to-end — plan, shoot, edit, write, post

* Support the funnel with email marketing, SEO, and paid ad campaigns — method-based: hypothesis, test, measure, iterate

* Track analytics across every channel and refine the playbook week over week

* Build a content playbook that outlives your term

WHO YOU ARE

* Currently enrolled in a Canadian college or university (required)

* Canadian citizen or Permanent Resident (required)

* A genuine storyteller — you see narrative everywhere and you can't help but capture it

* Visual instincts — you know what stops the scroll and why a piece of content is working

* Up to date on trending formats, video styles, hooks, and sounds

* Strong writer with a clear, human voice

* Comfortable with creative work and data — you don't post and pray, you measure and adjust

* Curious about food, supply chains, and why things cost what they cost

STRONG ASSETS

* Hands-on experience with content tools (Canva, CapCut, Adobe Creative Suite, or similar)

* Comfort with phone-shot photo and video that still looks good

* Familiarity with social analytics across major platforms

* Exposure to email marketing, SEO, or paid ads (Meta, TikTok, Google)

* Past experience growing a brand, personal account, or community account on social

* Interest in food, agriculture, supply chains, or small business

WHY JITTO

Food is one of the hottest topics in Canada right now. Prices are rising, supply chains are stressed, and most people don't understand why. At Jitto, you'll get unfiltered access to all of it: the terminal at 3 AM, the chefs, the grocers, the farmers, and the underserved small communities we're built to serve. You'll be the one telling that story to Canadians.

You'll work directly with the founder and the Business Development Lead, build the content engine from the ground up, and leave with a portfolio that's hard to fake — real reach, real growth, real impact on a real business.
