Full Description

Junior Social Media Coordinator & Content Creator

Company: RAW Sports

Location: Calgary, AB (In-Office & On-Site)

Pay: $25.00 – $30.00 per hour

Position Type: Subcontractor (15–40 hours per week)

Job Summary

RAW Sports is seeking a creative and detail-oriented Social Media Coordinator to join our team. This role is a "hands-on" hybrid of digital marketing and boots-on-the-ground content creation. You will be responsible for developing and executing engaging digital content across various platforms to enhance brand awareness and drive engagement within the online sports community.

The ideal candidate is someone who can capture high-intensity action at our camps and games, then pivot to the office to edit, analyze, and manage our digital presence.

Responsibilities

  • Content Capture: Attend RAW Sports camps, games, and practices in Calgary to provide full photography and videography coverage.
  • Creative Production: Create visually appealing graphics and high-end videos using Adobe Creative Suite (Photoshop, Illustrator, Premiere Pro) to support marketing initiatives.
  • Social Management: Develop, curate, and schedule compelling content for Instagram, TikTok, Facebook, and/or LinkedIn.
  • Campaign Strategy: Manage social media advertising campaigns and implement SEO strategies/keyword research to optimize content visibility.
  • Analytics: Monitor and analyze social metrics using Google Analytics and platform-specific tools to assess performance and inform future content.
  • Brand Consistency: Proofread all content for accuracy and clarity, ensuring the RAW Sports voice remains consistent across all platforms.
  • Innovation: Stay updated on digital marketing trends and platform updates to ensure our content stays ahead of the curve.

Equipment & Technical Requirements

  • Photography: We provide a DSLR camera for all photography needs.
  • Videography: You must provide your own video-capable camera and gear (stabilizers, etc.). We prefer candidates who have their own established equipment kit.
  • Software: Proficiency in Adobe Creative Suite (Premiere Pro, Photoshop, Illustrator) is required.

Qualifications

  • Proven experience in social media management or digital marketing.
  • Strong understanding of sports flow—knowing how to anticipate the "big moment" on the court or field.
  • Ability to manage multiple projects simultaneously while meeting tight deadlines.
  • Strong communication skills and a creative mindset.
  • Reliable transportation to travel to various local gyms and fields across Calgary.

The Details

  • Schedule: Hours vary based on our event calendar, ranging from 15 to 40 hours per week. This includes flexibility for evening and weekend work.
  • Growth: This is a junior role, perfect for a motivated individual eager to leverage their technical skills in a fast-paced, collaborative sports environment.

How to Apply: Please submit your resume along with a link to your portfolio or demo reel. We are looking for examples of both your shooting (photo/video) and your graphic design/editing work.
