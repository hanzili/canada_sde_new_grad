Full Description

Join us in pioneering breakthroughs in healthcare. For everyone. Everywhere. Sustainably.

Our inspiring and caring environment forms a global community that celebrates diversity and individuality. We encourage you to step beyond your comfort zone, offering resources and flexibility to foster your professional and personal growth, all while valuing your unique contributions.

Acquires further qualification for a Bachelor's degree, and gathers respective working experience in different administrative fields of expertise, in the course of a respective assignment.

We are looking for a detail-oriented and motivated Finance Intern to join the Canadian Business Performance Controlling (BPC) team. This internship offers hands-on exposure to core financial processes, including cost centre controlling, allocation cycles, SG&A monitoring, and commission payout validation.

You will support both operational and analytical financial routines, working closely with the BPC Canada team and interacting with cross-functional stakeholders across Canada and North America. This is an excellent opportunity to gain practical experience in financial planning, reporting, and performance management within a dynamic corporate environment.

Key Responsibilities

  • Cost Centre Controlling Support: Assist in maintaining accuracy and transparency of cost centre data. Support spending analysis, prepare reports, and help coordinate periodic reviews with cost centre owners.
  • Allocation Cycles Assistance: Support the maintenance and execution of cost allocation cycles (e.g., IT, Real Estate, Global Functions), ensuring data accuracy and documentation.
  • SG&A Monitoring: Assist in tracking and analyzing SG&A expenses, helping improve visibility and cost accountability across the organization.
  • Commission Payout Validation: Support the review and validation of commission calculations and related data, ensuring alignment with policies and timelines.
  • Collaborate with the CAN and NAM BPC teams to support monthly closing, forecasting, budgeting, and reporting activities.
  • Prepare ad-hoc analyses, dashboards, and presentations to support decision-making and continuous improvement initiatives.

Qualifications & Skills

  • Currently pursuing a Bachelor’s degree in Finance, Accounting, Economics, Business Administration, or a related field.
  • Strong analytical mindset with attention to detail.
  • Proficiency in Microsoft Excel; familiarity with SAP or business intelligence tools (e.g., Power BI, Power Query) is considered an asset.
  • Strong willingness to learn financial processes, systems, and digital tools.
  • Good organizational skills and ability to manage multiple tasks.
  • Effective communication and teamwork skills.
  • Proactive attitude with curiosity and eagerness to grow within a corporate finance environment.

Why Join Us?

You’ll be part of a collaborative and high-performing Finance team, gaining meaningful exposure to real business challenges and core financial processes. This internship provides a strong foundation in financial controlling and business performance management, offering valuable experience for your future career in Finance.
