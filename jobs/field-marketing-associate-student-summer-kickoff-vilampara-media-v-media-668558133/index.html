Full Description

About the Role

JPS Property Solutions is North York’s premier estate management firm. We specialize in high-end property restoration and precision lawn care for elite residential communities.

We are looking for 4-6 high-energy students to join our "Ground Crew" for a high-intensity marketing blitz from April 26th to May 2nd. Your mission is to help us dominate the North York corridor by distributing professional door hangers to targeted high-value estates.

What You’ll Be Doing

  • Precision Distribution: Placing premium door hangers on residential properties in specific North York zones (Bedford Park, Armour Heights, etc.).
  • Route Tracking: Using a GPS-tracking app to log your routes and ensure 100% neighborhood coverage.
  • Brand Representation: Serving as the face of JPS. While you aren't "selling," you are representing a premium brand—professionalism and a clean appearance are mandatory.

What We’re Looking For

  • The "Hustle": You enjoy being active. This is a physical role—you’ll be walking 15–20km a day.
  • Reliability: You show up on time, every time.
  • Tech Savvy: You have a smartphone with a data plan to run our tracking software.
  • Professionalism: You are polite to neighbors and respect property boundaries.

Why Join the JPS Crew?

  • Competitive Pay: We pay above minimum wage because we expect above-average effort.
  • Performance Bonuses: Hit your daily targets early? There’s a "completion bonus" waiting for you.
  • Get Fit: Skip the gym—get paid to get your steps in while enjoying the spring weather.
  • Networking: Work directly with the business owner and learn the ropes of a high-growth local service company.

Schedule

  • Dates: April 26 – May 02 (7-day blitz)
  • Hours: 9:00 AM – 5:00 PM (Flexible shifts available)

How to Apply: Send us a quick note about why you’re the right fit for the JPS Ground Crew. Interviews are happening immediately via Zoom.
