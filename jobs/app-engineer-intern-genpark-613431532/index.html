**Company Description**

GenPark leverages intelligent AI-driven Brand and User Agents to help brands understand, engage, and convert global Gen Z consumers more effectively. By integrating personalized AI-generated content, interactive agents, and immersive AI video experiences, GenPark enables brands to connect with their target audience in meaningful ways, leading to higher conversion rates with reduced customer acquisition costs. Our AI-native system continuously adapts to user behavior and feedback, fostering smarter matching between products, services, and global consumers.

**Role Description**

As an App Engineer Intern at GenPark, you will work in a hybrid capacity, based in Toronto, ON, with the flexibility of occasional remote work. In this internship role, you will collaborate with the engineering team to design, develop, and deploy app features, troubleshoot and debug software issues, and contribute to enhancing the overall user experience. You will gain hands-on experience with cutting-edge tools and technologies and participate in brainstorming sessions to explore new functionalities and optimize existing ones, all while following best practices in software development and agile methodologies.
