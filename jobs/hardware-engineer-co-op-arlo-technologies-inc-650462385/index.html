Job Title: Hardware Engineer (Co-op)

Term/Duration: 8 Month Term, Full Time (40 hours per week)

Positions Available: 2

Location: Arlo Canada

200-10851 Shellbridge Way

Richmond, BC.

Canada. V6X 2W8

Job Description Arlo Canada division is looking for students with a strong interest and knowledge in wireless data communication hardware development. As a part of the Hardware team, you will gain hands-on experience working on prototype to mass production life cycle development, exposure to a wide variety of test equipment and an introduction to BLE/WiFi technologies. Specifically, you will be responsible for the design of new IoT products on the world’s largest wireless networks.

Responsibilities

  • Test and design baseband circuits involving switching power supplies, battery charging systems, power consumption test
  • Follow design verification test plans and document reports for wireless devices
  • Assist with assembly, rework and debug of various wireless products

Qualifications

  • Experience with wireless products (BLE/WiFi) is a strong asset
  • Strong written and oral communication skills
  • Ability to work in a fast paced, dynamic environment
  • Software development, programming and/or scripting experience is preferred

  • Cover Letter
  • Resume
  • Transcript
