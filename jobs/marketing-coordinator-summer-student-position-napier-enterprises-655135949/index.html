About the Role

Support campaigns, content, and social media execution

About Napier Enterprises

Canadian outdoor brand offering hands-on marketing exposure

Full Description

Why are you here?

Maybe you’re the one in your group chat who actually makes the plans happen.
The one who notices the details others miss.
The one who rewrites a caption three times because it just feels off.

Or maybe you’ve been waiting for a chance to take everything you’ve learned in school and finally use it somewhere that moves a little faster, expects a little more, and lets you create something real.

Here’s why we’re here.

We’re the world leader in vehicle camping tents. So, we sell tents that attach to your truck, SUV, CUV, or minivan.

For 35+ years, our mission has been simple: help people explore in comfort. Behind that mission is a small but mighty team doing a lot of work to bring it all to life.

We’re looking for a Marketing Coordinator who’s ready to jump in, support the team, and learn by doing.

What you’ll get to do

This isn’t a “sit back and watch” internship. You’ll be contributing every day, working closely with our Brand Strategist and helping execute the work that keeps everything moving.

Campaigns
Support the planning and execution of marketing campaigns across digital and traditional channels. Sit in on brainstorms, contribute ideas, and help bring them to life.

Content
Assist with UGC initiatives by researching creators and supporting outreach. Help write blog posts, product copy, and content that connects with customers.

Social Media
Support day-to-day execution. Think graphics, short videos, reels, and carousels. Stay on top of trends and help keep our channels engaging.

Events
Research opportunities, maintain our event database, and assist with outreach and coordination.

Partnerships
Help identify brands and creators we should be collaborating with, and support early-stage outreach for things like giveaways, co-branded content, and more.

Customer Experience
Be part of the front line. Answer calls, respond to email inquiries, and assist walk-in customers.

Insights
Review customer feedback and product reviews to spot trends and identify opportunities.

Administration
Jump in where needed. We’re a small team, so being adaptable and willing to help across functions is key.

What we need from you

  • You’re currently enrolled in a post-secondary Business or Marketing program
  • You’ve had some exposure to marketing or customer service (a plus, not required)
  • You’re organized and detail-oriented. You catch the small things others miss
  • You take initiative and don’t wait to be told what to do next
  • You communicate clearly and professionally, both written and verbal

What’s in it for you?

  • Real, hands-on marketing experience in the CPG industry
  • Exposure to everything from content and social to partnerships and campaigns
  • A behind-the-scenes look at how an established brand operates
  • The chance to build skills you’ll actually use in your career

The details
