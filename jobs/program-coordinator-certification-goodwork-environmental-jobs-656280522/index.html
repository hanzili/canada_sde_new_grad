About The Association Since 1985, the Federation of Rental-housing Providers of Ontario (FRPO) has been the leading advocate for Ontario’s multi-family rental housing industry. Representing over 2,200 members who own or manage more than 370,000 households across Ontario, FRPO plays a vital role in promoting quality housing, advocating for landlords, and supporting the development of rental properties. The organization provides education, industry standards, research, and public advocacy to encourage a balanced housing market. With a focus on professional standards and best practices, FRPO serves as a trusted resource for rental housing providers and industry partners alike.

About The CRB Program The Canadian Certified Rental Building™ (CRB) Program is Canada’s national quality-assurance and certification program for the multi-residential rental housing sector.

Established in 2008 and administered by the Federation of Rental-housing Providers of Ontario (FRPO), the program provides third-party verification that rental properties are professionally managed, well-run, well-maintained, and meet defined operational, environmental, and resident service standards. The program operates across Canada and is recognized by GRESB as an approved building certification partner.

About The Role This is a full-time role, requiring a minimum of four days per week in-office, based in Toronto, Canada. The Program Coordinator, Certification will oversee certification programs, drive initiatives, and ensure compliance with established standards. Key responsibilities include developing programs, monitoring and evaluating progress, maintaining strong communication with stakeholders, and providing excellent support to program participants. The role also involves collaborating with internal teams to ensure program success and contribute to ongoing improvements. This newly created position supports the operations, communications, and national expansion of the CRB Program. It is ideal for someone with strong coordination skills and a keen interest in sustainability, real estate, or building operations.

Candidates May Come From

  • a sustainability or real estate background with strong administrative, analytical, or coordination experience, or
  • a communications or administrative background with a strong interest in ESG, sustainability, or housing quality standards.

Training will be provided on rental regulations, building standards, GRESB alignment, and the CRB certification framework.

  • Draft clear, professional communications, updates, and reminders.
  • Support onboarding of new members and guide property teams through program requirements.

  • Assist with light marketing tasks such as drafting content, supporting social media, and preparing updates.

  • Support development of materials that highlight program benefits and expand national visibility.
  • Create simple visual materials using Canva or PowerPoint (asset).
  • Event & Webinar Coordination
  • Support CRB events, webinars, training sessions, and conference activities.
  • Coordinate logistics, registration, materials, scheduling, and follow-up communications.
  • Assist with slide deck preparation and content organization.
