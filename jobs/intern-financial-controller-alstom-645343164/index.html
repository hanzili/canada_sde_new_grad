Accounting / FP&A Internship

Intern Financial Controller

at Alstom

📍 Location Saint-Bruno, Quebec
📋 Details Intern · Duration TBA · Pay TBA · Term Internship
📅 Posted Mar 17, 2026

About the Role

Coordinate budgeting, monthly reporting, cost productivity tools

About Alstom

Global engineering finance partner, multinational infrastructure leader

Full Description

At Alstom, we understand transport networks and what moves people. From high-speed trains, metros, monorails, and trams, to turnkey systems, services, infrastructure, signalling and digital mobility, we offer our diverse customers the broadest portfolio in the industry. Every day, more than 80 000 colleagues lead the way to greener and smarter mobility worldwide, connecting cities as we reduce carbon and replace cars.

Could you be the intern Global Engineering Finance Partner in Saint Bruno we’re looking for?

Your future role Take on a new challenge and apply your financial expertise in a new cutting-edge field. You’ll work alongside collaborative and dynamic teammates.

You'll play a key role in driving financial efficiency and productivity within the global Engineering function. Day-to-day, you’ll work closely with teams across the business (including Engineering, Finance, and Regional teams), coordinate budgeting and reporting activities, and ensure alignment with the company’s financial objectives.

You’ll specifically take care of maintaining cost models and tools for cost productivity, but also supporting the roll-out of Engineering Finance tools globally.

We’ll look to you for:

  • Coordinating budgeting and monthly reporting activities for the Engineering Function and maintaining associated scorecards
  • Ensuring the cost of the global Engineering function aligns with company objectives
  • Acting as a domain expert on subcontracting
  • Driving cost productivity using cost models and related tools
  • Partnering with the Alstom Global Engineering Leader and their staff as a trusted finance partner
  • Contributing to Alstom’s Delivery Plan (twice a year) for Engineering
  • Improving self-servicing capabilities and online access to finance data for the Engineering team
  • Ensuring successful roll-out of companywide Engineering Finance tools and adherence to related KPIs
  • Promoting the Alstom Code of Ethics and adhering to the highest standards of ethical conduct

All About You We value passion and attitude over experience. That’s why we don’t expect you to have every single skill. Instead, we’ve listed some that we think will help you succeed and grow in this role:

  • Degree in Finance, Accounting, or a related field
  • Experience or understanding of financial planning, budgeting, and reporting
  • Knowledge of cost productivity tools and cost models
  • Familiarity with global finance systems and processes
  • A professional certification in finance or accounting (e.g., CFA, CPA) is a plus
  • Strong analytical and problem-solving skills
  • Excellent communication and collaboration abilities

You don’t need to be a train enthusiast to thrive with us. We guarantee that when you step onto one of our trains with your friends or family, you’ll be proud. If you’re up for the challenge, we’d love to hear from you!

Important to note As a global business, we’re an equal-opportunity employer that celebrates diversity across the 63 countries we operate in. We’re committed to creating an inclusive workplace for everyone.
