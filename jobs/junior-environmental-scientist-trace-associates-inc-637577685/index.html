Full Description

The Opportunity

▪We are looking for junior environmental scientists who want to make a difference with a company that values its employees.

▪Opportunities exist across our hydrogeology & water, ecology, reclamation, restoration, and remediation & risk service areas.

Responsibilities

▪Work within your chosen practice area while being willing to broaden your skills and engage in work across multiple service areas (see above).

▪Assist field leads and site supervisors with field work.

▪Assist project managers with general office tasks including developing work plans, cost estimates, and data analysis/review.

About You and Your Qualifications

▪Graduated from a college or university with a diploma or degree in a science program with an environmental focus.

▪Can safely operate vehicles and have a valid class 5 driver’s licence.

▪Health, safety, and wellness are critical to you.

▪You are positive, humble, caring, genuine, and trustworthy.

▪You work hard and can balance your work and your life.

▪You take pride in your work.

Why Trace?

▪A culture of positivity, empowerment, empathy, trust, and teamwork.

▪You can “choose your own adventure” at Trace and we will help you get there.
