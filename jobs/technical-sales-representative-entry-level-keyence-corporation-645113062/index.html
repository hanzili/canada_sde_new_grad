About the Role

Provide technical sales support and consult with customers

About KEYENCE CORPORATION

Global automation leader with strong entry-level training

Full Description

KEYENCE CANADA Company Overview

  • Founded in Japan in 1974, KEYENCE Corporation has steadily grown to become a global leading force in industrial automation technologies with a global network of 250 offices in 46 countries, with annual sales of $7.06 Billion in USD, and 12,261 employees worldwide.

  • KEYENCE CANADA Inc. was established in 2004. We have 4 offices (Mississauga, Montreal, Windsor, and Burnaby). We are actively hiring to cultivate markets to grow our business.

  • At KEYENCE, we remain innovative providing the world's best automation technologies. Our true key to success is our highly trained direct sales force, committed to providing the most professional and consultative sales and support across a wide variety of industries.

The Opportunity for You

  • The Technical Sales Representative position with KEYENCE Canada is the premier opportunity for you to learn consultative, application-based sales. We have an established continuous training and mentoring program that will give you the knowledge and skills for success.
  • At KEYENCE Canada, our sales professionals work in a highly professional and fast-paced office . Being in office an average of 2 days week. Visiting manufacturers and various clients onsite within an assigned territory.

We offer face to face consultations to help solve our customers’ critical quality control, reliability, research and or development applications. KEYENCE products are highly technical; you will receive supportive training to become an expert on our products. Solving applications, following our pro-active sales approach, and conducting successful sales consultations.

Responsibilities and Duties

  • Provide technical consultation and service to customers to help solve their applications using KEYENCE’s products in various industries. Advising and promoting selection of the most appropriate products/services for their application.
  • Travel to customer locations 3 days per week and successfully sell products into a wide variety of manufacturing & related industries.
  • Provide technical consultation and service to customers to help solve their applications using KEYENCE’s products in a variety of industries. Selecting and promoting the proper company products/services for their application.

Requirements and Qualifications

  • Excellent presentation, oral and written communication skills
  • Bachelor’s degree (Engineering is an asset)
  • Ability to listen and proactively react to customer questions and requests.
  • Goal oriented and hard working with a desire to make measurable contributions to KEYENCE Canada’s success.
  • Desire to better yourself through our intensive training, mentorship, supportive and engaged management, & proactive, process-oriented sales approach.
  • Willingness to travel 60% of the time, including the possibility of overnight travel.
  • Must have own vehicle and a valid driver’s license.

Why Work for KEYENCE

  • Recognized by Forbes – World’s Top 100 as “Most Innovative” company 8 years in a row
  • KEYENCE hires into entry-level only, nurturing an environment within which to grow your sales expertise and career with us.
  • Competitive compensation
  • Health coverage for yourself and your family; Medical, Dental, Vision
  • Two weeks starting vacation
  • Paid shut-down period over winter holidays
  • Paid training period (3 months) & continuous development program
  • Fully paid travel expenses
  • Group RRSP plan after 6 months with KEYENCE
  • Great work-life balance – Hybrid way of working

Supplementary Information

Position requires the ability to lift and carry 30-90lbs of demonstration equipment in and out of vehicles, and through customer facilities with or without reasonable onsite accommodation(s). Incumbents are expected to demonstrate flexibility to manufacturing environments in terms of level of cleanliness, temperature, and/or noise levels. Incumbents must also abide by all safety regulations in offices and customer locations, including wearing safety equipment and /or gear whenever required.
