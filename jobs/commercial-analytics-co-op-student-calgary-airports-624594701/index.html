WHY CALGARY AIRPORTS

Powered by heart and dedicated to excellence, we’re the team that keeps Calgary connected – and you could be our newest member.

We’re in the business of uplifting people, and every day, we create runways to new places and possibilities, building a better airport experience for everyone.

What’s The Team Like The Commercial team at Calgary Airports works with airlines, retailers, restaurants, property partners, and business stakeholders to grow revenue and enhance passenger experience. Their work matters because commercial activity helps drive airport sustainability and supports the services guests rely on every day. The team collaborates with partners across the airport to support leasing, business development, retail strategy, and commercial performance. As a co‑op student, you’ll join a fast‑paced, highly engaged team that values creativity, curiosity, and data‑driven thinking.

What Are Some Of The Responsibilities

  • Assist with collecting, organizing, and analyzing commercial and operational data
  • Help prepare reports, dashboards, and presentations on commercial performance and guest trends
  • Support market research, benchmarking, and gather insights on retail, concessions, and commercial opportunities
  • Assist with lease administration tasks, including organizing documents, updating databases, and tracking agreements
  • Participate in site walks and tenant check‑ins to observe operations and gather information
  • Support commercial initiatives such as promotions, retail programs, and partner activities
  • Provide general administrative and project support to the Commercial team

What Do You Need To Bring

  • To qualify for a Co‑op or Internship position, you must be enrolled in a post‑secondary program in Business, Commerce, Economics, Marketing, Aviation Management, or a related field that requires a work term as a formal component of the curriculum, and you must provide documentation confirming that the work term is an academic requirement.
  • You’ll need to be eligible to obtain and maintain a RAIC, don’t worry – we’ll help you through this! For more details, check out: RAIC Program and Application Information
  • Coursework or experience involving business analysis, marketing, data, or customer experience
  • Ability to work with structured documentation, maintain organized records, and follow established processes

What Additional Skills Are An Asset

  • Bilingual in English and French
  • Experience working with Excel, spreadsheets, or basic data analysis for school or personal projects.
  • Exposure to market research, business cases, or financial analysis through coursework.
  • Comfort working with presentations, visuals, or digital tools to communicate ideas.
  • Interest in retail, commercial strategy, real estate, or aviation business.

What Does Diversity Mean To Us We are committed to building a team where exceptional leadership shines and our crew thrive both at work and at home. We believe that deliberate steps such as creating a workplace that is physically and psychologically safe, equitable to all, and actively and diligently taking steps along the path to reconciliation are the keys to making this a reality.

OUR VALUES

We prioritize genuine concern and compassion for everyone we engage with, from our team members to our partners and guests, always striving to help and support one another.

We actively seek diverse perspectives in our decision-making and collaboratively tackle challenges.

We work together with trust and transparency, supporting one another to achieve the collective goals of our organization.

We grow, evolve, and adapt with purpose, continually aligning our actions with our vision.

We embrace curiosity and boldly challenge the status quo, exploring new opportunities and taking thoughtful, responsible risks that drive purposeful innovation.
