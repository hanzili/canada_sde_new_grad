Full Description

Position: Junior Accounts Receivable Clerk
Company: The Aurum Group
Location: Alberta, Canada

About Us:
We are a dynamic, customer-focused organization committed to operational excellence and strong financial stewardship. We are seeking a motivated for a Junior Accounts Receivable Clerk to join our team and support day-to-day AR Activities.

Position Overview

The Junior Accounts Receivable Clerk will assist with invoicing, payments, account reconciliation, and general administrative support within the Finance department. This is an excellent opportunity for someone early in their accounting career who wants to learn and grow.

Key Responsibilities

· Assist in generating and sending customer invoices

· Apply payments and update customer accounts

· Support the monitoring of outstanding balances and follow‑up communication

· Help prepare AR aging and related reports

· Investigate and resolve minor billing discrepancies

· Provide administrative support and collaborate with the Finance team

· Assist with month‑end AR tasks as required

· Assist with month-end closing activities and audit requirements.

Qualifications

· 0–2 years of accounts receivable or general accounting experience (new grads welcome)

· Fluency in French and English is required

· Strong attention to detail and willingness to learn

· Good communication and customer service skills

· Proficiency with Microsoft Excel; experience with accounting software is an asset

· Ability to prioritize tasks and work in a team‑oriented environment

Company Culture

We foster a collaborative and inclusive environment where innovation thrives. Our team values curiosity, precision, and continuous learning. You’ll be surrounded by professionals who are passionate about technology and committed to delivering high-quality results.

Benefits

· Competitive salary and performance-based incentives

· Comprehensive health and dental coverage

· Paid vacation

· Pension Plan

· Professional development and training opportunities

· Access to cutting-edge 3D printing technology and tools
