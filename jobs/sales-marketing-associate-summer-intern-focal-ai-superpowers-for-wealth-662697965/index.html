About the Role

Source leads, engage prospects, coordinate events

About Focal | AI Superpowers for Wealth

AI wealth-advisory startup with rapid growth

Full Description

We're looking for a hungry, organized, and personable college student to join us as a Summer GTM Associate. You'll work directly with the founding team to drive top-of-funnel sales activity — sourcing leads at industry conferences, following up with prospects, and helping plan and execute events that put Focal in front of financial advisors. Note that this role will require extensive travel throughout May & June.

What you will be doing:

  • Conference Lead Generation: Attend financial services and wealth management conferences to identify, engage, and capture leads on behalf of Focal.
  • Prospect Follow-Up: Manage timely, personalized outreach to conference leads and inbound prospects via email and LinkedIn. Keep our CRM clean and up to date.
  • Event Coordination: Help plan and execute Focal-hosted events — webinars, happy hours, dinners, and demo sessions — including logistics, invitations, and day-of coordination.
  • Sales Support: Assist with research on target accounts, build prospect lists, draft outreach sequences, and prep materials for demos and meetings.
  • Market Intelligence: Gather on-the-ground insights from conferences and events — what advisors care about, what competitors are saying, and where opportunities exist.

Who You Are

  • Currently pursuing a bachelor's degree (rising junior or senior preferred)
  • Genuinely curious about startups, sales, fintech, or AI — bonus if you've followed the wealth management or financial advisory space
  • Strong communicator — comfortable striking up conversations with strangers at a conference booth and equally sharp in written follow-ups
  • Highly organized with strong attention to detail; you don't let leads slip through the cracks
  • Self-starter who can operate independently; you don't need to be told what to do next
  • Comfortable with ambiguity — we're an early-stage company and things move fast
  • Willing to travel to conferences and in-person events as needed

Pay & Timeline

  • $5k per month
  • Performance bonus (Up to 100% of base pay)
  • 3 month internship (With potential extension depending on performance)

About Focal AI

Focal AI is the number #1 AI Assistant for financial and wealth advisors in Canada. We already serve advisors at leading organizations like IPC, Designed Wealth Management and Assante. Focal's AI Assistant enables advisors to streamline their operations from providing note-taking and data entry automation workflows that help them save 15+ hours a week.
