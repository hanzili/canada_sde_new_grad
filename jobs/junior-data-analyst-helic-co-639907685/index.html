About Helic & Co.

Growing analytics team, data-driven decision making

Full Description

Overview

We are seeking a motivated Junior Data Analyst to support data collection, cleaning, and basic analysis for business insights. This entry-level role helps transform data into reports and visualizations for decision-making.

Key Responsibilities

  • Gather, clean, and organize data from databases and spreadsheets using SQL and Excel.
  • Perform basic statistical analysis and create dashboards with tools like Tableau or Power BI.
  • Generate routine reports and identify trends to support team recommendations.
  • Collaborate with stakeholders to understand data needs and validate results.
  • Assist in data quality assurance and documentation of processes.

Qualifications

  • Bachelor’s degree in Data Analytics, Statistics, Business, or related field.
  • Basic proficiency in SQL, Excel, and visualization tools; entry-level experience welcome.
  • Strong analytical thinking and attention to detail.
  • Good communication for presenting findings.

Preferred Attributes

  • Familiarity with Python/R or Google Analytics.
  • Eagerness to learn advanced tools and methods.

What You’ll Gain

  • Hands-on data skills in a real-world setting.
  • Growth toward mid-level analyst positions.
