Full Description

Associate Account Executive, Construction- 12 Month Contract Are you curious the inner workings of risk related to construction projects? Interested in working with a team of experts who are generous with sharing their knowledge, and joining an industry leading, highly inclusive organization? If this sounds like you, we should talk!

Note: This role is this a full-time hybrid role in our Vancouver office on a 12-month contract.

Applicants must be legally authorized to work in Canada. This role is not eligible for sponsorship, and we are unable to sponsor or take over sponsorship of an employment visa or work permit.

The salary range for this position is CAD $56,600-$75,400 The actual salary will vary based on applicant’s education, experience, skills and abilities, as well as internal equity.

Aon also offers you a generous incentive earning opportunity and a comprehensive benefits package.

Aon is in the business of better decisions At Aon, we shape decisions for the better to protect and enrich the lives of people around the world.

As an organization, we are united through trust as one inclusive, diverse team, and we are passionate about helping our colleagues and clients succeed.

What The Day Will Look Like Each day is unique in this role. The daily work may look like any combination of tasks such as:

  • Under the direction of the Account Executive, servicing existing clients' portfolios within the risk management group.
  • Working on a wide variety of clients, identify specific client risk exposures, as may be required.
  • Managing the client renewal process by collaborating with all members of the client service team to ensure coverage is secured and client deliverables are met.
  • Receiving and processing client requests and reviewing documentation for routine policy changes, additions, deletions, or cancellations.
  • Preparing client service summaries, program reports and managing client accounts receivables.
  • Assist with establishing account specific service plans and delivery approaches which support coverage solutions.
  • Respond to inquiries from client on policy coverages under the direction of the Account Executive as may be required.
  • Assist clients with interpretation of insurance clauses within contracts/agreements.
  • Explore with the client opportunities for expanded Aon products or services.
  • May be required to work with two or more Account Executives.
  • Assisting Account Executive in new business initiatives through tasks such as coordinating dissemination of marketing material, prompting regular contact with prospect, assisting in developing new business strategies and account penetration).
  • Other related duties and responsibilities as assigned.

How This Opportunity Is Different As an Associate Account Executive, new colleagues will have the opportunity to learn from industry leaders in Construction Risk . You will have access to and on-the-job training that will support your personal and professional development. We encourage and support all colleagues to obtain industry-recognized designations and certifications such as FCIP, CIP, CRM and CAIB.

Skills And Experience That Will Lead To Success Our ideal candidate brings 2+ years commercial risk experience, but it does not have to be construction-related. They have excellent communication and relationship-building skills with a broad range of audiences.

  • Meticulous attention to detail, refined organizational skills and the ability to multi-task;
  • Level I licence is required; Level II license preferred or working towards
  • Consistent track record of prioritizing tasks under ambitious timelines;
  • Ability to work collaboratively as a key member of a team and independently with minimum supervision;
  • Must have strong digital literacy and be proficient with the Microsoft Office Package.
  • Demonstrated ability to learn quickly, including both self-directed learning and in-role training.
