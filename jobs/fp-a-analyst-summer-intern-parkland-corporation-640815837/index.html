FP&A Analyst Summer Intern

at Parkland Corporation

📍 Location Calgary, Alberta
📋 Details Internship · 4 months · TBD · May 2026
📅 Posted Mar 11, 2026

About the Role

Assist FP&A, prepare monthly reporting and variance analysis

About Parkland Corporation

Canadian energy company with robust FP&A functions

Full Description

Description As of November 1, 2025, Sunoco LP has successfully completed its acquisition of Parkland Corporation . For more information, please visit www.sunocolp.com.

The Opportunity: The FP&A Analyst Intern will support the Finance team during a 4-month term, gaining hands-on experience in financial planning and analysis within a corporate environment. This role contributes to monthly management reporting, forecasting cycles, variance analysis, and KPI tracking. The intern will collaborate closely with Accounting and cross-functional stakeholders to ensure accurate financial reporting and actionable performance insights.

Responsibilities:

  • Assist in the preparation of monthly management reporting packages
  • Compile and analyze financial results to support business decision-making
  • Support variance analysis (actual vs. budget vs. forecast) and identify key drivers
  • Facilitate and support the monthly accruals process
  • Perform data extraction and analysis from ERP and reporting systems
  • Conduct data reconciliations to ensure accuracy and integrity of financial data
  • Assist with KPI tracking and dashboard updates
  • Collaborate with Accounting to align financial reporting and forecasting assumptions
  • Participate in ad hoc financial analysis and special projects
  • Support continuous improvement initiatives within reporting processes

Education:

  • Currently enrolled in a Canadian post-secondary institution pursuing a Bachelor’s degree in Accounting, Finance, or a related discipline. Students in 3rd or 4th year of study preferred.

Requirements:

  • Available for a 4-month term beginning in May 2026
  • Strong analytical and problem-solving skills with high attention to detail
  • Advanced proficiency in Microsoft Excel; familiarity with ERP systems is an asset
  • Strong verbal and written communication skills
  • Self-motivated and capable of working in a fast-paced, deadline-driven environment
  • Demonstrated ability to collaborate effectively within a team setting
