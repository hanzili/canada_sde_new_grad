Marketing / Digital Marketing Internship

Marketing Assistant, Student

at Skyline Group of Companies

📍 Location Guelph, Ontario
📋 Details Full-time contract · 4 months · $21/hour · Summer
📅 Posted Apr 20, 2026

About the Role

Assist marketing tasks, write blogs, update calendars

About Skyline Group of Companies

Real estate and investment firm with growth potential

Full Description

Summer is right around the corner and we’re looking for a motivated and detail-oriented Marketing Assistant, Student to join our Marketing team at our Head Office in Guelph.

Compensation: Starting at $21.00 per hour.

This will be a full-time, in person, 4 month contract.

Job Description This is a great opportunity for a student looking to gain hands-on marketing experience in a fast-paced and supportive environment. As a Marketing Assistant, Student you will provide general administrative assistance to Skyline’s Marketing team through content creation, blog writing and administrative coordination. You’ll contribute to meaningful projects while building practical skills that will support your future career in Marketing.

What You’ll Be Doing

  • Assisting the Marketing team with administrative tasks
  • Learn and implements basic SEO and readability best practices.
  • Researching and writing blog articles aligned with company themes
  • Helping to update and maintain content calendars
  • Contributing to a positive and collaborative team environment

What We Look For:

  • Currently enrolled in a post-secondary program in Marketing, Business or similar.
  • Strong attention to detail and good organizational skills
  • Comfortable working in a fast-paced environment
  • A proactive attitude—you take initiative and look for ways to help
  • Strong communication and teamwork skills
  • Someone who aligns with Skyline’s values and acts with PRIDE: Professionalism, Respect, Integrity, Drive, and Efficiency

Why You Want to Work Here:

  • Gain valuable real-world marketing experience with a growing company
  • Work with a supportive and collaborative marketing team
  • Be part of a workplace that works hard and celebrates success
  • Learn about the real estate and investment management industry
  • Join a company committed to environmental stewardship and social responsibility

Skyline is a capital management company that acquires, develops, and manages real estate properties and clean energy assets, and offers them as private alternative investment products

We offer students competitive pay, hands-on experience, training and development opportunities, and a supportive, friendly environment where you can learn, grow, and build valuable skills for your future career.
