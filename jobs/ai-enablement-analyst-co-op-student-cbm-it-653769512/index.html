Full Description

About CBM IT

CBM IT is a managed IT services provider that helps businesses run securely, efficiently, and with confidence. We work closely with our clients and our internal teams to improve technology, reduce friction, and create better ways of working.

We are looking for a curious, organized, and business-minded co-op student to help us better understand our internal processes and explore how AI and automation can improve them.

This role is a fit for a student who wants to learn how a business really works, document what they see, ask smart questions, test modern tools, and turn ideas into practical recommendations.

The Opportunity

As our AI Enablement Analyst Co-op Student, you will be introduced to key business functions across our company. Your role will be to learn how those functions operate today, document current workflows, identify opportunities for improvement, and evaluate where AI tools or automation could save time, improve quality, or reduce repetitive work.

You will work across teams to map processes, support small experiments, test tools, and present your findings in a clear, practical way. This is not just a research role. We want someone who can turn observation into action.

Targeted start date of June 1st.

What You’ll Do

  • Learn our core business processes across service delivery, administration, internal operations, and other functions
  • Meet with process owners and team members to understand how work is currently done
  • Document current-state workflows, handoffs, tools, pain points, and repetitive tasks
  • Run small pilots or proof-of-concept tests using tools such as Microsoft Copilot, ChatGPT, Power Automate, Claude, n8n, Zapier, etc.
  • Assist with analysis, testing, troubleshooting, and documentation of proposed solutions
  • Create process maps, SOPs, user guides, summaries, and presentation materials
  • Prepare recommendations for leadership on where AI or automation could create value
  • Track findings, action items, and outcomes, and help communicate progress across teams
  • Stay current on practical AI tools, workflow platforms, and business use cases relevant to a small business environment

What Success Looks Like

By the end of your term, you will have:

  • Documented several key internal business processes clearly and accurately
  • Identified meaningful opportunities for process improvement
  • Tested a set of AI or automation tools against real business workflows
  • Produced practical recommendations with clear pros, cons, and next steps
  • Helped create documentation or playbooks that improve consistency and adoption

Who You Are

You are a student who is excited by the idea of combining business analysis, technology, and creativity. You do not need to be a machine learning expert, but you should be comfortable learning new tools quickly and thinking critically about how work gets done.

You likely fit this role if you are:

  • Enrolled in a co-op or internship program at a recognized post-secondary institution
  • Studying computer science, business, business technology management, information systems, or a related field
  • Curious about AI, automation, and process improvement
  • Strong at listening, organizing information, and asking thoughtful questions
  • Comfortable documenting workflows and turning messy information into something clear
  • Able to work independently while collaborating well with others
  • A strong written and verbal communicator
  • Detail-oriented, adaptable, and willing to explore new tools and approaches

Nice to Have

  • Exposure to AI tools such as ChatGPT, Microsoft Copilot, Gemini, or similar platforms
  • Exposure to workflow tools such as Power Automate, Zapier, n8n, or similar platforms
  • Exposure to AI coding tools such as Claude Code, Loveable, Cursor
  • Experience creating process maps, SOPs, or business documentation
  • Experience with presentations, dashboards, or reporting
  • Basic technical comfort with APIs, scripting, or data tools is an asset, but not required

What You’ll Gain

  • Hands-on exposure to how a real business operates across multiple functions
  • Experience interviewing stakeholders and documenting business processes
  • A chance to evaluate modern AI tools in practical business settings
  • Mentorship from leadership and experienced technical professionals
  • Experience presenting findings and recommendations to decision-makers
  • A meaningful opportunity to help shape how our company uses AI and automation

Pay: $20.00-$25.00 per hour

Expected hours: 40.0 per week

Work Location: In person
