About the Role

About VIR Properties

Growth-focused marketing team with mentorship

Full Description

We are looking for a motivated and organised Entry-Level Project Manager to join our growing marketing team. This is an excellent opportunity for someone at the beginning of their career who is eager to gain hands-on experience managing marketing campaigns and coordinating projects.

You will work closely with team members to support the planning, execution, and delivery of client campaigns, ensuring projects run smoothly and efficiently.

Key Responsibilities

  • Support the planning and coordination of marketing campaigns and projects
  • Assist with managing timelines, deliverables, and deadlines
  • Work closely with internal teams to ensure smooth project execution
  • Help organise meetings, briefings, and project updates
  • Track campaign progress and assist with reporting
  • Identify potential challenges and support problem-solving efforts
  • Ensure tasks are completed on time and aligned with project goals

What We’re Looking For

  • Strong organisational and time management skills
  • Excellent communication and interpersonal abilities
  • A proactive attitude and willingness to learn
  • Ability to multitask in a fast-paced environment
  • Attention to detail and problem-solving mindset
  • Interest in marketing, project management, or business operations

What We Offer

  • Full training and ongoing mentorship
  • A supportive and collaborative team environment
  • Clear career progression opportunities
  • Hands-on experience working on marketing campaigns
  • Opportunity to develop project management and leadership skills

Ideal Candidate This Role Is Perfect For Someone Who Is

  • Starting their career in project management or marketing
  • Highly organised and eager to learn
  • Confident working in a team environment
  • Motivated to grow within a fast-paced company
