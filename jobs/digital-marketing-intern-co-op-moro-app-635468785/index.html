Digital Marketing Intern (Co-Op)

at Moro App

📍 Location Scarborough, Ontario Hybrid
📋 Details Type: Co-op Internship
📅 Posted Mar 03, 2026

About the Role

Plan and manage social campaigns; create content

About Moro App

Caregiver-focused app; growth through digital marketing

Full Description

** PLEASE READ FULLY - DONT JUST APPLY **

SUMMARY:

  • Currently enrolled in a school co-op or internship program (must be returning back to school after co-op)
  • Eligible for wage subsidy or government co-op grants
  • Strong interest in social media and content creation
  • Experience using software such as Canva and CapCut, IG-Edits and similar tools
  • Has Portfolio to show off short form videos personally made.
  • Must be willing to work out of Scarborough Office. ( Not a remote role)

Thank you for applying :)

Company Description

MORO is an innovative app designed to empower caregivers by helping them understand and respond to their loved ones' unique communication styles. The app captures personal routines, subtle cues, and individual expressions to enhance the quality of care. By promoting consistency and continuity across families, schools, and healthcare centers, MORO has become a trusted tool for caregivers. Available on Google Play and the App Store, MORO thrives through meaningful collaborations and partnerships that drive its growth and impact.

Role Description

This is a full-time hybrid role for a Digital Marketing Intern (Co-Op) located in Scarborough, ON, mostly on site. The Digital Marketing Intern will assist in planning and managing digital and social media campaigns, analyze web analytics data, contribute to online marketing initiatives, and develop engaging content. They will collaborate closely with the marketing team to enhance the app's online presence and brand awareness.

Qualifications

  • Proficiency in Social Media Marketing and Digital Marketing strategies
  • Familiarity with Web Analytics and Online Marketing tools
  • Strong Communication skills for creating engaging content and collaborating effectively
  • Basic knowledge of SEO and experience with social media platforms
  • Creativity and adaptability in a fast-paced environment
  • Currently enrolled in or recently graduated from a relevant program (Marketing, Communications, or similar field)
