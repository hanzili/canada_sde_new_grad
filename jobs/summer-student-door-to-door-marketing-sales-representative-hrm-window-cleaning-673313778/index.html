Full Description

Job Overview

Are you energetic, outgoing, and motivated to build real-world skills this summer?

We are looking for driven students to join our growing exterior home cleaning team as a Summer Student Door-to-Door Marketing & Sales Representative. This is an exciting opportunity for students who want to earn great money, meet new people, gain hands-on sales experience, and develop confidence that will benefit them for life.

In this role, you will engage directly with homeowners in local neighborhoods, promote our professional exterior cleaning services, and generate leads for services such as window cleaning, gutter cleaning, siding washing, and more.

This is not your average summer job. It is a fast-paced, fun, and growth-focused opportunity where you’ll work alongside other hardworking students while learning valuable business, communication, and leadership skills.

What You’ll Be Doing

  • Conduct door-to-door outreach in residential neighborhoods with energy and professionalism
  • Speak with homeowners, build rapport, and identify their needs
  • Promote our services and book free estimates for interested customers
  • Represent the company in a positive, confident, and professional manner
  • Track leads, customer interactions, and appointments accurately using company systems
  • Follow up on leads and help generate new business opportunities
  • Work with team members to improve sales strategies and hit team goals
  • Deliver a strong customer experience during every interaction

Why Students Love This Opportunity

Earn Great Money

Base hourly pay + performance bonuses. Strong performers can earn well above base pay.

Build Confidence & Communication Skills

Learn how to talk to anyone, handle rejection, negotiate, and lead conversations.

Gain Real Business Experience

Develop hands-on experience in sales, marketing, customer service, and entrepreneurship.

Fun Team Environment

Work with ambitious, like-minded students who want to grow, compete, and have a productive summer.

Stay Active Outdoors

Spend your summer moving, interacting, and working outside instead of sitting indoors all day.

Make an Impact Locally

Help homeowners improve and maintain their homes while supporting a student-run business.

Qualifications

  • Strong communication skills in English
  • Outgoing, friendly, and confident personality
  • Positive attitude and strong work ethic
  • Comfortable speaking with new people daily
  • Ability to work independently and with a team
  • Reliable and dependable
  • Eager to learn sales, marketing, and communication skills
  • Previous customer service or sales experience is an asset, but not required
