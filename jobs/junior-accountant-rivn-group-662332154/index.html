Full Description

About Rivn Group

Rivn Group is a Calgary based real estate investment and management firm focused on disciplined ownership, operational precision, and capital efficient value creation. We operate a growing portfolio of office buildings in the Beltline and Downtown Calgary and manage them directly with a hands-on owner-operator mindset.

Position Overview

The Junior Property Accountant supports the day-to-day accounting operations for Rivn's commercial portfolio. Reporting directly to the Senior Accountant and working closely with the Property Manager, this role offers direct mentorship and real exposure to full-cycle property accounting from day one.

This is an ideal entry point for a detail oriented accounting professional who wants to build a career in commercial real estate finance. You will take ownership of core accounting functions, support financial reporting, and grow your understanding of how a real estate platform operates end-to-end.

Key Responsibilities 1. Full-Cycle Accounting

  • Process day-to-day accounting transactions including AP, AR, and journal entries.
  • Perform monthly bank reconciliations and general ledger reconciliations.
  • Prepare and post monthly accruals and recurring entries.
  • Code and process supplier invoices with accuracy and proper documentation.

2. Tenant Billing & Receivables

  • Monitor AR aging and assist with collections follow-up on overdue balances.
  • Respond to tenant billing inquiries and prepare statements of account.
  • Assist with year-end tenant reconciliations, including operating cost recoveries.
  • Assist with tenant chargebacks and intercompany billing.

3. Financial Reporting Support

  • Assist in preparing monthly financial reporting packages for ownership.
  • Support the preparation of annual budgets and forecasts.
  • Maintain audit-ready records for all transactions and financial documentation.
  • Perform monthly variance analysis.

4. Compliance & Tax

  • Prepare and file monthly GST returns accurately and on time.
  • Assist with year-end working paper preparation and any external review processes.
  • Ensure compliance with relevant reporting (ASPE and RECA) requirements.

5. Systems & Administration

  • Maintain organized digital filing for invoices, leases, contracts, and financial records.
  • Work within Rivn's accounting and property management software.
  • Identify process inefficiencies and contribute to building scalable systems.

Qualifications Required

  • Post-secondary education in accounting, finance, or a related field.
  • 1–3 years of accounting experience.
  • Solid understanding of full-cycle accounting: journal entries, bank recs, and accruals.
  • Intermediate proficiency in Microsoft Excel.
  • Strong attention to detail and ability to manage competing deadlines.
  • Clear written and verbal communication skills.

Assets (Nice-to-have)

  • Experience in property accounting or commercial real estate finance.
  • Familiarity with Yardi, QuickBooks Online, or similar accounting platforms.
  • Understanding of CAM reconciliations, rent rolls, or lease accounting concepts.
  • Currently pursuing or planning to pursue a CPA designation.

Why Join Rivn Group?

  • Work directly with ownership and leadership across commercial real estate accounting, operations, and finance.
  • Clear path to grow into a Property Accountant or Senior Accountant role.
  • Entrepreneurial, low-bureaucracy environment where your contributions are visible and growth is recognized.
  • Broad experience across accounting, leasing, and asset operations.
