About Enabled Talent

Inclusive mission-driven platform for diverse talent

Full Description

Company Description

Enabled Talent is an inclusive workforce platform that combines AI-driven job matching, accessibility solutions, and DEI training to help organizations build stronger, more diverse teams. We enable employers to access skilled, often overlooked talent while turning inclusion into a measurable driver of productivity, innovation, and long-term growth.

Role Description

This is a full-time hybrid role for a Digital Marketing Intern, based in Brampton, ON, with opportunities to work remotely. The intern will assist in planning and executing digital marketing campaigns, developing online marketing strategies, managing social media channels, and analyzing web traffic using analytics tools. Other responsibilities include monitoring trends, creating digital content, and supporting overall marketing efforts to promote Enabled Talent’s mission and services.

Key Duties:

• Create and manage content across social media and digital channels

• Develop storytelling content aligned with disability inclusion and impact

• Write blogs, posts, and campaign materials

• Support marketing campaigns and outreach initiatives

• Track performance metrics and optimize content strategies

• Assist in brand positioning and messaging

• Create simple visual and written content assets

• Support promotion of events and key initiatives

Qualifications

  • Skills in Social Media Marketing and Online Marketing
  • Proficiency in Digital Marketing and Web Analytics tools
  • Strong Communication skills for both written and verbal content creation
  • Ability to collaborate effectively in a hybrid work environment
  • Interest in diversity, equity, and inclusion to align with the company mission
  • Currently pursuing or recently completed a degree in Marketing, Communications, or a related field
  • Familiarity with marketing platforms, graphic design tools, or basic HTML is a plus

Why Join Enabled Talent

  • Contribute to meaningful employment opportunities for individuals with disabilities
  • Be part of building inclusive workforce systems across communities
  • Gain hands-on experience in events, partnerships, and workforce development
  • Work in a mission-driven, high-impact organization
  • Opportunity to grow as the organization expands

Accessibility & Inclusion

Enabled Talent is committed to equitable and inclusive hiring practices. We encourage applications from individuals of all backgrounds and abilities. Accommodations are available throughout the recruitment process upon request.
