Job Title: Junior Accountant

Company Overview

We are a fast-growing, Canada-wide distribution company operating at the intersection of natural health, grocery, and nutritional supplements. We partner with leading, trusted, and innovative brands to bring high-quality, responsibly sourced products to retailers and consumers across the country.

Our business operates in a dynamic, highly regulated environment where reliability, speed, and execution are critical. As we scale, our teams are building robust systems, refining processes, and tackling real operational challenges that directly shape how products move through the Canadian market.

At Peak, people have ownership and influence. We value collaboration, accountability, and continuous improvement, and we seek individuals energized by growth, problem-solving, and making a measurable impact. If you’re motivated by meaningful work, high standards, and the opportunity to help build a stronger, smarter distribution platform, you’ll feel at home here.

About the Role:

As a Junior Accountant , you will support the day-to-day accounting operations of the company, contributing to the overall financial accuracy and health of the organization. This role involves a combination of accounts receivable, accounts payable, reconciliations, and general ledger support. You will work cross-functionally with Sales, Trade Marketing, and Customer Service to ensure accurate financial records, timely payments, and compliance with customer and vendor agreements.

Key Responsibilities :

·       Process and record AR transactions including credits, deposits, and credit card payments

·       Monitor aging reports and proactively follow up with customers on outstanding invoices

·       Ensure accurate and timely application of incoming payments

·       Reconcile and investigate customer deductions related to trade promotions, returns, and terms

·       Validate promotional activity and pricing discrepancies against contracts and agreements

·       Issue and manage chargebacks for vendor non-compliance, damages, or returns

·       Process accounts payable (Expenses, Vendor Bills, payments …. etc.).

·       Record and post journal entries accurately

·       Maintain and reconcile general ledger accounts

·       Perform bank, customers, and vendors reconciliations.

Compliance & Documentation:

·       Track and enforce customer/vendor contract compliance and pricing agreements

·       Maintain accurate documentation of communications, disputes, and resolutions

·       Assist with month-end close processes and provide support for internal and external audits

Cross-Functional Collaboration:

·       Work closely with internal teams (Sales, Marketing, Operations) to support strategic account health

·       Identify trends in deductions or disputes and provide actionable insights

·       Help resolve recurring billing or payment issues through proactive engagement

Required Qualifications

· Diploma or degree Accounting, Finance, or a related field

·       2+ years of experience in AR, AP, and accounting operation.

·       Experience with ERP systems or financial software (NetSuite preferred)

·       Strong Excel skills and ability to analyze data for trends and anomalies

·       Ability to manage multiple priorities and work cross-functionally

·       Strong communication and relationship-building skills

·       High attention to detail and investigative mindset

Why Work at Peak Performance Products Inc.

At Peak Performance Products Inc., we believe our people are at the core of our success. We are committed to fostering a positive, inclusive, and supportive workplace where employees feel valued and motivated to do their best work.

With over 25 years in the Canadian distribution industry, we offer stability, a strong team culture, and an environment that supports both personal well-being and professional growth.
