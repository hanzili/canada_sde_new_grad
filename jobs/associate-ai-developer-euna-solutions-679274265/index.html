Associate AI Developer

at Euna Solutions

About the Role

Prototype AI apps, build tools, integrate with APIs

About Euna Solutions

Public sector software for procurement and AI tooling

Full Description

The Opportunity

We're seeking an Associate AI Developer to join our team and drive rapid innovation through proof-of-concept development and AI tooling. In this hands-on role, you'll work at the intersection of AI and practical application, helping teams across the organization leverage cutting-edge AI capabilities to solve real problems.

What You'll Do

  • Rapid Prototyping & POC Development Build and iterate on proof-of-concept applications that demonstrate AI capabilities and validate use cases. You'll move quickly from idea to working prototype, helping stakeholders understand what's possible and refining concepts based on feedback.
  • AI Tooling & Integration Develop custom tools and integrations that make AI accessible to technical and non-technical teams alike. This includes building MCP (Model Context Protocol) servers that enable AI systems to interact with internal services, APIs, and data sources.
  • Cross-Functional Collaboration Partner with product, engineering, and business teams to understand their needs and translate requirements into functional AI-powered solutions. You'll act as both a technical implementer and an AI consultant.
  • Technical Evangelism Share knowledge about AI capabilities, best practices, and emerging technologies with the broader organization through documentation, demos, and hands-on support.

What We're Looking For

Required Skills & Experience

  • 0-3 years of software development experience with strong programming fundamentals
  • Proficiency in Python or TypeScript/JavaScript
  • Experience with APIs, webhooks, and service integrations
  • Familiarity with LLMs and AI/ML concepts
  • Comfort with rapid iteration and working in ambiguous problem spaces
  • Strong communication skills and ability to explain technical concepts clearly

Preferred Qualifications

  • Experience building with OpenAI, Anthropic, or other LLM APIs
  • Knowledge of Model Context Protocol (MCP) or similar integration frameworks
  • Background in building internal tools or developer platforms
  • Experience with FastAPI, Express, or similar web frameworks
  • Familiarity with cloud platforms (AWS, Azure)
  • Understanding of prompt engineering and LLM application patterns

Personal Attributes

  • Naturally curious about AI and emerging technologies
  • Pragmatic problem-solver who values shipping over perfection
  • Comfortable learning new technologies quickly
  • Collaborative mindset with a bias toward helping others succeed
  • Self-directed with strong project management instincts

What Success Looks Like

In Your First 6 Months, You'll Have

  • Delivered multiple POCs that informed product or process decisions
  • Built and deployed MCP servers or similar integrations that teams actively use
  • Established yourself as a go-to resource for AI implementation questions
  • Contributed to internal documentation and best practices around AI development

Location:

This position will be hybrid in our Oakville, ON office.

Compensation Range: $81,500 - $101,900

We believe in openness, so we publish our range from the start. Where an offer will land depends on your background, strengths, and how you’ll contribute to the work ahead.

This role fills an existing vacancy. Euna uses AI responsibly to assist in screening, assessing, or selecting applicants. AI helps us work smarter, but people lead the process.

AI Mindset at Euna Solutions

We believe the future of work is human + AI. At Euna Solutions, we encourage our team members to leverage AI tools to enhance creativity, efficiency, and decision-making. We’re looking for people who are curious about emerging technologies, eager to experiment, and committed to using AI responsibly to augment—not replace—their expertise.

If you enjoy exploring new ways to solve problems, learning continuously, and applying AI to make your work smarter and more impactful, you’ll thrive here.
