Full Description

Geology Summer Student

Atlantic Operations is a wholly owned subsidiary of St Barbara Limited, an ASX listed gold producer and explorer with mining assets in Canada and Australia. We are proud to be a Company recognized by industry awards for operational excellence and performance, sustainability, gender diversity and pay equity.

We are currently seeking Geology Summer Students to support our upcoming 2026 field season campaign.

Geology Summer Students will spend significant amounts of time in the field to support the planned exploration activities aimed at evaluating the many exploration targets and resources under development by St Barbara Atlantic throughout Nova Scotia.

Primary Duties and Responsibilities Including but Not Limited to:

  • Providing support to St Barbara staff geologists in the field
  • Undertaking rock chipping and soil sampling programs
  • Logging, cutting and sampling of drill core
  • Sorting and collating historical pulp samples
  • Always ensuring a safe working environment
  • Completing other geological activities as assigned

Essential Requirements:

  • Working toward a Geology degree
  • Willingness to adhere to St Barbara safety procedures
  • Computer literacy, particularly MS Office Suite products
  • Strong communication skills
  • Current unencumbered Class 5 Driver’s Licence
  • Own transportation to/from St Barbara’s Bedford office
  • Competent with outdoor field work which includes being proficient on the use and operation of a GPS and compass and being able to hike long distances while carrying heavy packs

Working Conditions:

  • Physically demanding – 8 to 12-hour days hiking through the woods in variable weather conditions
  • 40 hour working week
  • Must be available to undertake regional field work which will require overnight stays away from Halifax
  • Must wear Personal Protective Equipment

We would like to thank all those who apply; however, only those selected for an interview will be notified.

For further information about St Barbara, visit https://stbarbaragold.ca

Please understand that screening and interviewing may take place prior to the advertised close date.
