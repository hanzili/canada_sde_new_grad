We are seeking a detail-oriented and proactive Junior Accountant to join a growing organization. Reporting to the Controller, you will play a key role in overseeing full-cycle accounting while supporting operational efficiency and system improvements, including SAP implementation.

Key Responsibilities: - Handle month end, journal entries, adjusting entries

  • Prepare full financial statements

  • Prepare GST/QST reports

  • Prepare bank reconciliations

  • Monitor cash flow

  • Maintain accurate ERP data (customers, suppliers, products)

  • Prepare financial reports

Requirements: - 2 years of full-cycle accounting experience

  • BAC, Accounting certificate or DEC in Accounting or equivalent education

  • SAP experience (asset)

  • Strong organizational, analytical, and time-management skills

  • Comfortable with collaborative tools (Teams, SharePoint)

  • Detail-oriented with a team-focused mindset
