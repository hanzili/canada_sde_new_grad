About the Role

About March Consulting LP

Full Description

Introduction We are looking for a temporary full-time Summer Student-Intern, Admin & Human Resources, to join our Saskatoon team! The contract term for this position is May 4th, 2026, until September 28th, 2026. This position will report to the Manager, Admin & Human Resources. Indigenous applicants are encouraged to apply. Company March Consulting Associates Inc. (“March”) is an Indigenous-owned & employee owned, multi-disciplinary engineering company headquartered in Saskatoon, Saskatchewan. We maintain the values that we began with and made a name for ourselves by our dedication to the development and success of our clients. î-nitawîthimihcik nîhithaw athisitiniwak kâ-kî-kîsîhtâcik sâpo-ayamihcikîwin (qualified Indigenous professionals are encouraged to apply). Tiniki March takes pride in providing a family-like culture that supports our client’s needs and well-being of our employees. We hire the right employees for the long-term, where they can come learn and grow their career with the company. We welcome those who have a strong sense of integrity and a passion for the province we live and work in. March provides engineering, procurement, project, and construction management services to a variety of resource-based industrial and commercial enterprises. In addition, March and its employees have developed specialized expertise in feasibility studies and asset integrity in the heavy industrial sector.
Position Overview
Key Responsibilities

  • Work closely with the Admin and HR teams to support in daily admin and HR-related activities
  • Update and maintain confidential employee records and ensure data integrity in HR and Payroll systems
  • Create and maintain job descriptions
  • Support recruitment activities (job postings, resume reviews, pre-screenings, candidate correspondence, interview organization, reference Support and organize employee onboarding and offboarding initiatives
  • Conduct employee surveys
  • Support with data analysis for reporting purposes
  • Support benefits administration through the benefits provider’s online portal and facilitate and coordinate short-term disability claims with the insurance provider
  • Coordinate seminars and trainings; Aid in identifying training and development opportunities and optimal delivery methods
  • Organize Drug & Alcohol testing
  • Create and maintain March resumes
  • Support March’s contractor management
  • Coordinate internal and external HR-related requests and questions
  • Keeping up to date with the latest HR trends and best practices
  • Collaboration and cross-training with the Administrative Assistant as needed
  • Other duties as assigned

Candidate Profile & Assets

  • Enrolled in Bachelor’s degree in Human Resources, Business Administration, or equivalent.
  • Knowledge of employment regulations, incl. familiarity with the Canada Labour Code, Employment Standards, Labour Law, and Human Rights Legislation will be considered an asset
  • HR experience in small to medium sized private sector businesses will be considered an asset
  • Proficiency with MS Office 365 (Word, Excel, Power Point and Outlook)
  • Ability to maintain strict confidentiality is required
  • Excellent oral and written communication skills
  • Remarkable interpersonal and problem-solving skills
  • Cooperative and dedicated work mentality
  • Strong administrative and organizational skills including attention to detail and multi-tasking

We look forward to receiving your cover letter and resume. We thank all applicants who apply; however only those selected for an interview will be contacted. Compensation:Commensurate with education and experience. Benefits: March offers a comprehensive benefits program which includes Life, Disability, Health and Dental as well as competitive retirement benefits (RRSP), a health spending account, paid sick time and more! Work hours: 40 hours per week, Monday – Friday.
Work location: Saskatoon.
