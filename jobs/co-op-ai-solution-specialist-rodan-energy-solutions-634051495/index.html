About Rodan Energy Solutions

Energy solutions for utilities and grids.

Full Description

About Us:

Rodan Energy is a North American leader in distributed energy resources and energy intelligence, enabling a more reliable, cost-effective, and decarbonized grid. We partner with large energy users, utilities, and grid operators to design, finance, and operate high-impact DER solutions, including asset optimization, demand response, metering, and power system engineering. Managing over 1,400 MW of flexible capacity across Ontario, NYISO, PJM, MISO, and AESO, we make sustainable attainable.

Why Join Rodan:

  • Thriving Industry: Join a rapidly growing sector at the forefront of innovation and sustainability, shaping the future of energy management.
  • Product Market Fit: With a 98% client retention rate and consistent YOY growth, Rodan has built a reputation for delivering trusted, long-term partnerships with industry leaders.
  • Top Talent & Strong Culture: Collaborate with high-performing, passionate individuals who are committed to excellence and sustainability.

What Makes This Role Great:

  • This co-op provides hands-on experience working on real AI solutions used across our lines of business. You’ll help build, test, and support AI tools and agents, learning by doing, not just observing. By the end of the term, you’ll have contributed to production AI solutions and gained insight into how modern organizations adopt and scale

Key Responsibilities:

  • Help build, test, and support AI agents, AI workflow automation, and AI-powered tools used across the business.
  • Assist with AI use cases including support, analytics, forecasting, compliance, and internal knowledge or chatbot tools.
  • Create training materials, documentation, and knowledge-sharing resources to support AI adoption.
  • Collaborate with teams to translate business needs into practical AI-enabled solutions.
  • Participate in structured onboarding and learning with tools such as Microsoft Copilot.
  • Support enterprise AI initiatives and rollout of high-impact use cases.

Required Skills and Qualifications:

  • Currently pursuing a degree in Computer Science, Engineering, Data Science, or a related program.
  • Basic understanding of AI concepts with interest in real-world applications.
  • Interest in AI agents, automation, and Microsoft Copilot.
  • Strong communication skills and ability to work with cross-functional teams.
  • Organized, adaptable, and comfortable working in a fast-paced environment.
  • Awareness of AI governance and responsible AI best practices.

Compensation & Term

Hourly Rate: $20-$24/hour

Work Term: 4 months
