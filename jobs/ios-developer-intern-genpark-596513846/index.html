About the Role

About GenPark

AI-powered branding platform for Gen Z audiences.

Full Description

Company Description

GenPark leverages intelligent Brand and User Agents to help brands effectively understand, engage, and convert Gen Z consumers globally. By integrating AI-generated personalized content, interactive agents, and immersive AI video experiences, GenPark ensures brands connect with the right audiences in the right contexts. This innovative platform helps achieve higher conversion rates while maintaining lower customer acquisition costs. Functioning as an AI-native system, GenPark's Agents continuously learn from user behavior and feedback to optimize product and consumer matching across worldwide markets.

Role Description

This is a full-time hybrid role based in Vancouver, BC, with the flexibility to work from home part of the time. As an iOS Developer, you will be responsible for developing high-performance iOS applications, improving existing features, and collaborating with cross-functional teams to execute project goals. You will write clean, reusable code, perform testing, troubleshoot, and resolve issues. The role involves participating in regular sprint cycles, keeping up with the latest trends in mobile development, and ensuring applications align with user needs and company objectives.

Qualifications

  • Proficiency in Mobile Application Development, including building user-friendly and efficient apps
  • Strong expertise in iOS Development and knowledge of the iOS ecosystem
  • Experience with Mobile Applications and a solid understanding of software development principles
  • Proficiency in iOS development tools such as Swift, Objective-C, and Xcode
  • Familiarity with version control tools like Git and continuous integration practices
  • Strong problem-solving skills with attention to detail
  • A collaborative mindset and ability to work both independently and as part of a team
  • Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Software Engineering, or equivalent experience
  • Experience with AI technologies or integration with AI-driven systems is a plus
