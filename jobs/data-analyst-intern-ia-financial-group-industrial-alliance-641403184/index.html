Full Description

Description Summer 2026 Internship / Databases / SQL / Python / Location: Québec City or Montreal / Hybrid Build the future with us Are you driven by actuarial science, data science, and analytical innovation and eager to contribute to the purpose of a company that aims to help its clients feel confident and secure about their future? As As a Data Analyst Intern , you will play a key role in developing analyses, models, and tools that support the strategy and evolution of Group Insurance and Group Savings.

This position is a great opportunity to showcase your skills and fully realize your potential within a caring and trustworthy company. Here, people and their development are at the heart of our priorities, fostering an environment that encourages collaboration and innovation.

What You Will Accomplish With Us As a Data Analyst Intern , you will be at the core of our mission. Here are the main responsibilities:

Explore and learn:

  • Deepen your knowledge of insurance while contributing to analysis and modeling projects;

Create concrete solutions:

  • Develop analytical tools and models to address real business challenges.

Strategic analysis and business problem-solving:

  • Analyze and interpret data to address key needs, such as experience studies;
  • Deliver analyses aligned with business objectives and present them in a clear and accessible way to leadership teams;
  • Explore and process large datasets to identify opportunities for improvement and innovation.

Collaboration and learning:

  • Work closely with mentors and experts to understand life insurance challenges and analytical processes;
  • Discover the steps involved in deploying analytical models into production, with support from other internal teams.

What Could Accelerate Your Success In This Role We are looking for someone who:

  • Is currently studying actuarial science, data science, or a related field;
  • Is recognized for strong analytical rigor and critical thinking;
  • Stands out for their ability to learn quickly and solve complex problems;
  • Demonstrates strong curiosity and a keen interest in data, programming, and life insurance;
  • Is known for a collaborative mindset and the ability to communicate analyses clearly and effectively;
  • Is passionate about programming (Python, SQL, or others) and enthusiastic about applying data science skills to tackle concrete challenges.

Why you will love working with us

  • A work environment where learning and development merge with a collective pursuit of excellence.
  • A healthy, safe, fair, and inclusive environment where potential can be freely expressed and developed.
  • The possibility to work in a hybrid environment supported by flexibility and access to inspiring, innovative workspaces.
  • Student benefits: Access to internal training, mentoring, involvement in real projects, flexible schedules adapted to studies, and a rewarding experience for your professional journey.
