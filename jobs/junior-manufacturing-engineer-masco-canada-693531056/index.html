Full Description

WE ARE HIRING! JUNIOR MANUFACTURING ENGINEER (On-Site – St. Thomas, ON Office) Build your future with Masco Canada! At Masco Canada, we’re passionate about delivering innovative solutions and exceptional customer experiences. We’re looking for a Junior Manufacturing Engineer to join our Engineering team. If you thrive in a dynamic, collaborative environment and are excited to make a meaningful impact, we want to hear from you.

WHAT WILL YOU DO? As the Junior Manufacturing Engineer , you will play a key role in providing daily support with our production equipment, developing and commissioning automation projects in manufacturing, from initial concept to installation.

KEY RESPONSIBILITIES?

  • Provide day-to-day support to the operations and maintenance teams with troubleshooting active equipment breakdowns or deficiencies
  • Maintain all control systems including but not limited to Robotic Cells, CNC Machines, Automated Cells, and Tester Units.
  • Maintain all electrical documentation and support the creation of standard formatting.
  • Investigate and implement equipment optimizations and cycle time reduction events
  • Provide electrical and controls input for a detailed scope of work, design basis document, and project execution plan.
  • Develop electrical designs for projects or proposals.
  • Develop operator’s work instructions and assist in training.
  • Prepare process reports by collecting, analyzing, and summarizing information and trends.
  • Support in the commissioning of automation projects as needed
  • Coordinate prototype and production runs through new program launches.
  • Support requests from the operations team to improve equipment efficiency
  • Drive long-term problem-solving through the implementation of CI projects.
  • Facilitate waste reduction initiatives as an SME in QCO, EP, Std Work, and PPS.
  • Assist in the creation of PFMEA, Control Plans, and other APQP / Stage Gate-related tasks

WHAT WILL YOU BRING?

  • Bachelor's Degree in Electrical Engineering or Electromechanical Engineering Technician Diploma
  • Minimum of 2 years of Control experience in a lean manufacturing environment
  • Knowledge and programming of PLCs
  • Knowledge of common manufacturing practices
  • Ability to read and create electrical drawings
  • Experience with Robotic (ABB and Fanuc) and G-Code programming
  • Experience with RSLogix 500 and Studio 5000
  • Experience with Connected Components Workbench
  • Experience with FactoryTalk View ME
  • Experience with AutoCAD Electrical Drawings
  • Proficiency in Microsoft Office (Outlook, Word, Excel, PowerPoint)
  • Strong presentation, written, and verbal communication skills

WHAT'S IN IT FOR YOU?

  • Competitive Rewards and Compensation
  • Defined Contribution Registered Pension Plan (optional) with employer matching
  • Deferred Profit-Sharing Plan (DPSP)
  • Benefit insurance coverage from Day 1, including health, dental, and travel insurance
  • Employee Assistance Program (Dialogue) – access to health and wellness resources
  • Employee Store, Employee Purchase Program, Dell Discount Program, GoodLife Fitness Discounts, and more
  • 2 paid volunteer days to support the communities we live and work in
  • Opportunities for development and growth, including Onboarding, Buddy and Mentoring programs, LinkedIn Learning, and other virtual and in-class learning opportunities
  • Participation in employee-led committees such as the Wellness Committee, Masco United (Inclusion & Belonging Committee), Philanthropy Committee, Organizational Pulse Committee, or employee impact groups

WHY JOIN MASCO CANADA?

  • Over a Century of Excellence – With more than 120 years of experience, Masco Canada has established itself as a leader in the building and home improvement industry.
  • Diverse Brand Portfolio – We proudly offer 12+ brands delivering 20,000+ innovative water solutions, shaping the future of plumbing and home improvement.
  • Innovative Solutions – Our commitment to cutting-edge plumbing and water technologies keeps us at the forefront of the industry.
  • Collaborative & Inclusive Culture – We foster an environment where all employees are valued, respected, and empowered to contribute and grow.
  • Opportunities for Growth – We support continuous learning, career development, and professional growth, helping you achieve your full potential.
  • Making an Impact in Our Communities – We believe in giving back and actively supporting initiatives that make a difference in the communities where we live and work.

Join us at Masco Canada , where your contributions drive innovation, excellence, and positive change !
