About Lantern

Full Description

At Lantern , our company culture stands as the bedrock of our success and a source of pride for our teams. We firmly believe that a culture founded on trust forms the basis for enduring relationships with clients, colleagues, and partners.

Within this culture, we nurture an environment of respect, inclusion, and belonging, fostering collaboration among inspired teams. We prioritize the well-being of our colleagues, the success of our clients, and our positive impact on society.

Embracing a growth mindset where curiosity thrives, we celebrate excellence and value individuals who inspire and mentor others, elevating the collective. Our driving force lies in personal and business growth. We go above and beyond to surprise and delight our clients, delivering tangible business value. Role Overview

We are seeking highly curious, driven individuals to join our team as Early Career Consultants specializing in Data & AI, Azure, and modern development practices.

This role is ideal for individuals with foundational skills or hands-on experience in Data, AI, or Azure-based solutions who are eager to accelerate their careers in consulting. You’ll work alongside experienced practitioners to solve real business challenges using emerging technologies.

If you’re passionate about learning quickly, solving complex problems, and building with modern tools, this role is for you.

Who You Are

You May Be a Great Fit If You Are A recent graduate with exposure to Data, AI, LLMs, or cloud technologies through coursework, bootcamps, or personal projects

An early-career professional (1-5 years) seeking rapid growth in a consulting environment

Transitioning from another field with strong transferable skills in problem-solving, analytics, or technology

Naturally curious, adaptable, and motivated to learn in a fast-paced environment

What You’ll Bring

Core Skills & Attributes

Strong communication and customer-facing skills

Demonstrated learning agility (ability to quickly pick up new tools and concepts)

Genuine interest in consulting and client delivery

High level of coachability, curiosity, and ownership

Positive attitude with a growth mindset

Technical Exposure (Foundational)

Academic Or Hands-on Experience In Data, Analytics, or AI/ML concepts

Cloud technologies (preferably Azure)

Modern tools or workflows (e.g., GitHub, version control, AI-assisted programming, React, TypeScript, .Net, C#, Microsoft tech-stack)

Why Join Us

Accelerated learning in one of the fastest-growing tech spaces (Data & AI)

Real client exposure early in your career

Strong mentorship and clear growth path into consulting roles

Collaborative, people-first culture focused on learning and impact
