About the Role

Assist customers, prepare quotes, manage sales pipeline daily

About Braid Flooring & Window Fashions

Local flooring company, mentorship and growth opportunities

Full Description

Join a growing company where you can build a long-term, high-income career.

Braid Flooring is hiring Sales Associates to join our growing team. This is an excellent opportunity for someone with some sales experience who wants to build a long-term career in sales with a successful, local, independently owned business.

We specialize in flooring products and work closely with homeowners and contractors. No flooring experience is required—we will train the right candidate.

This role is ideal for someone who has tried sales, enjoys it, and is ready to commit to developing into a high-earning sales professional.

Key Responsibilities

  • Assist customers in a sales floor/showroom environment, guiding product selection
  • Prepare quotes, follow up on leads, and manage customer inquiries
  • Manage jobs from start to finish while maintaining clear communication with clients and subcontractors
  • Support senior team members
  • Build and maintain relationships with clients and contractors
  • Learn product knowledge and sales processes
  • Develop and manage your own sales pipeline over time

Requirements

  • Previous sales experience required (retail, automotive, inside sales, etc.)
  • Comfortable working directly with customers in a sales floor or showroom environment
  • Strong communication and interpersonal skills
  • Organized, detail-oriented, and able to manage multiple priorities
  • Self-motivated and able to work independently
  • Interest in home design, construction, building materials, or trades-related industries
  • Valid driver’s license and vehicle

Our Team

At Braid Flooring, we’ve built a team of people who care—about our clients, our partners, and each other.

We work in a team-oriented environment, but also one where everyone takes ownership of their role and contributes to the overall success of the team.

We are looking for someone who will thrive in a culture that values professionalism, follow-through, and strong relationships, and who wants to be part of a team that takes pride in doing things well.

Compensation & Growth

  • Salary: $50,000 – $65,000 base + performance incentives (OTE $50,000 – $100,000+)
  • Realistic first-year earnings of $50K, and, if successful, earnings of $100K+
  • Clear path to higher earnings as you build your client base
  • Closed Sundays and most statutory holidays, typically for the entire holiday season
  • Paid vacation
  • Ongoing mentorship from experienced sales professionals
  • Long-term career opportunity in a stable, in-demand industry

This position is designed for individuals who are serious about building a career in sales.

If you have sales experience, enjoy working with people, and are ready to grow your career in construction sales, we encourage you to apply.
