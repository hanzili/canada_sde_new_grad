Accounting / FP&A Internship

Accounting Student (La Salle, MB)

at Tank Traders

📍 Location Winnipeg, MB
📋 Details Internship · Summer · $20/hour · Temporary
📅 Posted Mar 13, 2026

About the Role

Assist with reconciliations, invoicing, and postings

About Tank Traders

Canadian company with nationwide BBQ Tank network

Full Description

Are you an accounting student looking to gain real-world experience and apply what you’re learning in the classroom? Join our Accounting team in La Salle, MB as an Accounting Summer Student and take the first step toward building your accounting career.

This role is ideal for a motivated student who’s eager to learn, asks questions, and enjoys working as part of a supportive team. You’ll gain hands-on exposure to day-to-day accounting processes while developing practical skills that will set you up for future success.

Location: This position is on site in La Salle, MB and there is no public transportation to our office. Reliable personal transportation is required for this role.

What You’ll Learn & Do:

  • Assist with reconciling customer and vendor accounts
  • Follow up on payments and apply receipts
  • Generate and send customer invoices
  • Prepare statements for customers
  • Maintain organized and accurate accounting records
  • Review invoices for accuracy and help identify errors or discrepancies
  • Code, post, and process transactions in our accounting system

You won’t be expected to know everything - we’ll provide training, guidance, and ongoing support to help you grow your skills and confidence.

Why This is a Great Student Opportunity:

  • $20.00 per hour starting wage
  • Hands-on accounting experience
  • Mentorship from experienced accounting professionals
  • Open-door culture where questions and learning are encouraged
  • Friendly, collaborative, and positive work environment
  • Potential opportunity for a full-time, permanent role after graduation

Did you know?

Tank Traders® is a global leader in the BBQ Tank Exchange Program. As a proudly Canadian company, we have expanded our reach nationwide and continue to grow. Presently, Tank Traders® operates 19 locations across Canada and 8 in the United States.

Job Types: Full-time, Part-time

  • On-site parking
  • Paid time off

  • driver's license with reliable means of transportation (required)
