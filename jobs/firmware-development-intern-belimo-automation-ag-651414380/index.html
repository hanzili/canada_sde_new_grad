📍 Location Montréal, QC

About the Role

Develop and test embedded firmware for MCUs

About BELIMO Automation AG

Global HVAC control devices leader

Full Description

The Firmware Intern will collaborate closely with the development team to support the resolution of known firmware issues, contribute to the implementation of new features, and assist in executing firmware verification tests while documenting results with clarity.

What You'll Do

Design, develop, and maintain embedded firmware for microcontroller-based systems using Embedded C.

Develop and maintain bare-metal Embedded C firmware using a procedural architecture optimized for resource-constrained platforms.

Implement and manage global state, binary flags, and interrupt to support deterministic behavior and efficient execution on limited-memory microcontrollers.

Optimize firmware for performance, memory optimization, power consumption, reliability, and real-time constraints.

Work with multiple serial communication protocols such as UART, I2C, CAN, and other interfaces.

Debug electronic and firmware issues on PCBs using multimeters, logic analyzers, and oscilloscopes.

Collaborate with product leads, stakeholders, electronics engineers, verification and software teams to ensure reliable system integration and correct implementation of requirements.

Participate in code reviews, design documentation, and architecture discussions to maintain high code quality and long-term maintainability.

Who You Are

3 years Bachelor’s, or Master’s degree student in Electrical Engineering, Computer Engineering, Computer Science, or a related field.

Strong experience with embedded C programming in bare-metal environments.

Strong understanding of embedded system architecture (including interrupt handling, memory organization and memory-constrained systems, peripheral interfacing, ADC/DAC etc.)

Experienced drawing of UML flow diagrams.

Familiarity with reading and analyzing hardware schematics and component datasheets.

Strong analytical, troubleshooting, and debugging skills with attention to detail.

Ability to work effectively in cross-functional engineering teams.

Pluses:
Knowledge of C++ and object-oriented programming concepts.

Experience with version control systems (Git) and modern collaborative development workflows.

Knowledge of industrial communication protocols such as Modbus and/or BACnet.

Hands-on experience with bootloader development for embedded systems.

Experience with RTOS-based embedded systems, preferably one or more of:

FreeRTOS

μC/OS

Azure RTOS / ThreadX

Zephyr

Other RTOS platforms

Knowledge of MISRA C or embedded coding standards.

Experience integrating or developing wireless or network communication stacks, such as Thread or Single Pair Ethernet.

Who We Are

Belimo is the global market leader in the development, production, and marketing of field devices for the energy-efficient control of heating, ventilation and air conditioning systems. Damper actuators, control valves, sensors, meters, and gas monitors make up the company's core business. Founded in 1975, Belimo employs approximately 2,400 people in over 80 countries and is listed on the Swiss Exchange (SIX) since 1995.

Belimo is committed to paying all employees in a fair, equitable and transparent manner. The base pay for this internship is $23 CAD hourly. Belimo is proud to be an equal opportunity employer. #WeAreBelimo

This position is not eligible for visa sponsorship such as H1B, TN, E-3, STEM OPT.
