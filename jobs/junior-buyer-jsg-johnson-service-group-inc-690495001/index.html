About the Role

About JSG (Johnson Service Group, Inc.)

JSG: reputable staffing firm with procurement roles

Full Description

Our Utilities client is seeking a Junior Buyer in Edmonton with strong Excel/Power Bi skills.

The role will involve monitoring and extending expiring contracts, implementing contractor contracts, overseeing supplier implementation of reporting software and other administrative duties.

Must Haves

  • 2-5 years of experience with purchasing and contract experience within a supply chain environment
  • Strong Excel / Power Bi Skills
  • Experience facilitating execution of new & existing contracts, addendums and other documents requiring internal & external approvals
  • Experience supporting RFx process with review and issuance of RFx documents (EOI, RFI, RFx), clarifications, including post bid and BAFO
  • Bachelor’s Degree or Diploma in Business Administration, Supply Chain Management, or related field

Nice To Haves

  • Utilities industry experience
  • Experience with Oracle or other ERP systems
  • Experience with Pehta LP software
  • Experience onboarding new vendors in Oracle and Avetta

Key Responsibilities

  • Facilitate the execution of new & existing contracts, addendums and other documents requiring internal & external approvals
  • Support RFx process with review and issuance of RFx documents (EOI, RFI, RFx), clarifications, including post bid and BAFO, track and follow-up as required
  • Collaborate with and support SCM, vendors and suppliers in managing Purchase Orders and RFx processes
  • Complete monthly and quarterly reports as required
  • Maintain appropriate records related to SCM activities
  • Assist with onboarding of new vendors in Oracle and Avetta
