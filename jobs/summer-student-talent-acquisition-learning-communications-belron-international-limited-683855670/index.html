HR Summer Student – Talent Acquisition, Learning & Communications

Location: Montreal, QC
Department: People & Culture (HR)
Duration: Summer 2026 (Temporary student position)

About the Role

The HR Summer Student will support the People & Culture team with a primary focus on Talent Acquisition, Leadership & Learning, and internal communications. This role is ideal for a student interested in gaining hands-on experience in recruitment operations, learning program coordination, and HR communications within a fast-paced, collaborative environment.

Key Responsibilities

Talent Acquisition Support

  • Support candidate sourcing, resume screening, and basic shortlisting under guidance.
  • Assist with job postings, tracking recruitment activity, and maintaining accurate candidate records.
  • Provide administrative and coordination support throughout the recruitment process.

Leadership & Learning Support

  • Support the coordination of leadership development and learning activities (registrations, attendance, logistics).
  • Assist with preparation of materials, participant communications, and session follow-ups.
  • Help track learning data and support basic reporting and documentation.

Communications & Intranet Support

  • Support internal communications initiatives, including content creation for the intranet (rédaction, mise en page).
  • Assist in preparing simple communication materials such as presentations, posts, emails, or short videos.
  • Help maintain and update intranet pages related to HR programs, learning, and talent initiatives.

What We’re Looking For

  • Currently enrolled in a university or college program in Human Resources, Business Administration, Industrial Relations, Psychology, Communications, or a related field.
  • Strong organizational skills with attention to detail.
  • Comfortable working with Microsoft Office (Outlook, Excel, PowerPoint); interest in digital tools is an asset.
  • Ability to manage multiple tasks and meet deadlines.
  • Strong written and verbal communication skills; bilingual (French/English) is an asset.
  • Interest in talent acquisition, learning & development, and internal communications.

What You’ll Gain

  • Exposure to core HR functions, including recruitment, leadership development, and employee communications.
  • Hands-on experience supporting real HR programs and initiatives.
  • A solid understanding of how Talent Acquisition, Learning, and Communications intersect in a large organization.
  • Opportunities to work closely with experienced HR professionals.
