About This Role

This position is exclusively open to students pursuing a university degree.

Internship dates: May 4th to August 21st 2026

Are you looking for a work environment where you can innovate, reach greater heights and collaborate with experts from a wide range of sectors? You want to join a world-class company and industry leader with projects to which you would be proud to contribute? Come share your passion with us!

The role we are offering you:

By joining our team, you will have the opportunity to participate in all phases of the software development life cycle, while extending your knowledge in simulation and visual technologies. We are currently looking for a passionate individual to join our dynamic team in a motivating environment within the Immersive Tribe of the engineering group. Are you ready to:

- Work closely with the production team designing features to improve the overall client experience on our Service portal.

- Develop and maintain web applications used throughout the world by CAE customers and employees

- Develop in C#, JavaScript and SQL using Agile methodologies

- Troubleshoot problems by analyzing, creating, modifying, or amending existing code, design, and documentation.

Our ideal candidate has:

- Good knowledge of C#, ASP.NET, JavaScript, SQL and Web technologies (Web API) is a must

- Knowledge of nHibernate, ExtJs, CMS, Azure, WPF, knowledge of unit tests, networking (an asset)

- Practical usage of UML, OO concepts and Design Patterns

- Knowledge of Agile Methodologies

- Familiar with software development and integration, software lifecycle and configuration

- Bilingualism an asset (French and English)

- Strong planning and organizational skills

- Ability to adapt quickly to changes in technology

- Ability to multitask and work with individuals from different backgrounds

- Good teamwork and communications skills

Preferred Education

- Pursuing an undergraduate degree in Computer Science, Software Engineering, or any related field
