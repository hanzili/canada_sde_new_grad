Full Description

Junior Accountant (Mandarin-Speaking)

Location: Nisku, Alberta

Employment Type: Full-time

About the Role

We are seeking a detail-oriented Junior Accountant to join our Finance team. This role is ideal for someone who has completed an accounting certificate program, is eager to grow professionally, and can communicate effectively in both English and Mandarin. You will work closely with and provide direct support to our Finance Manager, contributing to daily accounting operations and financial reporting.

Key Responsibilities

  • Assist with accounts payable and accounts receivable processing
  • Prepare journal entries and maintain general ledger accuracy
  • Support month-end and year-end closing activities
  • Reconcile bank statements and company accounts
  • Maintain organized financial records and documentation
  • Assist the Finance Manager with financial analysis and reporting
  • Communicate with internal teams and external vendors in Mandarin and English as needed

Qualifications

  • Accounting certificate or equivalent post-secondary accounting education
  • Strong written and or verbal communication skills in Mandarin and English
  • Proficiency with Microsoft Excel and basic accounting software (Sage)
  • Strong attention to detail and organizational skills
  • Ability to work independently and as part of a team
  • Eagerness to learn and grow within the Finance department

What We Offer

  • Opportunities for professional development and career growth
  • Supportive team environment
  • Competitive compensation package
  • Full Benefit and RRSP package, after allotted time frame
