Supply Chain - Replenishment Optimization:

Ever wondered how that box of cereal appears on the shelf in the store every day? Or how we can move nearly 1 billion units of product from one of our 3,000 vendors, through one of our 27 distribution centers, to one of our 2,300 locations across Canada, and into the homes of millions of Canadians each year with the precision of a well-oiled machine?

Supply Chain at Loblaws is the engine which drives the business, and we want to show you how it all works and have you help us make it stronger, more efficient, and faster.

The Replenishment Optimization team is integral to making sure our store shelves are stocked with products that our customer needs through key job functions like responsible sourcing, demand forecasting, and inventory management to name a few. Our team lends a hand in this by building automated reporting solutions and tools that track performance against KPIs and identify ways to improve our operational efficiency.

We have a feeling a career with the Loblaw Supply Chain is the challenge you’ve been looking for. If you think so too, we’d love to hear from you.

Position Overview:

Join us for an exciting Co-op opportunity within Loblaw Supply Chain, a multifaceted area of our business where we are responsible for delivering products from our distribution centers across the country to nearly 2,500 grocery and pharmacy retail stores nationally, and ultimately the homes of millions of Canadians. The Data & Analytics Co-op will play a vital part in the team. You will collaborate with Supply Chain teams to create and deliver reports and reporting tools in support of automation. If you enjoy taking accountability of a task from start to finish—capturing business requirements, designing, developing, testing, and implementing solutions for reporting—this might be the perfect fit for you!

What you’ll do:

  • Develop and maintain supply chain metric dashboards with integrated reporting and analytical tools, leveraging modern functionality to deliver interactive, automated, and actionable insights for business stakeholders.
  • Provide analytical insight from large volumes of data to drive efficiency within the supply chain team and support the decision-making process, throughout the optimal mix of descriptive, predictive, and prescriptive analytics.
  • Capture to support the technical solutions (SOPs, job aids, flow charts, etc.), and act as a point of contact for reporting questions and issues.

What you’ll need:

  • Post-secondary education in relatable field
  • Must have strong analytical and problem-solving skills.
  • Must have strong digital literacy: Proven expertise of SQL, Python programing language, and web development skills (JavaScript and PHP).
  • Skills in MS Office (including MS Access), Python-Flask, and Git workflow would be an asset.
  • Experience working with large data sets, crafting robust reports.
