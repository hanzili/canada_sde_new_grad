Full Description

JUNIOR ACCOUNTANT

Why work at Hughes Decorr?

Hughes Decorr is an award-winning company with a proven track record anchored in quality and client service. At Hughes Decorr, we specialize in delivering marketing-at-retail solutions that bring brands to life through innovation and creativity.. Our state-of-the-art, 150,000-square-foot, ISO-certified facility is located in Concord, Ontario. With over 60 years in business, Hughes has experienced significant growth and continues to be an innovator in the industry.

We are currently looking for a Junior Accountant to join our team in a full-time, permanent, onsite position. Your positive attitude, time management, and organizational skills will be fundamental to your success in this role.

What you will be working on:

  • Assisting with monthly, quarterly, and annual closings
  • Preparing journal entries to ensure proper recording of all business transactions
  • Performing account reconciliations to ensure the accuracy and completeness of financial records
  • Tracking and managing inventory, assisting with inventory counts and reconciliations, analyzing inventory, and recommending solutions for inventory optimization
  • Handling accounts receivable tasks, including billing and collections, uploading invoices to customer portals, processing credit applications, managing orders on credit hold, and cash application
  • Posting, reconciling, and paying vendor bills, calculating rebates, and responding to vendor queries
  • Preparing and filing HST returns
  • Liaising with auditors to provide supporting documentation
  • Performing other ad hoc tasks as requested

Who we are looking for:

  • A Bachelor’s degree in accounting/finance (pursuing CPA designation)

·        Prior experience in a manufacturing industry is preferred but not required

  • Proficiency in Microsoft Office and accounting software; experience with QuickBooks/Sage X3 is an asset
  • A solid understanding of accounting principles with relevant work experience
  • Ability to multitask in a fast-paced manufacturing environment
  • Excellent communication, organizational, and time management skills
  • Detail-oriented, a team player, and a proactive initiative-taker
  • A high level of integrity and ownership of assigned tasks
  • Must be legally eligible to work in Canada (Canadian citizen, permanent resident of Canada, or possess a valid work permit) and provide a clear Criminal Background Check

What we offer:

  • Competitive pay
  • Health and dental care
  • Company events
  • Onsite parking
  • Learning opportunities and career advancement
  • A culture built on teamwork, diversity, inclusion, and growth

Salary Range:

$45,000-55,000

Work Location/Schedule:

  • Concord, ON: Must reliably commute or plan to relocate before starting work (required)
  • This is a full-time, in-office role. Standard work hours are 8:30 AM to 5:00 PM, Monday to Friday.
