About the Role

About BNP Paribas

Top Montreal employer; international banking institution

Full Description

In a changing world, unprecedented challenges require unmatched talent. Join one of Montreal’s Top Employers in 2025. We are a dynamic and growing organization having its main establishment located in downtown Montreal and part of a leading international banking institution fully committed to building a more sustainable future. Note that the position may be in the Canadian Branch of BNP Paribas or in one of its subsidiaries based in Montreal.

Internship program As an intern, you will benefit from a 4 month program that aims to give you the tools to develop yourself and discover your potential through customized trainings, networking sessions and transversal projects.

More information about the program available on our website:

BNP Paribas in Canada Students & Young graduates

The position at a glance The BNP Paribas PMO supports the FIP project manager and ensures the execution, monitoring, and governance of cross functional projects within the bank. The intern will join the PMO team in the Finance Operations Chief’s office (FCOO) and will be able to work closely with the business teams, business analysts, and external partners.

In detail The assignments you will be given will expose you to the different stages of a project, from the initial set up and creation of the project plan through to its execution. You will also have the opportunity to create presentations and deliver them to the department’s management. This internship will introduce you to various finance processes within the banking world and will teach you project management methodologies that you will apply directly on the job.

The team members are responsible for the following activities:

  • Project governance monitoring: populate dashboards (Kanbans, deliverables), prepare project reviews (steering committees, operational committees).
  • Planning & reporting: update schedules, consolidate task progress, identify gaps and propose corrective actions.
  • Risk & issue management: inventory, qualify and track risks and problems, feed the issues log and prepare mitigation plans.
  • Continuous improvement: propose PMO process enhancements, contribute to the update of tools and templates, and take part in best practice workshops.

Work conditions: This position provides for standard working conditions in an office and a normal work schedule from Monday to Friday.

*The Strengths And Skills That Will Help You Succeed Methodology & Governance:*

  • Knowledge of project management frameworks

Data Analysis:

  • Advanced proficiency in Excel

Written & Oral Communication

  • Clear drafting of meeting minutes, executive summaries and PowerPoint presentations
  • Ability to lead status review meetings and communicate with a variety of stakeholders (business, IT, compliance, management)

Time Management & Organization:

  • Proactive in raising alerts and proposing corrective actions

Interpersonal / Behavioral Skills – Soft Skills:

  • Analytical mindset and strong attention to detail
  • Ability to work in a team and manage conflicting priorities
  • Autonomy while knowing when to seek appropriate support
  • Discretion and respect for banking industry confidentiality rules

Education:

  • Student in a business school, engineering school or university, specializing in Project Management, Finance, Data Analysis or a related field
  • Academic internships or projects in which you produced dashboards, progress reports or managed a schedule are a plus

Language:

  • Bilingual in French and English*

Technical Skills:

  • Basic finance knowledge (budgeting, project costs, P&L)
  • Advanced Excel (pivot tables, VLOOKUP, macros)
  • Proficiency with PowerPoint
  • Experience with JIRA is a plus
  • Familiarity with collaborative platforms (SharePoint, Teams)
  • Given the vast majority of our clients, both internal and external, are based outside of Quebec and Canada, specific language requirements may apply. Professional working proficiency in French and English languages are required.

BNP Paribas provides excellent training and personal development programs, as well as opportunities for career development within the company and internationally.
