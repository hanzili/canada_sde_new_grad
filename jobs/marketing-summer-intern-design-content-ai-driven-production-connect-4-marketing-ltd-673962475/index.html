About Connect 4 Marketing Ltd.

Connect 4 Marketing Ltd. is a fast-moving, data-driven paid media and investor relations agency. We run digital marketing campaigns for publicly listed junior mining and resource companies across Canadian, US, German (DACH), and Quebec markets. Our work spans landing pages, native ads, content calendars, influencer campaigns, performance reporting, and full-funnel investor outreach — often on tight turnarounds.

We are looking for a sharp, AI-native intern who can keep up with the pace, ship fast, and treat tools like Claude, Canva, and InDesign as extensions of their hands.

Must be a Canadian Citizen & currently enrolled in school.

The role

This is a hands-on production internship. You will work directly with the senior team to design assets, build landing pages, edit short-form video, and help orchestrate content calendars across multiple client accounts. The role is heavily AI-assisted — we expect you to be using Claude (and similar tools) every day to accelerate design, copy, and research.

If you live in Figma tabs, prompt LLMs reflexively, follow the markets, and can turn a brief into a publish-ready asset before lunch, this role is built for you.

What you'll be doing

  • Content calendars — Drafting weekly and monthly content calendars across multiple client accounts (mining, resources, investor newsletters).
  • Graphic design — Producing static and animated graphics for ads, social, and editorial properties using Claude (design + ideation), Canva, and Adobe InDesign.
  • Landing pages — Building and editing landing pages on WordPress / Elementor and Webflow — clean, fast, on-brand, conversion-oriented.
  • Video editing — Cutting short-form video for social, ads, and CEO interview clips — quick turnarounds, polished output.
  • AI-assisted production — Using Claude and other AI tools to accelerate ideation, drafting, copy variants, and asset generation. We expect prompt fluency.
  • Operational hygiene — Keeping client folders, scripts, calendars, and approval threads tidy and up to date in Google Drive.
  • Process improvement — Spotting bottlenecks and suggesting better tooling — we like interns who improve our stack.

What we're looking for

  • Agile and self-driven — You move fast, ship often, and don't need to be micromanaged.
  • Highly tech-savvy — You instinctively reach for the right tool, learn new software in a day, and aren't intimidated by APIs, no-code platforms, or technical setup.
  • AI-native — You use Claude, ChatGPT, Midjourney, or similar tools daily and have opinions about prompts. You see AI as a force multiplier, not a novelty.
  • Interested in the stock market — You follow public markets, read tickers without flinching, and have at least a working understanding of how stocks, IPOs, and capital raises work. You don't need to be a trader — but you should be genuinely curious about what makes a stock move.
  • Design fluency — Strong working knowledge of Canva and Adobe InDesign. Bonus for Figma, Photoshop, Illustrator, After Effects.
  • Video editing — Comfortable cutting short-form video in CapCut, Premiere, DaVinci Resolve, or equivalent. Audio sync, captions, pacing, hooks.
  • Organized — You manage multiple deadlines, name files properly, and don't let things slip.
  • Sharp written communication — You can write tight, clear copy in English. Bonus for French and/or German.

Bonus points for

  • Familiarity with WordPress, Elementor, or Webflow.
  • Basic HTML/CSS — enough to fix a broken section or tweak a template.
  • Experience with Google Tag Manager, GA4, or Microsoft Clarity.
  • Personal exposure to the markets — a brokerage account, a watchlist, a Substack you actually read.
  • Prior interest in mining, commodities, or resource investing.
  • Past work running social accounts, newsletters, or content for any brand or creator.

What you'll get

  • Direct mentorship from a senior team running campaigns for publicly listed companies in multiple international markets.
  • Real ownership of deliverables that ship to real clients — no coffee runs, no busywork.
  • A front-row seat to how a modern, AI-leveraged paid media and IR agency actually operates.
  • A workflow stacked with the best tools — Claude, Canva Pro, Adobe CC, n8n, Supabase, Google Workspace.
  • Potential to convert into a full-time role for the right candidate.

How to apply

Send us a short note telling us why this role fits you, plus links to:

  • Your portfolio or any work samples (graphics, video, landing pages — even personal projects count).
  • One example of something you built or accelerated using an AI tool. Bonus if you can show the prompt.
  • Your LinkedIn or résumé.

We review applications on a rolling basis and aim to hire ASAP. If we're a fit, we move quickly.
