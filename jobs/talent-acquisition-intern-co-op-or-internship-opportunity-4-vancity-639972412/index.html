About Vancity

Top Canadian credit union, community-focused employer

Full Description

Our Story & Purpose:

Vancity is a member-owned credit union built on the principles of inclusion, social justice, and community impact. Since 1946, we’ve worked to challenge the status quo, break down barriers, and build a more equitable future. With bold commitments—including becoming net-zero by 2040 across all mortgages and loans—we are championing financial resilience, climate action, and belonging for our members and communities.

As the largest private sector Living Wage Employer in Canada and home to a team of 2,300 passionate employees, Vancity offers competitive rewards and benefits, diverse career pathways, and the opportunity to be part of something bigger.

The Opportunity:

As a Talent Acquisition Intern, you’ll support end‑to‑end recruitment activities and gain hands‑on experience in hiring strategies, candidate experience, and employer branding. You’ll work closely with recruiters and hiring managers to help attract and hire diverse talent across the organization.

A typical day would involve:

  • Supporting end‑to‑end recruitment activities, including job postings, resume screening, and interview coordination
  • Scheduling interviews and communicating with candidates throughout the hiring process
  • Assisting with candidate experience initiatives and responding to candidate inquiries
  • Maintaining recruitment trackers, dashboards, and reporting data
  • Supporting employer branding, campus recruitment, and early‑talent initiatives
  • Collaborating with recruiters and hiring managers on hiring projects and process improvements

You are:

  • Member-Centric - You define and measure success through the eyes of your customers and anticipate, understand, and respond to their evolving needs. Demonstrate strong follow-through: Consistently keep commitments to customers and team members.
  • Technology Savvy - You have education and/or experience working across a variety of technologies
  • Critical Thinker - You seek to facilitate data-driven decision making, including "rolling up your sleeves" to elicit requirements, research, analyze data
  • Curious & innovative - You always do your research and analyze it to assess and recommend a course of action considering industry best practices
  • A team player - you can engage with members of your team as well as cross-functional teams and are willing to collaborate across projects and initiatives.

You have:

  • Enrollment in an undergraduate or graduate program in Human Resources, Business, Psychology, or a related field
  • Strong written and verbal communication skills
  • Excellent organizational skills and attention to detail
  • Ability to manage multiple priorities in a fast‑paced environment
  • Interest in recruitment, talent programs, and inclusive hiring practices
  • Comfort working with Microsoft Office tools (Outlook, Excel, Word, Teams)

Bonus points, if you have:

  • Previous experience in recruitment, HR, customer service, or administrative roles
  • Exposure to applicant tracking systems or recruitment platforms
  • Experience supporting projects, events, or high‑volume coordination
  • Interest in employer branding, DEIR (Diversity, Equity, Inclusion and Reconciliation) initiatives, or early‑career talent programs
  • Strong analytical skills and comfort working with data or reports

What You’ll Earn:

This role offers a pay range of $27.85/hr.

Vancity Talent Programs:

Vancity supports an inclusive hiring process for candidates who self-identify as Indigenous, Black, or Trans. With special permission from the BC Human Rights Commissioner, this initiative provides access to career development opportunities, prioritized job screening, and feedback. Any information you choose to share will be stored securely and used only for recruitment and career development connected to this initiative, in line with the BC Personal Information Protection Act (PIPA). For details, please see our dedicated Talent Programs job posting.

Accommodations Process:

This role is an open vacancy, and our hiring process is grounded in fairness, transparency, and inclusion. We are committed to an inclusive, barrier-free and accessible recruitment experience for all candidates. If you require any accommodations or support at any stage of the recruitment process (including the application stage), we encourage you to let us know by contacting our Talent Acquisition team at recruitment@vancity.com. We’re here to work with you to ensure your needs are met promptly and effectively. All requests will be handled with the utmost respect and confidentiality, so you can participate fully in the process.
