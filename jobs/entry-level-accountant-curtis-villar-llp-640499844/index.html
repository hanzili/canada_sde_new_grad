About Curtis-Villar LLP

Full Description

Who are we?

Curtis-Villar was established over 30 years ago and has built a reputation of trust with our team and clients. We understand that the world is changing at a rapid rate and it is important to embrace change and adapt. We were early adopters of real-time accounting and have coached many of our clients through the process.

We are believers that while there are times of year where our choice of career does require overtime, we can also achieve balance in our lives. We provide our team with health spending account, personal hours, paid professional development, paid professional dues, study days, and team events/rewards.

What is the opportunity?

Curtis-Villar Chartered Professional Accountants is growing and looking for the next outstanding team member to join us! Are you someone who enjoys interacting with clients and brings their A-game each and every day? Did you recently graduate and want to be a part of a great team?

Your role on the team will include a variety of responsibilities working with a wide range of exciting clientele – the small to medium sized businesses that are driving Ontario’s economy. You will be responsible for learning deadlines, researching standards, preparing files and actively participating as a member of our team. As a key member of the team, you will be accountable for taking ownership of tasks you are assigned and researching issues to be addressed. You will be responsible to bring a positive attitude to meetings and to assist with identifying solutions that positively impact our team and workflows.

To provide the most up to date experience we are embracing cloud based applications and automation to free us up for the exciting, high-value work our clients are looking for.

Who are you?

As the ideal candidate you will have a can-do attitude and be a team player who enjoys interacting with people. You will display each of the following attributes:

  • Accounting degree or diploma
  • Team Player
  • Pride of Ownership
  • Dedication
  • Punctuality
  • Initiative
  • Attention to Detail
  • Organized
  • High Achiever
  • Positive Attitude

Effective and professional communication is your calling card. You aim to provide client service and partner with clients in achieving their goals. You are confident enough to develop client relationships and build rapport with team members, business partners and clients. You are excited about being exposed to new technology and you understand that client’s needs are rapidly changing how and what we do. You want to be at the forefront of this movement. You may have experience using a variety of programs such as (but not limited to) QuickBooks Online, Xero, Caseware, TaxPrep, Dext, Jobber, Wagepoint, Fathom, MoneyThumb, iFirm, etc. And if you don't - you are open to the challenge!

If this describes you and you are interested in working in a professional environment with other remarkable people, please submit a cover letter and resume.

Curtis-Villar LLP is committed to a fair and inclusive work environment. We will endeavor to accommodate the needs of qualified applicants in all parts of the hiring process
