Company Description

Shear Gold Exploration Corp. specializes in mineral exploration throughout the Canadian Shield, unlocking its untapped mineral resources. The company offers a broad range of early exploration services, including soil geochemical surveys, prospecting, geological mapping, drill program management as well as on-site and off-site core logging and cutting. At Shear Gold, we are committed to providing comprehensive exploration solutions driven by precision and efficiency.

Role Description

This is a full-time hybrid role for a Junior Geologist based in Thunder Bay, ON, with a combination of fieldwork and remote tasks. Responsibilities include conducting geological fieldwork, assisting with geological mapping, logging drill cores, and supporting geophysical surveys. The role also involves participating in drilling operations and assisting in the management of exploration programs. Candidates will be expected to maintain accurate records and provide reports, as well as collaborate with multidisciplinary teams to ensure the success of projects.
