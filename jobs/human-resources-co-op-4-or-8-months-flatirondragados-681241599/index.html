About the Role

Assist HR operations: onboarding, recruitment, records

About FlatironDragados

Toronto HR co-op, admin-focused, growth

Full Description

We are looking for a 4 or 8 months co-op student to support the HR team in Toronto starting May 2026.

What You Will Be Doing

  • Provide administrative support with employee records, data entry and tracking
  • Assist with updating a variety of HR-related documents
  • Collect, check and upload various documents from new hires and employees
  • Manage new hire documents and information; support new hire orientation process, including on-boarding and paperwork
  • Support the Talent Acquisition team with various tasks as required: posting jobs, tracking, doing research, scheduling interviews
  • Assist the Training and Development team with various tasks
  • Assist with the new hire background check process
  • Assist with the Co-op program
  • Participate in, oversee and assist with various projects
  • Perform other tasks as required

What We Are Looking For

  • Pursuing a bachelor’s degree in Human Resources, Business Administration or Psychology
  • Previous work experience in office, retail or other is required
  • Experience in an office environment will be considered a definite asset
  • Proficient in Microsoft Office Suite (Word, Excel, PowerPoint and Outlook)
  • Experience with various graphics software will be considered an asset
  • Excellent verbal and written communication skills
  • Fluency in French will be considered an asset
  • Strong attention to detail
  • Ability to maintain composure in a fast-paced environment
  • Excellent organizational skills with the ability to prioritize work
  • Team player who demonstrates enthusiasm, positive outlook and professional demeanor, and takes initiative
  • Ability to work in a team environment as well as independently

Why work for us Some of the benefits you may be eligible for as an employee are:

  • Comprehensive compensation package and paid time off program
  • Industry leading 401(k)/RRSP
  • Medical/Extended Health Care, Dental, Vision and/or Provincial Medical
  • Wellness benefits & Employee Assistance Program
  • Tuition Reimbursement Program

We are an EEO/ADA/Veterans employer.
