Full Description

*Entry-Level Telus Customer Acquisition Representative TELUS Communications | Partnered with The Acquisition Group Start your career in an environment where training, support, and growth are built into the role from day one. Whether you're a recent college graduate, someone exploring a new career path, or a professional with work experience* , this opportunity is designed to help you build valuable skills and is designed to help you build confidence, skills, and consistent income, even without prior sales experience.

The *Acquisition Group , in partnership with TELUS Communications , is growing people-focused teams across Canada. Unlike many sales roles that require previous experience, we focus on developing talent* through structured training, hands-on coaching, and daily team support.

*What You’ll Be Doing*

  • Represent TELUS Communications through The Acquisition Group
  • Engage with customers through direct, door to door community outreach
  • Identify customer needs and recommend TELUS home and connectivity solutions
  • Build trust through professional, respectful conversations
  • Work toward clearly defined daily and weekly performance goals
  • Receive ongoing feedback and coaching to continuously improve

*Why Candidates Choose This Role*

  • Uncapped commission-based earnings, tied directly to performance
  • Structured training from day one — no sales experience required
  • Clear expectations and measurable goals (you’ll always know where you stand)
  • Daily team support and leadership guidance (you’re never left guessing)
  • A performance-driven environment that values effort, consistency, and growth
  • Opportunities to advance into leadership and higher-earning roles over time

*Who We’re Looking For*

  • Friendly, dependable individuals who enjoy working with people
  • Strong communication skills (we’ll help you sharpen them)
  • Comfortable in a face-to-face, customer-focused environment
  • Legally eligible to work in Canada
  • Available full-time, Monday to Friday
  • No previous sales experience required. We hire for attitude and potential and provide the training needed for success.*
