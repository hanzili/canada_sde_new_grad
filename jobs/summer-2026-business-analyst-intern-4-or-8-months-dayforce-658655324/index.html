[SUMMER 2026] Business Analyst Intern 4 or 8 Months

at Dayforce

📍 Location None Remote
📋 Details Internship · Summer 2026 – 4 or 8 months · Summer 2026
📅 Posted Apr 07, 2026

About the Role

Support public sector planning and cross-functional programs

About Dayforce

Global HCM leader with scalable public sector focus

Full Description

Dayforce, a global leader in Human Capital Management (HCM) with headquarters in Toronto, Ontario, and Minneapolis, Minnesota, operates across North America, EMEA, and APJ regions. Our Cloud HCM platform, recognized for its unified database and continuous calculation engine, enhances efficiency, productivity, and compliance for global workforces. We are committed to "Making Work Life Better™" for employees, customers, and partners worldwide. As an Intern at Dayforce, you’ll gain real-world experience with meaningful responsibilities, access to development programs, and the chance to collaborate globally, bringing new ideas and optimizing processes across multiple business units. Job Title: Business Analyst Intern

Location : Virtual

Duration: Summer 2026 – 4 or 8 months (May 2026 – August 2026) OR (May 2026 - December 2026)

Availability : *Full-time availability of 37.5 – 40 hours weekly is required to be eligible for this opportunity. Benefits For Students

  • Experience working for one of the fastest growing Human Capital Management technology companies in the world Access to Dayforce’s development programs and resources
  • Ability to work with as well as create relationships with members across the globe
  • Autonomy to bring forth new ideas and optimize existing structures within the organization
  • Meaningful responsibilities, enabling you to obtain ‘real-world’ experience
  • Dynamic hands-on exposure to multiple business-units and stakeholders across Dayforce

About The Opportunity The public sector is a growth area for Dayforce with significant momentum. In the few short years, the public sector vertical has successfully delivered key new logos including the Government of Canada, State of California, City of Columbus, Milwaukee County and the City of Pittsburgh. These sales wins combined with the beginning of material internal product investments requires the public sector team to develop a detailed business plan and governance model which is scalable and will provide the business with a detailed, multiyear roadmap. This individual will be a critical contributor to the development of this business plan. In addition to this business plan, this individual will be a critical component to other internal and cross functional projects related to the public sector.

What You’ll Get To Do

  • Accelerate Public Sector Business Planning Globally: You will play a pivotal role in propelling the growth and strategic direction of our public sector business globally. This involves devising innovative strategies, identifying market opportunities, and collaborating with regional teams to execute plans effectively.
  • Support Cross-Functional Work Efforts: Foster collaboration and synergy across various teams within the organization. This entails aligning objectives, facilitating communication, and ensuring seamless coordination to achieve shared goals, particularly within the context of public sector initiatives.
  • Develop Scalable Training Material for Selling into the Public Sector: Supporting the team by contributing your expertise to the creation of comprehensive and scalable training materials tailored specifically for selling into the public sector. This entails collaborating with experts to simplify complex concepts, contribute to designing engaging training modules, and continuously refining resources to meet market demands.
  • Manage Public Sector User Group and PS Product Council: You will take ownership of nurturing and expanding our public sector user community, fostering engagement, and gathering valuable insights to inform product development initiatives. Additionally, you will oversee the Public Sector Product Council, driving collaboration between key stakeholders to shape product strategy and roadmap priorities.

Skills And Experience We Value

  • Currently enrolled in or recently completed a Master of Business Administration (MBA) program (or an equivalent qualification), demonstrating a strong foundation in business acumen, strategic thinking, and leadership
  • Strong analytical skills/insights to drive actionable outcomes in real-world business scenarios. Your ability to interpret data, identify trends, and derive actionable insights will be critical in shaping our strategic direction.

What’s In It For You Dayforce is fueled by the diversity of our talented employees. We are an equal opportunity employer and consider and embrace ALL individuals and what makes them unique. We believe our employees should be happy and healthy, with peace of mind and a sense of fulfillment.

We encourage individuals to apply based on their passions.

Dayforce encourages personal and professional growth. We offer excellent time away from work programs, comprehensive wellness initiatives and recognition through competitive pay and benefits.

With a commitment to community impact, including volunteer days and our charity, Dayforce Cares we provide opportunities for you to thrive both in your career and personal life. Our focus is not just on your job but on supporting you to be the best version of yourself.

Fraudulent Recruiting

Beware of fraudulent recruiting. Legitimate Dayforce contacts will use an @dayforce.com email address. We do not request money, checks, equipment orders, or sensitive personal data during the recruitment process. If you have been asked for any of the above, or believe you have been contacted by someone posing as a Dayforce employee, please refer to our fraudulent recruiting statement found here: https://www.dayforce.com/be-aware-of-recruiting-fraud

Dayforce actively monitors all job applications to ensure authenticity. Submissions determined to be fraudulent or misleading will be declined from the recruitment process.
