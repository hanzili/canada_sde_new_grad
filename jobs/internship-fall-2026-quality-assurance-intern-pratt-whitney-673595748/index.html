About Pratt & Whitney Canada Pratt & Whitney Canada (P&WC) is a global leader in aerospace innovation, proudly headquartered in Longueuil, Quebec, since 1928. Our engines support missions that truly matter, from transporting people and essential goods, to enabling emergency medical services, humanitarian operations, and wildfire suppression around the world.

Internship dates : August 31st to December 18th, 2026

Location: The position is based at our Lethbridge location; internship from Monday to Friday, with flexible hours and exclusively on-site.

Check out our YouTube channel: https://www.youtube.com/@PrattWhitneyCa

Our website: https://www.pwc.ca/en

Why join our team? Working in conjunction with the Quality Manager, Quality, Production, and cross-functional stakeholders, the Co-op Quality / Project Intern will report to the Manager of Quality & Project Management for day-to-day direction.

This position offers exposure to project coordination, quality processes, and operational support within a fast-paced aerospace manufacturing environment. The successful candidate will support project execution by coordinating activities, maintaining project documentation, and assisting with quality and continuous improvement initiatives.

The role provides hands-on experience across multiple functional areas, contributing to operational efficiency, project delivery, and quality outcomes.

What will your day-to-day look like?

  • Perform inspections, rework, and reporting of non-conforming products, participating in the escape management process.
  • Support project execution by tracking progress, maintaining schedules, and preparing status updates for stakeholders
  • Coordinate project activities, ensuring resources, materials, and information are available to meet project timelines
  • Assist in the development, maintenance, and control of project documentation (project plans, schedules, budgets, and scope)
  • Facilitate communication between project teams, stakeholders, and functional groups
  • Participate in risk identification and support implementation of mitigation strategies
  • Support quality initiatives, including data collection, reporting, and continuous improvement activities
  • Assist with analysis of project and operational data to identify trends, gaps, and opportunities
  • Contribute to process improvements aligned with CORE and operational excellence objectives
  • Provide administrative and coordination support to project managers and functional teams as required
  • Collaborate with cross-functional teams including Quality, Production, Engineering, and Supply Chain

What do you need to be successful?

  • Be eligible to work in Canada.
  • Be enrolled in a Canadian university throughout your internship.
  • Be able to work on-site in the province where you are hired if applicable.

  • Pursuing studies in Industrial Engineering, Business/Commerce, Project Management, Computer Science, or a related field.

  • Proficiency in MS Office (Excel, PowerPoint, Outlook) and data analysis tools.

  • Familiarity with project management tools or ERP systems (e.g., SAP).
  • Understanding of basic quality principles and process improvement concepts.
  • Strong interpersonal skills with the ability to collaborate across functions.
  • Demonstrated initiative and ability to work independently with guidance.
  • Interest in aerospace manufacturing and operational excellence.
  • Demonstrated ability to work in a team-oriented, multi-disciplinary environment.
  • Strong organizational and coordination skills with attention to detail.
  • Analytical thinking and problem-solving capability.
  • Effective communication skills, both written and verbal.
  • Ability to manage multiple priorities in a fast-paced environment.
  • Adaptability and willingness to learn in a dynamic operational setting.

Assets :

  • Experience with ISO audit or related programs
  • Experience or coursework in project management, quality systems, or continuous improvement methodologies
