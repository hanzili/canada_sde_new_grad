About the Role

Conduct in-depth equity research and present buy/sell notes

About Fidelity Canada

Major investment manager with strong research presence

Full Description

Job Description Please note You will be working on a Hybrid office schedule as part of Fidelity’s dynamic working arrangement. Current work authorization for Canada is required for all openings. Fidelity is a world leader in investment management. Our purpose is to help build a better future for investors. Committed to delivering the best possible experience for our clients, we offer a wide range of innovative investment products and services. Consider a career with us in and help Canadians reach their financial and life goals.

Reporting to the Director of Research and Portfolio Manager, the Equity Research Intern will

  • Be assigned a discrete industry sector and group of stocks (approximately 3-8) to analyze.
  • Conduct fundamental and in-depth research to make, buy and sell recommendations on stocks.
  • Have full and sole responsibility for those stocks and access to all resources available to a full-time Analyst.
  • Access to Equity Analysts covering related sectors and assigned a Portfolio Manager mentor.
  • Have regular communication with a Director in Equities. Training resources are available as required.
  • Identify key drivers and generate high-conviction conclusions on the outcome of each driver and resulting impact on stock performance.
  • Clearly articulate the analysis in written notes and slides, presenting final conclusions to our Equity Analysts and Portfolio Managers.

What We’re Looking For

  • An avid interest and intellectual curiosity about the market, demonstrated through academic career, prior work experience and/or personal investing
  • A high degree of motivation, initiative, and a tireless enthusiasm for discovering new investment opportunities
  • Excellent analytical, communication and interpersonal skills
  • Strong work ethic and desire to make an impact
  • Able to think logically, critically & quickly
  • Team player with a positive attitude

Please include the following in your application

  • Cover Letter
  • Current Resume (1 page is preferred), including your overall GPA for each school you attended
  • Transcripts (Unofficial)
