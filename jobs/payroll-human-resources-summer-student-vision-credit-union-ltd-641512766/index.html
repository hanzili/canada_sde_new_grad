Full Description

About the Role

We are seeking a detail-oriented and motivated Summer Student to support our Payroll and Human Resources team. This role is ideal for a post-secondary student interested in Human Resources, Business Administration, or a related field who wants hands-on experience in a professional office environment.

The successful candidate will assist with the organization, verification, and digitization of employee records, as well as support HR and payroll workflows and administrative processes.

This is a term position (May-August, flexible)

For complete job posting details, please visit our website www.visioncu.ca/en/about-vision/careers

Key Responsibilities

· Scan, upload, and index personnel file documentation into electronic systems

· Verify accuracy and completeness of employee records and payroll/HR data

· Assist with maintaining confidential personnel files in accordance with privacy requirements

· Support HR and payroll workflows, including document tracking and file management

· Assist with data clean-up, audits, and administrative projects as required

· Perform general administrative duties to support the HR team

Qualifications & Skills

· Currently enrolled in a post-secondary program (HR, Business, or related field preferred)

· Strong attention to detail and accuracy

· Ability to handle confidential information with discretion and professionalism

· Comfortable working with computers, scanners, and document management systems

· Strong organizational and time management skills

· Ability to work independently and as part of a team

· Proficiency with Microsoft Office (Outlook, Word, Excel) is an asset

· No previous HR experience is required — we’re looking for a student who is eager to learn.

What You’ll Gain

· Practical experience in Payroll and Human Resources operations

· Exposure to HR records management, compliance, and workflows

· Development of professional office and administrative skills

· Mentorship from experienced HR professionals

Compensation:

· Salary range $21.00 to $26.00/hourly.

· Comprehensive benefits package.
