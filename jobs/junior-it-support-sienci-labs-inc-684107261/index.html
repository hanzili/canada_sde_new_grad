About Sienci Labs Inc.

Full Description

About Sienci Labs

Sienci Labs is a fast-growing manufacturing technology company based in Waterloo, Ontario. We design CNC hardware and software that make advanced manufacturing accessible to small businesses, makers, and product developers worldwide. Our CNC routers help bridge the gap between ideas and production by simplifying and automating subtractive manufacturing, and we’ve delivered over 10,000 machines to customers in more than 70 countries.

Learn more at www.sienci.com

What You'll Do:

  • Troubleshoot basic hardware and software issues.
  • Install and replace computer components such as SSDs and RAM.
  • Configure and troubleshoot printers, monitors, USB devices, and network equipment.
  • Assist with basic networking and device connectivity.
  • Organize and track technical inventory, equipment, and office supplies.
  • Prepare and set up workstations, computers, and devices.
  • Support internal teams with day-to-day technical and operational needs.
  • Help maintain organized workspaces and technical areas.
  • Assist with various office and operational tasks as needed.
  • Handle occasional support tickets related to hardware or software troubleshooting.
  • Document setups, inventory, and technical information when required.

What We're Looking For:

  • Enjoys hands-on technical and operational work.
  • Basic to intermediate hardware and software troubleshooting skills.
  • Familiarity with Windows and Mac computers and common office hardware.
  • Basic networking knowledge.
  • Reliable, organized, and detail-oriented.
  • Comfortable helping in different areas when needed.
  • Positive attitude and willingness to learn.
  • Good communication and teamwork skills.
  • Interested in growing skills over time and contributing ideas or improvements.
  • Able to manage multiple tasks in a fast-paced environment.

Nice to Have:

  • Experience with Google Workspace.
  • Experience with inventory tracking or documentation.
  • Experience setting up printers or shared devices.
  • Personal IT projects, PC building, or hobbyist technical experience.

To apply, please submit a cover letter and resume. Applications without these items will not be considered.

Pay: $45,000.00-$54,000.00 per year

  • Casual dress
  • Company events
  • Dental care
  • Discounted or free food
  • Extended health care
  • On-site parking
  • Vision care

Work Location: In person
