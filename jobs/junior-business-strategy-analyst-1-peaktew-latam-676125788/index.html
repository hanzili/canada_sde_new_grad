About the Role

Analyze markets, synthesize insights, support community growth

About PeakTew LATAM

Global startup; strong mentorship and learning focus

Full Description

PeakTew is a social media app aiming to connect more people with the real world. Our team consists of passionate individuals from over a dozen different countries. This offer is aimed at those willing to take part in a startup project during their free time to learn more about the professional world and gain international work experience. This implies being ready to commit some time every week to the project.

As a Business Analyst , you will be responsible for creating and growing Peaktew’s community.

Your missions:

  • Develop and analyze business plans, market frameworks, and strategic recommendations.
  • Perform micro-level analysis (e.g., SWOT) and macro-level analysis (e.g., PESTEL).
  • Prepare market reports, summarizing key trends, competitor performance, and industry evolution.
  • Understand and anticipate market trends to guide business strategies.
  • Conduct benchmarking to assess competitive advantages and positioning.
  • Conduct field research, including interviews, questionnaires, and focus groups.
  • Attend the weekly team meetings to debrief your work.

This is an online job, meaning that you would be working remotely . You will be required to meet deadlines and produce qualitative work (reflecting the best you can deliver). Yet, we remain available to advise and help you anytime you need. This job will also be unpaid as PeakTew is a student-led project that isn’t (yet) generating revenue.

This opportunity will enable you to:

  • Gain international work experience (valuable on your resume).
  • Expand your global network, primarily in Europe, North America, and Asia-Pacific.
  • Receive high-quality mentorship (hard/soft skills & languages).
  • Obtain the opportunity to become a permanent employee in a future Unicorn Company.

Competencies required:

  • Convenient English level (PeakTew operates in English).
  • Be a business student or of a similar academic background, with the required skills.
  • Strong analytical skills and strategic thinking.
  • Ability to interpret data and create actionable insights.
  • Autonomous mindset (ability to have excellent communication and teamwork).
  • Quick learner & Outside-of-the-box thinker.

At PeakTew, we value anyone with true motivation and work consistency. This implies that you do not have to master your skills or have performed several work experiences before. Everyone is here to learn!

If you are passionate about the startup environment and ready to challenge yourself with an ambitious and international project, join our dynamic and growing team on this fabulous adventure!

Applicants with inquiries or seeking a faster process should send an email to careers@peaktew.com
