This is not a traditional copywriting role.

Your core responsibility will be refining, editing, proofreading, and humanizing AI-generated content . You will use tools like ChatGPT, Claude, and custom GPT workflows to generate structured drafts, then elevate them into polished, credible, natural-sounding content aligned with each client‚Äôs voice and industry.

This is a junior position with structured mentorship, particularly in SEO and agency systems.
