**About OmaScan**

OmaScan is a health tech startup helping seniors and those with accessibility challenges live safer, more independent lives at home by building the digital infrastructure for home accessibility assessments.

We use smartphone LiDAR scans and a web platform to help occupational therapists capture, measure, annotate, and communicate home modifications faster and more clearly, reducing delays, errors, and downstream healthcare costs.
