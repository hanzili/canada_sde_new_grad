About SRK Consulting

Full Description

Junior to Intermediate Water Resources/Environmental Engineer - Vancouver, BC, SRK Consulting is an independent, international consulting practice, providing globally integrated consulting services to the international mining and metals sector.

We are seeking a Junior to Intermediate Water Resources/Environmental Engineer.

Description Of Responsibilities We are seeking a Junior to Intermediate Water Resources (Civil) / Environmental Engineer to work with our Surface Water team focused on mine water management based in Vancouver. Our ideal candidate is eligible for registration with EGBC as an EIT or P.Eng.

Our Team Our surface water team interacts regularly with our other technical groups (geotechnical engineering hydrogeology, geochemistry) as part of our Canada Geoenvironmental group, creating substantial opportunity for cross-discipline learning. Our team is dedicated to providing sustainable water solutions for our clients in BC, North America and globally. Our work supports mining projects from a wide range of commodities and mining approaches to responsibly manage water through the full mining cycle, including planning, operations, closure, and reclamation of abandoned sites.

The responsibilities of this position will vary depending on applicant interest and qualifications, but will generally include:

  • Analysis and design of hydraulic structures, including but not limited to channels and ponds;
  • Developing water management plans, from conceptual design through to detailed engineering;
  • Water management and civil construction field work locally and internationally;
  • Hydrological studies, including climate change, in a variety of climatic regimes to inform site water management;
  • Development of water and load balance models to inform site water management decisions;
  • Technical reporting and presentations; and
  • Project coordination and project management support.

Essential Requirements

  • A bachelor’s degree in environmental, civil, mining, water resource, or chemical engineering.
  • 2-5 years of relevant consulting experience, ideally in the mining industry.
  • Eligibility for registration with EGBC as an EIT or P.Eng.
  • Strong analytical and computer skills in Excel are required, with additional specialized design/analysis software being an asset (e.g., HEC‑RAS, HEC-HMS, PCSWMM, ArcGIS, Global Mapper, Advanced Excel, R, Python, Leapfrog, AutoCAD, Civil 3D, Goldsim, etc.).
  • Suited to a consulting environment – no days will be the same or predictable.
  • Ability to work on multiple projects and prioritize effectively.
  • Self-motivated and directed, with initiative and ownership mentality.
  • Effective communication skills, both written and verbal.
  • Strong problem-solving skills.
  • Class 5 Driver’s Licence, or equivalent.

The estimated base pay range for this position is $80,000 to $95,000. The salary offered will be commensurate with relevant skills, knowledge, and experience.

In return for considering a future with us, we offer:

  • An attractive compensation package.
  • Diversity of projects and clients within a non-hierarchical consultancy environment.
  • Opportunity to purchase shares in the company; the SRK Group is an independent group, owned by its employees.

Applicants should respond by submitting their CV online by clicking the button below. The candidates under consideration will be contacted directly, no phone calls please.
