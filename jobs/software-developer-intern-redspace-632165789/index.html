About the Role

Build real features; learn full-stack development.

About REDspace

Bedford-based software agency; client-focused delivery.

Full Description

Spring 2026 Internships (5 week term)

At REDspace, we don’t just build websites; we solve business problems for our clients. We are looking for a Software Developer Intern to join our team. This isn't a "coffee-runner" internship. You will be deep in the code, learning how professional software engineering works in the real world. We are looking for some upcoming graduating students who are interested in 5-week internship opportunities that could lead to a permanent position with our team!

What You’ll Do

  • Ship Real Features: Use modern languages and tools to convert designs into interactive, data-driven sites.
  • Full Lifecycle Exposure: Participate in the development process for medium and large projects from inception to deployment.
  • Optimize & Debug: Troubleshoot applications and optimize performance to ensure client sites are fast and reliable.
  • Own Your Tasks: Analyze assigned work, provide estimates, and manage your time to keep projects on schedule.

Requirements

An Ideal Candidate

If this sounds like you, you should definitely be submitting your resume.

  • Problem Solver: You love a challenge and prefer practical solutions that work over theoretical perfection.
  • Reliable Team Player: You are trustworthy and support those around you. We believe coding is a social activity.
  • Clear Communicator: You can express your thoughts clearly, both in person and in writing (documentation matters!).
  • Adaptable Learner: You have a "hungry to learn" mindset and are comfortable switching between different technologies.

What You (Ideally) Know

  • Code Literacy: Ability to read existing code bases and understand how to reuse or extend it.
  • Technical Foundation: An understanding of Object-Oriented Programming (OOP) in both front and backend contexts.
  • Modern Web Interests: Familiarity with or curiosity about cloud architectures like AWS, Azure, or GCP.
  • Standard-Driven: An appreciation for coding standards and basic software architecture.

Benefits

At REDspace, our team’s diversity makes us stronger.

Different backgrounds, talents, skills, experiences, and perspectives strengthen our business and the communities in which we work and live. We are committed to growing and cultivating an inclusive environment that enables knowledge sharing and collaboration—empowering our employees to be their best.

We hire based on merit and skill and make all employment-related and hiring decisions regardless of race, colour, religion, sex, sexual orientation, gender identity, national or indigenous origin, disability, age, citizenship, marital status, or Veteran status.

Where to find us!

168-1595 Bedford Hwy

Bedford NS, Canada B4A 3Y4

This will be a 5-week paid internship position, based out of our Bedford, Nova Scotia office. We will be receiving applications until March 5th, 2026.
