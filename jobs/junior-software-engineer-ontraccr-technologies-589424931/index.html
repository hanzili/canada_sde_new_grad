Full Description

**Join the Exciting World of Startups and Technology!**

Are you ready to dive into the entrepreneurial journey? Do you dream of playing a key role in building a company and shaping a cutting-edge technology platform right from the ground up? Are you passionate about working with a dynamic team of creative minds, all committed to making a mark in the tech industry? Do you thrive on rapid development and crave real-world user feedback? If your answer is a resounding "YES!", then your next adventure awaits at Ontraccr!

**Unleash Your Potential with Ontraccr:**

We are on the lookout for an enthusiastic Junior Software Engineer to join our early-stage construction technology startup, Ontraccr. We're not just seeking a coder; we're searching for a future leader who shares our passion, values, and zeal to revolutionize the construction industry. Get ready to unleash your creativity, work closely with the founding team and CTO, and be part of an exhilarating journey that celebrates innovation, learning, and fun!

**Your Role and Impact:**

As a Junior Software Engineer, you will be at the forefront of our mission to elevate construction businesses to new heights. Collaborating with the UX team, you will bring mock designs to life, turning them into real products. Your partnership with the customer-facing team will translate business requirements into feature-rich functionalities. From architecture to release, you will own the delivery of full-stack features, making a tangible impact on both our product and processes.

**What We're Looking For:**

- 1-2 years Web Development (React + Node.js): Your proficiency in web development will be the cornerstone of your role. - Relational Database (MySQL, Postgres, etc): Strong database skills are essential for building robust and efficient solutions.

**Nice to Have:**

- Hybrid Mobile Development (React Native): Experience in mobile development will be a plus. - AWS: Familiarity with AWS adds an extra edge to your candidacy.

**Why Join Ontraccr?**

Ontraccr was born out of frustration with outdated processes in the construction industry. We're driven by the belief that technology can revolutionize work efficiency. Our team consists of seasoned leaders with proven startup success and deep construction industry expertise. Creativity flourishes in our culture, and we believe the best ideas can come from anyone. Be part of a mission to reshape the construction industry and contribute to a brighter future.

**The Perks:**

- Flexible Work Environment: Embrace the freedom of hybrid work while being based in the vibrant city of Vancouver. - Be a Change-maker: Join a SaaS platform that's transforming construction companies' operational workflows. - Growth & Impact**:** Work on key product features that shape how construction companies operate, while gaining hands-on experience and learning directly from the founding team.
