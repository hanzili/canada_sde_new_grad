About the Role

Transport samples; perform lab tests and QC

About Bureau Veritas

Global testing firm; strong QA culture

Full Description

  • Basic Purpose: What is the overall function of the job?

Jr. Sample Transporter will be responsible for transporting samples between Bellingham, Washington and Burnaby BC.

Oil and Petroleum Laboratory Technicians perform laboratory tests to determine chemical and physical characteristics or composition of solid, liquid, or gaseous materials for such purposes as quality control, process control, or product

development by performing the following duties.

Key Accountability/Responsibilities: What specifically is this job expected to accomplish?

  • Tests samples of manufactured products to verify conformity to specifications
  • Records test results on standardized forms and writes test reports describing procedures used; prepares graphs and charts.
  • Cleans and sterilizes laboratory equipment; calibrates laboratory instruments.
  • Prepares chemical solutions according to standard formulas.
  • Adds chemicals or raw materials to process solutions or products batches to correct or establish formulation required to meet specifications.
  • Review laboratory analysis, generates Certificate of Analysis, and is authorized to release and approve data when task is assigned by Lab Manager.
  • Prioritizes daily workload to accommodate daily rushes and specialty analysis & TAT
  • Follows Standard Operating Procedures (with emphasis on quality control)
  • Knowledge of and adherence to QA regulations and ensures a high level of accuracy in sample prep and analysis
  • Distinguish and communicate SOP non conformances to management
  • Develops SOP’s and general work instructions
  • Supports and complies with BV policies, Quality System and Partners in Safety program.
  • Understands analysis procedures and problem solves minor issues
  • Demonstrates proficiency and knowledge of all procedures within scope
  • Ensues maintenance and assessment of instruments
  • Validates data output
  • Participates in lab and safety meetings, as required
  • Maintains knowledge of relevant provincial legislation
  • Promotes a positive work environment for employees
  • Acts as a positive change agent in supporting BV’s new initiatives (operational and cultural)
  • All other responsibilities as assigned by Supervisor / Manage

Skills and Knowledge: What skills and knowledge are used to carry out job responsibilities and solve problems?

  • BSc. In Chemistry/technical diploma or three-year College diploma.
  • Dynamic, energetic, motivated, positive outlook, with a "can-do" attitude to be able to work in a fast paced environment
  • Good time-management, communication, multi-tasking and priority setting skills
  • Strong sense of urgency & the ability to adapt to a changing environment
  • Ability to work independently and as part of a team.
  • Strong attention to detail & record keeping skills
  • Ability to accurately follow Standard operating Procedures (SOP’s) and instructions
  • Ability to learn quickly

Work Relationships

  • Internal: Laboratory department, Inspectors, supervisors and managers.
  • External: Client facilities

Working Conditions: physical work environment, physical and mental effort, visual/listening attention and travel

  • A company vehicle will be provided to drive between Bellingham, Washington and Burnaby, BC. (approx. 20-30 hours each week will be spent transporting samples. The rest of the working hours will be working in the lab.

  • Must have a valid driver’s license, passport and be able to pass a background criminal record check.

  • Technicians are expected to perform repetitive somewhat physical work using a variety of machines and equipment as required and according to deadlines established by more experienced analysts &/ their supervisor.

  • The job requires flexible working hours, overtime and shift work.
  • May involve long periods of standing
  • Hazardous materials are handled using established safety procedures and appropriate equipment.
  • Fast paced environment
