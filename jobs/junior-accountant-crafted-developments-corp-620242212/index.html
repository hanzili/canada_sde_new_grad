About Crafted Developments Corp.

Full Description

ABOUT THE COMPANY

Crafted Developments Corp., and its group of companies, is a fast-growing, dynamic land development firm based in Kelowna, BC, with several thriving subsidiary companies. With strong momentum over the past year, we’re expanding our footprint across provinces and into new business opportunities.

Our work culture is fast-paced, collaborative, and focused on long-term growth. We’re a team of diverse, talented professionals united by a common vision to build smart, sustainable communities through our integrated development, manufacturing, and sales operations.

ABOUT THE ROLE

  • Accurate processing and coding of accounts payable invoices into the ERP/accounting system.
  • Retrieval, review, and coding of invoices from purchasing and P.O. systems.
  • Preparation and entry of accounts receivable invoices and monitoring of payments.
  • Bookkeeping and general ledger support, including journal entries and ledger maintenance.
  • Bank and credit card reconciliations, as well as other month-end accounting duties.
  • Identifying discrepancies, investigating variances, and resolving accounting and system-related issues.
  • Liaising with vendors, suppliers, and internal staff regarding invoices, payments, and financial inquiries.
  • Payroll support.
  • Administration support related to employee benefits.
  • Support purchasing and administration departments as required.
  • Undertake accounting, administrative, and process-improvement projects assigned by management.
  • Proactively identify opportunities to improve efficiency and streamline accounting processes.

EDUCATION

  • Completion of a post-secondary diploma in Advanced Accounting, Accounting, or a closely related field from a recognized Canadian institution.

EXPERIENCE

  • Minimum of 5 years of recent, hands-on experience in an accounting assistant role.
  • Demonstrated experience supporting the full accounting cycle, including accounts payable (AP), accounts receivable (AR), reconciliations, and journal entries.
  • Experience liaising with vendors and internal stakeholders regarding financial and operational matters.
  • Experience in a construction or project-based environment is considered a strong asset.

QUALIFICATIONS & SKILLS

  • Experience using ERP, preferably Explorer Software Eclipse, accounting systems to manage invoices, payments, and financial records.
  • Strong proficiency in Microsoft Excel and other computer software tools.
  • Ability to work independently with minimal supervision.
  • Proven ability to maintain strict confidentiality when handling sensitive financial information.
  • Strong problem-solving skills, including the ability to identify discrepancies and resolve issues efficiently.
  • High level of accuracy, attention to detail, and organizational skills.
  • Adaptability and willingness to learn new tools, systems, and tasks in a fast-paced environment.
  • Strong proficiency in English, both written and spoken.

ADDITIONAL INFORMATION

  • Closing date for job is February 20, 2026; only candidates selected for interviews will be contacted.
  • Must be able to reliably commute to the Kelowna office.
  • Applicants must be legally eligible to work in Canada. The employer does not provide immigration sponsorship.
