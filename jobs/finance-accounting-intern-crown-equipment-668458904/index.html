About Crown Equipment

Full Description

We are currently hiring a Finance Accounting Intern to join our rapidly growing team!

Crown Lift Trucks is the premier material handling dealer in Canada. We are a leader in the industry and are considered a one-stop shop for all material handling needs. Crown Lift Trucks Canada operates eight full-service branches in both Ontario and Quebec offering complete service solutions including maintenance programs, parts supply, and training.

POSITION SUMMARY

This is a temporary internship opportunity (Summer 2026) for a current student or recent graduate interested in gaining practical, hands-on experience in corporate finance and accounting. The Finance Intern will support the Finance team with data entry, reconciliations, reporting, and accounts payable/receivable activities, while gaining exposure to financial systems, reporting cycles, and professional business practices.

This role is ideal for a detail-oriented student who is eager to learn, comfortable working with numbers, and interested in developing a foundational understanding of finance and accounting within a dynamic business environment.

POSITION DESCRIPTION

  • Assist with data entry and maintenance of financial records to ensure accuracy and completeness
  • Support accounts payable and accounts receivable processes, including invoice review and basic reconciliations
  • Assist with account reconciliations and transaction-level reviews
  • Support month-end and year-end activities, including preparation of working papers and schedules
  • Assist with preparing financial reports and internal summaries
  • Perform basic data analysis to support reporting accuracy and identify variances or discrepancies
  • Support internal controls and audit-related activities as required
  • Work collaboratively with Finance team members and other departments to support timely information flow
  • Gain exposure to financial systems, reporting processes, and standard accounting practices
  • Perform other related duties and special projects as assigned

KNOW HOW & EXPERIENCE

  • Currently enrolled in or recently graduated from a post-secondary program in Accounting, Finance, Business Administration, or a related field
  • Strong attention to detail with an interest in finance and accounting principles
  • Proficient in Microsoft Office, particularly Excel
  • Strong organizational and time management skills
  • Ability to handle confidential information with professionalism and discretion
  • Willingness to learn, take initiative, and work both independently and as part of a team
  • Strong written and verbal communication skills

At Crown Lift Trucks, we believe in fairness and transparency. While most of our hiring decisions are made by people, we may use technology, including AI-based tools, to assist with parts of the process such as screening or assessments. If this happens, it is always supported by human review.

We welcome applicants with diverse backgrounds and do not require Canadian work experience for this role.
