About the Role

Develop dashboards, ingest data, build models

About Trust Science Inc.

Fintech startup focused on AI credit scoring

Full Description

Location: Hybrid, Edmonton, AB

Team: Data & Analytics

Employment Type: Temporary Full-Time Internship

Term: 4 months (May–August 2026), with potential to extend

Start Date: May 2026

What you’ll do

We’re recruiting for three Summer Student Interns for our Data & Analytics team!

You’ll support active projects through research, analysis, and execution including dashboards, data ingestion/reconciliation, transaction classification, and risk/fraud modeling support. Expect a mix of independent work and close collaboration with the team.

Responsibilities:

You’ll be supported by a Leader and contribute to defined components of larger initiatives. Each intern will focus on projects and deliverables in 1–2 of the following areas of expertise:

Development and Refinement of Dashboards:

  • Help design, implement, and optimize credit monitoring dashboards to provide actionable insights for key client relationships.
  • Ensure dashboards meet client-specific requirements and align with organizational goals.

Predictive Criteria and Attribute Determination:

  • Identify and evaluate predictive attributes for credit decisioning and fraud detection processes.
  • Develop and refine workflows for decision-making and evaluation processes, including know-your-client (KYC) compliance.

Data Ingestion, Reconciliation and Strategy/Database Development:

  • Perform data reconciliation, assist client data ingestion strategies and support/develop internal database structures for client reporting, strategy development and performance tracking.
  • Address data inconsistencies and provide solutions to enhance model development, data visualization and internal decision-making processes

Risk Model Development :

  • Help create, clean and define model attribute and target definitions for credit risk model development.
  • Assist in the D&A Modeling Team in the development, testing and documentation of machine learning based Risk Models.

Data Classification and Categorization:

  • Conduct research and analysis on banking data to determine the most appropriate classification structure and categorization of underlying day-to-day transactions.
  • Assist the D&A Strategy Team to amend or build code to operationalize classification practices within our UAT and production environments.

Each Intern will also be responsible for the following:

Industry Knowledge and Best Practices:

  • Stay current with industry best practices in credit risk management, data visualization, and decision strategy implementation.
  • Leverage data science techniques to enhance credit monitoring and evaluation processes.

Independent Project Management:

  • Manage time-sensitive projects and deliverables independently, ensuring accuracy and attention to detail.
  • Collaborate with cross-functional teams to align project outcomes with broader business objectives.

Stakeholder Collaboration and Reporting:

  • Partner with internal and external stakeholders to refine credit monitoring strategies.
  • Prepare clear, data-driven reports and presentations for decision-makers.

Continuous Improvement:

  • Identify areas for process improvement and implement innovative solutions to enhance credit monitoring and decision workflows.
  • Monitor the performance of dashboards and decision-making models, making necessary adjustments to maintain effectiveness.

Other projects and/or duties as assigned by your Supervisor from time to time.

What you’ll need to succeed:

  • Exceptional communication and interpersonal skills, with a demonstrated ability to manage multiple ongoing projects at the same time.
  • Strong problem-solving skills, with the ability to independently identify and solve problems in an effective and timely manner.
  • Collaboration and Teamwork skills: work with others to deliver results, meaningfully contributing to the team.
  • Ability to effectively multi-task, prioritize, re-prioritize, and think quickly on your feet.
  • “Roll up the sleeves” mindset – proactively identify areas of need and fill any gaps to ensure project success.
  • Demonstrated ability to succeed in a fast paced, growing company; adaptable and able to go with the flow when things change.
  • Curiosity: ask probing questions, dig deep and maintain an open-mind.
  • Stay up to date with relevant technologies and have a life-long learning mindset.

What we offer

  • Real ownership and meaningful work (you’ll contribute to real outcomes)
  • Mentorship from experienced Leaders
  • Hybrid work environment in Edmonton
  • A high-learning, high-collaboration culture
  • Experience in a fast-moving fintech company

About Trust Science

Trust Science® is a mission-driven fintech company advancing financial inclusion through AI-powered credit scoring and decisioning. Our platform helps lenders approve more good borrowers—especially people underserved by traditional credit systems.

We believe Better Data = Better Decisions = Better Outcomes. We’re building a modern credit intelligence platform that blends data science, alternative data, and explainable AI to improve how credit decisions are made.
