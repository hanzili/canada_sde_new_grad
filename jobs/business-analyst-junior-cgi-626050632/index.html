Full Description

Business Analyst (Junior)
Missions

  • Provide functional support to risk analysts and front office teams on credit risk processes
  • Gather requirements from business partners and manage them according to the priorities defined by the Product Owner
  • Perform data analysis in the context of understanding requirements, defining solutions, and testing
  • Communicate findings effectively to users and management to facilitate appropriate solutions
  • Collaborate with users and other business analysts to understand user needs and translate requirements into functional solutions
  • Write functional specifications and document system and process workflows
  • Ensure that agile and continuous delivery methodologies are followed
  • Ensure that features are properly tested and that required artifacts are produced
  • Coordinate and execute testing phases across different environments and present test results to users
  • Collaborate closely with stakeholders located in multiple regions (US, Europe, India)

Profile
Required / Must Have

  • University degree in a relevant field
  • At least one experience in a financial institution as a Business Analyst
  • Comfortable acting as a point of contact for business stakeholders and understanding/documenting business needs and priorities
  • Strong desire to learn and contribute ideas and solutions to the team
  • Curious mindset with the ability to understand business challenges and identify solutions
  • Critical and creative thinking skills to explore multiple approaches to problem-solving
  • Ability to read (and ideally perform small updates to) Python scripts
  • Experience working in an Agile environment (Scrum)
  • Strong organizational and communication skills
  • Ability to analyze and provide/receive constructive feedback

Functional and Technical Skills (Required)

  • Knowledge of Investment Banking
  • Strong technical documentation and professional written communication skills
  • Good working knowledge of SQL
  • Ability to write queries joining data across multiple tables for analysis and testing
  • Ability to read stored procedures for investigation purposes
  • Experience with Power BI
  • Familiarity with data integration concepts (APIs, databases, ETL, files, etc.)
  • Experience in Python scripting for automation, data manipulation, and troubleshooting
  • Ability to develop and maintain scripts to support business analysis and operational efficiency
  • Experience working in Unix/Linux environments, including command-line troubleshooting and script execution

Preferred (Nice to Have)

  • Understanding of Credit and Counterparty Risk concepts
  • High-level understanding of capital markets products (secured financing transactions, derivatives) or corporate loans
  • Knowledge of US or international financial regulations
  • Experience with ETL tools

Language Requirement
Fluency in English (written and spoken) is required, as this role involves regular collaboration with colleagues and partners in the United States.
