Full Description

Overview
We are seeking a motivated Junior Data Analyst to join our dynamic team. This entry-level position offers an excellent opportunity to develop your data analysis skills and gain hands-on experience with a variety of analytical tools and methodologies. The ideal candidate will have a strong foundation in data management, business analysis, and visualization, with a keen eye for detail and problem-solving. This role is perfect for individuals passionate about transforming data into actionable insights to support strategic decision-making.

Duties

  • Assist in collecting, cleaning, and analyzing large datasets using SQL, R, Python, and other data analysis tools.
  • Develop and maintain dashboards and reports using Tableau, Power BI, and Excel VBA to visualize key metrics.
  • Support project management activities within Agile frameworks, ensuring timely delivery of analytics projects aligned with SDLC best practices.
  • Collaborate with cross-functional teams to understand business requirements and translate them into technical specifications.
  • Contribute to database design and management using Microsoft SQL Server, Oracle, and other database systems.
  • Conduct business analysis to identify trends, patterns, and opportunities for process improvements.
  • Document processes and workflows using Visio and other documentation tools.
  • Participate in analysis activities related to database design, system integration, and data governance initiatives.
  • Support the team in maintaining compliance with data security standards and best practices.

Requirements

  • Bachelor’s degree in Data Science, Computer Science, Business Analytics, or related field.
  • Proven experience or coursework involving SQL, R, Python, and Bash (Unix shell).
  • Familiarity with data visualization tools such as Tableau and Power BI.
  • Knowledge of SDLC processes and Agile project management methodologies.
  • Strong understanding of business analysis principles and database design concepts.
  • Experience with Microsoft SQL Server, Oracle databases, and VBA scripting is preferred.
  • Excellent analytical skills with the ability to interpret complex data sets accurately.
  • Effective communication skills for presenting insights clearly to non-technical stakeholders.
  • Ability to work collaboratively in a fast-paced environment while managing multiple priorities effectively. This position offers a valuable platform for aspiring data professionals eager to grow their expertise in analytics, visualization, and database management within a supportive team environment.
