Today we are confirming our search for a Junior Property Accountant to support the accurate and timely accounting of a portfolio of our properties. This role is well suited for an early career accountant who is eager to learn, take ownership of their work, and grow into a strong performer within a high accountability environment. The successful candidate will work closely with senior team members and property operations to ensure reliable financial reporting and day-to-day execution.

This is a hands-on role with exposure to core property accounting activities and opportunities to develop technical skills, business understanding, and professional judgement. In addition to the duties typically expected of a junior level accounting professional, responsibilities will include:

Property Accounting and Reporting

  • Support the Property Accounting team in preparing monthly, quarterly, and annual financial statements for an assigned portfolio of properties.
  • Prepare monthly journal entries, accruals, and adjustments related to property operations, capital activity, and renovations.
  • Prepare and complete bank reconciliations and other assigned balance sheet reconciliations within established timelines.
  • Maintain the general ledger and ensure transactions are accurately recorded and properly supported.
  • Assist with variance analysis and preparation of basic financial summaries for property operations.
  • Prepare and maintain supporting schedules including property taxes, utilities, and other recurring items.
  • Prepare all routine and recurring journal entries for the year for a portfolio of assigned properties.
  • Assist with the preparation and submission of annual property filings.

Operations Support and Learning

  • Work closely with property management teams to respond to questions and resolve routine accounting issues.
  • Support tracking and reporting of work order, maintenance, and renovation activity as required.
  • Follow established accounting processes and controls consistently.
  • Identify issues, errors, or inefficiencies and raise them with senior team members.
  • Assist with budgeting, forecasting, and audit activities by preparing schedules and supporting documentation.
  • Use Yardi and other systems effectively, building confidence and efficiency over time.

Performance and Team Contribution

  • Take ownership of assigned work and consistently meet deadlines.
  • Demonstrate strong attention to detail and commitment to accuracy.
  • Be proactive in learning property accounting processes and understanding how operations impact financial results.
  • Contribute positively to a collaborative, accountable, and high-performance team environment.
  • Seek feedback and apply it to improve performance and skills.

Qualifications

  • Bachelor’s degree in accounting.

  • 1-3 years of accounting experience, gained through co-op placements, internships, or full-time post-graduate roles.

  • Advanced Microsoft Excel skills, with demonstrated experience using Excel in a professional accounting environment.

  • Strong foundational full-cycle accounting knowledge and willingness to learn.
  • High level of organization and ability to manage multiple tasks.
  • Strong work ethic with a hands-on, roll up your sleeves approach.
  • Clear and professional communication skills.

Most importantly, we are a small yet mighty team. This is a roll up your sleeves, help where you are needed kind of role. We are looking for a new team member who will support our success when and as needed, as our team will do for you when you need it.
