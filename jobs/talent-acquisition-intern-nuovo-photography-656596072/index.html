About the Role

Support recruitment, coordinate interviews, optimize processes

About Nuovo Photography

Leading North American artistic portrait photography studio

Full Description

Nuovo Artistic Studios is the premier destination for all things self-love. A by-women company and one of the largest artistic photography companies in North America, Nuovo is on a mission to empower individuals by fostering confidence and self-expression while celebrating intimacy and individuality.

As a Talent Acquisition Intern, you will gain hands-on experience supporting the recruitment process and contribute to HR and Talent initiatives. In addition to recruitment activities, you will support key projects that help improve hiring processes, strengthen employer branding, and contribute to the evolution of Nuovo’s talent programs.

This is a great opportunity for someone eager to learn, grow, and explore a career in Talent Acquisition and Human Resources.

This Internship will take place from May 4th, 2026 to August 28th, 2026

What You’ll Do:

Sourcing & Talent Attraction

  • Support in posting job ads across platforms
  • Assist in the selection process with CV Screenings
  • Help identify and reach out to potential candidates
  • Support building candidate pipelines for future hiring needs

Interviewing & Candidate Evaluation

  • Participate in initial candidate interviews and learn how to assess fit to the role and company
  • Help coordinate interviews and communicate with candidates throughout the process

Reporting & Recruitment Administration

  • Maintain accurate candidate records and recruitment activity within the ATS
  • Support the team with recruitment reporting and performance tracking

Talent Projects

  • Contribute to talent projects such as employee referral programs, campus recruitment, interviewer training, and employer branding.
  • Assist with research and ideas to improve recruitment processes and candidate experience

Requirements

About You:

  • Currently pursuing or recently completed a degree/diploma in Human Resources, Labour Relations, or related field
  • Strong interest in recruitment and talent acquisition
  • Knowledge in recruitment platforms and softwares (Indeed, Linkedin, Workable, are an asset)
  • Excellent communication and organizational skills
  • Active listening, collaboration and adaptability
  • Detail-oriented and eagerness to learn
  • Fluent in English, French and Spanish is considered a strong asset

Benefits

What We Offer:

  • Inspiring Work Environment: Immerse yourself in a creative, collaborative company where artistry and empowerment converge.
  • Growth Opportunities: Be part of a fast-growing company committed to nurturing talent and fostering career development.
  • Recognition and Support: A team that values your contributions and celebrates your success.
  • Exclusive Perks: Access to Nuovo’s services and products, ensuring you, too, can experience the power of portraiture. We also provide you with a 50% Family & Friends discount.

About Us:

Nuovo Artistic Studios is not just a photography studio; it’s a movement designed to uplift, empower, and transform. Our mission is to provide every guest with a luxury experience that inspires confidence and celebrates individuality. With studios across North America, we are redefining the art of modern portraiture, one empowered story at a time.

This is your opportunity to join a team dedicated to excellence and artistry. Become part of the Nuovo legacy and make an impact, one unforgettable moment at a time.

This is your moment. Apply today!
