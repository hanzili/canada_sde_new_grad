About the Role

Support product discovery and document feature specs

About GoMaterials

Deloitte-recognized fast-growing Canadian startup

Full Description

Who we are:

GoMaterials is one of Canada's fastest-growing companies, recognized by Deloitte, the Globe & Mail, and the Lazaridis Scaleup Program. We’re revolutionizing how landscape contractors source plant and hardscape materials through a B2B marketplace that simplifies procurement in a traditionally outdated industry.Since our inception, we have helped landscapers save time, money, and stress and plant over 1.5 million plants and trees. Led by a young and eager group of entrepreneurs, GoMaterials is aggressively expanding across North America. We are therefore looking for amazing people to add to our team!

About the role:

We’re looking for a curious, detail-oriented Product & AI Enablement Intern to support the ongoing development and evolution of our platform as we scale. In this role, you’ll work closely with our Product Manager, designers, and cross-functional teams to conduct research, analyze user insights, and keep product documentation organized and up to date. This is an ideal opportunity for someone eager to learn how great software products are built, contribute directly to feature discovery, and make a meaningful impact in a fast-paced startup environment.

What you’ll do:

  • Conduct product research to support feature exploration, competitive analysis, and broader market understanding.
  • Assist in writing user stories and preparing materials for product kickoffs.
  • Tag, organize, and summarize user research recordings to highlight key insights.
  • Support user discovery activities by coordinating sessions, preparing materials, and capturing key findings.
  • Maintain clear and up-to-date documentation across product workflows, research notes, and feature specifications.

What you’ll need:

  • Be currently enrolled in an undergraduate or graduate degree program, preferably in Business, Engineering, Computer Science, UX, or a related field.
  • Strong interest in product management, user research, and technology-driven problem solving.
  • Excellent written and verbal communication skills, with the ability to summarize information clearly.
  • Strong organizational skills with attention to detail and the ability to manage multiple tasks.
  • Comfort with conducting research, analyzing qualitative data, and identifying patterns.
  • Proactive attitude, curiosity, and willingness to learn in a fast-paced startup environment.
  • Familiarity with tools like Trello, Figma, Jira, or project management software is a plus (not required).
