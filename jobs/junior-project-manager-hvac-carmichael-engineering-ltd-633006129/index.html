About the Role

Assist bids, coordinate HVAC project execution

About Carmichael Engineering Ltd.

Historic HVAC leader with national project exposure

Full Description

Company Overview: At Carmichael Engineering, we pride ourselves on being a leading provider of comprehensive mechanical and HVAC solutions across Canada. With over 100 years of experience, we have established ourselves as industry experts in delivering innovative and sustainable solutions to our diverse clientele.

Founded in 1922, Carmichael Engineering has grown from a small family-owned business into a trusted national brand with a presence in major cities and communities throughout Canada. Our rich history is built on a foundation of integrity, professionalism, and a commitment to excellence in everything we do.

This is a full-time permanent position working on-site in our Ottawa branch and supporting our Lead Project manager with various Hvac and construction projects. Company vehicle will be provided. Junior Project Manager Location: 2320 St-Laurent Blvd., Ottawa, ON K1G 6C4

Salary range: 75k-85k

Job Description:

  • Read; draw plans and specifications for HVAC retrofit and construction projects.
  • Assist with bids on retrofit and construction projects in the HVAC industry for new and existing customers.
  • Assist with estimating, designing and presenting thermal system solutions to prospective customers; both independently and in collaboration with the estimating team.
  • Assuming project management responsibilities over work execution, providing for functional leadership and technical support;
  • Ordering and managing deliveries of projects.
  • Coordinating with sub-contractors.
  • Attending site project meetings and coordinating project schedule.
  • Tracking job costing and profitability.
  • Working in conjunction with the service team to maximize prospective opportunities.
  • Providing operation and maintenance manuals on project as required.

Job Requirements :

  • Ideal candidates would have 2+ years of experience in a related field or 5+ years in Project coordination
  • HVAC or Building Systems education or knowledge acquired through experience.
  • Knowledge of natural gas, propane, and oil is considered an asset.
  • Ability to read, draw and provide equipment specification.
  • Excellent verbal/written communication, strong negotiation and presentation skills.
  • Highly disciplined, independent, entrepreneurial, confident, well organized self-starter.
  • Ability to travel to job sites as required with a company vehicle provided
  • Team Player.

What We Offer:

  • Competitive Starting Pay
  • Paid Training

  • Comprehensive Benefits:

  • Medical/Dental/Vision/Travel Insurance

  • Life Insurance
  • Pension plan paid fully by us.
  • Vacation
  • Paid Time Off
  • Short-Term and Long-Term Disability
  • Employee Family Assistance Program paid by us.

Health and Safety is a core paramount value of Carmichael Engineering. Given the importance of keeping one another safe, you are expected to comply with our Health and safety policies at all times and client Health and Safety policies when working at client locations. Carmichael Engineering welcomes and encourages applications from people with disabilities.
