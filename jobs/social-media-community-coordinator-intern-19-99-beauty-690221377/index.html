About 19/99 Beauty

Full Description

Position Overview

19/99 Beauty is seeking a Social Media Community Coordinator Intern to support day-to-day community engagement across our social platforms.

This role is ideal for a student or recent graduate interested in digital marketing, social media strategy, and brand communications , with a strong interest in beauty and culture. The intern will gain hands-on experience managing an active online community and contributing to brand growth.

Key Responsibilities

  • Monitor and respond to comments, mentions, and direct messages on platforms such as Instagram and TikTok
  • Engage with followers, creators, and customers in a clear and consistent brand voice
  • Identify and escalate customer inquiries or feedback to the appropriate internal team
  • Conduct social listening to track trends, conversations, and emerging creators
  • Assist in organizing user-generated content and community engagement initiatives
  • Support the marketing team with content coordination and content creation tasks, including video editing, filming, and social media post creation

Qualifications

  • Must be currently enrolled in or recent graduate of an Ontario university (e.g., Marketing, Creative Industries, Fashion, Communications, or related field)
  • Strong familiarity with Instagram, TikTok, and current social media trends
  • Excellent written communication skills with attention to tone and detail
  • Organized, reliable, and able to manage multiple tasks
  • Comfortable working in a fast-paced, collaborative environment

Assets:

  • Experience managing social media accounts or online communities (personal or professional)
  • Familiarity with tools such as Canva, CapCut, Adobe Creative Suite, or Microsoft Office

Learning Outcomes / What You Will Gain

Through this internship, the student will:

  • Gain hands-on experience in community management and social media strategy
  • Develop skills in brand voice, digital communication, and audience engagement
  • Learn how to analyze trends and contribute to real-time marketing decisions
  • Work collaboratively within a growing entrepreneurial brand environment

Location:

Hybrid (Downtown Toronto + Remote)

Duration:

Summer 2026 (June–August, flexible start, possibility to extend)

Hours:

Part-time (~20 hours/week)

Compensation:

$22/hour

﻿ About the Company

19/99 Beauty is a Toronto-based beauty brand redefining modern makeup through a multi-generational lens. We create products that are creative, expressive, and inclusive—encouraging individuals of all ages to explore beauty on their own terms.
