Junior Marketing Coordinator

at MARK.AGE

📍 Location Toronto, ON Remote
📋 Details Full-time · Permanent · $18/hour · Ontario
📅 Posted Feb 27, 2026

About the Role

Coordinate marketing tasks; update websites; prepare content

About MARK.AGE

Growth-focused marketing agency with structured development path

Full Description

Location: Remote (Toronto-based candidates preferred)
Type: Full-time (37.5 hours per week)
Compensation: $18 per hour

Role Overview

Markage is hiring a Junior Marketing Coordinator to support website updates, content preparation, and marketing task coordination.

This is an entry-level role with structured training. You will learn our systems and standards while supporting both internal projects and client work.

You do not need to arrive as a specialist. You need to be coachable, steady, and ready to grow.

What You Will DoMarketing Coordination

  • Maintain organized task lists with clear updates.
  • Turn rough notes into structured checklists.
  • Help move work from draft to approval to publish.

Website Support

  • Make guided updates in Shopify and WordPress.
  • Apply consistent formatting across desktop and mobile.
  • Flag issues clearly when something needs review.

Email and Content Preparation

  • Format email campaigns using templates.
  • Prepare simple landing page content.
  • Resize and format images for social and web.
  • Schedule approved content.

Marketing Tech Assistance

  • Support form setup, booking links, and simple integrations.
  • Keep files organized and structured.
  • Follow documented processes.

Communication Support

  • Join internal or client calls when needed.
  • Take clear notes.
  • Communicate professionally in writing.
  • Apply feedback constructively.

Required Skills

  • Strong written English (clear, professional, concise).
  • Comfortable learning digital tools quickly.
  • Basic familiarity with Shopify or WordPress (or strong willingness to learn).
  • Basic design awareness (clean spacing, readable layouts, attention to detail).
  • Ability to manage multiple small tasks while staying organized.

Preferred

  • Canva or Figma experience.
  • Exposure to email platforms (Mailchimp, Constant Contact, etc.).
  • Basic image editing skills (Adobe Creative Cloud).
  • Interest in practical AI tools to support workflow (with human review).

What We Value

  • Coachability.
  • Professional communication.
  • Reliability.
  • Consistency.
  • Calm problem-solving.

This role is ideal for someone early in their career who wants real hands-on experience and structured growth.

Compensation & Work Structure

  • $18 per hour.
  • 37.5 hours per week.
  • Overtime, if applicable, is paid in accordance with Ontario employment standards.
  • Vacation and statutory entitlements provided in accordance with Ontario ESA.

Growth Path

This role follows a structured development track within Markage’s marketing execution model. Increased responsibility is tied to performance and consistency.

How to Apply

  • Resume
  • Short written paragraph (5–10 lines) explaining why this role fits you
  • Portfolio or sample work (student work accepted; optional but encouraged)
