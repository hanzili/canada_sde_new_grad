About Volatus Aerospace

Aerospace firm supporting CPA development

Full Description

Company Overview:

Volatus Aerospace Inc. is a global leader in aerial solutions, with 100+ years of combined aviation expertise. We specialize in delivering innovative, customer-focused solutions that integrate both piloted and remotely piloted systems. Our offerings include remote operations, enterprise equipment sales, training, and aerial services, aimed at enhancing real-world applications across industries. Our headquarter is based in Vaughan, but we have a global reach and support clients across multiple sectors including energy, public safety, and infrastructure.

Role Overview:

As part of our continued growth, Volatus Aerospace is seeking a Junior Corporate Accountant to support our corporate finance team. This role is ideal for someone early in their accounting career who is eager to build strong technical skills and gain hands-on exposure to the full accounting cycle.

This is a primarily remote position, with occasional in-office attendance required for collaboration and operational needs.

Role Requirements

Due to the administrative and transactional nature of the Junior Corporate Accountant role, strong organizational skills and a high level of attention to detail are essential. As this position is primarily remote, the successful candidate will be self-motivated, highly independent, and able to manage responsibilities effectively while working with minimal supervision. Exposure to or hands-on experience with NetSuite ERP is considered an asset and would support success in this role.

Key Responsibilities

Support transactional accounting processes through extensive use of QuickBooks Online and NetSuite ERP –

  • Accounts Payable (AP): Process and code vendor invoices, match PO and supporting documentation, and assist with the preparation of weekly payment runs. Track approval status and follow up with internal stakeholders on pending approvals
  • Accounts Receivable (AR): Prepare and issue customer invoices, apply incoming payments (including cheque deposit), and support the follow-up of overdue accounts.
  • Expense Reports/Credit Card: Review and audit employee travel and entertainment expenses claims for compliance.

Handle basic matching process of reconciliations:

  • Bank & Credit Card reconciliations: Matching bank statements to the company's internal ledger to identify missing receipts or bank fees.
  • Subsidiary Ledgers: Support intercompany account matching and reconciliation activities.

Audit and compliance assistance:

  • Sourcing Evidence: Exporting data and schedules from accounting software into Excel. Pulling specific invoices or bank statements requested by external auditors.

General administrative and office support:

  • Document filing: Scan and Upload documents and maintain basic tracking logs (Excel or shared tools). Organize and maintain digital accounting records, ensuring files are properly labeled, securely stored, and easily retrievable for audit and reference purposes.
  • Administrative: Manage incoming and outgoing accounting-related communications, including monitoring and triaging shared accounting mailboxes.
  • Other duties as assigned.

Qualification Requirement:

  • Bachelor’s degree in accounting, Finance, or related discipline. Active pursuit of the CPA designation.
  • Exposure to hands-on experience with NetSuite ERP is a plus.
  • Proficiency with MS Excel (pivot tables, lookups, formulas) and familiarity with other ERP/finance tools.
  • Knowledge of Canadian accounting standards (IFRS) and CRA compliance (GST/HST, payroll, corporate tax basics).
  • Detail-oriented with strong organizational and problem-solving skills.
  • Excellent communication and interpersonal skills to work effectively across teams.
  • Strong work ethic, with a willingness to learn and adapt in a dynamic environment.
  • Bilingual (English/French) is considered an asset.

Why Join Volatus Aerospace

At Volatus Aerospace, you’ll join an innovative and fast-growing organization at the forefront of aerospace autonomy. We offer:

  • Competitive salary and performance-based incentives
  • Comprehensive benefits package
  • Professional development and career growth opportunities
  • A collaborative and supportive work environment

How to Apply

If you are ready to take your career to new heights, please apply with your resume and a brief cover letter outlining your relevant experience and interest in the role.
