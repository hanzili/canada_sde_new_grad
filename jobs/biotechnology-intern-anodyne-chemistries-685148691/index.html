Full Description

Biotechnology Intern

Anodyne Chemistries is redefining how the world makes chemicals through breakthrough bio-electrochemical technology. By combining advanced electrochemistry with precision, biological catalysts, we’re creating entirely new pathways to transform non-fossil feedstocks into a growing portfolio of high-value, sustainable chemicals.

Our approach challenges the status quo, unlocking cleaner, more efficient production methods designed for real-world impact. With our pilot plant set to be commissioned in the summer of 2026, we are rapidly scaling from innovation to industrial deployment.

At Anodyne, you won’t just witness the future of chemistry, you’ll help build it, driving solutions that have the power to reshape industries and accelerate the transition to a circular carbon economy.

Job Brief

We are currently looking for a full-time Biotechnology Intern to support and work with our Biochemistry and Bioprocessing teams. The ideal candidate will be enthusiastic about applying their research skills to important real-world problems while learning from our talented team of researchers. This will be a technical role and having familiarity working in a wet lab environment will be essential. The successful applicant will be responsible for mutagenesis, subcloning, enzyme production and characterization, optimization, and bioreactor scale-up.

Specifically, you will:

  • Plan and perform standard biochemistry workflows including mutagenesis, subcloning, enzyme production, enzyme purification, enzyme activity assays, and bioconjugations.
  • Plan and perform standard bioprocessing workflows such as large scale enzyme expressions and purifications.
  • Troubleshoot workflows, identify routes for optimisation, and improve standard operating procedures.
  • Analyse and interpret experimental data, translating findings into clear insights and effectively communicating results to cross-functional teams.
  • Conduct literature surveys to answer critical, interdisciplinary questions.
  • Maintain a safe lab environment by following existing safety procedures.
  • Prepare detailed lab records at an industry standard.
  • Work within our multidisciplinary R&D team to support ongoing and future projects.
  • Work independently, and collaboratively with scientists and engineers to meet project deliverables and timelines.

Requirements

  • BSc (or equivalent) in molecular biology, bioprocessing, biotechnology, biochemistry, biology, or a relevant field.

  • 2 years of experience carrying out laboratory research in a relevant field.

  • Excellent quantitative skills, including standard lab calculations and error analysis.
  • Proven attention to detail, with excellent organisational skills and a commitment to accurate data collection, documentation, and record-keeping.
  • Effective communication skills, with strong written and verbal skills and the ability to collaborate seamlessly across multidisciplinary teams.
  • Ability to take initiative in problem solving and addressing challenges.
  • Ability to lift, arrange and transport objects up to 23 kg.
  • Ability to work independently, manage your time effectively, and learn quickly.

Salary Guidance

Salary range is $50,000-$60,000

Benefits

  • Medical, dental, and vision coverage.
  • Paid sick days and vacation.
  • Competitive salary and equity compensation commensurate with experience.
  • Diverse and inclusive work environment.
