About the Role

Analyze processes, map workflows, enable automation

About ZenaTech Inc.

Nasdaq-listed tech innovator in AI drones and automation

Full Description

About Us:

ZenaTech (Nasdaq: ZENA | FSE: 49Q) is a technology company specializing in AI drones, Drone-as-a-Service (DaaS), enterprise SaaS, and quantum computing solutions for mission-critical business applications. Since 2017, the company has leveraged its software development expertise and expanded its drone design and manufacturing capabilities through ZenaDrone to innovate and enhance customer inspection, monitoring, safety, security, compliance, and surveying processes.

Overview

Join our dynamic team as a Junior Business Analyst and become a vital contributor to our data-driven decision-making processes! In this energetic role, you will support the analysis, design, and implementation of business solutions that enhance operational efficiency and strategic growth. Your enthusiasm for data analysis, combined with your eagerness to learn and grow within an innovative environment, will drive impactful insights and foster continuous improvement. This paid position offers an exciting opportunity to develop your skills in analytics, project management, and business analysis within a collaborative setting.

Key Responsibilities

  • Conduct process discovery sessions with stakeholders across operations, finance, HR, and technology teams
  • Document current-state (“As-Is”) workflows and design optimized future-state (“To-Be”) processes
  • Develop BPMN diagrams, process maps, flowcharts, and detailed automation requirement documents
  • Maintain and support prioritization of the automation opportunity backlog
  • Write clear user stories, acceptance criteria, and test cases for automation initiatives
  • Track and report on automation impact metrics, including time savings, error reduction, and cost avoidance
  • Act as a liaison between technical teams and non-technical stakeholders to ensure alignment and clarity
  • Identify opportunities for process improvement and increased automation adoption

Required Qualifications

  • Bachelor’s degree in Business, Management Information Systems, Industrial Engineering, or a related field
  • Up to 3 years of experience in business analysis, process improvement, or operations
  • Proficiency with process mapping and documentation tools (e.g., Lucidchart, Visio, Miro, or similar)
  • Strong written and verbal communication skills, including stakeholder interviewing and documentation
  • Ability to manage competing priorities and maintain a structured requirements backlog

Nice to Have

  • Familiarity with BPMN 2.0, Lean, or Six Sigma methodologies
  • Experience with project management tools such as Jira, Asana, or Notion
  • Conceptual understanding of automation technologies (RPA, API integrations, AI-driven workflows)
  • CBAP, CCBA, or entry-level Business Analysis certification
