What you’ll do:

Eaton's Electrical Engineering Services & Systems division (EESS) is currently seeking a motivated Intern in Power Systems Engineering (R&D) for its team. This role is based at our Eaton Americas Innovation Center in Brossard (Québec) and offers hybrid work opportunities, as well as benefits from day one.

What you’ll do:
CYME International T&D, a division of Eaton Corporation, is a world leader in power grid simulation and analysis software. CYME has built a strong reputation through both its technical expertise and its diligent after-sales service. Our solutions are at the heart of thousands of T&D projects in over 100 countries around the world. As a member of a multidisciplinary development team, the intern will participate in the design and development of high-tech software and projects.

At Eaton, we make what matters work, thanks to the passion of all our employees around the world. We create an environment where creativity, invention, and discovery are honored every day. It is a place where bold and competent professionals like you can reach their full potential and help us reach ours.

These values allow us to tackle some of the most significant challenges on the planet, while never losing sight of what is important. As a team, we have the power to make a difference.

Essential Responsibilities:
We are looking for an intern to assist the engineers of the Research and Development group. As a member of a multidisciplinary development team, the intern will participate in the development of projects in the field of distribution network analysis and modeling, as well as renewable energy.

  • Support the engineering team in research and development for the CYME power grid simulation software.
  • Participate in writing and updating technical documentation and user manuals for developed features.
  • Execute manual validation tests to ensure solutions meet client requirements.
  • Document basic technical procedures within CYME (e.g., import/export, synchronization) to assist the development team.
  • Automate and document several Quality Assurance (QA) cases.

Qualifications:

Required Qualifications

  • Currently enrolled in a university program with co-op internships.
  • Good knowledge of basic electrical engineering: Ohm's Law, Kirchhoff's Laws.
  • Mathematical knowledge of complex numbers and matrix calculations.
  • Ability to be present at our Brossard facility at least 3 days per week.
  • Fluency in English and French (working proficiency in both languages). Fluency in French and English is required for this position to communicate with colleagues, customers, and partners located inside and outside of Québec, as well as to understand technical documentation in the field of our industry.
  • Be legally authorized to work in Canada without corporate sponsorship now or in the future.

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Good knowledge of electrical engineering: symmetrical components and electrical apparatus.
  • Good knowledge of programming in Matlab and Python.
  • Knowledge of network simulation software (e.g., CYME, PowerWorld, EMTP-RV).

Skills:

  • Being autonomous.
  • Showing initiative.
  • Being rigorous and detail-oriented.
