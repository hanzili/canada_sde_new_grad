Our Story

About You:

You are a high achiever looking to thrive in a fast-paced environment. You take pride in your own work but are comfortable collaborating with a team of highly motivated individuals. You can communicate clearly and concisely with teammates and clients, and you enjoy strong company culture and camaraderie. You can navigate multiple corporate functions, including global lines of service and corporate centers of excellence. You possess strong interpersonal skills and are willing to take on diverse tasks to achieve the team’s common goal. You value personal and professional growth and are ready to take the next step in advancing your career.

If this sounds like you, well, then you will love the culture at Avison Young!

About Us:

Avison Young is a global commercial real estate brokerage and advisory firm, offering transaction, management, financial and consulting services. We’ve designed our corporate structure to best serve our clients by enhancing collaboration across our organization.

Real estate can have an enormous positive impact on people’s lives – and we’re in the business of making spaces and places work better for people. Our purpose is to create real economic, social and environmental value as a global real estate advisor, powered by people.

We care about each other and we have each other’s backs. This makes Avison Young a great place to be a client, and a great place to work. We support the whole person and their complete wellness – economic, mental and physical – because what’s best for our business comes from our people bringing their whole selves to work.

Of course we love it here, but outsiders think we’re pretty great too! Avison Young has been recognized as one of Canada's Best Managed Companies for the 15th year in a row, with a Platinum Club designation!

Overview

As a Marketing Associate, you are a highly motivated and strategic individual with strong interpersonal skills who can create well-designed print and digital collateral for property marketing, new business proposals and presentations, service line collateral, and general marketing materials with focus on complete project ownership and management.

You can manage a high-volume of projects in a fast-paced environment, have solid design skills and enjoy an entrepreneurial environment where strategic and proactive solutions are celebrated and rewarded.

This specific role supports the brokerage operations from the Downtown Toronto office with marketing, graphics, and communications. You will support the strategy and production of campaigns that drive our marketing, sales-enablement and communications goals. You’ll also build and sustain a strong relationship with our sales teams and business leaders while you focus on quality control and process improvements. Integrity and resourcefulness are essential for this position.

The base salary is aligned with market data and is estimated to be between $50,000 to $62,000 with the ability to achieve additional compensation through a bonus. This salary range reflects base compensation for the position across all Canadian locations. Within this range, individual pay is determined by work location and other factors, including relevant education/training, experience, and internal equity. This posting is for an existing vacancy withing our organization.

Responsibilities

  • Provide creative and innovative approaches to marketing programs for various teams across the region
  • Manage the design and layout of various marketing materials using programs within the Adobe Creative Cloud such as InDesign, Illustrator, Photoshop or Microsoft Office programs such as PowerPoint, Word and Excel
  • Create proposals, presentations, and marketing collateral, including e-blasts, flyers, floor plans, maps, invitations, boards, signage, and more
  • Responsible for tracking workflow and ensuring that jobs are completed in required time frames to client specifications
  • Create best-in-class email marketing campaigns using our email marketing platform
  • Design research materials, including market reports, newsletters, white papers, and other market positioning deliverables
  • Create presentations, layout and design of proposals, flyers, and brochures working closely with key stakeholders on concept and messaging
  • Maintain brand consistency in line with global corporate standards at all times
  • Create promotional videos and interactive websites for key listings
  • Arrange signage or special printing requirements for properties as required
  • Oversee outsourcing and management of large print jobs and mailings, when needed
  • Remain abreast of Avison Young’s new technology and innovation initiatives and identity areas of use in the local market; commitment to evolve skills with changing needs
  • Participate in the firm’s strategic marketing and communications best practices meetings, conference calls, and strategy sessions

Qualifications

  • Minimum 2 years of graphic design experience
  • Degree in marketing, communications, or other related field, or 3 years of relevant work experience in a corporate environment
  • Must submit a digital portfolio in your application to be considered for this role
  • Proficient in Adobe Creative Suite - InDesign, Photoshop and Illustrator; and Microsoft Office (Nice to have: Adobe Rush and Premiere Pro)
  • Attitude — positive, always seeking to better our team, striving to add value each day
  • Proven ability to provide creative marketing solutions upon receiving project brief
  • Strong project management skills and attention to detail
  • You thrive under pressure and are comfortable with tight deadlines
  • You are self-motivated and can prioritize and manage multiple projects with little guidance; you work effectively both independently and in teams
  • Excellent and effective verbal and written communication skills
  • HTML, video editing, website and print design experience are all considered assets
  • Experience in commercial real estate or the professional services industry considered an asset
