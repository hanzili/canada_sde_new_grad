Kick start your career journey! Experience@Siemens is an exciting opportunity for new Graduates from college or university to transition from academic to the workplace. Siemens Canada will provide you with practical and meaningful work experience as you start your career journey and help you build business skills.

We are passionate about innovations that mean real progress. We are excited about technologies that still need to be developed. What about you? Do you want to use your curiosity, passion, and creativity to make the lives of millions of people easier and better? Join us as a recent graduate –by beginning your early career through Experience@Siemens New Graduate Program. Whichever path you take, we’re looking forward to seeing your perspective.

Recent graduates enrolled in this program will be partnered with a mentor and receive one on one coaching and guidance in support of their development and to help navigate their early career. In addition, this program will help students establish contacts and widen their network to excel in their career journey. Experience@Siemens offers experiential learning and flexibility, allowing you to balance your personal life and career goals.

Why you’ll love working for Siemens.

  • Freedom and a healthy work- life balance– Embrace our flexible work environment with flex hours, telecommuting and digital workspaces.
  • Solve the world’s most significant problems – Be part of exciting and innovative projects.
  • Engaging, challenging, and fast evolving, cutting edge technological environment.
  • Opportunities to advance your career and mentorship programs on a local and global scale.
  • Contribute to our social responsibility initiatives focused on access to education, access to technology and sustaining communities and make a positive impact on the community.
  • Opportunities to contribute your innovative ideas and get paid for them!
  • Diversity and inclusivity focused.

Siemens is proud to be an eight-time award winner of Canada’s Top 100 Employers, Canada’s Greenest Employers 2026 and Canada’s Top Employers for Young People 2026.

What will you do?

  • Perform and assist with period-end financial duties and reporting through compiling information and numbers, preparing presentations, performing variance analysis, journal entries, and account reconciliations
  • Collaborate within and outside of the finance organization to support business processes
  • Build and improve financial models to assist with regular and ad hoc analysis and reporting
  • Participating in problem-solving activities that relate to quality improvement and operational efficiency
  • Ad-hoc requests as required

What will you need to succeed?

  • Recent graduate from a university degree in Accounting/Finance
  • Above average study performance (Accumulative average > 2.5 (B-)). Transcripts to be provided upon request.
  • Graduated from post-secondary school within the last 12 months.
  • Working knowledge of accounting
  • Having completed at least one Co-op
  • Strong skills in MS-Excel, MS-Word and MS-PowerPoint
  • Experience with Tableau Data Analytics is a must
  • Keen attention to detail, ability to prioritize, multitasking skills and organizational skills
  • Positive attitude, willing to learn, grow and be an integral member of the team

Hourly rate is commensurate with experience, and ranges between $ 25.00 CAD - $ 31.00 CAD.
