About the Role

Analyze data to drive process improvements

About WorkSafeNB

Provincial safety regulator with strong internship programs.

Full Description

WorkSafeNB is looking to hire a Continuous Improvement Student to support our Planning and Operational Performance team. You will take day-to-day directions from the Performance Specialist and will be given guidance and support from the entire Planning and Operational Performance team. You will help the Performance Specialist solve operational and strategic process problems to improve service delivery, client experience and employee engagement. You will achieve these goals by applying statistical analysis techniques, process mapping, and data trending, to inform business decisions.

Salary and Hours of Work:

  • Hours of work are those required to perform the assigned tasks but shall not be less than 35 hours per week.
  • Hourly Rates for Students:

High school completed $16.40.

1 year of post-secondary completed $16.90.

2 years of post-secondary completed $17.40.

3 years of post-secondary completed $17.90.

4 years of post-secondary completed $18.40.

  • Must be a high school graduate and currently enrolled in a post-secondary education program.
  • Comfortable with Microsoft Office products (PowerPoint, Excel, Word). An aptitude for using data, mathematics, and/or statistics in real-life situations.

Competencies:

  • Adaptability
  • Client-focused Service
  • Ensuring Accountability
  • Teamwork and Collaboration
  • Problem Solving
  • Improving Business Processes Data Literacy

Written and spoken competency in English is required.

If you are interested in an exciting and rewarding summer student opportunity with WorkSafeNB, please apply online at www.worksafenb.ca by May 11, 2026. We kindly ask that you please include your current enrolled and completed years of university within your application.

We thank all those who apply however only those selected for further consideration will be contacted.

For more information about WorkSafeNB, or this and other employment opportunities, please visit our website at worksafenb.ca.

  • Working with the Performance Specialist to clearly identify specific business problems to be solved.
  • Gather data and apply statistical techniques to analyze the data in a meaningful way.
  • Create process flow diagrams.
  • Create summary documents of findings, observations, and recommendations.
