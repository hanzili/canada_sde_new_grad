Junior Accountant/Bookkeeper

at Social Planning Council of Ottawa

About the Role

Assist AP/AR, reconciliations, budgeting, reporting

About Social Planning Council of Ottawa

Ottawa-based non-profit focused on social planning

Full Description

Job Overview
We are seeking a detail-oriented and motivated Junior Accountant to join our finance team. This entry-level position offers an excellent opportunity to develop accounting skills within a dynamic corporate environment. The Junior Accountant will assist with various financial tasks, ensuring accuracy and compliance across multiple accounting functions. The ideal candidate will have a foundational understanding of accounting principles and experience with accounting software such as QuickBooks, Xero, Sage, or PeopleSoft. This role is integral to maintaining the financial health of the organization and supporting ongoing financial operations.

Responsibilities

  • Assist with accounts payable and accounts receivable processes to ensure timely payments and collections
  • Perform account reconciliation and bank reconciliation to verify accuracy of financial data
  • Support bookkeeping activities using accounting software including QuickBooks, Sage, Xero, or PeopleSoft
  • Aid in preparing financial statements, reports, and audit documentation
  • Conduct account analysis to identify discrepancies or irregularities
  • Assist with budgeting, forecasting, and financial planning activities
  • Support payroll processing and related reporting functions
  • Collaborate with team members on auditing procedures and internal controls
  • Maintain organized records of financial transactions in compliance with company policies

Skills

  • Proficiency in accounting software such as QuickBooks, Sage, Xero, or PeopleSoft is highly desirable
  • Strong understanding of accounts payable, accounts receivable, and bookkeeping processes
  • Experience with account reconciliation and bank reconciliation techniques
  • Knowledge of corporate finance principles and financial services industry standards
  • Ability to perform account analysis and assist with budgeting tasks
  • Familiarity with auditing procedures and internal control practices
  • Excellent organizational skills with attention to detail
  • Effective communication skills for collaborating within a team environment
  • Basic understanding of payroll processing and related compliance requirements

This position provides an excellent foundation for individuals aspiring to grow within the accounting or finance fields. Candidates should demonstrate a proactive attitude toward learning and a strong commitment to accuracy in all financial tasks.
