About the Role

Assist aquatic surveys; collect data; report results

About SEM Ltd

Regional aquatic biology contractor with provincial projects

Full Description

SEM is currently seeking a Junior or Intermediate Biologist for a three month contract, from June 1 to September 1, to be based out of our St. John’s office. This contract will have a possibility of extension.

The biologist will assist the SEM team in completing project-based aquatics work for a variety of clients across Newfoundland and Labrador.

Responsibilities

  • Assisting with various freshwater aquatic surveys
  • Data analysis, interpretation of technical reporting and report preparation.
  • Preparing federal and provincial permit applications.
  • Utilizing various field instruments for the collection of data while maintaining accurate records as per company quality assurance/quality control protocols.
  • Training will be provided for specialized data collection tasks.

Requirements

  • Post-secondary education in Biology or a related field of study
  • A passion for aquatic biology and the outdoors
  • Valid driver’s license
  • Willingness to travel extensively around the province during this contract
  • Willingness to work overtime and long hours in challenging conditions (heat or cold, rain, insects, etc.)

Other

  • Positive attitude and team player
  • Basic to Intermediate Microsoft product skills
  • Experience operating boats would be considered an asset
  • A “safe work mindset”

Please provide your resume if you feel your skills would be a good fit for this role. To learn more about our company, please visit www.semltd.ca

We thank all applicants for their interest; however, only those candidates selected for interviews will be contacted.

Work Location: In person
