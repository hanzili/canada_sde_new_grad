Junior Accountant

at Accurate Screen & Grating Ltd.

About Accurate Screen & Grating Ltd.

Canadian manufacturing firm with CPA development program

Full Description

Company Summary

Accurate Screen and Grating is a Canadian leader in the fabrication and distribution of grating, perforated, expanded, and wire metal products. With over 20 years in business, the company has grown from a single Calgary branch to operations across Canada. We take pride in our people, many of whom have built long-term careers with us.

Position Overview

We are seeking a Junior Accountant to join our Head Office team in Calgary. This role is intended for a recent university graduate who is actively pursuing the CPA designation and is looking to begin their accounting career in a hands-on, supportive environment.

Reporting to the Assistant Controller, this position will focus on core accounting functions including, accounts receivable, reconciliations, and month-end support. The successful candidate will gain exposure to a wide range of accounting processes and develop practical experience to support their CPA studies.

This is an excellent opportunity for someone who is detail-oriented, motivated, and eager to grow within a structured accounting environment.

Key Responsibilities

Accounting & Finance Support

  • Process accounts receivable transactions accurately and efficiently
  • Processing credit applications from customers and assessing credit risk
  • Perform bank and credit card reconciliations
  • Assist with month-end close activities, including journal entries and account reconciliations
  • Maintain accurate and organized financial records and supporting documentation
  • Assist with data entry and maintenance within the ERP system
  • Support the Assistant Controller and accounting team with ad hoc reporting and analysis
  • Other office misc. tasks as assigned by the Assistant Controller

Qualifications & Skills

  • Recent university graduate required with a degree in Accounting, Finance, or a related field
  • Actively enrolled in or planning to enroll in the CPA program (CPA designation required in progress)
  • Strong attention to detail and high level of accuracy
  • Solid analytical and problem-solving skills
  • Strong communication and interpersonal skills
  • Ability to manage repetitive and deadline-driven tasks
  • Proficiency in Microsoft Excel and general computer systems
  • Basic understanding of accounting principles and financial statements

What We Offer

  • Competitive salary
  • Profit sharing program
  • Comprehensive benefits package
  • Pension plan
  • Full support for CPA development and professional growth
  • Training and mentorship under experienced accounting leadership
  • Long-term career growth opportunities within a stable Canadian company

Accurate Screen and Grating (ASG) has an unmatched, expansive product line, with expert custom manufacturing. We are recognized throughout North America as an industry leader in full range metal screen and grating products.

We are proudly Canadian, with distribution and manufacturing locations in BC, AB, ON and QC. Through the strongest supply chain in Canada, we bring top-quality products for the industrial and commercial markets to every part of North America, all at very competitive prices.
