About nutrimeals

Full Description

Job Title: Mobile Developer Co-op (4 Months) May-August, with option to extend.
Company: Nurish’d (Nutrimeals Inc.)
Location: Calgary, Alberta (In-office Mon–Thurs, WFH Friday)
Term: 4-month Co-op (Potential to extend or convert full-time)
Start Date: ASAP
Compensation: Competitive (based on experience)

About Nurish’d

Nurish’d is a health-tech platform focused on making “food as medicine” accessible through personalized nutrition, medically tailored meals, and virtual dietitian services. We’re building digital infrastructure that connects healthcare, food, and data to improve health outcomes at scale.

About the Role

We’re looking for a Mobile Developer Co-op to help build and enhance our mobile application used by patients and dietitians. You’ll work closely with our product and engineering team to bring core user experiences to life from onboarding and meal selection to health tracking and communication features.

This is a hands-on role where you’ll ship real features used by real users.

What You’ll Do

  • Building core mobile app features used by real users
  • Shipping UI/UX directly from Figma to production
  • Working on flows like onboarding, meal selection, appt. booking, and health tracking
  • Integrating APIs and owning features end-to-end
  • Contributing to product decisions—not just executing tickets

What You’ll Work On

  • User onboarding & account setup
  • Meal discovery, selection, and ordering
  • Dietitian selection and booking
  • Health tracking & progress dashboards
  • Notifications and engagement features
  • Performance improvements and mobile UX optimization

Tech Stack

  • React Native (primary)
  • TypeScript / JavaScript
  • REST APIs
  • Modern state management tools
  • Figma (design handoff)

This role is for someone who:

  • Is in 3rd or 4th year of Computer Science / Software Engineering
  • Wants to build real-world, production-level mobile apps
  • Is comfortable figuring things out independently
  • Cares about writing clean, scalable code
  • Moves fast, but doesn’t cut corners

Requirements

  • Experience with JavaScript or TypeScript
  • Exposure to mobile development (React Native, Flutter, etc.)
  • Understanding of APIs and how apps communicate with backends
  • Strong problem-solving ability
  • Ability to work in-person in Calgary

Bonus Points

  • Experience with React Native specifically
  • Built or shipped a mobile app (even a personal project)
  • Familiar with state management (Redux, Zustand, etc.)
  • Eye for UI/UX and attention to detail
  • Interest in health-tech, fitness, or wellness

Why This Role is Different

  • You’ll own features, not just tasks
  • Your work will be live and used by real people
  • You’ll work directly with founders and move fast
  • You’ll gain experience most co-ops don’t offer
  • Strong performers may receive full-time offers

Perks

  • Free / discounted meals
  • Flexible Fridays (WFH)
  • Startup environment (fast-paced, high ownership)
  • Direct exposure to product, design, and business decisions
