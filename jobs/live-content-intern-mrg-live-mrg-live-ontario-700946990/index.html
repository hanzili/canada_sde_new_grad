Full Description

Live Content Intern

MRG Live | The MRG Group

Toronto, ON

MRG Live is seeking a Creative Content Intern to join our team! In this role, you’ll create and amplify engaging digital content that captures the energy of MRG Live events. You’ll assist in producing videos, graphics, and written content to support our social media, email campaigns, and marketing initiatives. Ideal candidates are skilled in content creation, up-to-date on social media trends, and passionate about live events.

About Our Internship Program

The MRG Group Internship Program is designed to provide you with practical skills, valuable experiences, and exposure to real-world scenarios in the Experience industry.

The program is structured to ensure that 80% of your time is dedicated to performing activities specific to their role, while the remaining 20% is allocated to job shadowing, skill development, and team projects across various businesses within the MRG Group.

About The MRG Group

Founded in 2008, The MRG Group is Canada's largest independent entertainment company and a leader in experience creation. Our mission is to bring experiences to life for creators, fans, brands, and partners.

The MRG Group is an integrated network of companies operating across the hospitality, venue, media, ticketing (including the ticketing service Admit One), and insurance sectors. With a portfolio of 18 hospitality properties across Canada, The MRG Group also owns and operates an events business, a travel company, and a digital publication.

Key Responsibilities

  • Concert Photography: Attend MRG Live events to capture high-quality photos that showcase the atmosphere, energy, and audience experience.
  • Content Creation: Generate compelling written and visual content including blog posts, graphics, and videos to support marketing initiatives and engage our audience.
  • Social Media Management: Assist in writing and scheduling show announcements across various MRG Live social accounts.
  • Email Marketing Campaigns: Assist in the creation, scheduling, and analysis of email campaigns to promote events and special offers, ensuring optimal audience engagement and conversion rates.
  • Brainstorming: Participate in brainstorming sessions to generate new and creative ideas for event promotions, fan engagement, and digital content.
  • Administrative Support: Assist with various administrative tasks such as maintaining marketing databases, organizing files, and coordinating meetings to ensure smooth operation of the marketing department.

Knowledge, Skills, Abilities

  • Strong foundation in photography and videography techniques
  • Proficient in photography and video editing software (e.g., Adobe Photoshop, Premiere Pro, or similar)
  • Knowledge of current social media trends, platforms and creatives
  • Ability to handle multiple projects at any one time and prioritize effectively
  • Ability to work flexible hours, including evenings and weekends for events

Internship Program Term

Start Date: August 5, 2026

End Date: December 18, 2026

What’s In It For You?

  • Paid Internship (Minimum Wage)
  • A fun and casual environment. You will be joining a close-knit, innovative team of creative and energetic go-getters
  • Employee discounts to our concerts, shows and hospitality venues
  • A welcoming, fun and safe work environment where everyone can show up authentically to work each day and feel respected
  • Career development opportunities with a company that encourages individuals to learn and grow

Thank you for your interest in The MRG Group. Given the anticipated interest in this role, only those selected for an interview will be contacted.
