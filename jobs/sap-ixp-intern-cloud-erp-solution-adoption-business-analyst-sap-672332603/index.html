Position title: SAP iXp Intern - Cloud ERP Solution Adoption Business Analyst

Location: Toronto, Canada

Expected start date: July 2026

Duration: 6 Months (potential for extension)

Schedule: 40 Hours per week

The goal of the Adoption Office Team is to set up scalable adoption capabilities and support Customer Success Management (CSMs) globally with driving customer renewals, adoption, expansion/upsells, and customer references. The focus of this internship will be to learn about the different core dimensions of what it means to build a Customer Success organization. The intern will be able to develop knowledge and capabilities in the following areas: SAP S/4HANA Cloud and Digital Supply Chain Solutions, as well as Data Insights, Analytics, Customer Success Strategy and Operations.

Expectations & Tasks Solution Adoption

  • Build data reports, dashboards on SAP Analytics Cloud, and ad hoc data analysis tasks
  • Prepare and deliver accurate, up‑to‑date data insights for recurring strategic cadence calls
  • Present findings and use data to actively engage stakeholders to collect feedback, drive action items, and ensure clear ownership of next steps
  • Analyze and communicate the impact of customer engagement initiatives to support customer adoption and informed decision‑making
  • Develop Metrics standard to drive usage and consumption for Cloud Solutions

Operation Support

  • Collect, clean and process large quantities of data
  • Navigate through different data tools to ensure delivered business outcomes
  • Develop tools and SOP to help scale and increase business insights

Stakeholder Management

  • Collaborate across teams to deliver information, resources, and best practices
  • Engage and align with Customer Success Partners, Product and Solution Managers, Marketing and Analytics Teams
  • Own the end‑to‑end readiness of strategic decks, ensuring all required inputs are incorporated and finalized ahead of key meetings

Key Responsibilities Will Relate To

  • Customer Tracking
  • Data management and analytics
  • Adoption Campaign Impact Tracking
  • Customer Relationship Management
  • Customer Engagement Meeting Asset Creation

What You Bring We’re looking for someone who takes initiative, perseveres, and stay curious. You like to work on meaningful innovative projects and are energized by lifelong learning.

  • Currently pursuing or completed a degree (Bachelor’s, Master’s) in Business Administration, Marketing, Economics, Management Information System (MIS) or Business Technology Management (BTM).
  • Language skills: Business fluent in English. Additional language is a plus.
  • The candidate must have a combination of technical and business experience.
  • The candidate should be a team player, with a self-starter attitude and the ability to work with minimal direction. Good communication/writing and organization skills are important.
  • The candidate should be able to synthesize and analyze large quantities of data, whilst showing a creative and pragmatic approach to problem solving.
  • Above all, they should be passionate about technology and business.

Preferred Skills Or Competencies

  • Strong Relationship/Interpersonal skills
  • Data Analysis
  • Excel
  • Understand and code in Python
  • Data Management
  • Detail Oriented
  • Curious
  • Demonstrated Self Starter
  • Strong written communication skills (email, PPT, case studies)
  • Strong verbal communication skills
  • Strong organizational skills
  • Business Acumen
  • Consultative Skills
  • Strong collaboration skills
  • Demonstrated previous experience in the above areas.
  • Previous SAP experience is an asset

Where You Belong Be part of SAP Next Gen, a global community for students, universities, schools and educational partners, who are passionate about innovation and technology.

  • Culture of Collaboration: Partner with experienced SAP colleagues and expert mentors who will support your growth. Grow professionally through personalized mentoring, coaching, and career development support.

You’ll have endless learning resources at your fingertips and gain future-ready skills from a variety of virtual, in-person, and hybrid learning sessions, cultivated just for you, and aligned with our learning approach.
  • Gain Visibility: Build relationships with leaders and peers across teams and functions. Showcase your ideas, skills, and creativity in a global, fast-paced environment. Open doors for future career opportunities within SAP and beyond
