Powered by Passion. United by Purpose. Build for Impact.

So, if you're ready to play with purpose, grow with passion, and win as one, we’d love to have you on our team.

Job Description

Reporting to the Manager, Accounting, the Accounting Intern will have the unique opportunity to gain hands-on experience in developing technical accounting skills as part of a leading Sports & Entertainment Company by assisting and coordinating a variety of core functions at MLSE such as ticketing, global partnerships, and live events and developing communication skills by liaising with other finance team members and cross-functional business units.

MLSE's Internship term dates are from May 4, 2026 - August 28, 2026

Responsibilities:

  • Assist in journalizing daily cash depository receipts (CDRs).
  • Assist in analyzing contracts in Salesforce month-over-month.
  • Assist in journalizing credit card chargebacks.
  • Assist in journalizing live event suite settlements.
  • Reconciling balance sheet accounts.
  • Assist in journalizing Ticketmaster advances.
  • Assist in journalizing daily merch sales activity
  • Participate in special projects as needed.

Qualifications:

  • You are working towards the completion of an undergraduate degree and the pre-requisites for entry into the CPA Professional Education Program. You also have the intention to pursue a CPA designation.
  • You have strong analytical skills and the ability to analyze and interpret data.
  • Experience with Microsoft Office Suites (Excel, PowerPoint, and Word) – candidates with demonstrated skill at Excel (performing VLOOKUP’s, pivot tables, and other data cleaning abilities) are considered an asset.
  • You demonstrate initiative and can work independently and as part of a team.
  • You have strong interpersonal and communication (written and verbal) skills.
  • You can establish and maintain effective working relationships with other departments.
  • You possess outstanding organization skills with the ability to independently manage your time and organize multiple work activities and inputs.
  • You love learning and constantly applying new skills to unfamiliar tasks.
  • Available for regular weekday shifts during normal business hours.
