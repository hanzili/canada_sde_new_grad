About the Role

Process entries, reconciliations, and tax filings

About eXp Realty

Remote real estate brokerage with large distributed team

Full Description

We are one of the first all-remote companies with 2,000+ team members utilizing our virtual office called eXp World. This role is work-from-anywhere in British Columbia, Canada.

  • Please note: This is a 12-month, fixed-term contract position.***

*Who is eXp?* Doing the audacious is part of eXp Realty’s DNA. We are a company that rewards creative minds who can offer bold solutions, where opinions matter, anything is possible and the outcome can be revolutionary. As a global full-service real estate brokerage utilizing a 3-D, fully immersive, cloud office environment, offering 24/7 access to collaborative tools & training, eXp has broken the traditional mold of real estate. We are doing big things. Come join us!

What You Will Do

  • All daily, weekly and monthly financial entries, reporting and reconciliations
  • Filing sales taxes (GST/HST, PST, QST) and make necessary payments
  • Monitoring inter-provincial balances
  • Assisting in preparing year-end tax forms and filing such as T4A
  • Assisting in preparing agents requested forms
  • Generating reports from Back Office
  • Downloading expected invoices on time from various vendor systems
  • Assisting in inputting invoices into Back Office
  • Monitoring vendor accounts
  • Following up with uncleared vendor payments
  • Assisting in answering agents’ questions (i.e. client enquiries) regarding their expenses
  • Assist in implementing effective internal controls and process improvement/documentation
  • Other duties that may arise from time to time as required by Financial Specialist, Finance Team Lead and AR/AP team

How You Will Grab Our Attention

  • Accounting diploma or equivalent required
  • Working towards a CPA designation an asset
  • Possess Strong written and verbal communication skills
  • Minimum 1-2 years’ experience in a related role
  • Tech-Savvy. Must be comfortable in a remote environment working with various
  • communication technologies
  • Working knowledge of Excel and Google Sheets
  • Strong organizational skills
  • Excellent analytical skills
  • Excellent time management skills

If you think you’d make a great match for this position but don’t meet all the requirements, we would still encourage you to apply!

What EXp Canada Provides

  • Fully remote environment
