Job Description Geotechnical Technician Cambridge, ON At EXP, we’re driven to provide innovative solutions for the world’s built and natural environments. As a team of engineers, architects, designers, scientists, creators and a community of professionals, we bring diverse and talented people together to solve the world’s most complex challenges. Here, you join a team that leverages differences, harnesses their entrepreneurial spirit in an employee-owned company, that believes diversity is what gives us strength, seeks sustainable results and shares ambitions for each other, our clients and the communities we are part of and serve. To be a part of EXP means to have your own experience, while staying connected to a global network of professionals, who believe we are a part of something bigger. Together, we are EXP.

Are you ready to design your future?

Be our next Geotechnical Technician! Your work environment at EXP We have an immediate opening for a Geotechnical Technicianto join our Materials Testing group in our Cambridge, ON office.

What a day at EXP has in store for you

  • Carrying out aggregate, soil tests in the field and within our Aggregate Laboratories
  • Soil Identification and Examination of Subgrade Soils
  • Insitu Density and Compaction Testing
  • Concrete, Mortar and Grout Sampling and Testing
  • Asphalt Sampling and Testing
  • Ensuring field and laboratory equipment is maintained in good condition
  • Assisting management team in field and laboratory certification, and record keeping
  • Interacting with Materials and Geotechnical Engineers routinely
  • Operating computerized and digital testing equipment
  • Field and Laboratory result report writing

What Your Experience Looks Like

  • Minimum 2 years' experience in field and Aggregate, testing Laboratory
  • College diploma an asset
  • Field experience in concrete, earthworks and asphalt
  • CCIL/CSA certified for field concrete testing (required), laboratory certification an asset
  • Strong communication skills and leadership capabilities
  • Ability to work overtime in peak periods
  • Excellent organizational skills and time management a must
  • Proficiency with the windows operating system, Microsoft Excel
  • Able to organize, prioritize, set and adjust priorities and schedules independently
