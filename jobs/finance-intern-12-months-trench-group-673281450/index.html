About the Role

About Trench Group

Energy equipment maker focused on reliability.

Full Description

On-site | Full time | Student (not yet graduated) About Us In Trench Canada, we deliver state-of-the-art, safe, and reliable instrument transformers, coils, and bushings for seamless energy transmission. With a legacy of customized engineering and innovation, we enable society to harness the power of electricity with reliability and performance. But we are more than just a company. We are a force for change, committed to advancing the energy sector through cutting-edge technology and sustainable practices. We genuinely care about continuous improvement, and our dynamic workplace values attention to detail and adaptability, fostering a culture of equality, diversity, and inclusion where everyone is respected and truly valued.

*Our Values*

  • Trust
  • Responsibility
  • Excellence
  • Navigating Change
  • Candor
  • Humility

Your Tasks:

  • Assist in ensuring internal controls related to revenue, cost monitoring, and budget tracking.
  • Provide support in the month-end close process, helping with the preparation of journal entries, accruals, and reconciliation activities.
  • Collaborate with the controlling team to maintain financial reports, KPIs, and dashboards for monthly analysis.
  • Support expense reviews and cost control initiatives.
  • Help prepare financial presentations for internal stakeholders.
  • Assist in documenting and improving internal processes and workflows, always under the team’s supervision.

Requirements:

  • Currently pursuing a degree in Finance, Accounting, Business Administration, or a related field.
  • Intermediate knowledge of Microsoft Excel (e.g., formulas, pivot tables, charts).
  • Intermediate knowledge of PowerPoint for developing corporate presentations.
  • Understanding of financial concepts.
  • Strong analytical skills, attention to detail, and willingness to learn.
  • Ability to collaborate with different teams and adapt to a corporate environment.
  • Excellent verbal and written communication skills in English.
  • Experience with SAP will be considered an asset.

We offer:

  • The opportunity to gain practical experience in the field of optical current measurement technology and sustainable energy transmission in an innovative company
  • You will work in a motivated team on forward-looking projects and gain insights into the latest technologies
  • Support from experienced professionals in handling the latest measurement technology
  • An hourly rate of pay of CAD $24 - $26.
