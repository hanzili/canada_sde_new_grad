Business Analyst (Contract / Internship)

About the Role

Conduct market research; support business development and marketing.

About Neutronium Headwear Ltd.

Growing apparel headwear company; learning-focused opportunity

Full Description

Job Description

Neutronium Headwear Ltd. is a Canadian company specializing in premium custom caps and headwear solutions for domestic and international clients.

We are looking for a self-driven, curious, and motivated Business & Marketing Associate to help lead key business and marketing initiatives. This role is ideal for someone who enjoys independent research, continuous learning, and exploring industry opportunities while working directly with the Director on real projects.

This is a hands-on role with meaningful responsibility, where you will gain exposure to marketing strategy, business development, and industry research.

This position is intended as a path toward a potential full-time role, depending on performance and company needs.

Responsibilities

Market Research & Industry Analysis

  • Research and analyze relevant trade shows and industry events, creating structured timelines and evaluating them based on cost, attendance size, and B2B opportunity potential.
  • Conduct independent research on industry trends, competitors, and market developments.

Business Development & Client Communication

  • Maintain relationships with existing clients and periodically introduce new products or updates.
  • Conduct outreach to potential clients identified through trade show and market research.
  • Maintain organized prospect lists and communication records.
  • Coordinate effectively with internal team members and communicate professionally with suppliers and clients.

Marketing & Social Media

  • Manage and maintain company social media channels such as Instagram, LinkedIn, and other platforms as the company expands its digital presence.
  • Develop basic social media content and coordinate with the internal design team for visual assets.
  • Work with the Director to develop social media strategies aligned with business goals, while managing day-to-day posting and engagement.

Business Operations Support

  • Assist in preparing monthly business performance summaries, including basic cost tracking and revenue reporting.
  • Support operational coordination and internal project updates.

Qualifications

  • Currently pursuing or recently completed a Bachelor’s degree in Business, Marketing, or a related field. Candidates from Fashion, Apparel, or Design programs are also welcome.
  • 0–3 years of relevant experience (internships or project experience welcome).
  • Strong proficiency in Microsoft Office, particularly Excel and Outlook.
  • Comfortable using modern tools such as AI tools (e.g., ChatGPT) for research and organization.
  • Strong written and verbal communication skills.
  • Self-motivated with strong curiosity and willingness to learn independently.
  • Interest in the apparel, fashion, or headwear industry.
  • Ability to manage tasks independently and take initiative.
  • Work proficiency in English; ability to read and understand Mandarin for supplier communication.

Preferred Qualifications (Nice to Have)

The following qualifications are considered assets but are not required. Candidates who meet some, but not all, of these criteria are encouraged to apply:

  • Experience in digital marketing or social media management
  • Experience in the fashion, apparel, or accessories industry
  • Basic content creation or graphic design experience
  • Experience managing online advertising campaigns
  • Knowledge of French

Work Arrangement

  • Primarily remote
  • Occasional in-person meetings in Richmond Hill, Ontario
  • Average workload: 15–20 hours per week
  • Strong performers may be considered for a future full-time role
