Company Description

RenderSEO is a marketing technology company dedicated to helping local businesses enhance their online visibility. With a powerful local search management platform and exceptional client support, RenderSEO provides tailored solutions for businesses to connect with customers effectively. The team comprises experienced professionals from the SEO industry, offering a diverse range of services for enterprise and agency clients. Known for flexibility and customization, RenderSEO thrives on creating bespoke solutions to meet unique client needs.

Role Description

This is a full-time remote role for a Junior Full Stack Engineer. The successful candidate will work on developing and maintaining web applications, collaborating with cross-functional teams to implement new features and ensure seamless functionality. The role involves working on both front-end and back-end development tasks, troubleshooting issues, and delivering high-quality software solutions. Candidates should exhibit a strong passion for learning, coding, and working in a dynamic environment to deliver exceptional user experiences.

  • Strong understanding of Back-End and Front-End Development, including HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, React, Python, Lambda, AWS, Cloudfront
  • Knowledge of Back-End Web Development and Full-Stack Development methodologies
  • Experience in Software Development with a focus on building scalable applications
  • Ability to effectively collaborate with teams and adapt to new technologies
  • Strong problem-solving skills and a keen attention to detail
  • Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Software Engineering, or a related field, or equivalent practical experience
  • Familiarity with Angular or Node.js is a plus
