Commercial Banking Advisor - Intern- EN

at RBC

📍 Location Montreal, Quebec
📋 Details Full-time
📅 Posted Apr 10, 2026

About the Role

Coordinate onboarding, service clients, deliver banking solutions

About RBC

Big Canadian bank; strong client service culture

Full Description

Job Description What is the opportunity?

  • Partnering with Account Managers to enable the identification, planning and delivery of solutions to client and prospects
  • Enabling delivery of the best sales solutions with Account Managers based on strong knowledge of client’s operational requirements (primarily Cash Management and Deposit Solutions)

What will you do?

  • Deliver the best-in-class Onboarding experience by coordinating the on-boarding of products and services for existing and new clients
  • Act as service champion for our CFS clients; responding to client concerns by thoroughly analyzing the situation, searching for solutions, identify and leverage appropriate partners to resolve client concerns.
  • Serve clients well by proactively reviewing the ever changing day to day banking needs and identifying and recommending the best solutions for the client.
  • Actively participate in ongoing contact programs to forge strong relationships with clients by staying in touch.

What do you need to succeed? Must-have

  • Approximately 2 years of demonstrated excellence in a service and sales support role working in collaboration with a sales team providing advice, responding to client requests and navigating your organization to solve client issues
  • Proven customer service skills in a financial or services industry e.g. retail, hospitality
  • Requires fluency in French and English in order to serve our clients in the community with English speaking needs.
  • Bilingualism (English and French) required, as you will regularly serve our clients with English and French-speaking needs.

Nice-to-have

  • Strong sales orientation with a passion for fulfilling client needs
  • Strong interpersonal and negotiation skills
  • Entrepreneurial attitude and self-motivation to achieve above average results
  • Continuous learner with a deep curiosity for understanding his/her diverse customers and is up to date with changes in client needs, procedures, and relevant products.
