About The Job:

As a Junior Accountant – Accounts Receivable you will be responsible for the following:

  • Process all accounts receivable information in Great Plains via POS imports for a section of 25+ restaurant locations.
  • Review and reconcile the weekly Cashout Control Logs and Gift Card balances.
  • Assist with bank reconciliations for the Quebec restaurant locations.
  • Follow up on discrepancies when applying cash and credit card receivables.
  • Review and reconcile all inter-company receivable accounts and balance the AR subledgers.
  • Reconcile supplier statements as needed.
  • Deal with supplier inquiries on a timely basis.
  • Assist Senior Accountants.
  • Provide assistance with external auditor requests and pull back-up as required.
  • Other administrative duties as required.

About You:

  • Degree/diploma in Business/Accounting or equivalent working experience.
  • Working knowledge of Microsoft Excel, Word & an accounting software application (Great Plains preferred), as well as SutiSoft online application.
  • Experience working in a fast paced and deadline oriented environment.
  • Excellent written and verbal communication skills.
  • The ability to prioritize, multi-task and self-motivate.
  • Possess five values that define our people:
  • Hospitality. Enhancing the dignity, comfort and well-being of Our People
  • Authenticity. Being forthright, truthful and transparent with Our People
  • Quality. Working to always improve the experience of Our People

  • Courage. Acting with conviction in the balanced interest of Our People
