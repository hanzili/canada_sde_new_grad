About the Role

About BBA Consultants

Full Description

Type of position: Regular

Your future role on our team By joining our team, you’ll be asked to help with the various stages of project execution, including proposals, site surveys, the design and drafting of technical reports and final delivery to the site. You’ll benefit from BBA’s network of recognized experts to support you in completing your mandates and in your professional development.

You’ll also be responsible for ensuring quality professional services and maintaining strong client relationships.

With us, you’ll get the opportunity to...

  • Carry out engineering projects involving power systems (power distribution, grid connection)
  • Carry out engineering projects involving specialized studies (power quality, grounding, electromagnetic interference,
  • NERC compliance, transient)
  • Perform system modelling using specialized software
  • Draft high-quality technical reports to support our clients
  • Demonstrate skills in managing relationships with clients and partners
  • Inspire respect and trust in others and create a climate conducive to teamwork

Do your qualities and values match our corporate culture?

  • Rigorous and ethical
  • Ability to see beyond established standards
  • Ability to share and pass on knowledge
  • Results-driven
  • Thirst to learn and excel
  • Constantly seeking sustainable and agile solutions

Overview Of Certifications And Job Requirements

  • Bachelor’s degree in Electrical Engineering from a recognized university
  • Member in good standing with EGBC and registered as an EIT
  • Knowledge of system simulation software: CYME, EMPT, PSS/E
  • Strong communication (verbal and written in English), interpersonal, and relationship-building skills
  • A valid driver’s license
  • Able to travel to customer sites and other BBA offices within Canada, the USA, and international destinations for project short-term assignments, such as site meetings, field reviews, site assessments, tests, etc.

An Overview Of BBA’s Total Awards

  • Annual base salary
  • Annual premium program for regular employees.
  • Access to a time bank.
  • Onsite mobility premium.
  • Cellphone Program.
  • Group insurance plan starting day one including short-term and long-term disability insurance for regular employees.
  • Retirement saving plan for regular employees.
  • Vacation and sick leave.
  • Premium offered through the Employee Referral program.

BBA considers many factors to determine the annual base salary such as, but no limited to: market, education, relevant and demonstrated work experience, technical assessment, qualifications, certifications, and other relevant competencies.

With Us, You’ll Get Many Benefits

  • A flexible schedule that allows you to work at the office and from home
  • A caring environment where everyone’s ideas are listened to and where there’s no sense of hierarchy
  • Access to many experts and coaching
  • Custom training and development plans
  • Opportunity for site work: an amazing career accelerator
  • A vibrant social club with something for everyone

About BBA BBA is one of Canada’s leading private consulting engineering firms, with over 45 years of experience serving the energy and natural resources industry.

Our people are the foundation of our success. Their passion and excellence have earned us recognition as one of Canada’s Best Employers and Best Managed Companies , and we’re committed to fostering a workplace where everyone feels empowered to grow, lead and be themselves.

Our teams bring together engineering, environmental and commissioning expertise to deliver practical, innovative and sustainable solutions—from strategy to execution. With 20 offices across Canada, the U.S. and Latin America, we combine local presence with international reach, offering clients close collaboration and field-ready support.
