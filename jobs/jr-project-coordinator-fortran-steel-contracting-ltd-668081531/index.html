About Fortran Steel Contracting Ltd

Mentorship, growth, long-term steel project progression

Full Description

Fortran Steel Contracting Ltd. is a leading structural steel and miscellaneous metals fabrication company serving commercial, industrial, and institutional construction projects. We are currently seeking a motivated and career-driven Junior Project Coordinator to join our growing Ottawa team.

This is an excellent opportunity for someone looking to build a long-term career in the structural steel industry. The successful candidate will work closely with our Senior Project Coordinators and leadership team, gaining hands-on experience in all aspects of project execution with a clear path for growth into a Senior Project Coordinator role.

We are looking for someone who is organized, proactive, eager to learn, and ready to take ownership in a fast-paced construction environment.

Key Responsibilities

(Including but not limited to)

  • Work closely with Project Coordinators, subcontractors, site supervisors, shop foremen, and internal teams to support active projects
  • Assist with project planning, scheduling, tracking, and day-to-day coordination of multiple jobs
  • Source materials and assist with procurement for upcoming and ongoing projects
  • Confirm drawings, materials, and site readiness prior to deliveries
  • Take field measurements for fabrication, installation, and coordination purposes
  • Attend site meetings, progress meetings, and internal coordination meetings as required
  • Review erection drawings with subcontractors prior to installation
  • Help manage RFIs, submittals, change orders, extras, schedules, and project documentation
  • Maintain accurate project records including costs, inspections, timelines, and communications
  • Assist with remedial / repair details and field issue resolution
  • Conduct site safety walks while promoting safe work practices and company standards
  • Support estimating or pricing for project changes when required

Qualifications & Skills

  • Ability to read and interpret structural and architectural drawings and specifications accurately
  • Working knowledge of structural steel fabrication, erection, and installation practices
  • Familiarity with OBC, CISC, CWB, CSA standards, and applicable construction codes
  • Strong understanding of construction materials, methods, and project sequencing
  • Highly organized with strong time-management and multitasking skills
  • Strong communication skills and confidence dealing with trades, clients, and project teams
  • Able to work independently, take initiative, and contribute as part of a team
  • Thrives in a fast-paced, deadline-driven environment
  • Proficient with Microsoft Office (Excel, Outlook, Word); project management software is an asset
  • Valid driver’s license required
  • Must be legally authorized to work in Canada

Experience Required

  • Minimum 1–2 years of experience in structural steel, miscellaneous metals, or related construction industry
  • Prior project coordination or construction management experience is considered a strong asset

Language Requirements

  • Fluent in English (spoken, reading, and writing)
  • French is considered an asset

Career Growth Opportunity

At Fortran Steel Contracting Ltd., we believe in promoting from within. This role is designed for someone who wants to grow into a Senior Project Coordinator position with increased responsibility, leadership opportunities, and long-term career advancement.

Why Join Fortran Steel?

  • Strong mentorship from experienced industry professionals
  • Clear path for advancement
  • Diverse and exciting construction projects
  • Stable, growing company with long-term opportunities
  • Competitive compensation based on experience

If you’re ready to grow your career in structural steel project management, we’d like to hear from you.
