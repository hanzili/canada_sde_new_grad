About the Opportunity

Our client is a private equity-backed leader in Canada's hospitality sector, operating a diverse portfolio of restaurants, event spaces, and strategic partnerships with revenues in the $250–500M range. With a high-growth agenda and a finance team actively modernizing its systems and processes, this is an exciting opportunity to build strong accounting fundamentals in a fast-paced, multi-location environment. Reporting to the AP Manager, the Junior Accountant will play a key role in the accounts payable cycle, supporting high-volume invoice processing and vendor management as the organization scales.

Key Responsibilities

• Process high-volume AP invoices end-to-end, including coding, three-way matching, and approvals.

• Prepare and support regular payment runs while maintaining accurate AP aging.

• Reconcile vendor statements and resolve payment discrepancies with internal and external stakeholders.

• Reconcile credit cards and post AP and expense accruals.

• Support process improvement and ERP implementation initiatives as the finance function evolves.

What You Bring

• Currently enrolled in or recently graduated from a post-secondary Accounting program.

• Intermediate Excel skills (formulas, pivot tables) are required.

• Strong attention to detail and comfort processing high volumes of transactions accurately.

• Experience or exposure to accounts payable and procure-to-pay processes is an asset.

• A coachable, detail-oriented mindset with a drive to build strong accounting fundamentals.
