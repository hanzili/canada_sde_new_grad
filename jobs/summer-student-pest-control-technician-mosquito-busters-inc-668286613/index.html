About the Role

About Mosquito Busters Inc.

Seasonal pest control company with training program

Full Description

Mosquito Busters is seeking a Summer Student to assist Pest Control services, treatments, inspections and consultations to residential throughout the Muskoka and Parry Sound regions. We are growing our team and looking for a motivated and hard-working team member who is passionate about solving problems and helping people. We will provide you with training and mentorship from experienced technicians, hands-on experience in the pest management field and support for obtaining your exterminator license.

REQUIREMENTS:

  • We will help you obtain a Structural Exterminator License (we will provide you training material, help you study, and apply for the exam)
  • Ability to drive on all type of roads including highways and rural roads.
  • Willing to work weekends and over time as required according to the needs of our clients.
  • Time management skills
  • Must be willing to learn how to do small maintenance repairs on sprayers and other equipment.
  • Ability to lift 30lbs and work on ladders
  • Be reliable and work well with little supervision

This is a yearly seasonal position April-October

Job Types: Full-time, Seasonal
Contract length: 6 months

Pay: $20.00-$25.00 per hour

Expected hours: 35 – 44 per week

Education:

  • Secondary School (preferred)

Language:

  • English (preferred)
