Capital Markets Operations Associate

About the Role

Coordinate deal closings, monitor drawdowns, support operations

About Societe Generale

Global bank with diverse capital markets operations

Full Description

Reference 25000M90

Responsibilities

  • Prepare and complete the Agency Team Closing Checklist for new deals & amendments.
  • Management of deals throughout life of deal (i.e. amendments, terminations)
  • Monitor all drawdown activity to ensure sufficient availability by reviewing the Borrowing Request notice, Borrowing Base report & related documents (i.e. Borrowing Base Certificate, Compliance Certificate).
  • Create & monitor required covenants for each deal and ensure completeness.
  • Collaborate with the Transaction Management and Life Cycle Management teams within the Capital Markets Finance Operations team.
  • Coordinate deal closings/amendments with the Distribution & Credit Solutions (DCS) & Credit Portfolio Management (CPM) teams.
  • Manage client requests and ensure swift resolution
  • Manage deal site for Agency Team
  • Assist with the build out of the Agency Team within the Capital Markets Finance Operations team.

SKILLS AND EXPERIENCE NEEDED:

Technical Skills Required/Must have:

  • Excel (Advanced)
  • Microsoft Office
  • High level of accuracy

Desired/Plus:

  • Knowledge of Fund Financing business lines
  • Understanding of Loan IQ operating system
  • Basic understanding of financial accounting principles

COMPETENCIES

Required/Must have:

  • Strong analytical skills
  • Good communication and writing skills
  • High computer aptitude
  • Orientation to details and deadlines
  • Must be able to work under pressure and manage time efficiently to meet daily deadlines
  • Strong organizational skills and ability to multi-task with minimal follow-up
  • Problem solving abilities
  • Professionalism in interacting with clients and management
  • High degree of organizational skills
  • Teamwork
  • Willingness to cross-train on several varied functions

Desired/Plus:

  • Ability to promptly escalate Operational Risk issues
  • Flexibility - Adaptability

Prior Work Experience Required/Must have:

3-5 years experience in financial operations

Desired/Plus:

Work experience in Operations or related field a plus

EDUCATION

Required/Must have:

  • Bachelor’s degree

Required Profile required LANGUAGE: Ability to communicate in English, both orally and in writing, is a requirement as the person in this position will need to collaborate regularly with colleagues and partners in the United States.
