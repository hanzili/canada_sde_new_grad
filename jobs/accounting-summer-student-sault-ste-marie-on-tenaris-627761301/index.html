Full Description

At Tenaris, you will join a global network of professionals working together toward a common goal: shape the future of energy through advanced tubular products and services.

If you want to become part of a diverse team that values results, analytical approach to problem solving and teamwork, Tenaris is the place for you.

Accounting Summer Student

LOCATION: Sault Ste. Marie, ON

DEPARTMENT: Accounting and Finance

How do you plan on spending your summer? Does joining an innovative team, making a great wage and building your portfolio for future work, while being supported by a dedicated mentor peak your interest? If so, we want to hear from you!

Tenaris is looking for a number of outstanding students with a safety-first mindset, who are eager to learn, and are innovators in search of a better way. Our Summer Student Program provides students with the opportunity to take their first steps towards a challenging, international and diverse environment.

Summer Student Program

Through the Summer Student Program, you’ll work collaboratively with multiple areas of our business, while receiving high-quality training and mentoring. Tenaris believes that career and development opportunities are key to our future success, which is why we make it a priority to engage our junior team members in strategic, impactful initiatives.

To be eligible for the Summer Student Program, you must have recently completed at least one year of post-secondary education, ending in April 2026, with intent to continue on with your studies in the fall. Whether you’re enrolled in university, college, or technical institution levels, it is expected that you intend to complete an undergraduate degree through the course of your studies.

We are looking for an Accounting major student to lead and improve stock count process of Tenaris Yards in Canada. The successful candidate will:

  • Complete the stock counts in a timely manner
  • help improve the stock count process in Tenaris Canada
  • Assist with other accounting responsibilities as required

NOTE: the role will require the summer student to travel to Alberta for 1 week to assist with the physical count of pipes

Skills

  • Proficient in Microsoft Office, specifically Excel
  • Effective communication skills
  • Analytical, result oriented and collaborative team member
  • Adaptable and resourceful

Ideal Characteristics

  • Innovative– anticipates the implications of situations, and shares out-of-the box solutions to business problems.
  • Demonstrate Business Acumen – keenly and quickly understand and manage business situations, risks, and outcomes, in a manner that is likely to lead to a good outcome.
  • Committed to Safety – genuinely committed to be a champion of health and safety, both in personal and professional capacities.
  • Committed to Teamwork & Relationship Development – collaborative, authentic approach to developing internal and external relationships, to understand and achieve common goals.
  • Accountable – takes responsibility for one’s own performance by setting clear goals and tracking progress against those goals.
  • Analytical – ability to collect and analyze information and data, making recommendations and decisions based on reasonable analysis.
  • Flexible – effectively manages multiple assignments, adapts to changing priorities and is able to work independently or as part of a team.

Compensation

The hourly range for this role is $24.00 - $31.25 / hour.

Actual compensation within this range will be determined based on factors such as the candidate’s experience, education, skills, and qualifications, as well as internal equity and market considerations.
