About MX3 Connections

Full Description

Looking for a full-time role where you can start quickly, stay active, and earn based on your effort?

We are hiring Entry Level Sales Representatives to support our expansion across the Surrey area. This is a people-focused role where you will be speaking directly with customers, introducing essential services, and helping them choose options that fit their needs.

No previous sales experience is required. If you are reliable, comfortable speaking with people, and ready to learn, you will be trained and supported from day one.

Key Responsibilities:

  • Speak with customers in person across local areas
  • Introduce internet, wireless, and home service options
  • Keep conversations simple, clear, and focused on customer needs
  • Answer questions and help customers make confident decisions
  • Stay consistent with daily activity and maintain a strong work routine
  • Work alongside your team to improve results and stay motivated

Qualifications:

  • Comfortable working in a face-to-face, people-focused role
  • Positive attitude and willingness to learn
  • Strong communication skills
  • Reliable and able to stay consistent day-to-day
  • Customer service, retail, hospitality, or general labour experience is helpful, but not required
  • Full-time availability
  • Able to start within 1–2 weeks

What We Offer:

  • Full-time, consistent schedule
  • Hands-on training with ongoing support
  • Uncapped earning potential + performance bonuses
  • Car allowance and travel support
  • Growth opportunities into leadership roles
  • Supportive, team-oriented environment

This is a full-time position that involves working within local Surrey communities.

If you are looking for a role where you can get started quickly, build confidence, and earn based on your effort, apply today.

Job Types: Full-time, Permanent
