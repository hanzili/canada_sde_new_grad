Full Description

Job Title & Type:

Junior Project Coordinator - Full-Time

Location: Sudbury, ON
Company: Mansour Canadian Rail Motive Power
Application Deadline: February 27, 2026
Salary: Competitive salary based on experience ($45,000–$55,000 annually)

Company Overview:

Interested in working in an industry that powers Canada’s economy? With over 40 years of proven expertise, Mansour Canadian Rail Motive Power is a trusted leader in delivering tailored solutions for the industrial, mining, and rail sectors. We are fully certified to perform both surface and underground rail track repair and maintenance, ensuring the highest standards of safety and quality. When you join our team, you’ll be part of a company that supports critical infrastructure and prioritizes safety, collaboration, and mutual support while offering plenty of opportunities for professional growth.

Job Description:

We are seeking a Junior Project Coordinator to join our growing team. This entry-level role is ideal for individuals who are detail-oriented, organized, and eager to learn. You will assist in coordinating projects, managing documentation, and supporting business operations to ensure smooth workflows and timely delivery.

What We Offer:

  • 100% employer paid benefits (health, dental, vision, life)
  • RRSP/DPSP matching
  • Boot allowance
  • Paid uniforms
  • Supplemented winter gear program
  • Mon-Fri
  • EFAP (Employee & Family Assistance Program)
  • Travel insurance
  • Gym membership discount
  • Training and development opportunities

Duties and Responsibilities:

  • Assist in coordinating projects across departments and with external vendors and suppliers.
  • Prepare project estimates and develop work scopes.
  • Process time sheets and handle project-based invoicing for customers.
  • Prepare quotes and progress reports for ongoing projects.
  • Update project management records and dashboards to reflect current status.
  • Coordinate with suppliers and vendors to ensure timely delivery.
  • Support document control and compliance strategies.
  • Willingness to visit project sites within the city when required.

Requirements and Qualifications:

  • Post-secondary education in Project Management, Business Administration, or a related field.
  • 0–2 years of experience in project coordination or related role.
  • Ability to interpret technical instructions and read plans/specifications.
  • Understanding of safety standards and compliance requirements.
  • Knowledge of general contract and subcontract documents.
  • Proficiency with Microsoft Office and basic computer systems.
  • Must be able to successfully complete all pre‑employment requirements, including a drug and alcohol test, criminal background check, and driver’s abstract.

Preferred Skills:

  • Familiarity with project management software (e.g., Smart Sheets, Microsoft project, GP Dynamics).
  • Experience with invoicing and vendor coordination.
  • Exposure to technical drawings, specifications, or industrial projects.

Apply now to gain hands-on experience in project coordination while contributing to critical infrastructure projects in Northern Ontario!

Schedule & Work Conditions

  • Monday-Friday 7:00 AM – 3:30 PM
  • Reliable transportation to report to assigned work locations

Eligibility Information:

Funding for this position may be provided through the Northern Ontario Heritage Fund Corporation (NOHFC) under the Workforce Development Stream.

To qualify for NOHFC internship funding, applicants must:

  • Be new entrants to the workforce, transitioning to a new career, or unemployed/underemployed and entering a new field
  • Not have previously participated in a NOHFC-funded internship
  • Be at least 18 years of age
  • Reside in Canada and be legally entitled to work in Canada

For full program eligibility details, please visit Workforce Development Program - Home | NOHFC
