September 2026 - Data Enablement (CPA) - 8 month Co-op - Vancouver

at PwC Canada

About the Role

Build data pipelines; support audits through automation

About PwC Canada

Big 4 firm; CPA support program

Full Description

At PwC, our people in data and analytics engineering focus on leveraging advanced technologies and techniques to design and develop robust data solutions for clients. They play a crucial role in transforming raw data into actionable insights, enabling informed decision-making and driving business growth.

Those in data and automation at PwC will focus on automating data internally using automation tools or software to streamline data-related processes within the organisation. Your work will involve automating data collection, data analysis, data visualisation, or any other tasks that involve handling and processing data.

At PwC Canada, we’re more than a professional services firm—we’re a community of solvers tackling the world’s most important problems. As an Intern in our Risk Services practice, you’ll be part of a diverse, inclusive team that values your perspective and empowers you to make a real impact. You’ll build relationships, gain hands-on experience with cutting-edge technologies, and contribute to work that matters—whether it’s helping clients navigate complex challenges or driving trust in financial reporting.

You’ll be joining a firm that’s invested in your success from day one. As a new Associate, you’ll benefit from PwC Canada’s industry-leading CPA support program, which includes paid study days, reimbursement for exam materials, and personalized coaching to help you succeed in your designation journey. You’ll also gain hands-on experience with our digital tools and participate in our national upskilling program, designed to help you build future-ready capabilities in data, technology, and business acumen. Working with market-leading clients across diverse industries, you’ll be exposed to complex, real-world challenges that accelerate your professional growth.

Beyond your work, PwC Canada offers a comprehensive health and wellness benefits package, including mental health support, flexible work options, and paid time off to volunteer in your community. You’ll also have access to leadership development opportunities, inclusive employee networks, and a vibrant culture that values belonging, purpose, and continuous learning.

Grow here. Go further.

The Opportunity: Data Enablement Intern At PwC, our people in data and analytics focus on leveraging data to drive insights and helping our clients make informed business decisions. A career in our Data Enablement practice, within Risk Services, will provide you with the opportunity to empower our business partners (external clients and other PwC operational units) to solve the problems that matter using data driven insights. Being human-led, tech-powered, we put the right controls, processes, and structures in place so our people and clients make confident, data-backed decisions to deliver on our promise of trust.

In this role, you will:

  • Assurance Development: Redefine how PwC delivers the audit of tomorrow by building a strong understanding of commonly used client information systems (ERPs) to develop automated solutions to problems encountered by existing engagement teams.
  • Specialised Assurance: Engage with clients in highly specialised assurance tasks, using advanced data analysis and automation to deliver results. Includes testing of key client reports, testing over client's ERP system conversions, creating Extract-Transform-Load (ETL) pipelines and developing solutions for industry specific assurance.
  • Data Analysis: Help clients manage their data better and feel confident that the information generated from their business data is reliable and can help them make the right business decisions using advanced data analysis techniques and capabilities.
  • Acquire Data: Work with clients to understand and set up and optimize the process to acquire comprehensive datasets used as the foundation of PwC's audits. This includes implementing the cutting-edge technology (such as PwC Extract) for data extraction and transformation to facilitate end to end automation.

With connectivity being a key enabler of our strategy, our objective is to become the most connected firm - connected to our clients, our communities and to each other. PwC follows an Activity Based Work model, where our PwC professionals engage in a diverse range of activities from our offices, from client sites, or our home.

Experiences and Skills You’ll Use to Solve:

  • Enrolled in post-secondary education and working to obtain the Canadian CPA required prerequisite courses
  • Motivated to learn about technologies, IT practices and standards, infrastructure-related best-practices in the areas of security and IT, as well as IT control frameworks
  • Motivated to learn programming languages such as Python, SQL and utilizing advanced data-platforms such as Databricks
  • Motivated to learn about analyzing the impact of data and IT risks and internal controls on organizations’ financial, strategic, and operational objectives
  • Proven ability to manage multiple tasks in a dynamic and constantly evolving environment
  • Recognized strong analytical, conceptualization, and problem-solving skills
  • Ability to work independently or as part of a team
  • Strong skills in prioritization and problem-solving

Note: We only allow you to submit one application; however you are able to select a second and third office preference.
