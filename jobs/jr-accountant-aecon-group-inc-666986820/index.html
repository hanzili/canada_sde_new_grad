What is the Opportunity? Faster. Smarter. Safer. This is how Aecon Utilities is committed to delivering projects. We simple do it better. We are Ontario’s premier utility contractor and are ready to become Canada’s #1 utility construction provider.

Reporting to the Sr. Manager, Finance, the Jr. Accountant will be responsible for providing financial and cost control support for various business units within the Utilities group.

Location: Head Office, Etobicoke

What You'll Do Here

  • Support job cost analysis and work‑in‑progress (WIP) reporting
  • Assist with the month‑end close process including preparation of journal entries for divisional financial statements, elimination entries, recurring entries, and accruals
  • Assist with the preparation of monthly management reports including financial statements and supporting schedules
  • Complete monthly inter‑company reconciliations and confirmations with other Aecon divisions
  • Prepare monthly joint venture billings and perform associated reconciliations
  • Post regional and corporate overheads to job cost
  • Perform account analysis and prepare working papers and schedules
  • Support interim and year‑end audit activities and reporting requirements
  • Assist with client reporting
  • Support preparation of the business plan and quarterly forecasts including entering and maintaining budgets and forecasts in the accounting system
  • Perform reconciliations and variance analysis ensuring accuracy and timely completion
  • Build and maintain Excel reporting models used for monthly and quarterly reporting
  • Document processes and contribute to continuous improvement initiatives within the function
  • Participate in special projects and initiatives as assigned

What You Bring To The Team

  • Related post‑secondary education and actively working toward an accounting designation such as CPA, CGA, CMA, or CA
  • Minimum 1 year of construction accounting experience
  • Strong proficiency with Microsoft Office with advanced Excel skills considered a strong asset
  • Experience with computerized accounting systems related to project costing with SAP experience preferred
  • Excellent organizational skills with the ability to manage multiple deadlines and tasks in a high‑pressure environment
  • Strong interpersonal and communication skills both written and verbal
  • Ability to work effectively as part of a team
  • Ability to work with a sense of urgency and shift priorities to accomplish objectives
  • Strong time‑management skills with the ability to maintain commitments to internal and external stakeholders
  • Clear and effective communication skills with the ability to convey information and clarify requirements both orally and in writing
