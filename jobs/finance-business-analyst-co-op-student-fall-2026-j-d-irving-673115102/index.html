Full Description

Our Finance team has over 200 finance professionals supporting our diverse group of Companies. We have a long-standing Student Work Term Program focused on providing students with hands on experience in a progressive future focused finance team. We are a proud CPA (Chartered Professional Account) Training Program office.

The Finance Shared Services team provides transactional, payroll, credit, treasury, data analysis and client services to the J.D. Irving, Limited group of Companies. The student opportunities will provide exposure to these financial services areas.

  • Work as part of a team focused on business analysis and reporting
  • Gain exposure and hands on learning to Tableau, Alteryx & Power BI
  • Assist with project work, and process improvements
  • Review of data and provide insights for opportunities for improvement

  • Assist in supporting Management and implementation of Finance initiatives

  • Please include your transcript, along with your resume and co-op eligiblity letter in your application.

  • University Business/Commerce Degree Student in Good Academic Standing
  • Available for the Fall 2026 Co-op Work Term Period
  • Proficiency with Microsoft Office Suite with an emphasis on MS Excel
  • Team Player with a Can-Do Attitude
  • Strong Communicator
  • Curious with a Process Improvement Mindset
  • Long Term Career interest in Accounting, Finance and/or Analytics

Irving Business Services is a vital support function within J.D. Irving, Limited, delivering expertise in essential areas such as Finance, Human Resources, Supply Chain, Health and Safety, Communications, and Legal.

By managing resources, delivering fast and flexible outcomes, and fostering innovation, Irving Business Services plays a critical role in driving operational success and supporting the company’s long-term strategic goals. With a focus on collaboration and continuous improvement, Irving Business Services offers exciting opportunities for growth and development within a leading organization.
