Full Description

Job Overview
We are seeking a highly skilled Full Stack Developer to join our dynamic development team. The ideal candidate will be responsible for designing, developing, and maintaining scalable web applications and software solutions. This role offers an exciting opportunity to work on innovative projects utilizing a broad range of technologies including JavaScript, Java, Python, C#, and cloud platforms such as AWS and Azure. The successful applicant will collaborate with cross-functional teams in an Agile environment to deliver high-quality software products that meet business needs.

Responsibilities

  • Develop and maintain robust web applications using front-end technologies like JavaScript, HTML, and CSS.
  • Build and optimize server-side components with languages such as Java, C#, Python, or Ruby on Rails.
  • Design, implement, and manage RESTful APIs to facilitate seamless communication between services.
  • Work with databases including MySQL, SQL Server, and NoSQL solutions, ensuring efficient data storage and retrieval.
  • Collaborate with product managers and designers to translate requirements into technical specifications.
  • Utilize version control systems such as GitHub, Git, and SVN for code management and collaboration.
  • Deploy applications on cloud platforms like AWS and Azure, ensuring scalability, security, and performance.
  • Participate in code reviews, testing, and debugging to ensure high-quality deliverables.
  • Follow best practices in software development methodologies such as Agile to ensure timely project delivery.
  • Maintain documentation of codebase, architecture, and deployment procedures for future reference.

Qualifications

  • Proven experience in full stack web development with proficiency in both front-end (JavaScript, HTML/CSS) and back-end (Java, C#, Python, or similar).
  • Strong understanding of database management systems including SQL, MySQL, and related technologies.
  • Experience working with cloud services such as AWS or Azure is highly desirable.
  • Familiarity with RESTful API design, integration, and consumption.
  • Knowledge of version control systems like GitHub, Git, or SVN.
  • Experience with application development within an Agile environment.
  • Ability to work independently or collaboratively within a team setting.
  • Excellent problem-solving skills with attention to detail in coding practices.
  • Knowledge of Linux operating systems is a plus.
  • Familiarity with additional languages such as C++, VBA, or frameworks like .NET can be advantageous. Join our team to contribute to innovative software solutions that drive our business forward while enhancing your skills across a diverse technology stack!
