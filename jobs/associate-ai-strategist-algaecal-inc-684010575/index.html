Full Description

Associate AI Strategist

Want to learn how AI opportunities turn into million-dollar business wins? As our Associate AI Strategist, you’ll learn how a fast-growing health brand spots high-value AI opportunities before everyone else, turns them into custom agentic solutions, and uses them to unlock serious efficiency, better decisions, and real business results. This is the kind of experience that can put you years ahead of your peers.

In this once-in-a-lifetime opportunity, you’ll:

  • Build AI workflows that solve real business problems.
  • Uncover high-impact AI opportunities most companies never even notice.
  • Turn slow, manual work into smarter systems that save serious time and money.
  • Learn to judge AI by what actually matters: efficiency, cost savings, and ROI.
  • Work on high-visibility projects that most interns never get near.
  • This isn’t a “sit in meetings and watch” internship. If you’ve got the chops, it’s a launchpad into a lifetime of success.

This job will give you ALL the feels:

At AlgaeCal, we’re driven by a single idea. To end the fear of bone loss. In the United States, an estimated 54 million people have low bone density.

The good news? AlgaeCal has the world’s only clinically-supported natural solution to this problem. We give hope to everyone worried about bone loss. If that excites you, you’ll find no better place to launch your career than at AlgaeCal.

What You Bring to the Table

You’re the kind of person who sees a clunky process and immediately thinks, “There has to be a smarter way to do this.” You’re fascinated by how AI can make a business run better, faster, and with far less friction. You want to understand why one workflow saves hours of work… while another creates confusion, bottlenecks, and more mess than it solves. You love systems. You love logic. You notice details other people miss. Prompts, workflows, outputs, follow-ups, handoffs — that stuff matters to you. You care about getting it right. And when something works brilliantly, you don’t just appreciate it… you want to know exactly why it works.

If that sounds like you, you’ll fit right in.

About Your Opportunity

You’ll get hands-on experience doing the kind of AI transformation work most people only talk about.

How to Spot AI Opportunities That Others Miss

You’ll help identify repetitive workflows, bottlenecks, and hidden inefficiencies across the business, then turn those opportunities into practical AI solutions with measurable value.

How to Build Custom Agentic Solutions

  • You’ll help design workflows where multiple specialized AI agents collaborate to complete meaningful tasks. Real systems. Real business problems. Real impact.

How to Move Fast With No-Code and Low-Code Tools

  • You’ll use modern tools to build, test, and improve AI systems quickly. You’ll see how useful solutions get launched fast, without months of waiting.

How to Strengthen RAG and Knowledge Systems

  • You’ll help improve the knowledge behind AI outputs so systems are more accurate, more useful, and more reliable in the real world.

How to Measure What Actually Matters

  • You’ll help evaluate AI projects based on labor savings, maintenance costs, efficiency gains, and ROI. You’ll learn the difference between something that looks exciting… and something that actually creates value.

How to Help Teams Actually Adopt New Technology

  • You’ll help different business units embrace better tools and workflows. You’ll see how strong communication, empathy, and follow-through turn new technology into real change.

How AI Fits Into the Bigger Picture

  • You’ll understand how AI connects with governance, privacy, compliance, risk, and business operations — and why the best solutions are not just clever, but responsible too.

How High Standards Actually Work

You’ll see what it’s like to work on a team that cares about quality. Clear thinking. Clean systems. Strong communication. Stellar follow-through. If you’ve got high standards, you’ll enjoy our excellence-driven culture.

If you’re reading this and thinking, “That’s the kind of experience I want,” we’d love to hear from you. If not? No worries. This opportunity is designed for people who want to grow fast and take responsibility early.

And just so we’re clear: We won’t hold your hand forever. Once we teach you something, we’ll expect you to roll up your sleeves and actually do it. If you’re just looking to “learn” without executing—this isn’t the role for you.

Still Early in Your Career? Good.

We don’t expect mastery. We’re looking for strong fundamentals, curiosity, sharp thinking, and the ability to learn quickly inside a fast-moving, operationally focused environment. If you’re nodding right now, that could be a sign that THIS is the opportunity for you.

What Will You Earn?

We believe in rewarding top talent. So let’s talk about salary once we’ve had the chance to get to know you better. Rest assured, we’re generous and offer above-market pay for the right person. If you bring the skills and experience, we’ll make sure the compensation reflects that.

You’ll also enjoy the following benefits:

  • Monthly team events and activities
  • Weekly team lunches
  • Casual dress code
  • Incredible dog friendly, Yaletown office one block from SkyTrain

And above all, you’ll join a team that you’ll never want to leave. Culture is everything to us. Yes, we work extremely hard to change as many people’s lives as possible. But we know how to have fun and reward ourselves too.

How To Apply

Opportunities like this don’t come along often, and we have much to offer our successful applicants. So, to make sure our future AI Business Intern is the real deal, will be a great fit, and will thrive at AlgaeCal, we ask a little more than your run-of-the-mill job application:

If this position is for you, please respond to this advertisement with your resume and cover letter. Please, read carefully:

1. Write a cover letter addressing:

  • Why you're the best Associate AI Strategist for this role. Give quantifiable examples of related achievements.
  • Describe why you fit perfectly with our values. Read them here:
  • What's the toughest challenge you’ve ever overcome in your life? How did you overcome it?
  • What are the last three books you’ve read?
  • What do you do for fun?

2. Upload your cover letter and resume

Ensure that your cover letter and resume are saved and sent as one file.

Yes, our hiring process is challenging, but it’s our way of selecting the best--and your way of ensuring your AlgaeCal teammates are tops.

Click the “Apply Now” button on this page and upload your cover letter and resume.

Note: Please include your cover letter WITH your resume in one document. Applications without a cover letter will not be read!

We expect a LOT of interest in this position, so act quickly. You can also expect a prompt response.
