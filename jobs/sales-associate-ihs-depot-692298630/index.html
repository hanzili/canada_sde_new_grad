About IHS Depot

Industrial cleaning equipment leader in local market

Full Description

Company Description

IHS Depot specializes in providing sales and services for floor cleaning equipment, including the nationally known brands such as Kaivac, ProTeam, Karcher and CleanFix. With a focus on high-quality cleaning solutions, including tobotics. We support our customers in achieving efficiency and cleanliness across various venue types. Located in St. Catharines, ON, IHS Depot is committed to innovation and delivering top-tier customer service. Our goal is to offer reliable and effective cleaning solutions tailored to the unique needs of our clientele.

Role Description

We are seeking a full-time Sales Associate to join our team. This hybrid role is based in St. Catharines, ON, with flexibility for remote and in-field work. The Sales Associate will be responsible for developing and maintaining client relationships, presenting our range of cleaning equipment, identifying customer needs, and creating tailored solutions. Additional responsibilities include preparing sales quotations, meeting sales targets, following up with clients, and providing after-sales support.

Qualifications

  • Strong customer service and relationship-building skills, with a professional demeanor.
  • Proven experience in sales, client presentations, and meeting sales targets.
  • Ability to understand and explain technical product details effectively.
  • Time management and organizational skills with a focus on achieving goals.
  • Proficiency in Microsoft Office Suite and CRM software; knowledge of sales tools and systems is advantageous.
  • Demonstrated problem-solving skills and an aptitude for identifying customer needs.
  • Valid driver's license and willingness to travel locally as required.
  • Must be legally entitled to work in Canada.
  • Prior experience in industrial cleaning or equipment sales is a plus.
