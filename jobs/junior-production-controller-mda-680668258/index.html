About the Role

Release production orders; track materials; coordinate flow.

About MDA

Canadian space leader; long-standing aerospace innovator.

Full Description

Building the space between proven and possible, MDA Space is a trusted mission partner to the global space industry. A robotics, satellite systems and geointelligence pioneer with a 55-year+ story of world firsts and more than 450 missions, MDA Space is a global leader in communications satellites, Earth and space observation, and space exploration and infrastructure. The global MDA Space team of more than 3,800 space experts has the knowledge and know-how to turn an audacious customer vision into an achievable mission – bringing to bear a one-of-a-kind mix of experience, engineering excellence and wide-eyed wonder that’s been in our DNA since day one. For those who dream big and push boundaries on the ground and in the stars to change the world for the better, we’ll take you there. For more information, visit www.mda.space.

We’re on the hunt for a Junior Production Controller on our Satellite systems team at our Montreal office.

Role:

Coordinate and expedite the workflow and material to production, for work centers of low criticality level. Receives specific assignments with periodic supervision.

Tasks:

  • Release production orders as per schedule.
  • Prioritize kits and update the status for production meetings.
  • Track & Expedite Readiness and shortages with program and operation teams (material, equipment and documentation) and rationalize timing of launch to production.
  • Internally expedite shortages to ensure the AI&T plan is respected.
  • Follow-up on activities in different work centers of a lower criticality and provide priorities.
  • Ensure transactions are organized and reflect activities.
  • Follow-up of NCR, MRB on material and assemblies
  • Ensure completion of production order in ERP.

Minimum qualifications required:

  • In-depth knowledge of the Office suite
  • Good understanding of product nomenclature, planning concepts and an ERP system
  • Sense of urgency
  • Problem-solving and decision-making skills
  • Commitment and responsibility
  • Good analysis and communication techniques
  • Must be able to interact effectively. Customer orientation (internal and external)
  • Demonstrate excellent organizational skills and ability to adapt to changes.
  • Ability to process detailed documentation and data accurately
  • May be called upon to support different shifts
  • Education:DEC in a related field of study. A combination of other relevant experiences/education could be considered
  • Experience: 0- 2 years of experience
  • Level of language knowledge: Essential knowledge of French and English, oral and written (Contact with customers outside Quebec and head office staff in Ontario).

Comments/Special Considerations:

Successful candidates must obtain and hold security clearance at the reliability status level, and pass security assessment for the Controlled Goods Program (CGP).
