Full Description

We are seeking an immediate Business Analyst - Junior for the Ministry of Public and Business Service Delivery and Procurement in an onsite role (5 days/week) at 222 Jarvis St, Toronto, Ontario. This junior BA gathers/analyzes/documents business/system requirements, creates vision/swim lane diagrams/use cases/user stories, leads JAD sessions/stakeholder interviews, liaises between business/IT/vendors, and uses Jira/Confluence/Azure DevOps—aligning with OPS Unified Project Management Methodology and enterprise architecture standards.
