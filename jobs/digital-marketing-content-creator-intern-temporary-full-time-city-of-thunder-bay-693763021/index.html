About the Role

Create content, support digital marketing campaigns

About City of Thunder Bay

Municipal government, community-focused marketing experience

Full Description

:

The City of Thunder Bay provides exceptional quality of life to those who live, work, and play in Thunder Bay – a culturally vibrant, economically diverse community with a metro population of over 120,000. Located on the north shore of Lake Superior, under the protective watch of the Sleeping Giant, Thunder Bay is rich in people and resources, and connects Northwestern Ontario to the world. We value our high quality of life and promote a clean, green, beautiful, and healthy community that provides economic opportunity, respects diversity, and provides affordable and safe neighbourhoods that are accessible to all.

The City of Thunder Bay is a place where art, culture and nature come together!

Job Description:

Create content that inspires. Build campaigns that have an impact.

At the City of Thunder Bay, Our City Runs on People Like You. Together, we’re building a workplace where everyone belongs – where your creativity brings new ideas to life and builds brand awareness for our community that will create a lasting impact.

Here, you’ll contribute meaningful storytelling that strengthens the community’s engagement and brand visibility. You will be part of a work environment that supports respectful interactions, meaningful opportunities to grow, and a culture that promotes well-being and collaboration.

Thunder Bay offers more than a fulfilling career. With its natural beauty, affordability, and strong sense of community, it’s a city where you can grow personally and professionally.

Where You’ll Make a Difference Every Day

As a Digital Marketing & Content Creator Intern, you’ll play an active role in creating engaging content, supporting digital campaigns, growing online visibility, and helping strengthen brand awareness across multiple platforms.

You’ll collaborate with internal teams to create compelling digital content that supports community engagement, brand storytelling, newsletter and CEDC’s awareness initiatives.

Whether you live in Thunder Bay, you’re looking to return, or excited to make the city your new home, join a team where your skills, expertise, and presence is recognized, and make a real impact to our community.

How You’ll Support the Team

In this role, you will create content for digital marketing initiatives and help support marketing programs that promote economic growth and have the following accountabilities:

  • Assist marketing team in developing, implementing, monitoring and evaluating CEDC’s marketing efforts & initiatives.
  • Assist with creating content including filming, photography, and behind-the-scenes support at various locations around the city.
  • Create engaging written, visual, and video content for blogs, social media, websites, and other CEDC marketing initiatives.
  • Assist with the development of content featuring local entrepreneurs, organizations and stakeholders.
  • Attend community and corporate events to support the team and capture content.
  • Use design templates to create content for digital and traditional marketing assets.
  • Help manage social media by scheduling posts, monitoring activity and responding to comments.
  • Stay up to date on social media trends and online tools.
  • Assist with preparing and sending the monthly CEDC newsletter.

What You Can Count On With Us

We’re proud to offer a workplace where your well-being, growth, and sense of purpose come first. As the Digital Marketing & Content Creator Intern, you’ll enjoy:

  • A competitive pay that reflects your expertise
  • A matching pension plan to help you build long-term financial security (optional)
  • Generous 4% vacation pay
  • Access to a confidential Employee and Family Assistance Program (EFAP) and wellness initiatives to support your overall health
  • Training development programs, so you can continue to grow your skills and career
  • A culture of recognition and appreciation, with staff events and programs that celebrate your contributions

What You’ll Bring to the Team

Here’s what will help you succeed as the Digital Marketing & Content Creator Intern:

  • Diploma in marketing, or communications.
  • Demonstrated experience with photography, videography, content editing, and graphic design for digital and social media
  • Knowledge of social media trends, content development, creative marketing, storytelling, and tactics to drive community engagement
  • Knowledge of Thunder Bay’s local business landscape, community activities, and major events within the City
