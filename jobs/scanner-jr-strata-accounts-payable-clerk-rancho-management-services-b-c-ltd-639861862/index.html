About the Role

About Rancho Management Services (B.C.) Ltd.

Large Western Canada property management leader

Full Description

We are one of the largest property management companies in Western Canada and are currently looking to hire a full time Scanner/Jr. Accounts Payable Clerk to join our busy Downtown Vancouver office.

We strongly value our employees by providing ongoing training and encourage growth within the company.

Job Responsibilities:

  • Input invoices into our AP system;
  • Ensures the efficient operating of the A/P department by successfully take direction and complete work within a timely manner;
  • Ensures effective interpersonal Communications/Relations with Supervisors, Strata Agents and fellow colleagues;
  • Maintain good relationship with vendors/suppliers;
  • Conducts business at all times with the highest standards of personal, professional and ethical conduct.

Education & Experience:

  • Knowledge of property management;
  • Some experience with Accounting

Knowledge & Skills:

  • Excellent Microsoft Office and keyboarding skills (ability to type a minimum of 50 wpm);
  • Exceptional prioritizing and time management skills;
  • Outstanding customer service skills;
  • Ability to work well under pressure in a fast paced environment;
  • Fluency in English.

Remuneration:

  • Competitive compensation;
  • Extended Health Benefits (i.e. vision, dental, drug prescriptions, etc.) with no waiting period;
  • 15 days annual vacation after 3 months probation;
  • Group RRSP program with no waiting period;
  • Employer matching DPSP program with no waiting period;
  • CPR training available after 3 months probation;
  • Many exciting social events throughout the year.

If you are interested in this position, please submit your cover letter and resume.

Only selected candidates will be contacted for a formal interview.

  • Disability insurance
