About Gorman Bros. Lumber Ltd

Full Description

Gorman Bros. Lumber Ltd., a division of the Gorman Group, is a modern, progressive sawmill located in West Kelowna, in British Columbia’s Okanagan Valley. At Gorman Bros., we believe in our people and believe that the involvement and contribution of each employee is essential to our long-term success.

We are accepting applications for a Marketing Assistant Summer Student to join our team. This role offers an excellent opportunity for a student looking to gain experience in both marketing and sales. The successful candidate will be responsible for the development of product information sheets, creating digital content, and assist with marketing initiatives that directly support the sales team. As a summer student, you'll have the opportunity to work with experienced sales professionals and gain practical skills and knowledge in the field. If you are creative, motivated, and eager to learn, we encourage you to apply.

RESPONSIBILITIES:

  • Coordinate with sales for priority marketing needs.
  • Investigate market trends in specific locations and build promotional boxes for customers.
  • Developing product information sheets in collaboration with our sales, production, and quality control teams.
  • Assist with internal communication materials and newsletters.
  • Creating and scheduling social media content focused on product and brand awareness.
  • Exploring geo-marketing or campaign-style initiatives as interest and skills allow.
  • Travel across our operations to understand our products and how they're made to create materials that are meaningful to the organization.

QUALIFICATIONS:

  • Currently enrolled or recently completed a Marketing or Communications degree or program.
  • Self-motivated with ability to work independently and a willingness to learn.
  • Creative mindset with experience in digital content creation and marketing strategies.
  • Strong computer skills, including experience with social media platforms and tools such as Canva.
  • Valid driver’s license required and ability to travel locally as required.

HOURS & PAY:

  • This is a contract position from May to August, with the potential for extension.
  • The role offers $25-$28 per hour, depending on experience.
  • Hours will vary 24-40 hours per week based on workload and business needs.
  • This position is based in-office at our West Kelowna location. Occasional travel to other locations within BC may be required, with flexibility in hours as needed.

ABOUT US:

The Gorman Group of Companies is family owned and proudly made up of Gorman Bros. Lumber Ltd., Canoe Forest Products, Oroville Reman and Reload, Downie Timber, Selkirk Cedar, and Lumby Pole Divisions. For over 70 years, we have been known for creating The Finest Boards and other specialty wood products. Perhaps even more, we are known for working together.

The Gorman Group is more than just a group of companies…we are a family! We value the family roots that connect us and the employees that work for us. We believe our employees are fundamental to our success as we strive to become the employer of choice through our strong family values, culture, community involvement, innovation, and constant drive for excellence.

The Gorman Group is committed to the well-being of future generations and is fully committed to responsible and sustainable forest management of the lands where our operations are conducted. Our commitment to the environment encompasses the highest levels of stewardship and welcomes accountability.
