Marketing / Digital Marketing Internship

Digital Marketing Intern

at Hungry Bird

📍 Location Scarborough, ON Hybrid
📋 Details Internship · 4 months · $17.60/hour · Hybrid
📅 Posted Mar 26, 2026

About the Role

Create and post engaging food content daily

About Hungry Bird

Local Scarborough restaurant; growing digital presence

Full Description

Schedule: Flexible (hybrid) | Occasional visit to the restaurant for taking pictures/videos

About Us:
We are a vibrant, customer-focused restaurant in Scarborough, ON known for our delicious food, welcoming atmosphere, and unique dining experience. We’re looking for a creative Digital Marketing Intern who can bring our food, ambiance, and brand story to life online.

Key Responsibilities:

  • Create and post engaging food content on a daily basis (photos, reels, and videos of dishes, drinks, and behind-the-scenes kitchen moments)
  • Manage daily social media activity on Instagram and TikTok
  • Capture high-quality content of menu items, plating, and in-restaurant experiences
  • Track performance of posts and suggest ideas to increase reach and foot traffic
  • Other duties assigned

Requirements:

  • Passion for food, restaurants, and social media content creation
  • Basic photography/videography skills (especially food content)
  • Familiarity with tools like Canva, Instagram Reels, TikTok, and Meta Ads
  • Creative, trend-aware, and detail-oriented
  • Strong communication skills and ability to work in a fast-paced environment

What You’ll Gain:

  • Real-world experience in restaurant marketing and branding
  • Opportunity to build a strong portfolio with food and lifestyle content
  • Flexible working hours (including some on-site content shooting)
  • Potential for a full-time role based on performance

Job Type: Internship / Co-op
Contract length: 4 months

Pay: From $17.60 per hour

Work Location: Hybrid remote in Scarborough, ON
