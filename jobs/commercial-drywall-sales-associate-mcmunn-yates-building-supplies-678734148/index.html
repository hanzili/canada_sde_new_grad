Full Description

Drywall Sales – Commercial Division, Winnipeg Branch

Are you an energetic, customer-focused sales professional with a passion for building strong business relationships? McMunn & Yates Building Supplies is seeking a Drywall Sales expert to join our Commercial Division in Winnipeg. This is your chance to play a key role in supporting our commercial and residential drywall applicators while contributing to the continued growth of our organization.

The Opportunity

As a Drywall Sales professional, you’ll be the primary point of contact for commercial and residential drywall customers, delivering accurate estimates, expert product knowledge, and outstanding service. You’ll work closely with our Oak Bluff delivery centre to ensure seamless coordination of product orders and deliveries. By actively engaging with customers, nurturing long-term relationships, and identifying new opportunities, you’ll strengthen McMunn & Yates’ reputation as a trusted industry partner.

Your Key Responsibilities

  • Provide timely, accurate sales and estimates to drywall customers.
  • Offer knowledgeable product guidance and full account management for both commercial and residential clients.
  • Develop and maintain strong, long-term customer relationships.
  • Process transactions efficiently and accurately within the point-of-sale system.
  • Source and coordinate special-order products in a timely, cost-effective manner.
  • Collaborate closely with operations and the Oak Bluff delivery team to ensure efficient and well-organized product delivery.

What We’re Looking For

  • A customer-service champion who brings enthusiasm and professionalism to every interaction.

  • Strong organizational and time-management skills suited to a fast-paced environment.

  • A self-motivated individual who actively seeks out new sales opportunities and takes initiative to grow their portfolio.

  • A collaborative team player who also thrives working independently.

  • A valid driver’s license and reliable transportation.

  • Experience working with Drywall materials in any capacity.

We offer a positive team environment and a full range of benefits. If you’re ready to build your career with a COR-certified industry leader, please apply in confidence:

McMunn & Yates Building Supplies
Attn: Scott Mckee – Sales Manager
Email: smckee@mcmunnandyates.com
