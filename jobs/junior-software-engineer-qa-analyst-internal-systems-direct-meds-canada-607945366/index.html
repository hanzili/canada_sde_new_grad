About Direct Meds Canada

Full Description

Overview
Parkway Pharmacy is hiring a Junior Software Engineer / QA Analyst to support the development, testing, and maintenance of our internal software platforms.

This role is ideal for someone early in their career who wants real exposure to production systems, automation, APIs, and internal tooling, not just marketing websites. You’ll work closely with senior developers and pharmacists to build software that reduces human error, improves operational efficiency, and supports regulated pharmacy workflows.

This is a hands-on, growth-oriented role with mentorship, but it requires curiosity, accountability, and a willingness to learn fundamentals.

Qualifications

  • Work experience, Personal projects. If not available then a Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science, Software Engineering, or related field will suffice.

Responsibilities

Software & QA

  • Assist in developing and maintaining internal web applications and tools.
  • Perform functional, regression, and edge-case testing.
  • Reproduce, document, and track bugs clearly and accurately.
  • Validate data integrity and system behavior in regulated workflows.

Automation & Internal Tools

  • Help build small automation scripts or utilities (with guidance).
  • Work with APIs (testing, validation).
  • Assist in improving internal tooling and developer workflows.

Support & Operations (Secondary)

  • Provide limited internal software support when required.
  • Assist with onboarding or troubleshooting internal systems (not general IT helpdesk).

Occasionally support sister pharmacy systems when needed (training provided).

  • Perform functional, regression, and edge-case testing.
  • Reproduce, document, and track bugs clearly and accurately.
  • Validate data integrity and system behavior in regulated workflows.

What We’re Looking For

  • Strong interest in software engineering and systems, not just UI work.
  • Basic programming knowledge and willingness to learn fundamentals.
  • Comfort reading and understanding existing code.
  • Logical thinking and attention to detail.
  • Clear written communication (especially for bug reports and documentation).
  • Reliability and professionalism in a regulated environment.

Technical Exposure (at least some of the following)

  • JavaScript / TypeScript
  • React or Next.js
  • Python
  • PHP (Laravel is a plus)
  • REST APIs and JSON
  • Git / version control basics

Preferred (Nice to Have)

  • Exposure to automated testing tools or frameworks.
  • Familiarity with SQL or databases.
  • Understanding of QA concepts and SDLC.
  • Experience using AI tools responsibly to assist development (not replace understanding).

Important Notes

  • This role is not a senior developer position.
  • This role is not primarily IT support.
  • AI tools may be used, but candidates must demonstrate basic technical understanding.
  • Growth and raises are performance-based and tied to skill progression and reliability.

Compensation & Benefits

  • Competitive pay based on experience and skill level
  • Paid time off
  • Employer-paid health benefits
  • Full-time, on-site role (8-hour shifts, 5 days/week)
