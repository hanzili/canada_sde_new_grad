Geoscience / Mining

Junior Geologist

at Doubleview Gold Corp

📍 Location Vancouver, BC V6C 1V5
📋 Details Full-time · Rotational (4 weeks on/2 weeks off) · $350-$500/day · May-Dec rotation
📅 Posted Apr 02, 2026

About the Role

Core logging, data management, field sampling

About Doubleview Gold Corp

Canadian mineral exploration firm with remote camp work

Full Description

Company Overview:

Doubleview Gold Corp is an innovative and growth-driven Canadian exploration and development company headquartered in Vancouver, British Columbia. As an industry leader, Doubleview focuses on discovering, exploring and advancing high-value mineral resources, including copper, scandium, gold, silver, and other precious metals.

Responsibilities:

As a Junior Geologist, you will be a key part of our technical team, ensuring high-quality data collection from our diamond drilling programs. Duties include:

  • Core Processing: Execute all aspects of core logging, including detailed descriptions of lithology, alteration, mineralization, and structural features.
  • Data Management: Prepare "quick logs" for immediate drill hole assessment and integrate detailed logging data into the project database.
  • Sampling & QAQC: Follow strict sampling protocols and prepare shipments for assay laboratories while adhering to established Quality Assurance/Quality Control procedures.
  • Field Exploration: Participate in regional geochemical surveys, including the collection of soil, silt, and rock samples as required.
  • Safety & Stewardship: Maintain an unwavering commitment to health, safety, and environmental protection in line with Doubleview Gold’s corporate policies.

Qualifications

  • Must be legally entitled to work in Canada at the time of application.
  • Completion of a post-secondary degree in Geosciences, Geology, or a closely related field.
  • 0.5–3 years of relevant industry experience.
  • Familiarity with porphyry copper-gold systems and experience with geological logging software is an asset.
  • Valid driver’s license.
  • Able to work at a remote, live-in exploration camp in Northwest British Columbia.
  • Must be capable of lifting and moving core boxes weighing up to 50lbs repeatedly throughout a shift.
  • Ability to work outdoors in variable and sometimes extreme mountain weather conditions.
  • This is a rotational position (e.g., 4 weeks on / 2 weeks off), requiring long work days and commitment to the rotation schedule from May to December.

Job Type: Full-time

Pay: $350.00-$500.00 per day

Work Location: In person
