Why This Opportunity?

This position gives you the chance to grow your career while taking on work that matters. You’ll have the opportunity to deepen your expertise, learn new skills, and contribute to initiatives that make a real impact on our business. It’s a role where your experience is valued, your ideas are heard, and your development is supported. We want you to succeed and continue building your future here at CRH Canada.

Job Responsibilities

Reporting to CFO & SVP, the Financial Analyst placement supports the business in compiling and creating financial reports, presentations, and analysis.

  • Compile and prepare monthly reconciliations, reports and presentations.
  • Prepare any ad-hoc reporting and presentations and support management on any ad-hoc financial analysis.
  • Assist in budgeting and forecasting relative to Income Statement, Balance Sheet, and Cash Flows
  • Assist and Execute month-end activities for closing the accounting periods in a timely manner.
  • Assist in Financial Analysis of monthly results and provide commentary explaining the variances against forecast, budget, and prior year performance.
  • Assist in budgeting with respect to financial indicators Qualitative and quantitative analysis of budgets, forecasts, actual results, and Ad Hoc projects to assist in management's review and decision making.
  • Assist in gathering and reconciling fixed and mobile asset counts
  • Review routine and complex business transactions to identify relevant accounting and reporting requirements.
  • Gather and complete the necessary planning/budgeting reports to be submitted to Corporate.
  • Positively interface with Corporate Finance Team
  • Other ad-hoc duties as required

Requirements

  • Working towards degree with specialization in accounting or business administration.
  • Enrolled or interested in CPA program.
  • Intermediate to advanced proficiency in Excel and PowerPoint.
  • Ability to manipulate large data sets, demonstrate critical thinking in producing various analyses and tables, as well as develop charts/graphs in both Excel and PowerPoint.
  • Proven understanding of financial statements, and proven knowledge of financial analysis, variance analysis, and forecasting.
  • Excellent attention to detail, accuracy, and ability to self-review.
  • Ability to adapt to versatile deliverables/analyses and demonstrate logic/reasoning.
  • Exhibit strong time management skills, ability to perform under pressure in a fast-paced environment with ad-hoc deliverables.
  • Strong communication, writing, and presentation skills.
  • Desire to learn and be involved in a variety of ad-hoc projects and changing reporting needs.
  • Previous Construction Materials Accounting experience is considered an asset.
