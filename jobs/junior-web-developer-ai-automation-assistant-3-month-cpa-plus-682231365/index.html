About the Role

Develop internal web tools, automate AI workflows

About CPA Plus

Public accounting firm focused on automation and AI

Full Description

Job Overview
Plug CPA Professional Corporation is a modern public accounting firm focused on helping business owners simplify their accounting, bookkeeping, and tax processes. We are continuously looking for ways to improve efficiency through technology, automation, and AI-driven workflows.

We are currently looking for a motivated junior web developer / software developer who enjoys building things, experimenting with AI tools, and solving real-world business problems. This role is ideal for a recent graduate or self-taught developer who codes outside of school and has personal projects they are excited to showcase.

This is not a traditional accounting firm role. We are looking for someone creative, curious, and proactive who wants hands-on experience helping modernize a growing business.

Duties

Over the next few months, you’ll assist with a variety of projects, including:

  • Assisting with the development of a small internal web application
  • Helping structure and improve our company website
  • Exploring AI tools and automation opportunities for the firm
  • Assisting with website integrations, workflows, and process improvements
  • Debugging and troubleshooting issues related to AI-generated or custom-built code
  • Researching and proposing new technologies that can improve efficiency
  • Supporting ad hoc tech and automation projects as they arise

Qualifications

We are less focused on formal work experience and more interested in someone who genuinely enjoys coding and building projects.

Ideal candidates may have:

  • A diploma or degree in Computer Science, Software Engineering, Web Development, or a related field
  • Experience with coding projects outside of school
  • A portfolio, GitHub, or examples of apps/websites you’ve built
  • ChatGPT
  • Cursor
  • GitHub Copilot
  • Basic understanding of web development concepts
  • Strong problem-solving skills and willingness to learn independently

Experience with any of the following is considered an asset:

  • HTML/CSS/JavaScript
  • React
  • WordPress
  • Python
  • API integrations
  • Database management
  • AI workflow automation tools
  • UI/UX design

What Makes Someone a Great Fit

We’re looking for someone who:

  • Enjoys experimenting and learning new technologies
  • Likes building side projects for fun
  • Can troubleshoot and figure things out independently
  • Is comfortable working in a fast-moving environment
  • Has strong communication skills
  • Is interested in AI and business automation

Why This Role Is Different

This role offers the opportunity to:

  • Work directly with business owners and decision makers
  • Build real-world tools that will actually be used internally
  • Gain hands-on experience with AI implementation in a professional environment
  • Have flexibility and creative input on projects
  • Potentially continue working together beyond the initial contract if it’s a good fit

To Apply

Please send:

  • Your resume
  • Portfolio, GitHub, or examples of projects/apps/websites you’ve built
  • A short description of a project you enjoyed building the most and why

We care much more about curiosity, initiative, and real project experience than perfect grades or years of experience.

Pay: $25.00-$30.00 per hour

Benefits:

  • Casual dress
  • Flexible schedule
  • On-site parking
  • Paid time off

Work Location: Hybrid remote in Ottawa, ON K1Z 7T1
