Junior Digital Marketing Manager

at Smart Capital Center

📍 Location Alberta Remote
📋 Details Type · Duration · Pay · Term
📅 Posted Mar 11, 2026

About the Role

Run digital campaigns; create marketing assets

About Smart Capital Center

AI-driven marketing in fintech real estate tech

Full Description

Smart Capital Center is building the first AI-powered platform for commercial real estate investment and financial management. Our technology combines real-time data, automation, and AI insights to help investors move faster, reduce risk, and identify better opportunities.

We’re looking for a Junior Digital Marketing Associate who is creative, data-driven, and comfortable using modern marketing tools and AI to execute campaigns and produce high-quality digital content.

What You'll Do

  • Run digital marketing campaigns across email, web, social media, and paid ads
  • Create marketing assets such as social graphics, email templates, landing pages, and ads
  • Support Google Ads and LinkedIn Ads campaigns
  • Use AI tools to accelerate marketing content, research, and campaign execution
  • Track campaign performance and improve results using analytics
  • Assist with SEO, website updates, and marketing automation

What We're Looking For

  • 1–3 years experience in digital marketing or growth marketing
  • Strong design skills
  • Experience with paid ads, email marketing, or analytics tools
  • Comfortable writing clear marketing copy
  • Interest in AI tools and modern marketing technology
  • Basic technical skills (HTML, tracking, analytics, automation) are a plus

Why Join Smart Capital Center

  • Work on cutting-edge AI technology
  • Learn modern AI-driven marketing
  • Be part of a fast-growing fintech and real estate technology company
