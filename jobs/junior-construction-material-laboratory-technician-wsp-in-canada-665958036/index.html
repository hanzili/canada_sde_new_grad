The Opportunity Your Impact Starts Here. As a Junior Construction Material Lab Technician, you will help deliver accurate, high‑quality laboratory work that supports environmental, materials, and infrastructure projects across the region. You will be part of a team that values precision, safety, and innovation while helping shape healthier, more resilient communities.

Your Impact In this role, you will:

  • Perform routine laboratory testing on construction materials such as soils, aggregates, concrete, and asphalt in accordance with standard methods and specifications.
  • Prepare, label, log, and manage samples to maintain accurate tracking and traceability.
  • Operate standard construction materials laboratory equipment, including balances, ovens, sieves, mixers, molds, and compression testing machines.
  • Accurately record observations and test results in laboratory logs, templates, and digital systems.
  • Follow health, safety, and quality procedures to maintain a safe and organized laboratory environment.
  • Support senior technicians and technologists by assisting with test setup, equipment cleaning, and general lab organization.

The Skills That Set You Apart

  • A college diploma or certificate in civil engineering technology, construction engineering, materials engineering, or a related field (completed or in progress).
  • Exposure to construction materials testing through academic labs, co‑op terms, internships, or entry‑level roles.
  • Familiarity with basic laboratory practices and materials testing equipment used for concrete, soils, and aggregates.
  • Ability to work in a laboratory environment with repetitive testing procedures and attention to detail.
  • Ability to safely lift and carry items weighing up to 50 lbs as part of routine laboratory duties.
  • A strong commitment to safety, quality, and teamwork, with an interest in building technical skills in construction materials testing.
