About Candor Engineering Ltd.

Electrical engineering firm; strong EIT/early-career focus

Full Description

ENGINEERING EXCELLENCE

Candor Engineering Ltd. (“Candor”) sets the standard for Project execution. Our core focus is the Client’s expectations and needs. Cost, schedule, and execution are the driving forces behind Candor’s success.

Founded in 1989, Candor has grown in experience and technical abilities. Through many challenges, we have maintained our position as an Industry leader in Electrical, Instrumentation, and Automation Engineering, Design and Drafting.

Our commitment and dedication toward continual growth and improvements has established Candor as a leader in Generation, Cogeneration, Oil & Gas facility Design and Energy Optimization. For more information, please find us at www.candorengineering.com

POSITION:

We are currently seeking a full-time Junior Electrical Engineer/E.I.T.

Job Summary:

Qualified candidates must be able to complete projects in collaboration with other team members, the ability to work independently, applying their engineering knowledge to project work from conceptual design through to completion.

Roles and responsibilities:

  • Initiate and complete electrical engineering drawings for a multitude of projects
  • Perform hands-on designs, calculations, and evaluations to produce deliverables such as hazardous area classifications, single line diagrams, power, lighting and grounding layouts, cable schedules, control and wiring schematics
  • Prepare design and contract documents (reports, drawings and specifications)
  • Assist in evaluating tenders
  • Liaison with vendors and other professionals

Skills and Attributes:

  • Detail orientated
  • Team player
  • Strong communication skills both written and verbal
  • Strong time management skills with the ability to meet deliverables and deadlines
  • Analytical thinking
  • Problem solving
  • Strong interpersonal skills
  • Organized; able to prioritize various tasks and have a flexible approach to projects and assignment
  • Excellent problem solving and decision-making skills

Qualifications:

The successful candidate will have the following qualifications:

  • Degree in Electrical Engineering
  • Strong knowledge of electrical systems design including medium and low voltage power distribution
  • Minimum 1-3 years’ experience delivering projects in a broad range of sectors including Gas Facilities
  • Ability to work in a fast-paced team environment, meet quick deadlines, and handle multiple projects.
  • Possess sound communications skills as demonstrated through client and staff relations
  • Proficiency with Microsoft Suite (Word, Excel, MS Teams and Outlook)

WHAT WE OFFER:

Working conditions are primarily in-office from 8:00am to 4:30pm, Monday to Friday. Occasional field work will be required.

  • Competitive compensation package
  • Friendly and collaborative work environment
  • Benefits package that includes; life insurance, extended health care and dental
  • Free parking

If you feel this position matches your qualifications and are interested in learning more, please submit your resume along with a cover letter.

Candor Engineering is an equal opportunity employer and we would like to thank all applicants for their interest; however, only those selected for an interview will be contacted.
