Full Description

Project Coordinator (Junior Project Manager)

Location: Brantford, Ontario
Work Type: Full-Time (Minimum 30 hours per week) | In-Office
Compensation: $30 – $38 per hour (based on experience)

About the Role

We are seeking a motivated and organized Project Coordinator (Junior Project Manager) to join our growing team in Brantford.

This role is designed as an entry-level pathway into Project Management, making it ideal for someone who has experience supporting construction projects and is looking to develop their career.

You will support Project Managers on larger projects while also taking ownership of smaller projects from start to finish, gaining valuable experience in scheduling, coordination, subcontractor management, documentation, and project execution.

This is a full-time position (minimum 30 hours/week) with the opportunity to transition into a salaried Project Management track after the probation period.

Key ResponsibilitiesProject Coordination

  • Support Project Managers with project planning, scheduling, and budgeting
  • Track project progress, schedules, and productivity
  • Coordinate subcontractors, suppliers, and internal teams
  • Prepare project documentation, reports, and meeting minutes

Project Management Responsibilities

  • Manage smaller projects from initiation to completion
  • Assist with change orders, RFIs, and project documentation
  • Monitor subcontractor performance and project milestones
  • Identify potential risks and escalate issues when required

Scheduling & Resource Coordination

  • Assist with scheduling employees and subcontractors
  • Coordinate equipment and material deliveries
  • Generate look-ahead schedules and productivity reports

Permits & Compliance

  • Coordinate permits, locates, and regulatory approvals
  • Liaise with municipalities, utilities, and regulatory bodies

Administrative Support

  • Review employee timesheets and assist with payroll coordination
  • Maintain project files, purchase orders, and contracts
  • Assist with project close-out documentation

Qualifications

  • 2–4 years of experience supporting construction projects or project coordination
  • Diploma or Degree in Construction Management, Engineering, or related field (considered an asset)
  • Strong organizational and time-management skills
  • Ability to manage multiple tasks and deadlines
  • Strong communication and problem-solving abilities
  • Proficiency with Microsoft Office (Excel, Word, Outlook)

What We Offer

  • Competitive hourly pay: $30 – $38/hour depending on experience
  • Clear pathway to a salaried Project Manager role after probation
  • Health benefits plan after 3 months (company pays 80%, employee pays 20%)
  • Supportive team environment with mentorship from experienced Project Managers
  • Opportunity to work on a variety of construction projects
  • Stable, growing company with long-term opportunities
