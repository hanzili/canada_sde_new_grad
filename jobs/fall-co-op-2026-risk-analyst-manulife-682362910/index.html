Full Description

*Please note there are specific application instructions required to be considered for this role**

At Manulife John Hancock, we believe in investing in the future – starting with you. Our Risk Analyst Co-op is your launchpad to success, offering the support, structure, and exciting opportunities you need to thrive and make your mark.

Are you an enthusiastic undergraduate student looking to gain hands-on experience? In these roles, you'll have the chance to work on exciting projects, create dynamic data visualizations, and support digitalization initiatives. You'll develop your project management skills, learn to set goals, and manage deliverables, all while preparing detailed reports for senior management.

Available Roles for the Fall 2026 Term:

  • Global Risk Intelligence & Technology
  • Governance Reporting & Continuous Improvement
  • Governance & Strategy Risk
  • Standards Assurance & Risk Monitoring

Please note that your application will be considered for multiple positions within this job posting.

Position Responsibilities:

  • Assist in creating dynamic and interactive data visualizations using Python libraries (such as Matplotlib, Seaborn, Plotly) and Power BI.
  • Support digitalization projects aimed at automating risk data collection, analysis, and reporting.
  • Prepare detailed reports and presentations on risk forecasting findings for senior management and stakeholders.
  • Develop project management skills by working on multiple projects simultaneously. Learn to set project goals, create timelines, and manage deliverables.

Required Qualifications:

  • Currently pursuing an undergraduate degree in Economics, Business, Information Security, or a related field.
  • Experience with Python and Power BI.
  • Strong written and verbal communication skills.
  • Proficient writing skills and strong mathematical/financial competencies.
  • Demonstrated ability to work both independently with minimal supervision and as part of a team.
  • Knowledge of Python (prior programming experience is an asset).
  • Effective communication, time management, and attention to detail.

When you join our team:

  • We’ll empower you to learn and grow the career you want.
  • We’ll recognize and support you in a flexible environment where well-being and inclusion are more than just words.
  • As part of our global team, we’ll support you in shaping the future you want to see. As part of our co-op program, you will gain early access to Manulife John Hancock’s premier early talent program, GRO, tailored to graduating students! This program is designed to launch your career with the support, structure, and opportunities you need to thrive.

Application Instructions:

  • Submit your resume, cover letter, academic transcript, and work term evaluation (if any) in one PDF file.
