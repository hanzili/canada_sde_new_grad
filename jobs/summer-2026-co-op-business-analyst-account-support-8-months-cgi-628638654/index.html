About the Role

About CGI

Global IT and business consulting leader

Full Description

The Co-op Business Analyst supports client accounts and delivery teams by gathering requirements, analyzing data, and translating business needs into clear documentation and actionable insights. This role works closely with clients, Project Managers, and internal delivery teams to help ensure projects are aligned with client objectives, reporting is accurate, and processes continue to improve

Start Date: May 2026

Application Deadline: February 27, 2026

Perks of Being a CGI Co-op:

  • Participate in monthly socials to meet co-ops across the country.
  • Access professional development workshops hosted by domain experts on topics ranging from how to be a top consultant, how to apply the agile methodology, data & analytics and more!

To learn more about CGI and hear from our Co-ops and New Grads, visit www.cgi.com/startmycareer

Your future duties and responsibilities

Client & Stakeholder Support

  • Act as a point of contact for clients on assigned accounts, supporting communication and follow-ups
  • Participate in client and internal meetings to capture requirements, document action items, and communicate next steps
  • Help translate client needs into clear business requirements, user stories, or functional documentation
  • Build strong working relationships with clients and internal teams

Business Analysis & Reporting

  • Support the PMO and Delivery Teams with status reports, meeting notes, schedules, resource requests, and risk/issue tracking
  • Assist with reporting and data tracking across a portfolio of projects (dashboards, financials, deliverables, and change requests)
  • Analyze project and operational data to identify trends and improvement opportunities
  • Assist in designing and maintaining standard and ad hoc reports and templates

Project & Account Operations

  • Support billing and invoicing activities, including preparing invoice requests, applying payments, and following up on outstanding items
  • Maintain data management systems and contracts repositories
  • Report on internal project resources (people, capital, and expenses)
  • Assist with preparation of presentation materials (reports, charts, and graphs) for clients and senior leadership
  • Take initiative with on-going process improvements/enhancements to ensure data integrity and accuracy.

Objectives & Targets

  • Timely and accurate updates to work plans and project documentation
  • Consistent delivery of program and project reporting requirements
  • Provide effective analytical and operational support to Project Managers and client accounts
  • Adherence to established processes while helping identify opportunities for improvement

Required Required qualifications to be successful in this role

  • Strong communication and interpersonal skills, with comfort working directly with clients
  • Analytical mindset with attention to detail
  • Ability to organize information, document requirements, and communicate clearly
  • Effective listening skills and a collaborative approach to problem solving
  • Proficiency in Microsoft Excel, PowerPoint, and Word (or similar tools)

Nice to Have (not required for co-op)

  • Exposure to Business Analysis or Project Management concepts
  • Basic understanding of financial or cost metrics
  • Experience creating reports, dashboards, or presentations
