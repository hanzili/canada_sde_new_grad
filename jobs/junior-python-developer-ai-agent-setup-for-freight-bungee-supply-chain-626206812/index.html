AI Automation Engineer / Developer
Permanent Full-Time Position

About Us

We're a growing carrier-broker in Markham, Ontario revolutionizing freight brokerage through AI automation. Our initial 9-agent OpenClaw system (Supervisor + Orchestrator + 7 specialists) is designed, and we're now scaling AI across all company workflows - sales, operations, dispatch, accounting, carrier management, and customer service.

Current stack: OpenClaw AI agents, Rose Rocket TMS, Odoo ERP/CRM, Gmail/Google Workspace

The Role

You'll be our first dedicated AI Automation Engineer with ownership of:

  • Deploying our 9-agent BD team (detailed runbooks provided)
  • Expanding AI across 10+ additional workflows (dispatch optimization, carrier matching, invoice automation, etc.)
  • Building self-improving systems with KPI monitoring, A/B testing, and continuous optimization
  • Becoming our AI subject matter expert as we scale from 5 to 50 employees

This is founder-level impact - you'll shape how AI transforms every aspect of our company.

Phase 1 (Months 1-3): Launch BD Agent Team

Deploy 9-agent OpenClaw system:
├── Supervisor (KPI monitoring, optimization)
├── Orchestrator (routing + logging)
├── 7 BD specialists (SDR, Pricing, Ops, etc.)
└── Integrations: Odoo CRM, Rose Rocket TMS, Gmail

Detailed technical run-books provided - integration project, not research

Deliverables: Production system live, monitoring dashboards, self-improvement loops active

Phase 2 (Months 4-6): Company-Wide Automation

Target workflows:

  • Dispatch: Auto lane assignment, carrier matching, ETAs
  • Operations: POD chasing, exception handling, check calls
  • Accounting: Invoice matching, AR/AP automation
  • Carriers: Capacity alerts, scorecard automation
  • Customers: Proactive status updates, self-service portal

Expected outcome: 40-60% staff productivity gain across operations

Phase 3 (Months 7+): Self-Improving AI Factory

  • Build agent templates for rapid deployment
  • Implement company-wide KPI dashboards
  • A/B testing framework across all agents
  • Model optimization (cost/quality tradeoffs)
  • New agent development as business scales

Must-Have QualificationstextTechnical:
├── Bachelor's CS/Software Engineering (or equivalent)
├── 1-3 years Python development (APIs, automation)
├── Experience with REST APIs, webhooks, JSON/YAML
├── Linux/VPS deployment (Docker bonus)
└── Can read/write clear technical documentation

Nice-to-Have:
├── OpenClaw/LangChain/AI agent experience
├── Odoo ERP/CRM integration
├── Logistics/TMS domain knowledge
└── Observability tools (logging, monitoring)

What Success Looks Like (First 90 Days)

Week 2: Single agent prototypes (Gmail+Odoo) live
Week 6: 5 core BD agents deployed + basic monitoring
Week 8: Full 9-agent system live + self-improvement active
Month 3: 3 additional workflows automated (dispatch, ops, accounting)

KPIs: Agent uptime >99%, 30%+ staff time savings, positive ROI within 6 months

Your Impact

  • Immediate: Deploy BD system saving 2-3 FTEs ($150K+ annual value)
  • 6 months: Automate 60% of repetitive workflows
  • 12 months: AI-first brokerage with 10x operational leverage
  • Equity upside: Performance bonus + profit share potential

Daily Responsibilities

40% Agent development/deployment
20% Monitoring/optimization
20% New workflow discovery
10% Cross-team collaboration
10% Documentation/knowledge sharing

Tools you'll master: OpenClaw, Python, Odoo API, Rose Rocket API, observability platforms

Who You'll Work With

  • C-level team: Strategy, requirements, business context
  • 2-3 Operations staff: Workflow discovery, testing
  • External vendors: API support when needed
  • Growing engineering team: As we scale

Why This Role?

Most AI jobs are research experiments. This is production AI at brokerage scale:

  • Real revenue/business impact (not toy projects)
  • Full ownership of greenfield automation
  • Rapid iteration with clear success metrics
  • Logistics domain (high-growth, AI-disruptable)
