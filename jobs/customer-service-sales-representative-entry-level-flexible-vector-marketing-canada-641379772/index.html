About the Role

About Vector Marketing Canada

Student-focused sales org; structured training and growth

Full Description

Vector Marketing is currently interviewing for  customer service–focused Sales Representatives .

In this role, representatives present  CUTCO® Cutlery  to new and existing customers through  one-on-one appointments , focusing on product education and excellent service. Our representatives receive a  guaranteed base pay (paid weekly) , so there is no pressure to make a sale. Instead, we encourage reps to focus on creating a positive customer experience.

What We Offer

Flexible Work Options

Interview, train, and work online from home or wherever you have internet access.

Excellent Pay

Competitive base pay with additional incentives available based on performance. Paid weekly.

Comprehensive Training

We provide structured training before you begin and continued support throughout your time with us.

Flexible Scheduling

Work around your classes, internships, travel plans, or other commitments.

Choice of Work Location

Most interviews take place at our local office, but representatives have flexibility in where they work.

Requirements

  • Enjoy working with people
  • Age  17+  and currently in  Grade 12 or higher
  • Willing to learn and apply new skills

About Vector Marketing

Vector Marketing is the exclusive sales organization for  CUTCO® Cutlery , a premium line of kitchen products made in the USA.

Each year we work with thousands of students across North America, providing flexible work opportunities where individuals can develop valuable communication, presentation, and business skills.

Our Vision

To be universally recognized as the Premier Student Opportunity in Canada.

Our Mission

To provide an environment where people can  learn, grow, and dream  while gaining valuable real-world experience.
