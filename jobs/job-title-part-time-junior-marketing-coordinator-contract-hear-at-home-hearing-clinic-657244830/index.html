Job Title: Part-Time Junior Marketing Coordinator (Contract)

at Hear at Home Hearing Clinic

📍 Location North Vancouver, BC V7N 3Z4
📋 Details Part-Time Contract
📅 Posted Apr 04, 2026

About the Role

Execute marketing campaigns, outreach, and analytics

About Hear at Home Hearing Clinic

Local clinic with community-based client outreach

Full Description

Hear at Home is a boutique hearing clinic based in North Vancouver, providing both in-clinic and mobile hearing care services. We focus on a professional, relationship-driven approach to hearing health and have built a strong reputation in the community.

The Opportunity:
We’re looking for a junior marketer to support our outreach and client engagement efforts on a part-time, contract basis. This role is ideal for someone early in their marketing career who wants hands-on experience building and executing real campaigns, not just designing them.

You’ll be working with an existing client database and helping us strengthen connections with current, past, and potential clients through thoughtful, consistent communication.

What You’ll Be Doing:

  • Developing simple, clear marketing plans focused on client engagement
  • Reaching out to existing and past clients through email and other channels
  • Supporting outreach to new potential clients and community connections
  • Coordinating with our graphic designers to bring campaigns to life
  • Tracking results and helping us understand what’s working and what’s not
  • Suggesting improvements based on data and feedback

What We’re Looking For:

  • Early-career marketer or marketing student/recent graduate
  • Strong communication skills, both written and verbal
  • Organized, self-directed, and able to follow through
  • Comfortable working with data and basic analytics
  • Interest in healthcare or community-based businesses is an asset
  • Ideally based on the North Shore

Why This Role:

  • Real-world experience building and executing marketing campaigns
  • Opportunity to build a strong portfolio with a well-established local business
  • Potential for additional contract work through our network of healthcare professionals
  • Flexible, part-time contract structure

Location:
North Vancouver (some in-person collaboration preferred)

How to Apply:
Please send a short introduction along with your resume and any relevant work or school projects.
