Full Description

Survalent is seeking a dynamic, adaptable, and driven Quality Control coop student/intern for an exciting 8- or 12-months work term this May 2026. In this role, you’ll help support the development, enhancement, and release of features within Survalent’s Advanced Distribution Management System (ADMS). This is an excellent opportunity for a student interested in a career in software testing/development. If this opportunity aligns with your interests, read on to learn more about the position.

Responsibilities & Essential Functions:

•Verify and validate product and project requirements

•Analyze requirements, design test scenarios, and test cases

•Prepare and manage the test environment

•Execute functional, performance, and integration test cases

•Develop and maintain test automation scripts and packages

•Report defects, and follow up on resolutions

•Support customer and sales teams with technical consultancy

•Collaborate with Product Development Teams to build the most reliable solutions

•And other duties as required.

Education, Knowledge, Skills, and Abilities:

•Must be enrolled in a university program specializing in a software development, engineering, or related field

•Experience in the software development lifecycle, software testing, and system integration

•Familiar with waterfall, iterative and agile methodology

•Must have exceptional interpersonal skills, effective verbal, and written communication skills

•Must be self-motivated and proactive with a strong work ethic

•Good teamwork skills

•Computer programming and object-oriented design concepts is an asset

•Familiarity with ADMS, SCADA, OMS, DMS, TCP/IP, FTP, SOA/XML, Modbus, DNP3, SNMP, is an asset

The successful candidate will report to the software Quality Control & Release Management Manager. We have a casual, friendly, and highly collaborative environment. If you want to join a growing organization in this exciting role, please apply with a resume and cover letter by selecting the following link:

About Us:

Survalent Technology Corporation is the leading provider of real-time smart grid management system for enabling visualization, optimization, and improved operations for utilities. For six decades, we have helped more than 800 utilities use operational data to increase reliability, efficiency, and customer service. With our proven and reliable SCADA (Supervisory Control and Data Acquisition), Distribution Management System (DMS), Outage Management System (OMS), and Substation Automation (SA) solutions, utilities transform data into actionable intelligence. We partner with utilities across the globe to create mission critical solutions that will achieve the promise of Smart Grid.
