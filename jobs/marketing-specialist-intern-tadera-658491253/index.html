Job Description Marketing Intern (Aviation Software Industry) Location: Remote

About Us We are a leading provider of innovative software solutions for managing operations, finance, and security in the aviation industry. Our mission is to empower airports and aviation companies with cutting-edge tools to drive efficiency, safety, and growth. We are looking for a motivated and creative Marketing Intern to join our team and help us achieve our ambitious goals for 2025.

Job Responsibilities As a Marketing Intern, you will work closely with our Sales and Marketing team to support marketing strategies, campaigns, and projects that align with our business goals. Your responsibilities will include:

  • Content Creation & Management: Assist in developing and designing marketing materials such as brochures, presentations, blog posts, videos, and social media content. Write engaging copy for email campaigns, website updates, and advertisements.
  • Market Research & Analysis: Research industry trends, competitors, and customer behavior to identify marketing opportunities. Analyze campaign performance metrics and provide actionable recommendations.
  • Digital Marketing: Support the execution of digital marketing campaigns across platforms like LinkedIn, Google Ads, and email marketing tools. Monitor and optimize social media content to increase engagement and brand visibility.
  • Event Coordination: Help organize webinars, trade shows, and client events. Prepare event materials and coordinate logistics for smooth execution.
  • Sales Enablement Support: Collaborate with the sales team to create lead-nurturing workflows and sales decks. Assist with CRM data entry and reporting to track leads and opportunities.
  • Administrative Tasks: Provide general support to the marketing team, including maintaining marketing calendars and schedules.

Qualifications & Skills We are looking for an enthusiastic and proactive individual with the following qualifications:

  • Currently pursuing a degree in Marketing, Business, Communications, or a related field.
  • Strong written and verbal communication skills.
  • Knowledge of digital marketing concepts and social media platforms.
  • Proficiency in Microsoft Office (Word, Excel, PowerPoint).
  • Familiarity with tools like Canva, Adobe Creative Suite, or any CRM (e.g., HubSpot, Salesforce) is a plus.
  • Analytical mindset and attention to detail.
  • Interest in the aviation or technology sector is a bonus.

What We Offer

  • Hands-on experience in marketing for a fast-paced aviation software company.
  • Opportunities to contribute to real-world projects and make an impact.
  • Mentorship and training from experienced marketing professionals.
  • Networking opportunities within the aviation and tech industries.
  • Projects to add to your portfolio.

How To Apply Please submit your resume, a cover letter, and two relevant work samples (e.g., writing or design portfolio)
