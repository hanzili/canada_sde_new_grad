About the Role

Create and manage wellness content across platforms

About SkyFlow Yoga

Small local wellness studio with community focus

Full Description

We are a small, community‑focused Yoga and wellness studio in Markham looking for a part‑time assistant who enjoys creating content, understands social platforms used by local communities, and thrives in a calm supportive environment.

Who This Role Is Perfect For:

This position is ideal for a student or recent grad who:

  • Has experience with content creation or social media marketing
  • Actively uses platforms like WeChat, Little Red Book (Xiaohongshu), Instagram, WhatsApp
  • Understands the operating logic and content rules of Chinese social media platforms with the content and operating rules of WeChat Official Accounts, WeChat Channels, and Xiaohongshu.
  • Is comfortable filming short videos and doing simple edits
  • Enjoys wellness, yoga or fitness environments
  • Lives locally in Markham or nearby
  • Is reliable, kind and eager to learn
  • Can communicate with diverse audiences (English + Chinese and others)

Role Responsibilities

  • Create engaging short‑form content tailored for WeChat and Little Red Book (Xiaohongshu)
  • Manage posting schedules and publish content directly on WeChat, Little Red Book, and Instagram
  • Capture and edit short videos and photos during events/classes
  • Write simple, clear captions that resonate with diverse audiences
  • Monitor comments/messages and support community engagement on these platforms
  • Bring creative ideas and trends, topics, that are culturally relevant
  • Help shape our presence within local wellness communitie(s)
  • Light admin or studio support as needed

This role is ideal for someone who already uses these platforms in their daily life and understands how content spreads within local cultural communities.

Compensation

  • Part‑time role with 5–10 flexible hours per week in studio
  • Compensation aligned with entry‑level/student roles
  • Includes a complimentary 10‑class yoga pack (valued at $200) as part of our wellness benefits

Benefits

  • Complimentary yoga classes
  • Flexible scheduling
  • Calm, supportive work environment
  • Opportunity to build a real portfolio
  • Great for students interested in wellness, fitness, and content creation

How to Apply:

CV + Include:

  • A short intro about yourself
  • Examples/portfolio of content you’ve created
  • The platforms you’re most active on

email : angasaflow@gmail.com
