Full Description

About Echelon Engineering Solutions

Echelon Engineering Solutions Ltd. is a growing engineering consultancy providing practical, field‑driven solutions across land development, transportation, and construction projects. We pride ourselves on technical excellence, strong client relationships, and a collaborative leadership culture.

The Opportunity

We are seeking a Civil Engineer in Training (EIT) to support a variety of stormwater management and land development projects. This role is ideal for someone with strong technical foundations who is ready to take the next step into project coordination, client communication, and team leadership.

Working with senior technical and project management staff, the successful candidate will help design and specify stormwater management (SWM) features, including ponds, Low Impact Development (LID) measures, oil-grit separators (OGS), and storage tanks, for site plan and subdivision projects. The EIT will also support design work for land development and public sector projects, including grading, site servicing, pavement markings, traffic management, and erosion and sediment control. Over time, the successful candidate will take on increasing project management responsibilities, such as coordinating tasks, communicating with clients, tracking schedules, and supporting quality review.

This is a growth‑oriented position with a clear path to full Project Engineer or Project Manager responsibilities.

Key Responsibilities

  • Support the delivery of stormwater management and land development projects
  • Prepare technical analyses, calculations, figures, and draft reports
  • Coordinate project tasks, schedules, and deliverables with internal team members
  • Assist with client communication and meeting preparation
  • Apply municipal and provincial engineering standards to project work
  • Conduct site visits and field reviews as required
  • Support proposal preparation and business development activities
  • Implement company tools, QA/QC processes, templates, and best practices
  • Track task‑level budgets and contribute to project financial monitoring
  • Collaborate with senior staff to ensure high‑quality project outputs

Qualifications & Experience

  • 3-5 years of experience in stormwater management and/or municipal engineering.
  • Degree or diploma in Civil Engineering, or a related discipline
  • Current registration as EIT, or eligibility for P.Eng.
  • Experience with stormwater management and site servicing and grading design
  • Proficiency with:
  • PCSWMM
  • Civil3D, including Land Project Tools
  • Microsoft Office 365
  • Familiarity with Ontario municipal / provincial as well as conservation authority standards
  • Strong written and verbal communication skills
  • Ability to work independently in a hybrid environment
  • Valid Ontario Class G driver’s licence and access to a personal vehicle for site work
  • Experience with project coordination or light project management considered an asset

Work Environment

  • Hybrid work model with remote flexibility
  • Mileage reimbursed for project‑related travel

Why Join Echelon?

  • Join a growing consultancy with long‑term leadership and career advancement opportunities
  • Meaningful work on exciting land development and transportation projects
  • Supportive, collaborative, and mentorship‑focused environment
  • Flexibility, benefits, and opportunities to grow into a full Project Manager role
