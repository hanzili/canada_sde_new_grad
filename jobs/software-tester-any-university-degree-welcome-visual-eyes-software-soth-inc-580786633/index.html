About the Role

Hands-on software QA tester focusing on functional testing and regression.

About Visual-Eyes Software (SOTH Inc.)

Canada-based practice management software provider for eye-care clinics.

Full Description

Inviting Candidates from Alberta, Canada

Visual-Eyes is a leading practice management software for the eye care industry in Canada. We have been in business over 25 years and are looking for a new Software Tester to join our Red Deer team. Our Core Purpose is to be "Partners in the journey to success" with our clients and what we do best is to "Streamline Business Processes".

As a member of the Quality Assurance team, the Software Tester’s primary focus is to ensure our web products and applications are thoroughly tested, documented and demonstrated prior to release. To ensure our customers have a positive software experience, the agent will functionally test as the application is developed and build upon our existing regression testing.

A secondary focus of the quality assurance agent includes supporting our beta clinics, customer support, and internal teams. You will be aiding our technical support team to track down solutions to difficult and complex problems that relate to the software. The agent will need to be the point of contact when updating a client's version and have a rapport with the beta tester on feedback on the potential final product. Trainers and Developers will need the assistance of the QA agent to perform data comparisons and running SQL scripts to ensure information is properly imported into our database format.

Quality assurance agents will be expected to keep active in the testing community to ensure they are acquainted with testing best practices.

Relationships:

Reports to:

  • Lead QA Analyst
  • Product Owners
  • President/CEO

Internal Contacts:

  • Application/Web Development Teams
  • Product Support Team
  • Practice Consultants

External Contacts:

  • Clients
  • Client Vendors
  • Partners

Key Responsibility Areas:

Testing: 50%+

  • Strictly adheres to Agile Testing methodologies.
  • Functionally tests all user stories passed by developers throughout the development process.
  • Provide constructive feedback, upholding integrity and quality of product.
  • Review backlog for any items that require testing to ensure validity.
  • Maintain customer relations with our alpha clinics.
  • Communicate with Product Owners and/or developers for clarifications.
  • Discuss changes with Product Owners, Developers and Testers to ensure intended use of product.
  • Document changes completed within each release.
  • Demonstrate new functionalities to internal team.
  • Receive and process defects from various sources including internal systems, customer service team, and training team.
  • Keep internal and external tracking systems up to date.
  • Help maintain regression and run tests for our application based products (Testcomplete). There will be less focus on this initially for this position.

Technical Support/Trainer Support: 25 - 40%

  • Data Comparisons between legacy systems and ours.
  • Support others in investigating application related issues.
  • Installation/Update of SOTH products.
  • Write/run scripts as needed to support client and internal staff.
  • Document work in case management system (JIRA and Zoho CRM).
  • Cover for staff shortages, following established customer service procedures.

Other: 5 - 10%

  • Stay current on email correspondence.
  • Provide internal training to staff.
  • Perform a variety of other projects and assignments as required.

Performance Measures/Metrics:

  • Logs testing time in JIRA.
  • Provide functional testing results within 48hrs to Developers.
  • Communicate with internal teams on current development priorities, facilitating transparency.
  • Address any backlog tickets within our ticketing system marked as “Requires Testing”.
