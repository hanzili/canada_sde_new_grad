About the Role

Prepare POs, coordinate suppliers, track orders

About Hörmann North America

Global leader in access solutions, innovative supplier partnerships

Full Description

Roles & Responsibilities

  • Prepare and issue purchase orders in SAP for raw materials, hardware, and services.
  • Gather specifications and documentation required for supplier submittals.
  • Process non-stock item purchase requisitions in SAP.
  • Create purchase orders for vendor-managed inventory (VMI) items.
  • Process tag orders and create new material cards as required.
  • Monitor, investigate, and resolve SAP COGI errors.
  • Perform root cause analysis and coordinate corrective actions for material, system, and supplier-related issues.
  • Source, solicit, evaluate, and assist in selecting supplier quotes and proposals.
  • Track and manage open purchase orders, delivery dates, and schedules to ensure alignment with production requirements.
  • Maintain ongoing communication with Engineering, Production, Operations, and Sales Support regarding job completion and delivery timelines.
  • Administer and maintain supplier non-conformance reports.
  • Support corrective and preventative action activities related to supplier performance.
  • Request MSDS documentation for hazardous materials and review with Health & Safety prior to ordering.
  • Ensure compliance with purchasing policies, procedures, regulations, and applicable laws.
  • Participate in Continuous Improvement teams and activities and identify Continuous Improvement opportunities and support solution-based initiatives.
  • Perform monthly Visa purchasing card reconciliation and ensure proper documentation.
  • Maintain accurate purchasing records, documentation, and audit trails within SAP.
  • Perform other duties as assigned to support efficient company operations.
  • Duties and responsibilities may be amended from time to time in order to support efficient company operations.

Qualifications

  • Bachelors or Diploma in a related field preferred.
  • 1–2 years of related experience in purchasing or an equivalent administrative function, ideally in a manufacturing environment.

Our Ideal Candidate

  • Possesses a solid understanding of purchasing processes and supply chain fundamentals.
  • Strong interpersonal and communication skills, with the ability to collaborate across departments and build team rapport.
  • Capable of managing multiple priorities and meeting deadlines in a fast-paced environment.
  • Highly proficient in Microsoft Excel, Word, Outlook, and ERP systems.

Open-minded, adaptable, and eager to take on new challenges and support cross-functional initiatives.

Why You’ll Love Working Here

  • Competitive compensation with performance-based incentives
  • Comprehensive health, dental, vision, and life insurance coverage
  • Generous vacation and paid time off
  • Company-supported retirement savings plan
  • Career development and continuous learning opportunities
  • A collaborative culture where innovation and new ideas are encouraged and valued

Our Values At Hörmann, our core values guide how we work and grow together:

  • Respect – Be Kind: We treat everyone with empathy, fairness, and professionalism.
  • Teamwork – Stronger Together: We succeed as one team across departments and geographies.
  • Passion – Heart and Soul: We bring pride, purpose, and energy to everything we do.
  • Courage – Be Brave: We act with integrity, accountability, and confidence in every decision.

We are proud to foster a culture of Diversity, Equity, Inclusion, and Belonging, where every team member is encouraged to bring their authentic self to work.

About Hörmann Hörmann is a global leader in access solutions serving residential, commercial, and industrial markets. Our North American team is growing rapidly, and we are focused on building something exceptional — together. If you’re passionate about delivering outstanding service, solving technical challenges, and supporting innovative products, we’d love to hear from you.
