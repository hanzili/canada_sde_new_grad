Accounting Intern (Summer 2026)

at Salas O'Brien Canada Inc.

📍 Location Toronto, ON
📋 Details On-site · Summer 2026

About the Role

Code invoices, process expenses, assist accounting

About Salas O'Brien Canada Inc.

Engineering consultancy with strong Canadian project portfolio

Full Description

Accounting intern (On-site)

Position Summary

The Accounting intern will support billing processes, assist with cost tracking, and maintain accurate financial records. The Jr. Project Accountant will be working in a dynamic environment where attention to detail and accuracy are essential.

Key Responsibilities

  • Enter projects in the accounting system at the start of the project once it has been reviewed and approved by the project management team.
  • Review all sub-consultant invoices to proper coding, ensure sub-consultant invoices included on the monthly client billing are posted in a timely manner.
  • Assist in processing of employee expenses
  • Performs other related duties as required

Education & Experience

  • Relevant university degree or college diploma with an accounting focus
  • Strong analytical and problem-solving skills.
  • Strong time-management skills, the ability to manage priorities and meet tight deadlines.
  • Ability to work independently and within a team.
  • Strong attention to detail.
  • Excellent computer skills, specifically with Excel.
  • Experience with Deltek Vision is an asset but not required.
