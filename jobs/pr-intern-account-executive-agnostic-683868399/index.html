About the Role

Support media outreach, social content, and reporting

About Agnostic

Fast-growing Canadian PR and communications agency

Full Description

Agnostic, one of Canada’s fastest-growing communications agencies, is searching for an Intern or Account Executive to join our growing Consumer team. Someone who is excited by fast-paced, insight-driven work with an agency that believes in making time for better thinking.

You will have the opportunity to develop your communications and marketing skills, including: writing and content development, media relations, social media, research, influencer relations, creative thinking, reporting and more.

If you’re ready, we’re interested in hearing from you! Apply here: https://bit.ly/agnosticjobs

This is a paid position. Compensation and role will be based on level of experience.

Roles & Responsibilities

  • Assist account teams with earned media, social media and influencer outreach;
  • Effective written and verbal communications through media pitches, media briefing documents, social copy, backgrounders, fact sheets, etc.;
  • Support the team using strong research skills and a curiosity that can be applied to client work;
  • Conduct media and social monitoring and reporting;
  • Assist with administrative tasks;
  • Be an active participant in the Agnostic community.

Skills & Experience

  • You have a passion for the PR industry and its best practices;
  • You are a graduate of a relevant program with up to one year of work experience in the communications industry - relevant college, university or post-secondary programs include public relations, marketing, journalism or media studies;
  • You possess an understanding of social and digital media which includes drafting content, monitoring and measuring influencer engagement;

Start date : June 2026

We thank all applicants for their interest, however due to the volume of resumes we receive, only those candidates selected for an interview will be contacted.
