About Silversmith Brewing Company

Local Ontario craft brewery with community focus

Full Description

Founded in 2012, Silversmith Brewing Company (SBC) is more than just a brewery and beer hall—it’s a team of enthusiastic and knowledgeable individuals dedicated to delivering a truly memorable beer experience. SBC continues to push the boundaries of craft beer and tourism in Niagara, and more recently expanded with the addition of the Black Oak Brewing facility in Etobicoke, Ontario. The brewery has cultivated a welcoming space where both visitors and locals can come together and enjoy exceptional beer. In late 2022, SBC acquired the assets of Black Oak Brewing Company, incorporating both their product lineup and team into the organization. United, we’re excited about the future of craft brewing and the opportunity to grow stronger together. All of our beers are brewed in-house, and we also provide contract brewing, packaging, and warehousing services for our partners.

We are currently looking for a full-time Junior Social Media and Content Creator in Virgil, ON. In this role, you’ll work closely with the Marketing Manager and be responsible for bringing our brand to life across all social platforms, ensuring our vibe remains authentic, community-focused, and visually appealing. We don’t expect you to have all the answers yet, but we do expect you to have a camera in your hand and a passion for storytelling.

Responsibilities:

  • Daily Content Capture: Be on-site at the taproom to capture authentic, high quality, "of-the-moment" photos and videos.
  • Social Coordination: Schedule posts, write engaging captions, and keep our community conversations flowing across Instagram, Facebook and TikTok.
  • Trends & Reels: Stay on top of social trends and adapt them to the Silversmith brand in a way that feels authentic, not forced.
  • Event Support: Help promote brewery events, live music nights, and community pop-ups.
  • Asset Management: Organize our library of photos and videos for use in newsletters and marketing materials.

Requirements:

  • The Portfolio: Experience in photo/video/content creation/storytelling.
  • Technical Chops: Proficiency in Adobe Creative Suite (Premiere, Lightroom, Photoshop) or mobile editing tools (CapCut, Canva).
  • Reliability: You’re organized, meet deadlines, and are comfortable working in a fast-paced (and sometimes loud) taproom environment.
  • Local Love: A deep appreciation for the Niagara Region and the unique intersection of tourism and local community

Portfolio Requirements (The Important Part)

We value talent and "the eye" over a long resume. We are specifically looking for:

  • Photography: Examples of product (food/drink) or lifestyle photography.
  • Video: At least 2-3 examples of short-form video (Reels/TikToks) that show your ability to edit to a beat and maintain a consistent mood.
  • Voice: Examples of captions or writing that show you can be witty and professional at the same time.
  • Bonus: Show us something "Silversmith-adjacent"—anything that captures a rustic, community, or craft-focused aesthetic.

What We Offer

  • $18-$20/hour
  • Opportunity to grow with an award-winning independent Ontario brewery
  • Fun, team-oriented work environment
  • Staff beer perks and employee discounts

How to Apply: Email your resume, portfolio link, a social media handle that showcases your work, and quick note about why you would be a good fit for the role to josh.kellett@silversmithbrewing.com. Applications without a portfolio or visual examples will not be considered.

We thank all applicants for their interest; however, only those selected for an interview will be contacted.

Pay: $18.00-$20.00 per hour

Benefits:

  • Casual dress
  • Discounted or free food
  • On-site parking

Work Location: In person
