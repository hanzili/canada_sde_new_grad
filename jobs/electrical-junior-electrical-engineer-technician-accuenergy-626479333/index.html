Full Description

About Us

Accuenergy is a Toronto-based company dedicated to research, design and manufacturing of multifunction power meters and other instruments. We also provide advanced technological solutions for distribution and industrial power system automation.

Extraordinary outcomes need exceptional teams. We believe deeply in the power of a growth mindset for individuals and team development.

DUTIES & RESPONSIBILTIES:

· Deliver technical designs for projects of varying complexity that could involve technical complexity

· Assists in creating project plans, participates in project planning and project management meetings and provides improvement recommendations to ensure project deliverables and timelines are met

· Create production instruction documents (AutoCAD drawings, Assembly Work Instructions, etc)

· Instruct and supervise manufacturing workflow following product design and quality control requirements

· Record, compile and maintain production record

· Other duties as assigned

KNOWLEDGE & SKILLS

· Post-Secondary education diploma or equivalent, related to Mechanical/Electrical Engineering is an asset.

· Minimum 1 year of AutoCAD drafting experience

· Work background in electrical engineering and/or manufacturing is preferred

· Self-starter and a team player in a diversified and dynamic and fast-paced product development environment

· Energetic, self-motivated and enthusiastic

· Ability to handle multiple diverse assignments successfully in a consulting firm environment

· Detail-oriented with respect to quality and accuracy

· Capable of working independently, while being a collaborative member of the team

· Takes initiative to complete tasks in progress and seek creative solutions

· Problem-solving and resourcefulness

· Ability to manage time effectively to meet deadlines

· Uncompromised commitment to confidentiality
