About Davina s Swim House

Full Description

Junior Marketing Coordinator

A Bit About Us

Core Values: Family, Growth, Integrity, Safety, and Fun

Founded in 2003, Davina’s Swim House started with a backyard pool and a passion for teaching swimming. Today, we’re proud to be a leader in the private swim school industry for over 20 years.

Our mission is simple: to provide high quality swimming lessons in a warm, welcoming environment. We ensure that safety, comfort, and a personal touch is given to each and everyone who comes to our facilities!

The Opportunity

As DSH continues to grow, we are expanding our marketing team to include a Junior Marketing Coordinator. In this role you will be working closely with our Marketing team, Aquatic Managers, and Aquatic Instructors. The role is a full-time position requiring evening and weekend hours.

You’ll be in charge of making sure that DSH and it’s associated brands online presence is as vibrant as our swim classes. As well as supporting the communication of the company’s vision through branding. From creating eye-catching digital and printed content to engaging with our community, you’ll help us make a splash in the world.

Who You Are

You are looking for an opportunity to increase your skills in marketing and play a key role at a growing company. You value personal and professional growth and are ready to take the next step in advancing your career. You are creative, enthusiastic, thrive in a fast-paced environment, and know how to have fun!

If this sounds like you, then you would fit in well with the culture at DSH!

Responsibilities

  • Content Creation: Design and create engaging content for our marketing channels using Canva, CapCut (or similar editing tools), and other creative platforms.
  • Social Media Management: Schedule and post content across all social media channels using Meta Business Suite.
  • Content Calendar Management: Plan, organize, and maintain a content calendar to ensure consistent posting and alignment with marketing campaigns.
  • Community Engagement: Actively engage with our online and offline communities, respond to comments, messages, and create a positive experience for our followers.
  • Blog Writing: Write and publish blog posts that resonate with our audience, focusing on topics related to swimming and water safety.
  • Analytics & Reporting: Monitor social media performance and provide regular updates on engagement, growth, and trends.

The Skill Set

  • 1-2 years of work experience in a similar capacity
  • College or university degree in marketing, communications, graphic design, or another related field
  • Experience with social media management tools, preferably Meta Business Suite.
  • Proficiency in Canva and video editing software like CapCut (or similar).
  • Strong writing skills with the ability to create engaging blog posts and social media captions.
  • A keen eye for design and attention to detail.
  • Ability to work independently and manage multiple tasks in a fast-paced environment.
  • Passion for swimming is a plus!

Job Requirements

  • Able to commute to the office on a hybrid work schedule
  • Availability to work on a flexible schedule, sometimes including evenings and weekends
  • Ability to travel across locations in the GTA

What the Recruitment Process Looks Like

  • A phone screen with HR who will discuss your interest in DSH and how your background aligns with this role
  • A virtual interview with the Growth, Strategy, and Development Manager and HR will allow you to expand on your experience and ask further questions about the role and team
  • You’ll be asked to create a social media post to show off your skills
  • A final virtual interview with management to answer any remaining questions you may have.

At DSH, we’re committed to diversity, equity, and inclusion. We are an equal opportunity employer. We welcome and encourage applications from ALL qualified candidates. If, at any point in the recruitment process, you require any accommodations or assistance, please contact us at hr@davinasswimhouse.com. We thank all candidates for their interest in DSH however, only those candidates selected for an interview will be contacted.
