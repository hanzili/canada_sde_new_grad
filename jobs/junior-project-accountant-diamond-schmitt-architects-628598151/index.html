Full Description

We are looking for an energetic and enthusiastic individual to join our Finance team as a Junior Project Accountant.

This is a finance role designed to support Project Managers through the life of a project, where attention to detail and accuracy are essential. The Junior Project Accountant will support billing processes, assist with cost tracking, and maintain accurate financial records.

The ideal candidate will have excellent verbal and written communication skills, very good organizational skills, and ability to multi-task in a busy office.

This full-time position offers a hybrid work schedule with a minimum requirement of four days in the office. Working hours are Monday to Friday, 9:00 a.m. ‚Äì 6:00 p.m. with summer hours and holiday closure.
