Internal Auditor Co-Op

at IKO North America

📍 Location Mississauga, Ontario
📋 Details Co-op · 8-16 months · CAD 22-27/hour · Summer 2026
📅 Posted May 05, 2026

About the Role

Support internal audits; document processes; analyze risks

About IKO North America

Canadian manufacturing firm; strong audit program

Full Description

IKO Industries Ltd. is a market leader in the manufacturing of roofing and building materials. IKO is a Canadian owned and operated business with production facilities worldwide and has many years of unparalleled success in the roofing materials industry. Quality, integrity, and trustworthiness are the values that underlie this success, and we have built this company by hiring people who hold these values. People like you!

Job Description Internal Audit Co‑Op (Summer 2026 | 8, 12, or 16‑month work term | Conversion potential) WHAT’S IN IT FOR YOU?

  • Competitive compensation: $22.00 – $27.00 per hour
  • Start date: Summer 2026
  • Term length: 8, 12, or 16 months depending on availability
  • Hands‑on exposure to enterprise‑level internal audit, risk, and controls
  • Meaningful responsibility and mentorship within a collaborative audit team
  • Potential for full‑time hire upon successful completion of the work term
  • Be part of a stable, long‑standing industry leader with operations across North America

When You Join Us, You Will Reporting to the Internal Audit Manager, you’ll support a variety of audit, risk, and advisory engagements across the business. This role provides broad exposure to operational, financial, regulatory, and IT‑related risks.

You Will

  • Support the planning and execution of internal audits and advisory engagements across business units
  • Document business processes, procedures, and controls using established audit methodologies
  • Perform audit procedures including walkthroughs, sample testing, data analysis, and evidence review
  • Identify, document, and help assess issues, risks, and root causes
  • Support evaluation of operational, financial, and IT controls and governance processes
  • Assist in preparing clear, concise audit findings and value‑added recommendations
  • Help draft audit reports and presentations for Internal Audit Management and business stakeholders
  • Participate in risk assessments and contribute to the annual audit plan
  • Support investigations of unusual or suspicious transactions as required
  • Maintain audit documentation in audit software in accordance with timelines and standards
  • Complete special projects and advisory work as assigned

This is a hands‑on role where you’ll be treated as a contributing member of the team, not a shadow resource.

Our Ideal Candidate We’re looking for a motivated, mature, and curious student who is eager to learn and ready to take ownership of their work.

You Are

  • Enrolled in or recently completed a university‑level program in Accounting, Finance, Engineering, or a closely related field

  • Final‑semester or near‑graduation students are strongly preferred

  • Master’s or post‑graduate students with an interest in the CPA path are encouraged to apply

  • Familiar with accounting, auditing, risk, or controls concepts through coursework, projects, or practical exposure

  • Analytical, detail‑oriented, and comfortable working with data and documentation
  • A strong communicator who can interact professionally with stakeholders at various levels
  • Able to work independently, manage multiple priorities, and handle sensitive or confidential information appropriately
  • Proficient with Microsoft Office (Excel, Word, PowerPoint)

  • Able and willing to travel (approximately 10–20% domestic and international, primarily within Canada and the U.S.)

  • Travel is typically short‑duration (4–5 business days)

  • In possession of a valid driver’s license; access to a vehicle is preferred

Benefits of Employment: IKO recognizes that its success is due to the strength of its employees. A primary goal of IKO is to promote individual employee's sense of accomplishment and contribution so that employees enjoy their association with IKO. The Company invests in its employees so that they are the most knowledgeable in the industry, and undertakes great efforts to nurture loyalty to, and teamwork at, IKO. We are pleased to offer competitive compensation, health care, a progressive and challenging workplace and a commitment to teamwork and integrity.

Diversity and Equal Opportunity Employment: IKO Industries Ltd. is an equal opportunity employer. We are committed to diversity and inclusion and are pleased to consider all qualified applicants for employment without consideration to race, religion, creed, color, national origin, age, gender, sexual orientation, marital status, veteran status or disability. IKO Industries Ltd. encourages and welcomes applications from people with disabilities. Accommodations are available on request for candidates taking part in all aspects of the selection process.
