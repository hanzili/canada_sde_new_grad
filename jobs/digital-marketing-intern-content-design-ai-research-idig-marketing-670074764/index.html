About idig Marketing

Full Description

Company Overview

iDIG Marketing specializes in delivering high-impact digital marketing and tailored technology solutions. We are a team that loves a challenge, seamlessly combining custom web development, data analytics, digital advertising, and content strategy to help our clients grow.

About the Role

iDIG Marketing is seeking a creative and detail-oriented Digital Marketing Intern to join our content team this summer. This is a hands-on role where you will support the development of content for a diverse range of clients, primarily in home service related industries like HVAC.

Working remotely, you will collaborate daily with our Lead Copywriter and Graphic Designer to assist in developing content, specifically visual creative, utilizing Adobe Creative Suite, similar software, and AI tools. You will also play a key role in supporting iDIG Marketing’s own social media presence, while dedicating a significant portion of your time to performing the legwork and research for our innovative AI initiatives.

Key Responsibilities

  • Creative Content Production: Collaborate with the design and copy teams to produce high-quality visual assets for a wide range of client social media platforms and websites.
  • iDIG Brand Support: Heavily assist in the production of content designed to grow iDIG Marketing’s own social media and brand presence.
  • Website Creative Support: Assist the team in developing and updating creative elements for client websites, ensuring a cohesive look and feel across all digital touchpoints.
  • Cross-Channel Assets: Assist with creative components for integrated campaigns, including digital ads, blog posts, landing pages, and e-newsletters.
  • Creative Innovation & AI: Brainstorm "outside the box" engagement concepts and utilize AI tools to experiment with new ways to streamline and enhance our creative output.
  • AI Research & Integration: Act as the "boots on the ground" for new technology. You will research emerging AI tools, test their practical applications for marketing workflows, and help the team implement them to improve efficiency.

What We’re Looking For

  • Design Proficiency: You are proficient in Adobe Creative Suite (Photoshop and InDesign), or similar softwares, and can manage visual layouts and image editing independently.
  • Self-Starter: The ability to manage multiple projects independently in a fast-paced, remote environment.
  • Digital Knowledge: A basic understanding of SEO and experience working within content management systems is a plus.
  • Tech-Savvy & Adaptable: You have a strong aptitude for technology, actively staying ahead of digital trends and quickly mastering new platforms.
  • Task-Oriented: You are detail-oriented and capable of conducting thorough research to support the team.
  • Education: Currently pursuing a degree or diploma in Marketing, Design, Visual Studies, or a related field (upper-year students preferred).

Why You Should Apply

  • Paid Agency Experience: Earn a wage while gaining full-time experience within a professional agency environment.
  • Portfolio Building: Work on real-world projects and live campaigns that you can showcase to future employers.
  • Remote Flexibility: Work from anywhere while remaining an active part of a collaborative creative team.
  • Mentorship: Work directly with senior copywriters and designers to hone your craft.

Job Type: Internship / Co-op
Contract length: 4 months

Pay: $18.00 per hour

Benefits:

  • Paid time off
  • Work from home
