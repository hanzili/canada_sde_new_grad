Full Description

ABOUT ONEX:

Onex is an investor and asset manager that invests capital on behalf of Onex shareholders and clients across the globe. Formed in 1984, we have a long track record of creating value for our clients and shareholders. Onex became a public company in 1987 and is listed on the Toronto Stock Exchange under the symbol ONEX. Onex’ two primary businesses are Private Equity and Credit. In Private Equity, we raise funds from third-party investors and invest them, along with Onex’ own investing capital, through the funds of our private equity platforms: Onex Partners and ONCAP. Similarly, in Credit, we raise and invest capital across several private credit, liquid credit and public equity strategies.

Our investors include a broad range of global clients, including public and private pension plans, sovereign wealth funds, insurance companies, family offices and high net worth individuals. Onex has $55.9 billion in assets under management, of which $8.4 billion is Onex’ own investing capital. We generate value for our shareholders through two segments: Investing and Asset Management.

To explore more opportunities at Onex, visit: https://job-boards.greenhouse.io/onexgeneral.

The Opportunity:

Reporting to the Manager, Finance based in Toronto, Onex is seeking to hire an Accounting Summer Intern to join the Finance team. The Accounting Summer Intern will play an important role in supporting the finance team with accounting and administrative tasks.

Key Responsibilities:

  • Set up tax payments in various tax portals
  • Clean up of Microsoft Business Central – clear unposted entries, billings and unapplied credit memorandums
  • Update master list of accounts for new/closed bank accounts and ensure that these are tagged with the correct legal entity names
  • Streamline wire payment templates for our US and Canadian banks
  • Roll forward surveys for Statistics Canada
  • Prepare annual BEA Filings
  • Review bank statements and prepare bank reconciliations for certain entities

Candidate Profile:

Professional Characteristics

  • Must be pursuing a university degree in accounting (major or minor)
  • Strong business acumen and work ethic, with the desire, ability, and curiosity to navigate competing issues
  • Strong communication skills and the ability to work to complete tasks within tight deadlines
  • Ownership of work and desire to take tasks to the next level
  • Attention to detail and excellent communication skills
  • Proficiency in Microsoft Excel

Personal Characteristics

  • A pattern of excellence, both personally and professionally, demonstrated by the individual’s education track record
  • A high standard of integrity and ability to maintain the highest degree of confidentiality
  • Self-awareness and irreproachable integrity
  • A strong work ethic and seeks to complete work effectively and efficiently

Compensation and Additional Information:

The expected compensation for this position is C$850/week. This internship will be 8-weeks in duration, starting in early June.

This position is for a current vacancy.

Our culture is guided by our strong commitment to accountability, intellectual honesty and respect for all our partners and stakeholders. Also crucial to our long-term success is the alignment of interests between the Onex management team, the Onex Board of Directors, shareholders and our limited partners.
