About Dawson Group

Full Description

Dawson Construction is looking for a Project Coordinator/Junior Estimator who has a team-oriented mindset, and the ability to work effectively in a fast-paced construction environment.

As a Project Coordinator/Junior Estimator at Dawson Construction, you will play a key role in supporting project operations and ensuring smooth execution from start to finish. This is a seasonal, full-time position, where you will report directly to the Project Manager & Estimator and collaborate with project teams to help deliver high-quality construction projects. Your base location will be Kamloops, British Columbia and will require travel throughout our province.

// Click here to learn more about Dawson Construction!

WHAT SUCCESS LOOKS LIKE

  • Assist in project planning, scheduling, and coordination to ensure timely and cost-effective completion.
  • Monitor project progress, track key deliverables, and report on project performance to the Project Manager.
  • Prepare and maintain project documentation, including submittals, RFIs, change orders, and progress reports.
  • Coordinate with subcontractors, suppliers, and field teams to ensure smooth workflow and adherence to project timelines.
  • Conduct site visits to monitor work quality, safety compliance, and progress.
  • Assist with bid preparation and tender submissions, including takeoffs, pricing support, and bid documentation.
  • Ensure compliance with contract documents, specifications, and regulatory requirements.
  • Assist in the preparation and negotiation of change orders and claims.
  • Facilitate communication between internal teams, clients, and stakeholders to address project challenges.

WHAT YOU BRING

  • Engineering Degree, Civil Engineering Technology diploma, or a related field is an asset.
  • Minimum 1-3 years in construction, with experience in project coordination, estimating, or field engineering.
  • An understanding of construction methodologies, scheduling, and cost control. Experience with estimating software (preferably HCSS) is an asset.
  • Familiarity with paving, highways, and municipal works.
  • Strong interpersonal skills with the ability to coordinate with diverse teams.
  • Ability to anticipate project challenges and assist in developing proactive solutions.
  • Strong organizational skills with an emphasis on accuracy and efficiency.

BENEFITS

Looking to be part of a well-established family-owned company? Our core values include: People First, Integrity, Excellence and Respect, and as such we are committed to providing an environment that supports the growth and effectiveness of our employees and the organization. You will work for a company that offers an excellent compensation package, pension, and medical benefits, as well as learning and long-term advancement opportunities.

The Dawson Group is expanding our reach and need people like you to join us on our journey, and to help us continue safely connecting our communities.
