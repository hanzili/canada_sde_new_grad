Full Description

Job Summary:

CIMCO is looking to hire a Automation Engineer Co-op student on a 16 month term starting May 2026. This position will be based out of our Burlington office.

Join our team! This is an existing vacancy that we are actively looking to fill.

Role Responsibilities

  • Provide support in the design, configuration, integration, implementation and support of automation solutions for variety of systems in
  • Recreation and Industrial refrigeration facilities. This includes the creation of DDC/PLC code and HMI interfaces based on company standards and specifications
  • Panel and package testing/checkout for manufactured equipment to ensure that the electrical drawings and design have been followed
  • Assist with tasks in the office such as: shipping/receiving preparation of automation components and preparing HMI computers for customers
  • Take the lead on small jobs such as equipment and software upgrades. Depending on qualifications, experience, and demonstrated abilities, a candidate could be considered for more complex projects during the progression of the co-op work term
  • Document engineering designs, prepare record files
  • Contribute to the continuous design improvement process

Required Professional Knowledge, Skills & Qualifications

  • Currently pursuing a bachelors degree in Automation Engineering, Mechatronics Engineering or similar
  • Ability to work with customers, consultants, internal staff, and external vendors to coordinate projects.
  • Ability to troubleshoot automation control systems from the field element up through operator interfaces and databases.
  • Experience with control system networking technologies.
  • Working knowledge of electrical and electronic instrumentations
  • Excellent written and verbal communication skills.
  • Strong interpersonal skills and the ability to work in a team environment.
  • Ability to work effectively in a fast paced multi-tasking environment.
  • Ability to work alone or in a team and accept responsibility for the work performed.
  • Ability to prioritize work and deal with conflicts in order to meet deadlines.

We thank all applicants for their interest, however, only those selected for an interview will be contacted.

Company Profile

CIMCO specializes in the engineering, design, manufacture, installation and service of complex thermal building solutions for industrial, process cooling and recreational refrigeration and mechanical systems. For more than 100 years, CIMCO’s leadership and unparalleled research, development and manufacturing programs has put us at the forefront of industry advancements and technological breakthroughs. Our professional engineers, technicians and designers apply innovative state-of-the-art equipment and refrigerants to develop systems that meet even the most complex thermal challenges.

We offer our employees excellent compensation, medical and dental benefit programs, retirement savings options, career development opportunities, mentoring and continuing education.
