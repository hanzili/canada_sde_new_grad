Full Description

JSI Telecom is seeking a Junior Systems Engineer who is eager to develop technical, analytical, and business skills in a collaborative environment. This role is ideal for someone early in their career who enjoys problem-solving, learning enterprise infrastructure technologies, and automating systems through Infrastructure as Code (IaC).

This position is based in Kanata, Ontario and hybrid with the expectation of two in-office days per week.

Responsibilities:

  • Assist with the qualification, automation, configuration, and operation of new infrastructure components.

  • Participate in projects involving the implementation of new hardware and enterprise infrastructure.

  • Collaborate with senior systems engineers, Sales, Production, and Technical Support teams to engineer and deliver infrastructure solutions.

  • Contribute to feature development and user stories related to hardware and infrastructure engineering under senior guidance.

  • Work with Systems Engineering and Production teams to implement, document, and standardize new technologies and configurations.

  • Develop delivery and implementation documentation for use by internal teams.

  • Provide training to the Technical Support team on new and upgraded technologies.

  • Deliver third-tier (Tier 3) technical support for infrastructure-related issues.

  • Occasional travel as required to support projects and deployments.

Required Skills & Qualifications

  • Strong interest in Infrastructure as Code (IaC) and automation using tools such as GitHub, Terraform/OpenTofu, or Ansible.

  • Interest in learning enterprise hardware platforms including Dell and Cisco server, networking, and storage technologies.

  • Interest in virtualization technologies such as VMware vSphere, Proxmox, and KubeVirt.

  • Aptitude for troubleshooting computer systems and infrastructure-level issues.

  • Ability to communicate effectively with both technical and non-technical stakeholders.

  • Curiosity, creativity, and a strong willingness to learn.

  • Basic programming background with an interest in automation or scripting.

  • Ability to obtain and maintain a Top-Secret Security Clearance.

Preferred Education

Degree in Engineering, Computer Science, or a related field

Preferred Technical Experience (Assets)

  • Networking fundamentals (switching, routing, firewalling)

  • Cisco ASA firewall configuration and implementation

  • Software-Defined Networking (VMware NSX, CentOS 9 iptables, Kubernetes service mesh)

  • Dell networking equipment (N1500, N2000, S4000, S5000)

  • Cisco switching platforms (IOS, NX-OS)

  • Kubernetes networking concepts (CNI, Calico, NCP)

  • Dell server platforms (R7x0, VRTX/M6x0, T6x0)

  • iSCSI SAN and NAS solutions (EMC Unity, Isilon, PowerScale)

  • Software-defined storage (vSAN, Ceph)

  • Enterprise Windows and Linux environments

  • Scripting or automation using PowerShell, Python, Ruby, or Excel VBA

  • Exposure to cloud technologies

  • Experience working in an Agile development environment

About JSI Telecom

JSI Telecom (www.jsitelecom.com) is a privately owned technology company founded in 1979, with offices in Canada, the United States, Germany, and Australia. Employing more than 300 professionals worldwide, JSI has built a strong reputation as a highly successful and profitable organization that consistently exceeds customer expectations.

JSI is a recognized leader in the design and delivery of acquisition, collection, and analysis solutions for law enforcement and intelligence communities. Our flagship Unified 4Sight platform provides a powerful and flexible data management solution, enabling customers to integrate and analyze data from multiple disparate sources within an intuitive, visually focused environment.
