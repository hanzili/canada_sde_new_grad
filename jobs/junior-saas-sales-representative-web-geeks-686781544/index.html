Full Description

Job Title: Junior Saas Sales Representative (Hybrid - Part-time to Full-time)

Location: Remote/Windsor, ON

Company: Aloha Sales by Web Geeks Inc

Website: webgeeks.com

Role Summary
Web Geeks Inc. is looking for ambitious and self-motivated Junior SaaS Sales Representatives to help grow our AI-powered business growth & automation platform designed for contractors, service businesses, and growing companies.

For over 30 years, Web Geeks has helped businesses grow through digital marketing. Now, we’re expanding into the future of AI-powered marketing, communication, and business automation.

This is an exciting ground-floor opportunity for someone looking to break into high-ticket tech and SaaS sales. We’re building the sales structure from the ground up, which means there’s significant room for growth, leadership, and long-term earning potential for the right people.

This role is best suited for highly driven individuals who are motivated by performance, personal growth, and uncapped income potential.

What You'll Do:
- Build relationships with business owners and decision-makers
- Introduce prospects to Aloha Sales and how it helps businesses automate communication, marketing, follow-up, and customer management
- Generate and manage leads through outreach, networking, referrals, and inbound opportunities
- Conduct discovery conversations to understand client needs and business pain points
- Present software solutions in a simple, confident, and consultative way
- Follow up with leads consistently through phone, email, text, and social communication
- Collaborate with our internal team to improve sales systems, scripts, and processes as we continue building the department
- Learn the fundamentals of SaaS sales, automation systems, AI tools, and high-ticket consulting sales

What We're Looking For:
- Strong communication and people skills
- Self-motivated with a strong work ethic and entrepreneurial mindset
- Comfortable talking with business owners and professionals
- Coachable and willing to learn new systems and sales strategies
- Confident using technology, social media, and online communication tools
- Ability to stay organized and follow up consistently
- Positive attitude with resilience and persistence
- Sales experience is an asset but not required; we are open to training the right person

Bonus:
- Experience in customer service, sales, trades, hospitality, fitness sales, or business development
- Interest in AI, technology, SaaS, marketing, or entrepreneurship
- Experience using CRM systems, social media, or marketing tools

Compensation & Growth:
- Commission-based with uncapped commission potential
- Commission earned on both onboarding fees AND recurring monthly subscriptions
- Opportunity to build long-term recurring income as your client base grows
- Flexible schedule with hybrid/remote opportunities available
- Hands-on training and mentorship from a marketing company with 30+ years of industry experience
- Opportunity to grow into leadership and senior sales roles as Aloha Sales expands

Why Join?
You’ll be entering the fast-growing world of AI and SaaS while working with a company that already has decades of business experience and credibility behind it.

We’re looking for people who are hungry, driven, and excited to grow with us as we build the next chapter of Web Geeks and Aloha Sales together.

If you’re looking for a high-upside opportunity where your effort directly impacts your income and future growth, we’d love to hear from you.

Job Types: Full-time, Part-time
