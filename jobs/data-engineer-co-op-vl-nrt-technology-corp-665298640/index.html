About NRT

At NRT, we build more than solutions: we build up each other.

NRT is a worldwide leader in future-ready FinTech and Information Technology designed to elevate and enhance the enterprise gaming industry. Our next-generation solutions encompass every aspect of the modern integrated resort, including secure payment systems, AML compliance and reporting tools, credit information and electronic marker services to intelligent and engaging table game platforms, dynamic financial and marketing kiosks, and our completely customizable digital gamification and mobile experiences. Our strategic partnerships have resulted in the most convenient, reliable, and secure omni-channel payment ecosystem in the industry. Our collective solutions are used by casinos, race and sports operators, lotteries, banks, and retailers-- globally-- to dazzle their guests.

We believe in the value of connection and community. Our diversity in skills and background make us a unique and formidable culture. We encourage the growth of individual development and recognize that our success depends on developing each other. To stand firm is to stand together.

Joining NRT is more than a job-- it’s an opportunity to grow and discover.

To learn more about who we are and what we do, visit our website at www.nrttech.com.

The Data Engineer Co-Op will support end-to-end data operations by collecting, organizing, validating, preparing, documenting, and reporting on datasets while supporting data pipelines, labeling tasks, and workflow improvements.

Core Competencies

  • Initiative
  • Results Oriented
  • Teamwork
  • Customer Focus
  • Adaptability
  • Detail Oriented
  • Problem-solving
  • Relationship Building

Responsibilities

  • Collaborate with cross-functional teams to support the design and maintenance of data pipelines for analytics and AI-driven applications
  • Assist in data collection and retrieval from various sources, including APIs, databases, and internal systems
  • Organize and manage datasets, ensuring data quality, consistency, and proper structure
  • Perform data labeling and annotation tasks to support model development and evaluation
  • Conduct data review and validation, identifying inconsistencies, missing data, and anomalies
  • Support the preparation of datasets for downstream use, including cleaning, formatting, and basic transformations
  • Generate and assist in preparing reports, summaries, and dashboards for internal teams
  • Document data sources, workflows, and processes for clarity and reproducibility
  • Contribute to improving data workflows and identifying opportunities for automation
  • Other projects/duties as assigned

Qualifications

  • Currently pursuing a degree in Computer Science, Engineering, Data Science, or a related field
  • Basic understanding of data handling and data processing concepts
  • Familiarity with Python, SQL or willingness to learn
  • Experience with Excel, Google Sheets, or similar tools for data organization
  • Strong attention to detail and ability to work with large datasets
  • Good analytical and problem-solving skills
  • Strong written and verbal communication skills
  • Ability to work independently and collaboratively in a team environment

Preferred Qualifications

  • Basic knowledge of Python or another scripting language
  • Familiarity with data visualization tools (e.g., Power BI, Tableau)
  • Exposure to databases or APIs
  • Interest in AI, data engineering, or data analytics
