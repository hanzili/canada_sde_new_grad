Join a company that is passionately committed to the pursuit of a better world through positive change. With more than 65 years of business and technical expertise in mining , energy , and infrastructure , our 10,000 colleagues in 150 countries tirelessly work toward bettering the industries in which we operate. With practical solutions that are safe, innovative and sustainable, we think globally while acting locally.

Join our Project Delivery Group (PDG), a dynamic team of professionals passionately committed to world-class project delivery across the globe. With expertise in sustainable studies, asset building and operations, and industry-leading efficiencies, our best-in-class team leverages methodologies, governance and systems that are unparalleled in the engineering space. Are you looking for an opportunity to join a diverse group of professionals who are committed to remaining exceptional? We look forward to hearing from you.

Looking to get the most out of your upcoming internship? Hatch is seeking a Geotechnical Engineering Student for an internship starting Summer 2026 . Reporting to Geotechnical you will support offices globally working from our Niagara Falls office location.

You Will What Will an Internship at Hatch Offer You:

  • Be given opportunities to contribute to complex projects around the world in meaningful ways.
  • Work on problems and gain experience relevant to your field of study in an office environment.
  • Collaborate with diverse teams locally and globally.
  • Work in an exciting environment where you can apply your academic learning on a daily basis.
  • Benefit from formal and informal mentorship from some of the leading minds and authorities in the industry.
  • Participate in local Lunch and Learns, Hatch Charity Events, the triannual Student Showcase, and more to develop your skills.
  • Join in charitable, social, and sports activities with other Hatch employees.

We’re entrepreneurs with a technical soul. Our flat organizational structure creates an atmosphere of openness and participation. We build practical solutions that are safe, innovative, and sustainable. Our ultimate goal is to achieve no harm for our people, our clients, and our communities.

As The Successful Candidate, You Will

  • Working closely with our engineers, geoscientists, and designers, your primary role will be to act as a technical resource on challenging projects.
  • You will apply analytical, conceptual, research, and planning skills to support geotechnical investigations, geological characterization and geotechnical design and analysis as well as construction.
  • Candidates will be exposed to modern design tools, including 3D modeling, geotechnical analysis, and complex calculation software.
  • Work closely with experienced professional engineers and geoscientists who will provide guidance and mentoring.
  • You will gain experience in a variety of technical areas.

You Bring To The Role

  • Pursuing a degree in Geological engineering or Earth Sciences program
  • Collaborative team player with excellent interpersonal skills.
  • Intellectually curious and innovative.
  • Comfortable initiating new ways of getting things done.
  • Love to learn and willing to explore new ventures.
  • Persistent and resilient.
  • Critical and creative thinker and problem solver, who brings forward solutions for our clients' toughest challenges.
  • Strong sense of community and is aware of one’s impact on colleagues and the larger team.

Competencies (knowledge, Skill, Attributes)

  • Good understanding of engineering and business fundamentals as they apply to engineering problems and projects.
  • Excel academically while being actively involved in extra-curricular and/or volunteer activities at school or in the community.
  • Ability to learn quickly and to take on new challenges.
  • Collaborative team player with excellent interpersonal skills.
  • Excellent organizational skills and attention to detail.
  • Technical proficiency in CAD, Microsoft Word, and Microsoft Excel.
  • Strong written and verbal communication skills.
  • Shows critical and creative thinking and problem solving and brings forward solutions for our clients' toughest challenges.
  • Adapts and embraces change.
