About the Role

Build dashboards, analyze data, develop BI reports

About Welbilt

Global foodservice equipment brand Garland

Full Description

We are Garland! We design, manufacture and supply best-in-class commercial ovens, ranges, grills and countertop cooking equipment for the global foodservice market. We empower you to grow with us. We recognize you and reward you. We win together as one team.

Garland Commercial Ranges is looking for a motivated and detail-oriented Data & Business Intelligence Intern on an 8 to 12-month contract!

This role will support ongoing data initiatives across multiple business functions. This role is ideal for someone who wants hands-on experience working with real-world datasets, building dashboards, and translating business requirements into actionable insights.

You will work to develop and enhance reporting solutions that contribute directly to dashboards, KPIs, and decision-making.

Reporting to the Director, Information Technology I, this position is 100% based in-office at our Mississauga, Ontario facility. This is an 8 to 12-month contract, which is an addition to headcount.

Major Accountability:

  • Design, build, and maintain Power BI dashboards and reports
  • Write and troubleshoot DAX calculations (e.g., KPIs, aggregations, filters)
  • Assist in data validation and troubleshooting discrepancies
  • Collaborate with business users to gather requirements and translate them into reporting solutions
  • Perform data extraction, transformation, and cleansing from multiple sources (e.g., SQL databases, Excel, Salesforce)
  • Support ongoing improvements to existing dashboards (performance, usability, accuracy)
  • Document data definitions, logic, and reporting processes

Qualifications

  • Specialized Skills, Knowledge, and Abilities

    • Strong analytical thinking and attention to detail
  • Basic experience with Power BI or other data visualization tools (academic or personal projects acceptable)
  • Familiarity with Excel (pivot tables, formulas, data cleansing)
  • Understanding of relational data concepts (tables, joints, keys)

  • Ability to communicate clearly with both technical and non-technical stakeholders

  • Nice to have’s

    • Experience with DAX and Power Query (M language)
  • Basic knowledge of SQL
  • Exposure to tools such as Salesforce, ERP systems, or data warehouses
  • Experience working with large datasets or business reporting

  • Familiarity with data visualization best practices

  • Education and Experience

    • Currently pursuing or recently completed a degree in Data Analytics, Computer Science, Mathematics, Business Analytics, or related field

  • Working Conditions

  • Heavy computer use.

  • Normal office environment; must be able to sit for long periods

  • What you will gain

  • Hands-on experience working on production-level dashboards
  • Exposure to real business problems and cross-functional collaboration
  • Practical knowledge of data modeling, DAX, and reporting architecture
  • Mentorship and guidance in analytics and data best practices
  • Opportunity to contribute to impactful projects used by leadership teams

We value curiosity, initiative, and a willingness to learn. Even if you don’t meet all qualifications, we encourage you to apply if you’re passionate about data and analytics.

  • Garland, a Welbilt brand, has been a leading manufacturer for over 60 years which designing and producing a full line of commercial ovens, ranges, griddles, grills, conveyor ovens & countertop cooking equipment. The products are sold to leading fast food chains as well as independent restaurants in over +100 countries. The facility, based in Mississauga, supports our Garland brand and is a division of Ali Group.

For more information about Garland, visit www.garland-group.com
