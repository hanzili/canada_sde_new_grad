Full Description

About Us

Williams Meaden and Moore Inc. provides expert forensic accounting and litigation support services to insurance providers, legal counsel and government agencies. Our firm specializes in measuring the value of economic damages in legal disputes and insurance claims and have extensive experience in a wide variety of industries and areas of insurance.

Joining our team will provide you with opportunities to develop your skills and be a key member in the growth of our firm. We are looking for an Associate with solid communication and analytical skills who wants to become a part of this passionate team.

Responsibilities and Duties:

  • Gather/review and analyze financial data
  • Prepare financial schedules and reports for insured losses
  • Respond to client inquiries in a timely manner
  • Meet time budget and deadlines as expected
  • Maintain appropriate legislative knowledge on an ongoing basis
  • Assist with attending conferences, events and presentations
  • Other duties as required

Education and Professional Skills/Knowledge:

  • Minimum college accounting program
  • 2-3 years of accounting experience
  • Highly detail oriented, excellent organizational/time management and communication skills
  • Proficient in Microsoft Office (Word, Excel, Powerpoint)
