Full Description

Description Mechanical Engineer Co-op Placement Onsite Vernon, BC 12-month position starting May, 2026 Join Kal Tire as a Mechanical Engineer Co-op Student and help us design and enhance mechanical systems and equipment that make a real impact. You will gain valuable experience while contributing to the development of products used in demanding industries, such as mining.

Key Highlights of this Role:

  • Hands-on experience working with mechanical and electromechanical systems.
  • Collaborative environment working with a team of skilled engineers and professionals.
  • Opportunity to develop and implement cost-effective solutions for real-world challenges.
  • Focus on safety, innovation, and performance within the mining sector.

What We Offer:

  • This position pays $25 per hour depending on experience.
  • Positive work culture, opportunities for growth and development, and work-life balance.
  • Opportunities for professional development, such as training programs and mentorship.
  • Structured work schedule and supportive, safety-focused work environment.
  • Opportunity for professional growth through training and mentorship.
  • A company that values collaboration, innovation, and personal development.

Day-to-Day Responsibilities:

  • Design, test, and evaluate mechanical and electromechanical systems and products by conducting research and applying mechanical principles.
  • Develop cost-effective equipment modifications to improve safety and reliability.
  • Create 3D CAD models and detailed shop drawings using SolidWorks.
  • Assist in developing prototypes and evaluating results to recommend design modifications.
  • Participate in mine site visits and collaborate with field operators to gather insights for product improvements.
  • Coordinate maintenance and repair services to ensure operational equipment reliability.
  • Stay current with industry developments by attending workshops, seeking out training, and expanding your technical knowledge.

Requirements

  • Enrolled into 3rd or 4th year of a bachelor’s degree studies in Mechanical Engineering or equivalent at an accredited institution.
  • Strong understanding of mechanical design principles and Lean Manufacturing techniques.
  • Experience or familiarity with 3D CAD modeling (SolidWorks) and Microsoft Office.
  • Practical, hands-on background with exposure to machining, fabrication, and assembly.
  • Exceptional problem-solving skills with a keen eye for detail and efficiency.
  • Able to work independently or within a team to meet deadlines and project goals.
  • Strong communication skills, with the ability to contribute in a fast-paced, collaborative environment.

Special Requirements

  • Ability to work full-time onsite in our Vernon Office location.
  • Successful candidates will be required to bring their laptop and project materials for the in-person project walk-through as a part of the interview process.

Our Inclusive Culture At Kal Tire, we're dedicated to creating a workplace where everyone feels valued, included, and empowered to succeed. We believe in the strength of diverse perspectives and skills, and we encourage applications from candidates of all backgrounds, identities, and experiences.

Hiring Process Upon shortlisting, a member of our recruiting team will contact you and invite you to a telephone interview.

Successful candidates will then have the opportunity to meet in person with members of our Innovations Center and present their relevant work/project experience during the panel interview. To be considered for this position at this time, candidates must be legally eligible to work for any Canadian employer. A Canadian Criminal Record Check may be required for certain positions as part of the final stages of the hiring process.

Solving our customers’ automotive challenges and making a positive impact - It’s how we roll, and it’s been that way since Tom Foord opened the doors to Kal Tire in 1953 in beautiful Vernon, BC. Over the years, we’ve become a trusted partner for large and small industries and fleets across Canada. Our customers rely on our expertise, our make-it-happen approach to service, and our lineup of the most trusted tire brands in over 260 retail locations coast to coast. Kal Tire is also the largest truck re-treader in Canada, with 11 retread facilities across Canada and internationally, and has a state-of-the-art tire recycling facility in Chile, where 40% of the weight of scrap tires is recycled to return to mines as an alternative fuel. In addition, Kal Tire’s Mining Tire Group is an international leader in mining tire service and supply, servicing more than 150 mine sites across five continents. Kal Tire offers a culture that fosters inclusion and respect. We are committed to providing accessible employment practices in compliance with the AODA and AMA. Accommodation requests can be made at any stage of the recruitment or employment process. Applicants are asked to make their needs/requirements known.
