About you:

You are a detail‑oriented and motivated Accounting Co‑op student eager to gain hands‑on experience supporting both accounts payable and accounts receivable functions. In this role, you will assist with processing and maintaining vendor invoices, payments, and records; reconciling vendor statements; managing customer accounts; applying payments and invoices; and maintaining accurate financial records and databases. You will perform data entry and filing tasks, follow up on outstanding accounts, resolve discrepancies, support month‑end and year‑end close activities, and collaborate closely with the accounting team on various projects, while developing strong organizational and analytical skills in a professional accounting environment.

Generating response.

Responsibilities:

  • Assist with Accounts Receivable (AR) and Accounts Payable (AP) functions, including posting bills and processing invoices
  • Process and apply customer and vendor payments accurately and in a timely manner
  • Reconcile customer and vendor statements and investigate and resolve discrepancies
  • Support month‑end and year‑end close activities
  • Assist with annual audit preparation and related tasks
  • Draft, review, and update Standard Operating Procedures (SOPs) as required
  • Participate in ERP system testing and support system enhancements
  • Assist with inventory counts and related documentation

Recipe to success:

  • Currently enrolled in a post‑secondary Accounting, Finance, or related Co‑op program; recent graduates are also welcome to apply.
  • Basic understanding of accounting principles, including Accounts Payable and Accounts Receivable
  • Strong attention to detail with the ability to handle numerical data accurately
  • Proficient in Microsoft Excel and other Microsoft Office applications
  • Ability to learn and work with ERP/accounting systems
  • Strong organizational and time‑management skills
  • Effective written and verbal communication skills
  • Ability to work independently as well as collaboratively in a team environment
