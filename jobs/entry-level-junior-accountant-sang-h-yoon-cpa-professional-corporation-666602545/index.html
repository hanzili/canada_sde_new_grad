Full Description

Overview
We, Public accounting firm, are seeking a motivated Entry Level Staff Accountant to join our dynamic finance team. This role offers an excellent opportunity for individuals interested in developing their accounting skills and gaining practical experience in a professional environment. The ideal candidate will have a strong foundation in accounting principles, proficiency with accounting software, and a keen eye for detail. As an integral part of our team, you will support various accounting functions, ensuring accuracy and compliance across financial operations.

Responsibilities

  • Assist with bookkeeping tasks, including data entry and record maintenance
  • Manage accounts payable and accounts receivable processes
  • Perform account reconciliations and bank reconciliations to ensure financial accuracy
  • Support account analysis to identify discrepancies or irregularities
  • Aid in budgeting processes and financial reporting activities
  • Utilize accounting software such as QuickBooks, Sage, or Xero to record transactions and generate reports
  • Help prepare financial statements and supporting documentation for audits
  • Contribute to maintaining organized financial records in compliance with company policies

Requirements

  • Basic understanding of accounting principles and practices
  • Experience in public practice is an asset.
  • Experience with accounting software such as QuickBooks, Sage, or Xero is preferred
  • Strong proficiency in bookkeeping, data entry, and record keeping
  • Knowledge of payroll processing is a plus
  • Familiarity with accounts payable, accounts receivable, and account reconciliation procedures
  • Excellent attention to detail and organizational skills
  • Ability to analyze financial data and prepare reports accurately
  • Effective communication skills and the ability to work collaboratively within a team
  • A degree in Accounting, Finance, or a related field is preferred but not required; relevant internship experience is a plus
  • Bilingual between Korean and English is preferred
