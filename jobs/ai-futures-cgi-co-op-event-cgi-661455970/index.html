Full Description

In today's fast-paced, tech-driven world, the ability to adapt and leverage cutting-edge technologies is paramount. The CGI Campus Recruitment team is excited to announce an exclusive AI Futures @ CGI event designed for top AI Co-op students across the GTA.

We are looking for innovative thinkers to apply for this unique opportunity. Selected participants will be invited to an exclusive session where they can engage deeply with the future of Artificial Intelligence at CGI. By participating, you will have the chance to see firsthand how CGI is shaping real- world AI Solutions and discover co-op career paths, including roles such as AI Engineering, GenAI Developer, and Data Engineering, where you could be building and deploying machine learning models, developing applications powered by large language models, or designing data pipelines that enable real-world AI systems. This event is a premier opportunity to network with CGI professionals and industry leaders to gain insights you won't find online.

Why Apply?

  • Inside Look: See how CGI is shaping real-world AI solutions.
  • Career Paths: Discover co-op opportunities, internships, and career paths, including roles such as AI Engineering, GenAI Developer, and Data Engineering.
  • Direct Networking: Connect directly with CGI professionals and industry leaders.
  • Expert Insights: Ask questions and gain industry knowledge you won't find online.

At CGI, we are committed to investing in the next generation of tech leaders. We believe that diversity of thought and experience is integral to pushing the boundaries of emerging technologies. This event is designed to prepare and equip future innovators through direct exposure to what CGI has to offer and professional networking.

Please submit your updated resume for consideration.This is an exclusive opportunity, and selected students will be contacted with further details regarding the event.

Your future duties and responsibilities

What Your AI Future at CGI Could Look Like

AI Engineering

  • Identify AI/ML use cases (predictive analytics, automation, NLP).
  • Prototype AI solutions using Python, Azure/OpenAI, HuggingFace, Gemini, CoPilot, LLMs.
  • Support data preprocessing, cleaning, and analysis for model feasibility.
  • Develop and experiment with AI-driven prototypes (using LLMs, RAG, or Agentic workflows) to automate manual QE tasks.
  • Research and benchmark emerging AI tools to see if they meet our enterprise security and performance standards.
  • Analyze test data to identify patterns where AI can improve "Shift-Left" quality and defect detection.
  • Work closely with senior engineers to document findings and present PoC results to leadership.

GenAI Developer

  • Build LLM- and NLP-based solutions using best-in-class techniques.
  • Handle end-to-end data and prompt engineering to enhance user experience.
  • Develop, fine-tune (LoRA, QLoRA), deploy, and monitor ML solutions (RAG & Agentic AI).
  • Mitigate risks and optimize costs in GenAI architectures.

Collaborate with innovation teams, clients, and account teams for GenAI solutions.

  • Work with Data Scientists and Engineers on client engagements.
  • Implement proofs of concept and scale them to full ML solutions on cloud or on-prem.

Data Engineering

  • Opportunity to develop the end-to-end (E2E) conversion of Informatica code to Databricks.
  • Utilize the EDH tool for data handling and management during the migration process.
  • Ensure smooth and high-quality code migration, aligning the conversion process with the organization's technical standards and business needs.
  • Participate in code reviews to ensure that the converted code meets all performance, scalability, and functional standards before it is handed over to the Application Development (AD) team.
  • Own the quality assurance process, identifying and troubleshooting any issues that arise during the code migration.

Required qualifications to be successful in this role

  • Students currently enrolled in Computer Science, Data Science, Software Engineering, or related programs
  • 1+ work term of hands-on AI experience
  • Familiarity with AI/ML concepts
  • Proficiency in Python (essential) and familiarity with frameworks like LangChain, OpenAI API, or Hugging Face.
  • Curious thinkers eager to learn and solve meaningful problems
  • Strong communication and collaboration skills
