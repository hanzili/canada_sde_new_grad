About the Role

Assist metallurgical testing and process optimization

About B2Gold Corp.

Mining company with global operations; Nunavut site

Full Description

The Company

B2Gold is a responsible international senior gold producer headquartered in Vancouver, Canada. Founded in 2007, today, B2Gold has operating gold mines in Mali, Namibia and the Philippines, the Goose Project under construction in northern Canada, and numerous development and exploration projects in various countries including Mali, Colombia and Finland.

Position

Junior Metallurgist, Nunavut

Location and Reporting Structure

Reporting to the Plant Metallurgist, this position will be a fly in- fly out 3x3 rotation based in Nunavut, Canada.

Responsibilities

  • Support daily metallurgical monitoring and reporting of plant performance, including throughput, recovery, reagent consumption, and product quality.
  • Assist with metallurgical testing programs, including sample collection, laboratory test work, test work analysis, and interpretation of results.
  • Participate in plant trials and process optimization initiatives aimed at improving recovery, efficiency, and operating stability.
  • Collect, validate, analyze, and trend metallurgical and process data, identifying opportunities for operational improvement.
  • Support troubleshooting of metallurgical and process-related issues in collaboration with Operations and Maintenance teams.
  • Assist with the preparation of technical reports, metallurgical balances, operating procedures, and presentations for site and corporate stakeholders.
  • Ensure metallurgical activities comply with safety, environmental, and regulatory requirements.
  • Promote and adhere to company safety standards, procedures, and continuous improvement practices.

Desired Competencies and Requirements

  • One (1) year of experience in a mining or mineral processing environment, including co-op or internship experience with prior experience working in remote camp setting considered an asset.
  • Bachelor’s degree in Metallurgical Engineering, Mineral Processing, Chemical Engineering, or a related discipline.
  • Registered or eligible to register as an Engineer-in-Training (EIT)) with Northwest Territories and Nunavut Association of Professional Engineers and Geoscientists (NAPEG).
  • Foundational understanding of mineral processing principles (crushing, grinding, flotation, leaching, and gold recovery), with strong analytical skills and proficiency in Excel or similar tools.

Compensation and Benefits

For informational purposes, the estimated annual earnings are $127,465.00 for 2184 scheduled hours (combined regular hourly rate of $46.50 and overtime rate of $69.75) in a work year.

B2Gold Nunavut offers a competitive compensation package, including salary commensurate with experience, knowledge, and skills, plus a group benefits package for employees and their dependents.

Application and Contact Information

Kitikmeot Inuit, Nunavut Inuit, and Residents receive priority hiring consideration, if one these categories applies, please state it after the reference number in the subject line. We thank all for their interest but are only considering applicants with working rights in Canada.
B2Gold Nunavut prioritizes local employment, supports empowerment initiatives, and continuously works towards a people profile that matches the demographics of the respective national settings in which we operate, while appreciating our inclusive culture that attracts talent from around the world. As an equal opportunity employer, B2Gold Nunavut values diversity and we encourage all qualified candidates to apply.

The B2Gold Back River Corp. owned Back River Project is a significant undertaking with contributions from both B2Gold Nunavut and valued Contractors. Although we appreciate your interest in working for B2Gold Nunavut directly; if you are not selected for employment with B2Gold Nunavut or we have no current work available that is suitable for your skill set, B2Gold Nunavut may share your application material with our contractors for positions that they are seeking to fill. If you do not want your resume shared, please indicate so in the subject line of your email and we will retain a copy of your application material for our internal records only.

We will retain application material on file for future vacancies (openings within 6-12 months) for consideration; however, you are encouraged to apply to any opening you may be suited for. If selected to move forward a member of our team will be in touch.

Closing Date

This post will remain open until 12:00AM MST May 11, 2026, unless a suitable candidate is not found. We thank all applicants for their interest in B2Gold Nunavut; however, only those candidates selected for an interview will be contacted.
