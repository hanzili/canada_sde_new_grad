About the Role

Process orders, support sales, coordinate with production

About Robertson Plastics

Small plastics distributor with growth path

Full Description

Robertson Plastics is a fast-growing plastics distributor and custom cutting shop serving machine shops, sign companies, OEMs, and industrial customers across British Columbia.

We specialize in:

  • Plastic sheet and rod distribution
  • Custom cut-to-size components
  • Fast, reliable service with a strong focus on accuracy and turnaround

We are building a high-performance, low-headcount team focused on:

  • speed
  • accuracy
  • strong systems
  • continuous improvement

If you’re someone who likes working in a structured, fast-paced environment and takes pride in doing things right the first time, this is a great place to grow.

The Role

This is an entry-level inside sales / operations role focused on supporting our sales and production workflow.

You will be responsible for:

  • responding quickly to inbound customer requests
  • entering and processing orders accurately
  • assisting with quotes and customer communication
  • keeping our CRM and order system clean and organized
  • helping ensure orders flow smoothly to production

This role is ideal for someone who wants to build a career in:

  • sales
  • operations
  • or business systems

What You’ll Be Doing:

  • Respond to customer emails and calls promptly
  • Enter orders accurately into our system
  • Assist with preparing quotes for customers
  • Confirm order details (material, size, quantities, etc.)
  • Support inside and outside sales team
  • Maintain clean and organized customer records
  • Coordinate with our shop team to ensure smooth order flow

What We’re Looking For:

Core Traits

  • Highly organized and detail-oriented
  • Fast and responsive — you don’t let things sit
  • Takes pride in accuracy and doing things right
  • Calm under pressure
  • Willing to learn and improve

Experience

  • 1–2 years in customer service, admin, or inside sales is an asset
  • Experience in distribution, manufacturing, or trades is helpful but not required

Skills

  • Strong communication (written and verbal)
  • Comfortable using computers and learning new systems
  • Basic math and measurement understanding
  • Ability to manage multiple tasks at once

Growth Opportunity

This role is the starting point for a clear career path:

  • Junior Inside Sales → Inside Sales Representative → Senior Inside Sales

As you develop:

  • you will take on more responsibility
  • handle more complex work
  • increase your earning potential

We promote based on capability and performance, not just time.

Compensation

  • Competitive hourly wage
  • Performance-based bonus structure
  • Opportunity for rapid growth based on performance

Schedule

  • Full-time
  • Monday to Friday, 8:00AM - 4:30PM

Why Join Robertson Plastics

  • Small, focused team — no bureaucracy
  • Clear expectations and structured workflow
  • Opportunity to grow into a high-value role
  • Learn both sales and operations in a real business environment
  • Be part of building a modern, system-driven company

Job Type: Full-time

Pay: $24.00-$28.00 per hour

Expected hours: 40 per week

Benefits:

  • Dental care
  • Disability insurance
  • Extended health care
  • On-site parking
  • Paid time off
  • Store discount

Application question(s):

  • Have you worked in a business that handled physical products, inventory, manufacturing, trades, or distribution?
  • How comfortable are you working in a fast-paced environment where speed and accuracy both matter every day?
  • Are you comfortable learning product details, measurements, and order specifications as part of your job?
  • Why are you interested in this position?
