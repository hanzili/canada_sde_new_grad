Student Digital Construction Coordinator

at Bird Construction

About the Role

Coordinate digital construction models and field layout

About Bird Construction

Established Canadian construction leader with strong safety culture

Full Description

Our internships and Co-ops are designed to provide you with a business relevant project that will allow you to independently manage your own area of responsibility, while receiving support from an assigned Supervisor and Technical Mentor. We’ll help you discover your strengths and development areas in a challenging, fun, and supportive work environment.

What You Will Be Working On We are seeking a student to join us for a 4-month co-op position starting in September 2026 . This position will be based out of Mississauga, Ontario

  • Support Bird Construction's Projects team
  • Review and analyze coordination models for spatial coordination and quality control
  • Manage the spatial coordination process by holding recurring collaboration meetings with project teams
  • Keep record of the issue resolution process and coordination meetings
  • Ensure project deliverables and information meet Digital Construction Department requirements
  • Provide a communication link between design and construction teams
  • Provide support for laser scanning and model-based field layout
  • Support the use of mobile technology and other Digital Construction applications in the field
  • Support preconstruction activities
  • Assist in the development of hardware and software standards, standards for modeling, and maintaining an element library when required
  • Have a passion for applying Digital Construction to improve productivity in design and construction, and encourage creativity and innovation.

What We Are Looking For

  • You are currently enrolled in an Architectural Technology, Civil Engineering, Construction Management or equivalent program
  • You will complete at least one more semester of education following your internship and possess a GPA of 3.0 or higher
  • Ability to lead and operate within an environment where change happens frequently
  • Ability to forge, grow and maintain positive relationships
  • Exemplify the Ideal Team Player – Hungry, Humble, Smart
  • Highly developed interpersonal, communication, and organizational skills
  • Sense of urgency and strong commitment to achieving goals
  • Ability to work collaboratively with a positive attitude
  • Residency, or a valid work permit which would allow you to work for Bird Construction or one of our wholly owned subsidiaries
  • If you are selected for a role with client specific requirements, you may be required to successfully complete a pre-employment Medical and/or Alcohol & Drug testing.
  • All applications must include a current transcript

For Those Who Seek To Redefine The greatest achievements in history are borne from the greatness within people – where human potential meets vision, and passion fuels evolution. Unlocking this potential is the most important thing we do at Bird.

As a leader in Canadian construction for over 100 years, the impact of our team is etched deeply within the core of our legacy. Our impact is greater than ever, and we are looking for those who seek to redefine their story.

What We Believe In We Put Safety First A healthy and safe work environment is non-negotiable. We build a culture of operational and psychological safety through engagement, learning and leadership.
