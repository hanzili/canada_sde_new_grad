Full Description

Digital Marketing & Influencer Outreach Summer Internship

We are building next-generation gaming and technology products focused on performance, ergonomics, innovation, and creator-driven marketing. Our company works closely with YouTubers, streamers, esports players, influencers, and online communities to grow brand awareness through authentic partnerships, social media campaigns, and content-focused marketing strategies.

We are seeking a motivated, organized, and highly driven Summer Intern to support our Digital Marketing, Influencer Outreach, and Paid Advertising initiatives. This role is heavily focused on creator outreach, influencer relationship management, and digital campaign execution.

Eligibility Requirements

Applicants must:

  • Be currently enrolled in a college or university program
  • Be returning to school in September 2026
  • Be legally eligible to work in Canada
  • Have a strong interest in digital marketing, gaming, esports, social media, influencer marketing, or content creation

What You’ll DoInfluencer & Creator Outreach (Mission Critical Responsibility)

  • Conduct high-volume outreach emails and partnership communications with YouTubers, streamers, influencers, esports players, content creators, and creator agencies
  • Research, build, and maintain targeted influencer outreach lists across YouTube, TikTok, Twitch, Instagram, and X/Twitter
  • Identify gaming, esports, technology, and lifestyle creators aligned with the brand’s audience and marketing goals
  • Organize and maintain influencer databases including:
  • follower counts
  • engagement metrics
  • contact information
  • management agencies
  • audience demographics
  • content categories
  • campaign tracking status
  • Track outreach performance, creator responses, and partnership opportunities
  • Coordinate product seeding campaigns, sponsorship discussions, creator follow-ups, and campaign execution
  • Research and identify emerging creators before they become mainstream
  • Maintain organized reporting and campaign tracking spreadsheets

Paid Advertising & Growth Marketing

  • Plan, launch, and manage targeted advertising campaigns across Facebook, Instagram, TikTok, and other digital platforms
  • Create and test audience targeting strategies focused on gaming, esports, technology, and creator communities
  • Monitor ad performance metrics including click-through rates, conversions, engagement, and return on ad spend (ROAS)
  • Assist in creating ad creatives, campaign copy, promotional content, and campaign assets
  • Optimize campaigns based on analytics, audience insights, and performance trends
  • Research competitor advertising strategies and emerging digital marketing trends
  • Support retargeting campaigns and customer acquisition initiatives

Digital Marketing Support

  • Monitor social media trends, viral content, and creator marketing campaigns
  • Assist with product launch marketing campaigns and influencer activations
  • Support community engagement initiatives across social media platforms
  • Help organize content calendars, campaign reports, and marketing assets
  • Research competitor sponsorships, partnerships, and brand collaborations

Market Research

  • Research gaming peripherals, esports brands, creator partnerships, and consumer trends
  • Analyze creator audiences, engagement quality, and partnership fit
  • Identify opportunities for brand growth within gaming and esports communities

Preferred Qualifications

  • Familiarity with YouTube, TikTok, Twitch, Instagram, and gaming culture
  • Strong research, communication, and organizational skills
  • Experience using Google Sheets, Excel, Airtable, or similar tools
  • Excellent written communication and outreach skills
  • Passion for gaming, technology, content creation, or esports is a major asset

Bonus Skills

  • Experience creating social media content
  • Understanding of influencer marketing tools and platforms
  • Graphic design, Photoshop, Canva, or video editing experience
  • Knowledge of esports communities such as Call of Duty, Valorant, Fortnite, Apex Legends, or League of Legends
  • Experience running social media advertisements or managing online campaigns

Pay: $17.20-$20.00 per hour

Work Location: Hybrid remote in Etobicoke, ON M9B 1A8
