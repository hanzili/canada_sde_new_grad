About VIR Properties

Full Description

We are looking for a driven and organised Junior Account Manager to join our growing marketing team. This is an exciting opportunity for someone early in their career who is eager to gain hands-on experience managing client relationships, supporting campaigns, and contributing to business growth.

You will work closely with clients and internal teams to ensure marketing campaigns are delivered successfully and client expectations are exceeded.

Key Responsibilities

  • Support the management of client accounts and marketing campaigns
  • Build and maintain strong relationships with clients
  • Assist in coordinating campaign deliverables and timelines
  • Communicate campaign updates and performance insights
  • Collaborate with marketing and sales teams on campaign execution
  • Identify opportunities to enhance client experience and results
  • Track campaign performance and support reporting

What We’re Looking For

  • Strong communication and interpersonal skills
  • Organised and detail-oriented approach
  • Positive attitude and willingness to learn
  • Ability to manage multiple tasks in a fast-paced environment
  • Customer-focused mindset
  • Interest in marketing, account management, or business development

What We Offer

  • Full training and ongoing mentorship
  • Supportive and collaborative team environment
  • Clear progression opportunities within the company
  • Hands-on experience managing client accounts
  • Opportunity to work with a variety of brands and campaigns

Ideal Candidate This Role Is Perfect For Someone Who Is

  • Early in their career and eager to grow in account management
  • Confident communicating with clients and team members
  • Organised, motivated, and detail-oriented
  • Interested in building a long-term career in marketing
