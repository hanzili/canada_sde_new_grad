About Turner & Townsend Pty Limited

Full Description

Company Description

Turner & Townsend is a global professional services company with over 22,000 people in more than 60 countries.

Working with our clients across real estate, infrastructure, energy and natural resources, we transform together delivering outcomes that improve people’s lives. Working in partnership makes it possible to deliver the world’s most impactful projects and programmes as we turn challenge into opportunity and complexity into success.

Our capabilities include programme, project, cost, asset and commercial management, controls and performance, procurement and supply chain, net zero and digital solutions.

We are majority-owned by CBRE Group, Inc., the world’s largest commercial real estate services and investment firm, with our partners holding a significant minority interest. Turner & Townsend and CBRE work together to provide clients with the premier programme, project and cost management offering in markets around the worl

Job Description

As a Junior Buyer you will be involved in:

  • Facilitate the execution of new & existing contracts, addendums and other documents requiring internal & external approvals
  • Support RFx process with review and issuance of RFx documents (EOI, RFI, RFx), clarifications, including post bid and BAFO, track and follow-up as required
  • Collaborate with and support SCM, vendors and suppliers in managing Purchase Orders and RFx processes
  • Complete monthly and quarterly reports as required
  • Maintain appropriate records related to SCM activities
  • Assist with onboarding of new vendors in Oracle and Avetta

Qualifications

  • Bachelor’s Degree or Diploma in Business Administration, Supply Chain Management, or related field
  • Minimum two to five years purchasing and contract experience within a supply chain environment is preferred

Additional Information

  • 100% covered health and dental benefits for you and your dependents (including paramedical services)
  • Health spending account, to top up expenses not covered in the benefits program
  • RRSP option with company matching
  • Annual learning and development budget
  • Access to a range of online learning tools, and support for career development and growth
  • Coverage of one professional membership or license fee per year, if directly related to your role
  • We believe in work-life balance, so you can recharge and take care of what matters most with vacation, flex and sick days
