Systems Data Analyst Intern

at Clearpoint Health Network

📍 Location Etobicoke, ON M9W 6H8 Hybrid
📋 Details Intern · 4mo · $21/hr-$30/hr
📅 Posted Apr 22, 2026

About the Role

Prepare, clean, and validate master data for IMS

About Clearpoint Health Network

Canada's largest private surgical network

Full Description

Clearpoint Health Network is Canada’s largest trusted provider of independent, multi- disciplinary surgical centres. Working in close collaboration with hospitals, insurance providers, provincial and regional health organizations and workers’ compensation boards, Clearpoint delivers high-quality care across Canada meeting the needs of patients, while alleviating capacity pressure on public health systems.

Position Type: Contract, (4 Months)
Location: Etobicoke, ON (Hybrid) 190 Attwell Dr, Suite 490 Toronto
Vacancy Status: We are actively recruiting

Wage Range - $21/hr-$30/hr

Position Overview

We are seeking a Supply Chain & Data management Intern. This role is ideal for a final year data analyst program student or recent business graduate with strong analytical and data management skills.

The intern will play a key role to support the locations already using Inflow Inventory management system and to help with preparing, uploading and maintaining data for IMS implementation for new sites and its integration across other systems.

Key Responsibilities

  • Prepare, clean, and validate master data to ensure data accuracy and consistency prior to IMS implementation and system integration
  • Upload item master and vendor master data to Inflow IMS
  • Provide support for data updates within Inflow IMS and HST Pathways EMR system
  • Update Pickl list/Preference cards before uploading to IMS
  • Perform data audits to identify discrepancies or missing information
  • Develop reports and document training material for key processes

Qualifications

  • Currently enrolled in or recently completed a program in Supply Chain, Data management, Business Analytics, or a related field
  • Strong Excel skills (Vlookups, pivot tables, data cleaning)
  • Strong Power BI skills
  • High attention to detail and accuracy
  • Comfortable working with team across different locations

Preferred Competencies

  • Exposure to inventory management systems or ERP platforms
  • Familiarity with business operations, procurement, or supply chain environment
  • Understanding of Item Master, Pick List, and Vendor Master

What You’ll Gain

  • Great company culture, supportive team, and hybrid working environment
  • Hands-on experience supporting Inflow Inventory and HST EMR system updates
  • Exposure to Canada’s one of the largest private healthcare business operations
  • Practical experience in healthcare data management and system support
  • Opportunity to turn it into permanent position
