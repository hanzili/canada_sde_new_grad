Full Description

Nutrien is a leading provider of crop inputs and services, and our business results make a positive impact on the world. Our purpose, Feeding the Future, is the reason we come to work each day. We’re guided by our culture of care and our core values: safety, inclusion, integrity, and results. When we say we care, we mean it. We efficiently serve growers' needs and strive to provide a more profitable, sustainable, and secure future for all stakeholders. Help us raise the expectation of what an agriculture company can be and grow your career with Nutrien.

The Maintenance Analytics Developer Co-op Student will support the Maintenance and Reliability team by transforming operational and asset data into actionable insights. This role focuses on building high-quality Power BI reports and dashboards that enhance understanding of asset performance and reliability—driving data-led decisions within maintenance and reliability teams.

This position is located at Nutrien's Calgary, Alberta corporate site and is not eligible for remote work.

What You'll Do:

  • Develop and maintain Power BI dashboards and reports to track maintenance and reliability KPIs.
  • Perform data extraction, transformation, and loading (ETL) from multiple sources using Power Query.
  • Create calculated columns, DAX measures, and impactful visualizations that tell clear data stories.
  • Ensure data accuracy and consistency across all reporting solutions.
  • Collaborate with Maintenance & Reliability teams to translate business needs into technical solutions.
  • Conduct ad-hoc analyses to identify trends, anomalies, and root causes.
  • Improve and organize electronic files to improve accuracy and accessibility

What You'll Bring:

  • Current enrollment in a co-op program pursuing a degree in Data Science, Business Analytics, Computer Science, Statistics, Engineering, or related field.
  • Coursework in Database Management/SQL, Data Modeling, and Data Visualization.
  • Intermediate skills in Power BI and experience with ETL processes.
  • Strong analytical thinking, attention to detail, and communication skills.
  • Ability to turn complex business questions into structured data solutions.
  • Eagerness to learn and adapt in a dynamic environment.
  • Experience with SQL databases, Python, CMMS/EAM systems (e.g., SAP), or maintenance/reliability concepts (preferred).
