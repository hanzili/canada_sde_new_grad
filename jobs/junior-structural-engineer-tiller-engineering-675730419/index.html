About the Role

About Tiller Engineering

Regional structural firm, strong EIT path

Full Description

About Tiller Engineering: Tiller Engineering Inc. is a structural engineering firm based in St. John’s, NL, providing engineering services across Canada. Our work includes structural design, inspections and condition assessments, and project management for a wide range of infrastructure, telecommunications, energy and industrial projects. We pride ourselves on delivering practical, high-quality engineering solutions and fostering a collaborative team environment.

Reports to: Engineering Team and Engineering Manager

Job Summary: This role involves supporting structural engineering work, including analysis, design, inspection, and assessment of various structures. The successful candidate will work under the guidance of senior engineers while gaining hands-on experience in both office and field environments. This position will be based in our St. John’s office, primarily in-office with the possibility of some remote work. Field work may include a combination of local site visits and occasional travel to regional and remote project sites.

Overall Responsibilities:

  • Engineering Support
  • Assist with structural analysis and design calculations (for both infrastructure and telecommunication projects)
  • Support the preparation of engineering drawing and specifications
  • Assist in the development of tender documents and conceptual / detailed designs
  • Field Work & Inspections
  • Participate in site visits, field surveys, and structural inspections (may include a combination of local assignments and occasional travel to more remote project sites)
  • Telecommunication tower inspections
  • Assist in condition assessments of structures and facilities
  • Collect field data and document observations in engineering reports and verbally to clients
  • Project Coordination
  • Coordinate with senior engineers, designers/draftsperson, field teams, and clients
  • Assist with project planning, management, scheduling, and cost estimates
  • Quality and Review
  • Check and review designs and drawings done by other employees
  • Work with other designers and clients to reach agreement about safe designs and how they may fit in with the aesthetic concept of the construction
  • Other work responsibilities that senior engineers and engineering manager may have

Consults with:

  • Engineers
  • Sector Managers
  • Drafts people
  • Field Inspectors
  • Field Operations Manager
  • HR/HSQE Manager

Qualifications:

  • Degree in Civil or Structural Engineering
  • Registered as an E.I.T. or eligible for registration
  • 0-5 years of experience (new graduates encouraged to apply)
  • Strong attention to detail, organizational skills and time management
  • Competent in structural analysis software and excel spreadsheets.
  • Ability to develop solutions and solve problems reasonably or completely independently.

Preferred skills:

  • Co-op or internship experience in structural engineering
  • Exposure to structural inspections or field work, and ability to drive a work truck into the field
  • Familiarity with Canadian design codes
  • Experience with climbing structures or willingness to climb structures (e.g., telecommunication towers) for inspection purposes is considered an asset

Salary: $60,000.00-$80,000.00depending on qualification and experience

Benefits: We offer a competitive salary based on experience and qualifications, along with a comprehensive benefits package including health and dental coverage, paid vacation, and support for professional development and career growth. Employees may also be eligible for performance-based bonuses and/or profit sharing.

Job Type: Permanent

Pay: $60,000.00-$80,000.00 per year
