About the Role

Create short smartphone videos for brands

About Temisin LLC

Emerging digital marketing firm focused on content

Full Description

We’re currently looking for casual remote video creators to help produce simple short-form content for consumer and lifestyle brands.

This role is beginner friendly and fully remote. If you’re comfortable speaking naturally on camera and using your phone to record short clips, you may be a good fit.

What You’ll Do

  • Record short videos using your smartphone (usually 5–45 seconds)
  • Create simple talking videos, product demos, or casual lifestyle clips
  • Follow basic instructions provided by the team
  • Submit content remotely on a flexible schedule

Requirements

  • Comfortable appearing on camera
  • Able to follow simple recording instructions
  • Reliable communication and timely submissions
  • Smartphone with a working camera

Compensation

  • Paid trial available for selected applicants
  • Ongoing paid opportunities available weekly
  • Typical projects pay around CAD $25 per video set
  • Flexible remote work
