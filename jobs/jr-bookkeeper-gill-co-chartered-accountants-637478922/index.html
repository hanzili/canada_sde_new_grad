About the Role

Maintain GL, reconcile accounts, assist audits

About Gill & Co. Chartered Accountants

Mid-sized local CA firm in Mississauga

Full Description

Bookkeeper must possess strong time management and analytical skills, attention to detail, and solid communication abilities. Candidates must have a bachelor’s degree or college diploma in accounting. Strong technology skills are critical, particularly expertise with QuickBooks and Microsoft Excel.

  • Maintaining general ledger accounts and preparing adjusting journal entries
  • Performing account analysis and reconciliation, including bank statements and intercompany general ledger accounts
  • Bookkeeping to bank statements and credit card statements
  • Posting monthly, quarterly and yearly accruals
  • Making payments and deposits
  • Assist with audits
  • Liaise with CRA
  • HST filings
  • Payroll processing
  • Filing invoices
  • Corporate tax filings
