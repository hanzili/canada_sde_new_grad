Full Description

On-site | Full time | Student (not yet graduated) About Us In Trench Canada, we deliver state-of-the-art, safe, and reliable instrument transformers, coils, and bushings for seamless energy transmission. With a legacy of customized engineering and innovation, we enable society to harness the power of electricity with reliability and performance. But we are more than just a company. We are a force for change, committed to advancing the energy sector through cutting-edge technology and sustainable practices. We genuinely care about continuous improvement, and our dynamic workplace values attention to detail and adaptability, fostering a culture of equality, diversity, and inclusion where everyone is respected and truly valued.

*Our Values*

  • Trust
  • Responsibility
  • Excellence
  • Navigating Change
  • Candor
  • Humility

Your Tasks:

  • Development of 3D models and 2D drawings of parts, assemblies, and final products using SolidWorks, Autodesk Inventor.
  • Contributing to development and revision of material specifications for raw materials and parts.
  • Revision of the database of the seismic calculations for the instrument transformers, execution of the seismic calculations in accordance with IEEE 693 and internal procedures.
  • Quality control of Bill of material (BOM) for the instrument transformer to ensure correct Part number, material descriptions, quantity, material type, MRP type.
  • Revision of the material descriptions in SAP to comply with internal standards.

Requirements:

  • Must be currently enrolled in a Mechanical Engineering program and have completed at least two years of study.
  • Proficiency in development of 3D models, 2D drawings is required.
  • Basic knowledge and academic background in material science, understanding of material properties, selection, and specification standards.
  • Ability to interpret and update mechanical/structural calculations and ensure compliance with relevant standards.
  • Strong ability to analyze technical data, identify inconsistencies, and ensure accuracy in engineering designs and specifications.
  • Ability to maintain focus on the work and complete the tasks in-time, meeting quality standards.
  • Ability to understand the work assignments, breakdown the task into steps and plan the progress based on available time.
  • Are open minded, willing to learn, and solution oriented.

We offer:

  • The opportunity to gain practical experience in the field of optical current measurement technology and sustainable energy transmission in an innovative company
  • You will work in a motivated team on forward-looking projects and gain insights into the latest technologies
  • Support from experienced professionals in handling the latest measurement technology
  • An hourly rate of pay of CAD $24 - $26.
