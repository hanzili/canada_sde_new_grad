Backend Intern

at GenPark

📍 Location Vancouver, British Columbia Hybrid
📋 Details Intern
📅 Posted Apr 28, 2026

About the Role

Build scalable backend services and APIs

About GenPark

GenPark, AI-driven discovery platform for brands

Full Description

Company Description

GenPark is a cutting-edge agentic discovery platform transforming global commerce by ushering in the agent-first era of marketing. Through the use of Intelligent Brand Agents and User Agents, GenPark creates personalized and adaptive connections between consumers and the products, content, and experiences that suit them best. By combining AI-driven content personalization, interactive agents, and immersive video, GenPark empowers brands to effectively engage, discover, and convert Gen Z audiences worldwide.

Role Description

This is an internship role for a Backend Intern. As a Backend Intern, you will collaborate with the engineering team to develop, optimize, and maintain backend services and APIs. Your day-to-day responsibilities will include building scalable server-side applications, writing clean and efficient code, testing APIs, and troubleshooting technical issues. This is a hybrid role based in Vancouver, BC, allowing a mix of on-site and remote work.

Qualifications

  • Proficiency in backend programming languages such as Python, Java, or Node.js
  • Experience working with databases, especially SQL and NoSQL
  • Understanding of RESTful API development and integration
  • Exposure to cloud platforms such as AWS, Azure, or Google Cloud is a plus
  • Problem-solving skills and a strong understanding of data structures and algorithms
  • Ability to quickly learn and adapt to new technologies and frameworks
  • Currently pursuing a degree in Computer Science, Software Engineering, or a related field
  • Team-oriented with strong communication and collaboration skills
