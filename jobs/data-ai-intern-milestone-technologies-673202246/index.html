Full Description

Company Overview

Milestone Technologies is a global IT managed services firm that partners with organizations to scale their technology, infrastructure and services to drive specific business outcomes such as digital transformation, innovation, and operational agility. Milestone is focused on building an employee-first, performance-based culture and for over 25 years, we have a demonstrated history of supporting category-defining enterprise clients that are growing ahead of the market. The company specializes in providing solutions across Application Services and Consulting, Digital Product Engineering, Digital Workplace Services, Private Cloud Services, AI/Automation, and ServiceNow. Milestone culture is built to provide a collaborative, inclusive environment that supports employees and empowers them to reach their full potential.

Our seasoned professionals deliver services based on Milestone’s best practices and service delivery framework. By leveraging our vast knowledge base to execute initiatives, we deliver both short-term and long-term value to our clients and apply continuous service improvement to deliver transformational benefits to IT. With Intelligent Automation, Milestone helps businesses further accelerate their IT transformation. The result is a sharper focus on business objectives and a dramatic improvement in employee productivity. Through our key technology partnerships and our people-first approach, Milestone continues to deliver industry-leading innovation to our clients. With more than 3,000 employees serving over 200 companies worldwide, we are following our mission of revolutionizing the way IT is deployed.

Job Overview

Job Overview

As an intern, you will support technical teams and program managers by assisting with client demonstration preparation, managing demo assets, and contributing directly to AI development activities including coding, data handling, and automation workflows. This role offers practical exposure to AI programming, project coordination, and enterprise technology consulting practices.

Key Responsibilities

Demo Preparation & Technical Support

Assist in preparing, testing, and validating client demo environments and AI agent workflows to ensure readiness and successful execution.

Organize, catalog, and manage technical demo assets, code repositories, and documentation to support client engagements and internal knowledge sharing.

Support briefing sessions by helping gather and share relevant client objectives, technical context, and messaging as directed by supervisors.

AI Development & Coding

Contribute to AI solution development by supporting coding tasks such as prompt engineering, automation scripting, and prototype development, leveraging creativity and interdisciplinary knowledge.

Participate in data-related tasks including collection, cleaning, preprocessing, and feature engineering that support AI projects.

Assist in designing tests, collecting and analyzing user or system data to evaluate AI demo effectiveness and aid iterative improvement.

Collaboration & Innovation

Collaborate under guidance with technical and sales teams to support alignment on project activities, demo logistics, and client readiness.

Apply critical thinking to help improve user experience and interaction design of AI demos and solutions.

Contribute fresh, interdisciplinary perspectives to project brainstorming and innovation labs to support novel AI solution approaches.

Qualifications

Required:

Currently pursuing a Bachelor's or Master's degree in computer science, Data Science, Engineering, Information Systems, or related field

Basic programming knowledge, preferably Python or similar languages commonly used in AI and automation workflows

Authorization to work in the United States (or specify your location)

Preferred:

Minimum GPA of 3.0/4.0 (or equivalent)

Demonstrated interest in AI/ML through coursework, personal projects, or extracurricular activities

Desired Skills & Experience

Technical Skills:

Strong programming fundamentals (preferred: Python) - solid understanding of data structures, algorithms, clean/testable code, async patterns and OOP; ability to prototype and productionize code.

Hands‑on experience building AI agents and agentic workflows - building, orchestrating, and debugging agent workflows and automation (examples: LangChain, LangGraph, custom agent code, n8n flows).

Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) & vector/search pipelines - designing indexing, embedding, retrieval, and prompt/response routing for LLMs and multimodal retrieval.

Graph databases & knowledge-graph design - experience modeling, querying, and integrating graph stores (Neo4j/graph-DB concepts, or knowledge-graph approaches) into agent workflows and RAG pipelines.

API integration & automation engineering - designing and implementing REST APIs, webhooks, connectors and orchestration for ServiceDesk automation and third‑party systems (including n8n connector development).

Familiarity with LLM/AI developer tools and APIs - working knowledge of OpenAI APIs, Hugging Face, model selection, prompt engineering and model invocation patterns.

Data handling & ML fundamentals - practical use of data libraries (pandas, NumPy), data preprocessing, and basic supervised/unsupervised concepts or feature engineering as applied to agent/ML pipelines.

Cloud, deployment and ops basics - exposure to cloud platforms (AWS/Azure/GCP), containerization (Docker), and deployment/monitoring concepts; helpful but not required.

Optional / Nice-to-have

Experience with workflow orchestration and low-code/no-code automation platforms beyond n8n.

Familiarity with CI/CD and testing for AI systems.

Knowledge of privacy, safety, or prompt/agent guardrails.

Soft Skills & Attributes:

Strong problem-solving, creativity, and design thinking skills beneficial for prototyping and agentic coding tasks across technology domains.

Excellent organizational and time management skills with meticulous attention to detail.

Effective verbal and written communication skills, capable of clearly conveying technical ideas to diverse audiences.

Ability to work collaboratively in a team environment and adapt to shifting priorities.

Proactive learner with enthusiasm for technology consulting, AI development, and client support.

You'll Thrive in This Role If You:

Are curious about how AI transforms business operations and eager to explore emerging technologies.

Enjoy both coding/technical work and cross-functional collaboration with diverse teams.

Can balance multiple tasks while maintaining quality and attention to detail.

Are comfortable asking questions, seeking feedback, and learning from experienced mentors.

Bring a growth mindset and aren't afraid to experiment with new tools and approaches.

Have a genuine interest in understanding client needs and delivering value through technology.

What You Will Gain

Professional Development:

Real-world experience developing AI-driven digital transformation solutions within a global IT firm.

Practical coding experience in AI agent workflows, prompt engineering, and automation within enterprise settings.

Exposure to client-facing activities including demos, technical preparation, and cross-functional coordination.

Mentorship and guidance from experienced Data & AI professionals fostering your technical and professional growth.
