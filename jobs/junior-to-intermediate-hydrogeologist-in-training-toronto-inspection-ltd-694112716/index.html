About Toronto Inspection Ltd.

Ontario geo-environmental consultant with professional affiliations.

Full Description

Toronto Inspection Ltd. is a medium-sized geo-environmental consulting firm specialized in geotechnical, environmental, hydrogeological and building science services. We are a member of the Association of Professional Engineers Ontario, the Association of Professional Geoscientists Ontario and the Ontario Institute of Professional Agrologists.

We are currently looking for a junior or intermediate hydrogeologist in-training to join our dynamic hydrogeology/environmental team. Strong preference will be given to candidates who has an interdisciplinary background. Please see below for detailed job descriptions.

Responsibilities:

  • Preparing various hydrogeological and environmental reports.

  • Carrying out hydrogeological / environmental field activities, including but not limited to: borehole / monitoring well installation, monitoring well development, slug testing, groundwater/soil sampling, infiltration testing, percolation testing, and long-term monitoring.

  • Compiling and managing field data.

  • Helping to maintain inventory for both field and laboratory equipment.

We would like to hear from you if:

  • You have background in one of the following fields: hydrogeology, environmental science/engineering, geoscience, environmental biology, environmental chemistry, geotechnical / structural engineering or other similar programs.

  • Minimum one (1) year of previous consulting experience. Preference will be give to candidates with more than two (2) years of experience.

  • Knowledge of provincial and/or municipal hydrogeological/environmental regulations .

  • You are eager to learn about a variety of tasks across multiple disciplines and are familiar with Microsoft Office suite, AutoCAD, GIS and / or other drafting tools.

  • You are willing to work outdoors under inclement weather conditions and can lift up to 40lb of equipment.

  • You have your own vehicle and are comfortable navigating the GTA and surrounding areas.

  • Eligibility for an EIT or GIT designation will be an asset.

Thank you.
