About the Role

We are looking for a highly organised and creative Junior Marketing Coordinator to join our marketing team. This role is ideal for someone eager to develop their skills in campaign coordination and brand support within a fast-paced and dynamic environment.

As a Junior Marketing Coordinator, you will assist in planning, executing, and monitoring marketing activities across various channels. You’ll work closely with senior marketers, helping to ensure campaigns run smoothly and deliver strong results.

Key Responsibilities

-Support the coordination and delivery of marketing campaigns.

-Assist with content creation for face-to-face and email communications and promotional materials.

-Conduct market and competitor research to support campaign planning.

-Assist in organising events, promotions, and brand activations.

-Maintain organised records, reports, and marketing documentation.

Skills & Qualifications

-Strong interest in marketing, branding, and communications.

-Excellent written and verbal communication skills.

-Highly organised with strong attention to detail.

-Basic understanding of social media platforms and marketing tools.

-Creative thinker with a proactive and positive attitude.

-Ability to manage multiple tasks and work effectively in a fast-paced environment.

-Willingness to learn and grow within the marketing field.

What We Offer

-Practical, hands-on experience working on real marketing campaigns.

-Training, mentorship, and opportunities for professional growth.

-A collaborative and supportive team environment.

-Exposure to a variety of marketing channels and projects.

If you’re ready to start your marketing career, contribute to exciting campaigns, and expand your skills in a growing team, we’d love to hear from you. Apply today to take the next step in your marketing journey.
