**This role reports to:** Director, Corporate Reference & Valuations Data

**This role is hybrid (2-3 days/week in the office) - based in Toronto, ON.**

**Key Accountabilities:**

**Fixed Income Data**

- Automation of monthly fixed income trading data statistics and posting to Quicksight - Generation of Issuance/Maturity Waterfall and automate code to update monthly and post to Quicksight - Automation and posting to Quicksight of Government of Canada Cash trades vs. Futures in support of MX team - Evaluation of what we can do with the Terms & Conditions data currently maintained in PDF documents as we now have those centralized - Automate monthly graphs, charts and commentary email based on monthly summary statistics - Write code to automatically search daily Fixed Income Trade Data (FITD) - Corporate Bonds and FITD - Provincial Bonds daily files and where Coupon or Notional are missing then populate our Reference Google sheet with details and search for missing details

**Security Master and Corporate Actions**

- Create the MVP version of our Unified corporate actions offering - Review CDS corporate actions data loaded into EDP (bulletins + entitlements) and develop requirements to normalize, standardize data used to merge into a Golden Copy - Create a shareable AWS Quicksight dashboard providing insights from CDS Corporate actions (bulletins + entitlements) data loaded into EDP - Create a shareable AWS Quicksight dashboard providing longitudinal insights from CDS Security Master

**Must have(s):**

- Currently enrolled in a Bachelor's or Master's program in Computer Science, Data Science, Finance, or a related quantitative field - Strong proficiency in Python (Pandas, NumPy) and SQL - Experience with data visualization tools (Tableau, Quicksight) is a significant asset. - Familiarity with cloud platforms (AWS, Google Cloud) - Excellent analytical and problem-solving skills - Ability to work independently and manage multiple tasks effectively - Good communication skills, both written and verbal
