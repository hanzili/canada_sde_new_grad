Administrative/Marketing Assistant

at Patko Immigration Consulting

📍 Location Langley, BC Hybrid
📋 Details Full-time, Part-time, Fixed term contract, Internship/Co-op · Various · $18.00-$22.00 per hour · Hybrid Langley, BC

About the Role

Support admin tasks and marketing content creation

About Patko Immigration Consulting

Immigration consulting firm; bilingual admin/marketing support

Full Description

We are seeking a motivated and detail-oriented Administrative & Marketing Assistant who is fluent in both English and Spanish. This role combines administrative support with marketing coordination to help grow our client base and maintain excellent client service.

Key Responsibilities

Administrative Duties:

  • Manage emails, client inquiries, and appointment scheduling
  • Prepare and organize immigration documents and files
  • Assist with client follow-ups and communication (English & Spanish)
  • Maintain accurate records and update internal systems
  • Support consultant with daily administrative tasks

Marketing Duties:

  • Assist in creating content for social media (Facebook, Instagram, etc.)
  • Translate and adapt content between English and Spanish
  • Help design promotional materials (flyers, posts, presentations)
  • Support marketing campaigns and community outreach initiatives
  • Maintain and update website or online profiles

Qualifications

  • Fluent in English and Spanish (required)
  • Strong communication and organizational skills
  • Experience in administrative support or customer service
  • Basic knowledge of social media platforms and marketing tools
  • Proficiency in Microsoft Office / Google Workspace
  • Ability to multitask and work independently
  • Experience in immigration services (asset, not required)

Preferred Skills

  • Graphic design experience (Canva or similar)
  • Experience with CRM systems
  • Familiarity with immigration processes (LMIA, work permits, etc.)

Job Types: Full-time, Part-time, Fixed term contract, Internship / Co-op
Contract length: 6 months

Pay: $18.00-$22.00 per hour

Expected hours: 30.0 – 35.0 per week

Work Location: Hybrid remote in Langley, BC
