About Port Hope & District Chamber of Commerce

Port Hope Chamber, supports local businesses and events

Full Description

The Port Hope & District Chamber of Commerce is seeking a motivated and creative  Digital Marketing Coordinator  to support the Chamber’s communications, marketing, member engagement, and digital outreach initiatives during the summer of 2026.

As Port Hope’s trusted Voice of Business, the Chamber works to build a vibrant community where businesses thrive by advocating, educating, and connecting. This position will support the Chamber’s Strategic Plan by strengthening digital communications, promoting local businesses and events, and enhancing the Chamber’s online presence.

This position is funded through the  Canada Summer Jobs (CSJ) Program .

Position Details

Position Title: Digital Marketing Coordinator

Position Type: Full-Time Temporary Summer Position

Start Date: June 15, 2026

Duration: 8 Weeks

Hours: 35 hours per week

Wage: $18.15 per hour

Location: Port Hope, Ontario

Employer: Port Hope & District Chamber of Commerce

Position Summary

The Digital Marketing Coordinator will assist with social media management, website updates, digital content creation, event promotion, member communications, and community engagement initiatives.

The successful candidate will gain practical experience in digital marketing, communications, event promotion, website management, and emerging digital tools while supporting initiatives that increase visibility for local businesses and Chamber programs.

Key Responsibilities:Social Media & Content Creation

  • Develop and maintain a social media content calendar.
  • Create engaging digital content including graphics, captions, photos, and short-form videos.
  • Promote Chamber programs, member opportunities, events, and sponsorship initiatives across digital channels.
  • Monitor social media engagement and assist with basic analytics tracking.

Website & Digital Communications

  • Update Chamber website content including event listings, member information, and announcements.
  • Assist with digital newsletters and member communications.
  • Maintain accuracy of online business directory information.

Marketing & Promotion

  • Create simple digital advertisements and promotional graphics using Canva or similar tools.
  • Support promotion of Chamber initiatives including networking events, workshops, Business Excellence Awards, and community campaigns.
  • Assist with photography and content collection at Chamber events.

Event Support

  • Provide support at Chamber events and community activities.
  • Assist with setup, registration, guest engagement, and event promotion.
  • Capture event content for future marketing and social media use.

Digital Innovation & AI Tools

  • Assist with research and testing of emerging digital tools and AI-supported marketing resources.
  • Explore practical tools that support communications, scheduling, content planning, and workflow efficiency.
  • Support development of simple templates and digital processes to improve organizational efficiency and member communications.

Learning Opportunities

The participant will gain experience in:

  • Digital marketing and communications
  • Social media management
  • Website content management
  • Graphic design and promotional development
  • Event marketing and community engagement
  • Professional workplace communication
  • Emerging digital and AI-supported tools

Qualifications

The ideal candidate will:

  • Be enrolled in or recently graduated from a program related to marketing, communications, business, digital media, graphic design, or a related field.
  • Have strong written and verbal communication skills.
  • Be familiar with social media platforms including Facebook, Instagram, and LinkedIn.
  • Have experience with Canva or similar design tools.
  • Be organized, creative, and able to work independently and collaboratively.
  • Have an interest in digital trends, communications technologies, and emerging tools.

Preferred Assets

Preference may be given to candidates with:

  • Experience using ChatGPT or other AI-supported productivity and content development tools.
  • Basic understanding of AI-assisted content creation, research, scheduling, or workflow tools.
  • Photography, video editing, website editing, or event experience.

Canada Summer Jobs Eligibility Requirements

Applicants must:

  • Be between 15 and 30 years of age at the beginning of employment.
  • Be a Canadian citizen, permanent resident, or person to whom refugee protection has been conferred under the Immigration and Refugee Protection Act.
  • Be legally entitled to work in Canada and have a valid Social Insurance Number.

Work Environment

This position is primarily office-based with some community and event-related work. Occasional evening or weekend hours may be required to support Chamber events and activities.

  • The Port Hope & District Chamber of Commerce is committed to creating an inclusive, respectful, and welcoming workplace.
