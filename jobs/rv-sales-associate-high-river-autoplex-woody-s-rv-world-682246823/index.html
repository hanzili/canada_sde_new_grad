About the Role

Manage sales leads, close deals, build relationships

About Woody's RV World

Family-owned RV dealership with strong local presence

Full Description

Are you a driven sales professional who thrives in a high-energy, commission-based environment and loves the outdoor lifestyle? Join High River Autoplex & RV and turn that passion into a rewarding career helping customers find the RV of their dreams.

As an RV Sales Associate, you’ll guide everyone from first-time buyers to seasoned adventurers through their purchase journey. You’ll showcase top RV brands and deliver an exceptional experience from the moment they walk into our showrooms to their memorable delivery day. If you’re energized by the open road and motivated by helping others start their next adventure, this is your opportunity to become a trusted expert and a key part of the High River Autoplex team.

What We Are Looking For:

  • The outdoors is your happy place, and you know how to share your passion with others
  • Proven sales experience (automotive, RV, or other high-value sales preferred)
  • You have excellent customer service skills to support customers from first visit to delivery day
  • You are eager to grow within a dealership that values attitude and drive as much as experience
  • Excellent people skills – you enjoy and excel at both written and verbal communication
  • Positive, team-orientated mindset – we hire for culture first
  • Valid driver’s license and AMVIC license (or willingness to obtain)

What You’ll Do:

  • Help customers select the right RV to match their travel goals, lifestyle and budget
  • Manage and follow up on dealership leads to drive conversions
  • Handle inbound phone and showroom inquiries
  • Close deals and build long-term client relationships
  • Track and manage leads using Dealer Socket CRM
  • Develop strong product knowledge to confidently present features and benefits
  • Coordinate and support the delivery process to ensure exceptional customer experience
  • Maintain current knowledge of RV inventory, features, options and pricing
  • Work alongside a supportive, upbeat team that knows RVing isn’t just recreation – it’s a way of living

What we Offer:

  • Competitive commission structure with high earning potential
  • Performance-based bonuses
  • Ongoing training, including in-house and manufacturer programs
  • Opportunity to represent top RV brands customers know and trust
  • Comprehensive benefits package (dental, extended health, life and disability insurance, and a healthcare spending account) *Subject to qualification
  • Employee discounts on products and services

Why Woody's RV World - High River Autoplex?

As a family-owned business, we’re committed to the communities we serve and to building a supportive, team-driven workplace. With strong inventory and real earning potential, we offer the opportunity to build a long-term, successful sales career.

We would love to have you on our team!

Pay: $50,000.00-$200,000.00 per year

Benefits:

  • Company events
  • Flexible schedule
  • On-site parking
  • Store discount

Work Location: In person
