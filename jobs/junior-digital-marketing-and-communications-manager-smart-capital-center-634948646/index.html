Marketing / Digital Marketing

Junior Digital Marketing and Communications Manager

at Smart Capital Center

📍 Location Toronto, Ontario Remote
📋 Details Full-time
📅 Posted Mar 02, 2026

About the Role

Execute digital campaigns, design marketing assets

About Smart Capital Center

AI-powered fintech/CRE marketing platform

Full Description

Smart Capital Center is the only end-to-end, AI-powered platform for CRE investment and financial management. Purpose-built to power real-time underwriting, asset management, and portfolio analysis, the platform combines live data intelligence, workflow automation, and AI-driven insights to accelerate decision-making, reduce risk, and spot opportunity others miss.

We are looking for a Junior Digital Marketing Associate to help execute and optimize digital marketing campaigns across channels. This role is ideal for a hands-on, design-forward, and data-driven marketer who enjoys working with modern AI tools and performance marketing systems.

What You’ll Do

  • Execute and manage digital marketing campaigns across email, web, social, and paid channels
  • Design marketing assets such as:

-Social media visuals

-Email templates

-Landing pages

-Digital ads and presentations

  • Support paid advertising campaigns (Google Ads, LinkedIn, etc.), including setup, testing, and optimization
  • Use AI tools (e.g., ChatGPT, design and automation tools) to accelerate campaign creation and iteration
  • Monitor campaign performance using analytics tools and dashboards; recommend optimizations
  • Assist with SEO, keyword research, and website performance improvements
  • Help maintain marketing workflows, automation, and CRM systems
  • Conduct market and competitor research to support positioning and growth initiatives
  • Collaborate with product, sales, and leadership teams to ensure consistent branding

What We’re Looking For

  • Bachelor’s degree in Marketing, Business, Communications, or a related field (or equivalent experience)
  • 1–3 years of experience in digital marketing, growth marketing, or related roles
  • Strong design and visual communication skills (Figma, Canva, Adobe, or similar)
  • Familiarity with digital marketing tools and platforms:
  • Paid ads (Google, LinkedIn)
  • Email marketing platforms
  • Social media platforms
  • Analytics tools (Google Analytics or similar)
  • Ability to craft clear, concise marketing copy and adapt messaging across formats
  • Comfortable using AI tools to support marketing execution and optimization
  • Analytical, detail-oriented, and well-organized
  • Interest in fintech, SaaS, AI, or real estate technology is a plus

Why Join Smart Capital Center

  • Work on cutting-edge AI technology in fintech and commercial real estate
  • Gain hands-on experience with modern digital and performance marketing
  • Collaborate with enterprise clients across North America
  • Join a lean, fast-growing, and professional team
  • Competitive compensation, flexible work environment, and generous PTO
