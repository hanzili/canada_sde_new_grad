Full Description

Full job description

Royal De Versailles Jewellers is seeking a highly motivated, curious and customer-focused Sales Associate with a passion for creating memorable experiences for our worldly clientele.

Our Sales Associate is responsible for providing exceptional customer service by meeting our client's individual needs and expectations. The Sales Associate serves as a representative of the company by promoting the philosophy and values of all brands. By striving to discover your client's needs you will tailor their experience within our store in order to elevate their impression of the brands and build long-lasting relationships.

This is an opportunity for those who thrive in a fast-paced environment. You must have a strong strategic sales & business mindset, a willingness to learn, and a passion for forming meaningful relationships across our diverse client base. Your self-driven and curious nature will continuously drive you to improve your personal and team performance. We value forward-thinking individuals who are eager to use their creativity and skills to grow within our company.

KEY SKILLS & REQUIREMENTS

  • Identify and assess client needs to achieve satisfaction
  • Demonstrate sales leadership by playing an active role on the sales floor and ensuring the highest level of customer service is provided
  • Build sustainable relationships and trust with customer accounts through open and interactive communication
  • Keep records of customer interactions, process customer accounts and file documents
  • Keep customers informed of current products, trends, and collections
  • Communicate the key qualities and the characteristics of the brand to customers
  • Follow procedures, guidelines and policies

REQUIREMENTS

  • Minimum of 5 years of customer care/luxury sales
  • Excellent verbal and written communication skills to deliver exemplary customer service
  • Self-starter; the ability to be flexible and proactive as per the needs of the business
  • Ability to work effectively in a busy and high-profile environment
  • Ability to multi-task, prioritize and manage time effectively
