About Walter Capital Partners

Full Description

LOCATION: Westmount, Montreal

POSITION: Private Equity Intern

REPORTS TO: VP, Walter Capital Partners

Company Overview Launched in late 2015, Walter Capital Partners (« WCP ») is a private equity firm that embodies the entrepreneurial spirit and strategic vision of the Somers family, founders of the Walter Group.

The managing partners of WCP have all been corporate executives and have a strong financial profile. With our entrepreneurial vision and international network, we act as active shareholders in support of management teams to foster operational transformations and organic or acquisition-based growth. Beyond our capital investment, we are a partner of choice to accompany our portfolio companies in their expansion, whether on a national or international scale.

Internship Summary Walter Capital Partners is looking to hire an intern who will work closely with the entire WCP team.

The selected candidate will be a key contributor to the investment team. The intern will work directly with a diverse group of qualified professionals and will contribute to the full spectrum of private equity investing including research, company valuation, transaction execution, value-creation initiatives.

This internship will allow the candidate to develop the fundamental investment and business skills while contributing to the growth of market-leading companies.The duration will be determined according to the availability of the intern (at least 3 months).

Requirements Candidates should have the following qualifications:

  • Full-time student at the undergraduate (bachelor’s) or graduate (master’s, PhD) level
  • Completed a prior internship in a relevant field
  • Demonstrated academic excellence
  • Strong written and verbal communication skills
  • Excellent interpersonal and teamwork skills
  • Highly organized and detail-oriented
  • Ability to manage deadlines with multiple projects
  • Proficiency in MS Office Suite, including Outlook, Word, Excel and PowerPoint
  • Fluency in English and French

Why become an intern at Walter?

  • Renowned asset management firm that promotes intern development by engaging them in various projects
  • Competitive compensation
  • Modern offices including a private gym with amenities (showers and lockers)
  • Participation in corporate events
  • Easy public transport access (next to Atwater station)
  • Snacks, fresh fruit, barista-style coffee
  • And much more!

How To Apply Interested candidates should apply through their respective university’s career services or by sending their candidacy to resume@waltercm.ca.

Please Submit The Following Documents In PDF Format

  • Cover letter mentioning:

  • Your interest to join WCP

  • What makes you an interesting candidate

  • CV, including your complete contact information

  • Most recent university transcript

Our Walter business units bring passion to everything they do, and foster relationships based on trust, integrity, respect, and fairness. We thank all candidates for their interest towards Walter Capital Partners. Only selected candidates for an interview will be contacted.
