Full Description

Lakeshore Group Real Estate | Oakville, ON

Lakeshore Group Real Estate is a growing real estate team in Oakville looking for a Content Creator to support our marketing and social media. This role is ideal for a high school or university student seeking hands-on experience in content creation, marketing, and real estate.

What You’ll Do

  • Film short-form content (Reels, TikTok, YouTube Shorts)
  • Create photo and video content for property listings
  • Capture behind-the-scenes and day-to-day real estate content
  • Assist with basic editing and content ideas

Who This Is For

  • High school or university students
  • Creative, reliable, and comfortable with a camera
  • Interest in marketing, media, or real estate
  • No professional experience required

Details
