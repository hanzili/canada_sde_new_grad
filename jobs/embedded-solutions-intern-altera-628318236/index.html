About the Role

About Altera

Intel-backed FPGA company with strong hardware legacy.

Full Description

Job Details Job Description: Come join Altera working on embedded microprocessor based features of Altera’s FPGA-SoC devices. The Embedded Solutions team within the Software Engineering department is seeking a motivated intern to help implement FPGA designs and embedded software features and customer designs targeting our leading edge FPGAs. This broad and highly dynamic role provides unique opportunities to work directly on hardware and software, including the use of physical development kits.

This role requires a self-starter with strong interpersonal communication and collaboration skills, along with an exposure and interest to learn about to digital design and C programming. Experience in Altera FPGA design through course work or projects would be an advantage.

In this role, you will have the opportunity to work directly with Silicon design team, CAD tool teams, applications engineering, and product planning, to see how holistic customer solutions are developed that met market requirements and delight customers.
