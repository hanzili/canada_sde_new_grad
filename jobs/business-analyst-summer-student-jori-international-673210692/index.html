Full Description

About JORI Logistics

JORI is a client-focused, relationship-based, family-owned company that understands our client’s Customs Brokerage and transportation needs. We combine unparalleled customer service with robust technology solutions to transform logistics into a competitive advantage for importers and exporters. Since 1992, we’ve provided turnkey logistics services, including Canadian Customs Brokerage, U.S. Customs Brokerage, Air Freight, Ocean Freight, Ground Freight, Trade Consulting, and more. Our client relationships, some of which are as old as we are, have grown to include some of Canada’s foremost importers and exporters. With offices in Houston, Almaty, and Cebu City, JORI is small enough to care and big enough to matter.

About the Opportunity

Reporting to the Director of Information Technology, the Business Analyst Summer Student will work as a member of the Projects team. Applicants must have a strong interest in identifying opportunities for improvement, analyzing business processes and solving challenging problems in a team and/or individual setting. A strong desire to learn and contribute to meaningful projects is highly valued.

What is the role?

  • Working with various departments to ensure the successful progression of projects and adherence to deadlines.

  • Assist the Project Management Office team in reviewing, organizing, and monitoring projects to ensure timely progress.

  • Assist in creating and updating Standard Operation Procedures for various departments, fostering consistency and efficiency.

  • Provide documentation support, including drafting, organizing, and maintaining project records.
  • Deliver regular status updates to management and project committees, highlighting key milestones and addressing potential challenges,

About You

What do you need?

  • Strong analytical and problem-solving skills, with the ability to evaluate information and propose practical solutions/suggestions.
  • Strong organization skills to manage multiple tasks, prioritize effectively, and meet deadlines.
  • A collaborative mindset and ability to work well individually or part of a team.
  • Strong communication and documentation skillsfor documenting processes and presenting updates.
  • Proficiency in using tools like Microsoft Office (Excel, Word, PowerPoint, Outlook).
  • Registered in a recognized university or college and working towards a degree in Business Analyst, Project Management, Marketing, Communication or related programs Must be returning to school for the Fall semester

Nice to have skills

  • Interest in global trade, international business, Freight Forwarding, and/or Canadian Customs
  • Project Management
  • Excellent English (written and verbal)

Do our values resonate with you? If you want to be a part of a fast-growing and technology-driven company with a global reach, apply now!
