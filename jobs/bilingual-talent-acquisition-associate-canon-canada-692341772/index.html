Full Description

Bilingual Talent Acquisition Associate - Hybrid

  • Do you thrive in a fast-paced environment where you’re responsible for the entire recruitment process?
  • Do you enjoy the challenge of identifying and attracting top candidates through creative sourcing methods?
  • Do you have the skills to create strong, lasting relationships with hiring managers while delivering excellent recruitment support?

As a Bilingual Talent Acquisition Associate, you will report to the Manager of Compensation, Job Design, and Talent and will be responsible for the high-volume recruitment of sales-focused

positions. This role partners with Sales Hiring Managers to manage the full-cycle recruitment process, ensuring a steady pipeline of talent to meet business growth goals. The Associate will focus on standardized, high-frequency hiring while adhering to established branding and legislative guidelines.

Key Responsibilities

  • Sales Pipeline Management: Proactively source, build, and maintain candidate pipelines specifically for various Sales tiers using LinkedIn Recruiter and other sourcing channels.
  • Screening & Selection: Conduct high-volume phone screenings and preliminary interviews to assess candidate suitability and "sales DNA" before submitting to Hiring Managers.
  • Hiring Manager Partnership: Collaborate with Sales Managers and HRBPs to understand headcount needs, set expectations for candidate profiles, and schedule interviews.
  • Onboarding & Administration: Coordinate new hire documentation, submit payroll/IT requests, and maintain communication with candidates prior to their start date.
  • Branding & Sourcing: Create compelling sales job postings that adhere to Canon branding and utilize specific sourcing channels to reach sales professionals.
  • Reporting: Generate weekly recruitment status reports for sales leadership to track progress against hiring targets.

To Succeed, You Will Need

  • Fluency in French and English
  • Post Secondary education in Human Resources or related field
  • Minimum 1 year of work experience in full-cycle recruitment
  • Experience with ATS, LinkedIn Recruiter
  • Ability to effectively write attractive job descriptions when sourcing resumes to obtain an adequate number of quality results
  • Substantial project and process management skills to manage the requisition process from start to finish
  • Intermediate analytical skills to pull and analyze data in the Applicant Tracking System
  • Intermediate time management to balance around 20 open requisitions and the needs and requests of hiring managers and candidates

Why Join? HYBRID WORK

  • We offer a Hybrid work schedule. You will be in the office Mondays, Wednesdays and Thursdays each week, and can work from home for the remainder of the week (subject to specific business needs requiring office attendance)
  • When working from home, a reliable internet connection is required. Remote work is supported with cloud-based applications and collaboration tools
