About Us

MultiplAI builds AI-assisted compliance software for regulated financial institutions. Our systems are designed to be explainable, auditable, and production-ready, operating in environments where correctness and trust matter as much as performance.

We are looking for a Machine Learning Intern to support research and experimentation in synthetic data generation, document understanding, and model evaluation.

This role sits between applied ML research and product exploration and focuses on understanding model behavior, reliability, and limitations—not production deployment.

What You Will Work On

·      Designing and generating synthetic datasets from a limited set of document-style examples

·      Running controlled experiments to evaluate ML / LLM behavior under data-constrained settings

·      Analyzing model reliability, edge cases, and failure modes

·      Documenting findings, assumptions, and limitations in clear technical notes

·      Supporting work related to AI safety, evaluation, and model risk

Who We’re Looking For

·      3rd–4th year undergraduate in Computer Science, Data Science, AI, or related field

·      Completed at least one Machine Learning course

·      Strong Python skills and familiarity with ML tooling

·      Comfortable with experimentation, analysis, and technical writing

·      Interested in how ML systems behave in real-world, regulated settings

What You’ll Gain

·      Hands-on experience with industry-grade ML evaluation practices

·      Exposure to how AI safety and model risk are handled in practice

·      Experience producing reproducible experiments and evaluation artifacts

·      Close mentorship from senior engineers in a small, focused team

How We Work

·      Small team, real production systems, real enterprise users

·      Emphasis on rigor, clarity, and reproducibility over hype

·      AI is treated as a tool to support human accountability, not replace it

·      This role tends to suit people who enjoy depth, precision, and real-world impact.
