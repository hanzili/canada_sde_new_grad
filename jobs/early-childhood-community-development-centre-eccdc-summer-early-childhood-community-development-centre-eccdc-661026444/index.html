Job Description

The Early Childhood Community Development Centre (ECCDC) is seeking Summer Students for the positions outlined below. The positions will begin Monday May 25 and end Friday July 17, 2026. Renumeration for all 3 positions is $19/hour. Consideration for a blend of in-person and remote work may be possible.

The Event Marketing Specialist will support the work of the Training and Events Team through researching and developing a range of unique marketing plans and social media engagement strategies. This
position will also support the development of summer member engagement events to support networking within Niagara’s early learning sector. The Event Marketing Specialist will also support the development and implementation of events and training that are offered to educators across Niagara.

This position has been funded through the Canada Summer Jobs Program. The Early Childhood Community Development Centre is an equal opportunity employer.
Interested applicants are invited to apply on or before Friday May 8, 2026 by forwarding a résumé by email as per below or dropping it off at ECCDC, 3550 Schmon Parkway, Thorold.
