About the Role

Build AI tools and prototypes with teams.

About CGI

Global IT consulting firm with vast client network.

Full Description

We are looking for a Co-op AI Developer to help identify and build practical AI solutions that reduce manual work and improve efficiency. You will work closely with leadership and cross-functional teams to quickly turn business needs into simple, usable AI tools.

Start Date: September 2026
Application Deadline: May 25th, 2026
Work term length: 8 months term

Perks of Being a CGI Co-op:

  • Participate in monthly socials to meet co-ops across the country.
  • Access professional development workshops hosted by domain experts on topics ranging from how to be a top consultant, how to apply the agile methodology, data & analytics and more!

To learn more about CGI and hear from our Co-ops and New Grads, visit www.cgi.com/startmycareer

CGI is providing a reasonable estimate of the pay range for this role. The determination of this range includes factors such as skill set level, geographic market, experience and training, and licenses and certifications. Compensation decisions depend on the facts and circumstances of each case. A reasonable estimate of the current range is $18-32/hour. This role is an existing vacancy.

Your future duties and responsibilities

  • Work with developers, data scientists, and product managers to understand business needs
  • Help define AI use cases, roadmap, and basic technical requirements
  • Identify and prioritize opportunities to automate manual processes
  • Build simple AI solutions using AI/ML, LLMs, and workflow automation tools
  • Develop quick prototypes (e.g., chatbots, document summaries, automation scripts)
  • Support integration of AI solutions into existing workflows and systems
  • Ensure solutions follow data privacy, security, and responsible AI practices
  • Document solutions so teams can easily adopt and use them

What You’ll Gain

  • Hands-on experience working on real AI use cases with business impact
  • Exposure to AI strategy and enterprise environments
  • Opportunity to build solutions used by leadership and teams
  • Mentorship from experienced professionals

Required qualifications to be successful in this role

What We’re Looking For

  • Student in Computer Science, AI, Data Science, or related field
  • Basic knowledge of Python or similar programming language
  • Understanding of AI/ML concepts and Large Language Models (LLMs)
  • Interest in automation and solving real business problems
  • Strong communication skills and ability to work with different teams

Nice to Have

  • Experience with LLM tools, APIs, or workflow automation
  • Exposure to AI/ML frameworks (e.g., PyTorch, TensorFlow)
  • Contributions to open-source AI projects or research work
  • Familiarity with cloud platforms or basic application development
