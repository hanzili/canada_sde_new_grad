Jr. Content Creator - Vernon Hyundai

at Vernon Hyundai

📍 Location Vernon, BC V1T 4Y6
📋 Details Full-time · Permanent · Competitive compensation based on experience
📅 Posted Mar 04, 2026

About the Role

Create engaging social content, manage platforms, analyze performance

About Vernon Hyundai

Respected automotive group with growth opportunities

Full Description

Vernon Hyundai is looking for a creative, motivated, and detail-oriented Junior Content Creator to join our growing team. As part of the Wyant Group, we are committed to delivering exceptional customer experiences and innovative marketing that drives results.

If you’re passionate about social media, storytelling, digital trends, and the automotive industry, this is your opportunity to build your career in a fast-paced, collaborative environment.

Position Summary

The Junior Content Creator will support and execute social media strategies that drive brand awareness, customer engagement, and sales growth. This role is ideal for someone who thrives in a dynamic environment and enjoys creating compelling content that connects with audiences.

Key Responsibilities

Support the development and execution of social media objectives and strategies

Write dynamic, engaging, and compelling content to support sales, marketing, and recruiting initiatives

Ensure all content aligns with brand standards and OEM marketing compliance guidelines

Research, develop, and implement innovative social media tools and strategies aligned with business goals

Create and publish consistent, up-to-date content across all social media platforms

Monitor social media channels and manage digital conversations by responding to comments, messages, and customer feedback

Track, analyze, and report on social media performance metrics and KPIs

Support public relations initiatives, dealership events, and community engagement activities

Integrate social media messaging into broader marketing, sales, and recruitment campaigns

Contribute to integrated marketing and sales projects

Maintain awareness of industry trends and digital best practices, sharing insights with leadership

Assist with tracking and monitoring budget items related to social media initiatives

Qualifications & Skills

Previous relevant experience in content creation, social media, and or marketing

Experience creating engaging digital content (photo, video)

Strong written and verbal communication skills

Experience managing social media platforms (Facebook, Instagram, TikTok, LinkedIn, etc.)

Basic understanding of analytics tools and performance tracking

Creative mindset with strong attention to detail

Ability to multitask and manage priorities in a fast-paced environment

Automotive industry experience is considered an asset

What We Offer

Opportunity to grow within a respected automotive group

Hands-on experience across multiple marketing channels

Collaborative and supportive team environment

Competitive compensation based on experience

Career development opportunities within the Wyant Group

At Vernon Hyundai, we believe in driving success — for our customers and our team. If you’re ready to accelerate your marketing career, we’d love to hear from you.
