Frontend Software Developer, Jr.

at Sundayy

📍 Location Ontario Hybrid
📋 Details Full-time · $67,700–$94,700 CAD

About the Role

Develop frontend features in React/TypeScript

About Sundayy

Top Canadian tech platform for social media

Full Description

About The Company Hootsuite is a leading social media management platform dedicated to helping organizations and individuals harness the power of social media to achieve their business goals. With a global presence and a diverse client base, Hootsuite empowers users to manage multiple social channels efficiently, analyze performance, and engage with their audiences effectively. Our innovative solutions are built on a foundation of cutting-edge technology, collaborative teamwork, and a customer-centric approach. We foster an inclusive and dynamic work environment that encourages continuous learning, creativity, and growth. As part of our commitment to social responsibility, we actively contribute to community initiatives and promote sustainable practices across our operations.

About The Role We are seeking a Junior Software Developer to join our talented development team. In this role, you will be instrumental in designing, building, testing, and deploying high-quality software solutions that deliver tangible value to our customers. Working within an agile environment, you will collaborate closely with peers and senior team members to develop scalable, reliable, and secure applications. You will gain hands-on experience across the entire software development lifecycle, from beta releases to production deployment. Reporting to the Senior Manager of Software Development, you will be supported in your professional growth and encouraged to contribute ideas that enhance our products and processes. This position offers an excellent opportunity to develop technical expertise, deepen your understanding of modern web technologies, and make meaningful contributions to a globally recognized platform.

Qualifications

  • A degree in Computer Science, Software Engineering, or a related field
  • 1-2 years of experience in software development, including co-op terms or internships
  • Proficiency in TypeScript and React
  • Familiarity with modern web technologies and development tools
  • Experience with CI/CD platforms such as Jenkins
  • Understanding of software testing, debugging, and troubleshooting techniques
  • Knowledge of security, accessibility, and privacy best practices
  • Ability to learn and follow established coding patterns and architectural guidelines
  • Strong communication and collaboration skills
  • Ability to work effectively within an agile team environment
  • Basic understanding of dependency management and package ecosystems

Responsibilities

  • Develop and deliver low‑complexity projects aligned with coding, design, and security standards
  • Contribute to the team’s roadmap by providing input, asking questions, and escalating risks or scope changes
  • Participate actively in team activities, including code reviews, documentation, and knowledge sharing
  • Address minor bugs independently and collaborate on resolving complex issues
  • Maintain effective communication with team members and stakeholders
  • Utilize modern web technologies to improve developer workflows and infrastructure
  • Work with repositories primarily using TypeScript and React, supporting other teams across the organization
  • Assist in optimizing CI/CD pipelines to support reliable and efficient software delivery
  • Engage in technical initiatives aimed at enhancing the software development lifecycle
  • Manage dependencies and contribute to library adoption, upgrades, and deprecation decisions
  • Gain exposure to architectural decisions and tools that underpin modern front-end development

Benefits

  • Competitive salary range of $67,700—$94,700 CAD
  • Health, dental, and vision insurance plans
  • Opportunities for professional development and continuous learning
  • Flexible work arrangements and supportive work environment
  • Paid time off and wellness programs
  • Participation in innovative projects with a global impact
  • Inclusive culture that values diversity and collaboration
