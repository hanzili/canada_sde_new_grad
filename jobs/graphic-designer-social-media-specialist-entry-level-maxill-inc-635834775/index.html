Full Description

Graphic Designer/Social Media Specialist

In-House | Packaged Goods Company | Full-Time | On-Site
St. Thomas, Ontario
CA$20.00 – CA$24.00 per hour
40 hours per week

Ready to See Your Designs Shared Across Canada?

If you’re a creative thinker with a sharp eye for detail and a passion for branding, packaging, and digital content, this is your opportunity to launch your design career with a respected and established Canadian company.

Portfolio required — applications without one will not be considered.
Applicants must reside in the St. Thomas–London, Ontario area.

About maxill

For 38 years, maxill has been a leader in manufacturing, importing, and distributing dental and oral health products across Canada. We proudly support thousands of dental professionals, retailers, and government organizations nationwide.

Our core product lines include:

  • Oral hygiene products
  • Dental disposables
  • Infection control solutions

As a direct marketer, we bring our manufacturing and logistics strengths directly to dental professionals — delivering innovation, quality, and value without traditional distribution channels.

Learn more at: www.maxill.com

The Opportunity

Based in St. Thomas, this full-time, on-site role places you at the center of our in-house creative team.

You will collaborate closely with marketing and internal teams to develop:

  • Social media graphics and campaigns
  • Creative, engaging social media videos
  • Digital marketing assets
  • Promotional materials
  • Product photography and visual content
  • Product packaging
  • Flyers and catalogues

This is a hands-on, fast-paced position where creativity meets real-world production. You’ll gain valuable experience in packaging design, print production, branding, and content creation while contributing to products used across Canada.

What You Bring

  • Degree or Diploma in Graphic Design (completed or in progress)
  • Minimum 1 year of graphic design experience
  • Strong portfolio showcasing your work (required)
  • Experience planning and creating social media content
  • Proficiency in Adobe Creative Suite (InDesign, Photoshop, Illustrator)
  • Solid understanding of print production and prepress
  • Exceptional attention to detail
  • Ability to manage multiple projects and meet deadlines
  • Positive attitude and openness to feedback

Assets (Nice to Have)

  • Product photography experience
  • Basic video production skills
  • Familiarity with web design concepts, HTML, and CSS

Why You’ll Love Working Here

  • Supportive, collaborative team environment
  • Recognition for your contributions and achievements
  • Real-world experience designing products distributed nationwide
  • Stability of an established Canadian company
  • Full-time, permanent opportunity

Benefits

  • Casual dress
  • Dental care
  • Extended health care
  • Vision care
  • Life insurance
  • On-site parking
  • Monday to Friday schedule
  • No weekends
  • 8-hour shifts

Please Note

  • Applications without a portfolio will not be reviewed.
  • Applicants must currently reside in the St. Thomas–London, Ontario area.
  • No phone calls, please. Email applications only.

If you are ready to build your design career and contribute to a growing, innovative company, we look forward to reviewing your portfolio.
