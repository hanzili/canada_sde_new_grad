About the Role

About Ferrero

Full Description

About the Role: Working in the Quality Control Lab at Ferrero Canada, the Chemistry Lab - Co-op will aid in conducting Chemistry analysis and inspections of raw materials, in-process and finished products to evaluate quality and safety. Ensuring that all analysis is carried out in compliance with Safety policies, Good Manufacturing Practices (GMP/GLP), Standard Operating Procedures (SOPs) and the Group’s standard.

Main Responsibilities:

  • Check raw ingredients, in-process and finished products for safety and quality adhere to food standards, production specifications, safety regulations, waste management and water supply specifications.
  • Analysis following ISO 17025 on food ingredients for flavor, texture, color, nutritional content, etc.
  • Operate and maintain laboratory equipment and apparatus and prepare solutions, reagents, and sample formulations.
  • Ensure that procedures are performed correctly and in a timely manner.
  • Guarantee that the finished products meet the quality specifications established by Ferrero.
  • Apply food science knowledge to determine best ways to process, package, preserve, store, and distribute food.

About You: Qualifications:

  • Knowledge of microbiological procedures and techniques, GMPs and HACCP (Hazard Analysis and Critical Control Points).
  • Strong organizational skills and attention to detail
  • Effective use of Microsoft Office Suite (Excel, Word, PowerPoint)
  • Good oral and written communication skills

Targeted Programs:

  • Food Industry Management
  • Food Science
  • Chemical Physics
  • Chemistry
  • Biochemistry
  • Biological & Medical Physics
  • Biological & Pharmaceutical Chemistry
  • Biological Engineering
  • Biomedical Engineering
  • Biomedical Toxicology

Our Benefits & Perks: The hourly rate for this position is $25.00.

About Ferrero: Ferrero began its journey in the small town of Alba in Piedmont, Italy, in 1946. Today, it is one of the world’s largest sweet-packaged food companies, with many iconic brands sold in countries all over the world. Find out more about Ferrero at ferrero.com.
