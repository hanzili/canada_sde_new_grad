Human Resources Co-Op

at Alberici Constructors, LTD Canada

📍 Location Burlington, ON
📋 Details Co-op · 12–16 months · $25.00/hour
📅 Posted Feb 26, 2026

About the Role

Provide HR admin support, assist recruitment processes

About Alberici Constructors, LTD Canada

Large construction firm, invests in employee development

Full Description

Short Description:

We are seeking a Human Resources Co-op (12–16 months) to join our growing team in support of the Eglinton Crosstown West Extension (ECWE) transit project. This role provides hands-on HR support across core people programs while gaining exposure to a large-scale infrastructure and transit environment.

Pay: $25.00

ABOUT THE ROLE

The HR Co-op provides essential administrative and logistical coordination for HR programs and processes supporting the ECWE project team. Working closely with the HR team, this role contributes to learning and development initiatives, the employee life cycle, records management, talent acquisition activities, and internal communication platforms. With a strong focus on organization, accuracy, and collaboration, the HR Co-op plays an important role in ensuring smooth HR operations and a positive employee experience on a major transit infrastructure project.

Join our growing HR team, and be surrounded by a team of dedicated professionals that will help you learn, develop and grow in various areas of HR, early in your career!

Key Responsibilities

  • Provides essential administrative support and logistical coordination to various HR programs and processes, including but not limited to:
    • Employee life cycle, including HR related administration
  • Training, learning and development programs, onboarding and orientations
  • Employee events and social programs, including venue bookings, agenda development, planning and organization
  • Recruitment activities such as assisting with interviews, career fairs, recruitment events, and helping to coordinate candidate interactions
  • Assists with and performs administrative updates to internal communications, such as the Commons webpages, employee/manager guides, and policy updates
  • Answers questions and provides information to employees and leaders related to HR administration, policies and procedures
  • Exercises discretion and professional integrity in all matters involving confidential or sensitive information
  • Supports other HR programs and initiatives as required
  • Contributes as an active member of the HR team by providing administrative support and assisting team members to maintain a collaborative and productive work environment
  • Contributes positively to Alberici’s culture, values, and safe environment

Qualifications & Experience

  • Enrolled in a post-secondary education program in Business, Human Resources, or equivalent; courses in human resources or organizational behaviour preferred but not required
  • Experience working in an office environment preferred
  • Passion for human resources and serving others
  • Passion for organization and strong attention to detail
  • Ability to travel to and from the Burlington office
