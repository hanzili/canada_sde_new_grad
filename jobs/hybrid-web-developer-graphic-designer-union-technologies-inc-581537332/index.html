About the Role

We are hiring a full-time Hybrid Web Developer & Graphic Designer to join our growing team at Union Technologies Inc. This position requires someone who can work 100% EST hours, communicate clearly in English, and produce both high-quality design and modern front-end development work.

You will work on real projects for North American union clients, including:

  • Flyers
  • Posters
  • Social media graphics
  • Landing pages
  • Web UI screens
  • Email campaigns
  • Light front-end development
  • WordPress updates and new page builds

This is NOT a pure developer role and NOT a pure designer role ‚Äî you must be strong in both.

Responsibilities

Graphic Design (Print + Digital)

  • Design flyers, posters, banners, postcards, social graphics
  • Create union-wide announcements, meeting notices, event materials
  • Produce print-ready files (CMYK, bleed, margins)
  • Design assets for web, email templates, campaign materials

Web Development

  • Front-end development with HTML, CSS, JavaScript
  • Build landing pages, UI components, forms, and reusable sections
  • Update and style WordPress pages (Elementor, Gutenberg, custom HTML/CSS)
  • Collaborate on UI design for web apps (Figma/Adobe)
  • Optimize pages for speed and responsiveness

General

  • Work directly with our internal team
  • Communicate clearly and professionally
  • Use AI tools (ChatGPT, Midjourney, Canva AI, etc.) to speed up workflows
  • Meet deadlines and follow instructions precisely

Required Skills

Design

  • Adobe Photoshop / Illustrator / InDesign OR Figma
  • Strong typography, layout, and composition
  • Experience with print design required
  • Ability to follow existing branding guidelines

Development

  • HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript (required)
  • Responsive design
  • WordPress experience (big asset)
  • React/Next.js (bonus, not required)

Soft Skills

  • Strong English (written & spoken)
  • Reliable communication
  • Available full-time & during EST
  • Ability to work independently and finish tasks quickly
