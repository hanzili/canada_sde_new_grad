About Argo Digital Gold

Full Description

Role Overview

We are seeking a top-tier Junior Banking Analyst to work directly with the CEO and senior leadership on live transactions, valuations, and strategic initiatives. This role offers immediate exposure to corporate finance execution, with significant responsibility from day one.

The ideal candidate is technically strong, detail-oriented, and thrives in a high-performance, fast-paced environment.

Responsibilities:

Financial Modelling & Valuation

  • Build and maintain complex financial models, including:
  • Trading comparables, and precedent transaction analyses
  • Scenario, sensitivity, and downside cases
  • Support valuation analysis for M&A, capital raises, and strategic alternatives
  • Review and analyze financial statements, KPIs, and business drivers

Transaction Execution

  • Support execution of financing, M&A, and strategic transactions
  • Prepare and manage due diligence materials and data rooms
  • Assist in drafting transaction materials and internal investment memos
  • Conduct industry, market, and company-specific research

Pitch Books & Presentations

  • Create investor, client, and board-level pitch materials
  • Develop clear, concise PowerPoint presentations with strong financial narratives
  • Support preparation of decks, teasers, and management presentations

CEO & Senior Management Support

  • Work directly with the CEO on time-sensitive analyses and strategic initiatives
  • Provide ad-hoc modeling, valuation, and analytical support
  • Assist in decision-making with structured, data-driven insights

Qualifications

  • Bachelor’s degree in Finance, Economics, Accounting, Engineering, or a quantitative discipline
  • 1–3 years of experience in investment banking, private equity, corporate development, or strategy consulting
  • Strong technical foundation in accounting, corporate finance, and valuation
  • Advanced Excel and PowerPoint skills
  • Ability to work long hours under tight deadlines while maintaining accuracy

Core Attributes

  • Exceptional attention to detail and work ethic
  • Strong analytical and problem-solving skills
  • Clear written and verbal communication
  • High level of professionalism and discretion
  • Ability to operate independently and take ownership of workstreams

Preferred / Nice to Have

  • Prior transaction experience (M&A, capital markets, or private placements)
  • Familiarity with financial databases (Capital IQ, PitchBook, FactSet, Bloomberg)

Compensation & Development

  • Analyst-level compensation
  • Direct exposure to senior decision-makers and live deals
  • Steep learning curve with rapid skill development
  • Clear path to increased responsibility and promotion
