Role Overview

We are seeking a top-tier Junior Banking Analyst to work directly with the CEO and senior leadership on live transactions, valuations, and strategic initiatives. This role offers immediate exposure to corporate finance execution, with significant responsibility from day one.

The ideal candidate is technically strong, detail-oriented, and thrives in a high-performance, fast-paced environment.

Responsibilities:

Financial Modelling & Valuation

  • Build and maintain complex financial models, including:
  • Trading comparables, and precedent transaction analyses
  • Scenario, sensitivity, and downside cases
  • Support valuation analysis for M&A, capital raises, and strategic alternatives
  • Review and analyze financial statements, KPIs, and business drivers

Transaction Execution

  • Support execution of financing, M&A, and strategic transactions
  • Prepare and manage due diligence materials and data rooms
  • Assist in drafting transaction materials and internal investment memos
  • Conduct industry, market, and company-specific research

Pitch Books & Presentations

  • Create investor, client, and board-level pitch materials
  • Develop clear, concise PowerPoint presentations with strong financial narratives
  • Support preparation of decks, teasers, and management presentations
