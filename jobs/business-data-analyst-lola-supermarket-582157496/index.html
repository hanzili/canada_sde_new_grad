About Lola Supermarket

Full Description

Responsibilities

  • Collect, clean, and prepare datasets from sales, inventory, and customer loyalty systems to support business analysis and decision-making.
  • Analyse large and complex datasets to identify trends, patterns, performance issues, and operational opportunities related to sales, customer behaviour, and inventory levels.
  • Develop and maintain interactive dashboards and analytical reports (Power BI, Tableau) to track KPIs and provide insights to management.
  • Collaborate with cross-functional teams to translate analytical findings into actionable business strategies that improve sales performance and operational efficiency.
  • Forecast customer demand to optimize stock levels, minimize stockouts and overstocking, and reduce spoilage of perishable goods.
  • Evaluate customer purchasing behaviour and assess the performance and effectiveness of the customer loyalty program.
  • Conduct pricing and promotion analysis to measure profitability, assess discount strategies, and determine revenue impacts.
  • Analyse customer traffic patterns and develop staffing optimization recommendations to enhance workforce productivity.
  • Stay updated with emerging technologies, analytics tools, and industry best practices to strengthen data-driven business performance.
