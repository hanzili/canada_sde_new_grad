Full Description

Responsibilities

  • Collect, clean, and prepare datasets from sales, inventory, and customer loyalty systems to support business analysis and decision-making.

  • Collaborate with cross-functional teams to translate analytical findings into actionable business strategies that improve sales performance and operational efficiency.

  • Conduct pricing and promotion analysis to measure profitability, assess discount strategies, and determine revenue impacts.
  • Analyse customer traffic patterns and develop staffing optimization recommendations to enhance workforce productivity.
