About APR Beauty Group Inc.

Full Description

Role Overview

The Junior Account Manager will act as the primary point of contact for assigned clients, managing relationships, and identify business opportunities among existing clients. This role requires strong communication, project coordination, and business communication to support clients and internal team.

Key Responsibilities

  • Support the Director of Project Development in managing assigned client accounts, acting as a key point of contact for day-to-day account coordination and communication.
  • Execute daily sales functions, including order processing, fulfillment tracking, invoicing, and coordinating shortages and remittances.
  • Maintain and update tracking sheets to monitor and analyze sales performance, P&Ls, inventory levels, marketing campaigns, and production activities.
  • Communicate regularly with clients to provide updates on order status, replenishment timelines, new product launches, and marketing initiatives.
  • Complete, organize, and file vendor documentation and product setup forms accurately and in a timely manner.
  • Maintain and update line sheets, order forms, pricing lists, and other sales-related documents to ensure accuracy and accessibility.
  • Attend sales meetings, trade shows, and industry events as required.
  • Act as a liaison to facilitate the flow of information between Sales & Marketing, Accounting, Operations, Quality and Purchasing & Logistics teams.

Required Qualifications

  • Bachelor's degree in related fields
  • Proven work experience as Account Manager, Junior Account Manager or in similar client-facing roles
  • Attention to details with strong analytical and organizational skills
  • Demonstrated ability to manage multiple projects and work collaboratively in team environment
  • Proficiency with CRM Software and Microsoft Office Suite (especially MS Excel)
