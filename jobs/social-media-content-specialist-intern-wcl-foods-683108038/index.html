About the Role

Plan and create short-form social media content

About WCL foods

Local Canadian food brand with growing social reach

Full Description

Company Overview

GOGÜ K-Pizza is a Korean-style frozen pizza brand based in British Columbia, Canada. We create premium, handcrafted K-pizzas inspired by bold Korean flavours, generous toppings, and our signature sweet potato mousse crust. Our products are currently growing through retail and grocery channels, and we are looking for a creative Social Media Content Specialist Intern to help us share our brand story with more customers.

We are looking for someone who understands social media trends, enjoys creating short-form content, and can help us build a fun, engaging, and consistent online presence for our pizza brand.

Responsibilities

  • Plan and create short-form video content for Instagram, TikTok, and other social media platforms.
  • Visit our store/demo/production location or receive content materials to create weekly social media posts.
  • Manage our social media account once per week, including uploading posts, reels, stories, and captions.
  • Write engaging captions and post copy that match our brand voice.
  • Research current food, K-food, frozen food, and social media trends to suggest creative content ideas.
  • Help create content around product features, cooking methods, customer reactions, in-store demos, promotions, and behind-the-scenes moments.
  • Organize content ideas and posting schedules based on seasonal campaigns, store launches, promotions, and demo events.
  • Monitor post performance and suggest improvements to increase engagement and brand awareness.
  • Support basic photo/video editing, including reels, product shots, and simple graphic content.

Required Qualifications

  • Interest in food, Korean food, CPG brands, marketing, and social media.
  • Basic knowledge of Instagram, TikTok, short-form video trends, and content creation.
  • Basic video editing and/or graphic design skills.
  • Strong sense of visual style, storytelling, and brand presentation.
  • Good written communication skills.
  • Creative, organized, and able to work independently.
  • Comfortable creating food-related content and suggesting new ideas.
  • Experience managing a personal or business Instagram/TikTok account is an asset.

Application Requirement

Please include your Instagram account link/handle when applying.
We will review your Instagram profile as a portfolio to better understand your content style, creativity, and social media experience.

Job Type: Internship / Part-time
Schedule: Approximately once per week
Contract Length: 3–4 months, with potential for extension
Location: Port Coquitlam / Greater Vancouver area
Compensation: To be discussed based on experience

How to Apply

Please submit your resume along with your Instagram handle or portfolio link. We are excited to meet someone who loves food, creativity, and social media, and who wants to help grow a fun Korean-style pizza brand in Canada.

Pay: From $17.85 per hour

Benefits:

  • Casual dress
  • Flexible schedule

Work Location: In person
