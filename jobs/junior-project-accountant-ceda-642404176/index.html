Job Description

CEDA is hiring a Junior Project Accountant for joining our Finance team in the Edmonton, AB office.

This person will play a key role in ensuring projects run smoothly and efficiently. If you are flexible and enjoy a hybrid-working model, apply now!

Key Duties and Responsibilities

  • Act as the key liason, providing financial support for the Regional Controller and Project Accountants
  • You will be responsible for effectively communicating project set up and customer billing requirements to all internal stakeholders, ensuring compliance and accurate rates.
  • Prepare project margin and customer profitability analyses
  • Assist in troubleshooting service tickets and issues in our systems (FieldFX and Microsoft AX) to keep everything functioning properly, aligning to project needs
  • Collaborate with the team and follow up on any project billing or costing discrepancies as needed
  • Assist with month-end duties, including the preparation and review of journal entries and the creation and maintenance of internal reports
  • Other duties as assigned

Role Specifications:

  • 2+ years’ experience in a project accounting or similar position
  • Working towards a professional accounting designation is an asset
  • Exposure to a daily field capture system is an asset
  • Strong working knowledge of Microsoft Excel

Skills:

  • You have an uncompromising belief in teamwork that allows you to effectively collaborate with others while supporting the goals of the business
  • Strong organization, prioritization, and problem solving skills with a high attention to detail
  • You are highly motivated and proactive; eagerly accepting new challenges

Additional Information
