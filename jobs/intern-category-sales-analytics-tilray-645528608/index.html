Full Description

Summer Internship – Category & Sales Analytics
Location: Toronto, ON, On-Site
Full-Time, Paid Internship: $20.00 Per Hour
Duration: June 8 – August 14, 2026

About Tilray Brands Inc.
Tilray Brands, Inc. (“Tilray”) (Nasdaq: TLRY; TSX: TLRY) is a leading global lifestyle and consumer packaged goods company with operations in Canada, the United States, Europe, Australia, and Latin America that is leading as a transformative force at the nexus of cannabis, beverage, wellness, and entertainment, elevating lives through moments of connection. Tilray’s mission is to be a leading premium lifestyle company with a house of brands and innovative products that inspire joy, wellness and create memorable experiences. Tilray’s unprecedented platform supports over 40 brands in over 20 countries, including comprehensive cannabis offerings, hemp-based foods, and craft beverages.

Internship Summary:

Tilray is a global leader in the cannabis industry, and we are looking for a talented Category and Sales Analytics Intern to join our team this summer. As the Category and Sales Analytics Intern, you will be responsible for supporting the Senior Category Analyst through market analysis and providing actionable insights on in market trends in the cannabis industry. You will use your expertise and knowledge of the industry to effectively analyze data and provide meaningful insights that will help inform our commercial decisions. You will also be responsible for identifying new opportunities for business growth and continuous improvement. If you have the knowledge and skills to make an impact in this role, we would love to hear from you.

A Day in a life of….

As the Category and Sales Analytics Intern you will support the Adult Use Recreational division in conducting research and analysis that provide valuable data driven insights which ultimately assist in product development, promotional execution, key initiative deployment, department development, and strategic alignment.

Duties & Responsibilities :

  • Work with the Senior Category Analyst to prepare regional analysis for primary and secondary markets, and present research to cross-functional teams (marketing, sales)
  • Work with the Senior Category Analyst to present category analysis, product and sales insights to regional sales directors, key account managers, and brand managers
  • Work with the Senior Category Analyst to develop innovation and product launch tracking and analysis
  • Illicit market research and analysis
  • Assisting with preparing regional category presentations and analysis
  • Work with the BI (Business Intelligence) team on process improvements and dashboards; leverage existing dashboards and data sources when developing analysis and insights
  • Special projects, presentations, and ad hoc analysis

What You’ll Bring to Tilray…

  • A desire to use data and insights to drive results
  • A passion to support the organization to drive growth
  • Ability to take initiative and work proactively
  • Ability to meet deadlines and complete analysis in a timely fashion

You are…

  • Goal-oriented, taking initiative to establish plans and exceed goals
  • Always on time and arrive to work ready to collaborate
  • Analytical, detail oriented, and able to convert data to strategic business insights
  • Able to position yourself as a trusted advisor to your business partners
  • Genuine in your desire to help our consumers, customers, and patients
  • Dedicated, hardworking, organized, and honest
  • Upbeat, enthusiastic team player willing to support multiple teams’ requests

Qualifications :

  • The ability to work with a wide variety of individuals within the organization and maintain regular contact with all stakeholders
  • A high degree of self-motivation and discipline
  • Knowledgeable about industry players, brands, and products
  • Understand the fundamentals of economics (supply, demand, elasticity), accounting (COGs, margins, growth, price slope, breakeven)
  • Adept at using Microsoft Excel and PowerPoint (intermediate to advanced)
  • Computer savvy and data driven
  • Excellent written and verbal communication skills; you respond to emails and requests in a timely manner
  • Strong work ethic and a learning mindset
  • Effective time management skills
  • Experience in CPG, at a cannabis LP, or at a cannabis retailer is an asset
  • Experience with PowerBI is an asset
  • Currently completing a post-secondary degree in business administration, data analytics, statistics, computer science accounting, finance, or related field
