About SGS

Global testing, inspection, certification leader

Full Description

Company Description

We are SGS – the world’s leading testing, inspection and certification company. We are recognized as the global benchmark for quality and integrity. Our 96,000 employees operate a network of 2,600 offices and laboratories, working together to enable a better, safer and more interconnected world.

Job Description

  • Conduct required laboratory testing following SGS's Standard Operating Procedures
  • Perform Sample Preparation.
  • Perform and record preventive maintenance as needed.
  • Adhere to proper labelling and documentation procedures for solvents, standards, reagents, etc.
  • Familiarize with applicable standard operation procedures (SOP)
  • Participate in proficiency testing programs and support quality by assisting supervisor / manager in investigating questionable and unsatisfactory results.
  • Print control charts; maintain reagent logs, standards inventory, etc. documents.
  • Adhere to the Company’s Quality Policy and Procedures.
  • Be aware of customer requirements.
  • Directly responsible for their area housekeeping and cleanliness on a daily basis.
  • Data entry into LIMS, Excel, and other data entry modules.
  • Adhere to the Company’s Quality Policy and Procedures.
  • Use Laboratory Management Systems (LIMS).
  • Adhere to company health and safety policies.
  • Provide accurate and timely results.
  • Conduct proper sample storage and disposal practices and documentation.
  • Liaise and resolve customer’s queries/results with the supervisor / manager and contribute to the resolution of customer complaints.
  • Validate new methods for business growth or for customer requests, and work on improving the efficiency of existing methods and SOPs as per the instructions provided by the supervisor / manager
  • Conduct sample pre-processing (sub sampling, homogenization, splitting, cleaning, grinding) as necessary

Qualifications

  • A degree and/or diploma in a Science related program (Chemistry, Agriculture, Food Science etc.) or pursuing a related degree is a requirement.
  • Experience in an Agricultural laboratory setting is an asset.
  • Familiar with Agricultural and Food matrixes an asset.
  • Knowledge of ISO 9001:2008 and ISO 17025:1999 requirements an asset.
  • Sound knowledge of good laboratory practices.
  • Familiar with official accredited methods such as AOAC AOCS methodology an asset.
  • Must be proficient in using various type of computer software (Word, Excel, PowerPoint, Outlook etc.)
