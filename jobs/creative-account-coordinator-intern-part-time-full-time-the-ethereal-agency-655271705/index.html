Full Description

About the Job

Company: The Ethereal Agency (etherealpr.com)

Location: Remote / Hybrid (Toronto-based preferred)

Type: Internship (Part-Time → Full-Time Opportunity)

About Us

The Ethereal Agency is a boutique marketing, PR, and branding agency specializing in restaurants, franchises, and lifestyle brands. We don’t just create content we build brands that perform.

Through social media, influencer marketing, creative campaigns, and digital storytelling, we help businesses turn attention into real, measurable growth.

About the Role

We’re looking for a highly organized, proactive, and detail-oriented Creative & Account Coordinator to support day-to-day execution across multiple client accounts.

This role is ideal for someone who wants to learn how an agency actually operates from content planning and client communication to campaign execution.

You will work closely with the founder and creative team to ensure everything runs smoothly behind the scenes.

Work Schedule

  • Monday to Friday, aligned with EST hours
  • Flexible internship hours (Part-Time → Full-Time transition based on performance)
  • Fully remote

Key ResponsibilitiesContent & Task Coordination

  • Manage and update content calendars across multiple clients
  • Assign tasks to designers, editors, and team members
  • Follow up on deliverables and ensure deadlines are met
  • Maintain clear visibility on project status across accounts

Creative Execution Support

  • Assist in planning posts, reels, and campaign ideas
  • Write basic captions, hooks, and content briefs
  • Coordinate shoot planning (references, shot lists, mood boards)
  • Ensure all content aligns with brand tone and direction

Client Communication Support

  • Take notes during client calls and summarize action items
  • Share updates, timelines, and follow-ups with clients
  • Manage feedback and coordinate revisions with the team
  • Help maintain smooth day-to-day client communication

Operations & Workflow Management

  • Maintain Trello boards and internal task trackers
  • Organize Google Drive folders, assets, and links
  • Ensure files and deliverables are properly labeled and stored
  • Help streamline internal workflows and processes

Trend Research & Growth Thinking

  • Stay updated with Instagram, TikTok, and social media trends
  • Bring new content ideas and references regularly
  • Understand what makes content perform not just look good

Requirements

  • Strong communication skills (written & verbal)
  • Highly organized and detail-oriented
  • Ability to manage multiple tasks and deadlines
  • Basic understanding of social media platforms (Instagram, TikTok)
  • Proactive mindset — takes initiative and ownership
  • Interest in marketing, branding, and content creation

Bonus Skills

  • Canva / Adobe basics (Photoshop, Premiere, etc.)
  • Experience managing or contributing to social media pages
  • Familiarity with Trello, Google Drive, or similar tools
  • Interest in hospitality, lifestyle, or restaurant brands

What You’ll Learn

  • Real agency workflows and systems
  • Client communication and account management
  • Content strategy and execution
  • Campaign planning and coordination
  • How brands grow on social media

Growth Opportunity

This role has strong potential to transition into:

👉 Full-Time Account Manager

👉 Creative Strategist

Compensation & Benefits

  • Internship stipend (based on experience)
  • Flexible remote work environment
  • Direct mentorship from the founder
  • Exposure to international clients and campaigns
  • Opportunity to grow within a scaling agency

What We’re Looking For

We’re not looking for someone who just completes tasks — we’re looking for someone who takes ownership, stays organized, and genuinely wants to learn how great work gets done.

If you’re proactive, detail-oriented, and excited to be part of a fast-paced creative environment, we’d love to hear from you.

How to Apply

To apply, please submit your application through:

👉 https://www.etherealpr.com/careers

Include:

  • Resume
  • Any relevant work / portfolio (if available)
  • A short note on why you’re interested in this role.
