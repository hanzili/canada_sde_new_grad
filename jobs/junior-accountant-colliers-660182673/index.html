About the role:

Are you a Junior Accountant seeking to advance your accounting capabilities and broaden your experience within a prominent publicly traded organization? Join us to further your skills and immerse yourself in the dynamic world of finance at our company.

In this role, you will:

  • You will be successfully supporting the accounting team in fulfilling their mandate to complete month, quarter and year end processes on time and accurately.
  • You'll be gaining new skills and experience through learning from your colleagues.
  • Your role will involve aiding in the preparation of working papers for accruals, allocations, journal entries, and account reconciliations across various business units.
  • Additionally, you'll compile data for financial analysis, handle data entry and invoice issuance, and contribute to monthly accounts receivable reporting.

What you bring:

  • 1+ year of experience in an accounting position is preferred.
  • CPA student currently in the program.
  • Degree in Accounting.
  • A self-motivated individual with customer service orientation and ‘can do’ attitude.
  • Excellent verbal and written communication skills.
