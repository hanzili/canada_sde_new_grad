About the Role

Capture and edit short-form Instagram videos

About LOLA DENIM

Local fashion brand; strong social media focus

Full Description

Lola Denim is looking for a Junior Content Creator (freelance) to join us in creating engaging, short-form video content for Instagram.

This role is ideal for someone who lives and breathes social , has a strong eye for fashion, and knows how to capture content that feels natural, current, and on-brand.

What You’ll Do

  • Capture and create short-form video content (Reels) for Instagram
  • Film behind-the-scenes moments , product features, and styling content
  • Edit quick, engaging videos aligned with current social trends
  • Work closely with our team on set to capture authentic, in-the-moment content
  • Bring a creative and intuitive approach , helping guide and shape content as it’s being filmed

What We’re Looking For

  • Strong understanding of Instagram and short-form video trends
  • A natural eye for composition, movement, and storytelling
  • Interest in fashion, denim, and brand storytelling
  • Someone proactive and intuitive — able to take direction but also lead creatively when needed
  • Ability to shoot and edit content efficiently

Details

  • Freelance / project-based
  • Montreal-based preferred
  • On-site shooting required (Montreal)

How to Apply

Send your portfolio, reels, or Instagram to: info@loladenim.com

Bonus if you already love denim and fashion content.
