Full Description

Sales Associate – Inside Sales (Detailing Leads)

Location: Regina, SK | Type: Full-time | Compensation: Hourly + commission opportunity

About the Role

We're hiring a closer. You'll work from your own office calling and texting a steady, essentially unlimited stream of warm leads for auto detailing jobs in Regina. No prospecting, no door-knocking, leads come to you. Your job is to dial, follow up, and book the work.

If you can hold a conversation, build trust quickly, and aren't afraid of a phone, you'll do well here.

What You'll Do

  • Call and text inbound leads to book detailing appointments
  • Follow up on warm leads until they close or clearly say no
  • Manage your pipeline and keep notes organized in our system
  • Handle quick questions about pricing, packages, and scheduling
  • Hit weekly booking targets

What We're Looking For

  • Comfortable on the phone — confident, friendly, not pushy
  • Strong closer instincts; you know when to push and when to back off
  • Organized enough to track dozens of conversations without dropping the ball
  • Reliable, self-directed, and able to manage your own day
  • Sales or customer service experience is a plus, not a must
  • Bilingual is a bonus, not required

What You Get

  • Your own private office — no shared desk, no open floor
  • Endless lead supply (you'll never run out of people to call)
  • Hourly base pay + commission opportunity (details discussed in interview)
  • Flexible hours within full-time — we care about results, not punching a clock
  • Clear path to grow earnings as you close more jobs

How to Apply

Send a short message about why you'd be a fit. No formal cover letter needed, just be real.

Pay: $15.35-$25.39 per hour

Benefits:

  • On-site parking

Work Location: In person
