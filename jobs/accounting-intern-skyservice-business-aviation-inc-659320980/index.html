Full Description

SKYSERVICE BUSINESS AVIATION

Skyservice is the North American market leader in business aviation. Built on the key principles of safety and service excellence, we offer full-service support and expertise in aircraft management, charter, maintenance, and sales from our fixed-based operator services across Canada and the United States. People and values are inseparable assets that fuel our commitment to Quality, Safety, Respect, Commitment, and Efficiency. These key values guide us every day and contribute to our reputation as industry leaders in aviation services. Skyservice is a team that is committed to supporting and developing our people. If you wish to advance your career and be part of team committed to reaching further and aiming higher in Business Aviation, the Skyservice family of companies is the team for you.

Get to know us: https://www.skyservice.com/

YOUR FUTURE AT SKYSERVICE:

Here at Skyservice, we are continuing to grow and provide high-level service to our clients, expanding our footprint and name throughout the Americas. You will bring strong attention to detail and a fast-paced way of working with dynamic schedules to a team that works together to exceed the expectations of our clients. We focus on excellence and are committed to support and develop our teams!

JOB PURPOSE:

We’re looking for a motivated and detail‑oriented Accounting Intern to join our team. This role is ideal for someone eager to gain hands‑on experience across core accounting functions, with a primary focus on Accounts Receivable (AR). You’ll support day‑to‑day AR operations and have opportunities to assist with other accounting tasks and month‑end process based on team needs.

SUMMARY OF RESPONSIBILITIES:

SUMMARY OF RESPONSIBILITIES: Accounts Receivable (Approx. 50%)

  • Customer management – create, update and maintain customer profiles in the accounting/operation system
  • Cash application – support cash application activities and ensure payments are recorded accurately
  • Documentation - organize and upload supporting documents as required
  • Billing - assist in invoicing, billing accuracy checks, and timely distribution
  • Collection - monitor outstanding receivables and support follow-ups on overdue accounts
  • AR Analysis - maintain AR aging reports and assist with weekly/monthly AR analysis

Other Accounting Functions (Responsibilities may vary depending on the team needs and your capability)

  • General administrative support - filing, documentation, and record maintenance
  • Accounts payable (AP) - process vendor invoices and ensure proper coding and approvals
  • Journal entries & general ledger - prepare basic journal entries under supervision; assist with account reconciliations and documentation
  • Month‑end closing - assist with accruals, reconciliations, and variance analysis
  • Ad-hoc projects - contribute to special projects and analysis as needed

QUALIFICATIONS AND SKILLS REQUIRED:

  • Currently pursuing a degree or diploma in Accounting, Finance, Business, or a related field
  • Strong attention to detail and organizational skills
  • Proficiency in Excel and comfort working with financial data
  • Ability to manage multiple tasks and meet deadlines
  • Strong communication skills and willingness to learn

What You’ll Gain

  • Hands‑on experience on AR, with exposure to other accounting functions across the Finance department
  • Mentorship from experienced finance professionals
  • Opportunities to contribute to meaningful projects that support business operations
