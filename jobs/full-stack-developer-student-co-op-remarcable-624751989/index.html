About the Role

Build features, fix bugs, participate in code reviews

About Remarcable

Series A SaaS startup in construction tech

Full Description

Remarcable is a Series A SaaS company transforming how electrical contractors and distributors work together. Our platform streamlines procurement and tool management, bringing clarity, efficiency, and modern technology to an industry that has been underserved for decades.

As a part of our Co-op program for Full Stack Developers, you will join a real engineering team where you will contribute to meaningful features, learn quickly, and see your work make an impact. You will collaborate with experienced engineers, solve real problems, and help shape a platform relied on by contractors and distributors across North America.

What You Will Do

  • Build and iterate on product features with guidance from senior engineers.
  • Diagnose and fix bugs that directly affect users.
  • Support the investigation of customer reported issues.
  • Participate in code reviews and grow through constructive feedback.
  • Work across the full stack including backend, frontend, APIs, and data.
  • Write clean, clear documentation that supports ongoing development.
  • Learn new tools, technologies, and engineering patterns with team mentorship.

What You Bring

  • Coursework or experience with Python (bonus if you have touched Django).
  • Exposure to web development and core full stack concepts.
  • Familiarity with REST APIs and basic web application architecture.
  • Understanding of SQL or relational database fundamentals.
  • Comfort using Git for version control.
  • A collaborative, positive mindset and eagerness to grow quickly.

Our Tech Stack

  • Backend: Python, Django, REST APIs, PostgreSQL
  • Frontend: AngularJS, JavaScript, HTML and CSS
  • Infrastructure and Tools: Docker, Git and GitHub, CI and CD pipelines, AWS services, internal developer tooling, logging, and monitoring systems

You are not expected to know all of this on day one. Your growth is part of the experience!

What You Will Gain

  • Ownership of features that ship to production.
  • Mentorship from experienced engineers invested in your growth.
  • Exposure to real world engineering workflows in a fast paced SaaS startup.
  • A stronger technical foundation and a portfolio of real, impactful work.
  • The opportunity to contribute to a product that solves meaningful problems for an underserved industry.

About Remarcable, Inc. Remarcable is a Series A–funded SaaS company revolutionizing how contractors work. Founded by an engineer to solve the inefficiencies he faced in the field; our platform eliminates procurement and operational friction for the construction industry—especially electrical contractors.

Our award-winning, SOC 2 Type II–certified B2B marketplace has processed billions in material purchases, connecting contractors, suppliers, and accounting systems in one seamless workflow. From procurement to AP automation, we deliver real-time visibility and control in a market long overdue for innovation.

Backed by Insight Partners, we’re scaling fast—fueling product innovation, deep ERP integrations, and rapid go-to-market growth. Join our builder-led, high-ownership team and make a real impact in an underserved, multi-billion-dollar industry.

Why Join Us? At Remarcable, you’ll work on a complex, high-value product that’s solving critical pain points for contractors across North America. You’ll be part of a small, talented team shaping the future of construction technology—operating in a vertical SaaS + marketplace model with ERP-style sales motion, sticky customer relationships, and the potential to reshape an industry.

We offer full benefits including Medical, Dental, Vision, STD and Life insurance (100% Company-paid for the Employee). We also offer a 401(k) with company match and bonus potential.

Our Mission To advance the adoption of technology in the construction industry and bring better efficiency, visibility, and communication to our customers.

  • Onsite at 333 Seymour Street, Vancouver, BC
