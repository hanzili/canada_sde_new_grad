Job Description:

DEPARTMENT OVERVIEW

TD Securities (TDS) Finance is responsible for developing and delivering financial strategy, information, and

management support on a segment-wide basis for TDS, consistent with TD's enterprise risk appetite.

The TD Securities Capital Reporting Group, within TDS Finance, is responsible for Wholesale Banking regulatory

reporting under the Basel III framework for OSFI reporting, financial statement disclosures, management reporting,

forecasting, and allocation of capital for business line performance measurement, in particular return on equity.

Additionally, the group leads key initiatives relating to capital optimization, capital usage, as well as streamlining of

processes and improvements in infrastructure in conjunction with diverse business partners, including partners in

accounting, chief’s financial and regulatory reporting, data governance, front office, risk management, technology,

and other groups in TD. The Capital Reporting Group's activities are pivotal as the group processes a vast quantity of

data and the information output from such data is used for regulatory reporting and relied upon by numerous

stakeholders in the bank.

The team plays an important strategic role for TDS, ensuring that the right metrics are developed and communicated

to the businesses to make decisions amid the continuously evolving market environment and regulatory

requirements.

Co-op and Internship opportunities allow you to gain valuable work experience across several of the businesses at TD. You will work with experienced colleagues, receive world class training, and be part of a community of students across TD, where you will have an impact, grow as individual and experience our culture of care.

Our Co-op/Intern Programming is offered with select Co-op and Internship roles and is designed to help you understand the TD business, build on critical career capabilities, and broaden your professional network. This program is designed to complement your on-the job experience and features:

  • Leadership talks with key Leaders from across the organization
  • Lunch and learns on topics such as Innovation
  • Diversity and Inclusion and Personal Branding and so much more

JOB DESCRIPTION

  • Participate in initiatives to identify and implement process improvements and capital optimization strategies.
  • Contribute to the maintenance and improvement of various technological tools (ie. Alteryx, SQL, Access databases, Excel files, Tableau)
  • Build and foster a positive working relationship with business partners and capital stakeholders.
  • Contributes to a positive work environment that encourages learning, productivity, professionalism and teamwork.
  • Report capital results to stakeholders. This includes preparing a variety of reports, schedules, variance analyses, and commentaries catered to the intended audience/recipient.
  • Synthesizing and interpreting large quantities of data from diverse sources, and processing data into accurate, complete, and meaningful information for reporting purposes and/or to inform key business stakeholders.
  • Grasping complex regulatory standards and correctly applying them across the range of TD Securities' financial assets in calculating capital.

JOB REQUIREMENTS

We’re proud to work with a group of diverse colleagues. If you have relevant experience that isn’t mentioned

below, tell us about it in your resume or cover letter.

  • Currently enrolled in third or fourth year of an undergraduate degree in Finance, Accounting, Commerce, Risk or related field; with the intent of going back to school at the start of your work term (requirement of all co-op/intern position)
  • Using computer tools for data retrieval and analysis, in particular Excel, SQL and PowerPoint
  • Record of achievement, high academic results and demonstrated leadership with community involvement
  • Quick to learn and apply new concepts
  • Ability to take initiative and balance multiple tasks
  • Demonstrated strong analytical abilities
  • Excellent interpersonal skills
  • Good verbal and written communication skills
