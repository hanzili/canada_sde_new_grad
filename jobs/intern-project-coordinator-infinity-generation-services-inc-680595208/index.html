Full Description

About the Opportunity

Infinity Generation Services Inc. is seeking a Project Coordinator Co‑op Student to join our team for the summer term. This role is ideal for a motivated student looking to gain hands‑on experience in project coordination and construction/project management while working in a supportive, professional environment.

Reporting to the Project Manager/Estimator, the Co‑op Student will assist with day‑to‑day project coordination tasks and gain exposure to real‑world projects while developing practical skills.

Key Responsibilities

With guidance and mentorship, the Co‑op Student will:

  • Support project managers with coordination and administrative tasks
  • Assist with project schedules, meeting agendas, and meeting minutes
  • Organize and maintain project documentation and files

  • Track project progress, deliverables, and action items

  • Support preparation of client‑ready documents and correspondence, under supervision

  • Assist with tracking project changes, change notices, and supporting documentation

  • Exposure to basic project compliance and reporting requirements through guided support tasks

  • Support estimating activities by organizing quotations and project data

  • Participate in project meetings as a learning opportunity
  • Assist with purchase order setup and general project administration
  • Help prepare internal progress summaries and reports
  • Shadow team members to learn about project planning, execution, and project close‑out documentation
  • Occasionally attend site visits or client meetings under supervision
  • Support process improvements by leveraging AI and automation technologies

What You'll Gain

  • Hands‑on experience supporting construction and engineering projects

  • An understanding of the project lifecycle and coordination processes

  • Exposure to scheduling, cost tracking, reporting, and estimating
  • Experience professional communication and teamwork experience

Education Requirements

Currently enrolled in a degree or diploma program in Engineering (Mechanical, Electrical, or related), Construction or Project Management, or a related field. Open to co‑op or internship students.

Experience & Skills

  • Previous skilled trade or construction related experience is an asset
  • Interest in learning about Indigenous relations and community engagement/participation is an asset
  • Strong organizational and communication skills
  • Basic proficiency in Microsoft Office (Word, Excel, Outlook)
  • Familiarity with project management software (e.g., MS Project or Primavera) is an asset or willingness to learn
  • Exposure to AI or automation technologies in supporting workflow or process improvements is an asset
  • Positive attitude and eagerness to learn
  • Proven ability to work effectively both independently and in a team based environment
  • Ability to obtain a Nuclear Security Clearance

Position Details

  • Term: Summer co‑op
  • Hours: Full‑time (approximately 40 hours per week)
  • Location: Bowmanville
  • Compensation: Paid co‑op position, $20-$25/hr based on education and experience
  • Future Opportunities: Potential for future employment for the right candidate

possibility to transition into a full time position for the right candidate.
