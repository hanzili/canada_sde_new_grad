About Brookfield

Full Description

Location Toronto - 181 Bay Street, Suite IN200

Brookfield Culture Brookfield has a unique and dynamic culture. We seek team members who have a long-term focus and whose values align with our Attributes of a Brookfield Leader: Entrepreneurial, Collaborative and Disciplined. Brookfield is committed to the development of our people through challenging work assignments and exposure to diverse businesses.

Job Description Halmont Properties Corporation Overview Halmont Properties Corporation is a diversified real estate investment company with a portfolio spanning institutional and commercial real estate, forestry investments, and financial holdings. This is a full-time position located in downtown Toronto.

We are seeking a highly organized and self-motivated professional to join our private family office team in a hands-on accounting and operations role. The ideal candidate will be comfortable taking ownership of a diverse set of responsibilities.

This role provides broad financial exposure, including public company reporting, corporate administration, and monitoring investment relationships within a small collaborative office environment.

Key Responsibilities Accounting & Financial Reporting

  • Maintaining accounting records across multiple asset classes, including holdings in public and privately held companies.
  • Supporting public company reporting processes and coordinating with external auditors.
  • Preparing tax schedules and supporting documentation for external advisors.

Corporate & Operational Support

  • Assisting with corporate administrative matters including banking relationships, maintenance of corporate records, and coordination of securities transfers.
  • Supporting the day-to-day operations of a small professional office.
  • Facilitating other operational and administrative requirements.

Qualifications & Attributes

  • Experience with accounting and financial statement preparation, holding or committed to pursuing CPA status.
  • Comfortable operating within a small, hands-on team environment.
  • Real estate, investment, and asset management accounting experience is considered a strong asset.
  • Organized and self-motivated with the ability to work independently and manage competing priorities.
  • High level of professionalism, with the ability to manage sensitive financial and corporate information with discretion.

Why Join Halmont

  • Ability to continuously learn and expand investment knowledge.
  • Meaningful responsibilities within a, high-performing team.
  • Exposure to a diverse and sophisticated real estate and investment platform.
  • Opportunity to work directly with senior leadership and external professional advisors.

Salary Range: $65,000 - $75,000 CAD

Our compensation structure is comprised of a base salary and a short-term incentive program (cash bonus). Cash compensation tends to vary based on geography to account for local market conditions and is set to be market competitive. Compensation decisions are based on a number of factors including relative experience, overall years of experience, industry experience, education and designations.
