Description

Build the future with us
Are you passionate about actuarial science and eager to contribute to the purpose of a company whose mission is to help clients feel confident and secure about their future? As an Intern/Student – Actuarial, you will play a key role in analysis, problem-solving, and supporting teams to meet business needs in the insurance sector.

This position represents a great opportunity to showcase your skills and fully leverage your potential within a caring and trustworthy organization. Here, people and their development are at the heart of our priorities, fostering an environment that encourages collaboration and innovation.

What you will accomplish with us
As an Intern/Student – Actuarial, you will be at the core of our mission. Your main responsibilities will include:

  • Participating in analytical projects to develop a practical understanding of actuarial concepts and the insurance industry.
  • Contributing to the development and enhancement of simple analytical tools to support the resolution of business issues.
  • Collaborating with various teams (actuarial, information technology, finance, marketing) on projects and initiatives.
  • Analyzing data and contributing to the production of reports or dashboards to support decision-making.
  • Supporting teams with quantitative analyses using basic statistical methods.
  • Participating in day-to-day actuarial activities and providing support for recurring analyses.
  • Assisting in the preparation of documentation and responding to data requests from internal teams.
  • Working closely with colleagues, mentors, and professionals to develop your skills and gain exposure to the insurance industry.

What could accelerate your success in this role
We are looking for someone who:

  • Is currently enrolled in a bachelor’s degree in actuarial science or another relevant field, and/or is required to complete a credited internship in Fall 2026 as part of their academic program.
  • Has relevant experience in quantitative analysis, acquired through academic projects or internships.
  • Is recognized for their rigor, attention to detail, and adherence to established procedures.
  • Stands out for strong analytical and complex problem-solving skills.
  • Demonstrates strong proficiency with analytical tools and large datasets, including Excel, Access, Python, and R (GLM, tidyverse).
  • Communicates clearly and effectively, both orally and in writing, in French.
  • Has an intermediate level of English, as the role involves collaborating with unilingual English-speaking internal partners and regularly consulting technical documentation.

Why you will enjoy working with us

  • A work environment where learning and development are combined with a collective pursuit of excellence;
  • A healthy, safe, fair, and inclusive environment where potential can be freely expressed and developed;
  • The opportunity to work in a hybrid environment, supported by flexibility and access to inspiring and innovative workspaces.

and take the lead in your career, where your talent truly matters!
Still hesitating? At iA, we believe in potential and value diverse experiences. If this role inspires you, take the leap and apply — your place may be with us, and we want to get to know you!

Following your internship/employment, exciting opportunities for permanent roles may become available to continue your career journey with us.
