About the Role

Create and modify SOLIDWORKS parts, assemblies, drawings

About JJ Home Products - Division of Renin Canada Corp.

Division of Renin Canada Corp; hardware design

Full Description

Duties

  • Create and modify 3D parts and assemblies using SOLIDWORKS (3DEXPERIENCE)
  • Support prototype builds and design iterations
  • Produce and update 2D manufacturing drawings
  • Apply basic GD&T, dimensions, and tolerances under engineering guidance
  • Maintain models, revisions, and configurations within the platform
  • Assist with BOM creation, updates, and data cleanup
  • Ensure models and drawings follow internal CAD standards and naming conventions
  • Organize and manage design files to ensure data accuracy and traceability

Qualifications

  • Diploma/certificate in Mechanical Engineering Technology, CAD Design, or related field
  • Strong hands-on SOLIDWORKS experience (parts, assemblies, drawings)
  • Ability to efficiently complete CAD tasks with minimal supervision
  • Familiarity with CAD data management (revision control, lifecycle states)
  • Basic understanding of mechanical drawings and tolerancing
  • High attention to detail and ability to follow structured processes

Preferred Qualifications

  • Experience with SOLIDWORKS on the 3DEXPERIENCE platform
  • Exposure to product development or manufacturing environments
  • Basic knowledge of manufacturing processes (machining, sheet metal, plastics)
  • Exposure to architectural products, sliding systems, or building hardware (asset)

Soft Skills & Attributes

  • Willingness to learn and take technical direction
  • Strong organizational and time‑management skills
  • Clear communication and a collaborative mindset
  • Ability to manage multiple small tasks and revisions efficiently

  • On-site parking
  • Work from home
