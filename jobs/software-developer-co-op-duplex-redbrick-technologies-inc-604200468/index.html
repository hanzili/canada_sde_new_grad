About the Role

About Redbrick Technologies Inc.

B Corp Certified, people-first culture, growth focus

Full Description

Part of the Redbrick portfolio of companies, Duplex provides white-label technology and services for syndicated content discovery. Through customizable, end-to-end solutions, Duplex powers personalized news and advertising feeds across browsers, ISPs, and innovative digital service providers. By combining flexible technology with decades of experience in search interactions, Duplex is revolutionizing the future of digital content consumption. Redbrick is proud to be B Corp Certified and has been named one of Canada’s Top Small and Medium Employers for five years running.

One thing that our team members say they value most is our fun and truly “people-first” culture. Ours is a place where you’re given the autonomy to do your best work, from home or in the office, with the tools and support you need to do so. We cheerlead your goals and accomplishments and are invested in your future.

“Redbrick is flexible, offers great work/life balance, and cares about growth—both personal and professional.”

–Amee, Creative Director at Redbrick

As a full-stack Software Developer Co-op at Duplex, you’ll be responsible for:

  • Fixing bugs and implementing new features.
  • Reviewing code written by other developers on the team.
  • Monitor the deployments of your changes to the production environment.
  • Testing changes to our software to ensure a high level of quality.

Halfway through your co-op:

  • You have made several code changes and they have been deployed into production..
  • You know our product well and can answer common technical questions about it.
  • You can test most changes and be confident you’ve identified any problems.
  • Your work-term project is well underway.

At the end of your co-op:

  • You are a fully contributing member of the development team.
  • You know our product inside and out and are comfortable with the codebase.
  • Your work-term project is complete.

What we would like to see in you

We get it — you want to know exactly what you’re signing up for before hitting ‘apply.’ As a full-stack Software Developer, you’ll be responsible for:

  • Experience as a front-end or full-stack developer.
  • Developing knowledge of JavaScript, TypeScript and/or Python.
  • Exposure to JS frameworks such as React, Angular and Vue.js.
  • Knowledge of HTML, CSS, and web technology.
  • Passion for writing maintainable quality code.
  • A positive attitude and excellent communication skills.
  • Experience working on an Agile development team.
  • Comfortable with version control systems and code review.
  • Samples of demonstrating clean, well-commented, well-executed code.

Please note: This is an 8-month co-op term.
