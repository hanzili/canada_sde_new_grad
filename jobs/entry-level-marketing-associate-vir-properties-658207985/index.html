Entry-Level Marketing Associate

at VIR Properties

📍 Location Toronto, Ontario
📋 Details Full-time · Permanent · Competitive pay · N/A

About the Role

Execute marketing campaigns, engage customers, track performance

About VIR Properties

Growth-focused marketing team with mentorship and development

Full Description

We are looking for an enthusiastic and motivated Entry-Level Marketing Associate to join our growing marketing team. This is an exciting opportunity for someone looking to start their career in marketing and gain hands-on experience working on brand campaigns, customer engagement, and promotional activities.

You will support the delivery of marketing initiatives while developing key skills in communication, brand representation, and campaign execution.

Key Responsibilities

  • Assist in the execution of marketing campaigns and brand promotions
  • Engage with customers and represent client brands professionally
  • Support the team with campaign planning and coordination
  • Contribute ideas for creative marketing initiatives
  • Help track campaign performance and gather customer feedback
  • Participate in events, activations, and promotional activities
  • Support day-to-day marketing and administrative tasks

What We’re Looking For

  • A positive attitude and strong willingness to learn
  • Excellent communication and interpersonal skills
  • Outgoing and confident personality
  • Ability to work in a fast-paced, team-oriented environment
  • Strong organisational skills and attention to detail
  • Interest in marketing, branding, or customer engagement

What We Offer

  • Full training and ongoing mentorship
  • A supportive and energetic team culture
  • Clear progression and career development opportunities
  • Hands-on experience across multiple marketing campaigns
  • A fast-paced environment where your ideas are valued

Ideal Candidate This Role Is Perfect For Someone Who Is

  • Starting their career in marketing or business
  • Motivated, ambitious, and eager to grow
  • Comfortable working with people and representing brands
  • Looking for long-term career development opportunities
