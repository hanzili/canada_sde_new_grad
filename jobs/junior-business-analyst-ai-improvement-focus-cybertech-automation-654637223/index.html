Full Description

Junior Business Analyst: AI Improvement Focus (Co-op Student)

Location: Edmonton, AB

Cybertech is looking for an ambitious Junior Analyst to help us bridge the gap between traditional operations and modern AI. This is a unique opportunity for a student who wants to learn how a business works, document real-world workflows, and turn AI experiments into practical recommendations.

Why Cybertech?

We pride ourselves on providing outstanding career opportunities in a friendly, informal environment. Our projects span critical industries: oil and gas, manufacturing, pipelines, and municipal utilities.

The Opportunity

As our AI-focused Business Analyst, you will not just be doing research: you will be turning observation into action. You will map how our teams operate today and identify where automation or AI tools could save time and improve quality.

What You Will Do:

  • Process Mapping: Meet with team members to document current workflows, handoffs, and repetitive "pain points."
  • AI Piloting: Run small proof-of-concept tests using tools like Microsoft Copilot, ChatGPT, Claude, and Power Automate.
  • Documentation: Create SOPs, user guides, and clear process maps for newly optimized workflows.
  • Strategy: Prepare data-backed recommendations for leadership on where to leverage AI to improve our teams’ efficiency and quality.
  • Continuous Learning: Stay current on the latest AI platforms and how they apply to the industrial automation sector.

Who You Are

You are a curious student who enjoys organizing messy information and thinking critically about technology.

Requirements:

  • Enrollment: Currently in a co-op or internship program at a recognized post-secondary institution.
  • Academic Focus: Studying Business, Computer Science, Business Technology Management (BTM), or Information Systems.
  • Communication: Strong at listening, asking thoughtful questions, and writing clear summaries.
  • Mindset: Detail-oriented, adaptable, and comfortable working independently.

Bonus Skills (Not Required):

  • Direct experience with AI prompting or workflow automation (Power Automate).
  • Experience creating dashboards, reports, or process maps.
  • A basic technical comfort with APIs or data tools.

About Cybertech

Cybertech Automation is a full-service systems integration company located in Edmonton, Canada. Since 1994, Cybertech has offered expertise in systems integration, automation, instrumentation and electrical design/engineering. The combination of these various disciplines makes Cybertech Automation a one-stop control system service provider.

Our company hallmark is a strong team environment, and our engineers and technologists represent decades of combined industrial automation experience. The quality of our work has grown our client list to over 200 industry-leading companies, many of them long-term, repeat customers. Cybertech Automation has applied its knowledge and experience to the design and configuration of countless systems for all industry types.

The Perks

Through the development of numerous tangible and intangible benefits, Cybertech continually strives to be one of the best companies to work for in Canada.

Primary company benefits include:

  • Open, inclusive and friendly workplace
  • Flexible work schedule options
  • Standard work schedule includes every Friday afternoon off
  • Overtime compensation
  • Cell phone subsidy
  • Physical fitness subsidy
  • Numerous teambuilding events throughout the year
