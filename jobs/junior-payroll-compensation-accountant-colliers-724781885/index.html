About the role:

Are you a Junior Accountant ready to build your expertise in compensation and payroll within a dynamic, large-scale organization? Join our team to gain hands-on experience in accounting for payroll, bonuses, and accruals while strengthening your skills in a collaborative, growth-focused environment.

In this role, you will:

  • Support the team in delivering accurate and timely month ‑ end, quarter ‑ end, and year ‑ end close processes
  • Prepare and post compensation ‑ related journal entries, including payroll adjustments, bonus reclassifications, accrual entries and ad-hoc records
  • Perform balance sheet reconciliations for compensation-related accounts (e.g., payroll, wages, accruals, benefits)
  • Assist in the preparation of working papers, accrual calculations, and supporting schedules
  • Contribute to variance analysis by identifying and explaining changes in compensation expenses
  • Support internal stakeholders (e.g., Payroll, HR, FP&A) with inquiries related to compensation accounting
  • Assist with audit support and documentation, ensuring clear and traceable records
  • Participate in process improvements, documentation updates, and team initiatives
  • Handle confidential compensation and payroll data with a high level of accuracy, care, and discretion Provides support on ad hoc projects

What you bring:

  • 2–4 years of experience in accounting or finance (preferably with exposure to month ‑ end, accruals, and/or payroll-related activities)
  • Degree in Accounting or a related field
  • Workday and Payroll experience is an asset
  • Strong aptitude for improving and streamlining processes, with the ability to quickly learn new applications, procedures, and ways of working.
  • Ability to handle sensitive and confidential data with accuracy, discretion, and accountability
  • Ability to meet deadlines while maintaining a high standard of quality and attention to detail.
  • A collaborative and team-oriented mindset, with the ability to work effectively across Accounting, Payroll, and Finance teams
  • Eagerness to learn, take initiative, and grow into broader responsibilities over time
  • Ability to work independently and with minimal supervision
  • Ability to communicate clearly and professionally in writing and verbally
  • Proficiency in Excel and Microsoft Office
  • Experience with Microsoft Dynamics AX or similar ERP systems is an asset
