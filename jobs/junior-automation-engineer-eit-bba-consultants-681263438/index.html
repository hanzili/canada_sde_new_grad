About the Role

Program PLC/SCADA; support site projects.

About BBA Consultants

Established Canadian engineering consultancy with national reach.

Full Description

Type of position: Regular

Your future role on our team Are you passionate about automation and the fast-paced world of work sites and industrial plants? Do you want to see the direct impact your work has in the field while working closely with clients? If so, we’ve got an exciting opportunity for you!

As a CEP in Automation, you'll be joining the Process Control and Operational Technologies department at our Québec City office. This multidisciplinary team will support your career growth, whether you choose to specialize in a niche area or develop a broader, generalist profile. You'll work with a motivated, dynamic group, ready to coach you on projects and guide you throughout your professional journey.

With us, you’ll get the opportunity to...

  • Program automated systems (PLC, SCADA, DCS, operator interfaces)
  • Develop and maintain solid client relationships
  • Participate in executing detailed engineering projects
  • Draft technical documents, calculation notes and study reports
  • Participate in project management (execution, costs, schedules)
  • Attend internal or client coordination meetings
  • Perform site surveys
  • Assist with equipment commissioning, both onsite and remotely

Do your qualities and values match our corporate culture?

  • Results-driven
  • Willingness to work together while having fun
  • Rigour and ethics
  • Interest in solving highly complex challenges
  • Autonomy and adaptability

Summary Of Certifications And Job Requirements

  • Bachelor’s degree in electrical or automated production engineering or any other equivalent training
  • Member of a Canadian professional engineering association
  • Available for short- and long-term work assignments at client locations or sites
  • Valid driver’s licence
  • Advanced English skills to facilitate collaboration with other BBA offices and/or clients in English-speaking Canadian provinces and internationally; you may need to use English occasionally or daily, depending on current projects
  • Advanced French skills to facilitate collaboration with other BBA offices and/or clients in Québec
  • Asset: interest or relevant experience in automation and instrumentation; participation in detailed engineering of a project in the mining or energy sectors
  • Asset: experience in the mining sector
  • Asset: ASP Construction card

An Overview Of BBA’s Total Rewards

  • Annual base salary
  • Annual premium program for all regular employees
  • Onsite mobility premium
  • Access to a time bank
  • Cellphone program
  • Group insurance plan that starts on day 1, including short-term and long-term disability insurance and a telemedicine program
  • Retirement savings plan
  • Annual vacation and sick leave
  • A premium through the Employee Referral program

BBA considers many factors when determining the annual base salary, including market conditions, education, verified and relevant work experience, technical assessments, qualifications, certifications and other key skills and experience.

At BBA, You Get Many Benefits

  • A collaborative team that shares ideas and knowledge
  • Access to many experts and coaching
  • Growing business with many opportunities
  • On site and at the office: Health, safety and the environment are a priority
  • Involvement in innovation projects

About BBA BBA is one of Canada’s leading private consulting engineering firms, with over 45 years of experience serving the energy and natural resources industry.

Our people are the foundation of our success. Their passion and excellence have earned us recognition as one of Canada’s Best Employers and Best Managed Companies , and we’re committed to fostering a workplace where everyone feels empowered to grow, lead and be themselves.

Our teams bring together engineering, environmental and commissioning expertise to deliver practical, innovative and sustainable solutions—from strategy to execution. With 20 offices across Canada, the U.S. and Latin America, we combine local presence with international reach, offering clients close collaboration and field-ready support.

Learn more about our culture and projects on LinkedIn or connect with our talent team.
