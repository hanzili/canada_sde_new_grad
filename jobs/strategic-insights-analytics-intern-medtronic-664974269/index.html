About Medtronic

Global healthcare tech leader improving patient access.

Full Description

At Medtronic you can begin a life-long career of exploration and innovation, while helping champion healthcare access and equity for all. You’ll lead with purpose, breaking down barriers to innovation in a more connected, compassionate world.

A Day in the Life

The Commercial Strategy & Market Access function is managing an increasing volume of data driven initiatives across business development, strategic accounts, market access, and remote sales.

There is a critical need to centralize, automate, and visualize performance metrics to enable timely decision making and track progress against organizational goals.

This role will provide dedicated analytical capacity to accelerate priority projects that are currently constrained by bandwidth, particularly in analytics, dashboard development, and performance tracking.

The position offers a mutually beneficial opportunity: delivering tangible business value while giving the student hands on exposure to real world commercial strategy, analytics, and stakeholder engagement.

Key Projects / Areas of Responsibility

  • Commercial Performance Dashboards
  • Design and build executive ready dashboards to track KPIs across:

o Strategic Accounts

o Market Access initiatives

o Business Development pipelines

o Remote Sales performance

Responsibilities may include the following and other duties may be assigned.

  • This is a temporary position.
  • Responsible for performing and supporting research for teams operating within a service group, product group and/ or project group.
  • Assists with the development, monitoring, coordination and implementation of non-technical projects as assigned.
  • Provides analysis, reporting and internal communication services with team members.
  • Develops knowledge of industry and organizational processes.
