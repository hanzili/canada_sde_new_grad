Receptionist/Junior Bookkeeper — Job Description

Position Information

Department: Accounting / Administration

Reports To: Senior Bookkeeper

Location: Woodstock, Ontario

Employment Type: Full-Time

Position Summary

The Junior Bookkeeper / Receptionist supports accounting operations while serving as front-desk reception for H5. This dual-role position combines customer-facing administrative responsibilities with entry-level bookkeeping tasks performed under supervision, while progressing through a structured professional development pathway.

Key Responsibilities — Reception

· Greet visitors and manage front desk operations

· Answer and route incoming calls

· Manage mail, deliveries, and front-office organization

· Provide administrative support to sales and service teams

Key Responsibilities — Bookkeeping

· Enter vendor invoices and code transactions

· Record sales receipts and customer payments

· Assist with accounts payable processing

· Maintain organized digital and physical records

· Escalate unclear transactions for review

· Assist with reconciliations and month-end preparation

4-Month Internal Audit Participation

· Prepare organized documentation for review periods

· Conduct self-review of entries and identify errors

· Assist with audit corrections and reconciliations

· Track performance improvement goals

Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

· Accurate daily transaction entry

· Complete and organized document management

· Timely task completion

· Proper escalation of coding uncertainties

· Professional front-desk coverage during business hours

Qualifications

· High school diploma required

· Post-secondary business courses are an asset

· Strong organizational and communication skills

· Basic computer and office software proficiency

· Interest in accounting and professional development

Work Environment

· Combination front-office and administrative setting

· Frequent customer interaction

· Multi-tasking between reception and accounting duties

Operating System & Structure

· Role functions within EOS accountability framework

· Lightspeed ERP

· Structured supervision and scheduled performance reviews

About the Role

As a Receptionist/Junior Bookkeeper at H5, you will be the key connection between our customers and our entire team. Your goal is to make everyone feel welcome!

We aim to be the best in the industry — the kind of place customers recommend to their friends and family every time.

You’ll be a great fit if you are:

· Friendly, confident, and customer-focused

· Organized and detail oriented

· Comfortable in a busy retail/service environment

· A clear communicator who follows through

· A team player who works well with the entire staff

· Someone who takes pride in being reliable and consistent
