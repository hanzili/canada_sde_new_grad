About the Role

About Robocart.ai Inc

AI-focused startup building enterprise automation solutions

Full Description

Our Cognitive Innovation Lab in Mississauga develops and deploys applied AI, automation agents, and quantum optimization frameworks designed to enhance real-world enterprise systems.

We are a team of innovators committed to building scalable cognitive technologies that redefine enterprise performance through AI and Quantum convergence.

About the Role

We are seeking a part-time onsite intern to join our Cognitive Innovation Lab in Mississauga.

You will work directly with our Founder & CTO on developing AI agents, LLM-based workflows, automation systems, and enterprise intelligence tools. The role also includes building and managing AI-driven CRM campaigns and LinkedIn outreach automation using large language models and workflow orchestration tools.

The position combines advanced AI development with enterprise demand generation activities, where AI agents and automation workflows are used to identify, engage, and communicate with organizations that may benefit from our enterprise technology solutions.

This is a contract-to-hire opportunity with potential for increased hours or a full-time role based on performance and results.

The ideal candidate is disciplined, hardworking, and capable of operating in a fast-paced innovation environment. Strong communication, presentation, and analytical skills are essential.

Key Responsibilities

Design, test, and deploy AI and automation workflows using LangChain, LangGraph, OpenAI APIs, and n8n

Integrate AI and LLM components into existing PHP (Laravel) enterprise systems

Develop and fine-tune LLMs (e.g., Llama, Mistral, GPT models) for enterprise use cases

Build and manage AI-driven CRM workflows and automated outreach systems

Develop automation agents that interact with LinkedIn and other platforms using LLM-based workflows

Support enterprise demand generation activities by identifying organizations that may benefit from SAP-related services and platforms

Manage large data sets of potential enterprise clients and maintain structured engagement records

Support marketing and outreach activities that convert research contacts into marketing-qualified leads (MQL) and sales-qualified leads (SQL) through AI-driven automation

Assist in preparing presentations, demonstrations, and communications for potential enterprise clients

Manage and support multiple company websites and digital platforms used for product information and outreach

Participate in quantum optimization experiments (QUBO/QAOA) for resource modeling and advanced research initiatives

Prepare technical documentation, prototype summaries, and internal research reports

Support rapid development and testing cycles under tight timelines

Required Skills

Proficient in Python, PHP (Laravel), JavaScript, and REST APIs

Understanding of LLMs, Transformers, RAG pipelines, and prompt engineering

Experience or familiarity with LangChain, LangGraph, OpenAI APIs, or AI orchestration tools

Strong foundation in data handling (Pandas, NumPy, JSON) and version control (Git)

Experience working with automation tools, CRM systems, or campaign management tools

Strong communication and presentation skills

Ability to manage large data sets and maintain structured records

Excellent time management, analytical ability, and attention to detail

Ability to work onsite in Mississauga and manage multiple priorities in a fast-paced environment
