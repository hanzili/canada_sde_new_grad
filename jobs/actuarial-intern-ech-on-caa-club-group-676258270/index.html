Position Details

What You Will Do

  • Possess experience in the Insurance field to provide trend analysis, forecastsreports and findings that anticipate outcome
  • Ability to utilize EDW data / GW data, industry information and trend analysis to provide managementrecommendations
  • Provide detailed analysis of statistics that are outside of anticipated norms
  • Create trend analysis and forecast reports to provide business with anticipated results
  • Provide Manager and Director level with month over month forecasting both in Sales and Claims data
  • Create Interactive reports using data contained in EDW

Please note, salary range is not inclusive of total compensation. Who You Are

  • University Degree in Economics or Actuarial Science
  • 3-5 years experience in a similar role
  • Forecast reporting, data collection, data analysis, report generation and qualityassurance experience is required
  • Power BI and Python experiences are required
  • Database administration experience is preferred
  • Knowledge of the Insurance industry an asset
  • Professional designation preferred but not required: CIP/FCIP, CA. CMA. CPA, CGA orequivalent
  • Working experience with MS Offi ce products, SQL or database query tools including VBM andVBA is an asset
  • Excellent interpersonal, verbal and written communication skills
  • Excellent problem solver, detail oriented, ability to work in a team environment as well aswork independently
  • Strong analytical and decision-making skills
