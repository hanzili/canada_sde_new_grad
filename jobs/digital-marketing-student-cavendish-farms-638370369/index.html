Marketing / Digital Marketing Internship

Digital Marketing Student

at Cavendish Farms

📍 Location Dieppe, NB
📋 Details Internship · Summer
📅 Posted Mar 05, 2026

About the Role

Coordinate marketing projects; support website development and assets

About Cavendish Farms

Leading Canadian frozen foods producer; diverse brands

Full Description

Digital Marketing is more than ads and social posts- it is about how content, data, and technology work together to create great customer experiences. As our Digital Marketing Summer Student , you’ll work closely with our Digital Marketing, Brand, and Product Marketing teams on meaningful projects that shape how our marketing platforms and website operate every day.

This role is ideal for a marketing student who enjoys problem-solving, organization, and understanding how digital experiences are built and improved. You’ll gain hands-on experience with enterprise marketing platforms, collaborate with business partners across the organization, and see how marketing strategy turns into execution.

  • Working on the New Website development- By coordinating action items between marketing, IT and external partners.
  • Helping in improving how marketing assets (images, videos, documents) are organized and used.
  • Work on improving how marketing work gets planned and executed by supporting project coordination for marketing workflows.
  • Support ongoing improvements to our marketing product content platforms by assisting with the build and launch of new Brand stores.

What You’ll Learn

  • How enterprise websites and marketing platforms are built, launched, and maintained
  • How content strategy, technology, and process come together in modern marketing
  • How to work with cross‑functional teams, stakeholders, and real business requirements
  • How data and dashboards are used to improve marketing effectiveness

  • Practical experience that builds a strong foundation for a career in marketing operations, digital marketing, or marketing technology

  • Currently enrolled in a post‑secondary program in Marketing or a related field

  • Strong organizational skills with the ability to manage multiple tasks and deadlines
  • Analytical mindset with an interest in data, structure, and process improvement
  • Clear communicator who is comfortable working with different teams
  • Able to work independently while also being a strong team player
  • Working knowledge of Microsoft Office (Excel, PowerPoint, Word)

What Sets You Apart - Interest in websites, content management, or marketing technology - Curiosity about how digital experiences are designed and improved - Desire to learn tools that are widely used in professional marketing environments

Cavendish Farms is a leading producer of frozen food products for retail and foodservice markets throughout Canada, the United States, and around the world. We have two state-of-the-art potato processing plants in PEI, one in Lethbridge, AB and another in Jamestown, ND. Headquartered in Dieppe, NB, the company also operates an appetizer plant in Wheatley, ON and has sales office locations in Toronto and Boston.

Founded in 1882, J.D. Irving, Limited (JDI) has operations in Canada and the United States and are a team of 20,000 dedicated employees. At J.D. Irving, Limited, you are part of a dynamic team of professionals who lead with integrity and are passionate about delivering results. With a culture that nurtures talent and creates an inclusive workplace, you can grow your career across our various operating divisions.
