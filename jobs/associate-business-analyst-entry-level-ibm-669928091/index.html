Associate Business Analyst - Entry Level

at IBM

📍 Location Bedford, Nova Scotia Hybrid
📋 Details Full-time · Permanent · Competitive pay · -
📅 Posted Apr 23, 2026

About the Role

Gather requirements, analyze data, coordinate stakeholders

About IBM

Global tech leader with strong training programs

Full Description

Introduction A career in IBM Consulting is built on long-term client relationships and close collaboration worldwide. You’ll work with leading companies across industries, helping them shape their hybrid cloud and AI journeys. With support from our strategic partners, robust IBM technology, and Red Hat, you’ll have the tools to drive meaningful change and accelerate client impact. At IBM Consulting, curiosity fuels success. You’ll be encouraged to challenge the norm, explore new ideas, and create innovative solutions that deliver real results. Our culture of growth and empathy focuses on your long-term career development while valuing your unique skills and experiences.

Your Role And Responsibilities

  • Strong analytical, problem-solving, and critical-thinking skills.
  • Proficiency in Agile methodologies, Jira, Confluence, and product management tools.
  • Excellent communication and stakeholder management abilities.
  • Experience with data-driven decision-making and product analytics.
  • Knowledge of UX/UI principles and technical architecture is a plus.
  • Create overall application roadmap
  • Interlock with other stakeholders (business, other applications, product modeling team) on timelines, dependencies and plan interlocks
  • Experience in the areas of technical requirements definition, analysis, and documentation
  • Ability to analyze technical documents for legacy systems and assist with consolidation of these systems, if possible
  • Excellent organizational skills, ability to set priorities and multi-task
  • Ability to work as part of a team.
  • Excellent written and verbal Communication, Interpersonal and presentation skills

Preferred Education Master's Degree

Required Technical And Professional Expertise

  • You will use your experience in Business Analysis to plan, gather, elicit, design, organize, and manage business requirements and provide industry leading solutions for our clients
  • Experience in banking, financial services, or healthcare industry. Understands the lending lifecycle or knowledge of Business Analysis
  • Support the development of business requirements for a banking or healthcare systems
  • Can work with the client to identify the current product offering in application design, understand the product suite, and the differences between the two
  • This resource will be supporting clients that are migrating from the legacy core to the modernized core, so any experience in migration is a plus
