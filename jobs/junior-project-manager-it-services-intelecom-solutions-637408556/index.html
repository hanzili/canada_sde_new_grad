Junior Project Manager – IT Services

About the Role

Coordinate IT projects, track progress, report updates

About Intelecom Solutions

Telecom IT services provider, growing PM practice

Full Description

We are looking for a motivated Junior Project Manager to support the delivery of IT services projects, including IT infrastructure, software implementation, and managed services.

This role is ideal for an early-career professional with technical awareness and strong organizational skills who wants to grow within project management.

Responsibilities

  • Support planning and execution of IT projects from start to finish
  • Coordinate timelines, resources, and deliverables with technical teams
  • Track tickets, project progress, risks, and action items
  • Prepare project documentation, schedules, and status reports
  • Facilitate meetings and communicate updates to internal teams and clients
  • Assist with vendor coordination and change management

Qualifications

  • Bachelor’s degree in IT, Business, or related field (or equivalent experience)
  • 1–3 years of experience in project coordination or IT-related roles
  • Understanding of IT infrastructure or managed services environments
  • Familiarity with project tools (Jira, MS Project, Asana, FM Pilot, Service Channel)
  • Strong communication, organization, and problem-solving skills

Nice to Have

  • CAPM certification or working toward PMP
  • Experience with Agile/Scrum
  • Exposure to Facilities management software.

Job Type: Full-time
