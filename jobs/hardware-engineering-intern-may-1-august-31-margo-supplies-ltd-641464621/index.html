About the Role

Design, prototype, and test hardware components

About Margo Supplies Ltd.

Wildlife conservation tech hardware company

Full Description

Do you want to help protect wildlife (and the people who live and work alongside it) while building real engineering experience in a small, passionate R&D team? Margo Supplies is hiring a Hardware Engineering Intern for a full‑time summer placement focused on hands‑on design, prototyping, and testing.

You’ll work directly with our engineering team on projects ranging from ruggedized field electronics to smart‑technology wildlife deterrents. This is a rare opportunity to contribute to meaningful conservation technology while developing practical skills that matter.

Note: A licensed electrical P.Eng. is available to supervise your work, and sign off on your hours.

ATTENTION: Please read and follow all application instructions at the bottom of this posting.

What we offer

We pride ourselves on hiring excellent people. You will enjoy being part of a growing company doing interesting work, filled with dedicated and intelligent colleagues. This is a hybrid position with 4 office days in our Manchester office, and 1 day from home. We are a short walk from the 39th Avenue C-Train station. This position is for a 4 month internship.

What Will You Do?

You will apply your skills to assist in the design and testing of our in-house products, as well as assist in improving existing product lines. You will be exposed to a wide variety of electrical, CAD and circuitry design, radio communication protocols over various platforms.

Required Qualifications and Skills:

  • 2 year technical diploma as a mechanical engineering technologist or 3 years into a bachelor's of engineering with a mechanical specialization.
  • Experience in CAD design and 3D printing.
  • Experience in 3 axis industrial CNC milling is an asset.
  • Coursework or experience in material testing, quality control, and commercialization.
  • Demonstrate a passion for engineering technology.
  • Thrive in a small team setting.
  • Applicants who have a history of personal technology projects or related hobbies are preferred.
  • Must demonstrate ability to communicate clearly and professionally.

Margo Supplies is a worldwide leader in wildlife management technology. We design, manufacture, and distribute environmentally technology that solves human-wildlife conflict. Our new admin, sales, and R&D office is located just an 8 minute walk from a c-train station and has free parking. Our warehouse is located in High River, AB, a 20-minute drive away from South Calgary. We also operate two warehouse facilities in Las Vegas and Utah.

Established in 1980, we provide solutions to some of the most challenging wildlife conservation problems. If it involves wildlife and technology, we are there. Airports, government agencies, farmers, and industrial groups rely on us to conserve wildlife and prevent damage to people and property.

We may wear t-shirts to work but we take our business seriously. Our company is in a new growth push in smart technology and international expansion. We have led the industry for the last 40 years but we don't rest on our laurels. We have a strong wildlife conservation mandate, are the recognized leaders in bear conflict prevention, and have developed many of the benchmarked standard wildlife conflict prevention tools used across the globe.
