Full Description

Job Summary:

This job posting is for a remote, 8-week full-time AI Product Intern role at Windward Software Systems Inc. The intern will work with the Product Team to help build AI agents that gather, analyze, and prioritize product information, while collaborating with Product, Engineering, and Customer Success. It’s aimed at someone curious, detail-oriented, and excited to gain hands-on experience in AI development.

Job Description:

Are you eager to gain practical experience in AI development this summer? Do you have a passion for technology and a curious mindset, always seeking innovative ways to improve how information is gathered, understood, and acted upon? We’re searching for an Intern to join our Product Team with a focus on AI advancement to join our team this summer, focusing on building intelligent agents that support product-related information gathering, understanding, gap identification, and prioritization. As the AI Product Intern, you’ll gain hands-on experience in every stage of agent development and product intelligence, including:

  • Product information gathering: Designing and implementing agents to collect, organize, and synthesize data from various sources
  • Information analysis and understanding: Using AI techniques to process, interpret, and extract actionable insights from product data
  • Gap identification: Building tools and agents to detect gaps in product knowledge, documentation, or customer feedback
  • Prioritization and reporting: Developing systems to help prioritize product improvements and generate actionable reports for stakeholders

As an AI Product Intern, you’ll gain invaluable experience that will shape your future career. This is not an internship where you’re on the sidelines—expect to actively participate in meetings, share your ideas for agent designs and product intelligence, and collaborate directly with the Product, Engineering, and Customer Success teams to drive innovation. You’ll learn new AI techniques and skills that will set your career up for success. If you’re driven, inquisitive, detail-oriented (accuracy matters!), and excited to launch your AI development career, apply today and tell us why you’d be a great fit. Bonus points if you already have experience using AI tools to advance information gathering and product intelligence. This internship is remote, for 40 hours a week, for 8 weeks.

Salary Range $20-$34/hour

Excited to grow your career? We value our talented employees, and whenever possible strive to help one of our associates grow professionally before recruiting new talent to our open positions. If you think the open position, you see is right for you, we encourage you to apply! Our people make all the difference in our success.
