Our Commitment to Students

At Canadian Tire, we are committed to providing students with a supportive and collaborative environment that fosters knowledge sharing and offers key experiences to help them develop their capabilities through projects and objectives. We believe in setting specific and measurable goals that align with our business objectives to support learning and help students achieve their full potential. Our culture emphasizes learning from others, continuous improvement, agility, growth, and innovation, and we are invested in building a talented, diverse workforce for the future of Canada and Canadian Tire. Additionally, we are dedicated to building strong relationships with our student employees by engaging with them throughout their education and career pursuits and creating opportunities for ongoing communication and relationship building.

What You’ll Do:

Reporting to the Manager, FP&A - Forecasting, the student will be responsible for daily A/R tracking, analyzing and forecasting, as well as support in the completion and review of forecasting, ad hoc analyses, and month end reporting.

We are looking for students who are:

  • Outcome-focused critical thinkers with the ability to analyze financial results and draw meaningful insights from financial information.
  • Collaborative team players who build relationships easily across various stakeholder groups.
  • Equipped with strong technical skills, capable of conducting simple to moderately difficult financial analyses, and adept at learning new financial software and systems with ease.
  • Courageous individuals who thrive in an environment of change and ambiguity, willing to take on new assignments and requirements based on client or management requests.
  • Action-oriented and comfortable working independently when necessary, taking initiative to manage their own timelines and prioritize tasks.

What You Bring:

  • Currently pursuing a university degree in Finance/Accounting, Business, Economics, Math, or another related field.
  • Superior analytical and problem-solving abilities.
  • Strong interpersonal and communication skills (both written and verbal), with the ability to collaborate across various levels within the organization.
  • Advanced knowledge of Microsoft Excel and PowerPoint (or the ability to learn these tools on the job).
  • Individual with a can-do attitude, who is not afraid to take on challenges

We’re always looking for great talent! In addition to competitive pay, we offer:

  • Career growth opportunities and product discounts
