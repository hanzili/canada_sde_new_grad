Recognized as a top destination for innovators and champions of inclusion, BNY is where bold ideas meet advanced technology and exceptional talent. Together, we power the future of finance – and this is what is all about. Join us and be part of something extraordinary.

We’re seeking a future team member for the role of Junior Securities Lending Trader, North American Equities to join our Securities Finance team. This role is located in Toronto, Canada

In this role, you’ll make an impact in the following ways:

  • Execute and monitor securities lending transactions, which include order flow, and connections from vendors to counterparts.
  • Maintain and build external relationships among clients and brokers and internally across operations, trade support, and relationship management.
  • Determine the drivers to securities lending transactions and price these accordingly.
  • Monitor market movements and help manage risk within the trading desk.
  • Responsible for pricing and providing liquidity to clients when necessary

To be successful in this role, we’re seeking the following:

  • Education: Bachelor’s degree or the required equivalent and the Canadian Securities Course preferred
  • 0-2 years’ experience in securities lending or trading preferred
  • An innovative, forward-thinking mindset with technology and AI experience strongly preferred
