About the Role

Bridge design, assessment, and construction supervision

About LEX3 Engineering

Growing structural firm with mentorship and growth

Full Description

LEX3 is seeking a self-motivated and practically minded Junior Bridge Engineer to work with our Bridge Engineering Team in delivering a variety of bridge projects. You can expect to enjoy a mix of autonomous work and on-the job training and support through a pairing with experienced staff members. This opportunity will provide invaluable work experience immersed in a wide array of bridge-related projects including inspection, assessment, and design of structures ranging from bridge-size culverts to major bridges, as well as various other assignments. You will have the opportunity to be involved in bridge projects from project pursuit through to construction completion, and take pride in your contributions.

In this role, you can expect to be involved in some or all of the following tasks:

  • Project coordination
  • Design and assessment of bridges and other structures
  • Hydraulics and Hydrology Analysis
  • Report Preparation
  • Construction Contract Preparation
  • Construction Contract Administration
  • Construction Supervision
  • Bachelors Degree in Engineering or Applied Sciences from an accredited University;
  • 0 to 4 yrs of relevant experience in bridge or building structures project work. New grads are welcome to apply, although some experience in a previous position, co-op term, or summer employment will be considered an asset.

Time Commitment / Term

This is a full time, permanent position (40 hours per week).

Location

The position is required in our Red Deer office. Our flexible work environment allows employees to work from home up to two days per week if preferred.

About LEX3

LEX3 Engineering is a growing and dynamic structural engineering consulting firm offering services to the bridges, buildings and industrial sectors. Our structural engineering solutions range from buildings to bridges, civil infrastructure, and retaining structures. Big, small, and everything in between. The more complex, the better. Check out some of our projects here. (https://www.lex3.ca/work)

We collaborate across offices in Calgary, Edmonton, and Red Deer on projects around Alberta and beyond. Our offices are purposefully located in the cultural and economic cores of the communities we call home.

Wholeheartedly, we believe our teammates drive the success of our clients and our company. We take pride in providing a welcoming and inclusive work environment, empowering our staff to learn and grow as we progress in our careers. We want to work with people that share our commitment to delivering projects that both we and our clients can be proud of.

What’s it like to work at LEX3 Engineering?

  • We get to know you. At LEX3, you’re not a numbered employee. We’re a small team and we work together across all of our offices. See who you'll work with here. (https://www.lex3.ca/team) Mentorship and coaching is embedded in everything we do. Our growth philosophy is developing talent within the team, and we truly love to see everyone succeed.
  • Work-life balance is important. Everyone belongs in a family outside of LEX3, and we encourage balance with vacation time, scheduling accommodations, parental leave, and hybrid work environments. We genuinely want our teammates to succeed inside and outside office hours.
  • Engineering is a serious profession, but we balance that with a lot of fun. We gather all offices together in person a couple times per year, and there’s often something social in the works within every office. Fun spans a wide range at LEX3, whether it’s sharing a meal, playing darts after work, celebrating teammates’ milestones, organizing a sports team, or even Type 2 fun in the mountains.
  • Learn more about LEX3 here. (https://www.lex3.ca)

Additional Benefits

  • Competitive salaries. Above-industry-average compensation based on current APEGA industry benchmarks.
  • Holistic wellness. Comprehensive benefit package including coverage for a variety of healthcare practitioners, plus a health and wellness spending account.
  • Helping you plan ahead. Generous RRSP matching program – up to 5% of your annual salary.
  • Growing with you. Professional development opportunities aligned with your unique career aspirations.
  • Happy holidays, every year. Paid time off for the last week of the calendar year.

Next Steps

We look forward to reviewing your application, and will contact selected applicants to learn more about you and your professional experience. Thanks for your interest in joining the LEX3 Engineering team.
