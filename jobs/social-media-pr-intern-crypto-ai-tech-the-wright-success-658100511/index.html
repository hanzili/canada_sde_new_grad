Full Description

About The Wright Success

The Wright Success is a digital platform focused on financial education, digital assets, and emerging technology. Through global events, partnerships, and educational programs, the company supports individuals in navigating opportunities across crypto, AI, and tech.

The company has collaborated with leading organizations including CoinDesk, Stellar, Chainlink, and others, and has supported the growth of thousands of individuals globally through events and education.

Role Overview

The Wright Success is seeking a Social Media & PR Intern to support the growth and expansion of the founder’s digital presence across LinkedIn, Instagram, and TikTok.

This is a unique opportunity to gain hands-on experience in content strategy, brand growth, and public relations within the fast-growing digital assets and technology space.

**Key Responsibilities

• Support social media growth strategies across Instagram, TikTok, and

LinkedIn

• Assist with content planning, scheduling, and engagement

• Identify and support PR opportunities (media, podcasts, features)

• Conduct trend research and propose content ideas

• Assist in building brand visibility and audience growth

• Research and identify speaking opportunities at conferences, panels, podcasts, and industry events

**Qualifications

• Student or early-career professional in PR, Marketing, Communications, or rela

ted field

• Strong interest in social media, digital branding, and storytelling

• Interest in crypto, AI, or emerging technology is an asset

• Creative, proactive, and detail-oriented

**What You Will Gain

• Hands-on experience in social media growth and PR

strategy

• Exposure to the digital assets, AI, and tech ecosystem

• Opportunities to attend select industry events and networking experiences

• Mentorship and direct collaboration with leadership

• Portfolio-building experience with measurable outcomes

• Monthly honorarium

**Additional Details

• Remote

(Global)

• Priority for candidates based in Toronto or the United States

• Part-time, flexible hours**
