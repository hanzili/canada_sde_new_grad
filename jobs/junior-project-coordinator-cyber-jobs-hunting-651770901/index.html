🚀 We’re Hiring: Junior Project Coordinator (Australia) 🇦🇺

Full-time & Part-time Opportunities Available

Every successful project has someone behind the scenes keeping things on track.

If you’re naturally organized, detail-oriented, and love bringing order to chaos—this might be your moment 👇

We’re looking for a Junior Project Coordinator who’s ready to learn fast, take ownership, and grow into project leadership .

✨ What Makes This Role Different

  • You won’t just “assist”—you’ll actively drive progress
  • You’ll be involved in real projects from day one
  • Your ideas and improvements are genuinely valued 💡
  • We focus on growth, not just experience

🛠️ What You’ll Be Doing

  • Supporting project planning, timelines, and coordination
  • Tracking tasks, deadlines, and deliverables
  • Communicating with stakeholders to keep everyone aligned
  • Preparing reports, updates, and documentation
  • Helping identify risks and keeping projects on track

🎯 What We’re Looking For

  • Some experience in coordination, admin, or project support (internships count!)
  • Strong organizational and multitasking skills
  • Clear and confident communication
  • Attention to detail with a proactive mindset
  • A willingness to learn and grow in project management

🌱 Why Join Us?

  • A strong foundation to grow into a Project Manager role
  • Hands-on experience across real business projects
  • A supportive team that values initiative and learning
  • Flexible full-time or part-time options
