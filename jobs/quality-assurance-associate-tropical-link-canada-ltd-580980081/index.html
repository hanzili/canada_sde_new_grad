About the Role

Support QA systems, audits, compliance, and process improvements.

About Tropical Link Canada Ltd

Natural and organic foods company focused on quality and regulatory compliance.

Full Description

Job Overview:

Tropical Link Canada is a natural and organic foods company based in Burnaby. We're passionate about delivering high-quality, health-focused products to our customers across Canada—and we're looking for a part-time QA Associate who’s just as enthusiastic as we are.

We value team players who are adaptable, eager to learn, and ready to take on a variety of responsibilities as our company grows.

Key Responsibilities:

  • ⁠Assist in maintaining and managing the quality management system.
  • ⁠Write reviews and update documented procedures and policies pertaining to quality management system.
  • Conduct annual supplier verifications.
  • Propose improvements to methods, systems, and procedure in order to ensure compliance with regulatory requirements.
  • Conduct periodic self-inspection, GMP, and internal audits.
  • Investigate customer complaints and provide solutions through CAPA’s.
  • Review product labels for Canada, USA to ensure regulatory compliance.
  • Training and coaching company staff on GMP’s HACCP and Sanitation.
  • ⁠Perform all work in accordance with all establishment regulatory compliance and safety requirements.
  • ⁠Maintain accurate documentation and records of all quality assurance activities.
  • ⁠Complete all other tasks related to the position as assigned by QAM.
  • Assist in R&D and project management activities.

What We’re Looking For:

  • Bachelor’s degree or diploma in a related field. (Food Science, Biology, Chemistry)
  • ⁠1-2 year experience preferred in quality assurance roles within food and beverage industry.
  • ⁠Knowledge of food safety regulations and quality management systems.
  • ⁠Strong attention to detail and analytical skills.
  • Strong knowledge on MS Office
  • ⁠Capable of multitasking with minimal supervision.
  • ⁠Excellent written and verbal communications skills in English.
  • Interest in the natural and organic food industry is a plus

What We Offer:

  • A collaborative, dynamic, and supportive work environment
  • Opportunities to grow and diversify your skills in a wide array of fields
  • Competitive salary based on experience
  • Frequent travel opportunities (within Canada and International) for trade shows

Training:

We will provide the necessary training for the right candidate even if you do not have enough experience in all areas of highlighted work.

If you're ready to be part of a mission-driven team in a dynamic and flexible setting, we’d love to hear from you!

To apply, please send your resume via indeed and a short cover letter to vihan@tropicallinkcanada.ca

Job Type: Part-time

Pay: From $23.50 per hour

Education:

  • Bachelor's Degree (preferred)

Work Location: In person
