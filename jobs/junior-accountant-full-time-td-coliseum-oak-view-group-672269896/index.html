About Oak View Group

Entertainment venue finance role with growth opportunity.

Full Description

Oak View Group (OVG) is the global leader in premium live entertainment infrastructure and services, with a platform spanning venue development and end-to-end capabilities across venue management, hospitality, and sponsorship sales. Founded in 2015, the company serves a collection of seven world-class owned venues and a client roster of the most iconic arenas, stadiums, convention centers, music festivals, performing arts centers, and cultural institutions, spanning four continents.

Position Summary We are hiring an Junior Accountant to support the Finance team with daily financial operations. This role is well suited for someone early in their accounting career who is eager to gain experience and develop professionally within the entertainment and hospitality industries.

You’ll mainly work alongside the Accounting Manager to help maintain accurate financial records and support key processes that keep operations running smoothly. As part of OVG, you’ll have internal opportunities, allowing high-performing team members to grow their careers and pursue advancement opportunities across our venues and markets globally.

This role pays an annual salary of $52,000-$62,000 CAD.

Benefits for Full-Time roles: Health, Dental and Vision Insurance, Pension matching, and Paid Time Off (vacation days, sick days, and statutory holidays). This position will remain open until June 5, 2026. About The Venue TD Coliseum , a modern venue rooted in the proud history of Hamilton. Hosting world-class concerts, sports, and special events, TD Coliseum, FirstOntario Concert Hall and The Studio play key roles in the region’s cultural and economic landscape. As we enter an exciting new era of growth and reinvention, we are seeking passionate professionals who thrive in fast-paced, high-impact environments.

Responsibilities

  • Work closely alongside staff accountant during introductory period.
  • Support accounts payable and accounts receivable activities.
  • Prepare journal entries and maintain supporting documents.
  • Maintain accuracy of coding and all general ledger transactions.
  • Preparation of bank payments and transfers.
  • Perform daily cash activities within Archtics ticketing system.
  • Maintain detailed spreadsheets in Excel for tracking of P&L, Budgets and rolling forecasts.
  • Perform other duties as assigned by management to support the overall goals of the finance team.

Qualifications

  • Diploma or degree in Finance, Accounting, Business Administration, or a related field.
  • Basic to intermediate knowledge of accounting principles and financial controls.
  • 1–3 years experience in a finance, accounting, or admin support role.
  • Experience in venue management, events, hospitality, entertainment, or tourism environments an asset.
  • Exposure to high-volume transactional processing, including invoices, payments, and reconciliations.
  • Intermediate Microsoft Excel skills (V-lookups, Sum-ifs, Pivot Tables, etc.) is a strongly valued asset.
  • Demonstrate excellent written and verbal communication skills, including the ability to successfully communicate with other employees and vendors.
  • Extremely organized, detail oriented, resourceful, and a quick learner.
