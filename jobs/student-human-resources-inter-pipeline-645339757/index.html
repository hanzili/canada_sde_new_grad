HR / People / Recruiting Internship

Student, Human Resources

at Inter Pipeline

📍 Location Calgary, Alberta
📋 Details Internship · May-Aug 2026 · Not disclosed · 4 months
📅 Posted Mar 17, 2026

About the Role

Assist HR systems, data, and admin tasks

About Inter Pipeline

Inter Pipeline; large Canadian energy company

Full Description

Description The HR Summer Student will provide support to the HR Operations, HR Systems, and HR Business Partner teams through a wide range of administrative, data quality, and process support activities. This role is ideal for a student currently pursuing post secondary education in human resources or a related field who enjoys detail-oriented work, working with systems, and contributing to process improvement across the HR function.

Key Activities And Responsibilities HR Systems & Data Support

  • Manage the general inbox for the HR Systems team, responding to basic inquiries and routing more complex questions to the appropriate team member.
  • Review HR transaction workflow requests to ensure completeness, accuracy, and appropriateness; follow up with HRBPs and functional HR teams for required clarification or information.
  • Assist with coordination and execution of ongoing HR data audits in collaboration with the HRSA team, HRBPs, and other HR groups.
  • Support secondary testing for HR system enhancements, configuration updates, and reporting changes.
  • Review and update HR systems and program Quick Reference Guides (QRGs) to ensure accuracy, relevance, and alignment with current processes.

File and Records Management

  • Assist with file management activities including digitizing employee files, cleaning up records, and archiving documents that meet retention requirements.
  • Review and organize HR policy and program documentation, ensuring alignment with Records Management Guidelines and proper storage in the Enterprise Content Management system.

Project & Administrative Support

  • Support Total Rewards, HR Systems, and HR Business Partner teams with various tasks and initiatives.

Education, Skills And Experience

  • Currently enrolled in a post-secondary program in Human Resources, Business Administration, or a related field.
  • Previous experience working in an administrative or coordination-focused role is an asset.
  • Comfortable working with HR systems and handling sensitive data; familiarity with SuccessFactors is considered an asset.
  • Proficient with Microsoft Office, with strong skills in Word, Excel, and PowerPoint.
  • High attention to detail, strong organizational abilities, and a willingness to learn.
  • Strong communication skills and ability to collaborate within a team environment.

Hours Monday - Friday, 8 hours per day

May - August 2026, 4 month term

As this position is identified as security sensitive, employment of a final candidate will be conditional upon completion of a criminal background check assessments with satisfactory results.
