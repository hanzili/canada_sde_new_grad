Job Summary

This is a paid internship offering hands-on, project-based experience within a finance/accounting team. The intern will be assigned to a specific business unit and work closely with an Intern Coordinator and business Sponsor. The role is designed to build foundational skills through on-the-job learning, exposure to real business priorities, and structured coaching.

Key Responsibilities

  • Support monthly financial processes, including:

  • Rolling forward FX analysis

  • Budget file rollovers
  • Headcount analysis
  • Prepare and assist with monthly reporting (e.g., charitable donations)
  • Conduct research to support technical accounting requests
  • Coordinate and schedule SOX walkthroughs and preparatory calls with control owners

  • Contribute to ad hoc projects, including:

  • Reducing manual journal entries

  • Supporting the setup and maintenance of an intercompany matrix

Qualifications

  • Currently enrolled in, or recently completed, a post-secondary program in Accounting, Finance, Business, or a related field
  • No prior related work experience required
  • Basic proficiency with Excel and data files
  • Strong attention to detail and organizational skills
  • Ability to follow structured processes and work on clearly defined tasks
  • Interest in gaining practical experience in a corporate finance environment
