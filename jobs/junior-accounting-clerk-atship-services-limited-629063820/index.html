Full Description

Job Overview
We are currently seeking a reliable and detail-oriented accounting clerk to join our team. This role is responsible for day to day accounting and administrative support with a primary focus on accounts payable and receivables.

Responsibilities

  • Entering bills, creating invoices and payments accurately.
  • Assist with accounts payable and accounts receivable processes to ensure timely payments and collections.
  • Reconcile accounts and prepare account analysis reports to identify discrepancies or errors.
  • Maintaining organized and up to date financial records.
  • Daily data entry in accounting software. (Quick Books).
  • Assist in budgeting activities and financial reporting under supervision.
  • Collaborate with team members to improve data accuracy and streamline accounting procedures.
  • Assisting with general office and admin tasks as needed.

Skills

  • Accounting course or related education considered an asset.
  • Grade 12 diploma or equivalent.
  • Basic understanding of accounts payable, accounts receivable, and account reconciliation processes.
  • Knowledge of bookkeeping principles and financial analysis techniques.
  • Proficiency in accounting software such as QuickBooks, is highly desirable.
  • Ability to learn new software quickly and adapt to changing processes.
  • Strong organizational skills with the ability to prioritize tasks effectively. This role provides an excellent foundation for a career in accounting or finance and offers opportunities for professional growth within our organization.

Technical Skills

  • Comfortable using Excel, Outlook, and Word Document.
  • Computer savvy, Candidates with strong computer skills will be given preference.
  • Ability to work independently and as part of a team.
  • Good communication skills.
