About the Role

Assist data analysis, manage reports, support operations daily

About SurveyMonkey

Global survey platform powering experience insights

Full Description

SurveyMonkey is the world's most popular platform for surveys and forms, built for business—loved by users. We combine powerful capabilities with intuitive design, effectively serving every use case, from customer experience to employee engagement, market research to payment and registration forms. With built-in research expertise and AI-powered technology, it's like having a team of expert researchers at your fingertips.

Trusted by millions—from startups to Fortune 500 companies—SurveyMonkey helps teams gather insights and information that inspire better decisions, create experiences people love, and drive business growth. Discover how at surveymonkey.com.

What we're looking for

We're looking for a motivated, curious, and analytical Operations Intern to support key projects and initiatives that are driving our success and aligned to our biggest priorities in our Customer Success org. This is a hands-on opportunity to see how a high-growth company scales its operations and successfully supports thousands of customers each quarter.

This position reports to the Senior Manager of Post Sales-Operations.

What you'll be working on

  • Operational Support: Contribute to projects and reports that help track success metrics and key performance indicators.
  • Data Management: Help audit and review various reports and metrics to ensure consistency across teams
  • Insight Generation: Clean and organize data to help identify trends, presenting your findings to stakeholders
  • Cross-Functional Collaboration: Coordinate with various stakeholders to help support ongoing programs, projects, and initiatives.

We'd love to hear from people with

  • Academic Background: Currently pursuing a Bachelor's or Master's degree in Finance, Economics, Business, Mathematics, or a related quantitative field.
  • Analytical Mindset: A strong comfort level with numbers and an interest in providing insights.
  • Technical Foundations: Proficiency in Microsoft Excel (vlookups, pivot tables) and Google Suite. Previous exposure to Salesforce is a major plus but not required.
  • Communication: Ability to communicate clearly in written and oral English; you should be comfortable explaining the "why" behind the data.
  • Adaptability: A "no task is too small" attitude and a desire to learn in a fast-paced environment.

SurveyMonkey believes in-person collaboration is valuable for building relationships, fostering community, and enhancing our speed and execution in problem-solving and decision-making. As such, you will be required to work from a SurveyMonkey office up to 1 day per week.

We live our company values—like championing inclusion and making it happen—by embedding them into how we hire, collaborate, and grow. They help shape everything from our culture to our business decisions. Come join us and see where your curiosity can take you.
