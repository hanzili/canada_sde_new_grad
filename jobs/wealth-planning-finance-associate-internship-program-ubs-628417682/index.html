Your role

Are you a detail-oriented multitasker? Do you know how to make sure things run smoothly? We’re looking for someone like that who can provide administrative support to the Wealth Management team.

  • prepare and enter data into wealth plans and track their progress
  • manage settlement of accounts, process invoices and expenses, print and mail cheques to employees and vendors, conduct cheque runs twice a month and prepare cheque log for sign off
  • assist with compliance reports, including daily trade reports and consolidation on a quarterly basis
  • administer filing systems, including the filing of invoices and journal entries, prepare boxes for offsite storage and order cheques
  • coordinate various internal communications materials such as preparation of daily and quarterly reports, minutes for weekly Finance meetings, and training material on Travel & Expense claim form
  • assist with preparation for external audit

Your team

You’ll be working as part of our Wealth Management Team and share Wealth Planning and Finance responsibilities in Toronto, Canada.

We tailor custom wealth management and investment solutions based on our client's monetary requirements. We seek to understand our client's objectives and make suggestions on how best to achieve their money management goals.

Your expertise

  • have administrative experience in a fast-paced, highly professional environment is ideal
  • demonstrate proficiency in Microsoft Office (Outlook, Word, PowerPoint, Excel)
  • strong written and verbal communication skills
  • organized, with an innate sense of how to prioritize and willingness to learn new systems (e.g., SUN GL)
  • able to work flexible hours as needed

About us

UBS is a leading and truly global wealth manager and the leading universal bank in Switzerland. We also provide diversified asset management solutions and focused investment banking capabilities. Headquartered in Zurich, Switzerland, UBS is present in more than 50 markets around the globe.
