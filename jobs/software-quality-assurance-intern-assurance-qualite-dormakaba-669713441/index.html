Full Description

Position Overview dormakaba is seeking a Software Quality Assurance Intern based in Montreal. This role will report directly to the Software and Firmware Quality Assurance Senior Manager. The Software Quality Assurance Intern is responsible for assisting in the development and execution of test plans, documenting defects, and contributing to continuous quality improvement initiatives.

Rising Talent Internship Program

Looking to apply your skills to real-world business challenges alongside experienced professionals? dormakaba’s Rising Talent Internship Program offers hands-on projects that make a real impact—from manufacturing and product design to customer engagement and innovation.

Learn more about the program by watching this short video: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=qD_8FLLdkOU

HIRING SALARY RANGE: Base Salary 24.00$ 28.00$ (Salary to be determined by the education, experience, knowledge, skills, and abilities of the applicant, internal equity, and alignment with market data.) Please visit our career site for more information on benefits.

Applicants must be authorized to work for any employer in Canada. We are unable to sponsor or take over sponsorship of an employment visa at this time.

What You Will Do

  • Support software and firmware testing activities to ensure products meet quality standards
  • Execute test plans and document test results, bugs, and defects in tracking systems
  • Participate in regression testing and validate software functionality across different scenarios
  • Collaborate with development teams to reproduce, verify, and communicate software issues
  • Contribute to the creation and maintenance of test documentation and quality metrics
  • Assist in continuous improvement initiatives for QA processes and testing automation

What we require

  • Currently enrolled in a Bachelor's or Master's degree program in Computer Science, Software Engineering, Information Technology, or related field
  • Basic understanding of software testing concepts and quality assurance principles
  • Analytical mindset with strong attention to detail and problem-solving abilities
  • Good communication skills in English (written and verbal)
  • Ability to work collaboratively in a team environment
  • Proficiency with Microsoft Office (Excel, Word, PowerPoint)

What we prefer

  • Familiarity with software testing methodologies (manual and/or automated testing concepts)
  • Basic programming or scripting knowledge (Python, Java, C++, or similar)
  • Understanding of software development lifecycle (SDLC) and agile methodologies
  • Experience with bug tracking tools (Jira, Bugzilla, Azure DevOps, or similar)
  • French language skills (advantage for Montreal location)
  • Previous internship or co-op experience in software development or QA
  • Knowledge of test automation frameworks (Selenium, JUnit, pytest, etc.)

Who we are We are at the heart of every place that matters. From automatic doors to cloud-based access management – as a leading global provider in the access solutions market for schools, banks, airports, hospitals, hotels, and many more, we enable seamless movement within secure, safe, and sustainable places. With over 160 years of experience and a focus on excellence and innovation, we offer exciting opportunities to grow in an environment where you can make a real impact. At dormakaba, you are encouraged to take initiative, develop your skills, and build your career alongside 16,000 experts worldwide who are setting industry standards every day.

Work in a place where you matter – apply now!

  • Participer aux tests de régression et valider les fonctionnalités des logiciels dans différents scénarios
  • Collaborer avec les équipes de développement pour reproduire, vérifier et communiquer les problèmes logiciels
  • Contribuer à la création et à la maintenance de la documentation de test et des métriques de qualité
  • Participer aux initiatives d'amélioration continue des processus d'assurance qualité et d'automatisation des tests

Ce que nous demandons

  • Être actuellement inscrit(e) dans un programme de baccalauréat ou de maîtrise en informatique, en génie logiciel, en technologie de l'information ou dans un domaine connexe
  • Compréhension de base des concepts de test de logiciels et des principes d'assurance qualité
  • Esprit analytique avec une grande attention aux détails et des capacités de résolution de problèmes
  • Bonnes compétences en communication en anglais (écrit et oral)
  • Capacité à travailler en collaboration dans un environnement d'équipe
  • Maîtrise de Microsoft Office (Excel, Word, PowerPoint)

Ce que nous préférons

  • Familiarité avec les méthodologies de test de logiciels (concepts de test manuel et/ou automatisé)
  • Connaissances de base en programmation ou en script (Python, Java, C++ ou similaire)
  • Compréhension du cycle de vie du développement logiciel (SDLC) et des méthodologies agiles
  • Expérience avec les outils de suivi des bogues (Jira, Bugzilla, Azure DevOps ou similaire)
  • Compétences en français (un atout pour le poste à Montréal)
  • Expérience préalable de stage ou de coop en développement logiciel ou en assurance qualité
  • Connaissance des frameworks d'automatisation des tests (Selenium, JUnit, pytest, etc.)
