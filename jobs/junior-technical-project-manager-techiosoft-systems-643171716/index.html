Junior Technical Project Manager

at TechioSoft Systems

📍 Location Burnaby, British Columbia
📋 Details Full-time

About TechioSoft Systems

TechioSoft: enterprise software, digital modernization

Full Description

About the Company

TechioSoft builds enterprise software and digital modernization solutions for clients across Canada. Our teams work on large-scale digital transformation initiatives including cloud platforms, AI-enabled systems, data platforms, and enterprise integrations.

About the Role

We are looking for a Junior Technical Project Manager who is early in their career and eager to grow within a technical delivery environment. This role supports project coordination across development teams while gaining exposure to real enterprise software projects.

Responsibilities

  • Support coordination between developers, architects, and project stakeholders
  • Assist with project meetings, notes, and follow-ups
  • Help maintain project trackers, milestones, and timelines
  • Track action items, risks, and blockers across projects
  • Support communication between engineering teams and clients
  • Maintain documentation and delivery updates

Qualifications

  • 1–2 years experience in project coordination or technology teams

Required Skills

  • Basic understanding of software development environments and SDLC
  • Strong communication and organizational skills
  • Motivated to learn within a fast-moving technical environment
  • Comfortable working with technical teams and business stakeholders

Preferred Skills

  • Exposure to APIs, integrations, or cloud technologies
  • Experience with Agile environments
  • Background in computer science, information systems, or engineering

Pay range and compensation package

Location: Burnaby, BC Hybrid: 3 days per week in the Burnaby office Work Authorization: Must be legally authorized to work in Canada (no visa sponsorship)
