Junior Condominium Accountant

at Forest Hill Residential

📍 Location Greater Toronto Area
📋 Details Full-time
📅 Posted Mar 10, 2026

About the Role

Prepare monthly financials, reconcile accounts, support audits

About Forest Hill Residential

Residential condo management firm in GTA

Full Description

Forest Hill Residential (FHR) specializes in condominium property management across the Greater Toronto Area, delivering exceptional resident service, accurate financial management, and strong corporate governance. Our team is focused exclusively on condominium operations, supporting Boards of Directors through fiscally responsible management and accountability.

The Junior Condominium Accountant plays a key role in ensuring accurate financial reporting and administrative support for assigned condominium corporations. Working closely with the Senior Accounting Team and Property Managers, this role assists in the preparation of monthly financial statements, accounts payable and receivable processing, bank reconciliations, and document management.

The ideal candidate is detail-oriented, process-driven, and eager to develop a strong foundation in condominium financial accounting.

Primary Responsibilities

Financial Reporting & Support

· Assist in the preparation of monthly financial statements for assigned condominium corporations.

· Support review and accuracy efforts by maintaining organized supporting documentation.

· Assist with reconciliation of balance sheet accounts.

Accounts Payable / Receivable

· Process invoices accurately and in a timely manner.

· Confirm proper GL coding and budget allocation prior to approval.

· Support tracking and follow-up on arrears as required.

· Maintain accurate vendor files and ensure required documentation is collected.

Banking & Cashflow

· Assist with monthly bank reconciliations.

· Support monitoring of cash balances and reserve transactions.

· Assist in preparing supporting schedules for audit requirements.

Administration & Compliance

· Maintain proper accounting records in accordance with company standards and the Condominium Act.

· Support coordination of documents required for annual audits.

· Assist with maintaining accurate owner billing and ledger information.

· Ensure confidentiality and accuracy when handling sensitive financial data.

Collaboration & Communication

· Work closely with Property Managers to support financial inquiries and reporting obligations.

· Provide timely responses to internal requests.

· Participate in ongoing professional development and process improvement efforts

Qualifications

· 1–2 years experience in accounting, finance, or administrative roles (property management or condominium experience preferred).

· Post-secondary education in Accounting, Finance, Business Administration, or related discipline.

· Strong proficiency in Microsoft Excel and general MS Office Suite.

· Experience with Yardi, Vendor Café, Condo Café, Condo Manager, or similar financial software is an asset.

· High attention to detail with strong organizational and data-entry skills.

· Ability to work in a fast-paced environment and manage multiple priorities.

· Strong communication skills and willingness to learn condominium accounting practices.

---

Professional Attributes

· Reliable, accountable, and proactive.

· Team-oriented with a willingness to assist colleagues.

· Committed to accuracy, timeliness, and continuous improvement.

---

Work Location: Head Office (with some hybrid capability depending on department needs) Reports To: Senior Accounting Team / Corporate Controller

---

This role provides opportunities for growth toward intermediate and senior condominium accounting responsibilities within Forest Hill Residential
