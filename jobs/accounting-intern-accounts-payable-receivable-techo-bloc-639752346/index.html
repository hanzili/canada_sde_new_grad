Accounting Intern (Accounts Payable & Receivable)

About the Role

Process AP/AR, post invoices, reconcile accounts

About Techo-Bloc

Canadian manufacturing firm; hands-on accounting internship

Full Description

At Techo-Bloc , we’re innovators and doers at heart — we love rolling up our sleeves, jumping in, and turning ideas into action. With 9 state-of-the-art manufacturing facilities , 13+ distribution centers , and a dynamic team of 900+ employees across 20+ locations in North America , we’re proud to be a leader in our industry.

We’re ambitious, driven, and always looking to do things better — from creating cutting-edge products to supporting bold ideas. Collaboration, inclusivity, and positivity are at the core of how we work, and we invest in our people just as much as in our products. At Techo-Bloc, your ideas matter, your contributions make an impact, and your growth is part of our journey.

Job Description About the Internship We are looking for a full-time Accounting Intern to join our Finance team from April/May to August/September . This is a hands-on internship where you’ll gain real experience in both Accounts Payable and Accounts Receivable , working across multiple company entities and contributing to key finance operations.

Possibility of extension and/or permanent employment at the end of the internship!

What You’ll Learn & Gain

  • Comprehensive understanding of Accounts Payable and Accounts Receivable processes
  • Hands-on experience with a new ERP system and other modern finance tools
  • Exposure to multi-company accounting operations
  • Mentorship and guidance from experienced finance professionals
  • Professional skills such as organization, attention to detail, problem-solving, and teamwork

Key Responsibilities You will be trusted with meaningful responsibilities and real work, including:

  • Posting vendor invoices and customer payments in our new ERP system
  • Creating and maintaining vendor and customer records in the ERP system
  • Assisting with payment processing, invoicing, and account reconciliations
  • Providing backup support across AP and AR functions as needed
  • Supporting process documentation and contributing to process improvements

Qualifications Who We’re Looking For

  • Currently enrolled in a college or university program in Accounting, Finance, or a related field
  • Interest in finance operations and accounting processes
  • Organized, detail-oriented, and eager to learn
  • Comfortable with Excel (an asset)
  • No prior AP/AR experience required — we value curiosity, motivation, and potential above all
  • Bilingual (French/English), with strong English for US client communication.

Additional Information Why You’ll Love This Internship

  • Real responsibilities with a direct impact on finance operations
  • Structured mentorship and support to help you grow professionally
  • Exposure to both Accounts Payable & Accounts Receivable for a well-rounded experience
  • Collaborative, approachable, and supportive team environment
  • Internship experience that enhances your résumé and opens doors to future opportunities
