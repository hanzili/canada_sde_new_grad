About the Role

About Pura Mama Inc.

Early-stage woman-led Calgary startup in health/wellness

Full Description

About Pura Mama Pura Mama is an early-stage, woman-led Calgary startup with a live mobile and web app in the health and wellness space. Small team, real users, growing fast. You'll work directly with the founder.

Important — eligibility (please read first) This is a Government of Canada SWPP-funded student placement. To be considered, you must be:

  • Currently enrolled (full- or part-time) at a Canadian post-secondary institution, and remain enrolled through August 2026
  • A Canadian citizen, permanent resident, or protected person (international students on study/work permits are not eligible)
  • Located in or able to commute to Calgary, AB for occasional in-person events

The role Help us grow. We set the strategy together — you execute it, track it, and report back. Hands-on, real-world digital marketing with measurable outcomes.

What you'll do

  • Run and schedule social media content (Instagram, TikTok, etc.)
  • Help execute paid and organic campaigns and track performance with analytics tools
  • Run digital outreach to community partners — midwives, doulas, and clinics — via email and simple CRM tracking
  • Support founder-led community events: digital promotion, registrations, and follow-up
  • Use AI tools (such as Claude) for content drafting, research, and learning new skills fast
  • Bring your own ideas — small team, your input ships

You are

  • Enrolled in business, marketing, communications, or a related program
  • Organized, reliable, and comfortable executing a plan independently
  • A strong written communicator
  • Curious about AI tools and quick to learn new software
  • Bonus: Spanish-language ability, Canva/CapCut, event experience

Details

  • Summer 2026, starting early July, through August 31, 2026
  • 35 hrs/week, $21/hour
  • Hybrid (Calgary)
  • Possibility of extension into Fall 2026

We encourage applications from students of all backgrounds, including under-represented groups.

Pay: $20.00-$22.00 per hour

Work Location: Hybrid remote in Calgary, AB
