About The Role We are seeking curious, detail-oriented students to join our AI Delivery team as AI Quality Assurance Interns .

This is a hands-on role where you will evaluate the behavior, accuracy, and reliability of Cresta‚Äôs AI Agents and Agent Assist systems.

  • Run bias, redaction, and transcription accuracy checks
  • Spot patterns or emerging issues across test sets
  • Contribute ideas for improving internal QA processes and tools
