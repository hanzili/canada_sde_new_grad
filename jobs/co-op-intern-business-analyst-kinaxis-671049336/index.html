About Kinaxis

Full Description

About Kinaxis Are you looking to join an innovative, market-leading company where you can truly elevate your career? At Kinaxis we are serious about culture, we are serious about technology, we are serious about customers, and we are serious about not taking ourselves too seriously. If you are looking to be part of an incredible growth story, then we might just be the place for you!

In 1984, we started out as a team of three engineers. Today, we have grown to become a global organization with over 2000 employees around the world, 6 global office and a best-in-class HQ in Ottawa, Canada. As winners of several Top Employer awards globally, we are proud to work with our customers and employees towards solving some of the biggest challenges facing supply chains today.

Kinaxis is a global leader in modern supply chain orchestration, powering complex global supply chains, and supporting the people who manage them. Our powerful, AI infused platform provides full transparency and visibility across end-to-end supply chains, enabling our customers to make faster, better decisions. We are trusted by renowned global brands to provide the agility and predictability needed to navigate today’s volatility and disruption. With more than 40,000 users in over 100 countries, we are expanding our team as we continue to innovate and revolutionize how we support our customers.

About The Team As part of the IT Application Excellence team, the Intern Business Analyst is responsible for supporting wide range of activities related to needs assessments, technology selection, managing technology implementation and process improvement projects, and supporting business systems.

The Intern Business Analyst will proactively partner with Kinaxis internal stakeholders and IT Business Partners to understand the business objectives, operating model and processes. Through this business knowledge, you will work with other team members to identify areas where efficiency and productivity could be improved using business process improvements and technology solutions. Essential responsibilities include supporting relationships, communications, business process development, improvement and analysis, data analysis, requirements gathering and definition, solution configuration, project coordination, documentation and training.

About The Role Location

This is a hybrid position. You must be in the Ottawa or Toronto office, at least three days a week.

Term Duration

This is a full-time 8, or 12-month position, with an immediate start in May or early June 2026.

Co-op/Intern Eligibility

This position is open to co-ops and interns. To be eligible, you must be currently enrolled in full-time education or be a recent/upcoming graduate within 12 months of the placement end date.

Compensation range

$19.46- $28.55 hourly rate. The final offer within this range will reflect the candidate’s skills, year of education, and experience.

Vacancy Status

This is an existing job vacancy.

*What you will do*

  • Work closely with team members and business users to identify and analyze business processes, user and system requirements
  • Administer systems like Atlassian (Jira, Confluence), ServiceNow, Kantata OX and more.
  • Help the team with ticket management by processing requests, incidents and tasks.
  • Assist in conduct impact analysis of requirements
  • Conduct meetings with various stakeholders
  • Manage and update project and application specific documentation
  • Assist the IT Business Solutions team in system documentation, auditing, testing and application support
  • Coordinate new system implementation including timely data migration, user training and support
  • May perform additional projects upon request

*What we are looking for*

  • Currently enrolled in Engineering, Computer Science, MIS, or equivalent
  • With interest or experience in Business Analysis
  • With interest in Systems Administration
  • With interest in IT Application / Solution Delivery and Management
  • Who is curious, and interested in investigating and troubleshooting issues
  • With strong analytical skills
  • Who loves how technology can improve the way we work, is well organized and results driven.
  • Who is detail oriented, has a passion for solving problems, and who wants to be part of a highly effective team with an opportunity to make an impact!
  • Who has exceptional communication & presentation skills, and a can-do attitude

We’re accepting applications now through end of day on Friday, May 8, 2026 . Please note that we may begin reviewing applications before the posting closes, so early submission is encouraged.
