About the Role

Build campaigns, drive leads, optimize sales conversions

About Healthcord Cryogenics

Biomedical tech startup with rapid growth plans

Full Description

Who We Are: Healthcord Cryogenics – A stable, growing biomedical technology company expanding our sales and marketing team in Vancouver, BC.

Employment Type: Part-time or contract-to-hire (20–30 hours/week to start), with rapid progression to full-time based on results. Opportunity for freelancers open to transitioning in-house.

About the Role: We are building a high-impact sales and marketing function from the ground up and are seeking ambitious recent graduates who want to make a measurable difference early in their careers. This is a rare opportunity to join a stable, growing company at the perfect moment—your contributions will directly shape our growth trajectory and be highly visible.

You will:

  • Optimize inside sales workflows and improve lead conversion rates
  • Build and manage digital marketing campaigns (SEM/PPC, SEO, paid and organic social media on platforms like Google, Meta, LinkedIn, TikTok)
  • Leverage AI tools extensively to work smarter and faster (e.g., AI for ad copy, content creation, audience targeting, automation, analytics, A/B testing)
  • Experiment rapidly, measure results, and iterate to drive measurable revenue growth

Who We're Looking For:

  • Recent graduate (2024–2026) in Digital Marketing, Business/Commerce, Data Science/Analytics, Computer Science, Communications, or related fields
  • Native proficiency with AI tools and a track record of using them to achieve results (show us examples!)
  • Self-starter who learns quickly on your own time—prerequisite skills (e.g., Google Ads, SEO basics, CRM tools, design platforms) should be acquired independently before applying
  • Results-obsessed: you understand that impact > hours worked
  • Highly driven, entrepreneurial mindset—excited by the chance to build something meaningful and grow rapidly with the company

Compensation & Structure

  • Base pay: $20–$25 per hour (part-time/flexible hours to start, reflecting the performance-driven nature of the role and entry-level experience)
  • Performance-based incentives: Uncapped commissions and bonuses directly tied to clear, achievable quotas and metrics (e.g., lead volume/generated, conversion rates, attributable revenue growth above baseline)
  • Expected total earnings: We fully expect strong performers to reach $100,000+ equivalent in the first year by meeting these targets—with exceptional results driving even higher compensation
  • This role is designed for rapid impact: The initial phase is flexible and trial-oriented, allowing you to prove results quickly on your schedule. Those who meet expectations will transition rapidly to full-time hours, higher base pay, and leadership opportunities
  • Success here means delivering measurable outcomes—if targets aren't met, the role (and compensation) won't align long-term. We're seeking confident, results-obsessed individuals excited by this high-expectation, high-reward structure

If you're a recent grad who sees this as a once-in-a-lifetime launchpad and is ready to prove yourself through results, we want to hear from you.

To Apply: Send your resume, a brief cover letter explaining why this opportunity excites you, and 1–2 examples of how you've used AI to achieve a concrete result (personal projects welcome).
