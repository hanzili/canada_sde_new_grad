Full Description

Employment Opportunity Junior Equipment Sales Associate

Brooks, Alberta

WHO WE ARE:

For over 50 years, Vanee Farm Centre Inc. has been Southern Alberta's trusted agricultural equipment dealer. We are more than a dealership—we are a partner to producers, delivering top-tier products, unmatched service, and reliable aftermarket support.

At Vanee, we believe in growing talent from within. Your career progression is built on performance, initiative, and teamwork. If you’re looking for a career where you can make an impact and build lasting relationships with customers, we want to hear from you.

THE OPPORTUNITY:

We are seeking a Junior Equipment Sales Associate to join our Brooks branch. This entry-level sales role will focus primarily on zero-turn mowers, turf equipment and Bobcat equipment, while providing hands-on training and development towards agricultural equipment sales.

What You’ll Do

· Sales & Promotion

o Sell zero turn mowers, turf equipment and Bobcat compact equipment.

o Assist with walk-in customers, inquires and product recommendations.

o Conduct outbound calls with past and prospective customers to identify equipment needs, upgrades, and trade-in opportunities.

o Support promotions, seasonal campaigns and in-store displays.

o Assist with product demonstrations and sales campaigns.

· Customer Service & Relationship Building

o Act as an ambassador for Vanee Farm Centre, representing our brand with professionalism and care.

o Build long-term relationships with residential, acreage and light commercial customers.

o Follow up on leads and ensure a positive customer experience.

· Product Knowledge

o Develop a strong knowledge of turf and compact equipment product lines.

o Learn core sales skills, financing options and equipment applications.

o Support customers with informed advice tailored to their operation.

o Begin building fundamental knowledge of agricultural equipment and customer needs.

· Operational Support

o Assist with deliveries, appraisals, and yard organization.

o Provide sales support to the Sales Manager and team as required.

o Maintain showroom and equipment presentation standards.

What We’re Looking For

This role is best suited for someone with:

· Interested in building a career in equipment or agricultural sales.

· Strong communication and relationship-building skills.

· Initiative, self-motivation, and drive to achieve sales goals.

· Ability to work both independently and within a team.

· Mechanically inclined or interested in equipment.

· Flexibility to occasionally work extended hours or travel to other branch locations.

Are you a good match? Apply today!
