What will you do?

Siemens Canada Digital Industries is seeking a dynamic and results-driven Marketing and Communications Coordinator (summer student) to join our team. This individual will play a crucial role in supporting the development and execution of marketing and communication initiatives, ensuring alignment with Siemens' business objectives.

The successful candidate will be a versatile professional with a passion for marketing, excellent communication skills, and a keen understanding of digital trends.

Responsibilities

  • Develop compelling and engaging content for various marketing channels, including social media, websites, and collateral materials.
  • Collaborate with internal stakeholders to gather information and insights for content creation.
  • Ensure brand consistency and adherence to Siemens' messaging guidelines.

Digital Marketing

  • Assist in the planning and execution of digital marketing campaigns, including email marketing, social media advertising, and content distribution.
  • Monitor and analyze digital performance metrics to optimize campaigns and enhance online presence.

Event Coordination

  • Sales, Partner, and Lead Management Support
  • Support sales and partners to ensure they are equipped with appropriate demos and marketing collateral.
  • Assist in managing and tracking marketing-generated leads, including lead entry, follow-up coordination, and reporting.

What will you need to succeed?

  • Working towards a Bachelor's degree in Marketing, Communications, Business Administration or a related field.
  • Creative thinker with a proactive approach to problem-solving.
  • Detail-oriented and organized with the ability to manage multiple projects simultaneously.
  • Team player who can collaborate effectively with cross-functional teams.
  • Adaptable to a fast-paced and dynamic work environment.
  • Passion for technology and innovation.

Hourly rate is commensurate with experience, and ranges between $ 22.70 CAD - $ 26.40 CAD.

About us.

We share our ideas and champion the people behind them.

Siemens Canada is a leading technology company focused on industry, infrastructure, mobility and healthcare. The company’s purpose to is to create technology with purpose, transforming the everyday, for everyone, since 1912. By combining the real and the digital worlds, Siemens empowers its customers to accelerate their digital and sustainability transformations, making factories more efficient, cities more liveable, and transportation more sustainable. Siemens also owns a majority stake in the publicly listed company Siemens Healthineers, a leading global medical technology provider pioneering breakthroughs in healthcare. For everyone. Everywhere. Sustainably.
In fiscal 2024, which ended September 30, 2024, Siemens Canada had revenues of approx. $2.2 billion CAD. The company has approximately 4,400 employees from coast-to-coast and 37 office and production facilities across Canada.
