About Milestone Marketing Inc.

Full Description

Job Overview

We’re on the lookout for a driven and energetic Sales Representative to join our fast-growing team. In this role, you’ll play a key part in expanding our customer base, nurturing existing client relationships, and contributing to the company’s overall success. We are an authorized retailer for Rogers Telecom.

This role is ideal for individuals who are hungry for personal and professional growth — those who set big goals, chase results, and are constantly looking to level up. If you're someone who wants more than just a paycheck and is ready to build a career with unlimited potential, we want to meet you.

What You’ll Be Doing

  • Build lasting relationships with clients by providing value and support
  • Identify potential customers and turn conversations into closed deals
  • Present our products and services in a clear, compelling way
  • Stay ahead of industry trends and competitor activity
  • Collaborate with marketing to boost exposure
  • Use customer feedback to help us refine and improve our offerings
  • Maintain organized records of your pipeline and activities using CRM tools
  • Set personal goals and consistently push to exceed them

What We’re Looking For

  • Sales experience (field, telesales, or retail — especially in tech or services)
  • Strong interpersonal skills and the ability to connect with people easily
  • A natural closer who enjoys the thrill of winning
  • Sharp decision-making skills backed by data and intuition
  • Experience finding and converting new leads into long-term customers
  • Goal-oriented mindset with a relentless drive to improve and succeed
  • Self-disciplined and capable of managing your own schedule effectively
  • Strong communicator who thrives in a team setting

Why This Role is Different

  • No cap on commissions — the more you produce, the more you earn
  • Clear path to leadership roles and personal development
  • Supportive team culture with ongoing training and coaching
  • Performance-based recognition and incentive programs
  • Work in a fast-paced environment surrounded by like-minded go-getters

This is more than just a job — it’s a launchpad.
We’re building a team of high-performers who are ready to grow, lead, and make an impact. If you’re ready to invest in yourself and join a company that invests in you — apply now.

Take the first step today. Let’s build something great together.
