Full Description

Company Overview

Bayside Geoscience Inc. specializes in helping companies within the mineral and exploration industry unlock the potential of their projects. We are committed to connecting talented professionals with the right opportunities, fostering rewarding careers, and supporting innovative exploration efforts.

Summary

We are seeking a Junior Geologist to join our team in Canada. This role is vital in supporting our exploration projects through data collection, analysis, and fieldwork, contributing directly to our mission of discovering mineral potential.
Responsibilities

  • Conduct geological fieldwork and collect samples to support exploration activities, inclusive of diamond drilling, field mapping, surface sampling and re-logging.
  • Analyze geological data using GIS and ArcGIS tools to identify promising sites.
  • Ensuring best practices for logging, sampling, and database management
  • Assist in preparing technical reports and documentation.
  • Collaborate with senior geologists and other team members on project planning.
  • Utilize data analysis skills to interpret findings and support decision-making.
  • Support research initiatives related to mineral exploration.
  • Maintain accurate records of field activities and data collected.
  • Contribute to a safe and efficient work environment during field operations.
  • Qualifications
  • Bachelor's degree in Geology or related field.
  • Proficiency in GIS, ArcGIS, and data analysis tools.
  • Strong research skills and attention to detail.
  • Excellent communication skills and ability to work both independently and as part of a team.

Ready to make your mark in the mineral exploration industry? Join Bayside Geoscience Inc. as a Junior Geologist and be part of exciting discoveries that shape the future of resource development.

Pay: $35.00-$45.00 per hour
