Accounting / FP&A Internship

Intern, Accounting/Finance (Summer 2026)

at Bombardier

📍 Location Dorval, Quebec Hybrid
📋 Details Internship · 8 months · N/A · Summer 2026
📅 Posted Feb 25, 2026

About the Role

Prepare month-end reports, reconciliations, and journal entries

About Bombardier

Major Canadian aerospace company; strong internship program

Full Description

Innovation is in our DNA… is it in yours? Bombardier is a global leader in aviation, creating innovative and game-changing planes. Our products and services provide world-class experiences that set new standards in passenger comfort, energy efficiency, reliability and safety. We are a global organization focused on working together with a team spirit.

Your boarding pass will include… Several conferences, including:

  • Meet An Executive
  • Women Taking Flight

Learning more about Bombardier, including:

  • Bombardier Products conference
  • Visits of the Bombardier sites
  • Bombardier Academy of Learning

Many social/networking opportunities, including:

  • Volunteering
  • Networking for Success
  • 5 à 7, Potluck, and much more!

Team Description Our team is made up of financial analysts who enjoy combining rigorous work with fun, guided by an energetic manager, all collaborating in a positive and constructive environment. We are located at the Bombardier plant in Ville Saint-Laurent. Moreover, in 2026, we will be the first plant to implement SAP S4 HANA. So many exciting challenges await you!”

During your internship, you will contribute to :

  • Prepare month end reports and support related analysis
  • Produce monthly account reconciliations and journal entries
  • Prepare supporting documentation for internal & external audits
  • Maintain databases and Excel files (Production cost details etc.)
  • Provide support on special projects (for example the implementation of PowerBI) and ad hoc requests
  • Tasks will evolve to meet students’ abilities.

How to thrive in this role?

  • You are currently completing a Bachelor’s degree in Accounting (or equivalent) OR a CPA Specialized Graduate Diploma (DESS)
  • If you are currently enrolled in a Bachelor’s degree, you have completed a minimum of 2 semesters of studies in this program
  • You have a good level of written and speaking French and English
  • You have good organizational & planning skills
  • You have good computer skills (Windows and MS Office suite), including a strong knowledge of Excel, Access, and Power Point (PowerBI an asset)
  • You possess good judgement and are a good team worker in a multidisciplinary team

Boarding Information:

  • Location: Administrative Centre (CA)
  • Duration: 8 months
  • Flexible workplace-Hybrid

It is important to note that our internship opportunities are open to students only, not new graduates. All our interns may be required to occasionally travel outside of Canada for training/work purpose.
