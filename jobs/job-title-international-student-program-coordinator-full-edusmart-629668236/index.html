Full Description

About EduSmarts

EduSmarts is a premier education consultancy based in Coquitlam, BC, dedicated to redefining the "Early Study Abroad" experience. We manage comprehensive long-term guardianship programs and seasonal (Summer/Winter) school camps for elementary and secondary students. We are looking for a dynamic, passionate, and career-driven individual to join our growing team and help shape the future of our students.

Key Responsibilities

  • Student Mentorship: Provide academic guidance and holistic life management for G5-G12 students.
  • Parent Consultation: Act as a bridge between the school, students, and parents, providing regular updates and curriculum management.
  • Daily Logistics: Facilitate safe transportation for school drop-offs/pick-ups and extracurricular activities.
  • Operations & Admin: Support the planning and execution of educational programs and general administrative tasks.

Required Qualifications

  • Education: Bachelor’s Degree or higher (Graduates from Canadian or US universities preferred).
  • Language: Native or near-native fluency in both English and Korean (Professional communication skills are essential).
  • Legal Status: Must be legally entitled to work in Canada (Citizen, PR, or valid Work Permit).
  • Technical Skills: Proficient in MS Office (Excel, Word, PowerPoint).
  • Driving: Valid BC Driver’s License with a clean driving record.

Who You Are

  • An energetic professional who thrives in a fast-paced, youth-oriented environment.
  • A responsible individual with a genuine love for mentoring children and teenagers.
  • A team player looking for long-term career growth in the education industry.
  • Note: We are looking for candidates who can bring youthful energy and a modern perspective to our dynamic team culture.

Why Join Us?

  • Competitive Compensation: Salary is negotiable based on experience and qualifications.
  • Growth Opportunity: We provide a clear career path to becoming an education specialist.
  • Supportive Culture: Work in a horizontal, collaborative, and warm team environment.
  • Stability: Long-term employment in a stable and growing company.
