Full Description

Dayforce, a global leader in Human Capital Management (HCM) with headquarters in Toronto, Ontario, and Minneapolis, Minnesota, operates across North America, EMEA, and APJ regions. Our Cloud HCM platform, recognized for its unified database and continuous calculation engine, enhances efficiency, productivity, and compliance for global workforces. We are committed to "Making Work Life Better™" for employees, customers, and partners worldwide. As an Intern at Dayforce, you’ll gain real-world experience with meaningful responsibilities, access to development programs, and the chance to collaborate globally, bringing new ideas and optimizing processes across multiple business units. Job Title: Market Analytics & Intelligence Intern

Team: Market Positioning & Strategy Team (Market Sensing)

Location : Virtual

Duration: Summer 2026 - 8 months (May 2026 – December 2026)

Availability : *Full-time availability of 37.5 – 40 hours weekly is required to be eligible for this opportunity. Benefits For Students

  • Experience working for one of the fastest growing Human Capital Management technology companies in the world Access to Dayforce’s development programs and resources
  • Ability to work with as well as create relationships with members across the globe
  • Autonomy to bring forth new ideas and optimize existing structures within the organization
  • Meaningful responsibilities, enabling you to obtain ‘real-world’ experience
  • Dynamic hands-on exposure to multiple business-units and stakeholders across Dayforce

About The Opportunity Market sensing is a newly formed innovative capability within the Market Positioning and Strategy team, unique to Dayforce in the HCM market. The market sensing model combines and matches internal and market data, structured and unstructured, into meaningful insights to drive positioning and inform corporate, business, and functional strategies. Next to always-on insights through the market sensing portal, productized market sensing insights are communicated through regular publications across functions (sales, product, marketing) and partners. In practical terms, this means helping teams across Dayforce better understand market trends, customer perceptions, and competitive dynamics through data, dashboards, and insights.

The intern will play a critical part in scaling and extending our market sensing model by adding new sources and evolving our visualizations and data products, to make market sensing more relevant and impactful to our internal partners.

What You’ll Get To Do

  • Support ongoing data updates by working with sources such as user reviews, analyst insights, surveys, and win-loss interviews, while learning how data is collected, cleaned, and mapped at scale.
  • Help build and enhance data visualizations that turn complex data into clear, actionable insights, contributing to dashboards and tools used across the business (including the Market Sensing portal).
  • Create visualizations and explore the use of AI and custom GPT tools to tailor insights for different teams such as sales, product, marketing, and strategy.
  • Assist with the preparation of regular reports and insight summaries shared with internal stakeholders.

Skills And Experience We Value

  • Currently enrolled in a university or college program in Business Analytics, Information Systems, Data Science, Business/Commerce, Economics, or related fields
  • Ownership and x-functional collaboration mindset
  • Passion for using data to understand trends and support decision-making
  • Foundational knowledge of data extraction, analysis, mapping, and related automation tools.
  • Proficiency with Excel (lookups, pivots, data cleaning).

  • Interest or experience in data visualization using tools such as Power BI, as well as curiosity about LLM/AI tools and data storytelling.

  • Familiarity with Power BI concepts (datasets, refreshes, basic measures/filters) and data QA best practices.

  • Working knowledge of SQL fundamentals (e.g., joining multiple tables together, using the “where” clause to filter data) to support data extraction and analysis from structured datasets.

What Would Really Make You Stand Out

  • Understanding of/interest in market intelligence in technology providers
  • Understanding of/interest in the HCM technology market
