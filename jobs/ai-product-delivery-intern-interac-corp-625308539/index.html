About the Role

About Interac Corp.

Canada's trusted digital payments network.

Full Description

Who We Are: Every transaction matters. Every Canadian matters. At Interac, we protect both — driving trust, security, and inclusion, so our digital economy thrives.

Founded in 1984, Interac connects Canadians through secure digital payments, advanced identity verification and industry-leading fraud protection. Connecting banks, businesses, and individuals, Interac enables millions to send, receive, and manage money safely and effortlessly every day — across both digital and physical environments.

As the backbone of Canada’s financial ecosystem, Interac facilitates over 20 million transactions daily, supported by trusted partnerships with government and financial institutions. Consistently ranked as Canada’s most reputable financial technology brand, Interac is deeply embedded in the daily lives of Canadians.

Who You Will Work With: The Data Strategy and Delivery team is part of the broader Data & Analytics (D&A) function. This team works closely with various departments, including marketing, IT, and customer service, to deliver data-driven insights and data products. The team is made up of several functions and squads that collaborate to manage data as an asset, enable business self-service, and facilitate a data-driven culture. An intern in the Data Strategy and Delivery team would have a dynamic and engaging role, contributing to various data-driven projects and initiatives. Here are some key responsibilities and tasks that an intern might undertake

What You Will Do:

  • Lead the requirements gathering workshops by hosting stakeholder interviews and workshops
  • Ask thoughtful questions to clarify user needs, assumptions, and constraints
  • Document requirements, user flows, and basic success criteria
  • Help prototype AI solutions and gather user feedback Contributing to data projects by collaborating with internal data and analytics consumers to deliver end-to-end data-related projects (includes understanding project requirements, analyzing data, and providing actionable insights)
  • Support Squad leaders in understanding business needs and defining scope and requirements
  • Support the implementation of pilots in the Data analytics and innovation space
  • Performing collaborative research by engaging in collaborative research efforts, providing deliverables such as research insights, data assets, and visualizations to help end-users develop a better understanding of various topics.

What You Bring:

  • Basic understanding of LLMs, prompts, agents, and workflows
  • Familiarity with APIs, data sources, and system integrations
  • Ability to prototype solutions quickly and iterate based on feedback
  • You are currently enrolled in a university or college program in a relevant discipline, like Data science, computer science, cybersecurity or IT
  • Proven experience in data analysis, project management and/or business analyst
  • Understanding of cloud infrastructure, data engineering and data management technologies
  • Strong collaborative skills to work effectively with fraud teams and other stakeholders.
  • Experience in data analysis, statistical modeling, and feature development within relevant industries
  • Proficiency in SQL and Python for data manipulation and analysis
  • Strong collaborative skills to work effectively with other teams and other stakeholders
  • You have excellent communication skills to document requirements and workflows clearly
  • Have strong attention to detail to improve analysis and data accuracy
  • Although not required, you have some familiarity or previous experience with the following:
  • Understanding of the payments/banking industry

What We’re Offering: The hourly pay range for this position is $21 to $23, and the internship term runs from May to August 2026. The exact amount will depend on factors such as skills, experience, and job-related knowledge. An internship opportunity at Interac also features:

  • Flexible hybrid work model with monthly intern socials for better work-life balance
  • Interac learning bootcamp designed to help you hit the ground running
  • Convenient downtown Toronto office located minutes away from Union Station

Why Join Us? At Interac, the impact we make, and the people who drive it, is profound. When you become part of our team, you’re joining a purpose-driven organization that’s shaping the future of digital finance in Canada. Here’s what you can expect:

  • Investing in the Future – Help us unlock digital prosperity for all Canadians.
  • Innovative Thinking – Collaborate on products, practices, and platforms that redefine what’s possible.
  • Inclusive Culture – Be empowered to bring your whole self to work and realize your full potential.
  • Inspiring Community – Work in an ecosystem where we lift each other up and rise together.
  • Intentional Support – Enjoy flexible, supportive offerings that prioritize your total wellness.
