Junior Accountant

About the Role

About Datametrex

Toronto-based tech company; growing finance team

Full Description

COMPANY PROFILE:

Datametrex AI Limited is a Toronto‑based public technology company specializing in artificial intelligence (AI), machine learning, cybersecurity, and healthcare solutions. As an innovative leader in data‑driven technologies and mission‑critical operational platforms, Datametrex is committed to building cutting‑edge tools that drive efficiency, insights, and real‑world impact across diverse industries.

SUMMARY:

We are seeking a motivated and detail-oriented Junior Accountant to join our finance team. The ideal candidate will assist with day-to-day accounting tasks, ensure accuracy in financial records, and support month-end and year-end closing processes. This is an excellent opportunity for individuals looking to build a career in accounting.

ESSENTIAL DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES (included, but not limited to):

  • Perform general daily accounting functions and bookkeeping using QuickBooks. Tasks include generating the daily bank report, posting account transactions, and journal entries.
  • Conduct month-end reconciliations to ensure accuracy and completeness of financial records.
  • Assist in payroll administration for both employees and contractors, including all year-end requirements.
  • Support in conducting Fiscal Year End Audits by providing necessary documentation and assistance.
  • Complete various other accounting-related tasks as needed.
  • Reconciling bank accounts, credit cards, and accounts receivable/payable balances.
  • Recording daily revenue recognition entries in the journal.
  • Manages government filings including HST, EHT, WSIB, payroll remittances, and T4 forms.

REQUIRED KNOWLEDGE, SKILLS & ABILITIES:

  • Bachelor’s degree in Accounting, Finance, or related field
  • 1–2 years of accounting or finance experience
  • Experience in quick books online
  • Strong technical skills and knowledge in Microsoft Office: Excel, Word, Outlook
  • Excellent communicator and strong attention to detail
  • Positive attitude and team player
  • Flexibility to assist with various tasks within the accounting environment
  • Ability to self-manage and take initiative
  • Bilingual English/Korean is preferred
