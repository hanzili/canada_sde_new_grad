About Us

Morson Edge is part of the Morson Group, a global technical recruitment and engineering services business with over 50 years of industry expertise.

Operating across Canada and internationally, Morson Edge delivers specialist talent solutions across engineering, energy, infrastructure, aerospace, and advanced manufacturing sectors. With a strong presence in the Power Generation market, we partner with utilities, EPC firms, OEMs, and renewable energy developers to support critical infrastructure projects.

Due to continued growth, we are seeking a motivated Junior Recruiter to join our Toronto team. This is an excellent opportunity for a recent graduate to launch a career in technical recruitment with full training, structured development, and support from an established, industry-leading team.

The Role

As a Junior Recruiter specialising in Engineering within the Power Generation sector, you will play a key role in identifying and engaging top engineering talent. You will work closely with senior consultants and hiring managers to support recruitment campaigns across roles such as:

  • Mechanical Engineers
  • Electrical Engineers
  • Power Systems Engineers
  • Project Engineers
  • Commissioning Engineers
  • Plant Operations & Maintenance professionals

You will gain hands-on exposure to recruitment strategy, candidate management, and client engagement within a highly technical and fast-moving industry.

Key Responsibilities

  • Source and identify engineering professionals using job boards, LinkedIn, and internal databases
  • Screen and interview candidates to assess technical skills and cultural fit
  • Build and maintain strong talent pipelines within the Power Generation sector
  • Support senior recruiters with job briefings and candidate shortlists
  • Coordinate interviews and manage candidate communications
  • Maintain accurate records within the company CRM/ATS
  • Develop market knowledge of engineering disciplines and power infrastructure projects
  • Contribute to team targets and business development initiatives
