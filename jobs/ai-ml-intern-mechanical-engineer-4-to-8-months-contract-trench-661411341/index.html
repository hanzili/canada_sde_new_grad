About us

In Trench Canada, we deliver state-of-the-art, safe, and reliable instrument transformers, coils, and bushings for seamless energy transmission. With a legacy of customized engineering and innovation, we enable society to harness the power of electricity with reliability and performance. But we are more than just a company.

We are a force for change, committed to advancing the energy sector through cutting-edge technology and sustainable practices. We genuinely care about continuous improvement, and our dynamic workplace values attention to detail and adaptability, fostering a culture of equality, diversity, and inclusion where everyone is respected and truly valued.

Overview

Are you passionate about AI/ML and have hands-on experience in practical applications? Join us in revolutionizing the energy industry! We're forming a special task force to identify efficiency needs from various perspectives and develop cutting-edge solutions using AI and data management. Be a part of Project NOVA (Next-gen Optimization with Visionary AI) and work directly with our CEO and senior leadership team, enjoying full freedom to innovate.

Help bridge the gap between engineering and AI innovation in Project NOVA. As an AI & ML Mechanical Engineer Intern, you’ll apply your technical expertise to guide model selection and ensure AI solutions meet real-world needs.

Your Tasks:

  • Collaborate with cross-functional teams to identify AI/ML opportunities.
  • Design, build, and deploy AI/ML models.
  • Analyze large datasets to extract insights.
  • Document development processes and model results.
  • Present findings to stakeholders using visualizations and reports.
  • Stay updated with AI/ML advancements.
  • Bridge engineering and AI requirements.

Requirements:

  • Pursuing or recently completed a degree in Mechanical Engineering, Mechatronics, or related field.
  • Foundational understanding of finite element analysis — structural dynamics, modal analysis, eigenvalue problems, and mode shapes

  • Proficient in Python with hands-on experience using NumPy and SciPy for numerical data processing

  • Experience with 3D visualization libraries — PyVista, Matplotlib, or Plotly

  • OpenFOAM or fluid-structure interaction experience is a bonus but not required

  • Coding experience (Python or similar).
  • Applicants must submit their Github profiles alongside resumes.
