Full Description

About the Role

We are seeking a motivated and detail-oriented Entry-Level Structural Engineer (EIT) to join our growing team. This role offers the opportunity to gain hands-on experience in structural design, drafting, and project coordination across a variety of building and infrastructure projects. You will work closely with senior engineers and multidisciplinary teams, contributing to projects from concept through construction.

Key Responsibilities

Structural Design & Analysis

  • Assist in the design and analysis of structural systems including steel, reinforced concrete, and masonry structures with the support of Finite Element analysis software such as Risa3D
  • Perform design calculations for trusses, beams, columns, and foundations in accordance with Canadian design standards
  • Support the design of structural connections, including bolted and welded steel connections
  • Develop and review shop drawings to ensure it resembles structural design intent.
  • Prepare and revise structural drawings and details using Revit and AutoCAD

Project Coordination

  • Assist in coordinating project deliverables, schedules, and documentation
  • Participate in internal design reviews and project meetings
  • Support site visits, inspections, and construction administration tasks as required

Qualifications

  • Bachelor’s/Master’s degree in Civil or Structural Engineering
  • Registered or eligible for registration as an Engineer-in-Training (EIT) in a Canadian province
  • Strong understanding of structural engineering fundamentals

Technical Skills

  • Familiarity with Canadian codes and standards, including:
  • National Building Code of Canada (NBCC)
  • CSA S16 (Design of Steel Structures)
  • CSA A23.3 (Design of Concrete Structures)
  • CSA A23.1/A23.2 (Concrete Materials and Methods)
  • Basic knowledge of structural analysis software (e.g., Risa3D, ETABS, SAP2000, STAAD, or similar)
  • Proficiency in AutoCAD and/or Revit is an asset
  • Experience with connection design concepts is an asset
