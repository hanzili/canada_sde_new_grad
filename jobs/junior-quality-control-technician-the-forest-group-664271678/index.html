Full Description

Junior Quality Control Technician

Company Overview
The Forest Group is a vertically integrated and diversified group of companies providing exterior construction material supply and services across the Greater Toronto Area. With over 30 years of experience in asphalt paving, concrete, and landscape construction, Forest is proud to be recognized as one of Canada’s Best Managed Companies.

Position Summary

We are seeking a reliable and detail-oriented Junior Quality Control Technician to support our Quality Control operations. This role is primarily field-based and involves collecting samples of concrete, asphalt, and other construction materials from active job sites, as well as assisting with testing and reporting.
This is an excellent opportunity for someone looking to start a career in construction quality control, materials testing, or civil engineering.

Key Responsibilities

  • Collect field samples of concrete, asphalt, and aggregates from job sites across the GTA
  • Perform basic on-site testing including slump, air, temperature, and density testing
  • Transport samples to the lab for further testing and analysis
  • Accurately record test results and maintain proper documentation
  • Assist the QC Manager with inspections and quality assurance processes
  • Ensure all testing is conducted in accordance with applicable standards and specifications
  • Maintain and clean testing equipment
  • Support other QC-related tasks as required

Qualifications

  • Post-secondary education in Civil Engineering Technology, Construction Engineering, or a related field is an asset
  • Previous construction or field experience is an asset but not required
  • Strong attention to detail and willingness to learn
  • Ability to work outdoors in varying weather conditions
  • Valid G driver’s license is required
  • Ability to work flexible hours, including early mornings and extended shifts during peak season

What We’re Looking For

  • Someone dependable who shows up and takes ownership of their work
  • Strong work ethic and ability to work independently in the field
  • Interest in construction materials, testing, and quality control
  • A candidate looking to grow into a long-term role within the company

Why Join Forest Group

  • Hands-on experience across multiple divisions (asphalt, concrete, civil construction)
  • Clear path for growth within Quality Control and Operations
  • Work alongside experienced industry professionals
  • Competitive compensation and long-term opportunity
