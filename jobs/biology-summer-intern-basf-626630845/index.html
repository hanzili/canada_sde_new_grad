Full Description

Biology Summer Intern – Grow Your Future with BASF Come create chemistry with us! At BASF Agricultural Solutions, we’re not just about crops—we’re about cultivating innovation, sustainability, and the next generation of leaders. As a Biology Intern, you’ll be part of a team that’s shaping the future of agriculture while building your own career foundation

Internship Details:

  • Duration: 4 months (Summer 2026)
  • Locations: Saskatoon
  • Compensation: $24 - $31 / Hour

Why Join Us? This isn’t your average internship. At BASF, you’ll:

  • Make an Impact: Contribute to real-world agricultural solutions through hands-on field research.
  • Build Your Network: Engage with mentors, managers, and peers across the business.
  • Learn by Doing: Plan and execute small plot field experiments—seed, spray, evaluate, harvest, and present results.
  • Grow Your Career: Explore career tracks and present your research through field tours or summary presentations.
  • Have Fun: Work in a collaborative team atmosphere with opportunities for personal and professional development.

What You’ll Learn:

  • Trial methodology and crop production techniques.
  • Pest identification and management.
  • Operation of small plot equipment.
  • Communication, leadership, and decision-making skills.
  • Safe and healthy work practices in agricultural research.

What You’ll Do:

  • Support the research program to develop agricultural solutions for Canadian growers.
  • Plan and execute field experiments at BASF research farms.
  • Summarize and present research findings.
  • Collaborate with a hardworking and detail-oriented team.

What We’re Looking For:

  • Currently enrolled in an agriculture science or related degree/diploma program (e.g. environmental science, biology, agronomy, soil science).
  • Enthusiastic and committed to gaining experience in field research.
  • Strong communication and leadership skills.
  • Understanding of agricultural practices and farm equipment is an asset.
  • Valid driver’s licence required.

To be eligible for this internship, you must be currently enrolled in a post-secondary program. We do not accept applications from students who have already graduated. Ready to grow with us? Apply now and let’s shape the future of agriculture—together.

Create your own chemistry with you@BASF Adding value to our customers begins with adding value to you. you@BASF is the suite of benefits, perks, programs and unique opportunities we offer to support you – the whole you – in all stages of your life and career.
