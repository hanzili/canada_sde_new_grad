Full Description

We are seeking a detail-oriented and motivated Junior Accountant to join our client's finance team.

Agilus is recruiting for a Junior Accountant in the retail industry in Vancouver, British Columbia. This role is ideal for individuals early in their accounting career who are looking to gain hands-on experience in financial reporting, reconciliations, and general accounting operations. The Junior Accountant will support daily accounting activities and ensure accurate financial record-keeping.

Responsibilities

  • Assist with day-to-day accounting tasks, including journal entries and general ledger maintenance
  • Prepare and process accounts payable and accounts receivable transactions
  • Perform bank and account reconciliations
  • Support the month-end and year-end close process
  • Assist in preparing financial statements and reports
  • Handling intercompany transactions
  • Maintain and update financial records and documentation

Successful Candidates Will Have

  • 2+ years of experience in the accounting field
  • Bachelor’s degree or diploma in Accounting, Finance, or a related field
  • Intermediate MS Excel user
  • Experience working with multiple currencies highly desirable
  • Experience handling accounting duties for multiple companies at a time will be an asset

Total Rewards

  • $50000.00 - $55000.00 annually
  • Permanent position
  • Monday to Friday in office (9-5:30pm)
  • Free street parking near office
  • Competitive salary based on experience
  • Supportive team environment

Soft Skills

  • Excellent communication (written and verbal) skills
  • Strong attention to detail and organizational skills
  • Good analytical and problem-solving abilities
  • Ability to work independently and as part of a team
  • Can-do attitude with the ability to juggle priorities and be a team player
