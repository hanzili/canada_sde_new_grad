Social Media & Content Intern

at Formme

About the Role

Builds social channels; creates and edits content.

About Formme

Early-stage fashion platform linking indie brands and manufacturers

Full Description

ABOUT FORMME

Formme is an early-stage platform solving one of fashion's most frustrating problems: connecting independent brands to vetted manufacturers. We handle matching and real-time production tracking — all in one place. We're live, we have paying customers, and we're growing fast.

This is a fast-moving team building something that genuinely hasn't existed before in the

Canadian fashion space. Every week looks different. You'll have a front-row seat to what

it actually takes to build a startup from zero.

THE ROLE

What you'll own

  • Build and manage Formme's Instagram and TikTok presence — content calendar, posting cadence, creative direction.
  • Shoot, edit, and produce short-form video content (Reels, TikToks) showcasing the behind-the-scenes of fashion production.
  • Write captions and craft posts that speak directly to independent brand founders — clear, human, and scroll-stopping.
  • Engage authentically with fashion communities — comment sections, DMs, relevant threads — not just as promotion but as a real presence.
  • Track what's working. Report weekly on growth, reach, and engagement — and use that data to iterate.
  • Bring ideas. If you see a trend, a creator, a format that fits — pitch it. Speed of iteration is our competitive advantage.

What we're looking for

  • You live on Instagram and TikTok and genuinely understand what makes content perform in those environments.
  • You have some experience creating content — doesn't have to be professional.
  • Your own account, a school project, a side hustle — show us your work.
  • You're interested in fashion, startups, or both. You don't need to be an expert, but you need to care.
  • You can move fast and figure things out independently. We don't have a playbook yet — you're writing it.
  • You communicate clearly and follow through. In a small team, reliability matters more than a perfect portfolio.

What you get

  • Real portfolio work. You'll have full creative ownership over a brand's social presence from day one — not a side task, not an internship assignment.
  • Startup exposure. You'll see how a real company is built — GTM strategy, customer conversations, product decisions.
  • Fashion industry network. Direct access to independent brand founders, manufacturers, and fashion insiders across Canada.
  • A reference that means something. We'll go to bat for you — letters, LinkedIn, introductions — when you're ready to use this experience.
  • First-mover advantage. As Formme grows, the people who showed up early are the ones we'll think of first for paid roles, advisory positions, or future opportunities.
