About Walton Systems

Full Description

NO BUREAUCRACY, MICROMANAGING OR POLITICS

We're a growing tech startup in Montreal, and we're building out our marketing engineering service for financial institutions and fintechs. We need your help scaling it to $1 million ARR by the end of 2026.

YOUR EXPERIENCE

What you will be working on:

  • Workflow design & automation
  • Data orchestration & engineering
  • Event-based architectures
  • Back-end development

To excel:

  • Familiar with banking models and concepts
  • Great communication skills in English
  • Comfortable wearing many hats

OUR CULTURE

We offer lots of time for deep work

We offer consistent constructive feedback

We offer an environment to ship and grow quickly

We don’t offer mentorship

We don’t offer lots of meetings

We don’t hold back if we think something is wrong

OUR HIRING PROCESS

Application

We're looking to see how your skills and experience align with our needs.

Culture interview (45-min video call)

This interview is meant to help us understand how you work, solve problems, handle feedback, stay motivated, and adapt in a fast-moving environment. It also gives you space to ask questions and see whether our culture and expectations align with what you’re looking for.

Technical interview (45 minutes, varies by role)

You'll meet the founder for a technical interview, he will evaluate skills needed to be successful in your role.

Offer
