Full Description

Job Title: Junior/Associate Controller

Location: On‑site (Two Businesses)
Employment Type: Full‑Time
Reports To: Senior Controller / Ownership

About the Role

We are seeking a motivated, tech‑savvy Junior/Associate Controller to oversee the financial operations of two businesses:

  • A home building & construction company (4 employees)
  • A cattle farming operation (4 employees)

This role is ideal for someone entrepreneurial, organized, and eager to take ownership of all financial and administrative functions while working closely with a senior controller.

Key Responsibilities

Financial Management & Bookkeeping

  • Manage full‑cycle bookkeeping for both businesses.
  • Maintain the general ledger, chart of accounts, and all financial records.
  • Handle accounts payable and accounts receivable.
  • Reconcile bank accounts, credit cards, vendor accounts, and loan statements.
  • Manage cash flow tracking and provide weekly updates.

Payroll & Employee Administration

  • Process payroll for both businesses (hourly, salary, and any bonuses).
  • Track employee hours, deductions, and benefits.
  • Maintain payroll records and ensure compliance with government requirements.

Financial Reporting & Analysis

  • Prepare monthly, quarterly, and annual financial statements.
  • Manage job costing, project budgets, and cost tracking for the construction business.
  • Track cattle inventory, feed costs, purchase/sale records, and profitability for the farm.
  • Provide variance analysis and financial insights to ownership.

Legal & Administrative Paperwork

  • Prepare and file government remittances (HST, WSIB, payroll taxes, etc.).
  • Maintain corporate records and assist with compliance documentation.
  • Coordinate insurance renewals, vendor contracts, and corporate filings.
  • Ensure both businesses meet legal and reporting deadlines.

Operational Support & Systems Improvement

  • Work directly with the senior controller to streamline systems and improve internal controls.
  • Implement or optimize accounting software, digital tools, and automated workflows.
  • Support budgeting, forecasting, and annual planning.

What We’re Looking For

  • A self‑starter who takes initiative and thrives in an entrepreneurial environment.
  • Strong tech skills; comfortable learning new systems and improving processes.
  • Ability to manage responsibilities across two different businesses with minimal supervision.
  • High integrity, strong organization, and excellent attention to detail.

Qualifications

Required:

  • Post‑secondary education in Finance, Accounting, Business, or similar field.
  • Experience with bookkeeping, payroll, and financial reporting.
  • Strong Excel/Google Sheets skills.
  • Clear communication skills and ability to work with small teams.

Preferred:

  • Experience in construction accounting or agriculture/livestock operations.
  • Familiarity with QuickBooks
  • Knowledge of job costing or inventory accounting.

Why Join Us?

  • Direct mentorship from an experienced senior controller.
  • Hands‑on exposure to two unique small businesses.
  • Opportunity to build new systems and make a measurable impact.
  • Entrepreneurial environment with room for growth.

Pay: $72,249.29-$136,908.03 per year

Benefits:

  • Flexible schedule

Work Location: In person
