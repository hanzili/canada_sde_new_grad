About The Role ATB 101 is a student work experience and engagement program! Our ATB 101 students play an integral role in the success of our business. As an ATB 101 student you will have a unique opportunity to contribute to ATB through challenging, meaningful and diverse opportunities and make a positive impact for our clients and each other. ATB is more than just a bank and ATB 101 is more than just a student job. Do you want to have an opportunity to work with customers, businesses and industries that drive Alberta’s economy? Do you have a desire to understand the very core of our business and be part of the evolution of serving our customers? If so, ATB might be right for you.

Please see below to learn more about this great summer opportunity.

As ATB’s next Risk Analyst Summer Student , you are the first line of defense, fostering a culture of risk awareness in alignment with ATB Advisory Services' Risk Appetite Statement. The Risk Analyst will work within the Advisory Services Risk team and will collaborate with various stakeholders across Risk and ATB to help analyze and reduce existing manual risk reporting with automated solutions. .

Accountabilities

  • AppSheet Development for Loan Control Oversight: Assist with the development and implementation of AppSheet applications to automate and enhance loan control oversight processes, ensuring compliance and efficiency.
  • Process Automation and Capacity Building: Provide assistance in identifying and developing use of AI for other manual processes across the team to improve operational efficiency and free up team capacity.
  • Post funding file reviews: Provide oversight and reporting on client loan files to ensure that ATB Business is operating within our risk appetite

Skills, Experience & Requirements

  • Data Analysis and Visualization: Proven, strong understanding and proficiency in data analysis and visualization tools.
  • Software Expertise: Strong experience with Google AppSheet, Gemini, Tableau, and Google Workplace Suite
  • Data Transformation/Automation: Experience in designing and implementing solutions to automate data usage and move to robust data platforms.
  • Critical Thinking: Demonstrated ability to apply critical thinking skills.
  • Problem-Solving: Inquisitive nature, not afraid to challenge the status quo, and able to identify and develop solutions for manual processes.
  • Autonomy & Ownership: Self-starter who can own a project from requirements to delivery and complete tasks independently with minimal input from leadership.
  • Attention to Detail: High level of accuracy and focus on details.
  • Communication: Excellent communication skills.
  • Adaptability: Demonstrated capacity to handle multiple tasks and interruptions to ensure deadlines are meet.
  • Business Knowledge: Understanding of the business lending process and accounting principles would be an asset.
  • Mentorship: Ability to effectively mentor others on Appsheet and processes (as you will be transferring knowledge to the team).
