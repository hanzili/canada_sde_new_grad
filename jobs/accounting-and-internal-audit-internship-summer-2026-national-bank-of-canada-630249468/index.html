About the Role

Perform accounting analyses and internal audit tasks daily

About National Bank of Canada

National Bank of Canada; major Canadian bank

Full Description

An internship in accounting and internal audit at National Bank means contributing directly to financial discipline and the continuous improvement of our processes. This job allows you to make a positive impact with your knowledge of accounting, financial data analysis, and auditing.

Your role

  • Optimize analysis environments (SQL, SAP, Excel) by improving tools and developing dashboards.
  • Perform financial and operational analyses to support various business lines and simplify disclosure and management reporting.
  • Support the production of calculations related to hedge accounting, quarterly and annual reports.
  • Participate in internal audit mandates to evaluate processes
  • Communicate findings and contribute to the formulation of recommendations.
  • Contribute to financial audit and internal control work, including support to the external auditors in the audit of the Bank's financial statements.

Your team

The Vice-President, Finance, Accounting and Internal Audit means more specialists who work in an agile, proactive and collaborative manner to seize opportunities, stay on the cutting edge of financial practices and continuously improve processes.

Within the Accounting and Internal Audit sector, you are part of a large team of colleagues and report to an experienced manager who is committed to your development. Our team stands out for its rigor, collaborative spirit, intellectual curiosity and constant desire to simplify and innovate. We aim to offer you maximum flexibility to promote your quality of life: hybrid environment and flexible schedule according to the needs of the projects.

Internships are currently available in these teams:

  • Financial Markets and Subsidiary Accounting
  • Accounting, Consolidation and Disclosure
  • Internal Audit

Prerequisites

  • Be an undergraduate or graduate student in a field related to accounting or auditing.
  • Be proficient in Excel and demonstrate an interest in financial data analysis.
  • Have an understanding of financial and accounting concepts.
  • Have an interest in the CPA pathway.
  • Have good analytical and synthesis skills, and demonstrate initiative and rigor.

Important: To apply, create your online profile and upload a PDF file (maximum 2 GB) containing your cover letter, CV and most recent transcripts. The name of the document must follow this format: Nom_Prénom_Nom of the university.
