Full Description

Filipe®

Accepting New Members!

Position: Full Stack Developer

No formal experience required:

We care about execution, clarity of thought, and the ability to turn ideas into working systems and able to show a portfolio of what you’ve built.

Required Skills:

⦁ Proficiency in end-to-end web development (Front-end & Back-end).

⦁ A precise UI/UX instinct treating interface quality as a technical responsibility, not a cosmetic layer.

⦁ Strong command of modern frameworks and languages (e.g., HTML/CSS, JavaScript, React, Node.js, etc.).

⦁ App Development for IOS and Android

⦁ Proven experience managing database technologies.

Other Skills (Advantageous):

Decentralized Systems (Blockchain):

⦁ Basic understanding of how blockchain works.

⦁ Experience using or building blockchain technology.

⦁ Ability to connect a web app to a wallet or blockchain network.

Artificial Intelligence (AI):

⦁ Experience using AI APIs or open‑source models.

⦁ Ability to integrate AI features into a real product (chat, recommendations, automation).

⦁ Understanding how to structure data for AI‑powered features.

Project Overview

Project Name: New Social Media Project

Help us launch a next-generation platform designed to reshape online interactions. We are building an environment that fosters authentic community engagement while maintaining a strict "privacy-first" architecture.

About Us

Filipe is a forward‑thinking startup dedicated to building a secure, private, and user‑centric technological landscape. We specialize in refining digital interactions to ensure innovation always prioritizes user empowerment and privacy.

We value builders who understand the product, anticipate needs, and move with initiative, not instruction.

Why Join Us?

Radical Experience:

This is a “ground floor” opportunity. You will have more influence over a live product here than in a year at a corporate desk.

You’ll gain hands on product experience that most developers don’t see until years into their career the kind that shapes your portfolio, not just your résumé.

Performance Incentives:

Start at a minimum of $500/month, with clear milestones to increase your compensation as we scale.

Commitment

⦁ Primarily remote work throughout the week.

⦁ Friday/Saturday meets: These sessions are where alignment happens quickly, decisions get made, and meaningful product movement takes place.

Desired Candidate Attributes:

⦁ We aren't looking for employees; we are looking for visionaries.

⦁ You have a "design-first" on how the code looks and feels.

⦁ Deep technical expertise and problem-solving capabilities.

⦁ A vision-driven mindset dedicated to creating meaningful digital impact.

⦁ You are driven by legacy

Join Filipe as we embark on this journey to shape a more secure and user-friendly digital future.
