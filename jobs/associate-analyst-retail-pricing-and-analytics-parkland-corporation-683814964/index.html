Company Description

Parkland Corporation, now part of Sunoco LP as of November 2025, is a leading global business with operations in 25 countries. With nearly 6,000 employees, Parkland operates over 3,200 retail service stations across Canada, the U.S., and the Caribbean, serving over one million customers daily. Parkland offers dynamic and diverse career opportunities, fostering growth and solutions-driven innovation. To learn more, visit our careers page: “Power Your Journey at Parkland.”

Role Description

The Associate Analyst – Retail Pricing and Analytics will support pricing strategy and analytics for retail operations, ensuring competitiveness and optimized business performance. Day-to-day tasks include collecting and analyzing pricing data, supporting the team in developing actionable insights, and preparing reports to help drive strategic decision-making. This is a full-time, on-site role located in Calgary, AB.

• Price Execution - Coordinate fuel pricing at Sunoco’s retail gas stations by collecting information from the field, determining prices, and communicating prices.

• Communications - Engage wide variety of stakeholders including individual station operators, operations management, pricing execution, performance optimization, and IT on day-to-day execution, training and integration, and improvement opportunities. Prepare and distribute several reports and statistics on pricing activity and market conditions.

• Operating Model Improvements and Projects - Support evolution of retail pricing execution capability at Sunoco. Develop and implement tools and processes to improve pricing execution (i.e., information collection, price determination and communication) and performance optimization (i.e., data management and analysis).

• Business Performance Analysis - Identify opportunities to improve Sunoco’s existing pricing strategies by analyzing movements in sales and margins across markets, monitoring competitor activity, and measuring impacts on Sunoco’s network. Support analyses and special projects related to Sunoco’s pricing strategies.

Qualifications

  • BS in Business, Finance, Economics, Administration, or Marketing
  • Proficiency in data analysis tools and techniques (e.g., Distinctive proficiency in Excel, data visualization software)
  • Strong Analytical Skills to collect, analyze, and interpret complex data for pricing and market strategies. High analytical and negotiation competence.
  • Handles complex analyses well with little supervision
  • Strong work ethic and drive. Ability to work effectively in a fast-paced, collaborative environment
  • Required experience is commensurate with the selected job level
  • The Associate Analyst level requires a Bachelor’s degree and 0-2 years of relevant job related experience
  • Excellent Communication skills, including the ability to present findings clearly to stakeholders and collaborate across teams

Responsibilities

  • Responsible for driving and margin and sales of more than 1.5 billion annual gallons of branded fuel across Sunoco’s network
  • Leads cross functional coordination across trading, supply, scheduling, and sales management teams to independently price > 1,200 sites daily
  • Regularly requires exceptional critical judgement, thought leadership and cross-functional collaboration skills to perform the job at satisfactory level
  • Provides product, terminal, site level pricing decisions that drive $400M of annual gross margin
  • Position requires the daily review of all sites, building analytical tools, technical models, strong judgement, leadership skills and work ethic
  • Takes comprehensive view of market and channel ownership, with a degree of independence
  • Provide accurate statistical data and recommend changes in pricing strategies to maximize profitability
  • Provide oversight for actual to budget performance, sales forecasts and identify areas of risk/opportunity
  • Monitor and analyze the competitive environment, staying apprised of all issues relating to pricing and supply
  • Usually, normal office working conditions. The position will collectively staff a price desk which operates between 4:00AM and 10:00PM MST.
  • Must be able to remain in a stationary position 50% of the time due to prolonged periods of sitting or standing
