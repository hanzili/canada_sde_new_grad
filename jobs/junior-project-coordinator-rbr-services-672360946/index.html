Full Description

Job Overview
We are seeking a motivated and detail-oriented Junior Project Coordinator to join our construction management team. This role offers an excellent opportunity for individuals passionate about construction and project coordination to develop their skills in a dynamic environment. The Junior Project Coordinator will assist in overseeing project workflows, coordinating between teams, and ensuring project milestones are met efficiently. Candidates with a strong foundation in construction principles and familiarity with industry-standard software will thrive in this position.

Duties

  • Assist in developing project schedules, budgets, and resource allocations using construction management software such as Microsoft Office.
  • Review and interpret blueprints, schematics, and civil drawings to support project planning and execution.
  • Coordinate with subcontractors, suppliers, and internal teams to ensure timely delivery of materials and services.
  • Support the preparation of construction estimates and proposals utilizing construction estimating tools.
  • Utilize Adobe Pro for document management, plan review, and project updates.
  • Monitor project progress, track milestones, and update project schedules accordingly.
  • Assist in maintaining compliance with safety standards and quality control procedures on-site.
  • Support project managers with administrative tasks including report generation, meeting coordination, and documentation control.
  • Contribute to risk management by identifying potential issues early through effective time management and communication.

Requirements

  • Basic understanding of construction processes, management practices, and industry standards.
  • Proficiency in construction management softwares.
  • Knowledge of blueprint reading, schematics, and civil drawings.
  • Experience or familiarity with construction estimating techniques.
  • Strong organizational skills with excellent time management capabilities.
  • Ability to work collaboratively within a team environment while managing multiple priorities effectively.
  • Excellent communication skills to liaise between stakeholders at various levels of the project lifecycle.
  • Valid G Drivers License.
  • A degree or coursework in Construction Management, Civil Engineering, or related fields is preferred for entry-level candidates willing to learn on the job. This position provides an excellent platform for aspiring construction professionals to grow their expertise while supporting impactful projects across the industry.

Pay: $1.00-$2.00 per year

Work Location: In person
