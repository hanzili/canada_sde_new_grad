Investment Banking / Capital Markets Internship

Intern, Capital Markets

at Laurentian Bank Financial Group

📍 Location Montréal, QC
📋 Details Internship · duration not disclosed · paid · not disclosed
📅 Posted Apr 23, 2026

About the Role

Assist fixed income analyses and FX reporting

About Laurentian Bank Financial Group

Canadian bank with diverse capital markets

Full Description

Founded in Montreal in 1846, Laurentian Bank is committed to serving its customers and fostering deep relationships with specialized groups. Laurentian Bank runs operations across Canada – primarily in Québec and Ontario – as well as in the United States and competes where it sees market opportunity and has an edge, while harnessing the power of partnerships and collaboration.

This role sits within Laurentian Bank.

The internship within the Capital Markets team offers a hybrid experience combining fixed-income markets and foreign exchange.

The Fixed Income team facilitates and executes Canadian fixed-income securities transactions for institutional client base, contributing to the Bank’s growth through short- and long-term financing solutions.

The intern will also support sales and trading teams by conducting analyses, producing reports, and implementing initiatives designed to support transactions and enhance productivity, thereby gaining hands-on exposure to markets.

He or she will also participate in research and business development projects aimed at identifying new opportunities, while actively contributing to the day-to-day activities of the foreign exchange team.

Responsabilities

Fixed Income:

  • Maintain and update spreadsheets related to issuances, transaction notes, and perform ongoing maintenance of the Government Finance Group’s databases.
  • Maintain transaction notes and client intentions; develop tools to facilitate allocation and coverage of existing clients.
  • Analyze economic data and contribute to the publication of reports for investors and issuers.
  • Enhance existing tools to analyze the firm’s positioning and that of the industry, and identify emerging trends.

Foreign Exchange:

  • Assist the trading team in analyzing results, activities, and predictive sales indicators by observing correlations and performing projections under the supervision of the team’s lead trader.
  • Participate in the collection and management of sales-related data, assisting in the creation of performance reports.
  • Support traders in the analysis of sales results, including activities related to various performance programs.
  • Support the lead trader and all team members on cross-functional or corporate projects by participating in meetings and providing administrative support.
  • Observe and contribute to the development of a business development plan.

Qualifications

  • Currently enrolled in a relevant undergraduate or graduate (master’s-level) program
  • Intellectual curiosity, determination, and strong initiative
  • Ability to rigorously analyze complex problems and break them down into manageable components
  • Strong writing and communication skills
  • Excellent attention to detail
  • Knowledge of fixed-income securities and skills in mathematics or programming are considered an asset
  • Proficiency in MS Excel/VBA
  • Experience with Bloomberg is desirable and considered an asset

What we have to offer

  • Meaningful, paid work experience and professional skills development
  • Gaining insights into the business sectors which align with your career goals
  • Customized development program
  • Networking and opportunities for full time work upon graduation
  • Flexible & hybrid work environment to accommodate different needs
  • Sense of Belongingness and a Diverse & Inclusive Culture

Open to students pursuing diploma, undergraduate, or post-graduate degrees.

Offers opportunities across diverse business lines such as finance, accounting, risk management, marketing and more!
