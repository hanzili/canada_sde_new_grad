About the Role

Sell mining hardware, manage leads, close deals

About Lazy Miners

Canadian crypto mining hardware reseller

Full Description

About Lazy Miners Lazy Miners is a Canadian-based ASIC mining reseller and hosting connector - and we're growing. We're looking for a driven sales intern or recent graduate who wants to break into the crypto space and build real commercial experience selling cutting-edge mining equipment to customers across Canada and North America.

The Role You'll work across both in-person and online channels - from managing eBay and e-commerce listings to attending events, closing deals, and building lasting customer relationships. This isn't a "shadow and observe" internship. You'll be selling from day one, backed by full onboarding and a team with 25 years of combined experience in the crypto mining industry.

What You'll Do

  • Sell ASIC miners and mining hardware through online platforms and direct in-person channels
  • Generate and follow up on leads via email, phone, and social media outreach
  • Create and maintain product listings, pricing, and promotional materials
  • Build relationships with mining farms, individual miners, and resellers across North America
  • Represent the brand at trade shows, expos, and local crypto community events
  • Track pipeline activity and report on sales performance

What We're Looking For

  • Current student, recent graduate, or career-switcher with a genuine interest in cryptocurrency and blockchain
  • Natural communicator - comfortable on the phone, over email, and in person
  • Self-motivated and results-driven; you don't need to be managed to chase a deal
  • Tech-comfortable - you pick up new tools and platforms quickly
  • Genuine interest in crypto, blockchain, or mining (big plus)
  • Sales or outreach experience is a bonus - but not required, we'll train you

What You'll Get

  • Comprehensive onboarding covering ASIC hardware, mining fundamentals, and our full product catalogue
  • Mentorship from a team with 25 years of combined experience in the crypto mining industry
  • Hands-on experience across multiple sales channels - marketplace, direct, and event-based
  • A fast-moving environment where your contributions are visible and rewarded
  • Pathway to a permanent role for top performers
  • Commission-based compensation
  • Fully remote, flexible schedule

How to Apply Send us a message on LinkedIn or email us directly. Tell us why you're excited about crypto mining and why you'd be a great fit for Lazy Miners. No formal cover letter needed - just talk to us like a human.
