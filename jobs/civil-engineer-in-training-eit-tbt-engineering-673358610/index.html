Position Description

Contribute to a wide range of Civil Engineering projects while collaborating across multidisciplinary teams under the mentorship of experienced Professional Engineers. Apply and expand your technical knowledge in design, analysis, and project delivery as you develop toward professional licensure in a dynamic and growth-oriented environment.

Position Responsibilities

  • Assist in the preparation of civil designs, engineering analyses, plans, specifications, cost estimates, and contract documents.
  • Support technical and project management activities through design development, construction review, and coordination with multidisciplinary teams.
  • Prepare, verify, and finalize technical reports, calculations, and other project deliverables with attention to accuracy and quality standards.
  • Conduct fieldwork, including research, site reviews, inspections, testing, and technical investigations to support project design and delivery.
  • Contribute to proposal preparation and actively participate in engagement with clients, regulatory agencies, the public, and suppliers.
  • Collaborate closely with Professional Engineers to apply engineering principles, develop practical solutions, and advance toward professional licensure.

Qualifications

The ideal candidate will demonstrate strong technical skills in Civil Engineering design and contract administration, supported by a degree in Civil Engineering from an accredited Canadian university or an equivalent combination of education and experience. Field experience is considered an asset, along with a solid understanding of relevant codes, standards, regulations, and engineering software.

This position is classified as full time. Rate of pay will be commensurate with related experience and qualifications ($62.400.00 per year +) TBT Engineering provides health and dental benefits, group retirement savings programs, robust personal paid time off policies, wellness incentives, workwear and clothing allowances and competitive salaries!

TBT Engineering is an equal opportunity employer. We would like to thank all applicants for their interest; however, only those selected for an interview will be contacted.
