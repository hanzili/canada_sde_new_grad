Intern - Project Manager

at Schneider Electric

📍 Location Calgary, Alberta Hybrid
📋 Details Internship
📅 Posted Apr 08, 2026

About the Role

Coordinate orders, manage project timelines and deliverables

About Schneider Electric

Global electrical solutions leader; strong internship program

Full Description

Discover the opportunity to join an international, dynamic, and responsible company that fosters the development of all its people around the world. Every day, we empower employees to achieve more and experience exciting careers. Find out how our values and unique position make Schneider Electric the employer of choice.

Schneider Electric Sales Operation Team has an outstanding opportunity for a passionate/motivated individual to assume the role of intern - project manager in our Calgary office.

The intern - project manager will coordinate, manage project timelines and track project deliverables using appropriate tools.

Start Date: 13 July, 2026

What will you do?

  • Be the single point of contact for all internal and external customers throughout the life cycle of multiple orders;
  • Review purchase orders and quotation with Sales reps to ensure clean order entry
  • Convert orders based on PO requirements;
  • Compile and issue approval and record drawings packages;
  • Keep track of drawing schedules in order to ensure a timely release to manufacturing;
  • Diligently manage “change order” process;
  • Continually monitor and effectively communicate order status to relevant parties;
  • Ensure customer deliverables have been satisfied to Schneider Electric terms and conditions;
  • Complete post shipment tasks, such as warranty claims and product returns;
  • Continually update and maintain order files with all pertinent order information;
  • Initiate and facilitate team and client meetings as needed;
  • Follow up and work with distributors to resolve any claims and debit memos;
  • Other duties as required.

What qualifications will make you successful for this role?

  • Recently graduated or currently pursuing a Bachelor’s or Diploma in Electrical engineering or equivalent.
  • Project management experience desirable.
  • Electrical distribution (low and medium voltage) experience desirable.
  • Critical thinking and problem-solving skills are required;
  • Ability to plan and organize effectively and has strong decision-making skills;
  • Effective communication and facilitation skills;
  • Must be detail oriented with the ability to multi-task;
  • General instructions will be provided, but is expected to perform tasks independently;
  • Proficient Computer skills: MS Office (excel, word and Adobe Acrobat), experience with ERP systems;
  • Functional knowledge of French and English to facilitate collaboration with our teams, clients and stakeholders at the provincial and international levels
