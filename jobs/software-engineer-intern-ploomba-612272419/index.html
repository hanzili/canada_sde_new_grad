About Ploomba

Full Description

Company Description

Ploomba is an innovative agri-tech robotics company focused on developing autonomous solutions to revolutionize modern farming. By designing advanced field robots, Ploomba empowers growers to monitor crops, detect potential hazards, and enhance yields while optimizing labor costs and efficiency. Utilizing cutting-edge vision systems, real-time mapping, and AI-driven decision-making, Ploomba's technology aims to make farming smarter and more sustainable, benefiting both growers and the environment.

Role Description

This is a paid, remote role based in Montreal, QC, for a Software Engineer Intern. As an intern, you will assist in designing, developing, and testing software solutions for Ploomba's agricultural robotics platforms. Your responsibilities will include contributing to backend web development, implementing innovative software features, and collaborating closely with cross-functional teams. You will also be expected to troubleshoot and debug software systems while learning from experienced engineers in a supportive environment.

Qualifications

  • 1+ years of experience with Typescript, Rust, and Python
  • Experience with CI/CD Pipelines
  • Experience or understanding of Version Control Principles (Git)
  • Experience or understanding of Object-Oriented Programming (OOP) principles
  • Ability to collaborate effectively within a team environment
  • Strong problem-solving skills and eagerness to learn and adapt
  • Knowledge of YOLO inferencing algorithms, ROS, robotics, or agricultural technology is an asset
  • Currently pursuing or recently completed a degree in Computer Science, Software Engineering, or a related field
