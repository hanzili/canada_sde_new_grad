Full Description

We are seeking a highly organized and detail-oriented Financial Analyst (FA) to join our Toronto Financial Services team. The FA will be responsible for supporting project management activities, ensuring that financial deliverables are completed on time, and meet quality standards. This role requires strong coordination skills, an ability to manage multiple priorities, and experience in project control activities.

Start Date: September 2026
Application Deadline: May 25th, 2026
Work Term Length: 8 months

Perks of Being a CGI Co-op:

  • Participate in monthly socials to meet co-ops across the country.
  • Access professional development workshops hosted by domain experts on topics ranging from how to be a top consultant, how to apply the agile methodology, data & analytics and more!

To learn more about CGI and hear from our Co-ops and New Grads, visit www.cgi.com/startmycareer

Your future duties and responsibilities

  • Financial Management: Monitor project budgets, track actual costs against forecasts, and manage financial reporting for the project. Ensure adherence to financial controls and approval processes. Also work on monthly activities such as invoicing, financial forecast, and accounting adjustments.
  • Risk and Issue Management: Assist in identifying, monitoring, and managing project risks and issues. Ensure timely escalation and resolution by working closely with stakeholders.
  • Resource Management: Help in tracking of resource allocations and availability.
  • Documentation & Reporting: Assist/complete monthly status reports, financial reports, and metrics logs. Provide regular updates to management and stakeholders.
  • Change Control: Assist with change management activities and ensuring proper approval processes are followed.
  • Client Communication: Work with all levels of Management, communicate and issue resolution, and maintaining client satisfaction.
  • Process Improvement: Recommend and implement process improvements to enhance the efficiency and effectiveness of project delivery within the AMS team.

Required qualifications to be successful in this role

  • Bachelor’s degree in Business Administration, Information Technology, or a related field.
  • Experience as a Project Control Officer (PCO)/Financial Analyst or in a similar project support role.
  • Knowledge of project management methodologies (e.g., Agile, Waterfall).
  • Excellent organizational skills with a keen attention to detail.
  • Strong communication and interpersonal skills to collaborate effectively with cross-functional teams and clients.
  • Ability to manage multiple priorities in a fast-paced environment.

Preferred Skills:

  • Moderate/Advanced Excel skills for financial tracking.
