Full Description

Job Title: Junior Project Coordinator

Location: [Canada]

Job Type: Full-time

About Us:

At [Company Name], we are committed to delivering high-quality projects on time and within budget. We are looking for a Junior Project Coordinator to join our growing team. This entry-level role is perfect for someone eager to gain hands-on project management experience while supporting the coordination of key projects across various departments. If you're organized, proactive, and enjoy collaborating in a fast-paced environment, we want to hear from you!

Key Responsibilities:

  • Project Support: Assist senior project managers and project teams in the planning, execution, and monitoring of projects. Help ensure project tasks are completed on time and within scope.
  • Scheduling & Coordination: Coordinate project timelines, schedules, and milestones. Track project progress and communicate updates to team members and stakeholders.
  • Documentation & Reporting: Maintain and organize project documentation, including project plans, reports, and meeting minutes. Prepare status reports and provide updates to management on project performance.
  • Resource Allocation: Assist with the allocation and tracking of project resources, ensuring team members have the necessary tools and support to complete their tasks.
  • Stakeholder Communication: Act as a liaison between project teams, clients, and vendors to ensure clear and effective communication. Help schedule meetings, follow up on action items, and resolve any issues that arise.
  • Risk Management: Assist in identifying potential risks and challenges related to the project timeline, scope, or resources. Support in developing solutions to mitigate these risks.
  • Quality Control: Help monitor project deliverables to ensure that they meet quality standards and align with project objectives.
  • Process Improvement: Suggest improvements to project coordination and management processes to increase efficiency and effectiveness.

Required Qualifications:

  • Education: Bachelor’s degree in Business Administration, Project Management, or a related field.
  • Experience: 0-2 years of experience in project coordination, project support, or a related role. Internships or volunteer experience in project management are also considered.
  • Skills:
  • Strong organizational skills with the ability to manage multiple tasks and deadlines.
  • Good understanding of project management methodologies (e.g., Agile, Waterfall).
  • Excellent communication skills, both written and verbal.
  • Proficiency in Microsoft Office Suite (Excel, Word, PowerPoint) and familiarity with project management tools (e.g., MS Project, Trello, Asana).
  • Ability to work collaboratively in a team environment and across departments.
  • Problem-solving mindset and attention to detail.
  • Language: Fluency in English; French proficiency is a plus (especially for bilingual regions).

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Exposure to project management tools and software (e.g., Jira, Monday.com).
  • Knowledge of basic project management principles or methodologies (e.g., PMP, CAPM).
  • Familiarity with scheduling tools and resource management software.

Why Work With Us?

  • Competitive salary and benefits package.
  • Opportunities for career growth and development.
  • A dynamic, supportive, and collaborative work environment.
  • Flexible work arrangements, including the possibility of remote work.
  • Health, dental, and wellness benefits.
