About GenPark

Full Description

**Company Description**

GenPark is revolutionizing the connection between emerging Oriental direct-to-consumer brands and Gen Z consumers worldwide through its AI-driven personalized marketing platform. Powered by a multi-agent intelligence system, GenPark delivers tailored, engaging, and culturally resonant discovery experiences for shoppers. For brands, the platform provides a next-generation AI marketing engine, helping to target audiences, create localized content, and optimize campaigns for maximum impact. Expanding beyond retail, GenPark's multi-agent architecture seamlessly facilitates marketing and distribution across digital and physical channels, providing a unified and dynamic retail experience.

**Role Description**

This is a full-time hybrid role for a Back End Developer Intern. Located in Vancouver, BC, the position allows for a mix of in-office and remote work. The intern will assist with back-end web development tasks, contribute to software development projects, and work on enhancing system performance and reliability. Collaborating with the team, the intern will debug and optimize code, implement object-oriented designs, and may occasionally support front-end development efforts.

**Qualifications**

- Proficiency in Back-End Web Development and Software Development - Knowledge of Object-Oriented Programming (OOP) principles - Basic understanding or experience in Front-End Development and Programming - Strong problem-solving and analytical skills - Ability to collaborate effectively in a hybrid work environment - Pursuing or completed a degree in Computer Science, Software Engineering, or a related field - Familiarity with cloud-based environments and database management is a plus
