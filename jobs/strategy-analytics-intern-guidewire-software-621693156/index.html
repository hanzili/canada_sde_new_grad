About the Role

Analyze data, map processes, design AI automation

About Guidewire Software

Insurance tech platform with global footprint

Full Description

Summary Join Guidewire’s Business Technology (BizTech) team in Mississauga, where we drive technology innovation, operational excellence, and data-driven decision-making to support Guidewire’s growth and transformation. Our team collaborates globally, values diverse perspectives, and fosters a culture of curiosity, agility, and continuous improvement. We focus on delivering resilient solutions and excellent employee experiences in a hybrid work environment.

As a Strategy & Analytics Intern, you will play a key role in optimizing business processes and supporting strategic initiatives. You’ll gain hands-on experience in data analysis, process improvement, and AI automation, working alongside experienced professionals. This is an exciting opportunity to develop your skills, contribute to impactful projects, and help shape the future of insurance technology.

Job Description *What you’ll do*

  • Help map, analyze, and document operational processes across procurement, fulfillment, and internal support functions.
  • Identify bottlenecks and inefficiencies, recommending and supporting the implementation of process improvements and automation.
  • Collect, clean, and analyze operational data to uncover trends and performance drivers.
  • Assist in designing dashboards and reports using business intelligence tools to provide actionable insights.
  • Conduct ad-hoc data analysis to support strategic decision-making and project evaluations.
  • Contribute to the management of operational tools and systems, ensuring data integrity and usability.
  • Support special Procurement and Travel initiatives, creating job aids and communications for end users.
  • Evaluate workflows for AI automation opportunities and research emerging AI tools to recommend solutions.
  • At Guidewire, we foster a culture of curiosity, innovation, and responsible use of AI—empowering our teams to continuously leverage emerging technologies and data-driven insights to enhance productivity and outcomes.

*What you’ll bring Required*

  • Demonstrated ability to embrace AI and apply it to your current role as well as data-driven insights to drive innovation, productivity, and continuous improvement.
  • Outstanding verbal and written communication skills, with the ability to translate data findings into clear, actionable business recommendations.
  • Passion for understanding and streamlining complex business workflows.
  • Experience with data analysis tools (e.g., Excel, SQL, BI Tools) and methodologies.
  • Understanding of business process improvement principles (e.g., Lean, Six Sigma, Agile).
  • Ability to work effectively in multidisciplinary teams.
  • Strong analytical thinking and problem-solving skills.
  • Collaborative mindset and openness to feedback.
  • Demonstrated ability to embrace AI and apply it to your current role as well as data-driven insights to drive innovation, productivity, and continuous improvement.

Preferred

  • Degree Preferred - Currently enrolled in a Business or Management program (degree preferred).
  • Experience leveraging generative AI tools (e.g., ChatGPT, Gemini, Glean) to streamline workflows.
  • Interest in technology innovation and operational excellence.

*Your Impact We believe in clarity and setting you up for success. In your first six months, you’ll collaborate with cross-functional teams to analyze and improve key business processes, deliver data-driven insights, and support the adoption of AI automation. Your work will directly contribute to Guidewire’s mission of enabling insurers to adapt and succeed in a rapidly changing industry, while helping BizTech achieve its priorities of operational excellence, innovation, and employee experience. Your contributions will have a lasting impact on our team, our customers, and the future of insurance technology. What’s in it for you The people we employ give their all, and in return, we offer flexibility wherever we can, such as:*

  • Flexible work environment
  • Health and wellness benefits
  • Paid time off programs including volunteer time off
  • Market-competitive pay and incentive programs
  • Continual development and internal career growth opportunities
  • All roles include a new in-person orientation process to help you connect and thrive at Guidewire.

By joining Guidewire, you’ll help transform the way the world’s P&C insurers do business and contribute to our wider mission of delivering innovative, customer-centric solutions that drive industry success. The Canadian CAD base salary range for this full-time position is $50,000 - $62,000 . Your base pay will depend on your experience, skills, education, training, and location among other factors. All full-time positions or part-time roles working 30 hours or more a week at Guidewire are eligible for benefits that support their health and well-being including health, dental, and vision insurance, paid time off, and a company sponsored retirement plan. In addition, some roles may be eligible for the annual company bonus plan, commissions, and/or long term incentive awards which are contingent on a variety of factors including, but not limited to, company and employee performance.

About Guidewire Guidewire is the platform P&C insurers trust to engage, innovate, and grow efficiently. We combine digital, core, analytics, and AI to deliver our platform as a cloud service. More than 540+ insurers in 40 countries, from new ventures to the largest and most complex in the world, run on Guidewire.

As a partner to our customers, we continually evolve to enable their success. We are proud of our unparalleled implementation track record with 1600+ successful projects, supported by the largest R&D team and partner ecosystem in the industry. Our Marketplace provides hundreds of applications that accelerate integration, localization, and innovation.

For more information, please visit www.guidewire.com and follow us on Twitter: @Guidewire_PandC.
