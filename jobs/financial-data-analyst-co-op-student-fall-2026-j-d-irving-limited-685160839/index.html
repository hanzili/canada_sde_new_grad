About J.D. Irving, Limited

Full Description

Job Description The Finance Shared Services Student Data Analyst position offers an excellent opportunity to gain exposure in the Finance field within a large and diversified organization. The successful candidate will work closely with experienced professionals, gaining hands-on experience in a fast-paced and collaborative environment while contributing to meaningful projects that drive business insights.

Responsibilities

  • Work as part of a team focused on data analysis and visualization to support Finance operations.
  • Gain exposure and hands-on learning with Power BI, Tableau, and Alteryx.
  • Assist with project work and process improvement initiatives to enhance efficiency and data quality.
  • Review financial and operational data, providing insights and identifying opportunities for improvement.
  • Support the management and implementation of Finance initiatives, including reporting and analytics enhancements.

Qualifications

  • Please include your transcript along with your resume in your application.
  • University student enrolled in a Business/Commerce, Information Technology, or related program and in good academic standing.
  • Available for the Fall 2026 Work Term.
  • Proficiency with Microsoft Office Suite, with strong skills in Excel.
  • Experience in Power BI is considered an asset.
  • A team player with a positive can-do attitude.
  • Strong communication skills and ability to work collaboratively.
  • Curious and detail-oriented with a process improvement mindset.
  • Demonstrated interest in a long-term career in Analytics, Finance, and/or Accounting.
