Full Description

Software Engineer (New Grads Welcome!)

Are you a recent graduate eager to kick-start your career in software development? Join our innovative team, where you'll grow your skills, solve exciting problems, and make meaningful contributions from day one.

We are looking for an entry-level Software Engineer passionate about technology, learning, and problem-solving. You'll work closely with experienced developers and engineers, gaining hands-on experience building cutting-edge applications.

Your Background

  • Recent graduate (or soon-to-be graduate) in Computer Science, Physics, Mathematics, Electrical Engineering, or a related field.
  • A strong foundation in programming principles and algorithms.
  • Curiosity, exceptional problem-solving skills, and a passion for continuous learning.

Tech Stack & Training

  • We primarily use C#, .NET, ReactJS, SQL, NoSQL, and specialized time-series databases.
  • No worries if you’re unfamiliar—we'll train you on all relevant frameworks and languages. What's important is your ability to learn quickly and tackle challenging problems creatively.

What We Offer

  • A collaborative environment where your ideas matter.
  • Opportunities for rapid professional growth.
  • A supportive mentorship structure to accelerate your learning.

If you're driven, intelligent, and eager to build something great—this is the opportunity for you!
