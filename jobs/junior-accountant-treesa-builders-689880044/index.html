About the Role

About Treesa builders

Growing construction company; expanding finance team

Full Description

Treesa Builders is seeking a motivated and detail-oriented Junior Accountant to join our finance team in Vancouver. This role is ideal for a candidate who has a strong understanding of accounting principles and is eager to build a career in the construction industry while supporting day-to-day financial operations.

Key Responsibilities:

  • Assist in maintaining accurate financial records and updating accounting systems.
  • Support accounts payable and accounts receivable processes.
  • Prepare invoices, payment vouchers, and financial documents.
  • Reconcile bank statements and monitor daily transactions.
  • Assist in preparing monthly financial reports and expense summaries.
  • Maintain organized financial records and documentation.
  • Support budgeting and expense tracking activities.
  • Assist with audit preparation and financial reporting tasks.
  • Coordinate with internal departments regarding financial information.

Requirements:

  • Bachelor’s degree in Accounting, Finance, or a related field.
  • 1–2 years of accounting experience; fresh graduates may also apply.
  • Basic knowledge of accounting principles and financial procedures.
  • Proficiency in MS Office, especially Excel, and accounting software.
  • Strong numerical and analytical skills.
  • Excellent attention to detail and organizational abilities.
  • Ability to manage confidential financial information responsibly.

Skills & Competencies:

  • Strong numerical and analytical abilities
  • Attention to detail and accuracy
  • Time management and multitasking skills
  • Good communication and coordination abilities
  • Ability to work independently and as part of a team

At Treesa Builders, accurate financial management is essential for supporting successful construction projects and business operations. This Junior Accountant position in Vancouver offers an excellent opportunity to grow your career while contributing to a professional and expanding construction company.
