Junior Project Coordinator

Calgary, AB | Full-Time

Gator Construction Group Inc. is looking for an energetic, highly organized, and people-focused Junior Project Coordinator to join our Calgary team.

At Gator, we deliver specialized solutions in concrete restoration, structural repair, protective coatings, and related infrastructure work. Our team is known for technical expertise, quality execution, and a practical, client-first approach. We work on projects that protect and extend the life of critical assets across commercial, industrial, and institutional environments.

This is an excellent opportunity for an early-career construction professional who wants to build a long-term career in project delivery. You will work closely with experienced Project Managers and field teams, gaining exposure to estimating, planning, scheduling, procurement, document control, cost tracking, and client coordination.

What you’ll do

As a Junior Project Coordinator at Gator, you will support the successful delivery of projects from pre-construction through closeout by:

  • Assisting the Project Manager with planning, scheduling, organizing, and executing projects from start to finish
  • Supporting project administration, including cost tracking, schedule updates, change order management, submittals, RFIs, and document control
  • Reviewing project drawings, specifications, and contracts to help identify scope requirements, field issues, and potential risks
  • Coordinating project documentation and communication with clients, consultants, suppliers, subcontractors, and internal teams
  • Helping with procurement and material planning to ensure projects are properly resourced and delivered efficiently
  • Monitoring project progress, work hours, budgets, and key milestones
  • Preparing meeting minutes, tracking action items, and participating in internal and external project meetings
  • Assisting with subcontractor and workforce coordination as required
  • Contributing to problem-solving in the field and helping keep projects on schedule and on budget
  • Supporting a culture of safety, accountability, teamwork, and high-quality execution
  • Taking on other duties as required to support the project team

What will help you succeed

To succeed in this role, you are organized, proactive, and eager to learn. You communicate well, follow through on details, and enjoy working in a fast-paced construction environment where priorities can shift quickly.

Qualifications and experience

  • Post-secondary education in Construction Engineering Technology, Civil Engineering, Construction Management, Construction Administration, or a related discipline
  • 1–2 years of experience in commercial, institutional, industrial, or civil construction
  • Ability to read and interpret drawings, specifications, and contract documents
  • Strong organizational, communication, problem-solving, and time management skills
  • Proficiency with Microsoft Office, Adobe, SharePoint, and Smartsheet
  • Experience with concrete construction, restoration, repair, patching, or coatings is considered an asset
  • CET designation or progress toward certification is considered an asset
  • Valid Class 5 driver’s licence and reliable transportation
  • Willingness to travel within Alberta as required; travel and living allowances are provided for out-of-town work

Why join Gator

At Gator, you’ll join a team that values technical excellence, practical thinking, and strong relationships. We offer the opportunity to work on meaningful projects, learn from experienced construction professionals, and advance your career in a stable, expanding company.

We offer:

  • Competitive compensation
  • Comprehensive health and dental benefits
  • Bonus incentives
  • Career development and mentorship
  • The opportunity to work with an established, respected, and growing company

We thank all applicants for their interest. Only those selected for an interview will be contacted.
