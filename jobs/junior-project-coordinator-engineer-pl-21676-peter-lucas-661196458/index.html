About the Role

About Peter Lucas

Industrial project leader in mining sector

Full Description

Peter Lucas Project Management invests in people, community, and cutting-edge technology to provide gold standard project delivery. We are currently looking for a Jr Project Coordinator to join our team.

At Peter Lucas we offer a variety of work, professional development, and learning opportunities that come from working on diverse projects in the mining, energy, and heavy industrial sectors. Our dynamic environment, filled with high-achieving professionals, enables our staff to take on incredible opportunities, gain mentorship from a diverse group of colleagues, and succeed in their careers.

We are seeking a motivated Jr Project Coordinator / Jr Project Engineer to support a greenfield uranium mill and wellfield project transitioning from operational readiness into the construction phase.

This role will support project execution activities across engineering, construction, and commissioning, helping ensure the safe, timely, and efficient delivery of key project milestones in a highly regulated uranium mining environment.

The successful candidate will work closely with engineering, construction, procurement, and operations teams to coordinate project activities and support field execution.

Key Responsibilities

  • Support coordination of greenfield mill and wellfield construction activities
  • Assist with planning and execution of operational readiness and transition-to-construction activities
  • Track and manage project deliverables, schedules, and action items
  • Support engineering and construction teams with technical coordination and documentation control
  • Assist with contractor coordination, progress tracking, and site reporting
  • Support field execution activities, ensuring alignment with project scope and design requirements
  • Help identify and resolve project issues in collaboration with engineers and site leadership
  • Maintain project documentation, including drawings, RFIs, and technical records
  • Support safety and environmental compliance across all project activities
  • Assist in commissioning and startup preparation activities

Requirements

  • Diploma or Bachelor's degree in Engineering, Construction Management, or related technical discipline.
  • 2-4 years of experience in industrial projects (construction, mining, oil & gas, or heavy industry).
  • Experience working on project teams in field or site-based environments
  • Exposure to uranium mining, mill operations, or wellfield systems is an asset
  • Strong organizational and coordination skills with attention to detail.
  • Ability to manage multiple priorities in a fast-paced project environment.
  • Strong communication and teamwork skills

Benefits

  • Health and Dental Benefit
  • Company Pension Matching,
  • Company Incentive

We appreciate all applicants' interest; however, only those selected for an interview will be contacted. Wages are negotiable based on experience and qualifications.

Global reference checks may be conducted after the interview process.
