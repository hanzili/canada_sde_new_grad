Accounting / FP&A Internship

Accounting Intern

at AMACON

📍 Location Vancouver, BC V6B 2W5
📋 Details Contract · 4 months · Not disclosed · Apr 2026
📅 Posted Mar 17, 2026

About the Role

Process journal entries, AP/AR, assist financials

About AMACON

Canadian real estate developer with notable projects

Full Description

About Us

Driven by a passion for detail and a tradition of excellence in design and architecture, Amacon is recognized as one of Canada’s most influential real estate development and construction firms. With five decades of development and construction expertise, a precise level of design and craftsmanship is evident in all the homes and commercial developments that Amacon builds.

Position Summary

Amacon is looking for an energetic team player with a strong work ethic and who is proactive to take on the role as our Accounting Intern for a short-term contract. This position can handle any administrative task and demonstrates a strong ability to be organized, analytical, and enables efficiency and productivity. We are looking for post-secondary students in their final year of school and recent graduates who want hands-on experience in accounting.

This position is a 4-month contract starting in April 2026 with the potential for extension.

Position Responsibilities

  • Data entry into our accounting software (Yardi).
  • Filing invoices.
  • Scanning documents.
  • Accounts payable.
  • Bank deposits.
  • Download bank statements from various institutions we hold accounts in.
  • Create legal documents from templates.
  • Prepare simple year end working papers to analyze corporate results, and prepare financial statements using a template.
  • Create and analyze simple journal entries to post into accounting software.
  • Other ad hoc duties as required.

Experience & Qualifications

  • Education: Currently enrolled as a final-year student or recent graduate from an Accounting or Business Management program.
  • Detail oriented with a high level of accuracy.
  • Comfortable with repetitive tasks.
  • Comfortable working with Microsoft suite of products: specifically Word, Excel, Teams.
  • Strong time management and organizational skills.
  • Ability to meet tight timelines.
  • Excellent interpersonal and communication skills, both written and verbal.
  • Willing to take on new tasks and projects with a team-based focus.
  • Ability to work in a fast-paced work environment.
  • Must be punctual and reliable.
  • Able to work independently.

Additional Information

This contract hourly position works 8:30 am - 5:00 pm in the office, Monday through Friday and is in downtown Vancouver. Amacon is an equal opportunity employer who offers competitive compensation and benefits programs. We thank you for your interest in this position but only those who are short-listed for an interview will be contacted.

Please apply through amacon.com via the link provided: https://amacon.bamboohr.com/careers/457
