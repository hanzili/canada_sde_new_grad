About the Role

Represent brands at events, engage customers, support campaigns

About One Direction

Entry-level training program in events marketing

Full Description

Role Overview

As a Junior Sales and Marketing Coordinator, you will support the delivery of marketing campaigns at events, venues, and promotional sites. This role is ideal for someone confident, energetic, and excited to work in a dynamic, customer-facing environment. Full training is provided, making it a great starting point for anyone looking to build a career in events or marketing.

Key Responsibilities

-Represent client brands at events, pop-up stands, and promotional sites

-Engage with customers, answer questions, and provide product or service information

-Support event setup, including displays, materials, and equipment

-Assist in delivering interactive and engaging experiences for attendees

-Maintain a professional and friendly presence throughout the event

-Gather customer insights and feedback for ongoing campaign improvements

-Work collaboratively with the events and marketing team to achieve goals

Requirements

-No previous experience required, full training provided

-Confident, outgoing, and comfortable engaging with the public

-Strong communication and interpersonal skills

-Energetic, positive, and able to work in busy event environments

-Reliable, proactive, and adaptable

-Team-oriented with a willingness to learn

What We Offer

-Full training and ongoing professional development

-Opportunities to progress into event management, marketing coordination, or leadership roles

-A fun, supportive, and fast-paced team culture

-Hands-on experience across a variety of events and campaigns

-A role with daily variety and plenty of room to grow

If you’re enthusiastic, people-focused, and excited to kick-start your career in event sales and marketing, we’d love to hear from you. Apply today and take the first step towards an exciting future.
