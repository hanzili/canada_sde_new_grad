About the Role

Assist geotechnical field tests and reports

About GHD

Global geotechnical engineering leader with global reach

Full Description

Job Description Help us build the future and we’ll help you build a rewarding and purposeful career. Our global network is made up of architects, designers, planners, engineers, and environmental scientists all working towards the same goal.

Join a team that brings inspirational architecture, landscapes, townscapes and places to our world, and we’ll provide you unlimited space to grow.

Who are we looking for? We’re looking for a Geotechnical Engineer to join our Geotechnical team in Peterborough! As a graduate Geotechnical Engineer, you will work alongside our Senior Engineers to provide support for our geotechnical and construction materials testing projects throughout the Southeast region.

Be part of the dynamic culture! Our workplace thrives on a hybrid model, combining in-office collaboration and flexibility. Join us in office, three days a week to contribute, connect and excel in our vibrant environment. Working with an energetic and high performing team, this position offers a variety of work and will see you involved in:

  • Drilling oversight and field work related to soils and construction materials testing
  • Visual classification of soil samples and review of laboratory testing data; assist with laboratory testing, as needed
  • Preparation of geotechnical services reports, proposals and data analysis
  • Project invoice review/summaries
  • Occasional support to survey field crews

What you will bring to the team:

  • Bachelor’s and/or Master’s Degree in Civil Engineering, Geotechnical Engineering or related field.
  • 0-2 years of experience in geotechnical / materials testing experience a plus
  • Ability and willingness to perform field work and travel as needed; this could include some overnight travel
  • Strong written and verbal communication with the ability to work both within a team and independently

The salary range for this position is $67,500 - $70,500. The posted salary range represents the expected hiring range for GHD locations in its major city centres. Ranges may vary for positions in other locations. At GHD, base salary is determined by your skills, experience, qualifications and work location. We are committed to offering competitive compensation and adhere to all relevant pay transparency legislation.
