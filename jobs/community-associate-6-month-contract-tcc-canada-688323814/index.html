Full Description

Community Associate (6-Month Contract)

TCC Canada is looking for an energetic and detail-oriented person who excels at offering outstanding client experience.

Our Community Associates are the front line and represent the TCC brand. The role is very diverse; therefore, you must excel in and enjoy multi-tasking.

What you will be doing:

  • Provide exceptional client service by going the extra mile for our members
  • Create a welcoming environment: assist members in a friendly, patient and professional manner
  • Assist in managing the location in daily activities
  • Relationship management with property manager and landlord
  • Conduct tours to show the office space
  • Help cover front desk and answer phone during business hours
  • Inventory and procurement for kitchen and common areas
  • Enter details for client monthly invoicing
  • Accounts receivable reporting & follow up with members
  • Receive, sort and organize mail and send packages

About you…

  • Customer service and/or hospitality experience an asset
  • Detail-oriented with high standards
  • Self-motivated with strong work ethic
  • Enthusiastic, positive attitude and able to build strong relationships with our members
  • Easily adapts to fast-changing situations
  • Must have strong verbal and written communication skills
  • Exceptional organizational and multitasking skills

What we offer:

  • An inspiring work environment with a great team – your opinion is valued!
  • Training and growth opportunities
  • Free premium coffee, tea and snacks

Job Types: Full-time, Permanent

Pay: From $20.00 per hour

Benefits:

  • Dental care
  • Extended health care
  • Paid time off
  • Vision care

Flexible language requirement:

  • French not required

Ability to commute/relocate:

  • Kanata, ON: reliably commute or plan to relocate before starting work (required)

Work Location: In person
