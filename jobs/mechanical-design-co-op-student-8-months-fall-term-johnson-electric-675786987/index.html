About the Role

About Johnson Electric

Global motion systems leader; robust co-op program

Full Description

Join Our Team as a Mechanical Design Co-op Student at Johnson Electric! Location: Ancaster, ON, Canada (onsite) Salary Range: $22-$24 per hour

Vacancy Type: Co-op Position

Schedule: Full-time, Day shift

Relocation: Not Applicable

Travel: Minimal

Why You’ll Love Working Here:

  • Global Presence: Be part of a global company with diverse teams and the opportunity to work on international projects.
  • Diverse Projects: Work on a variety of projects across different industries, gaining broad experience.
  • Mentorship Opportunities: Access to mentorship programs that help you grow both personally and professionally.
  • Innovation Hubs: Access to state-of-the-art facilities and innovation hubs that foster creativity and development.

What You’ll Be Doing:

  • Ensuring the adherence to ISO 45001, ISO 14001, IATF 16949, ISO 9001 all Johnson Electric's policies and any other industry standards in all activities
  • Working on Solidworks drawings
  • Working with the Automation design team of the company in order to make sure that we are making world class drawings for our assembly lines
  • Interaction with production operators, technicians and other key resources to assist with the documentation, standardization and improvement of processes
  • Create and Update our PM system
  • Create & maintain documentation for new and existing assembly lines
  • Coordinate continuous improvement initiatives
  • Perform other related duties as required

What We’re Looking For:

  • Coordinate continuous improvement initiatives
  • Perform other related duties as required
  • Working toward a technical degree or diploma in Mechanical Engineering/Design
  • Experience in a design role or previous co-op considered an asset
  • Excellent interpersonal skills
  • Attention to detail
  • Independent and self-motivated worker
  • Hands on experience is a PLUS
  • Committed to meeting deadline requirements
  • Willing and able to multi-task and change priorities based on organizational needs
  • Strong ability to use Microsoft Office Software
  • Strong Auto Cad or CAD experience (Both 3D & 2D)
  • Troubleshooting skills
  • Able to communicate effectively, both written and verbally, at all levels of the organization
  • High level of integrity and dependability with a strong sense of urgency and results orientation

How to Apply: Apply online at www.johnsonelectric.com – We can’t wait to meet you!
