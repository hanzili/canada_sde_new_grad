Full Description

Junior IT Support Analyst (Entry-Level Opportunity)

$50,000-60,000 + In-office - Downtown Toronto

Are you early in your IT career and looking for an opportunity to build hands-on experience in a fast-paced, professional environment? We’re seeking a Junior IT Support Analyst who is curious, service-driven, and eager to grow.

This is an excellent opportunity for recent graduates with co-op or internship experience , strong references, and a passion for technology and client service.

About the Role

As a key member of the IT team, you will be the first point of contact for technical support, helping users troubleshoot issues and ensuring technology runs smoothly across the organization. You’ll gain exposure to a wide range of systems, tools, and environments while developing your technical and client service skills.

What You’ll Do

  • Act as the first point of contact for all technical support requests (in person, phone, email, chat)
  • Troubleshoot and resolve issues related to software, hardware, mobile devices, and connectivity
  • Log, track, and escalate support tickets to ensure timely resolution
  • Support meeting room technology, including Teams, Zoom, and AV equipment
  • Assist with device setup, configuration, repairs, and inventory management
  • Provide basic user training and “how-to” support to enhance user independence
  • Collaborate with team members to solve problems and share knowledge
  • Maintain documentation, procedures, and knowledge base articles
  • Contribute to reporting and identifying trends in support requests
  • Participate in IT projects and provide after-hours support on a rotational basis

What You Bring

  • Diploma or degree in Information Technology or a related field
  • 1-2 year of experience in an IT support environment (co-op/internship experience welcome!)
  • Strong knowledge of Windows 11 and Microsoft 365
  • Basic understanding of networking, VPN access, and device configuration
  • Excellent communication skills with a client-first mindset
  • Strong problem-solving ability and attention to detail
  • Highly organized with the ability to manage multiple priorities
  • A proactive approach and eagerness to learn in a fast-paced environment

What Success Looks Like

  • Delivering exceptional client service with professionalism and responsiveness
  • Managing a high volume of requests with accuracy and efficiency
  • Resolving issues independently while knowing when to escalate
  • Building trust and credibility across the organization
  • Contributing to a collaborative, solutions-focused team environment
  • Continuously learning and improving technical skills

What We Offer

  • Competitive base salary
  • Comprehensive benefits package, including HSA
  • RRSP with employer contributions
  • Fitness reimbursement
  • Education assistance and ongoing development opportunities

Additional Details

  • Full-time, in-office role
  • Standard hours with flexibility between 8:30 AM – 5:30 PM
  • Participation in paid after-hours support rotation
