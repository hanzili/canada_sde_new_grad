About the Role

Assist QC testing, analyze polymers and materials

About NexKemia

Polystyrene materials company with QC and R&D

Full Description

Fall 2026 *This position is based in Mansonville, located in the Eastern Townships of Quebec, Canada. As such, it is essential that the candidate resides within close proximity to the office.* The intern will be responsible for assisting the members of the quality control team working within the quality control laboratory.

Primary Responsibilities

  • The intern will take part in release testing for ongoing production as well as R&D products, performing routine analyses on expandable polystyrene beads, including styrene and pentane content by GC, molecular weight and coating analysis by HPLC, sieve analysis, moisture, bromine, and graphite content.
  • The intern will participate in the required analyses of raw materials, as well as of densified and recycled polystyrene.
  • The intern will assist in the qualification of new raw materials by participating in polymerization tests carried out in a glass reactor.
  • The intern will take part in the analysis of physical tests performed on foams according to ASTM standards (flexural strength, compression, R‑value, LOI, water absorption, water vapor permeability).

Minimum Requirements

  • In the process of obtaining a bachelor's degree
