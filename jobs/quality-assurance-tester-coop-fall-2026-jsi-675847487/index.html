About the Role

Test software manually, document defects, report findings

About JSI

Law enforcement analytics software leader

Full Description

JSI Telecom requires that you pass a Police Information Check (PIC) **
** It is a condition of employment that you must be successful in obtaining and maintaining up to a Top-Secret security clearance through the Company's sponsorship. **
** Candidates must not be subject to the Government of Canada OCV (Out-of-country verification) process to obtain security clearances. Typically, this means that at the time of application all candidates for co-op terms must not have left Canada for more than 6 consecutive months in the past 10 years. The government of Canada OCV requirements are subject to change without notice.**

We are looking for Software Quality Assurance Co-op students to support our QA team in ensuring the highest standards of software quality. The position is focused exclusively on Manual QA tasks, and does not involve coding responsibilities. As a co-op, you will be responsible for testing software applications to identify defects and ensure the functionality meets the requirements and expectations.

Key Responsibilities

  • Conduct manual testing of software applications to ensure functionality, performance, and usability meet the required standards.
  • Create, execute, and document test cases and test plans based on project specifications and requirements.
  • Identify, report, and track defects in the software using our defect tracking system.
  • Collaborate with developers, project managers, and other QA team members to ensure a smooth and efficient testing process.
  • Participate in team meetings and provide input on quality assurance processes and practices.
  • Assist in the preparation of test data and perform regression testing as needed.
  • Verify that fixes are correctly implemented by retesting issues and ensuring resolution.
  • Maintain thorough documentation for testing activities and results.
  • Automating, maintaining and executing tests in a timely manner.
  • Running and analyzing automated tests
  • Ability to code in languages like Python, C#
  • Experience with tools like Playwright, Selenium, Nunit, Postman
  • Familiar with CI/CD integration for automated pipelines
  • Knowledge of non-functional requirements, including performance, stability, supportability, extensibility etc.
  • Uses and stays current with AI tools for quality engineering.
  • Ideally, has working knowledge of at least some of the following:
    • Data Lakehouse
    • Kubernetes
    • Helm
    • Checkmk
    • Cloud platforms like AWS
  • Has a technical mindset, for example:
    • Uses AI for efficiency and productivity in daily tasks
    • Experienced with scripting or programming
    • Experienced with API's
    • Experienced with system configuration and networking
    • Experienced with data collection and management

Requirements

  • Enrollment in a recognized post-secondary program (e.g., Computer Science, Information Technology, Engineering).
  • Strong attention to detail and a passion for ensuring quality in software products.
  • Excellent written and verbal communication skills.
  • Ability to work well both independently and in a team-oriented environment.
  • Prior experience in manual software testing or quality assurance (preferred but not required).
  • Familiarity with software development lifecycle (SDLC) and testing methodologies is an asset.
  • Ability to learn quickly and adapt to new tools and technologies.
  • A proactive, problem-solving mindset with a keen interest in software quality.

Perks

  • Gain hands-on experience in software testing and quality assurance processes.
  • Work in a collaborative and innovative environment with experienced professionals.
  • Opportunity to contribute to impactful projects and improve software quality.
  • Hybrid work model (2 days a week in the office).

How We Work

You will be expected to work in a remote/hybrid environment reporting to our Kanata, Ontario location 2 or more times per week.

About JSI

JSI is built on purpose, that of making a difference in the world.

Founded in 1979, this privately-owned technology company is the North American leader in designing and developing acquisition, collection and analysis solutions for law enforcement and intelligence communities.

With 4Sight – JSI’s single, unified, product suite – customers can combine any number of disparate data sources into a highly intuitive, visually-focused platform. The result? JSI’s customers spend less time working with data and more time seeing patterns, understanding trends, and gaining perspective (and making the world a safer place).

With over 400 employees and a strong, growing global presence in Canada, the U.S., Australia, and Germany, JSI is not only the dominant player in its industry, it is also known for its fun, high-performing, purpose-driven corporate culture.
