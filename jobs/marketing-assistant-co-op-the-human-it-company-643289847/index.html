Marketing Assistant - Co-Op

at The Human IT Company

About the Role

Create social content, blog posts, and SEO tasks

About The Human IT Company

Full Description

Job Overview
The Human IT Company is seeking a passionate and detail-oriented Marketing Assistant – Co‑Op to support our day‑to‑day marketing functions. This is a paid, part‑time (20 hours/week), in‑person co‑op role based in our East Vancouver office near the PNE.

This role is ideal for students who want hands‑on experience in content marketing, SEO, and organic growth, working on real initiatives in a small, collaborative team. The focus is execution, learning, and consistency — not sales, phone calls, or ad buying.

Responsibilities

  • Create, schedule, and publish social media content aligned with our brand voice
  • Assist with writing, editing, and posting blog and website content
  • Support organic SEO initiatives, including keyword research and on‑page optimization
  • Assist with Generative Optimization to ensure content performs well in modern search and AI‑assisted discovery
  • Perform list cleaning and list scrubbing in marketing and CRM systems (no phone calls)
  • Maintain accurate contact records, tagging, and documentation
  • Assist with basic marketing research and competitive analysis
  • Track completion of assigned marketing tasks and support basic reporting
  • Support the marketing team with planning, brainstorming, and ad‑hoc tasks as assigned

Skills

  • Strong written communication skills
  • Interest in digital marketing, content creation, and SEO
  • Comfortable working with computers, web tools, and online platforms
  • Attention to detail, especially with content and data accuracy
  • Ability to follow direction, apply feedback, and work independently
  • Organized and reliable, with the ability to manage assigned tasks week‑to‑week

Training is provided. This is a paid co‑op position designed to give students practical, real‑world marketing experience and support academic co‑op requirements.

About The Human IT Company

The Human IT Company is a Vancouver‑based managed IT services provider working with small businesses, charities, and non‑profit organizations. We believe technology should support people, not get in their way — and that philosophy carries through to how we work as a team.

We’re a small, collaborative company that values clear communication, attention to detail, and continuous learning. Students and co‑op team members at HITCo work on real projects, receive mentorship, and gain practical experience in a supportive, people‑first environment. The work can be challenging at times, but we’re intentional about helping you learn and grow.
