Full Description

Company Description

SPIETH America, with over 50 years of experience, is a trusted leader in the design and manufacturing of high-quality gymnastics and sports equipment. As part of the ABEO Group, a global leader in sports and fitness equipment, SPIETH America provides specialized products for gymnastics clubs, schools, and various organizations across North America. Our equipment is also featured in local, national, and international competitions. We are committed to supporting the development of gymnastics through partnerships with the US and Canadian Federations and delivering superior product quality to our customers. For more information, visit www.spiethamerica.com or follow us on Facebook and Instagram @spiethamerica.

Role Description

Are you a recent graduate or graduating this semester? This is a full-time on-site position located in Orillia, ON for a Junior Mechanical Design Engineer. In this role, you will work on the design and development of innovative gymnasium and sports equipment. Responsibilities include creating product concepts, utilizing SolidWorks for detailed engineering drawings, collaborating with production teams, and ensuring compliance with quality standards. Additionally, you will participate in prototype development and testing, and work alongside project teams to improve efficiency and optimize designs.

Qualifications

  • Proficiency in Design Engineering, Machine Design, and Mechanical Engineering
  • Experience in Product Design and development processes
  • Proficiency in using SolidWorks software
  • Strong problem-solving skills and ability to work collaboratively in a team environment
  • Good organizational and project management skills
  • Relevant degree or diploma in Mechanical Engineering or a related field
  • Prior experience in sports equipment design or related industries is a plus
