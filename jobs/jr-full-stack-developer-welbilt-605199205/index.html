About Welbilt

Full Description

We are Garland! We design, manufacture and supply best-in-class commercial ovens, ranges, grills and countertop cooking equipment for the global foodservice market. We empower you to grow with us. We recognize you and reward you. We win together as one team.

Garland Commercial Ranges is seeking a highly motivated and skilled Jr. Full Stack Developer (Contractor) to join our in-person team at our Mississauga, Ontario facility. The ideal candidate is passionate about clean, efficient, and well-structured code, has strong problem-solving skills, and can work collaboratively in a fast-paced environment.

This role will involve developing and maintaining web applications using modern frameworks, integrating APIs, and working closely with internal teams to support our ongoing digital initiatives. This is an exciting opportunity to work on impactful projects within a collaborative and supportive team.

Reporting to the IT Director, this position is 100% based onsite in our Mississauga, Ontario facility. This is a full-time, temporary role that is an addition to headcount. There may be a possibility of a contract extension or moving to a full-time position.

Key Tasks & Responsibilities:

  • Design, develop, test, and maintain web applications using MVC frameworks (Laravel).
  • Build and optimize user interfaces with HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and modern front-end frameworks (Svelte).
  • Develop and maintain object-oriented code in PHP.
  • Design and implement relational database structures, write efficient queries, and perform data manipulation tasks.
  • Integrate and design RESTful APIs following modern best practices.
  • Participate in code reviews, version control management (Git), and Agile/Scrum processes.
  • Collaborate with cross-functional teams to define, design, and ship new features.
  • Troubleshoot, debug, and resolve software defects and performance issues.
  • Write and maintain clear, concise technical documentation.

Qualifications

  • Specialized Skills, Knowledge, and Abilities

    • Fluency in PHP or another object-oriented programming language.
  • Strong proficiency with front-end technologies (HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript).
  • Experience with at least one modern JavaScript framework (Svelte, React, or Vue).
  • Intermediate-level knowledge of relational and non-relational databases.
  • Solid understanding of API design principles and integration.
  • Proficiency with Git and Agile development workflows.
  • Excellent communication, analytical, and critical-thinking skills.
  • Ability to work independently and as part of a collaborative team.

  • Nice to have:

    • Experience with Laravel or other PHP frameworks.
    • Exposure to NoSQL databases (MongoDB preferred).
    • Familiarity with Docker and containerized environments.
    • Experience deploying or maintaining applications in AWS environments.

  • Education and Experience

    • Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science, Software Engineering, or a related field (or equivalent practical experience).

  • 2+ years of experience with MVC-based development.

  • Working Conditions

  • Heavy computer use.

  • Typically, an office environment and must be able to sit for extended periods.

  • Garland, a Welbilt brand, has been a leading manufacturer for over 160 years which designing and producing a full line of commercial ovens, ranges, griddles, grills, conveyor ovens & countertop cooking equipment. The products are sold to leading fast food chains as well as independent restaurants in over +100 countries. The facility, based in Mississauga, supports our Garland brand and is a division of Ali Group.

For more information about Garland, visit www.garland-group.com
