Full Description

Are you a strong communicator who enjoys building relationships and helping businesses grow?

We’re hiring a Junior Sales Representative in Whitby, ON, for a well-established, North American frame manufacturing company with a strong reputation in the market. This is a great opportunity for someone early in their sales career who thrives in a consultative environment and wants long-term growth - without the pressure of commission-based selling.

  • What’s In It for You? $23–$25 per hour
  • $1,200 annual profit-sharing bonus
  • Full benefits package
  • 2 weeks vacation
  • Stable, established company (corporate-owned North American operations)
  • Clear career progression into a senior sales role
  • Collaborative, supportive team environment

  • Casual dress code (professional for client calls)

  • What You’ll Be Doing? Manage and grow a portfolio of approximately 600 existing B2B accounts

  • Conduct outbound calls to re-engage customers and strengthen relationships
  • Introduce new product lines and recommend solutions that support customer growth

  • Use a CRM system to track activity and performance

  • What We’re Looking For? Strong communication and interpersonal skills

  • Experience in customer service
  • Comfortable making outbound calls and having business conversations
  • Upselling or cross-selling experience is a strong asset
