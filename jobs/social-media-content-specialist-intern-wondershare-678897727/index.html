About Wondershare

Global leader in creativity software solutions

Full Description

Company Overview

As a renowned global leader in creativity and productivity solutions, Wondershare is dedicated to making cutting-edge technology accessible to everyone, fostering increased efficiency and creativity. With a user base spanning over 100 million individuals across 150 countries, we offer a diverse array of software solutions encompassing video editing, PDF manipulation, data recovery, diagram creation, graphic design, and more. Our mission is to build technology and software products that make every idea achievable for the new generation of creatives. And we empower our Canadian team to take on the most creative challenges to make this possible.

Responsibilities

  • Independently produce short-form video content optimized for social media platforms.
  • Write compelling captions, video titles, and post copy that reflect the brand voice and incorporate relevant keywords.
  • Develop strategic content planning for social media and forums aligned with marketing objectives and seasonal campaigns.
  • Conduct keyword research, trend analysis, and competitive research to guide creative direction across platforms.
  • Execute content rollout by uploading, scheduling, and organizing posts across all relevant platforms.
  • Ensure alignment with the content calendar, platform best practices, and campaign timelines.
  • Monitor and review the performance data of videos after they go live across distribution platforms.
  • Based on the results, implement dynamic optimization and content adjustments to enhance overall effectiveness, audience engagement, and dissemination efficiency.

Required Qualifications:

  • An interest in marketing and branding for digital creativity products (ex. video and photo editing software).
  • Basic knowledge in video editing and/or graphic design.
  • Excellent written and verbal communication skills, with the ability to craft compelling copy and engage audiences effectively.
  • Creative mindset with a keen eye for design aesthetics and storytelling.
  • Comfortable appearing on camera and presenting to an audience is a plus.
