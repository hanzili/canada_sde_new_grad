About the Role

About EventConnect

Sports-tourism tech platform connecting events worldwide

Full Description

Location: London, Ontario (Hybrid)

EventConnect is a sports-tourism technology company that connects event organizers, teams, hotels, and destinations on one platform. We streamline registration, scheduling, housing, and payments to drive room nights and deliver a smoother travel experience. Headquartered in London, ON, we support tournaments across North America with dependable, customer-focused software.

What you’ll do

  • Build and own core product features across our PHP + JS stack.
  • Design, implement, and review performant APIs and responsive UI components.
  • Collaborate with product and UX; participate in design & code reviews; ship reliably.

Must-have qualifications

  • PHP framework: 1–2+ years with Laravel (preferred) or Symfony at comparable depth.
  • Front-end: Vue.js (preferred) with 1+ years or React with 1+ years and a willingness to ramp to Vue (we’ll support cross-training).
  • SQL/RDBMS experience (migrations, indexing, query tuning).
  • Git workflow.
  • Hybrid requirement: Able to work from our London, ON office 3 days/week after onboarding; onboarding is onsite 5 days/week.
  • Eligible to work in Canada; commutable to London, Ontario Canada

Working conditions & perks

  • Standard hours: Mon–Fri, 9 a.m.–5 p.m., with flex time.
  • Hybrid: 3 days in office post-onboarding (up to 2 remote days/week).
  • Competitive base salary, and benefits.

Accessibility

  • EventConnect welcomes and encourages applications from people with disabilities. Accommodations are available upon request throughout the selection process.
