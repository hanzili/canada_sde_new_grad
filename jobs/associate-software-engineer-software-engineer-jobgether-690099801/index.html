About Jobgether

Canada-based SaaS platform for insurance agencies

Full Description

This position is posted by Jobgether on behalf of a partner company. We are currently looking for an Associate Software Engineer / Software Engineer in Canada. This role offers the opportunity to contribute to the modernization of SaaS platforms that are reshaping how independent insurance agencies operate in a fast-evolving digital landscape. You will work within a collaborative, product-driven engineering team focused on building scalable, high-quality web applications and cloud-ready services. The environment emphasizes innovation, continuous learning, and strong software craftsmanship, where engineers are empowered to contribute across the full stack. You will be involved in designing, building, testing, and improving features that directly impact users in production environments. This is a highly collaborative role where attention to detail, problem-solving, and curiosity are key drivers of success. You will also gain exposure to modern development practices, automation, and evolving technologies while contributing to meaningful product outcomes in a real-world SaaS ecosystem.

Accountabilities

  • Collaborate with cross-functional engineering teams to design, develop, and deliver scalable full-stack software solutions.
  • Build, test, and maintain new features while supporting the evolution of existing product functionality.
  • Write clean, maintainable code aligned with engineering standards and architectural guidance.
  • Participate in code reviews, design discussions, and technical decision-making processes.
  • Develop and maintain automated test suites to ensure product quality and reliability.
  • Debug, troubleshoot, and resolve application issues, including performance and production incidents.
  • Monitor application performance across environments and contribute to optimization efforts.
  • Continuously learn and apply new tools, frameworks, and engineering practices.

Requirements

  • 1-3 years of experience for Associate Software Engineer, or 3+ years for Software Engineer, in software development roles.
  • Strong experience with Microsoft stack technologies including C#, ASP.NET/.NET Core, and SQL.
  • Experience building front-end applications using Angular (preferably Angular 17+), TypeScript, HTML, and CSS/SCSS.
  • Solid understanding of software engineering principles, including SDLC, code reviews, testing, and CI/CD practices.
  • Familiarity with frameworks and tools such as Entity Framework, Dapper, xUnit, Moq, Git, and REST APIs.
  • Experience working with distributed systems concepts such as messaging, caching, and web services.
  • Strong debugging, performance tuning, and problem-solving skills in complex application environments.
  • Excellent communication skills and ability to collaborate effectively in team-based environments.
  • For Software Engineer level: demonstrated ability to mentor peers and contribute to technical leadership discussions.
  • Nice to have: exposure to cloud platforms (AWS or Azure), CI/CD quality gating, and modern test automation strategies.

Benefits

  • Remote work environment with flexibility across Canada (and eligible US locations).
  • Competitive salary range from $70,000 to $120,000 CAD depending on experience and role level.
  • Comprehensive health coverage including medical, dental, and vision plans.
  • Paid vacation, holidays, and dedicated health & wellness days.
  • Additional paid day off for your birthday.
  • Bonus and potential additional compensation opportunities depending on role.
  • Strong culture of collaboration, inclusion, and continuous learning.
  • Access to modern tools, training resources, and professional development opportunities.
