Job Title: Hollow Metal Project Coordinator (Entry Level Training Opportunity)

Gunther’s Building Center – Calgary, AB

Pay: $45,000 – $60,000 per year

Job Type: Full-time

Company Overview

Gunther’s Building Center has been serving Calgary’s construction industry for over 72 years. In addition to our interior finishing division, Gunther’s supplies, fabricates, and installs hollow metal doors and pressed steel frames for commercial, multifamily, and residential projects throughout Calgary and surrounding areas.

With an in-house fabrication shop and experienced field installation teams, we coordinate projects from blueprint to final installation.

We are currently looking for a motivated and detail-oriented individual to join our team as a Hollow Metal Project Coordinator.

This is an entry-level career opportunity with full training provided by Gunther’s. No previous hollow metal experience is required.

You will work directly with experienced industry professionals who will train you in estimating, blueprint reading, project coordination, fabrication processes, and construction operations.

Position Overview

This role is ideal for someone who wants to build a long-term career in construction project coordination, estimating, and building materials.

The successful candidate will learn how to:

  • Read blueprints and architectural drawings
  • Determine hollow metal door and frame requirements
  • Coordinate fabrication, supply, and installation of projects
  • Manage projects from quotation through completion
  • Work with site supervisors, installers, fabrication staff, and customers

This position combines office coordination, technical learning, problem-solving, and project management in a fast-paced construction environment.

Key Responsibilities

  • Review blueprints, specifications, and architectural drawings
  • Assist with material takeoffs and estimating for hollow metal doors and frames
  • Prepare quotes, work orders, and project documentation
  • Coordinate fabrication schedules with our in-house metal shop
  • Coordinate deliveries, installations, and project timelines
  • Communicate with contractors, site supervisors, installers, and suppliers
  • Track project progress, changes, and deficiencies
  • Assist with project closeout and customer support
  • Maintain accurate records and organized project files

What You’ll Learn

  • Hollow metal door and frame systems
  • Blueprint and specification reading
  • Construction estimating and takeoffs
  • Commercial hardware coordination
  • Project scheduling and logistics
  • Fabrication and installation processes
  • Construction project management fundamentals

Qualifications

  • Strong attention to detail
  • Strong organizational and time management skills
  • Ability to work in a fast-paced environment
  • Good communication skills
  • Comfortable learning technical products and drawings
  • Strong problem-solving mindset
  • Basic computer skills (Microsoft Office)

Assets (Not Required)

  • Construction experience
  • Blueprint reading exposure
  • Estimating or project coordination experience
  • Knowledge of doors, frames, or hardware
  • Experience in building materials or construction supply

Ideal Candidate

  • Highly organized and detail-oriented
  • Motivated to learn a specialized construction trade
  • Reliable and consistent
  • Strong communicator
  • Able to manage multiple moving pieces at once
  • Interested in long-term career growth within construction operations

Growth Opportunity

This role is intended as a long-term career path with advancement opportunities into:

  • Estimating
  • Senior Project Coordination
  • Hollow Metal Project Management
  • Commercial Door & Hardware Sales
  • Operations Management
