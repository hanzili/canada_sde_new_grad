About the Role

About Index Exchange

Programmatic ad exchange platform, Canadian tech leader

Full Description

At Index Exchange, we’re reinventing how digital advertising works—at scale. As a global advertising supply-side platform, we empower the world’s leading media owners and marketers to thrive in a programmatic, privacy-first ecosystem.

We’re a proud industry pioneer with over 20 years of experience accelerating the ad technology evolution. Our proprietary tech is trusted by some of the world’s largest brands and media owners and plays a crucial role in keeping the internet open, accessible, and largely free.

We process more than 550 billion real-time auctions every day (in comparison, Google processes 8.5 billion searches per day) with ultra-low latency. Our platform is vertically integrated from servers to networks and runs primarily on our own metal and cloud infrastructure. This end-to-end infrastructure is designed to provide both stability and agility, enabling us to adapt quickly as the market evolves.

At the core of it all is our engineering-first culture. Our engineers tackle internet-scale problems across tight-knit, global teams. From moving petabytes of data and optimizing with AI to making real-time infrastructure decisions, Indexers have the agency and influence to shape the future of advertising. We move fast, build thoughtfully, and stay grounded in our core values.

What We’re Looking For

  • Team Player: You will work in an agile team to help accomplish the team’s goals while interacting with other teams. How do you work with others to find the best solution?
  • Curiosity: Do you enjoy asking questions, learning, and creating solutions?
  • Problem Solvers: You don't stop until the problem gets solved, and you find more than one way to solve it. What problem have you solved recently?
  • Passion: You love tech and top-notch code, and plan to make it better. What tech are you passionate about?
  • Knowledge Hungry: Learning new systems, designs, patterns and languages is exciting to you. Which elegant design do you admire?
  • Well-rounded: Love to learn about the business domain as much as you love technology. What business excites you?
  • Driven: You are looking to grow your career quickly and to support those around you while you do it. Do you have what it takes?

Here’s What You’ll Be Doing

  • Solve challenging problems in a high-scale, low-latency environment

  • Develop our foundational platform that enables other engineers to build upon

  • Provide standards, templates

  • Experiment with new technologies

  • Contribute to code reviews, testing and deployments into our containerized environment

  • Participate in agile ceremonies
  • Opportunities to work with Golang, K8S, Ansible, Grafana and Kafka

Here's What You Need

  • Currently enrolled in a BSc/MSc in Computer Science or a related field/equivalent degree
  • You are talented, a fast learner and embrace change
  • Exceptional problem-solving skills, the ability to think differently, and an understanding that compromise, negotiation and practicality are important qualities
  • Self-motivated with a go-getter attitude
  • Proficiency in Golang, Python, Java, Scala or other high-level languages
  • A good grasp of SQL and NoSQL databases
  • Basic fluency using Linux/Unix command line
  • The ability to push our product to the max, inspire those around you and have fun doing it
  • A desire to work with and learn from the people around you – we greatly value people and interactions
  • Team player, receptive to feedback and learn by doing

Why You’ll Love Working Here

  • Employee assistance program
  • Mental health first aid program that provides an in-the-moment point of contact and reassurance
  • Bi-weekly town halls and regular community-led team events
  • Multiple resources and programming to support continuous learning
  • A workplace that supports a diverse, equitable, and inclusive environment
