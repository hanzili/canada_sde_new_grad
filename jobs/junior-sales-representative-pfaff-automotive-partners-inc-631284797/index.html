Full Description

Junior Sales Representative

BMW Markham, Markham, ON L3R 5M2

Are you ready to elevate your career in the automotive industry? BMW Markham is on the hunt for a motivated and enthusiastic Junior Sales Representative to join our team. Now in partnership with North America’s largest automotive retailer, Lithia and Driveway, it’s your chance to work for a Fortune 500 company and grow with us! If you're passionate about automobiles and dedicated to delivering top-notch customer service, you could be the perfect fit.

As a Junior Sales Representative, you'll have the opportunity to build strong customer relationships, share your love for all things automotive, and help clients discover their dream cars.

Join us in this exciting role and be a part of our continued success. Drive your career forward with BMW Markham!

Primary Responsibilities:

  • Greet customers and offering assistance or direction to any customer who enters the dealership showroom or lot.

  • Generate positive impressions during the shopping and buying experiences by serving customers in a friendly, professional, and prompt manner.

  • Provide customers with complete explanation of manufacturer and dealership service procedures and policies.

  • Prepare sold vehicles for customer delivery prior to customer arrival.

  • Deliver vehicle to customers, ensuring that the customer understands the vehicle’s operating features, warranty, and paperwork.

  • Perform other duties as required by the Sales Manager to ensure the successful operation of the dealership.

Required Skills and Experience:

  • Automotive luxury vehicle sales experience preferred, but not required.

  • 1-3 years experience in a similar role preferred.

  • Valid OMVIC licensed

  • Valid Driver’s License and clean driver’s abstract

  • Excellent interpersonal, communication (verbal and written) and customer service skills.

  • Exceptional sales skills and a commitment to sales objectives.

Why WorkwithPfaff?

  • CompetitiveCompensation - whether you are working in sales, service, parts, or in a support function, Pfaff offers a competitive compensation package.

  • Health Benefits – full-time employees have access to health, dental, long-term disability and life insurance.

  • Vacation and Paid Time Off

  • Track Days

  • Growth Potential– we want to support your success! We provide access to opportunities for growth in both Canada and the U.S.

  • Stock Purchase Program - Pfaff employees can purchase the Lithia & Driveway stock (NYSE: LAD) at a discount.

Pay: $50,000-$60,000

Vacancy: This is an existing vacancy
