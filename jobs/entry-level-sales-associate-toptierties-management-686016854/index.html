About Toptierties Management

Full Description

Looking for a full-time role where you can build real skills, work with people, and actually see your progress?

We are currently hiring an Entry Level Sales Associate to join our team. This is a hands-on position where you will be interacting with customers daily, learning how to guide conversations, and helping people make confident decisions around services that fit their needs.

No previous experience is required. If you are reliable, open to learning, and comfortable speaking with people face-to-face, you will be given the training and support to succeed.

You will be working directly with customers, having conversations, answering questions, and helping them understand their options. Some days are fast-paced, some require patience and attention to detail, but the goal is always the same: create a smooth, professional experience for every person you interact with.

What your day might look like:

  • Speaking with customers and introducing available services
  • Asking questions to understand what they are actually looking for
  • Explaining options in a clear and straightforward way
  • Handling questions and concerns without overcomplicating things
  • Working alongside your team to stay consistent and improve results
  • Learning as you go and building confidence with each interaction

This role is a good fit if you:

  • Prefer being active and working with people rather than sitting behind a desk all day
  • Enjoy staying active throughout the day and feel comfortable being on your feet while engaging with customers
  • Communicate clearly and carry yourself in a professional way
  • Are dependable and show up ready to work
  • Want to learn practical skills that can carry into long-term opportunities
  • Have experience in customer service, retail, hospitality, or are looking to break into something new
  • A valid Driver’s License and a personal vehicle are considered an asset

What you get out of it:

  • Full-time, consistent work
  • Training that is straightforward and easy to follow
  • Competitive earnings averaging $850 weekly
  • A team environment where people actually support each other
  • The chance to build confidence in communication and client interaction
  • Room to grow into more responsibility over time

This is a full-time, in-person role, daily office attendance is required

Job Types: Full-time, Permanent

Pay: $45,000.00-$56,000.00 per year
