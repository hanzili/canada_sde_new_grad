About the Role

This summer, we’re offering a 16-week paid internship (starting May 2026) for a Web Development Intern. You’ll get real hands-on experience developing, optimizing, and troubleshooting websites while working alongside experienced developers.

Candidates must be located in Calgary and currently enrolled in post-secondary school.

Responsibilities:

  • Build, customize, and maintain client websites using WordPress and Gutenberg.
  • Support front-end development: HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and responsive design.
  • Assist with back-end tasks including PHP, MySQL, and plugin implementation.
  • Conduct QA, debugging, and troubleshooting of web projects.
  • Implement on-page SEO best practices and monitor site performance.
  • Collaborate with designers, content specialists, and marketing strategists.
  • Learn and use Git/version control for project workflows.
  • Participate in internal culture events—foosball tournaments, office games, and team lunches.

Candidate Profile

Experience / Skills:

  • Familiarity with WordPress development (themes, plugins, CMS)
  • Knowledge of PHP, MySQL, HTML, CSS, JavaScript, MySQL/MariaDB, WordPress plugin development
  • Exposure to Git or other version control systems
  • Understanding of responsive design and website performance optimization
  • Nice-to-have: Elementor, Divi or Shopify development experience
  • Portfolio of websites/projects is a plus

Qualities:

  • Detail-oriented: you sweat the small stuff.
  • Curious and eager to learn: you love experimenting with new tools and technologies.
  • Problem-solver: you enjoy debugging and finding elegant solutions.
  • Team player: you communicate well and collaborate easily.
  • Passionate about web development and building high-quality websites.

Internship Details & Perks

  • Duration: 16-week paid internship starting May 2026
  • Compensation: $20.00 per hour
  • Grant Funding: Eligibility: Must be a full-time student in a post-secondary program and a Canadian citizen, permanent resident, or hold refugee status. International students are not eligible.
  • Potential Full-Time Opportunity: High-performing interns may be offered full-time positions with competitive salaries.

Perks include:

  • Fun office culture and games (foosball, tournaments, office challenges)
  • Fully stocked kitchen and free parking
  • Work alongside a fast-growing team in a collaborative environment

Portfolio Links: Please include links to websites or projects you’ve worked on.
