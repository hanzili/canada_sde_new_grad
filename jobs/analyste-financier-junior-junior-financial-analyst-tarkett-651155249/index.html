Soyez le Champion de votre avenir avec Tarkett Sports et FieldTurf. Joignez-vous à un chef de file mondial en construction sportive et revêtement de surfaces sportives.

FieldTurf, chef de file mondial en gazon artificiel avec plus de 25 000 installations.
Présente de la NFL à la MLS en passant par les universités renommées, les établissements scolaires d'élite et les installations locales, notre équipe façonne l’avenir du sport.

Axé sur l'innovation et l'efficacité, FieldTurf recherche un·e Analyste financier junior prêt·e à soutenir notre croissance et à contribuer à l’excellence. Relevant du Directeur financier, l’Analyste financier junior appuiera la performance financière des projets en Amérique du Nord en analysant les marges, les budgets et les écarts, tout en collaborant étroitement avec les gestionnaires de projets et de construction. Il s’agit d’une excellente occasion pour une personne en début de carrière de développer de solides compétences analytiques, de se familiariser avec des opérations basées sur des projets et de contribuer à l’amélioration continue des processus dans un environnement en croissance et en évolution. Ce poste suit un horaire hybride basé au bureau de Montréal.

FieldTurf fait partie de Tarkett Sports. Issue d’un esprit entrepreneurial et animée par la recherche de solutions toujours plus performantes, notre équipe remet en question le statu quo et continue de transformer le jeu pour les athlètes et les propriétaires d’installations, en proposant des innovations qui ont redéfini le secteur.

Notre équipe est composée d’experts les plus chevronnés et les plus compétents dans leur domaine.

De la conception architecturale à la construction intégrale, en passant par une gamme complète de solutions de revêtements sportifs de calibre mondial, nous menons nos activités avec passion, fierté et détermination.

Reconnue à tous les niveaux — des stades professionnels aux installations communautaires locales — Tarkett Sports a réalisé plus de 80 000 projets, notamment dans le cadre de la NFL, la LCF, la MLS et de prestigieux programmes de division I de la NCAA.

Joignez-vous à l’équipe derrière des marques de calibre telles que FieldTurf, Beynon Sports, ClassicCourt, GrassMaster, Tarkett Sports Indoor et Tarkett Sports Construction. Contribuez à bâtir la prochaine installation sportive de calibre mondial.

  • Suivre les budgets de projets et signaler les risques ou écarts

Soutien ponctuel et interfonctionnel

Amélioration des processus et outils

  • Utiliser des outils tels qu’Excel, Power BI et d’autres technologies pour accroître l’efficacité

Champion Your Future with Tarkett Sports and FieldTurf. Build your career with a world leader in sports construction and surfacing.

We are seeking a Junior Financial Analyst to support our team at FieldTurf, a world leader in artificial turf with over 25,000 installations worldwide. Trusted at every level, FieldTurf is at the surface of NFL and MLS stadiums, renowned collegiate programs, elite high schools, and local community venues. Focused on innovation with purpose, FieldTurf is helping Change the Game for athletes and facility owners.

Reporting to Financial Director, the Junior Financial Analyst will support project financial performance across North America by analyzing margins, budgets, and variances, while partnering closely with Project and Construction Managers. This is an excellent opportunity for someone early in their career to build strong analytical skills, gain exposure to project-based operations, and contribute to process improvements in a growing, evolving environment. This role follows a hybrid schedule based out of the Montreal Office.

FieldTurf is part of Tarkett Sports. Born from an entrepreneurial spirit and driven to find better solutions, we challenge the status quo and continue to change the game for athletes and facility owners—pioneering innovations that have reshaped entire industries.

Our team is made up of the most experienced and knowledgeable experts in the field.

From architectural design and full-scope construction to a comprehensive portfolio of industry-leading sports surfacing solutions, we lead with passion, pride, and purpose.

Trusted at every level, from professional stadiums to local community venues, Tarkett Sports is at the surface of over 80,000 installations – highlighted by projects in NFL, CFL, MLS, and at renowned NCAA division I programs.

Join the team behind renowned names like FieldTurf, Beynon Sports, ClassicCourt, GrassMaster, Tarkett Sports Indoor and Tarkett Sports Construction. Help us build the next world-class venue.

What you’ll do:

Project Financial Analysis

  • Support month-end reporting by preparing and reviewing project margins
  • Partner with Project Managers and Construction Managers to ensure accurate financial data
  • Monitor project budgets and flag risks or variances
  • Assist with variance analysis and contribute insights for Monthly Business Reviews
  • Maintain and update recurring financial files and datasets

Financial Planning & Reporting

  • Prepare daily sales reports and track performance against forecasts
  • Investigate variances and provide actionable insights
  • Support forecasting and budgeting activities

Ad Hoc & Cross-Functional Support

  • Assist with audit requests (internal and external)
  • Support ad-hoc financial analysis and reporting
  • Collaborate with Finance, Operations, Pricing, Tax, and IT on various initiatives
  • Contribute to sales commission calculations

Process Improvement & Tools

  • Identify opportunities to improve financial processes and reporting
  • Support automation and data quality initiatives
  • Leverage tools like Excel, Power BI, and other technologies to enhance efficiency

What you need for success:

  • Bachelor’s degree in Finance, Accounting, or a related field
  • 1+ year of experience in financial analysis, cost accounting, or project accounting

Skills & Competencies

  • Strong analytical mindset with attention to detail
  • Ability to identify trends, variances, and key drivers
  • Comfortable working in a fast-paced, evolving environment
  • Strong problem-solving skills and proactive attitude
  • Ability to work both independently and collaboratively
  • Effective communication skills (English, written and spoken)

Technical Skills

  • Proficiency in Microsoft Excel
  • Interest in learning data and reporting tools (e.g., Power BI, Power Query, automation tools)
  • Open to challenging the status quo and improving processes
  • Adaptable and comfortable with change

What you can expect:

  • A culture built on teamwork, respect, and entrepreneurial spirit
  • Leadership that invests in your growth and career development
  • Opportunities to take on new challenges, cross-train, and expand your skillset
  • Recognition and rewards for your contributions and achievements
  • A fast-paced, sports-driven environment where your work makes an impact
