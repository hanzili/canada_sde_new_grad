Full Description

Company Description

Milya AI is revolutionizing dental care by creating advanced AI-powered solutions designed to simplify clinical and administrative workflows in dental practices. Our mission is to enable dental professionals to focus more on providing quality patient care by reducing paperwork and automating routine tasks. With innovative tools like Josh, an AI dental scribe for real-time clinical documentation, and Lucy, a 24/7 virtual receptionist, we help practices operate more efficiently without compromising the human touch. Headquartered on a patient-centric approach, we serve practices of all sizes, helping them work smarter and faster to deliver better experiences for both staff and patients.

Role Description

We are seeking a Software Engineer Intern to join our dynamic team on-site at our Montreal, QC office. As a Software Engineer Intern, your day-to-day responsibilities will include developing, debugging, and enhancing software solutions, collaborating with team members to implement back-end web development tasks, and contributing to the optimization of our intelligent AI systems. You will also perform hands-on programming and support the integration of object-oriented programming principles into various development projects.

Qualifications

  • Foundational knowledge in Computer Science, with an understanding of data structures and algorithms
  • Proficiency in Back-End Web Development and Software Development processes
  • Strong Programming skills with experience in languages such as Python, Java, or C++
  • Knowledge of Object-Oriented Programming (OOP) concepts and their practical applications
  • Ability to collaborate in a fast-paced team environment and learn new technologies quickly
  • Bachelor's degree in progress in Computer Science, Software Engineering, or a related field
  • Prior experience in AI and automation projects is a plus
