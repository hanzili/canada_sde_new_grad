About A Plus Coating Solutions Inc.

ISO 17025 accredited lab, coatings testing

Full Description

We are a third-party ISO 17025 accredited laboratory, specialized in protective coating testing services. Our team is committed to hands-on training and professional development for entry-level staff.

Role Overview:
We are seeking a motivated and detail-oriented Laboratory Technician to assist with sample preparation, testing, and organizing data. Full training will be provided.

Key Responsibilities:

· Perform testing according to established procedures and standards

· Prepare samples and maintain accurate test records

· Assist with laboratory equipment setup, calibration, and maintenance

· Ensure compliance with ISO/IEC 17025 quality and safety requirements

  • Maintain an organized and clean laboratory workspace

Ideal Candidate

· Hands-on and comfortable working with tools and laboratory equipment

· Detail-oriented with strong record-keeping skills

  • Reliable, organized, and able to follow standardized procedures

Requirements:

  • Minimum College, CEGEP or other non-university certificate or diploma from a program of 1 year to 2 years, or equivalent

  • Hands-on and comfortable working with tools and laboratory equipment

  • attention to detail with strong organizational and record-keeping skills

  • Ability to understand, interpret data accurately, and communicate clearly and effectively with coworkers

Job Type: Full-time

Pay: $18.00-$22.00 per hour

Expected hours: 40 per week

Benefits:

  • On-site parking
  • Paid time off

Work Location: In person
