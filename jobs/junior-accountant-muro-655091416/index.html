Junior Accountant

at MURO

About the Role

About MURO

Manufacturing tools company with long industry presence

Full Description

Company Description

MURO is a leader in innovative and adaptable auto feed systems, offering advanced tools, screws, and accessories for a wide range of applications, including roofing, decking, drywall, and more. With over 35 years of experience, MURO has built a reputation for delivering dependable, user-friendly products that save time and enhance efficiency for professionals. Designed with versatility in mind, MURO’s solutions prioritize the needs of customers, making installation easier and more effective. As a trusted provider, the company combines manufacturing, engineering, and distribution to deliver high-quality products and exceptional customer service.

Role Description

This is a full-time, on-site role based in Brampton, ON, for a Junior Accountant. The Junior Accountant will assist in managing daily financial transactions, including the preparation of financial reports, maintaining accurate records, and supporting month-end processes. The role involves processing accounts payable/receivable, reconciling accounts, and assisting with audits and regulatory compliance. Collaboration with multiple departments to ensure accuracy and efficiency will be a key aspect of the position.

Qualifications

  • Proficiency in financial tasks such as accounts payable/receivable management, account reconciliation, and month-end reporting
  • Familiarity with accounting software and tools, as well as intermediate to advanced Excel skills
  • Strong attention to detail and accuracy in record-keeping and data entry
  • Knowledge of regulatory compliance and basic auditing processes
  • Excellent analytical, organizational, and time management skills
  • Bachelor's degree in Accounting, Finance, or a related field preferred but not required
  • Effective communication skills and ability to work collaboratively in a team
  • Work experience in accounting or finance is an asset but not mandatory
  • Experience in the manufacturing or tools industry is an advantage

Benefits

  • Dental Care
  • Extended Health Care
  • PTO
  • Profit Sharing
  • RRSP Match
  • Tuition Reimbursement
  • Vision Care
  • Casual Dress

This position is an excellent entry point for individuals looking to grow their career in accounting within a professional setting that values accuracy, integrity, and continuous learning. This role reports directly to the Senior Manager and Controller.
