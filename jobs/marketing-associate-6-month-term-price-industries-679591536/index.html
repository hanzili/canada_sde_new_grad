About the Role

About Price Industries

Leading air distribution products manufacturer and distributor

Full Description

Who We Are Price Industries Limited, North America’s leading manufacturer and distributor of air distribution products (www.priceindustries.com). The founding principles of Price have never changed – business integrity, first-class service, innovation and a commitment to people. Our dependable quality, reliable delivery, and knowledgeable follow-up service have made Price a market leader in supplying air distribution, critical controls, and noise control products.

After more than 60 years, Price remains a privately held family company with a deep heritage and commitment to innovation and service. Our long-standing vision, values, and the Price way of doing business are the cornerstones of our success.

We are a dynamic, progressive and innovative organization looking for new talent in our growing Winnipeg manufacturing facility. If you thrive on new challenges, and working in a friendly, team-driven environment, you should consider the following opportunity.

What You Can Expect

  • Personal and Professional Development - We are committed to helping you reach your potential through training and education.
  • Community Involvement - Generously supporting health, charities, youth, and education opportunities is our passion, and you’re invited to be a part of that.
  • Our convenient Price Café offers free Tea and Coffee.

The Account Coordinator plays a key role in shaping and executing the company’s marketing strategy across multiple channels and product categories. This individual is responsible for developing integrated marketing campaigns that drive brand awareness, support sales growth, and position the company as an industry leader in innovative solutions. The ideal candidate combines strategic thinking with hands-on execution and has experience in in both strategic and project management

The ideal candidate is a detail-oriented multitasker who thrives in a fast-paced, collaborative environment and understands both creative production workflows and marketing operations.

The successful candidate's responsibilities will include:

Project Planning & Coordination

  • Develop detailed project plans, timelines, and schedules for marketing initiatives, ensuring alignment with overall business objectives.
  • Coordinate with creative teams, and external vendors to define project scope, deliverables, and milestones.
  • Allocate and manage resources effectively to balance workload across projects.

Campaign & Content Development

  • Lead the creation of integrated campaigns, including digital, print, events, and trade marketing initiatives.
  • Oversee the development of product literature, case studies, videos, presentations, and other sales tools.
  • Manage content calendars for web, email, and social platforms, ensuring consistent brand voice and messaging.

Sales Enablement

  • Partner with sales teams and manufacturer’s representatives to develop tools and resources that drive engagement and conversion.
  • Support new product launches with coordinated communications, collateral, and promotional activities.
  • Measure campaign performance and provide data-driven insights to optimize future programs.

Brand & Communications Management

  • Ensure alignment of all marketing materials with brand standards and corporate messaging.
  • Oversee trade show planning, event coordination, and sponsorships to maximize visibility and lead generation.
  • Manage external agencies and vendors for creative, digital, and media services.

Qualifications

  • Education: Bachelor’s degree in Marketing, Communications, Design Management, or related field preferred.
  • Experience: 1–2 years in marketing project management, traffic coordination, or creative operations.
  • Strong organizational, communication, and time-management skills.
  • Ability to manage multiple priorities and deadlines in a collaborative environment.
  • Understanding of creative production processes for print, digital, and multimedia assets.
  • Detail-oriented with a passion for maintaining structure and efficiency.

Should this excellent opportunity interest you, please submit your resume, including salary expectations.

Price Industries Limited hires on the basis of merit and is strongly committed to equity and diversity. We welcome applications from all qualified candidates, including all genders, Indigenous peoples, persons with disabilities, members of visible minorities, individuals of diverse gender and sexual orientation and all groups protected by the Human Rights Code.

We are happy to provide reasonable accommodation throughout the selection process and while working at Price. If you require support applying online because you are a person with a disability, please contact us at HR@priceindustries.com.

We appreciate the interest shown by all applicants, however only those being considered for an interview will be contacted.
