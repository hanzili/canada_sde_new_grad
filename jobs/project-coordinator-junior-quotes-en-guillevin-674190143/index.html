Full Description

Project Coordinator / Junior Quotes

Status Regular, Full time

Location: Burnaby, British Columbia

Job Description Interested?

  • Steady local commercial customers.
  • Reliable, high-quality products.
  • Have an impact on the business centre’s profitability and be rewarded for that.

Do you have what we’re looking for?

  • Experience with project coordination and estimating in the construction industry.
  • Understanding of the dynamics of supplier relationships.
  • Good knowledge of our product lines.
  • Excellent communication, problem-solving and negotiating skills.
  • Self-reliant, well-organized and good at prioritizing.
  • Dynamic, proactive person known to be adaptable and to work well in a team.

Challenges Awaiting You

  • Provide contractors with accurate estimates and work with key suppliers and other internal associates to provide the right products at the right time in the right place.
  • Check the requirements, scope and objectives of each project to ensure that it is managed efficiently and profitably. Liaise with internal and external stakeholders to identify and meet project requirements.
  • Rigorously manage all aspects of purchase orders: documentation, communication, fulfillment and billing.
  • Monitor the progress of projects and deal with problems as they arise (flag and escalate to management if necessary). Follow up with the customer to guarantee the brand promise and a delivery that meets the needs of the project.
  • As necessary, negotiate prices and conditions of sale with suppliers in the best interests of the customer, while respecting acceptable margins.
  • Establish, develop and maintain strong business relationships with customers.
  • Keep your technical knowledge of product lines up to date and stay abreast of new developments in order to respond effectively to customers’ needs. Demonstrate a keen interest in improving your business skills.

What’s In It For You We are a major player, involved in the energy transformation in Canada. Our dynamic work environment promotes autonomy and decision making, and offers great career opportunities.

What Sets Us Apart Is Our

  • Annual profit-sharing offered to all in addition to our competitive compensation: rewarding accomplishments is part of our culture.
  • A strong national network offering real opportunities to grow: our people make the difference.

Among Our Benefits

  • Flexible group insurance plan customizable to your needs.
  • Free health resources available 24/7: Telemedicine and Employee Assistance Program (EAP).
  • Group RRSP with employer contribution and TFSA.
  • Postsecondary Scholarship Program for our employee’s children.
  • Charging terminals available at our facilities.
  • Years of Service Recognition Program.

Come build your career with us, a growing network where our people make it happen!

Our recruitment process ensures equality and diversity. Please note that only successful candidates will be contacted.
