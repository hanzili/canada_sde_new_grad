About the Role

Support project management tasks and reporting

About Modern Niagara

Co-op construction firm fostering project coordination skills

Full Description

Role Summary We currently have Project Coordinator co‑op opportunities for talented and technically inclined students who enjoy a wide variety of tasks, learn quickly, and want to contribute to a successful and growing company. These roles offer hands‑on experience, creative challenges, and opportunities to develop new skills.

This is a 12-16 month co-op term starting in May 2026, working out of our Southwest Ontario region office and/or site locations.

Why This Role Matters to Modern Niagara This co‑op role provides students with meaningful exposure to real project operations and the chance to work closely with experienced industry professionals. It allows students to see firsthand how project teams coordinate work, maintain schedules, solve challenges, and bring buildings to life. Co‑op students play an important role in supporting successful project delivery while building valuable technical and professional skills that cannot be gained in a classroom. This experience helps develop the next generation of project leaders for Modern Niagara and the construction industry.

Key Impact / Purpose

  • Gain hands‑on experience in real construction project delivery
  • Strengthen communication and collaboration across project teams
  • Build foundational project management and coordination skills
  • Contribute directly to active project teams and field operations

Duties and Responsibilities:

  • Work with Project Managers to gather information and support reporting
  • Assist with site contract document control
  • Coordinate with general contractors on project priorities and activities
  • Support and participate in meetings with subcontractors and clients
  • Assist in managing and monitoring construction schedules
  • Relay information between office personnel and field staff
  • Support Project Managers, Coordinators, and Forepersons on additional project tasks as needed

Must Haves:

  • Working toward a university degree or college diploma in Mechanical, Civil, or Electrical Engineering (or related education)
  • Strong organizational skills and attention to detail
  • Strong computer skills, including MS Office (Excel and Word)
  • Team‑oriented, energetic, resourceful, reliable, and punctual
  • Eager to work and learn in a fast‑paced construction environment
  • Comfortable working both on‑site and in an office setting
  • Eligible for reliability security clearance.

Nice to Haves:

  • Previous co‑op, internship, or project experience
  • Experience in the construction industry (asset but not required)
  • Basic understanding of plumbing or HVAC systems (asset but not required)
  • Working at Heights Certificate (training can be provided)

Expected hourly compensation; $21 - $25.

About Modern Niagara Who We Are Modern Niagara helps building owners, managers, and general contractors meet the mechanical, electrical, integrated building technology, and building services needs of their buildings across Vancouver, Calgary, Edmonton, Toronto, Southwestern Ontario, and Ottawa. We are committed to having a positive and meaningful impact on Canada’s infrastructure — and on the communities where we live, learn, work, and heal. We build for life.

Our Culture & Values We value passion, initiative, determination, professionalism, and teamwork. Health and safety are embedded in everything we do, and our award-winning safety culture consistently performs well above industry standards.

Diversity, Equity & Inclusion at Modern Niagara We are committed to building a diverse, equitable, and inclusive organization where everyone has access to opportunity, feels a sense of belonging, and can thrive. We actively work to remove barriers faced by members of equity-seeking groups (including women, Indigenous, Black, racialized, 2SLGBTQIA+ people, people with disabilities, and newcomers to Canada) in the construction industry. We encourage applications from candidates of all backgrounds and lived experiences and are committed to a barrier-free hiring process.

If you require accommodation at any stage of the recruitment process, please contact us at careers@modernniagara.com so we can make appropriate arrangements.

We appreciate every applicant’s interest in joining Modern Niagara. Only those selected for an interview will be contacted.
