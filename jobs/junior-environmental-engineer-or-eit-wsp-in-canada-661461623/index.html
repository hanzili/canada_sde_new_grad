The Opportunity Your Impact Starts Here - As a Junior Environmental Engineer / EIT in our Prairies & North region, you will contribute to meaningful environmental projects that support resilient communities and sustainable development. You’ll grow your technical skills while collaborating with industry‑leading specialists who are shaping Canada’s environmental future.

Your Impact

  • Support field programs for contaminated sites, including Phase I/II ESAs, soil and groundwater sampling, remediation, and monitoring activities.
  • Assist with data collection, data management, and preparation of technical deliverables related to site assessment and remediation.
  • Contribute to environmental engineering design tasks for remediation systems and site‑specific solutions under the guidance of senior professionals.
  • Participate in environmental impact studies, risk assessments, permitting, and regulatory submissions.
  • Apply relevant provincial and federal environmental regulations, with a focus on contaminated sites guidance and standards.
  • Collaborate with multidisciplinary teams across the Prairies & North region to advance environmental and remediation projects.

The Skills That Set You Apart

  • Bachelor’s degree in Environmental Engineering or a related engineering discipline.
  • Registered, or eligible to register, as an Engineer‑in‑Training (EIT).
  • Experience with fieldwork in environmental assessment, remediation, or monitoring.
  • Familiarity with tools such as GIS, AutoCAD, environmental sampling equipment, or data analysis software.
  • A collaborative mindset and a desire to learn from seasoned environmental practitioners.
  • Ability to travel for project work across the Prairies & North region.
