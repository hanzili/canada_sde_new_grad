What you will do:

  • RAN Operations: Assist in the deployment, configuration, testing, and troubleshooting of RAN nodes and functions
  • Automation Development: Contribute to the development and refinement of automation scripts to streamline operations
  • AI Integration: Participate in AI-driven use-cases, including the automation of processes using AI agents and LLMs
  • Collaborative Projects: Work alongside team members on projects involving complex 5G feature testing and validation
  • Documentation and Reporting: Help in maintaining clear documentation and reporting of activities and results.

  • Knowledge and experience in using LLM APIs, understanding of LLM-agents and AI agentic frameworks and RAG
  • Knowledge with Kubernetes, including Container Network Interface (CNI)
