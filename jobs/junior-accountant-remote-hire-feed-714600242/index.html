About the Role

Review US tax regulations; ensure translation accuracy

About Hire Feed

Global financial services firm; AI tax solutions

Full Description

  • Role : Junior Accountant (Remote)
  • Location : Remote (Work from Anywhere)
  • Payout : $55 - $75/hour

Role Overview:

One of our clients, a global leader in the Financial Services industry, seeks a highly skilled Accountant (CPA/CA) to join their team on a contract basis. This is a unique opportunity to leverage expertise in US tax regulations to advance the next generation of AI-powered tax solutions. The role involves working remotely and collaborating with a global network of experts to enhance AI-generated translations of US tax statutes.

Key Responsibilities:

• Read and interpret original US tax laws, statutes, and IRS regulations to ensure accuracy and clarity.

• Review AI-produced plain-English translations to ensure alignment with original US tax laws and IRS regulations.

• Provide structured written communication to stakeholders regarding review findings and recommendations.

• Utilize analytical reasoning and attention to detail to identify and resolve discrepancies in AI-generated translations.

• Collaborate with a global network of experts to enhance AI-generated translations and ensure consistency across platforms.

Required Skills & Qualifications:

• Expertise in US tax law interpretation and IRS regulation analysis.

• Strong analytical reasoning and attention to detail.

• Excellent written communication skills, with ability to provide structured written communication to stakeholders.

• Familiarity with digital review platforms and AI-powered tax solutions.

• Ability to work independently and collaboratively in a remote setting.

More About the Opportunity:

This is a contract opportunity that allows for flexible scheduling and remote work. The ideal candidate will have strong technical skills and expertise in US tax regulations, with a passion for advancing AI-powered tax solutions.

Equal Opportunity Employer:

We hire based on skills and expertise. All qualified candidates are welcome regardless of background, experience, or prior employment history. Applications are reviewed solely on demonstrated technical ability and qualifications.
