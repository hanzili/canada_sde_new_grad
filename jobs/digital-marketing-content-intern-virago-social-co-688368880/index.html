Full Description

Job Title: Digital Marketing & Content Intern

Virago Social Co. is a growing marketing agency based in the Edmonton area, specializing in social media management, content creation, and website design for businesses across Alberta and beyond.

We’re looking for a creative, organized, and motivated individual to join our team as a Digital Marketing & Content Intern for the summer. This role is ideal for someone pursuing studies in marketing, communications, digital media, business, PR, graphic design, or a related field who is looking for hands-on agency experience.

This is a paid position with part-time hours and mentorship opportunities across multiple areas of marketing and content creation.

What You’ll Be Doing

  • Assisting with social media content planning and scheduling
  • Supporting Instagram, Facebook, TikTok, Google Business, and LinkedIn accounts for clients in a variety of industries
  • Writing captions and brainstorming content ideas
  • Creating high quality graphics and templates in Canva
  • Assisting with short-form video editing for Reels/TikTok
  • Supporting monthly content shoots and behind-the-scenes tasks
  • Assisting with analytics reporting and trend research
  • Helping maintain organized client folders and workflows
  • Learning agency systems and digital marketing strategies

What We’re Looking For

  • Currently enrolled in a related program
  • Able to drive, with your own vehicle for getting to and from shoots around the Edmonton area
  • Strong written communication skills
  • Comfortable using social media platforms professionally
  • TikTok/Reels editing experience
  • Strong familiarity with Canva and video editing apps
  • Creative mindset with attention to detail
  • Very organized and able to manage multiple tasks in a fast paced environment
  • Positive attitude and willingness to learn
  • Outgoing personality, able to connect with clients and go to in-person content shoots
  • Comfortable on camera

Bonus Skills

  • Photography or videography experience
  • Experience with Lightroom photo editing
  • Experience with website editing and/or email marketing
  • Understanding of branding and social media trends
  • Previous Experience in Social Media Marketing Role

Position Details

  • Part-time (3 days a week)
  • Flexible schedule
  • Must be available July 16-26 (will be needed to help with on site coverage for our client, Taste of Edmonton)
  • In-person shoots and meetings in the Edmonton area
  • Paid position
  • Start mid June to end of August
  • Opportunity for position extension and long-term growth within the company

To Apply

Please send:

  • Your resume
  • A cover letter or short introduction about yourself
  • Either a portfolio OR 2 social media graphics/posts you’ve created that showcase your style and skills

We’re excited to meet creative individuals who are passionate about marketing, storytelling, and helping brands grow!

Job Types: Part-time, Internship / Co-op
Contract length: 10 weeks

Pay: $20.00-$25.00 per hour

Work Location: Hybrid remote in Edmonton, AB
