Full Description

Gladiator Joe is seeking students who are required to complete an internship, work placement, practicum, co-op term, or stage as part of their educational program.

We welcome applications from students enrolled in vocational training, CEGEP, college, university, and other recognized educational programs where a workplace placement is required or recommended by the institution.

Opportunities may be available in the following areas:

  • Marketing
  • Accounting
  • Bookkeeping

Interns will have the opportunity to gain practical experience, develop industry skills, participate in real projects, and work alongside experienced professionals in a manufacturing and engineering environment.

Requirements:

  • Currently enrolled in a recognized educational program
  • Educational institution approval or placement requirement, where applicable
  • Strong interest in learning and professional development

Location: Montreal, Quebec

To apply, please submit your résumé and a brief description of your educational program and placement requirements.

Exigences :

Lieu : Montréal, Québec
