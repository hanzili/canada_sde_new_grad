About Trulioo

Full Description

Are you ready to embark on a career that truly affects people around the world? Trulioo invites you to be a catalyst for change in the dynamic realm of digital identity verification. As the global front-runner in our industry, we are redefining how businesses grow, innovate and comply online.

Picture yourself at the forefront of innovation, contributing to our award-winning platform that enables organizations worldwide to quickly onboard customers, optimize costs and combat fraud. Fueled by Silicon Valley support, Trulioo stands as the trusted platform that can verify more than 5 billion people and 700 million business entities spanning 195 countries.

But Trulioo is more than a tech company. We are a united force of dedicated experts committed to establishing trust online.

Headquartered in Vancouver and with strategic hubs in San Diego and Dublin, we foster a culture of collaboration and open communication. Our offices support a hybrid model and staff typically work three days per week at a hub location. Join us where excitement meets innovation and contribute to a world where trust and technology unite.

Position Summary:

We are looking for a motivated Junior Quality Assurance Engineer to join our QA team and support the Fraud and Risk solution. Reporting to the Manager, Quality Assurance, the successful candidate will be comfortable working in a fast-paced, collaborative environment, working directly with development team members.

This is a full-time hybrid position based out of our Mount Pleasant office in Vancouver, with three in-office days per week

What You’ll Be Doing:

  • Work closely with the software engineering team to capture requirements for test automation framework, define processes, set up and maintain automation framework and library of automated tests for web-based SaaS app
  • Work with the product team to identify, document requirements, and provide advice on company systems strategy, policy, security and service delivery
  • Write, execute, and monitor automated tests to analyze and publish results and interpretation, within scheduled deadlines
  • Set up test data as required to reproduce issues and execute positive, negative and edge case automation testing
  • Follow the process for defect management, release testing and overall product quality assurance
  • Create and maintain test process documentation, scripts and tools
  • Execute manual smoke and final acceptance testing as needed for software releases
  • As needed, assist support with reproducing, diagnosing and resolving customer issues

What You’ll Bring:

  • 1+ years of experience supporting the delivery of quality products in agile teams using cutting-edge technology
  • Strong development/programming skills are required
  • Course experience with the following technologies: C++/Java/.Net, JavaScript, SQL, HTML
  • Project experience with source control systems such as Git, SVN, etc an asset
  • Experience working in project team
  • Self-starter looking to learn and contribute significantly to the team
  • Able to work independently with minimal supervision
  • Proficient in the English language, with excellent communication skills, both written and verbal are a must
