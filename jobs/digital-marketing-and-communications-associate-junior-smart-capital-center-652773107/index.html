About the Role

About Smart Capital Center

AI-powered real estate analytics startup

Full Description

Smart Capital Center is building the first AI-powered platform for commercial real estate investment and financial management. Our platform combines real-time data, automation, and AI insights to help investors move faster, reduce risk, and uncover better opportunities.

We’re looking for a Junior Digital Marketing Associate who is hands-on, creative, and uses AI tools and agents daily to execute campaigns, create content, and drive growth.

What You’ll Do

  • Execute campaigns across email, website, social media, and paid ads
  • Create and publish marketing content (social posts, blogs, email campaigns, landing pages)
  • Support and optimize Google Ads and LinkedIn Ads
  • Use AI tools and agents daily to generate content, run research, and automate workflows
  • Track performance and continuously improve campaigns using data
  • Support SEO, website updates, and marketing automation

What We’re Looking For

  • 2+ years experience in digital, content, or growth marketing
  • Strong content writing and copywriting skills
  • Experience with paid ads, email marketing, or analytics tools
  • Daily user of AI tools (ChatGPT, Claude, etc.) and comfortable working with AI-driven workflows
  • Basic understanding of technical marketing (tracking, analytics, automation)
  • High ownership mindset with an exceptional work ethic (work hard, play hard)
  • Growth-focused, fast learner who thrives in a fast-paced environment

Why Join Smart Capital Center

  • Real responsibility from day one, not just execution, but ownership
  • Work in a fast-paced, Silicon Valley-style startup environment
  • Learn quickly, move fast, and grow with the company
  • Build and market a cutting-edge AI product
