Full Description

About A1 Innovation Group.:

Specializing in People Counting and Parking Guidance systems, A1 Innovation Group has been helping businesses collect and leverage data since 1994. With integrated layers of technology and our hardware agnostic software, we assist clients by providing them with the tools they need to assure their asset is operating at peak performance. Our success stems from our exceptional customer support and commitment to value based partnerships with our clients.

Position Overview:

We are looking for a curious, detail-oriented, and technically capable Junior BI Developer & Data Analyst to support our reporting, dashboard, and analytics environment. This role will work closely with internal team members to support Dundas BI dashboards and reports, assist with PostgreSQL and database-related tasks, document reporting logic, and help strengthen backup coverage across the team. This is a hands-on opportunity for someone early in their career who wants to build practical experience in business intelligence, reporting tools, and data support in a growing organization.

Key Responsibilities:

  • Support the development, maintenance, and testing of dashboards, reports, and visualizations within Dundas BI and related reporting tools.
  • Assist with SQL queries, database updates, data validation, and report-related scripting.
  • Help troubleshoot dashboard, report, and data issues, including refresh failures, logic discrepancies, and data quality concerns.
  • Document data sources, calculations, business rules, report logic, and technical dependencies to improve knowledge sharing and backup coverage.
  • Support approved reporting requests and enhancements based on defined business needs and internal priorities.
  • Help maintain standard reports and dashboards while clearly identifying when a request falls out side standard scope.
  • Participate in testing and quality checks before report or dashboard changes are released.
  • Work with internal stakeholders to understand reporting requirements and translate them into practical, supportable outputs.
  • Support cross-training efforts within the team to reduce dependency on a single subject matter expert.

Qualifications:

  • 1-3 years of experience in business intelligence, reporting, analytics, data support, or a related role.
  • College diploma or university degree in Information Systems, Computer Science, Business Analytics, Data Analytics, or a related field.
  • Exposure to BI or visualization tools such as Dundas BI, Power BI, Tableau, or similar platforms.
  • Basic to intermediate PostgreSQL skills and comfort working with structured data and databases.
  • Strong attention to detail and ability to validate data accuracy and identify inconsistencies.
  • Good problem-solving skills and willingness to learn new systems, tools, and business processes.
  • Strong organization and follow-through, with the ability to manage multiple tasks in a small team environment.
  • Clear written and verbal communication skills.

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Exposure to Dundas BI or similar enterprise reporting platforms.
  • Familiarity with PostgreSQL or other relational databases.
  • Experience supporting operational reporting or client-facing dashboards.
  • Exposure to report calculations, scripting, or data transformation logic.

Why A1 Innovation Group

  • Be part of an innovative company with exciting growth opportunities.
  • Work on meaningful reporting and analytics solutions that support real client and business needs.
  • Gain hands-on experience in BI, data, and reporting within a collaborative and fast-paced environment.
  • Help strengthen and mature a growing analytics function while building valuable technical skills.
