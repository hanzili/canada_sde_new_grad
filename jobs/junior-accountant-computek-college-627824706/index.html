Position Summary

The Junior Accountant supports the Accounting team in maintaining accurate financial records and ensuring timely processing of accounting transactions. This role is ideal for an early-career professional looking to develop hands-on experience across core accounting functions, including reconciliations, invoicing, month-end support, and financial reporting.

Reporting to the Accounting Manager, the Junior Accountant plays an important role in supporting day-to-day accounting operations while ensuring compliance with internal controls and accounting standards.

Key Responsibilities

Duties include but are not limited to the following:

  • Perform bank reconciliations and investigate discrepancies.
  • Process and record invoices, ensuring accuracy and proper approvals.
  • Assist with month-end closing activities, including preparing schedules and supporting documentation.
  • Prepare and post journal entries as required.
  • Complete account reconciliations to ensure accuracy of general ledger accounts.
  • Assist in the preparation of financial statements and supporting schedules.
  • Support audit activities by preparing documentation and responding to audit inquiries.
  • Assist with internal and external financial reporting requirements.
  • Maintain organized and accurate financial records.
  • Support the Finance team with ad hoc accounting tasks and projects.
  • Collaborate with internal departments to resolve invoice and payment-related issues.
  • Other duties as assigned.
