Job description

Staff Accountant

St. Arnaud Pinsent Steman Chartered Professional Accountants, will report to Dan St. Arnaud

Edmonton AB

Job Summary:

The Staff Accountant is responsible for full-cycle accounting and the management of various bookkeeping, payroll, tax return preparation activities that are required by our clients.

Job Type:

Full-time, Permanent

Schedule:

  • 40 hour work week
  • Monday to Friday
  • In office, not a virtual position.

Experience:

  • Education :
  • Related post secondary diploma or degree CPA Student enrolled or expecting to enroll
  • Accounting: 3 years related experience in full cycle accounting (preferred), willing to train the right candidate
  • Accounting Software: related experience with accounting software (Caseware, TaxCycle, Sage, QuickBooks, QBO, Xero, etc.)

Job Duties:

  • Bookkeeping
  • Preparation of financial statements (e.g. balance sheet, P&L, statement of cash flows)
  • Preparation of Compilation statements and working papers
  • Preparation of assurance engagements (Reviews and Audits)
  • Tax preparation: T1 and T2
  • Preparation of T4 and T5 slips
  • Assist with payroll preparation and submission
  • Providing support to partners and senior team management in related administrative tasks
  • Work effectively with partners and the staff team

Skills & Abilities:

  • Detail-oriented with excellent organization and analytical skills
  • Excellent communication skills, written and in person
  • Strong critical thinking and reasoning skills
  • Able to complete work on deadlines and with little direct supervision
  • Driven, self-motivated, and goal-oriented

Application deadline: No set deadline

Expected start date: March 1, 2026, or as soon as possible

Job Type: Full-time

Salary: We are offering a competitive compensation package.

We thank all candidates that apply.

Only those selected for an interview will be contacted.
