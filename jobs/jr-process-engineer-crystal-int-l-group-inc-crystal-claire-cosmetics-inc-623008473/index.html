Job Title: Junior Process Engineer

Department: Automation Engineering

Wage Category: Annual Salary with Benefits

Report to: Director of Automation Engineering

Working Location: 165 Milner Avenue, Scarborough, ON M1S 4G7, Canada

Job Type: Full-time Permanent

Salary: $50,000 - $60,000 per annum

We are seeking a Junior Process Engineer to join our team and support the troubleshooting, analysis, and improvement of production processes. The ideal candidate will be a proactive problem-solver with a passion for process optimization and continuous improvement.

Responsibilities:

• Analyze and evaluate existing manufacturing processes to identify areas for improvement.

• Collaborate with senior engineers, technicians, and production teams to implement process optimizations.

• Identify and execute cost-reduction initiatives and process enhancements.

• Participate in the testing, validation, and commissioning of new production equipment.

• Prepare technical documentation, including automation processes, equipment manuals, and standard operating procedures (SOPs).

• Generate detailed technical reports and present findings to cross-functional teams.

• Perform other related duties as assigned.

Qualification:

• Post-secondary education in Science, Engineering, or a related discipline.

• Minimum of 2 years of relevant experience in a manufacturing environment (internship or co-op experience will be considered an asset).

• Strong analytical skills with the ability to interpret data and drive actionable insights.

• Excellent verbal and written communication skills.

• Demonstrated problem-solving abilities and a strong desire to learn.

• Ability to prioritize tasks, manage multiple projects, and meet deadlines in a fast-paced environment.

• Team-oriented mindset with strong interpersonal and collaboration skills.

We care for our employees:

• Convenient public transit (bus stop right outside the facility)

• On-site free parking

• On-site gym

• Employee Health Benefits

• Corporate Discount Program

• Group Retirement Saving Plan / Deferred Profit-Sharing Plan (GRSP/DPSP)
