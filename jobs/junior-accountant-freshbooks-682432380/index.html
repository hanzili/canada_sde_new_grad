Full Description

About The Role The Accountant supports core accounting operations with a primary focus on Accounts Payable processing in NetSuite and secondary support for general ledger activities. This role is responsible for ensuring accurate, timely, and controlled processing of vendor invoices in accordance with company policies and approval workflows, while also contributing to month-end close and financial reporting.

What You'll Do Accounts Payable (AP; Primary Focus)

  • Process vendor invoices in NetSuite in accordance with company approval workflows
  • Ensure all invoices meet internal control requirements (vendor setup, documentation, approvals)
  • Monitor invoice approval status and follow up with approvers as needed
  • Review of invoices for accuracy, duplicate payments, and proper coding
  • Support vendor setup and maintain accurate vendor records
  • Assist with weekly payment runs and resolve vendor inquiries

General Ledger (GL) and Close Support

  • Prepare and post journal entries
  • Assist with monthly account reconciliations
  • Support month-end and year-end close processes
  • Maintain organized supporting documentation for audit readiness
  • Assist with variance analysis and reporting as needed

Process and Controls

  • Follow and support AP and accounting SOPs, including invoice approval workflows
  • Ensure compliance with internal controls and audit requirements
  • Identify opportunities to improve efficiency and accuracy in processes
  • Support documentation and continuous improvement of accounting procedures

What You'll Bring

  • Bachelor’s degree in accounting (required)
  • Solid understanding of basic accounting principles (e.g., debits/credits, accruals)
  • Strong attention to detail and ability to work with a high degree of accuracy
  • Proficiency in Microsoft Excel (e.g., sorting, filtering, basic formulas)
  • Ability to manage multiple priorities and meet deadlines in a fast-paced environment
  • Strong organizational and problem-solving skills
  • Clear written and verbal communication skills

You'll Stand Out If You Have

  • 1–3 years of accounting or Accounts Payable experience preferred
  • Experience with NetSuite or similar ERP/accounting systems
  • Experience with high-volume Accounts Payable or invoice processing environments
  • Exposure to month-end close and account reconciliations
  • Experience working in a process-driven or audit-focused environment
  • Familiarity with internal controls and approval workflows
  • Experience supporting internal or external audits
  • Intermediate Excel skills (e.g., pivot tables, lookups)

Compensation At FreshBooks, we approach each individual's compensation with a promise of fair market value and internal equity commensurate with experience and specific skill set.

The total compensation for this role ranges from CA$52,000 - CA$64,000 per year, which may include participation in our incentive programs. Additionally, this position offers an equity grant and immediate enrollment in FreshBooks' comprehensive benefits program, detailed below.
