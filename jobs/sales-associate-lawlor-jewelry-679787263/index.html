Full Description

Join Our Team at Lawlor Jewelry – Sales Associate

Lawlor Jewelry is a cherished family-owned and operated jewelry company with a remarkable legacy spanning 30 years. Since our establishment in 1994, we have been a trusted destination for fine jewelry and have expanded to include custom design, goldsmith production, and serving customers both online and in-store.

What sets us apart is our dedication to crafting quality, custom-designed jewelry that captures the unique beauty of each wearer. Every piece tells a story and becomes a cherished heirloom for generations to come.

At Lawlor Jewelry, we thrive on collaboration, creativity, and a customer-first approach. We foster a fun, supportive, and inclusive team environment where ideas are valued, and innovation flourishes. Working with us is more than a job- it’s an opportunity to be part of meaningful love stories and help clients bring their dreams to life.

About the Role:
We’re looking for passionate, self-motivated individuals to join our team as Sales Associates (Full-Time or Part-Time). This role offers opportunities for growth into specialized areas that fuel your long-term passions.

Your Responsibilities:

  • Assist customers with product selection, ensuring exceptional service and a memorable shopping experience.
  • Provide personalized guidance and recommendations tailored to each customer’s needs.
  • Maintain meticulous attention to detail with a keen eye for quality and aesthetics.
  • Support a positive and inclusive team environment through collaboration and strong communication.
  • Stay organized and manage time effectively in a fast-paced environment.

Ideal Candidate Qualities:

  • A natural ability to connect with customers and create lasting impressions.
  • Strong multitasking skills and composure under pressure.
  • Self-motivated, with the ability to problem-solve independently.
  • Proficiency with computers and technology.
  • Enthusiastic, joyful, and eager to learn and grow in the jewelry industry.

Why Join Us?

  • No jewelry experience required! We provide comprehensive training to set you up for success.
  • Opportunities to develop skills in areas that align with your long-term aspirations.
  • Be part of a family-owned company with a supportive and collaborative culture.

How to Apply:
Please bring your resume in person to Mike at Lawlor Jewelry, or email your resume and a cover letter explaining why you’re the perfect fit to info@lawlorjewelry.com.

Please note: Only candidates selected for an interview will be contacted.

Thank you for considering Lawlor Jewelry as your next career destination. We can’t wait to see how your talents can make a difference!
