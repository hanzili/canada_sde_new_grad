Full Description

At Manulife John Hancock, we believe in investing in the future – starting with you. Our co-op program is your launchpad to success, offering the support, structure, and exciting opportunities you need to thrive and make your mark.

As a Software Engineering Co-op, you’ll gain hands-on experience developing applications and systems that support business and customer solutions. You’ll work with modern development tools and frameworks to build scalable, high-quality software while contributing to the full software development lifecycle. We offer multiple roles within this program, providing support from our diverse, passionate, and talented technology teams. You'll receive guidance from skilled technical leaders who value your teamwork and coordination abilities, and gain exposure to industry-leading, enterprise technologies.

We are currently looking for highly motivated students to join us in our Waterloo, Ontario, CAN office for a 8-month co-op, running from September 1st, 2026 – April 23rd 2027. This is a hybrid position, where you are expected to be in the office Tuesday-Thursday, with the opportunity to work remotely on Mondays and Fridays.

Position Responsibilities:

  • Contribute to a team of engineers focused on building scalable, reliable applications
  • Support application development, testing, and debugging efforts
  • Assist in feature development and system enhancements
  • Collaborate with engineering and business teams to build user-focused solutions
  • Participate in code reviews and maintain code quality standards Support deployment, monitoring, and ongoing application maintenance

Required Qualifications:

  • Currently pursuing an undergraduate degree in Computer Science, Engineering, or a related field
  • Experience with at least one programming language (e.g., Java, Python, Go, JavaScript)
  • Familiarity with version control systems (e.g., Git, Bitbucket) Strong communication skills and willingness to learn in a collaborative environment

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Exposure to software frameworks, libraries, or development tools
  • Understanding of software development lifecycle and best practices Familiarity with APIs, web services, or application architecture

When you join our team:

  • We’ll empower you to learn and grow the career you want.
  • We’ll recognize and support you in a flexible environment where well-being and inclusion are more than just words.
  • As part of our global team, we’ll support you in shaping the future you want to see. As part of our co-op program, you will gain early access to Manulife John Hancock’s premier early talent program, GRO, tailored to graduating students! This program is designed to launch your career with the support, structure, and opportunities you need to thrive.

Application instructions:

  • Submit your resume, cover letter, academic transcript, and work term evaluation (if any) in one PDF file.
  • Note: Applications are reviewed on a rolling basis.

Please note the salary range is expected to be between $52,650 USD to $70,200 CAN.

The actual salary will vary depending on local market conditions, geography and relevant job-related factors such as knowledge, skills, qualifications, experience, and education/training. The annual salary will be pro-rated based on the length of the work-term
