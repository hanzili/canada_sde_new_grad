What is the Opportunity?

Reporting to the Procurement Manager, the Jr. Procurement Specialist is responsible for supporting our Utilities Procurement team in all purchasing related activities. This person will also ensure the timely receipt of goods in SAP, as well as tracking deliveries and return of rental equipment. This position acts as a liaison between offices and field workers and must possess strong knowledge of procurement policies and procedures.

Location: 437 Ofield Road south, Dundas, ON

What You‚Äôll Do Here:

  • Support the team in obtaining product quotes and maintaining current price lists

  • Accountable for driving toward a zero exception procure to pay process
