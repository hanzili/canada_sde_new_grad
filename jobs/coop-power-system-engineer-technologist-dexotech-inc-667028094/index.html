About the Role

Assist power system design, calculations, testing, reporting

About Dexotech Inc

Employee-owned, growing power engineering firm

Full Description

Dexotech Power:

The leader in providing Power Engineering, Testing, Advanced Diagnostics and Root Cause Analysis. Dexotech is an employee owned, diverse, rapidly growing, socially responsible service company, with a global project portfolio.

Summary:

Dexotech Power is looking for a determined individual to grow and deliver WOW experiences to our trusted clients. This individual will assist in performing highly advanced technical work, prepare proposals and reports in accordance with internal guidelines, assist in content development and R&D. The successful candidate will have a strong understanding of Power System design and operation including system design and studies, relaying, control and protection, equipment engineering, High Voltage insulation systems, commissioning, acceptance, interconnect, operations, maintenance and end of life evaluations. Strong communication and technical writing skills is a must for this role.

This position is based out of our Etobicoke office located at 200 Evans Ave Unit 9, Etobicoke, Ontario. This is a field position and upwards of 100% on site work should be expected.

We invite applications from candidates with the following qualifications:

Technical:

· Enrolled in an electrical engineering program.

· Good understanding of all substation equipment.

· Some knowledge of electrical calculation and computer applications.

· Some knowledge of power system protection and power system calculations such as load flow and impedance calculations.

· Valid drivers license with no infractions along with good driving skills and proven experience. Experience with light duty trailer is an asset.

General:

· Strong communication skills.

· Superb research and technical writing abilities.

  • Excellent organization, time management and negotiation skills.
  • Strong leadership capabilities when needed.
  • Autonomous and self management capability.
  • A valid driver's license.
  • Ability to perform field duties at utility sites when needed.

General Responsibilities:

· Assist in project execution, proposal and report preparation.

· Assist in shop organization, vehicle care and general transportation needs.

· Perform electrical calculations, system modeling and simulations under supervision of the Principal Engineer.

· Perform data collection, organization and reporting.

· Assist in field testing and inspection projects.

· Manage light duty projects and clients.

· Prepare technical research documents.

· Assist in laboratory preparation, testing and cleanup.

If site work is required, for the safety of yourself and others:

· The ability to respond quickly to sounds.

· The ability to move safely over uneven terrain.

· The ability to see and respond to dangerous situations.

· The ability to comprehend and follow electrical safety and approach distances.

· The ability to wear personal protective gear correctly most of the day.

We offer you:

· Autonomy, expertise, and purpose.

· A flexible work environment targeted to teach and train and build the next generation of experts.

· Professional and personal development opportunities.

· Mentorship and guidance for exponential growth.

· An energetic, passionate and positive work atmosphere.

[END]
