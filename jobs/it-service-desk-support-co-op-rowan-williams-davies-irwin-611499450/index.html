Full Description

Description

RWDI’s Guelph office is currently accepting applications for the position of an eight-month, IT Service Desk Support Co-opbeginning May of 2026.

Key Responsibilities

Students are provided with the opportunity to:

  • Work with the latest and greatest technology
  • Provide Level 1 phone support. Respond to emails, voice mails, and walk-in customers
  • Act as the single point of contact to resolve end user incidents and service requests
  • Configure, install, maintain, and troubleshoot desktop issues
  • Manage user accounts and permissions
  • Work in a dynamic and busy environment, that provides new experiences every day
  • Develop customer relations skills

Skills, Knowledge and Expertise

Background and qualifications should include:

  • Solid understanding of computer hardware and software applications
  • Understanding of Active Directory and Group Policy Objects
  • Basic knowledge of networking, cabling, LAN technologies
  • Hands-On experience with Windows 10 and Microsoft Office 2016
  • Excellent written and verbal communication skills
  • Customer-service focused
  • Good organizational and coordination skills

Additional Information

Application Requirements:

  • Resume
  • Copy of academic transcripts

Salary: Commensurate with experience.

About RWDI

RWDI is a leading climate and performance engineering firm that has been helping clients tackle complex engineering challenges in the built environment for more than 50 years. With deep technical expertise, we drive success on ambitious building, industry, and infrastructure projects — enhancing performance, resiliency, and efficiency. Our extensive technical services span every project stage to help maximize performance while minimizing environmental impact. With a team of more than 800 engineers, scientists, and sustainability specialists in more than 30 offices across North America, Europe, and the Asia-Pacific region, we’re a truly global company. For more information, visit rwdi.com and connect on LinkedIn.
