About the Role

Support marketing campaigns and brand initiatives

About VIR Properties

Growing property marketing team with mentorship

Full Description

We are looking for an enthusiastic and motivated Entry-Level Marketing Assistant to join our growing marketing team. This is a fantastic opportunity for someone at the beginning of their career who is eager to gain hands-on experience across multiple areas of marketing while contributing to impactful campaigns.

You will support the team in delivering engaging marketing initiatives, helping to drive brand awareness and client growth.

Key Responsibilities

  • Assist in the planning and execution of marketing campaigns
  • Support brand promotions, events, and client activations
  • Work closely with the team to develop creative marketing ideas
  • Engage with customers and represent brands in a professional manner
  • Help track campaign performance and gather feedback
  • Contribute to daily marketing operations and administrative tasks
  • Maintain a strong understanding of client products and services

What We’re Looking For

  • A positive, proactive attitude and willingness to learn
  • Strong communication and interpersonal skills
  • Creativity and an interest in marketing and brand development
  • Good organisational skills and attention to detail

What We Offer

  • Comprehensive training and ongoing mentorship
  • A supportive and energetic team culture
  • Clear career progression opportunities
  • Hands-on experience across multiple marketing channels
  • A fast-paced environment where your ideas are valued

Ideal Candidate This Role Is Perfect For Someone Who Is

  • Recently graduated or looking to start a career in marketing
  • Outgoing, confident, and people-oriented
  • Eager to develop new skills and grow professionally
  • Ambitious and motivated to succeed in a competitive industry

Apply Today If you’re ready to take your first step into the world of marketing and be part of a dynamic and growing team, we’d love to hear from you. Apply today.
