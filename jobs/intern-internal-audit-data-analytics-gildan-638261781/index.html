Intern, Internal Audit (Data Analytics)

at Gildan

📍 Location Montreal, Quebec Hybrid
📋 Details Intern
📅 Posted Mar 06, 2026

About the Role

Build Tableau dashboards and SQL-based reports

About Gildan

Global apparel manufacturer with strong brands

Full Description

Who We Are Gildan is a leading manufacturer of everyday basic apparel, with a strong portfolio of brands, including Gildan®, Hanes®, Comfort Colors®, American Apparel®, ALLPRO™, GOLDTOE®, Peds®, Bali®, Playtex®, Maidenform®, Bonds®, as well as Champion® which is under an exclusive licensing agreement for the printwear channel in the U.S. and Canada. Together with our global team of dedicated employees, Gildan is united in its vision of Making Apparel Better®. Discover the full scale of Gildan and prepare to be surprised at gildancorp.com .

The opportunity The Internal Audit (Data Analytics) Intern will support the development and enhancement of the Internal Audit data analytics program, with a primary focus on building automated, scalable reporting solutions in Tableau. This role will be responsible for designing and developing dashboards and reports that enable continuous monitoring, audit testing, and detection of unusual or potentially suspicious transactions. The intern will work closely with auditors to understand data needs, translate audit requirements into analytical outputs, and help modernize how we use data to drive insights . The role

  • Cleanse, transform data and create visual reports for Internal Audit using Tableau
  • Utilize SQL queries to extract, manipulate, and analyze data from data warehouse
  • Collaborate with cross-functional teams to validate accuracy of developed reports

The Requirements

  • Engineering, Computer Science, Statistics, Mathematics or other related technical fields
  • Strong proficiency in SQL for data extraction, transformation, and analysis
  • Familiarity with Business Intelligence tools such as Tableau
  • This position requires proficiency in a language other than French to support customers, employees or markets located outside the province of Quebec or requiring services in a language other than French, mainly English

What's In It For You

  • Join a leader in apparel manufacturing with strong safety and environmental standards
  • Be part of a diverse workplace with fun workplace activities
  • Benefit from continuous training opportunities
  • Take advantage of our attractive benefits packages
