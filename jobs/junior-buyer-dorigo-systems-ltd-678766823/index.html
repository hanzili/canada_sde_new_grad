Description

Ready to be Part of Something Extraordinary?. Join a great Electronics Manufacturing Service’s (EMS) company in a state-of-the-art purpose-built facility at Glenlyon Business Park in Burnaby. Dorigo Systems Ltd. is focused on achieving positive changes in everything we do for our customers. We are a manufacturer of technology products, from surface mount to full box build, for innovative design and manufacturing companies in North America.

We are seeking a motivated and detail-oriented Junior Buyer to join our procurement team, The Junior Buyer will be responsible for supporting the procurement process, ensuring timely and cost-effective acquisition of materials and components required by the company. This role is an excellent opportunity for career growth and development within the procurement field.

KEY REPONSIBILITIES

Purchasing Functions:

  • Assist in the procurement of materials and components for turnkey contract build requirements as outlined by Operations and Business Development departments
  • Validate component costing as researched by Quotations staff and determine best supply options or source alternatives if necessary (includes comparison of vendor pricing, lead times, stock and minimum order quantities)
  • Work directly with customers to confirm substitutions or part replacements
  • Anticipate long lead time items or potential shortages and propose solutions
  • Negotiate price, delivery, and other special terms with suppliers
  • Place purchase orders with approved suppliers and follow upon delivery schedules.
  • Report status of purchasing cycle to Operations Department, noting special circumstances, instructions and any expected surplus components at completion of the build
  • Actively pursue opportunities to reduce total cost and inventory investment

Supplier Development:

  • Maintain relationships with existing suppliers and assist in identifying new suppliers
  • Work with suppliers to continuously improve quality, cost and delivery times

Materials Planning and Management

  • Coordinate with the materials planner to ensure timely availability of materials
  • Communicate with inventory team and suppliers to ensure receipt of components on-time

Document and Reporting:

  • Maintain accurate and up-to-date procurement records, including purchase orders, invoices and delivery notes.

Qualifications:

  • 1-2 years of experience in a procurement or purchasing role, preferably in the electronics or manufacturing industry
  • Knowledge and experience of using MRP systems
  • Basic understanding of supply chain management and procurement processes
  • Directly related experience with local electronic component distributors, related industry suppliers, electronic component nomenclature, and manufacturer's part numbers
  • Strong negotiating skills
  • Excellent interpersonal, organizational, and verbal/written communication skills
  • Strong organizational and time management abilities
  • Demonstrated tenacious follow-up ability, attention to detail, and problem-solving skills
  • Ability to work independently while also supporting a team environment
  • Strong work ethic, self-directed, determined, customer/team-oriented, energetic, enthusiastic
  • Ability to multi-task, judge priorities, and handle a variety of duties/assignments at once (sometimes under pressure) with a positive attitude
  • Computer proficiency, particularly with Microsoft Office suite of tools (Excel, Word, Outlook, etc.)
  • Relevant post-secondary education with strong math skills
  • Experience within an ISO9001:2015 environment is an asset

What We Offer:

  • Exceptional state-of-the-art facility
  • Competitive Compensation
  • On-site gym, weekly yoga and cross-fit classes
  • 2 weeks paid vacation at time of hire
  • Company paid health benefits after 1 year of employment
  • RRSP matching after 1 year of employment
  • Opportunities for in-house training for industry standards
  • Opportunities for on-the-job training

Job Type: This is an in-person full-time position (40 hours per week) from Monday to Friday at our warehouse in Burnaby, BC

Salary: $40,000 to $45,760 based on experience ($20.00-$22.00 per hour).

If you are interested in exploring this exciting opportunity, please apply with your cover letter and resume.

Final compensation for the successful candidate will be determined on a variety of factors, including but not limited to: the nature of the position, work experience, key skills, education and training, location, and applicable regulations (such as minimum wage).
