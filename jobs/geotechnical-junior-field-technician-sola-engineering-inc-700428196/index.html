About the Role

Perform geotechnical field testing and reporting

About Sola Engineering Inc.

Geotechnical engineering firm in GTA

Full Description

About Us:

Sola Engineering Inc. (Sola) is a trusted provider of geotechnical engineering, materials testing, inspection, and environmental services across the Greater Toronto Area. Operating under a Certificate of Authorization from Professional Engineers Ontario, Sola is a growing firm built on technical excellence and a strong commitment to quality and client service.

Position Overview

We are seeking a Geotechnical Junior Field Technician with 1–2+ years of relevant experience in construction inspection and materials testing.

The ideal candidate will have hands-on experience in:

  • Concrete field testing
  • Compaction testing using a nuclear density gauge

This role involves field-based testing, inspection, and reporting on construction projects across the GTA.

Key Responsibilities

Field Testing & Inspection

  • Perform compaction testing using a nuclear density gauge (ASTM D6938)
  • Conduct fresh concrete testing, including slump, air content, temperature, and cylinder casting (CSA A23.2)
  • Carry out field inspection of footing subgrades and general earthworks in accordance with project specifications
  • Perform asphalt field testing (temperature and compaction), as required
  • Report any non-conformance or site concerns to project managers in a timely manner

General Duties

  • Visually classify soils and document field conditions
  • Maintain accurate and detailed field reports and test records
  • Communicate test results and site observations to project managers
  • Coordinate with contractors and site personnel during testing activities

Health & Safety

  • Comply with Sola’s Health and Safety Policy and all applicable provincial regulations
  • Follow proper procedures for handling and operating nuclear density gauges

Qualifications

Education

  • Diploma or degree in Civil Engineering, Geotechnical Engineering, or related field

Experience

  • 1–2+ years of experience in materials testing and field inspection
  • Practical experience in:
  • Concrete testing (CSA standards)
  • Nuclear density gauge operation

Skills

  • Strong understanding of CSA, ASTM, and MTO testing standards (basic working knowledge)
  • Good written and verbal communication skills
  • Ability to work independently in field environments
  • Organized and detail-oriented

Other Requirements

  • Valid driver’s license and access to a vehicle
  • Physically able to perform field work and lift equipment (~50 lbs)
  • Eligibility to work in Canada

Preferred (Not Mandatory)

  • CCIL certification (or working toward certification)
  • Experience with asphalt or basic soil testing

Why Join Sola?

At Sola Engineering Inc., you will be part of a growing and dynamic team involved in a wide range of geotechnical and materials testing projects across the Greater Toronto Area. We offer a supportive and collaborative work environment where your contributions are valued, and your technical skills are developed through hands-on field experience. Sola is committed to maintaining high standards of quality and safety while providing opportunities for career growth and professional development. Our team-oriented culture, diverse project exposure, and steady workload make Sola an excellent place to build and advance your career in the geotechnical and materials engineering field.
