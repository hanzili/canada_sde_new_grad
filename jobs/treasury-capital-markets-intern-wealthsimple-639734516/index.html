About the Role

Assist treasury operations, analyze financial data

About Wealthsimple

Canadian fintech, growth-focused wealth platform

Full Description

Who you are

  • ✔ Currently enrolled as a student at a Canadian post-secondary institution or technical bootcamp
  • ✔ New graduates - Within 6 months of your graduation date
  • ✔ Available to work full-time hours
  • ✔ Residing in Canada
  • ✔ Able to commute to our Toronto HQ on Wednesdays and Thursdays
  • Currently pursuing a degree in Accounting, Business, Finance or related field,
  • Has basic understanding of accounting principles and concepts,
  • Has ability to work in a fast-paced environment and meet deadlines,
  • Is passionate about technology, specifically fintech, and AI,
  • Combines energy, enthusiasm, and intellectual curiosity, with a demonstrated interest in accounting, finance, and analytics,
  • Excellent attention to details, combined with strong communication and collaboration skills

What the job involves

  • At Wealthsimple, we offer 4 to 8-month internships that are open to co-op and non-co-op students, and recent grads
  • During your internship, you will have the opportunity to contribute to projects that are changing the landscape of financial services for Canadians
  • You will be on a team that supports your growth, provides mentorship, and connects you to the broader Wealthsimple community!
  • Reporting to the Director, Treasury & Capital Markets, the successful candidate will build and shape Wealthsimple’s Treasury initiatives and support the broader product launches. This is a 4-month term that will run from May 11 - August 28
  • Conduct research and data analysis to support the development of strategic proposals, and assist in drafting business proposals and presentation materials for both internal and external partners
  • Support negotiations with banks, fintechs, and other financial partners in order to expand partnerships to support Wealthsimple products
  • Assist the treasury team with ad hoc projects and operational tasks
  • Learn to identify process improvement opportunities (including AI adoptions & AI-led workflows) and develop remediation plans for deficiencies
  • Learn about the fintech industry and how to analyze and interpret financial data and trends
