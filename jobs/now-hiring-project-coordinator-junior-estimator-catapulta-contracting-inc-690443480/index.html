Full Description

Project Coordinator / Junior Estimator

Catapulta Contracting Inc.

Calgary, AB · Full-Time · Hybrid

Salary: $60,000 - $70,000 (based on experience)

About Us

Catapulta Contracting Inc. is a Calgary-based general contracting company specializing in construction project management for residential and commercial developments. We deliver quality builds on time and on budget, supported by a collaborative and experienced team. Our commitment to innovative delivery methods including the Build & Operate (B&O) framework sets us apart in the Calgary market.

About the Role

We’re looking for a motivated, detail-oriented Project Coordinator / Junior Estimator to join our growing team.

This hybrid role supports our Senior Estimator across estimating, pre-construction, and project coordination activities — helping manage key tasks and keep projects running smoothly from award through to closeout and handover.

You’ll be joining a team that values accuracy, teamwork, and initiative. This is a great opportunity to grow your career across both estimating and project management, with mentorship and hands-on experience in the Build & Operate model.

Key Responsibilities

Estimating Responsibilities

  • Assist the Senior Estimator with quantity take-offs, cost analyses, and tender/budget submissions.
  • Review and interpret drawings and specifications to identify scope, materials, and construction methods.
  • Solicit and analyze quotes from suppliers and subcontractors.
  • Maintain cost databases and assist with project budgeting and forecasting.
  • Support pre-construction planning and value engineering within scheduling/estimating platform.
  • Assist with post-bid analysis and budget reconciliation.

Project Coordination Responsibilities

  • Coordinate documentation, schedules, and communication between clients, trades, and consultant teams.
  • Prepare and track RFIs, shop drawings submittals, change orders, and progress reports, using construction cloud-based software.
  • Support project managers with site monitoring, material deliveries, and quality control documentation.
  • Assist with procurement, logistics, and materials throughout the project lifecycle.
  • Track milestones and proactively flag schedule risks.
  • Maintain accurate project records and documentation.
  • Support post-construction activities, including client handover, warranty documentation, and operational follow-up.
  • Contribute to continuous improvement by tracking data, lessons learned, and performance feedback.

Qualifications

  • Diploma or degree in Construction Management, Architectural Technology, Civil/Structural Engineering Technology, or a related field.
  • 1–3 years of experience in estimating, project coordination, or construction management.
  • Skilled in construction takeoff software and cloud-based construction coordination platforms.
  • Solid understanding of construction drawings and specifications.
  • Skilled in MS package (Excel, Project, Word) or similar.
  • Highly organized, analytical, and detail oriented.
  • Strong communication skills to secure trades collaboration.
  • Ability to manage multiple priorities in a fast-paced, team environment.

Assets

  • Valid driver’s license and reliable vehicle.
  • Familiarity with construction methods, materials, and sequencing.

Why Join Us

  • Gain hands-on experience across estimating and project management.
  • Work on diverse residential and commercial projects in Calgary.
  • Learn directly from an experienced Senior Estimator.
  • Be part of a collaborative, close-knit team that values innovation and growth.
  • Opportunity for long-term advancement within a supportive, Calgary-based company.

How to Apply

Submit your resume and a brief cover letter outlining your relevant experience, including your familiarity with construction methodology experience applied to estimating, project coordination, and your career goals to admin@catapulta.ca.

We thank all applicants for their interest — only those selected for an interview will be contacted.

Work Location: In person
