About the Role

Design graphics, motion graphics, branding assets

About SOTI

Mobile and IoT device management leader

Full Description

SOTI is committed to providing its employees with endless possibilities; learning new things, working with the latest technologies and making a difference in the world.

Who We Are: At SOTI, we are committed to delivering best in class mobile and IoT device management solutions. We are looking for out of the box thinkers that appreciate the art of creating great software.

To us, being visionary is more important than doing things the way they’ve always been done.

What’s in it for you? The People - From our humble origins in our founder’s basement, to our industry leading position today, SOTI has worked hard to foster a company culture that we can all believe in. A culture that emphasizes personal growth, continuous innovation and fun.

The Growth - Our environment fosters new ideas, fresh perspectives, and the ability to take them over the goal line. SOTI is a fast-paced environment with a global reach that encourages you to make your mark and be part of something big!

The Technology - You’ll get the chance to work with leading edge technologies and take on complex and interesting projects, as part of highly collaborative and agile teams. You will work alongside SOTI’s partners which include leading tech giants that will keep you on the cusp of emerging technologies.

What You’ll Do

  • Design new and edit existing graphic content, illustrations, infographics and PowerPoint decks
  • Create AI agents using Copilot Studio that achieve efficiencies throughout the organization
  • Manage graphic designs from conception to delivery
  • Generate fresh concepts
  • Ensure our established branding is consistent throughout various projects
  • Work with Instructional Designers to grow SOTI’s capabilities for creating digital content using interactive applications, animation and videos including redesigning/repurposing print media for use in a digital environment
  • Produce motion graphics designs for eLearning courses, titles, graphics, backgrounds and animations
  • Continuously enhance design skills, knowledge of design industry trends, and remain current with technological advancements within the creative industry
  • Drive and contribute to the overall creative acumen and growth of the creative team
  • Other tasks as required

Qualifications

  • Currently pursuing a degree in Graphic Design
  • Background in digital graphic design through school projects, freelance work, or internships
  • Interest or basic understanding of AI tools such as Copilot agents and workflows
  • A portfolio showing your creative work — especially motion graphics and editing
  • Passion for creating bold, eye-catching visuals and campaigns
  • Basic understanding of design principles like layout, typography, and color theory, with a willingness to grow
  • Exposure to tools such as Adobe InDesign, Photoshop, Illustrator, After Effects, and Premiere, or interest in learning them
  • Comfortable working on design, motion graphics, and editing projects
  • Enjoy storytelling through visuals — including animation, video editing, and storyboarding
  • Open to feedback and able to adjust designs based on input from others
  • Feel free to share a portfolio to highlight your design skills

If you want to bring your ideas to life, apply at SOTI today.
