About the Role

Support paid media campaigns and social content

About Gerber Media

Vaughan growth marketing agency with focused team

Full Description

In-Office Role at Thornhill (Dufferin & Clark)

Gerber Media is seeking a motivated, eager-to-learn Digital Marketing Intern  who wants real, hands-on experience in digital marketing and paid media.

This internship is ideal for someone looking to understand how agency campaigns actually run - not just theory, but real client work, real campaigns, and real results.

Strong performers will have the opportunity to grow into a long-term role with the company.

High Level-

• 3-month paid internship

• Opportunity for long-term employment based on performance. Full-time or part-time during semesters

• Hands-on experience working with active client campaigns.

What you'll work on:

• Supporting paid media campaigns across Google, Meta and other paid media platforms.

• Assisting with social media and marketing content

• Helping with campaign setup and reporting

• Supporting client marketing requests and projects

• Learning how performance marketing works inside real client accounts.

Who We're looking for:

  • Highly motivated and eager to learn
  • Organized and reliable
  • Comfortable using digital platforms and social media
  • Comfortable using AI.
  • Strong communication skills
  • Interest in marketing, advertising, or business
  • High school diploma required
  • Currently studying or recently graduated in marketing, business, or a related field (0–4 years).
  • Great communication skills.

Location-

This is an  in-office position  at our Vaughan office, located at Dufferin & Clark (Staples Plaza).

How to apply

Please share your CV along with a short letter.

Please write shortly about

  • The program/degree you learn, the stage you are (years or semesters)
  • If you are available to work full-time or part-time after the internship.
  • If you have any experience with Paid media, Content, Canva, WordPress, or any other digital marketing skills, we would like to hear that!

About the company

Gerber Media is a growth marketing agency based in Vaughan, ON, specializing in helping clients grow their paid media activities while maintaining a strong Return on Ad Spend.

We are a small but mighty team, and we are looking for someone who will be a great fit to the team.

Please do not send us long letters written by AI; we will not read them.

Send a short note using bullet points.
