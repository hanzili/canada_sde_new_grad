About MyDreamFinance Advisory

Full Description

Company Description

MyDreamFinance Advisory is dedicated to helping Canadian small and medium-sized businesses achieve financial efficiency and sustainable growth. We specialize in offering tailored end-to-end financing solutions, ensuring clients secure optimal capital through avenues like government-backed loans and subsidies. With over a decade of experience, we leverage our extensive network and expertise to streamline financing processes, reduce costs, and optimize capital structures. Our fractional CFO consulting services empower businesses with improved systems, enhanced governance, and practical, customer-focused financial solutions. Located in Markham, ON, MyDreamFinance is passionate about supporting businesses in overcoming financial and operational challenges.

Role Description

We are seeking an Associate Accountant Intern for a hybrid internship role based in Markham, ON, with opportunities to work remotely. The successful candidate will support the preparation and management of financial records, assist with budgeting and forecasting, and provide insights into cost control initiatives. Responsibilities include reconciling bank statements, assisting in tax preparation and filings, collaborating on financial reporting, and ensuring accuracy in data entry and account analysis. The intern will also help improve financial processes and support internal audits.

Qualifications

  • Proficiency in financial reporting, budgeting, and account reconciliation
  • Familiarity with accounting principles, bookkeeping, and tax preparation
  • Strong analytical, organizational, and attention-to-detail skills
  • Proficiency in Microsoft Excel and accounting software
  • Effective written and verbal communication skills
  • Ability to work both independently and collaboratively in a hybrid setting
  • Pursuing or possessing higher education in Accounting, Finance, or a related field
  • Prior experience or coursework in corporate finance or operational consulting is a plusa
