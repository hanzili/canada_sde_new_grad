Entry-Level Sales Representative

at LiftBridge Collective

📍 Location North York, ON
📋 Details Full-time · Permanent · $53,000-$56,000 per year
📅 Posted Mar 06, 2026

About the Role

Sell wireless solutions through in-person customer interactions daily

About LiftBridge Collective

In-person wireless sales firm with direct client engagement

Full Description

At LiftBridge Collective, we believe that great things happen when people connect in-person. We are a forward-thinking wireless sales firm specializing in direct in-person sales for major clients in the wireless industry. We are currently hiring individuals to join our team as Entry-Level Sales Representatives. If you’re ready to level up your life through sales, this is the opportunity for you.

As an Entry-Level Sales Representative at LiftBridge Collective, you will play a role in representing our partners in the wireless space. You’ll engage directly with customers in-person, building genuine relationships while offering wireless solutions that enhance their everyday lives. Success here is built through in-person sales conversations, active listening, and a commitment to excellence.

Key Responsibilities of an Entry-Level Sales Representative:

  • Conduct in-person interactions with customers to promote and sell wireless products and services as an Entry-Level Sales Representative
  • Generate new customer accounts through effective sales strategies and personal rapport
  • Deliver an exceptional customer experience while guiding clients through available wireless options
  • Collaborate with your sales team and managers to meet and exceed daily, weekly, and monthly sales goals
  • Continuously improve product knowledge, sales techniques, and interpersonal skills through on-the-job coaching and team training
  • Represent the LiftBridge Collective brand and our wireless clients with professionalism and energy

What Makes You a Great Fit for the Entry-Level Sales Representative Role:

  • A passion for in-person communication and a people-first mindset
  • High energy, goal-driven, and excited about learning sales from the ground up
  • Strong work ethic with a willingness to go the extra mile to hit your sales goals
  • Comfort working in a fast-paced in-person environment with direct customer interaction
  • Previous experience in sales, customer service, or wireless is a plus—but not required
  • Open to feedback, coaching, and personal development

If you’re ready to kickstart a fulfilling sales career with LiftBridge Collective, apply today!

Job Type: Full-time
