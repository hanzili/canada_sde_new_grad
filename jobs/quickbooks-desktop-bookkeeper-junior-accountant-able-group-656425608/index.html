QuickBooks Desktop Bookkeeper / Junior Accountant

at Able Group

About the Role

Perform full-cycle bookkeeping and monthly financial statements accurately

About Able Group

Regional distributor with stable, long-standing operations

Full Description

Job description:

We are a distribution warehouse seeking a Junior Accountant / Bookkeeper with strong full-cycle accounting experience.

Key Responsibilities:

Full-Cycle Bookkeeping

  • Manage full-cycle bookkeeping, including general ledger, journal entries, and month-end close
  • Perform bank, credit card, and account reconciliations
  • Maintain accurate financial records and supporting schedules
  • Investigate and resolve discrepancies

Accounts Receivable (A/R) & Credit Control

  • Manage A/R, invoicing, billing, and payment application
  • Perform collections and monitor aging reports
  • Investigate and dispute chargebacks, deductions, and short payments
  • Reduce overdue accounts and improve cash flow

Accounts Payable (A/P) & Payroll

  • Process accounts payable, vendor invoices, and payments
  • Reconcile vendor statements
  • Process bi-weekly payroll and maintain payroll records
  • Prepare and file GST/HST, payroll remittances, and CRA filings

Inventory & Operational Accounting

  • Reconcile inventory and cost of goods sold (COGS)
  • Audit inbound and outbound shipments
  • Match purchase orders, shipping documents, invoices, and inventory records
  • Investigate discrepancies related to shipping, billing, and inventory

Prepare monthly financial statements (P&L, Balance Sheet)

  • Generate A/R aging and A/P aging reports
  • Monitor cash flow and collections performance
  • Analyze customer accounts, inventory discrepancies, and margins

Qualifications

  • 5+ years of full-cycle accounting / bookkeeping experience (Canada)
  • Degree in Accounting or Finance
  • Advanced QuickBooks Desktop experience at most recent job
  • Strong Microsoft Excel (pivot tables, VLOOKUP, data analysis)
  • GST/HST, payroll, and CRA compliance & remittance experience
  • Experience in distribution, warehouse, logistics, or inventory accounting preferred

What’s in It for You

  • Performance-driven compensation growth tied directly to measurable value creation, cost savings, margin improvement, and operational impact
  • Long-term stability within a diversified organization with over 20 years of operational success
  • Strong and established client base across multiple retail channels, providing consistent business growth
  • Direct reporting to company stakeholders with real visibility and influence
  • High-level decision-making authority in financial strategy, pricing, and operational initiatives

Pay: $22.00-$30.00 per hour

Application question(s):

  • What is your highest level of education and which major did you graduate in?
  • At which job and in which time period did you use Quickbooks Desktop?
  • At which job and in which time period was your most recent experience in monthly governmental remittances and payroll?
  • What monthly reports have you prepared and reviewed?
  • When can you start work if offered a job.

Work Location: In person
