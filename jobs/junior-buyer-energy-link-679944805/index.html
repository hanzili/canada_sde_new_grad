Full Description

JUNIOR BUYER
INTRODUCTION
EnergyLink International is a leading supplier of specialized and balanced solutions for air emissions and noise management, acoustic consulting, gas turbine auxiliary systems and turnkey buildings. Backed by more than 25 years of ingenuity and a strong commitment to customer excellence we have an unwavering focus on developing leading-edge technologies, environmental advancements, superior construction, and quality manufacturing through our team of dedicated professionals who ensure our customers are well positioned for both today and the future.
As a customer-centric organization, EnergyLink International is always searching for top talent who can assist us in delivering above and beyond for our valued clients. If you are looking to make a difference, have the drive to go further and achieve even more, we would like to meet you and invite you and join our growing team.
Position Overview:
The Junior Buyer ensures the services and supply of materials for assigned projects and executes all sourcing and procurement activities. The Junior Buyer ensures a high level of services is delivered to internal clients, mitigates operating risk while achieving optimum value while following EnergyLink purchasing policies and procedures. Previous experience within a procurement environment focused on Construction, pre-fab steel construction would be a definite asset. Candidate must have at least 2-4 years of relevant procurement experience.
Reporting Structure:
This position will report to the Senior Buyer based in Calgary
Key Responsibilities:
Gathers short and long-term demand information for goods and services and works collaboratively with Project Managers to understand needs and develop solutions;

  • Reviews and analyzes requests for goods and services including detailed specifications and confers with the originator to eliminate ambiguities and to gain maximum procurement efficiency in terms of price, delivery and suitability of materials, services and equipment;
  • Proactively generates cost savings through supplier negotiation of existing contracts and competitive bids;
  • Administers sourcing process including requirements and specification development, bid evaluation, vendor selection, negotiates contracts and purchase orders;
  • Maintains vendor relationships, monitors vendor performance, and collects vendor performance metrics. Measures supplier performance against key performance indicators and identifies opportunities for improvement;
