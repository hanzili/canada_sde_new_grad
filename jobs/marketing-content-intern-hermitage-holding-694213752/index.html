Full Description

Position: Marketing & Content Intern (Graphic Design & Content)

Location: Richmond Hill, ON (In-office)

Term: 4 months

Compensation: $18–$20 / hour

About Hermitage

Hermitage is a Richmond Hill-based real estate development and investment firm. We acquire, develop, and manage residential and mixed-use projects across the GTA, with a growing modular construction division. Our team blends 30+ years of architectural and investment experience with a hands-on, entrepreneurial culture.

The Role

We’re looking for a creative, self-directed “intern” to work alongside our Director of Public Relations to build and elevate Hermitage’s brand. You will be the design and storytelling engine behind how we present ourselves to investors, partners, municipalities, and the public.

This is not a coffee-runs internship. You will own real deliverables and see your work go out to a sophisticated audience.

What You’ll Do

•      Design marketing collateral: brochures, one-pagers, investor presentations, project pitch decks, and signage

•      Produce visual content for social media (LinkedIn, Instagram) and our website — posts, carousels, short-form video, project reels

•      Develop and refine Hermitage’s visual identity (templates, style guide, photography direction)

•      Write and edit copy for posts, captions, project descriptions, and press materials

•      Coordinate on-site photography and videography of our projects (construction sites, completed buildings, team)

•      Support PR initiatives: media kits, event materials, award submissions

•      Help build a content calendar and maintain consistent output across channels

What We’re Looking For

•      Currently enrolled in or recently graduated from a program in Graphic Design, Marketing, Communications, Visual Arts, or a related field

•      Strong portfolio demonstrating layout, typography, and brand work

•      Proficiency in Adobe Creative Suite (InDesign, Photoshop, Illustrator); Figma is a plus

•      Solid writing in English — clear, professional, and on-brand

•      Comfortable shooting and editing simple video content (CapCut, Premiere, or similar)

•      Interest in architecture, real estate, or the built environment is a strong asset

•      Self-starter who can take a brief and run with it

•      Legally eligible to work in Canada

What You’ll Gain

•      Direct mentorship from a senior architect and PR lead with 30+ years of industry experience

•      A real portfolio of published work across print, digital, and social

•      Exposure to the full lifecycle of real estate development — from acquisition to delivery

•      A small-team environment where your contributions are visible and valued

How to Apply

Send the following to info@hermitageholding.ca:

·       Resume (required)

·       Portfolio (required — link or PDF)

·       Cover letter (optional, but encouraged)

We review applications on a rolling basis. Only shortlisted candidates will be contacted.
