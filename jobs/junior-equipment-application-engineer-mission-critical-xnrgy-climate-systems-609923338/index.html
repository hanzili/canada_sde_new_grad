***Junior Equipment Application Engineer ‚Äì Mission Critical Equipment***

**XNRGY Climate Systems** is a leading North American company in sustainable design and advanced manufacturing of custom air treatment systems. It specializes in next-generation chiller equipment for mission-critical applications.

XNRGY is looking for an **entry-level** Mission-Critical Sales Application Engineer/Specialist to join its team at our Brampton, Ontario, location.

***What You Will Do***

The person in this position is responsible for design, preparation, and/or coordination of bid documents and proposals (performance data, drawings, component selections, construction details) and creating the submittals for Mission-Critical Systems, including but not limited to large-tonnage Centrifugal Chillers, CRAH units, CDUs and AHUs.

Under general supervision, this position supports the sales team by assisting with pre- and post-sales activities related to mission-critical equipment opportunities. While the role mainly focuses on executing our equipment business, the balance of pre- and post-sales activities will vary depending on the project type, client, assigned Account Executives, and customer expectations.

Pre-sales activities encompass system configuration validation, selection of Chiller, CRAH, and CDU equipment and controls for the basis of design, along with bidding, application, and pricing. This role will receive guidance, coaching, and support from the Application Engineering Director and assigned account executives regarding pre-sale requirements and expectations. Team members will regularly interact with and receive coaching from company ownership, who hold years of multi-faceted experience in the field.

Post-sales activities include executing profitable, accurate equipment projects on time, within scope, and aligned with customer expectations. Develop detailed engineering info and submittals to communicate design, configuration, operation, and installation requirements to customers, consultants, Xnrgy service, and subcontractors. Collaborate with the sales and commissioning team to balance customer satisfaction and project financial results in pre-sale and post-sale duties.

- Facilitates detailed project estimates for sales teams to finalize and present to clients. Verifies assumptions during proposal and development phases. Attends job progress meetings as required. - Prepares customer proposals and quote letters for assigned projects. Includes project takeoffs, specification review, special quote generation and RFQ and RFI issuance. - Supports sales efforts to become the basis of design through timely selections, specifications, sequences, drawings, and document reviews as required. - Assist with project Profit and Loss (P&L) tracking and customer satisfaction for all assigned projects. Including the execution of equipment orders, startup, commissioning, and final payment. - Interprets engineering drawings and specifications to develop and provide engineered equipment submittals in compliance with contract drawings, site coordination, and site installation requirements. - Assist in executing engineering analysis to validate overall chiller design and component selection, to provide energy modeling.
