Full Description

Junior Project Manager

Orillia Drywall

Orillia Drywall is seeking a motivated and detail-oriented Junior Project Manager to support the planning and coordination of both residential and commercial drywall projects. Working closely with senior management and site staff, this role plays an important part in ensuring projects are completed on schedule, within budget, and to the highest quality standards our customers have come to expect from us.

This is an excellent opportunity for someone looking to grow within a respected, family-owned company who is well established in the community .

Key Responsibilities

Assist in creating estimates, organizing projects, both commercial and residential from start to completion

Pricing out materials and labor costs per project

Track project progress, assist with budget monitoring and prepare status reports

Review drawings, specifications, and project documents to determine scope of work

Prepare detailed and accurate material and labour take-offs

Help manage project documentation, contracts, and any change orders

Qualifications

Post-secondary education in Construction Management, Business, Project Management, or related field

1–3 years of experience in construction, trades coordination, or project support

Strong organizational and time-management skills

Ability to read and interpret blueprints and construction drawings

Excellent mathematical and analytical skills

If interested, please send a resume to Heidi at info@orilliadrywall.ca

** please note we do not accept walk in resumes

Pay: $25.00-$35.00 per hour

Expected hours: 35 – 40 per week

Benefits:

  • Company events
  • Dental care
  • Extended health care

Work Location: Hybrid remote in Orillia, ON
