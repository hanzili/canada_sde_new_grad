Capital Markets Intern

JLL SUMMER INTERNSHIP

Experience the perfect blend of professional growth and exciting opportunities with JLL's 2026 Summer Internship Program!

As a JLL Intern, you will tackle meaningful projects that impact our Commercial Real Estate business by collaborating with industry professionals and providing fresh perspectives to innovative teams. Our structured program balances professional development with exciting real-world experience.

JLL is seeking a Capital Markets Intern based in Toronto, ON to support deal execution and market analysis in commercial real estate investment sales. This role is perfect for someone eager to gain hands-on experience in capital markets through client outreach, market research, and transaction support.

AS A CAPITAL MARKETS INTERN AT JLL YOU WILL:
Gather market information and provide support for the brokerage team

Prepare for pitches and create other marketing tools

Manage the database of property owners and reaching out to new and potential clients

Provide market and research support to the Capital Markets group

Participate in weekly team meetings, internal meetings, and mentor/mentee meetings

WHAT YOU BRING:
Currently enrolled in post-secondary education in Business Administration, Commerce, Economics, or other related area of study with a curiosity for how businesses operate

Demonstrates proficiency with software packages including but not limited to MS Windows and MS Office: MS Word, Excel, PowerPoint, Teams & Adobe Standard

Proficiency in Argus Software is preferred but not required

Demonstrates proficiency with software packages including but not limited to MS Windows and MS Office: MS Word, Excel, PowerPoint, Teams & Adobe Standard

Strong analytical skills combined with attention to detail and excellent organizational abilities

Clear and effective communicator who can express ideas confidently both in writing and conversation with a professional yet personable demeanor

Natural self-motivated problem-solver with the initiative to find answers and a drive to learn quickly in a dynamic environment

Collaborative team player who brings enthusiasm, fresh perspectives, and a professional attitude to every project

Experience in the commercial real estate sector is an asset

Fluency in written and spoken French and English

WHAT’S IN IT FOR YOU:
Hands-on experience in a global commercial real estate firm, building practical skills in contract analysis and performance optimization that complement your academic knowledge

Mentorship from industry professionals who are invested in your growth and development

Exposure to different business units and the opportunity to build a valuable professional network across JLL

Insight into potential career paths within commercial real estate sourcing and facilities management, and the opportunity to find your professional passion!

Program Details: Full time, 16-week program from May 2026 to August 2026

Estimated Compensation: $20-24/h

  • Please submit your resume & cover letter with your application
  • Oral and/or written interactions with tenants or external partners based outside Quebec (for example, service providers, law firms, peers, bankers, brokerage firms, credit analysis firms, credit agencies, non-French-speaking clients, etc.)
