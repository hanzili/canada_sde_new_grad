The Opportunity Your Impact Starts Here. As a Junior Lab Technician, you will help deliver accurate, high‑quality laboratory work that supports environmental, materials, and infrastructure projects across the region. You will be part of a team that values precision, safety, and innovation while helping shape healthier, more resilient communities.

Your Impact In this role, you will:

  • Conduct routine laboratory testing following established methods and quality protocols.
  • Prepare, label, and process samples for chemical, physical, or geotechnical analysis.
  • Operate standard laboratory equipment and assist with basic maintenance and calibration.
  • Follow strict health, safety, and quality procedures to ensure reliable test results.
  • Record observations and results accurately in logs, templates, and digital systems.
  • Support senior technicians and technologists by organizing samples, maintaining work areas, and contributing to continuous lab efficiency improvements.

The Skills That Set You Apart

  • A college diploma or certificate in environmental technology, chemistry, materials science, or a related field (or ongoing completion).
  • Experience in a laboratory setting—academic, co‑op, or professional—where attention to detail and method compliance were essential.
  • Familiarity with basic lab equipment (balances, ovens, sieves, pH meters, etc.).
  • This role requires the ability to safely lift and carry items weighing up to 50 lbs as part of routine laboratory duties
  • A commitment to safety, quality, and teamwork, with a passion for hands‑on technical work.
