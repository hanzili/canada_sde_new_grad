Full Description

Junior Accountant, Coop/InternJob Description North Horizon Holdings Inc. is a Canadian institutional investor with a global investment footprint. We are value-oriented investors in public markets, private equity, and real estate.

About the role : The junior accountant Co-op will have opportunities to expose to full accounting cycle and assist the senior financial analyst on recording business transactions and analyzing business performance.

Responsibilities

  • Assist with operation of finance and accounting functions, including journal entries, trial balances, and general ledgers
  • Engage in monthly and quarterly closing processes
  • Assist with tracking banking records and reconciliations
  • Assist with reviewing third-party’s books
  • Track rental income and cash receipts for both residential and commercial properties
  • Organize and archive supporting documents
  • Assist with ad-hoc projects as required

What You Need To Succeed Must-haves

  • 3rd or 4th year student majoring in Accounting or any other related fields
  • Strong communication abilities
  • Advanced MS Office and excellent Excel skills
  • Ability to adapt to the rapidly changing business environment and work efficiently
  • Available to begin by May 11, 2026, with an expected commitment of approximately 8 months

  • Have a direct line of sight to the high-level workings of all deals (including but not limited legal, accounting and others).
