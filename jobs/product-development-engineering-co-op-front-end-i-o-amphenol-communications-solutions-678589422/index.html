Amphenol Communications Solutions (ACS), a division of Amphenol Corporation, is a world leader in interconnect solutions for Communications, Mobile, RF, Optics, and Commercial electronics markets. Amphenol Corporation is one of the world’s largest designers and manufacturers of electrical, electronic and fiber optic connectors and interconnect systems, antennas, sensors and sensor-based products and coaxial and high-speed specialty cable. ACS has an expansive global presence in research and development, manufacturing, and sales. We design and manufacture a wide range of innovative connectors as well as cable assemblies for diverse applications including server, storage, data center, mobile, RF, networking, industrial, business equipment, and automotive.

Product Development Engineering Co-op – Front-End I/O Connectors for AI/ML Location: Markham Department: High-Speed IO Core Development Position Overview Amphenol is seeking a motivated and detail-oriented Engineering Co-op student to support the design and development of front-end I/O connectors used in next-generation Artificial Intelligence (AI) and Machine Learning (ML) platforms.

This role provides hands-on exposure to high-speed connectivity solutions, including electrical, mechanical, and thermal design considerations. The Co-op will work closely with experienced engineers and contribute to real product development programs supporting advanced compute architectures.

Key Responsibilities Design & Engineering Support

  • Assist in the design and development of high-performance front-end I/O connectors for AI/ML applications.
  • Support CAD modeling and drawing creation using tools such as SolidWorks or Creo.
  • Contribute to design activities focused on signal integrity, mechanical reliability, and thermal performance.
  • Collaborate with cross-functional teams (electrical, mechanical, manufacturing) to support product integration.

Testing & Validation

  • Support the execution of electrical, mechanical, and environmental testing of connector designs.
  • Help collect, organize, and analyze test data to identify performance trends and issues.
  • Assist in preparing test reports and documenting validation results.
  • Participate in lab activities and prototype evaluations.

Project & Documentation Support

  • Assist engineers in tracking project timelines, deliverables, and design iterations.
  • Maintain organized engineering documentation, including drawings, test results, and reports.
  • Support continuous improvement efforts in development workflows and documentation practices.

Customer & Application Exposure

  • Gain exposure to customer requirements and application use cases in AI/ML hardware systems.
  • Support preparation of materials for design reviews, prototypes, and technical discussions.
  • Assist in translating customer needs into engineering inputs under guidance.

Qualifications

  • Currently pursuing a Bachelor’s degree in Electrical Engineering, Mechanical Engineering, Mechatronics or a related field.
  • Completed at least the 2nd or 3rd year of study (preferred).
  • Basic understanding of electrical fundamentals and mechanical design principles.
  • Familiarity with CAD tools (SolidWorks, Creo, or similar) is an asset.
  • Strong analytical, organizational, and communication skills.

Preferred Skills

  • Exposure to high-speed signaling concepts or electronics hardware.
  • Familiarity with simulation tools (e.g., ANSYS, HFSS).
  • Interest in data center hardware, AI/ML infrastructure, or high-performance computing.
  • Experience with hands-on lab work, prototyping, or testing.

What You’ll Gain

  • Direct experience in cutting-edge connector development for AI/ML systems.
  • Exposure to real-world engineering workflows from concept to validation.
  • Mentorship from experienced engineers in high-speed interconnect design.
  • Opportunity to contribute to next-generation computing platforms.

The base pay range for this position is $20-$30 per hour.
