Full Description

Job Requisition ID # 25WD92295

Position Overview Do you have an interest in understanding the corporate finance function and its role in a dynamic, public company environment? Do you have strong analytical skills, an ability to communicate complex ideas to diverse populations, and an interest in driving process improvement? Join us and gain exposure to investment analysis, financial modeling, key metrics support, and global process management.

Responsibilities

  • Help develop and manage the preparation of critical financial analyses and executive level presentations
  • Lead analysis of investments and develop easily repeatable methodology to evaluate and compare different investments
  • Help management understand the context or 'why' behind our financial results
  • Present findings to Finance Leadership

Minimum Qualifications

  • Students pursuing an MBA or Masters in Business or related field
  • Student must be actively enrolled in a degree granting academic institution or accredited boot camp at the time of internship

Preferred Qualifications

  • Strong PowerBI, Excel, and PowerPoint skills
  • Previous corporate finance experience
  • Adept at analyzing and understanding the trends and context within multiple views, including internal/management and external views

About The Canada Intern Program The 2026 Canada program runs for 16 weeks (May 4 – August 21). All internships are paid. As an intern, you will contribute to meaningful projects, be mentored by industry leaders, and participate in tech talks and other activities designed to support your personal and professional development. Our internships align with Autodesk’s Flexible Workplace approach, which is designed to meet the needs of our business while providing flexibility in support of office, remote and hybrid work preferences.
