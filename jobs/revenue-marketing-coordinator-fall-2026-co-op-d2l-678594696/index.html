Full Description

D2L is a cloud company that is modernizing education and building the Future of Work. The old models of teaching and learning are in the midst of the largest transformation in history, and D2L is at the heart of that fundamental shift.

New models of teaching and learning enable a personalized, student-centric experience – and deliver improved retention, engagement, satisfaction, and results for learners of all ages – in schools, campuses, and companies.

D2L is disrupting the way the world learns, by providing the next generation learning environment and solutions to engage and inspire learners. And most importantly, by giving customers a platform that is easy, flexible, and smart. No other company provides a solution as robust and innovative as D2L.

D2L has had a singular mission for 25 years and is dedicated to that same mission in the years ahead: to transform the way the world learns – and by doing so, we will help improve human potential globally.

Every application we receive is personally reviewed by a member of our Talent Acquisition team - yes, a real person looks at your resume! While we use AI tools internally to streamline tasks like meeting notes, summaries, and administrative work, these tools never rank resumes, make hiring decisions, or influence candidate evaluations.

We may continue to review applications on a rolling basis, but recommend applying by January 20th at 9:00am ET to ensure you are included in initial application reviews. Job Summary As a Marketing Coordinator Co-op on the D2L Corporate Marketing team, you’ll be working with a small and tight team of cross-functional marketers who are laser focused on growing demand for our corporate segment in integrated marketing. You’ll have the opportunity to work cross-functionally with many team members and gain exposure to various marketing disciplines with hands-on campaign and asset execution responsibilities.

Whether you are an experienced Co-op or a first year Co-op, we are looking for all around superstars to help create and improve our suite of advanced communication and collaboration tools. You will be constantly learning and looking for ways to innovate. You thrive in a culture where teams speak freely and collaborate to solve interesting problems.

HOW WILL I MAKE AN IMPACT? Email/Web Copywriting and Collateral – Support executing our messaging strategy on multiple channels

  • Copywriting for campaign emails, landing pages, digital ads, sponsorships, enablement toolkits, and other internal and external client communications and digital assets
  • Coordinate the proper proofread and legal reviews of communication/copywriting
  • Coordinate the creation, updating, and deployment of campaign asset updates, graphics, and supporting digital and print assets with appropriate groups (e.g. web, automation, social, creative, enablement, dg, community, etc.)

Campaign communication and execution– Aid cross-functional campaign comms and campaign alignment

  • Maintain campaign schedule and tactic communications schedule
  • Coordinate campaign updates/changes and related communications with sales, SEs, enablement, etc. (internal and external)
  • Help to provide awareness and align campaign communication plans with cross segment teams (automation, web, social, international, demand gen, pmk, etc.)

Webinar Coordination and Support

  • Support and assist team with webinar coordination, administrative, and logistics duties
  • This would include things like managing pre webinar coordination, maintaining reg/lists/systems, webinar data/reporting, coordinating tasks and action follow-up, assisting with planning/set-up logistics, review and testing Zoom webinar platform and related tech, day-of event support, etc.

Vendor Research and Sourcing

  • Research local vendors that can be leveraged for direct mail to key accounts/territories
  • Research sponsorship and vendors that where can amplify our customer voice
  • Work with vendors on executing deliverables such as coordinating direct mail options, sponsorship opportunities, swag, etc.
  • Monitor success metrics

Reporting and Metrics

  • Leverage SFDC, GA, PowerBI to pull campaign metrics
  • Scrub lists, pull email addresses, create custom segmentation
  • Suggest improvements and optimization to existing tactics to improve overall performance

What You'll Bring To The Role Work Experience Recommendations:

  • Advanced communication, collaboration, and project management skills
  • Excellent communication writing and/or copywriting skills
  • Exceptional organizational skills including multi-tasking and time management.
  • High attention to detail
  • Ability to learn quickly, think fast under pressure, and work independently in a fast-paced environment.
  • Comfortable with learning new technology systems quickly
  • Creative mindset and stamina to try new things and figure out how to continuously improve
  • Experience with planning or coordinating multiple people and projects to achieve a common goal and/or deliverable
  • Familiarity and interest in AI tools (ChatGBT, CoPilot, etc)

Education Recommendations Currently enrolled in Business Administration or related with the Co-op option
