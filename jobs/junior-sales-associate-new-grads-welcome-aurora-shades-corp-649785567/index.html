Full Description

About Aurora Shades

Our story began with a simple belief: window coverings are more than just functional, they're a form of expression. From our humble beginnings in Toronto to serving homes and businesses across the GTA, Aurora Shades is committed to transforming ordinary windows into inspiring focal points.

The Opportunity

Are you a recent graduate eager to kickstart a career in sales and home decor? We are looking for a driven and results-oriented Junior Sales Associate to join our fast-growing team.

In this role, your primary focus will be driving sales by meeting with clients and creating beautiful window covering solutions. This is an incredible opportunity to hone your sales skills, learn about the industry, and grow with a dynamic brand.

What You Will Do (Key Responsibilities)

  • Drive Sales & Consultations: Conduct in-home consultations, measure windows, discuss design options, and recommend the best products for our clients.
  • Proposals & Closing: Prepare accurate quotes, proposals, and design suggestions tailored to client needs to successfully close sales.
  • Client Education: Educate clients on product benefits, fabric selections, and motorized smart-home options.
  • Project Coordination: Work closely with our production and installation teams to ensure smooth delivery and top-notch customer satisfaction.
  • Team & Outreach Support: Assist management with daily operational tasks and provide support on local B2B outreach (such as connecting with real estate agents or new homeowners) as needed.

Who You Are (Requirements)

  • Language: Fluency in both English and Mandarin is REQUIRED (Cantonese is a strong asset).
  • Experience: Recent graduates are strongly encouraged to apply! Sales experience or a background in home décor/renovation is a bonus, but a proactive attitude and willingness to learn are most important.
  • Driving: Valid Ontario driver’s license (G). A company vehicle is provided for client visits, no need to use your own car.
  • Skills: Strong interpersonal skills, self-motivated, and basic understanding of design principles (light, color, space). Familiarity with basic installation concepts is a plus (full training provided).

Compensation & Growth (What We Offer)

  • Base Pay: Competitive hourly base (aligned with Ontario minimum wage).
  • Tiered Commission: 2.5% – 4.5% of monthly sales volume.
  • Order Bonuses: $100 per order ≥ $5,000; $300 per order ≥ $10,000.
  • Discount Incentives: Extra rewards for low-discount deals.
  • Earning Potential: Avg $3,800–$4,500/month; top performers $6,500–$7,000+.
  • Growth Path: Opportunities to grow into Senior Consultant or Sales Manager roles.

Apply now and help our clients design beautiful spaces with Aurora Shades!
