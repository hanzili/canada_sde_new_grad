About the Role

About Tbooth wireless

Major wireless retailer, strong training and growth

Full Description

At tbooth wireless, we bring the latest from Apple, Samsung, Google, and more to customers nationwide. Backed by Glentel Inc.- a billion-dollar company owned by Bell and Rogers- we're the go-to destination for choice and connectivity, and we’re proud to be recognized as a Best Place to Work by HR Director Magazine. Join us in a rewarding, people-first environment where opportunity and growth are always within reach!

What We Offer

  • Annual Earnings: Typically ranging from $45,000 to $70,000+, including hourly rate plus uncapped commissions (The lower end reflects new team members during their learning period, while experienced associates have earned significantly more).
  • New: Maternity Leave Top‑Up Program: We’re proud to introduce a new benefit designed to support employees during one of life’s most important milestones. Our Maternity Leave Top‑Up Program provides additional financial support so you can focus on what matters most: your growing family.
  • Well-Being: Medical & dental benefits, flexible scheduling, sick days, EFAP, and 3 weeks' vacation.
  • Extras: RRSP matching, discounts on your wireless/residential services, accessories, and more!
  • Support: Award-winning training, tools, resources, and coaching needed to help you succeed.
  • Career Opportunities: Industry-leading development programs- we promote our managers from within!
  • Inclusivity: A community that values and encourages diversity, inclusion, and respect, supported by initiatives from our ERGs.

Who You Are

  • Customer-focused: You have a passion for finding the right solutions for customers.
  • Connector: You're skilled at building relationships and have effective communication skills.
  • Tech-Savvy: You're interested in the wireless industry and comfortable using technology and social media.
  • Goal-Driven: You're motivated to learn, grow, and achieve targets in a performance-driven environment.
  • Flexible: You have a good work ethic and can adapt in a fast-paced, ever-changing industry.
  • Team-Player: You collaborate, share knowledge, support your peers, and enjoy friendly competition.
  • Dependable & Experienced: You're available for full-time work, including evenings and weekends, with 1-2 years of proven success in customer-facing roles.

What You Will Do

  • Deliver Solutions: Provide personalized support that helps customers find the right wireless and residential products and services. You have a passion for finding the right solutions for customers.
  • Be the Expert: Complete training and stay up-to-date on products, networks, promotions, and industry trends so customers can count on you for the latest knowledge.
  • Create Experience: Turn every interaction into an exceptional experience that earns customer loyalty, repeat business, and referrals.
  • Drive Results: Achieve and exceed sales targets while staying adaptable to business needs and contributing to the success of your store and district.
  • Live Our Values: Demonstrate trust, respect, ownership, passion, humility, and integrity in everything you do.
