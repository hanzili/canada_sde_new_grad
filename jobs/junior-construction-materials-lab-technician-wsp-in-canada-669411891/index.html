The Opportunity Build the Future with Us As a Junior Construction Materials Lab Technician , you will play an important role in delivering accurate, high‑quality laboratory testing that supports environmental, materials, and infrastructure projects across the region. You will be part of a collaborative laboratory team that values precision, safety, and innovation while helping shape healthier, more resilient communities.

Your Impact In this role, you will:

  • Perform routine laboratory testing on construction materials such as soils, aggregates, and concrete in accordance with recognized standards and specifications.
  • Prepare, label, log, and manage samples to ensure accurate tracking and full traceability throughout the testing process.
  • Operate standard construction materials laboratory equipment, including balances, ovens, sieves, and compression testing machines.
  • Accurately record observations and test results using laboratory logs, templates, and digital systems.
  • Follow established health, safety, and quality procedures to maintain a safe, organized, and efficient laboratory environment.
  • Support senior technicians and technologists by assisting with test setup, equipment cleaning, and general laboratory organization.

The Skills That Set You Apart

  • Previous experience in laboratory testing, particularly involving soils, aggregates, and concrete.
  • Comfort working in a laboratory environment with repetitive testing procedures and detailed methodologies.
  • Strong attention to detail and well‑developed organizational skills.
  • Ability to safely lift and carry items weighing up to 50 lbs as part of routine laboratory duties.
  • A strong commitment to safety, quality, and teamwork.
  • An interest in building and developing technical skills within construction materials testing.
