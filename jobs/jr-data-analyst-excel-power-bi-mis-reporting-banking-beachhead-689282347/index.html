About the Role

About BeachHead

Full Description

Are you a data-driven professional seeking a dynamic opportunity? Apply Now!

Working with one of our top financial clients, this role calls for a Junior Data Analyst – Excel, Power BI & MIS Reporting (Banking). This position involves supporting business management teams in data analysis, reporting, and process improvement within a collaborative environment.

Pay rate range (CAD): $32.61/hr – $39.13/hr

Responsibilities

  • Support B2B portfolio by delivering accurate data analysis and reporting solutions.
  • Validate and analyze large datasets to ensure data integrity and support business decision-making.
  • Develop and maintain reports using Excel, Power BI, and other MS Office tools.
  • Collaborate with cross-functional teams to gather requirements and document findings.
  • Assist in process improvements and automation of MIS reports.
  • Document processes and ensure consistent reporting standards.
  • Engage with stakeholders to provide insights and support ongoing business needs.

Desired Skill-Set

  • 2-3 years of experience in a similar data analysis or reporting role.
  • Strong analytical, numerical, and problem-solving skills.
  • Proven expertise in data validation and analysis for reporting purposes.
  • Excellent proficiency in Microsoft Office 365, especially Excel; Power BI experience is essential.
  • Effective collaboration and communication skills for cross-functional teamwork.
  • Ability to work with large datasets and focus on data accuracy.
  • Eagerness to learn new systems, tools, and business processes.
  • Strong attention to detail and data integrity.
  • Business acumen with the ability to connect technical insights to business context.

Nice to Have

  • Experience with MIS reporting and automation.
  • Prior experience in the banking or financial industry.
