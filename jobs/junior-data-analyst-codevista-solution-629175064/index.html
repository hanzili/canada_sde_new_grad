Job Description

Dive into the world of data at Codevista as a Junior Data Analyst in a fully remote position! You'll support our team by collecting, cleaning, and analyzing data to drive insights for business decisions. Enjoy the flexibility of working from anywhere in Canada, with tools like SQL, Excel, and Python. Ideal for entry-level candidates passionate about data and eager to learn in a collaborative remote environment. Join us to kickstart your career with flexible hours and growth opportunities.

As a Junior Data Analyst at Codevista, you'll play a vital role in our remote-first team, helping to transform raw data into actionable insights that fuel our innovative projects. This entry-level position is perfect for recent graduates or early-career professionals looking to build a foundation in data analysis while enjoying the freedom of remote work.

Your primary responsibilities will include gathering and organizing data from various sources, such as databases and spreadsheets, to ensure accuracy and completeness. You'll perform basic data cleaning and preprocessing tasks using tools like Microsoft Excel and Google Sheets, identifying patterns and discrepancies to maintain high data quality. Under the guidance of senior analysts, you'll assist in creating simple visualizations and reports using Tableau or Power BI, presenting findings in clear, digestible formats for non-technical stakeholders.

In this role, you'll collaborate on entry-level projects involving descriptive analytics, such as summarizing sales trends or customer behaviors, to support business strategies. You'll learn to write basic SQL queries to extract data from relational databases, and may explore introductory Python scripting for automation tasks. Expect to participate in regular virtual meetings via Zoom or Microsoft Teams, where you'll share updates and receive feedback in a supportive atmosphere.

Codevista embraces a remote work model that prioritizes work-life balance and productivity. Our team uses asynchronous communication tools like Slack for daily interactions, allowing you to manage your schedule flexibly—whether you're based in Toronto, Vancouver, or anywhere else in Canada. We provide access to cloud-based platforms like Google Workspace and Jira for task tracking, ensuring seamless collaboration across time zones. As an entry-level hire, you'll receive mentorship through one-on-one video calls and access to online learning resources to develop your skills quickly.

This position offers exposure to real-world data challenges in a dynamic tech environment, without the pressure of advanced expertise. You'll contribute to cross-functional teams, gaining experience in data ethics, privacy compliance (like GDPR and Canadian PIPEDA), and basic statistical methods. Over time, you'll grow into more complex analyses, building a portfolio that showcases your remote collaboration skills and analytical mindset.

At Codevista, we value diversity and inclusion in our remote workforce, fostering a culture where every voice matters. This role is an excellent stepping stone to mid-level positions, with opportunities for professional development through webinars, certifications in tools like SQL or Excel, and performance-based advancement. If you're self-motivated, detail-oriented, and excited about the impact of data, join our team to analyze, learn, and thrive from the comfort of your home office.

Requirements

• Bachelor's degree in a related field such as Computer Science, Statistics, or Business Analytics (or equivalent experience) • Basic proficiency in Microsoft Excel or Google Sheets for data manipulation • Foundational knowledge of SQL for querying databases; willingness to learn advanced queries • Familiarity with data visualization tools like Tableau or Power BI is a plus but not required • Strong self-motivation and time management skills for independent remote work • Excellent communication abilities for virtual collaboration using tools like Slack and Zoom • Eagerness to learn and adapt in a fast-paced, entry-level environment • Ability to work effectively across Canadian time zones
