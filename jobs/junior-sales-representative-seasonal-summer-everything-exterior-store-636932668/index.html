Junior Sales Representative (Seasonal/Summer)

at Everything Exterior Store

📍 Location Edmonton, AB T6E 5E9
📋 Details Seasonal · Summer · $16-18/hr · Seasonal
📅 Posted Mar 05, 2026

About the Role

Assist customers, prepare estimates, process orders

About Everything Exterior Store

Local building supply dealer; learn industry basics

Full Description

Job Overview
The Role: Are you looking to break into the building materials or construction industry? We are seeking a motivated Junior Sales Coordinator to support our busy showroom. This is a "boots-on-the-ground" role where you will handle the essential work that keeps our projects moving. You’ll learn the business from the ground up—from assisting customers with product selection to processing complex contractor orders.

The Company:

Everything Exterior is a locally owned and operated building supply dealer. Cladding, Decking, Railing, Interior Feature Walls are the main product categories with a growing stocking catalog.

The Details:

  • Schedule: Monday to Friday 8am-5pm (weekday hour flexibility). Saturdays (10:00 AM – 2:00 PM) are required.
  • Duration: This is a seasonal position, with the opportunity to switch to part-time or return the following season
  • The "Work": You will assist the sales team with customer consultations, estimating, quoting, take-offs, price checking, organizing samples, and helping walk-in customers with basic needs.

What You’ll Do:

  • Support the Pros: Type up estimates and double-check material quantities for senior reps.
  • Showroom Success: Keep the showroom organized, update pricing labels, and refresh product displays.
  • Customer Service: Be the first point of contact on the floor; help DIYers find the right tools and accessories.

What We’re Looking For:

  • Hustle
  • Math Skills
  • Reliability
  • A "Service" Mindset

Role is available to anyone who qualifies, including summer students.

Pay: $16.00-$18.00 per hour

Expected hours: 20.0 – 40.0 per week

Benefits:

  • Flexible schedule

Work Location: In person
