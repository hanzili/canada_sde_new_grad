Because normal gets you nowhere.

We are DECIEM, The Abnormal Beauty Company.

We are growing rapidly and we’re looking for someone who shares this vision and wants to grow with us!

WHAT WE'RE LOOKING FOR We are looking for a Summer Intern to join our Finance - Accounting & Controllership team supporting our Compliance & Control Activities . This role will be based out of our head office in Liberty Village .

Work Term Duration : Summer 2026 - May 18th, 2026 to August 28th, 2026

Location: DECIEM Head Office -15 Fraser Ave, Toronto, ON M6K 1Y7

Hourly rate: $22.00

WHAT YOU'D BE DOING IN THIS ROLE

  • Assist in documenting and maintaining key financial reporting processes and controls.
  • Support walkthroughs and control testing for SOX 404 key controls and track and update control documentation (e.g., narratives, control matrices (RCM)).
  • Help identify and control deficiencies and remediation action items.
  • Support documentation and follow‑up of disclosure‑related controls and help coordinate and track evidence for control activities tied to quarterly disclosures.
  • Assist in preparing summaries or checklists for 302 reporting cycles.
  • Participate in internal and external audit requests by gathering and organizing evidence.
  • Perform basic testing procedures under guidance (e.g., sample selections, testing of controls).
  • Maintain an audit tracking log and follow up on outstanding items.
  • Develop maps and document business processes along with related controls.
  • Update control documentation templates and maintain version control.

SKILLS AND QUALIFICATIONS NEEDED TO GET THE JOB DONE:

  • Currently enrolled in an undergraduate degree in Accounting, Finance, Business, Commerce, or a related discipline, returning to school after the work term.
  • Strong interest in SOX 404, SOX 302, internal controls, and compliance processes, with relevant coursework in accounting, auditing, or business controls.
  • Basic familiarity with control documentation, risk assessment, or audit support concepts (coursework, case studies, or prior co-op experience).
  • Proficiency with Microsoft Excel. Word, Power Point, PowerBI, and comfort working with structured documentation and tracking lists.
  • Excellent attention to detail, clear written and verbal communication skills, and the ability to follow guidance while taking initiative on assigned tasks
