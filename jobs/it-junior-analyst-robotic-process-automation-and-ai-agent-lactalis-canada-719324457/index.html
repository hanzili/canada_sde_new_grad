Full Description

Job Title: IT Junior Analyst – Robotic Process Automation and AI Agent Developer

Location: Toronto, ON

Duration: 4-month

Contract term: September 8, 2026 - December 18, 2026

About Us:

Welcome to Lactalis Canada! We’re proud to be Canada’s #1 branded dairy company and to be consistently recognized as one of the country’s top employers by Forbes. Our purpose is to enrich and nurture the lives of Canadians by offering the best of dairy from Canada’s favourite dairy brands like Cracker Barrel, Black Diamond, Balderson, Astro and Lactantia. In fact, our products can be found in over 90% of Canadian refrigerators in any given time. As part of Lactalis Group, the world’s leading dairy company, we remain focused on innovation and quality and are seeking like-minded individuals to join us as we continue our growth journey.

Lactalis Student Program:

At Lactalis, we are committed to cultivating talent and providing enriching opportunities that ignite passion and foster growth in our future leaders. Our student program is designed to nurture your potential by providing you with enriching work experience in a dynamic and innovative environment.

Lactalis Canada’s student program encourages curiosity, creativity, professional development, and personal growth. Not only will you develop your expertise through hands-on experiences, but you will also join a thriving network of Lactalis Students across Canada. All students will have the chance to participate in social events, group trainings and workshops, manufacturing plant tours, and an end-of semester project!

We are invested in your long-term success and aim to equip you with skills and experiences that will fuel your future!

About the opportunity:

As a member of the Information Technology (I.T.) team, you’ll work alongside a cross-functional group across the organization. This internship offers a unique opportunity to gain real-world experience and understand how a business operates from the inside. You’ll be introduced to our digital transformation journey and learn how RPA and AI powered agents are developed and implemented across the company. If you're a student who is innovative, enthusiastic, and organized, this internship is a great way to apply your skills, grow professionally, and make a meaningful impact. By the end of the internship, you’ll have developed the ability to identify and remove roadblocks, and you’ll demonstrate strong creative problem-solving skills—valuable assets for any future career in tech or business.

The attributes we are looking for:

  • Analyze, understand, and document the business processes in detail where required and identify automation opportunities
  • Identify and communicate the technical infrastructure requirements
  • Design RPA process solutions in accordance with standard RPA design principles and conventions
  • Design and develop Copilot agents (M365 Copilot Build / Copilot Studio)
  • Configure knowledge sources (SharePoint, files, knowledge bases)
  • Create prompts, topics, and logic for agent interactions
  • Build automation flows using UIPATH and Power Automate
  • Test the automated processes with real business scenarios and refine responses
  • Create and maintain solution documentation
  • Problem-solve issues that arise in day-to-day running of RPA processes
  • Communicate with RPA vendors on software-related issues, suggested improvements, and participating with other users in the RPA community
  • Provide technical support to end-users, troubleshoot issues, and identify solutions
  • Work within project planning constraints and provide timely updates
  • Passionate about I.T Systems and have a keen interest in contributing to the RPA strategy
  • Able to work both independently and collaboratively

What you will bring:

  • Knowledge of software development and coding in various languages
  • Experience in Computer Science is an asset
  • Understanding of workflow-based logic
  • Understanding of Windows, mainframe, or web-based platforms
  • Knowledge of RPA technologies (e.g., UIPath) is a strong asset
  • Knowledge of Microsoft Copilot and Copilot Studio is a strong asset
  • Able to quickly learn necessary technical tools
  • Good problem-solving and debugging skills
  • Excellent planning and organization skills and ability to take direction while working under tight deadlines
  • Excellent interpersonal skills, an aptitude for problem solving, and the ability to work in a team environment
  • Currently enrolled in a bachelor’s program, majoring in Information Technology, Computer Science or Computer Engineering.

This job description is intended to summarize the overall level of responsibility of the job and is not an exhaustive list of all the duties performed.
