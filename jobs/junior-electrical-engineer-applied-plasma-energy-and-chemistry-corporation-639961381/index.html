Full Description

Company Description

Applied Plasma Energy and Chemistry Corporation (APEC) specializes in plasma arc torch technology with over six decades of expertise in plasma heating, chemistry, and environmental applications. Initially developed for space capsule heat shield testing, metal refinement, and melting, APEC has broadened its innovation to include applications in the sustainable green economy. The company is pioneering advancements with its Non-Equilibrium Plasma (NEP) torch systems, helping to drive cutting-edge technology development. APEC is committed to creating impactful solutions for a more sustainable future.

Job Description:

This is a hybrid early-career engineering role based in Ottawa, Ontario , reporting to the Director of Plasma Development and supported by external electrical consultants. The successful candidate will contribute to the development, integration, and testing of electrical and control systems in a plasma research and development environment.

Key responsibilities include:

  • Work on developing high-voltage equipment
  • Selection and procurement of DC power supplies and protective electronics
  • Instrumentation selection, sourcing, integration, and operational testing for high-voltage plasma systems
  • Develop and test an in-house DC power supply system
  • Assist in plasma torch operation and test diagnostics
  • Conduct electronics failure analysis and interface with suppliers to support technical solutions
  • Support integration of instrumentation and control systems
  • Travel to client facilities (occasional)
  • Additional tasks as assigned

Position Details:

  • Hybrid work model (remote and on-site as required)
  • Includes approximately 10% travel to client facilities
  • Based in the National Capital Region (Ottawa)
  • Candidate must have a valid driver’s license and access to personal transportation
  • Must hold a valid passport and be able to travel internationally (subject to project requirements)

Required Qualifications:

  • 0–3 years of relevant professional experience
  • Degree in Electrical Engineering or a similar discipline from an accredited post-secondary Canadian institution
  • Legally entitled to work in Canada and meet Ontario employment regulations
  • Fluent in English; additional fluency in other languages is considered an asset

Compensation:

In accordance with Ontario employment transparency requirements, the compensation range for this position is $60,000-75,000 per year.

APEC is an equal opportunity employer and encourages applications from candidates of all backgrounds. Accommodations are available upon request for candidates taking part in all aspects of the selection process.
