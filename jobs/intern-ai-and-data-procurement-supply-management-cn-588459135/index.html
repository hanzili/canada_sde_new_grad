Intern, AI and Data - Procurement & Supply Management Performance This is a full-time summer internship lasting from May 11, 2026, until August 28, 2026.

Job Summary The Intern, AI and Data - Procurement & Supply Management Performance is responsible for driving improvement in Procurement & Supply Management (P&SM) by evaluating, planning and implementing improvements in processes, data analysis and practices, as well as facilitating and coaching improvement teams within work units. The incumbent works with the P&SM groups to will support the identification, prioritization, and delivery of Artificial Intelligence (AI) use cases aimed at enhancing procurement performance and operational efficiency. This role involves collaborating with cross-functional stakeholders, analyzing data, and contributing to the development of innovative solutions using AI technologies. In addition, the role involves a substantial communication and stakeholder management component including working closely with Senior Management.

Major Responsibilities

  • Assist the Performance team in defining the data strategy to enable the different use case and the overall AI roadmap. Perform assessments utilizing analytical skills to identify process improvement opportunities, automation and AI applications.
  • Work cross-functionally to design new processes and present business requirements based on process knowledge for technology implementations
  • Introduce and sustain change across multiple initiatives in both the business and technology areas using a variety of change management and communication techniques
  • Prepare supporting documents and materials for discussions with key stakeholders
