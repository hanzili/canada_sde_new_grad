About the Role

Film short social videos for brands

About Temisin LLC

Top marketing agency with notable brand clients

Full Description

We're a Top Marketing agency with clients such as Temu, Tiktok and other Brand clients. We’re looking for confident, camera-friendly people (18–40, any gender) to film short videos on your phone. No acting experience needed — just be comfortable talking to the camera. What you’ll do:

  • Film a sets of tasks roughly 1 hour of work per set (1x Action, 1x Reading)
  • Tasks consisting of multiple 5–45 sec video recordings of: reviews, tutorials, casual talking
  • Selfie-style, filmed at home (kitchen, living room, etc.)
  • Memorize 2–3 simple sentences
  • Do light actions or talk naturally on camera

Requirements:

  • English speaker
  • HD phone camera
  • Comfortable on camera
  • Phone + tripod/stand

Details:

  • Content used for social media, marketing & PR
  • Paid 1-hour trial period
  • Consistent Weekly work after trial period
  • Pay: CAD $20 for the entire project of all the readings and actions (1 hour equivalent of work)
