About mynexttrip.ai

AI-powered travel platform startup

Full Description

**Salary: 80k

Company Description**

At My Next Trip, we are revolutionizing travel planning with an AI-powered platform, mynexttrip.ai. Our innovative travel companion creates personalized itineraries to simplify and enhance how people explore the world. From intelligent flight and hotel bookings to curated recommendations for attractions, tickets, and local dining, we deliver a seamless, all-in-one experience for travelers worldwide.

Role Description

This is a full-time remote role for a Junior Software Engineer. The Junior Software Engineer will contribute to designing, developing, and maintaining software solutions, focusing on back-end web development. Day-to-day responsibilities include collaborating with cross-functional teams, implementing object-oriented programming principles, troubleshooting technical challenges, and ensuring optimal performance of the platform.

Qualifications

  • Strong foundation in Computer Science concepts and principles
  • Hands-on experience with Back-End Web Development and Software Development
  • Proficiency in Programming and Object-Oriented Programming (OOP)
  • Ability to write clean, maintainable, and scalable code
  • Familiarity with modern development environments, tools, and workflows
  • Strong problem-solving and analytical thinking skills
  • Effective communication skills and ability to work in a collaborative, remote team environment
  • Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science, Software Engineering, or a related field
