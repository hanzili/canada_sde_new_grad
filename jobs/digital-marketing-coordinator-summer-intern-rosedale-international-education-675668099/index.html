About the Role

Create content, manage social, coordinate events

About Rosedale International Education

Education-focused organization; strong marketing exposure

Full Description

We’re looking for a Marketing Coordinator to support management across a variety of digital platforms, including our annual virtual conference GEIS 2026. You’ll collaborate with the Senior Digital Marketing Specialist and external vendors to develop brand-aligned content, coordinate speakers, event logistics, social media channels, create visual/audio content, and engage with the Rosedale Global School Network community.

Key Responsibilities:

  • Work with the Senior Digital Marketing Specialist to develop content that enhances user experience, maintains and builds brand consistency, and aligns with GTM activities.
  • Support the management of social media channels, including content creation, scheduling, posting, community engagement, and maintaining a social media calendar.
  • Create and edit visual and/or audio content, such as webinar recordings, video clips, photos, and manage the posting process.
  • Engage with external vendors/speakers and internal stakeholders to coordinate event logistics, speaker sessions etc.
  • Contribute to writing engaging, SEO-optimized blog posts, website content, and marketing copy.
  • Support the creation of performance-driven content for paid social campaigns, landing pages, and other marketing assets.
  • Assist with speakers and sponsorship outreach efforts.
  • Support PR activities by crafting press releases, distributing content to media outlets, and assisting with digital event coordination and logistics.

Qualifications and Requirements

  • Currently enrolled in an undergraduate program in communications, marketing, or a related field at an accredited university or college.
  • Proficient in Microsoft Office Suite (Word, Excel, PowerPoint).
  • Exceptional writing and editing skills with strong attention to detail.
  • Experience using social media platforms such as Meta, YouTube, LinkedIn, and TikTok, along with familiarity with scheduling tools.
  • Comfortable coordinating, recording, and editing visual content, with a willingness to appear on camera when needed.
  • Strong time management and organizational skills, with the ability to manage multiple projects simultaneously.
  • Self-motivated, proactive, and able to work independently as well as collaboratively in a team setting.
  • Driven to exceed KPIs and contribute to organizational success.
  • Interest in international education, transformative learning, and K12/higher education.

Assets

  • Graphic design skills with proficiency in Adobe Creative Suite (Photoshop, Illustrator, InDesign, After Effects), Canva, and similar tools.
  • Experience developing and editing written, video, or audio content.
  • Familiarity with AI tools like ChatGPT, Claude, Perplexity etc. for use in content creation.

Length of the position: 8 weeks  (starting June 1)+ possibility for 3-month extension

Work Hours: 5 days/week, 9am-5pm

Work Location: Hybrid - 7030 Woodbine Avenue, Suite 800, Markham ON L3R 5J2

Compensation $20 per hour
