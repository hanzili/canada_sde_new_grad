About the Role

Oversee field E&I installation and site construction

About Ausenco

Mining-focused EPCM firm with strong training program

Full Description

Ausenco is a fast-growing company with big ideas. We redefine what’s possible in some of the world’s most complex projects and toughest environments. Delivering innovative, value-add consulting, project delivery, asset operations and maintenance solutions is what we do. From 22 offices in 9 countries, with projects in over 80 locations worldwide, we create sustainable outcomes for our people, clients and communities.

Our team in Vancouver is growing. We are currently recruiting for a Junior Field E&I Engineer, to join our team in the Vancouver or Calgary. This is a field position, at a Northern BC mine-site on a 2-week on, 2-week off rotation with 2 site travel days. The successful candidate will join an EPCM site team, overseeing the green-field construction of a mining processing facility.

About The Role

  • Promote compliance with quality controls during the execution of construction works.
  • Review daily reports, contribute to weekly and monthly reports on the physical progress of electrical and instrumentation discipline activities.
  • Review contractor’s progress, quantity validations, and track performance against contractual obligations
  • Support contracts team in validating change notices, LEMs, and scope variances
  • Support Senior Engineers in reviewing contractor’s schedules, resourcing against project baseline and implement mitigation measures as needed
  • Coordinate contractor RFIs with Engineering team and vendors for clarification where required.
  • Ensure IFC drawings, details, and vendor information are packaged for contractors in advance of work commencing.

About You

  • Electrical, Electronics Engineer – EIT with EGBC or equivalent
  • 1 - 3 years of experience in Mineral Process Plants or Oil/Gas Process Plant Construction will be considered an asset
  • Pragmatic approach to Engineering with a big-picture mindset
  • Agile approach to solutions and ability to de-bottleneck work for contractors
  • A team-first attitude, with a focus on achieving the best outcomes for the project
  • Available for regular site work

We’re committed to a fair and human hiring process. All applications are reviewed by a member of our team and are not automatically screened or rejected by AI.
