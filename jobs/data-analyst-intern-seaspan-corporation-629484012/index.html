About Seaspan Corporation

Global maritime asset owner and operator

Full Description

Seaspan provides many of the world's major shipping lines with alternatives to vessel ownership by offering long-term leases on large, modern containerships and pure car, truck carriers (PCTCs) combined with industry leading ship management serves. Seaspan's fleet has evolved over time to meet the varying needs of our customer base. We own and operate vessels in a wide range of sizes, from 2,500 TEU to 24,000 TEU vessels. As a wholly owned subsidiary of Atlas Corp, Seaspan delivers on the company's core strategy as a leading asset management and core infrastructure company.

Seaspan is the world's leading independent maritime asset owner and operator, dedicated to providing an unmatched level of quality and service to our customers. Seaspan provides the world’s major shipping companies with long-term leases on over 190 large, modern and technologically advanced vessels, combined with industry-leading ship management services. As owner and operator of the vessels, we provide skilled officers and crews dedicated to the safe, reliable, and economical operation of our fleet.

We are seeking a motivated and detail-oriented Data Analyst Intern to join our team. In this role, you will work closely with cross-functional teams to analyze business processes, gather and document requirements, and contribute to quality assurance (QA) efforts and gap analysis to ensure successful implementation of business solutions.

Responsibilities:

  • Collaborate with business and technical stakeholders to gather, analyze, and document business and data requirements.
  • Develop process maps, functional specifications, user stories, data definitions, and other documentation as required.
  • Support the creation and maintenance of reports and dashboards (e.g., Power BI, Plotly Dash), including data validation and refresh monitoring.
  • Write and validate queries to extract, analyze, and test data in support of development and reporting activities.
  • Assist with data validation, transformation checks, and preparation of reporting datasets.
  • Perform basic root cause analysis on data quality issues or dashboard defects and support corrective actions.
  • Maintain and update the corporate data dictionary and business glossary to ensure consistency and governance alignment.
  • Support cross-functional meetings and workshops by documenting key decisions, action items, and follow-ups.

Requirements:

  • Currently pursuing a degree in Data Analytics, Computer Science, Information Systems, Business Analytics, Engineering, or a related field.
  • Strong analytical and problem-solving skills with high attention to detail.
  • Foundational knowledge of data querying and transformation using tools such as SQL, PySpark, or similar data processing technologies.
  • Basic understanding of data manipulation using Python or comparable programming languages is an asset.
  • Experience with Excel for data analysis and validation.
  • Exposure to data visualization or reporting tools (e.g., Power BI, Plotly Dash) is a plus.
  • Basic understanding of data validation, data quality concepts, and testing principles (QA/UAT exposure is an asset).
  • Ability to document processes, requirements, and analytical findings clearly and accurately.
  • Strong written and verbal communication skills.
  • Ability to work both independently and collaboratively within cross-functional teams.
  • Eagerness to learn and adapt in a fast-paced, data-driven environment.

Compensation: This is a 4-month temporary full-time role. Seaspan’s compensation is based on our pay-for-performance philosophy that rewards team members who deliver on and demonstrate our high-performance culture. The compensation range for this position is $21-$23/hr. The exact base pay offered will be commensurate with the incumbent’s experience, job-related skills and knowledge, and internal pay equity.
