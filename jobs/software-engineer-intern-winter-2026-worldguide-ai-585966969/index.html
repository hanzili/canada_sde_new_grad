Full Description

About WorldGuide AI

WorldGuide AI is developing a platform designed to simplify personalized travel planning. Our goal is to create a unified tool that allows travelers to manage itineraries, bookings, and collaboration efficiently. We are a small, focused team building the foundational technology for our web and mobile applications.

The Opportunity

We are seeking a highly motivated Software Developer Intern to join our core engineering team for a part-time or full-time internship. This is a hands-on role where you will work directly alongside experienced engineers, contributing to the development of our core product features. You will be instrumental in implementing key integrations with external travel services and building relevant user-facing components.

What You’ll Do

As a key member of our small team, you will focus on core software engineering tasks:

  • Feature Development:  I mplement new travel service integrations and relevant user-facing components.
  • Engineering Lifecycle:  Participate actively in the full software development lifecycle—from writing initial code and reviewing specifications to testing and deployment.
  • Debugging & Testing:  Collaborate on identifying, debugging, and resolving software defects to ensure application stability and reliability.
  • API Integration:  Assist in consuming and integrating external APIs for necessary travel-related data services.
  • Code Quality:  Contribute to maintaining a clean, well-tested, and documented codebase.

What We’re Looking For (Required Qualifications)

  • Academic:  Currently pursuing a post-graduate program in Computer Science, Software Engineering, or a related field
  • Fundamentals:  A strong grasp of computer science principles, including  Data Structures and Algorithms
  • Core Technology:  Proven professional or project experience with  JavaScript/TypeScript .
  • Web Knowledge:  Understanding of how to build or interact with  REST APIs .
  • Communication:  Strong verbal and written communication skills and a dedicated, collaborative approach to learning and problem-solving.

Why Join WorldGuide AI?

  • Foundational Impact:  You will work on core, visible components of a product that is currently being built.
  • Direct Mentorship:  Benefit from direct guidance and a steep learning curve working closely with experienced developers.
  • Agile Environment:  Experience the focused, fast-paced environment of a startup in its earliest stages.
  • Flexibility:  This is a  remote  position open to candidates located anywhere in Canada.
