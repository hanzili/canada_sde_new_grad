About the Role

Analyze data, automate workflows, support projects

About Insight Global

Global staffing leader with diverse client portfolio

Full Description

6 Months contract- Potential to extension or conversion

Hybrid 2x per week in downtown Montreal

Salary: $35-$37/hr

Job Description:

  • Collaborate with cross-functional teams to identify operational inefficiencies and propose data-driven solutions
  • Support ongoing and new projects aimed at enhancing existing processes and static data structures
  • Assist in the development and automation of workflows using tools like Power Automate
  • Analyze and visualize data using Excel, Power BI, and SQL to support business decisions
  • Contribute to documentation, reporting, and presentation of findings to stakeholders
  • Participate in project planning, execution, and delivery phases
  • Work with technical and non-technical stakeholders to translate business needs into actionable insights
  • Support ad hoc analysis and reporting requests from business units

Must Have Requirements:

  • 1/2 years exp ( the more the marrier)
  • Bachelor’s degree in Finance, Economics, Accounting, or a related field
  • Strong proficiency in tools such as Excel, Power Automate, Power BI, SQL, HTML, JavaScript
  • Demonstrated ability to work on project-based initiatives
  • Excellent communication and problem-solving skills
  • Tech-savvy mindset with a passion for process improvement

Power automate and UI

Nice to Have:

  • Java Script experience
  • Capital Markets and Banking exp
  • exposure to machine learning concepts or tools
  • Prior experience in banking operations or financial services
  • Internship/work experience in a business analyst/data centric role
  • Bilingual English/French
