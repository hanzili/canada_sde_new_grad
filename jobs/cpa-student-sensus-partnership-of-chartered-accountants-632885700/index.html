Full Description

Join Sensus CPA Ltd. – Shape the Future of Accounting with Us!

At Sensus CPA Ltd., we are more than just accountants; we are a close-knit team of professionals dedicated to delivering top-tier accounting services across our offices in Manitoba and Saskatchewan.

We strive to build lasting relationships with our clients and offer personalized solutions that drive their success. Our mission is to deliver the highest quality assurance, accounting, and tax engagements for our clients, positioning our firm as a leader in the field. At our core we believe every team member plays a vital role in achieving this mission, contributing their unique skills and perspectives. We pride ourselves on our collaborative culture, continuous professional development, and a supportive work environment.

We’re currently looking to welcome a CPA Student to our Russell office!

What you’ll be doing:

  • Perform compilation and review engagements for various business entities, including financial statement preparation.
  • Conduct audits for small and medium-sized not-for-profit organizations.
  • Participate in municipal audits, gather required information, and assist the audit supervisor/manager.
  • Prepare financial statements and ensure accuracy and compliance with accounting standards.
  • Manage general ledgers, journal entries, and trial balances.
  • Prepare and file GST/PST returns and T2 corporate tax returns for clients.
  • Communicate with clients to gather necessary financial information and provide support.

Who we’re looking for:

  • Someone who is currently enrolled or planning to enroll in a CPA program.
  • Minimum 1 year experience in an accounting, public practice is preferred.
  • Brings strong analytical and problem-solving skills.
  • A detail-oriented person with strong organizational skills.
  • A team player attitude with a desire to support others.
  • Excellent written and verbal communication skills.
  • The ability to handle multiple tasks and meet deadlines.
  • A person with integrity and strong ethics that can be trusted with confidential financial information.

Why join us:

At Sensus, we believe in taking care of our team members. Here are some of the benefits you can expect when you join us:

  • RRSP Matching Plan: Secure your future with our competitive RRSP matching program.
  • Overtime Pay: Enjoy overtime compensation at 1.5 times your regular wage for hours over 8 hours a day and 40 hours a week.
  • Comprehensive Insurance: Access to health, dental, and life insurance plans to keep you and your family protected.
  • Paid Education/Training: We support your professional growth with paid education and training opportunities for both non-CPA students and CPAs.
  • CPA Support: For CPA students, we cover your CPA course fees and annual student fees. For CPAs, we pay all professional development costs and annual CPA membership dues.
  • Extended Long Weekends: Enjoy extended (4-day) long weekends from July to December, on weekends that include a statutory holiday, to enhance your work life balance.
  • Holiday Closure: Our office closes for approximately two weeks at the end of the year, allowing you to enjoy the festive season.

We appreciate the interest of all applicants; however, only candidates selected for interviews will be contacted. If you are ready to embark on a rewarding career with Sensus CPA Ltd., please send your resume and cover letter to srichards@sensuscpa.ca
