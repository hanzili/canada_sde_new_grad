Full Description

Junior Bookkeeper

Eagleeye Bookkeeping Inc.
Downtown Kelowna, BC
Full-Time | In-Office Position

Build Your Career with a Growing Bookkeeping Firm

Eagleeye Bookkeeping Inc. is a growing bookkeeping firm located in downtown Kelowna. We provide accurate, complete, and audit-ready bookkeeping services to small and medium-sized businesses across a variety of industries.

We are seeking a motivated Junior Bookkeeper who is eager to learn, embraces accountability, enjoys solving problems, and takes pride in producing accurate work. This role is ideal for someone who has a solid accounting foundation and is looking to grow their technical skills under the mentorship of experienced bookkeeping professionals.

At Eagleeye Bookkeeping, we value:

  • Accuracy
  • Integrity
  • Accountability
  • Continuous Learning
  • Professional Growth
  • Exceptional Client Service

This is a full-time, in-office position located in Kelowna, BC. Remote work is not available.

Position Summary

The Junior Bookkeeper supports the accounting team by performing day-to-day bookkeeping and data management for multiple clients. This role focuses on accurate data entry, account reconciliations, client support, and assisting with monthly and year-end bookkeeping processes.

The successful candidate will work under the supervision of a Senior Bookkeeper or Accounting Manager while developing the technical and professional skills necessary to manage increasingly complex client files.

Key Responsibilities

Bookkeeping & Data Management

  • Perform data entry with a high degree of accuracy and attention to detail
  • Assist with accounts payable and accounts receivable processing
  • Record bills, expenses, deposits, and journal entries
  • Perform bank, credit card, and general ledger reconciliations
  • Assist with month-end bookkeeping procedures
  • Assist with GST and PST filings
  • Assist with payroll processing and remittances
  • Maintain organized and complete digital client files
  • Review transactions for accuracy and identify discrepancies

Client Reporting

  • Assist with preparing monthly financial statements and reports
  • Support the review and correction of bookkeeping discrepancies
  • Maintain complete and accurate supporting documentation
  • Communicate professionally with clients regarding document requests and bookkeeping inquiries

Year-End Support

  • Assist with year-end reviews and reconciliations
  • Prepare supporting schedules and working papers
  • Assist with gathering documentation required for year-end packages
  • Ensure client records are complete and audit-ready

Team Collaboration & Development

  • Participate actively in training and professional development
  • Accept coaching and feedback positively
  • Demonstrate initiative in finding solutions and improving processes
  • Support team members during busy periods and deadline-driven projects
  • Contribute positively to Eagleeye's collaborative culture

Qualifications

Required

  • Bookkeeping Certificate, Accounting Diploma, or higher
  • Minimum 1-2 years of bookkeeping or accounting experience (internships and practicum experience considered)
  • Experience using QuickBooks Online, Sage, Xero, or similar accounting software
  • Intermediate computer skills
  • Working knowledge of Microsoft Excel
  • Understanding of basic bookkeeping principles
  • Understanding of account reconciliations
  • Strong written and verbal communication skills
  • Strong organizational and time management skills
  • Ability to manage multiple priorities and deadlines
  • Ability to work full-time in-office in Kelowna, BC

Preferred

  • Experience in a bookkeeping firm or accounting practice
  • Experience managing multiple client files
  • Experience with payroll processing
  • Experience preparing GST and PST returns
  • Experience working in cloud-based accounting environments

Technology & AI Competencies

Technology is a key part of our business. Successful candidates should be comfortable learning and using new software and digital tools.

Candidates should demonstrate the ability to:

  • Research technical bookkeeping and software questions independently
  • Troubleshoot issues using online resources, training materials, and approved AI tools
  • Verify information before implementing solutions
  • Learn new technology quickly
  • Follow strict confidentiality and cybersecurity protocols
  • Never disclose client-identifiable or confidential information to unauthorized third-party systems

The ability to responsibly leverage AI tools while exercising professional judgment is considered a valuable skill.

Performance Expectations

Successful candidates will:

  • Maintain a high level of accuracy in bookkeeping work
  • Meet established deadlines consistently
  • Demonstrate accountability and ownership of their work
  • Continuously improve technical and professional skills
  • Communicate professionally with clients and coworkers
  • Follow established company procedures and documentation standards
  • Contribute positively to team collaboration and workplace culture

Conditions of Employment

The successful candidate will be required to:

  • Successfully complete a Criminal Record Check
  • Obtain and maintain QuickBooks Online ProAdvisor Certification
  • Obtain and maintain QuickBooks Online Advanced ProAdvisor Certification
  • Maintain eligibility to work in Canada
  • Work on-site at our Kelowna office

Candidates who do not currently hold ProAdvisor Certifications may still be considered; however, successful completion of these certifications will be a condition of hire and continued employment.

Application Requirements

To be considered, applicants must submit:

  • Resume
  • Cover Letter
  • References (minimum of 2)
  • Responses to the Pre-Screening Questions provided

In the first sentence of your cover letter, please include the words "Accuracy Matters."

Applicants selected for interviews will also be required to:

  • Complete a bookkeeping skills assessment
  • Complete an accounting knowledge assessment
  • Provide a minimum of two professional references

Please bring references to your interview.

Why Join Eagleeye Bookkeeping?

  • Work alongside experienced bookkeeping professionals
  • Receive mentorship and ongoing training
  • Build your technical accounting skills
  • Gain exposure to a variety of industries and client files
  • Grow your career within an expanding local firm
  • Join a team that values accuracy, integrity, accountability, and continuous improvement

Only applicants selected for an interview will be contacted.
applicants will not be considered if they:

  • Do not reside in Kelowna or the surrounding area.
  • Are unable to work full-time in-office.
  • Do not possess a Basic Accounting Certificate, Accounting Diploma, or higher.
  • Are unwilling to complete a Criminal Record Check.
  • Are unwilling to obtain QBO ProAdvisor and QBO Advanced ProAdvisor certifications as a condition of employment.
