About the Role

Develop automation pipelines and CI workflows

About Electronic Arts (EA)

Global game developer and publisher

Full Description

Description & Requirements Electronic Arts creates next-level entertainment experiences that inspire players and fans around the world. Here, everyone is part of the story. Part of a community that connects across the globe. A place where creativity thrives, new perspectives are invited, and ideas matter. A team where everyone makes play happen.

Electronic Arts is looking for students to join our world-class internship experience. For more information about our Emerging Talent Program, visit https://www.ea.com/careers/interns-and-university-graduates

We are DRE (Development & Release Engineering) - a central EA team at the intersection of DevOps, Release Management and Build Engineering. We build, manage and maintain the build pipelines for the majority of EA's studios worldwide.

What You’ll Do

  • You will report to the Development Director and Technical Leads to develop and maintain automation pipelines in our CI system, and work with our internal customers on designing, prototyping and implementing new automated workflows.

Qualifications

  • Analytical approach to problem solving, debugging, and troubleshooting
  • Experience with scripting languages (e.g. Groovy, Python, Powershell, Batch)
  • Experience with creating automation jobs in CI systems (e.g. Jenkins, GitLab CI)
  • Experience deploying and maintaining virtual infrastructure (e.g. Virtual Machines)
  • Experience with metrics gathering and visualization tools (e.g. Grafana, Elastic Search, Kibana)

You must be available for a full-time paid internship in the Summer of 2026

We are only considering students who will be enrolled in an accredited degree program in the Summer of 2026, slated to graduate no earlier than December 2026. Applicants must be legally authorized to work in Canada on a full-time basis during the 16-week internship. Visa sponsorship is not available for this position.

Please ensure your graduation date is visible on your resume.
