Marketing / Digital Marketing Internship

Intern, Digital Marketing (Summer 2026)

at Bombardier

📍 Location Dorval, QC
📋 Details Internship · 8 months
📅 Posted Mar 04, 2026

About the Role

Support digital marketing campaigns and social media

About Bombardier

Global aviation leader; strong marketing academy

Full Description

Innovation is in our DNA… is it in yours?
Bombardier is a global leader in aviation, creating innovative and game-changing planes. Our products and services provide world-class experiences that set new standards in passenger comfort, energy efficiency, reliability and safety. We are a global organization focused on working together with a team spirit.

Your boarding pass will include…

Several conferences, including:

  • Meet An Executive
  • Women Taking Flight

Learning more about Bombardier, including:

  • Bombardier Products conference
  • Visits of the Bombardier sites
  • Bombardier Academy of Learning

Many social/networking opportunities, including:

  • Volunteering
  • Networking for Success
  • 5 à 7, Potluck, and much more!

During your internship, you will contribute to:

  • Work with cross-functional teams, to develop and implement a world-class customer experience online that will strengthen the Bombardier brand, improve market perception, and drive new and repeat business through the use of digital marketing solutions & Social Media tools.
  • Implement updates for all websites, including content, creative and visual aspects.
  • Support the day-to-day operations of the social media strategy across various social media platforms.
  • Using social media monitoring tools, monitor Bombardier social media channels, identifying opportunities for engagement and sharing key insights.
  • Evaluate website/campaign analytics to analyze KPIs, identify end-user patterns and make recommendations to continually improve website/campaign effectiveness.
  • Assist in the development and execution of digital strategies and marketing campaigns such as email, SEO, SEM, display, social and cross-media advertising that incorporate strong analytical measurement capabilities and supports the growth and promotion of Bombardier.
  • Continually promote our brands and educating internal collaborators on our branding strategy

What we are looking for:

  • Is working toward a University degree in Marketing, Communications or an associated field.
  • Is bilingual (French and English) with good writing skills and communication skills in English.
  • Has experience using MS Office and familiarity with design applications.
  • Has the ability to perform under pressure and deadline oriented.
  • Has the ability to work in a team environment, show a sense of commitment and professionalism in work.

Boarding Information:

  • Location: Administrative Centre (CA)
  • Duration: 8 months
  • Flexible workplace-Hybrid

It is important to note that our internship opportunities are open to students only, not new graduates. All our interns may be required to occasionally travel outside of Canada for training/work purpose.
