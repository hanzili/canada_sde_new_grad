About Cryopeak Energy Solutions Corporation

Canada’s largest private LNG solutions provider

Full Description

Are you a detail-oriented and organized individual with a passion for supply chain efficiency?

We are seeking a highly motivated and proactive Junior Buyer/Expeditor to support day-to-day purchasing and procurement operations for our team in Richmond, BC. This role ensures company -wide purchasing functions across Projects, Operations and Corporate teams, ensuring the timely and cost-effective procurement of materials, equipment and services.

This position is essential in ensuring efficient procurement processes, including the timely issuance of purchase orders, proactive monitoring and expediting of orders, and on-time delivery of materials to support company operations.

About the Role:

The Junior Buyer/Expeditor will be based in Richmond, BC, reporting to the Director, Corporate Services supporting purchasing management functions - ensuring timely and cost-effective procurement of materials, equipment and services for the organization.

Requirements & Qualifications:

Who You Are:

  • Bachelor’s degree or diploma in Supply Chain Management or a related discipline (Business Administration, Commerce, or Economics), with knowledge of procurement, logistics, and supply chain operations within a technical/engineering environment.
  • A degree of technical aptitude and familiarity with various materials, fabrication processes and service requirements.
  • Elementary knowledge of preservation and packaging procedures
  • Familiarity with multi-discipline coordination
  • Excellent communication skills, both written and verbal.
  • Proficient in Microsoft Office Suite (Excel, Word, Outlook)
  • Knowledge of ISO 9001

What You’ll Do

  • Assist in the procurement process by obtaining quotes, issuing purchase orders, and following up on deliveries
  • Expediting orders, ensuring deliveries are made on time, and resolving any issues with suppliers
  • Ensure that the data for reporting is complete and accurate
  • Coordinate information relating to packing lists, shipping releases and delivery documentation including any documentation required for import/export purposes
  • Interface with team members regarding expediting issues
  • Coordinate quality surveillance and inspection activities
  • Monitor supplier/vendor engineering, documentation, fabrication and quality inspection schedules to ensure committed deadlines are maintained and pro-actively managed
  • Coordinate and support logistics activities to facilitate deliveries as per Incoterms conditions specified in purchase orders
  • Assist with the identification and management of change orders

Send Resume to: HR@cryopeak.com

We thank all applicants for their interest; however, only those selected for interviews will be contacted.

About Cryopeak Energy Solutions Corporation – Cryopeak is Canada’s largest private, vertically integrated LNG producer and service provider—delivering reliable, end-to-end LNG Virtual Pipeline solutions. From procurement and transportation to onsite equipment and support, we power remote industries safely and efficiently. We’re ISO 9001 certified, backed by a Transport Canada-approved ERAP, and active on the Z276 technical committee. In 2024, Cryopeak merged with Ferus Natural Gas Fuels and acquired Campus Energy Partners’ LNG assets, creating a powerhouse LNG solutions company now known as Cryopeak Energy Solutions Corporation.

We’re growing fast and looking for high-performing individuals who want to be part of something big.
