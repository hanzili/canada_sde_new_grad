About the Role

Create and post social content, engage audience

About Kam Kam Cafe

Local cafe with hands-on marketing mentorship

Full Description

Your Role:

As our first Social Media Manager, you’ll help launch kam kam café by creating buzz around the opening and setting the tone for the brand’s online presence. We’re looking for a creative and curious human to tell our brand story and build a community through in-person events, pop-ups, visual story-telling, and showing our human side. You won’t be stuck in a cubicle. You’ll be hanging out in the cafe, attending events, talking to staff, filming latte art fails, and capturing the little moments that make our place feel like home.

What you'll actually be doing:

  • Taking photos and short videos of the team, customers, and daily life at the cafe
  • Creating and posting content on Instagram
  • Brainstorming fun, low-pressure content ideas (think: "Day in the life of a barista" or "Our dishwasher is the real MVP")
  • Replying to DMs and comments in a friendly, human way

You're probably a good fit if:

  • You're always the one taking pics or making Reels for fun
  • You get what makes a moment feel real vs. staged
  • You're not afraid to ask a coworker if you can film them wiping a counter
  • You work well with others and actually like feedback

Perks (aka the good stuff):

  • Free coffee/food during your shifts
  • Real portfolio work (not fake projects)
  • Working alongside an experienced marketing mentor
  • Flexible hours (we know you might have another job or summer plans)

How to apply:

  • Fill in the form
  • Short call to explain the role (15-20 min)
  • In-person interview with small task to see how we work together (1hr)

Pay: $20.00 per hour

Benefits:

  • Flexible schedule

Application question(s):

  • Tell us briefly about your experiences in marketing, promotion, or organizing events. (This doesn’t have to be professional experience, you could include personal projects, school events, or community projects for example.)
  • Do you have any experience with community organization, school councils, team or group organization? (Basically anything where you have worked with others to do something.)

Work Location: Hybrid remote in North Bay, ON P1B 9E4
