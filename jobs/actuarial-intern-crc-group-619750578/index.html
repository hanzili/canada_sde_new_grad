Please review the following job description:

The role reports to the Actuarial Manager, Life Reinsurance with a focus on pricing and preparing reinsurance quotations, reporting, experience analytics, statistical analysis and providing technical support in response to customer requests.

KEY RESPONSIBILITIES

Following is a summary of the essential functions for this job. Other duties may be performed, both major and minor, which are not mentioned below. Specific activities may change from time to time.

  • Pricing and quotation of business within agreed parameters.
  • Provide actuarial and technical support in respect of life reinsurance business (traditional and non-traditional structures) for client companies and reinsurance partners.
  • Conducting experience studies, statistical analysis and performing profitability analysis of reinsurance accounts.

EDUCATION AND EXPERIENCE

The requirements listed below are representative of the knowledge, skill and/or ability required. Reasonable accommodations may be made to enable individuals with disabilities to perform the essential functions.

  • Degree in Actuarial Science, Math, Statistics or related field (Actuarial Science strongly preferred)
  • Minimum of 2 SOA or equivalent Actuarial Exams passed (3-4 strongly preferred)
  • Good knowledge of life insurance products
  • Knowledge of pricing principles and profit testing techniques
  • Knowledge of experience analysis
  • Knowledge of sources of data for pricing purposes.

CERTIFICATIONS, LICENSES, REGISTRATIONS

None

FUNCTIONAL SKILLS

  • Proficient in Moodys/GGY AXIS
  • Proficient in MS-Office tools, especially MS-Excel and MS-Access.
  • Proficient in other statistical / data, or math related languages such as R or Python.
  • Ability to access and analyze data using SQL.
  • Strong communication skills (oral and written)
  • Strong analytical, problem-solving and decision-making skills.
