Full Description

Junior Project Manager, Marketing – Mississauga, ON

Role: New

Salary: $50,000 - $55,000

PATTISON Outdoor Advertising is Canada’s leading Out-of-Home (OOH), Transit and Digital display advertising company.

With the widest variety of products, our advertisers benefit from unparalleled advertising opportunities. Our sales professionals and support associates across the country, provide clients with unmatched service, insights and

campaign innovation. As leaders in the Canadian advertising industry, PATTISON Outdoor Advertising is determined to initiate change starting from within, lead by example and reflect the diversity of the communities within which we work and live, coast-to-coast. By supporting internal and external efforts of inclusion and diversity we aim to better ourselves and the world around us. We are PATTISON Outdoor Advertising and here everyone belongs.

Job Information: We are seeking a highly organized and creative Junior Project Manager who will coordinate and execute a wide range of marketing initiatives. This role is cross-functional, requiring strong organizational skills, attention to detail, and the ability to manage multiple projects simultaneously in a fast-paced environment. A successful candidate will play a key role in ensuring marketing projects are delivered on time and aligned with business objectives while collaborating closely with internal stakeholders, external vendors and creative teams. This role plays a key part in driving brand visibility, supporting sales efforts, and delivering impactful campaigns across our Out-of-Home (OOH) portfolio, including digital, transit, airport, and place-based media.

The ideal candidate is detail-oriented, proactive, and passionate about marketing, with strong project management and communication skills.

What you’ll do…

Campaign & Project Coordination

  • Assist in the planning and execution of national and regional ad campaigns
  • Coordinate timelines, deliverables, and approvals across internal teams and external partners
  • Track project progress, flag risks and ensure deadlines are met on time
  • Support campaign rollouts across Digital Out-of-Home formats (digital screens, transit, billboards, etc.)

Marketing Production & Content Support

  • Assist in the development of product overviews and sales materials
  • Support creation and deployment of:

o Product Marketing

o Internal communications

  • Maintain up-to-date product and market knowledge

Content & Communications

  • Support the creation of content for product marketing
  • Draft and edit marketing copy (EN, FR bilingual is an asset)
  • Assist with internal communications
  • Ensure brand consistency across all communications

Administrative & Coordination Tasks

  • Manage marketing calendars and project trackers
  • Coordinate approvals and internal workflows
  • Maintain marketing asset libraries and databases
  • Support budget tracking and invoice processing

Who you are …

  • Bachelor’s degree in Marketing, Communications, Business, or related field
  • 1–3 years of experience in marketing, advertising, or communications
  • Experience in media, advertising, or OOH industry is a strong asset
  • Strong organizational and project management skills
  • Excellent written and verbal communication skills
  • Ability to manage multiple projects and deadlines
  • Attention to detail and problem-solving mindset
  • Collaborative and team-oriented
  • Proactive, adaptable, and eager to learn
  • Microsoft Office (PowerPoint, Excel, Word)
  • Familiarity with CRM tools and project management platforms (Atlassian Products – JIRA or Confluence; Asana an asset)
  • Basic knowledge of Adobe Creative Suite (asset)
  • Experience with CMS platforms (asset)

Languages

  • English fluency (French is a strong asset, especially for national communications)

What’s in it for you…

We understand that there is life at work and life outside of work. Here are a few benefits we offer that support us to be our creative best.

  • Hybrid work mode – 2 day per week can be worked from home
  • Competitive Group Benefit coverage
  • Annual Health and Wellness Credits
  • Paid Time Off
  • Discount rates at other JPG divisions
  • Employee and Family Assistance program
  • Education Assistance Program (Tuition Reimbursement Program)
  • Gym discounts
  • Extra Long Weekends in Summer
