Job Description We are seeking a Jr Data Scientist to develop solutions in-house, with scalable platforms that can be extended globally. This person will support with piloting, productizing and deploying high impact, global data analytics use cases, using specialized data science/AI techniques. This person will additionally help in identifying new strategic application fields or products by applying innovative AI methods and technologies.

Responsibilities

  • Develops large-scale decision support tools based on optimization e.g., LP, MIP, NLP, etc...).
  • Proposes new algorithms and heuristics for solving new problems.
  • Prepares program test data, test programs, and debug programs.
  • Derives analytical requirements, select and execute appropriate mathematical methods and interpret the results in consultation with the business teams.
  • Implements pilots or POCs for prioritized use cases with country/regional/functional teams.

  • Collaborates with other teams to understand their needs for a project and tailor models and analysis accordingly.
