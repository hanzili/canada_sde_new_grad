About the Role

Audit websites, optimize campaigns, analyze performance

About GWL Realty Advisors

Real estate firm with digital marketing focus

Full Description

GWLRA is a leader in both the commercial and multi-residential real estate industries. We believe in generating value by creating vibrant, sustainable communities that engage, excite, and inspire. Our people live our values through the work they do and the results they deliver. Our core values are keeping our word, working better together and embracing diversity.

We are seeking a motivated and detail‑oriented Marketing Intern for the summer term to support the Marketing department in reducing vacancies across our portfolio during the critical leasing season. This role is ideal for a marketing student who is technologically savvy, analytical, and eager to gain hands‑on experience in digital marketing and property marketing operations.

Responsibilities

  • Audit and update property websites to ensure all content—photos, amenities, pricing, floor plans, and descriptions—is accurate, current, and optimized for prospective renters.
  • Optimize digital advertising campaigns across platforms by reviewing performance metrics, recommending adjustments, and supporting ongoing improvements to lead generation.
  • Review and validate call‑tracking data to ensure accuracy, improve data integrity, and strengthen lead‑to‑lease performance.
  • Identify outdated or inconsistent information across online listings and coordinate updates to maintain competitive visibility.
  • Assist with content accuracy checks, quality control, and digital channel performance reviews.
  • Support additional marketing tasks as needed during peak leasing months.

Qualifications

  • Currently enrolled in a Marketing, Communications, Business, or related post‑secondary program.
  • Strong analytical skills and comfort working with data‑centric tasks.
  • Highly detail‑oriented with excellent organizational skills.
  • Technologically inclined, with an interest in digital marketing tools, website content management, and performance analytics.
  • Ability to work independently, manage multiple priorities, and meet deadlines.
  • Strong written and verbal communication skills.

What You'll Gain

  • Hands‑on experience with real‑world digital marketing and property advertising tools.
  • Exposure to performance optimization strategies across multiple digital channels.
  • Insight into the operational and strategic components of reducing vacancies in a competitive rental market.
  • A valuable learning opportunity for building foundational skills in marketing, analytics, and digital operations.

The base salary for this position is $21.00 hourly.

What We Offer

  • Half-day every Friday before a long weekend
  • A friendly, welcoming, and supportive culture
  • Many social and team events!
