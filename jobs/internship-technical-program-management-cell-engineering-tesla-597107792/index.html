About the Role

Coordinate cross-functional PM tasks in manufacturing.

About Tesla

Tesla: innovative EV leader, mass manufacturing.

Full Description

What To Expect Consider before submitting an application:

This position is expected to start around May 2026 and continue through the entire Summer term (i.e. through August 2026) or into Fall 2026 if available. We ask for a minimum of 12 weeks, full-time and on-site, for most internships.

International Students: If your work authorization is through CPT, please consult your school on your ability to work 40 hours per week before applying. You must be able to work 40 hours per week on-site. Many students will be limited to part-time during the academic year.

The Internship Recruiting Team is driven by the passion to recognize and develop emerging talent. Our year-round program places the best students in positions where they will grow technically, professionally, and personally through their experience working closely with their Manager, Mentor, and team. We are dedicated to providing an experience that allows the intern to experience life at Tesla by including them in projects that are critical to their team’s success.

Location: Toronto, ON

What You'll Do Qualified applicants may be reviewed by one or more of the following teams:

Materials Operations Planner Intern

  • Material Planner Intern is a pivotal role within the operations department, that will manage the procurement of materials, planning production, initiate and coordinate the successful completion of equipment build. This person will also manage inventories while also coordinating with suppliers and internal stakeholders. Experience in material planning or related experience working in a manufacturing and warehouse environment is preferred but not required. Technical Project Management Intern

  • The Technical Project Management Intern on the TTA Project Management Team will be a cross-functional point of contact leading tool activity coordination, support new product introduction on new & existing equipment, lead the project roadmap with engineering team members, and jump in to support gaps within the project as necessary. Production exposure, understanding of how complex production lines operate including industrial workings between cross-functional facilities/departments, line balance, OEE, KPI, etc. Manufacturing exposure, understanding of the balance of equipment, process, metrology, controls, production interactions/coordination. Experience in Program/Project Management or Technical Leadership. Project Management Administration Intern

  • Seeking a highly motivated undergraduate to contribute as Project Management Administration in Manufacturing Department. There are many manufacturing projects which are running in TTA. coordinating these projects from beginning to end is highly important. This is an exciting opportunity to join a world-class manufacturing facility. What You'll Bring- Currently pursuing a degree in Engineering, Electrical, Mechanical, Mechatronics, Computer Science, or a related field , Tesla
