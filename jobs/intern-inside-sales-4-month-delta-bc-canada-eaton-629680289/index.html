About Eaton

Full Description

Eaton’s ES AMER PCS division is currently seeking a Intern, Inside Sales (4-month) - Delta, BC Canada .

The hourly rate for this position is $20 - $25 per hour. Please note the salary information shown above is a general guideline only. Salaries are based upon candidate skills, experience, and qualifications, as well as market and business considerations.

What You’ll Do Eaton Industries (Canada) Company located in Delta, BC, has an opening for a 4-month internship opportunity in our Sales Department.

Our Delta, BC sales office is responsible for sales, service and support of Eaton product including Electrical Distribution, Automation & Control, Power Distribution and Circuit Protection in the Sales region. The successful candidate will assist the Sales office with inside sales functions including, but not limited to, quotations, technical / product support, data entry & analysis, order entry, and order management.

This position is scheduled to start in May 2026. Primary Responsibilities

  • Assist with inside sales – quotations, data entry & analysis, order entry, and sales support.
  • Assist our quotation department with project / RFQ single line and specification review.
  • Assist our quotation department with assembling quote packages and RFQ file management.
  • Supply our outside sales team with inside sales support, such as fielding technical product inquiries, order entry, and order expedites.
  • Perform all job functions with adherence to Eaton’s Values and Philosophy, as well as the Safety and Environmental Standards

Qualifications Basic Qualifications:

  • Currently enrolled in an accredited Electrical, Mechanical or Power Systems Engineering or business-related Degree or Diploma Co-op program,
  • Legally authorized to work in Canada without company sponsorship.
  • This position does not offer relocation. Candidates must reside within 120 kms of the work location.

Preferred Qualifications

  • Experience in manufacturing electrical power distribution and/or control products.
  • Previous sales or customer service experience is considered an asset.
  • Must have excellent communication skills, both verbal and written.
  • Strong problem-solving skills and time management capabilities.
  • Ability to read and understand blueprints and schematics.
  • Strong knowledge of Microsoft Office, specifically PowerPoint and Excel.
  • Passion for innovation & continuous improvement, and willingness to challenge status quo.
