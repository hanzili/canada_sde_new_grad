Full Description

Junior Bookkeeper (Part-Time – Growth Opportunity)

About FTS Business Solutions

At FTS Business Solutions, we help businesses Flip the Switch by providing customized accounting and IT solutions. We support small and medium-sized businesses with everything from day-to-day bookkeeping and reconciliations to payroll, reporting, and year-end preparation.

We work both onsite at client locations and offsite from our office, offering flexible, high-quality support tailored to each client.

We are growing and looking for a Junior Bookkeeper who wants hands-on experience and the opportunity to grow into a larger role over time.

About the Role

This is a part-time position (approximately 15 hours per week) with flexibility in scheduling. As our client base continues to grow, there is strong potential for this role to transition into a full-time position.

This is an excellent opportunity for:

  • A recent business/accounting graduate
  • Someone early in their bookkeeping career
  • A detail-oriented individual looking to grow within a dynamic firm

Responsibilities

  • Assist with bookkeeping tasks for multiple clients
  • Data entry and maintaining accurate financial records
  • Bank and credit card reconciliations
  • Accounts payable and accounts receivable support
  • Assist with payroll processing and source deductions
  • Support preparation of financial statements and internal reports
  • Work both onsite at client locations and offsite as required
  • Learn and adapt to various client systems and workflows

You will gain exposure to multiple industries and accounting environments — no two clients are the same.

Qualifications

  • Bookkeeping experience preferred
  • Recent business/accounting graduates encouraged to apply
  • Diploma or certificate in Accounting, Business Administration, or related field considered an asset
  • Proficiency in QuickBooks Desktop and/or QuickBooks Online (or strong willingness to learn)
  • Solid Excel skills
  • Strong attention to detail and organizational skills
  • Deadline-oriented and able to manage multiple priorities
  • Ability to work independently and as part of a team
  • Professional, adaptable, and comfortable working directly with clients

What We Offer

  • Flexible part-time schedule
  • Mentorship from experienced accounting professionals
  • Hands-on exposure to a variety of businesses
  • Clear path for growth and increased responsibility
  • Opportunity to transition into full-time as the role expands

If you are motivated, reliable, and looking to build your career in accounting with a growing local firm, we would love to hear from you.

Please submit your resume and a brief cover letter outlining your experience and interest in the position.
