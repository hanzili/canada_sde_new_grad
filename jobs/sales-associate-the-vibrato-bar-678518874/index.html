Full Description

Join The Vibrato Bar

Luxury Guitar Gallery & Content Associate

Location: Stoney Creek / Burlington, OntarioType: Part-Time or Full-TimeCompensation: Hourly + Performance Bonuses + Growth Opportunities

The Vibrato Bar is not a traditional guitar store.

We are building a modern luxury guitar gallery focused on exceptional instruments, boutique builders, high-end customer experiences, and content-driven growth. We specialize in unique and inspiring guitars from independent luthiers and world-class brands, presented more like an art gallery than a retail shop.

We’re looking for someone who genuinely loves guitars, music, creativity, and people, someone excited to help us grow a unique business from the ground floor.

What You’ll Do

This is a hybrid role combining:

  • customer experience
  • operations
  • shipping/receiving
  • content creation
  • and helping grow the business overall

No two days are the same.

Responsibilities include:

  • Assisting customers in-store, online, and via social media
  • Photographing and filming guitars and gear
  • Helping create Instagram, TikTok, and YouTube content
  • Packing and shipping sold instruments safely and professionally
  • Receiving, inspecting, and organizing incoming inventory
  • Maintaining a clean, visually elevated showroom experience
  • Helping merchandise guitars and displays
  • Assisting with Reverb, Shopify, and online listings
  • Supporting marketing initiatives and creative ideas
  • Helping us continue building one of the coolest guitar businesses in North America

What We’re Looking For

You do not need decades of retail experience.

We care more about:

  • personality
  • initiative
  • creativity
  • reliability
  • and passion

Ideal qualities:

  • Strong communication skills
  • Passion for guitars/music/gear culture
  • Comfortable around social media and content creation
  • Organized and detail-oriented
  • Positive energy and team mentality
  • Comfortable learning new systems and technology
  • Self-starter mindset
  • Interest in photography/video/content is a major plus

Why Work Here?

We’re building something special.

This is an opportunity to work in a highly creative, fast-growing environment surrounded by incredible instruments, music, and passionate people.

We offer:

  • Competitive hourly pay
  • Performance-based bonuses
  • Employee discounts
  • Flexible and collaborative environment
  • Significant opportunity for growth as the business expands
  • Creative input and ownership opportunities
  • A genuinely fun atmosphere with a small, passionate team

Job Type: Full-time

Pay: $18.00-$24.57 per hour

Benefits:

  • Paid time off

Work Location: In person
