About the Role

About BBA Consultants

40-year private engineering firm focused on energy/resources

Full Description

Type of position: Regular

Your future role on our team As a junior biologist or junior environmental professional, you’ll help with environmental project delivery in sectors such as energy, mining, infrastructure, commercial and private development.

Working as part of a multidisciplinary team and under the supervision of experienced professionals, you’ll support biophysical characterization, environmental impact studies and environmental permit applications.

With us, you’ll get the opportunity to... (Approx. 50% fieldwork and 50% office work)

  • Support project managers in preparing and executing mandates
  • Participate in environmental impact assessments and related studies
  • Assist with field surveys (flora, fauna, aquatic environments, soil and ecosystems)
  • Contribute to environmental permit and authorization applications
  • Help develop methodologies and sampling plans
  • Compile, enter and validate environmental data
  • Help draft technical reports (inventories, baseline studies, environmental assessments)
  • Collaborate with team members to meet project deadlines

Do your qualities and values match our corporate culture?

  • Thirst to learn and excel
  • Willingness to work together while having fun
  • Desire to be in the thick of the action
  • Rigorous and ethical

Summary Of Certifications And Job Requirements

  • Bachelor’s degree in biology, environmental science or related field
  • From 1 to 3 years of relevant experience
  • Interest or initial experience in field surveys (fauna, flora, aquatic environments, soils, etc.)
  • Excellent writing and communication skills
  • Knowledge of MS Office Suite
  • Asset: knowledge of ArcGIS
  • Asset: master’s degree is a relevant field
  • Asset: bilingual (English and French), spoken and written

An Overview Of BBA’s Total Rewards

  • Annual premium program for regular employees
  • Time bank
  • Cellphone fee reimbursements
  • Group insurance plan from the first day of employment, including disability insurance and a telemedicine program
  • Retirement savings plan
  • Annual vacation and sick leave
  • Premium for employee referrals

BBA considers many factors when determining the annual base salary, including location, market, education, relevant and demonstrated work experience, technical assessment, qualifications, certifications and other relevant skills.

At BBA, You Get Many Benefits

  • A vibrant social club with something for everyone
  • Access to many experts and coaching
  • A collaborative team that shares its ideas and knowledge

  • Participation in our firm’s major projects up to 2030:

  • Going carbon neutral

  • Achieving 30% women on our teams

  • And much more!

  • Custom training and development plans

About BBA For over 40 years, we’ve been pushing the boundaries of engineering to serve our clients in the Energy and Natural Resources sector. At BBA, we develop innovative, flexible and sustainable solutions, from strategy to execution.

BBA is one of Canada’s leading private consulting engineering firms, focused on practice (PCB), with a network of 15 offices. Our teams work closely to deliver projects that shape tomorrow’s industry at the local, national and international levels.

Our people’s passion and excellence make BBA a Canada’s Best Employer and one of Canada’s Best Managed Companies .

We have unique talents, and we’re committed to providing a workplace where you can be yourself . Job postings are written in gender-neutral language.
