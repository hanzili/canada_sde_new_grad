About the Role

Build and scale full-stack product systems

About ThinkFlix

Startup building AI-powered edtech platform

Full Description

ThinkFlix is an AI-powered education platform transforming learning material into engaging, binge-worthy audio and video learning experiences. We’re launching across campuses and building the future of how students learn.

Our mission is to make learning entertaining, personalized, and addictive. We are building the future of edutainment, where education feels as engaging as watching your favorite series.

Role Overview

As a ThinkFlix Full-Stack / Product Systems Engineer, you will build and scale the core product systems that sit on top of ThinkFlix’s AI infrastructure.

This is not a traditional frontend role.

You will:

  • work directly with the CEO
  • rapidly ship features
  • extend and evolve an existing product
  • turn rough ideas into working systems

You will operate across frontend, backend, and system design, ensuring that our AI capabilities translate into a fast, reliable, and scalable product experience.

Responsibilities

  • Build and extend core product systems on top of existing infrastructure
  • Rapidly implement new features across content generation workflows, user onboarding, video delivery and interaction
  • Design and maintain backend services and APIs ,data flows between systems
  • Integrate AI pipelines into production-ready user experiences
  • Improve system performance, reliability, scalability
  • Work closely with the CEO to prioritize features, iterate quickly and ship continuously
  • Translate product ideas into functional, well-structured systems

Qualifications

  • Strong experience building full-stack systems in production
  • Proficiency in JavaScript / TypeScript, React / Next.js
  • Backend frameworks (Node.js, APIs)
  • Experience working with existing codebases (not just greenfield projects), APIs and system integrations
  • Strong understanding of system design, data flow and performance optimization

What We’re Looking For

  • A builder, not just a coder
  • Someone who can take a rough idea → working feature in days
  • Comfortable working directly with founders
  • Strong ownership mindset

Why Join Now

This is a ground-floor opportunity to:

  • Help shape a product before mass adoption
  • Build real ownership in a growing platform
  • Gain hands-on startup growth experience
  • Be part of redefining how education is consumed

Job Types: Full-time, Part-time, Internship / Co-op

Work Location: Remote
