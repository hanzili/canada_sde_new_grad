ABOUT THE ROLE

  • This role offers the chance to work with data trends, inform targeting strategies, and contribute to the development of privacy-conscious advertising tools.

KEY RESPONSIBILITIES

  • Explore datasets to extract meaningful insights and improve audience segmentation strategies.
  • Contribute to the creation of predictive models and ML solutions tailored to privacy-first targeting.
  • Leverage tools like SQL and Python to manipulate, analyze, and present complex data effectively.
  • Ensure data quality and model reliability while adhering to strict compliance and privacy guidelines.
  • Work closely with product and solutions teams to convert strategic goals into actionable analytics.
  • Respond to internal and client-facing data inquiries with clear, well-supported analysis

QUALIFICATIONS

  • 1–2 years of hands-on experience working in data science, analytics, or a similar domain.
  • Skilled in using Python and SQL to explore data, build models, and generate insights.
  • Solid grasp of core statistical concepts, machine learning techniques, and forecasting methods.
  • Comfortable translating complex analyses into clear, actionable recommendations for diverse teams.
  • Bonus points for exposure to datasets, ad tech environments, or privacy-centric platforms.
