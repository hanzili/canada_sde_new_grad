About the Role

About Primaris REIT

Real estate investment trust with data-driven analytics

Full Description

Head Office - Toronto, ON

JOB TYPE

Internship - 4 months

Position Summary

Primaris REIT is seeking a Portfolio Analyst Intern to join our team in downtown Toronto reporting to the Director, Portfolio Analytics. You will have a keen interest in the real estate/retail industry and support the team in market research, KPI tracking and data analysis.

Vacancy Status: Internship

Role

  • Conduct market research across key Canadian Markets, summarising key economic indicators and trends (e.g. employment rates, key industries, transportation developments etc)
  • Build and maintain a historical KPIs database; update with new metric information as needed (e.g. recovery rates, leasing spreads, sales etc)
  • Review and validate data in PowerBI reports, whilst working with IT to develop a PDF training manual
  • Analyse portfolio KPIs including tenant sales, GROC and leasing activity and provide monthly summary reports
  • Help with proformas, and present research and insights through written and verbal methods with higher management

What You’ll Gain

  • Exposure to a dynamic corporate environment and learning how to take large information and build concise presentations and reports to understand historical trends and how they impact future outcomes
  • Networking opportunities with professionals to build your industry knowledge
  • Mentorship from experienced team members
  • Possibility of future career opportunities within the company

Requirements

  • 2nd or 3rd year in a Business, Commerce, Economics or Real Estate Degree
  • Recommended courses taken in marketing, economics, finance, operations, geography or accounting
  • Interest in market research, retail and data analysis
  • Proficiency in MS Office Suite (Excel, PowerPoint, Outlook)
  • Familiar with PowerBI an asset
  • Strong attention to detail and organisational skills; experience in case study projects an asset
  • Ability to apply critical thinking and solve problems
  • Excellent communication skills (verbal & written) to work in a team environment

Join our public and growing REIT, hone your skills and provide invaluable hands-on experience in the dynamic world of Commercial Real Estate. Contribute to meaningful projects, gain real-world expertise, and build a foundation for a successful career!

Compensation This is a 4 month paid internship starting May 2026 until end of August 2026. Internship hourly wage ranges are dependent on your year of study: 1st Year – 5th Year ranges between CAD $22.00 - $25.00

Competencies

  • Communicate clearly, concisely, professionally and convey active listening and openness
  • Build positive relationships and demonstrate commitment, effort and a "take charge" attitude
  • Produce accurate work with attention to detail and consistently meet deadlines
  • Take ownership for actions, accept constructive criticism and make the necessary changes to behaviour
  • Overcome obstacles to get the job done, identify problems and recommend solutions
  • See change positively and adapt quickly to change in work requirements

  • Empowerment: Use the means provided to carry out responsibilities to the best of your ability and to implement creative ideas
