Position Summary

Primaris REIT is seeking a Portfolio Analyst Intern to join our team in downtown Toronto reporting to the Director, Portfolio Analytics. You will have a keen interest in the real estate/retail industry and support the team in market research, KPI tracking and data analysis.

Vacancy Status: Internship

Role

  • Conduct market research across key Canadian Markets, summarising key economic indicators and trends (e.g. employment rates, key industries, transportation developments etc)
  • Build and maintain a historical KPIs database; update with new metric information as needed (e.g. recovery rates, leasing spreads, sales etc)
  • Review and validate data in PowerBI reports, whilst working with IT to develop a PDF training manual
  • Analyse portfolio KPIs including tenant sales, GROC and leasing activity and provide monthly summary reports
  • Help with proformas, and present research and insights through written and verbal methods with higher management
