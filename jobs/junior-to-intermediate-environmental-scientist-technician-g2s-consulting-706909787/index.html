Full Description

At G2S, we value the contribution of our diverse staff. We believe that a balance between career and lifestyle leads to a healthy and productive work environment. JUNIOR TO INTERMEDIATE ENVIRONMENTAL SCIENTIST / TECHNICIAN

Position Summary

The successful candidate will support a wide range of environmental consulting projects involving field investigations, environmental site assessments, remediation programs, hydrogeological investigations, hazardous materials management, and excess soil projects. This position offers an excellent opportunity for hands-on field and office experience with mentorship and career development opportunities.

Responsibilities

  • Conduct and support:

o Phase One and Phase Two Environmental Site Assessments (ESAs)

o Soil, groundwater, and surface water sampling programs

o Hydrogeological investigations and groundwater monitoring

o Environmental remediation and monitoring programs

o Excess soil management and sampling programs

o Hazardous materials and designated substances surveys

o Monitoring well installation oversight and gauging

  • Maintain accurate field notes, chain of custody documentation, and site records

  • Assist with interpretation of environmental and hydrogeological data

  • Prepare technical reports, figures, tables, and regulatory documentation

  • Coordinate with project managers, laboratories, subcontractors, and clients

  • Ensure compliance with company health and safety procedures and Ontario regulations

  • Travel to project sites throughout Ontario as required

Qualifications

  • Degree or diploma in:

o Environmental Science

o Geoscience

o Environmental Engineering

o Or a related environmental discipline

  • Previous environmental consulting or field experience is considered an asset

  • Familiarity with Ontario environmental regulations and guidance documents is an asset

  • Strong organizational, communication, and problem-solving skills

  • Ability to work independently and within a team environment

  • Comfortable working outdoors in varying weather and site conditions

  • Valid driver’s license and access to a reliable vehicle are required

What We Offer

  • Competitive compensation based on experience

  • Opportunities for professional growth and advancement

  • Diverse and challenging project work

  • Training and mentorship opportunities

  • Supportive, collaborative team environment

How to Apply

Interested candidates are encouraged to submit their resume to info@g2sconsulting.com

We thank all applicants for their interest; however, only those selected for an interview will be contacted.
