Junior Project Coordinator

at Stantec

📍 Location Edmonton, Alberta
📋 Details Type: Regular; Duration: Full-time
📅 Posted Mar 25, 2026

About the Role

Coordinate projects, manage schedules, support stakeholders

About Stantec

Global design and engineering firm in buildings

Full Description

Stantec’s Buildings team is on a mission to become the world’s leading integrated design practice. Our architects, engineers, interior designers, consultants, sustainability specialists, and technologists are passionate about the power of design. Our collaborative culture and our innovative, sustainable approach to projects help us create buildings that matter to our world. Together, we are enhancing the quality of life globally through design. Join us and design your place with Stantec.

Your Opportunity

We have an exciting opportunity for a Project Coordinator in our Edmonton office. In this role you will report through a Project Manager on one or more projects. This will allow growth potential as you will be exposed to clients, design and construction team members, program and project management processes, and strong project leaders. You will have the support and mentorship of our leadership and Project Management team.

Your Key Responsibilities

  • Conduct cost, schedule and document management functions on project(s). As Project Coordinator, you will be required to develop, track and update, cost forecasts; maintain and update project cost reports; develop and update project schedules; support procurement; and maintain document management systems through all phases of the project.
  • Deliver day-to-day project tracking and communications with the clients, project teams, and other project stakeholders.
  • Prepare and distribute project correspondence and meeting notes.
  • Review, monitor and record all contracts, invoices, change orders and payments to all contractors, consultants, and equipment vendors.
  • Support the preparation of regular Progress Reports (internal and external).
  • Participate and coordinate project team activities and interact effectively with clients, Contractors, Architects, Engineers, Project Managers, and other consultants or stakeholders.
  • Coordinate and participate in proposal writing and business development activities.
  • Assist with organizing and coordinating team building activities such as social events and office team meetings.
  • Strategize and generate ideas to help manage project risks and add value for our clients and their projects.

Candidate Capabilities

  • Strong communication skills are essential.
  • General understanding of a project lifecycle including design and construction processes and requirements.
  • Understanding of and ability to read plans and specifications.
  • Ability to contribute positively to and flourish in a team focused environment.
  • Good interpersonal skills.
  • Excellent organizational skills and problem-solving abilities.
  • Strong computer skills and literacy in software including MS Power Point, MS Word, MS Outlook and MS Excel.
  • Experience with specialty software such as MS Project, Primavera, and document control software will be considered an asset.

Education and Experience

  • Bachelor's degree or technical diploma in a related field.
  • 1-3 years of related experience; or an equivalent combination of education and experience.
  • Acquired project management experience through on-the job consulting projects

Hybrid office environment working with computers and remaining sedentary for long periods of time. Field work may include exposure to the elements including inclement weather. Field work may include exposure to active construction sites.

This description is not a comprehensive listing of activities, duties or responsibilities that may be required of the employee.

Stantec is a place where the best and brightest come to build on each other’s talents, do exciting work, and make an impact on the world around us. Join us and redefine your personal best.

About Stantec Stantec is a global leader in sustainable engineering, architecture, and environmental consulting. The diverse perspectives of our partners and interested parties drive us to think beyond what’s previously been done on critical issues like climate change, digital transformation, and future-proofing our cities and infrastructure. We innovate at the intersection of community, creativity, and client relationships to advance communities everywhere, so that together we can redefine what’s possible.
