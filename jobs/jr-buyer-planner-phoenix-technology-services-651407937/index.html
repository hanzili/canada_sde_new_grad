Full Description

Jr. Buyer/Planner

Phoenix Technology Services LP is a progressive and innovative Calgary based Directional Drilling Company. Driven by our client’s needs, we are committed to providing more efficient and cost-effective drilling operations.

We have an opening for a Jr. Buyer/Planner at our SE Technical Centre. The primary responsibility of the Jr. Buyer/Planner is to maintain a supplier base in support of the Company’s Supply Chain/Operations. This position will require a dedicated, high energy, driven individual capable of executing procurement for products & services related to oilfield services, material, and equipment, through established & qualified vendors. The Jr. Buyer/Planner is to execute best practices in areas of procurement & planning when selecting vendors, requirement dates, availability, lead times, quality, price, and past vendor performance. Additional responsibilities include the review & recommendation of safety stock settings, economic order quantities, order/sales multiple and minimum order quantities, item coverage settings, vendors, strategic sourcing, QHSE, accounting, logistics, regional supply chains and engineering.

Responsibilities also include:

  • Review purchase requisitions for completeness and accuracy.
  • Purchase and expedite required materials, services, and parts.
  • Prepare and issue requests for quotes to determine optimal purchases.
  • Procurement of tactical commodities with best cost, quality, terms, and deliveries.
  • Maintain a strong supplier base and adhere to supply agreements set forth by Supply Chain Manager.
  • Ensure QHSE/Engineering has qualified vendor prior to procurement.
  • Manage the communication of materials and information to the vendor, ensuring NDA’s are in place.
  • Issue purchase orders using sound purchasing practices and applicable Company policy.
  • Negotiate best value and monitor order progress maximizing OTD performance.
  • Expedite the supply of material in accordance with the contracted delivery date and required date.
  • Communicate internally to customers of any required dates that cannot be met and provide updates of any changes.
  • Drive cost reduction on purchases by 5% annually without compromising quality or delivery performance.
  • Utilize MRP to identify and plan procurement requirements and establish strategies.
  • Utilize safety stock and forecasting practices methodology.
  • Maintain VMI levels set forth by Buyer/Planner and the maintenance thereof where applicable.
  • Arrange for transportation and/or storage of purchased products.
  • Recommend cost saving proposals including JIT, VMI, Bond programs or alternate sourcing.
  • Discuss defective or unacceptable goods or services with inspection or quality control.
  • Work closely with Engineering to ensure accurate information is shared with vendor.
  • Ensure proper approval has been obtained before issuing the PO to the supplier.
  • Ensure correctness of PO generations as per procedure.
  • Track and manage vendor performance.
  • Assist other departments with various projects when time permits.
  • Perform all duties so as to meet the responsibilities listed in the Employee and Corporate HSE Handbooks.
  • Perform duties according to the Company’s quality system procedures and work instructions.

Qualifications

  • Minimum 1 year procurement experience in drilling industry
  • Understanding of local vendor base and product knowledge
  • Knowledge of ERP/MRP systems - Preference to SAP
  • Knowledge of key supply chain/logistic processes, current best practices, & commercial terms & conditions
  • PMAC / APICS certification is considered an asset

Competencies Required for Effective Performance

  • Ability to simultaneously manage multiple tasks of competing priority, efficiently and independently
  • Self-directed and able to work with minimal supervision
  • Must excel in a demanding, fast-paced environment, with the ability to drive and maintain target dates
  • Strong organizational skills, written and verbal communication skills, and attention to detail
  • Ability to take direction with the desire to support a cohesive teamwork environment
