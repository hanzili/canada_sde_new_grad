Full Description

MARKETING COORDINATOR (SUMMER STUDENT POSITION)

Salary $ 21/hr

This posting is for a new Summer Student position.

WHO IS KEILHAUER?

Founded in 1981, Keilhauer is a privately owned, design-focused contract furniture manufacturer. Keilhauer manufactures seating and tables that makes work more comfortable for all. Keilhauer products are thoughtfully made to support communication and engagement in spaces such as meeting rooms, collaboration areas, lobbies and lunchrooms. Working with world-renowned furniture designers, Keilhauer is internationally recognized for award-winning design, built with extreme craftsmanship, to the highest environmental standards.

SUMMARY

The Marketing Coordinator is responsible for supporting the planning, execution and optimization of social media initiatives while contributing to forward-looking opportunities for growth, including evaluating new platforms and shaping our upcoming content strategy.

Reporting to the Senior Marketing Manager, the Marketing Coordinator ramps up quickly and focuses on adding value throughout the work term. They will also leave with valuable experience working on a marketing team and completing core marketing tasks. This position is best suited to a motivated and curious individual who takes pride in exploration and learning. Assisting with growing and guiding our public brand perception furthers Keilhauer’s mission of making work comfortable for all.

PRIMARY RESPONSIBILITIES

Social Media Strategy & Insights

  • Conducts ongoing social media trend analysis across platforms (LinkedIn, Instagram, Pinterest, etc.)
  • Monitors and reports on competitor social media activity, identifying opportunities and gaps.
  • Translates insights into actionable recommendations for content and campaigns.

Platform Exploration & Innovation

  • Researches and evaluates emerging and underutilized platforms, including Pinterest, to identify opportunities for brand growth.
  • Develops recommendations for how Keilhauer could incorporate Pinterest into its social media and paid media mix, including content strategy, audience targeting and success metrics.
  • Presents findings and proposed pilot approach to the marketing team.

Content Planning & Calendar Development

  • Supports ideation for the F27 social media content calendar, aligned with brand and product priorities.
  • Builds and maintains a social media calendar, including key themes, campaigns and due dates.
  • Collaborates with internal stakeholders to align content with launches, events and initiatives.

Paid Social & Campaign Support

  • Assists in social media buying, including audience research, budget allocation and campaign setup.
  • Supports A/B testing of creative, copy and targeting strategies.
  • Tracks and reports on campaign performance, identifying optimization opportunities.

Testing, Measurement & Reporting

  • Helps design and execute social media testing frameworks (content formats, messaging, visuals).
  • Monitors KPIs (engagement, reach, conversions) and compiles performance reports.
  • Provides insights and recommendations to improve effectiveness.

AI & Innovation

  • Explores and experiments with AI imagery and content tools (e.g. generative image platforms).
  • Supports development of new visual concepts and workflows using AI tools.
  • Stays informed on emerging digital and creative technologies relevant to social media.

QUALIFICATIONS

  • Currently enrolled in a post-secondary program in Marketing, Communications, Business or a related field.
  • Proficient in Microsoft Outlook, Word and Excel.
  • Familiarity with social media platforms and basic analytics tools are required; strong interest in social media, digital marketing and content creation is a plus.
  • Analytical mindset with the ability to interpret data and identify trends.
  • Organized and detail-oriented with the ability to manage timelines and multiple projects simultaneously.
  • Creative thinker with an eye for design, visuals and storytelling.
  • Excellent written and verbal communication skills.
  • Interest in design, architecture or the furniture industry is a plus.

WHAT YOU’LL GAIN

  • Hands-on experience in both organic and paid social media strategies.
  • Exposure to B2B marketing in the design and commercial furniture industries.
  • Experience contributing to platform expansion strategy.
  • Experience working with AI tools in a real-world marketing context.
  • Opportunity to contribute directly to a future-facing marketing plan.
  • Mentorship and collaboration with an experienced Marketing team.

WORKING CONDITIONS

This role is performed primarily in an office environment which is typically quiet. Most of your duties will be fulfilled with the use of a computer and office equipment, with communications taking place by phone, email, virtually or in person. Some functions of your role will be conducted in the manufacturing facility where you may be exposed to dust particles and noise.

  • This position requires frequent, dynamic interaction through face-to-face contact, email, phone and other virtual communications.
  • Must be able to multi-task and handle frequent interruptions to planned work activities.
  • Follows all company and OHSA Safety regulations; works in a safe manner and uses personal protective equipment, if required; reinforces housekeeping and Keilhauer’s core values.
