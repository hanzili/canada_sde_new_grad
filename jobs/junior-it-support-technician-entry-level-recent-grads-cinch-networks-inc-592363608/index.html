About the Role

Provide Level 1 IT support and troubleshooting

About Cinch Networks Inc.

Calgary-based IT services firm

Full Description

**Job Summary** We are seeking a motivated Junior IT Support Technician to join our dynamic IT team. This entry-level role is ideal for recent graduates or individuals with foundational IT knowledge who are eager to develop their skills in a professional environment. The successful candidate will provide technical support, troubleshoot software and hardware issues, and assist in maintaining our IT infrastructure across various platforms. This position offers an excellent opportunity to gain hands-on experience with a broad range of IT systems and tools, including Windows, macOS, Linux, and network administration.

**Responsibilities**

- Monitor and respond to incoming support calls and support email - Log and manage tickets in the support system - Provide Level 1 technical support for users (PCs, email, access issues) - Escalate unresolved issues to senior technicians - Communicate clearly with clients and internal staff - Maintain accurate documentation and notes on tickets - Assist with basic system checks and daily operational tasks - Provide technical support to end-users via help desk tickets, phone, or in-person interactions. - Troubleshoot and resolve software issues related to operating systems such as Windows, macOS - Assist with computer management tasks including hardware setup, upgrades, and maintenance. - Support network infrastructure including LAN, VPNs, firewalls, DNS, TCP/IP configurations on SonicWall and Ubiquiti devices. - Manage Active Directory accounts and permissions. - Support computer hardware components and peripherals such as mobile devices and printers. - Assist in monitoring and maintaining IT infrastructure including servers. - Collaborate with team members on projects involving network administration, system updates, and security enhancements.

**Experience**

- Recent graduates with a degree or certification in Information Technology, Computer Science, or related fields are encouraged to apply. - Prior internship or hands-on experience in IT support is a plus but not required; training will be provided for all necessary tools and systems. - Familiarity with operating systems including Windows (Windows 10/11), macOS, Linux. - Basic understanding of computer networking concepts such as TCP/IP, DNS, DHCP, LAN/WAN configurations. - Knowledge of remote support tools such as Helpdesk Tools and various monitoring tools. - Strong communication skills with the ability to explain technical issues clearly to non-technical users. - Demonstrated analysis skills for troubleshooting complex problems efficiently. This role is perfect for individuals passionate about technology and eager to grow their career in IT support within a collaborative environment that values continuous learning and professional development.
