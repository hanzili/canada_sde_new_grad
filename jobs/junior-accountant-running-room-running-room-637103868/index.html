Full Description

Junior Accountant (Entry-Level Accountant) - Full-Time, Permanent

Location: Edmonton Corporate Office.

Work Arrangement: 100% in-office (no remote or hybrid work).

Summary

The Running Room Group of Companies is seeking a detail-oriented and eager-to-learnJunior Accountant to join our Accounting and Finance team. This role is ideal for someone who has foundational accounting experience and is looking to expand their skills and take on new challenges in a collaborative, fast-paced environment. You’ll work closely with other experienced team members and cross-functional departments to support accurate financial reporting and day-to-day accounting operations.

Discover the Running Room Difference

For over 40 years, Running Room has been the heartbeat of the running community, delivering high-performance gear, expert advice, and unparalleled support for runners of all levels. What started as a single store in Edmonton, Alberta, has grown into a renowned brand with 80 corporately owned locations across Canada and the U.S., driven by an unwavering passion for running and fitness.

At Running Room, we do more than sell shoes and apparel—we create an experience. We empower runners to push boundaries, achieve goals, and connect with like-minded enthusiasts. Our commitment to innovation ensures that every product we offer is designed to elevate performance, making Running Room the ultimate destination for those who demand the best.

Whether you’re training for your first 5K or striving for a new personal best, Running Room is here to equip, educate, and inspire you every step of the way. Join us and experience the difference—because running isn’t just an activity, it’s a lifestyle.

Key Responsibilities

  • Assist with daily accounting tasks, including journal entries, general ledger postings, and account reconciliations.
  • Support month-end and year-end closing processes.
  • Help compile financial information for internal reporting and external accountants.
  • Ensure accuracy and compliance with established Canadian GAAP.
  • Respond to accounting inquiries and provide support to internal departments.
  • Contribute tovariance analysis.
  • Participate in process improvements and learn best practices from senior team members.
  • Other related duties as assigned.

Skills and Qualifications

  • Diploma or degreein Accounting, Finance, or a related field (or equivalent experience).
  • Basic understanding of accounting principles and full-cycle accounting.
  • Strong attention to detail and willingness to learn.
  • Proficiency in MS Office, especially Excel and Word.
  • Experience with Dynamics 365 Business Central or similar accounting software is an asset.

Why Join Us?

  • Competitive salaryand comprehensive benefits package.
  • Employee discount program.
  • Opportunity for career growth within a well-established Canadian brand.
