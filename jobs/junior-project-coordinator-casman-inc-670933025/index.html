Junior Project Coordinator

at Casman Inc

📍 Location Edmonton, AB T6E 5A6
📋 Details Full-Time · Permanent
📅 Posted Apr 24, 2026

About the Role

Coordinate project scope, schedules, and documentation

About Casman Inc

Construction-focused company with design-build capabilities

Full Description

Who We Are

CASMAN is a trusted leader in commercial and residential construction, recognized for delivering technical expertise, and attention to detail. Our teams work closely with clients, consultants, and trade partners to bring our projects to life—from early planning through final turnover.

We are seeking a Project Coordinator to play a key role in managing scope, schedule, cost, and quality for our clients.

About the Position

This role supports construction projects across all phases, from pre-construction to closeout. As a Project Coordinator, you’ll work directly with the Project Team as well as Clients, Consultants, Subcontractors, and Suppliers. This position is ideal for someone who enjoys both technical coordination and design-focused collaboration within a fast-paced construction environment.

How You’ll Contribute

  • Support projects from bidding through turnover
  • Prepare and coordinate RFIs, submittals, and contract change management documentation
  • Support communication between consultants, internal teams, and site staff
  • Assist with project schedules, scopes of work, and subcontract documentation
  • Review and interpret blueprints, specifications, contracts, and consultant deliverables
  • Prepare reports, presentations, and internal project documentation
  • Liaise with subcontractors, suppliers, consultants, and clients
  • Participate in site meetings and maintain familiarity with active construction sites
  • Assist with cost tracking, document control, and project administration
  • Promote compliance with safe work practices and company standards

Experience & Skills

  • Completion of min 2 year Post-secondary education in Construction Engineering, Architectural Technology, or Interior Design
  • Previous Construction or Design Industry Experience an asset
  • Comfort with working on active construction sites is required
  • Strong ability to interpret architectural and construction drawings
  • Strong written and verbal communication skills
  • Highly organized with strong time-management abilities
  • Proficiency with:
  • MS Project
  • Microsoft Office (Excel, Word, Outlook)
  • Familiarity with drafting, design, & publishing tools considered an asset (AutoCAD, Bluebeam, Revit, Sketchup, InDesign, Photoshop, etc)
  • Valid driver’s license and reliable vehicle
  • Ability to travel to sites across Alberta and BC as required
  • Candidate to be Based in the Edmonton area
  • This role is a Full Time In Office position

Why Build Your Career With CASMAN

  • Competitive wages
  • Clear opportunities for career advancement
  • Sponsorship for continued education and professional development
  • Comprehensive benefits package
  • Pension matching plan
  • Team-oriented, collaborative culture
  • Opportunity to contribute to projects from concept through completion

Casman welcomes all applications, and we thank you for your interest in our group. Only applicants of interest will be contacted.
