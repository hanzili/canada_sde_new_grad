About RenderSEO

SEO-focused marketing tech for local businesses

Full Description

Company Description

RenderSEO is a marketing technology company dedicated to helping local businesses enhance their online visibility through a robust local search management platform (SaaS) and unparalleled client support. Backed by a team of SEO industry experts, we combine our extensive knowledge to offer a comprehensive range of services tailored to enterprise and agency clients. At RenderSEO, we pride ourselves on providing customizable solutions, whether it’s a full-scale listings rollout or a targeted SEO enhancement. We take on challenges eagerly and work closely with clients to expand and refine our local search offerings based on their unique needs.

Role Description

This is a full-time remote role for a Junior Back End Developer. The role involves working on back-end web development, collaborating with the development team to build secure and scalable solutions, and contributing to software architecture and implementation. The developer will also assist in improving existing systems, resolving issues, and ensuring performance optimization. This role offers the opportunity to work on innovative projects and grow in a collaborative technical environment.

Qualifications

  • Proficiency in Back-End Web Development: AWS, Lambda, Python
  • Experience with Software Development and Programming
  • Familiarity with Front-End Development is a plus
  • Strong problem-solving and debugging skills
  • Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Software Engineering, or a related field
  • Willingness to collaborate in a team environment and learn new technologies
  • Previous experience in SEO or marketing technology is an advantage
