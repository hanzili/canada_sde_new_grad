Full Description

In a changing world, unprecedented challenges require unmatched talent. Join one of Montreal’s Top Employers in 2025. Graduate program As a graduate, you will benefit from an 18 month program that aims to give you the tools to develop yourself and discover your potential through customized trainings, networking sessions and transversal projects. This is a permanent role.

More information about the program available on our website:

BNP Paribas in Canada Students & Young graduates

The position at a glance The Americas CIB Transversal Operation Permanent Control team is responsible for strengthening and aligning the OPC teams embedded within our businesses and functions by standardizing and implementing a consistent OPC methodology across the region. For this role specifically, the Canada-based candidate will support Transversal OPC’s Artificial Intelligence (AI) strategy that aims to strengthen the permanent control framework by automating internal processes and developing self-service tools to proactively reduce operational risk.

The position is created as part of extended team headed in New York and localized in Montréal as part of the regional set up. The CIB Americas Transversal Operational Permanent Control (“OPC”) Team has a broad range of responsibilities, overseeing the operational controls related activities in various perimeters like front offices, Operations, and functions such as Finance and others. The responsibilities include among other tasks: procedure management, control design, monitoring, and assessment, control performance reporting, risk assessment, and audit recommendations follow-up.

In detail The team member will be responsible for the following activities:

  • Contribute actively to the implementation of Transversal OPC’s AI program by automating manual qualitative and quantitative analysis and building front-end interfaces intended for bank-wise use.
  • Collaborate transversally with internal stakeholders (i.e., Business Lines & Functions) to translate their business requirements into high-impact AI/ML solutions to generate savings.
  • Leverage advanced Python programming to build robust data pipelines, interact with ML models through APIs, and automate workflows.
  • Plan, organize, design and maintain data sources, ETL workflows and build dashboards in tools like Power BI for scalable, refreshable analysis and reporting
  • Communicate technical concepts and results to non-technical stakeholders, including upper management, in a clear and concise manner.
  • Assist in identifying areas for operational process improvements, control procedures, and workflow organization using AI to increase the team’s efficiency and effectiveness.

Work conditions: This position provides for standard working conditions in an office and a normal work schedule from Monday to Friday. This position requires little travel.
