Full Description

Job description

Daisy Chu & Company Inc, a CPA firm is seeking for a Staff Accountant. We are a small and fast growing professional accounting office located in Vancouver since 2010. We provide services for a variety of engagements types. Our client are from different industries, including constructions, investment holdings, real estate, restaurants, importing and exporting and professional etc. We provide expert tax planning advice and consultation for clients with corporate and personal income tax issues.

We are looking forward for you to join our team.

Your role:

  • Bookkeeping with QuickBooks and Sage accounting
  • Preparing payroll, GST, PST and WorkSafe reports
  • Preparing personal tax, corporate tax and trust return
  • Preparing Notice to Reader and Review Engagement
  • Some tax research and contact CRA to gather info
  • Assist to prepare CRA audit
  • Some receptionist duties is required

Qualifications and Skills

  • Accounting Degree or Diploma
  • Prefer to have one year of solid public practice experience
  • Detail oriented, fast learner, strong technical and organizational skills
  • Able to manage tax files with minimal supervision
  • Excellent computer skills with: Caseware, Caseview, QuickBooks, Sage 50 and Cantax
  • Strong written and oral skills
  • Mandarin or Cantonese speaking is an asset

Please email your resume along with salary expectations to us. We will contract the successful candidates.
