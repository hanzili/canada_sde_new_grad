Full Description

Junior Brand Coordinator / Personal Assistant (Contract Role)

Work directly with an experienced entrepreneur to support the development and launch of a modern lifestyle brand from the ground up, with opportunities to contribute across additional ventures.

We are seeking a highly organized, detail-oriented, and proactive Junior Brand Coordinator / Personal Assistant to provide a blend of brand support and day-to-day executive assistance in a fast-paced, entrepreneurial environment.

What You’ll Do:

  • Support the day-to-day coordination and execution of a growing lifestyle brand

  • Assist with content planning, content creation support, and social media coordination (Meta platforms)

  • Research and help develop written content, including articles, book reviews, and related materials

  • Manage scheduling, calendars, and coordination across multiple priorities

  • Maintain organized digital files, documents, and records across projects and business activities

  • Support website updates and content management (WordPress / Gutenberg)

  • Assist with product research, selection, and light product coordination

  • Provide personal and executive support, including errands, follow-ups, and task management

  • Assist with administrative coordination related to business setup, including basic corporate and accounting organization

  • Provide support across multiple ventures as needs evolve

Must Haves:

  • Hands-on experience with social media platforms, particularly Meta (Instagram/Facebook)

  • Strong research and written communication skills

  • Highly organized with strong attention to detail

  • Self-starter with the ability to manage tasks independently and follow through

This Role Is Ideal for Someone Who:

  • Is interested in marketing, branding, digital content, or entrepreneurship

  • Is comfortable balancing creative work with administrative responsibilities

  • Has strong organizational and time management skills

  • Is adaptable and able to manage multiple priorities in a dynamic environment

Assets:

  • Experience with WordPress and Gutenberg

  • Familiarity with tools such as Canva or basic content editing platforms

  • Previous experience in an administrative, coordinator, or assistant role

  • Basic understanding of bookkeeping or expense tracking

Details:

  • Full-time hours for the summer

  • Temporary contract role with potential for ongoing part-time employment

  • Role may include work across multiple ventures, with primary focus on the lifestyle brand

  • Located in Burlington, On

Job Type: Fixed term contract
Contract length: 4 months

Pay: $25.00 per hour

Work Location: In person
