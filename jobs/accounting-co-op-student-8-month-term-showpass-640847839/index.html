Full Description

Why Showpass?

At Showpass, we’re redefining how people experience live events. From music festivals to sports arenas, we create seamless, unforgettable moments that bring people together.

We’re a company built on experiences—not just for our customers, but for our team. We move fast, think big, and empower our team to make decisions. Here, you’ll have the freedom to lead, the support to grow and the opportunity to make a real impact.

THE ROLE: Accounting Co-op Student (8 month term)

CO-OP TERM: May - Dec, 2026

THE LOCATION: Calgary, AB

In this role, you will support the day-to-day accounting and bookkeeping functions of Showpass. This is an exciting opportunity to work alongside and learn from experienced team members and gain hands-on experience across multiple areas of finance and accounting.

THE RESPONSIBILITIES:

  • Process weekly payouts and advances to event organizers (clients)
  • Create and distribute weekly and monthly reports for clients
  • Assist with generating client donation payment reports monthly
  • Assist with maintaining client records for donations
  • Assist with the reconciliation of employee credit card transactions in Float
  • Maintain accounts payable and accounts receivable
  • Assist with day-to-day bookkeeping and accounting tasks using QuickBooks Online Advanced
  • Support ad hoc tasks and basic financial analysis as needed

THE QUALIFICATIONS:

  • Working toward certificate or degree in Accounting, Business Administration, Finance or a related field
  • 1 year of experience in accounting administration/bookkeeping is an asset
  • Strong proficiency in AI tools and technology
  • Strong multitasking skills with the ability to manage various priorities and tight deadlines
  • Experience with QuickBooks Online Advanced is an asset
  • Ability to handle sensitive information with confidentiality and care
  • High level of accuracy and meticulous attention to detail
  • Strong verbal and written communication skills, with the ability to explain complex information clearly
  • Excitement about implementing operational improvements and automation to enhance efficiency

Showpass is committed to employment equity, welcomes diversity in the workplace, and encourages applications from all qualified individuals. We thank all interested applicants; however, only qualified applicants selected for an interview will be contacted.
