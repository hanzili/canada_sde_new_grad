Job Description:

The position is responsible for supporting the Personal Insurance underwriters with administrative duties.

Vacancy: 1 Temporary

Salary Range: $19.84/hour

What We Offer

  • Award winning culture
  • Flexible hybrid working conditions (1 day in the office per week)
  • Growth opportunities

Responsibilities:

  • Maintain, service and distribute work from the Personal Insurance mailbox within service standards
  • Index Personal Insurance documentation
  • Underwrite at a level of moderate risk for policy changes and renewals
  • Order inspections
  • Produce Confirmations of Insurance
  • Review IBC Errors
  • Handle mail, filing, and scanning
  • Other duties as assigned

Qualifications:

  • Ability to handle large volume of transactions and to meet defined service levels
  • Self-motivated, detail oriented and innovative
  • Strong communication skills (written & verbal)
  • Proficient in Microsoft Office products
  • Excellent time management skills in order to prioritize and meet deadlines
