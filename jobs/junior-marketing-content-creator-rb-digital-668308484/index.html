Full Description

About RB Digital

RB Digital is a national distributor of professional print, garment decoration, and finishing equipment serving customers across Canada. We represent industry-leading global brands and support businesses in apparel decoration, signage, print production, and promotional markets. As our marketing team continues to grow, we are looking to add a creative, driven, and detail-oriented Junior Marketing Content Creator to support our expanding digital presence.

Position Overview

The Junior Marketing Content Creator is responsible for developing, producing, and scheduling engaging digital content across multiple platforms. This hands-on, on-site role focuses on photography, videography, editing, and social media coordination. The position reports directly to the Marketing Manager and collaborates closely with the sales, product, and showroom teams.

Key Responsibilities

  • Develop engaging social media content for Instagram, LinkedIn, YouTube, and other digital channels
  • Create graphics, short-form videos, reels, and promotional assets
  • Assist with product launches, campaigns, and event promotions
  • Capture product demos, team content, behind-the-scenes footage, and event coverage
  • Edit short-form and long-form video content
  • Photograph equipment, products, and marketing materials
  • Maintain consistent brand identity across all content
  • Manage and schedule content calendars
  • Monitor performance metrics and assist in campaign optimization

Required Skills & Qualifications

  • Proficiency in Adobe Creative Suite (Photoshop, Illustrator, Premiere Pro; After Effects is an asset)
  • Strong video editing and photography skills (DSLR knowledge is a plus but not required)
  • Solid understanding of social media platforms and content trends
  • Ability to manage multiple projects and meet deadlines
  • Strong attention to detail and brand consistency
  • Self-motivated, organized, and able to work independently and within a team

Bonus Skills

  • DSLR camera knowledge
  • Basic understanding of Shopify or website content updates
  • Experience creating YouTube thumbnails and social media cover graphics
  • Ability to take direction and iterate quickly
  • Comfortable working in a fast-paced environment with shifting priorities

Preferred Qualifications

  • Final-year student or recent graduate in Marketing, Media Production, Digital Communications, or related field
  • Experience managing business or professional social media accounts
  • Basic understanding of B2B marketing
  • Interest in print, apparel decoration, or industrial technology

What We Offer

  • Real-world marketing experience within a growing national company
  • Exposure to product launches, trade shows, and major campaigns
  • Hands-on involvement in brand development
  • Collaborative and entrepreneurial work environment
  • Opportunities for growth and career development
