Full Description

Job Type: Full-Time Student
Term End Date: August 28, 2026

Location: Sioux Lookout, Ontario

Rate of Pay: Hourly $ 22.136 - $29.733

Hours of Work: 35 hours per week

Application Deadline: Open until filled

Qualifications:

  • Persons of First Nations ancestry will be given preference (OHRC, Part II, Special Employment).
  • Ability to communicate in one of the First Nations dialects in the Sioux Lookout Zone is an asset.
  • Third year or higher in post-secondary program with an accredited College/University in Business/Business Administration, Human Resources, Marketing, or a related program
  • Ability to work with all levels of staff (independently or part of a team) internally/externally and build relationships with stakeholders.
  • Excellent client service skills with the ability to earn and retain client confidence.
  • Proficient written and oral communication skills.
  • Strong computer skills: Proficiency with Microsoft Office Programs
  • Knowledge and experience related to First Nations and corporate governance including the First Nations political environment and background is an asset.
  • Knowledge of all Provincial legislative standards and guidelines as they relate to the recruitment process (e.g., ESA, OHSA, AODA, Ontario Human Rights Code, etc.).
  • Ability to perform the requirements of the role on a regular basis.

Roles and Responsibilities:

  • Learn and support various HR generalist activities including recruitment, onboarding, HR reporting and analytics, creation and maintenance of HR databases, and health and safety
  • Assist with day-to-day employee requests
  • Assist with data entry in Human Resources Information System (UKG)
  • Support the HR team to ensure deadlines are met
  • Assist with administrative functions
  • Participate in Other duties as assigned
