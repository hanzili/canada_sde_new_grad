Komatsu develops and supplies technologies, equipment and services for the construction, mining, forklift, industrial and forestry markets. Headquartered in Tokyo, Japan, Komatsu employs more than 64,000 people worldwide, operating in more than 140 countries. For more than a century, the company has been creating value for its customers through manufacturing and technological innovation, partnering with others to empower a sustainable future where people, business and the planet thrive together. Since the company’s founding in 1921, Komatsu has been committed to supporting individuals and communities through job training, skills development and giving back. As a Komatsu employee, you will be encouraged to grow alongside our global company, contributing to a more sustainable future for all. If you are looking for a company that values your talent and potential, join Komatsu to be a part of something big and help advance modern society.

Job Overview Are you a motivated and organized student looking to gain hands-on Human Resources experience in a fast‑paced and supportive environment? We’re looking for an HR Student to join our Fort McMurray team for the summer term (May–August). This role is perfect for someone who is eager to contribute, learn, and get exposure to a wide variety of HR functions.

Reports to: HR Business Partner Fort McMurray

Shift: Monday- Friday

Term: May- August- 4 Months

Safety Sensitive: No- RCMP criminal record check and submission of 5-year provincial driving record will be required

Relocation: This position does not offer relocation

What You'll Do As our HR Student, you will support the team through a mix of project work and day‑to‑day tasks, including:

  • Safety Passport Implementation: Assist with rollout, tracking, communication, and coordination with employees
  • Policy Updates: Support updates and other administrative changes
  • Office Support: Provide general office and administrative assistance as needed
  • SharePoint Organization: Help clean up, reorganize, and enhance our SharePoint sites for improved usability
  • Reporting & Analytics (Nice to Have): Support HR reporting; Power BI skills would be an asset for building dashboards or refreshing existing reports

Who You Are We’re looking for someone who is:

  • Currently enrolled in a Business, Human Resources, or related university program
  • Highly organized, dependable, and eager to learn
  • Comfortable working with confidential information
  • Tech‑savvy with strong attention to detail
  • A great communicator with a positive, can‑do mindset
  • Experience with Power BI, SharePoint, or data visualization is an asset, but not required

Additional Information This posting reflects an existing vacancy within our organization. The position is currently open, and we are actively seeking a candidate for immediate placement. The successful applicant will fill a vital role that is ready to be stepped into without delay. Artificial Intelligence (AI) may be used to assist with interview scheduling and other administrative tasks during the hiring process. However, AI is not used in candidate screening, evaluation, or selection decisions. All hiring decisions are made by our human hiring teams and your application will be reviewed and considered personally by one of them.

Accommodation during the recruitment process is available on request. While we appreciate all applicant submissions, only those considered to be most qualified will be contacted for further assessment.

Note to Recruiters: Komatsu and its subsidiaries do not accept unsolicited resumes from any source other than directly from a candidate. Any unsolicited resumes sent to Komatsu, directly or indirectly, will be considered Komatsu property. Komatsu will not pay a fee for any placement resulting from the receipt of an unsolicited resume. A recruiting agency must first have a valid, written, and fully executed agency agreement contract for service to submit resumes.
