SUMMER STUDENT Community Development and Marketing Assistant

at Big Brothers Big Sisters of North Simcoe

📍 Location Midland, ON L4R 3K7
📋 Details Summer internship · 3 months · $18.50 per hour · Seasonal
📅 Posted Apr 10, 2026

About the Role

Create social content, support campaigns and events

About Big Brothers Big Sisters of North Simcoe

Nonprofit youth mentorship org with community outreach

Full Description

Job Summary
Big Brothers Big Sisters of North Simcoe is seeking an energetic and creative Summer Student to support our Fundraising and Community Development Coordinator. This position will focus on assisting with marketing, community engagement, fundraising initiatives, and awareness campaigns. The successful candidate will play a key role in helping increase community visibility, strengthen partnerships, and support events and initiatives such as Turn the Town Teal for BBBS Month.

This is an excellent opportunity for a student interested in nonprofit work, marketing, community development, communications, or social impact.

Key Responsibilities

Marketing & Communications

  • Assist in the creation, scheduling, and posting of social media content (Facebook, Instagram, LinkedIn)
  • Draft captions, stories, and simple designs using Canva or similar platforms
  • Support the maintenance of a content calendar aligned with current campaigns and events
  • Take photos and short videos at events and program activities for promotional use
  • Assist with updates to the website and e-newsletters as needed

Campaign Support (including “Turn the Town Teal”)

  • Support planning and outreach for the Turn the Town Teal campaign during BBBS Month
  • Assist with contacting local businesses and encouraging participation
  • Track and maintain a list of participating businesses and partners
  • Distribute campaign toolkits and materials
  • Support recognition and thank-you efforts through posts and emails

Community Outreach & Recruitment

  • Attend community events, festivals, and local gatherings to support awareness and mentor recruitment
  • Engage with community members and families in a welcoming, professional manner
  • Assist in distributing promotional materials and information
  • Support outreach to schools, service clubs, and community organizations

Event Support & Fundraising

  • Assist with event planning, coordination, and logistics
  • Support set-up, take-down, registration tables, and guest support at events
  • Assist with volunteer coordination and materials preparation
  • Support donor and participant follow-up after events

Administrative & Coordination Support

  • Assist with updating databases and contact lists
  • Prepare thank-you letters and acknowledgment materials
  • Support basic reporting on campaign and event impact
  • Organize photos, files, and marketing resources

Learning & Development

  • Participate in weekly check-ins and planning sessions
  • Contribute creative ideas for engaging youth, volunteers, and the broader community
  • Gain exposure to nonprofit marketing, fundraising strategies, and community relationship-building

Qualifications

  • Currently enrolled in or recently graduated from a program related to Marketing, Communications, Media, Social Work, Public Relations, Nonprofit Management, or a related field
  • Strong written and verbal communication skills
  • Comfortable engaging with the public at community events
  • Basic graphic design skills (Canva or similar)
  • Familiarity with social media platforms
  • Organized, dependable, and able to work independently
  • Comfortable with occasional evenings and/or weekend events
  • Valid driver’s license and access to transportation is an asset

Pay: $18.50 per hour

Benefits:

  • On-site parking

Work Location: In person
