About Sun Life

Full Description

You are as unique as your background, experience and point of view. Here, you’ll be encouraged, empowered and challenged to be your best self. You'll work with dynamic colleagues - experts in their fields - who are eager to share their knowledge with you. Your leaders will inspire and help you reach your potential and soar to new heights. Every day, you'll have new and exciting opportunities to make life brighter for our Clients - who are at the heart of everything we do. Discover how you can make a difference in the lives of individuals, families and communities around the world.

Job Description: The Sun Life Health (SLH) business within Sun Life Canada is a leading provider of Group Benefits and Health Solutions. A market leader in the advancement of workplace mental health across Canada, through the introduction of new tools such Virtual Care and Stress Management and Well-Being program providing members access to mental health and medical specialists 24/7. SLH is seeking a Financial Analyst to join its fast-paced and energetic Finance team.

Reporting to the Manager, Financial Controls, the Financial Analyst will play a crucial role in executing and supporting control activities critical to the Sun Life Health business.

What will you do?

  • Prepare account reconciliations ensuring they are accurate and meet Sun Life account reconciliation standards.
  • Identify, investigate, and resolve differences in reconciliations in collaboration with business partners.
  • Support internal and external audits.
  • Validate, improve, and document key business processes and controls.

What do you need to succeed?

  • Relevant prior experience with reconciliations, audit, financial reporting and/or internal controls.
  • Intermediate knowledge of Microsoft Office Tools (including Excel, Word, Access, and PowerPoint).
  • Understanding of IFRS and SOX controls relevant to the insurance industry.
  • Self-motivated, results-oriented and flexible to changing priorities and timelines.
  • Proven technical, analytical, and problem-solving skills.
  • As a condition of the role, the successful candidate must be able to obtain a Government of Canada Reliability Status security clearance through Sun Life.

Preferred:

  • Previous experience with Frontier (or other reconciliation tools) and SAP is an asset.
  • CPA or other relevant designation is considered an asset.
  • Excellent verbal and written communication, interpersonal and collaboration skills.

What's in it for you?

  • Time off giving you the flexibility to focus on the moments that matter including 15 vacation days per year
  • Flexible Benefits from the day you join to meet the needs of you and your family
  • Pension Plan participation starts immediately, and you are automatically enrolled. We help you save more with Sun Life automatic and matching contributions and by including both your salary and bonus when making
  • contributions.
  • The opportunity to move along a variety of career paths with amazing networking potential
  • Flexible hybrid work model
  • Work and professional development that is united by our Purpose: to help Clients and Employees achieve lifetime financial security and live healthier lives
  • A friendly, collaborative, and inclusive culture
  • We’re honoured to be recognized as a 2025 Best Workplace in Canada by Great Place to Work® Canada
