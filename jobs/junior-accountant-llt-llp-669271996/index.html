Full Description

Join Our Team at LLT LLP

About Us

LLT LLP is a growing public practice firm based in Chilliwack. We’re large enough to offer meaningful career progression, yet small enough that individual contributions are recognized and valued.

We take pride in developing our people. Many of our team members have built their careers here—including a recent Partner who joined as a seasonal student and progressed through the firm while completing their degree.

Our client base continues to expand through strong community relationships, and we’re looking to add motivated individuals to support that growth.

The Opportunity

We’re hiring for accounting roles across experience levels—from students and new graduates to experienced professionals.

This role offers exposure to a broad range of clients and accounting work, along with the opportunity to develop technical skills, build client relationships, and grow within the firm.

Who you are:

For experienced candidates:

  • You produce high-quality work and take pride in delivering value to clients
  • You build and maintain strong client relationships
  • You actively look for ways to improve processes and efficiency

For students or recent graduates:

  • You demonstrate a strong work ethic and commitment to learning
  • You have an interest in pursuing a career in accounting
  • You’re motivated to gain exposure to a variety of clients and concepts

Across all levels, we value people who are reliable, collaborative, and focused on continuous improvement.

CPA Support

We support team members pursuing their CPA designation through:

  • Approved CPA Training Office status
  • Financial assistance for education
  • A structured mentorship program

Compensation & Benefits

We offer:

  • Competitive salary
  • Paid overtime (time-and-a-half, with option to bank)
  • Health spending account and group insurance
  • RRSP matching
  • Paid sick days
  • Fitness/wellness allowance

Flexibility

We understand that work is one part of a full life. We offer flexible scheduling to accommodate personal commitments, school, and appointments, while maintaining a strong team-oriented environment.

Location

Our office is conveniently located near Highway 1 in Chilliwack, offering a more accessible cost of living compared to much of the Lower Mainland.

If this sounds like a fit, please submit your resume and most recent transcript.
