Busy Bee Tools is privately owned retailer of Woodworking & Metalworking Tools. With locations across Canada, Busy Bee Tools has grown into one of Canada's top destinations for tool enthusiasts. If you are looking for a position in a growing company that offers challenging careers, competitive wages, profit sharing, health benefits plan & opportunity for growth within the company, you have come to the right place.

Junior Accountant / Bookkeeper

Busy Bee Tools is seeking a detail-oriented Junior Accountant/Bookkeeper to support the Finance team in maintaining accurate, timely, and complete financial records for Busy Bee Tools’ and AgeComfort multi-location retail and e-commerce operations. This position is responsible for assisting with full-cycle accounting activities including accounts payable, accounts receivable, reconciliations, month-end support, and inventory-related accounting. The role contributes to financial discipline, operational efficiency, and strong internal controls while collaborating cross-functionally to ensure smooth day-to-day accounting processes and scalable financial reporting.

Why Work at Busy Bee Tools?
We’re more than just a workplace - we are a community. Here’s what we offer:

  • Competitive Compensation: Base salary plus bonus opportunities, profit sharing, and incentives.
  • Health & Wellness: Comprehensive Group Benefit Plan including dental, vision and extended healthcare.
  • Professional Growth: Comprehensive training, performance-based reviews, and promotion opportunities.
  • Employee Perks: Store discounts, employee events, and a supportive team environment.

Key Responsibilities

Full‑Cycle Accounting

  • Perform day‑to‑day accounting functions including Accounts Payable (A/P) and Accounts Receivable (A/R).
  • Process vendor invoices, match to purchase orders, and ensure timely payments in accordance with company policies.
  • Prepare customer invoices, post receipts, and follow up on outstanding receivables.
  • Maintain accurate general ledger entries and supporting documentation.

Cash & Bank Reconciliations

  • Complete daily and monthly cash reconciliations across stores and bank accounts.
  • Prepare monthly bank and credit card reconciliations and investigate discrepancies.
  • Support cash‑flow tracking and reporting.

Month‑End & Financial Reporting

  • Prepare month‑end journal entries, accruals, and adjustments.
  • Assist with the preparation of month‑end financial packages, schedules, and reconciliations.
  • Support variance analysis and provide explanations as required.
  • Maintain fixed asset and prepaid expense schedules.

Inventory & Costing

  • Assist with inventory costing, adjustments, and reconciliations.
  • Support cycle counts, inventory reviews, and investigation of variances.
  • Ensure accurate posting of landed costs, freight, and inventory‑related journal entries.

Compliance & Process Support

  • Ensure compliance with internal controls, accounting policies, and documentation standards.
  • Support audit requests and provide required schedules and backup.
  • Assist in improving accounting processes and documentation.
  • Work collaboratively with Operations, Purchasing, and Store teams to resolve accounting issues.

Skills & Qualities

  • Strong technological aptitude and comfort working across multiple systems
  • Excellent verbal and written communication skills
  • High attention to detail and accuracy
  • Strong follow-through and accountability
  • Ability to work independently and take initiative
  • Problem-solving mindset with a calm, professional approach
  • Strong organizational and time-management skills
  • Eagerness to learn, develop, and grow within the organization

Experience & Qualifications

  • Diploma or degree in Accounting, Finance, or a related field.
  • 2–5 years of hands‑on accounting or bookkeeping experience with full‑cycle responsibilities.
  • Strong working knowledge of A/P, A/R, bank reconciliations, journal entries, and month‑end close.
  • Experience with inventory accounting and costing is a strong asset.
  • Retail, distribution, or multi‑location experience preferred.
  • Proficiency in accounting systems and ERPs (experience with Business Central, Dynamics, or similar systems is an asset).
  • Strong Excel skills, including reconciliations and data analysis.
  • Experience with POS and e‑commerce accounting integrations is an asset
  • High attention to detail and accuracy.
  • Strong organizational and time‑management skills.
  • Ability to meet deadlines in a fast‑paced environment.
  • Clear communicator with the ability to work cross‑functionally.
  • Proactive, solutions‑oriented mindset.
