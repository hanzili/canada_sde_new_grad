About the Role

Assist budgeting, forecasting, and financial analysis

About Kyndryl

Global IT services leader; strong growth potential

Full Description

Who We Are

At Kyndryl, we run and reimagine the mission-critical technology systems that drive advantage for the world’s leading businesses. We are at the heart of progress; with proven expertise and a continuous flow of AI-powered insight, enabling smarter decisions, faster innovation, and a lasting competitive edge. For our people—Kyndryls—that means doing purposeful work that powers human progress. Join us and experience a flexible, supportive environment where your well-being is prioritized and your potential can thrive.

The Role

As a Contract Financial Analyst Intern , you will operate as a trusted business advisor, providing financial insight that supports decision‑making and influences business strategy. You will gain hands‑on experience across a variety of financial activities while working closely with cross‑functional teams in a collaborative, fast‑paced environment.

This role is ideal for students who enjoy data analysis, problem‑solving, and teamwork, and who have an interest in the IT and technology services industry.

Salary: $20.00 - $25.00 hourly

Key Responsibilities

Your responsibilities may include, but are not limited to:

  • Supporting budgeting and forecasting activities
  • Preparing and reviewing accounting entries
  • Assisting with performance reporting and financial analysis
  • Tracking and reporting key financial metrics and KPIs
  • Supporting resource allocation and cost management initiatives
  • Conducting portfolio analysis and basic data modeling
  • Collaborating with internal stakeholders to provide financial insights and recommendations

Who You Are

Required Skills & Qualifications

  • Currently enrolled in an undergraduate degree program in Accounting, Finance, or Economics
  • Completed 2–3 years of undergraduate studies by the start of the work term
  • Experience with Microsoft Excel and the Microsoft Office Suite
  • Strong analytical and organizational skills
  • Ability to work both independently and collaboratively in a team environment
  • Positive attitude, curiosity, and a willingness to learn
  • Eligible to work in Canada without sponsorship

Why Kyndryl?

  • Gain real‑world financial experience in a global technology organization
  • Work in a hybrid environment that supports flexibility and collaboration
  • Learn from experienced professionals and build a strong professional network
  • Be part of a company committed to inclusion, equity, and continuous growth
