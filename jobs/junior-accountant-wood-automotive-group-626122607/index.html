About the Role

About Wood Automotive Group

Alberta auto retailer with 40-year presence

Full Description

The Wood Automotive Group, one of Alberta's largest automobile retailers for over 40 years, is offering an exciting opportunity for a Junior Accountant to join our Wood Automotive Group team!

The Wood team believes that its people are its greatest asset. We provide our employees with competitive salaries and careers that grow with them. As a group with 8 dealerships, a collision repair centre, and an online pre-owned sales lot, we believe our success and growth is built on the drive and dedication of our people.

About The Role Reporting to the Corporate Controller, the Junior Accountant will be responsible for the tasks assigned so that accurate financial statements are completed in a timely manner.

Responsibilities (but Not Limited To)

  • Posting invoices
  • Reconciling related schedules and processing journal entries
  • Processing cheques
  • Daily deposits
  • Bank reconciliation
  • Journal entries
  • Internal service invoice posting
  • Filing as required

Qualifications

  • Proficient in Microsoft Excel
  • Excellent communication skills

Requirements:

  • Over time flexibility as required (example: month end)
  • At least one year of accounting experience

Some Of The Rewards Of Joining Our Team

  • Exceptional benefits package
  • Pension Plan with employer contributions – free money!
  • Employee Assistance Program including Financial Advisory Services, Nutritional Counselling, Legal Advisory Services, Counselling Services, fitness program, and more!
  • Corporate rates at YMCA and Goodlife Fitness
  • Group social events and company perks

Our Unique Culture Are you the kind of person who wants to make a difference in their community and build a solid career with a growing organization? Consider joining Wood Automotive Group! For over 40 years, we have worked hard providing unparalleled customer service, competitive pricing, and quality products. Committed to education and community, we are proud sponsors of many organizations such as the SAIT Automotive Business Administration program, KidSport, the PREP program, The Salvation Army, the SSUBI ("Hope") Foundation and the Rogers Charity Classic.
