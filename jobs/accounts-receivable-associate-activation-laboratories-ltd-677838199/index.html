Job Title: Accounts Receivable Associate

Position Overview

Contributing to the accounting department with a focus on accounts receivable, reporting to the Accounting Manager of Canada.

Education & Experience

  • Accounting or bookkeeper diploma or 3 years accounting experience

Key Responsibilities

  • Accounts receivable
  • Perform reconciliation of sales ledger and department sales as required
  • Correspond to customers regarding sales invoice claims on invoice payments and other inquiries in order to update accounts
  • Perform account adjustments as needed through research and client contact
  • Provide credit information and balance confirmation on clients’ accounts
  • Manage incoming payment
  • Maintain accurate customer account records
  • Assist in generating monthly report and summarizing receivables data
  • Any other tasks as determined by Accounting Manager of Canada

Other Requirements

  • Common sense
  • Organizational skills
  • Ability to complete assigned tasks
  • Computer literate with knowledge of word, sage 50 accounting, excel
  • Strong attention to detail, accuracy and professional communication skills for customer interaction
