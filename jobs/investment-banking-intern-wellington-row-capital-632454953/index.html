Company Description

Wellington Row Capital is a next-generation advisory firm specializing in investment banking and capital advisory services for growth-stage companies. Combining digital marketing and AI-driven deal origination with traditional advisory expertise, we connect visionary businesses with the right investors to streamline the capital-raising process. Our focus is on serving entrepreneurs and companies in high-growth sectors, leveraging cutting-edge technology to deliver superior outcomes for our clients. Wellington Row Capital is redefining how investment banking serves the modern economy.

Wellington Row Capital (WRC) 📍 Remote | Commission-Based | Part-Time

About the Role:

We're looking for ambitious finance students and recent graduates to join our team as LinkedIn Outreach Interns. You'll be connecting with business owners, investors, and industry professionals to build relationships and generate opportunities for our firm.

What You'll Do:

  • LinkedIn Outreach : Connect your LinkedIn account to our automation systems for systematic outreach campaigns. You'll maintain full access to use LinkedIn normally while we handle scaled prospecting.
  • Lead Qualification : When prospects respond, you'll prequalify them using our framework and book qualified leads on discovery calls with our advisors.
  • Deal Learning : Join discovery calls to learn how we evaluate companies, assess deal fit, and determine which opportunities to pursue.
  • Deal Execution : When we sign a client, you'll be the lead intern supporting deal materials including: Teaser documents

Pitch decks (sizzle decks)

Confidential Information Memorandums (CIMs)

Data room preparation

Financial modeling (as needed)

  • Real Experience : Get hands-on exposure to the full investment banking process from prospecting through deal execution.

What You'll Get:

  • Commission-based compensation
  • your earnings grow with your results (our deals are typically between $20-50K, and you will receive 1% of collected engagements for anyone you refer). You will also receive 1% of the final success fee.
  • Direct mentorship from Wall Street veterans on our team
  • Real-world experience in finance, sales, and networking
  • Strong resume builder
  • tangible skills employers want
  • Flexible schedule
  • work around your classes or other commitments
  • Career development
  • potential for full-time opportunities

Ideal Candidate:

  • Current student or recent graduate in Finance, Business, or related field
  • Strong written communication skills
  • Self-motivated and results-driven
  • Has at least 300 LinkedIn Connections
  • Is open to connecting their LinkedIn account to our automation system
  • Is open to making their LinkedIn account on-brand with our company branding
  • Interest in investment banking, private equity, or financial services
  • Be located in USA or Canada

Time Commitment: 5 hours/week (flexible scheduling)
