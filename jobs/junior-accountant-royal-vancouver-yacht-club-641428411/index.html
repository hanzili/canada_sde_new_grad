Full Description

About Us

The Royal Vancouver Yacht Club (RVYC) is one of the world's premier yacht clubs and the finest in Vancouver. Our mission is to promote yachting, seamanship, and fellowship among current members and those who share our values of tradition, sportsmanship, and community service, among many others.

Job Description

Reporting to the Controller, the Junior Accountant will play a crucial role in supporting the Senior Accountant with payables, month-end processes, year-end audit, and reporting processes. This role involves accurately processing vendor invoices, ensuring timely payments, and maintaining effective communication with internal and external stakeholders.

Duties and Responsibilities

  • Process invoices accurately and efficiently, ensuring timely payments to vendors.
  • Prepare and process electronic transfers and payments.
  • Reconcile vendor statements and resolve any discrepancies.
  • Maintain vendor files and update contact information as needed.
  • Respond to vendor inquiries in a timely and professional manner.
  • Month-end close processes, including journal entries and reconciliations.
  • Prepare and maintain accounting documents and records.
  • Assist with financial reporting tasks as assigned.
  • Assist with data entry and maintaining accurate records in the accounting system.
  • Collaborate with other departments to gather necessary information and documentation.
  • Ensure compliance with company policies and procedures.
  • Assist with internal and external audits as required.
  • Assist with accounts receivables and member billings as needed.
  • Provide administrative support to the finance department, including filing and organizing documents.
  • Support the accounting team with ad-hoc projects as needed.

Requirements & Qualifications:

  • Post-secondary education in accounting, or a related field.
  • 1-2 years of experience with bookkeeping, accounting, or a similar skill.
  • Proficiency in Microsoft Excel; ability to learn new software and processes quickly.
  • Strong attention to detail and accuracy.
  • Excellent communication and interpersonal skills.
  • Ability to manage multiple tasks and meet deadlines.
  • Excellent teamwork skills; ability to work towards Club objectives effectively with a diverse team.
