Industrial / Quality Internship

Manufacturing Engineer Intern

at CoolIT Systems

📍 Location Calgary, Alberta
📋 Details Internship · May 2026 – Aug 2027
📅 Posted Apr 03, 2026

About the Role

Assist design processes and fixtures

About CoolIT Systems

Innovation-focused cooling tech leader, strong growth

Full Description

About Coolit Systems Inc. Founded in Calgary, Alberta in 2001, CoolIT Systems is the leading Direct Liquid Cooling technology provider for data centers, supercomputers, and desktop computers. We design and manufacture solutions used by the largest tech companies globally.

Recognized as one of “The Americas’ Fastest Growing Companies 2023” by Financial Times and honored with the Deloitte Fast 50 Clean Technology award in 2022, as well as the Deloitte Fast 500 distinction in both 2021 and 2022, CoolIT's rapid growth is capturing global attention.

As demand for generative AI, high performance computing and cloud solutions rapidly expands, there has never been a more exciting time to join our team.

Role WHAT YOU’LL DO: This will be a internship program starting on May 4, 2026 and ending August 31, 2027. The role involves helping with creating and improving manufacturing processes. The intern will work on designing processes, tools, and fixtures for new products. The successful candidate will also help make current manufacturing processes more efficient by implementing novel Industry 4.0 tools.

Responsibilities

  • Support manufacturing engineering activities associated with defining processes and supporting designing or procuring related equipment and fixtures.
  • Support deployment of new process equipment and assist with commissioning of machinery and roll-out exercises for production.
  • Support the implementation of our Manufacturing Execution System (MES) on the production lines.
  • Document work instructions and assist with the development of PFMEAs as well as Quality Control Plans for manufacturing processes.

  • Assist in the management of continuous improvement projects.

  • Assessment of processes by collection and interpreting measurements/data—participating regularly in continuous improvement activities.

  • Application of lean principles to identify, analyze, and improve production KPIs.

  • Manufacturing-related capital project management support through different stages, i.e., justification, execution, implementation, and rollout.

  • Assist manufacturing/process engineers & production technicians in troubleshooting in the event of a problem.

Please note that the responsibilities include but are not limited to the ones mentioned above.

Who You Are Basic Qualifications:

  • Completion of 3rd year of Bachelors of Science program in Mechanical/Manufacturing Engineer.
  • Should return to school after successful completion of their tenure as an intern at CoolIT Systems.
  • Must be a Canadian Citizen / Permanent Resident of Canada.

Preferred Qualification

  • Understanding of Project Management practices.

Skills

  • Proficiency in Microsoft Office Suite.
  • Demonstrated problem-solving skills with respect to engineering design.
  • Self-motivated and able to work within an interdisciplinary team.
  • Proficiency or passion for designing with SolidWorks, AutoCAD, or equivalent CAD software.
  • Be able to express technical concepts to peers and a non-technical audience through proficient verbal and written communication skills.

WHY COOLIT? We strive to be an employer of choice and as such we believe in rewarding our employees with career and development opportunities that will maintain and strengthen our culture while aligning to our vision and values.

We are a company that is full of vibrant, innovative people who love what we do.

We thank every candidate in advance, however, only those selected for an interview will be contacted.

Successful candidates for this position will be required to undergo background checks including criminal records check and education.
