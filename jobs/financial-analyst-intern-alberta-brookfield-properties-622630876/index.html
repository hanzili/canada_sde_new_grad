Overview Job Description The Financial Analyst Intern (Alberta) supports the Alberta Homes team by delivering high-quality financial analysis and market research to inform strategic, investment, and development decisions. Key Deliverables

  • Conducting market research through site visits to new home sales centres, MLS analysis, consumer data review, and primary research
  • Compiling macro-economic, housing, and demographic data; maintaining and updating internal data warehouses
  • Preparing market data and insights for quarterly market updates, project approvals, stage-gate reviews, executive presentations, and land supply studies
  • Developing and maintaining dashboards and recurring market reports
  • Supporting quarterly partnership reporting with relevant market data
  • Identifying new third-party research sources and integrating updated datasets into internal reporting tools
  • Assisting with financial models and analysis for stage-gate approvals
  • Supporting the evaluation of housing and community development acquisitions
  • Performing financial analysis across products, segments, and builder strategies
  • Preparing financial materials to support investment, planning, and strategic decision-making
