Full Description

Location Calgary - 4906 Richard Road SW

Business At Brookfield Residential, a leading North American land developer and homebuilder for over 65 years, we have had one goal in mind – creating the best places to call home. From the development of award-winning, master-planned communities to the creation of well-designed homes, we are committed to cultivating exceptional life experiences for our customers, partners, employees, and members of the community.

If you're ready to be a part of our team, we encourage you to apply.

Overview Job Description The Financial Analyst Intern (Alberta) supports the Alberta Homes team by delivering high-quality financial analysis and market research to inform strategic, investment, and development decisions. This role contributes to the preparation of financial models, dashboards, reporting packages, and executive presentations, along with ad hoc analysis as required.

The position is primarily focused on Homes & Communities financial analysis , with a secondary focus on market research and insights

Key Deliverables

  • Conducting market research through site visits to new home sales centres, MLS analysis, consumer data review, and primary research
  • Compiling macro-economic, housing, and demographic data; maintaining and updating internal data warehouses
  • Preparing market data and insights for quarterly market updates, project approvals, stage-gate reviews, executive presentations, and land supply studies
  • Developing and maintaining dashboards and recurring market reports
  • Supporting quarterly partnership reporting with relevant market data
  • Identifying new third-party research sources and integrating updated datasets into internal reporting tools
  • Assisting with financial models and analysis for stage-gate approvals
  • Supporting the evaluation of housing and community development acquisitions
  • Performing financial analysis across products, segments, and builder strategies
  • Preparing financial materials to support investment, planning, and strategic decision-making

What You'll Bring

  • Currently enrolled in a Bachelor's or Master's program in a related field (e.g., Real Estate, Construction Management, Business, Architecture, Engineering, Urban Planning).
  • Strong analytical and problem-solving skills.
  • Excellent communication and interpersonal skills.
  • Proficiency in Microsoft Office Suite (Excel, Word, PowerPoint).
  • A keen interest in real estate and development.
  • Self-motivated, eager to learn, and a strong team player.
  • A positive and can-do attitude.
  • Ability to work 40 hours per week Monday – Friday in office.
  • Experience creating and utilizing financial models

What We Offer

  • Hands-on experience in a real estate development environment.
  • Mentorship from industry professionals.
  • Exposure to diverse projects and challenges.
  • Networking opportunities within the real estate industry.
  • Competitive compensation for the internship duration.
  • Potential for future career opportunities within Brookfield Residential.

We are proud to create a diverse environment and are proud to be an equal opportunity employer. We are grateful for your interest in this position, however, only candidates selected for pre-screening will be contacted.
