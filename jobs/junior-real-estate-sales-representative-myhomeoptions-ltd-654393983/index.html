Junior Real Estate Sales Representative

at MyHomeOptions Ltd.

📍 Location Calgary, AB
📋 Details In-person · Full-time · CA$15/hour · Permanent
📅 Posted Mar 31, 2026

About the Role

Knock doors, qualify leads, close acquisitions

About MyHomeOptions Ltd.

Calgary-based home buying company with growth

Full Description

CALLING ALL UNIVERSITY STUDENTS & ASPIRING SALES PROFESSIONALS!!

MyHomeOptions is looking for a Junior Real Estate Acquisitions Representatives (Door-to-Door). This role is for driven individuals who want to learn door-to-door marketing, real estate acquisitions, and sales, or leave a role with limited growth.

MyHomeOptions is a Calgary-based home buying company that helps homeowners sell their properties quickly and stress-free. We work directly with property owners to provide fair, fast purchase options without the traditional listing process.

We are growing rapidly across Calgary and building a team of individuals who want real experience, real income potential, and real growth.

Sales Team Vision

  • We only promote from within. Top performing setters have early opportunity to become acquisitions closers
  • We invest in you with hands-on training in real estate, lead generation, and sales fundamentals
  • Uncapped earning potential — top performers will ALWAYS be compensated based on results

Who This Is For — Do You Identify With Any of the Following?

  • You are young, dissatisfied with low ceilings, and want upward mobility
  • You are interested in real estate, sales, or entrepreneurship
  • You want real reps, coaching, and mastery of fundamentals
  • You value autonomy, performance-based pay, and ownership of outcomes
  • You are competitive and thrive in results-driven environments
  • You have a strong work ethic and want to be rewarded for it

What We Expect From You

  • High work ethic — consistent door-to-door outreach in assigned neighbourhoods
  • Follow our proven outreach and lead generation process
  • Be a team player
  • Be coachable and open to feedback
  • Maintain professionalism while representing the company

What You’ll Be Doing

  • Knock doors in assigned Calgary communities
  • Speak directly with homeowners and introduce MyHomeOptions
  • Explain our home buying process clearly and confidently
  • Identify potential sellers and gather basic property information
  • Distribute flyers and marketing materials
  • Generate and submit qualified leads to the acquisitions team
  • Track daily activity and performance

Compensation

  • Starting from CA$15.00/hour + performance-based bonuses
  • Bonuses for qualified leads and closed deals
  • Flexible scheduling

MUST COMPLETE

OR record on your phone/desktop and upload to Google Drive, then submit your link.

Licence/Certification:

  • Drivers License and a Vehicle (required)

Pay: $15.00 per hour

Work Location: In person
