About the Role

Create content, shoot, edit, post daily, analyze performance

About Thread Collective Inc

Fashion brand; fast-paced ecommerce content team

Full Description

Company Overview

Thread Collective Inc (TCI) is a prominent player in the North American fashion industry, renowned for its innovative apparel and accessories designed for all genders. We are committed to quality, style, and sustainability, serving a diverse range of retail partners from mass-market to high-end boutiques.
Summary

You’ll play a key role in creating scroll-stopping content that showcases our products, drives engagement, and supports our ecommerce growth.
Responsibilities

  • Create and execute social-first content for platforms like TikTok, Instagram Reels, and Stories
  • Shoot photo and video content featuring products, styling, and lifestyle moments
  • Edit short-form videos (cuts, transitions, captions, music, effects) optimized for performance
  • Write captions, product storytelling, and light copy for marketing and social assets
  • Bring fresh, trend-driven ideas weekly (hooks, formats, sounds, concepts)
  • Style outfits and products for shoots in a way that aligns with brand identity
  • Work closely with the marketing and ecommerce teams to support launches, drops, and campaigns
  • Assist in planning and maintaining the content calendar
  • Own day-to-day posting on Instagram, TikTok, and other platforms
  • Engage with the community and respond to messages/comments
  • Monitor content performance and iterate based on what’s working

Who You Are

  • Passionate about fashion, styling, and current trends
  • Deeply familiar with TikTok, Instagram, and what makes content perform
  • Comfortable being behind the camera (and occasionally on camera)
  • Skilled in video editing tools (CapCut, Premiere Pro, Final Cut, etc.)
  • Have a strong visual eye for lighting, framing, and composition
  • Organized and able to manage multiple shoots and deadlines
  • Eager to learn, experiment, and grow in a fast-paced ecommerce environment

Nice to Have

  • Experience working with fashion brands or ecommerce companies
  • Basic photography skills and understanding of lighting setups
  • Familiarity with Shopify or ecommerce workflows
  • Experience with UGC-style content or working with creators/influencers
  • Graphic design skills (Canva, Adobe Suite)
  • Bilingual- English & French

What You’ll Gain

  • Hands-on experience creating content that directly drives sales
  • Exposure to product launches, drops, and full-funnel marketing
  • A fast-moving, creative environment with room to experiment
  • Opportunity to grow into a more senior creative or content role

Job Types: Full-time, Permanent
