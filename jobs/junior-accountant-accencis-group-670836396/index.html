Company Overview

Accencis Group is a fast-growing Canadian franchisor and brand management company, dedicated to accelerating growth, empowering partners, and spearheading sustainable expansion. Since 2020, we’ve built a diverse, multi-branded portfolio spanning over 10 distinct brands and + restaurants across Canada.

Our success stems from a powerful blend of strategic innovation and hands-on execution. We don’t just dream - we deliver. Underpinned by integrity and commitment, our team of industry experts supports brand development from concept to scaling, empowering franchisees and partners with both vision and operational guidance

Our portfolio includes standout names such as The Captain’s Boil, %Arabica, Midori Ramen, Dear Saigon, Glass Kitchen, Bakebe, Osha Mookata, Susu Siam, Pears, The One Fusion, Altitude Golk, AKUA, and more—each delivering unique, immersive customer experiences that set us apart in the hospitality landscape.

Job description

The Junior Accountant will assist in managing the day-to-day financial operations of the organization, including accounts payable, accounts receivable, and general ledger activities. The successful candidate will have a strong foundation in accounting principles and practices, as well as experience working in the food and beverage industry.

Summary of Key Responsibilities

  • Assist with daily accounting tasks, including accounts payable, accounts receivable, general ledger and bank reconciliations.
  • Daily deposits and sales reconciliation for multi-unit network.
  • Support month-end and year-end close processes, general ledger reconciliation, journal entries and monthly accruals.
  • Balance Sheet reconciliations and review.
  • Assist accurate financial records and documentation in accordance with company policies.
  • Assist with tax and audit preparation as needed.
  • Perform ad hoc analysis and other duties as assigned by the finance team.

Qualifications:

  • Degree or diploma in Accounting required
  • Minimum 2 years working experience in accounting preferred
  • Excellent written and verbal communication skills both in English and Mandarin/Cantonese
  • Computer literate, including effective working skills of MS Excel and E-mail
  • Able to work well under pressure and meet assigned deadlines
  • Good organizational, time management and prioritizing skills
  • Detail oriented and organized in all areas of work
  • QuickBooks and ERP experience is an asset
  • Maintains a high level of integrity and works independently
