Job Description Join the Corporate Finance team at The Boyd Group, a Canadian-based, publicly traded company and one of North America's largest operators of collision repair centers. This internship offers hands-on experience in audit support, financial reporting, and corporate accounting projects.

You’ll be based full-time at our Winnipeg head office, working alongside experienced finance professionals on a variety of meaningful projects. From audit preparation to financial reconciliations, you’ll gain exposure to the core functions that support the financial operations of a large, publicly traded company.

This is an excellent opportunity for accounting or finance students who want practical experience and real responsibility in a corporate setting.

About The Boyd Group The Boyd Group is one of the largest operators of collision repair centers in North America, with more than 950 locations and over 13,000 employees across Canada and the U.S. Headquartered in Winnipeg and publicly traded, we provide high-quality auto collision and glass repair under several well-known brands.

Our corporate office supports the operational, financial, and strategic backbone of the business. This internship gives you the opportunity to work with a national company, contribute to real business processes, and gain insight into a growing, publicly traded organization.

Why Join Us? At The Boyd Group, interns are treated like valued team members—not observers. You’ll work on real projects, receive mentorship from experienced professionals, and gain insight into how a large finance team operates.

We Offer

  • Exposure to a public company finance environment
  • A variety of project work, not just repetitive tasks
  • A collaborative, supportive workplace culture
  • Mentorship from senior leaders who want to see you succeed

About Us Gerber Collision & Glass has been WOWing customers with our collision repair services for over 80 years. Please visit gerbercareers.com to learn more about our company.

*What We’re Looking For*

  • Currently enrolled in an undergraduate or graduate program in Accounting or Finance
  • Proficient in Excel and comfortable working with financial data

  • Detail-oriented, organized, and eager to learn

Preferred Qualifications

  • Business students (especially those from the Asper School of Business, University of Manitoba) are encouraged to apply
  • Interest in corporate audit, reporting, or operational finance
