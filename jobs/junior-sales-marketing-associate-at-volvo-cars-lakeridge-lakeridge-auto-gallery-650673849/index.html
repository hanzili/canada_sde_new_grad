Junior Sales & Marketing Associate at Volvo Cars Lakeridge

at Lakeridge Auto Gallery

About the Role

Support sales, create content, manage CRM

About Lakeridge Auto Gallery

Luxury automotive brand; strong training and growth

Full Description

Volvo Cars Lakeridge is seeking a polished, driven Sales & Marketing Associate to support our luxury retail environment while strengthening our digital presence.

This role blends premium client experience with modern marketing execution. The successful candidate will learn the ins and outs of luxury retail sales, create elevated social content and assist in executing refined in-store and community events

You will learn all aspects of the automotive retail environment under the care and guidance of our leadership team. We provide a coaching-style approach along with a refined sales process. This position is ideal for you if you are passionate about helping people, possess excellent communication skill and are eager to learn and grow in a fast-paced environment.

Core Responsibilities:

Sales Support:

  • Deliver professional showroom support and assist with vehicle presentations
  • Manage and respond promptly to internet and phone inquiries
  • Coordinate appointments and follow-up communication
  • Maintain CRM accuracy and lead tracking standards
  • Support vehicle deliveries and ensure presentation excellence

Digital & Social Media Presence:

  • Create refined, brand-aligned social media content (Reels, Stories, short-form video)
  • Capture vehicle highlights, client deliveries, and dealership lifestyle content
  • Monitor digital engagement and route client inquiries appropriately
  • Maintain consistent visual and messaging standards aligned with Volvo brand guidelines

Events & In-Store Promotions:

  • Assist in planning and executing elevated dealership events and launches
  • Coordinate showroom promotions and ensure merchandising standards are maintained
  • Support community partnerships and sponsorship activations
  • Capture professional event content for digital distribution

Benefits:

  • Competitive salary and performance-based bonuses.
  • Comprehensive training and development programs.
  • Opportunities for career advancement and growth within the company.
  • Dynamic and supportive work environment.
  • Company events and group benefits.

Requirements:

  • Willingness to learn, adapt and take on challenges with a positive attitude
  • Professional, well-presented, and confident communicator
  • Ability to work independently and as part of a team.
  • Comfortable on camera and digitally savvy
  • Highly organized with strong attention to detail
  • Self-motivated and performance-driven
  • Valid driver’s license
