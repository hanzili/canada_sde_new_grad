About The Arlen Tool Group

Long-established tooling firm in plastics industry

Full Description

Arlen Tool Co. Ltd. has been Tooling the Plastics Industry for over 50 years! We believe that focusing on quality and the development of our people and processes are the driving forces behind our success.

As we continue to grow, we are actively looking for a Jr. Buyer to fill an open position on our team at Arlen Tool Co. Ltd. located at 3305 Deziel Dr., Windsor, ON.

If you meet the requirements below, we encourage you to apply today!

Essential Functions:

  • Prepare and distribute Request for Quotes (RFQ) to obtain best pricing and delivery commitments per company guidelines.
  • Prepare purchase orders for:
  • Mould components
  • Raw Materials
  • Manifolds
  • Tooling
  • Office Supplies
  • Manage consignment system.
  • Coordinating material certifications.
  • Assist with customs and logistics paperwork.
  • Receiving: Monitor shipments to ensure goods are delivered on time and resolve problems related to undelivered goods.
  • Ability to multi-task and to prioritize by importance.
  • Maintains adherence to company policies, safety/ergonomic standards, and good housekeeping practices.
  • Assists with any other duties/projects given by supervisor(s).

Requirements

  • 2+ years of purchasing experience is required
  • 2+ years of receiving experience, is an asset
  • 2+ years of logistics experience, is an asset

What We Offer:

  • Overtime after 40 hours
  • Comprehensive Benefit Program
  • Profit Sharing Program
  • Pension program
  • Safety Boot benefit
  • Weekly Pay
  • Free Coffee
  • Free lunches every month

Salary: $20.00 - $25.00 - depending on experience
