What’s in it for you

We are a major player, involved in the energy transformation in Canada. Our dynamic work environment promotes autonomy and decision making, and offers great career opportunities.

What sets us apart is our:

  • Annual profit-sharing offered to all in addition to our competitive compensation: rewarding accomplishments is part of our culture.

Among our benefits:
