Ecopia AI (Ecopia) is an industry-leading artificial intelligence (AI) company specializing in extracting land-cover and land-use insights from geospatial big data. Millions of images of the Earth are captured every day by satellites, airplanes, vehicles, and personal devices, but turning that imagery into actionable insights has traditionally been a resource-intensive and time-consuming endeavour. Ecopia converts geospatial imagery into high-precision mapping data at global scales, leveraging AI to empower a wide range of applications such as stormwater management, natural resource management, environmental hazard mitigation, sustainable land use planning, and climate resilience. This data is used by governments, civil engineering firms, insurance carriers, and technology companies around the world.
