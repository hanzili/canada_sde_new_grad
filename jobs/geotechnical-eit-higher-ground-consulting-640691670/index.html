About Higher Ground Consulting

Calgary-based EPCM geotech firm with mentorship

Full Description

Higher Ground Consulting Inc. (HGC) is seeking a Geotechnical Engineer-In-Training to join our Engineering team. Reporting to our Lead Geotech Engineer, the successful candidate will work throughout the lifecycle of projects from initiation to design to construction. The successful applicant will have the opportunity to work in a fast-paced environment, working on various water resources and civil earthworks related projects while receiving training and mentoring from experienced personnel. The position will require the successful applicant to perform field related work for a 3 week on/1 week off schedule to oversee and execute civil engineering related projects.

RESPONSIBILITIES MAY INCLUDE THE FOLLOWING:

  • Geotechnical engineering analysis and design.
  • Working with more senior engineers, plan, coordinate and oversee field geotechnical site investigations.
  • Collaborate with project managers, engineers, and field staff to support project execution.
  • Follow all safety protocols and wear appropriate personal protective equipment (PPE) while on-site.
  • Communicate with contractors and site personnel to ensure project requirements are met.
  • Conduct laboratory testing on soil and rock samples.
  • Conduct material testing (e.g., soil, asphalt, concrete) following standard procedures.
  • Field inspection and quality assurance (QA) / quality control (QC) testing.
  • Report preparation and writing.

NECESSARY QUALIFICATIONS

  • A post-secondary degree in Civil Engineering.
  • Registration as, or immediate ability to register, as an Engineer-In-Training with APEGA.
  • Adaptable to a flexible work schedule and willing to travel to project sites and work out of town as needed
  • Ability to travel to locations for up to three (3) weeks at a time
  • Willingness to work overtime if field programs require
  • Capable of working in various weather conditions, including heat, cold, rain, and snow, while maintaining safety and productivity.
  • Ability to read and interpret engineering drawings and specifications.
  • Strong analytical and problem-solving skills.
  • Ability to work independently and collaboratively in a team.
  • Excellent interpersonal, verbal and written communication, and organizational skills.
  • Must have a valid driver’s licence.
  • Must be legally entitled to work in Canada.
  • Flexible and open to changing priorities and multi-tasking.
  • Creative, strategic, forward-thinking mindset.
  • Adaptable to flexible work schedule when required to meet deadlines.

ADDITIONAL INFORMATION

This position will be based in our head office in downtown Calgary, Alberta. Higher Ground Consulting works on a basis of mutual trust and respect with our employees. We want our people to be regularly in the office to foster communication, culture, and

efficiency, however, we do not mandate specific office time or working schedules. We have a relaxed, flexible and dog-friendly office.

Opportunities for Growth: Higher Ground is a growing consulting company that has thrived on being adaptive to the market and staying open to new strategic growth initiatives. That means when our people come to us with a plan or idea to pursue a client, industry, or geographic region, we want to throw our support behind it. We are always open to supporting our team to grow and explore business opportunities and look to share in the rewards from successful ventures.

Career Development: Our performance management and feedback programs are a core part of how we grow as a company and as professionals. We have an open-door policy and encourage mentorship and active feedback loops. We seek to both provide and collect constructive feedback and collaborate on how to do things better. We are constantly finding areas to improve, both as an individual and as project teams. To support continuous growth, we have training budgets allocated for people to explore ways to grow their technical and soft skills. We understand investing time and resources into our staff is investing in our business.
