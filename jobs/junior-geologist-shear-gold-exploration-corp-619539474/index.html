Full Description

Company Description

Shear Gold Exploration Corp. is a mineral exploration contracting company specializing in uncovering the untapped resources of the Canadian Shield. The company offers a comprehensive range of early exploration services, including soil geochemical surveys, prospecting, geological mapping, remote camp setup, and logistical support. They also manage drill programs and provide on-site and off-site services such as core logging and cutting. As industry leaders, Shear Gold Exploration Corp. is dedicated to delivering high-quality services to exploration projects.

Role Description

This is a full-time on-site role for a Junior Geologist based in Thunder Bay, ON. The Junior Geologist will perform tasks such as assisting with fieldwork, conducting geological mapping, supporting drilling programs, and performing geophysics-related tasks. Daily responsibilities include logging rock cores, collecting and analyzing soil and rock samples, and maintaining detailed field notes. The candidate will also support the setup of remote camps and other logistical operations as required.

Qualifications

  • Skills in logging core and data collection
  • Familiarity with drilling operations in mineral exploration
  • Strong problem-solving abilities and critical thinking skills
  • Ability to work effectively in remote field conditions and outdoor environments
  • Bachelor's degree in Geology, Earth Sciences, or related field
  • Previous experience in mineral exploration is a plus
