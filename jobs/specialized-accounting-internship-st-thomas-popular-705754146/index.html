Specialized Accounting Internship (St. Thomas) *Job Type General Description* Popular offers a well-established Internship Program designed for high-performing sophomore, junior, and senior college students. Selected participants will gain valuable hands-on experience over several weeks, exploring the financial industry from within. Interns will collaborate on an accounting-focused project under the guidance of dedicated mentors, who will support their development and help them maximize their learning experience.

Responsabilities

  • Review loan portfolio data to ensure accuracy and alignment with supporting documentation.
  • Assist in identifying data discrepancies, compliance exceptions, and operational risks.
  • Support covenant compliance reviews and maintain tracking logs of findings.
  • Analyze inactive loan files and recommend appropriate actions for portfolio maintenance.
  • Document observations, prepare reports, and provide process improvement recommendations.
  • Participate in risk review activities and shadow experienced professionals to gain insight into portfolio risk rating methodologies.
  • The information provided here is only a general guide as to the nature of the position and does not constitute an exact description of the goals, tasks, duties and responsibilities of the position.

*Education* Two years of business related college courses in Accounting or Business Administration

Other Qualifications The ideal candidates must:

  • Be pursuing a career in Business Administration, Finance, Accounting or related fields
  • Must be currently a sophomore, junior or senior college student
  • Authorization to work in U.S. and territories
  • Have a GPA of 3.00 or more
  • Strong attention to detail and analytical thinking skills.
  • Proficient in Microsoft Excel and other Microsoft Office applications.
  • Effective written and verbal communication skills.
  • Ability to manage multiple tasks and work independently in a professional environment.

*Region Locations* Banco Popular main office located in St. Thomas
