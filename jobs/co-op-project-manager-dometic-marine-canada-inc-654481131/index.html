About the Role

Assist planning, scheduling, tracking cross-functional projects

About Dometic Marine Canada, Inc.

Global boating solutions; expanding into electrified future

Full Description

Our Marine organization is a trusted provider of innovative solutions for the worldwide boating and boat building industry, and we recently expanded our scope to lead the boating industry into an electrified future. Dometic has won numerous NMMA Innovation Awards over the years, and we are always striving for new ways to make boating easier, safer, and more enjoyable.

We are on a journey of continuous growth – now looking for our next star – a passionate Co-op - Project Manager Student. This position reports to the Program Manager and will work in Vancouver, B.C.

**Please note that this position is open to current students enrolled in a Canadian accredited university and co-op program.

Vancouver:

Dometic Marine’s headquarters are in Vancouver, British Columbia in Canada. The facility develops and manufactures hydraulic, electronic, and combustion-based products.

As Co-op - Project Manager on the Vancouver team, you will support Program Managers, engineering, manufacturing and quality teams throughout the full hardware development lifecycle. You’ll learn how products are built from the ground up while helping keep the team organized, aligned, and moving forward.

General Responsibilities

  • Assist Program Managers with planning, scheduling, and tracking project activities
  • Develop project management tracking tools and dashboards to keep projects on track and bring visibility to status
  • Help coordinate cross functional meetings, and track follow up actions
  • Support risk identification, mitigation tracking, and issues management
  • Contribute to process improvements within the PMO
  • Work with Mechanical, Electrical, Test, Quality, and Manufacturing stakeholders on day to day execution
  • Participate in sprint planning, and team scrums

What do we offer?

You are offered an interesting role in a dynamic, fast paced and global environment with great opportunities to grow, evolve with the company and take on new challenges.

  • Statutory Holidays
  • Free on-site parking
  • Company events
  • Opportunities to make an impact

Skills & Requirements

The ideal candidate for this role will have acquired the following qualifications and experiences:

  • Currently enrolled in a Bachelor’s program in Engineering, Business or a related field
  • Interest in hardware product development and technology
  • Strong organizational skills with attention to detail
  • Comfortable communicating with technical teams
  • Ability to manage multiple priorities and meet deadlines
  • Working knowledge of Excel, PowerPoint, and project tools
  • Enthusiasm, curiosity, and willingness to ask questions and learn

To be successful in this role, we believe that you possess the following skills, competencies & characteristics:

  • Experience with technical environments (labs, design teams, or engineering courses)
  • Exposure to bill of materials (BOMs) or product lifecycle concepts
  • Basic understanding of mechanical/electrical systems

Dometic’s Core Values

To thrive and succeed in this role, you understand the importance of our core values – Together We build our future, We play to win, We embrace change and We walk the talk; these values reflect the heart and soul of Dometic and they define what it takes to work here and how we do things.
