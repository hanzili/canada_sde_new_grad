About the Role

About HORIZONES

International HR internship with tech-focused program

Full Description

Horizones Consulting has a CSR online arm, the Global Institute For Technology Competitiveness (GITCOMP) that is a brand focused on promoting the creation and growth of businesses through the reduction of the digital and technological gap. Students interested in completing their professional internships or social service remotely while gaining professional experience in the technology field are invited to become part of our team, where you will collaborate on international initiatives such as:

  • Tech Nights
  • Tech Value Awards
  • Global Digital Week

Working alongside some of the most recognized technology brands such as Google, HP, Siemens, Nvidia, Lenovo, among others.

https://gitcomp.org/en/gdw-2025-en/

Main Responsibilities:

As an HR Intern at GITCOMP, you will support various tasks within the Human Resources department, including recruitment processes, candidate selection, onboarding, and personnel administration. You will also collaborate in organizing employee wellness activities and managing HR-related documentation. This is a temporary hybrid position located in the Greater Mexico City Metropolitan Area, with the possibility of partially working from home.

Requirements:

  • Active university student (Social Service or Professional Internship)
  • Interest in technology, innovation, or digital business areas
  • Availability from Monday to Friday (flexible schedule)
  • Access to a computer and stable internet connection
  • Conversational Spanish is highly valued
  • Previous experience is not required

We Offer:

  • 100% remote work modality
  • Opportunity to develop in an international environment
  • Validation of Social Service and/or Professional Internships
  • Continuous training
  • Participation in real-world projects
  • Professional growth and development opportunities within the organization
  • Networking opportunities with professionals from the technology sector

Next Young Talent Program:

The Next Young Talent Program is an international program focused on developing young talent through participation in real projects, collaborative work, and strategic challenges in partnership with embassies, business chambers, associations, and international organizations such as UNICEF, the UN, OECD, and the World Bank, among others, within a global and fully remote environment.

Likewise, this program allows participants to gain professional experience, and they may request a recommendation letter highlighting the projects, brands, and organizations they collaborated with, helping strengthen their professional projection.

https://gitcomp.org/en/next-young-talent-eng/
