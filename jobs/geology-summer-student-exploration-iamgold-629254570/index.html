About the role:

We're actively searching for a Geology Summer Student to join our Exploration Geological Team this Summer. This student will assist the team by completing a variety of field and technical tasks to ensure the successful execution of exploration projects. This position requires outdoor work on a regular basis in a variety of weather conditions and the student must carry out regular hazard reviews and conduct work in accord with our safety practices. The student will learn important mineral exploration techniques in an Archean geological environment within the Abitibi Sub province and will benefit from a team work environment.

Responsibilities:

  • Provide geological field support to the exploration geology team and exploration programs in the field, in the core shack or other locations. Duties will include (but are not limited to) geological mapping, prospecting, and geochemical sampling.
  • Be prepared for traversing at the start of each day and assist the supervising Geologist in field preparations and planning of the day.
  • Ensure FLRA and JHA are completed as well as vehicle circle checks
  • Assist as required in field sampling of outcrops including chip ‚Äì channel sampling with rock hammers and portable rock saws. Complete geological descriptions of samples when needed.
  • Review rock saws and related equipment to ensure it is in good working order the day before the planned channel sampling. Make sure that previous training on the equipment is completed before using. Report any equipment issues to the supervising Geologist.
  • Make sure that all the correct PPE are available and are being used. Take all precautions to work safely and to protect the environment. Make sure spill kits are available for use.
  • Assist in aspects of the drill program when requested including drill core geotechnical measurements, S.G. measurements, and moving of drill core
  • Participation in core shack technician activities, to assist geologist and technician.
  • Participation in field activities related to drilling to assist the geologists including drill hole spotting, drill site inspections, drill trail inspections, and other related tasks.
  • May be asked to participate in other projects of the company
