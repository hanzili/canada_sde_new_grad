About the Role

About Tielman Group

Co-op marketing team in packaging industry

Full Description

Title: Digital Marketing Specialist, Tielman North America Ltd.

Reports to: Marketing Manager, Tielman Group

Marketing Team: Marketing Manager, Tielman Group

Director of Innovation, Tielman North America Ltd.

Digital Marketing Specialist, Tielman North America Ltd.

Sales Coordinator, Tielman North America Ltd.

Employment details:

  • Location: On-site at 180 Middlefield Road, Scarborough, Ontario, 5 days per week
  • Hours: 35 hours per week, Monday through Friday (typical business hours)
  • Wage and Time Period: $20 and 12 Week Co-op
  • Proposed start date: June 1, possibility for earlier start
  • Tielman North America is committed to providing an accessible and inclusive workplace. Accommodations are available throughout the recruitment process and during employment for candidates with disabilities, upon request.

Tasks and Responsibilities for Tielman North America

Content Creation

o  Assist in creating engaging content for our website, social media platforms, and email campaigns.

o  Develop visual and written content to highlight our products, their uses, and benefits

o  Create and manage blogs for our website about engaging customer focused topics

o  Help create and manage a content calendar to ensure consistent posting and engagement.

Social Media Management

o  Assist in managing and growing our social media presence on platforms such as Instagram, Facebook, LinkedIn, and Twitter

o  Schedule and publish posts, stories, and updates.

o  Engage with followers, respond to comments and messages, and participate in relevant conversations.

Email Marketing

o  Help design and execute email marketing campaigns to our B2B clients and prospects

o  Segment email lists and tailor messages to different audiences.

o  Monitor and report on email campaign performance.

SEO and Website Management:

o  Assist in optimizing website content for search engines (SEO).

o  Help update and maintain product pages, blog posts, and other website content.

o  Use analytics tools to track website traffic and performance.

Market Research:

o  Conduct research on industry trends, competitor activities, and potential marketing opportunities.

o  Assist in identifying target audiences and developing strategies to reach them effectively.

Analytics and Reporting:

o  Monitor and analyze the performance of digital marketing campaigns using tools like Google Analytics and social media insights.

o  Prepare reports summarizing key metrics and provide recommendations for improvement.

Communication:

o  Provide weekly updates to the Marketing Manager and Director of Innovation

o  Review goals, activities and results with the team

Job Qualifications

Education

  • Currently enrolled in or recently completed a post-secondary program in marketing, communications, business, digital media, journalism, or a related field
  • Secondary school completion required as a minimum

Required skills and experience

  • Strong written and verbal communication skills in English, with attention to grammar, tone, and brand voice
  • Working knowledge of major social media platforms (Instagram, Facebook, LinkedIn, X/Twitter) including how content performs differently on each
  • Comfort with basic content creation tools (Canva, Adobe Express, or similar) for producing graphics, short videos, and social posts
  • Familiarity with Microsoft Office or Google Workspace, particularly spreadsheets for tracking campaign performance
  • Strong organizational skills and ability to manage multiple projects, deadlines, and a content calendar simultaneously
  • A curious, self-directed approach to learning new tools and platforms

Preferred (assets, not required)

  • Hands-on experience with one or more of: Google Analytics, Meta Business Suite, LinkedIn Campaign Manager, Mailchimp, HubSpot, or similar email marketing platforms
  • Familiarity with SEO concepts and basic website content management (WordPress or similar)
  • Experience creating B2B content or content for a manufacturing, food, or packaging-adjacent industry
  • Basic photography or short-form video editing skills
  • Interest in sustainability, environmentally friendly products, or food packaging innovation

Personal attributes

  • Collaborative team player who works well across departments
  • Comfortable taking direction and giving updates in weekly reviews with the Marketing Manager and Director of Innovation
  • Detail-oriented, with pride in producing polished, professional work
  • Open to feedback and motivated to develop skills throughout the placement

This position is funded through the Canada Summer Jobs program; applicants must meet all program eligibility requirements: https://www.canada.ca/en/employment-social-development/services/funding/canada-summer-jobs/youth/plan.html

Commitment to inclusive hiring:

In accordance with our Canada Summer Jobs commitments, Tielman North America actively encourages applications from racialized youth and Black youth, and from candidates facing barriers to employment. We engage with community partners and employment organizations to broaden access to this opportunity. All applications will be considered on the basis of merit, skills, and fit for the role.

Expectations: All efforts will be made to provide mentoring in the areas of skills development and career related training by the Marketing Manager and the Director of Innovation. This role will need to complete training at the beginning and an evaluation of their work term at Tielman at the end.

To apply for this job, please send your resume and cover letter to TNAmail@tielman.com by May 11, 2026.
