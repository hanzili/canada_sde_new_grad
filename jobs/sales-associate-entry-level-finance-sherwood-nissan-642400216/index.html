About Sherwood Nissan

Full Description

Job description

If you're a Go-Getter and want a chance to prove yourself, we have a position available that you're gonna love!

Whether you're brand new to auto sales or have some experience in the past, we provide you with a whole new angle to your career and give you all the tools, knowledge, and resources to make you successful.

Your role is to assist and support online shoppers with their vehicle purchase needs. You will be managing a portfolio of customers that rely on you to be their agent as you work with your team and dealer partners to get them the vehicle of their dreams.

We welcome ambitious individuals willing to learn in a fast and fun-paced environment.

You must be...

  • Self-motivated and driven to achieve high levels of success
  • Able to problem solve and build your skills in the sales & finance role
  • Be a team player
  • An excellent communicator

Role & Responsibilities

  • Submit & work approvals effectively and in a timely manner
  • Work close and effectively with a partner to capitalize on every opportunity
  • Source vehicles and coordinate deliveries alongside your team and our dealer partners
  • Prepare & execute loan agreements through lenders
  • Manage delinquencies and funding in a high priority manner

Our Responsibilities To You

  • Provide you with the tools, knowledge, and resources to be successful
  • Provide you with as many leads that you can effectively and efficiently manage and convert
  • Provide you with a positive work environment and a great work culture that you will love

If you're interested in becoming a part of the team, please contact us right away. We have limited positions available

Job Type: Full-time

Pay: $65,000.00-$135,000.00 per year

Benefits:

  • Dental care
  • Paid time off
  • Vision care

Experience:

  • automotive sales: 1 year (preferred)

Licence/Certification:

  • Amvic License (preferred)

Work Location: In person
