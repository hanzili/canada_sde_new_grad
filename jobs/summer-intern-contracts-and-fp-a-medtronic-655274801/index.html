Summer Intern - Contracts and FP&A

at Medtronic

📍 Location Brampton, Ontario
📋 Details Temporary · Summer · Not disclosed · Internship
📅 Posted Apr 01, 2026

About the Role

Support FP&A, contract data, and Power BI dashboards

About Medtronic

Global medical technology leader with strong training

Full Description

At Medtronic you can begin a life-long career of exploration and innovation, while helping champion healthcare access and equity for all. You’ll lead with purpose, breaking down barriers to innovation in a more connected, compassionate world.

A Day in the Life Responsibilities may include the following and other duties may be assigned.

  • This is a temporary position.
  • Responsible for performing and supporting research for teams operating within a service group, product group and/ or project group.
  • Assists with the development, monitoring, coordination and implementation of non-technical projects as assigned.
  • Provides analysis, reporting and internal communication services with team members.
  • Develops knowledge of industry and organizational processes.

Additional Responsibilities:

  • Support the review, organization, and standardization of the Contract Library to improve structure, consistency, and accessibility
  • Assist with categorizing and filing executed contracts, amendments, and supporting documents in line with defined naming and folder standards
  • Partner with the Contracts & Pricing team to help prepare contract data for digital tools that extract key terms and enable data‑driven analysis
  • Contribute to process improvements that enhance contract management efficiency, data quality, and readiness for future digital solutions
  • Support the FP&A team by hands‑on development of digital and automated financial reporting and analytics through hands‑on work with Power BI
  • Assist in transitioning existing Excel‑based reports into interactive Power BI dashboards to improve automation, consistency, and scalability
  • Learn how financial insights support business planning and decision‑making by working closely with FP&A and Commercial stakeholders
  • Contribute to digital transformation initiatives that deliver clearer, faster and more actionable insights for leadership
