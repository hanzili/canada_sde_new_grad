About the Role

Analyze processes, map workflows, deliver dashboards

About CIBC

Major Canadian bank; extensive retail lending reach

Full Description

We’re building a relationship-oriented bank for the modern world. We need talented, passionate professionals who are dedicated to doing what’s right for our clients.

At CIBC, we embrace your strengths and your ambitions, so you are empowered at work. Our team members have what they need to make a meaningful impact and are truly valued for who they are and what they contribute.

To learn more about CIBC, please visit CIBC.com

What You’ll Be Doing Reporting to the Senior Director, Operational Excellence, the Product Manager, summer student will support the RESL (Real Estate Secured Lending) Operational Excellence team. This role focuses on Process mapping, process improvements, and updating frontline user procedures. The student will help ensure distribution needs are considered in RESL projects and will work with various internal partners to improve how RESL products are delivered.

At CIBC we enable the work environment most optimal for you to thrive in your role you’ll have the flexibility to manage your work activities within a hybrid work arrangement where you’ll spend 2-3 days per week on-site, while other days will be remote. How You’ll Succeed Work with internal partners – This will include distribution channels, retail operations, adjudication, business controls, legal, and compliance, to support RESL initiatives.

Analyzing data - Conducting root cause analysis to identify opportunities for process improvement across the end-to-end RESL process. You will also support the development and regular review of dashboards and performance reports (KPIs, trends, variances) to proactively identify areas of opportunity.

Strategy Development - Help map current and future state processes and identify areas for operational improvement.

Process Improvement - Work with the distribution network to update frontline procedures and standard operating documents as needed.

Who You Are

  • An undergraduate, MBA, or Masters level student graduating in December 2026 and beyond.
  • Strong attention to detail and analytical skills.
  • Ability to manage multiple tasks and prioritize effectively in a fast-paced environment.
  • Creative problem-solving skills and willingness to suggest improvements.
  • Good communication and teamwork skills for working with different departments.
  • Willingness to learn about mortgages, lending, and process improvement.
  • Comfortable working with some ambiguity and learning new things.
  • You're passionate about people. You find meaning in relationships and surround yourself with a diverse network of partners. You build trust through respect and authenticity.
  • You look beyond the moment. You know what you do will make a difference today and tomorrow. You look for new opportunities to define what's possible.
  • Values matter to you. You bring your real self to work and you live our values – trust, teamwork, and accountability.

  • Our spaces and technological toolkit will make it simple to bring together great minds to create innovative solutions that make a difference for our clients.
