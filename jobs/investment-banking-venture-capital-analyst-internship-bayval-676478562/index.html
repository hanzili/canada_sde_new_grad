Full Description

About Bayval

Bayval is a Toronto-based private markets platform focused on small and medium-sized enterprises (SMEs) across Canada. We operate at the intersection of investment banking, private equity, and venture capital, providing deal sourcing, capital advisory, and transaction execution.

Our approach combines:

• Venture-style sourcing to originate differentiated opportunities

• Private equity-style underwriting focused on fundamentals and scalability

• Investment banking execution across M&A and capital raising

We work closely with founder-led businesses, investors, and strategic partners to help companies scale and stay in Canada, addressing the domestic growth capital gap. As a lean and entrepreneurial team, we offer direct exposure to live opportunities and meaningful responsibility early on.

Role Overview

We are seeking a highly motivated summer analyst to support deal sourcing, investment evaluation, and transaction execution across M&A, growth equity, and venture opportunities. This role provides hands-on experience across the full lifecycle: Origination → Investment Analysis → Advisory → Execution. It is ideal for candidates interested in investment banking, private equity, venture capital, or entrepreneurship.

Application Process

Applications are reviewed on a rolling basis. Please submit your resume as a single PDF file. Selected candidates will be contacted via email for further steps in the interview process.

Key Responsibilities

Deal Sourcing and Market Mapping

• Identify and research Canadian SMEs and growth-stage companies across target sectors

• Support outreach to founders, operators, intermediaries, and ecosystem participants

• Build and maintain deal pipeline tracking and market maps

• Assist in evaluating inbound opportunities

Financial Analysis and Investment Evaluation

• Analyze company financials, KPIs, and operating drivers

• Support financial modeling and valuation, including:

  • Comparable companies
  • Precedent transactions
  • Scenario and sensitivity analysis

• Evaluate unit economics, growth potential, and capital efficiency

Advisory and Transaction Execution

• Support M&A and capital raising processes

• Assist in preparing client materials:

  • Pitch decks
  • Information memorandums (CIMs)
  • Investor presentations

• Participate in due diligence and data room preparation

• Coordinate with clients, investors, and external advisors

Venture and Strategy Support

• Conduct industry and thematic research (e.g., AI, healthcare, tech-enabled sectors)

• Support development of investment theses and market perspectives

• Assist in evaluating product-market fit and competitive positioning

Who We’re Looking For

• Undergraduate student in business, finance, economics, engineering, or related field

• Strong interest in investment banking, private equity, and/or venture capital

• Solid understanding of financial statements and valuation fundamentals

• Strong analytical, quantitative, and problem-solving skills

• Proficiency in Excel and PowerPoint

• Strong written and verbal communication skills

• High attention to detail and ability to manage multiple work streams

• Self-starter mindset with comfort in a fast-paced, entrepreneurial environment

• Prior experience in investment banking, consulting, accounting, venture capital, or corporate finance is an asset, but not required

What You’ll Gain

• Direct exposure to M&A, private equity, and venture capital transactions

• Hands-on experience across deal sourcing, underwriting, and execution

• Involvement in both startup and traditional SME opportunities

• Insight into the Canadian private markets and venture ecosystem

• Mentorship from experienced professionals in investing and advisory

• Strong foundation for careers in investment banking, private equity, venture capital, or startups

Compensation

This role is structured as a stipend-based internship, providing baseline compensation.

Additional performance-based bonuses may be offered based on:

• Contributions to live transactions

• Execution support and project impact

• Participation in business development and sourcing initiatives

Deal sourcing exposure is optional and intended as a learning opportunity. Compensation is not solely commission-based.
