Full Description

E-Commerce & Digital Marketing Coordinator (Co-op / Internship)
The Wellness Group – Aurora, ON

The Wellness Group is a multidisciplinary clinic located in the heart of Aurora (LA Fitness Plaza), offering Chiropractic Care, Physiotherapy, Registered Massage Therapy, Chiropody, Acupuncture, and more. As we continue to grow, we are expanding into e-commerce and digital marketing to better serve our patients and community.

We are currently seeking a motivated and tech-savvy E-Commerce & Digital Marketing Coordinator (Co-op/Internship) to lead the development and management of our online store and digital presence.

Key Responsibilities

E-Commerce Development & Management

  • Set up and manage an online store integrated with our website (Wix)
  • Coordinate and manage product listings through platforms such as Fullscript
  • Source and upload products including:
  • Vitamins and supplements
  • Wellness products (e.g., orthopedic pillows, compression socks, rehab tools)
  • Optimize product pages for conversions (descriptions, images, pricing, promotions)

Digital Marketing & Social Media

  • Plan, create, and manage content for Instagram and Facebook
  • Develop campaigns to promote both e-commerce products and in-clinic services
  • Create engaging posts, reels, and stories to increase brand awareness and drive sales
  • Monitor engagement, trends, and performance metrics

AI & Creative Advertising

  • Utilize AI tools to design visual ads, marketing campaigns, and branded content
  • Create high-converting graphics and promotional materials
  • Assist in developing creative strategies to attract new patients and increase online sales

Operations & Administrative Support

  • Assist with basic bookkeeping and financial tracking using Sage Accounting
  • Help monitor online orders, inventory, and sales performance
  • Support internal initiatives to increase patient visits and cross-referrals

Qualifications

  • Currently enrolled in a Business, Marketing, or related program (preferred)
  • Strong interest in e-commerce, digital marketing, and health/wellness
  • Familiarity with social media platforms (Instagram, Facebook)
  • Experience with Canva or similar design tools (AI tools an asset)
  • Knowledge of Wix, Fullscript, or basic e-commerce platforms is an asset
  • Basic understanding of bookkeeping or willingness to learn (Sage experience an asset)
  • Self-motivated, creative, and detail-oriented
  • Strong communication and organizational skills

What You’ll Gain

  • Hands-on experience launching and managing an e-commerce business
  • Real-world exposure to digital marketing strategy and execution
  • Opportunity to work with a growing healthcare brand
  • Experience integrating AI tools into business and marketing workflows
  • Development of business, analytics, and entrepreneurial skills

Position Details

  • Term: May – August 2026
  • Location: Aurora, ON (in-clinic)

If you’re looking to gain practical, hands-on experience in building a business, growing a brand, and working in the health and wellness space, we would love to hear from you.

Please apply with your resume and a brief description of your interest in the role.
