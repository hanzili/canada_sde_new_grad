About Hi-Kalibre Equipment Ltd.

Full Description

1 Job Description

Hi‑Kalibre Equipment Ltd. is seeking a Marketing Analyst (Summer Student) to support marketing, business development, and analytical initiatives during the summer term. This role is designed to provide a post‑secondary student with hands‑on experience applying marketing and business analytics concepts within a professional manufacturing environment.

The position combines practical marketing execution with data‑driven analysis, including the responsible use of AI‑enabled tools to support research, insight generation, and reporting. The successful candidate will work closely with internal stakeholders and gain exposure to real‑world decision‑making in a growing industrial business.

2 Primary Job Responsibilities:

2.1 Marketing & Communications Support

· Assist with the preparation and scheduling of social media content, primarily for LinkedIn and company communications

· Support ongoing updates and content management for the company website

· Assist in the development of marketing collateral, including brochures, presentations, and capability statements

· Ensure consistency with Hi‑Kalibre’s brand standards and messaging

2.2 Marketing Analysis & Reporting

· Collect, analyze, and summarize marketing performance data, including website traffic and engagement metrics

· Assist with market and competitor research to support business development initiatives

· Prepare summary reports and basic dashboards for internal review and management use

· Identify trends, patterns, and insights to support data‑driven marketing decisions

2.3 AI‑Enabled Analysis & Process Support

· Use company‑approved AI tools to assist with research, analysis, and content drafting

· Support the development of repeatable marketing and reporting templates

· Document processes and assist with continuous improvement initiatives

· All AI‑assisted outputs will be reviewed and finalized by management

3 Learning & Development Objectives

· B2B and industrial marketing practices

· Marketing analytics and performance measurement

· Responsible and supervised use of SalesForce (CRM) and AI tools for business analytics

· Professional communication and reporting

· Working within a sales and operations driven manufacturing organization

4 Qualification Requirements

· Currently enrolled in a post-secondary degree program in either Marketing, Business Administration, Business Analytics or Communications.

· Strong communication and interpersonal skills

· Keen attention to detail.

· Comfortable working with Microsoft Office products and CRM software.

· Interest in digital marketing, analytics and emerging tools such as AI.

· Organized, detail-oriented, and able to manage multiple tasks in a professional environment.

Please provide cover letter with resume, as well as any transcripts that you feel may be useful.
