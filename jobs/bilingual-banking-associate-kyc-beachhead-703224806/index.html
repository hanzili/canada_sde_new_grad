Full Description

Are you bilingual in French and English? Apply Now!

Working with one of our top financial clients, this role calls for a Bilingual Banking Associate – KYC. This position offers an excellent opportunity to provide operational support within a leading financial institution, working in a dynamic team environment with potential for contract extension and valuable industry experience.

Pay rate range (CAD): $17.60/hr – $30.97/hr Responsibilities

  • Perform operational transactions accurately and efficiently according to established procedures and SLAs
  • Verify customer information and conduct KYC checks via inbound and outbound calls
  • Manage back-office administrative tasks such as data entry, documentation review, and record maintenance
  • Provide customers with information about different wire transfer options, confirming details without processing transactions
  • Collaborate with internal partners and escalate issues as needed to ensure service quality
  • Support incoming transactions, validating information, and assisting with adjustments within authorized limits
  • Adhere to compliance standards, including Anti-Money Laundering and other regulatory requirements
  • Identify opportunities for process improvements and contribute to reporting on critical operations projects
  • Maintain strict confidentiality in handling customer and internal data
  • Participate in team meetings and training sessions to stay informed on policies and product updates

Desired Skill-Set

  • Fully bilingual in English and French, both written and verbal
  • Minimum of 4 years' experience in the banking or financial services sector
  • Proven experience conducting KYC procedures and verifying customer data
  • Call center background handling inbound and outbound customer interactions
  • Proficiency in Microsoft Office 365, especially Excel, Outlook, and Word
  • Strong organizational and time-management skills
  • Ability to work independently and collaboratively within a team environment
  • Excellent attention to detail and high accuracy in work
  • Good understanding of financial products such as wire transfers, foreign exchange, and cash products
  • Ability to meet strict deadlines and handle high work volumes

Nice to Have

  • Experience with bank wire transfers and other financial transactions
  • Familiarity with AI tools such as Microsoft Copilot or similar platforms
  • Knowledge of global and WSS platforms in banking operations
