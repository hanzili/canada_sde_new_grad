Full Description

JOB DESCRIPTION SUMMARY

DMA Consulting & Engineering is looking for Junior Civil Engineer/EIT with experience in residential construction, building envelope restoration, as well as experience with wood framing, concrete, and aluminum design.

In collaboration with the Principal Engineer, the Junior Engineer/E.I.T. will be responsible for ensuring project design drawings are produced efficiently and in a timely manner to meet project deadlines. The Junior Engineer/E.I.T. will also have the opportunity to conduct site visits and prepare field reports. Depending on the individual’s skills and career aspirations, the Junior Engineer/E.I.T. may have the opportunity to be involved in design calculations and structural design, under the supervision of the Principal Engineer.

This position is excellent for an individual who is looking to broaden their experience and skills in residential construction. This individual must be willing to learn beyond their current skillset.

POSITION TYPE: Full time (a 3 months probationary period will apply)

LOCATION: In office. Travel to various project work sites within the Lower Mainland will be required.

QUALIFICATIONS:

  • Must currently reside in the Lower Mainland. International, or out-of-town applicants will be rejected
  • Bachelor degree in Civil or Structural Engineering
  • Registered with EGBC as a P.Eng or E.I.T
  • 2-3 years experience in residential construction or building envelope
  • Previous experience creating AutoCAD drawings (there will be a practical assignment as part of the interview process)
  • Experience creating modular blocks and tool pallets would be an asset
  • Class 5 driver’s licence and ability to use personal vehicle for work related travel within the Lower Mainland

DUTIES & RESPONSIBILITIES:

  • Set up CAD drawings with generic blocks and standards
  • Prepare project drawings for temporary structures & residential construction projects
  • Conduct project site visits and prepare field reports
  • In collaboration with the Principal Engineer, contribute to higher complexity residential renovations projects
  • Other job duties, as they may arise

PERSONAL QUALIFICATIONS:

  • A natural curiosity and desire to learn
  • Critical thinking skills and willingness to constantly improve
  • Flexibility to adapt to the changing environment of a growing company
  • Motivated to meet tight deadlines, whether working alone or with a team
  • Ability to prioritize tasks when assigned multiple projects
  • Ability to take initiative and bring up issues to the Principal Engineer for quick resolution, to ensure deadlines are met

ABOUT US:

We are a Structural and Building Restoration Engineering & Consulting firm in New Westminster, who is committed to quality in our work, collaboration with industry peers, and continuous learning.

To learn more about us, please visit our website at www.dmaprojects.ca
