Core Logger / Junior Geologist

at Brixton Metals Corp

📍 Location Toronto Pearson International Airport, ON
📋 Details Full-time/Seasonal · Drill program duration · Not disclosed · Seasonal
📅 Posted Mar 17, 2026

About the Role

Core logging, sampling, QAQC, data integration

About Brixton Metals Corp

Junior mining company, robust field training

Full Description

Work location: Haileybury / Cobalt, ON and surrounding area

JOB DESCRIPTION

Brixton Metals Corporation is a publicly traded (TSXV: BBB) (OCTQB: BBBXF), Canadian mineral exploration company focused on the advancement of its copper-gold-silver exploration projects. Brixton Metals is hiring for an extended drill program at its Langis-HudBay Project (silver-nickel).

We are currently seeking a Core Logger/Jr. Geologist starting in early May 2026 and will continue for the duration of the drill program.

RESPONSIBILITIES

Duties may include, but are not limited to, the following:

  • All aspects of core logging (including mineralization, alteration lithology) procedures, moving core boxes, analyzing and interpreting drill core, collecting photographs and following sampling protocols;
  • Preparing sample submissions to assay laboratories following established QAQC procedures;
  • Preparing quick logs of core;
  • Participating in daily geologist team meetings to discuss properties of the drill core;
  • Downloading and integrating logging data into database;
  • Experienced core loggers may assist with training and mentoring others, as required;
  • Additional work in and around the work site as required; and
  • Demonstrate awareness and commitment to health, safety, community and environmental protection in keeping with Brixton Metals’ goals and objectives.

QUALIFICATIONS

  • Legally entitled to work in Canada at the time of application.
  • Minimum B. Sc. Degree in Geological Sciences or equivalent.
  • 1-3 years of relevant work experience in a similar role.
  • Valid driver’s license.
  • Structurally controlled precious metals logging experience is preferred.
  • Reliable with a strong work ethic.
  • Cooperative team player.

ADDITIONAL INFORMATION

  • Reporting to various field work locations (Cobalt, Ontario & surrounding areas).
  • Must be able to lift/move up to 50lbs throughout a work shift.
  • Ability to work in areas of extreme and difficult weather conditions.
  • Personal outdoor work gear is required (i.e. warm clothing, steel-toed boots, rain gear, gloves/mittens, etc.) and is not provided by the Company. PPE (i.e. safety glasses, respirators and high-vis) is provided by the Company.
  • Rotational schedule, typically 2-weeks on/2-weeks off.

Job Types: Full-time, Seasonal, Freelance

Application question(s):

  • Are you located in Ontario?
  • How many years of experience do you have in core logging?

Work Location: In person
