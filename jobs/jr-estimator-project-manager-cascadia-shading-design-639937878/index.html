Jr. Estimator / Project Manager

at Cascadia Shading Design

📍 Location Vancouver, British Columbia
📋 Details Full-time · Permanent · $65,000-$75,000 CAD · N/A
📅 Posted Mar 09, 2026

About the Role

Perform high-accuracy take-offs; support PM

About Cascadia Shading Design

Architectural shading firm; data-driven project support

Full Description

Location: Vancouver, BC (Office-based)

Salary Range: $65,000 – $75,000

Reports To: Lead Estimator and PM

Who We Are

Cascadia Shading Design Ltd. is a premier Architectural Shading Products company. Our work is as diverse as the BC landscape—we supply and install manual and motorized shading systems for everything from private homeowners and art galleries to hospitals and entire commercial towers. We pride ourselves on a very positive company culture and a tight-knit team with a shared goal: providing the best experience possible for our clients and having fun while we do it.

The 2026 Mission

Our mission is to enhance the beauty, health, and happiness of every space through effective shading solutions and Service that foster lifelong customer loyalty. Our motto this year is to " Create Space and Fun to Power Momentum ." We’ve realized that to support our Lead Estimator and Project Management teams effectively, we need a technical "Turbocharger" who brings energy to the "Engine Room" and ensures our data is surgical so the team has the breathing room to do their best work.

The Role: Architectural Apprentice & Technical "Turbocharger"

You don't just read blueprints—you dissect them. While others see a standard window schedule, you see the missing power pockets, the complex mounting depths, and the 1/8" discrepancy that could stall a project. You aren't just an assistant; you are the high-level technical support for our Lead Estimator and Project Manager. Your goal is to be a Force Multiplier: by handling the high-velocity data and technical take-offs, you empower the rest of the team to focus on high-level strategy, effectively recovering the "space and fun" that defines our culture. Looking for someone who wants to learn and grow within the company for years to come.

  1. Estimating Excellence & Technical Analysis (50%)

You ensure our technical data aligns perfectly with the architectural vision.

  • The Deep Take-Off: Perform high-accuracy quantity counts and technical measurements from complex architectural sets.
  • Estimating Foundations: Build the baseline "shells" of our bids, ensuring every line item and motorization requirement is captured accurately before the final pricing strategy is applied.

  • Sample Coordination: Manage the lifecycle of fabric and hardware samples during the Product Specification phase to ensure Architects and Designers are fully supported.

  • Project Precision & Documentation (50%)

You bridge the gap between an awarded bid and a perfect installation through rigorous document control.

  • Surgical Order Entry: Translate awarded bids into precise Purchase Orders
  • AI-Enhanced Analysis: Utilize custom Gemini AI tools to scan 100+ page specs for hidden requirements and draft technical RFIs to prevent site errors.
  • Close outs: Assemble final close-out documentation—including warranties and service agreements—that fosters lifelong customer loyalty.

Who You Are

  • The Blueprint Specialist: You have a high "Technical IQ" and find satisfaction in the details others miss.
  • Precise: You understand that "close enough" is a friction point we can't afford in high-end construction and order entry.
  • Tech-Native: You lean into modern tools like Monday.com and AI to find efficiencies and automate repetitive tasks.
  • Service-Minded: You realize that a perfect estimate is the first step in building happiness for the end user.

This role is NOT for you if:

  • You find 100-page architectural specs or complex spreadsheets boring or frustrating.
  • You have a "close enough" attitude toward measurements and data entry.
  • You are "tech-resistant" and prefer manual logs over integrated project management platforms.

Benefits & Environment

  • Culture: A relaxed, positive environment with a casual dress code.
  • Health: Extended health and dental care options available after a 3-month trial period.
  • Space to Think: New building and office space with Gym, Shower, Ping pong, Golf SIM, in a great neighborhood to ensure our team is relaxed so they can stay sharp and effective
  • Tools: Office runs on Apple Mac OS, Google Business Suite, and Custom Monday Project Management Software. We are also making a big push into Gemini AI tools to leverage team capabilities.

Schedule

  • Monday to Friday, Day Shift.

How to Apply (Please follow closely):

To show us you have the attention to detail required for this technical role, please:

  1. Use the subject line: "2026 Turbocharger: [Your Name]".
  2. Briefly tell us about one specific time your attention to detail caught a technical error before it became a problem and email to kevin@cascadiadesign.ca.

Job Types: Full-time, Permanent Work Location: In person
