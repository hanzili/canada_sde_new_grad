Full Description

Full-Time (Seasonal) Junior Geologist / Core Logger

(Work location: Northwest British Columbia)

JOB DESCRIPTION

Brixton Metals Corporation is a publicly traded (TSXV: BBB) (OCTQB: BBBXF), Canadian mineral exploration company focused on the advancement of its copper-gold-silver exploration projects. Brixton Metals is hiring for its Thorn Project (gold-copper).

We are currently seeking a Junior Geologist / Core Logger for May - October 2026 (anticipated).

RESPONSIBILITIES

Duties may include, but are not limited to, the following:

  • All aspects of core logging (including mineralization, alteration lithology) procedures, moving core boxes, analyzing and interpreting drill core, collecting photographs and following sampling protocols;
  • Preparing sample submissions to assay laboratories following established QAQC procedures;
  • Preparing quick logs of core;
  • Participating in daily geologist team meetings to discuss properties of the drill core;
  • Downloading and integrating logging data into database;
  • Collection of soil, silt, stream and/or rock samples for regional geochemical survey may be required;
  • Additional work in and around the work site as required; and
  • Demonstrate awareness and commitment to health, safety, community and environmental protection in keeping with Brixton Metals' goals and objectives.

QUALIFICATIONS

  • Eligible to work in Canada at time of application.
  • Post secondary studies in Geosciences or equivalent.
  • 1-3 years of relevant work experience in a similar role.
  • Valid driver’s license.
  • Reliable with a strong work ethic.
  • Cooperative team player.

ADDITIONAL INFORMATION

  • Working at a remote, live-in camp which is only accessible by air.
  • Must be able to lift/move up to 50lbs throughout a work shift.
  • Ability to work in areas of extreme and difficult weather conditions.
  • Rotational schedule is required (long work rotations).
  • Work location: Northwest British Columbia (near Whitehorse, YT).

HOW TO APPLY:

Professional reference check is required.
