About the Role

About Major Motion Association

Entry-level sales role with coaching and growth

Full Description

We are continuing to expand throughout British Columbia, creating new opportunities for motivated individuals looking to gain hands-on experience in a fast-paced, people-focused environment.

We are currently hiring Entry Level Sales Associates to support our continued growth and join our team at our brand-new Abbotsford, BC office!

This is a customer-facing role where you will be speaking directly with people, introducing widely used services and solutions, and helping customers explore options that fit their needs and lifestyle.

We are looking for individuals who are professional, dependable, comfortable working with customers one-on-one, and motivated by results and personal growth. From the start, you will receive hands-on support and practical guidance from experienced team members.

Key Responsibilities:

  • Speak with customers directly throughout local communities
  • Introduce widely used services and solutions in a simple and professional way
  • Keep conversations clear, straightforward, and focused on customer needs
  • Answer questions and help customers feel informed and comfortable with their options
  • Turn customer interest into completed sales through professional and informative conversations
  • Maintain strong daily activity and consistent work habits
  • Work alongside your team to stay motivated, productive, and goal-oriented

Qualifications:

  • Full-time availability
  • Able to start within 1–2 weeks
  • Experience working in sales, customer support, or other customer-oriented environments is considered an asset
  • Positive attitude and willingness to learn
  • Strong communication and interpersonal skills
  • Reliable and professional
  • Comfortable working in a face-to-face, people-focused environment

What We Offer:

  • A strong entry-level opportunity to gain hands-on industry experience with ongoing guidance from experienced professionals
  • Uncapped earning potential and performance-based incentives
  • Ongoing support and hands-on coaching from experienced team members
  • Tools for success, including company-provided devices, branded business cards, CRM access, and ongoing sales support
  • Car allowance and travel support for qualifying team members
  • Opportunities for advancement into leadership and higher-responsibility roles
  • A supportive, team-oriented environment focused on consistency, growth, and professionalism
  • Company-sponsored travel opportunities, including industry conferences, luxury retreats, and fully paid vacations for top performers

This is a full-time position involving work throughout local communities.

If you are looking for a role where you can stay active, build confidence, and grow within a fast-paced environment, we welcome your application.

Job Types: Full-time, Permanent

Pay: $45,500.00-$62,400.00 per year

Work Location: In person
