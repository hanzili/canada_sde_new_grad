Junior Content Creator

at Foreshew Design Associates Inc.

About the Role

Capture, edit, and distribute on-brand content

About Foreshew Design Associates Inc.

Luxury architecture firm with rising media exposure.

Full Description

Most firms deliver a service. We deliver outcomes. At Foreshew Design Associates, we design and fully coordinate high-end homes that are built exactly as envisioned—no gaps, no guesswork, no watered-down ideas. Every project is a one-of-one response to its site, engineered to perform and built to last.

We don’t wait for recognition—we create it. Our in-house marketing team captures every stage and turns real projects into content that cuts through the noise and commands attention.

If you’re here, you’re not on the sidelines—you’re shaping the brand. You move fast, think sharp, and create work people can’t ignore.

Role Overview

This is not a passive creative role. This is a production-heavy, execution-first position focused on documenting, editing, and distributing content that elevates Foreshew Design Associates as a category leader in luxury residential architecture.

Core Responsibilites

Content Capture (Field + Office)

  • Assist in filming on-site project visits, walkthroughs, and team interactions
  • Assist in setting up shots, framing, lighting, and audio for short-form and long-form content
  • Travel to active job sites as needed

Content Editing & Production

  • Edit short-form video (Reels, Shorts, TikTok-style) with speed and precision
  • Assist in editing long-form YouTube content (story structure, pacing, basic cuts)
  • Add captions, music, overlays, and branding elements aligned with FDA’s voice
  • Organize raw and edited files in a structured, scalable system

Brand Alignment

  • Learn and apply Foreshew’s brand voice
  • Avoid generic trends that dilute positioning
  • Maintain consistency across all visual and written outputs

Team Support & Workflow

  • Work directly under Marketing Manager
  • Take feedback quickly and apply it without friction
  • Assist in building repeatable systems for content production

Required Skills & Traits

Non-Negotiables

  • Strong video editing ability (Premiere Pro, Final Cut, or equivalent)
  • Basic understanding of composition, lighting, and framing
  • Highly organized with digital file management
  • Comfortable being on job sites and around construction environments
  • Need to know general film terminology and process

Mindset

  • Fast executor (speed > perfection)
  • Takes direction well without needing constant hand-holding
  • High ownership mentality—if something’s off, you fix it
  • No ego around feedback

Benefits & Perks

  • Full-time, permanent position (40 hours/week)
  • Semi-flexible work schedule
  • Extended health benefits
  • Exposure to high-end architectural projects and environments
  • Direct mentorship from a high-performing marketing team
  • Opportunity to grow

Please send a sample portfolio of past/current work for review.

Salary can range from experience.
