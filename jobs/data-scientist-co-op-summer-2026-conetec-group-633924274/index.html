ABOUT THE COMPANY

ConeTec is an industry-leading provider of geotechnical site investigation services and specializes in the testing of subsurface conditions in unique environments. As a member of our growing research team, the Data Scientist Co-op will assist in developing and applying advanced data analytics and machine learning models to enhance our understanding of geotechnical data. They will collaborate closely with engineers and scientists to analyze large datasets, generate insights, and contribute to innovative solutions for complex subsurface challenges. This role provides a unique opportunity to gain hands-on experience in cutting-edge geotechnical research and to make a meaningful impact in the field.
