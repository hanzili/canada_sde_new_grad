Full Description

*Junior Accountant, Residential Finance Toronto, ON Salary range: $55,000 - $65,000/yr Who are we?* Established in 1974, CMLS Financial is the leading provider of capital solutions for residential and commercial property owners, developers and real estate investors. We have over 50 years of experience, 30,000 clients, $45 billion in assets under administration, and offices coast to coast. Some of Canada's most prominent banks, investment managers, insurance companies, and pension funds rely on CMLS Financial for a variety of critically important services. Building on our solid foundation of financial strength and experience, we continue to deliver unparalleled capital solutions for all our clients needs.

About The Role The Junior Accountant will work closely with the Finance team to record and analyze the transactions relating to

our Residential and Commercial Capital Markets divisions. Reporting to the Finance Manager, Capital Markets, the

Junior Accountant will support our dynamic team of accounting professionals by performing daily and monthly

accounting activities.

In This Role, You Will

  • Assist in the monthly financial reporting process for the Residential & Commercial Capital Markets divisions, including preparing accrual entries, analyzing results, drafting monthly MD&As, and presenting findings to the Finance team;
  • Prepare daily warehouse drawdown and repayment requests;
  • Monitor upcoming balance sheet fundings, renewals, and warehouse/TRS facility capacity;
  • Prepare, instruct, and verify cash transfers, accounting entries, and EFT and wire payments;
  • Monitor the shared finance email inbox on a daily basis to ensure that requests are being addressed in a timely manner;
  • Assist with the month-end close process;
  • Actively participate in development projects to increase efficiencies and improve internal processes;
  • Perform other related duties as requested.

What You Will Need *Professional Qualifications and Experience*

  • 0-2 years of related work experience in accounting and/or finance;
  • Undergraduate degree in one of the following concentrations: business administration, accounting, or finance;
  • Proficiency in Microsoft Excel;
  • Prior experience with an accounting software and the ability to analyze and investigate the general ledger is an asset;
  • Working towards CPA designation considered an asset.

Required Skills And Abilities

  • You possess strong organizational skills with a high attention to detail;
  • You have excellent time management skills and understand the importance of deadlines;
  • You take ownership of your work and demonstrate a high degree of accountability and commitment at all times;
  • You have excellent communication skills, both written and verbal, and are able to work well independently as well as within a team environment;
  • You have superior problem-solving skills and the ability to analyze various data sets;
  • You are a driven individual who has a strong desire to learn;
  • You are a quick learner with a curious mindset;
  • You possess strong process and systems skills.
