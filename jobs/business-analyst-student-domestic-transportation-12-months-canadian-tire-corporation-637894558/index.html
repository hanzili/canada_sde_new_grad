About the Role

About Canadian Tire Corporation

Full Description

Our Commitment to Students At Canadian Tire, we are committed to providing students with a supportive and collaborative environment that fosters knowledge sharing and offers key experiences to help them develop their capabilities through projects and objectives. We believe in setting specific and measurable goals that align with our business objectives to support learning and help students achieve their full potential. Our culture emphasizes learning from others, continuous improvement, agility, growth, and innovation, and we are invested in building a talented, diverse workforce for the future of Canada and Canadian Tire. Additionally, we are dedicated to building strong relationships with our student employees by engaging with them throughout their education and career pursuits and creating opportunities for ongoing communication and relationship building.

What You'll Do Analytics & Reporting:

  • Analyze a wide range of operational topics—from straightforward trends to complex, multi‑variable challenges—and present clear findings, insights, and recommendations.
  • Produce and distribute regular daily, weekly, monthly, and quarterly reporting for stakeholders across Domestic Transportation, senior leadership, CTR Associate Dealers, and carrier partners.
  • Translate raw data into meaningful visualizations and insights that support operational decision‑making.

Process Improvement

  • Identify opportunities to reduce supply chain costs and enhance service performance by applying continuous improvement techniques to transportation processes.
  • Map current‑state workflows, evaluate gaps, and recommend improvements supported by data and operational understanding.
  • Partner with operations teams to implement and monitor changes that drive measurable results.

Project Support & Solution Development

  • Participate in identifying business needs and developing recommendations for analytical tools, reporting enhancements, and process improvements.
  • Work collaboratively with the team to design, build, test, and launch new solutions and tools.
  • Prepare clear documentation, presentations, and summaries of project outcomes and analytical insights.
  • Present findings, risks, and recommendations to various stakeholders, ensuring alignment and understanding across functions.

What You Bring

  • Currently enrolled in a Post‑secondary program in a relevant field such as Data Science, Engineering, Business, Supply Chain, or Mathematics
  • 1–3 years of experience in data analytics, business analytics, supply chain, logistics, transportation, or a related field.
  • Foundational Python programming skills, with experience using libraries such as Pandas and NumPy; willingness to learn more advanced tools (e.g., scikit‑learn).
  • Working knowledge of SQL, including basic querying and experience with relational databases (e.g., SQL Server, Access).
  • Experience building reports and dashboards using tools like Power BI, Tableau, or Excel.
  • Strong mathematics and statistics fundamentals, with the ability to apply analytical thinking to real business questions.
  • Exposure to or strong interest in machine learning concepts, including data preparation, feature engineering, and model evaluation.
  • Strong business acumen, with the ability to understand operational challenges and identify analytical solutions.
  • Excellent communication and presentation skills, able to explain analytical findings in clear, simple terms to non‑technical stakeholders.
  • Demonstrated ability to manage multiple tasks, prioritize effectively, and meet deadlines with minimal supervision.
  • Familiarity with project management principles, process analysis, or continuous improvement methodologies is an asset.
