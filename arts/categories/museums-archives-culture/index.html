Frequently Asked Questions

What cultural-sector jobs fit history or humanities students?

Museum assistant, archival assistant, gallery coordinator, library assistant, heritage program assistant, collections assistant, and cultural programming roles are common fits.

Are museum and archive roles usually paid?

Many entry roles are paid, but unpaid internships also exist. Hanzilla aims to surface paid student-friendly and early-career postings.
