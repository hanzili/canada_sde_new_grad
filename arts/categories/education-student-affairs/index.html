Frequently Asked Questions

What education roles do not require a teaching license?

Education program coordinator, student affairs assistant, academic advising support, curriculum assistant, learning coordinator, and youth program roles may not require teacher certification.

What experience helps for student-affairs jobs?

Campus leadership, tutoring, orientation work, residence life, peer mentoring, club operations, and clear student-facing communication experience are useful signals.
