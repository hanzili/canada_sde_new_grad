Frequently Asked Questions

What research roles fit humanities and social-science students?

Research assistant, research analyst, evaluation assistant, survey research coordinator, market research analyst, and program evaluation roles are common early-career paths.

Are research roles only academic?

No. Many research jobs sit in nonprofits, governments, consulting firms, healthcare organizations, market research teams, and social-impact companies.
