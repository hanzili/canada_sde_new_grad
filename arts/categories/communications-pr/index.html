Frequently Asked Questions

What communications jobs are realistic for Arts new grads?

Look for communications assistant, PR coordinator, public affairs intern, media relations assistant, content coordinator, and community relations roles with clear support responsibilities.

Do communications roles require a communications degree?

Not always. Employers often value writing samples, campus media, club leadership, research, bilingual ability, and public-facing experience from Arts and social-science programs.
