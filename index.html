Frequently Asked Questions

How often are new jobs added?

Our job listings are updated daily at 09:00 UTC. We use automated pipelines to fetch fresh positions across tech, finance, and other industries from across Canada, ensuring you see the latest opportunities.

What types of jobs are listed?

We cover entry-level and junior positions across multiple industries. In tech, that includes Software Engineering, Data Science/ML, DevOps/SRE, Mobile Development, and more. In finance, we list Investment Banking, Accounting, FP&A, and Capital Markets roles. Both new grad and internship positions are available, and we're always expanding into new categories.

Are these jobs only for Canadian citizens?

Most positions are open to candidates with valid Canadian work authorization. Some employers sponsor work permits. Check individual job listings for specific requirements.

How do I apply for a job?

Click on any job listing to see full details, then click 'Apply Now' to be redirected to the company's official application page. We don't handle applications directly.

What industries and cities do you cover?

We currently cover tech and finance roles, with more industries coming soon. Our listings span all major Canadian cities including Toronto, Vancouver, Montreal, Ottawa, Calgary, and more, plus remote positions that let you work from anywhere in Canada.
