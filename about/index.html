About / verification

Hanzilla Jobs is a free Canadian student and recent-grad job board.

Built by Hanzi Li, a recent McGill Computer Science graduate, to help Canadian students find internships, co-ops, summer roles, new-grad, junior, and entry-level jobs across fields.

Browse current jobs Contact hello@hanzilla.co

What the site does

Hanzilla Jobs organizes public early-career postings for Canadian university students and recent graduates. The site covers internships, co-ops, summer student roles, new-grad, junior, and entry-level roles in tech, data, finance, business, engineering, sciences, lab/biotech, operations, and related fields.

The goal is simple: Canadian student jobs are scattered across company career pages, public job boards, city pages, and field-specific sources. Hanzilla Jobs brings those opportunities into one Canada-specific resource with field, city, internship, and new-grad pages.

Safety notes for students and career services teams

  • The site is free for students.
  • Students can browse jobs without creating an account.
  • Job pages link to the original employer or public application source.
  • Old or closed jobs are removed from active listing pages when detected.
  • Career services and student-resource teams should review the site before listing it publicly.
