How to use this sciences jobs board

This daily-updated page is built for Canadian students and recent grads searching for sciences internships and co-op roles: biology, biotech, lab, chemistry, environmental science, geoscience, clinical research, and science operations roles. Hanzilla gives science students a dedicated path for lab, research, environmental, biotech, quality, and field roles that are usually buried across university boards and employer career pages.

  • Use this page for Canadian science internships, lab assistant roles, research technician openings, biotech jobs, environmental field roles, geoscience roles, chemistry positions, and quality-assurance jobs.
  • Browse student and new-grad opportunities across universities, hospitals, labs, biotech companies, environmental consultancies, mining/geoscience employers, and public agencies.
  • Check posting dates, required lab/field skills, location, and work authorization before applying because science roles often have specific credential or site requirements.
  • Current listings include active openings from employers such as Teck Resources Limited, Finning, Suncor, Ontario Power Generation, targetjobs UK when those roles are available.
  • Check back daily: stale or closed jobs are removed from listings so the board stays useful for real applications.
